1. industry /ˈɪndəstri/ n. 工业；行业；勤勉
📖 英文解释
Economic activity concerned with the processing of raw materials and the manufacture of goods; a particular branch of economic activity.
✏️ 例句
The rise of the gig economy has disrupted traditional industry models and raised questions about workers' rights.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the ... industry ...行业（阅读高频结构：the fashion/tech industry） ★ heavy/light industry 重工业/轻工业 ★ industrial revolution 工业革命
🧠 助记
indus-（勤）+ try → 勤奋努力地做 → 工业（需要大量劳动）；也有「勤勉」之意。
industry::/ˈɪndəstri/:n. 工业:The tourism industry suffered greatly during the pandemic.
2. themselves /ðəmˈselvz/ pron. 他们自己；他们亲自（反身代词）
📖 英文解释
The reflexive form of 'they'; used when the subject and object of the action are the same people.
✏️ 例句
Students who teach themselves a foreign language often develop stronger problem-solving abilities than those who rely solely on classroom instruction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ by themselves 独自；靠自己（= alone / on their own） ★ in themselves 就其本身而言 teach/support themselves 自学/自力更生
🧠 助记
them + selves → 他们（them）的自我（selves）→ 他们自己。构词逻辑同 myself、yourself。
themselves::/ðəmˈselvz/:pron. 他们自己:They completed the project entirely by themselves.
3. process /ˈprɑːses/ n. 过程；进程；程序 v. 处理；加工
📖 英文解释
A series of actions or steps taken to achieve a particular end; to perform a series of operations on data or materials.
✏️ 例句
The process of globalization has accelerated cultural exchange while also intensifying economic competition between nations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in the process of 在…过程中 ★ decision-making process 决策过程 ★ process information 处理信息（认知科学语境）
🧠 助记
pro-（向前）+ cess（走，同 access）→ 向前走的步骤 → 过程。
process::/ˈprɑːses/:n. 过程:Learning a language is a long process requiring persistence.
4. rate /reɪt/ n. 比率；速率；费率 v. 评价；估价
📖 英文解释
A measure, quantity, or frequency expressed as a proportion of another; to assign a value or rank to something.
✏️ 例句
The unemployment rate has fallen to its lowest level in a decade, reflecting a significant recovery in the labor market.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at a rate of 以…的速度/比率 ★ growth/crime/birth rate 增长/犯罪/出生率 ★ rate sth as 把某事评为
🧠 助记
rate → r + ate（吃）→ 「吃」掉的速度 → 速率；引申为评分。
rate::/reɪt/:n. 比率:The literacy rate in the country rose sharply after the reform.
5. house /haʊs/ n. 房子；家庭；议院 v. 给…提供住所
📖 英文解释
A building for human habitation; to provide accommodation or storage space.
✏️ 例句
Rising house prices in major cities have made homeownership increasingly unaffordable for young professionals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ house prices 房价（社会话题热词） ★ housing market 房产市场 ★ the House of Representatives 众议院（政治文章）
🧠 助记
house → h + ouse → 联想汉字「屋」→ 房子。
house::/haʊs/:n. 房子:Many young adults cannot afford to buy a house.
6. culture /ˈkʌltʃər/ n. 文化；文明；培养；培植
📖 英文解释
The ideas, customs, and social behaviour of a particular people or society; the cultivation of bacteria or cells.
✏️ 例句
Understanding the culture of a society is essential for designing effective public health interventions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ cultural diversity 文化多样性 ★ pop culture 流行文化 ★ corporate culture 企业文化（管理学语境）
🧠 助记
cult-（耕种、培育）+ ure → 人类精心培育的精神成果 → 文化。agriculture（农业）同根。
culture::/ˈkʌltʃər/:n. 文化:Culture shapes how people perceive the world around them.
7. power /ˈpaʊər/ n. 力量；权力；功率；电力 v. 驱动；供电
📖 英文解释
The ability to do something or act in a particular way; political authority; energy produced by mechanical, electrical, or other means.
✏️ 例句
The concentration of economic power in the hands of a few corporations raises serious concerns about market competition.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ power to do sth 做某事的能力 ★ in power 执政；当权 ★ nuclear/solar power 核能/太阳能（环境话题）
🧠 助记
power → pow（打击声）+ er → 打出去的力 → 力量、权力。
power::/ˈpaʊər/:n. 力量:Renewable power sources are crucial for a sustainable future.
8. share /ʃɛr/ v. 分享；共享；分担 n. 份额；股份
📖 英文解释
To have, use, or give a portion of something; one of the equal parts into which a company's capital is divided.
✏️ 例句
Sharing economy platforms such as ride-hailing apps have transformed urban transportation by enabling individuals to share resources.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ share sth with sb 与某人分享某事 ★ market share 市场份额（商业阅读必考） ★ share the view/opinion 持相同观点
🧠 助记
share → sh + are → 「are」（是）的部分 → 各自的一份 → 分享、份额。
share::/ʃɛr/:v. 分享:The company's market share grew by 15% last year.
9. million /ˈmɪljən/ num./n. 百万；大量
📖 英文解释
The number 1,000,000; an extremely large number or amount.
✏️ 例句
Over three million people are expected to attend the annual cultural festival this year, making it the largest in the country's history.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ millions of 数以百万计的（强调大量） ★ one in a million 百里挑一 billion（十亿）、trillion（万亿）数量级词同组记忆
🧠 助记
mill（磨）+ ion → 磨了一百万转 → 百万。联想 millionaire（百万富翁）。
million::/ˈmɪljən/:num./n. 百万:Millions of people worldwide lack access to clean water.
10. lot /lɒt/ n./adv. 很多；大量；抽签；地块
📖 英文解释
A large number or amount; to divide into lots; a plot of land.
✏️ 例句
A lot of research has been conducted on the relationship between sleep deprivation and cognitive performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a lot of / lots of 大量（口语书面都常用） ★ quite a lot 相当多 ★ a lot 作副词修饰动词：improve a lot
🧠 助记
lot → 联想「乐透」（lottery）→ 抽签一次赢很「多」→ 大量。
lot::/lɒt/:n./adv. 很多:She has done a lot to improve community education.
11. sense /sens/ n. 感觉；意义；判断力 v. 感知；意识到
📖 英文解释
A faculty by which the body perceives an external stimulus; meaning; good judgment or wisdom.
✏️ 例句
In a broad sense, education encompasses not only formal schooling but also the learning that takes place throughout life.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ make sense 有意义；讲得通（超高频） ★ in a sense 在某种意义上 ★ sense of responsibility/identity 责任感/身份认同
🧠 助记
sens-（感，同 sensitive）+ e → 感知 → 感觉；引申为「感觉到的意义」。
sense::/sens/:n. 感觉:It makes sense to invest in education early.
12. lose /luːz/ v. 失去；迷失；输掉；亏损
📖 英文解释
To be deprived of or cease to have something; to fail to win; to become unable to find.
✏️ 例句
Communities that lose their traditional industries often struggle to attract new investment and rebuild their economies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ lose sight of 忽视；忘记目标 ★ lose one's way 迷失方向（比喻义） ★ lose vs. loose 注意拼写区别
🧠 助记
lose → l + ose → 联想「唉~哦（o-se）」→ 输了叹气的声音 → 失去、输掉。
lose::/luːz/:v. 失去:We cannot afford to lose focus on the key issues.
13. against /əˈɡenst/ prep. 反对；违反；靠着；对比
📖 英文解释
In opposition to; in contact with; in contrast or competition with.
✏️ 例句
The new legislation was passed despite strong opposition from environmental groups who argued against further industrial expansion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be against sth 反对某事（最常见） ★ argue/vote against 争辩/投票反对 ★ against a background of 在…背景下（书面高频）
🧠 助记
a-gainst → 「gain」（获得）被「a-」否定 → 不让你获得 → 反对、对抗。
against::/əˈɡenst/:prep. 反对:Many citizens voted against the proposed tax increase.
14. rise /raɪz/ v. 上升；升起；增长 n. 上涨；兴起
📖 英文解释
To move from a lower position to a higher one; to increase in number, level, or strength.
✏️ 例句
The rise of social media has fundamentally altered the way political campaigns are conducted and how voters receive information.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ give rise to 引起；导致（书面高频搭配） ★ rise to power 掌权 ★ on the rise 在上升中（形容趋势）
🧠 助记
rise → r + ise → 联想「日升」（日/sun rises）→ 太阳升起 → 上升。
rise::/raɪz/:v. 上升:Temperatures have been on the rise for the past decade.
15. energy /ˈenərdʒi/ n. 精力；能量；能源
📖 英文解释
The strength and vitality required for sustained physical or mental activity; power derived from physical or chemical resources.
✏️ 例句
Transitioning to renewable energy sources is widely regarded as the most effective strategy for combating climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ renewable/clean energy 可再生/清洁能源（环保话题必考） ★ energy consumption 能源消耗 ★ full of energy 精力充沛
🧠 助记
en-（进入）+ ergy（工作，同 work/erg）→ 进入工作状态的驱动力 → 能量、精力。
energy::/ˈenərdʒi/:n. 精力:Solar energy is becoming increasingly cost-effective.
16. control /kənˈtroʊl/ v./n. 控制；管理；抑制
📖 英文解释
To exercise authoritative or dominating influence over something; the power to direct or restrain something.
✏️ 例句
The government introduced stricter regulations to control carbon emissions from industrial facilities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ under control 处于控制之中 ★ out of control 失控 ★ control over/of 对…的控制
🧠 助记
con-（一起）+ trol（转，同 roll）→ 把所有方向都转过来 → 全面掌控。
control::/kənˈtroʊl/:v./n. 控制:Effective leaders know how to control their emotions.
17. space /speɪs/ n. 空间；太空；间距 v. 隔开
📖 英文解释
A continuous area or expanse which is free, available, or unoccupied; the universe beyond the earth's atmosphere.
✏️ 例句
Urban planners argue that green spaces in cities are essential for residents' mental health and well-being.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ outer space 外太空 ★ living/working space 生活/工作空间 ★ space for 留有…空间（比喻义：space for creativity）
🧠 助记
space → s + pace（步）→ 走几步就能到达的「空间」 → 空间、太空。
space::/speɪs/:n. 空间:Cities need more open space for community activities.
18. ability /əˈbɪlɪti/ n. 能力；才能；才干
📖 英文解释
The capacity to do something; the possession of the means or skill to do something.
✏️ 例句
Critical reading is an ability that students must develop in order to succeed in graduate-level academic work.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the ability to do sth 做某事的能力（最常见结构） ★ ability in/at 在某方面的能力 ★ natural/innate ability 天生能力
🧠 助记
able（能够）+ -ity（名词后缀）→ 能够做的程度 → 能力。
ability::/əˈbɪlɪti/:n. 能力:Children have an amazing ability to learn new languages.
19. animal /ˈænɪməl/ n. 动物；兽 adj. 动物的
📖 英文解释
A living organism that feeds on organic matter, typically having specialized sense organs and nervous system.
✏️ 例句
The rapid destruction of natural habitats threatens thousands of animal species with extinction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ animal rights 动物权益（伦理话题） ★ animal behavior/instinct 动物行为/本能 endangered animal 濒危动物
🧠 助记
anim-（生命、灵魂，同 animate）+ al → 有生命的 → 动物。
animal::/ˈænɪməl/:n. 动物:Animal experiments are strictly regulated in many countries.
20. enough /ɪˈnʌf/ adj./adv. 足够的；充分地
📖 英文解释
As much or as many as required; to the required degree or extent.
✏️ 例句
Many critics argue that governments are not doing enough to address the root causes of poverty and inequality.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ enough to do sth 足以做某事 ★ not ... enough 不够…（adj./adv. + enough） ★ sure enough 果然（书面常用副词短语）
🧠 助记
e + nough → 联想「已经够了（e-nough）」→ 够了，足够。nough 谐音「够」。
enough::/ɪˈnʌf/:adj./adv. 足够的:There is not enough funding for public education.
21. value /ˈvæljuː/ n. 价值；重要性；价值观 v. 重视；估价
📖 英文解释
The regard that something is held to deserve; the importance or usefulness of something; principles and standards.
✏️ 例句
Economic analyses rarely capture the full value of ecosystem services such as clean water and biodiversity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ place/attach value on 重视（某事） ★ core values 核心价值观 ★ face value 表面价值；票面价值
🧠 助记
val-（强壮、价值，同 valid）+ ue → 有价值的东西 → 价值；引申为重视。
value::/ˈvæljuː/:n. 价值:Society should place greater value on environmental protection.
22. week /wiːk/ n. 周；星期
📖 英文解释
A period of seven days; the period from Monday to Friday as contrasted with the weekend.
✏️ 例句
The government announced that the new policy would come into effect within two weeks of parliamentary approval.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ this/last/next week 本/上/下周 ★ week after week 一周又一周（表持续） ★ on a weekly basis 每周（副词短语）
🧠 助记
week → w + eek → 联想「一周七天就一声 eek（叹气）」→ 过了一个星期。
week::/wiːk/:n. 周:Students should review vocabulary at least once a week.
23. individual /ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl/ adj. 个人的；独特的 n. 个人；个体
📖 英文解释
Relating to a single person or thing rather than to a group; a single human being as distinct from a group.
✏️ 例句
While collective action is necessary, individual choices about consumption and lifestyle also play a significant role in reducing environmental impact.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ individual rights 个人权利 ★ on an individual basis 逐个地 ★ individual vs. society 个人与社会（哲学/社会常考对比）
🧠 助记
in-（不）+ divid-（分）+ ual → 不可再分割的单位 → 个人、个体。divide（分割）同根。
individual::/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl/:adj. 个人的:Each individual is responsible for their own learning.
24. term /tɜːrm/ n. 条款；期限；学期；术语 v. 称为
📖 英文解释
A word or phrase used to describe a thing or express a concept; a fixed period of time; conditions under which action is taken.
✏️ 例句
The contract included several terms that restricted the company's ability to hire from competitors.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in terms of 就…而言（超高频，写作必备） ★ long-term / short-term 长期/短期 ★ term of office 任期
🧠 助记
term → t + erm → 联想「这个…嗯（erm）…术语」→ 术语；也联想到「期限」像期末（term）。
term::/tɜːrm/:n. 条款:In terms of quality, this product is superior.
25. away /əˈweɪ/ adv. 离开；在远处；消失
📖 英文解释
To or at a distance from a particular place, person, or thing; so as to be removed or gone.
✏️ 例句
As rural populations continue to move away from agriculture, governments must find ways to support displaced workers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ give away 放弃；赠送；泄露 ★ take/throw away 拿走/扔掉 ★ far away 遥远的（常修饰地点）
🧠 助记
a + way → 向那条「路（way）」走去 → 离开、远去。
away::/əˈweɪ/:adv. 离开:Young people are moving away from rural areas to cities.
