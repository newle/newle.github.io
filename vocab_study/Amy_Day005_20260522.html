1. fall /fɔːl/ v. 下降；倒下；降落 n. 秋天；下降；瀑布
📖 英文解释
To move downward under the force of gravity; to decrease in amount or level; the autumn season.
✏️ 例句
Global stock markets fell sharply following the announcement of new trade tariffs between major economies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ fall behind 落后（学业/工作） ★ fall into 陷入（困境） ★ fall short of 未达到（期望/标准）
🧠 助记
fall → 联想：「瀑布（waterfall）」往下「落」 → 下降、秋天（万物凋落）。
fall::/fɔːl/:v. 下降:Temperatures fall sharply in the northern regions during winter.
2. art /ɑːrt/ n. 艺术；美术；技巧；人文学科
📖 英文解释
The expression of human creative skill, typically in visual form; the various branches of creative activity; skill in doing something.
✏️ 例句
The integration of arts education into school curricula has been shown to foster creativity and improve academic performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ work of art 艺术作品 ★ arts and sciences 文科与理科 ★ the art of ... 做某事的艺术/技巧（如 the art of communication）
🧠 助记
art → ar（联想「啊」感叹）+ t → 让人发出「啊！」感叹的东西 → 艺术。
art::/ɑːrt/:n. 艺术:Great art often reflects the social issues of its time.
3. decision /dɪˈsɪʒən/ n. 决定；决议；决策
📖 英文解释
A conclusion or resolution reached after consideration; the action of deciding something.
✏️ 例句
The board's decision to cut funding for the research department sparked widespread criticism from the scientific community.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ make a decision 做出决定（最常见搭配） ★ reach/come to a decision 达成决定 ★ decision-making process 决策过程
🧠 助记
de-（向下）+ cis-（切，同 scissors）+ ion → 切断犹豫 → 决定。
decision::/dɪˈsɪʒən/:n. 决定:Making an informed decision requires careful analysis.
4. interest /ˈɪntrəst/ n. 兴趣；利息；利益 v. 使感兴趣
📖 英文解释
A feeling of wanting to learn about something; money paid regularly for the use of money lent; advantage or benefit.
✏️ 例句
Conflicts of interest within regulatory agencies can undermine the effectiveness of consumer protection policies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ take/show interest in 对…感兴趣 ★ in the interest(s) of 为了…的利益 ★ interest rate 利率（经济话题）
🧠 助记
inter-（在中间）+ est → 夹在中间有「最强烈的」吸引力 → 兴趣；银行把钱放中间生的利息。
interest::/ˈɪntrəst/:n. 兴趣:A conflict of interest should always be declared upfront.
5. drive /draɪv/ v. 驾驶；驱使；推动 n. 驱动；冲劲；车道
📖 英文解释
To operate a motor vehicle; to force or compel someone to act; a strong motivation or ambition.
✏️ 例句
Competition is often cited as the key force that drives innovation in technology-intensive industries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ drive sb to do sth 驱使某人做某事 ★ drive innovation/growth 驱动创新/增长 ★ hard drive 硬盘（计算机语境）
🧠 助记
drive → d + rive → 联想「河流（river）」推动物体向前 → 驱使、驾驶。
drive::/draɪv/:v. 驾驶:Curiosity drives scientific discovery.
6. issue /ˈɪʃuː/ n. 问题；争议；期号；发布 v. 发布；发行
📖 英文解释
An important topic or problem for debate or discussion; to supply or distribute officially; an edition of a periodical.
✏️ 例句
Climate change remains the most pressing environmental issue facing humanity in the 21st century.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ address/tackle an issue 处理问题（写作高频动宾） ★ raise an issue 提出问题 ★ key/major issue 关键/重大问题
🧠 助记
issue → i（一）+ ssue（说）→ 一说就是个「问题」 → 问题；或：issue = 「发射（issue orders）」。
issue::/ˈɪʃuː/:n. 问题:The government must address the issue of housing affordability.
7. produce /prəˈdjuːs/ (v.) /ˈprɒdjuːs/ (n.)/ v. 生产；产生；出示；制作 n. 农产品
📖 英文解释
To make or manufacture something; to cause something to happen; fresh fruits and vegetables.
✏️ 例句
Developing countries often struggle to produce enough food to meet their growing populations' nutritional needs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ produce evidence/results 提供证据/产生结果 ★ mass-produce 大量生产 ★ produce emissions 产生排放（环境话题）
🧠 助记
pro-（向前）+ duce（引导，同 educate）→ 引导出来 → 生产、产生。
produce::/prəˈdjuːs/ (v.) /ˈprɒdjuːs/ (n.)/:v. 生产:The factory produces over 10,000 units per day.
8. complete /kəmˈpliːt/ adj. 完全的；完整的 v. 完成；填写
📖 英文解释
Having all the necessary or appropriate parts; to finish making or doing something.
✏️ 例句
The research team worked for three years to complete the study, which was eventually published in a leading scientific journal.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ complete a task/project 完成任务/项目 ★ complete with 配有…（= including） ★ a complete failure/success 彻底的失败/成功
🧠 助记
com-（一起全部）+ plete（充满，同 plenty）→ 全部充满 → 完整、完成。
complete::/kəmˈpliːt/:adj. 完全的:Students must complete all assignments to pass the course.
9. activity /ækˈtɪvɪti/ n. 活动；活跃；行动
📖 英文解释
The condition in which things are happening or being done; a specific thing that a person or group does.
✏️ 例句
Physical activity in childhood is strongly associated with improved academic performance and social development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ physical/outdoor activity 体育/户外活动 ★ economic activity 经济活动 ★ extracurricular activity 课外活动
🧠 助记
act-（行动）+ ivity → 行动状态 → 活动。active（积极的）同根。
activity::/ækˈtɪvɪti/:n. 活动:Regular physical activity reduces the risk of chronic disease.
10. national /ˈnæʃənəl/ adj. 国家的；全国的 n. 国民
📖 英文解释
Relating to or characteristic of a nation; common to or characteristic of a whole nation.
✏️ 例句
The national curriculum was revised to include more emphasis on digital literacy and critical thinking skills.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ national security 国家安全 ★ national identity 国家认同 ★ national interest 国家利益（政治话题常见）
🧠 助记
nation（国家）+ -al（形容词后缀）→ 属于国家的 → 国家的。
national::/ˈnæʃənəl/:adj. 国家的:The national literacy campaign reached millions of people.
11. brain /breɪn/ n. 大脑；脑力；聪明的人 v. 用力击头
📖 英文解释
The organ of soft nervous tissue enclosed in the skull, functioning as the coordinating centre of sensation and intellectual activity.
✏️ 例句
Neuroscientists have found that the human brain remains capable of forming new neural connections well into old age.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ brain drain 人才外流（社会经济话题必考） ★ brain activity 大脑活动 ★ use your brain 动脑筋（非正式）
🧠 助记
brain → br（啤酒 beer）+ ain（联想「爱」）→ 人类「爱喝啤酒但伤大脑」 → 大脑（警示记忆）。
brain::/breɪn/:n. 大脑:Regular reading stimulates brain activity and memory.
12. level /ˈlevəl/ n. 水平；等级；层次 adj. 平坦的 v. 使平坦
📖 英文解释
A position on a scale of amount, quantity, extent, or quality; a horizontal plane or line.
✏️ 例句
Rising sea levels caused by global warming pose a serious threat to low-lying coastal communities worldwide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at all levels 在各个层面 ★ on a ... level 在某层面上（intellectual/emotional level） ★ level playing field 公平竞争的环境
🧠 助记
level → 对称词（正着反着都是 level）→ 平衡、平坦 → 水平、等级。
level::/ˈlevəl/:n. 水平:The noise levels in the city have become increasingly concerning.
13. win /wɪn/ v. 赢得；获胜；获得 n. 胜利
📖 英文解释
To be successful or victorious in a contest or conflict; to acquire or secure as a result of a contest.
✏️ 例句
Scientists who win prestigious awards often use their platform to advocate for increased funding for basic research.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ win over 说服；争取到（支持） ★ win support/approval 赢得支持/认可 ★ win-win situation 双赢局面
🧠 助记
win → w（双手举起）+ in（进入）→ 双手举起冲进终点 → 赢。
win::/wɪn/:v. 赢得:Teamwork is often the key to winning competitions.
14. present /ˈprezənt/ (n./adj.) /prɪˈzent/ (v.)/ adj. 现在的；出席的 n. 礼物；现在 v. 呈现；提出；颁发
📖 英文解释
Existing or occurring now; in a particular place; to bring to the attention of; a gift.
✏️ 例句
The chairman presented the annual report to shareholders, highlighting the company's strong performance in the present fiscal year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ present sth to sb 向某人呈递某物 ★ at present 目前（= currently） ★ present with 提供；呈现（sb is presented with a challenge）
🧠 助记
pre-（在前面）+ sent（送）→ 送到眼前的 → 礼物；当下眼前的 → 现在。
present::/ˈprezənt/ (n./adj.) /prɪˈzent/ (v.)/:adj. 现在的:The researcher presented her findings at the international conference.
15. quality /ˈkwɒlɪti/ n. 质量；品质；特性 adj. 优质的
📖 英文解释
The standard of something as measured against other things; a distinctive attribute or characteristic.
✏️ 例句
Access to quality education is widely regarded as one of the most powerful tools for reducing poverty and inequality.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ quality of life 生活质量（高频词组） ★ quality control 质量控制 ★ high/poor quality 高/低质量
🧠 助记
qual-（怎样，同 how in Latin）+ ity → 「怎样」的程度 → 质量、特性。
quality::/ˈkwɒlɪti/:n. 质量:Water quality in many urban areas needs significant improvement.
16. difficult /ˈdɪfɪkəlt/ adj. 困难的；棘手的
📖 英文解释
Needing much effort or skill to accomplish, deal with, or understand; characterised by hardships.
✏️ 例句
It is difficult to overestimate the importance of early childhood education in shaping a person's long-term cognitive development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ it is difficult to do sth 做某事是困难的（形式主语结构） ★ difficult to deal with 难以处理 ★ face difficult challenges 面临艰难挑战
🧠 助记
dif-（不）+ ficult → 联想「non-faculty（没能力）」→ 对能力要求高 → 困难的。
difficult::/ˈdɪfɪkəlt/:adj. 困难的:Solving environmental problems requires difficult policy choices.
17. easy /ˈiːzi/ adj. 容易的；舒适的 adv. 轻松地
📖 英文解释
Achieved without great effort; free from difficulty; comfortable and relaxed.
✏️ 例句
While technological solutions to climate change may seem easy in theory, implementing them on a global scale presents significant challenges.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ easy to do 容易做 ★ take it easy 放轻松（口语） ★ easier said than done 说起来容易做起来难（谚语）
🧠 助记
easy → e + asy → 联想「Easy！一声唉（e）就过去了」→ 容易。
easy::/ˈiːzi/:adj. 容易的:There are no easy answers to complex social problems.
18. tend /tend/ v. 趋向于；倾向于；照料
📖 英文解释
To regularly or frequently behave in a particular way; to care for or look after someone.
✏️ 例句
People who grow up in urban environments tend to have different attitudes toward nature compared to those raised in rural areas.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ tend to do sth 倾向于做某事（最高频结构） ★ tend toward 趋向于（名词宾语） ★ tend a garden/patient 打理花园/照料病人
🧠 助记
tend → t + end → 最终走向哪个「end（终点）」→ 趋向于。
tend::/tend/:v. 趋向于:Students tend to perform better when they are intrinsically motivated.
19. reduce /rɪˈdjuːs/ v. 减少；降低；简化
📖 英文解释
To make smaller or less in amount, degree, or size; to bring to a certain state.
✏️ 例句
Implementing energy-efficient technologies in manufacturing could significantly reduce carbon emissions without sacrificing productivity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ reduce sth by... 减少某数量（reduce costs by 20%） ★ reduce the risk/impact of 降低…风险/影响 ★ reduce to 减少到；归结为
🧠 助记
re-（回）+ duce（引导）→ 引导回去（更小的量）→ 减少。
reduce::/rɪˈdjuːs/:v. 减少:Recycling helps reduce the amount of waste sent to landfills.
20. expect /ɪkˈspekt/ v. 预料；期待；要求
📖 英文解释
To regard something as likely to happen; to anticipate or look forward to something; to require something as right or due.
✏️ 例句
Analysts expect that global demand for renewable energy will triple over the next two decades.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ expect to do sth 期望做某事 ★ be expected to 被期望；预计会（被动高频） ★ as expected 正如预期
🧠 助记
ex-（向外）+ spect（看，同 spectacle）→ 向外看着等待 → 期待、预料。
expect::/ɪkˈspekt/:v. 预料:Students are expected to submit their thesis by the end of the term.
21. law /lɔː/ n. 法律；法则；规律；法学
📖 英文解释
The system of rules that a particular country or community recognizes as regulating the actions of its members; a statement of fact that is invariably true.
✏️ 例句
Environmental activists are pushing for stronger laws to penalize companies that illegally dispose of industrial waste.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ obey/break the law 遵守/违反法律 ★ pass a law 通过一项法律 ★ law of nature 自然规律（科学语境）
🧠 助记
law → l + aw → 联想：违法要「挨打（aw！）」→ 法律就是规矩。
law::/lɔː/:n. 法律:New environmental laws require companies to disclose their emissions.
22. environment /ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt/ n. 环境；自然环境；氛围
📖 英文解释
The surroundings or conditions in which a person, animal, or plant lives; the natural world as a whole.
✏️ 例句
Protecting the environment requires coordinated international action, as pollution and climate change do not respect national borders.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ protect/preserve the environment 保护环境（必背） ★ learning environment 学习环境 ★ hostile/supportive environment 恶劣/有利环境
🧠 助记
en-（使）+ viron（圈，同 viral/around）+ ment → 围绕着我们的东西 → 环境。
environment::/ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt/:n. 环境:A positive classroom environment promotes student engagement.
23. paper /ˈpeɪpər/ n. 纸；论文；试卷；报纸 v. 贴墙纸
📖 英文解释
Material in thin sheets, used for writing on or wrapping; a newspaper; a scholarly article.
✏️ 例句
The researcher published a paper in a leading journal arguing that social inequality is the primary driver of poor health outcomes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ academic/research paper 学术/研究论文（考研语境最重要） ★ on paper 在纸面上；理论上 ★ paper over 掩盖（问题）

paper → pa（爸爸）+ per（通过）→ 爸爸通过考「试卷（paper）」→ 试卷。
paper::/ˈpeɪpər/:n. 纸:Her paper on climate policy was widely cited by researchers.
24. mind /maɪnd/ n. 思维；想法；意见 v. 介意；注意；照看
📖 英文解释
The element of a person that enables them to be aware of the world and their experiences; to be bothered by something.
✏️ 例句
Some philosophers argue that the relationship between mind and body remains one of the most profound unsolved problems in science.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ keep/bear in mind 记住；牢记 ★ change one's mind 改变主意 ★ mind the gap 注意差距（伦敦地铁语；引申义很广）

mind::/maɪnd/:n. 思维:Keep in mind that consistent effort leads to long-term success.
25. kid /kɪd/ n. 孩子；小孩 v. 开玩笑；欺骗

A child or young person; to deceive someone in a playful way.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

kid::/kɪd/:n. 孩子:Every kid deserves access to quality education.
