1. economy /ɪˈkɒnəmi/ n. 经济；节约
📖 英文解释
the system by which a country's money and goods are produced and used; careful management of resources
✏️ 例句
The global economy has been severely disrupted by a series of geopolitical conflicts and trade disputes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ economic adj. 经济的（注意与 economical 节约的 区分） ★ market economy 市场经济 ★ economy of scale 规模经济
🧠 助记
eco（生态/家庭）+ nomy（管理）→ 管理家庭资源 → 经济
economy::/ɪˈkɒnəmi/:n. 经济:The country's economy grew by 5% last year.
2. percent /pəˈsent/ n./adv. 百分之……
📖 英文解释
one part in every hundred; a proportion expressed as a fraction of 100
✏️ 例句
Approximately sixty percent of respondents agreed that environmental policy needed urgent reform.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ percentage n. 百分比（注意区分 percent 和 percentage） ★ a high/low percentage 高/低百分比 percent 后接单复数根据数字决定
🧠 助记
per（每）+ cent（一百）→ 每一百 → 百分比
percent::/pəˈsent/:n./adv. 百分之……:Eighty percent of students passed the exam.
3. sleep /sliːp/ n./v. 睡眠；睡觉
📖 英文解释
a natural state of rest for the body and mind; to be in this state
✏️ 例句
Insufficient sleep has been shown to negatively affect memory consolidation and academic performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ sleep deprivation 睡眠剥夺（健康话题） ★ get enough sleep 获得充足睡眠 ★ go to sleep / fall asleep 入睡
🧠 助记
sleep → sl（慢慢地）+ eep → 慢慢地闭上眼睛 → 睡觉
sleep::/sliːp/:n./v. 睡眠:Adults need at least seven hours of sleep per night.
4. recent /ˈriːsnt/ adj. 近来的；近期的
📖 英文解释
having happened or begun only a short time ago
✏️ 例句
Recent studies suggest that mindfulness meditation can significantly reduce levels of academic stress.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in recent years 近年来（写作开篇常用） ★ recent developments 近期进展 recently adv. 最近
🧠 助记
re（回）+ cent（百）→ 回头看最近一百天 → 近来
recent::/ˈriːsnt/:adj. 近来的:In recent years, online learning has become increasingly popular.
5. half /hɑːf/ n./adj./adv. 一半；半
📖 英文解释
one of two equal parts of something; to a certain degree
✏️ 例句
More than half of the surveyed students reported feeling overwhelmed by academic workload.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ half of sth 一半的…… ★ half the time 一半时间 halve v. 减半（词形变化）
🧠 助记
half = ha（哈）+ lf → 哈哈，分成两半 → 一半
half::/hɑːf/:n./adj./adv. 一半:Half of the participants chose option A.
6. attention /əˈtenʃn/ n. 注意；关注；照料
📖 英文解释
the mental focus on something; special care or treatment
✏️ 例句
Researchers have drawn public attention to the long-term consequences of social media addiction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pay attention to 注意……（最高频搭配） ★ draw/attract attention 引起注意 ★ attention to detail 对细节的关注
🧠 助记
at + tent（帐篷）+ ion → 在帐篷前站好注意 → 注意
attention::/əˈtenʃn/:n. 注意:Please pay attention to the instructions carefully.
7. body /ˈbɒdi/ n. 身体；主体；机构
📖 英文解释
the physical structure of a person or animal; the main part; an organization
✏️ 例句
Regular physical activity benefits not only the body but also cognitive function and mental health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a governing body 管理机构 ★ body language 肢体语言（写作表达） ★ the body of an essay 文章主体
🧠 助记
body → 支撑你的整个人 → 身体
body::/ˈbɒdi/:n. 身体:A healthy body supports a strong mind.
8. measure /ˈmeʒər/ v./n. 衡量；测量；措施
📖 英文解释
to find the size or amount of something; an action to achieve a goal
✏️ 例句
The government implemented a series of measures to reduce carbon emissions and combat climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ take measures to do 采取措施做……（写作常用） ★ measure sth by/against 用……衡量 ★ beyond measure 无法衡量；极度
🧠 助记
mea（我的）+ sure（确定）→ 我确定了尺寸 → 测量
measure::/ˈmeʒər/:v./n. 衡量:The school took measures to improve exam preparation.
9. office /ˈɒfɪs/ n. 办公室；职务；机关
📖 英文解释
a room for administrative work; a position of authority; a government department
✏️ 例句
The new policy was announced by the Office of the President following months of consultation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in office 在职（政治话题） ★ take office 就职 ★ office hours 办公时间
🧠 助记
off（离开）+ ice → 从冰上下来进入温暖的办公室
office::/ˈɒfɪs/:n. 办公室:She works in a government office downtown.
10. travel /ˈtrævl/ v./n. 旅行；传播
📖 英文解释
to go from one place to another; to move or spread over a distance
✏️ 例句
Frequent international travel exposes students to diverse cultural perspectives and broadens their worldview.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ travel abroad/overseas 出国旅行 ★ travel broadens the mind 旅行开阔眼界（谚语） traveler n. 旅行者
🧠 助记
travel = trav（旅）+ el（走）→ 到处走 → 旅行
travel::/ˈtrævl/:v./n. 旅行:Travel can teach you things that books cannot.
11. major /ˈmeɪdʒər/ adj./n./v. 主要的；专业；主修
📖 英文解释
greater in size or importance; a student's main subject of study; to study as a main subject
✏️ 例句
Major changes in educational policy often take years to have a measurable impact on student outcomes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ major in 主修（大学专业） ★ major problem/challenge 主要问题/挑战（写作） ★ majority n. 多数
🧠 助记
major = ma（妈）+ jor → 妈妈最重要 → 主要
major::/ˈmeɪdʒər/:adj./n./v. 主要的:Her major challenge was balancing work and study.
12. professor /prəˈfesər/ n. 教授
📖 英文解释
a senior academic teacher at a university or college
✏️ 例句
The professor's latest research on cognitive bias has attracted considerable attention from the scientific community.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ associate professor 副教授 ★ professor of 某领域的教授 profession n. 职业（词形辨析）
🧠 助记
profess（宣称）+ or → 宣称懂知识的人 → 教授
professor::/prəˈfesər/:n. 教授:The professor gave an inspiring lecture on history.
13. natural /ˈnætʃrəl/ adj. 自然的；天然的；本能的
📖 英文解释
existing in nature; not artificial; instinctive or expected
✏️ 例句
It is natural that students feel anxious before high-stakes examinations like the postgraduate entrance exam.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ it is natural that 自然而然……（写作句式） ★ natural resources 自然资源 ★ naturally adv. 自然地；当然
🧠 助记
nature（自然）+ al → 大自然的 → 自然的
natural::/ˈnætʃrəl/:adj. 自然的:It is natural to feel nervous before an exam.
14. remain /rɪˈmeɪn/ v. 保持；剩余；留下
📖 英文解释
to continue to be in the same state; to be left after others have gone
✏️ 例句
Despite technological advances, many fundamental questions about consciousness remain unanswered by science.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ remain + adj. 保持某种状态（linking verb） ★ remain to be seen 有待观察（写作表达） ★ the remaining 剩余的
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ main（主要）→ 主要部分再次留下 → 保持；剩余
remain::/rɪˈmeɪn/:v. 保持:The question remains whether this policy will succeed.
15. though /ðəʊ/ conj./adv. 尽管；虽然；但是
📖 英文解释
despite the fact that; however; used to introduce a contrasting statement
✏️ 例句
Though the sample size was small, the findings provided valuable preliminary insights into the topic.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ even though 即使（强调） ★ as though 好像（方式状语从句） ★ though = although（though 更口语化）
🧠 助记
though…还是 → 尽管（转折）
though::/ðəʊ/:conj./adv. 尽管:Though he was tired, he kept studying.
16. design /dɪˈzaɪn/ v./n. 设计；构思；图案
📖 英文解释
to plan and create something for a specific purpose; the art of making plans
✏️ 例句
A well-designed research methodology is crucial for producing valid and reliable academic findings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be designed to do 被设计用来…… ★ by design 故意地 ★ design a system/plan 设计系统/方案
🧠 助记
de（强调）+ sign（标志）→ 强调标志的规划 → 设计
design::/dɪˈzaɪn/:v./n. 设计:This course is designed to improve academic writing.
17. history /ˈhɪstri/ n. 历史；来历
📖 英文解释
the study of past events; a record of past events related to a person or thing
✏️ 例句
Throughout history, social revolutions have often been preceded by widespread dissatisfaction with existing institutions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ throughout history 纵观历史（写作开篇） ★ make history 创造历史 ★ historical adj. 历史的（vs. historic 有历史意义的）
🧠 助记
his + story → 他的故事 → 历史
history::/ˈhɪstri/:n. 历史:China has a rich and long history.
18. nation /ˈneɪʃn/ n. 国家；民族
📖 英文解释
a large body of people united by common descent, culture, or language; a country
✏️ 例句
The United Nations plays a crucial role in mediating conflicts between member nations worldwide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ national adj. 国家的；民族的 ★ nationwide adv. 全国范围内 ★ nation-state 民族国家
🧠 助记
nat（出生）+ ion → 同一出生地的人 → 民族；国家
nation::/ˈneɪʃn/:n. 国家:Every nation has its own cultural traditions.
19. almost /ˈɔːlməʊst/ adv. 几乎；差不多
📖 英文解释
very nearly but not completely or exactly
✏️ 例句
Almost all participants in the survey agreed that work-life balance was their top priority.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ almost all 几乎所有（写作替换 most） ★ almost + never/nothing 几乎从不/没有 ★ almost = nearly（可互换）
🧠 助记
al（全部）+ most（最多）→ 几乎全部 → 几乎
almost::/ˈɔːlməʊst/:adv. 几乎:She has almost finished her research paper.
20. customer /ˈkʌstəmər/ n. 顾客；客户
📖 英文解释
a person who buys goods or services from a business
✏️ 例句
Companies are increasingly using big data to better understand and predict customer behavior and preferences.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ customer satisfaction 客户满意度 ★ loyal customer 忠实顾客 ★ customer service 客户服务（考研经济类话题）
🧠 助记
custom（习惯）+ er → 习惯来这里买东西的人 → 顾客
customer::/ˈkʌstəmər/:n. 顾客:The store provides excellent customer service.
21. subject /ˈsʌbdʒɪkt/ n. /səbˈdʒekt/ v. n./v. 科目；主题；使遭受
📖 英文解释
a branch of knowledge studied; the topic of discussion; to cause someone to experience something
✏️ 例句
The subject of artificial intelligence ethics has become increasingly prominent in academic discourse.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ subject to sth 受……约束；可能遭受 ★ on the subject of 关于某个话题 ★ be subject to change 有可能变动
🧠 助记
sub（下）+ ject（扔）→ 被扔到下面 → 科目（下层知识）
subject::/ˈsʌbdʒɪkt/ n. /səbˈdʒekt/ v.:n./v. 科目:Mathematics is a compulsory subject for all students.
22. focus /ˈfəʊkəs/ v./n. 聚焦；专注；焦点
📖 英文解释
to concentrate attention or effort on something; the center of interest or activity
✏️ 例句
The essay should focus on analyzing the causes of inequality rather than merely describing its effects.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ focus on 专注于（最高频搭配） ★ in focus / out of focus 清晰/模糊 ★ the focus of attention 注意焦点
🧠 助记
focus → fo（佛）→ 像佛一样专注 → 聚焦
focus::/ˈfəʊkəs/:v./n. 聚焦:Focus on understanding, not just memorizing.
23. really /ˈrɪəli/ adv. 确实；真正地；非常
📖 英文解释
in actual fact; used for emphasis; to a great extent
✏️ 例句
The experiment's results really challenged the assumptions that had dominated the field for decades.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ really = actually / indeed（语气词） ★ not really 不完全是 real adj. / reality n.（词族）
🧠 助记
real（真实）+ ly → 真实地 → 确实，真正
really::/ˈrɪəli/:adv. 确实:She really worked hard to pass the entrance exam.
24. certain /ˈsɜːtn/ adj. 确定的；某些
📖 英文解释
having no doubt; specific but not explicitly named
✏️ 例句
It is certain that technological change will continue to reshape the labor market in coming decades.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be certain that 确定…… ★ make certain 确保 ★ certainly adv. 当然；肯定地
🧠 助记
cert（确定）+ ain → 全部确定 → 确定的
certain::/ˈsɜːtn/:adj. 确定的:Make certain you review all key vocabulary before the exam.
25. success /səkˈses/ n. 成功；成就
📖 英文解释
the accomplishment of an aim or purpose; a person or thing that achieves desired results
✏️ 例句
Academic success is not solely determined by intelligence but also by persistence and effective study habits.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ achieve/attain success 取得成功 ★ key to success 成功的关键 ★ successful adj. / succeed v.（词族）
🧠 助记
suc（下）+ cess（走）→ 走向成功的台阶 → 成功
success::/səkˈses/:n. 成功:Hard work is the key to success.
