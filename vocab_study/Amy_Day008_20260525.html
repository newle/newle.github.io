1. store /stɔː/ v./n. 储存；商店
📖 英文解释
to keep something for future use; a retail establishment
✏️ 例句
Modern cloud systems can store vast amounts of data without requiring physical infrastructure.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ store data/information 存储数据/信息 ★ in store for sb 等待着某人（有好/坏事） storage n. 储存；仓储
🧠 助记
store → 开辟空间来储存东西
store::/stɔː/:v./n. 储存:The library stores thousands of academic journals.
2. check /tʃek/ v./n. 检查；核对；支票
📖 英文解释
to examine something to verify its accuracy; to restrain; a written order for money
✏️ 例句
Before submitting your research paper, always check your citations for accuracy and consistency.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ check in/out 登记入住/离开 ★ check on 查看……情况 ★ double-check 仔细复核
🧠 助记
check 谐音切克 → 切开来看看 → 检查
check::/tʃek/:v./n. 检查:Please check your answers before handing in the paper.
3. deal /diːl/ v./n. 处理；交易；发牌
📖 英文解释
to take action to solve a problem; a business transaction; to distribute cards
✏️ 例句
The government must deal with the rising inequality between urban and rural populations effectively.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ deal with 处理（最常用搭配） ★ a great deal of 大量（修饰不可数名词） ★ make a deal 达成交易
🧠 助记
deal → 大量地处理事务
deal::/diːl/:v./n. 处理:We need to deal with this problem right away.
4. opportunity /ˌɒpəˈtjuːnɪti/ n. 机会；时机
📖 英文解释
a time when a particular situation makes it possible to do something
✏️ 例句
Studying abroad provides students with a valuable opportunity to develop both academic and interpersonal skills.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ seize/grasp an opportunity 抓住机会 ★ opportunity to do sth 做……的机会 ★ equal opportunity 平等机会（社会话题）
🧠 助记
opport（接近）+ unity（统一）→ 靠近一致的时机 → 机会
opportunity::/ˌɒpəˈtjuːnɪti/:n. 机会:Don't miss the opportunity to improve yourself.
5. relationship /rɪˈleɪʃnʃɪp/ n. 关系；联系；恋爱关系
📖 英文解释
the way two or more people or things are connected; an emotional connection between people
✏️ 例句
The relationship between economic development and environmental protection remains a central debate in policy research.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ relationship between A and B A与B之间的关系 ★ build/maintain a relationship 建立/维持关系 relate v. / relation n.（词形辨析）
🧠 助记
relate（关联）+ ion + ship → 关联状态的船 → 关系
relationship::/rɪˈleɪʃnʃɪp/:n. 关系:There is a strong relationship between reading and writing skills.
6. usually /ˈjuːʒuəli/ adv. 通常；平常
📖 英文解释
under normal or typical circumstances; more often than not
✏️ 例句
Students who usually review vocabulary daily tend to perform significantly better on standardized tests.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ usually = generally / typically（写作替换） ★ more than usually 超乎寻常地 usual adj. 通常的（as usual 照常）
🧠 助记
use（使用）+ ual + ly → 平时经常使用的方式 → 通常
usually::/ˈjuːʒuəli/:adv. 通常:She usually wakes up early to study.
7. average /ˈævərɪdʒ/ adj./n./v. 平均的；平均值
📖 英文解释
the result obtained by adding several quantities and dividing by their number; typical or ordinary
✏️ 例句
The average income of urban residents has grown considerably faster than that of rural populations over the past decade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on average 平均来说（写作常用） ★ above/below average 高于/低于平均水平 ★ average out 平均分配
🧠 助记
aver（均匀）+ age → 均匀地分配到每个年龄 → 平均
average::/ˈævərɪdʒ/:adj./n./v. 平均的:On average, students spend four hours studying each day.
8. although /ɔːlˈðəʊ/ conj. 尽管；虽然
📖 英文解释
in spite of the fact that; even though
✏️ 例句
Although the sample was limited, the study's findings were consistent with broader patterns in the literature.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ although / though 引导让步状语从句 ★ although 不可与 but 同用 ★ although vs. despite（although 接句，despite 接名词/动名词）
🧠 助记
al（全部）+ though（尽管）→ 全都如此尽管 → 虽然
although::/ɔːlˈðəʊ/:conj. 尽管:Although it was difficult, she never gave up.
9. eye /aɪ/ n./v. 眼睛；注视
📖 英文解释
the organ of sight; to look at carefully or with suspicion
✏️ 例句
Eye contact is an important aspect of nonverbal communication that varies significantly across cultures.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ keep an eye on 注意；监视 ★ in the eyes of 在……看来 ★ eye-catching 引人注目的
🧠 助记
eye 形状像眼睛 → 两个圆圈夹一个 i → 眼睛
eye::/aɪ/:n./v. 眼睛:Keep an eye on the time during the exam.
10. break /breɪk/ v./n. 打破；中断；休息
📖 英文解释
to separate into pieces by force; an interval of rest; to fail to comply with a rule
✏️ 例句
The study suggests that taking regular short breaks can significantly enhance concentration and learning efficiency.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ take a break 休息一下 ★ break through 突破 ★ break down 分解；崩溃
🧠 助记
break → br（断裂音）+ eak → 啪嗒一声断了 → 打破
break::/breɪk/:v./n. 打破:Take a ten-minute break after every hour of study.
11. common /ˈkɒmən/ adj. 普通的；共同的；常见的
📖 英文解释
occurring or found frequently; shared by two or more things; not special
✏️ 例句
It is common knowledge that regular exercise improves both physical health and academic concentration.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ common knowledge 常识（写作引用） ★ have sth in common 有共同之处 ★ commonly adv. 通常；普遍地
🧠 助记
com（共同）+ mon（人）→ 大家共同的 → 普通；共同
common::/ˈkɒmən/:adj. 普通的:It is common to feel nervous before a big exam.
12. condition /kənˈdɪʃn/ n. 条件；情况；状况
📖 英文解释
the state of something; a factor necessary before something else is possible
✏️ 例句
Under optimal learning conditions, students demonstrated significantly higher retention of complex material.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on condition that 条件是……（写作结构） ★ living/working conditions 生活/工作条件 ★ conditional adj. 有条件的
🧠 助记
con（加强）+ dition（说法）→ 强调的说法 → 条件
condition::/kənˈdɪʃn/:n. 条件:The experiment was conducted under controlled conditions.
13. behavior /bɪˈheɪvjər/ n. 行为；举止；表现
📖 英文解释
the way a person or animal acts in response to a situation
✏️ 例句
Research in behavioral economics challenges the assumption that human decision-making is always rational.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ human behavior 人类行为（写作话题） ★ good/bad behavior 良好/不良行为 ★ behavioral adj. 行为的
🧠 助记
be（是）+ have（有）+ ior → 拥有某种方式存在 → 行为
behavior::/bɪˈheɪvjər/:n. 行为:His study behavior improved dramatically this semester.
14. global /ˈɡləʊbl/ adj. 全球的；全面的
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
globe（地球仪）+ al → 地球的 → 全球的
global::/ˈɡləʊbl/:adj. 全球的:Global cooperation is essential to tackle environmental problems.
15. difference /ˈdɪfrəns/ n. 不同；差异；差别

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

difference::/ˈdɪfrəns/:n. 不同:There is a big difference between knowing and doing.
16. light /laɪt/ adj./n./v. 轻的；光；点亮
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
In light of recent evidence, the committee decided to revise its initial recommendations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
light 既是光也是轻的 → 光是轻盈的 → 双重记忆
light::/laɪt/:adj./n./v. 轻的:In light of the new data, we revised our conclusion.

growth::/ɡrəʊθ/:n. 生长:The growth in online learning has been remarkable.
