1. fail /feɪl/ v. 失败；不及格；辜负 n. 不及格
📖 英文解释
To be unsuccessful; to not meet required standards; to let someone down.
✏️ 例句
The policy failed to address the root causes of poverty in rural communities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ fail to do 未能做到……（高频） ★ without fail 一定；必定 fail in 在……方面失败
🧠 助记
fail 谐音费了——费了很大劲还是失败了
fail::/feɪl/:v. 失败:The experiment failed to produce results.
2. influence /ˈɪnfluəns/ n./v. 影响；感化；势力
📖 英文解释
The capacity to have an effect on the character or behavior of someone; to affect.
✏️ 例句
Peer influence plays a significant role in shaping adolescents' values and behaviors.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ have an influence on 对……有影响 ★ under the influence of 在……影响下 influential adj. 有影响力的
🧠 助记
influ（流入）+ence→流入某人思想中→影响
influence::/ˈɪnfluəns/:n./v. 影响:Social media has great influence on youth.
3. avoid /əˈvɔɪd/ v. 避免；回避；躲避
📖 英文解释
To keep away from or stop oneself from doing something.
✏️ 例句
Companies must adopt sustainable practices to avoid long-term environmental damage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ avoid doing sth 避免做某事（后接 doing） ★ avoid conflict 避免冲突 avoidance n. 回避
🧠 助记
a+void（空）→留出空位→躲避
avoid::/əˈvɔɪd/:v. 避免:We should avoid making hasty decisions.

🧠 助记
muse（缪斯女神）+ic→缪斯女神给我们的礼物→音乐
music::/ˈmjuːzɪk/:n. 音乐:Music can express what words cannot.
5. add /æd/ v. 添加；补充说；增加
📖 英文解释
To join or unite; to say something further; to increase the quantity of.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ add to 增加；加剧 ★ in addition (to) 此外（过渡用语） add up 合计；累积
🧠 助记
add 就是数学加法——加上去
add::/æd/:v. 添加:The data adds weight to this argument.
6. policy /ˈpɒləsi/ n. 政策；方针；保险单
📖 英文解释
A course or principle of action adopted by a government or institution.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

7. item /ˈaɪtəm/ n. 项目；条款；一则（新闻）

explain::/ɪkˈspleɪn/:v. 解释:Please explain your reasoning clearly.
