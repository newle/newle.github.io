1. graduate /ˈɡrædʒuət/ n. 毕业生 v. 毕业；逐渐变化
📖 英文解释
A person who has successfully completed a course of study; to complete academic requirements.
✏️ 例句
Many graduates struggle to find employment that matches their qualifications in today's competitive market.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ graduate from 从……毕业 ★ graduate school 研究生院 college graduate 大学毕业生
🧠 助记
gradu（逐渐）+ate→一步步走→逐渐完成学业→毕业
graduate::/ˈɡrædʒuət/:n. 毕业生 v. 毕业:She graduated with honors last spring.
2. decade /ˈdekeɪd/ n. 十年；十年期
📖 英文解释
A period of ten years.
✏️ 例句
Over the past decade, China's economy has grown at an unprecedented rate, lifting millions out of poverty.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ over the past decade 在过去十年里（作文常用） ★ in recent decades 在近几十年里 a decade ago 十年前
🧠 助记
dec（十）+ade→十个年头→十年
decade::/ˈdekeɪd/:n. 十年:Technology has transformed lives this decade.

🧠 助记
med（治愈）+ical→用药治愈的→医学的
medical::/ˈmedɪkl/:adj. 医学的:Medical research has saved countless lives.
4. miss /mɪs/ v. 错过；思念；未命中 n. 小姐

🧠 助记
miss 联想迷失——迷失方向就错过了
miss::/mɪs/:v. 错过:Don't miss the deadline for applications.

train（火车）→火车沿轨道走→有规律的训练
train::/treɪn/:v. 训练:It takes time to train professionals.

return::/rɪˈtɜːn/:v./n. 返回:Investment in skills generates high returns.

🧠 助记
af+fect（做）→对某人某事做出动作→影响
affect::/əˈfekt/:v. 影响:Stress adversely affects academic performance.
