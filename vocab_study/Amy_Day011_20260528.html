1. translate /trænsˈleɪt/ v. 翻译；转化；解读
📖 英文解释
To express the sense of words in another language; to convert one thing to another.
✏️ 例句
Effective leadership requires the ability to translate vision into concrete actions and measurable results.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ translate ... into ... 翻译成；转化为 ★ translate theory into practice 将理论化为实践 translation n. 翻译
🧠 助记
trans（跨越）+late（携带）→跨越语言携带意思→翻译
translate::/trænsˈleɪt/:v. 翻译:Policies must translate into real change.
2. describe /dɪˈskraɪb/ v. 描述；描绘；形容
📖 英文解释
To give a detailed account in words; to characterize or portray.
✏️ 例句
The report describes a worrying trend of increasing income inequality in most developed economies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ describe ... as 将……描述为 ★ be best described as 最恰当地描述为 description n. 描述
🧠 助记
de+scribe（写）→写下→描述
describe::/dɪˈskraɪb/:v. 描述:She described the process step by step.
3. purpose /ˈpɜːpəs/ n. 目的；意图；决心
📖 英文解释
The reason for which something exists or is done; a sense of resolve or determination.
✏️ 例句
Education should serve the dual purpose of developing individuals and contributing to social progress.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on purpose 故意 ★ for the purpose of 为了……目的 serve a purpose 达到目的
🧠 助记
pur+pose（姿势）→保持某种姿势去做→有目的
purpose::/ˈpɜːpəs/:n. 目的:Each section serves a clear purpose.
4. appear /əˈpɪər/ v. 出现；似乎；发表
📖 英文解释
To come into sight; to seem to be; to be published.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ appear to do 似乎…… ★ it appears that 看来…… appearance n. 外观；出现
🧠 助记
ap+pear（梨）→梨突然出现了→出现
appear::/əˈpɪər/:v. 出现:New solutions appear to be emerging.
5. impact /ˈɪmpækt/ n./v. 影响；冲击
📖 英文解释
A marked effect or influence; to have a strong effect on.
✏️ 例句
The impact of digital technology on employment patterns is both disruptive and transformative.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ have an impact on 对……有影响 ★ negative/positive impact 负面/正面影响 high-impact 高影响力的
🧠 助记
im+pact（打包压）→用力打击→冲击、影响
impact::/ˈɪmpækt/:n./v. 影响:Climate change has a global impact.
6. period /ˈpɪəriəd/ n. 时期；阶段；句号
📖 英文解释
A length or portion of time; a punctuation mark.
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记

period::/ˈpɪəriəd/:n. 时期:This period marked a turning point.

attitude::/ˈætɪtjuːd/:n. 态度:A positive attitude leads to better outcomes.
