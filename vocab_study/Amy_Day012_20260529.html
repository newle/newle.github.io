1. bear /beər/ v. 携带；承受；生育；n. 熊
📖 英文解释
to carry or support; to endure something difficult; to give birth to
✏️ 例句
Society must bear the long-term costs of neglecting mental health infrastructure.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ bear in mind（记住）；bear the cost/burden（承担费用/负担）；bear fruit（取得成果）；考研高频多义词。
🧠 助记
bear（熊）→ 熊能carry重物、endure严寒 = 携带、承受。一词多义靠场景记忆。
bear::/beər/:v. 携带:Society must bear the long-term costs of neglecting mental health infrastructure.
2. importance /ɪmˈpɔːrtəns/ n. 重要性
📖 英文解释
the quality or condition of being important or significant
✏️ 例句
The report emphasized the importance of early intervention in addressing childhood poverty.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ attach importance to（重视）；of great importance（非常重要）；考研写作常用短语。
🧠 助记
im-port-ance → port（携带/运送）+ ance，被大量运送的东西 = 重要的事。
importance::/ɪmˈpɔːrtəns/:n. 重要性:The report emphasized the importance of early intervention in addressing childhood poverty.
3. decline /dɪˈklaɪn/ v./n. 拒绝；下降；衰落
📖 英文解释
to refuse politely; to become smaller, fewer, or less; a gradual decrease
✏️ 例句
Birth rates have declined steadily in many developed countries over the past three decades.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ decline in（……的下降）；on the decline（在衰退中）；decline to do（婉拒做……）；考研图表作文常用词。
🧠 助记
de（向下）+ cline（斜坡）= 沿着斜坡向下 = 下降、衰落；向下走路叫摇头 = 拒绝。
decline::/dɪˈklaɪn/:v./n. 拒绝:Birth rates have declined steadily in many developed countries over the past three decades.
4. claim /kleɪm/ v. 声称；索取；n. 主张；索赔
📖 英文解释
to assert something as a fact; to demand something as one's right
✏️ 例句
The study claims that prolonged screen time negatively affects the sleep quality of teenagers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ claim that（声称……）；lay claim to（主张拥有）；insurance claim（保险索赔）；考研阅读中常标记作者立场。
🧠 助记
claim → 喊（cla-）出来（-im）自己的 = 声称/索取。
claim::/kleɪm/:v. 声称:The study claims that prolonged screen time negatively affects the sleep quality of teenagers.
5. model /ˈmɒdl/ n. 模型；典范；模特；v. 建模
📖 英文解释
a representation or simulation of something; a person or thing as an example to be imitated
✏️ 例句
Economists have developed complex models to predict the long-term effects of climate change on GDP.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ role model（榜样）；model after（仿照）；mathematical model（数学模型）；考研多义词，注意语境。
🧠 助记
mod（现代/模式）+ el → 展示现代模式的东西 = 模型/典范/模特。
model::/ˈmɒdl/:n. 模型:Economists have developed complex models to predict the long-term effects of climate change on GDP.
6. wrong /rɒŋ/ adj. 错误的；不道德的；adv. 错误地；n. 错事
📖 英文解释
not correct; unjust or dishonest; in an unsatisfactory manner
✏️ 例句
There is something fundamentally wrong with a system that punishes poverty rather than addressing its causes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ go wrong（出错）；wrong with（……有问题）；two wrongs don't make a right（以错制错行不通）。
🧠 助记
wrong → 联想'弯（w形）曲的绳（rong）'，弯曲的东西是不正确的。
wrong::/rɒŋ/:adj. 错误的:There is something fundamentally wrong with a system that punishes poverty rather than addressing its causes.
7. expert /ˈekspɜːrt/ n. 专家；adj. 专门的，熟练的
📖 英文解释
a person who has special knowledge or skill in a particular area
✏️ 例句
Leading experts in climate science warn that the window for meaningful action is rapidly closing.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ expert in/at（……方面的专家）；expert opinion（专家意见）；expertise（专业知识，名词）；考研必备词。
🧠 助记
ex（出来）+ pert（拉丁语expertus，经历过）= 经历过的人 = 专家。
expert::/ˈekspɜːrt/:n. 专家:Leading experts in climate science warn that the window for meaningful action is rapidly closing.
8. carry /ˈkæri/ v. 携带；传递；承担；通过（议案）
📖 英文解释
to hold and move someone or something from one place to another; to have with you
✏️ 例句
The new legislation was carried by a narrow majority in parliament after months of debate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ carry out（执行）；carry on（继续）；carry weight（有分量）；carry the day（取胜）；考研多义高频词。
🧠 助记
carry → 联想'卡（ca）入（rry）背包里带走' = 携带。
carry::/ˈkæri/:v. 携带:The new legislation was carried by a narrow majority in parliament after months of debate.
9. accept /əkˈsept/ v. 接受；承认；认可
📖 英文解释
to agree to receive or take something; to acknowledge a fact as true
✏️ 例句
It is widely accepted that early childhood education has a lasting impact on cognitive development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ accept that（承认……事实）；widely accepted（被普遍接受）；acceptable（可接受的）；考研写作表观点常用。
🧠 助记
ac（to）+ cept（take，拿取）= 向某物伸手拿取 = 接受。
accept::/əkˈsept/:v. 接受:It is widely accepted that early childhood education has a lasting impact on cognitive development.
10. potential /pəˈtenʃl/ adj. 潜在的；n. 潜力，潜能
📖 英文解释
having or showing the capacity to develop into something in the future; latent qualities or abilities
✏️ 例句
Renewable energy technologies have enormous potential to reduce greenhouse gas emissions globally.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ potential for（……的潜力）；unlock potential（释放潜力）；potential risk（潜在风险）；考研超高频形容词。
🧠 助记
pot（罐子）+ ent + ial → 罐子里装着隐藏的能量 = 潜力。
potential::/pəˈtenʃl/:adj. 潜在的:Renewable energy technologies have enormous potential to reduce greenhouse gas emissions globally.
11. degree /dɪˈɡriː/ n. 程度；学位；度（温度/角度）
📖 英文解释
the amount or level of something; an academic qualification; a unit of measurement for angles or temperature
✏️ 例句
To a large degree, success in academic life depends on the ability to think critically and independently.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ to a degree/to some degree（在某种程度上）；by degrees（逐渐地）；degree of（……的程度）；考研写作常见。
🧠 助记
de（强调）+ gree（grade，等级）= 等级/程度；大学颁发的等级证书 = 学位。
degree::/dɪˈɡriː/:n. 程度:To a large degree, success in academic life depends on the ability to think critically and independently.
12. resource /ˈriːsɔːrs/ n. 资源；财力；应对手段

★ natural resources（自然资源）；resource allocation（资源分配）；human resources（人力资源）；考研热词。
🧠 助记
re（再）+ source（来源）= 可以再次来源（被使用）的东西 = 资源。
resource::/ˈriːsɔːrs/:n. 资源:The allocation of scarce natural resources is a central challenge for sustainable development.
13. pause /pɔːz/ v./n. 暂停，停顿
📖 英文解释
to stop temporarily before continuing; a temporary stop or rest
✏️ 例句
The negotiators paused the talks to allow both sides to consult with their respective governments.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ give pause（使人犹豫）；pause for thought（停下来思考）；考研阅读叙事类文章中描写动作节奏常用。
🧠 助记
pause → 联想'泡（pau）滋（se）'，泡泡停在空中那一瞬间 = 暂停。
pause::/pɔːz/:v./n. 暂停，停顿:The negotiators paused the talks to allow both sides to consult with their governments.
14. opinion /əˈpɪnjən/ n. 意见，看法；舆论
📖 英文解释
a view or judgment formed about something; a belief or conclusion held with confidence
✏️ 例句
Public opinion has shifted significantly in favor of stricter gun control measures in recent years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in my/one's opinion（依我/某人看）；public opinion（舆论）；opinion poll（民意调查）；考研写作首段常用。
🧠 助记
opine（表达意见）+ ion → 表达出来的想法 = 意见。
opinion::/əˈpɪnjən/:n. 意见，看法:Public opinion has shifted significantly in favor of stricter gun control measures in recent years.
15. income /ˈɪnkʌm/ n. 收入，所得

🧠 助记
in（进入）+ come（来）= 来到你口袋里的钱 = 收入。
income::/ˈɪnkʌm/:n. 收入，所得:The widening gap between high and low income groups is a major driver of social instability.
16. amount /əˈmaʊnt/ n. 数量；总额；v. 总计；相当于
📖 英文解释
a quantity of something; to add up to a total; to be equivalent to
✏️ 例句
The amount of time students spend on social media has increased dramatically over the past decade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a large amount of（大量，修饰不可数名词）；amount to（总计；等同于）；考研语法中与quantity/number区分。
🧠 助记
a + mount（山）= 像山一样堆积的数量 = 数量/总量。
amount::/əˈmaʊnt/:n. 数量:The amount of time students spend on social media has increased dramatically over the past decade.

disease::/dɪˈziːz/:n. 疾病，病:Infectious disease prevention requires coordinated global efforts, as demonstrated during the COVID-19 pandemic.

argue::/ˈɑːrɡjuː/:v. 争论:Critics argue that over-reliance on standardized testing fails to capture students' full potential.
