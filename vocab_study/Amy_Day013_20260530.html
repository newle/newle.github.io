1. prove /pruːv/ v. 证明；证实；结果是
📖 英文解释
to demonstrate the truth or existence of something; to turn out to be
✏️ 例句
The experiment proved that the new drug was significantly more effective than existing treatments.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ prove to be（结果是/证明是）；prove one's point（证明论点）；proof（名词）；考研阅读中常见结果动词。
🧠 助记
prove → probe（探查）的近亲，通过探查来证明 = 证明。
prove::/pruːv/:v. 证明:The experiment proved that the new drug was significantly more effective than existing treatments.
2. compare /kəmˈpeər/ v. 比较；比喻；相比
📖 英文解释
to examine the similarities and differences between two things; to liken one thing to another
✏️ 例句
When comparing education systems internationally, Finland consistently ranks among the highest performers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ compare with/to（与……相比）；compared to（与……相比，引导状语）；beyond compare（无与伦比）；考研写作对比段必备词。
🧠 助记
com（一起）+ pare（准备，pair同源）= 把两样东西放在一起准备好 = 比较。
compare::/kəmˈpeər/:v. 比较:When comparing education systems internationally, Finland consistently ranks among the highest performers.
3. competition /ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃn/ n. 竞争；比赛
📖 英文解释
the activity of competing against others; an event in which people compete
✏️ 例句
Fierce competition in the global marketplace forces companies to continuously innovate and reduce costs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ intense/fierce competition（激烈竞争）；competition for（对……的争夺）；competitive（有竞争力的）；考研经济类核心词。
🧠 助记
com（共同）+ pete（追求/争取）+ ition = 大家共同争取 = 竞争。
competition::/ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃn/:n. 竞争:Fierce competition in the global marketplace forces companies to continuously innovate and reduce costs.
4. nothing /ˈnʌθɪŋ/ pron. 没有事；没有东西；adv. 一点也不
📖 英文解释
not anything; no thing of importance; not at all
✏️ 例句
Nothing short of fundamental reform will address the structural problems in the healthcare system.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ nothing but（只不过是）；nothing short of（简直是/完全）；for nothing（徒劳/免费）；考研中注意强调短语用法。
🧠 助记
no（没有）+ thing（事物）= 没有任何东西。
nothing::/ˈnʌθɪŋ/:pron. 没有事:Nothing short of fundamental reform will address the structural problems in the healthcare system.
5. perhaps /pərˈhæps/ adv. 也许，可能
📖 英文解释
used to express uncertainty or possibility; maybe
✏️ 例句
Perhaps the most significant challenge facing modern democracies is restoring public trust in institutions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ 考研写作中perhaps可软化绝对陈述，显示学术客观性；perhaps因此/perhaps more importantly（也许更重要的是）。
🧠 助记
per（通过）+ haps（运气，happen同源）= 通过运气决定 = 也许。
perhaps::/pərˈhæps/:adv. 也许，可能:Perhaps the most significant challenge facing modern democracies is restoring public trust in institutions.

🧠 助记
priv（剥夺，deprive同源）+ ate → 被剥夺公众权利的 → 私人的/秘密的。
private::/ˈpraɪvɪt/:adj. 私人的:The debate over private versus public healthcare provision remains politically divisive in many countries.

please::/pliːz/:v. 使满意:Companies increasingly struggle to please consumers whose expectations are shaped by digital convenience.
