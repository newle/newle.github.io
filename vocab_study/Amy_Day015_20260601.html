1. driver /ˈdraɪvər/ n. 驾驶员；驱动力；驱动程序
📖 英文解释
A person who drives a vehicle; a factor that causes something to happen; a program that controls a device.
✏️ 例句
Economic growth is often seen as the key driver of job creation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
driver of change/growth（变革/增长的驱动力）；device driver 设备驱动；backseat driver 瞎指挥的人
🧠 助记
drive（驾驶/驱动）+ r → 驾驶的人 → 驾驶员/驱动力
driver::/ˈdraɪvər/:n. 驾驶员:Innovation is the main driver of economic progress in modern societies.
2. oil /ɔɪl/ n. 石油，油；v. 加润滑油
📖 英文解释
A thick liquid from underground used as fuel; any greasy liquid; to put oil on something.
✏️ 例句
Oil prices fluctuated significantly due to geopolitical tensions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
oil crisis/price/spill；crude oil 原油；oil industry；oil the wheels 使顺利运作（习语）
🧠 助记
oil → 发音像哦有 → 哦，有油了 → 石油/油
oil::/ɔɪl/:n. 石油，油:The oil spill caused irreversible damage to the coastal ecosystem.
3. anything /ˈeniθɪŋ/ pron. 任何事（物），什么事（物）
📖 英文解释
Used in questions and negatives to refer to any thing, event, or situation; (in positive sentences) whatever.
✏️ 例句
She was willing to do anything to improve her grades.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
anything but（绝非）；if anything（如果有的话）；anything goes（一切皆可）；anything like（丝毫像）
🧠 助记
any（任何）+ thing（事物）→ 任何事物
anything::/ˈeniθɪŋ/:pron. 任何事（物），什么事（物）:The government must do anything possible to reduce carbon emissions.
4. access /ˈækses/ n./v. 进入，使用权；存取（数据）
📖 英文解释
The right or ability to enter a place or use something; to gain entry to a computer system.
✏️ 例句
Students from rural areas often lack access to quality education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
have/gain access to；access to information/education/healthcare；accessible adj.；accessibility n.
🧠 助记
ac（去）+ cess（走）→ 走进去 → 进入/使用权
access::/ˈækses/:n./v. 进入，使用权:Equal access to education is a fundamental human right.
5. sport /spɔːrt/ n. 体育运动；v. 炫耀地展示
📖 英文解释
A physical activity that requires skill and is governed by rules; to wear or display something proudly.
✏️ 例句
Participating in sport can improve both physical and mental health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
take up/do sport；sport a new look（炫耀新形象）；professional sport；sport science 运动科学
🧠 助记
sport → s + port（港口）→ 港口是运动汇聚地 → 体育
sport::/spɔːrt/:n. 体育运动:Sport plays a significant role in fostering national identity and unity.
6. exist /ɪɡˈzɪst/ v. 存在，生存
📖 英文解释
To be real; to live, especially in a difficult situation.
✏️ 例句
A wide gap exists between the rich and the poor in many societies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
exist in/as；existence n.（bring into existence 使产生）；existing adj. 现有的；coexist 共存
🧠 助记
ex（出来）+ ist（存在）→ 出来存在 → 存在
exist::/ɪɡˈzɪst/:v. 存在，生存:Significant inequality exists between urban and rural educational resources.
7. advantage /ədˈvɑːntɪdʒ/ n. 优势，有利条件；利益
📖 英文解释
A condition or circumstance that puts one in a better position; a benefit.
✏️ 例句
Having a second language is a distinct advantage in today's job market.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
take advantage of（利用）；have an advantage over；competitive advantage 竞争优势；to sb's advantage
🧠 助记
ad（朝向）+ vantage（视点高处）→ 朝向高点 → 优势
advantage::/ədˈvɑːntɪdʒ/:n. 优势，有利条件:Early childhood education provides a lasting advantage for cognitive development.
8. newspaper /ˈnjuːzpeɪpər/ n. 报纸
📖 英文解释
A daily or weekly publication on large sheets of paper that contains news, articles, and advertisements.
✏️ 例句
The newspaper ran a front-page story about the environmental crisis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
daily/weekly newspaper；newspaper article/column；print newspaper 纸质报纸；newspaper circulation 发行量
🧠 助记
news（新闻）+ paper（纸）→ 印新闻的纸 → 报纸
newspaper::/ˈnjuːzpeɪpər/:n. 报纸:The newspaper's investigative report triggered a national policy debate.
9. remember /rɪˈmembər/ v. 记得，记住；怀念
📖 英文解释
To have or keep an image in your mind of a past event or fact; to not forget to do something.
✏️ 例句
It is important to remember the lessons learned from historical mistakes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
remember doing（记得曾做过）；remember to do（记得要做）；in memory of（纪念）；memorable adj.
🧠 助记
re（再）+ member（成员）→ 再次想起成员 → 记得
remember::/rɪˈmembər/:v. 记得，记住:We must remember the sacrifices made by previous generations.
10. agree /əˈɡriː/ v. 同意，赞成；一致
📖 英文解释
To have the same opinion as someone; to say yes to a suggestion; to be consistent with something.
✏️ 例句
Both parties agreed to the terms of the contract after lengthy negotiations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
agree with/on/to/that；agree to disagree（求同存异）；agreement n.；disagreement n.
🧠 助记
a + gree（表示好感）→ 感觉好 → 同意
agree::/əˈɡriː/:v. 同意，赞成:All committee members agreed that urgent action was necessary.
11. manager /ˈmænɪdʒər/ n. 经理，管理者
📖 英文解释
A person who is responsible for controlling or administering all or part of a company or organization.
✏️ 例句
An effective manager knows how to motivate and empower the team.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
general/senior/project manager；management n.；manage v.；managerial adj. 管理的
🧠 助记
manage（管理）+ r → 管理的人 → 经理
manager::/ˈmænɪdʒər/:n. 经理，管理者:A good manager fosters collaboration and respects diverse perspectives.
12. land /lænd/ n. 陆地，土地；v. 着陆，降落；获得
📖 英文解释
The solid part of the earth's surface; to come down from the air onto the ground; to succeed in getting something.
✏️ 例句
The plane landed safely despite the heavy fog.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
land reform 土地改革；land on（降落在）；land a job 获得工作；the promised land 应许之地
🧠 助记
land → l + and → 陆地上
land::/lænd/:n. 陆地，土地:Land reform was essential for reducing rural poverty in the region.
13. solve /sɒlv/ v. 解决，解答
📖 英文解释
To find an answer to a problem or question; to deal successfully with a difficult situation.
✏️ 例句
Governments need to work together to solve global environmental problems.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
solve a problem/mystery/equation；solution n.；solvable adj.；resolve（更正式的解决）
🧠 助记
sol（太阳）+ ve → 太阳照亮让问题曝光解决 → 解决
solve::/sɒlv/:v. 解决，解答:Technology alone cannot solve the complex social problems we face today.
14. function /ˈfʌŋkʃn/ n. 功能，职能；函数；v. 运作，起作用
📖 英文解释
The purpose for which something is designed; a mathematical relationship; to work or operate properly.
✏️ 例句
The primary function of the immune system is to protect the body from disease.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
serve a function；function as（作为……发挥功能）；in function（在功能上）；functional adj.
🧠 助记
func（执行）+ tion → 执行的东西 → 功能/函数
function::/ˈfʌŋkʃn/:n. 功能，职能:The function of education extends beyond academic knowledge to social development.
15. management /ˈmænɪdʒmənt/ n. 管理，经营；管理层
📖 英文解释
The process of dealing with or controlling things or people; the people in charge of a company.
✏️ 例句
Effective time management is crucial for academic and professional success.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
management of；crisis/risk management；management style；under new management；senior management
🧠 助记
manage（管理）+ ment → 管理这件事 → 管理/经营
management::/ˈmænɪdʒmənt/:n. 管理，经营:Sound financial management is critical for any sustainable organization.
16. phrase /freɪz/ n. 短语，词组；习语；v. 措辞表达
📖 英文解释
A group of words that function as a unit in a sentence; a way of expressing something in words.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a catch phrase 口头禅；coin a phrase（创造短语）；to phrase it differently 换种说法；prepositional phrase 介词短语
🧠 助记
phrase → ph + raise → 提出词语 → 短语
phrase::/freɪz/:n. 短语，词组:The phrase sustainable development encapsulates a complex global agenda.
17. experiment /ɪkˈsperɪmənt/ n./v. 实验，试验
📖 英文解释
A scientific test done to discover or prove something; to try new things.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
conduct/carry out an experiment；experiment with（尝试）；experimental adj.；experimentally adv.
🧠 助记
ex（外）+ per（通过）+ ment → 通过外部过程 → 实验
experiment::/ɪkˈsperɪmənt/:n./v. 实验，试验:The scientists conducted a landmark experiment to disprove the old theory.
18. structure /ˈstrʌktʃər/ n. 结构，构造；组织；v. 构建，安排
📖 英文解释
The way in which something is organized or built; a building or other object constructed from parts.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
social/political/organizational structure；structure of；well-structured 结构严谨的；infrastructure 基础设施
🧠 助记

structure::/ˈstrʌktʃər/:n. 结构，构造:The organizational structure of the company was redesigned for greater efficiency.
19. everything /ˈevriθɪŋ/ pron. 每件事，一切

everything::/ˈevriθɪŋ/:pron. 每件事，一切:Everything in the policy proposal points toward a need for structural reform.
20. sit /sɪt/ v. 坐，位于；开庭；孵蛋

sit on（坐在……上/担任成员）；sit back（放松/袖手旁观）；sit for an exam 参加考试；sit in on 旁听
🧠 助记
sit → 坐下，简单直觉记忆
sit::/sɪt/:v. 坐，位于:The review board sits quarterly to evaluate project outcomes.

message::/ˈmesɪdʒ/:n. 消息，信息:The campaign's core message resonated with young voters across the country.

per::/pɜːr/:prep. 每，按照，经由:GDP per capita is a common measure of a country's economic well-being.
