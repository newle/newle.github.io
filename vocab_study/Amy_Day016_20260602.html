1. limit /ˈlɪmɪt/ n./v. 限制，极限；界限
📖 英文解释
The greatest or smallest amount that is possible or allowed; to restrict the size or number of something.
✏️ 例句
The new policy sets strict limits on carbon emissions from industrial plants.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
set/exceed a limit；limit to/on；limitation n.；limited adj.；unlimited 无限制的；off-limits 禁区
🧠 助记
lim（线）+ it → 画出界线 → 限制/极限
limit::/ˈlɪmɪt/:n./v. 限制，极限:Setting a limit on campaign financing can help reduce political corruption.
2. sometimes /ˈsʌmtaɪmz/ adv. 有时，偶尔
📖 英文解释
On some occasions; not always.
✏️ 例句
Sometimes the most effective solution to a problem is the simplest one.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sometimes + 句子；sometimes...sometimes...（有时……有时……）注意与 sometime（某时）区分
🧠 助记
some（一些）+ times（次数）→ 有一些次 → 有时
sometimes::/ˈsʌmtaɪmz/:adv. 有时，偶尔:Economic growth sometimes leads to greater inequality rather than shared prosperity.
3. fear /fɪər/ n./v. 恐惧，害怕；担心
📖 英文解释
An unpleasant feeling of worry or alarm about a danger or bad thing; to be afraid of something.
✏️ 例句
The fear of failure can sometimes motivate people to work harder.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fear of failure/change；for fear of（为了避免）；fearful adj.；fearless adj.；fear that（担心）
🧠 助记
fear → 谐音非儿 → 非常怕某事 → 恐惧
fear::/fɪər/:n./v. 恐惧，害怕:Many people act irrationally out of fear of losing their economic security.
4. supply /səˈplaɪ/ n./v. 供应，供给；提供
📖 英文解释
A stock or amount of something available; to provide what is needed.
✏️ 例句
The global supply of clean water is under increasing threat from pollution.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
supply and demand；supply chain 供应链；supply sb with；food/energy supply；supply-side economics 供应侧经济学
🧠 助记
sup（吃）+ ply（满足）→ 吃饱满足 → 供给
supply::/səˈplaɪ/:n./v. 供应，供给:Disruptions to the global supply chain led to widespread shortages of essential goods.
5. argument /ˈɑːrɡjumənt/ n. 争论，争议；论据，论点
📖 英文解释
A discussion or disagreement; a reason given to support or oppose a view.
✏️ 例句
The author presents a compelling argument for educational reform.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
argue v.；make/present an argument；argument for/against；counterargument 反驳；argumentative adj.
🧠 助记
argue（争论）+ ment → 争论这件事 → 论点/争论
argument::/ˈɑːrɡjumənt/:n. 争论，争议:The argument for stricter environmental regulations is supported by scientific evidence.
6. relate /rɪˈleɪt/ v. 讲述；使……相互关联；认同
📖 英文解释
To give an account of; to show a connection between two things; to understand or identify with something.
✏️ 例句
How does economic inequality relate to educational outcomes?
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
relate to（与……有关/认同）；related adj.；relation n.；relationship n.；relative adj.
🧠 助记
re（再）+ late（迟到）→ 再次联系上 → 讲述/关联
relate::/rɪˈleɪt/:v. 讲述:Poor nutrition is directly related to reduced cognitive performance in children.
7. effective /ɪˈfektɪv/ adj. 有效的；实际上的；生效的
📖 英文解释
Producing the intended result; actual as opposed to nominal; in operation.
✏️ 例句
Effective communication is essential for building strong professional relationships.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
effective in/at doing；cost-effective 划算的；effectiveness n.；take effect 生效；effective immediately
🧠 助记
effect（效果）+ ive → 有效果的 → 有效的
effective::/ɪˈfektɪv/:adj. 有效的:The most effective policies address root causes rather than symptoms.
8. prevent /prɪˈvent/ v. 阻止，预防
📖 英文解释
To stop something from happening; to keep someone from doing something.
✏️ 例句
Regular vaccinations help prevent the spread of infectious diseases.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prevent sb from doing；prevent disease/conflict；prevention n.；preventable adj. 可预防的
🧠 助记
pre（提前）+ vent（来）→ 提前到来阻止 → 预防/阻止
prevent::/prɪˈvent/:v. 阻止，预防:Early intervention programs are designed to prevent juvenile delinquency.
9. pattern /ˈpætərn/ n. 模式，规律；图案；v. 仿照
📖 英文解释
A repeated or regular way in which something happens; a decorative design; to model after something.
✏️ 例句
Researchers identified a clear pattern of income inequality across regions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
behavior/speech/sleep pattern；pattern of；follow a pattern；role pattern 角色模式
🧠 助记
pat（轻拍，重复）+ tern（飞鸟，规律迁徙）→ 重复规律 → 模式/图案
pattern::/ˈpætərn/:n. 模式，规律:There is a consistent pattern of gender disparity in leadership positions.
10. baby /ˈbeɪbi/ n. 婴儿，宝贝；adj. 小型的；v. 娇惯
📖 英文解释
A very young child; a term of endearment; to treat someone with excessive care.
✏️ 例句
The baby boom after World War II had a lasting impact on global demographics.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
baby boom 婴儿潮；baby steps 小步前进；baby boomer 婴儿潮一代；cry-baby 爱哭的人
🧠 助记
baby → 发音像贝贝 → 小婴儿的昵称 → 婴儿
baby::/ˈbeɪbi/:n. 婴儿，宝贝:Baby boomers are now entering retirement age, straining pension systems globally.
11. search /sɜːrtʃ/ v./n. 搜索，搜寻；调查
📖 英文解释
To look carefully for something; an act of looking for something.
✏️ 例句
Authorities launched a thorough search for the missing documents.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
search for/through；in search of；search engine 搜索引擎；body search 搜身；search warrant 搜查令
🧠 助记
sear（烤/灼）+ ch → 用眼光扫过 → 搜索
search::/sɜːrtʃ/:v./n. 搜索，搜寻:The committee conducted an extensive search for evidence of financial misconduct.
12. itself /ɪtˈself/ pron. 它自己，其本身（强调/反身）
📖 英文解释
Used as the object of a verb or preposition when referring to an animal or thing that is the subject; used to emphasize.
✏️ 例句
The technology itself is not harmful; it depends on how it is used.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
by itself（单独/本身）；in itself（本身）；prove itself（证明自身）；the system itself
🧠 助记
it（它）+ self（自己）→ 它自己
itself::/ɪtˈself/:pron. 它自己，其本身（强调/反身）:The policy, in itself, is sound, but implementation remains a challenge.
13. response /rɪˈspɒns/ n. 回应，答复；反应
📖 英文解释
A verbal or written answer; a reaction to something.
✏️ 例句
The government's response to the crisis was widely criticized as inadequate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in response to（回应……）；response rate 回应率；emergency response 紧急响应；responsive adj.
🧠 助记
re（回）+ spons（承诺）→ 回来承诺/回复 → 回应
response::/rɪˈspɒns/:n. 回应，答复:In response to the pandemic, governments implemented unprecedented public health measures.
14. beyond /biˈɒnd/ prep./adv. 超出，超越；在……之外
📖 英文解释
On or to the far side of; outside the range of; more than.
✏️ 例句
The consequences of this decision go beyond the immediate economic effects.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
beyond doubt/belief/control；go beyond（超越）；beyond one's reach 无法企及；above and beyond 超额完成
🧠 助记
be + yond（那边）→ 在那边之外 → 超越/在……之外
beyond::/biˈɒnd/:prep./adv. 超出，超越:The scope of climate change extends beyond national borders and requires global cooperation.
15. regard /rɪˈɡɑːrd/ v. 看待，认为；n. 敬重；关系；pl. 问候
📖 英文解释
To think of in a particular way; respect or concern; in relation to.
✏️ 例句
Education is widely regarded as the foundation of social progress.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
regard as（视为）；with regard to（关于）；in this regard；regardless of（不管）；high regard 敬重
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ gard（看守）→ 再次守望/看待 → 看待/敬重
regard::/rɪˈɡɑːrd/:v. 看待，认为:Innovation is widely regarded as the engine of modern economic development.
16. reader /ˈriːdər/ n. 读者；读本；阅读机
📖 英文解释
A person who reads; a book designed to help people learn to read; a device for reading data.
✏️ 例句
The article was written with a general reader in mind, not an academic audience.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
target reader；reader response 读者反应（文学批评）；proof-reader 校对者；e-reader 电子阅读器
🧠 助记
read（阅读）+ er → 阅读的人 → 读者
reader::/ˈriːdər/:n. 读者:The author tailored the argument to engage both specialist and general readers.
17. serious /ˈsɪəriəs/ adj. 严肃的，认真的；严重的
📖 英文解释
Thoughtful or solemn; not trivial; causing worry or concern.
✏️ 例句
Climate change poses a serious threat to global food security.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
serious about（认真对待）；seriously adv.；seriousness n.；a serious issue/problem/concern
🧠 助记
ser（稳定）+ ious → 稳稳当当、严肃认真 → 严肃的
serious::/ˈsɪəriəs/:adj. 严肃的，认真的:Corruption poses a serious threat to democratic institutions and public trust.
18. serve /sɜːrv/ v. 服务，供职；提供；用于
📖 英文解释
To perform duties or services for someone; to provide food or drinks; to be used for a purpose.
✏️ 例句
Public institutions must serve the interests of all citizens, not just the elite.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
serve as（担任/充当）；serve a purpose；serve a sentence 服刑；service n.；server n.
🧠 助记
serve → 谐音色福 → 给大家带来福气 → 服务
serve::/sɜːrv/:v. 服务，供职:The report serves as a comprehensive reference for policymakers and researchers.
19. president /ˈprezɪdənt/ n. 总统；校长；总裁，会长
📖 英文解释
The elected head of a republic; the head of an organization, company, or university.
✏️ 例句
The president announced a new series of economic reforms in the annual address.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
president of；presidential adj.；vice president 副总统/副校长；ex-president 前任总统
🧠 助记
pre（前面）+ sident（坐着）→ 坐在前面的人 → 总统/主席
president::/ˈprezɪdənt/:n. 总统:The president outlined an ambitious agenda for educational reform in the inaugural speech.
20. climate /ˈklaɪmɪt/ n. 气候；（社会/政治）氛围
📖 英文解释
The general weather conditions in an area; the general attitudes or feelings in a situation.
✏️ 例句
The current political climate makes it difficult to pass meaningful reform legislation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
climate change/crisis/action；business/political climate；climate agreement；favorable climate 有利环境
🧠 助记
clim（爬升）+ ate → 温度爬升 → 气候；也联想攀登环境
climate::/ˈklaɪmɪt/:n. 气候:Creating a climate of trust between citizens and government is essential for democracy.
21. heart /hɑːrt/ n. 心，内心；核心；勇气
📖 英文解释
The organ that pumps blood; the central or most important part; courage or enthusiasm.
✏️ 例句
At the heart of the debate is the question of economic versus environmental priorities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
at the heart of（核心）；heart of the matter；by heart 背诵；heart-felt 真诚的；take heart 鼓起勇气
🧠 助记
heart → 心形 → 心
heart::/hɑːrt/:n. 心，内心:At the heart of the conflict is a fundamental disagreement over resource distribution.
22. traffic /ˈtræfɪk/ n. 交通；贸易；v. 非法买卖
📖 英文解释
The movement of vehicles on roads; the movement of people or goods; to trade in something illegally.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
traffic jam/light/control；air traffic；drug/human trafficking 毒品/人口贩卖；traffic in（从事非法交易）
🧠 助记
traffic → 谐音拖了废 → 车堵住什么都废了 → 交通/堵塞
traffic::/ˈtræfɪk/:n. 交通:Reducing urban traffic congestion requires investment in public transportation infrastructure.
23. determine /dɪˈtɜːrmɪn/ v. 决定，确定；查明；下定决心
📖 英文解释
To cause something to happen or exist; to officially decide after investigation; to resolve.
✏️ 例句
A child's early environment largely determines their future educational achievement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
determine + 宾语/从句；determined adj. 坚定的；self-determination 自决；factor determining
🧠 助记
de（加强）+ termin（终点）→ 确定终点 → 决定/确定
determine::/dɪˈtɜːrmɪn/:v. 决定，确定:Early childhood nutrition largely determines cognitive development and academic performance.
24. gain /ɡeɪn/ v./n. 获得，增加；收益
📖 英文解释
To obtain something useful or positive; to increase; a benefit or advantage.
✏️ 例句
Students gain valuable skills through internship programs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
gain experience/knowledge/weight；gain access to；financial gain 经济利益；no pain, no gain
🧠 助记

gain::/ɡeɪn/:v./n. 获得，增加:International cooperation allows nations to gain from shared expertise and resources.
25. speech /spiːtʃ/ n. 演讲，言语；讲话能力
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
The prime minister delivered a powerful speech on the importance of education reform.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make/give/deliver a speech；freedom of speech 言论自由；speech act 言语行为；speech impediment 言语障碍
🧠 助记
spe（speak）+ ech → 说话这件事 → 演讲
speech::/spiːtʃ/:n. 演讲，言语:Freedom of speech is a cornerstone of democratic governance and civil society.
