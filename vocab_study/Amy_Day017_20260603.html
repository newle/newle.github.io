1. award /əˈwɔːrd/ v. 颁发，授予；n. 奖项，奖励
📖 英文解释
To officially give someone a prize or sum of money; a prize or sum of money given for an achievement.
✏️ 例句
The scientist was awarded the Nobel Prize for her groundbreaking research.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
award sb sth；win/receive an award；award ceremony 颁奖典礼；award-winning 获奖的
🧠 助记
a + ward（守卫）→ 守卫授予你的东西 → 奖项
award::/əˈwɔːrd/:v. 颁发，授予:The film was awarded three major international prizes for its portrayal of social inequality.
2. prepare /prɪˈpeər/ v. 准备，预备；做（饭）
📖 英文解释
To make ready for use or consideration; to get ready for an event; to make food.
✏️ 例句
Students must prepare thoroughly for graduate entrance examinations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prepare for；prepare to do；well/poorly prepared；preparation n.；preparatory adj. 准备性的
🧠 助记
pre（提前）+ pare（配对）→ 提前配齐 → 准备
prepare::/prɪˈpeər/:v. 准备，预备:Governments must prepare comprehensive contingency plans for future pandemics.
3. except /ɪkˈsept/ prep./conj. 除了……之外；v. 把……排除在外
📖 英文解释
Not including; other than; used to introduce the only thing or person not affected by a statement.
✏️ 例句
All environmental policies were approved except the one on carbon trading.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
except for（除……之外）；except that（只是，除了……之外）；exception n.；exceptional adj.；without exception
🧠 助记
ex（出去）+ cept（抓）→ 抓出去 → 除了……之外
except::/ɪkˈsept/:prep./conj. 除了……之外:All committee members agreed with the proposal except for the financial provisions.
4. publish /ˈpʌblɪʃ/ v. 出版，发表；公布
📖 英文解释
To prepare and issue a book, journal, or piece of writing for public sale or distribution; to make official information public.
✏️ 例句
The journal publishes only peer-reviewed research on environmental science.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
publish a paper/book/report；publisher n.；publication n.；publicly publish；unpublished adj. 未出版的
🧠 助记
public（公众）+ ish → 使公众看到 → 出版/公布
publish::/ˈpʌblɪʃ/:v. 出版，发表:The government published a comprehensive report on the state of public education.
5. digital /ˈdɪdʒɪtl/ adj. 数字的，数码的；数位的
📖 英文解释
Using or relating to computer technology; involving digits or numbers.
✏️ 例句
The digital divide refers to inequality in access to digital technology and the internet.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
digital technology/economy/media；go digital（数字化）；digital native 数字原住民；digitize v.
🧠 助记
digit（手指/数字）+ al → 与数字相关 → 数字的
digital::/ˈdɪdʒɪtl/:adj. 数字的，数码的:Bridging the digital divide is essential for achieving educational equity in the 21st century.
6. suffer /ˈsʌfər/ v. 遭受，受苦；忍受；受损
📖 英文解释
To experience pain, illness, or difficulty; to become worse in quality; to tolerate.
✏️ 例句
Millions of people suffer from the effects of air pollution every year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
suffer from（患有/遭受）；suffer a loss/defeat；sufferer n.；suffering n.；long-suffering adj. 忍耐已久的
🧠 助记
suf（sub，在下）+ fer（带来）→ 将苦难带到下面 → 遭受/受苦
suffer::/ˈsʌfər/:v. 遭受，受苦:Rural communities often suffer disproportionately from the effects of climate change.
7. mental /ˈmentl/ adj. 精神的，心理的；智力的
📖 英文解释
Relating to the mind or an intellectual process; relating to mental health.
✏️ 例句

mental health/illness/disorder；mental capacity；mental note 心里记下；mentality n. 心态
🧠 助记
ment（心思/想法）+ al → 与想法相关 → 精神的/心理的
mental::/ˈmentl/:adj. 精神的，心理的:Mental health support systems must be integrated into national healthcare frameworks.
8. positive /ˈpɒzɪtɪv/ adj. 积极的，乐观的；肯定的；阳性的
📖 英文解释
Showing confidence and optimism; expressing or implying agreement; above zero; confirmed (of a test result).
✏️ 例句
A positive attitude toward challenges leads to better problem-solving outcomes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
positive change/impact/attitude；positively adv.；positivity n.；false positive 假阳性；positive reinforcement
🧠 助记
posit（放置）+ ive → 放置在好的位置 → 积极的/肯定的
positive::/ˈpɒzɪtɪv/:adj. 积极的，乐观的:Positive reinforcement has been shown to improve academic performance in students.
9. outside /ˌaʊtˈsaɪd/ prep./adv./adj./n. 外面，在……之外；外部的
📖 英文解释
On or to the outer side; beyond the limits of something; not belonging to a group.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
out（外）+ side（侧面/面）→ 外部的一面 → 外面
outside::/ˌaʊtˈsaɪd/:prep./adv./adj./n. 外:Solutions to local problems often require input from outside the immediate community.
10. party /ˈpɑːrti/ n. 派对；政党；当事方；v. 聚会
📖 英文解释
A social gathering; a political organization; one of the people or groups involved in an agreement.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
party → 大家聚到一起 → 派对/政党
party::/ˈpɑːrti/:n. 派对:Both parties in the negotiation agreed to a binding arbitration process.
11. dream /driːm/ n. 梦想，理想；梦；v. 做梦；渴望
📖 英文解释
A series of thoughts and images in sleep; a cherished aspiration; to experience dreams; to imagine.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dream of/about；beyond one's wildest dreams 超乎想象；dream job；daydream 白日梦；pipe dream 白日梦
🧠 助记
dream → dr + eam（向前奔跑）→ 向前追的梦 → 梦想
dream::/driːm/:n. 梦想，理想:The dream of a sustainable future requires collective action and long-term commitment.
12. increasingly /ɪnˈkriːsɪŋli/ adv. 越来越多地，日益
📖 英文解释
To an ever greater degree; more and more.
✏️ 例句
Governments are increasingly recognizing the need for climate action.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
increasingly + adj./v.；increasingly important/common/difficult；increasing adj. 增加的；increase v./n.
🧠 助记
increase（增加）+ ing + ly → 不断增加地 → 越来越
increasingly::/ɪnˈkriːsɪŋli/:adv. 越来越多地，日益:Automation is increasingly replacing routine jobs in manufacturing and services.
13. trade /treɪd/ n. 贸易，交易；行业；v. 交易，贸易
📖 英文解释
The activity of buying and selling goods; a particular job or profession; to exchange.
✏️ 例句
International trade plays a vital role in global economic development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
trade in/with；trade balance/deficit/surplus；trade war 贸易战；free trade 自由贸易；trade-off 权衡取舍
🧠 助记
trade → trad（传统路线）→ 沿传统路线做生意 → 贸易
trade::/treɪd/:n. 贸易，交易:The trade-off between economic growth and environmental protection remains a central debate.
14. contribute /kənˈtrɪbjuːt/ v. 贡献，捐献；有助于；投稿
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

contribute to（有助于/贡献于）；contribute sth to；contribution n.；contributor n.
🧠 助记

contribute::/kənˈtrɪbjuːt/:v. 贡献，捐献:Education contributes significantly to reducing poverty and improving social mobility.
15. drink /drɪŋk/ v. 喝，饮；n. 饮料，饮酒
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

drink → d + rink（容器）→ 从容器里喝 → 喝
drink::/drɪŋk/:v. 喝，饮:Providing safe drinking water remains a critical challenge in developing regions.
16. therefore /ˈðeərfɔːr/ adv./conj. 因此，所以
📖 英文解释
As a logical consequence of something; for that reason.
✏️ 例句
The experiment yielded significant results; therefore, a follow-up study was warranted.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
therefore + 句子；由因到果的逻辑连词；学术写作中频繁使用
🧠 助记
there（那里）+ fore（前面）→ 从那里得出的结论 → 因此
therefore::/ˈðeərfɔːr/:adv./conj. 因此，所以:Carbon emissions are rising; therefore, immediate policy intervention is necessary.
17. himself /hɪmˈself/ pron. 他自己，他本人（反身/强调）
📖 英文解释
Used as the object of a verb or preposition when the subject of the sentence is masculine; used to emphasize.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
by himself（独自/独立地）；himself as subject emphasis（强调用法）；he did it himself
🧠 助记

himself::/hɪmˈself/:pron. 他自己，他本人（反身/强调）:The minister himself acknowledged the shortcomings of the current education system.

achieve::/əˈtʃiːv/:v. 实现，完成:Sustainable development goals can only be achieved through coordinated global action.
