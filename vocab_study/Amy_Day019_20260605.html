1. catch /kætʃ/ v. 抓住；赶上；染上；理解 n. 捕获；陷阱
📖 英文解释
to intercept and hold something thrown or moving; to contract an illness; to hear or understand
✏️ 例句
The new regulation is designed to catch companies that fail to comply with environmental standards.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
catch up (赶上); catch on (流行/理解); catch sight of; catch a disease; catch a bus; catch phrase
🧠 助记
catch → 想象棒球接球（catch）→ 抓住
catch::/kætʃ/:v. 抓住:The police managed to catch the suspect within 24 hours.
2. select /sɪˈlekt/ v. 选择；挑选 adj. 精选的；优质的
📖 英文解释
to carefully choose from a number of alternatives; of high quality or exclusively chosen
✏️ 例句
The committee will select the most qualified candidates for the final round of interviews.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
select from; select for; selection (n.); selective (adj.); natural selection; select few
🧠 助记
se（分开）+ lect（挑选，如collect）→ 把要的那个分出来 → 选择
select::/sɪˈlekt/:v. 选择:The university selects students based on academic merit.
3. card /kɑːrd/ n. 卡片；纸牌；名片；信用卡
📖 英文解释
a small piece of stiff paper; a playing card; a credit or debit card
✏️ 例句
Identity cards are used in many countries to verify citizens' personal information.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
credit/debit card; ID card; playing cards; business card; on the cards (可能发生); card game
🧠 助记
card → 想象各种卡片（名片、扑克牌）→ 卡片
card::/kɑːrd/:n. 卡片:Please show your membership card at the entrance.
4. cover /ˈkʌvər/ v. 覆盖；涉及；报道；包括 n. 封面；掩护；覆盖物
📖 英文解释
to put something over to protect or conceal; to deal with or include; to report on an event
✏️ 例句
The research paper covers a wide range of topics, from genetics to environmental science.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cover up; cover story; cover a topic; insurance coverage; under cover; cover letter; take cover
🧠 助记
cover → 想象用盖子（cover）盖住 → 覆盖；涉及（涵盖范围）
cover::/ˈkʌvər/:v. 覆盖:The documentary covers the history of the civil rights movement.
5. earn /ɜːrn/ v. 挣得；赢得；博得
📖 英文解释
to obtain money in return for labor or services; to get something deserved through one's actions
✏️ 例句
Hard work and dedication are essential to earn the trust and respect of your colleagues.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
earn a living/salary; earn respect; earn interest; well-earned; earnings (n.); earner (n.)
🧠 助记
earn → 谐音「嗯」→ 努力工作后赚到了 → 挣得
earn::/ɜːrn/:v. 挣得:She worked two jobs to earn enough money for tuition.
6. farmer /ˈfɑːrmər/ n. 农民；农场主
📖 英文解释
a person who owns or manages a farm
✏️ 例句

farmer::/ˈfɑːrmər/:n. 农民:Many farmers are adopting sustainable agricultural practices.
7. trend /trend/ n. 趋势；潮流；走向 v. 呈趋势；流行
📖 英文解释
a general direction in which something is developing; a fashion or preference
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
trend toward/in; global/current trend; set a trend; trendy (adj.); trending (adj.); mainstream trend
🧠 助记
trend → 趋势（直接联想）→ 向某方向延伸
trend::/trend/:n. 趋势:There is a growing trend toward plant-based diets worldwide.
8. attract /əˈtrækt/ v. 吸引；引起（兴趣/注意）
📖 英文解释
to cause to come to a place or participate in an activity; to evoke a positive response
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
attract attention/investment/talent; be attracted to; attract customers; attraction (n.); attractive (adj.)
🧠 助记
at（朝向）+ tract（拉，如extract）→ 朝自己方向拉 → 吸引
attract::/əˈtrækt/:v. 吸引:The scholarship program attracts top students from across the country.
9. clean /kliːn/ adj. 干净的；清洁的；无污染的 v. 清洁；打扫
📖 英文解释
free from dirt, pollution or contamination; not yet used or written on; to make free from dirt
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记
clean → 联想到清洁（clean）→ 干净的
clean::/kliːn/:adj. 干净的:The government is investing in clean energy to reduce pollution.
10. method /ˈmeθəd/ n. 方法；方式；条理
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

method::/ˈmeθəd/:n. 方法:The scientific method requires forming and testing hypotheses.
11. promise /ˈprɒmɪs/ n. 承诺；诺言；希望 v. 承诺；有希望
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

promise::/ˈprɒmɪs/:n. 承诺:The government must keep its promise to reduce carbon emissions.
12. desire /dɪˈzaɪər/ n. 渴望；欲望；愿望 v. 渴望；希望

desire::/dɪˈzaɪər/:n. 渴望:There is a widespread desire for political reform.
