1. discover /dɪˈskʌvər/ v. 探索；发现；了解到
📖 英文解释
to find information, a place, or an object, especially for the first time; to become aware of
✏️ 例句
Researchers recently discovered a new species of deep-sea fish in the Pacific Ocean.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
discover that; discover a new method; discovery (n.); discoverer (n.); self-discovery
🧠 助记
dis（去掉）+ cover（盖子）→ 揭开盖子 → 发现
discover::/dɪˈskʌvər/:v. 探索:Scientists discovered a link between diet and mental health.
2. behind /bɪˈhaɪnd/ prep./adv. 在…后面；落后于 n. 臀部
📖 英文解释
at or to the back; in the past; at a lower standard than; responsible for
✏️ 例句
The motivation behind the policy change was to attract more foreign investors to the region.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
behind schedule/time; fall behind; leave behind; the force behind; behind the scenes; from behind
🧠 助记
be + hind（后面，如hinder）→ 在后面 → 在…后面
behind::/bɪˈhaɪnd/:prep./adv. 在…后面:The company is falling behind its competitors in innovation.
3. introduce /ˌɪntrəˈdjuːs/ v. 介绍；引进；提出；推出
📖 英文解释
to bring something into use or existence for the first time; to present one person to another
✏️ 例句
The government plans to introduce stricter regulations on plastic waste to protect the environment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
introduce sb to; introduce a law/product; introduction (n.); introductory (adj.); reintroduce
🧠 助记
intro（向内）+ duce（引导）→ 把人/事物引导进来 → 引进、介绍
introduce::/ˌɪntrəˈdjuːs/:v. 介绍:The company introduced a new product line aimed at young consumers.
4. else /els/ adv. 其他；另外 adj. 别的
📖 英文解释
in addition; different from what is mentioned; other
✏️ 例句
If nothing else, the crisis has demonstrated the need for stronger international cooperation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
something/anything/nothing else; someone else; or else (否则); else's (别人的); where/when/how else
🧠 助记
else → 联想到 elsewhere（别处）→ 其他、另外
else::/els/:adv. 其他:Is there anything else you would like to add?
5. drop /drɒp/ v. 掉下；减少；放弃 n. 下降；滴；少量
📖 英文解释
to fall or allow to fall; to decrease; to stop doing or talking about; a small amount of liquid
✏️ 例句
The company's profits dropped sharply after the introduction of new environmental regulations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
drop in/on; drop out; a drop in (小幅下降); temperature drop; drop everything; drop off
🧠 助记
drop → 想象水滴（drop）往下落 → 掉落、下降
drop::/drɒp/:v. 掉下:Unemployment dropped to its lowest level in a decade.
6. device /dɪˈvaɪs/ n. 装置；设备；方法；手段
📖 英文解释
a thing made for a particular purpose; a plan or method; a technique used in a work of art
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记

device::/dɪˈvaɪs/:n. 装置:The safety device prevents the machine from operating at unsafe speeds.

10. range /reɪndʒ/ n. 范围；幅度；系列；山脉 v. 覆盖；排列

range::/reɪndʒ/:n. 范围:The organization works across a range of social issues.
11. solution /səˈluːʃn/ n. 解决方案；溶液；答案

12. essential /ɪˈsenʃl/ adj. 重要的；必要的；本质的 n. 要素；必需品

essential::/ɪˈsenʃl/:adj. 重要的:Clean water is essential for human health and development.
13. particular /pərˈtɪkjələr/ adj. 特定的；特别的；挑剔的 n. 细节；详情
📖 英文解释

particular::/pərˈtɪkjələr/:adj. 特定的:In particular, the report focuses on youth unemployment.

15. indicate /ˈɪndɪkeɪt/ v. 表明；指出；暗示；打方向灯
