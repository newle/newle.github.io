1. fire /faɪə/ n. 火；v. 开除、射击
📖 英文解释
The process of burning that produces heat and light; to dismiss someone from a job.
✏️ 例句
The manager fired two employees for violating company policy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ fire sb 解雇某人 ★ catch fire 着火 辨析：fire / dismiss / sack：fire 最口语；dismiss 正式；sack 英式口语
🧠 助记
fire（火）→ 被解雇 = 被炒鱿鱼，炒需要火
fire::/faɪə/:n. 火:The company fired ten employees due to budget cuts.
2. site /saɪt/ n. 景点、站点、遗址
📖 英文解释
A place where something is located; a location used for a particular purpose.
✏️ 例句
The ancient site attracts archaeologists and tourists from around the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ construction/heritage/historical site 建筑工地/遗产/历史遗址 ★ website 网站 辨析：site / location / venue：site 侧重某一特定场所；location 强调地点坐标；venue 多指活动场地
🧠 助记
site 谐音 赛特 = 赛事特定地点 → 场所、站点
site::/saɪt/:n. 景点、站点、遗址:The construction site was closed after safety violations were discovered.
3. moment /ˈməʊmənt/ n. 时刻、片刻
📖 英文解释
A very brief period of time; a particular point in time.
✏️ 例句
At that moment, everyone in the room fell silent.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at the moment 此刻 ★ the moment (that) 一……就……（时间状语从句） 辨析：moment / instant / second：三者都表极短时间，moment 最口语；instant 强调瞬间
🧠 助记
mo（模）+ ment（心，mental）= 留在心里的瞬间 → 时刻
moment::/ˈməʊmənt/:n. 时刻、片刻:The moment she heard the news, she burst into tears.
4. belief /bɪˈliːf/ n. 信仰、相信
📖 英文解释
An acceptance that something exists or is true; trust or confidence in someone.
✏️ 例句
His strong belief in education motivated him to study harder.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ belief in sb/sth 对……的信任 ★ beyond belief 难以置信 辨析：belief / faith / trust：belief 侧重认知上的相信；faith 带宗教色彩或深度信任；trust 侧重人际信任关系
🧠 助记
be（是）+ lief（爱，源自love）= 内心认为是真的 → 信仰
belief::/bɪˈliːf/:n. 信仰、相信:There is a widespread belief that education is the key to social mobility.
5. museum /mjuːˈzɪəm/ n. 博物馆
📖 英文解释
A building where objects of historical, scientific, or artistic importance are kept.
✏️ 例句
The national museum holds thousands of artifacts from ancient civilizations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ natural history/art/science museum 自然历史/艺术/科学博物馆 ★ visit a museum 参观博物馆 ☆ museum piece 博物馆展品（也比喻过时的东西）
🧠 助记
muse（缪斯女神）+ um（地方后缀）= 缪斯的地方 → 博物馆
museum::/mjuːˈzɪəm/:n. 博物馆:The city's art museum attracts over two million visitors each year.
6. finally /ˈfaɪnəli/ adv. 最终地、终于
📖 英文解释
After a long time or series of events; used to introduce a last point.
✏️ 例句
After years of research, scientists finally developed an effective vaccine.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ firstly...secondly...finally（列举结构） 辨析：finally 侧重期盼后的结果；eventually 侧重经过过程后的结果
🧠 助记
final（最终）+ ly（副词后缀）= 最终地
finally::/ˈfaɪnəli/:adv. 最终地、终于:The two countries finally reached a peace agreement after decades of conflict.
7. address /əˈdres/ n. 演说、地址；v. 处理、讲话
📖 英文解释
A speech to an audience; the place where someone lives; to speak to or deal with.
✏️ 例句
The president addressed the nation on the issue of climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ address a problem/issue/challenge 解决/处理问题/挑战 ★ give an address 发表演说 辨析：在考研写作中，address 和 tackle 比 deal with 更正式
🧠 助记
ad（向）+ dress（穿戴整齐）= 穿戴整齐向人讲话 → 演说、讲话
address::/əˈdres/:n. 演说、地址:The report addresses several key environmental challenges facing modern cities.
8. concept /ˈkɒnsept/ n. 概念
📖 英文解释
An abstract idea or general understanding; a thought or notion.
✏️ 例句
The concept of sustainability is central to modern environmental policy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the concept of 关于……的概念 ★ introduce/develop a concept 引入/发展一个概念 辨析：concept / idea / notion：concept 最正式、偏学术；idea 最宽泛；notion 带主观推测色彩
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ cept（抓住）= 共同能抓住理解的东西 → 概念
concept::/ˈkɒnsept/:n. 概念:The concept of lifelong learning has gained popularity in modern education.
9. crime /kraɪm/ n. 犯罪
📖 英文解释
An action that violates a law and is punishable by the state.
✏️ 例句
Poverty and unemployment are often linked to rising crime rates.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ commit a crime 犯罪 ★ crime rate/prevention 犯罪率/犯罪预防 辨析：crime / offence / sin：crime 违法行为；offence 轻微违法或冒犯；sin 宗教意义上的罪
🧠 助记
crime 谐音 扣了买 = 扣下来抢买 → 犯罪行为
crime::/kraɪm/:n. 犯罪:The government is committed to reducing violent crime in urban areas.
10. damage /ˈdæmɪdʒ/ v./n. 损害、破坏
📖 英文解释
Physical harm that impairs the value or usefulness of something; to cause harm.
✏️ 例句
The flood caused extensive damage to homes and infrastructure.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ cause/suffer/do damage to 对……造成损害 ★ environmental/property damage 环境/财产损害 辨析：damage / harm / injury：damage 侧重物体损坏；harm 既可指物也可指人；injury 专指人体受伤
🧠 助记
dam（大坝）+ age（时代）= 大坝决堤的破坏 → 损坏
damage::/ˈdæmɪdʒ/:v./n. 损害、破坏:Heavy rainfall caused serious damage to agricultural land.
11. negative /ˈneɡətɪv/ adj. 消极的、负面的
📖 英文解释
Expressing denial or refusal; harmful or unpleasant; less than zero.
✏️ 例句
Social media can have negative effects on teenagers mental health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ negative impact/effect/consequence 消极影响/效果/后果 辨析：negative / pessimistic：negative 指具体行为或结果；pessimistic 指对未来持悲观态度的心理状态
🧠 助记
negate（否定）+ ive（形容词）= 否定的 → 消极的
negative::/ˈneɡətɪv/:adj. 消极的、负面的:Excessive screen time has a negative impact on children's development.
12. restaurant /ˈrestrɒnt/ n. 饭馆、餐厅
📖 英文解释
A place where meals are cooked and served to customers.
✏️ 例句
The restaurant industry suffered greatly during the pandemic.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ dine at a restaurant 在餐厅用餐 ★ fast food/upscale restaurant 快餐/高档餐厅 ☆ restaurant chain 连锁餐厅
🧠 助记
restore（恢复）+ ant（人）= 让人恢复体力的地方 → 餐厅
restaurant::/ˈrestrɒnt/:n. 饭馆、餐厅:The restaurant was awarded a Michelin star for its innovative cuisine.
13. federal /ˈfedərəl/ adj. 联邦的
📖 英文解释
Relating to a system of government in which states are united under a central authority.
✏️ 例句
The federal government announced new policies to stimulate economic growth.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ federal government/law/budget 联邦政府/法律/预算 辨析：federal / national / central：federal 专指联邦体制；national 泛指全国；central 强调中央集权
🧠 助记
fed（联合）+ eral = 各州联合在一起 → 联邦的
federal::/ˈfedərəl/:adj. 联邦的:The federal government allocated billions of dollars to improve infrastructure.
14. labor /ˈleɪbə/ n. 劳动力、劳工；v. 努力
📖 英文解释
Work, especially physical work; workers collectively; to work hard.
✏️ 例句
The rise of automation is expected to reduce the demand for manual labor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ labor market/force 劳动力市场/劳动力 ★ labor-intensive 劳动密集型的 辨析：labor / work / employment：labor 强调体力劳动或劳动阶层；work 最通用；employment 侧重就业状态
🧠 助记
labor（劳动）= 直接对应，lab（实验室）也需要 labor 劳动
labor::/ˈleɪbə/:n. 劳动力、劳工:Many developing countries compete by offering cheap labor to attract foreign investment.
15. progress /ˈprəʊɡres/ n./v. 进步、进展
📖 英文解释
Development toward an improved or more advanced condition; to move forward.
✏️ 例句
The country has made remarkable progress in reducing poverty over the past decade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ make progress in 在……方面取得进展 ★ technological/social/economic progress 技术/社会/经济进步 ★ in progress 进行中 辨析：progress 持续整体向好；advance 侧重某领域重大突破；improvement 具体某项改善
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ gress（走）= 向前走 → 进步
progress::/ˈprəʊɡres/:n./v. 进步、进展:Science has made great progress in understanding the human genome.
16. robot /ˈrəʊbɒt/ n. 机器人
📖 英文解释
A machine capable of carrying out a complex series of actions automatically.
✏️ 例句
Robots are increasingly used in manufacturing to improve efficiency.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ industrial/medical robot 工业/医疗机器人 ★ robotic adj. 机器人式的，机械的
🧠 助记
robot 来自捷克语 robota（强迫劳动）→ 替人劳动的机器 → 机器人
robot::/ˈrəʊbɒt/:n. 机器人:The introduction of robots into the factory doubled production efficiency.
17. movement /ˈmuːvmənt/ n. 运动（社会/政治）、移动
📖 英文解释
The act of moving; a group of people working together to achieve a shared goal.
✏️ 例句
The civil rights movement transformed American society in the 1960s.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ social/political movement 社会/政治运动 ★ the environmental movement 环保运动 辨析：movement / campaign / revolution：movement 持续时间长、范围广；campaign 有组织短期行动；revolution 根本性变革
🧠 助记
move（移动/运动）+ ment（名词后缀）= 运动、移动
movement::/ˈmuːvmənt/:n. 运动（社会/政治）、移动:The feminist movement has achieved significant gains in gender equality.
18. weather /ˈweðə/ n. 天气；v. 经受住
📖 英文解释
The atmospheric conditions at a particular time; to endure difficult conditions.
✏️ 例句
The company managed to weather the financial crisis through cost-cutting measures.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ weather conditions/forecast/pattern 天气状况/预报/模式 ★ weather a storm/crisis 熬过风暴/危机（比喻） 辨析：weather 短期大气状态；climate 长期气候特征
🧠 助记
weather the storm = 经受住风暴考验 → weather 有动词义：经受住
weather::/ˈweðə/:n. 天气:The company weathered several economic downturns and emerged stronger.
19. mistake /mɪˈsteɪk/ n./v. 错误、误解
📖 英文解释
An action or judgment that is wrong; to incorrectly identify something.
✏️ 例句
It is important to learn from your mistakes rather than repeat them.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ make a mistake 犯错误 ★ by mistake 错误地、无意中 ☆ mistake A for B 把A误认为B 辨析：mistake / error / fault：mistake 泛指任何错误；error 更正式；fault 侧重应受责备的过失
🧠 助记
mis（错）+ take（拿）= 拿错了 → 错误
mistake::/mɪˈsteɪk/:n./v. 错误、误解:Making mistakes is a natural part of the learning process.
20. infer /ɪnˈfɜː/ v. 推断、推理
📖 英文解释
To reach a conclusion by reasoning from evidence or premises.
✏️ 例句
From the evidence, we can infer that the accident occurred at midnight.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ infer from sth that... 从……推断出…… ★ inference n. 推断 辨析：infer / imply / deduce：infer 是听者/读者的推断；imply 是说者/作者的暗示；deduce 基于逻辑步骤推导
🧠 助记
in（内）+ fer（带）= 将内在信息带出来 → 推断
infer::/ɪnˈfɜː/:v. 推断、推理:Scientists infer the age of fossils from the surrounding rock layers.
21. enter /ˈentə/ v. 进入、参加
📖 英文解释
To come or go into a place; to join or become involved in something.
✏️ 例句
She decided to enter the field of artificial intelligence after graduation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ enter a room/competition/field 进入房间/参赛/进入领域 ★ enter into an agreement 签订协议 辨析：enter / join / participate in：enter 侧重进入；join 侧重成为成员；participate in 侧重主动参与活动
🧠 助记
enter = 进入（直接记忆）
enter::/ˈentə/:v. 进入、参加:Many graduates enter the workforce with high expectations but limited experience.
22. charge /tʃɑːdʒ/ v. 收费、充电；n. 费用、指控
📖 英文解释
To demand payment; to accuse formally; the amount of electricity stored.
✏️ 例句
The hotel charges extra for parking facilities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ charge sb for sth 为某事向某人收费 ★ in charge of 负责…… ☆ free of charge 免费 辨析：charge / fee / cost / price：charge 强调服务收费；fee 指专业服务费；cost 指成本；price 指商品定价
🧠 助记
charge（充电/收费）= 两义联想：手机充电要收费
charge::/tʃɑːdʒ/:v. 收费、充电:The government charged the company with violating environmental regulations.
23. medicine /ˈmedsɪn/ n. 医药、药物
📖 英文解释
The science and practice of treating illness; a drug used to treat illness.
✏️ 例句
Traditional Chinese medicine is gaining recognition around the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ take/prescribe medicine 服药/开药 ★ evidence-based medicine 循证医学 辨析：medicine / drug / medication：medicine 最通用；drug 可指处方药也可指毒品；medication 强调用于治疗的处方药
🧠 助记
medic（医生）+ ine（物质后缀）= 医生用的东西 → 医药
medicine::/ˈmedsɪn/:n. 医药、药物:Advances in medicine have dramatically increased human life expectancy.
24. engage /ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ v. 吸引、使参加、从事
📖 英文解释
To participate or become involved in; to attract and hold someone's attention.
✏️ 例句
The teacher uses interactive activities to engage students in the learning process.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ engage in sth 从事某事 ★ engage sb in conversation 与某人交谈 辨析：engage / involve / participate：engage 侧重吸引或使投入；involve 使成为一部分；participate 主动参与
🧠 助记
en（使）+ gage（承诺）= 使承诺参与 → 吸引、参与
engage::/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/:v. 吸引、使参加、从事:The new policy aims to engage young people in environmental protection activities.
25. executive /ɪɡˈzekjʊtɪv/ n. 高管、经理；adj. 实行的、行政的

A person with senior management responsibility; relating to carrying out plans or policies.

Senior executives are responsible for the strategic direction of the company.

★ chief executive officer (CEO) 首席执行官 ★ executive power/decision 行政权力/决定 辨析：executive / manager / administrator：executive 最高层；manager 中层管理；administrator 偏行政事务
🧠 助记
execute（执行）+ ive（人）= 执行决策的人 → 高管
executive::/ɪɡˈzekjʊtɪv/:n. 高管、经理:Top executives must balance profit with social responsibility.
