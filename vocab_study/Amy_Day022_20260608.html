1. wide /waɪd/ adj. 广泛的、宽的；adv. 大大地
📖 英文解释
Having a great extent from side to side; covering a large range.
✏️ 例句
The policy change has attracted wide criticism from academics and the public.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ wide range/variety/application 广泛的范围/多样/应用 ★ wide open 完全开放 辨析：wide / broad：wide 指物理宽度或范围；broad 多用于抽象（broad agreement/support）
🧠 助记
wide（宽）= w（两臂展开）+ ide → 张开双臂，很宽
wide::/waɪd/:adj. 广泛的、宽的:There is wide agreement that education reform is urgently needed.
2. variety /vəˈraɪəti/ n. 多样化、种类
📖 英文解释
The state of being diverse; a number of different things or people.
✏️ 例句
A variety of factors contribute to the success of a new business.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a variety of / a wide variety of 各种各样的 ★ variety show 综艺节目 辨析：variety / diversity / range：variety 强调种类多；diversity 强调异质性；range 强调范围跨度
🧠 助记
vary（变化）+ iety（名词）= 变化多样 → 多样化，种类
variety::/vəˈraɪəti/:n. 多样化、种类:The curriculum offers a wide variety of courses to suit different interests.
3. race /reɪs/ n. 比赛、竞速；种族；v. 赛跑
📖 英文解释
A competition to see who is fastest; a major division of humankind.
✏️ 例句
The arms race between superpowers dominated the Cold War era.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ arms/space race 军备/太空竞赛 ★ racial discrimination 种族歧视 辨析：race / ethnicity：race 指生理特征上的族群；ethnicity 指文化、语言上的归属
🧠 助记
race（赛跑/种族）= r（快速）+ ace（精英）→ 赛跑中的精英
race::/reɪs/:n. 比赛、竞速:The election was a close race between two well-qualified candidates.
4. object /ˈɒbdʒɪkt/ n. 目标、物体；v. 反对
📖 英文解释
A material thing; a goal; to express opposition to something.
✏️ 例句
Many residents objected to the construction of the new highway.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ object to sth/doing 反对某事 ★ the object of the exercise/study 练习/研究的目的 辨析：object / oppose / protest：object 个人表达异议；oppose 正式反对；protest 公开抗议
🧠 助记
ob（对着）+ ject（扔）= 向对面扔东西 → 反对；扔出来的东西 → 物体
object::/ˈɒbdʒɪkt/:n. 目标、物体:Many experts object to the simplistic way the problem is being framed.
5. manage /ˈmænɪdʒ/ v. 管理、设法做到
📖 英文解释
To be in charge of; to succeed in doing something difficult.
✏️ 例句
She managed to complete the research despite limited resources.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ manage to do sth 设法做到某事（=succeed in doing） ★ manage a company/team/crisis 管理公司/团队/危机 辨析：manage / control / handle：manage 有成效地处理；control 控制；handle 应对（情况）
🧠 助记
man（人）+ age（时代）= 掌管一个时代的人 → 管理
manage::/ˈmænɪdʒ/:v. 管理、设法做到:Effective leaders know how to manage conflict within their teams.
6. hospital /ˈhɒspɪtəl/ n. 医院
📖 英文解释
An institution providing medical and surgical treatment and nursing care.
✏️ 例句
The new hospital will serve a population of over 500,000 people.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be admitted to/discharged from hospital 入院/出院 ★ hospital ward/staff 病房/医院工作人员 辨析：hospital / clinic / infirmary：hospital 综合大医院；clinic 诊所；infirmary 较小的医疗设施
🧠 助记
hosp（host 主人）+ ital = 收留病人的地方 → 医院
hospital::/ˈhɒspɪtəl/:n. 医院:Underfunding has put enormous pressure on hospital resources.
7. official /əˈfɪʃəl/ adj. 官方的；n. 官员
📖 英文解释
Relating to an authority or public body; having formal authorization.
✏️ 例句
The official report confirmed that economic growth had slowed significantly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ official statement/visit/capacity 官方声明/访问/职务 ★ government official 政府官员 辨析：official / formal / authoritative：official 有官方来源；formal 指形式或礼节；authoritative 权威性的
🧠 助记
office（办公室）+ ial = 来自办公室的 → 官方的，官员
official::/əˈfɪʃəl/:adj. 官方的:The official statistics show a significant increase in youth unemployment.
8. huge /hjuːdʒ/ adj. 大的、巨大的
📖 英文解释
Extremely large in size, amount, or degree.
✏️ 例句
The internet has had a huge impact on how people access information.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ huge difference/impact/success 巨大差异/影响/成功 辨析：huge / enormous / vast：huge 最口语；enormous 强调数量或程度上的惊人；vast 强调空间或范围上的广大
🧠 助记
huge（巨大）= hu（hug 拥抱）+ ge → 需要双臂合围的大 → 巨大
huge::/hjuːdʒ/:adj. 大的、巨大的:Urbanization has created a huge demand for affordable housing.
9. hire /haɪə/ v. 雇佣；n. 租用
📖 英文解释
To employ someone for wages; to pay for the use of something.
✏️ 例句
The company decided to hire more engineers to meet the growing demand.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ hire sb for a job 雇用某人做工作 ★ for hire 出租 辨析：hire / employ / recruit：hire 最口语；employ 较正式；recruit 强调招募过程
🧠 助记
hire（雇用）= h（握手）+ ire → 握手达成雇用协议
hire::/haɪə/:v. 雇佣:Companies that hire diverse employees tend to perform better overall.
10. hotel /həʊˈtel/ n. 酒店
📖 英文解释
An establishment providing accommodation and meals for travelers.
✏️ 例句
The hotel industry has been severely affected by the decline in tourism.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ luxury/budget hotel 豪华/廉价酒店 ★ check in/out of a hotel 入住/退房 辨析：hotel / motel / hostel：hotel 综合型酒店；motel 路边汽车旅馆；hostel 廉价旅社/青年旅馆
🧠 助记
hotel = host（主人）+ el（地方）= 主人接待的地方 → 酒店
hotel::/həʊˈtel/:n. 酒店:The tourism board urged travelers to book hotels in advance during peak season.
11. treat /triːt/ v. 对待、治疗；n. 款待
📖 英文解释
To behave toward someone in a certain way; to give medical care to; a special pleasure.
✏️ 例句
The new drug is used to treat patients with chronic heart disease.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ treat sb with respect/kindness 以尊重/善意对待某人 ★ treat a disease/patient 治疗疾病/病人 辨析：treat / handle / deal with：treat 含有态度评价；handle 侧重处理方式；deal with 最中性
🧠 助记
treat（对待/款待）= 请客吃好东西 → 款待，也延伸为对待
treat::/triːt/:v. 对待、治疗:Doctors treat patients not just with medicine, but with empathy and care.
12. perform /pəˈfɔːm/ v. 演奏、执行、表现
📖 英文解释
To carry out an action; to entertain an audience; to function at a level.
✏️ 例句
Students who perform well academically often have strong self-discipline.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ perform an operation/experiment 进行手术/实验 ★ perform music/a play 演奏音乐/演出话剧 辨析：perform / execute / accomplish：perform 侧重执行或表演；execute 正式执行；accomplish 强调完成目标
🧠 助记
per（完全）+ form（形式）= 完全按形式表现出来 → 演出、执行
perform::/pəˈfɔːm/:v. 演奏、执行、表现:The company performed well in the second quarter despite challenging conditions.

Employers are increasingly looking for candidates with digital skills.

★ employer-employee relationship 雇主-雇员关系 ★ potential/major employer 潜在/主要雇主 辨析：employer / employee / recruit：employer 雇主；employee 雇员；recruit 新招募的员工

employ（雇用）+ er（人）= 雇用别人的人 → 雇主
employer::/ɪmˈplɔɪə/:n. 雇主:The employer offered competitive salaries to attract top talent.
14. literature /ˈlɪtrɪtʃə/ n. 文学、文献
📖 英文解释
Written works, especially those with artistic merit; all published material on a subject.
✏️ 例句
Studying literature helps students develop critical thinking and empathy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ academic/scientific literature 学术/科学文献 ★ classic/modern literature 古典/现代文学 辨析：literature / writing / text：literature 最正式，指文学或文献体系；writing 泛指写作；text 侧重文本内容
🧠 助记
liter（文字）+ ature（名词）= 文字的集合 → 文学、文献
literature::/ˈlɪtrɪtʃə/:n. 文学、文献:The research literature suggests a strong link between reading and cognitive development.
15. profit /ˈprɒfɪt/ n. 利润、收益；v. 获益
📖 英文解释
A financial gain; to gain an advantage or benefit from something.
✏️ 例句
The company reported record profits despite the economic downturn.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ make/earn a profit 获利 ★ profit from sth 从……中获益 辨析：profit / revenue / income：profit 净利润（revenue减cost）；revenue 营业收入；income 个人或机构总收入
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ fit（适合）= 向前走适合方向 → 获益、利润
profit::/ˈprɒfɪt/:n. 利润、收益:Non-profit organizations reinvest any surplus back into their mission.
16. article /ˈɑːtɪkəl/ n. 文章、条款、冠词

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ write/publish an article 写/发表文章 ★ article of law/agreement 法律/协议条款 辨析：article / essay / paper：article 期刊/报纸文章；essay 学术或议论文；paper 学术论文
🧠 助记
art（艺术/技艺）+ icle（小的）= 小型作品 → 文章
article::/ˈɑːtɪkəl/:n. 文章、条款、冠词:The journal article was cited over 500 times by other researchers.

A balanced diet and regular exercise help maintain a healthy weight.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
weigh（称重）+ t（名词后缀）= 称出来的重量 → 体重
weight::/weɪt/:n. 体重、重量:Evidence carries more weight when it is supported by multiple studies.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

trip（旅行/绊倒）= tr（trail 路径）+ ip → 走在路上 → 旅行；也可能绊倒

investment::/ɪnˈvestmənt/:n. 投资:Education is widely regarded as the best long-term investment a society can make.
20. due /djuː/ adj. 预期的、到期的；n. 应得之物；adv. 正好
📖 英文解释
Expected or planned; owed as a debt or obligation; directly.
✏️ 例句
The report is due by the end of the month, so we need to work quickly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be due to do sth 预期做某事 ★ due to sth 由于、因为 辨析：due to / owing to / because of：三者都表原因，due to 最正式
🧠 助记
due（到期/应得）= d（dead line 截止日）+ ue → 到截止日 → 到期
due::/djuː/:adj. 预期的、到期的:The project's failure was due to poor planning and lack of communication.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ all aspects of sth 某事的各方面 ★ in terms of this aspect 就这个方面而言 辨析：aspect / dimension / facet：aspect 最通用；dimension 强调多维度；facet 强调多切面

aspect::/ˈæspekt/:n. 样子、方面、角度:Researchers must consider all aspects of the problem before drawing conclusions.
