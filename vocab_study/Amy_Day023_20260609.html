1. consumption /kənˈsʌmpʃən/ n. 消费、消耗
📖 英文解释
The using up of a resource; the amount used or eaten; buying of goods.
✏️ 例句
Global energy consumption has increased dramatically over the past century.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ energy/fuel/water consumption 能源/燃料/水消耗 ★ consumer consumption 消费者消费 辨析：consumption / use / expenditure：consumption 强调消耗掉；use 最通用；expenditure 强调金钱支出
🧠 助记
consume（消耗）+ ption（名词）= 消耗 → 消费、消耗
consumption::/kənˈsʌmpʃən/:n. 消费、消耗:Reducing carbon consumption is essential for addressing climate change.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ character education 品格教育 ★ a character in a novel/film 小说/电影中的人物 辨析：character / personality / nature：character 道德品质；personality 个性特点；nature 天性
🧠 助记
char（刻，如 chart 图表）+ acter = 刻下的特质 → 性格、特征
character::/ˈkærɪktə/:n. 性格、特征、人物:The company looks for candidates who demonstrate strong leadership character.

🧠 助记
cri（crit 关键）+ sis = 关键的节点 → 危机
crisis::/ˈkraɪsɪs/:n. 危机:Governments need long-term strategies to prevent future financial crises.

immigrant::/ˈɪmɪɡrənt/:n. 移民（入境者）:The country needs a comprehensive policy to integrate immigrants into society.

21. predict /prɪˈdɪkt/ v. 预测
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

pre（提前）+ dict（说）= 提前说出 → 预测
predict::/prɪˈdɪkt/:v. 预测:Models predict a significant increase in extreme weather events over the next decade.
