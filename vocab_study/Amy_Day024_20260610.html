1. stage /steɪdʒ/ n. 阶段，时期；舞台；v. 组织，上演
📖 英文解释
A point or period in a process; a raised platform for performance; to organize an event.
✏️ 例句
The project is currently in its final stage of development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
at this/that stage 在这个/那个阶段 stage by stage 逐步地 stage a protest/performance 举行抗议/表演 center stage 中心位置
🧠 助记
stage谐音“斯达吉”→逐步达到目的→阶段；舞台
stage::/steɪdʒ/:n. 阶段，时期:The research is at an early stage and needs more testing.
2. kill /kɪl/ v. 杀死；消灭；使…极度痛苦；n. 猎获物
📖 英文解释
To cause the death of; to destroy or end; to cause great pain.
✏️ 例句
Smoking kills thousands of people every year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
kill time 消磨时间 kill two birds with one stone 一石二鸟 be killed in 在…中遇难 killer n. 杀手；killer disease 致命疾病
🧠 助记
kill→K(击倒)+ill(生病)→击倒生病→杀死
kill::/kɪl/:v. 杀死:Overwork can kill your creativity and motivation.
3. specific /spəˈsɪfɪk/ adj. 具体的，特定的；明确的
📖 英文解释
Particular or exact; relating to one thing and not others.
✏️ 例句
You need to provide specific evidence to support your argument.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be specific about sth 对某事具体说明 specific to sth 特定于某事物 in specific terms 用具体语言 specifically adv. 具体地；specification n. 规格说明
🧠 助记
speci(种类)+fic(制作)→针对特定种类→具体的
specific::/spəˈsɪfɪk/:adj. 具体的，特定的:Please give a specific example to illustrate your point.
4. highly /ˈhaɪli/ adv. 非常，高度地；极其
📖 英文解释
To a great degree; very much; at or to a high level.
✏️ 例句
She is highly regarded by her colleagues for her expertise.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
highly + adj./v. 修饰形容词或动词：highly effective/recommended think highly of sb 对某人评价很高 highly skilled/educated/qualified 高技能/高学历/高资格 highly 不用于空间意义，用 high 表示高度
🧠 助记
high(高)+ly(副词)→高度地→非常
highly::/ˈhaɪli/:adv. 非常，高度地:The new drug is highly effective against the virus.
5. purchase /ˈpɜːrtʃɪs/ v./n. 购买，采购
📖 英文解释
To buy something; the act of buying something.
✏️ 例句
Consumers are encouraged to purchase environmentally friendly products.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
purchase sth from sb/somewhere 从某处购买某物 make a purchase 进行一次购买 online/bulk purchase 网上/批量购买 purchaser n. 购买者
🧠 助记
pur(=pro向前)+chase(追)→追着去买→购买
purchase::/ˈpɜːrtʃɪs/:v./n. 购买，采购:She made a large purchase at the electronics store.

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
executive/managing/artistic director 执行/总/艺术总监 board of directors 董事会 film/movie director 电影导演 direct v./adj. 指挥；直接的
🧠 助记
direct(指挥)+or(人)→负责指挥的人→主任；导演
director::/dɪˈrektər/:n. 主任，导演:The director gave clear instructions to the team.
7. significant /sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt/ adj. 重要的，显著的；有意义的

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
sign(标志)+ific+ant→有明显标志→显著的；重要的
significant::/sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt/:adj. 重要的，显著的:Education has a significant impact on career opportunities.

10. yourself /jɔːrˈself/ pron. 你自己（反身代词）
