1. conduct /kənˈdʌkt/ v. 进行，实施；指挥；n. 行为，品行
📖 英文解释
To carry out or manage something; to direct musicians; behavior or way of acting.
✏️ 例句
The university will conduct a survey to assess student satisfaction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
conduct a study/survey/experiment/interview 进行研究/调查/实验/采访 conduct oneself 行事，表现 code of conduct 行为准则 conductor n. 指挥；导体；列车员
🧠 助记
con(共同)+duct(引导)→引导大家→指挥；进行
conduct::/kənˈdʌkt/:v. 进行，实施:Researchers conducted a study on the effects of social media.
2. direct /dɪˈrekt/ adj. 直接的；v. 指导，导演；adv. 直接地
📖 英文解释
Without interruption or detour; to control or guide; to make a film.
✏️ 例句
She gave direct instructions to ensure there was no misunderstanding.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
direct access/contact/result 直接获取/联系/结果 direct sb to do sth 指示某人做某事 directly adv. 直接地；direction n. 方向
🧠 助记
di(=two两)+rect(直)→两点之间取直→直接的
direct::/dɪˈrekt/:adj. 直接的:The manager gave direct feedback to the team.
3. ensure /ɪnˈʃʊər/ v. 确保，保证
📖 英文解释
To make certain that something will happen or be the case.
✏️ 例句
The government must take action to ensure equal access to quality education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ensure that... 确保…（写作超高频） ensure sth 保证某事 ensure the safety/quality/success of 确保…的安全/质量/成功 辨析：ensure / assure / insure
🧠 助记
en(使)+sure(确定)→使其确定→确保
ensure::/ɪnˈʃʊər/:v. 确保，保证:We must ensure that everyone has access to clean water.
4. safety /ˈseɪfti/ n. 安全，平安
📖 英文解释
The condition of being protected from danger or harm.
✏️ 例句
Road safety regulations have helped reduce traffic accident fatalities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
food/road/public safety 食品/道路/公共安全 safety measures/standards/regulations 安全措施/标准/法规 for safety reasons 出于安全考虑 safe adj. 安全的
🧠 助记
safe(安全)+ty(名词后缀)→安全状态
safety::/ˈseɪfti/:n. 安全，平安:The company takes employee safety very seriously.
5. region /ˈriːdʒən/ n. 地区，区域；领域
📖 英文解释
An area of a country or the world; a part of the body or a domain of knowledge.
✏️ 例句
Economic development varies greatly across different regions of the country.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in the region of 大约（书面语） polar/coastal/mountainous region 极地/沿海/山区 regional differences 地区差异 regional adj. 地区的
🧠 助记
reg(统治)+ion→被统治管辖的区域→地区
region::/ˈriːdʒən/:n. 地区，区域:The northern region has a colder climate than the south.
6. tourist /ˈtʊərɪst/ n. 游客，观光者
📖 英文解释
A person who travels to a place for pleasure.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tourist attraction/destination/industry 旅游景点/目的地/产业 foreign/domestic tourist 外国/国内游客 tourist season 旅游旺季 tourism n. 旅游业
🧠 助记
tour(旅游)+ist(人)→旅游的人→游客
tourist::/ˈtʊərɪst/:n. 游客，观光者:The city is very popular among tourists in the summer.
7. legal /ˈliːɡl/ adj. 合法的；法律的
📖 英文解释
Permitted by law; relating to the law.
✏️ 例句
It is a legal requirement for all drivers to carry insurance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
legal action/advice/rights 法律行动/建议/权利 legal system/framework 法律体系/框架 legally adv. 合法地；legality n. 合法性；illegal adj. 非法的
🧠 助记
leg(=law法律)+al(形容词)→与法律有关的→合法的
legal::/ˈliːɡl/:adj. 合法的:All residents have the legal right to a fair trial.
8. link /lɪŋk/ n. 联系，链接；环节；v. 连接，联系
📖 英文解释
A connection between two things; a hyperlink; to connect or associate.
✏️ 例句
Researchers have established a clear link between air pollution and lung disease.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a link between A and B A与B的联系 link A to/with B 将A与B联系 chain link 链节；weak link 薄弱环节 linkage n. 联系
🧠 助记
link→链(link像链条)→链接；联系
link::/lɪŋk/:n. 联系，链接:Scientists found a strong link between stress and heart disease.
9. reflect /rɪˈflekt/ v. 反映；反射；深思，反思
📖 英文解释
To show or express; to bounce back light or sound; to think carefully.
✏️ 例句
The survey results reflect the general concerns of the public.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reflect sth 反映某事 reflect on sth 对某事进行反思 reflect light/sound 反射光/声音 reflection n. 反映；反射；深思
🧠 助记
re(回)+flect(弯曲)→光弯回来→反射；反映
reflect::/rɪˈflekt/:v. 反映:His words reflect a deep concern for social justice.
10. aim /eɪm/ v. 旨在，以…为目标；瞄准；n. 目标，目的
📖 英文解释
To have as a goal; to direct a weapon; a purpose or intention.
✏️ 例句
The new policy aims to reduce carbon emissions by 30% within a decade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
aim to do sth 旨在做某事（写作超高频） aim at doing sth 以…为目标 with the aim of 以…为目的 aimless adj. 漫无目的的
🧠 助记
aim→箭(aim=瞄准)→瞄准目标→目标；旨在
aim::/eɪm/:v. 旨在，以…为目标:The program aims to help students develop critical thinking skills.
11. husband /ˈhʌzbənd/ n. 丈夫；v. 节约使用（资源）

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
hus(=house)+band(绑)→被绑在家里的男人→丈夫
husband::/ˈhʌzbənd/:n. 丈夫:Her husband encouraged her to pursue her dreams.
12. underline /ˌʌndəˈlaɪn/ v. 强调；在…下面画线；n. 下划线
📖 英文解释
To draw a line under words; to emphasize the importance of something.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
underline the importance/need/fact 强调重要性/必要性/事实 underline a word/sentence 在词/句子下划线 underscore v. 强调（更正式）
🧠 助记
under(下)+line(线)→在下面画线→强调
underline::/ˌʌndəˈlaɪn/:v. 强调:The findings underline the need for immediate action.
13. worth /wɜːrθ/ adj. 值得的，有价值的；prep. 价值…；n. 价值，财富
📖 英文解释
Having a certain value; deserving of something; the value of something.
✏️ 例句
It is worth investing in education for long-term social and economic benefits.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be worth doing 值得做某事（注意：worth 后接动名词） be worth sth 值…（金额） net worth 净值，净资产 worthwhile adj. 值得做的；worthless adj. 毫无价值的
🧠 助记
worth→值(worth≈价值)→有价值的→值得
worth::/wɜːrθ/:adj. 值得的，有价值的:It is worth reading this book twice to fully understand it.

freedom::/ˈfriːdəm/:n. 自由，自由权:Freedom of speech is essential in a democratic country.

16. reveal /rɪˈviːl/ v. 揭示，透露；显示

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

reveal::/rɪˈviːl/:v. 揭示，透露:The study revealed a strong connection between sleep and productivity.
