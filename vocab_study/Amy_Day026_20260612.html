1. holiday /ˈhɒlɪdeɪ/ n. 假日，节日；假期
📖 英文解释
A day of recreation when no work is done; a vacation period.
✏️ 例句
Many families use the summer holiday as an opportunity to travel together.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
public/national holiday 公众/国家假日 go on holiday 去度假 holiday destination/resort 度假目的地/胜地 vacation n. 假期（美式）
🧠 助记
holy(神圣)+day→神圣的日子→节日；假日
holiday::/ˈhɒlɪdeɪ/:n. 假日，节日:The government declared a public holiday to celebrate the occasion.
2. limited /ˈlɪmɪtɪd/ adj. 有限的，受限制的
📖 英文解释
Small in amount or extent; restricted.
✏️ 例句
Many students in rural areas have limited access to quality educational resources.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
limited resources/time/access 有限的资源/时间/机会 a limited number of 有限数量的 limited company (Ltd) 有限公司 limit n./v. 限制；limitation n. 局限性
🧠 助记
limit(限制)+ed(过去分词形容词)→被限制的→有限的
limited::/ˈlɪmɪtɪd/:adj. 有限的，受限制的:Students in remote areas have limited access to the internet.
3. push /pʊʃ/ v. 推；推动；施压；n. 推力；努力
📖 英文解释
To apply force to move something; to pressure someone; to promote strongly.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
push for sth 极力争取某事 push sb to do sth 施压某人做某事 push the boundaries 突破边界 pushy adj. 咄咄逼人的
🧠 助记
push→push(推)→推动；施压
push::/pʊʃ/:v. 推:Parents often push their children to achieve higher academic results.
4. twenty /ˈtwenti/ num. 二十
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
twen(=twin双)+ty(十)→两个十→二十
twenty::/ˈtwenti/:num. 二十:Twenty students volunteered to help with the community project.

police→polis(城邦)+ce→维护城邦秩序的人→警察
police::/pəˈliːs/:n. 警察（集合名词）:The police work to maintain law and order in society.
6. film /fɪlm/ n. 电影；胶片；薄膜；v. 拍摄

film::/fɪlm/:n. 电影:The film raised important questions about social justice.
