1. extra /ˈekstrə/ adj./adv. 额外的，特别的；n. 额外的事物
📖 英文解释
More than what is usual or expected; in addition to what is normal.
✏️ 例句
Students who want to improve should put in extra effort every day.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ extra time / money / effort 额外的时间/钱/努力 ★ go the extra mile 付出额外努力（习语） ☆ extra charge 附加费用 辨析：extra / additional / more：extra 强调超出正常；additional 正式补充；more 最通用
🧠 助记
extra 发音像「诶克斯它」→ 超出X之外的那个 = 额外的
extra::/ˈekstrə/:adj./adv. 额外的，特别的:She always puts in extra effort to help others.
2. electronic /ɪˌlekˈtrɒnɪk/ adj. 电子的；电子设备的
📖 英文解释
Relating to electrons or devices that use electricity and computer technology.
✏️ 例句
Electronic devices such as smartphones have transformed the way we communicate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ electronic device / mail / commerce 电子设备/邮件/商务 ★ electronic payment system 电子支付系统（科技类写作） ☆ electronic engineering 电子工程 辨析：electronic（电子技术）/ electric（用电的）/ electrical（与电相关的）
🧠 助记
electr（电）+ onic → 与电子技术有关的
electronic::/ɪˌlekˈtrɒnɪk/:adj. 电子的:The store sells a wide range of electronic products.
3. ignore /ɪɡˈnɔː(r)/ v. 忽视，不理睬；忽略
📖 英文解释
To pay no attention to someone or something; to disregard intentionally.
✏️ 例句
We cannot afford to ignore the warning signs of climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ ignore the fact that 忽视…这一事实（写作强调句） ★ be ignored by 被…忽视 ☆ ignore sb's advice 无视某人的建议 辨析：ignore / overlook / neglect：ignore 有意不理；overlook 无意忽略；neglect 忽视（职责）
🧠 助记
i + gnore（谐音「诺」）→ 不给你一个诺（回应），无视你
ignore::/ɪɡˈnɔː(r)/:v. 忽视，不理睬:She ignored his rude comment and walked away.
4. survive /səˈvaɪv/ v. 幸存，生还；经受住；继续存在
📖 英文解释
To continue to live or exist, especially after a difficult experience.
✏️ 例句
Small businesses need to adapt quickly to survive in a competitive market.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ survive a crisis / disaster 经历危机/灾难后存活 ★ manage to survive 设法生存下来 ☆ survival of the fittest 适者生存（考研必背） 辨析：survive / endure / withstand：survive 强调结果（活下来）；endure 强调过程（忍受）；withstand 强调抵抗力
🧠 助记
sur（超过）+ vive（生命，活）→ 超越死亡，活下来 = 幸存
survive::/səˈvaɪv/:v. 幸存，生还:Only the strongest animals survive in harsh conditions.

fuel = f + uel（谐音「油啊」）→ 油是燃料
fuel::/fjuːəl/:n. 燃料:Fossil fuels are a major source of greenhouse gas emissions.
