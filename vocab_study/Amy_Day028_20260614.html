1. grant /ɡrɑːnt/ v. 同意，授予，给予；n. 拨款，助学金
📖 英文解释
To give officially; to agree to give something requested; money given for a specific purpose.
✏️ 例句
The university granted scholarships to students with outstanding academic records.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ grant sb sth / grant sth to sb 授予某人某物 ★ take sth for granted 认为…理所当然（考研常考短语） ☆ research grant 科研经费 辨析：grant（正式授予）/ give（日常给予）/ award（颁奖给予）
🧠 助记
grant 谐音「贯他」→ 贯穿审批，把钱给他 = 授予
grant::/ɡrɑːnt/:v. 同意，授予，给予:The government granted funding for the new research project.
2. factory /ˈfæktri/ n. 工厂，制造厂
📖 英文解释
A building where goods are manufactured or assembled in large quantities.
✏️ 例句
The closure of the factory led to hundreds of job losses in the local community.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ factory worker / owner 工厂工人/所有者 ★ factory production / output 工厂生产/产量 ☆ sweatshop / factory farming 血汗工厂/工厂化农业（社会议题） 辨析：factory（工厂）/ plant（大型生产设施）/ workshop（小型作坊）
🧠 助记
fact（做出来的事实）+ ory → 制造事实（产品）的地方 = 工厂
factory::/ˈfæktri/:n. 工厂，制造厂:The new factory will produce electric vehicles.
3. authority /ɔːˈθɒrəti/ n. 权力，权威；当局；权威人士
📖 英文解释
The power or right to give orders; an official organization; an expert in a field.
✏️ 例句
Local authorities have the power to enforce environmental regulations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in authority 掌权 ★ the authorities 当局，有关部门 ☆ an authority on sth 某领域的权威 辨析：authority（正式权力）/ power（更广泛的权力）/ influence（影响力）
🧠 助记
author（作者，创始人）+ ity → 有创立权的人 = 权威
authority::/ɔːˈθɒrəti/:n. 权力，权威:The health authority issued new guidelines on diet and exercise.
4. association /əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃn/ n. 协会，联合；联系，联想
📖 英文解释
A group of people organized for a common purpose; a connection or relationship between things.
✏️ 例句
There is a clear association between smoking and lung cancer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in association with 与…合作 ★ trade / professional association 贸易/职业协会 ☆ word association 词语联想 辨析：association（组织或联系）/ organization（组织架构）/ connection（具体联系）
🧠 助记
associ（associate，联合）+ ation → 联合在一起的团体 = 协会
association::/əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃn/:n. 协会，联合:The research found an association between diet and heart disease.
5. tree /triː/ n. 树，乔木；树状图
📖 英文解释
A tall plant with a woody trunk and branches.
✏️ 例句
Deforestation destroys trees that are vital for absorbing carbon dioxide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ plant / cut down trees 种树/砍树 ★ decision tree 决策树（逻辑/数学） ☆ family tree 家谱 考研：生态环境类文章基础词，常与 forest / deforestation 连用
🧠 助记
tree 形似一棵树，t 是树干，ree 是树枝
tree::/triː/:n. 树，乔木:The park is famous for its ancient trees.
6. arrive /əˈraɪv/ v. 到达，抵达；达到
📖 英文解释
To reach a destination; to come to a decision or conclusion.
✏️ 例句
After years of debate, scientists have arrived at a consensus on climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ arrive at / in（arrive at 小地方；arrive in 大地方） ★ arrive at a conclusion / agreement 得出结论/达成协议 ☆ arrive on the scene 出现在现场 辨析：arrive（到达）/ reach（强调到达某点）/ get to（口语）
🧠 助记
ar（向）+ rive（river，河）→ 渡河到达彼岸 = 到达
arrive::/əˈraɪv/:v. 到达，抵达:Delegates arrived at the conference from over fifty countries.
7. ready /ˈredi/ adj. 准备好的，愿意的；adv. 已经
📖 英文解释
Fully prepared; willing to do something; available for immediate use.
✏️ 例句
Students must be ready to adapt to new learning environments in the digital age.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be ready for / to do 准备好做某事 ★ get ready 准备好（口语） ☆ ready-made 现成的 辨析：ready（状态准备好）/ prepared（经过准备）/ set（正式比赛口令）
🧠 助记
ready = read（阅读）+ y → 读完书就准备好了
ready::/ˈredi/:adj. 准备好的，愿意的:She was ready to face any challenge that came her way.
8. cheap /tʃiːp/ adj. 便宜的；廉价的；吝啬的
📖 英文解释
Low in price; of poor quality; unwilling to spend money.
✏️ 例句
Cheap labor in developing countries has attracted multinational corporations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ cheap labor / goods 廉价劳动力/商品（经济类写作） ★ dirt cheap 极其便宜 ☆ cheap shot 不公平的批评 辨析：cheap（价格低或质量差）/ inexpensive（价格实惠，无负面含义）/ affordable（负担得起的）
🧠 助记
cheap 谐音「奇破」→ 奇怪地破旧 = 廉价的
cheap::/tʃiːp/:adj. 便宜的:The company used cheap materials to reduce costs.
9. patent /ˈpeɪtnt/ n. 专利，专利权；adj. 专利的；明显的
📖 英文解释
An official right to be the only producer of a product; obvious or clear.
✏️ 例句
The company applied for a patent to protect its innovative technology.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ apply for / hold a patent 申请/持有专利 ★ patent protection / infringement 专利保护/侵权 ☆ it is patent that 显然的是 辨析：patent（专利）/ copyright（版权）/ trademark（商标）
🧠 助记
pat（拍）+ ent → 拍板确认，专属权利 = 专利
patent::/ˈpeɪtnt/:n. 专利，专利权:Tech companies spend billions to protect their patents.
10. flight /flaɪt/ n. 飞行，航班；逃跑；一段楼梯
📖 英文解释
The action of flying; a journey by plane; the act of running away.
✏️ 例句
Capital flight from developing countries remains a serious economic problem.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ take a flight 乘飞机 ★ capital flight 资本外流（经济术语） ☆ flight of stairs 一段楼梯 辨析：flight（飞行或逃跑）/ escape（逃跑）/ journey（旅程）
🧠 助记
fli（fly）+ ght → 飞起来 = 飞行
flight::/flaɪt/:n. 飞行，航班:The direct flight from Shanghai to London takes about twelve hours.
11. spread /spred/ v. 传播，蔓延；展开；n. 扩散，范围
📖 英文解释
To extend over a large area; to distribute or share widely.
✏️ 例句
Social media can spread both accurate information and dangerous misinformation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ spread rapidly / widely 迅速/广泛传播 ★ the spread of disease / technology 疾病/技术的蔓延 ☆ spread one's wings 展翅高飞（开始独立发展） 辨析：spread（扩散，强调范围）/ circulate（循环，强调流通）/ distribute（分配）
🧠 助记
spread 谐音「斯普瑞的」→ 像喷雾一样扩散开来
spread::/spred/:v. 传播，蔓延:The virus spread rapidly throughout the community.
12. expense /ɪkˈspens/ n. 费用，花费；代价
📖 英文解释
The cost required for something; money spent on something.
✏️ 例句
Education expenses are rising faster than household incomes in many countries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at the expense of 以…为代价（考研写作高频表达） ★ living / medical expenses 生活/医疗费用 ☆ spare no expense 不惜代价 辨析：expense（具体花费）/ cost（成本）/ price（价格）/ fee（服务费）
🧠 助记
ex（出）+ pense（花）→ 把钱花出去 = 费用
expense::/ɪkˈspens/:n. 费用，花费:Economic growth should not come at the expense of the environment.
13. famous /ˈfeɪməs/ adj. 著名的，有名的
📖 英文解释
Known by many people; widely recognized.
✏️ 例句
The city is famous for its ancient architecture and vibrant culture.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be famous for 以…闻名（必背搭配） ★ world-famous 世界著名的 ☆ infamous 臭名昭著的（注意区分） 辨析：famous（广为人知）/ well-known（知名的，更低调）/ renowned（学术/正式场合）/ notorious（因坏事出名）
🧠 助记
fame（名声）+ ous → 有名声的 = 著名的
famous::/ˈfeɪməs/:adj. 著名的，有名的:She became famous for her groundbreaking research in medicine.
14. indeed /ɪnˈdiːd/ adv. 的确，确实；实际上；真正地
📖 英文解释
Used to emphasize a statement; in fact; really.
✏️ 例句
The results were indeed surprising, contradicting previous research findings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ indeed + 形容词/副词（加强语气） ★ indeed, ...（句首状语，强调后句） ☆ a friend in need is a friend indeed 患难见真情（谚语） 辨析：indeed（加强肯定）/ certainly（肯定）/ admittedly（承认地）
🧠 助记
in + deed（行动）→ 在行动上确实如此 = 的确
indeed::/ɪnˈdiːd/:adv. 的确，确实:This is indeed a significant breakthrough in medical science.
15. establish /ɪˈstæblɪʃ/ v. 建立，创立；确立；证明
📖 英文解释
To set up an organization or system; to show something to be true.
✏️ 例句
The United Nations was established in 1945 to promote international peace.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ establish a relationship / system / fact 建立关系/体系/确立事实 ★ well-established 成熟的，公认的 ☆ establish oneself as 确立自己…的地位 辨析：establish（正式建立）/ found（创立，侧重开始）/ set up（建立，口语化）
🧠 助记
e + stable（稳定）+ ish → 使稳固地建立起来 = 建立
establish::/ɪˈstæblɪʃ/:v. 建立，创立:The company was established over a hundred years ago.
16. observe /əbˈzɜːv/ v. 观察，注视；遵守；注意到
📖 英文解释
To watch carefully; to follow a law or custom; to notice or remark.
✏️ 例句
Scientists observe natural phenomena carefully before drawing conclusions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ observe sb doing sth 观察某人做某事 ★ observe rules / laws 遵守规则/法律 ☆ as observed earlier 如前所述（学术写作） 辨析：observe（有意识地观察或遵守）/ watch（看）/ notice（注意到）
🧠 助记
ob（对着）+ serve（服务）→ 对着目标服务 = 认真观察并遵守
observe::/əbˈzɜːv/:v. 观察，注视:Researchers observed significant changes in the animals' behavior.
17. chief /tʃiːf/ adj. 主要的，首要的；n. 首领，主任
📖 英文解释
Most important; the leader of a group or organization.
✏️ 例句
The chief reason for the failure was a lack of communication between departments.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ chief concern / goal / cause 主要关切/目标/原因 ★ chief executive officer (CEO) 首席执行官 ☆ in chief 主要地，尤其（如 commander in chief） 辨析：chief（首要的）/ main（主要的）/ primary（首要的，学术）/ principal（最重要的）
🧠 助记
chief 谐音「奇夫」→ 奇异的丈夫，是族里的头儿 = 首领
chief::/tʃiːf/:adj. 主要的，首要的:The chief challenge facing modern cities is sustainable development.
18. safe /seɪf/ adj. 安全的，无危险的；n. 保险箱
📖 英文解释
Not in danger; protected from harm; a locked metal container.
✏️ 例句
It is important to create safe environments where children can learn and grow.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ safe and sound 安全无恙 ★ safe to assume / say 可以放心地认为/说 ☆ play it safe 谨慎行事 辨析：safe（安全的）/ secure（安全感强，有保障）/ protected（受保护的）
🧠 助记
safe 谐音「赛福」→ 比赛得了福，平安胜出 = 安全
safe::/seɪf/:adj. 安全的，无危险的:Ensure that all employees work in a safe environment.
19. nor /nɔː(r)/ conj. 也不（用于否定句之后）
📖 英文解释
Used to introduce a further negative statement after 'neither' or another negative.
✏️ 例句
The new policy neither reduced poverty nor improved access to education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ neither...nor... 既不…也不…（写作否定并列） ★ nor does/did/is... 也不…（倒装结构） ☆ nor I, nor anyone 我也不，任何人也不 辨析：nor（接续否定）/ or（接续选择）/ neither（两者都不）
🧠 助记
nor = no + r → 在 no 的基础上再加一个否定 = 也不
nor::/nɔː(r)/:conj. 也不（用于否定句之后）:Neither the government nor the public was prepared for the crisis.
20. foot /fʊt/ n. 脚；英尺；底部；v. 步行；支付（账单）
📖 英文解释
The lower extremity of the leg; a unit of measurement (12 inches); the base of something.
✏️ 例句
Many businesses struggled to get back on their feet after the economic downturn.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ get back on one's feet 重新站起来（恢复） ★ foot the bill 付账（习语） ☆ at the foot of 在…底部 辨析：foot（整体足部）/ feet（复数）/ step（步伐）
🧠 助记
foot 谐音「腐特」→ 腐烂了特别难受的脚 = 脚
foot::/fʊt/:n. 脚:The company had to foot the bill for the damages.
21. debate /dɪˈbeɪt/ n./v. 辩论，讨论；争议
📖 英文解释
A formal discussion in which opposing views are expressed; to argue about something.
✏️ 例句
There is ongoing debate about whether economic growth is compatible with environmental protection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ debate whether / how 辩论是否/如何 ★ heated / open debate 激烈/公开辩论 ☆ beyond debate 无可争议的 辨析：debate（正式辩论）/ discuss（讨论）/ argue（争论，含对抗性）
🧠 助记
de（彻底）+ bate（打）→ 彻底打一场口头战 = 辩论
debate::/dɪˈbeɪt/:n./v. 辩论，讨论:The policy sparked a national debate on immigration.
22. agency /ˈeɪdʒənsi/ n. 机构，代理处；代理权；能动性
📖 英文解释
A business providing a service; a government body; the capacity to act independently.

International agencies such as the WHO play a critical role in managing global health crises.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ government / news / travel agency 政府/新闻/旅行机构 ★ human agency 人的主观能动性（学术类高频） ☆ agency fee 代理费 辨析：agency（机构或代理权）/ organization（组织）/ institution（机构，更正式）
🧠 助记
agent（代理人）+ cy → 代理人工作的地方 = 代理机构
agency::/ˈeɪdʒənsi/:n. 机构，代理处:The advertising agency created a powerful campaign for the brand.
23. educate /ˈedʒukeɪt/ v. 教育，培养；使了解

★ educate sb about sth 教育某人了解某事 ★ well-educated 受过良好教育的 ☆ educate oneself 自我教育 辨析：educate（系统培养）/ teach（传授知识）/ train（职业技能训练）

educate::/ˈedʒukeɪt/:v. 教育，培养:Programs that educate children about nutrition can improve public health.
24. compete /kəmˈpiːt/ v. 竞争，比赛；参加比赛

compete::/kəmˈpiːt/:v. 竞争，比赛:Small businesses find it hard to compete with multinational corporations.
25. wish /wɪʃ/ v. 希望，祝愿；但愿；n. 愿望，祝愿
