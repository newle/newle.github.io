1. staff /stɑːf/ n. 员工，全体职工；v. 配备员工
📖 英文解释
The people who work for an organization; to provide workers for an organization.
✏️ 例句
The hospital relies on a dedicated staff of doctors, nurses, and support workers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ staff member / management 员工/管理人员 ★ be fully / adequately staffed 配备充足员工 ☆ staff turnover 员工流动率 辨析：staff（集体概念，不可数）/ employee（单个雇员）/ workforce（劳动力，更广）
🧠 助记
staff 形似「手杖」→ 像手杖一样支撑公司运作的人们 = 员工
staff::/stɑːf/:n. 员工，全体职工:The company hired additional staff to handle the increased workload.
2. adapt /əˈdæpt/ v. 适应，改编；调整
📖 英文解释
To change behavior or approach to suit different conditions; to modify something for a new purpose.
✏️ 例句
Species that fail to adapt to changing environments risk extinction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ adapt to sth 适应某事（必背搭配） ★ adapt oneself to 使自己适应 ☆ adapt a novel 将小说改编 辨析：adapt（主动调整适应）/ adjust（微调）/ accommodate（容纳适应）
🧠 助记
ad（向）+ apt（适合）→ 向适合方向努力 = 适应
adapt::/əˈdæpt/:v. 适应，改编:Students must adapt to new learning environments in the digital age.
3. boss /bɒs/ n. 老板，上司；v. 指挥，发号施令
📖 英文解释
A person in charge of a worker; to give orders to someone.
✏️ 例句
A good boss motivates employees rather than simply issuing orders.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ report to one's boss 向上司汇报 ★ boss sb around 对某人发号施令 ☆ the big boss 大老板（口语） 辨析：boss（日常非正式，上司）/ manager（管理者）/ supervisor（监督者）/ employer（雇主）
🧠 助记
boss 谐音「不斯」→ 不像普通员工，是发号施令的人 = 老板
boss::/bɒs/:n. 老板，上司:She impressed her boss with her dedication and creativity.
4. unless /ənˈles/ conj. 除非，如果不
📖 英文解释
Except on the condition that; if not.
✏️ 例句
Economic inequality will not improve unless governments take bold action.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ unless + 主语 + 谓语（条件状语从句） ★ unless otherwise stated 除非另有说明（正式文书） ☆ unless 和 if not 区别：unless 更强调排除情况 辨析：unless（排除性条件）/ if not（简单否定条件）
🧠 助记
un（不）+ less（少）→ 如果不减少障碍的话 = 除非
unless::/ənˈles/:conj. 除非，如果不:The project will fail unless everyone commits to working together.
5. unemployment /ˌʌnɪmˈplɔɪmənt/ n. 失业，失业率
📖 英文解释
The state of being without a paid job; the number of unemployed people.
✏️ 例句
High unemployment rates can lead to social unrest and economic stagnation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ unemployment rate / benefit 失业率/失业救济金 ★ tackle / reduce unemployment 应对/降低失业率（写作） ☆ structural unemployment 结构性失业（经济学术语） 辨析：unemployment（失业现象）/ joblessness（失业状态）/ redundancy（裁员）
🧠 助记
un（非）+ employment（就业）→ 非就业状态 = 失业
unemployment::/ˌʌnɪmˈplɔɪmənt/:n. 失业，失业率:The government introduced new measures to combat rising unemployment.
6. fix /fɪks/ v. 修理，固定；确定；安排；n. 困境；固定
📖 英文解释
To repair; to fasten firmly; to decide on a time or price; an awkward situation.
✏️ 例句
Policy makers must fix the structural flaws in the educational system.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ fix a problem / system 解决问题/修复系统 ★ be in a fix 陷入困境 ☆ fix on / upon 确定，把目光集中于 辨析：fix（修理或确定）/ repair（修理，更正式）/ mend（缝补，修复）
🧠 助记
fix 谐音「非可斯」→ 非得用技术使其稳定 = 固定/修理
fix::/fɪks/:v. 修理，固定:The government promised to fix the broken healthcare system.
7. scale /skeɪl/ n. 规模，尺度；刻度；天平；鱼鳞；v. 缩放，攀登
📖 英文解释
The relative size or extent; a measuring instrument; to climb; to change in size.
✏️ 例句
The scale of climate change requires a coordinated global response.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on a large / global scale 大规模地/在全球范围内（写作频率极高） ★ scale up / down 扩大/缩小规模 ☆ pay scale 薪酬级别 辨析：scale（规模或尺度）/ size（大小）/ extent（程度）
🧠 助记
scale 谐音「斯卡了」→ 用尺子卡量大小 = 规模/尺度
scale::/skeɪl/:n. 规模，尺度:The problem of homelessness exists on a large scale in major cities.
8. possibility /ˌpɒsəˈbɪləti/ n. 可能性；可能的事物
📖 英文解释
The likelihood of something happening; something that might be done or achieved.
✏️ 例句
Advances in technology open up new possibilities for treating previously incurable diseases.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the possibility of / that... ...的可能性（写作引出论点） ★ explore the possibility 探讨可能性 ☆ a remote / distinct possibility 微小/明显的可能性 辨析：possibility（可能性本身）/ probability（概率，更正式）/ chance（机会/可能）
🧠 助记
possible（可能的）+ ity → 可能性
possibility::/ˌpɒsəˈbɪləti/:n. 可能性:There is a strong possibility that temperatures will continue to rise.
9. application /ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃn/ n. 申请；应用；应用程序
📖 英文解释
A formal request; the practical use of something; a computer program.
✏️ 例句
The practical application of artificial intelligence raises important ethical questions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ job / university application 工作/大学申请 ★ practical application of technology 技术的实际应用 ☆ application software 应用软件 辨析：application（申请或应用）/ request（请求）/ use（使用）
🧠 助记
apply（应用/申请）+ cation → 应用/申请的行为
application::/ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃn/:n. 申请:She submitted her application for the scholarship on time.
10. participant /pɑːˈtɪsɪpənt/ n. 参与者，参加者
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
parti（部分）+ cipant（取）→ 取走一部分参与 = 参与者
participant::/pɑːˈtɪsɪpənt/:n. 参与者，参加者:Over five hundred participants took part in the annual conference.

union::/ˈjuːniən/:n. 联盟，工会:The workers' union negotiated for better wages and working conditions.

13. emotion /ɪˈməʊʃn/ n. 情绪，情感；激动

e + motion（运动）→ 内心情感的涌动 = 情绪
emotion::/ɪˈməʊʃn/:n. 情绪，情感:The speech stirred deep emotions in the audience.
