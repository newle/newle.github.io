1. voice /vɔɪs/ n. 声音；嗓音；表达意见的权利；v. 表达，说出
📖 英文解释
Sound produced in speech or singing; the right to express opinions; to express an opinion.
✏️ 例句
Citizens must have a voice in decisions that directly affect their daily lives.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ give voice to 表达（思想/情感） ★ voice concerns / opinions 表达关切 / 意见 ☆ active / passive voice 主动 / 被动语态（语法） 辨析：voice / sound / noise：voice 人或动物的声音；sound 任何声音；noise 噪音
🧠 助记
voice = 我(v谐音)+ice(冰)——冰冷的声音，铿锵有力
voice::/vɔɪs/:n. 声音:The media should give voice to the concerns of ordinary citizens.

★ movie industry / theater 电影行业 / 影院 ★ blockbuster movie 大片 ☆ 辨析：movie / film / cinema：movie 口语为主；film 更正式学术；cinema 指电影院或电影艺术
🧠 助记
movie = move(移动)+ie——移动的画面=电影
movie::/ˈmuːvi/:n. 电影，影片:The movie explores themes of identity and belonging in modern society.

✏️ 例句
One characteristic of successful entrepreneurs is their ability to adapt quickly to change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ characteristic of 是…的典型特征 ★ distinctive / defining characteristic 显著 / 决定性特征 ☆ characterize v. 以…为特征 辨析：characteristic / feature / trait：characteristic 较正式书面；feature 泛用；trait 人格特质
🧠 助记
character(性格/人物)+istic——人物的特有性格=特征
characteristic::/ˌkærəktəˈrɪstɪk/:n. 特征，特性:Adaptability is a key characteristic of successful organizations.

★ join in (活动) 参与 ★ join forces with 与…联合 ☆ 辨析：join / participate in / take part in：join 强调加入组织/群体；participate in 参与活动；take part in 口语通用
🧠 助记
join = 聚(谐音)——大家聚在一起=加入
join::/dʒɔɪn/:v. 加入，参加:She decided to join the environmental protection volunteer group.

★ operate on sb 给某人做手术 ★ operate a business 经营企业 ☆ operation n. 运作；手术；行动（一词多义） 辨析：operate / function / run：operate 较正式；function 侧重正常运转；run 口语

6. pollution /pəˈluːʃn/ n. 污染，污染物

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ air / water / soil / noise pollution 空气 / 水 / 土壤 / 噪声污染 ★ combat / tackle pollution 治理污染 ☆ pollutant n. 污染物 辨析：pollution / contamination：pollution 广义环境污染；contamination 特指有害物质污染（食品/医疗）

pollute(污染)+ion——污染的名词=污染
pollution::/pəˈluːʃn/:n. 污染，污染物:Industrial pollution has severely damaged the local river ecosystem.
7. discuss /dɪˈskʌs/ v. 讨论，商讨；论述
📖 英文解释
To talk about a subject in detail; to consider something from various perspectives.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ discuss sth with sb 与某人讨论某事（注意：discuss不接about） ★ discuss the issue / problem / possibility 讨论问题/可能性 ☆ discussion n. 讨论；under discussion 正在讨论中 辨析：discuss / debate / argue：discuss 中性交流；debate 正式辩论；argue 争论
🧠 助记
dis(分开)+cuss(敲打)——分开敲打不同观点=讨论
discuss::/dɪˈskʌs/:v. 讨论，商讨:Students are encouraged to discuss ethical issues in class.
8. extend /ɪkˈstend/ v. 延伸，扩展；延长；提供，给予

🧠 助记
ex(出)+tend(伸)——向外伸展=延伸/扩展
extend::/ɪkˈstend/:v. 延伸，扩展:The university plans to extend its library facilities next year.

🧠 助记
appro(接近)+priate(私有)——接近私有=合乎规矩=适当
appropriate::/əˈprəʊpriət/:adj. 适当的，合适的:It is not appropriate to use informal language in academic writing.
