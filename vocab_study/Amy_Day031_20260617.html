1. conclusion /kənˈkluːʒn/ n. 结论，结尾；缔结（条约）
📖 英文解释
A judgment or decision reached by reasoning; the end or final part of something.
✏️ 例句
The report's conclusions were based on extensive data collected over a five-year period.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ draw / reach a conclusion 得出结论 ★ in conclusion 总之，最后（作文结尾高频） ☆ jump to conclusions 仓促下结论 辨析：conclusion / finding / result：conclusion 逻辑推断；finding 研究发现；result 实验结果
🧠 助记
con(完全)+clusion(关闭)——完全关上=得出最终结论
conclusion::/kənˈkluːʒn/:n. 结论，结尾:In conclusion, the evidence strongly supports the need for policy reform.

🧠 助记
moral = 摩落(谐音)——道德是灵魂不能让它摩落（磨落）
moral::/ˈmɒrəl/:adj. 道德的，道义的:Leaders have a moral obligation to act in the public interest.
