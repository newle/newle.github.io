1. innovation /ˌɪnəˈveɪʃn/ n. 创新，革新；新事物
📖 英文解释
The introduction of new ideas, methods, or products; a new idea or method.
✏️ 例句
Technological innovation is the driving force behind economic growth in the modern era.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ foster / drive / encourage innovation 培育 / 推动 / 鼓励创新 ★ innovation in technology / education 技术 / 教育创新 ☆ innovative adj. 创新的；innovate v. 创新 辨析：innovation / invention / discovery：innovation 将新想法付诸实用；invention 首创发明；discovery 发现已存在之物
🧠 助记
innov(新)+ation——引入新东西=创新
innovation::/ˌɪnəˈveɪʃn/:n. 创新，革新:Investment in research and development is essential for sustaining innovation.

★ pick up 捡起；学会；(车)接人；振奋 ★ pick out 挑出，辨认出 ☆ take your pick 随便选 辨析：pick / choose / select：pick 口语随意选；choose 一般选择；select 正式仔细筛选
🧠 助记
pick = 辟可(谐音)——辟开选出来最好的
pick::/pɪk/:v. 挑选，采摘:The university can pick the best candidates from a large pool of applicants.
