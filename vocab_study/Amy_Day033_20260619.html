1. assume /əˈsjuːm/ v. 假设；假定；承担
📖 英文解释
to suppose something to be true without proof; to take on a responsibility or role
✏️ 例句
Don't assume that everyone agrees with you.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
assume responsibility 承担责任；assume that 假定；take/assume office 就职
🧠 助记
as（加强）+ sume（拿取）→ 先拿来当作真的 → 假设
assume::/əˈsjuːm/:v. 假设:The plan assumes that all participants will cooperate.
2. invite /ɪnˈvaɪt/ v. 邀请；招致；吸引
📖 英文解释
to make a polite or formal request for someone to go somewhere or do something; to attract something undesirable
✏️ 例句
They invited us to dinner at their new house.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
invite sb to do sth 邀请某人做；invite trouble 招惹麻烦；invitation letter 邀请函
🧠 助记
in（进入）+ vite（联想 visit 拜访）→ 叫人进来访问 → 邀请
invite::/ɪnˈvaɪt/:v. 邀请:The school invited parents to attend the open day.
3. collect /kəˈlekt/ v. 收集；聚集；领取
📖 英文解释
to bring or gather things together; to accumulate as a hobby; to fetch someone or something
✏️ 例句
She collects stamps from different countries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
collect data 收集数据；collect taxes 征税；collect oneself 镇定自若
🧠 助记
col（共同）+ lect（选择）→ 共同挑选聚在一起 → 收集
collect::/kəˈlekt/:v. 收集:Researchers collected data from over 500 participants.
4. campaign /kæmˈpeɪn/ n./v. 运动；战役；竞选
📖 英文解释
an organized course of action to achieve a goal; to work in an organized way towards a goal
✏️ 例句
The environmental campaign raised public awareness about pollution.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
election campaign 竞选活动；campaign for/against 为……/反对……而活动；launch a campaign 发起运动
🧠 助记
camp（营地）+ aign → 军营里发起的行动 → 战役；运动
campaign::/kæmˈpeɪn/:n./v. 运动:The anti-smoking campaign had a significant impact on public health.
5. native /ˈneɪtɪv/ adj. 本地的；天生的；n. 本地人
📖 英文解释
belonging to or associated with the place or circumstances of a person's birth; born in a particular place
✏️ 例句
She is a native speaker of Mandarin Chinese.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
native language 母语；native country 祖国；go native 入乡随俗
🧠 助记
nat（出生）+ ive → 出生在那里的 → 本地的；本地人
native::/ˈneɪtɪv/:adj. 本地的:The program trains native speakers to teach English abroad.
6. interesting /ˈɪntrəstɪŋ/ adj. 有趣的；引人入胜的
📖 英文解释
arousing curiosity or interest; holding or catching the attention
✏️ 例句
The documentary was very interesting and informative.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
find sth interesting 觉得某事有趣；interesting in 对……感兴趣（较少用，多用 interested in）
🧠 助记
interest（兴趣）+ -ing → 带来兴趣的 → 有趣的
interesting::/ˈɪntrəstɪŋ/:adj. 有趣的:The professor gave an interesting lecture on climate change.
7. shortage /ˈʃɔːrtɪdʒ/ n. 不足；短缺；缺乏
📖 英文解释
a situation in which something is not available in sufficient quantities
✏️ 例句
There is a severe shortage of clean water in the region.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
food/water/housing shortage 食物/水/住房短缺；shortage of 缺乏；in short supply 供不应求
🧠 助记
short（短缺的）+ -age（名词后缀）→ 短缺的状态 → 不足
shortage::/ˈʃɔːrtɪdʒ/:n. 不足:The hospital faces a shortage of qualified medical staff.
8. reform /rɪˈfɔːrm/ v./n. 改革；改良；革新
📖 英文解释
to make changes in order to improve something; a change that brings improvement
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
economic/political reform 经济/政治改革；reform measures 改革措施；carry out reforms 实施改革
🧠 助记
re（重新）+ form（形成）→ 重新塑造形态 → 改革
reform::/rɪˈfɔːrm/:v./n. 改革:Educational reform is essential to meet the demands of a modern economy.
9. corporate /ˈkɔːrpərət/ adj. 公司的；法人的；共同的
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
corp（身体/公司）+ orate → 属于公司团体的 → 公司的
corporate::/ˈkɔːrpərət/:adj. 公司的:The company released its annual corporate social responsibility report.
10. powerful /ˈpaʊərfəl/ adj. 强有力的；有权势的；效果显著的

powerful enough to 足够强大以至于；powerful tool 有力的工具；powerful nation 强国
🧠 助记

powerful::/ˈpaʊərfəl/:adj. 强有力的:Social media has become a powerful tool for communication.
11. central /ˈsentrəl/ adj. 中心的；中央的；主要的
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

central::/ˈsentrəl/:adj. 中心的:Trust is central to a successful working relationship.
12. efficiency /ɪˈfɪʃənsi/ n. 效率；效能；效益
📖 英文解释
the state or quality of being efficient; accomplishing something with minimum waste of time or effort
✏️ 例句
New technology has greatly improved the efficiency of the factory.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
effici（做成）+ -ency → 把事情做成的能力 → 效率
efficiency::/ɪˈfɪʃənsi/:n. 效率:The new software greatly improved the efficiency of data processing.
13. gender /ˈdʒendər/ n. 性别；（语法）性

gender::/ˈdʒendər/:n. 性别:The company is committed to gender equality in the workplace.
14. dangerous /ˈdeɪndʒərəs/ adj. 危险的；有害的

dangerous::/ˈdeɪndʒərəs/:adj. 危险的:Driving at high speed in foggy weather is extremely dangerous.

16. emerge /ɪˈmɜːrdʒ/ v. 出现；浮现；显露

emerge::/ɪˈmɜːrdʒ/:v. 出现:New technologies are emerging in the field of artificial intelligence.
17. enhance /ɪnˈhæns/ v. 提高；增强；改善

enhance::/ɪnˈhæns/:v. 提高:The new training program aims to enhance employee productivity.

round::/raʊnd/:adj. 圆的:The two sides held a new round of negotiations last week.

connect::/kəˈnekt/:v. 连接:The internet connects people from all over the world.

handle::/ˈhændəl/:v. 处理:The customer service team handled over 300 complaints this month.
22. debt /det/ n. 债务；负债；恩情

debt::/det/:n. 债务:The company accumulated significant debt during the economic downturn.

diet::/ˈdaɪət/:n. 饮食:Doctors recommended a low-sodium diet to reduce blood pressure.

make a connection 建立联系；in connection with 与……有关；lose connection 断开连接

connection::/kəˈnekʃən/:n. 联系:Researchers found a clear connection between exercise and mental health.

color::/ˈkʌlər/:n. 颜色:The designer chose a warm color palette for the new brand identity.
