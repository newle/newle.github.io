1. door /dɔːr/ n. 门；出入口
📖 英文解释
a hinged, sliding, or revolving barrier at the entrance to a building, room, or vehicle
✏️ 例句
Please close the door quietly when you leave.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
door to door 挨家挨户；open the door to 为……打开大门；next door 隔壁
🧠 助记
形象记忆：门的形状 D 像半扇门 → door
door::/dɔːr/:n. 门:The new policy opened the door to greater economic cooperation.
2. hot /hɒt/ adj. 热的；辣的；激烈的
📖 英文解释
having a high degree of heat; feeling or producing heat; exciting or very popular
✏️ 例句
The soup is too hot to eat right now.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hot topic 热门话题；hot spot 热点地区；in hot water 陷入麻烦
🧠 助记
联想 heat（热）→ hot 是热的直接表达
hot::/hɒt/:adj. 热的:Climate change has made summers hotter across the globe.
3. personality /ˌpɜːrsəˈnæləti/ n. 人格；性格；名人
📖 英文解释
the combination of characteristics or qualities that form an individual's distinctive character; a celebrity
✏️ 例句
A positive personality helps in forming good relationships.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
strong personality 强烈个性；personality disorder 人格障碍；TV personality 电视名人
🧠 助记
person（人）+ ality → 一个人的特质 → 个性；人格
personality::/ˌpɜːrsəˈnæləti/:n. 人格:Her outgoing personality made her very popular in the workplace.
4. eventually /ɪˈventʃuəli/ adv. 最终；终于
📖 英文解释
in the end, especially after a long delay, dispute, or series of problems
✏️ 例句
They worked hard and eventually succeeded.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
eventually lead to 最终导致；sooner or later/eventually 迟早/最终
🧠 助记
event（事件）+ ually → 经过很多事件后 → 最终
eventually::/ɪˈventʃuəli/:adv. 最终:After years of research, they eventually found a solution.
5. manufacture /ˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/ v./n. 制造；捏造；生产
📖 英文解释
to make (something) on a large scale using machinery; to invent a story or excuse
✏️ 例句
The factory manufactures electronic components for export.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
manufacture goods 制造商品；manufacturing industry 制造业；manufactured evidence 捏造的证据
🧠 助记
manu（手）+ facture（制造）→ 手工制造 → 制造
manufacture::/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/:v./n. 制造:The company manufactures over 10,000 units per day.
6. ocean /ˈəʊʃən/ n. 海洋；大洋
📖 英文解释
a very large expanse of sea; one of the main areas into which the sea of the earth is divided
✏️ 例句
Marine pollution is threatening the world's oceans.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ocean floor 海底；ocean current 洋流；Pacific/Atlantic Ocean 太平洋/大西洋
🧠 助记
联想 ocean = o（圆）→ 地球上大圆形水体 → 海洋
ocean::/ˈəʊʃən/:n. 海洋:Scientists are studying the role of oceans in regulating global climate.
7. north /nɔːrθ/ n./adj./adv. 北；北方的；向北
📖 英文解释
the direction that is on your left when you are watching the sun rise; the northern part of a country
✏️ 例句
The company's headquarters are located in the north of the country.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
north of 在……以北；due north 正北；North Pole 北极
🧠 助记
联想 nor（也不，表示远离）→ 远离赤道的方向 → 北
north::/nɔːrθ/:n./adj./adv. 北:The cold front moved southward from the north of the country.
8. chemical /ˈkemɪkəl/ adj. 化学的；n. 化学物质
📖 英文解释
relating to chemistry or the interactions of substances; a compound or substance that has been purified or prepared
✏️ 例句
Some cleaning products contain harmful chemical substances.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
chemical reaction 化学反应；chemical industry 化工行业；chemical weapon 化学武器
🧠 助记
chemi（化学词根）+ cal → 化学的
chemical::/ˈkemɪkəl/:adj. 化学的:The factory was fined for illegally dumping chemical waste.
9. afford /əˈfɔːrd/ v. 承担得起；买得起；提供
📖 英文解释
to have enough money to pay for something; to be able to do something without difficulty or risk
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
can't afford to do 不能承担做……的后果；afford an opportunity 提供机会
🧠 助记
af（加强）+ ford（联想 forward 前进）→ 能够前行/负担 → 承担得起
afford::/əˈfɔːrd/:v. 承担得起:Many students cannot afford the high cost of university tuition.
10. sugar /ˈʃʊɡər/ n. 糖；v. 加糖；使甜
📖 英文解释
a sweet crystalline substance obtained from various plants; to make something more pleasant
✏️ 例句
The doctor advised him to reduce his sugar intake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
blood sugar 血糖；sugar-free 无糖的；sugar-coat 粉饰；sugar cane 甘蔗
🧠 助记
联想：甜甜的发音 sugar → 糖
sugar::/ˈʃʊɡər/:n. 糖:Excess sugar consumption has been linked to obesity and diabetes.
11. apple /ˈæpəl/ n. 苹果
📖 英文解释
the round fruit of the apple tree with firm, sweet-tasting flesh; the tree bearing such fruit
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
形象记忆：联想 apple 苹果的红圆形状
apple::/ˈæpəl/:n. 苹果:The orchard produces thousands of tonnes of apples each year.
12. noise /nɔɪz/ n. 噪音；声响；v. 传播
📖 英文解释
a sound, especially one that is loud or unpleasant; irregular fluctuations accompanying a signal
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make noise 发出声音；background noise 背景噪音；noise pollution 噪音污染
🧠 助记

noise::/nɔɪz/:n. 噪音:Noise pollution in urban areas has been linked to sleep disorders.
13. employment /ɪmˈplɔɪmənt/ n. 雇用；就业；工作
📖 英文解释

employment::/ɪmˈplɔɪmənt/:n. 雇用:Rising employment rates indicate a recovering economy.
14. advocate /ˈædvəkət/ v. 主张；倡导；n. 倡导者；律师
📖 英文解释
to publicly recommend or support a particular cause; a person who supports or recommends a particular cause
✏️ 例句

advocate for/of 倡导……；strong advocate 强力倡导者；devil's advocate 唱反调的人
🧠 助记

advocate::/ˈædvəkət/:v. 主张:She is a passionate advocate for environmental protection.
15. mass /mæs/ n. 大量；质量；adj. 大众的
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
联想 massive（巨大的）→ 大量的 → mass
mass::/mæs/:n. 大量:Social media enables the rapid spread of information to a mass audience.
16. contrast /ˈkɒntræst/ v./n. 对比；对照；形成对比
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in contrast to/with 与……形成对比；by contrast 相比之下；contrast between 两者之间的对比
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ trast（拉扯）→ 两者相互拉扯 → 对比
contrast::/ˈkɒntræst/:v./n. 对比:In contrast to last year, sales have increased significantly this quarter.
17. ideal /aɪˈdɪəl/ adj. 理想的；完美的；n. 理想

ideal::/aɪˈdɪəl/:adj. 理想的:The proposal represents an ideal solution to the housing crisis.
