1. page /peɪdʒ/ n. 页码；页；v. 翻页；呼叫
📖 英文解释
one side of a sheet of paper in a book, magazine, or newspaper; to send a message to someone
✏️ 例句
Please turn to page 42 for the next exercise.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
home page 主页；on the same page 意见一致；page through 翻阅
🧠 助记
联想 package（包裹）→ 书的每一层 → 页
page::/peɪdʒ/:n. 页码:The report spans over 100 pages of detailed analysis.
2. park /pɑːrk/ n. 公园；v. 停车
📖 英文解释
a large public garden or area of land used for recreation; to leave a vehicle in a particular place
✏️ 例句
Families often gather in the park on weekends.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
car park 停车场；national park 国家公园；park and ride 停车换乘
🧠 助记
联想 spark（火花）→ 公园里有活力的地方 → 公园
park::/pɑːrk/:n. 公园:The city invested heavily in expanding its network of public parks.
3. largely /ˈlɑːrdʒli/ adv. 大量地；主要地；在很大程度上
📖 英文解释
to a great extent; mostly or mainly
✏️ 例句
The success of the project was largely due to teamwork.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
largely due to 主要是因为；largely ignored 在很大程度上被忽视
🧠 助记
large（大的）+ ly（副词后缀）→ 在很大程度上 → 主要地
largely::/ˈlɑːrdʒli/:adv. 大量地:The policy has been largely successful in reducing carbon emissions.
4. cent /sent/ n. 分（货币单位）；百分之一
📖 英文解释
a monetary unit equal to one hundredth of a dollar or other decimal currency unit
✏️ 例句
The price was reduced by fifty cents.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
per cent 百分之；not a cent 一分钱也没有；cent sign ¢
🧠 助记
cent（百，拉丁词根）→ 一百分之一 → 分（货币）
cent::/sent/:n. 分（货币单位）:Inflation rose by three per cent over the past year.
5. analysis /əˈnæləsɪs/ n. 分析；分析结果
📖 英文解释
detailed examination of the elements or structure of something; a statement of such an examination
✏️ 例句
A thorough analysis of the data revealed unexpected patterns.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
analysis of 对……的分析；in the final analysis 归根结底；cost-benefit analysis 成本效益分析
🧠 助记
ana（向上）+ lysis（分解）→ 把东西拆分向上研究 → 分析
analysis::/əˈnæləsɪs/:n. 分析:The report presents a detailed analysis of consumer spending patterns.
6. outline /ˈaʊtlaɪn/ n. 轮廓；提纲；v. 概述
📖 英文解释
a line or set of lines enclosing or indicating the shape of an object; a description of the main features; to give a summary of something
✏️ 例句
She outlined the main points of her presentation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
outline a plan 概述计划；general outline 大纲；in outline 概括地
🧠 助记
out（外）+ line（线）→ 在外面画的线 → 轮廓；提纲
outline::/ˈaʊtlaɪn/:n. 轮廓:The manager outlined the key objectives for the new project.
7. citizen /ˈsɪtɪzən/ n. 公民；市民
📖 英文解释
a legally recognized subject or national of a state or commonwealth; an inhabitant of a particular town or city
✏️ 例句
Every citizen has the right to vote in a democracy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
senior citizen 老年公民；global citizen 世界公民；citizenship n. 公民身份
🧠 助记
citi（城市）+ zen → 住在城市的人 → 市民；公民
citizen::/ˈsɪtɪzən/:n. 公民:Citizens are encouraged to participate in local government decision-making.
8. code /kəʊd/ n. 代码；法典；v. 编码
📖 英文解释
a system of words, letters, or symbols used to represent others; a set of rules or laws; to write computer programs
✏️ 例句
She spent hours writing the code for the new application.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
source code 源代码；zip code 邮政编码；code of conduct 行为准则
🧠 助记
联想 decode（解码）→ code 是编码 → 代码
code::/kəʊd/:n. 代码:The team developed a new code to improve the security of the system.
9. strength /streŋθ/ n. 力量；强度；优势
📖 英文解释
the quality or state of being physically strong; the capacity of an object to withstand force; a good or beneficial quality
✏️ 例句
The strength of the material makes it ideal for construction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
strength of character 意志力；at full strength 全力以赴；strengthen v. 加强
🧠 助记
strong（强壮的）→ strength 是名词形式 → 力量
strength::/streŋθ/:n. 力量:The company's greatest strength lies in its innovative research team.
10. display /dɪˈspleɪ/ v./n. 陈列；显示；展示
📖 英文解释
to put something in a place where people can see it; a performance or show
✏️ 例句
The museum displays artifacts from ancient civilizations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
on display 展示中；display screen 显示屏；display of emotion 情感的表达
🧠 助记
dis（分散）+ play（玩/表演）→ 把东西展开来演示 → 展示
display::/dɪˈspleɪ/:v./n. 陈列:The exhibition displays over 200 artworks from the 20th century.
11. advanced /ədˈvɑːnst/ adj. 高级的；先进的；高等的
📖 英文解释
far on or ahead in development; complex or difficult; at a higher level
✏️ 例句
She enrolled in an advanced course in computer science.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
advanced technology 先进技术；advanced degree 高级学位；advanced level 高级水平
🧠 助记
advance（前进）+ d → 已经前进到高级阶段的 → 先进的
advanced::/ədˈvɑːnst/:adj. 高级的:The research team uses advanced imaging technology to study brain activity.
12. son /sʌn/ n. 儿子；孩子
📖 英文解释
a boy or man in relation to his parents
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
联想 sun（太阳）同音 → 儿子如太阳般耀眼 → 儿子
son::/sʌn/:n. 儿子:He took over the family business from his father, continuing his son's proud legacy.
13. sun /sʌn/ n. 太阳；日光；v. 晒太阳
📖 英文解释
the star around which the earth orbits; the light or warmth received from the sun
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sunshine 阳光；sunset/sunrise 日落/日出；under the sun 世界上
🧠 助记
形象记忆：sun → 发光的圆形天体 → 太阳
sun::/sʌn/:n. 太阳:Solar energy harnesses the power of the sun to generate electricity.
14. whom /huːm/ pron. 谁（宾格）；哪个人
📖 英文解释
used instead of 'who' as the object of a verb or preposition
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
to whom it may concern 致相关人士；with/for whom 与/为谁；whom did you meet? 你遇到了谁？
🧠 助记
who（谁）→ whom 是宾格形式，用在介词或动词后
whom::/huːm/:pron. 谁（宾格）:The award was presented to those on whom the community depended most.
15. budget /ˈbʌdʒɪt/ n./v. 预算；做预算
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

budget::/ˈbʌdʒɪt/:n./v. 预算:The project was completed on time and within budget.
16. smart /smɑːrt/ adj. 聪明的；漂亮的；v. 刺痛

smart::/smɑːrt/:adj. 聪明的:The company uses smart technology to optimize energy use in its buildings.
17. manner /ˈmænər/ n. 方式；态度；礼貌（常复数）

manner::/ˈmænər/:n. 方式:The new employee handled customer queries in a polite and efficient manner.
18. explore /ɪkˈsplɔːr/ v. 探索；探险；研究

explore::/ɪkˈsplɔːr/:v. 探索:The study explores the relationship between education and income inequality.
19. estimate /ˈestɪmɪt/ v./n. 估计；估算；判断
