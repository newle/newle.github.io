1. regulation /ˌreɡjuleɪʃn/ n. 规定；法规；管理；调节
📖 英文解释
An official rule or law that controls how something is done; the act of controlling or supervising something.
✏️ 例句
The new environmental regulation requires factories to reduce their carbon emissions by thirty percent.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
regulation+on/of（对……的规定）；under regulation（受规定约束）；government regulation（政府监管）—考研法律、经济类高频词。
🧠 助记
regulate（调节/规范）+ ion → 规范行为的制度 → 规定、法规
regulation::/ˌreɡjuleɪʃn/:n. 规定:government regulation of industry 政府对产业的监管
2. nurse /nɜːrs/ n. 护士 v. 护理；喂奶；培育
📖 英文解释
A person trained to care for sick people; to look after or tend to someone; to feed an infant.
✏️ 例句
The experienced nurse carefully monitored the patient's condition throughout the night.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nurse+名词（nurse a patient/wound/ambition）；nursing home（养老院）；考研中nurse有时作动词出现，如nurse a grievance（心怀怨恨）。
🧠 助记
联想：nurse = 护士，care护理
nurse::/nɜːrs/:n. 护士 v. 护理:nurse a patient back to health 护理病人康复
3. expose /ɪkspəʊz/ v. 暴露；揭露；使接触；曝光
📖 英文解释
To uncover or make visible; to reveal the true nature of; to subject to an experience or influence.
✏️ 例句
Investigative journalists worked to expose corruption within the local government.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
expose sb to sth（使接触某事）；be exposed to（暴露于）；exposure（n.）—考研新闻、社会类文章高频词。
🧠 助记
ex-（向外）+ pose（放置）→ 放到外面 → 暴露、揭露
expose::/ɪkspəʊz/:v. 暴露:expose children to diverse cultures 让孩子接触多元文化
4. being /biːɪŋ/ n. 生物；生命；存在 v. be的现在分词
📖 英文解释
The state or fact of existing; a living creature; used as a present participle of be.
✏️ 例句
Human beings have a unique ability to reflect on their own existence and make moral choices.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
human being（人类）—考研高频搭配；being as a noun（存在/生物）；for the time being（暂时）—习语。
🧠 助记
be（存在）+ ing → 正在存在的状态 → 生命体、存在
being::/biːɪŋ/:n. 生物:human beings and the environment 人类与环境
5. truth /truːθ/ n. 真相；事实；真理
📖 英文解释
The quality or state of being true; that which is true or in accordance with fact or reality.
✏️ 例句
The documentary revealed the uncomfortable truth about the environmental impact of fast fashion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tell the truth（说实话）；truth about（关于……的真相）；in truth（事实上）；half-truth（半真半假）—考研常见于论证类文章。
🧠 助记
true（真实）→ truth（真相），直接词根变形
truth::/truːθ/:n. 真相:reveal the truth about 揭示……的真相
6. distance /dɪstəns/ n. 距离；远处 v. 与……保持距离
📖 英文解释
The amount of space between two places or things; to make or become distant.
✏️ 例句
Modern technology has made it possible to maintain friendships across vast distances.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
at a distance（在远处）；keep one's distance（保持距离）；in the distance（在远处）；distance oneself from（与……划清界限）—考研常考。
🧠 助记
dis-（分开）+ stance（站立）→ 分开站着 → 距离
distance::/dɪstəns/:n. 距离:keep a safe distance 保持安全距离
7. count /kaʊnt/ v. 计数；算作；重要 n. 计数；总数；伯爵
📖 英文解释
To add up; to be considered as; to matter or be significant.
✏️ 例句
Every vote counts in a democratic election, so citizens are urged to participate actively.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
count on（依靠）；count as（算作）；count for（有价值）；lose count of（数不清）；at last count（最新统计）—考研多义词重点。
🧠 助记
count = 数数 → 数数很重要 → 重要；也是伯爵（Count Dracula）
count::/kaʊnt/:v. 计数:every vote counts 每一票都重要
8. hit /hɪt/ v. 打；碰撞；到达；影响 n. 打击；热门
📖 英文解释
To strike or cause to come into contact with; to reach a particular level; a great success.
✏️ 例句
The economic recession hit the manufacturing sector particularly hard, causing widespread job losses.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hit+名词（hit a target/record/snag）；hard hit（受到重创）；hit the headlines（登上头条）—考研常见于社会经济类文章。
🧠 助记
联想：hit（打）→ 被打到 → 受到冲击；big hit = 大热门
hit::/hɪt/:v. 打:hit the headlines 登上头条
9. ticket /tɪkɪt/ n. 票；罚款单；标签
📖 英文解释
A piece of paper or card that gives the holder a right to enter a place or travel; a fine for a traffic violation.
✏️ 例句
Tickets for the championship game sold out within minutes of going on sale.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ticket for/to（……的票）；one-way/round-trip ticket（单程/往返票）；a speeding ticket（超速罚款单）；考研交通、出行类文章常见。
🧠 助记
联想：tick（标记）+ et → 有标记的小纸 → 票
ticket::/tɪkɪt/:n. 票:buy a ticket for the concert 购买音乐会门票
10. combine /kəmbaɪn/ v. 结合；合并；兼顾
📖 英文解释
To join or merge two or more things together; to do two or more things at the same time.
✏️ 例句
The new treatment combines traditional medicine with modern technology for better results.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
combine A with B（将A与B结合）；combine to do（共同做）；combination（n.）；考研科技类文章常见：combine theory with practice（理论与实践结合）。
🧠 助记
com-（共同）+ bine（绑）→ 绑在一起 → 结合
combine::/kəmbaɪn/:v. 结合:combine theory with practice 理论与实践结合
11. ban /bæn/ v. 禁止 n. 禁令
📖 英文解释
To officially refuse to allow something; an official prohibition.
✏️ 例句
The government imposed a ban on single-use plastics to reduce environmental pollution.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ban on（对……的禁令）；ban sb from doing（禁止某人做某事）；lift a ban（解除禁令）—考研政策类文章高频词组。
🧠 助记
联想：ban = 板（不让通过）→ 禁止
ban::/bæn/:v. 禁止 n. 禁令:impose a ban on smoking 禁止吸烟
12. bit /bɪt/ n. 一点；小片；钻头 adv. 有点
📖 英文解释
A small amount or piece of something; slightly or rather.
✏️ 例句
Learning a new language can be a bit challenging at first, but it becomes easier with practice.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a bit（有点，修饰形容词/副词）；bit by bit（逐渐地）；not a bit（一点也不）；every bit as（完全一样）—考研阅读中常见程度副词搭配。
🧠 助记
bit = bite的过去式（咬）→ 咬一小口 → 一小点
bit::/bɪt/:n. 一点:a bit of a challenge 有点挑战
13. impossible /ɪmˈpɒsɪbl/ adj. 不可能的；做不到的；难以忍受的
📖 英文解释
Not able to occur, exist, or be done; very difficult to deal with.
✏️ 例句
It is virtually impossible to eliminate all forms of bias from any scientific research process.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
it is impossible to do（做某事是不可能的）；impossible+for sb to do；make it impossible（使……不可能）—考研阅读推断题常见。
🧠 助记
im-（否定）+ possible（可能的）→ 不可能的
impossible::/ɪmˈpɒsɪbl/:adj. 不可能的:virtually impossible to achieve 几乎不可能实现
14. nuclear /njuːkliər/ adj. 原子核的；核能的；核武器的
📖 英文解释
Relating to the nucleus of an atom; relating to the energy released in nuclear reactions.
✏️ 例句
Many countries are reconsidering nuclear energy as a low-carbon alternative to fossil fuels.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nuclear energy/power（核能）；nuclear weapon（核武器）；nuclear family（核心家庭，仅父母子女）—考研能源和家庭结构文章均见。
🧠 助记
nucle-（核）+ ar → 与核有关的 → 核能的
nuclear::/njuːkliər/:adj. 原子核的:nuclear energy as a clean source 核能作为清洁能源
15. basis /beɪsɪs/ n. 基础；依据；方式
📖 英文解释
The foundation or starting point for something; the reason or justification for something.
✏️ 例句
The new policy was developed on the basis of extensive research and public consultation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
on the basis of（基于……）—考研高频介词短语！on a daily/regular basis（每天/定期地）；basis for（……的基础）。
🧠 助记
base（基础）+ is → 基础就是basis
basis::/beɪsɪs/:n. 基础:on the basis of evidence 基于证据
16. boost /buːst/ v. 促进；提高；增强 n. 推动；增加
📖 英文解释
To help something increase or improve; an increase or improvement.
✏️ 例句
The government's economic stimulus package was designed to boost consumer spending and create jobs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
boost+名词（boost confidence/economy/productivity）；give a boost to（促进……）；考研经济类文章中boost使用频率极高。
🧠 助记
boost像push向上推 → 促进、提升
boost::/buːst/:v. 促进:boost economic growth 促进经济增长
17. rain /reɪn/ n. 雨；降雨 v. 下雨；大量降落
📖 英文解释
Water that falls from clouds; to fall as rain; to fall or send down in large quantities.
✏️ 例句
Acid rain caused by industrial pollution has severely damaged forests in many parts of Europe.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
acid rain（酸雨）；rain forest（雨林）；it never rains but it pours（祸不单行）—习语；考研环境类文章常见acid rain。
🧠 助记
联想：rain（雨）→ 下雨 → 雨水统治大地
rain::/reɪn/:n. 雨:acid rain and environmental damage 酸雨与环境破坏
18. reserve /rɪzɜːrv/ v. 预订；保留；保存 n. 储备；保护区；内敛
📖 英文解释
To keep for a future purpose; to arrange for something to be kept for a particular person; a supply kept for future use.
✏️ 例句
The government has decided to reserve a portion of the national budget for environmental protection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reserve+名词（reserve the right/judgment/seat）；in reserve（储备着）；nature reserve（自然保护区）；reserved（adj. 内敛的）。
🧠 助记
re-（再次）+ serve（保管/供给）→ 再次存起来以备用 → 预留、储备
reserve::/rɪzɜːrv/:v. 预订:nature reserve 自然保护区
19. dog /dɒɡ/ n. 狗 v. 跟踪；困扰
📖 英文解释
A common domesticated carnivorous mammal; to follow someone closely; to cause continual trouble for someone.
✏️ 例句
The project was dogged by technical problems and funding shortages from the very beginning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dog sb（困扰某人，作动词）—考研高频！dog-eat-dog（弱肉强食）；underdog（弱势一方）；考研阅读dog作动词出现时需注意语义。
🧠 助记
dog（狗）像追踪猎物一样 → 狗跟着你 → 跟踪、困扰
dog::/dɒɡ/:n. 狗 v. 跟踪:dogged by controversy 被争议困扰
20. productivity /ˌprɒdʌkˈtɪvɪti/ n. 生产力；生产率；效率
📖 英文解释
The efficiency of production; the state or quality of producing results, goods, or crops.
✏️ 例句
Investing in employee training can significantly increase a company's overall productivity and profitability.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
increase/boost productivity（提高生产力）；labor productivity（劳动生产率）；productivity gap（生产力差距）—考研经济管理类核心词。
🧠 助记
productive（有效率的）+ ity → 有效率的程度 → 生产力
productivity::/ˌprɒdʌkˈtɪvɪti/:n. 生产力:boost labor productivity 提高劳动生产率
21. discipline /dɪsɪplɪn/ n. 纪律；学科；训练 v. 训练；惩戒

The practice of training people to obey rules; a branch of knowledge; to train someone in discipline.
✏️ 例句
Self-discipline is widely regarded as one of the most important qualities for academic and professional success.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
self-discipline（自律）；academic discipline（学科领域）；discipline+sb for（因……惩戒某人）—考研教育类文章高频词。
🧠 助记
disciple（门徒）+ ine → 门徒要遵守纪律 → 纪律、训练
discipline::/dɪsɪplɪn/:n. 纪律:self-discipline and academic success 自律与学业成功

The new concert hall can seat up to two thousand people and features state-of-the-art acoustics.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

seat::/siːt/:n. 座位:win a seat in parliament 赢得议会席位
23. revolution /ˌrevəluːʃn/ n. 革命；旋转；重大变革
📖 英文解释
A forcible overthrow of a government or social order; a dramatic and wide-reaching change; circular motion.
✏️ 例句
The Industrial Revolution transformed manufacturing processes and reshaped the global economy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
Industrial Revolution（工业革命）；scientific/digital revolution（科学/数字革命）；revolutionary（adj. 革命性的）—考研历史、科技类文章核心词。
🧠 助记
revolve（旋转）+ ution → 转了一圈 → 旋转；社会也翻转了 → 革命
revolution::/ˌrevəluːʃn/:n. 革命:the Industrial Revolution 工业革命
24. prize /praɪz/ n. 奖；奖品 v. 珍视 adj. 获奖的

win/award a prize（赢得/颁发奖项）；Nobel Prize（诺贝尔奖）；prize sth（珍视某物，动词用法）—考研常见于文化教育类文章。
🧠 助记
prize和price（价格）相近 → 有价格才有价值 → 奖品、珍视
prize::/praɪz/:n. 奖:prize intellectual freedom 珍视学术自由
