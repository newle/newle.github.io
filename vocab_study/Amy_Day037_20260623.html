1. previous /priːviəs/ adj. 先前的；以前的；过早的
📖 英文解释
Coming before in time or order; existing or occurring before.
✏️ 例句
The new director's approach was quite different from that of his previous counterpart.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
previous+名词（previous experience/year/research）；previously（adv. 以前）；prior to = previous to（在……之前）—考研阅读指代常见。
🧠 助记
pre-（之前）+ vious → 在之前的 → 先前的
previous::/priːviəs/:adj. 先前的:previous research findings 以前的研究发现
2. attractive /əˈtræktɪv/ adj. 吸引人的；有魅力的；诱人的
📖 英文解释
Pleasing or appealing to the senses; having beneficial or desirable qualities.
✏️ 例句
The city's affordable housing and vibrant culture make it an attractive destination for young professionals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
attractive to（对……有吸引力）；attractive+名词（attractive offer/option）；attract（v.）；attraction（n.）；考研常见于城市规划、经济政策文章。
🧠 助记
attract（吸引）+ ive → 有吸引力的 → 吸引人的
attractive::/əˈtræktɪv/:adj. 吸引人的:an attractive investment opportunity 有吸引力的投资机会
3. communicate /kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ v. 传达；交流；沟通；传播
📖 英文解释
To share or exchange information, news, or ideas; to convey or express clearly.
✏️ 例句
The ability to communicate effectively in writing is one of the most valued skills in the workplace.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
communicate with（与……交流）；communicate ideas/feelings（传达想法/情感）；communication（n.）—考研写作常用。
🧠 助记
com-（共同）+ municate（分享）→ 共同分享信息 → 交流
communicate::/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/:v. 传达:communicate ideas clearly 清晰地传达想法
4. disaster /dɪˈzɑːstər/ n. 灾难；灾祸；彻底的失败
📖 英文解释
A sudden accident or event causing great damage or loss of life; a complete failure.
✏️ 例句
The earthquake was the worst natural disaster to hit the region in over a hundred years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
natural disaster（自然灾害）；disaster relief（灾难救援）；a disaster for（对……是灾难）；disastrous（adj. 灾难性的）—考研环境、社会类文章常见。
🧠 助记
dis-（不好）+ aster（星星）→ 星星不在轨道 → 灾难
disaster::/dɪˈzɑːstər/:n. 灾难:respond to a natural disaster 应对自然灾害
5. stock /stɒk/ n. 股票；库存；股份 v. 储备；进货 adj. 老套的
📖 英文解释
Shares in a company; a supply of goods kept for future use; standard and unoriginal.
✏️ 例句
Investors reacted nervously when the stock market fell sharply following the unexpected announcement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stock market（股票市场）；out of stock（缺货）；in stock（有货）；livestock（牲畜）；stock response（老套回应）—考研多义词重点。
🧠 助记
联想：stock = 仓库里的一堆 → 库存；股票也是储存价值
stock::/stɒk/:n. 股票:stock market volatility 股市波动
6. quick /kwɪk/ adj. 迅速的；快的；敏捷的 adv. 迅速地
📖 英文解释
Moving fast or doing something in a short time; happening in a short time; intelligent and alert.
✏️ 例句
The quick response of emergency services prevented the fire from spreading to nearby buildings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
quick+名词（quick decision/fix/response）；be quick to do（迅速做）；quickly（adv.）；quick-witted（机智的）；考研阅读中quick思维类比较常见。
🧠 助记
quick = 快速，联想闹钟快速响起 → 迅速
quick::/kwɪk/:adj. 迅速的:a quick response to the crisis 对危机的迅速响应
7. fly /flaɪ/ v. 飞；乘飞机；飞逝 n. 飞行；苍蝇
📖 英文解释
To travel through the air; to move or pass quickly; a type of insect with wings.
✏️ 例句
Time seems to fly by when you are engaged in work that you find genuinely meaningful.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fly by（飞逝）；fly in the face of（公然违抗）—考研高频习语！on the fly（仓促地）；flying colors（大获全胜）。
🧠 助记
fly = 飞 → 时间飞逝；苍蝇也会fly
fly::/flaɪ/:v. 飞:fly in the face of convention 公然挑战惯例
8. heat /hiːt/ n. 热；热度；激烈 v. 加热；使激动
📖 英文解释
The quality of being hot; high temperature; great intensity of feeling; to make warm or hot.
✏️ 例句
Global warming is causing more frequent and intense heat waves across the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
heat wave（热浪）；in the heat of（在……激烈时）；heat up（升温/激化）；dead heat（平局）；考研气候、社会冲突类文章常见。
🧠 助记
heat = 热 → 加热 → 情绪也heating up
heat::/hiːt/:n. 热:heat wave and public health 热浪与公共健康
9. candidate /kændɪdeɪt/ n. 候选人；申请人；参试者
📖 英文解释
A person who applies for a job or is nominated for election; a person taking an examination.
✏️ 例句
The candidate's strong communication skills and relevant experience made her stand out from the competition.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
candidate for（……的候选人）；presidential candidate（总统候选人）；job candidate（求职者）—考研政治、就业类文章高频词。
🧠 助记
candid（诚实的）+ ate → 诚实地被提名的人 → 候选人
candidate::/kændɪdeɪt/:n. 候选人:presidential candidate debate 总统候选人辩论
10. identity /aɪˈdentɪti/ n. 身份；特性；一致性
📖 英文解释
Who or what a person or thing is; the characteristics determining who or what a person or thing is.
✏️ 例句
Social media has profoundly changed the way people construct and present their personal identity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
national/cultural identity（民族/文化认同）；identity crisis（身份认同危机）；identify（v. 识别）；identification（n. 身份证明）—考研社会、文化类高频词。
🧠 助记
ident-（相同的）+ ity → 是同一个人的特质 → 身份、认同
identity::/aɪˈdentɪti/:n. 身份:cultural identity and globalization 文化认同与全球化
11. repair /rɪˈpeər/ v./n. 修理；修缮；补救；弥补
📖 英文解释
To fix something that is broken or damaged; to restore something to a good condition.
✏️ 例句
It is more sustainable to repair damaged goods than to discard them and purchase new replacements.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
repair+名词（repair a car/relationship/damage）；beyond repair（无法修复）；repair to（前往某地—旧用法）—考研阅读偶见。
🧠 助记
re-（再次）+ pair（配对/修好）→ 再次配好 → 修理
repair::/rɪˈpeər/:v./n. 修理:repair damaged infrastructure 修复受损基础设施
12. tear /tɪər/ or /teər/ n. 眼泪 v. 撕；扯；猛冲
📖 英文解释
A drop of clear salty liquid from the eye; to pull something apart or to pieces.
✏️ 例句
The news of the tragedy brought tears to the eyes of everyone who heard it.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tear apart（撕裂/使痛苦）；tear down（拆除）—考研高频！in tears（流泪）；tear into（猛烈攻击）；注意两种发音区别。
🧠 助记
两个发音：泪水like滴落；撕开用力拉
tear::/tɪər/ or /teər/:n. 眼泪 v. 撕:tear down old buildings 拆除旧建筑
13. web /web/ n. 网；蜘蛛网；万维网；错综复杂的关系
📖 英文解释
A network of fine threads made by spiders; the World Wide Web; a complex system.
✏️ 例句
The world wide web has made information more accessible but also created new challenges around privacy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
web of lies（谎言织成的网）；website（网站）；web-based（基于网络的）；考研科技类文章中web多指互联网相关内容。
🧠 助记
web = 网 → 蜘蛛网 → 互联网都是网状结构
web::/web/:n. 网:a web of complex relationships 错综复杂的关系网
14. press /pres/ n. 报刊；新闻界；压力 v. 按；压；催促；熨烫
📖 英文解释
Newspapers and news broadcasting; to push firmly; to persuade or urge forcefully.
✏️ 例句
Freedom of the press is considered a cornerstone of democratic governance and an open society.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
freedom of the press（新闻自由）—考研高频！press conference（新闻发布会）；press for（力争）；press sb to do（施压某人）。
🧠 助记
press = 按压 → 新闻界给社会施压；印刷机也要press（压印）
press::/pres/:n. 报刊:freedom of the press 新闻自由
15. original /əˈrɪdʒɪnl/ adj. 最初的；原创的；独特的 n. 原件；原著
📖 英文解释
Present from the beginning; not copied; new and creative; the first form of something.
✏️ 例句
The museum's collection includes several original manuscripts by famous eighteenth-century philosophers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
original+名词（original idea/purpose/work）；originally（adv. 最初）—写作时间线铺垫常用；the original（原件）。
🧠 助记
origin（起源）+ al → 最初的 → 原创的
original::/əˈrɪdʒɪnl/:adj. 最初的:the original purpose of the policy 政策的最初目的
16. conference /kɒnfərəns/ n. 会议；讨论会；联合会
📖 英文解释
A formal meeting of people with a shared interest, typically in a particular profession.
✏️ 例句
The annual international climate conference brings together world leaders to discuss environmental policy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
conference on（关于……的会议）；hold/attend a conference（举行/参加会议）；press conference（新闻发布会）；conference call（电话会议）。
🧠 助记
con-（共同）+ fer（带来）+ ence → 共同带来想法的场合 → 会议
conference::/kɒnfərəns/:n. 会议:international climate conference 国际气候会议
17. spot /spɒt/ v. 发现；认出 n. 地点；污点；困境 adj. 当即的
📖 英文解释
To see or notice; a particular place; a small mark or stain; a difficult situation.
✏️ 例句
Researchers were able to spot a significant pattern in the data that had previously been overlooked.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
spot+名词（spot an error/opportunity）；on the spot（当场）；a blind spot（盲点）；in a tough spot（处于困境）—考研多义词重点。
🧠 助记
spot = 斑点 → 看见斑点 → 发现；也是一个地点
spot::/spɒt/:v. 发现:spot potential problems early 早期发现潜在问题
18. signal /sɪɡnəl/ n. 信号；迹象 v. 发信号；表明 adj. 显著的
📖 英文解释
A gesture, action, or sound conveying information; an indication of something; to make a signal.
✏️ 例句
Rising unemployment is often seen as a warning signal of deeper economic problems ahead.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
signal+名词/that（表明……）；give/send a signal（发出信号）；traffic signal（交通信号）；signal adj.（显著的）—考研偶见形容词用法。
🧠 助记
sign（符号/迹象）+ al → 有迹象的符号 → 信号
signal::/sɪɡnəl/:n. 信号:a signal of economic recovery 经济复苏的信号
19. generate /dʒenəreɪt/ v. 产生；引起；生成；发电
📖 英文解释
To produce or create; to cause a situation, feeling, or result to exist.
✏️ 例句
Renewable energy sources like solar and wind power can generate electricity without producing harmful emissions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
generate+名词（generate energy/income/controversy/jobs）—考研极高频动词！generation（n.）；generator（n. 发电机）。
🧠 助记
gen-（产生）+ erate → 产生出来 → 产生、生成
generate::/dʒenəreɪt/:v. 产生:generate renewable energy 产生可再生能源
20. expectation /ˌekspekˈteɪʃn/ n. 期望；预期；期待
📖 英文解释
A strong belief that something will happen or be the case; a belief that someone will or should achieve something.
✏️ 例句
The new government raised public expectations but struggled to meet them within its first year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
meet/exceed/fall short of expectations（达到/超越/未达到期望）—考研写作常用句式！expectation of（对……的期望）；beyond expectation（出乎意料）。
🧠 助记
expect（期望）+ ation → 期望的状态 → 期待、预期
expectation::/ˌekspekˈteɪʃn/:n. 期望:exceed public expectations 超出公众期望
21. recession /rɪˈseʃn/ n. 经济衰退；后退；凹处
📖 英文解释
A period of temporary economic decline; the action of receding.
✏️ 例句
The global recession of 2008 triggered widespread unemployment and financial hardship around the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
economic recession（经济衰退）；in recession（处于衰退期）；recession-hit（受衰退冲击的）—考研经济类文章核心词。
🧠 助记
re-（向后）+ cession（让出）→ 经济向后退 → 衰退
recession::/rɪˈseʃn/:n. 经济衰退:the economic recession of 2008 2008年经济衰退
22. clothe /kləʊð/ v. 给穿衣；提供衣物给
📖 英文解释
To provide clothes for someone; to dress someone.
✏️ 例句
The charity organization was established to feed and clothe homeless families during the winter months.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
clothe sb（给某人穿衣）；be clothed in（穿着……）；clothing（n. 衣物总称）；clothes（n. 衣物复数）—考研注意词形变化。
🧠 助记
cloth（布）+ e → 用布做衣服穿上 → 给穿衣
clothe::/kləʊð/:v. 给穿衣:feed and clothe the poor 给穷人提供食物和衣物
23. facility /fəˈsɪlɪti/ n. 灵巧；天资；设施；便利条件
📖 英文解释
A natural ability to do something easily; a building, service, or piece of equipment provided for a purpose.
✏️ 例句
The university has invested millions in upgrading its research facilities and laboratory equipment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
facility for（在某方面的天赋）；facilities（pl. 设施）—注意单复数语义差别；公共设施：public facilities；考研两个含义均考。
🧠 助记
facil-（容易）+ ity → 容易做到的能力 → 天资；引申到为人提供的设备 → 设施
facility::/fəˈsɪlɪti/:n. 灵巧:upgrade research facilities 升级科研设施
24. directly /dɪˈrektli/ adv. 直接地；径直地；立即；正好
📖 英文解释
Without changing direction or stopping; without anybody or anything in between; immediately.
✏️ 例句
The report argues that poverty is directly linked to limited access to quality education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
directly+动词（directly affect/cause/relate to）；directly opposite（正对面）；考研阅读中directly常修饰因果关系动词。
🧠 助记
direct（直接）+ ly → 直接地 → 直接地
directly::/dɪˈrektli/:adv. 直接地:directly linked to economic inequality 与经济不平等直接相关
25. ourselves /aʊərˈselvz/ pron. 我们自己；我们亲自（反身代词）
📖 英文解释
Used as the object of a verb or preposition when the subject is we; used to emphasize the subject we.
✏️ 例句
We must ask ourselves whether our current lifestyle is truly sustainable for future generations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
by ourselves（靠我们自己）；for ourselves（为了我们自己）；consider ourselves（认为我们自己）；考研写作中ourselves有时出现在哲学性句子中。
🧠 助记
our（我们的）+ selves（自我）→ 我们自己
ourselves::/aʊərˈselvz/:pron. 我们自己:we must rely on ourselves 我们必须依靠自己
