1. bus /bʌs/ n. 公共汽车；总线 v. 乘公共汽车；用巴士运送
📖 英文解释
A large motor vehicle for passengers; a distinct set of conductors in a computer; to transport by bus.
✏️ 例句
Investing in efficient bus networks can reduce car dependency and lower urban carbon emissions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
miss the bus（错过公共汽车；错失良机）；school bus（校车）；bus route（公交路线）；data bus（数据总线）—考研科技文章偶见计算机总线义。
🧠 助记
bus = 公共汽车，直接联想；也是数据传输的总线
bus::/bʌs/:n. 公共汽车:public bus network efficiency 公共汽车网络效率
2. refuse /rɪˈfjuːz/ v. 拒绝；不接受 n. 垃圾；废物
📖 英文解释
To indicate that one is not willing to do something; garbage or waste matter.
✏️ 例句
The government refused to sign the international agreement, citing concerns about economic competitiveness.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
refuse to do（拒绝做某事）；refuse+名词（refuse permission/offer）；refuse n.（垃圾）—注意名词/动词重音区别；考研常见于立场、政策类文章。
🧠 助记
re-（再次）+ fuse（熔断）→ 熔断了不再接受 → 拒绝
refuse::/rɪˈfjuːz/:v. 拒绝:refuse to cooperate with authorities 拒绝与当局合作
3. burn /bɜːrn/ v. 燃烧；灼伤；消耗 n. 烧伤；灼痛
📖 英文解释
To be on fire; to destroy or injure by fire; to use a fuel for energy.
✏️ 例句
Burning fossil fuels releases greenhouse gases that contribute to climate change and global warming.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
burn+名词（burn fuel/calories/bridges）；burn out（精疲力竭）；burn down（烧毁）；bridge-burning（断绝关系）—考研能源、心理学文章常见。
🧠 助记
burn = 燃烧 → 消耗能量 → 也引申为情感上的燃烧
burn::/bɜːrn/:v. 燃烧:burn fossil fuels for energy 燃烧化石燃料获取能量
4. decrease /dɪˈkriːs/ v./n. 减少；降低；下降
📖 英文解释
To become smaller or fewer; a reduction in the size or amount of something.
✏️ 例句
The introduction of stricter regulations led to a significant decrease in industrial pollution levels.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
decrease in（在……方面减少）；decrease by（减少了……）；on the decrease（在减少中）；区分decrease（数量减少）vs. decline（数量/质量均可下降）。
🧠 助记
de-（向下）+ crease（增加）→ 向下增加 → 减少
decrease::/dɪˈkriːs/:v./n. 减少:a decrease in carbon emissions 碳排放的减少
5. fat /fæt/ adj. 肥胖的；丰厚的 n. 脂肪；肥肉
📖 英文解释
Having too much flesh; containing much fat; a natural oily substance found in animal bodies.
✏️ 例句
A diet high in saturated fat has been linked to an increased risk of cardiovascular disease.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
saturated fat（饱和脂肪）；body fat（体脂）；fat chance（几乎不可能—反语）；fat cat（大款）—考研健康、讽刺类文章出现。
🧠 助记
fat = 胖 → 吃了脂肪会变胖 → 脂肪、肥胖
fat::/fæt/:adj. 肥胖的:reduce saturated fat intake 减少饱和脂肪摄入
6. table /teɪbl/ n. 桌子；表格；餐桌 v. 提交（议案）；搁置
📖 英文解释
A piece of furniture; an arrangement of information in rows and columns; to propose or postpone.
✏️ 例句
The committee agreed to table the discussion on budget cuts until more data became available.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
table a motion（英式：提出议案；美式：搁置）—考研注意英美语义差异！turn the tables（扭转局势）；on the table（可供讨论）。
🧠 助记
table = 桌子 → 把文件放桌上讨论 → 提出/搁置议案
table::/teɪbl/:n. 桌子:table a proposal for discussion 提出提案讨论
7. sample /sɑːmpl/ n. 样本；样品；例子 v. 取样；品尝；尝试
📖 英文解释
A small part or quantity intended to show what the whole is like; to take a sample of.
✏️ 例句
The survey was conducted on a random sample of five hundred university students across the country.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
random sample（随机样本）；sample size（样本量）；representative sample（代表性样本）；sample+名词（sample food/opinion）—考研统计研究类文章必备词。
🧠 助记
sam（相同的）+ ple → 代表整体的一小部分 → 样本
sample::/sɑːmpl/:n. 样本:a representative sample of the population 具代表性的人口样本
8. gold /ɡəʊld/ n. 黄金；金色 adj. 金的；金色的
📖 英文解释
A precious yellow metallic element; made of gold; of the color of gold.
✏️ 例句
Gold has traditionally been viewed as a safe-haven asset during periods of economic uncertainty.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
gold standard（黄金标准）；good as gold（非常乖/可靠）；gold rush（淘金热）；heart of gold（好心肠）—考研经济、文化类文章常见。
🧠 助记
gold = 黄金 → 闪闪发光的贵金属
gold::/ɡəʊld/:n. 黄金:gold as a safe-haven asset 黄金作为避险资产
9. obtain /əbˈteɪn/ v. 获得；得到；(规则等)适用；存在
📖 英文解释
To come into possession of; to be prevalent or accepted (of rules or situations).
✏️ 例句
Students must obtain a minimum grade of sixty percent in all subjects to progress to the next level.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
obtain+名词（obtain information/permission/results）—考研常见于学术类文章；conditions that obtain（存在的条件）—正式书面语义，较少见。
🧠 助记
ob-（朝向）+ tain（持有）→ 朝向目标持有 → 获得
obtain::/əbˈteɪn/:v. 获得:obtain permission from authorities 从当局获得许可
10. ahead /əˈhed/ adv. 在前面；提前；超前 adj. 领先的
📖 英文解释
In the direction one is facing; in the lead; in advance.
✏️ 例句
Planning ahead is essential for any organization that wants to remain competitive in a rapidly changing market.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ahead of（在……前面/提前）；go ahead（继续；请便）；think ahead（提前考虑）；ahead of time/schedule（提前）—考研写作常用。
🧠 助记
a-（方向）+ head（头）→ 头在前 → 在前面、领先
ahead::/əˈhed/:adv. 在前面:plan ahead for future challenges 提前规划应对未来挑战
11. domestic /dəˈmestɪk/ adj. 国内的；家庭的；驯养的 n. 家庭纠纷；家政工
📖 英文解释
Of or relating to the home or family; of or inside a particular country; tame, not wild.
✏️ 例句
Domestic violence is a serious social problem that affects millions of families across all income levels.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
domestic+名词（domestic policy/market/violence）；domestic vs. international/foreign—考研对比常见；domestic animal（家养动物）。
🧠 助记
dome-（家）+ stic → 家庭相关的 → 国内的、家庭的
domestic::/dəˈmestɪk/:adj. 国内的:domestic economic policy 国内经济政策
12. airport /eərpɔːrt/ n. 机场；航空港
📖 英文解释
A complex of runways and buildings for the take-off, landing, and maintenance of civil aircraft.
✏️ 例句
The construction of a new international airport is expected to boost tourism and trade in the region.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
airport+名词（airport security/terminal/staff）；international airport（国际机场）；考研交通、经济类文章基础词汇。
🧠 助记
air（空气/航空）+ port（港口）→ 飞机的港口 → 机场
airport::/eərpɔːrt/:n. 机场:international airport construction 国际机场建设
13. obesity /əʊˈbiːsɪti/ n. 肥胖症；过度肥胖
📖 英文解释
The condition of being grossly fat or overweight, posing health risks.
✏️ 例句
Childhood obesity has become a major public health concern due to the rise in processed food consumption.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
obesity epidemic（肥胖流行病）；childhood obesity（儿童肥胖）；combat obesity（对抗肥胖）；obese（adj. 肥胖的）—考研健康政策类必备词。
🧠 助记
obese（肥胖的）+ ity → 肥胖的状态 → 肥胖症
obesity::/əʊˈbiːsɪti/:n. 肥胖症:tackle the obesity epidemic 解决肥胖流行问题
14. guest /ɡest/ n. 客人；嘉宾；旅客 adj. 客座的
📖 英文解释
A person who is invited to visit someone's home or attend an event; a person staying at a hotel.
✏️ 例句
The conference featured several distinguished guest speakers from leading research institutions worldwide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
guest+名词（guest speaker/lecturer/room）；be my guest（随便，请便）；uninvited guest（不速之客）；guest list（嘉宾名单）。
🧠 助记
guest联想gusto（热情）→ 热情招待的人 → 客人
guest::/ɡest/:n. 客人:guest speaker at the conference 会议嘉宾演讲者
15. threaten /θretn/ v. 威胁；恐吓；预示不妙
📖 英文解释
To express an intention to harm someone; to put at risk; to seem likely to produce an unpleasant result.
✏️ 例句
Rising sea levels threaten to submerge low-lying coastal communities within the coming decades.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
threaten+名词/to do（威胁……）；threaten sb with（用……威胁某人）；threatening（adj. 威胁性的）；threat（n.）—考研环境、安全类文章高频词。
🧠 助记
threat（威胁）+ en → 构成威胁 → 威胁、恐吓
threaten::/θretn/:v. 威胁:threaten biodiversity and ecosystems 威胁生物多样性和生态系统

fair+名词（fair treatment/trade/game）；fair enough（算了，还行）；fair share（公平份额）；job fair（招聘会）；World's Fair（世博会）—考研多义词重点。
🧠 助记
fair = 公平 → 也是金发（fair hair）；集市上公平交易
fair::/feər/:adj. 公平的:ensure fair access to education 确保公平获得教育

✏️ 例句
The decision to marry should be based on mutual respect, shared values, and genuine affection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
marry sb（与某人结婚）；marry into（嫁入/入赘）；be married to（已与……结婚）；marry ... with/to（将……结合）—考研家庭社会类文章常见。

18. historical /hɪˈstɒrɪkl/ adj. 历史的；历史上的；与历史有关的
📖 英文解释
Of or concerning history; used in or based on the study of past events.
✏️ 例句
The historical evidence suggests that climate change has caused the collapse of several ancient civilizations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
historical+名词（historical event/record/context）；区分historical（历史上的）vs. historic（有历史意义的）—考研常考区别！
🧠 助记
history（历史）+ ical → 与历史有关的 → 历史的
historical::/hɪˈstɒrɪkl/:adj. 历史的:historical evidence of climate change 气候变化的历史证据
19. formal /fɔːrml/ adj. 正式的；形式上的；正规的 n. 正式场合
📖 英文解释
Done in accordance with convention or etiquette; relating to outward form; officially recognized.
✏️ 例句
A formal complaint was filed against the company for violating environmental protection regulations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
formal+名词（formal education/complaint/agreement）；formally（adv. 正式地）；formality（n. 礼节/手续）；informal（非正式的）—考研写作风格要求formal。
🧠 助记
form（形式）+ al → 按照形式规范的 → 正式的
formal::/fɔːrml/:adj. 正式的:a formal education system 正规教育体制
20. criminal /krɪmɪnl/ n. 罪犯；犯罪分子 adj. 犯罪的；刑事的
📖 英文解释
A person who has committed a crime; relating to crime or its punishment.
✏️ 例句
The new legislation aims to rehabilitate criminals rather than simply punishing them with longer prison sentences.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
criminal law（刑法）；criminal record（犯罪记录）；criminal justice system（刑事司法体系）；crime（n.）；criminal offense（刑事犯罪）。
🧠 助记
crime（犯罪）+ inal → 与犯罪有关的人 → 罪犯；与犯罪相关的 → 刑事的
criminal::/krɪmɪnl/:n. 罪犯:the criminal justice system 刑事司法体系
21. familiar /fəˈmɪliər/ adj. 熟悉的；常见的；亲密的 n. 密友；魔鬼(旧义)
📖 英文解释
Well known; often encountered; having a thorough knowledge of something.
✏️ 例句
Students who are familiar with the exam format tend to perform better than those who are not prepared.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be familiar with（熟悉……）；familiar to sb（某人熟悉的）；familiarity（n.）；familiarity breeds contempt（熟悉生鄙视）—习语。
🧠 助记
family（家庭）+ iar → 像家人一样熟悉 → 熟悉的
familiar::/fəˈmɪliər/:adj. 熟悉的:be familiar with research methods 熟悉研究方法

friendly+名词（friendly relationship/reminder/competition）；eco-friendly（环保的）；user-friendly（用户友好的）—考研科技类文章常见合成词。

friendly::/frendli/:adj. 友好的:eco-friendly production methods 环保生产方法

planet Earth（地球）；save the planet（拯救地球）；planetary（adj. 行星的）；考研环境类文章中planet常作the planet指地球。

strange::/streɪndʒ/:adj. 奇怪的:strange as it may seem 尽管看似奇怪

useful::/juːsfl/:adj. 有用的:a useful strategy for learning 学习的实用策略
