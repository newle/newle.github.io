1. nowadays /ˈnaʊədeɪz/ adv. 如今；现在
📖 英文解释
at the present time, in contrast with the past
✏️ 例句
Nowadays, most people get their news from social media.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用于句首，引出与过去的对比；考研写作常用于开篇
🧠 助记
now(现在) + a + days(日子) → 现在的日子 → 如今
nowadays::/ˈnaʊədeɪz/:adv. 如今:Nowadays, technology plays a vital role in education.
2. priority /praɪˈɒrəti/ n. 优先；优先事项
📖 英文解释
the fact or condition of being regarded as more important than others
✏️ 例句
Environmental protection should be a top priority for governments.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
top priority 首要任务；give priority to 优先考虑；priority queue 优先队列
🧠 助记
prior(之前/优先) + ity → 优先的状态 → 优先权
priority::/praɪˈɒrəti/:n. 优先:Safety is always our first priority.
3. military /ˈmɪlɪtri/ adj. 军事的 n. 军队
📖 英文解释
relating to soldiers, arms, or war; the armed forces of a country
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
military force 军事力量；military base 军事基地；serve in the military 服役
🧠 助记
mil(千，军队人数多) + itary → 大量士兵组成的 → 军事的
military::/ˈmɪlɪtri/:adj. 军事的 n. 军队:The country increased its military budget this year.
4. cope /kəʊp/ v. 应对；处理
📖 英文解释
to deal effectively with something difficult
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记
cope → 谐音'扛啊' → 扛住困难 → 应对
cope::/kəʊp/:v. 应对:She learned to cope with stress through meditation.
5. propose /prəˈpəʊz/ v. 提议；求婚；打算
📖 英文解释
to put forward a plan or suggestion for others to consider
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
pro(向前) + pose(放置) → 把方案放到前面 → 提议
propose::/prəˈpəʊz/:v. 提议:She proposed a new approach to the problem.
6. relative /ˈrelətɪv/ adj. 相对的；有关联的 n. 亲戚

relative::/ˈrelətɪv/:adj. 相对的:Success is relative and means different things to different people.
