1. humanity /hjuːˈmænɪti/ n. 人类；人道；人文学科
📖 英文解释
human beings collectively; the quality of being compassionate; the humanities
✏️ 例句
Literature helps us explore the full complexity of humanity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
crime against humanity 反人类罪；humanities 人文学科；sense of humanity 人道主义感
🧠 助记
human(人) + ity → 人类的本质 → 人类/人性
humanity::/hjuːˈmænɪti/:n. 人类:Art reflects the best and worst of humanity.
2. preserve /prɪˈzɜːrv/ v. 保存；保护；维持 n. 专属领域
📖 英文解释
to maintain in its original state; to keep safe from harm
✏️ 例句
We must preserve cultural heritage for future generations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
preserve nature 保护自然；preserve food 保存食物；well-preserved 保存完好的
🧠 助记
pre(提前) + serve(服务/保持) → 提前保持好 → 保存
preserve::/prɪˈzɜːrv/:v. 保存:The government passed laws to preserve endangered species.
3. explanation /ˌekspləˈneɪʃn/ n. 解释；说明；理由
📖 英文解释
a statement that makes something clear
✏️ 例句
The teacher gave a clear explanation of the difficult concept.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
give an explanation 提供解释；in explanation of 作为对某事的解释；without explanation 无故
🧠 助记
explain(解释) + ation → 解释的行为 → 说明
explanation::/ˌekspləˈneɪʃn/:n. 解释:He demanded an explanation for the delay.
4. flow /fləʊ/ v./n. 流动；涌出；流量
📖 英文解释
to move steadily and continuously in one direction
✏️ 例句
Ideas began to flow freely during the brainstorming session.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cash flow 现金流；flow of information 信息流；go with the flow 随大流
🧠 助记
flow → 联想 river(河流) 里的水往下流
flow::/fləʊ/:v./n. 流动:Traffic flow improved after the new road opened.
5. technique /tekˈniːk/ n. 技巧；技术；手法
📖 英文解释
a way of carrying out a particular task; skill in a particular activity
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
teaching technique 教学技巧；research technique 研究方法；technical vs. technique 技术性的 vs 技巧
🧠 助记
techni(技术) + que → 技术性的技巧
technique::/tekˈniːk/:n. 技巧:She learned new painting techniques from her instructor.
6. wake /weɪk/ v. 唤醒；醒来 n. 守夜；尾迹

wake::/weɪk/:v. 唤醒:The accident served as a wake-up call for safety reforms.
7. hurt /hɜːrt/ v. 伤害；疼痛 adj. 受伤的

hurt::/hɜːrt/:v. 伤害:She was deeply hurt by his careless words.
8. consist /kənˈsɪst/ v. 由…组成；在于
📖 英文解释

consist::/kənˈsɪst/:v. 由…组成:The committee consists of ten members.
9. master /ˈmɑːstər/ n. 主人；硕士；大师 v. 掌握

🧠 助记

master::/ˈmɑːstər/:n. 主人:She mastered three languages before she was twenty.

smile::/smaɪl/:v./n. 微笑:She smiled warmly at the nervous student.
11. convince /kənˈvɪns/ v. 说服；使相信
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
con(完全) + vince(征服) → 完全征服对方 → 说服
convince::/kənˈvɪns/:v. 说服:The evidence convinced the jury of his guilt.
12. delay /dɪˈleɪ/ v./n. 延迟；延误
📖 英文解释
to make something happen later than expected; a period of time by which something is late

sufficient::/səˈfɪʃnt/:adj. 足够的:The rations were barely sufficient for the long journey.
