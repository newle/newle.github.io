1. proper /ˈprɒpər/ adj. 适当的；正确的；真正的
📖 英文解释
truly what something is said to be; suitable for the purpose
✏️ 例句
Proper planning is essential for any successful project.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
proper use 正确使用；proper conduct 得体行为；property n. 财产/性质（同根词）
🧠 助记
prop(支撑) + er → 支撑起来的标准 → 适当的/正确的
proper::/ˈprɒpər/:adj. 适当的:Students should show proper respect in academic discussions.
2. invest /ɪnˈvest/ v. 投资；投入；赋予
📖 英文解释
to put money into financial schemes with the expectation of achieving a profit
✏️ 例句
Governments must invest in education to drive long-term economic growth.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
invest in 投资于；return on investment 投资回报；invest time and energy 投入时间精力
🧠 助记
in + vest(穿着) → 把钱穿在身上带进去 → 投资
invest::/ɪnˈvest/:v. 投资:She invested all her savings in the startup.

🧠 助记
tough → 联想橡皮(rubber)很tough，拉不断
tough::/tʌf/:adj. 艰难的:She made a tough choice to leave her stable job.
