1. transfer /trænsˈfɜːr/ v./n. 转移；传输；换乘；调动
📖 英文解释
To move from one place, person, or situation to another; the act of moving something.
✏️ 例句
He transferred to a different university to pursue his passion for art.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：transfer money/data；technology transfer（技术转让）；transfer between schools/jobs。考研写作：transfer knowledge into practice。
🧠 助记
trans-（跨越）+ fer（携带）→ 携带着跨越到另一处 → 转移、传输。同根：transport（运输）、transform（转变）。
transfer::/trænsˈfɜːr/:v./n. 转移:the transfer of technology to developing countries remains a key global issue
2. copy /ˈkɒpi/ v./n. 复制；抄写；副本；一册
📖 英文解释
To reproduce something; a thing made to be similar to an original; a single printed item.
✏️ 例句
Please make a copy of the report for each committee member.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：copy of a document；copyright（版权）；copy and paste（复制粘贴）；copycat（模仿者）。考研：intellectual property protection against copying。
🧠 助记
copy 与 cop（警察）相近 → 警察（cop）复制（copy）证据。或：co-（共同）+ py → 共同拥有相同内容 → 副本。
copy::/ˈkɒpi/:v./n. 复制:reproducing an author's work without permission is a violation of copyright
3. vary /ˈveəri/ v. 变化；不同；多样化
📖 英文解释
To change from one form or state to another; to be different from each other.
✏️ 例句
The results vary significantly depending on the experimental conditions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：vary from…to…（从…到…不等）；vary with sth（随…而变化）；various adj.；variety n.；variation n.。考研高频词族。
🧠 助记
var-（变化）+ -y → 变化。联想：variety（多样性）是 vary 的名词，如 variety show（综艺节目）。
vary::/ˈveəri/:v. 变化:cultural norms and values vary widely from one society to another
4. participate /pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪt/ v. 参与；参加
📖 英文解释
To take part in an activity or event.
✏️ 例句
All students are encouraged to participate actively in class discussions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：participate in sth；participation n.（参与）；participant n.（参与者）。考研写作：encourage active participation in community activities。
🧠 助记
part（部分）+ -icipate → 成为整体的一部分 → 参与。想象：参加（participate）一项活动，就是成为其中一 part（部分）。
participate::/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪt/:v. 参与:students who participate in extracurricular activities develop stronger social skills
5. advise /ədˈvaɪz/ v. 建议；劝告；通知
📖 英文解释
To recommend a course of action; to give advice to someone.
✏️ 例句
The doctor advised the patient to reduce stress and get more sleep.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：advise sb to do sth；advise against sth；adviser/advisor n.（顾问）；advice n.（建议，不可数）。
🧠 助记
ad-（向）+ vise（看）→ 向某人提供看法 → 建议。vice 也有"看"的意思，如 revise（再看 = 修改）、supervise（监督）。
advise::/ədˈvaɪz/:v. 建议:experts advise against relying solely on social media for news
6. loan /ləʊn/ n. 贷款；借出物 v. 借出
📖 英文解释
A sum of money that is borrowed and expected to be paid back; to lend something.
✏️ 例句
Many students rely on student loans to finance their university education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：bank loan（银行贷款）；take out a loan（取得贷款）；loan sth to sb（把…借给某人）。考研常见：student loan burden。
🧠 助记
loan 与 alone（独自）关联 → 孤独一人需要借钱 → 贷款。或：lone（单独的）→ 单独给你一笔钱 → 贷款。
loan::/ləʊn/:n. 贷款:the rising cost of tuition has led many students to take out large loans
7. demonstrate /ˈdemənstreɪt/ v. 证明；示范；示威游行
📖 英文解释
To show evidence for; to show how something works; to take part in a public protest.
✏️ 例句
The experiment clearly demonstrates the relationship between heat and molecular movement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：demonstrate that…（证明…）；demonstrate skills/ability；demonstration n.（示威；示范）。考研写作：research demonstrates that…
🧠 助记
de-（完全）+ monster（展示）+ -ate → 完全展示出来 → 证明、示范。demon 里有个"展示怪物"，把真相完全展示出来。
demonstrate::/ˈdemənstreɪt/:v. 证明:the study demonstrates a clear link between exercise and academic performance
8. fundamental /ˌfʌndəˈmentl/ adj. 基本的；根本的 n. 基本原则
📖 英文解释
Forming a necessary base; of central importance; a basic rule or principle.
✏️ 例句
A fundamental understanding of grammar is essential for effective writing.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：fundamental change/difference/principle；fundamentally adv.（根本上）。考研写作高频词：a fundamental shift in perspective。
🧠 助记
fund-（基础/资金）+ -amental → 基础层面的 → 基本的、根本的。fund 本身有"基金/基础"的意思，基础就是最根本的。
fundamental::/ˌfʌndəˈmentl/:adj. 基本的:respect for human dignity is a fundamental value in any just society
9. origin /ˈɒrɪdʒɪn/ n. 起源；出身；由来
📖 英文解释
The point or place where something begins; a person's social background or ancestry.
✏️ 例句
Scholars have long debated the origin of language in human evolution.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：the origin of sth；of humble/noble origin（出身卑微/高贵）；original adj.（原始的；独创的）；originate v.（起源）。
🧠 助记
ori-（东方/升起）+ -gin → 太阳升起的地方 → 起源、起点。orient（东方）和 origin 同根，太阳从东方升起象征起点。
origin::/ˈɒrɪdʒɪn/:n. 起源:understanding the origin of a problem is essential to finding a lasting solution
10. recommend /ˌrekəˈmend/ v. 推荐；建议；劝告
📖 英文解释
To suggest something as being suitable or appropriate; to advise a course of action.
✏️ 例句
The committee strongly recommended that the policy be reviewed annually.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：recommend sb/sth to sb；recommend doing sth / that sb do sth（虚拟语气）；recommendation n.（推荐；建议）。
🧠 助记
re-（再次）+ commend（赞扬）→ 再次赞扬某人/物 → 推荐。commend = 称赞，recommend = 为他人称赞介绍。
recommend::/ˌrekəˈmend/:v. 推荐:doctors recommend regular exercise to reduce the risk of chronic disease
11. valuable /ˈvæljuəbl/ adj. 有价值的；宝贵的；值钱的
📖 英文解释
Worth a great deal of money; extremely useful or important.
✏️ 例句
The internship provided valuable hands-on experience for the students.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：valuable experience/resource/lesson；invaluable（无价的，极宝贵的，反义并非"无价值"）。考研写作：valuable insights into…
🧠 助记
value（价值）+ -able → 有价值的。想象：能 value 的东西当然是 valuable！价值高的 = 宝贵的。
valuable::/ˈvæljuəbl/:adj. 有价值的:volunteering abroad provides valuable cross-cultural communication skills
12. addition /əˈdɪʃən/ n. 添加；加法；增加物
📖 英文解释
The action of adding one thing to another; something that has been added.
✏️ 例句
In addition to studying hard, he also participated in many extracurricular activities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
核心短语：in addition to（除…之外还）——考研写作最高频衔接词之一！区分：besides（口语化）vs in addition to（正式书面）。
🧠 助记
add（加）+ -ition → 加的动作/结果 → 加法；添加。add → addition，就像 act → action 一样规律。
addition::/əˈdɪʃən/:n. 添加:in addition to academic performance, universities value leadership and social skills
13. contract /ˈkɒntrækt/ n. 合同；契约 v. 签订合同；收缩
📖 英文解释
A written or spoken agreement intended to be enforceable by law; to make or become smaller.
✏️ 例句
Both parties signed the contract before the project officially began.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：sign/breach/fulfill a contract；under contract（在合同约束下）；contract v. 收缩（与 expand 相对）。考研法律/经济类阅读常见。
🧠 助记
con-（共同）+ tract（拉）→ 共同拉在一起 → 合同（把双方拉在一起的协议）。同根：attract（吸引）、extract（提取）。
contract::/ˈkɒntrækt/:n. 合同:employers and employees must both honor the terms of their contract
14. description /dɪˈskrɪpʃən/ n. 描述；描写；说明
📖 英文解释
A spoken or written account of a person, object, or event.
✏️ 例句
The job description clearly outlined the responsibilities and qualifications required.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：give/provide a description；job description（工作职责描述）；descriptive adj.（描述性的）；describe v.。
🧠 助记
de-（完全）+ script（写）+ -ion → 完整写下来的内容 → 描述。script = 剧本/写，describe = 详细写下来。
description::/dɪˈskrɪpʃən/:n. 描述:an accurate description of the problem is the first step toward solving it
15. alcohol /ˈælkəhɒl/ n. 酒精；乙醇；含酒精饮料
📖 英文解释
A colorless volatile liquid; drinks containing this substance such as wine and beer.
✏️ 例句
Research shows that excessive alcohol consumption can severely damage liver function.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：alcohol abuse（酗酒）；alcohol consumption；alcoholic adj./n.（酒精的；酒鬼）；prohibit alcohol。考研健康类文章高频词。
🧠 助记
al- + kohl（阿拉伯语：眼影粉）→ 古代阿拉伯人蒸馏眼影时发现乙醇 → alcohol 的历史词源。记：酒精 = 蒸馏的精华。
alcohol::/ˈælkəhɒl/:n. 酒精:the government launched a campaign to reduce alcohol abuse among teenagers
16. district /ˈdɪstrɪkt/ n. 地区；区域；行政区
📖 英文解释
An area of a country or city, especially one regarded as a distinct unit.
✏️ 例句
The school district announced plans to build three new elementary schools.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：school/business/residential district；district court（地区法院）；district government（地方政府）。考研社会类阅读常见。
🧠 助记
dis-（分开）+ strict（严格/区划）→ 严格划分开的地区 → 区域。strict 有"划线/严格"的感觉，district = 划定的地区。
district::/ˈdɪstrɪkt/:n. 地区:investment in underdeveloped districts is crucial for balanced regional growth
17. dry /draɪ/ adj. 干的；干燥的 v. 弄干；晾干
📖 英文解释
Free from moisture or liquid; lacking rain; to make or become dry.
✏️ 例句
After the heavy rain, they dried their clothes in the sun.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：dry season（旱季）；dry up（干涸；枯竭）；blow-dry（吹干）。引申义：dry humor（冷幽默）；dry run（彩排/演练）。
🧠 助记
dry 联想：drive（驾驶）→ 车在干燥路面驾驶最好。或：d + ry → die（死）的感觉，缺水就 dry 了。
dry::/draɪ/:adj. 干的:prolonged dry weather has caused severe water shortages in the region
18. evening /ˈiːvnɪŋ/ n. 傍晚；晚间；晚会
📖 英文解释
The period of time at the end of the day, usually from about 6 p.m. to bedtime.
✏️ 例句
Many professionals use their evenings to pursue further education or hobbies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：in the evening；this evening（今晚）；evening class（夜校）；good evening（晚上好）。注意：介词用 in the evening，但 on Monday evening（具体某晚用 on）。
🧠 助记
even（平衡）+ -ing → 白天和夜晚交接、平衡的时段 → 傍晚。even 有"平整"之意，evening = 天色趋于平衡的时刻。
evening::/ˈiːvnɪŋ/:n. 傍晚:evening adult education programs help working professionals gain new qualifications
19. breakfast /ˈbrekfəst/ n. 早饭；早餐 v. 吃早餐
📖 英文解释
The first meal of the day, eaten in the morning.
✏️ 例句
Research suggests that eating a nutritious breakfast improves concentration in school.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：have/eat breakfast；skip breakfast（不吃早餐）；breakfast table（早餐桌）；continental breakfast（欧式早餐）。
🧠 助记
break（打破）+ fast（禁食）→ 打破夜间禁食 → 早餐。overnight fasting = 过夜禁食，breakfast = 打破禁食的第一餐。
breakfast::/ˈbrekfəst/:n. 早饭:children who eat breakfast regularly tend to perform better academically
20. gene /dʒiːn/ n. 基因；遗传因子
📖 英文解释
A unit of DNA that carries hereditary information and determines specific traits.
✏️ 例句
Scientists have identified the gene responsible for a rare genetic disorder.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：gene expression（基因表达）；genetic adj.（遗传的）；genome（基因组）；gene editing（基因编辑）。考研生物/科技类文章必备词。
🧠 助记
gene 联想 generate（产生）→ 基因产生遗传特征。或想：jean（牛仔裤）→ 每个基因（gene）都穿着独特的"遗传牛仔裤"。
gene::/dʒiːn/:n. 基因:gene therapy holds great promise for treating previously incurable diseases
21. wave /weɪv/ n. 浪；波；波动 v. 挥动；波动
📖 英文解释
A ridge of water on the surface of the sea; a sudden increase in something; to move as a signal.
✏️ 例句
A new wave of technological innovation is transforming the global economy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：a wave of（一股/一浪…）；sound/radio wave（声波/无线电波）；wave goodbye（挥手告别）；heat wave（热浪）。
🧠 助记
wave 形似 cave（洞穴）→ 海浪拍打洞穴。想象海边的波浪 wave 打来，海水钻进 cave（洞穴）里。
wave::/weɪv/:n. 浪:a new wave of automation is reshaping the labor market worldwide
22. moreover /mɔːrˈəʊvər/ adv. 此外；而且；更重要的是
📖 英文解释
As a further matter; besides; in addition and more importantly.
✏️ 例句
The policy is cost-effective; moreover, it has received strong public support.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研写作核心衔接词！表递进关系，比 also/besides 更正式。常用于文章第二段开头或论点补充。辨析：furthermore（更进一步）同义但更强调程度。
🧠 助记
more（更多）+ over（超过）→ 在"更多"之上还有 → 此外、更重要的是。比 more 还 over = 超额添加。
moreover::/mɔːrˈəʊvər/:adv. 此外:the new curriculum saves time; moreover, it produces consistently better learning outcomes
23. capable /ˈkeɪpəbl/ adj. 有能力的；能干的
📖 英文解释
Having the ability, fitness, or quality necessary to do something.
✏️ 例句
With proper training, every student is capable of achieving academic success.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：be capable of sth/doing sth；a capable person；incapable（无能力的）；capability n.（能力）。考研写作：be capable of making significant contributions。
🧠 助记
cap-（抓住/容纳）+ -able → 能够容纳/抓住任务 → 有能力的。cap = 头/帽，"有帽子"能做到 = 有能力。
capable::/ˈkeɪpəbl/:adj. 有能力的:a well-educated workforce is capable of driving economic growth and innovation
24. ordinary /ˈɔːrdɪnəri/ adj. 普通的；平凡的；平常的
📖 英文解释
With no special or distinctive features; normal; not different from usual.
✏️ 例句
Extraordinary results often come from ordinary people who persevere.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：ordinary people（普通人）；out of the ordinary（不寻常）；extraordinary（非凡的）。考研写作反转句：ordinary efforts → extraordinary achievements。
🧠 助记
ordin-（顺序/有序）+ -ary → 按常规顺序的 → 普通的、平常的。order（顺序）→ ordinary = 按照正常顺序来的，没有特别。
ordinary::/ˈɔːrdɪnəri/:adj. 普通的:ordinary citizens often underestimate their ability to influence social change
25. strengthen /ˈstreŋθən/ v. 加强；巩固；使更强壮
📖 英文解释
To make or become stronger; to reinforce.
✏️ 例句
Regular exercise can significantly strengthen both physical and mental health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：strengthen cooperation/ties/bonds；strengthen one's resolve；strength n.（力量）；strong adj.。考研写作：strengthen international cooperation。
🧠 助记
strength（力量）+ -en（使动）→ 使变得有力量 → 加强。strength → strengthen，就像 wide → widen（拓宽）一样。
strengthen::/ˈstreŋθən/:v. 加强:educational exchanges between countries help strengthen mutual understanding and trust
