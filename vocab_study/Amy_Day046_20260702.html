1. suggestion /səˈdʒestʃən/ n. 建议；暗示；迹象；少许
📖 英文解释
An idea or plan put forward for consideration; a slight trace or indication.
✏️ 例句
The professor welcomed students' suggestions for improving the course content.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：make/offer/follow a suggestion；at sb's suggestion（按照某人的建议）；suggestion that…（虚拟语气：the suggestion that sb do）。
🧠 助记
suggest（建议）+ -ion → 建议这个行为 → 建议、暗示。suggest v. → suggestion n.，如 object → objection 规律。
suggestion::/səˈdʒestʃən/:n. 建议:the teacher's suggestion to form study groups significantly improved student performance
2. cash /kæʃ/ n. 现金；现款 v. 兑换；兑现
📖 英文解释
Money in coins or notes; to exchange a check or money order for cash.
✏️ 例句
Many small businesses still prefer cash transactions to avoid processing fees.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：cash in hand（现金）；cash flow（现金流）；cash register（收银机）；cash out（套现）；pay in cash（用现金支付）。
🧠 助记
cash 联想：clash（冲突）→ 现金是最直接的交换，没有多余的冲突（clash）。或：cash = 卡（card）之前用的东西，现金。
cash::/kæʃ/:n. 现金:maintaining a healthy cash flow is critical for the survival of any small business
3. secret /ˈsiːkrɪt/ n. 秘密；奥秘 adj. 秘密的；保密的
📖 英文解释
Something kept hidden from others; not known or seen; something unexplained.
✏️ 例句
The secret to academic success often lies in consistent effort and curiosity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：keep a secret（保守秘密）；the secret of/to success（成功的秘诀）；in secret（秘密地）；secretly adv.。
🧠 助记
se-（分离/单独）+ cret（筛选）→ 单独筛选出来不告诉别人的 → 秘密。secretary（秘书）词根相同，秘书保守机密。
secret::/ˈsiːkrɪt/:n. 秘密:the secret to mastering a new language is consistent daily practice over time
4. quarter /ˈkwɔːrtər/ n. 四分之一；季度；地区；25美分
📖 英文解释
One of four equal parts; a period of three months; a district of a town.
✏️ 例句
Sales increased by over 30 percent in the third quarter of this year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：a quarter of sth；first/fourth quarter（第一/四季度）；headquarters（总部）；quarter past/to（…点一刻/差一刻）。
🧠 助记
quart-（四）+ -er → 四分之一。quart = 夸脱（四分之一加仑）；quartet（四重奏）同根。记：4 → quarter。
quarter::/ˈkwɔːrtər/:n. 四分之一:the company reported record profits in the fourth quarter of the fiscal year
5. persuade /pərˈsweɪd/ v. 说服；劝说；使相信
📖 英文解释
To cause someone to believe or do something through reasoning or argument.
✏️ 例句
The documentary persuaded many viewers to change their eating habits.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：persuade sb to do sth；persuade sb that…；persuasion n.（说服）；persuasive adj.（有说服力的）。考研写作：use persuasive arguments。
🧠 助记
per-（完全）+ suade（劝告）→ 完全劝说 → 说服。词根 suad/suas = 劝，dissuade（劝阻）是反义词。
persuade::/pərˈsweɪd/:v. 说服:effective speakers can persuade audiences by combining logic with emotional appeal
6. software /ˈsɒftweər/ n. 软件；程序
📖 英文解释
Programs and other operating information used by a computer.
✏️ 例句
Open-source software has greatly democratized access to digital tools worldwide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：software developer/engineer；software update/upgrade；hardware（硬件，对义词）；install software。考研科技类文章背景词。
🧠 助记
soft（软）+ ware（产品）→ 软性产品（不是实体的）→ 软件。对比：hardware（硬件）= 硬的实体产品。
software::/ˈsɒftweər/:n. 软件:educational software can provide personalized learning experiences for students of all levels
7. afraid /əˈfreɪd/ adj. 害怕的；担心的；恐惧的
📖 英文解释
Feeling fear or anxiety; worried about the consequences of something.
✏️ 例句
Many students are afraid of making mistakes when speaking a foreign language.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：be afraid of sth/doing sth；be afraid to do sth；I'm afraid that…（恐怕…，表婉转歉意）。注意：afraid 一般作表语，不作定语。
🧠 助记
a-（前缀加强）+ fraid（frighten 的变形）→ 被惊吓到 → 害怕的。afraid = a（完全）+ fraid（害怕）。
afraid::/əˈfreɪd/:adj. 害怕的:students should not be afraid of asking questions in class
8. steal /stiːl/ v. 偷窃；窃取；悄悄移动
📖 英文解释
To take something without permission or legal right; to move quietly without being noticed.
✏️ 例句
Identity theft, where criminals steal personal information online, is a growing concern.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：steal sth from sb；steal the show（抢尽风头）；steal a glance（偷偷看一眼）；steal away（悄悄离开）。stole/stolen 不规则变化。
🧠 助记
steal 联想 steel（钢铁）→ 像钢铁一样无声无息地拿走 → 偷窃。两者发音相同，区分拼写：steal（偷）vs steel（钢）。
steal::/stiːl/:v. 偷窃:cybercriminals can steal sensitive data from millions of users in a single attack
9. evolution /ˌiːvəˈluːʃən/ n. 进化；演变；发展
📖 英文解释
The process by which different kinds of organisms develop from earlier forms; gradual development.
✏️ 例句
The evolution of communication technology has transformed how people interact globally.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：theory of evolution（进化论）；cultural/social evolution；evolve v.（进化；演变）；evolutionary adj.。考研常见：the evolution of language/society。
🧠 助记
e-（出来）+ volu-（滚动/卷）+ -tion → 从卷中逐渐展开 → 进化、演变。revolve（旋转）、revolution（革命）同词根 vol = 滚动。
evolution::/ˌiːvəˈluːʃən/:n. 进化:the rapid evolution of artificial intelligence poses both opportunities and ethical challenges
10. corporation /ˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən/ n. 公司；法人；社团
📖 英文解释
A large company or group of companies legally authorized to act as a single entity.
✏️ 例句
Multinational corporations have a significant influence on the global economy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：multinational corporation（跨国公司）；corporate adj.（公司的）；corporate responsibility（企业责任）。考研经济类阅读必备词。
🧠 助记
corp-（身体/团体）+ -oration → 组成一个整体的团体 → 公司、法人。corpus（身体/语料库）同根，corporation = 有"躯体"的集体组织。
corporation::/ˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən/:n. 公司:large corporations are under increasing pressure to adopt sustainable business practices
11. homework /ˈhəʊmˌwɜːrk/ n. 家庭作业；（课前）准备工作
📖 英文解释
School work that a student is required to do at home; preparation done in advance.
✏️ 例句
Research shows that moderate amounts of homework can reinforce classroom learning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：do/finish homework；assign homework；the debate over homework（关于家庭作业的争议）。引申义：do your homework（做好准备工作）。
🧠 助记
home（家）+ work（工作）→ 在家里做的工作 → 家庭作业。最直白的组合词，home + work = homework。
homework::/ˈhəʊmˌwɜːrk/:n. 家庭作业:doing homework regularly helps students consolidate and apply what they learn in class
12. sight /saɪt/ n. 视力；视野；景象；名胜
📖 英文解释
The faculty of seeing; the range of vision; something worth seeing; a glimpse.
✏️ 例句
The sight of students engaged in deep discussion filled the teacher with pride.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：lose one's sight（失明）；at first sight（初见）；in sight（在视野内）；out of sight（看不见）；tourist sights（旅游景点）。
🧠 助记
sight 联想 light（光）→ 有光才能看见 → 视力、视野。看（sight）需要光（light），两者押韵好记。
sight::/saɪt/:n. 视力:the city's historic sights attract millions of tourists from around the world each year
13. athlete /ˈæθliːt/ n. 运动员；田径运动员
📖 英文解释
A person who is proficient in sports and physical exercises, especially track and field.
✏️ 例句
Many elite athletes attribute their success to both talent and relentless training.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：professional/amateur athlete；athletic adj.（运动的；体格健壮的）；athletics n.（田径运动）。考研体育类文章常见词。
🧠 助记
athl-（竞赛）+ -ete（人）→ 参加竞赛的人 → 运动员。athlon（竞技）是希腊语，如 decathlon（十项全能）、triathlon（铁人三项）。
athlete::/ˈæθliːt/:n. 运动员:elite athletes often demonstrate the same discipline and focus required for academic excellence
14. outcome /ˈaʊtˌkʌm/ n. 结果；结局；成效
📖 英文解释
The way a situation ends; a result or effect of an action or event.
✏️ 例句
The outcome of the experiment confirmed the researchers' initial hypothesis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：the outcome of sth；positive/negative outcome；outcome-based learning（以结果为导向的学习）。考研写作：achieve the desired outcome。
🧠 助记
out（出来）+ come（来）→ 最终出来的东西 → 结果。从过程中"出来"的就是 outcome（结果）。
outcome::/ˈaʊtˌkʌm/:n. 结果:a positive educational outcome depends on strong teacher-student relationships
15. surface /ˈsɜːrfɪs/ n. 表面；外表 v. 浮现；出现 adj. 表面的
📖 英文解释
The outside part of something; the top layer of the earth or a liquid; to come to the top.
✏️ 例句
On the surface, the proposal looks promising, but the details are problematic.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：on the surface（从表面上看）；surface level（表面层次）；surface tension（表面张力）；beneath the surface（深层）。
🧠 助记
sur-（在…上面）+ face（脸/面）→ 在上面的那张脸 → 表面。sur = over/above，如 surpass（超越）、survey（俯视→调查）。
surface::/ˈsɜːrfɪs/:n. 表面:on the surface, the data looks positive, but deeper analysis reveals significant problems
16. apart /əˈpɑːrt/ adv. 分离；相距；除…之外
📖 英文解释
Separated by a distance; into pieces; other than; besides.
✏️ 例句
Apart from a few minor errors, the essay was well-written and clearly argued.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：apart from（除…之外）；fall apart（崩溃；瓦解）；set apart（使与众不同）；tear apart（撕开）。考研写作衔接词：apart from this…
🧠 助记
a-（向）+ part（部分）→ 各自成部分 → 分离。联想：apartment（公寓）= 分开的居住空间，公寓里的房间是 apart（分离）的。
apart::/əˈpɑːrt/:adv. 分离:apart from academic knowledge, social skills are equally important for career success
17. pleasure /ˈpleʒər/ n. 愉悦；享乐；乐事
📖 英文解释
A feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment; something that gives this feeling.
✏️ 例句
It gives me great pleasure to introduce tonight's keynote speaker.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：take pleasure in sth（以…为乐）；with pleasure（乐意）；for pleasure（为了消遣）；It's a pleasure to…。考研：the pleasure of reading。
🧠 助记
please（取悦）+ -ure → 被取悦的状态 → 愉悦。please → pleasure，就像 close → closure 一样规律。
pleasure::/ˈpleʒər/:n. 愉悦:reading for pleasure is one of the most effective ways to build vocabulary naturally
18. entry /ˈentri/ n. 进入；入口；参赛作品；条目
📖 英文解释
The action of entering; a place of entrance; an item written in a diary or list; a submission.
✏️ 例句
Entry to the competition is open to students of all grade levels.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：entry point（入口点）；deny entry（拒绝入境）；journal/diary entry（日记条目）；free entry（免费入场）。考研常见：ease of market entry。
🧠 助记
entr-（进入）+ -y → 进入的行为/地点 → 进入；入口。enter v. → entry n.，想象考试的 entry point（入口）。
entry::/ˈentri/:n. 进入:reducing barriers to market entry encourages competition and benefits consumers
19. justice /ˈdʒʌstɪs/ n. 公正；正义；司法；法官
📖 英文解释
The quality of being fair and reasonable; the legal system; a judge of the supreme court.
✏️ 例句
Education plays a crucial role in promoting social justice and equal opportunity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：social justice（社会公正）；justice system（司法体系）；do justice to sth（公平对待）；injustice（不公正）。考研写作核心词汇。
🧠 助记
just（公正的）+ -ice（名词后缀）→ 公正的状态 → 公正、正义。just = fair，justice = the state of being just。
justice::/ˈdʒʌstɪs/:n. 公正:a fair and transparent education system is fundamental to social justice
20. electricity /ɪˌlekˈtrɪsɪti/ n. 电；电力；电流
📖 英文解释
A form of energy resulting from the existence of charged particles; electric power.
✏️ 例句
Access to electricity is essential for improving the quality of life in rural areas.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：generate/supply electricity；electricity consumption；renewable electricity（可再生电力）；electric adj.；electronic adj.（电子的）。
🧠 助记
electric（电的）+ -ity → 电的性质/状态 → 电、电力。electron（电子）同根，记住 electric 就能推出 electricity。
electricity::/ɪˌlekˈtrɪsɪti/:n. 电:expanding access to clean electricity is key to achieving sustainable development goals
21. notice /ˈnəʊtɪs/ v. 注意到；察觉 n. 注意；通知；公告
📖 英文解释
To become aware of something; attention or observation; a formal announcement.
✏️ 例句
Did you notice any significant differences between the two research papers?
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：take notice of（注意）；at short notice（临时通知）；give notice（预告离职）；notice board（公告栏）。考研阅读：without prior notice。
🧠 助记
not-（知道/注意）+ -ice → 知道了某事 → 注意到。词根 not = know，如 notion（概念）、annotate（注释）。
notice::/ˈnəʊtɪs/:v. 注意到:teachers who notice early signs of struggle can intervene before students fall behind
22. final /ˈfaɪnl/ adj. 最终的；最后的 n. 决赛；期末考试
📖 英文解释
Coming at the end; last; not to be changed; a conclusive examination or sports competition.
✏️ 例句
The final decision rests with the committee after reviewing all the proposals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：final exam（期末考试）；the final answer/decision；finally adv.（最终）；finalize v.（使最终确定）。考研写作：in the final analysis（归根结底）。
🧠 助记
fin-（结束）+ -al → 结束的 → 最终的。finish（完成）、fine（结束）同词根 fin = end。final 就是 finish 的形容词形式。
final::/ˈfaɪnl/:adj. 最终的:in the final analysis, a society's progress depends on the quality of its education
23. rarely /ˈreərli/ adv. 很少；难得；不常
📖 英文解释
Not often; seldom; exceptionally well (archaic).
✏️ 例句
Remarkable achievements are rarely the result of a single moment of inspiration.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：rarely do sb do sth（倒装强调）；rarely if ever（几乎从不）。考研写作用法：Rarely does a single factor determine academic success.（倒装句）
🧠 助记
rare（罕见的）+ -ly → 以罕见的方式 → 很少。rare = 稀少的，rarely = 稀少地发生。联想：rare steak（三分熟的牛排，不常点的）。
rarely::/ˈreərli/:adv. 很少:a single cause rarely explains a complex social phenomenon completely
24. privacy /ˈprɪvəsi/ n. 隐私；私密；独处
📖 英文解释
The state of being free from public attention; freedom from intrusion.
✏️ 例句
The widespread use of social media has raised serious concerns about personal privacy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：right to privacy（隐私权）；invade/violate sb's privacy（侵犯隐私）；privacy policy（隐私政策）。考研热点话题：digital privacy。
🧠 助记
priv-（私有）+ -acy → 私有状态 → 隐私。private（私人的）→ privacy（私密状态）。
privacy::/ˈprɪvəsi/:n. 隐私:balancing national security and individual privacy remains a controversial policy debate

email::/ˈiːmeɪl/:n./v. 电子邮件:professional email communication is an essential skill in the modern workplace
