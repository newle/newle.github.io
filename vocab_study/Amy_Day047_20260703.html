1. cycle /ˈsaɪkl/ n. 周期；循环；自行车 v. 骑自行车；循环
📖 英文解释
A series of events that happen repeatedly; a bicycle; to ride a bicycle.
✏️ 例句
The water cycle is a fundamental concept in understanding global climate patterns.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：life cycle（生命周期）；economic cycle（经济周期）；vicious cycle（恶性循环）；cycle to work（骑车上班）；recycle（回收）。
🧠 助记
cycl-（圆/循环）→ 圆形运动 → 循环。bicycle（自行车）= bi（两）+ cycle（轮子），recycle = re（再）+ cycle（循环）。
cycle::/ˈsaɪkl/:n. 周期:breaking the cycle of poverty requires sustained investment in quality education
2. satellite /ˈsætəlaɪt/ n. 卫星；人造卫星；卫星国 adj. 附属的
📖 英文解释
A natural or artificial body orbiting a planet; a country controlled by another; a subsidiary.
✏️ 例句
Satellite technology has revolutionized global communication and navigation systems.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：satellite TV/phone；communication/weather satellite；satellite state（卫星国）；launch a satellite（发射卫星）。
🧠 助记
satel-（陪伴者）+ -lite → 陪伴在旁边的小星体 → 卫星。satellite = 陪伴大行星运转的小天体。
satellite::/ˈsætəlaɪt/:n. 卫星:communication satellites have made real-time global information sharing possible
3. burden /ˈbɜːrdən/ n. 负担；重担；责任 v. 使负担
📖 英文解释
A heavy load; something difficult or worrying; a responsibility; to weigh down.
✏️ 例句
The burden of student debt has become a serious social issue in many countries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：bear/carry/shoulder a burden；financial/emotional burden；burden of proof（举证责任）；burden sb with sth（让某人承担）。
🧠 助记
bur-（沉重）+ den（山洞）→ 像山洞压着你一样 → 负担。bear（熊）bur（sth）den（进山洞）→ 熊背着重东西进山洞 = 负担。
burden::/ˈbɜːrdən/:n. 负担:reducing the financial burden of education is essential for promoting equal access
4. king /kɪŋ/ n. 国王；君王；王者
📖 英文解释
The male ruler of an independent state; a person or thing regarded as best in its domain.
✏️ 例句
Content is king in the digital age — relevant and engaging information attracts loyal audiences.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：king of…（…之王）；kingdom（王国）；kingpin（核心人物）；content is king（内容为王）。引申义在考研社会/商业类文章中常见。
🧠 助记
king 联想：ring（戒指）→ 国王戴着戒指。或：king 就是 kind（善良）的人 → 善良的领袖才是真正的 king。
king::/kɪŋ/:n. 国王:in the information economy, knowledge is king and education is the key to success
5. salary /ˈsæləri/ n. 薪水；工资（月薪/年薪）
📖 英文解释
A fixed regular payment for employment, typically expressed as an annual sum.
✏️ 例句
A university degree does not always guarantee a high salary in today's job market.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：annual/monthly salary；receive/earn a salary；salary increase/raise；辨析：salary（月薪/年薪，白领）vs wage（时薪/日薪，蓝领）。
🧠 助记
sal-（盐）→ 古罗马士兵以盐（salt）作报酬 → salary 源自拉丁语 salarium（盐钱）。"值这份薪水" = "worth his salt"。
salary::/ˈsæləri/:n. 薪水:starting salary alone should not determine a graduate's choice of career path
6. mountain /ˈmaʊntən/ n. 山；山岳；大量
📖 英文解释
A large natural elevation of the earth's surface; a very large amount of something.
✏️ 例句
She faces a mountain of challenges in pursuing her dream of becoming a scientist.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：mountain range（山脉）；move mountains（创造奇迹）；a mountain of work/evidence（大量工作/证据）；climb a mountain。
🧠 助记
mount（攀登）+ -ain → 需要攀登的高地 → 山。mount 本身有"骑上/登上"之意，mountain = 可以 mount（攀登）的高地。
mountain::/ˈmaʊntən/:n. 山:overcoming the mountain of bureaucratic obstacles requires both patience and persistence
7. religious /rɪˈlɪdʒəs/ adj. 宗教的；虔诚的；严格遵守的
📖 英文解释
Relating to religion; very careful to fulfill obligations; scrupulous.
✏️ 例句
Religious diversity should be respected in multicultural societies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：religious belief/practice；religious freedom（宗教自由）；be religious about sth（严格遵守…）；religion n.；irreligious（不信教的）。
🧠 助记
religi-（绑定/义务）+ -ous → 被（宗教）义务绑定的 → 宗教的；虔诚的。religion = 将信徒与神明绑定的纽带。
religious::/rɪˈlɪdʒəs/:adj. 宗教的:religious tolerance is a cornerstone of stable and harmonious multicultural societies
8. guarantee /ˌɡærənˈtiː/ v./n. 保证；担保；保障
📖 英文解释
A formal assurance that certain conditions will be fulfilled; to promise with certainty.
✏️ 例句
Hard work does not guarantee success, but it significantly increases the chances.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：guarantee sth/that…；no guarantee（没有保证）；money-back guarantee（退款保证）；guarantee quality。考研写作：cannot guarantee equal outcomes。
🧠 助记
guarant-（保护）+ -ee → 被保护的结果 → 保证。warrant（保证/搜查令）同族词根，guarantee = official warrant of quality。
guarantee::/ˌɡærənˈtiː/:v./n. 保证:equal access to education does not guarantee equal outcomes for all students
9. annual /ˈænjuəl/ adj. 每年的；年度的 n. 年刊；一年生植物
📖 英文解释
Happening once a year; relating to a year's period; a book published once a year.
✏️ 例句
The university holds an annual conference to showcase student research achievements.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：annual report/meeting/income；annually adv.（每年地）；per annum（每年，书面）；semi-annual（半年一次）。
🧠 助记
annu-（年）+ -al → 每年的。annus = year（拉丁语），anniversary（周年纪念）同根，an = year。
annual::/ˈænjuəl/:adj. 每年的:annual investment in education infrastructure reflects a government's long-term priorities
10. ease /iːz/ n. 轻松；安逸 v. 减轻；缓解
📖 英文解释
Absence of difficulty or effort; freedom from discomfort; to make something less severe.
✏️ 例句
Technology can help ease the burden of administrative tasks for teachers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：ease of use（易用性）；with ease（轻松地）；at ease（放松地）；ease tension/pain；ease into（逐渐进入）。考研写作：ease the pressure on…
🧠 助记
ease 联想：please（取悦）→ ease = 让人 please（放松）的状态。easy（容易的）是形容词，ease 是名词/动词。
ease::/iːz/:n. 轻松:well-designed educational tools can ease the transition from passive to active learning
11. telephone /ˈtelɪfəʊn/ n. 电话；电话机 v. 打电话
📖 英文解释
A system for transmitting voice over a distance; to call someone by phone.
✏️ 例句
The invention of the telephone fundamentally changed how people communicate across distances.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：telephone call/conversation；telephone number；over the telephone（通过电话）；mobile/cell telephone。prefix tele- = far/distance。
🧠 助记
tele-（远距离）+ phone（声音）→ 远距离传声 → 电话。tele = far，如 television（远距离看），telescope（远距离看）。
telephone::/ˈtelɪfəʊn/:n. 电话:the telephone transformed business communication by enabling real-time long-distance conversations
12. garden /ˈɡɑːrdən/ n. 花园；园地 v. 种花种草
📖 英文解释
A piece of land used for growing plants; to work in a garden.
✏️ 例句
The school garden project teaches children about biology, ecology, and responsibility.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：school/community garden；garden party（花园聚会）；botanical garden（植物园）；garden variety（普通的，口语化）。
🧠 助记
guard（守护）+ -en → 被守护的一块绿地 → 花园。garden = a guarded land，人们精心呵护的土地。
garden::/ˈɡɑːrdən/:n. 花园:community gardens in urban areas promote social cohesion and environmental awareness
13. comfort /ˈkʌmfərt/ n. 安慰；舒适；慰藉 v. 安慰
📖 英文解释
A state of physical ease and freedom from pain; to make someone feel less unhappy.
✏️ 例句
Knowing that others have faced similar challenges can provide great comfort.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：comfort zone（舒适区）；take comfort in sth（在…中得到安慰）；comfortable adj.；discomfort n.（不适）。考研写作：step out of one's comfort zone。
🧠 助记
com-（加强）+ fort（力量）→ 给予力量 → 安慰、舒适。fort = 力量/堡垒，comfort = 给予内心堡垒般的安慰。
comfort::/ˈkʌmfərt/:n. 安慰:stepping out of one's comfort zone is essential for personal growth and development
14. request /rɪˈkwest/ n./v. 请求；要求；申请
📖 英文解释
An act of asking politely or formally for something; to ask for something.
✏️ 例句
The student submitted a formal request for an extension on the assignment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：make a request；at sb's request（应某人要求）；on request（应要求）；request that sb do sth（虚拟语气）；request for information。
🧠 助记
re-（再次）+ quest（寻找）→ 再次寻找（希望对方提供）→ 请求。quest = 探索/寻找，request = 向他人寻求帮助。
request::/rɪˈkwest/:n./v. 请求:many students request additional feedback to improve their academic writing skills
15. foundation /faʊnˈdeɪʃən/ n. 基础；地基；基金会；建立
📖 英文解释
The lowest load-bearing part of a building; an underlying principle; an organization endowed for a purpose.
✏️ 例句
A solid foundation in mathematics is essential for success in engineering.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：lay the foundation for sth（为…奠定基础）；educational foundation（教育基金会）；without foundation（毫无根据）。考研写作核心词。
🧠 助记
found（建立/找到）+ -ation → 建立的基础 → 地基；基础。found → foundation，如 found a company（创立公司）→ lay the foundation（奠定基础）。
foundation::/faʊnˈdeɪʃən/:n. 基础:early childhood education lays the foundation for lifelong learning and development
16. lunch /lʌntʃ/ n. 午餐；午饭 v. 吃午饭
📖 英文解释
A meal eaten in the middle of the day.
✏️ 例句
The school provides free lunch to students from low-income families.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：have/eat lunch；lunch break（午休）；business lunch（商务午餐）；free school lunch（免费校餐）。短语：no such thing as a free lunch（天下没有免费的午餐）。
🧠 助记
lunch 和 munch（大口吃）押韵 → 大口吃东西的中午那顿饭 → 午餐。或：l + unch → 中文"陆地上的一餐"。
lunch::/lʌntʃ/:n. 午餐:providing free school lunches can significantly improve children's concentration and academic performance
17. engine /ˈendʒɪn/ n. 发动机；引擎；机车
📖 英文解释
A machine that converts energy into mechanical force; the driving force behind something.
✏️ 例句
Education is often described as the engine of social mobility and economic growth.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：search engine（搜索引擎）；steam engine（蒸汽机）；engine of growth（增长引擎）；engineer n./v.（工程师；策划）。
🧠 助记
engin-（聪明/发明）→ 聪明人发明的驱动装置 → 发动机。engineer（工程师）同词根，engine = 工程师制造的核心装置。
engine::/ˈendʒɪn/:n. 发动机:innovation is often described as the engine of long-term economic competitiveness
18. symbol /ˈsɪmbəl/ n. 象征；符号；标志
📖 英文解释
A thing that represents or stands for something else; a mark or character used as a sign.
✏️ 例句
The dove is a universal symbol of peace and hope across many cultures.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：symbol of sth；status symbol（身份象征）；symbolic adj.（象征性的）；symbolize v.。考研写作常用于文化分析段落。
🧠 助记
sym-（共同）+ bol-（抛出）→ 共同认可的（抛出的）代表物 → 象征、符号。sym = together，symbol = something thrown together as a sign。
symbol::/ˈsɪmbəl/:n. 象征:education is widely regarded as a symbol of hope and upward mobility for disadvantaged groups
19. earthquake /ˈɜːrθkweɪk/ n. 地震；大变动
📖 英文解释
A sudden violent shaking of the ground; a major disruption in society or politics.
✏️ 例句
The earthquake caused widespread destruction and displaced thousands of families.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：earthquake relief（地震救援）；earthquake resistant（抗震的）；a political earthquake（政治大震荡）。考研灾难/自然类阅读词汇。
🧠 助记
earth（地球）+ quake（颤抖）→ 地球在颤抖 → 地震。quake = shake violently，earth + quake = 大地颤抖 = 地震。
earthquake::/ˈɜːrθkweɪk/:n. 地震:communities that invest in earthquake-resistant infrastructure suffer far fewer casualties
20. arise /əˈraɪz/ v. 产生；出现；上升；起来
📖 英文解释
To emerge or occur; to get up; to result from a situation.
✏️ 例句
New ethical questions arise as artificial intelligence becomes more integrated into daily life.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：arise from/out of sth（由…产生）；problems/issues arise；opportunities arise。注意不规则变化：arise - arose - arisen。考研写作核心动词。
🧠 助记
a-（加强）+ rise（升起）→ 从底部升起来 → 产生、出现。rise 本身有"升起"之意，arise 更强调事物从无到有地出现。
arise::/əˈraɪz/:v. 产生:new challenges arise as societies become increasingly reliant on digital technologies
21. partly /ˈpɑːrtli/ adv. 部分地；在一定程度上
📖 英文解释
To some extent but not completely; in part.
✏️ 例句
The success of the program was partly due to strong community support.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：partly because/due to；partly…partly…（一部分…一部分…）。考研写作分析原因时常用：The problem is partly caused by…
🧠 助记
part（部分）+ -ly → 以部分的方式 → 部分地。part → partial → partly，三者都表示"部分"的意思。
partly::/ˈpɑːrtli/:adv. 部分地:academic achievement is partly determined by individual effort and partly by environmental factors
22. existence /ɪɡˈzɪstəns/ n. 存在；生存；实体
📖 英文解释
The fact or state of living or having objective reality; all that exists.
✏️ 例句
The existence of income inequality undermines equal access to quality education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：in existence（存在着）；come into existence（出现；产生）；existence of sth；existing adj.（现存的）；exist v.。考研写作核心词。
🧠 助记
ex-（向外）+ sist-（站立）+ -ence → 站立出来被看见 → 存在。exist v. → existence n.，如 insist → insistence 规律。
existence::/ɪɡˈzɪstəns/:n. 存在:acknowledging the existence of systemic barriers is the first step toward meaningful reform
23. rapid /ˈræpɪd/ adj. 快速的；迅速的；急速的
📖 英文解释
Happening in a short time; fast.
✏️ 例句
The rapid development of digital technology has transformed every aspect of modern life.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：rapid growth/change/development；rapid increase；rapidly adv.（迅速地）；at a rapid pace（以快速的步伐）。考研写作高频词。
🧠 助记
rapid 联想：rapid fire（速射）→ 像机枪一样快速射击 → 快速的。rap（快速说唱）也有快速的感觉，rapid = very fast。
rapid::/ˈræpɪd/:adj. 快速的:rapid urbanization has created both opportunities and significant social challenges in developing countries
24. willing /ˈwɪlɪŋ/ adj. 愿意的；乐意的；自愿的
📖 英文解释
Ready, eager, or prepared to do something; given or done cheerfully.
✏️ 例句
Successful learners are willing to take risks and learn from their mistakes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：be willing to do sth（愿意做…）；willing participant；willingness n.（意愿）；unwilling（不愿意的）。考研写作：show willingness to cooperate。
🧠 助记
will（意志）+ -ing → 有意志在驱动 → 愿意的。will = 意愿，willing = having the will to do sth（有意志去做）。
willing::/ˈwɪlɪŋ/:adj. 愿意的:students who are willing to seek help from teachers tend to make faster academic progress
25. curiosity /ˌkjʊəriˈɒsɪti/ n. 好奇心；求知欲；奇特的事物
📖 英文解释
A strong desire to know or learn something; something interesting and unusual.
✏️ 例句
Curiosity is one of the most powerful drivers of scientific discovery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：satisfy one's curiosity；intellectual curiosity（求知欲）；curiosity killed the cat（好奇心害死猫）；curious adj.。考研写作：cultivate curiosity in students。

curious（好奇的）+ -ity → 好奇的状态 → 好奇心。curious = full of curiosity，记住 curious 就能推出 curiosity。
curiosity::/ˌkjʊəriˈɒsɪti/:n. 好奇心:nurturing curiosity in children is essential for developing a lifelong love of learning
