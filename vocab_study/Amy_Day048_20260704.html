1. childhood /ˈtʃaɪldhud/ n. 童年；幼年时期
📖 英文解释
the period of time when someone is a child
✏️ 例句
She spent her childhood in a small village near the mountains.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
childhood memories/dream 童年记忆/梦想；early childhood 幼年；childhood friend 児时好友
🧠 助记
child（小孩）+ hood（时期）→ 小孩的时期 → 童年
childhood::/ˈtʃaɪldhud/:n. 童年:Her childhood in rural China shaped her perspective on life.
2. tie /taɪ/ v. 系；捆 n. 领带；联系；平局
📖 英文解释
to fasten or connect with a string or rope; a strip of cloth worn around the neck; a connection
✏️ 例句
Family ties kept him from moving abroad for work.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tie sb/sth to 把…系在；tie up 捆绑；tie with sb 与某人打平；blood ties 血缘纽带
🧠 助记
tie 形状像领带 → 领带要系（tie）在脖子上
tie::/taɪ/:v. 系:Strong cultural ties bind the two nations together.
3. climb /klaɪm/ v. 攀登；上升 n. 攀登；上升
📖 英文解释
to go up or towards the top of something; to rise to a higher position
✏️ 例句
She climbed the corporate ladder through hard work and determination.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
climb a mountain/ladder 爬山/梯子；climb to power 爬上权位；social climb 向上爬；a steady climb 稳步上升
🧠 助记
climb 含 limb（四肢）→ 用四肢爬 → 攀登
climb::/klaɪm/:v. 攀登:Temperatures climbed to record highs last summer.
4. net /net/ n. 网；净额 adj. 净的 v. 净赚
📖 英文解释
a material made of crossed threads with spaces between; remaining after all deductions
✏️ 例句
The company reported a net profit of two million dollars.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
net income/profit 净收入/利润；cast a net 撕网；safety net 安全网；net weight 净重
🧠 助记
net 就是网 → 像网一样交织
net::/net/:n. 网:The government's social safety net protects the most vulnerable.
5. none /nʌn/ pron. 没有一个；一点也不
📖 英文解释
not any; not one; not any part of something
✏️ 例句
None of the witnesses could identify the suspect.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
none of 没有一个；none at all 一点也没有；none other than 正是；second to none 首屈一指
🧠 助记
none = no + one → 一个也没有
none::/nʌn/:pron. 没有一个:None of the solutions proposed were satisfactory.
6. widespread /ˈwaɪdspred/ adj. 广泛的；普遍的
📖 英文解释
existing or happening in many places or among many people
✏️ 例句
There is widespread concern about the impact of climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
widespread use/support/damage 广泛使用/支持/破坏；become widespread 变得普遍；widespread belief 普遍观点
🧠 助记
wide（广）+ spread（传播）→ 广泛传播 → 普遍的
widespread::/ˈwaɪdspred/:adj. 广泛的:The drought caused widespread crop failure across the region.
7. stem /stem/ n. 茎；词干 v. 阻止；起源于
📖 英文解释
the main long thin part of a plant; to stop something from spreading; to originate from
✏️ 例句
Many social problems stem from poverty and inequality.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stem from 源于；stem the tide/flow of 遗制；stem cell 干细胞；word stem 词干
🧠 助记
stem 像 stream（溪流）→ 溪流从源头（stem from）流出
stem::/stem/:n. 茎:His confidence stems from years of careful preparation.
8. abandon /əˈbændən/ v. 放弃；遗弃 n. 放纵；纵情
📖 英文解释
to leave a person, place, or thing permanently; to give up a plan or activity
✏️ 例句
The project was abandoned due to lack of funding.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
abandon a plan/hope 放弃计划/希望；abandon oneself to 放纵于；with abandon 放纵地
🧠 助记
a + band（帮）+ on → 帮里的人跑了 → 遗弃
abandon::/əˈbændən/:v. 放弃:The government was forced to abandon its economic reform plans.
9. pound /paʊnd/ n. 英镑；磅 v. 猛击；捣碎
📖 英文解释
a unit of British currency; a unit of weight; to hit repeatedly with force
✏️ 例句
Her heart was pounding with excitement as she waited for the results.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pound sterling 英镑；pound sth to pieces 把…打碎；pound on the door 猛敲门；per pound 每磅
🧠 助记
pound 含 sound（声音）→ 猛击时发出的声音
pound::/paʊnd/:n. 英镑:The exchange rate between the pound and dollar fluctuates daily.
10. custom /ˈkʌstəm/ n. 风俗；习惯；海关 adj. 定制的
📖 英文解释
a traditional practice in a society; a habit; the official process of checking goods at borders
✏️ 例句
It is a local custom to exchange gifts during the harvest festival.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
local/social custom 当地/社会风俗；go through customs 过海关；custom-made 定制的
🧠 助记
custom → customer（顾客）→ 顾客的习惯 → 风俗习惯
custom::/ˈkʌstəm/:n. 风俗:The ancient custom of bowing has survived for centuries.
11. fee /fiː/ n. 费用；酬金；学费
📖 英文解释
an amount of money paid for a service or for professional advice
✏️ 例句
The school charges a tuition fee of five thousand dollars per semester.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
entrance/tuition fee 入场费/学费；legal fee 律师费；charge a fee 收费；fee-paying school 收费学校
🧠 助记
fee 音似“飞”→ 錢飞走了 → 付费
fee::/fiː/:n. 费用:The doctor's consultation fee was surprisingly affordable.
12. island /ˈaɪlənd/ n. 岛；岛屿；孤立的地方
📖 英文解释
a piece of land completely surrounded by water; something isolated from its surroundings
✏️ 例句
The island nation relies heavily on fishing and tourism.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
desert island 荒岛；island state 岛国；traffic island 交通安全岛；be an island 孤立
🧠 助记
is + land → 是陆地 → 四面环水的陆地 → 岛
island::/ˈaɪlənd/:n. 岛:The island's ecosystem is threatened by rising sea levels.
13. suitable /ˈsuːtəbl/ adj. 合适的；适宜的
📖 英文解释
right or appropriate for a particular purpose or situation
✏️ 例句
This film is not suitable for young children due to violent content.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
suit（适合）+ able → 能适合的 → 合适的
suitable::/ˈsuːtəbl/:adj. 合适的:We need to find a suitable location for the new factory.
14. favor /ˈfeɪvər/ n. 好感；帮助 v. 偏爱；支持
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
favor 含 favor（恩惠）→ 给予恩惠 → 偏爱支持
favor::/ˈfeɪvər/:n. 好感:Public opinion has shifted in favor of stricter gun control.
15. secure /sɪˈkjʊər/ adj. 安全的；稳固的 v. 获得；使安全
📖 英文解释
free from danger or risk; firmly fixed; to obtain something important

🧠 助记
se（分开）+ cure（治愈）→ 把危险分离治好 → 安全
secure::/sɪˈkjʊər/:adj. 安全的:They need to secure funding before the project can begin.
16. presence /ˈprezns/ n. 存在；出席；仪态；临场感
📖 英文解释
the fact of being in a place; someone's bearing or personality that attracts attention
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
present（现在/在场）+ ce → 在场状态 → 存在出席
presence::/ˈprezns/:n. 存在:Her calm presence reassured the nervous students.
17. transport /ˈtrænspɔːt/ n. 运输；交通 v. 运输；运送
📖 英文解释
a system for moving people or goods; to move something from one place to another
✏️ 例句
Public transport in the city has improved significantly in recent years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
public transport 公共交通；transport goods/people 运输货物/人员；means of transport 交通方式

transport::/ˈtrænspɔːt/:n. 运输:The government invested heavily in public transport infrastructure.
18. independent /ˌindɪˈpendənt/ adj. 独立的；不受控制的；自主的

independent of 独立于；independent thinking 独立思考；financially independent 经济独立；independent state 独立国家
🧠 助记
in（不）+ dependent（依赖）→ 不依赖的 → 独立的
independent::/ˌindɪˈpendənt/:adj. 独立的:The media should remain independent from government influence.
19. notion /ˈnəʊʃn/ n. 观念；概念；想法
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

notion::/ˈnəʊʃn/:n. 观念:The notion that success requires sacrifice is widely accepted.
20. distinguish /dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/ v. 区分；辨别；使杰出
📖 英文解释

distinguish::/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/:v. 区分:What distinguishes this university is its research output.

🧠 助记
collect（收集）+ ion → 收集的行为/结果 → 收藏
collection::/kəˈlekʃn/:n. 收藏:Her collection of rare books is valued at over a million pounds.
22. perspective /pəˈspektiv/ n. 观点；视角；透视；正确判断

per（通过）+ spect（看）→ 通过某方式看 → 视角观点
perspective::/pəˈspektiv/:n. 观点:The documentary offers a fresh perspective on climate change.

careful::/ˈkeəfl/:adj. 小心的:Careful analysis of the data revealed several important trends.

toy::/tɔɪ/:n. 玩具 v. 玩弄:He toyed with the idea of writing a novel for years.
