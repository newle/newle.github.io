1. throw /θrəʊ/ v. 扔；投；抛 n. 投掛
📖 英文解释
to send something through the air by releasing it from your hand; to cause someone confusion
✏️ 例句
The unexpected question threw him completely off balance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
throw away/out 扔掉；throw a party 举办派对；throw light on 阐明；throw oneself into 全心投入
🧠 助记
throw 与 grow（生长）压韵 → 把东西扔出去让它生长
throw::/θrəʊ/:v. 扔:The new evidence threw doubt on the original verdict.
2. ambition /æmˈbɪʃn/ n. 雄心；志向；抱负
📖 英文解释
a strong desire to achieve something or to succeed
✏️ 例句
Her ambition to become a scientist drove her to study day and night.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
have ambition/ambitions 有雄心；fulfilled ambition 实现抱负；lack ambition 缺乏上进心；political ambition 政治野心
🧠 助记
ambi（两方）+ tion → 想两边都走 → 雄心勃勃
ambition::/æmˈbɪʃn/:n. 雄心:With hard work and ambition, she rose to the top of her profession.
3. giant /ˈdʒaɪənt/ n. 巨人；巨大的事物 adj. 巨大的
📖 英文解释
an imaginary being of enormous size; an extremely large or powerful person, thing, or organization
✏️ 例句
Tech giants like Apple and Google dominate the global market.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
industry giant 行业巨头；giant step/leap 巨大跨越；a giant among 在…中的杰出人物
🧠 助记
giant 与 ant（蚂蚁）相对 → 比蚂蚁大无数倍的巨人
giant::/ˈdʒaɪənt/:n. 巨人:The merger created a new retail giant that dominated the market.
4. definition /ˌdefɪˈnɪʃn/ n. 定义；清晰度；界定
📖 英文解释
a statement explaining the exact meaning of a word or concept; the degree of clarity in an image
✏️ 例句
The dictionary provides a clear definition of every word.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
by definition 根据定义；give a definition of 给出定义；strict/broad definition 严格/宽泛定义；high-definition 高清晰度
🧠 助记
define（定义）+ tion → 定义的行为/结果
definition::/ˌdefɪˈnɪʃn/:n. 定义:The law's definition of terrorism has been widely criticized as too broad.

🧠 助记
high（高）→ height → 高度
height::/haɪt/:n. 高度:The crisis was at its height in the summer of that year.
6. reject /rɪˈdʒekt/ v. 拒绝；否决；抛弃 n. 被拒绝的人/物
