1. pursue /pəˈsjuː/ v. 追求；追逐；从事；继续
📖 英文解释
to follow someone or something in order to catch them; to continue with a plan or activity
✏️ 例句
She decided to pursue a career in medicine after graduating.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pursue a career/goal/dream 追求事业/目标/梦想；pursue sb 追逐某人；pursue a matter 继续处理某事；actively pursue 积极追求
🧠 助记
pur（向前）+ sue（追）→ 一直向前追 → 追求
pursue::/pəˈsjuː/:v. 追求:The government intends to pursue economic reforms despite opposition.
2. complicated /ˈkɒmplɪkeɪtɪd/ adj. 错综复杂的；复杂的；难理解的
📖 英文解释
consisting of many different parts; difficult to understand or deal with
✏️ 例句
The legal situation is far more complicated than it first appeared.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
complicated process/relationship 复杂的过程/关系；overly complicated 过于复杂；it's complicated 关系复杂；complicate matters 使情况复杂
🧠 助记
complex（复杂）→ complicated → 使复杂
complicated::/ˈkɒmplɪkeɪtɪd/:adj. 错综复杂的:The negotiations were made more complicated by the political tensions.
3. bracket /ˈbrækɪt/ n. 括号；支架；范围 v. 括起来；归为一类
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🧠 助记
brac（手臂）+ ket → 手臂托起 → 支架
bracket::/ˈbrækɪt/:n. 括号:The tax changes benefit those in the middle income bracket.

solar::/ˈsəʊlər/:adj. 太阳的:The country plans to generate 50% of its electricity from solar power by 2030.

duty::/ˈdjuːti/:n. 职责:Nurses have a professional duty of care toward all patients.
