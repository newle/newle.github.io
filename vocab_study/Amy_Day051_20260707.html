1. retire /rɪˈtaɪər/ v. 退休；退役；撤退
📖 英文解释
to leave a job and stop working, usually because of age; to withdraw
✏️ 例句
She plans to retire at 60 and move to the countryside.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
retire from 从…退休；retirement age 退休年龄；retired soldier 退伍军人
🧠 助记
re-tire，tire（疲倦），反复疲倦后就'退休'了，retire = 退休
retire::/rɪˈtaɪər/:v. 退休:He retired after 35 years of service.
2. bed /bed/ n. 床；花坛；河床
📖 英文解释
a piece of furniture for sleeping on; a flat area of ground for growing plants
✏️ 例句
The river bed was completely dry during the drought.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
go to bed 上床睡觉；bed and breakfast 床位加早餐；flower bed 花坛；riverbed 河床
🧠 助记
bed形状像个字母b和d夹着e，'be sleeping'的简写，就是'床'
bed::/bed/:n. 床:She went to bed early after the long journey.
3. daughter /ˈdɔːtər/ n. 女儿
📖 英文解释
a female child in relation to her parents
✏️ 例句
Their youngest daughter just started university this year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
eldest daughter 长女；daughter-in-law 儿媳；stepdaughter 继女
🧠 助记
daugh-ter，联想'蛋糕ter'，女儿是父母眼中像蛋糕一样甜蜜的存在
daughter::/ˈdɔːtər/:n. 女儿:He walked his daughter down the aisle at her wedding.
4. substance /ˈsʌbstəns/ n. 物质；实质；要旨
📖 英文解释
a type of matter with particular properties; the real or important part of something
✏️ 例句
The substance of his argument was difficult to challenge.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
substance abuse 药物滥用；in substance 实质上；toxic substance 有毒物质
🧠 助记
sub（下面）+ stance（站立），'站在下面支撑'的就是'物质/实质'
substance::/ˈsʌbstəns/:n. 物质:The report lacks substance and concrete evidence.
5. clone /kləʊn/ n./v. 克隆；复制
📖 英文解释
an organism that is genetically identical to another; to make a copy of
✏️ 例句
Scientists successfully cloned a sheep named Dolly in 1996.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
clone a gene 克隆基因；human cloning 人类克隆；clone technology 克隆技术
🧠 助记
clone谐音'克隆'，这个词的中文翻译就是音译，直接记 clone = 克隆
clone::/kləʊn/:n./v. 克隆:Cloning technology has advanced significantly in recent years.
6. commission /kəˈmɪʃn/ n. 委员会；佣金；委托；v. 委托
📖 英文解释
a group of people with an official duty; a payment for completing a sale; to formally ask someone to do something
✏️ 例句
The artist was commissioned to paint a mural for the new library.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sales commission 销售佣金；commission a report 委托撰写报告；out of commission 停止运转
🧠 助记
com（共同）+ mission（任务），'共同任务'就是'委员会'的职责，commission = 委托/委员会
commission::/kəˈmɪʃn/:n. 委员会:The government set up a commission to investigate the scandal.
7. settle /ˈsetl/ v. 解决；定居；安定；支付
📖 英文解释
to end a dispute; to establish a home in a place; to become calm
✏️ 例句
The two sides finally settled their differences out of court.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
settle down 定居；settle a dispute 解决争端；settle for 凑合接受；settle in 适应新环境
🧠 助记
settle联想'安置(set)'，'安置'好了就'定居/解决'了，settle = 定居/解决
settle::/ˈsetl/:v. 解决:The company settled the lawsuit for $2 million.
8. insist /ɪnˈsɪst/ v. 坚持；坚决要求
📖 英文解释
to demand something forcefully; to maintain that something is true despite opposition
✏️ 例句
She insisted on paying for dinner despite our protests.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
insist on/upon 坚持；insist that 坚持认为；insist on doing 坚持做某事
🧠 助记
in-sist，sist = assist（协助），不断'协助'自己的立场就是'坚持'
insist::/ɪnˈsɪst/:v. 坚持:He insisted that he had nothing to do with the crime.
9. editor /ˈedɪtər/ n. 编辑；主编
📖 英文解释
a person who prepares written material for publication; a person in charge of a newspaper
✏️ 例句
The editor rejected the manuscript because it needed major revisions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
chief editor 主编；editor-in-chief 总编辑；photo editor 图片编辑
🧠 助记
edit（编辑）+ or（人），'做编辑工作的人'就是'editor'，逻辑简单
editor::/ˈedɪtər/:n. 编辑:The editor suggested several changes to improve the article.
10. scholar /ˈskɒlər/ n. 学者；奖学金获得者
📖 英文解释
a person who studies a subject thoroughly; a student who has been awarded a scholarship
✏️ 例句
He is a respected scholar in the field of medieval history.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
scholar ship 奖学金；visiting scholar 访问学者；scholar debate 学术讨论
🧠 助记
school（学校）→ scholar（学者），在学校里深耕的人就是'学者'
scholar::/ˈskɒlər/:n. 学者:The scholar published several influential papers on the topic.
11. institute /ˈɪnstɪtjuːt/ n. 研究所；学院；v. 建立；制定
📖 英文解释
an organization for the promotion of science or education; to establish or introduce
✏️ 例句
The institute conducts research on climate change and environmental policy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
research institute 研究所；institute a policy 制定政策；institute proceedings 提起诉讼
🧠 助记
insti-tute，联想'institution（机构）'的核心，institute = 建立机构/研究所
institute::/ˈɪnstɪtjuːt/:n. 研究所:The government instituted new regulations to protect consumers.
12. unusual /ʌnˈjuːʒuəl/ adj. 不寻常的；罕见的
📖 英文解释
different from what is usual, expected, or normal
✏️ 例句
It is unusual to see snow in this region in late spring.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
it is unusual for sb to do 某人做某事是不寻常的；unusual circumstances 异常情况
🧠 助记
un（不）+ usual（通常的），'不通常的'就是'不寻常的'，前缀法
unusual::/ʌnˈjuːʒuəl/:adj. 不寻常的:She has an unusual talent for languages.
13. rare /reər/ adj. 罕见的；稀有的；半熟的
📖 英文解释
not common; very unusual; (of meat) only slightly cooked
✏️ 例句
The museum houses a collection of rare artifacts from ancient civilizations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rare species 稀有物种；rare opportunity 难得的机会；rare earth 稀土；rare metal 稀有金属
🧠 助记
rare谐音'热儿'，'热得出奇'就说明稀罕，rare = 罕见的；牛排'稀罕地不熟' = 半熟
rare::/reər/:adj. 罕见的:The snow leopard is one of the world's rarest animals.
14. fun /fʌn/ n. 乐趣；adj. 有趣的

🧠 助记
fun谐音'凡'，不平凡的事情才有乐趣，fun = 乐趣/有趣
fun::/fʌn/:n. 乐趣:The children had a lot of fun at the party.

16. saving /ˈseɪvɪŋ/ n. 储蓄；节省；adj. 节约的

saving::/ˈseɪvɪŋ/:n. 储蓄:They used their savings to start a small business.
17. quiet /ˈkwaɪət/ adj. 安静的；沉默的；n. 宁静

quiet::/ˈkwaɪət/:adj. 安静的:Please keep quiet in the examination hall.
18. cognitive /ˈkɒɡnətɪv/ adj. 认知的；认识的
📖 英文解释
relating to the mental process of acquiring knowledge through thought and experience
✏️ 例句
Early childhood education greatly influences cognitive development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

cognitive::/ˈkɒɡnətɪv/:adj. 认知的:Regular exercise has been shown to improve cognitive function.

smell::/smel/:v. 闻:The flowers smell wonderful in the morning.

23. wisdom /ˈwɪzdəm/ n. 智慧；明智；名言

wisdom::/ˈwɪzdəm/:n. 智慧:She had the wisdom to know when to ask for help.
