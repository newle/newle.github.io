1. symptom /ˈsɪmptəm/ n. 症状；征兆；表征
📖 英文解释
a physical or mental sign indicating the presence of a disease; an indication of a problem
✏️ 例句
Fever and cough are common symptoms of the flu.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
withdrawal symptom 戒断症状；symptom of a problem 问题的征兆；show symptoms 显示症状
🧠 助记
symp（同情）+ tom，联想Tommy生病了大家同情他，symptom = 症状
symptom::/ˈsɪmptəm/:n. 症状:Early symptoms of the disease include fatigue and loss of appetite.
2. indifferent /ɪnˈdɪfrənt/ adj. 漠不关心的；无动于衷的；一般的
📖 英文解释
showing no particular interest or sympathy; not especially good or bad
✏️ 例句
She seemed indifferent to the suffering of those around her.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be indifferent to 对…漠不关心；indifferent attitude 漠然态度；indifferent quality 一般的质量
🧠 助记
in（不）+ different（不同的），'没有不同感受'就是'无动于衷'，indifferent = 漠不关心
indifferent::/ɪnˈdɪfrənt/:adj. 漠不关心的:The government appeared indifferent to the needs of the poorest citizens.
3. neglect /nɪˈɡlekt/ v. 忽视；疏忽；n. 疏忽；忽视
📖 英文解释
to fail to give proper care or attention to; failure to take proper care
✏️ 例句
Neglecting regular maintenance can lead to serious equipment failures.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
neglect one's duty 忽视职责；child neglect 儿童疏忽；criminal neglect 严重过失
🧠 助记
neg（否定）+ lect（选择/读），'不选择去关注'就是'忽视'，neglect = 忽视
neglect::/nɪˈɡlekt/:v. 忽视:She neglected her health for years before seeking medical help.
4. transform /trænsˈfɔːm/ v. 改变；变革；转化
📖 英文解释
to make a thorough or dramatic change in form, appearance, or character
✏️ 例句
Technology has transformed the way we live and work.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
transform into 转变为；transform society 改变社会；digital transformation 数字化转型
🧠 助记
trans（跨越）+ form（形式），'跨越原有形式'就是'转变/改变'，transform = 变革
transform::/trænsˈfɔːm/:v. 改变:Education can transform lives and break the cycle of poverty.
5. bright /braɪt/ adj. 明亮的；聪明的；鲜艳的；有前途的
📖 英文解释
giving out or reflecting strong light; intelligent and quick-witted; vivid in color
✏️ 例句
She has a bright future ahead of her with her talent and determination.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
bright idea 好主意；bright future 光明前途；bright color 鲜艳颜色；look on the bright side 往好的方面想
🧠 助记
bright联想'布莱特'，一个'光明/聪明'的人名，bright = 明亮的/聪明的
bright::/braɪt/:adj. 明亮的:The bright student quickly grasped all the complex concepts.
6. recovery /rɪˈkʌvəri/ n. 恢复；复原；追回
📖 英文解释
a return to a normal state of health or strength; the action of regaining possession
✏️ 例句
The patient made a full recovery after the surgery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
economic recovery 经济复苏；recovery plan 恢复计划；in recovery 在康复中
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ cover（覆盖/覆盖好），再次'覆盖好'就是'恢复'，recovery = 恢复
recovery::/rɪˈkʌvəri/:n. 恢复:The country's economic recovery has been slower than expected.
7. reputation /ˌrepjuˈteɪʃn/ n. 声誉；名声；名誉
📖 英文解释
the beliefs or opinions that are generally held about someone or something
✏️ 例句
The company has built a solid reputation for quality and reliability.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
good/bad reputation 好/坏名声；reputation for 以…著称；damage one's reputation 损害名誉
🧠 助记
repute（名誉）+ ation，repute的名词化，reputation = 名声/声誉
reputation::/ˌrepjuˈteɪʃn/:n. 声誉:A scandal like this could seriously damage the politician's reputation.
8. fault /fɔːlt/ n. 错误；缺点；过失；断层；v. 批评
📖 英文解释
a mistake; a weakness in something; responsibility for a problem
✏️ 例句
It was my fault for not double-checking the figures before submission.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
at fault 有责任；fault line 断层线；find fault with 挑剔；to a fault 过分地
🧠 助记
fault谐音'福尔特'，联想犯了'错误'后要自我惩罚，fault = 错误/过失
fault::/fɔːlt/:n. 错误:The accident was entirely the driver's fault.
9. inflation /ɪnˈfleɪʃn/ n. 通货膨胀；膨胀
📖 英文解释
a general increase in prices and a fall in the purchasing value of money
✏️ 例句
High inflation erodes the purchasing power of ordinary consumers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
inflation rate 通货膨胀率；control inflation 控制通胀；inflation target 通胀目标
🧠 助记
inflate（使膨胀）+ tion，'使价格膨胀'就是'通货膨胀'，inflation = 通胀
inflation::/ɪnˈfleɪʃn/:n. 通货膨胀:The central bank raised interest rates to combat rising inflation.
10. atmosphere /ˈætməsfɪər/ n. 大气层；气氛；氛围
📖 英文解释
the envelope of gases surrounding the Earth; the general mood or feeling of a place
✏️ 例句
The restaurant had a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
create an atmosphere 营造氛围；tense atmosphere 紧张气氛；atmosphere of trust 信任氛围
🧠 助记
atmo（蒸气）+ sphere（球），'蒸气球'包围地球就是'大气层'，也指'氛围'
atmosphere::/ˈætməsfɪər/:n. 大气层:The new policies created a positive atmosphere in the workplace.
11. religion /rɪˈlɪdʒən/ n. 宗教；宗教信仰

🧠 助记
reli（依赖）+ gion，'依赖'某种信仰就是'宗教'，religion = 宗教
religion::/rɪˈlɪdʒən/:n. 宗教:The country's constitution guarantees freedom of religion.
12. root /ruːt/ n. 根；根源；词根；v. 生根；扎根
📖 英文解释
the part of a plant that grows underground; the source or origin of something
✏️ 例句
The root causes of poverty are complex and multifaceted.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
root cause 根本原因；take root 生根；root out 根除；grass roots 基层；square root 平方根
🧠 助记
root谐音'路特'，树根是树的'路特'（路和特征），root = 根/根源
root::/ruːt/:n. 根:We must address the root causes of crime, not just the symptoms.

14. deny /dɪˈnaɪ/ v. 否认；拒绝；否定

15. assess /əˈses/ v. 评估；评价；评定

assess::/əˈses/:v. 评估:The committee will assess the environmental impact of the proposed project.
