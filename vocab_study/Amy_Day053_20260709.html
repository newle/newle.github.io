1. pose /pəʊz/ v. 摆姿势；造成；提出；n. 姿势；伪装
📖 英文解释
to present or cause a problem; to put yourself in a particular position; to pretend to be
✏️ 例句
Climate change poses a serious threat to biodiversity worldwide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pose a threat 构成威胁；pose a question 提出问题；strike a pose 摆姿势
🧠 助记
pose谐音'破斯'，'破（提出）斯（这个）'问题，pose = 提出/摆姿势
pose::/pəʊz/:v. 摆姿势:The new regulation poses challenges for small businesses.
2. chair /tʃeər/ n. 椅子；主席；v. 主持（会议）
📖 英文解释
a separate seat for one person; the person in charge of a meeting; to be in charge of a meeting
✏️ 例句
She was elected chair of the board of directors last month.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
take the chair 主持会议；chair a meeting 主持会议；wheelchair 轮椅
🧠 助记
chair谐音'切儿'，切开来坐的就是'椅子'，主持会议时坐主位，chair = 椅子/主席
chair::/tʃeər/:n. 椅子:He was asked to chair the committee on education reform.
3. beautiful /ˈbjuːtɪfl/ adj. 美好的；美丽的；出色的
📖 英文解释
pleasing the senses or mind aesthetically; of a very high standard
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

beautiful::/ˈbjuːtɪfl/:adj. 美好的:She gave a beautiful performance that moved everyone in the audience.
4. organic /ɔːˈɡænɪk/ adj. 有机的；有机物的；自然发展的

🧠 助记

organic::/ɔːˈɡænɪk/:adj. 有机的:The company achieved organic growth by investing in research and development.
5. vital /ˈvaɪtl/ adj. 至关重要的；生死攸关的；充满活力的
