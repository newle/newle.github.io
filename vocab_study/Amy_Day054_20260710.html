1. stimulate /ˈstɪmjʊleɪt/ v. 刺激；激励；促进
📖 英文解释
To raise levels of physiological or nervous activity; to encourage interest or activity in a positive direction.
✏️ 例句
Tax incentives were introduced to stimulate investment in the tech sector.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stimulate economic growth 刺激经济增长；stimulate interest 激发兴趣；考研经济高频
🧠 助记
stimul（刺激）+ate = 给予刺激
stimulate::/ˈstɪmjʊleɪt/:v. 刺激:stimulate economic growth through policy
2. arrange /əˈreɪndʒ/ v. 安排；整理；排列；改编
📖 英文解释
To put things in a neat organized way; to plan or prepare something; to organize an event or meeting.
✏️ 例句
The conference committee arranged a meeting between the two leaders.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
arrange for sb to do 安排某人做；arrange in order 按顺序排列；arrangement n.安排
🧠 助记
ar+range（范围）= 在一定范围内排好 = 安排
arrange::/əˈreɪndʒ/:v. 安排:arrange a meeting between senior officials
3. faculty /ˈfækəlti/ n. 能力；才能；（大学）全体教员；学院
📖 英文解释
A natural ability to do something; the teaching staff of a university; a division of a university.
✏️ 例句
The university's faculty includes several Nobel Prize winners.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
faculty member 教职人员；faculty of law 法学院；mental faculty 心智能力
🧠 助记
fact（事实）+ulty -> 有实力处理事实的能力
faculty::/ˈfækəlti/:n. 能力:the university faculty contributes to research
4. praise /preɪz/ v./n. 表扬；赞美；称赞
📖 英文解释
To express warm approval or admiration of; to commend highly; to worship or glorify.
✏️ 例句
The teacher praised the students for their outstanding performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
praise for 因...受到表扬；sing the praises of 大力称赞；worthy of praise 值得称赞
🧠 助记
praise 发音像 pu-lei-zi，普通事也能雷（惊）世，值得赞美
praise::/preɪz/:v./n. 表扬:receive widespread praise for innovative work
5. volunteer /ˌvɒlənˈtɪər/ n. 志愿者；v. 自愿做；主动提供
📖 英文解释
A person who freely offers to take part in an enterprise or undertake a task without payment.
✏️ 例句
Thousands of volunteers helped clean up after the earthquake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
volunteer for 自愿参加；volunteer service 志愿服务；考研公益类常考
🧠 助记
volunt（自愿，源于will）+eer（人）= 自愿的人
volunteer::/ˌvɒlənˈtɪər/:n. 志愿者:volunteer to help in community service
6. stone /stəʊn/ n. 石头；宝石；v. 用石头砸；adj. 石制的
📖 英文解释
The hard solid nonmetallic mineral matter forming part of the earth; a small piece of rock; a gem.
✏️ 例句
The ancient wall was built using stones from the local river.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stone cold 冷得像石头；stepping stone 踏脚石（喻为跳板）；leave no stone unturned 不遗余力
🧠 助记
stone 像 shi-tong（石通），石头做的通道
stone::/stəʊn/:n. 石头:use as a stepping stone to greater success
7. chapter /ˈtʃæptər/ n. 章节；时期；（组织的）分会
📖 英文解释
A main division of a book; a period of time in history or in someone's life; a local branch of an organization.
✏️ 例句
This discovery opened a new chapter in the history of medicine.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
open a new chapter 开启新篇章；chapter by chapter 逐章；考研文章结构类常用
🧠 助记
chap（家伙）+ter -> 每个家伙都有自己的一章（故事）
chapter::/ˈtʃæptər/:n. 章节:open a new chapter in international relations
8. meanwhile /ˈmiːnwaɪl/ adv. 与此同时；在这期间
📖 英文解释
In the period between two events or while something else is happening; at the same time.
✏️ 例句
The government promised to create new jobs; meanwhile, unemployment rates continued to rise.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
meanwhile 常用于转折或对比；in the meanwhile 在此期间；考研逻辑连词高频
🧠 助记
mean（中间）+while（时候）= 中间时候 = 与此同时
meanwhile::/ˈmiːnwaɪl/:adv. 与此同时:meanwhile social inequality continues to grow
9. perceive /pəˈsiːv/ v. 感知；察觉；认为；理解
📖 英文解释
To become aware of something through the senses; to regard or understand in a particular way.
✏️ 例句
How we perceive risk affects our decision-making process.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
perceive as 把...视为；be perceived to 被认为；perception n. 感知
🧠 助记
per（全面）+ceive（接受，如 receive）= 全面接收 = 感知
perceive::/pəˈsiːv/:v. 感知:perceive the issue from a different perspective
10. merely /ˈmɪərli/ adv. 仅仅；只不过；不过
📖 英文解释
Only; nothing more than; just; used to emphasize how small or unimportant something is.
✏️ 例句
The problem is not merely economic but also social and cultural.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
not merely...but also 不仅...而且；merely a coincidence 只不过是巧合
🧠 助记
mere（仅仅的）+ly = 副词 = 仅仅
merely::/ˈmɪərli/:adv. 仅仅:not merely an economic problem but a social one
11. absorb /əbˈzɔːrb/ v. 吸收；消化（知识）；使全神贯注
📖 英文解释
To take in or soak up energy or a substance; to assimilate knowledge; to engross attention completely.
✏️ 例句
The new policy aims to absorb excess labor from rural areas into urban industries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
absorb knowledge 吸收知识；absorb shock 吸收冲击；be absorbed in 专注于
🧠 助记
ab+sorb（喝），像海绵一样吸（sorb）收
absorb::/əbˈzɔːrb/:v. 吸收:absorb surplus labor into the urban workforce
12. belong /bɪˈlɒŋ/ v. 属于；归属；适合于
📖 英文解释
To be the property of; to be a member of a group; to be in the right place or right situation.
✏️ 例句
The mineral rights belong to whoever owns the land.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
belong to 属于；sense of belonging 归属感；where do you belong?
🧠 助记
be+long（长期）= 长期在某处 = 属于某处
belong::/bɪˈlɒŋ/:v. 属于:develop a sense of belonging to the community
13. weak /wiːk/ adj. 弱的；虚弱的；微弱的；不坚定的
📖 英文解释
Lacking physical strength; not having enough power or influence; not convincing or effective.
✏️ 例句
Weak consumer demand has slowed economic growth considerably.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
weak economy 经济疲软；weak argument 论点无力；weaken v.削弱
🧠 助记
weak 发音像 wei-ke（威客），一个威客不够强大，比较弱
weak::/wiːk/:adj. 弱的:a weak argument fails to convince the reader
14. affair /əˈfeər/ n. 事务；事情；事件；（私人）情事
📖 英文解释
An event or series of events; a matter of concern; a romantic or sexual relationship outside marriage.
✏️ 例句
Foreign affairs require careful diplomatic handling.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
foreign affairs 外交事务；current affairs 时事；affair of state 国事
🧠 助记
af（=ad，去）+fair（集会）= 去集会 = 处理事务
affair::/əˈfeər/:n. 事务:handle foreign affairs with diplomatic skill
15. undergraduate /ˌʌndərˈɡrædʒuət/ n./adj. 本科生；大学生的
📖 英文解释
A student studying for a first degree at a university; relating to such a student or their studies.
✏️ 例句
The university offers undergraduate programs in over 50 disciplines.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
undergraduate student 本科生；undergraduate thesis 本科论文；考研教育类常考
🧠 助记
under（在下面）+graduate（毕业生）= 还没毕业的下级 = 本科生
undergraduate::/ˌʌndərˈɡrædʒuət/:n./adj. 本科生:undergraduate research opportunities in science
16. assign /əˈsaɪn/ v. 分配；指派；布置（作业）
📖 英文解释
To allocate a job or duty to someone; to designate or set apart something for a specific purpose.
✏️ 例句
The professor assigned a research paper to be submitted by the end of the semester.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
assign a task 分派任务；assign to 分配给；assignment n.任务/作业
🧠 助记
as+sign（签名）= 签名分配 = 指派
assign::/əˈsaɪn/:v. 分配:assign responsibilities to each team member
17. mission /ˈmɪʃn/ n. 使命；任务；代表团；传教
📖 英文解释
An important assignment carried out for a government or organization; a strongly felt aim or calling.
✏️ 例句
The organization's mission is to eliminate poverty in developing countries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
mission statement 使命宣言；on a mission 执行任务；diplomatic mission 外交使团
🧠 助记
miss（发送，如 missile 导弹）+ion = 被发送出去执行的 = 使命/任务
mission::/ˈmɪʃn/:n. 使命:fulfill the mission of promoting social equality
18. joy /dʒɔɪ/ n. 喜悦；快乐；令人愉快的事

joy of life 生活的乐趣；for joy 因高兴；joyful adj.快乐的

joy 发音像 jiu（揪），揪出内心快乐的感觉
joy::/dʒɔɪ/:n. 喜悦:experience the joy of learning and discovery

A main road for public travel; a direct path or route to something desirable.

The new highway reduced travel time between the two cities by half.

highway construction 高速公路建设；information highway 信息高速路；highway to success 成功之路

high（高）+way（路）= 高级道路 = 高速公路
highway::/ˈhaɪweɪ/:n. 高速公路:build a highway to stimulate regional development

academic publication 学术出版物；publication date 出版日期；考研学术类高频

public（公开）+ation = 公开发行 = 出版
publication::/ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃn/:n. 出版:the publication of a landmark scientific paper

write poetry 写诗；poetry recitation 诗歌朗诵；prose vs poetry

poetry::/ˈpəʊɪtri/:n. 诗歌:Tang poetry represents the peak of classical literature
22. usual /ˈjuːʒuəl/ adj. 通常的；惯常的；平常的
📖 英文解释
Habitually or typically occurring or done; customary; what normally happens.
✏️ 例句
As usual, the report was submitted late.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
as usual 照常；out of the usual 不寻常；unusual adj.不寻常的
🧠 助记
us（我们）+ual = 我们都这样做 = 通常的
usual::/ˈjuːʒuəl/:adj. 通常的:follow the usual procedure for approval
23. necessity /nəˈsesɪti/ n. 必要性；必需品；需要
📖 英文解释
The fact of being required or indispensable; something that cannot be done without.
✏️ 例句
Access to clean water is a basic necessity for all human beings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
out of necessity 出于需要；a necessity of life 生活必需品；necessitate v.使成必要
🧠 助记
neces（需要，=need）+sity = 需要的东西 = 必需品
necessity::/nəˈsesɪti/:n. 必要性:address the necessity of educational reform
24. adjust /əˈdʒʌst/ v. 调整；适应；校准
📖 英文解释
To alter or move something slightly in order to achieve the desired fit or result; to adapt to new conditions.
✏️ 例句
Companies must adjust their strategies to keep pace with changing market conditions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
adjust to 适应；adjust for 校准；make adjustments 做出调整
🧠 助记
ad+just（正好）= 调到正好 = 调整
adjust::/əˈdʒʌst/:v. 调整:adjust policies to meet changing economic needs
25. announce /əˈnaʊns/ v. 宣布；宣告；通知
📖 英文解释
To make a public and typically formal declaration about a fact, occurrence, or intention.
✏️ 例句
The government announced new measures to combat inflation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
announce a decision 宣布决定；announcement n.通知；publicly announce 公开宣布
🧠 助记
an+nounce（讲，sound）= 向所有人讲 = 宣布
announce::/əˈnaʊns/:v. 宣布:announce new measures to address the crisis
