1. exposure /ɪkˈspəʊʒər/ n. 暴露；曝光；接触；揭露
📖 英文解释
The state of being exposed to contact with something; the revelation of an identity or fact; media coverage.
✏️ 例句
Early exposure to diverse cultures broadens children's understanding of the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
exposure to 接触/暴露于；media exposure 媒体曝光；exposure of corruption 揭露腐败
🧠 助记
ex（外）+posure（放置）= 放到外面 = 暴露
exposure::/ɪkˈspəʊʒər/:n. 暴露:early exposure to science shapes future career choices
2. acknowledge /əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/ v. 承认；致谢；告知收到
📖 英文解释
To accept or admit the existence or truth of something; to express gratitude for or recognition of.
✏️ 例句
The government acknowledged the need for urgent reform in the healthcare system.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
acknowledge that 承认...；acknowledge sb's contribution 致谢某人贡献；考研高频动词
🧠 助记
ac+knowledge（知识）= 接受已有的知识 = 承认
acknowledge::/əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/:v. 承认:acknowledge the limitations of current policy
3. imagination /ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃn/ n. 想象；想象力；创造力
📖 英文解释
The faculty of forming new ideas, images, or concepts not present to the senses; creativity.
✏️ 例句
Imagination is as important as knowledge in solving complex social problems.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
use imagination 发挥想象力；beyond imagination 超乎想象；fire the imagination 激发想象力
🧠 助记
imagine（想象）+ation = 名词形式 = 想象力
imagination::/ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃn/:n. 想象:fire the imagination of young scientists
4. locate /ləʊˈkeɪt/ v. 查找；定位；确定位置；设立于
📖 英文解释
To discover the exact place or position of something; to establish in a particular place.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
locate a problem 找出问题；be located in 位于...；locate resources 找到资源
🧠 助记
loc（地方，如 location）+ate = 确定地方 = 定位
locate::/ləʊˈkeɪt/:v. 查找:locate the source of social inequality
5. incentive /ɪnˈsentɪv/ n. 激励；动机；奖励；adj. 激励的
📖 英文解释
A thing that motivates or encourages someone to do something; a payment or concession to stimulate action.
✏️ 例句
The government provided tax incentives to attract foreign investment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
financial incentive 经济激励；provide incentive 提供激励；incentive scheme 激励方案
🧠 助记
in+cent（分，货币）+ive = 用小钱激励 = 激励/奖励
incentive::/ɪnˈsentɪv/:n. 激励:provide tax incentives to attract investment
6. victim /ˈvɪktɪm/ n. 受害者；牺牲品；受害人
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

victim::/ˈvɪktɪm/:n. 受害者:protect victims of social discrimination

airline::/ˈeəlaɪn/:n. 航空公司:the airline industry faces pressure to cut emissions

confident::/ˈkɒnfɪdənt/:adj. 自信的:be confident about achieving the research objectives
