1. somewhat /ˈsʌmwɒt/ adv. 有些；稍微；某种程度上
📖 英文解释
To a moderate extent or by a moderate amount; rather; a little.
✏️ 例句
The results were somewhat disappointing, though improvements were noted in some areas.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
somewhat different 有些不同；somewhat surprising 有点出人意料；考研程度副词
🧠 助记
some（一些）+what（什么）= 某种程度的什么 = 有些
somewhat::/ˈsʌmwɒt/:adv. 有些:the outcome was somewhat better than expected
2. skilled /skɪld/ adj. 熟练的；有技能的；需要技能的
📖 英文解释
Having or showing the knowledge, ability, or training to perform a certain activity; expert.
✏️ 例句
There is a growing demand for skilled workers in the technology sector.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
skilled worker 技术工人；skilled in 擅长于；highly skilled 高技能的
🧠 助记
skill（技能）+ed = 拥有技能的 = 熟练的
skilled::/skɪld/:adj. 熟练的:attract skilled workers to drive innovation
3. appreciate /əˈpriːʃieɪt/ v. 欣赏；感激；理解；增值
📖 英文解释
To recognize the full worth of; to be grateful for; to understand a situation fully; to increase in value.
✏️ 例句
It is important to appreciate the complexity of social problems before proposing solutions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
appreciate the importance of 认识到...的重要性；deeply appreciate 深深感谢；appreciation n.
🧠 助记
appre（向前）+ci（=see 看）+ate = 向前看全面认识 = 欣赏/理解
appreciate::/əˈpriːʃieɪt/:v. 欣赏:appreciate the importance of cultural diversity

entertainment industry 娱乐行业；for entertainment 为了娱乐；mass entertainment 大众娱乐
🧠 助记
entertain（娱乐）+ment = 名词 = 娱乐/娱乐活动
entertainment::/ˌentəˈteɪnmənt/:n. 娱乐:the entertainment industry shapes cultural attitudes

nobody::/ˈnəʊbɒdi/:pron. 没有人:nobody should be left behind in social progress

satisfaction::/ˌsætɪsˈfækʃn/:n. 满意:increase job satisfaction to boost productivity
