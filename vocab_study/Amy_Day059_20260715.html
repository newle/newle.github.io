1. confront /kənˈfrʌnt/ v. 使面临；勇敢地面对；对抗
📖 英文解释
to face up to and deal with a difficult situation; to meet face to face in opposition
✏️ 例句
Leaders must be willing to confront difficult truths to make meaningful progress.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
confront a challenge/problem 面对挑战/问题；confront sb. with 用证据面质某人；be confronted with 面临
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ front（前面）→ 面对面 → 使面临、对抗
confront::/kənˈfrʌnt/:v. 使面临:confront::kənˈfrʌnt:面对/对抗:confront the challenge
