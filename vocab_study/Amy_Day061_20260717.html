1. dress /dres/ v. 穿衣 n. 连衣裙；服装
📖 英文解释
To put on clothes; a woman's garment that covers the body and extends down over the legs.
✏️ 例句
How we dress can reflect our cultural identity and social status.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dress up（打扮）；dress code（着装规定）；well-dressed（穿着得体）；考研文化类文章常见
🧠 助记
dress → d + ress（打扮起来像公主）→ 穿衣
dress::/dres/:v. 穿衣 n. 连衣裙:Many companies have relaxed their dress code in recent years.
2. vacation /veɪˈkeɪʃn/ n. 假期；休假；度假
📖 英文解释
An extended period of leisure and recreation, especially one spent away from home.
✏️ 例句
Taking a vacation improves productivity and mental health in the long run.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
on vacation（在度假）；summer vacation（暑假）；= holiday（英式）；考研写作休闲生活类词汇
🧠 助记
vac-ation → vacant（空的）+ ation → 把时间空出来 → 假期
vacation::/veɪˈkeɪʃn/:n. 假期:Paid vacation is considered a basic employee right in many countries.
3. reasonable /ˈriːznəbl/ adj. 合理的；通情达理的；公道的
📖 英文解释
Having sound judgment; not excessive or extreme; fair and sensible.
✏️ 例句
A reasonable salary increase would help retain talented employees.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reasonable price（合理价格）；within reasonable limits（在合理范围内）；reasonably adv. 合理地
🧠 助记
reason-able → 有 reason（理性）的能力 → 合理的
reasonable::/ˈriːznəbl/:adj. 合理的:It is reasonable to expect transparency from public institutions.
4. odd /ɒd/ adj. 奇数的；奇怪的；偶尔的
📖 英文解释
Not even in number; unusual or different from what is expected; occasional.
✏️ 例句
It struck her as odd that no one had mentioned the change in plans.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
odd number（奇数）；odd behavior（奇怪行为）；odds n. 可能性；against all odds（克服重重困难）
🧠 助记
odd → o-d-d → 奇特，拼写也'奇怪'（两个d）
odd::/ɒd/:adj. 奇数的:It seems odd that the report omitted such critical findings.
5. significance /sɪɡˈnɪfɪkəns/ n. 重要性；含义；意义
📖 英文解释
The quality of being worthy of attention; meaning conveyed by something.
✏️ 例句
The significance of the discovery cannot be overstated in the history of science.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
of great/little significance（非常/不太重要）；significant adj. 重要的；significantly adv. 显著地
🧠 助记
signif-icance → sign（符号/标志）→ 值得标注的重要性
significance::/sɪɡˈnɪfɪkəns/:n. 重要性:The cultural significance of the artifact was widely recognized.
6. examination /ɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃn/ n. 考试；检查；审查
📖 英文解释
A formal test to assess knowledge or skills; a detailed inspection.
✏️ 例句
A thorough examination of the evidence revealed several inconsistencies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pass/fail an examination（通过/未通过考试）；medical examination（体检）；examine v. 检查；考研高频词
🧠 助记
exam（考试）+ ination → 考试的过程 → 考试、检查
examination::/ɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃn/:n. 考试:A close examination of the data revealed unexpected patterns.
7. detect /dɪˈtekt/ v. 查明；发现；察觉；检测
📖 英文解释
To discover or identify the presence or existence of something.
✏️ 例句
New technology can detect cancer at a much earlier stage than before.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
detect a problem/error（发现问题/错误）；detection n. 探测；detector n. 探测器；detective n. 侦探
🧠 助记
de-tect → de（去掉）+ tect（覆盖）→ 揭开覆盖物发现 → 发现
detect::/dɪˈtekt/:v. 查明:Early detection of disease significantly improves treatment outcomes.
8. host /həʊst/ n. 主持人；主人 v. 主办；接待
📖 英文解释
A person who receives or entertains guests; to act as the host of an event.
✏️ 例句
The country was chosen to host the international climate summit.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
host a party/event（主办聚会/活动）；host country（主办国）；considering host（考量主办方）；考研写作常用词
🧠 助记
host → h + ost（最后一位）→ 最后出来迎宾的主人 → 主人
host::/həʊst/:n. 主持人:China hosted the 2022 Winter Olympics in Beijing.
9. estate /ɪˈsteɪt/ n. 地产；遗产；庄园；房地产
📖 英文解释
An area of land with a house on it; all the money and property left by a person at death.
✏️ 例句
The real estate market in major cities has become increasingly unaffordable.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
real estate（房地产）；estate agent（房产经纪人）；estate tax（遗产税）；考研经济、法律类文章高频词
🧠 助记
e-state → state（状态/状况）→ 土地财产的状态 → 地产、遗产
estate::/ɪˈsteɪt/:n. 地产:Real estate prices have surged in cities worldwide.
10. initiative /ɪˈnɪʃətɪv/ n. 主动性；主动权；进取心；倡议
📖 英文解释
The ability to assess and initiate things independently; a new plan or process to achieve a goal.
✏️ 例句
The government launched a new initiative to promote renewable energy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
take the initiative（采取主动）；on one's own initiative（主动地）；initiative = a scheme/plan（一项计划）；考研写作政策类词汇
🧠 助记
init-iative → initiate（开始）→ 主动开始某件事 → 主动性
initiative::/ɪˈnɪʃətɪv/:n. 主动性:The environmental initiative received broad support from the public.
11. divorce /dɪˈvɔːrs/ v./n. 离婚；断绝关系；分开
📖 英文解释
The legal dissolution of a marriage; to end a marriage; to separate from something.
✏️ 例句
The divorce rate has risen significantly in many developed countries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
divorce sb from sth（使……与……分离）；get a divorce（离婚）；考研社会类文章常见
🧠 助记
di-vorce → divide（分开）+ voice（声音）→ 声音分开各说各话 → 离婚
divorce::/dɪˈvɔːrs/:v./n. 离婚:Divorce rates reflect broader changes in social attitudes to marriage.
12. dispute /dɪˈspjuːt/ n./v. 争论；争议；质疑
📖 英文解释
A disagreement or argument; to argue about or question the truth of something.
✏️ 例句
The two countries are in a territorial dispute over the islands.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in dispute（有争议）；dispute a claim（对……提出异议）；beyond dispute（无可争辩）；考研政治、法律类文章高频词
🧠 助记
dis-pute → dis（否定）+ pute（思考）→ 否定别人的想法 → 争议
dispute::/dɪˈspjuːt/:n./v. 争论:The trade dispute between the two nations damaged bilateral relations.
13. accurate /ˈækjərət/ adj. 精确的；准确的；正确无误的
📖 英文解释
Correct in all details; exactly right; free from errors or mistakes.
✏️ 例句
Accurate data is essential for making sound policy decisions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
accurate information（准确信息）；accuracy n. 准确性；accurately adv. 准确地；考研学术类文章核心词
🧠 助记
ac-cur-ate → ac（到）+ cur（关心）→ 到位地关心细节 → 准确的
accurate::/ˈækjərət/:adj. 精确的:Accurate reporting is a fundamental obligation of the press.
14. brown /braʊn/ adj./n. 棕色的；褐色
📖 英文解释
Of a color produced by mixing red, yellow, and black; the color of earth or wood.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

brown::/braʊn/:adj./n. 棕色的:Brown coal is still used for energy production in some countries.
15. snow /snəʊ/ n./v. 雪；下雪；像雪般覆盖
📖 英文解释
Frozen water vapor that falls as white flakes; to fall as snow.
✏️ 例句
Heavy snow brought traffic in the city to a near standstill.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

snow → sn + ow → 'sn'联想snowy（多雪的）→ 雪
snow::/snəʊ/:n./v. 雪:Reduced snowfall in mountain regions threatens water supplies.
16. confirm /kənˈfɜːrm/ v. 证实；确认；批准

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
con-firm → firm（坚定）地表示 → 确认、证实
confirm::/kənˈfɜːrm/:v. 证实:The study confirmed the link between diet and cardiovascular disease.
17. raw /rɔː/ adj. 生的；未加工的；原始的；未经训练的

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

raw → 'ra'（太阳）+ w → 未经加工直接晒着的状态 → 原始的、生的
raw::/rɔː/:adj. 生的:Raw data must be carefully analyzed before drawing conclusions.
18. unit /ˈjuːnɪt/ n. 单元；单位；部件；（一）组
📖 英文解释
An individual thing or person regarded as a single member of a larger group; a quantity chosen as a standard of measurement.
✏️ 例句

unit::/ˈjuːnɪt/:n. 单元:The family is often described as the basic unit of society.
19. hang /hæŋ/ v. 挂；悬挂；绞死；徘徊

hang::/hæŋ/:v. 挂:The outcome of the negotiations hangs on one critical decision.
20. weigh /weɪ/ v. 称重量；衡量；有影响；考虑

weigh::/weɪ/:v. 称重量:We must weigh short-term costs against long-term environmental benefits.

trial::/ˈtraɪəl/:n. 审判:The defendant received a fair trial in accordance with the law.

📖 英文解释

evolve::/ɪˈvɒlv/:v. 进化:Business models must evolve to remain relevant in a digital world.

mislead::/ˌmɪsˈliːd/:v. 误导:Headlines that mislead readers undermine public trust in media.
