1. automation /ˌɔːtəˈmeɪʃn/ n. 自动化；自动操作
📖 英文解释
The use of largely automatic equipment in a system of manufacturing or other process.
✏️ 例句
Automation has replaced many manual jobs in manufacturing industries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
factory automation（工厂自动化）；office automation（办公自动化）；automate v. 使自动化；考研科技、经济类文章核心词
🧠 助记
auto（自动）+ mation → 自动运作的过程 → 自动化
automation::/ˌɔːtəˈmeɪʃn/:n. 自动化:Automation poses both opportunities and challenges for the workforce.
2. craft /krɑːft/ n. 工艺；技艺；手艺 v. 精心制作
📖 英文解释
An activity involving skill in making things by hand; to make something with skill.
✏️ 例句
Traditional craft skills are at risk of being lost as technology takes over.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
craft beer（精酿啤酒）；craft a plan/speech（精心制定计划/演讲）；handicraft（手工艺）；craftsman n. 工匠
🧠 助记
craft → c + raft（木筏）→ 用手工技艺做的木筏 → 工艺、手艺
craft::/krɑːft/:n. 工艺:She crafted a compelling argument based on solid evidence.
3. plenty /ˈplenti/ n. 大量；充裕 adv. 足够地
📖 英文解释
A large or sufficient amount or quantity; more than enough.
✏️ 例句
There is plenty of evidence to support the theory of climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
plenty of（大量的）；in plenty（充裕地）；plentiful adj. 丰富的；考研写作中表示'充足'的常用词
🧠 助记
plen-ty → plentiful（丰富的）→ 充裕的、大量的
plenty::/ˈplenti/:n. 大量:There is plenty of room for improvement in the current system.
4. vegetable /ˈvedʒtəbl/ n. 蔬菜；植物人 adj. 蔬菜的
📖 英文解释
A plant or part of a plant used as food; relating to plants.
✏️ 例句
A diet rich in vegetables and fruit is associated with better long-term health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
vegetable market（蔬菜市场）；go/become a vegetable（变成植物人）；考研医学、饮食类文章常见
🧠 助记
veg-etable → veg（蔬菜的缩写）+ etable → 可以吃的植物 → 蔬菜
vegetable::/ˈvedʒtəbl/:n. 蔬菜:Access to fresh vegetables remains limited in many rural communities.
5. curriculum /kəˈrɪkjələm/ n. 课程；全部课程

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
curriculum design（课程设计）；curriculum vitae（个人简历，CV）；extra-curricular（课外的）；考研教育类文章核心词

curri-culum → curri（跑道）→ 学生在跑道上跑完的学习路程 → 课程
curriculum::/kəˈrɪkjələm/:n. 课程:The national curriculum should reflect the needs of a modern economy.

flexible::/ˈfleksɪbl/:adj. 灵活的:Flexible regulations encourage innovation and entrepreneurship.

royal::/ˈrɔɪəl/:adj. 皇家的:Royal traditions play a significant role in national cultural identity.
9. highlight /ˈhaɪlaɪt/ v. 强调；使显著 n. 最精彩部分；亮点
📖 英文解释
To draw attention to something important; the most interesting or memorable part.
✏️ 例句

highlight::/ˈhaɪlaɪt/:v. 强调:The conference highlighted the urgency of addressing inequality.
10. remark /rɪˈmɑːrk/ v. 评论；谈论 n. 评语；言论

remark::/rɪˈmɑːrk/:v. 评论:The official's remarks on immigration sparked a national debate.
11. comparison /kəmˈpærɪsn/ n. 比较；对比；相提并论
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

comparison::/kəmˈpærɪsn/:n. 比较:In comparison to developed nations, the gap in infrastructure is stark.

✏️ 例句

regulate::/ˈreɡjuleɪt/:v. 管理:Governments must regulate technology companies to protect user privacy.
13. contrary /ˈkɒntrəri/ adj. 相反的；对立的 n. 相反 adv. 相反地
📖 英文解释
Opposite in nature, direction, or meaning; the opposite.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
on the contrary（相反地）；contrary to（与……相反）；contrary opinion（相反意见）；考研写作逻辑转折高频词
🧠 助记
contra（对立）+ ry → 完全对立的 → 相反的
contrary::/ˈkɒntrəri/:adj. 相反的:Contrary to expectations, the reform led to reduced costs.

rush::/rʌʃ/:v. 冲:Rushing policy changes without consultation often leads to mistakes.
