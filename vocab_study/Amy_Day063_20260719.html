1. surround /səˈraʊnd/ v. 围绕；包围 n. 周围环境
📖 英文解释
To be situated all around something or someone; to encircle or enclose something.
✏️ 例句
The city is surrounded by mountains.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：surround oneself with（被……包围）；surrounded by（被……围绕）；surrounding area（周围地区）；surroundings n.（环境）
🧠 助记
sur（超过/周围）+ round（圆）→ 在四周画圆 → 包围
surround::/səˈraʊnd/:v. 围绕:be surrounded by talented colleagues
2. bottom /ˈbɒtəm/ n. 底部；底端 adj. 最低的
📖 英文解释
The lowest point or part of something; the least important position in a hierarchy.
✏️ 例句
There's a signature at the bottom of the page.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：at the bottom of（在……底部）；bottom line（底线；利润）；get to the bottom of（查明真相）；rock bottom（最低点）
🧠 助记
联想桶的底部 bottom → 底端
bottom::/ˈbɒtəm/:n. 底部:the bottom line of any business is profit
3. expansion /ɪkˈspænʃ(ə)n/ n. 扩张；膨胀；扩展
📖 英文解释
The action of becoming larger or more extensive; the increase in a country's territory or the scope of its activities.
✏️ 例句
The company's expansion into Asian markets was very successful.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：economic expansion（经济扩张）；expansion plan（扩张计划）；expand v.；expansive adj.
🧠 助记
expand（扩大）+ sion → 扩大的动作/结果 → 扩张
expansion::/ɪkˈspænʃ(ə)n/:n. 扩张:rapid expansion of the service sector
4. whereas /weərˈæz/ conj. 然而；而（表对比）
📖 英文解释
Used to indicate a contrast or comparison between two facts; while on the contrary.
✏️ 例句
She is very outgoing, whereas her brother is quite shy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：whereas 引导让步或对比从句；常用于学术写作；区别 while（可表对比，也表时间）
🧠 助记
where（哪里）+ as（如同）→ 在某种情况下相比另一种 → 然而、而
whereas::/weərˈæz/:conj. 然而:urban areas are wealthy whereas rural ones remain poor
5. license /ˈlaɪs(ə)ns/ n. 执照；许可证 v. 许可；授权
📖 英文解释
An official document giving permission to do, use, or own something; freedom to behave in an unrestrained way.
✏️ 例句
You need a license to drive a car.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：driver's license（驾照）；license to operate（运营许可）；under license（经许可）；licensed adj.
🧠 助记
联想 license plate（车牌）→ 持有许可证才能上路 → 执照
license::/ˈlaɪs(ə)ns/:n. 执照:obtain a license to practice medicine
6. journey /ˈdʒɜːnɪ/ n. 旅行；旅程；历程
📖 英文解释
A long trip from one place to another; a process of personal change and development.
✏️ 例句
The journey from London to Edinburgh takes about four hours by train.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：a long/short journey（长/短途旅行）；journey to（去……的旅途）；life journey（人生旅程）；区别 trip（短途）、tour（游览）
🧠 助记
jour（法语，天）→ 一天一天走过去 → 旅程
journey::/ˈdʒɜːnɪ/:n. 旅行:the journey toward sustainable development
7. anticipate /ænˈtɪsɪpeɪt/ v. 预期；预料；期望；先于……行动
📖 英文解释
To regard as probable; to expect or predict; to act before someone else in order to gain an advantage.
✏️ 例句
We didn't anticipate the level of demand for the product.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：anticipate + doing sth.（预料做某事）；anticipate problems（预料到问题）；beyond anticipated（超出预期）
🧠 助记
anti（之前）+ cip（取得）+ ate → 提前取得或预想 → 预期
anticipate::/ænˈtɪsɪpeɪt/:v. 预期:anticipate rapid growth in the coming decade
8. deprive /dɪˈpraɪv/ v. 剥夺；使丧失
📖 英文解释
To deny someone the possession or use of something; to take something away from someone.
✏️ 例句
Children deprived of education are at a serious disadvantage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：deprive sb. of sth.（剥夺某人某物）；sleep-deprived（缺乏睡眠的）；deprived area（贫困地区）；deprivation n.
🧠 助记
de（去掉）+ prive（私有）→ 去掉私有物 → 剥夺
deprive::/dɪˈpraɪv/:v. 剥夺:deprive citizens of basic rights
9. finance /ˈfaɪnæns/ n. 财政；金融 v. 为……提供资金
📖 英文解释
The management of large amounts of money; the money needed to support an activity; to provide money for a project.
✏️ 例句
She works in finance at a large bank.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：public finance（公共财政）；finance a project（资助项目）；Ministry of Finance（财政部）；financial adj.
🧠 助记
fine（罚款、完结）→ fin（结束，如 final）+ ance → 钱的最终结算 → 财政
finance::/ˈfaɪnæns/:n. 财政:secure finance for infrastructure projects
10. absence /ˈæbs(ə)ns/ n. 缺席；不在场；缺乏

🧠 助记
ab（离开）+ sence（存在）→ 离开了存在的状态 → 缺席
absence::/ˈæbs(ə)ns/:n. 缺席:in the absence of clear guidelines, confusion arose
11. guideline /ˈɡaɪdlaɪn/ n. 指导方针；准则；指导原则

🧠 助记
guide（引导）+ line（线）→ 给你划出的引导线 → 指导方针
guideline::/ˈɡaɪdlaɪn/:n. 指导方针:follow ethical guidelines in research

route::/ruːt/:n. 路线:the fastest route to economic recovery

whenever::/wenˈevər/:conj. 无论何时:seek help whenever difficulties arise
