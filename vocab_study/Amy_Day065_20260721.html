1. uniform /ˈjuːnɪfɔːm/ adj. 统一的；一致的 n. 制服
📖 英文解释
Remaining the same in all cases; not varying; a distinctive outfit worn by members of a group.
✏️ 例句
All students are required to wear uniform.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：school uniform（校服）；uniform standard（统一标准）；uniformly adv.；uniformity n.
🧠 助记
uni（一）+ form（形式）→ 一种形式 → 统一的；制服
uniform::/ˈjuːnɪfɔːm/:adj. 统一的:apply uniform standards across all regions
2. pair /peər/ n. 一对；一双；一副 v. 配对
📖 英文解释
A set of two things used together; two people considered as a couple; to join with another.
✏️ 例句
She bought a new pair of shoes for the interview.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：a pair of（一对/双/副）；pair up with（与……配对）；pair off（两两配对）
🧠 助记
pair 一对 → 联想 spare（备用的）→ 两件一组 → 一对
pair::/peər/:n. 一对:a pair of complementary policies works better than one alone
3. profitable /ˈprɒfɪtəb(ə)l/ adj. 有利可图的；有益的；盈利的
📖 英文解释
Yielding profit or financial gain; beneficial or useful.
✏️ 例句
The company's most profitable product is its smartphone line.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：highly profitable（高度盈利的）；profitable business（盈利业务）；profitable investment（有利可图的投资）；profit n./v.
🧠 助记
profit（利润）+ able → 能产生利润的 → 有利可图的
profitable::/ˈprɒfɪtəb(ə)l/:adj. 有利可图的:a profitable enterprise generates jobs and tax revenue
4. prison /ˈprɪz(ə)n/ n. 监狱；束缚 v. 监禁
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：go to prison（坐牢）；prison sentence（徒刑）；prison reform（监狱改革）；prisoner n.（囚犯）
🧠 助记
prison 监狱 → 联想 prisoner（囚犯）→ 被囚禁的地方
prison::/ˈprɪz(ə)n/:n. 监狱:prison reform aims to reduce reoffending rates

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：take a deep breath（深吸一口气）；catch one's breath（喘口气）；hold one's breath（屏住呼吸）；breathe v.
🧠 助记
breath 呼吸 → 联想 breathe（呼吸，动词）→ 名词形式
breath::/breθ/:n. 呼吸:a breath of fresh air in a stagnant political climate

barrier::/ˈbærɪər/:n. 障碍:remove barriers to education for underprivileged groups
