1. entrance /ˈentrəns/ n. 入口；进入；入学
📖 英文解释
a door or opening used for entering a place; the act of entering; the right to enter
✏️ 例句
Please use the side entrance to the building.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
entrance exam（入学考试）; make an entrance（登场）; entrance fee（入场费）
🧠 助记
enter（进入）+ ance → 进入的地方或行为 → 入口
entrance::/ˈentrəns/:n. 入口:The university entrance examination is held every June.
2. snack /snæk/ n. 零食；小吃 v. 吃零食
📖 英文解释
a small amount of food eaten between meals; to eat small amounts between meals
✏️ 例句
She grabbed a snack before the meeting.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
have a snack; snack on fruit（以水果作零食）; snack bar（快餐吧）
🧠 助记
联想 snake（蛇）→ 蛇吃小动物 → 随意吃点小东西 → 零食
snack::/snæk/:n. 零食:It is better to snack on vegetables than on crisps.
3. rat /ræt/ n. 老鼠；叛徒 v. 告密
📖 英文解释
a rodent similar to a large mouse; an untrustworthy person who betrays others
✏️ 例句
The lab used rats for scientific experiments.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
smell a rat（察觉可疑）; rat race（激烈竞争的生活）; like a drowned rat（狼狈不堪）
🧠 助记
形象记忆：r-a-t 三个字母，老鼠短小精悍
rat::/ræt/:n. 老鼠:She smelled a rat when the deal seemed too good to be true.
4. channel /ˈtʃænl/ n. 渠道；频道；海峡 v. 引导
📖 英文解释
a method of communication; a television station; a narrow stretch of water; to direct energy
✏️ 例句
The company uses social media as a marketing channel.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
distribution channel（销售渠道）; channel one's energy into（把精力引导到）
🧠 助记
chan（canal 运河）+ nel → 像运河一样的通道 → 渠道、海峡
channel::/ˈtʃænl/:n. 渠道:She channeled all her efforts into completing the project.
5. reply /rɪˈplaɪ/ v./n. 回答；答复
📖 英文解释
to say, write, or do something as an answer to something someone has said or written
✏️ 例句
He didn't reply to my email for three days.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reply to sb/sth; in reply to（作为对…的回应）; await a reply
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ ply（折叠，如apply）→ 重新折回来的话 → 回复
reply::/rɪˈplaɪ/:v./n. 回答:Please reply to the invitation before Friday.
6. virtue /ˈvɜːtʃuː/ n. 美德；优点；功效
📖 英文解释
a good quality or feature; a morally good or admirable quality
✏️ 例句
Patience is considered a great virtue.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
by virtue of（凭借，由于）; make a virtue of necessity（化被动为主动）; moral virtue
🧠 助记
virt（力量，如virile）+ ue → 内在力量和品质 → 美德
virtue::/ˈvɜːtʃuː/:n. 美德:By virtue of her experience, she was chosen to lead the team.
7. injury /ˈɪndʒəri/ n. 伤害；损伤；损害
📖 英文解释
physical harm or damage done to a person or animal; harm done to feelings or reputation
✏️ 例句
He suffered a knee injury during the match.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
personal injury; injury to one's reputation; add insult to injury（雪上加霜）
🧠 助记
injure（伤害）+ y → 受伤的结果 → 伤害
injury::/ˈɪndʒəri/:n. 伤害:The athlete withdrew from the competition due to injury.
8. remind /rɪˈmaɪnd/ v. 提醒；使想起
📖 英文解释
to make someone remember something they may have forgotten; to cause someone to think of something
✏️ 例句
Please remind me to call him tomorrow.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
remind sb of/about sth; remind sb to do sth; this reminds me of...
🧠 助记
re（再）+ mind（心智）→ 再次唤起心中的记忆 → 提醒
remind::/rɪˈmaɪnd/:v. 提醒:The smell of the flowers reminded her of her childhood.
9. trait /treɪt/ n. 特征；特点；性格特质
📖 英文解释
a particular quality or characteristic that is typical of a person, place, or thing
✏️ 例句
Honesty is one of his most admirable traits.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
personality/character trait; cultural trait（文化特征）; inherited trait（遗传特质）
🧠 助记
联想 trace（痕迹）→ 留下痕迹的特点 → 特质
trait::/treɪt/:n. 特征:Curiosity is an important trait in a researcher.
10. counter /ˈkaʊntə/ n. 柜台；计数器 v. 反驳 adv. 相反地
📖 英文解释
a long flat surface in a shop or bank; to respond to something with an opposing argument
✏️ 例句
She bought the medicine over the counter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
over the counter（非处方的）; counter an argument（反驳论点）; run counter to（与…相悖）
🧠 助记
count（计数）+ er → 计数器；在柜台前计数
counter::/ˈkaʊntə/:n. 柜台:His actions ran counter to the team's agreed strategy.
11. evaluate /ɪˈvæljueɪt/ v. 评估；评价；求…的值
📖 英文解释
to judge the value, quality, or importance of something carefully
✏️ 例句
Teachers evaluate students' progress through regular tests.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
evaluate performance/risk/impact; critically evaluate（批判性评估）
🧠 助记
e + value + ate → 找出价值 → 评估
evaluate::/ɪˈvæljueɪt/:v. 评估:The committee will evaluate all proposals before making a decision.
12. sacrifice /ˈsækrɪfaɪs/ n./v. 牺牲；奉献；祭祀
📖 英文解释
to give up something valuable for the sake of something more important; an offering to a god
✏️ 例句
She sacrificed her personal life for her career.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make a sacrifice; sacrifice sth for sth; self-sacrifice（自我牺牲）
🧠 助记
sacri（神圣）+ fice（做，如office）→ 为神圣目的而做 → 牺牲、祭祀
sacrifice::/ˈsækrɪfaɪs/:n./v. 牺牲:Parents often make great sacrifices for their children's education.
13. tone /təʊn/ n. 语气；音调；色调 v. 调整音调
📖 英文解释
the quality of a sound or voice; the general mood or character of a piece of writing
✏️ 例句
The teacher's tone was firm but encouraging.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tone of voice（语气）; set the tone（定下基调）; tone down（缓和）
🧠 助记
联想 tune（曲调）→ 语气与音调的基调
tone::/təʊn/:n. 语气:The report set a tone of caution regarding future investments.
14. minor /ˈmaɪnə/ adj. 较小的；次要的 n. 未成年人
📖 英文解释
less important or serious than others; someone who is under the legal age of adulthood
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
minor issue/injury/role; minor in sth（辅修）; minor key（小调）
🧠 助记
min（小，如minimum）+ or → 较小的 → 次要的
minor::/ˈmaɪnə/:adj. 较小的:The changes were minor and did not affect the overall plan.
15. stuff /stʌf/ n. 东西；材料 v. 填满；大量吃

a substance or material; informal word for things; to fill something tightly
✏️ 例句

stuff sb with sth（用…填满）; know one's stuff（很内行）; stuff and nonsense（胡说八道）
🧠 助记
联想 stuffed animal（毛绒玩具，填充物）→ 填充的东西
stuff::/stʌf/:n. 东西:She stuffed the envelope with important documents.
16. sum /sʌm/ n. 总和；金额 v. 总结；求和

He donated a large sum to charity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sum up（总结）; in sum（总而言之）; lump sum（一次性付清）
🧠 助记
联想 summary（总结）→ 总计、总结
sum::/sʌm/:n. 总和:In sum, the project was completed on time and within budget.
17. cheat /tʃiːt/ v. 欺骗；作弊 n. 骗子
📖 英文解释
to act dishonestly in order to gain an advantage; a person who behaves dishonestly
✏️ 例句
Anyone caught cheating will be disqualified.

cheat::/tʃiːt/:v. 欺骗:He was expelled for cheating on the final examination.
18. injure /ˈɪndʒə/ v. 伤害；损害；使受损
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
injure oneself; seriously/badly injure; injure sb's pride（伤害自尊）
🧠 助记
in（进入）+ jure（法律，如jury）→ 违法伤人 → 伤害
injure::/ˈɪndʒə/:v. 伤害:Three workers were injured in the factory accident.
19. superior /suːˈpɪəriə/ adj. 优越的；上级的 n. 上司

better than others in quality or ability; higher in rank or position
✏️ 例句

superior::/suːˈpɪəriə/:adj. 优越的:The new drug proved superior to existing treatments in clinical trials.
20. tackle /ˈtækl/ v. 处理；对付；抢截 n. 用具

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tackle a problem/issue/challenge; tackle sb（拦截某人）
🧠 助记
联想 attack（攻击）→ 主动出击解决问题 → 处理，对付
tackle::/ˈtækl/:v. 处理:We need to tackle climate change before it's too late.
21. maximum /ˈmæksɪməm/ n./adj. 最大值；最大限度的

🧠 助记
max（最大，如maximize）+ imum → 最大值
maximum::/ˈmæksɪməm/:n./adj. 最大值:The train was running at maximum speed to make up for lost time.
22. tuition /tjuːˈɪʃn/ n. 学费；教学；辅导

tuition fee（学费）; private tuition（私人辅导）; pay tuition

tuition::/tjuːˈɪʃn/:n. 学费:She received private tuition to improve her mathematics.
23. appointment /əˈpɔɪntmənt/ n. 预约；任命；约会
📖 英文解释
an arrangement to meet someone at a particular time; the act of officially choosing someone for a job
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make/keep/cancel an appointment; by appointment（须事先预约）; appointment to a post
🧠 助记
appoint（任命，指定）+ ment → 指定时间见面 → 约定；任命
appointment::/əˈpɔɪntmənt/:n. 预约:Her appointment as director was announced at the board meeting.
24. ingredient /ɪnˈɡriːdiənt/ n. 成分；原料；要素
📖 英文解释
one of the foods or substances that is used to make a dish; a component of something
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
key ingredient（关键要素）; ingredient of success（成功的要素）
🧠 助记
in（进入）+ gredi（走，如grade）+ ent → 走进（成分）配方里的东西 → 成分
ingredient::/ɪnˈɡriːdiənt/:n. 成分:Trust is an essential ingredient in any successful relationship.

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
spare time（空闲时间）; spare no effort（不遗余力）; spare part（备件）
🧠 助记
联想 space（空间）→ 有空间，有余力，可以给予 → 节省；多余的
spare::/speə/:v. 节约:He spared no effort in preparing for the presentation.
