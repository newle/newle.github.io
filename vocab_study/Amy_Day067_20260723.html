1. tour /tʊə/ n./v. 旅游；参观；巡演
📖 英文解释
a journey for pleasure visiting several places; a short trip to see an interesting place
✏️ 例句
They went on a tour of Europe last summer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
guided tour（导游参观）; on tour（巡演中）; tour of duty（服役期）
🧠 助记
联想 travel → tour 是有目的的旅行参观
tour::/tʊə/:n./v. 旅游:The band is currently on a world tour.
2. steel /stiːl/ n. 钢铁 v. 使下定决心
📖 英文解释
a strong hard metal made from iron and carbon; to make yourself ready for something difficult
✏️ 例句
The bridge is made of reinforced steel.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
steel oneself（下定决心，鼓起勇气）; steel industry; nerves of steel（钢铁意志）
🧠 助记
联想 still（坚硬不动）→ 坚硬的金属 → 钢铁
steel::/stiːl/:n. 钢铁 v. 使下定决心:She steeled herself for the difficult conversation ahead.
3. beneath /bɪˈniːθ/ prep./adv. 在…之下；低于
📖 英文解释
in or to a lower position than something; not as good as what is expected
✏️ 例句
The treasure was buried beneath the old oak tree.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
beneath the surface（表面之下）; beneath one's dignity（有失身份）
🧠 助记
be + neath（联想 underneath）→ 在下面
beneath::/bɪˈniːθ/:prep./adv. 在…之下:Beneath his calm exterior lay a burning desire to succeed.
4. discount /ˈdɪskaʊnt/ n. 折扣 v. 打折；忽略
📖 英文解释
a reduction in the usual price; to regard something as unimportant or unlikely to be true
✏️ 例句
The store offered a 20% discount on all items.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
at a discount（打折）; discount sb's views（忽视某人观点）; volume discount（批量折扣）
🧠 助记
dis（减少）+ count（计算）→ 从总数中减去 → 折扣
discount::/ˈdɪskaʊnt/:n. 折扣 v. 打折:Don't discount the possibility that conditions may change.
5. trace /treɪs/ v. 追溯；描绘 n. 痕迹；少量
📖 英文解释
to find or describe the origins or development of something; a small amount or sign left by something
✏️ 例句
The detective traced the call to a nearby location.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
trace back to（追溯到）; leave a trace（留下痕迹）; trace elements（微量元素）
🧠 助记
联想 track（轨迹）→ 追踪留下的痕迹 → 追溯、痕迹
trace::/treɪs/:v. 追溯:Scientists traced the virus back to a bat population in the region.
6. unfortunately /ʌnˈfɔːtʃənətli/ adv. 不幸地；遗憾地
📖 英文解释
used to say that something is sad or disappointing
✏️ 例句
Unfortunately, the event was cancelled due to bad weather.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
unfortunately for sb（对某人来说不幸的是）
🧠 助记
un（不）+ fortune（幸运）+ ately → 不幸运地
unfortunately::/ʌnˈfɔːtʃənətli/:adv. 不幸地:Unfortunately, the company failed to meet its targets this year.
7. laboratory /ləˈbɒrətri/ n. 实验室
📖 英文解释
a room or building used for scientific research, experiments, or testing
✏️ 例句
The scientists worked in the laboratory for hours.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
laboratory experiment/test/research; language laboratory（语言实验室）
🧠 助记
labor（劳动）+ atory → 辛苦工作的地方 → 实验室
laboratory::/ləˈbɒrətri/:n. 实验室:The new drug was developed and tested in a research laboratory.
8. belt /belt/ n. 皮带；地带 v. 用带子打；快速移动
📖 英文解释
a strip of leather worn around the waist; a long narrow area with a particular feature
✏️ 例句
She fastened her seat belt before the car moved.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
belt out a song（放声歌唱）; below the belt（不公正地）; conveyor belt（传送带）
🧠 助记
直接记忆：belt = 皮带，腰带，联想 region 的地带用法
belt::/belt/:n. 皮带:The Midwest is often called the agricultural belt of America.
9. march /mɑːtʃ/ v./n. 行进；游行；三月
📖 英文解释
to walk with regular steps like a soldier; an organized public protest walk; the third month of the year
✏️ 例句
Thousands of protesters marched through the city.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
march against（游行抗议）; on the march（在前进中）; military march（军乐）
🧠 助记
联想士兵踏步行进的声音 march → 行进
march::/mɑːtʃ/:v./n. 行进:Workers marched through the streets demanding better pay.
10. tendency /ˈtendənsi/ n. 趋势；倾向；癖好
📖 英文解释
an inclination to behave in a particular way or for something to happen
✏️ 例句
She has a tendency to talk too much when nervous.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tendency to do sth; tendency towards（倾向于）; general tendency（总体趋势）
🧠 助记
tend（倾向）+ ency → 倾向性 → 趋势、倾向
tendency::/ˈtendənsi/:n. 趋势:There is a growing tendency for people to work from home.
11. accomplish /əˈkʌmplɪʃ/ v. 完成；实现；达到
📖 英文解释
to succeed in doing or completing something
✏️ 例句
She accomplished all her goals within the first year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
accomplish a task/mission; accomplish nothing（一事无成）
🧠 助记
ac（加强）+ com（完全）+ plish（fill 填满）→ 完全完成 → 实现
accomplish::/əˈkʌmplɪʃ/:v. 完成:The team accomplished the project ahead of schedule.
12. ruin /ˈruːɪn/ v. 毁坏；破坏 n. 废墟；毁灭
📖 英文解释
to spoil or destroy something completely; the remains of a building that has been destroyed
✏️ 例句
The storm ruined the harvest.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in ruins（成废墟）; fall into ruin（陷入毁灭）; ruin sb's reputation（毁掉声誉）
🧠 助记
联想 ruin = rain（雨）的谐音 → 大雨毁坏一切 → 毁坏
ruin::/ˈruːɪn/:v. 毁坏:Poor financial decisions ruined the company within two years.
13. entertain /ˌentəˈteɪn/ v. 娱乐；招待；考虑
📖 英文解释
to keep people amused or interested; to invite people as guests; to consider an idea
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
entertain guests/visitors; entertain an idea（考虑一个想法）; entertainment industry
🧠 助记
enter（进入）+ tain（保持）→ 让人进入并保持愉悦状态 → 娱乐
entertain::/ˌentəˈteɪn/:v. 娱乐:The manager refused to entertain any compromise on the issue.
14. hall /hɔːl/ n. 大厅；走廊；宿舍楼
📖 英文解释

直接记忆：hall = 大厅，联想 call 让人进来的地方
hall::/hɔːl/:n. 大厅:The candidates debated in the main hall of the university.
15. regret /rɪˈɡret/ v./n. 遗憾；后悔；抱歉
📖 英文解释
to feel sad or sorry about something you have done or not done

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
regret doing/that; with regret（遗憾地）; express regret（表示遗憾）
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ gret（联想 grief 悲伤）→ 一再为之悲伤 → 后悔
regret::/rɪˈɡret/:v./n. 遗憾:He deeply regretted his hasty decision to resign.
16. furniture /ˈfɜːnɪtʃə/ n. 家具（总称）

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a piece of furniture; office furniture; furniture store
🧠 助记

furniture::/ˈfɜːnɪtʃə/:n. 家具（总称）:The office was equipped with modern furniture and technology.
17. ride /raɪd/ v. 骑；搭乘 n. 旅程；搭车

ride::/raɪd/:v. 骑:The company rode the wave of digital transformation to great success.
18. terrible /ˈterɪbl/ adj. 糟糕的；可怕的；极度的

terrible::/ˈterɪbl/:adj. 糟糕的:The earthquake caused terrible destruction across the region.

coast::/kəʊst/:n. 海岸:She coasted through the first few rounds of the competition.

camera::/ˈkæmərə/:n. 照相机:The security camera captured the entire incident on footage.

obstacle::/ˈɒbstəkl/:n. 障碍:She overcame every obstacle on her path to becoming a doctor.
22. finger /ˈfɪŋɡə/ n. 手指 v. 用手指触摸；指认

finger::/ˈfɪŋɡə/:n. 手指 v. 用手指触摸:She pointed an accusing finger at the company's management.
23. interval /ˈɪntəvl/ n. 间隔；间歇；幕间休息
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
at intervals（每隔一段时间/距离）; at regular intervals（定期地）
🧠 助记

interval::/ˈɪntəvl/:n. 间隔:The medicine should be taken at regular intervals throughout the day.
24. constitute /ˈkɒnstɪtjuːt/ v. 构成；组成；相当于
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
constitute a threat/crime/majority; be constituted of（由…构成）

con（共同）+ stitut（站立，如institute）+ e → 共同站在一起构成 → 构成
constitute::/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːt/:v. 构成:Failure to comply constitutes a breach of the contract.

Northern Europe/Hemisphere; northern dialect; Northern Lights（北极光）
🧠 助记

northern::/ˈnɔːðən/:adj. 北方的:The company opened a new branch in the northern city.
