1. aggressive /əˈɡresɪv/ adj. 好斗的；激进的；积极进取的
📖 英文解释
ready to attack or confront; very determined and forceful in pursuing goals
✏️ 例句
The company adopted an aggressive marketing strategy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
aggressive behavior; aggressive expansion（激进扩张）; be aggressive in doing sth
🧠 助记
aggress（攻击）+ ive → 有攻击性的 → 好斗的；积极进取的
aggressive::/əˈɡresɪv/:adj. 好斗的:An aggressive sales approach helped the startup grow rapidly.
2. influential /ˌɪnfluˈenʃl/ adj. 有影响力的；有势力的
📖 英文解释
having a lot of influence on someone or something
✏️ 例句
She is one of the most influential figures in modern literature.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
highly influential; influential in doing sth; influential figure/person
🧠 助记
influence（影响）+ ial → 有影响力的
influential::/ˌɪnfluˈenʃl/:adj. 有影响力的:The report was influential in shaping government education policy.
3. radical /ˈrædɪkl/ adj. 根本的；激进的 n. 激进分子
📖 英文解释
fundamental or thorough change; holding extreme political views
✏️ 例句
The government proposed radical reforms to the tax system.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
radical change/reform/idea; radical group（激进团体）
🧠 助记
radic（根，如radix）+ al → 从根部改变 → 根本的，激进的
radical::/ˈrædɪkl/:adj. 根本的:The radical overhaul of the healthcare system faced strong opposition.
4. abuse /əˈbjuːz/ v./n. 滥用；虐待；辱骂
📖 英文解释
to use something wrongly or badly; to treat a person cruelly or violently; cruel or violent treatment
✏️ 例句
He was accused of abusing his authority.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
abuse of power（滥用职权）; child abuse（儿童虐待）; verbal abuse（言语辱骂）
🧠 助记
ab（离）+ use（使用）→ 偏离正当使用 → 滥用，虐待
abuse::/əˈbjuːz/:v./n. 滥用:There are strict laws to prevent the abuse of public funds.
5. southern /ˈsʌðən/ adj. 南方的；南部的
📖 英文解释
in or from the south part of a country or region
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
Southern Europe; southern dialect; Southern Hemisphere（南半球）
🧠 助记
south（南）+ ern → 南方的
southern::/ˈsʌðən/:adj. 南方的:The southern regions experienced record rainfall last year.
6. elementary /ˌelɪˈmentri/ adj. 基础的；初级的；基本的
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

elementary::/ˌelɪˈmentri/:adj. 基础的:It is elementary to check your work before submitting it.
7. motor /ˈməʊtə/ n. 发动机；马达 adj. 汽车的
📖 英文解释
a machine that uses electricity or fuel to produce movement; relating to vehicles
✏️ 例句
The electric motor powers the car silently.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
联想 motion（运动）→ 产生运动的机器 → 马达、发动机
motor::/ˈməʊtə/:n. 发动机:Children develop fine motor skills through activities like drawing and writing.
8. reporter /rɪˈpɔːtə/ n. 记者；报道者
📖 英文解释

reporter::/rɪˈpɔːtə/:n. 记者:The investigative reporter uncovered widespread corruption in the government.
9. commerce /ˈkɒmɜːs/ n. 商业；贸易
📖 英文解释
the activity of buying and selling goods and services, especially between countries
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

commerce::/ˈkɒmɜːs/:n. 商业:The new policy aims to promote commerce between the two countries.
10. strain /streɪn/ n./v. 拉伤；紧张；过度使用；菌株
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
under strain（处于压力下）; strain a relationship; strain every nerve（全力以赴）
🧠 助记
联想 stress（压力）→ 被拉伸拉紧 → 紧张，拉伤
strain::/streɪn/:n./v. 拉伤:Years of overwork put a huge strain on his health.
11. resolve /rɪˈzɒlv/ v. 解决；决心 n. 决心
📖 英文解释
to find a satisfactory solution to a problem; a firm decision to do something
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
re（再）+ solve（解决）→ 反复解决直到解开 → 解决
resolve::/rɪˈzɒlv/:v. 解决:The manager resolved the conflict between the two team members swiftly.
12. thrive /θraɪv/ v. 繁荣；兴旺；茁壮成长
📖 英文解释

thrive::/θraɪv/:v. 繁荣:The startup thrived in the competitive technology market.
13. excuse /ɪkˈskjuːz/ v. 原谅；为…辩解 n. 借口；理由

🧠 助记

excuse::/ɪkˈskjuːz/:v. 原谅:There is no excuse for ignoring safety procedures in the workplace.

profound::/prəˈfaʊnd/:adj. 深刻的:The discovery had profound implications for the field of medicine.
