1. village /ˈvɪlɪdʒ/ n. 村庄；乡村
📖 英文解释
a group of houses and associated buildings, larger than a hamlet and smaller than a town
✏️ 例句
She grew up in a small village in the mountains.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
village life（乡村生活）；fishing village（渔村）；village community（村社）
🧠 助记
vill（ville = 城）+ age → 小城 → 村庄
village::/ˈvɪlɪdʒ/:n. 村庄:a remote mountain village
2. destruction /dɪˈstrʌkʃn/ n. 破坏；毁灭
📖 英文解释
the action or process of causing so much damage to something that it no longer exists
✏️ 例句
The hurricane caused widespread destruction along the coast.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cause/bring destruction（造成破坏）；weapon of mass destruction（大规模杀伤武器）；self-destruction
🧠 助记
de（向下）+ struct（建造）+ ion → 向下建造 = 破坏
destruction::/dɪˈstrʌkʃn/:n. 破坏:the destruction of natural habitats
3. bond /bɒnd/ n. 纽带；债券；结合 v. 建立感情
📖 英文解释
a force or feeling that unites people; a certificate of debt issued by a government
✏️ 例句
The shared experience created a strong bond between the two friends.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
form a bond（建立联系）；bond with sb（与某人建立感情）；government bond（政府债券）
🧠 助记
bond（邦德）→ 007的情感联系 → 纽带
bond::/bɒnd/:n. 纽带:a bond of trust and loyalty
4. weapon /ˈwepən/ n. 武器；手段
📖 英文解释
a thing designed or used for inflicting bodily harm or physical damage
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记
wea（谐音为啊）+ pon → 为啊才动武 → 武器
weapon::/ˈwepən/:n. 武器:a powerful weapon against disease

🧠 助记
谐音扫了它 → 用盐腌制食物
salt::/sɔːlt/:n. 盐 v. 腌:reduce salt intake daily

dioxide::/daɪˈɒksaɪd/:n. 二氧化物:carbon dioxide emissions
