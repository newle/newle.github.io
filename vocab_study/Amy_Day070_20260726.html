1. bias /ˈbaɪəs/ n. 偏见；偏重 v. 使偏向
📖 英文解释
inclination or prejudice for or against one person or group; an unfair tendency
✏️ 例句
The study found significant gender bias in hiring practices.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
racial/gender bias（种族/性别偏见）；be biased toward/against（偏向/反对）；confirmation bias（确认偏误）
🧠 助记
bi（两）+ as → 只看两方中的一方 → 偏见
bias::/ˈbaɪəs/:n. 偏见:overcome unconscious bias
2. photograph /ˈfəʊtəɡrɑːf/ n. 照片 v. 给…拍照
📖 英文解释
a picture made using a camera; to take a picture with a camera
✏️ 例句
She took a beautiful photograph of the mountain at sunset.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
take a photograph（拍照）；photograph of sb/sth；photography n.（摄影）
🧠 助记
photo（光）+ graph（写）→ 用光写下来 → 照片
photograph::/ˈfəʊtəɡrɑːf/:n. 照片 v. 给…拍照:take a photograph of the scene
3. rival /ˈraɪvl/ n./v. 竞争（者）；对手；与…竞争
📖 英文解释
a person or thing competing with another for the same objective; to be as good as
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fierce rival（强劲对手）；rival company（竞争公司）；rival for sth（为某事竞争）
🧠 助记
ri（river 河流）+ val → 在河两边竞速 → 竞争
rival::/ˈraɪvl/:n./v. 竞争（者）:a fierce rival in business
4. disorder /dɪsˈɔːrdər/ n. 混乱；骚乱；疾病（失调）
📖 英文解释
a state of confusion; a disruption of systematic functioning; a disease

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
dis（否定）+ order（秩序）→ 没有秩序 → 混乱
disorder::/dɪsˈɔːrdər/:n. 混乱:social disorder and unrest

🧠 助记
bl（黑暗 black）+ ind → 黑暗中什么都看不见 → 失明的
blind::/blaɪnd/:adj. 失明的:blind to obvious problems

tobacco::/təˈbækəʊ/:n. 烟草:tobacco products and health risks
