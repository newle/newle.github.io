1. restrict /rɪˈstrɪkt/ v. 限制；约束；限定
📖 英文解释
To put a limit on; to keep within certain bounds.
✏️ 例句
The law restricts the use of certain chemicals in food production.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ restrict sb/sth to sth 将…限制在…范围内 ★ restricted area 限制区域 ☆ restriction n. 限制；restrictive adj. 限制性的 辨析：restrict 强调设定界限；limit 强调上限；confine 强调局限于某处
🧠 助记
re(再)+strict(严格)——再次严格约束=限制
restrict::/rɪˈstrɪkt/:v. 限制:Access to the laboratory is restricted to authorized personnel.
2. democratic /ˌdeməˈkrætɪk/ adj. 民主的；民主主义的
📖 英文解释
Relating to or supporting democracy; favoring social equality.
✏️ 例句
The transition to a democratic government was peaceful and internationally recognized.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ democratic election 民主选举 ★ democratic principles 民主原则 ☆ democracy n. 民主；democrat n. 民主人士 辨析：democratic 指民主的；liberal 指自由主义的；republican 指共和制的
🧠 助记
demo(人民)+crat(统治)+ic——由人民统治的=民主的
democratic::/ˌdeməˈkrætɪk/:adj. 民主的:Free and fair elections are essential to a democratic society.
3. compensate /ˈkɒmpenseɪt/ v. 补偿；赔偿；弥补
📖 英文解释
To give someone something to make up for a loss, injury, or inconvenience.
✏️ 例句
The company compensated workers who were injured in the factory accident.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ compensate sb for sth 为某事补偿某人 ★ compensate for 弥补（某方面不足） ☆ compensation n. 补偿，赔偿 辨析：compensate 强调弥补损失；reimburse 强调报销退款；reward 强调奖励
🧠 助记
com(共同)+pens(支付)+ate——共同支付来弥补=补偿
compensate::/ˈkɒmpenseɪt/:v. 补偿:Nothing can fully compensate for the loss of a loved one.

📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ thin ice 薄冰；on thin ice 如履薄冰 ★ thin out 变稀疏 ☆ wafer-thin 极薄的 辨析：thin 中性；slim 含苗条之意；lean 含少脂肪；slender 含优雅之意
🧠 助记
thin = 拼音"厅"——像大厅一样空旷薄透
thin::/θɪn/:adj. 薄的:His argument was based on thin evidence and easily refuted.
