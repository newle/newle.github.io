1. modest /ˈmɒdɪst/ adj. 谦虚的；适度的；朴素的
📖 英文解释
Unassuming in the estimation of one's abilities; moderate in amount or extent.
✏️ 例句
Despite her remarkable achievements, she remained modest and always gave credit to her team.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be modest about one's achievements 对成就谦虚 ★ a modest salary/improvement 适中的薪水/小幅改善 ☆ modesty n. 谦虚；modestly adv. 辨析：modest 强调谦虚低调；humble 更强调谦卑；moderate 仅强调适度
🧠 助记
mod(适度)+est——保持适度的自我评价=谦虚的
modest::/ˈmɒdɪst/:adj. 谦虚的:He was modest about his success, always attributing it to teamwork.
2. personnel /ˌpɜːrsəˈnel/ n. 人员；员工；人事部门
📖 英文解释
People employed in an organization; the department of an organization that deals with the administration of staff.
✏️ 例句
All personnel are required to attend the mandatory safety training next Monday.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ military/medical personnel 军事/医疗人员 ★ personnel department 人事部门 ☆ personnel management 人事管理；key personnel 核心人员 辨析：personnel 指全体员工（不可数/可数皆可）；staff 指全体职员；employee 指单个员工
🧠 助记
person(人)+nel——与人相关的=人员/人事
personnel::/ˌpɜːrsəˈnel/:n. 人员:The company will hire additional personnel to support the new project.
3. collapse /kəˈlæps/ v./n. 崩溃；倒塌；垮台
📖 英文解释
To fall down suddenly; to fail completely; a sudden failure or breakdown.
✏️ 例句
The company's stock price collapsed following the news of the accounting scandal.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ collapse under pressure 在压力下崩溃 ★ the collapse of a system/economy 体系/经济崩溃 ☆ on the verge of collapse 濒临崩溃 辨析：collapse 强调突然全面崩溃；fall 泛指倒下；break down 指故障/失控
🧠 助记
col(一起)+lapse(滑落)——一起滑落下去=崩溃/倒塌
collapse::/kəˈlæps/:v./n. 崩溃:The bridge collapsed during the storm, fortunately with no casualties.
4. angry /ˈæŋɡri/ adj. 愤怒的；气愤的
📖 英文解释
Feeling or showing strong annoyance, displeasure, or hostility.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be angry at/with sb 对某人生气 ★ be angry about sth 对某事生气 ☆ angrily adv. 愤怒地；anger n. 愤怒 辨析：angry 表达愤怒情绪；furious 程度更强；annoyed 程度较轻
🧠 助记
ang(痛苦)+ry——感到痛苦难受=愤怒的
angry::/ˈæŋɡri/:adj. 愤怒的:The public was angry about the decision to cut funding for public schools.
5. extensive /ɪkˈstensɪv/ adj. 广泛的；大量的；大规模的
📖 英文解释
Covering or affecting a large area; of great extent; large in amount.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ extensive research/damage 广泛研究/大范围损坏 ★ extensive knowledge 渊博的知识 ☆ extensively adv. 广泛地；extension n. 延伸 辨析：extensive 强调范围广；comprehensive 强调内容全面；thorough 强调彻底
🧠 助记
extend(延伸)+ive——向四处延伸的=广泛的
extensive::/ɪkˈstensɪv/:adj. 广泛的:The earthquake caused extensive damage to the city's infrastructure.
6. resort /rɪˈzɔːrt/ v. 诉诸；采取；n. 度假地；手段
📖 英文解释
To turn to and adopt as a course of action; a place frequented for recreation.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ resort to sth 求助于/采取（常含消极意味） ★ as a last resort 作为最后手段 ☆ beach/ski resort 海滨/滑雪度假地 辨析：resort to 强调不得已而为之；turn to 中性；fall back on 强调退而用之
🧠 助记
re(又)+sort(选择)——再次寻找选择=采取（手段）
resort::/rɪˈzɔːrt/:v. 诉诸:The government resorted to emergency measures to prevent a financial crisis.

🧠 助记
assist(协助)+ance——协助的行为=帮助
assistance::/əˈsɪstəns/:n. 帮助:Humanitarian assistance was dispatched to the flood-affected regions.

in(内)+sect(切割)——身体被分节切割的小动物=昆虫
insect::/ˈɪnsekt/:n. 昆虫:Scientists are studying insects as a potential sustainable food source.

🧠 助记

metal::/ˈmetl/:n. 金属:The demand for rare metals has surged due to the growth of clean energy technology.

20. shall /ʃæl/ modal v. 将（用于第一人称）；应当（法律）；表提议

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

shall::/ʃæl/:modal v. 将（用于第一人称）:The contractor shall complete the work within the agreed timeframe.
