1. victory /ˈvɪktəri/ n. 胜利；战胜；成功
📖 英文解释
An act of defeating an opponent or enemy in a battle, game, or competition.
✏️ 例句
The team's victory in the championship was the result of years of hard training and sacrifice.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ win/achieve a victory 赢得/取得胜利 ★ a victory over sb/sth 对…的胜利 ☆ victorious adj. 胜利的；victor n. 胜利者 辨析：victory 强调胜利结果；triumph 强调辉煌胜利；success 泛指成功
🧠 助记
vict(征服)+ory——征服后的荣耀=胜利
victory::/ˈvɪktəri/:n. 胜利:The election victory gave the party a clear mandate to reform the economy.
2. feedback /ˈfiːdbæk/ n. 反馈；意见；回应
📖 英文解释
Information about reactions to a product or performance, used as a basis for improvement.
✏️ 例句
Regular feedback from students helps teachers improve the quality of their teaching.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ give/receive feedback 给予/接收反馈 ★ positive/negative feedback 积极/消极反馈 ☆ feedback loop 反馈循环；peer feedback 同伴反馈 辨析：feedback 强调信息回流改进；response 泛指回应；review 强调系统评估
🧠 助记
feed(喂入)+back(回)——把信息喂回去=反馈
feedback::/ˈfiːdbæk/:n. 反馈:The company collects customer feedback to continuously improve its services.
3. arouse /əˈraʊz/ v. 唤起；激起；唤醒
📖 英文解释
To evoke a feeling or response; to wake someone from sleep.
✏️ 例句
The documentary aroused public concern about the treatment of workers in supply chains.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ arouse interest/concern/suspicion 激起兴趣/关切/怀疑 ★ arouse sb from sleep 把某人从睡眠中唤醒 ☆ arousal n. 唤起；rouse v. 唤起（同义） 辨析：arouse 强调激发情感/注意；evoke 强调引发回忆/联想；stir 强调搅动情感
🧠 助记
a+rouse(唤醒)——唤醒或激发某种状态=唤起
arouse::/əˈraʊz/:v. 唤起:The report aroused widespread concern about air quality in major cities.
4. sad /sæd/ adj. 悲伤的；令人悲伤的；可怜的
📖 英文解释
Feeling or showing sorrow; causing or characterized by sorrow or regret.
✏️ 例句
It is sad to see so many talented graduates unable to find suitable employment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ feel sad about sth 对…感到悲伤 ★ a sad story/ending 悲伤的故事/结局 ☆ sadly adv. 遗憾地；sadness n. 悲伤；sadden v. 使悲伤 辨析：sad 中性悲伤；sorrowful 较正式；miserable 强调痛苦不幸
🧠 助记
sad = s+ad——like an ad(广告)失败了令人心酸=悲伤
sad::/sæd/:adj. 悲伤的:It is sad that so many people lack access to basic healthcare.

★ a central/recurring theme 中心/反复出现的主题 ★ theme park 主题公园 ☆ thematic adj. 主题的；theme music 主题曲 辨析：theme 强调核心思想；topic 指具体话题；subject 泛指科目/话题
🧠 助记
the+me(我)——就是关于我的核心话题=主题
theme::/θiːm/:n. 主题:The central theme of the novel is the struggle between tradition and modernity.

7. defense /dɪˈfens/ n. 防御；辩护；国防

facil(容易)+itate——使事情变容易=促进
facilitate::/fəˈsɪlɪteɪt/:v. 促进:Better infrastructure will facilitate trade between the two countries.
