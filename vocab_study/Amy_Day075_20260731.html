1. contemporary /kənˈtempəreri/ adj. 同时代的；现代的 n. 同时代的人
📖 英文解释
living or occurring at the same time; belonging to the present day
✏️ 例句
Shakespeare and his contemporaries wrote for the common people.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
contemporary art（当代艺术）；contemporary with（与…同时代）；contemporary society（当代社会）
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ tempor（时间）+ ary → 共同时间里的 → 同时代的
contemporary::/kənˈtempəreri/:adj. 同时代的:Contemporary art often challenges traditional aesthetic norms.
2. prevail /prɪˈveɪl/ v. 流行；占优势；说服
📖 英文解释
to prove more powerful; to be widespread or current; to persuade someone
✏️ 例句
Justice will prevail in the end despite all obstacles.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prevail over（战胜）；prevail upon sb to do（说服某人做）；prevailing wind（盛行风）
🧠 助记
pre（提前）+ vail（力量/vale）→ 提前展示力量 → 占优势
prevail::/prɪˈveɪl/:v. 流行:Common sense must prevail in making this decision.
3. trick /trɪk/ n. 诡计；技巧；把戏 v. 欺骗
📖 英文解释
a cunning act intended to deceive; a skillful act performed for entertainment; to deceive someone
✏️ 例句
The magician performed an amazing card trick for the audience.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
trick sb into doing（欺骗某人做）；do the trick（奏效）；bag of tricks（全套本领）
🧠 助记
trick → 谐音'踹人'→ 用计谋'踹'对手 → 诡计
trick::/trɪk/:n. 诡计:A simple change in diet can do the trick for weight management.
4. cautious /ˈkɔːʃəs/ adj. 谨慎的；小心翼翼的
📖 英文解释
making sure to avoid potential problems or dangers; careful about risks
✏️ 例句
Investors are cautious about entering volatile markets.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cautious approach（谨慎的做法）；be cautious about（对…谨慎）；cautious optimism（谨慎乐观）
🧠 助记
caut（小心/caution）+ ious → 充满小心的 → 谨慎的
cautious::/ˈkɔːʃəs/:adj. 谨慎的:The doctor advised a cautious approach to the new treatment.
5. convert /kənˈvɜːrt/ v. 转变；改变信仰；兑换 n. 改变信仰者
📖 英文解释
to change the form, character, or function of something; to change one's beliefs
✏️ 例句
The factory was converted into luxury apartments.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
convert to/into（转变为）；convert currency（兑换货币）；convert to a religion（皈依某宗教）
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ vert（转）→ 一起转变方向 → 转变
convert::/kənˈvɜːrt/:v. 转变:The company converted its production line to electric vehicles.
6. curious /ˈkjʊəriəs/ adj. 好奇的；奇特的
📖 英文解释
eager to know or learn something; strange and unusual
✏️ 例句

cur（care关心）+ ious → 对某事非常关心 → 好奇的
curious::/ˈkjʊəriəs/:adj. 好奇的:She was curious about the results of the experiment.
7. bury /ˈberi/ v. 埋；埋葬；隐藏
📖 英文解释
to place a dead body in the ground; to hide something by covering it; to become deeply absorbed
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

bury::/ˈberi/:v. 埋:She buried herself in work after the difficult breakup.
8. drought /draʊt/ n. 旱灾；干旱；缺乏
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
dr（dry干）+ ought（应该）→ 应该下雨却很干燥 → 旱灾
drought::/draʊt/:n. 旱灾:The ongoing drought has severely impacted food production.
9. pool /puːl/ n. 池塘；水潭；共用资源 v. 集中

pool::/puːl/:n. 池塘:The team pooled their ideas to solve the complex problem.
10. assure /əˈʃʊər/ v. 使确信；担保；确保

assure::/əˈʃʊər/:v. 使确信:The manager assured the team that their jobs were safe.
11. edition /ɪˈdɪʃən/ n. 版本；版次；期

12. induce /ɪnˈdjuːs/ v. 引诱；引起；诱导
