1. universal /ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsəl/ adj. 普遍的；通用的；宇宙的
📖 英文解释
relating to or applicable to all things; present or done everywhere
✏️ 例句
Access to clean water should be a universal right.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
universal truth（普遍真理）；universal design（通用设计）；universal healthcare（全民医疗）
🧠 助记
uni（一个）+ versal（转/verse）→ 转向所有人的 → 普遍的
universal::/ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsəl/:adj. 普遍的:The desire for happiness is a universal human experience.
2. react /riˈækt/ v. 反应；起反应；反抗
📖 英文解释
to respond to something in a particular way; to undergo a chemical reaction
✏️ 例句
People react differently to stressful situations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
react to（对…做出反应）；react against（反抗）；quick to react（反应迅速）
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ act（行动）→ 再次行动 → 对刺激作出反应
react::/riˈækt/:v. 反应:The stock market reacted negatively to the unexpected announcement.
3. representative /ˌreprɪˈzentətɪv/ n. 代表；代理人 adj. 有代表性的
📖 英文解释
a person chosen to act or speak for others; typical of a class or group
✏️ 例句
She was elected as the student representative on the board.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sales representative（销售代表）；be representative of（代表…）；elected representative（民选代表）
🧠 助记
re + present（呈现）+ ative → 代表他人呈现意见的人 → 代表
representative::/ˌreprɪˈzentətɪv/:n. 代表:The survey sample must be representative of the general population.
4. substantial /səbˈstænʃəl/ adj. 大量的；实质的；坚固的
📖 英文解释
of considerable importance, size, or worth; strongly built; relating to the essential nature
✏️ 例句
The company made a substantial profit in the first quarter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
substantial evidence（充足证据）；substantial progress（重大进展）；substantial amount（大量）
🧠 助记
sub（在下面）+ stanc（站立）+ ial → 站得住脚的、有实质的 → 大量的
substantial::/səbˈstænʃəl/:adj. 大量的:There is substantial evidence that diet affects mental health.
5. qualification /ˌkwɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ n. 资格；条件；资历
📖 英文解释
a quality or achievement that makes someone suitable for a job; an official certificate
✏️ 例句
A degree is a basic qualification for most professional positions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
academic qualification（学历资格）；meet the qualifications（符合资格）；without qualification（无条件地）
🧠 助记
qualify（有资格）+ cation → 达到资格的过程/证明 → 资格
qualification::/ˌkwɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/:n. 资格:She gained professional qualifications after completing the training course.
6. retain /rɪˈteɪn/ v. 保留；保持；记住
📖 英文解释
to continue to have something; to keep in one's memory; to employ a lawyer
✏️ 例句
The company worked hard to retain its best employees.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
retain control（保持控制）；retain information（记住信息）；customer retention（客户留存）
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ tain（抓住/hold）→ 再次抓住不放 → 保留
retain::/rɪˈteɪn/:v. 保留:It is difficult to retain new vocabulary without regular practice.
7. mouth /maʊθ/ n. 嘴；入口；河口 v. 用嘴说
📖 英文解释
the opening in the face used for eating and speaking; the opening or entrance of something
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
word of mouth（口耳相传）；keep one's mouth shut（保持沉默）；mouth-watering（令人垂涎的）
🧠 助记
mouth → 发音时嘴巴张开 → 嘴巴
mouth::/maʊθ/:n. 嘴:The restaurant became famous through word of mouth alone.
8. poll /pəʊl/ n. 民意调查；投票 v. 对…进行调查

poll::/pəʊl/:n. 民意调查:A recent poll revealed that most citizens support the new policy.
9. via /ˈvaɪə/ prep. 经过；通过；凭借
📖 英文解释
traveling through a place on the way to a destination; by means of
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
via email（通过电子邮件）；via satellite（通过卫星）；via the internet（通过互联网）
🧠 助记
via → 来自拉丁语via（路/方式）→ 经过某条路 → 经过、通过
via::/ˈvaɪə/:prep. 经过:The information was shared via the company's internal communication platform.
10. myth /mɪθ/ n. 神话；错误的观念；虚构的人物
📖 英文解释
a traditional story about ancient history or the supernatural; a widely held but false belief
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
myth → 谐音'迷斯'→ 迷人的故事（神话）→ 神话；虚构的谬误
myth::/mɪθ/:n. 神话:Scientists have worked hard to debunk common myths about nutrition.
11. stability /stəˈbɪlɪti/ n. 稳定；稳固；坚定
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

stability::/stəˈbɪlɪti/:n. 稳定:The new government's priority was to restore economic stability.
12. taxi /ˈtæksi/ n. 出租车 v. 乘出租车；滑行

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

taxi::/ˈtæksi/:n. 出租车 v. 乘出租车:The aircraft taxied slowly toward the departure gate.
13. slip /slɪp/ v. 滑；溜走；下降 n. 失误；小错

slip::/slɪp/:v. 滑:A small slip in judgment led to serious consequences for the team.
