1. dismiss /dɪsˈmɪs/ v. 解散；驳回；开除；不予理会
📖 英文解释
to order to leave; to regard as unworthy; to discharge from employment
✏️ 例句
The judge dismissed the case due to lack of evidence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dismiss an idea（驳回想法）；dismiss sb from work（解雇某人）；dismiss out of hand（断然拒绝）
🧠 助记
dis（否定）+ miss（错过/发送）→ 不让你留下 → 解散；开除
dismiss::/dɪsˈmɪs/:v. 解散:The committee dismissed the proposal without proper consideration.
2. reinforce /ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrs/ v. 加强；强化；增援
📖 英文解释
to strengthen or support; to make more forceful; to send additional troops
✏️ 例句
Regular practice reinforces the vocabulary learned in class.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reinforce a message（强化信息）；reinforce beliefs（强化信念）；reinforced concrete（钢筋混凝土）
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ in + force（力量）→ 再次加入力量 → 加强
reinforce::/ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrs/:v. 加强:The new data reinforced the researchers' original hypothesis.
3. bother /ˈbɒðər/ v. 打扰；烦恼；不介意 n. 麻烦
📖 英文解释
to cause trouble or inconvenience; to worry or upset; to take the trouble to do
✏️ 例句
She didn't bother to read the instructions before using the device.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
don't bother（不用麻烦）；bother sb with（以…打扰某人）；can't be bothered（懒得做）
🧠 助记
bother → 谐音'不舍'→ 令人心烦不舍得面对的事 → 打扰、烦恼
bother::/ˈbɒðər/:v. 打扰:Don't bother calling; just come directly to the office.
4. overcome /ˌəʊvəˈkʌm/ v. 克服；压倒；战胜
📖 英文解释
to succeed in dealing with a problem; to defeat an opponent; to be overwhelmed
✏️ 例句
She overcame numerous obstacles to achieve her goals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
overcome difficulties（克服困难）；overcome fear（克服恐惧）；be overcome by（被…压倒）
🧠 助记
over（超越）+ come（来到）→ 超越困难来到前方 → 克服
overcome::/ˌəʊvəˈkʌm/:v. 克服:With determination, anyone can overcome seemingly impossible challenges.
5. academy /əˈkædəmi/ n. 学院；学会；专科学校

a place of study or training; a society for the promotion of learning or art

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
academy → 来自古希腊哲学家Academus的花园 → 学院
academy::/əˈkædəmi/:n. 学院:The film won the Academy Award for Best Picture.

supermarket::/ˈsuːpəmɑːkɪt/:n. 超级市场:The new supermarket opened near our neighborhood last month.

ambitious::/æmˈbɪʃəs/:adj. 有雄心的:The government announced an ambitious plan to achieve carbon neutrality by 2060.

10. accumulate /əˈkjuːmjəleɪt/ v. 积累；堆积；积聚
