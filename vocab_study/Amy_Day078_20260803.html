1. occupy /ˈɒkjupaɪ/ v. 占据；占领；使忙碌
📖 英文解释
To fill or take up space or time; to take control of a place by force; to keep someone busy.
✏️ 例句
Social media increasingly occupies a large portion of young people's time and attention.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ occupy one's time / mind 占用时间/心神 ★ be occupied with/in sth 忙于某事 ★ occupation n. 职业；占领；occupant n. 占用者
🧠 助记
oc（+）+ cupy（cup杯子）——把杯子里的空间全占满，就是占据
occupy::/ˈɒkjupaɪ/:v. 占据:Meetings occupy most of her working day.
2. distribute /dɪˈstrɪbjuːt/ v. 分发；分配；分布
📖 英文解释
To give or supply things to a number of people or places; to spread something over an area.
✏️ 例句
Aid organizations distributed food and medicine to thousands of refugees in the affected region.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ distribute sth to/among sb 把某物分发给某人 ★ distribution n. 分配；分布（income distribution 收入分配） ★ distributor n. 分销商 辨析：distribute / allocate：distribute 侧重实物分发；allocate 侧重资源正式分配
🧠 助记
dis（分散）+ tribute（给予）——分散给予，就是分发
distribute::/dɪˈstrɪbjuːt/:v. 分发:The teacher distributed the exam papers to students.
3. phase /feɪz/ n. 阶段；时期；相位；v. 分阶段进行
📖 英文解释
A distinct period or stage in a process of change or development.
✏️ 例句
The construction project will be carried out in three phases over the next five years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in the early / final phase 在早期/最后阶段 ★ phase in / out 逐步引进/淘汰（高频） ★ a new phase of development 新发展阶段
🧠 助记
phase 谐音「飞速」——飞速变化中的一个阶段
phase::/feɪz/:n. 阶段:We are now entering a new phase of the project.
4. intensive /ɪnˈtensɪv/ adj. 密集的；加强的；深入的
📖 英文解释
Concentrated and thorough; involving a lot of effort or activity within a short time.
✏️ 例句
Students underwent an intensive training program before the national competition.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ intensive care 重症监护（ICU） ★ intensive course / training 强化课程/培训 ★ labor-intensive / capital-intensive 劳动/资本密集型 辨析：intensive / extensive：intensive 深度集中；extensive 广度广泛
🧠 助记
intense（强烈）+ ive——强烈深入的，就是密集的
intensive::/ɪnˈtensɪv/:adj. 密集的:She did an intensive revision before the exam.
5. revise /rɪˈvaɪz/ v. 修改；修订；复习
📖 英文解释
To examine and make corrections or improvements to something; to study again for an exam.
✏️ 例句
The author was asked to revise her manuscript before the publishing house would consider it.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ revise one's opinion / plan 修改意见/计划 ★ revise for an exam 备考复习（英式英语） ★ revision n. 修订；复习 ★ revised edition 修订版
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ vise（看，见vision）——再看一遍，就是修改/复习
revise::/rɪˈvaɪz/:v. 修改:Please revise your essay before submitting it.
6. straight /streɪt/ adj. 直的；笔直的；直接的；adv. 直接地；立刻
📖 英文解释
Extending without a curve or bend; honest and direct; without delay.
✏️ 例句
She looked straight into his eyes and told him the truth without hesitation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ go straight 走正路；直走 ★ straight away / off 立刻，马上（口语） ★ straightforward adj. 直接的，简单的（写作替换 simple）
🧠 助记
straight 谐音「直它」——拉直了它，就是straight
straight::/streɪt/:adj. 直的:Draw a straight line from point A to point B.
7. exceed /ɪkˈsiːd/ v. 超过；超出；胜过
📖 英文解释
To go beyond the permitted limits of something; to be greater than or superior to.
✏️ 例句
Carbon emissions continue to exceed the safe levels recommended by environmental scientists.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ exceed the limit / expectation 超出限制/预期（写作高频） ★ exceed in 在……方面超过 ★ excess n. 超出，过量；excessive adj. 过度的 辨析：exceed / surpass：exceed 侧重超过标准/数量；surpass 侧重能力上的超越
🧠 助记
ex（超出）+ ceed（走，见proceed）——走出界限之外，就是超过
exceed::/ɪkˈsiːd/:v. 超过:Demand for housing exceeds supply in many cities.
8. glance /ɡlɑːns/ v./n. 瞥一眼；匆匆一看
📖 英文解释
To look quickly at someone or something; a brief or hurried look.
✏️ 例句
He glanced at his watch and realized he was already ten minutes late for the meeting.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at a glance 一眼就……（at a glance, it seems...） ★ glance at 瞥一眼 ★ at first glance 乍一看（写作引入观点常用） 辨析：glance / glimpse：glance 主动快速扫视；glimpse 短暂看到（可能无意）
🧠 助记
glance 像 lance（长矛）扫过——视线如长矛飞快扫过，就是瞥一眼
glance::/ɡlɑːns/:v./n. 瞥一眼:At first glance, the problem seems simple.
9. implement /ˈɪmplɪment/ (n.) /ˈɪmplɪment/ (v.) v. 实施；执行；n. 工具；器具
📖 英文解释
To put a plan, decision, or agreement into effect; a tool or instrument used for a purpose.
✏️ 例句
The government announced plans to implement new environmental regulations by the end of the year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ implement a policy / plan / strategy 实施政策/计划/策略（写作必备） ★ implementation n. 实施，执行 ★ 近义：carry out, execute, enforce
🧠 助记
im（进入）+ plement（填充/填满）——把计划填满落实进去，就是实施
implement::/ˈɪmplɪment/ (n.) /ˈɪmplɪment/ (v.):v. 实施:The school implemented a new teaching method.
10. chronic /ˈkrɒnɪk/ adj. 慢性的；长期的；积习难改的
📖 英文解释
Persisting for a long time or constantly recurring; having a long-lasting condition.
✏️ 例句
Chronic stress is increasingly recognized as a major contributor to cardiovascular disease.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ chronic disease / illness 慢性病（阅读高频） ★ chronic poverty / unemployment 长期贫困/失业 ★ 反义：acute 急性的；短暂的
🧠 助记
chron（time时间）+ ic——与时间有关的，拖很长时间的，慢性的
chronic::/ˈkrɒnɪk/:adj. 慢性的:She has suffered from chronic back pain for years.
11. cup /kʌp/ n. 杯子；奖杯；一杯（的量）

cup 形状就像杯子——圆口容器，简单好记
cup::/kʌp/:n. 杯子:She drank a cup of tea every morning.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pension fund / scheme 养老基金/方案 ★ state pension 国家养老金 ★ pensioner n. 领养老金者 ★ retirement pension 退休金

pension::/ˈpenʃn/:n. 养老金:He receives a state pension after retiring at 65.
13. storm /stɔːm/ n. 暴风雨；风暴；v. 猛攻；怒冲

A violent disturbance of the atmosphere with strong winds and rain; to move angrily or forcefully.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a storm of protest / criticism 一阵抗议/批评浪潮 ★ brainstorm 头脑风暴 ★ storm into 怒气冲冲地闯入 ★ take ... by storm 迅速征服……
🧠 助记
storm 谐音「死闯」——死命闯进来，如暴风雨一样猛烈
storm::/stɔːm/:n. 暴风雨:A storm of protest followed the announcement.
14. sink /sɪŋk/ v. 下沉；沉没；降低；n. 水槽

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ sink into despair / debt 陷入绝望/债务 ★ sink or swim 自生自灭，靠自己 ★ sinking feeling 沉重/不祥的感觉 辨析：sink / decline：sink 更强调急速下跌；decline 为中性缓慢下降
🧠 助记
sink 谐音「沉咯」——沉咯，往下沉！
sink::/sɪŋk/:v. 下沉:The economy began to sink during the crisis.

★ be beneficial to sb/sth 对……有益（写作高频） ★ mutually beneficial 互利的（mutual benefit） ★ benefit n./v. 好处；受益 辨析：beneficial / helpful：beneficial 较正式，强调实质性好处；helpful 更口语化
🧠 助记

beneficial::/ˌbenɪˈfɪʃl/:adj. 有益的:Exercise is beneficial to your health.
