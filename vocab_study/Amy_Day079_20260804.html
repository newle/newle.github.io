1. storage /ˈstɔːrɪdʒ/ n. 储存；存储；仓储
📖 英文解释
The action of storing something for future use; space for storing things.
✏️ 例句
Cloud storage has revolutionized the way businesses manage and share large amounts of data.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ data storage 数据存储（科技阅读高频） ★ storage space / capacity 存储空间/容量 ★ cold storage 冷藏；in cold storage 搁置（比喻） ★ store v. 储存；store n. 商店
🧠 助记
store（储存）+ age（状态）——储存的那个状态，就是storage
storage::/ˈstɔːrɪdʒ/:n. 储存:The company upgraded its data storage system.
2. commodity /kəˈmɒdɪti/ n. 商品；大宗商品；有用之物
📖 英文解释
A raw material or primary agricultural product that can be bought and sold; a useful or valuable thing.
✏️ 例句
Water is increasingly being treated as a commodity to be bought and sold rather than a public good.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ commodity market / prices 大宗商品市场/价格（经济阅读高频） ★ commodity trade 商品贸易 ★ treat sth as a commodity 把……当作商品（写作批判用法）
🧠 助记
commod（common普通）+ ity——普通的可以买卖的东西，就是商品
commodity::/kəˈmɒdɪti/:n. 商品:Oil is one of the world's most traded commodities.
3. negotiate /nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt/ v. 谈判；协商；克服（障碍）
📖 英文解释
To discuss something in order to reach an agreement; to find a way through a difficult path.
✏️ 例句
The two sides agreed to negotiate a peaceful solution to the territorial dispute.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ negotiate a deal / treaty / settlement 谈判达成协议/条约/和解 ★ negotiation n. 谈判（under negotiation 谈判中） ★ negotiator n. 谈判者
🧠 助记
negoti（negotium事务）+ ate——处理事务性谈判，就是协商
negotiate::/nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt/:v. 谈判:The union and management agreed to negotiate.
4. compromise /ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/ n./v. 妥协；折中；损害
📖 英文解释
An agreement reached by each side making concessions; to accept standards that are lower than desirable.
✏️ 例句
The two parties finally reached a compromise that satisfied neither side completely but was workable.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ reach a compromise 达成妥协（写作高频） ★ compromise on sth 在……上妥协 ★ compromise sb's safety / integrity 损害某人的安全/诚信 ★ without compromise 毫不妥协
🧠 助记
com（共同）+ promise（承诺）——双方共同作出承诺，各退一步，就是妥协
compromise::/ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/:n./v. 妥协:Both sides had to compromise to reach an agreement.
5. endure /ɪnˈdjʊə(r)/ v. 忍受；承受；持续
📖 英文解释
To suffer something difficult or unpleasant without giving in; to continue to exist over a long time.
✏️ 例句
The ancient city has endured countless wars and natural disasters throughout its long history.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ endure hardship / pain / suffering 忍受苦难/痛苦 ★ enduring adj. 持久的（an enduring legacy 持久的遗产） ★ endurance n. 忍耐力；持久力 辨析：endure / tolerate：endure 侧重被动承受；tolerate 侧重主动接受
🧠 助记
en（使）+ dure（硬，持久）——使自己变硬，坚持下去，就是忍受
endure::/ɪnˈdjʊə(r)/:v. 忍受:The survivors had to endure weeks without food.
6. immune /ɪˈmjuːn/ adj. 免疫的；不受影响的；豁免的
📖 英文解释
Protected against a particular disease; not affected by something; exempt from an obligation.
✏️ 例句
No country is immune to the effects of global climate change, regardless of its level of development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ immune system 免疫系统（医学阅读高频） ★ be immune to sth 对……免疫/不受……影响 ★ immunity n. 免疫力；豁免权（diplomatic immunity 外交豁免权）
🧠 助记
im（不）+ mune（munus义务）——不承担义务，就是豁免，引申为免疫
immune::/ɪˈmjuːn/:adj. 免疫的:No society is immune to the effects of inequality.
7. pleasant /ˈpleznt/ adj. 令人愉快的；舒适的；和蔼的
📖 英文解释
Giving a sense of happy satisfaction or enjoyment; friendly and likeable.
✏️ 例句
The conference provided a pleasant opportunity for researchers from different countries to exchange ideas.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a pleasant experience / surprise 愉快的经历/惊喜 ★ pleasant weather / atmosphere 宜人的天气/氛围 ★ pleasantly adv. 令人愉快地 辨析：pleasant / pleasing：pleasant 侧重整体感受；pleasing 侧重令他人满意
🧠 助记
pleas（please取悦）+ ant——能取悦人的，令人愉快的
pleasant::/ˈpleznt/:adj. 令人愉快的:We had a very pleasant afternoon in the park.
8. celebrate /ˈselɪbreɪt/ v. 庆祝；颂扬；举行（仪式）
📖 英文解释
To do something enjoyable on a special occasion; to publicly acknowledge a significant event.
✏️ 例句
People around the world gathered to celebrate the new year with fireworks and festivities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ celebrate a victory / birthday / achievement 庆祝胜利/生日/成就 ★ celebrated adj. 著名的（a celebrated writer） ★ celebration n. 庆典；celebration of culture 文化庆典
🧠 助记
celeb（celebrity名人）+ rate——名人出场的仪式，就是庆典/庆祝
celebrate::/ˈselɪbreɪt/:v. 庆祝:The country celebrated its 100th anniversary of independence.
9. command /kəˈmɑːnd/ v. 命令；指挥；拥有；n. 命令；指挥；掌握
📖 英文解释
To give an authoritative order; to be in charge of; to have at one's disposal.
✏️ 例句
A good leader must be able to command respect through actions rather than simply by authority.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ command respect / attention 赢得尊重/吸引注意（写作高频） ★ be in command of 指挥；掌握 ★ command of a language 对语言的掌握（good command of English）
🧠 助记
com（完全）+ mand（mandare下令）——完全掌握并下令，就是命令/指挥
command::/kəˈmɑːnd/:v. 命令:The officer commanded his troops to advance.
10. span /spæn/ n. 跨度；一段时间；v. 跨越；涵盖
📖 英文解释
The full extent of something from end to end; to extend across a whole area or period.
✏️ 例句
The novel spans three generations of a family, tracing the changes in Chinese society over a century.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ life span 寿命（与attention span 注意力持续时间区分） ★ span of time 一段时间 ★ span across / over 跨越…… ★ time span 时间跨度
🧠 助记
span 谐音「跨嗯」——嗯，跨过去了！就是跨越/跨度
span::/spæn/:n. 跨度:Her career spanned more than four decades.
11. norm /nɔːm/ n. 标准；规范；常态
📖 英文解释
Something that is usual, typical, or standard; a social rule or standard of behavior.
✏️ 例句
Working from home has become the norm for many professionals since the pandemic.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ social norms 社会规范（阅读高频） ★ cultural norms 文化规范 ★ the norm 常态（become the norm 成为常态） ★ normal adj. 正常的；normalize v. 使正常化
🧠 助记
norm 谐音「标准」——社会的标准，就是norm
norm::/nɔːm/:n. 标准:Overtime work has become the norm in this industry.
12. hazard /ˈhæzəd/ n. 危险；危害；冒险；v. 冒险提出
📖 英文解释
A danger or risk; something likely to cause damage or harm.
✏️ 例句
Prolonged exposure to screen light poses a significant health hazard to the eyes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ health / fire / safety hazard 健康/火灾/安全隐患（阅读高频） ★ environmental hazard 环境危害 ★ hazardous adj. 危险的（hazardous waste 危险废物） 辨析：hazard / risk：hazard 更具体，指已存在的危险；risk 更抽象，指可能性
🧠 助记
hazard 谐音「害怕的」——让人感到害怕的危险
hazard::/ˈhæzəd/:n. 危险:Driving while tired is a serious traffic hazard.
13. joint /dʒɔɪnt/ adj. 联合的；共同的；n. 关节；接合处；v. 连接
📖 英文解释
Shared, held, or made by two or more people; a place where two things are joined; a body part.
✏️ 例句
The two universities launched a joint research program to study the effects of air pollution.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ joint effort / venture / statement 共同努力/合资企业/联合声明（写作高频） ★ joint account 联名账户 ★ out of joint 脱臼；混乱（比喻）
🧠 助记
joint = join（连接）+ t——两者连接在一起的地方，就是关节/联合
joint::/dʒɔɪnt/:adj. 联合的:The project was a joint effort between two universities.
14. clerk /klɑːk/ n. 职员；书记员；营业员
📖 英文解释
A person employed in an office or bank to keep records; a person who serves customers in a shop.
✏️ 例句
The bank clerk noticed an unusual pattern in the transactions and reported it to the manager.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ bank clerk 银行职员 ★ sales clerk 营业员（美式） ★ clerk of court 法庭书记员（法律阅读）
🧠 助记
clerk 谐音「可乐客」——给客人倒可乐的办公室服务人员，就是职员
clerk::/klɑːk/:n. 职员:The clerk filed all the documents carefully.
15. arrest /əˈrest/ v. 逮捕；阻止；吸引（注意）；n. 逮捕
📖 英文解释
To take someone into custody by authority of law; to stop or check; to attract someone's attention.
✏️ 例句
The documentary arrested the attention of audiences worldwide with its powerful visual storytelling.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ arrest sb for sth 因……逮捕某人 ★ under arrest 被逮捕 ★ arrest the spread of disease 阻止疾病蔓延（非法律语境） ★ arrest sb's attention 吸引注意（写作用法）
🧠 助记
ar（到）+ rest（停下）——让你停下来，就是逮捕/阻止
arrest::/əˈrest/:v. 逮捕:The police arrested the suspect near the crime scene.
16. dictionary /ˈdɪkʃənri/ n. 词典；字典

A book or electronic resource that lists words alphabetically and gives their meanings, pronunciation, and usage.
✏️ 例句
Using a good dictionary is still one of the most effective ways to build vocabulary over time.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ look up a word in the dictionary 在词典中查一个词 ★ bilingual dictionary 双语词典 ★ online / digital dictionary 在线/电子词典
🧠 助记
dict（说/言语）+ ionary——记录所有言语的书，就是词典
dictionary::/ˈdɪkʃənri/:n. 词典:She always carries a pocket dictionary.
17. hate /heɪt/ v. 憎恨；厌恶；n. 仇恨
📖 英文解释
To feel intense dislike or extreme aversion toward someone or something.
✏️ 例句
Hate speech poses a serious threat to social cohesion and democratic values in any society.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ hate speech 仇恨言论（社会议题高频） ★ hate crime 仇恨犯罪 ★ hatred n. 憎恨（intense hatred） 辨析：hate / dislike：hate 程度强烈；dislike 较温和
🧠 助记
hate 谐音「害它」——想伤害它，就是仇恨
hate::/heɪt/:v. 憎恨:Hate speech should not be tolerated in public discourse.
18. magic /ˈmædʒɪk/ n. 魔法；魔力；adj. 神奇的，有魔力的
📖 英文解释
The power to use supernatural forces; the art of producing illusions; a mysterious and enchanting quality.
✏️ 例句
There is no magic solution to the problem of inequality; it requires sustained structural reform.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ magic solution / formula 魔法解决方案/公式（否定用法常见） ★ magic wand 魔杖（wave a magic wand 一挥魔杖解决问题） ★ magical adj. 神奇的；magician n. 魔术师

magic 谐音「卖迹」——卖弄奇迹，就是魔法
magic::/ˈmædʒɪk/:n. 魔法:There is no magic formula for success.
19. numerous /ˈnjuːmərəs/ adj. 众多的；许多的
📖 英文解释
Great in number; many.
✏️ 例句
Numerous studies have confirmed the link between smoking and various types of cancer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on numerous occasions 在许多场合（正式写作替换 many times） ★ numerous examples / factors 众多例子/因素 ★ number n. 数字；数量；numerous 是形容词形式

numer（number数字）+ ous——数字很多的，就是众多的
numerous::/ˈnjuːmərəs/:adj. 众多的:Numerous studies support this conclusion.

★ shoot a film / photo 拍摄电影/照片 ★ shoot up 急速上升（prices shot up 价格飙升） ★ shoot down 击落；驳斥（shoot down an idea）

shoot 谐音「数它」——数着拍下去，就是射击/拍摄
shoot::/ʃuːt/:v. 射击:The director will shoot the final scene tomorrow.
