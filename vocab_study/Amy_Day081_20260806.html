1. assembly /əˈsembli/ n. 集会，集合；装配；议会
📖 英文解释
A group of people gathered together; the process of fitting parts together; a legislative body.
✏️ 例句
The principal addressed students at the morning assembly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ school assembly 晨会，集会 ★ assembly line 流水线，装配线 ★ National Assembly 国民议会 ☆ freedom of assembly 集会自由（政治话题） 辨析：assembly / meeting：assembly 更正式，常指机构性集会
🧠 助记
assemble(集合)+y → 召集在一起 → 集会；装配
assembly::/əˈsembli/:n. 集会，集合:The assembly line was shut down for maintenance.
2. rid /rɪd/ v. 使摆脱，清除；get rid of 处置，除掉
📖 英文解释
To free from something unwanted; to remove or eliminate.
✏️ 例句
It is difficult to get rid of deeply ingrained habits.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ get rid of sth/sb 摆脱，处理掉（最常考搭配） ★ rid sb/sth of sth 使某人/物摆脱某事 ☆ be rid of 已摆脱（be glad to be rid of sth 很高兴摆脱了…） 辨析：get rid of / remove / eliminate：rid 口语化；eliminate 书面正式
🧠 助记
rid → r+id(身份证) → 撕掉身份证 → 摆脱，除掉
rid::/rɪd/:v. 使摆脱，清除:We need to get rid of unnecessary regulations.
3. upper /ˈʌpə(r)/ adj. 上面的，较高的；上层的；n. 上部
📖 英文解释
Situated above or higher; relating to a higher position or rank.
✏️ 例句
Income inequality between upper and lower classes has widened in recent years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ upper class 上层阶级 ★ upper hand 优势（have/gain the upper hand） ★ upper limit 上限 ☆ upper body 上半身；upper house 上议院 辨析：upper / higher / top：upper 相对位置高；higher 程度高；top 最高级
🧠 助记
up(上)+per → 往上的 → 上面的，上层的
upper::/ˈʌpə(r)/:adj. 上面的，较高的:She gained the upper hand in the negotiation.
4. pet /pet/ n. 宠物；adj. 最喜爱的；v. 爱抚，抚摸
📖 英文解释
A domestic animal kept for companionship; to touch gently; a person's favorite.
✏️ 例句
Having a pet can reduce stress and provide emotional comfort.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ keep / own a pet 养宠物 ★ pet project / theory 最钟爱的项目/理论 ★ teacher's pet 老师的宠儿（贬义） ☆ pet peeve 最讨厌的事（固定短语） 辨析：pet / favorite：pet 有溺爱含义；favorite 是最喜欢的
🧠 助记
pet → p(爸爸)+et(爱它) → 爸爸爱它 → 宠物
pet::/pet/:n. 宠物:Her pet dog follows her everywhere.
5. dish /dɪʃ/ n. 菜肴；盘子；碟形天线；v. 盛菜
📖 英文解释
A particular type of food prepared in a certain way; a flat container used for cooking or serving food.
✏️ 例句
This restaurant's signature dish is a classic blend of local and international flavors.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ do/wash the dishes 洗碗 ★ a dish of sth 一盘… ★ satellite dish 卫星天线 ☆ main dish / side dish 主菜/配菜 辨析：dish / plate / bowl：dish 可指菜肴本身；plate 是平盘；bowl 是碗
🧠 助记
dish → d+ish → 谐音"递上"→ 端上来的菜肴
dish::/dɪʃ/:n. 菜肴:She prepared a delicious local dish for the guests.
6. chart /tʃɑːt/ n. 图表，图；排行榜；v. 绘制图表；记录
📖 英文解释
A sheet showing information in the form of diagrams or tables; to record or track systematically.
✏️ 例句
The bar chart clearly illustrates the rise in global temperatures over decades.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ bar/pie/flow chart 柱状图/饼图/流程图 ★ chart the progress 记录进程 ★ at the top of the charts 在排行榜上名列前茅 ☆ chart a course 规划路线（比喻：chart a new course 规划新方向） 辨析：chart / graph / table：chart 泛指图表；graph 侧重坐标图；table 是表格
🧠 助记
chart → char(字符)+t → 用字符画出来的图 → 图表
chart::/tʃɑːt/:n. 图表，图:The chart shows a steady increase in sales.
7. arrival /əˈraɪvl/ n. 到达，抵达；到来；新来者
📖 英文解释
The act of reaching a destination; the coming of something new; a person who has just arrived.
✏️ 例句
The arrival of the internet has transformed how people communicate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on arrival 一到达（on arrival, please sign in） ★ the arrival of sth 某物的出现/到来（技术/思想） ★ new arrival 新来者；（商店）新品 ☆ await sb's arrival 等待某人到来（作文正式表达） 辨析：arrival / coming：arrival 更正式；coming 更口语
🧠 助记
arrive+al → 到达的状态/动作 → 到达，抵达
arrival::/əˈraɪvl/:n. 到达，抵达:His arrival at the scene changed everything.
8. kingdom /ˈkɪŋdəm/ n. 王国；（动植物）界
📖 英文解释
A country ruled by a king or queen; a sphere of activity or knowledge.
✏️ 例句
The animal kingdom encompasses a vast diversity of species.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the United Kingdom 英国 ★ the animal / plant kingdom 动物/植物界 ★ kingdom of heaven 天国（宗教） ☆ a kingdom of one's own 自己的领地（比喻） 辨析：kingdom / empire：kingdom 由国王统治；empire 由皇帝统治，范围更大
🧠 助记
king(国王)+dom(领域) → 国王的领域 → 王国
kingdom::/ˈkɪŋdəm/:n. 王国:The United Kingdom is made up of four nations.
9. compose /kəmˈpəʊz/ v. 组成，构成；创作（音乐/文章）；使平静
📖 英文解释
To form or make up a whole; to create music or writing; to make oneself calm.
✏️ 例句
Water is composed of hydrogen and oxygen molecules.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be composed of 由…组成（= consist of，但被动语态） ★ compose music / a symphony 创作音乐 ★ compose oneself 使自己平静下来 ☆ composer n. 作曲家；composition n. 作文；成分；作品 辨析：compose / comprise：compose 强调部分构成整体；comprise 强调整体包含部分
🧠 助记
com(一起)+pose(放置) → 把各部分放在一起 → 组成；创作
compose::/kəmˈpəʊz/:v. 组成，构成:The committee is composed of ten members.

🧠 助记
ready(准备好)+ily → 一直准备好了 → 乐意地；容易地
readily::/ˈredɪli/:adv. 乐意地:She readily agreed to help with the project.

cloud::/klaʊd/:n. 云:Financial problems clouded their future plans.
12. interfere /ˌɪntəˈfɪə(r)/ v. 干涉，干预；妨碍，阻碍
📖 英文解释

★ interfere in/with sth 干涉某事；妨碍某事 ★ interfere with sb's rights 侵犯某人的权利 ☆ interference n. 干涉，干扰（无线电干扰） 辨析：interfere / intervene：interfere 含负面意义（不必要干预）；intervene 可为积极介入

interfere::/ˌɪntəˈfɪə(r)/:v. 干涉，干预:Government should not interfere in private businesses.
