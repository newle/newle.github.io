1. violent /ˈvaɪələnt/ adj. 暴力的；强烈的，剧烈的
📖 英文解释
Using or involving great physical force; very strong and intense.
✏️ 例句
Exposure to violent media may influence aggressive behavior in children.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ violent crime / conflict 暴力犯罪/冲突 ★ violent storm / reaction 强烈风暴/反应 ★ violence n. 暴力；violently adv. ☆ non-violent protest 非暴力抗议 辨析：violent / fierce / aggressive：violent 强调暴力；fierce 强调猛烈；aggressive 主动攻击性
🧠 助记
viol(违)+ent → 违背道德规范的行为 → 暴力的
violent::/ˈvaɪələnt/:adj. 暴力的:Violent crime has decreased in many cities.
2. bore /bɔː(r)/ v. 使厌烦；钻孔；n. 令人厌烦的人/事
📖 英文解释
To make someone feel uninterested or tedious; to make a hole by drilling; an uninteresting person or thing.
✏️ 例句
The lengthy lecture bored most students, who had difficulty staying awake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ bore sb to death / tears 把某人烦死 ★ bored adj. 感到厌烦的；boring adj. 令人厌烦的 ★ bore a hole 钻孔 ☆ boredom n. 无聊，厌倦 辨析：bored / boring：bored 是主观感受；boring 是客观描述
🧠 助记
bore → b+ore → 矿石(ore)一样枯燥无聊 → 使厌烦
bore::/bɔː(r)/:v. 使厌烦:Repetitive tasks can bore workers and reduce productivity.

🧠 助记
up(向上)+date(日期) → 日期往上提 → 更新到最新

ton::/tʌn/:n. 吨:Millions of tons of plastic enter the ocean each year.
