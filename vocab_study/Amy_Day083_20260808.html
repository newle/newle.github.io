1. fatigue /fəˈtiːɡ/ n. 疲劳，疲倦；v. 使疲劳
📖 英文解释
Extreme tiredness resulting from mental or physical exertion; to cause weariness.
✏️ 例句
Chronic fatigue can seriously impair a person's cognitive performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ mental / physical fatigue 心理/体力疲劳 ★ battle fatigue 战斗疲劳症（PTSD早期说法） ★ compassion fatigue 同情疲劳（对频繁悲剧麻木） ☆ fatigue fracture 疲劳性骨折（医学） 辨析：fatigue / exhaustion / tiredness：fatigue 偏医学/书面；exhaustion 极度耗尽；tiredness 日常表达
🧠 助记
fat(胖)+igue → 脂肪(fat)积聚后的沉重感 → 疲劳
fatigue::/fəˈtiːɡ/:n. 疲劳，疲倦:Long working hours lead to serious mental fatigue.
