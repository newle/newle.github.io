1. exploit /ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ v. 开发，利用；剥削；n. 功绩
📖 英文解释
To use or develop resources for profit; to take advantage of unfairly.
✏️ 例句
Companies that exploit natural resources must take responsibility for environmental impact.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ exploit resources/opportunities 开发资源/利用机会 ★ exploit workers 剥削工人(负面含义) ☆ exploitation n. 开发；剥削
🧠 助记
ex(出) + ploit(利用) = 把价值榨取出来 = 开发/剥削
exploit::/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/:v. 开发，利用:Some companies exploit cheap labor to maximize profits.
2. gesture /ˈdʒestʃə(r)/ n. 手势；姿态；示意行为；v. 做手势
📖 英文解释
A movement of the body to express an idea; an action done as a sign of intention.
✏️ 例句
He made a gesture of goodwill by donating money to the charity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ make a gesture 做手势；做出姿态 ★ a gesture of goodwill/support 友善/支持的姿态 ☆ 考研写作：a symbolic gesture 象征性姿态
🧠 助记
gest(表达) + ure = 用动作表达 = 手势
gesture::/ˈdʒestʃə(r)/:n. 手势:Sending flowers was a gesture of apology from him.
3. obligation /ˌɒblɪˈɡeɪʃn/ n. 义务；责任；债务
📖 英文解释
A moral or legal duty to do something.
✏️ 例句
Citizens have an obligation to obey the laws of their country.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ have an obligation to do sth 有义务做某事 ★ fulfill/meet an obligation 履行义务 ☆ obligatory adj. 强制性的；oblige v. 迫使
🧠 助记
oblige(迫使) + ation = 被迫履行的事 = 义务
obligation::/ˌɒblɪˈɡeɪʃn/:n. 义务:The government has an obligation to protect its citizens.

🧠 助记
sweet 发音甜蜜，联想吃糖时的甜蜜感
sweet::/swiːt/:adj. 甜的:Life is not always sweet; sometimes it requires real effort.

absent::/ˈæbsənt/:adj. 不在场的:He was absent from the meeting due to illness.

deficit::/ˈdefɪsɪt/:n. 赤字:The country's trade deficit widened significantly last year.

mechanical::/mɪˈkænɪkl/:adj. 机械的:He gave a mechanical answer without really thinking.
