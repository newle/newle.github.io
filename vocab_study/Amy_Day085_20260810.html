1. gallery /ˈɡæləri/ n. 画廊；长廊；楼座
📖 英文解释
A room or building for exhibiting works of art; a long narrow passage.
✏️ 例句
The art gallery attracted thousands of visitors during the exhibition.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ art/photo gallery 艺术/摄影画廊 ★ gallery of fame 名人廊 ☆ 考研文化/艺术类文章高频 辨析：gallery / museum：gallery 专注艺术作品；museum 更广泛，含历史/科学文物
🧠 助记
gall(展示) + ery = 展示的地方 = 画廊
gallery::/ˈɡæləri/:n. 画廊:The national gallery holds a priceless collection of paintings.
2. lower /ˈləʊə(r)/ adj. 较低的；下级的；v. 降低；放下
📖 英文解释
Being below in position or rank; to move something to a lower position; to reduce.
✏️ 例句
The government announced plans to lower income taxes for low-wage workers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ lower prices/costs 降低价格/成本 ★ lower one's voice 压低声音 ★ lower house 下议院 ★ lower standards 降低标准
🧠 助记
low(低) + er(比较级) = 更低 = 较低的；降低
lower::/ˈləʊə(r)/:adj. 较低的:The new policy aims to lower the unemployment rate significantly.
3. maintenance /ˈmeɪntənəns/ n. 维护；保持；赡养
📖 英文解释
The process of maintaining or preserving something; financial support.
✏️ 例句
Regular maintenance of machinery is essential to prevent breakdowns.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ routine/regular maintenance 日常/定期维护 ★ maintenance costs/work 维护费用/工作 ☆ maintain v. 维持，保持 考研：infrastructure maintenance 基础设施维护
🧠 助记
maintain(维持) + ance = 维持下去的行为 = 维护
maintenance::/ˈmeɪntənəns/:n. 维护:The city allocated a large budget for road maintenance.
4. corner /ˈkɔːnə(r)/ n. 角落；街角；v. 使走投无路
📖 英文解释
A place where two lines, edges, or streets meet; to drive into a difficult position.
✏️ 例句
The café on the corner of the street became my favorite place to work.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in the corner 在角落里 ★ around the corner 拐角处；即将来临 ★ corner sb 逼到走投无路 ☆ cornerstone n. 基石(重要比喻词)
🧠 助记
corner = 角(两线交角) = 角落
corner::/ˈkɔːnə(r)/:n. 角落:Economic recovery seems to be just around the corner.
5. pop /pɒp/ adj. 流行的；n. 流行音乐；砰的一声；v. 突然出现；弹出
📖 英文解释
Popular culture; a short sharp sound; to appear or move suddenly.
✏️ 例句
Pop music dominates the charts, appealing to a broad audience.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pop music/culture 流行音乐/文化 ★ pop up 突然出现 ★ pop into sb's mind 突然想到 ☆ pop art 波普艺术；popular 的缩写
🧠 助记
pop = 流行(popular缩写)；砰的一声 = 突然弹出
pop::/pɒp/:adj. 流行的:A new coffee shop seems to pop up on every street corner.
6. somewhere /ˈsʌmweə(r)/ adv. 在某处；大约；n. 某个地方
📖 英文解释
In, at, or to some unspecified place; approximately.
✏️ 例句
I know I left my keys somewhere in this room.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ somewhere around 大约 ★ get somewhere 取得进展 ★ somewhere between A and B 介于A和B之间 ☆ 对比：anywhere(任何地方)；nowhere(没有地方)
🧠 助记
some(某) + where(地方) = 某个地方
somewhere::/ˈsʌmweə(r)/:adv. 在某处:We need to go somewhere quiet to have this conversation.
7. accuse /əˈkjuːz/ v. 控告；指责；谴责
📖 英文解释
To charge someone with a fault or offense; to blame.
✏️ 例句
He was accused of embezzling company funds over several years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ accuse sb of sth 控告某人某事(固定搭配) ★ be accused of 被指控 ☆ accusation n. 指控；accuser n. 指控者 辨析：accuse / charge / blame：accuse 正式指控；charge 法律控罪；blame 归咎于
🧠 助记
ac(加强) + cuse(原因) = 强调找到原因归罪 = 控告
accuse::/əˈkjuːz/:v. 控告:The journalist was accused of fabricating the interview.
8. neutral /ˈnjuːtrəl/ adj. 中立的；中性的；n. 中立者
📖 英文解释
Not taking sides; not belonging to any group; having no strong characteristics.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ remain/stay neutral 保持中立 ★ neutral color/tone 中性色调 ★ in neutral gear 处于空档(汽车) ☆ neutralize v. 使中和/中立；neutrality n. 中立
🧠 助记
neuter(中性) + al = 不偏向任何一方 = 中立的
neutral::/ˈnjuːtrəl/:adj. 中立的:The mediator must remain neutral throughout the negotiation process.

🧠 助记
re(再) + new(新) = 再次变新 = 更新/续期
renew::/rɪˈnjuː/:v. 更新:The two countries agreed to renew their trade agreement.
10. fifteen /ˌfɪfˈtiːn/ num. 十五

21. equivalent /ɪˈkwɪvələnt/ adj. 相同的；等效的；n. 对等物
📖 英文解释
Equal in value, amount, meaning, or effect.
✏️ 例句
The new position is roughly equivalent to a senior manager role.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ equivalent to 等同于(固定搭配) ★ equivalent amount/value 等量/等值 ☆ equivalence n. 等价；equally adv. 同等地 辨析：equivalent / equal：equivalent 强调功能/效果相当；equal 强调数量完全相同

equi(相等) + valent(价值) = 价值相等 = 等值的
equivalent::/ɪˈkwɪvələnt/:adj. 相同的:One hour of exercise is not necessarily equivalent to one hour of dieting.
