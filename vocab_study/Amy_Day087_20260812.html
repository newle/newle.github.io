1. architecture /ˈɑːkɪtektʃə(r)/ n. 建筑学；结构，体系
📖 英文解释
the art and science of designing and constructing buildings; the overall design or structure of a system
✏️ 例句
The architecture of the ancient temple reflects the culture of that era.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
software/computer architecture 软件/计算机架构; architectural adj.; architect n. 建筑师
🧠 助记
archi（首要）+ tect（建造）+ ure → 首要建造 → 建筑学
architecture::/ˈɑːkɪtektʃə(r)/:n. 建筑学:The building's architecture is stunning.
2. curb /kɜːb/ v. 抑制，控制；n. 路缘石
📖 英文解释
to restrain or keep under control; the edge of a raised path next to a road
✏️ 例句
The government introduced measures to curb inflation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
curb inflation/spending/crime 抑制通胀/消费/犯罪; curb one's enthusiasm 抑制热情
🧠 助记
curb 像 curve（弯曲）→ 弯道控制车速 → 抑制
curb::/kɜːb/:v. 抑制，控制:We must curb our spending.
3. entrepreneur /ˌɒntrəprəˈnɜː(r)/ n. 企业家，创业者
📖 英文解释
a person who sets up and runs a business, taking on financial risks in the hope of profit
✏️ 例句
The young entrepreneur launched a successful tech startup.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
social entrepreneur 社会企业家; entrepreneurship n. 创业精神; entrepreneurial adj.
🧠 助记
entre（进入）+ preneur（拿取）→ 进入市场拿取利润的人 → 企业家
entrepreneur::/ˌɒntrəprəˈnɜː(r)/:n. 企业家，创业者:She is a successful entrepreneur.
4. forecast /ˈfɔːkɑːst/ v./n. 预测，预报
📖 英文解释
to predict what will happen in the future, especially regarding weather or economic conditions
✏️ 例句
Economists forecast a slowdown in growth next year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
weather/economic forecast 天气/经济预测; forecast + that clause; forecaster n.
🧠 助记
fore（前）+ cast（投掷）→ 向前投掷（预测）→ 预测
forecast::/ˈfɔːkɑːst/:v./n. 预测，预报:The forecast predicts heavy rain.
5. controversial /ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃl/ adj. 有争议的，引起争论的
📖 英文解释
giving rise to public disagreement or debate; not universally accepted
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
controversial issue/topic/decision 争议性问题/话题/决定; controversy n. 争论
🧠 助记
contra（反对）+ vers（转）+ ial → 反转来反转去 → 有争议的
controversial::/ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃl/:adj. 有争议的，引起争论的:The decision was highly controversial.
6. ladder /ˈlædə(r)/ n. 梯子；阶梯
📖 英文解释
a structure consisting of a series of bars for climbing up or down; a series of levels representing progress
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
ladder → 像 latter（后来的）→ 后来爬梯子上去 → 梯子
ladder::/ˈlædə(r)/:n. 梯子:She climbed the career ladder.
7. moon /muːn/ n. 月球，月亮；卫星
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
full/new moon 满月/新月; lunar adj. 月亮的; moonlight n. 月光
🧠 助记
moon → 形状像 o，满月是圆的 → 月亮
moon::/muːn/:n. 月球，月亮:The full moon lit up the sky.
8. oppose /əˈpəʊz/ v. 反对，阻碍
📖 英文解释

oppose::/əˈpəʊz/:v. 反对，阻碍:She strongly opposed the proposal.
9. subsidy /ˈsʌbsɪdi/ n. 津贴，补贴；补助金
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

subsidy::/ˈsʌbsɪdi/:n. 津贴，补贴:Farmers receive government subsidies.
10. confusion /kənˈfjuːʒn/ n. 混乱，困惑；混淆

confusion::/kənˈfjuːʒn/:n. 混乱，困惑:The instructions caused great confusion.
11. enthusiasm /ɪnˈθjuːziæzəm/ n. 热情，热忱
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

enthusiasm::/ɪnˈθjuːziæzəm/:n. 热情，热忱:Her enthusiasm for learning is inspiring.
12. somehow /ˈsʌmhaʊ/ adv. 以某种方式；不知何故

🧠 助记

somehow::/ˈsʌmhaʊ/:adv. 以某种方式:Somehow he passed the exam.
