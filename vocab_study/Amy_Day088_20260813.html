1. painful /ˈpeɪnfl/ adj. 痛苦的，疼痛的；费力的
📖 英文解释
causing physical or emotional pain; causing or characterized by suffering
✏️ 例句
The recovery from the surgery was long and painful.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
painful memory/experience 痛苦的记忆/经历; painfully adv. 极其; painless adj. 无痛的
🧠 助记
pain（疼痛）+ ful（充满）→ 充满疼痛的 → 痛苦的
painful::/ˈpeɪnfl/:adj. 痛苦的，疼痛的:It was a painful experience for him.
2. confine /kənˈfaɪn/ v. 限制，局限；禁闭
📖 英文解释
to restrict something to a particular area, time, or group; to keep someone or something within limits
✏️ 例句
Please confine your remarks to the topic at hand.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
confine sb./sth. to sth. 限制在……; be confined to bed 卧床; confinement n. 禁闭
🧠 助记
con（完全）+ fine（界限）→ 完全在界限内 → 限制
confine::/kənˈfaɪn/:v. 限制，局限:Please confine yourself to the facts.
3. assemble /əˈsembl/ v. 装配，组装；集合，召集
📖 英文解释
to fit together the parts of something; to gather together in one place
✏️ 例句
The students assembled in the school hall for the morning announcement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
assemble furniture 组装家具; assemble a team 组建团队; assembly n. 集会；组装
🧠 助记
as（加强）+ semble（同 similar 放一起）→ 把各部分放到一起 → 装配
assemble::/əˈsembl/:v. 装配，组装:They assembled the machine carefully.
4. colony /ˈkɒləni/ n. 殖民地；聚居地；群体
📖 英文解释
a country under the control of another; a group of people from the same country living in another; a group of animals
✏️ 例句
Hong Kong was a British colony until 1997.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
establish a colony 建立殖民地; colonial adj. 殖民地的; colonize v. 殖民
🧠 助记
col（耕种）+ ony → 去耕种的地方 → 殖民地
colony::/ˈkɒləni/:n. 殖民地:The colony gained independence.
5. wheel /wiːl/ n. 轮子；方向盘；v. 推（轮椅）
📖 英文解释
a circular object that revolves around a central axle; the steering wheel of a vehicle
✏️ 例句
The invention of the wheel revolutionized transportation in ancient times.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
steering wheel 方向盘; spinning wheel 纺车; wheel around 转身; reinvent the wheel 重复造轮子
🧠 助记
wheel → w + heel（脚跟）→ 脚跟圆形转动 → 轮子
wheel::/wiːl/:n. 轮子:The wheels of the cart squeaked.
6. literally /ˈlɪtərəli/ adv. 逐字地；字面上；简直
📖 英文解释
in a literal manner; used for emphasis; actually, without exaggeration
✏️ 例句
She was literally shaking with excitement before the performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
take sth. literally 从字面意思理解; literally speaking 从字面上说; figuratively（反）
🧠 助记
literal（字面的）+ ly → 字面地 → 逐字地
literally::/ˈlɪtərəli/:adv. 逐字地:He literally ran out of ideas.
7. worthy /ˈwɜːði/ adj. 有价值的，值得的；应得的
📖 英文解释
having or showing qualities that deserve respect, attention, or consideration
✏️ 例句
The charity does very worthy work in helping the homeless.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be worthy of sth. 值得……; worthy cause/goal 有价值的事业/目标; noteworthy 值得注意的
🧠 助记
worth（价值）+ y → 有价值的 → 值得的
worthy::/ˈwɜːði/:adj. 有价值的，值得的:The effort was worthy of praise.
8. crack /kræk/ n. 裂缝；v. 破裂；破解
📖 英文解释
a narrow opening or split; to break without separating completely; to solve a problem
✏️ 例句
There is a crack in the wall that needs to be repaired.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
crack a code/joke 破解密码/讲笑话; crack down on 严厉打击; crack open 打开
🧠 助记
crack → k像裂缝 → 裂缝；破裂
crack::/kræk/:n. 裂缝:I noticed a crack in the glass.
9. panic /ˈpænɪk/ adj./n. 惊慌的；恐慌；v. 使惊慌
📖 英文解释
sudden overwhelming fear; to feel or cause to feel sudden terror
✏️ 例句
There was widespread panic after the earthquake warning was issued.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
panic attack 恐慌发作; panic buying 抢购; in a panic 处于恐慌中; panic-stricken 恐慌的
🧠 助记
Pan（希腊牧神）→ panic → 牧神引起恐慌 → 恐慌
panic::/ˈpænɪk/:adj./n. 惊慌的:The crowd panicked and fled.
10. upgrade /ˌʌpˈɡreɪd/ v./n. 升级，提升
📖 英文解释
to raise to a higher standard or quality; an instance of upgrading something

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
upgrade a system/service 升级系统/服务; software upgrade 软件升级; downgrade（反）
🧠 助记
up（向上）+ grade（等级）→ 提升等级 → 升级
upgrade::/ˌʌpˈɡreɪd/:v./n. 升级，提升:We need to upgrade our equipment.
11. supplement /ˈsʌplɪmənt/ n. 补充，增补；v. 补充
📖 英文解释
something added to complete or enhance something else; to add to something in order to improve it
✏️ 例句
He takes vitamin supplements to maintain his health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dietary supplement 饮食补充剂; supplement sth. with sth. 以……补充; supplementary adj.
🧠 助记
sup（补充）+ ple + ment → 补充之物 → 补充
supplement::/ˈsʌplɪmənt/:n. 补充，增补:Diet supplements cannot replace food.
12. till /tɪl/ conj./prep. 直到……为止
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
till now 直到现在; not...till 直到……才; till vs. until（二者基本同义）
🧠 助记
till → until 的缩写 → 直到
till::/tɪl/:conj./prep. 直到……为止:Wait till I come back.
13. compound /ˈkɒmpaʊnd/ n. 化合物；复合词；adj. 复合的；v. 使恶化

compound::/ˈkɒmpaʊnd/:n. 化合物:This compound is highly reactive.
