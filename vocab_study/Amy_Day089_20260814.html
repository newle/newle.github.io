1. alliance /əˈlaɪəns/ n. 联盟，同盟；联合
📖 英文解释
a union formed for mutual benefit, especially between countries or organizations
✏️ 例句
The two countries formed a military alliance to defend against common threats.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
form/enter an alliance 结成联盟; alliance with sb. 与……的联盟; allied adj. 同盟的
🧠 助记
ally（同盟）+ ance → 同盟关系 → 联盟
alliance::/əˈlaɪəns/:n. 联盟，同盟:They formed a trade alliance.
2. copyright /ˈkɒpiraɪt/ n. 版权，著作权
📖 英文解释
the exclusive legal right to reproduce, publish, or sell a literary, artistic, or musical work
✏️ 例句
Reproducing the book without permission would violate copyright law.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
copyright law 版权法; copyright infringement 版权侵犯; copyright holder 版权持有者
🧠 助记
copy（复制）+ right（权利）→ 复制的权利 → 版权
copyright::/ˈkɒpiraɪt/:n. 版权，著作权:The song is protected by copyright.
3. expertise /ˌekspɜːˈtiːz/ n. 专业知识，专长
📖 英文解释
expert skill or knowledge in a particular field
✏️ 例句
The company hired a consultant with expertise in financial analysis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
expertise in sth. 在……方面的专长; technical expertise 技术专长; expert n. 专家
🧠 助记
expert（专家）+ ise → 专家的知识 → 专业知识
expertise::/ˌekspɜːˈtiːz/:n. 专业知识，专长:Her expertise in law is invaluable.
4. leg /leɡ/ n. 腿；（旅程/比赛的）一段
📖 英文解释
the limb used for standing and walking; a section of a journey or competition
✏️ 例句
She broke her leg during the skiing accident last winter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
leg of a journey 旅途的一段; pull sb.'s leg 愚弄某人; leg room 腿部空间; leg up 帮助
🧠 助记
leg → 腿的形状像字母l → 腿
leg::/leɡ/:n. 腿:He stretched his legs after sitting.
5. spite /spaɪt/ n. 恶意，怨恨；v. 故意激怒
📖 英文解释
a desire to hurt, annoy, or offend someone; in spite of: despite something
✏️ 例句
He did it out of spite, just to make her angry.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in spite of 尽管，不管; out of spite 出于恶意; spiteful adj. 恶意的
🧠 助记
spit（吐）+ e → 向人吐口水的恶意 → 恶意
spite::/spaɪt/:n. 恶意，怨恨:In spite of the rain, we walked.
6. suicide /ˈsuːɪsaɪd/ n. 自杀
📖 英文解释
the act of intentionally killing oneself; a person who commits this act
✏️ 例句
Mental health services are crucial for suicide prevention.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
commit suicide 自杀; suicide attempt 自杀企图; suicide rate 自杀率; political suicide 政治自杀
🧠 助记
sui（自己）+ cide（杀）→ 杀自己 → 自杀
suicide::/ˈsuːɪsaɪd/:n. 自杀:Suicide prevention needs more attention.
7. acquisition /ˌækwɪˈzɪʃn/ n. 收购，习得；获得物
📖 英文解释
the process of obtaining something; the purchase of one company by another
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记

acquisition::/ˌækwɪˈzɪʃn/:n. 收购，习得:The acquisition cost millions of dollars.
8. pole /pəʊl/ n. 杆，柱；极
📖 英文解释

pole → p + ole → 像一根竖立的杆 → 杆；极
pole::/pəʊl/:n. 杆，柱:The explorers reached the North Pole.
9. democracy /dɪˈmɒkrəsi/ n. 民主（制度），民主国家
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
demo（人民）+ cracy（统治）→ 人民统治 → 民主
democracy::/dɪˈmɒkrəsi/:n. 民主（制度），民主国家:Democracy requires active participation.
10. consequently /ˈkɒnsɪkwəntli/ adv. 因此，所以
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
consequence（结果）+ ly → 由于结果地 → 因此
consequently::/ˈkɒnsɪkwəntli/:adv. 因此，所以:He studied hard; consequently, he passed.

fame::/feɪm/:n. 名声，名望:His fame spread around the world.
12. fulfil /fʊlˈfɪl/ v. 履行，实现；满足（需求）

fulfil::/fʊlˈfɪl/:v. 履行，实现:She fulfilled all her obligations.
