1. mine /maɪn/ pron. 我的 n. 矿；地雷 v. 开采；布雷
📖 英文解释
Belonging to me; a place where minerals are dug from the ground; an explosive device; to extract minerals.
✏️ 例句
This seat is mine — I was here first.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多义：mine 作名词：coal mine(煤矿)，land mine(地雷)；作动词：mine for gold(开采金矿)，data mining(数据挖掘)。注意 mine 和 gold mine(金矿/宝库) 搭配。
🧠 助记
mine 是 my 的名词性形式，I→my→mine→me，四格变化，mine 表示'我的东西'。
mine::/maɪn/:pron. 我的 n. 矿:The decision about my future career is mine alone to make.
2. servant /ˈsɜːrvənt/ n. 仆人；公务员；服务者
📖 英文解释
A person who performs duties for others; one employed in a house to perform domestic duties.
✏️ 例句
The wealthy family employed several servants to maintain their large estate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：civil servant (公务员)；public servant (公仆)；servant leadership (仆人式领导)；serve v. 服务 → servant。
🧠 助记
serve(服务) + ant(人/蚂蚁) → 提供服务的人 → 仆人。蚂蚁也是辛勤服务的生物！
servant::/ˈsɜːrvənt/:n. 仆人:As a public servant, he was expected to act in the best interest of citizens.
3. reflection /rɪˈflekʃn/ n. 反射；倒影；深思；体现
📖 英文解释
An image seen in a mirror or shiny surface; careful thought; a sign or result of something.
✏️ 例句
She saw her reflection in the still water of the lake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多义高频：reflection of (…的体现/反映)；on reflection (经过思考)；reflect v. 反射；反映；深思；reflection as a sign(体现) vs. as thought process(深思)。
🧠 助记
re-(再) + flect(弯/折) + ion → 光线再次折回来 → 反射。想象光线弹回的动作。
reflection::/rɪˈflekʃn/:n. 反射:High crime rates are often a reflection of deeper social inequalities.
4. bread /bred/ n. 面包；生计；金钱
📖 英文解释
A food made from flour and water that is baked; a means of providing food or income.
✏️ 例句
She earns her bread as a freelance writer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考习语：bread and butter (基本生计/主要来源)；the best thing since sliced bread (极好的事物)；earn one's bread (谋生)；daily bread (每日所需)。
🧠 助记
bread 和 read(读) 押韵，都是短 e 音。联想：面包(bread)是人类最早阅读(read)的书——饮食文化。
bread::/bred/:n. 面包:Advertising revenue is the bread and butter of most commercial media outlets.
5. golden /ˈɡoʊldən/ adj. 金色的；宝贵的；极好的
📖 英文解释
Made of or resembling gold in color; extremely good or favorable; prosperous and happy.
✏️ 例句
The golden years of Hollywood cinema were in the 1930s and 1940s.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：golden opportunity/chance (千载难逢的机会)；golden rule (黄金法则)；golden age (黄金时代)；golden mean (中庸之道)；silence is golden (沉默是金)。
🧠 助记
gold(黄金) + en(形容词后缀) → 像黄金一样珍贵 → 宝贵的/极好的。
golden::/ˈɡoʊldən/:adj. 金色的:This is a golden opportunity to reform the education system.
6. precious /ˈpreʃəs/ adj. 宝贵的；珍贵的 adv. 非常
📖 英文解释
Of great value and importance; not to be wasted; highly esteemed; (informal) used for emphasis.
✏️ 例句
Time is precious, so we should not waste it on trivial matters.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：precious metal/stone (贵金属/宝石)；precious time/resources (宝贵的时间/资源)；precious few/little (少得可怜)。注意后者是副词用法，修饰 few/little。
🧠 助记
preci- 来自拉丁 pretium(价格/价值) → 有价值的 → 珍贵的。联想 price(价格) → precious。
precious::/ˈpreʃəs/:adj. 宝贵的:Clean water is a precious resource that must be carefully conserved.
7. accommodation /əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃn/ n. 住处；住宿；和解；适应
📖 英文解释
A place where someone can stay or live; an agreement or arrangement; adaptation to new circumstances.
✏️ 例句
The university provides accommodation for all first-year students.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多义：住宿(住处)；和解协商(reach an accommodation 达成妥协)；适应(accommodation to new environment)。注意双写 mm 和 cc：acCOMMOdation。
🧠 助记
accom(辅助) + mod(模式) + ation → 提供适合的模式/地方 → 住所。记住双 mm：两张床(mm=两张竖线)。
accommodation::/əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃn/:n. 住处:The two sides reached an accommodation that satisfied neither party fully.
8. charm /tʃɑːrm/ n. 魅力；吸引力；咒语 v. 迷住；施魔法
📖 英文解释
The power of pleasing or attracting; a small ornament worn on a bracelet; to attract or delight; to use magic.
✏️ 例句
Her natural charm made her popular with everyone she met.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：charm offensive (魅力攻势)；work like a charm (非常有效)；personal charm (个人魅力)；charming adj. 迷人的；charmed adj. 着迷的。
🧠 助记
charm 联想 arm(手臂) → 用手臂(arm)拥抱让人着迷(charm)。c + harm(害) → 不伤害的吸引力 → 魅力。
charm::/tʃɑːrm/:n. 魅力:The politician's charm offensive failed to win over skeptical voters.
9. consistent /kənˈsɪstənt/ adj. 一致的；连贯的；坚持的
📖 英文解释
Acting or done in the same way over time; compatible with; not contradictory.
✏️ 例句
Her performance has been consistent throughout the entire season.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：consistent with (与…一致/符合)；consistent efforts (持续不断的努力)；inconsistent (不一致的)；consistently adv. 一贯地；consistency n. 一致性。
🧠 助记
con(共同) + sist(站立) + ent → 一起站稳立场 → 一致的/坚持的。和 persist(坚持), resist(抵抗) 同族。
consistent::/kənˈsɪstənt/:adj. 一致的:The evidence is consistent with our hypothesis about climate change.
10. distinct /dɪˈstɪŋkt/ adj. 不同的；明显的；清晰的
📖 英文解释
Noticeably different; clearly separate; easily recognizable.
✏️ 例句
The two species are similar in appearance but genetically distinct.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频区分：distinct (清楚地不同) vs. distinctive (有独特风格的) vs. distinguished (杰出的)。搭配：a distinct possibility (明显的可能性)；distinct from (与…不同)；distinction n. 区别。
🧠 助记
dis-(分开) + tinct(染色/标记) → 被分开标记的 → 明显不同的。联想 distinguish(区分)。
distinct::/dɪˈstɪŋkt/:adj. 不同的:There is a distinct difference between knowing a fact and understanding it.
11. edit /ˈedɪt/ v. 编辑；剪辑；主编 n. 编辑
📖 英文解释
To prepare written material for publication by correcting, condensing, or adapting it; to be in charge of a publication.
✏️ 例句
She spent hours editing the manuscript before submitting it to the publisher.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：edit out (剪掉)；editor n. 编辑；editorial adj./n. 社论的/社论；edition n. 版本；edited volume (论文集)。
🧠 助记
edit 联想 edict(法令) → 法令需要仔细编写和校对 → 编辑。或记 e-dit → 电子(e)给(dit)出文字 → 编辑。
edit::/ˈedɪt/:v. 编辑:The documentary was edited down from 200 hours of footage to 90 minutes.
12. housework /ˈhaʊswɜːrk/ n. 家务（劳动）
📖 英文解释
Regular work done in a home, such as cleaning, cooking, and laundry.
✏️ 例句
The couple shared the housework equally between them.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常在社会学文章中出现，探讨性别平等、家务分工等议题。注意：homework(家庭作业) vs. housework(家务)。housework 不可数。
🧠 助记
house(房子) + work(工作) → 在房子里做的工作 → 家务。构词简单直接。
housework::/ˈhaʊswɜːrk/:n. 家务（劳动）:Studies show that women still perform the majority of housework in most societies.
13. lifetime /ˈlaɪftaɪm/ n. 一生；终生 adj. 终生的
📖 英文解释
The length of time that a person lives or a thing lasts; the duration of someone's life.
✏️ 例句
In my grandfather's lifetime, technology changed beyond recognition.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：in one's lifetime (在某人一生中)；once in a lifetime (一生一次的机会)；lifetime achievement (终生成就)；lifetime guarantee (终生保修)。
🧠 助记
life(生命) + time(时间) → 生命的时间 → 一生。两个简单词组合。
lifetime::/ˈlaɪftaɪm/:n. 一生:Discovering the ancient manuscript was a once-in-a-lifetime experience for the archaeologist.
14. shirt /ʃɜːrt/ n. 衬衫；上衣
📖 英文解释
A garment for the upper body with a collar, sleeves, and buttons down the front.
✏️ 例句
He always wears a white shirt and tie to important meetings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考习语：give the shirt off one's back (慷慨解囊)；keep one's shirt on (保持冷静)；lose one's shirt (倾家荡产)。注意 T-shirt 的拼写。
🧠 助记
shirt 和 skirt(裙子)发音相近，shirt 是上衣(男)，skirt 是裙子(下装)，头两个字母区分。
shirt::/ʃɜːrt/:n. 衬衫:He lost his shirt investing in the dot-com bubble of the late 1990s.
15. amid /əˈmɪd/ prep. 在…之中；在…期间
📖 英文解释
Surrounded by; in the middle of; during a period characterized by something.
✏️ 例句
She remained calm amid the chaos of the evacuation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频介词：amid controversy/uncertainty/concerns (在争议/不确定性/担忧之中)；amidst 是同义词(更正式)；常用于新闻语体。
🧠 助记
a + mid(中间) → 在中间 → 在…之中。联想 middle(中间)，amid = 在middle之中。
amid::/əˈmɪd/:prep. 在…之中:The company posted record profits amid growing public criticism of its practices.
16. identical /aɪˈdentɪkl/ adj. 完全相同的；同一的
📖 英文解释
Exactly the same in every detail; (of twins) developed from the same fertilized egg.
✏️ 例句
The two signatures were virtually identical, suggesting forgery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：identical to/with (与…完全相同)；identical twins (同卵双胞胎)；virtually/almost identical (几乎相同)。注意 identity n. 身份；identify v. 识别。
🧠 助记
ident(身份) + ical(形容词) → 身份一样的 → 完全相同的。联想 identity card(身份证)，同一身份。
identical::/aɪˈdentɪkl/:adj. 完全相同的:The two crime scenes presented virtually identical patterns of behavior.
17. instruct /ɪnˈstrʌkt/ v. 指导；指示；命令；通知
📖 英文解释
To teach someone how to do something; to direct or order someone to do something; to inform.
✏️ 例句
The officer instructed his troops to advance at dawn.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：instruct sb to do sth (命令某人做某事)；instruct sb in sth (在某方面指导某人)；instruction n. 指示/说明书；instructive adj. 有启发性的；instructor n. 教练/讲师。
🧠 助记
in + struct(建造) → 在内部建造知识结构 → 指导/教导。联想 construct(建造), structure(结构)。
instruct::/ɪnˈstrʌkt/:v. 指导:The judge instructed the jury to disregard the witness's last statement.
18. jacket /ˈdʒækɪt/ n. 夹克；外套；护封
📖 英文解释
A short coat; a protective outer covering for a book or other object.
✏️ 例句
She put on her jacket before going out into the cold.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：life jacket (救生衣)；dust jacket (书的护封)；jacket potato (带皮烤马铃薯)；straitjacket (拘束衣/严格限制)。
🧠 助记
jacket 联想 Jack(人名) + et → Jack 穿的小外套 → 夹克。或记：jack + et，jack 是顶(千斤顶)，外套像包裹身体的顶。
jacket::/ˈdʒækɪt/:n. 夹克:The book's striking dust jacket helped attract buyers in the bookstore.
19. sake /seɪk/ n. 缘故；目的；利益
📖 英文解释
Used to express the purpose or reason for doing something; the benefit or advantage of someone.
✏️ 例句
She moved to a better neighborhood for the sake of her children's education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研必考短语：for the sake of (为了…；为…的缘故)；for sb's sake (为了某人)；for goodness'/heaven's sake (看在老天的面上)；for its own sake (为了其本身)。
🧠 助记
sake 联想中文'撒客'→ 为了撒(sake)开让路 → 为了某人的缘故让步。或联想日本清酒(sake)，为了健康干一杯(for health's sake)。
sake::/seɪk/:n. 缘故:For the sake of clarity, let me summarize the main points of the argument.
20. virus /ˈvaɪrəs/ n. 病毒；病毒程序
📖 英文解释
A microorganism that is smaller than bacteria and causes disease; a harmful computer program.
✏️ 例句
The computer virus infected thousands of machines before being detected.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：viral adj. 病毒性的/病毒式传播的；go viral (在网上迅速传播)；computer virus (计算机病毒)；virus outbreak (病毒爆发)。
🧠 助记
virus 联想 vir(男人，拉丁)→ 男人生病了 → virus。或直接记 vi + rus → VI可怕的如 RUST(锈) → 病毒腐蚀系统。
virus::/ˈvaɪrəs/:n. 病毒:The video went viral within hours, garnering millions of views worldwide.
21. wound /wuːnd/ n. 伤口；创伤 v. 使受伤；伤害（感情）
📖 英文解释
An injury to the body; to injure someone physically or emotionally.
✏️ 例句
The soldier received a gunshot wound during the battle.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研注意：wound /wuːnd/ 伤口 vs. wound /waʊnd/ 是 wind 的过去式(缠绕)。搭配：open old wounds (揭旧伤疤)；emotional/psychological wound (情感/心理创伤)。
🧠 助记
wound 联想 sound(声音) → 受伤(wound)时会发出声音(sound)。注意两个 wound 读音不同！
wound::/wuːnd/:n. 伤口:His harsh criticism inflicted a deep psychological wound on the young artist.
22. beer /bɪər/ n. 啤酒；（泛指）饮料
📖 英文解释
An alcoholic drink made from fermented grain, flavored with hops.
✏️ 例句
He ordered a cold beer after a long day at work.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研习语：small beer (无足轻重的事/人)；beer and skittles (轻松愉快的事，常否定)；life is not all beer and skittles (生活并非都是乐趣)。
🧠 助记
beer 联想 bear(熊) → 熊喝啤酒的画面，beer 和 bear 发音相近。
beer::/bɪər/:n. 啤酒:Running a successful charity is not all beer and skittles, as she quickly discovered.
23. refugee /ˌrefjʊˈdʒiː/ n. 难民；流亡者
📖 英文解释
A person who has been forced to leave their country due to war, persecution, or natural disaster.
✏️ 例句
The UN agency coordinates the resettlement of refugees in host countries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：refugee camp (难民营)；refugee crisis (难民危机)；seek/apply for refugee status (申请难民身份)；asylum seeker (寻求庇护者)。常见于社会类阅读。
🧠 助记
refuge(庇护所) + e → 寻找庇护所的人 → 难民。记住 refuge(庇护)就记住了 refugee(难民)。
refugee::/ˌrefjʊˈdʒiː/:n. 难民:International organizations are struggling to provide adequate shelter for refugees.
24. nap /næp/ n. 小睡；打盹 v. 小睡
📖 英文解释
A short sleep, especially during the day; the raised fabric on textiles like velvet.
✏️ 例句
A short afternoon nap can improve alertness and productivity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考习语：catch sb napping (趁某人不备)；take/have a nap (小睡一会儿)；catnap (打盹)。注意考研阅读中关于睡眠研究的文章常出现 nap。
🧠 助记
nap 联想 snap(突然)→ 突然睡着了就是打盹(nap)。字母短小，睡觉时间也短。
nap::/næp/:n. 小睡:The army was caught napping when the enemy launched a surprise attack at dawn.
25. classical /ˈklæsɪkl/ adj. 古典的；经典的；传统的
📖 英文解释
Relating to ancient Greek or Roman culture; representing the most highly developed stage of an art form; following traditional principles.
✏️ 例句
She studied classical literature at university, focusing on Homer and Virgil.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研区分：classical (古典的，如 classical music) vs. classic (经典的，如 a classic film)。搭配：classical music/dance/architecture/education (古典音乐/舞蹈/建筑/教育)。
🧠 助记
class(阶级/经典) + ical → 经典阶层的→古典的/经典的。联想 class = 课堂上学的经典知识。
classical::/ˈklæsɪkl/:adj. 古典的:His approach to the problem was classical in its elegance and simplicity.
