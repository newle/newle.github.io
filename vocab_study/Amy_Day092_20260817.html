1. laundry /ˈlɔːndri/ n. 洗衣；待洗（或已洗）的衣物；洗衣房
📖 英文解释
Clothes and linens that need to be washed or have been washed; the process of washing clothes.
✏️ 例句
He does his laundry every Sunday to prepare for the working week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：do the laundry (洗衣服)；dirty laundry (脏衣服；喻指家丑)；laundry list (冗长的清单)；air one's dirty laundry (公开家丑)；laundry room (洗衣房)；money laundering (洗钱，注意拼写)。
🧠 助记
laundry 联想：LAUN + DRY → 洗(launch进水) + 干(dry干燥) → 洗完晾干 → 洗衣服。
laundry::/ˈlɔːndri/:n. 洗衣:The report turned out to be a laundry list of complaints without any concrete proposals.
2. bicycle /ˈbaɪsɪkl/ n. 自行车 v. 骑自行车
📖 英文解释
A vehicle with two wheels driven by pedaling; to ride a bicycle.
✏️ 例句
Cycling to work by bicycle reduces both carbon emissions and commuting costs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：bicycle lane/path (自行车道)；bicycle sharing (共享单车)；by bicycle (骑自行车)；bi(两) + cycle(轮) → 两轮的 → 自行车。注意与 motorcycle(摩托车), tricycle(三轮车)对比。
🧠 助记
bi(二) + cycle(轮子/循环) → 两个轮子循环转动 → 自行车。bi = 二，bicycle = 两轮车。
bicycle::/ˈbaɪsɪkl/:n. 自行车 v. 骑自行车:City governments are investing in bicycle infrastructure to reduce urban traffic congestion.
3. skirt /skɜːrt/ n. 裙子；边缘 v. 绕开；避开；沿…边缘走
📖 英文解释
A garment hanging from the waist; the edge or border of something; to go around the edge; to avoid dealing with directly.
✏️ 例句
The new proposal merely skirts around the fundamental issues without addressing them.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考动词用法：skirt around/round (绕开/回避)；skirt an issue/problem/question (回避问题)；outskirts n. 郊区/边缘地带。
🧠 助记
skirt 联想 shirt(衬衫) → skirt 在下面(裙子)，shirt 在上面(衬衫)，sk-irt vs sh-irt，一字之差。
skirt::/skɜːrt/:n. 裙子:Politicians often skirt the real issues to avoid alienating key voter groups.
4. eastern /ˈiːstərn/ adj. 东部的；东方的
📖 英文解释
Situated in or facing the east; coming from the east; relating to the East.
✏️ 例句
Eastern European countries experienced rapid economic growth after joining the EU.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：Eastern Europe/Asia/culture (东欧/亚洲/文化)；the Middle East (中东)；Far East (远东)；eastern philosophy/values (东方哲学/价值观)；note 大写 Eastern = 东方文明，小写 eastern = 地理方向东部。
🧠 助记
east(东方) + ern(方向形容词) → 东方的。和 western/northern/southern 同类，-ern 是方向形容词后缀。
eastern::/ˈiːstərn/:adj. 东部的:The company expanded its operations into eastern markets to offset declining domestic sales.

A large wild cat native to Africa and India; a brave or fierce person; a famous person.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
lion 联想：L-I-O-N，Loud, Impressive, Outstanding Nature → 自然界中引人注目的猛兽 → 狮子。
lion::/ˈlaɪən/:n. 狮子:The largest corporation took the lion's share of government contracts.
6. toilet /ˈtɔɪlɪt/ n. 厕所；马桶；梳妆
📖 英文解释
A large bowl attached to a drain used for disposing of bodily waste; a room containing this; the process of washing and dressing.
✏️ 例句
Access to clean toilet facilities is a basic human right often taken for granted.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：toilet paper/tissue (卫生纸)；go to the toilet (上厕所)；public toilet (公共厕所)；flush the toilet (冲马桶)；toilet training (如厕训练)。注意 toilet water 指花露水，非厕所水。
🧠 助记
toilet 来自法语 toilette(梳妆台) → 英语演变为卫生间。联想去厕所是日常生活(toil=辛苦+et)。
toilet::/ˈtɔɪlɪt/:n. 厕所:Improving sanitation and toilet access remains a challenge in many developing regions.
7. guilt /ɡɪlt/ n. 内疚；罪责；有罪
📖 英文解释
The fact of having committed a wrongdoing; a feeling of having done something wrong.
✏️ 例句
She felt overwhelming guilt about neglecting her elderly parents.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：feel guilt/guilty (感到内疚)；guilt trip (使某人产生愧疚感)；guilty conscience (有罪恶感的良心)；guilt-free (无愧疚的)；guilt by association (株连)；beyond reasonable doubt (毫无疑问有罪)。
🧠 助记
guilt 联想 gilt(镀金的) → 表面镀金但内心有罪 → 内疚/有罪。或 g + uilt → 'uilt' 像 built，建了罪行。
guilt::/ɡɪlt/:n. 内疚:Corporations feel little guilt about exploiting tax loopholes that are technically legal.
8. tolerance /ˈtɒlərəns/ n. 宽容；容忍；耐受性
📖 英文解释
The ability to accept things with which you do not agree; resistance to a drug or adversity.
✏️ 例句
A multicultural society requires tolerance and mutual respect among its citizens.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：tolerance for/of/towards (对…的宽容)；zero tolerance (零容忍)；tolerance and diversity (宽容与多样性)；drug tolerance (药物耐受性)；tolerate v. 容忍；tolerant adj. 宽容的。
🧠 助记
toler(忍受，来自拉丁 tolerare) + ance → 忍受的能力 → 宽容/容忍。联想 tolerate(容忍)去掉 -ate 加 -ance。
tolerance::/ˈtɒlərəns/:n. 宽容:Building tolerance between communities of different backgrounds requires sustained effort.
9. parallel /ˈpærəlel/ adj. 平行的；类似的 n. 平行线；类比 v. 与…相似
📖 英文解释
Side by side with the same distance apart; similar; a comparison of similar things.
✏️ 例句
There are striking parallels between the 2008 financial crisis and the current situation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：draw a parallel (作类比)；in parallel (同时进行)；run parallel to (与…平行/类似)；without parallel (无与伦比)；parallel development/system (并行发展/系统)。
🧠 助记
para(旁边) + llel(线) → 旁边的线 → 平行线。注意双 l：para-ll-el。联想 parallel lines 在地图上代表纬线。
parallel::/ˈpærəlel/:adj. 平行的:Researchers drew parallels between ancient Roman politics and modern democratic systems.
10. instinct /ˈɪnstɪŋkt/ n. 本能；直觉；天性
📖 英文解释
An innate tendency to behave in a certain way; a natural ability or skill.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：by instinct (凭本能)；survival instinct (生存本能)；instinctive adj. 本能的；instinctively adv. 本能地；follow one's instinct (跟随本能/直觉)；gut instinct (直觉)。
🧠 助记
in + stinct(刺激，来自 stinguere) → 内在的刺激反应 → 本能。联想 distinct(区别)，本能反应区别于理性思考。
instinct::/ˈɪnstɪŋkt/:n. 本能:Successful entrepreneurs often trust their instinct when data is inconclusive.
11. premium /ˈpriːmiəm/ n. 保险费；溢价；奖金 adj. 优质的；高档的
📖 英文解释
An amount paid for insurance; a sum added to the usual price; superior quality.
✏️ 例句
Consumers are willing to pay a premium for organic and sustainably sourced products.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：at a premium (供不应求/溢价)；premium price/quality/brand (高价/优质/高端品牌)；insurance premium (保险费)；place/put a premium on (非常重视)；premium service (高端服务)。
🧠 助记

premium::/ˈpriːmiəm/:n. 保险费:The company places a premium on innovation and invests heavily in R&D.
12. shuttle /ˈʃʌtl/ n. 航天飞机；穿梭巴士；梭子 v. 来回穿梭
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
She shuttles between New York and London on a monthly basis for her job.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：space shuttle (航天飞机)；shuttle bus/service (穿梭巴士/服务)；shuttle diplomacy (穿梭外交)；shuttle back and forth (来回穿梭)。
🧠 助记
shuttle 联想 battle(战斗) → 像战斗机一样来回 → 穿梭。或记 shut(关) + tle → 关了又开，开了又关 → 来回穿梭。
shuttle::/ˈʃʌtl/:n. 航天飞机:The diplomat engaged in shuttle diplomacy between the two capitals to broker a peace deal.
13. collective /kəˈlektɪv/ adj. 集体的；共同的 n. 集体；合作社
📖 英文解释
Done by people acting as a group; of or characteristic of a group; a cooperative enterprise.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：collective responsibility/memory/action/bargaining (集体责任/记忆/行动/谈判)；collective noun (集合名词，语法)；collectively adv. 共同地；collective farm (集体农庄)。
🧠 助记
collect(收集) + ive → 收集在一起的 → 集体的。大家聚集(collect)在一起就是集体(collective)。
collective::/kəˈlektɪv/:adj. 集体的:Climate change requires collective action from governments, businesses, and individuals.
14. flash /flæʃ/ n. 闪光；一瞬间 v. 闪烁；迅速传播 adj. 华而不实的
📖 英文解释
A sudden brief burst of bright light; to shine suddenly; to move or pass very quickly.
✏️ 例句
A flash of inspiration helped her solve the problem that had puzzled her for weeks.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：in a flash (一瞬间)；flash of lightning/light/inspiration (闪电/灵感一闪)；flashback n. (闪回/倒叙)；flash mob (快闪)；flash point (闪点/爆发点)；news flash (最新消息)。
🧠 助记
flash 联想 clash(冲突) → 冲突时会有火花(flash)闪现。flash 和 clash 押韵，快速闪过。
flash::/flæʃ/:n. 闪光:In a flash, she realized the solution to the problem that had eluded her for months.
15. continuous /kənˈtɪnjuəs/ adj. 连续的；持续的；不断的
📖 英文解释
Forming an unbroken sequence without interruption; without a pause.
✏️ 例句
The factory operates on a continuous basis, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频区分：continuous (无中断的连续) vs. continual (反复发生的，有间隔)。搭配：continuous improvement/growth/change (持续改进/增长/变化)；continuously adv.；continuity n. 连续性。
🧠 助记
continue(继续) + ous → 一直继续的 → 连续的。continue → continuous，规律构词。
continuous::/kənˈtɪnjuəs/:adj. 连续的:The region has experienced continuous economic growth for over two decades.
16. resistant /rɪˈzɪstənt/ adj. 抵抗的；有抵抗力的；耐…的
📖 英文解释
Offering resistance; not affected by something; capable of withstanding the action of something.
✏️ 例句
The bacteria had become resistant to all known antibiotics.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：resistant to change/disease/treatment (对变化/疾病/治疗有抵抗力)；antibiotic-resistant (抗生素耐药)；fire-resistant (防火的)；resistance n. 抵抗；resist v. 抵抗。
🧠 助记
resist(抵抗) + ant → 具有抵抗能力的 → 抵抗的。resist → resistant，加形容词后缀。
resistant::/rɪˈzɪstənt/:adj. 抵抗的:The emergence of drug-resistant strains of tuberculosis poses a serious public health threat.
17. variable /ˈveəriəbl/ adj. 多变的；可变的 n. 变量；可变因素
📖 英文解释
Not consistent or having a fixed pattern; likely to change; a factor that may change.
✏️ 例句
Economic growth is affected by a number of variables including inflation and employment rates.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：variable factors/conditions/results (多变的因素/条件/结果)；independent/dependent variable (自变量/因变量)；variable costs (可变成本)；variability n. 变异性；invariable adj. 不变的。
🧠 助记
vary(变化) + able → 能够变化的 → 可变的。vary → variable，注意 y 变 i + able。
variable::/ˈveəriəbl/:adj. 多变的:The experiment controlled all variables except the one being studied.
18. visible /ˈvɪzɪbl/ adj. 可见的；明显的；看得见的
📖 英文解释
Able to be seen; easily noticed.
✏️ 例句
The economic impact of the recession was clearly visible in rising unemployment rates.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：visible to the naked eye (肉眼可见)；highly/clearly visible (非常/清晰可见)；visible minority (可见少数族裔)；visibility n. 能见度/知名度；invisible adj. 不可见的。
🧠 助记
vis(看，来自 videre) + ible → 能看见的 → 可见的。联想 vision(视觉), visit(参观), visual(视觉的)，vis 都与视觉相关。
visible::/ˈvɪzɪbl/:adj. 可见的:The benefits of the new policy were not immediately visible to the general public.
19. withdraw /wɪðˈdrɔː/ v. 退出；撤回；取款；收回（话语）
📖 英文解释
To remove or take back; to leave a place or group; to take money from a bank account.
✏️ 例句
The company decided to withdraw its product from the market following safety concerns.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：withdraw from (从…撤出/退出)；withdraw support/funds/troops (撤回支持/资金/军队)；withdraw a statement/remark (收回声明/话语)；withdrawal n. 撤退/取款/戒断；withdrawn adj. 孤僻的。
🧠 助记
with(against/back) + draw(拉) → 往回拉 → 撤回/退出。联想 draw back = withdraw，回拉就是撤回。
withdraw::/wɪðˈdrɔː/:v. 退出:The government decided to withdraw its troops from the conflict zone by year-end.
20. elaborate /ɪˈlæbərət/ (adj.) /ɪˈlæbəreɪt/ (v.) adj. 复杂的；精心制作的 v. 详细阐述；精心制作
📖 英文解释
Involving many carefully arranged parts; very detailed; to explain in more detail.
✏️ 例句
Could you elaborate on your proposal? The committee needs more specific information.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考两种词性：adj. elaborate plan/design/system (复杂的计划/设计/系统)；v. elaborate on (详细说明)；elaborately adv. 精心地；elaboration n. 阐述。注意发音：形容词 /-ət/，动词 /-eɪt/。
🧠 助记
e + labor(劳动) + ate → 花了很多劳动力(labor)的 → 复杂精巧的。花心思劳作才能做出精细的东西。
elaborate::/ɪˈlæbərət/ (adj.) /ɪˈlæbəreɪt/ (v.):adj. 复杂的:The minister was asked to elaborate on the government's strategy for economic recovery.
21. guess /ɡes/ v. 猜测；猜对；认为 n. 猜测；推测

guess 联想 guest(客人) → 猜猜今晚来的客人(guest)是谁？guess → guest 只差一个 t。
guess::/ɡes/:v. 猜测:Based on available data, economists can make an educated guess about future growth rates.
22. indispensable /ˌɪndɪˈspensəbl/ adj. 不可缺少的；必不可少的
📖 英文解释
Too important to be without; absolutely necessary.
✏️ 例句
Skilled workers are indispensable to any modern manufacturing operation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：indispensable to/for (对…不可缺少)；indispensable tool/role/part (不可缺少的工具/角色/部分)；indispensably adv.；dispensable(可有可无的) 反义词。
🧠 助记
in(不) + dis(分离) + pens(花费/支出) + able → 不能不花心思的 → 必不可少的。联想 dispense with(省去)，indispensable = 无法省去的。
indispensable::/ˌɪndɪˈspensəbl/:adj. 不可缺少的:Access to clean water is indispensable to human health and economic development.

sweep::/swiːp/:v. 扫:Sweeping educational reforms were introduced to modernize the national curriculum.
24. concrete /ˈkɒŋkriːt/ adj. 具体的；确实的 n. 混凝土 v. 使具体化
📖 英文解释
Existing in a material form; definite and specific; a building material made from cement.
✏️ 例句
The report called for concrete measures to address the housing shortage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：concrete evidence/proposal/action/example (具体的证据/提议/行动/例子)；concrete jungle (水泥丛林，指城市)；in concrete terms (具体地说)；abstract vs. concrete (抽象 vs. 具体)。

con(共同) + crete(生长，来自 creare) → 共同结合生长 → 混凝土(各成分混合)；引申为具体的(实实在在的)。
concrete::/ˈkɒŋkriːt/:adj. 具体的:The plan lacks concrete proposals for actually implementing the ambitious targets.
25. electrical /ɪˈlektrɪkl/ adj. 电的；电气的；与电相关的

考研区分：electric (由电驱动，如 electric car 电动汽车) vs. electrical (涉及电气的，如 electrical engineer 电气工程师)；electrical system/fault/equipment (电气系统/故障/设备)；electrician n. 电工。

electrical::/ɪˈlektrɪkl/:adj. 电的:An electrical fault in the control panel caused the entire production line to shut down.
