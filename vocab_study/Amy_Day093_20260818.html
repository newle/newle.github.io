1. governor /ˈɡʌvərnər/ n. 州长；总督；管理者；理事
📖 英文解释
The elected executive head of a US state; a person appointed to govern a town or region; a member of a governing body.
✏️ 例句
The governor signed the new education bill into law.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：governor of a state（州长）；governor-general（总督）；board of governors（董事会）；governing body（管理机构）
🧠 助记
govern（管理）+ or（人）→ 管理的人 → 州长、总督
governor::/ˈɡʌvərnər/:n. 州长:The governor called a press conference to address the crisis.
2. incline /ɪnˈklaɪn/ v. 倾向于；使倾斜 n. 斜坡；倾斜
📖 英文解释
To feel willing or favorably disposed toward an action or belief; to have a tendency toward something; a slope; to lean or slope.
✏️ 例句
She was inclined to agree with his analysis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：be inclined to do sth（倾向于做某事）；incline toward/towards（倾向于）；an incline of 30 degrees（30度的坡）；inclination（倾向；爱好）
🧠 助记
in + cline（倾斜），向内倾斜 → 倾向；坡
incline::/ɪnˈklaɪn/:v. 倾向于:Most experts are inclined to support the new theory.
3. mount /maʊnt/ v. 登上；增加；安装；骑上 n. 山；支架
📖 英文解释
To climb up or onto something; to increase in amount or intensity; to fix an object onto a support; a mountain or large hill.
✏️ 例句
Tension mounted as the deadline approached.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：mount a campaign/challenge（发起运动/挑战）；mount to/up to（增加到）；mounting pressure/concern（不断增加的压力/忧虑）
🧠 助记
mount = mountain（山），往山上爬 → 登上、增加
mount::/maʊnt/:v. 登上:Pressure mounted on the government to take action.
4. straightforward /ˌstreɪtˈfɔːrwərd/ adj. 简单的；直接的；坦诚的
📖 英文解释
Uncomplicated and easy to do or understand; (of a person) honest and open; without difficulty.
✏️ 例句
The instructions were straightforward enough to follow.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：straightforward answer/explanation（直接的回答/解释）；straightforward process（简单的过程）；be straightforward about sth（对某事坦诚）
🧠 助记
straight（直）+ forward（向前）→ 直接向前 → 直接的；简单的
straightforward::/ˌstreɪtˈfɔːrwərd/:adj. 简单的:The solution to the problem was surprisingly straightforward.
5. habitat /ˈhæbɪtæt/ n. （动植物的）栖息地；生活环境
📖 英文解释
The natural home or environment of an animal, plant, or other organism; a place where a person or group lives.
✏️ 例句
The panda's natural habitat is the bamboo forests of China.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：natural habitat（自然栖息地）；destroy/protect a habitat（破坏/保护栖息地）；habitat loss（栖息地丧失）
🧠 助记
habit（习惯）+ at，动物习惯住的地方 → 栖息地
habitat::/ˈhæbɪtæt/:n. （动植物的）栖息地:The destruction of natural habitats threatens many species.
6. spoil /spɔɪl/ v. 损坏；宠坏；（食物）腐烂 n. 战利品；赃物
📖 英文解释
To diminish or destroy the value or quality of; to harm the character of a child by being too indulgent; (of food) to become unfit for eating.
✏️ 例句
Don't let one bad experience spoil your trip.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：spoil the fun/party（扫兴）；spoil a child（溺爱孩子）；the spoils of war（战利品）；spoiled/spoilt（腐烂的；被宠坏的）
🧠 助记
联想：spoil 谐音'死剥了'，把东西剥坏了 → 损坏
spoil::/spɔɪl/:v. 损坏:Heavy rain spoiled their outdoor wedding plans.
7. companion /kəmˈpænjən/ n. 同伴；伙伴；伴侣；（书的）手册
📖 英文解释
A person or animal with whom one spends time or travels; a person who shares an experience; a book or guide for a subject.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：travelling companion（旅伴）；close companion（亲密伙伴）；companion to sth（某事物的伴随手册）；companionship（陪伴；友谊）
🧠 助记
com（共同）+ pan（面包）+ ion，共同分享面包的人 → 伙伴
companion::/kəmˈpænjən/:n. 同伴:She found a lifelong companion in her college roommate.

🧠 助记
联想：quarrel 谐音'夸热'，夸得热烈到吵架了
quarrel::/ˈkwɔːrəl/:n./v. 争吵:They quarrelled bitterly over money matters.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
