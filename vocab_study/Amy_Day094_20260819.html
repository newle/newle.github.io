1. patience /ˈpeɪʃəns/ n. 耐心；忍耐；（纸牌游戏）接龙
📖 英文解释
The capacity to accept or tolerate delay, trouble, or suffering without becoming angry or upset; a card game for one player.
✏️ 例句
Learning a new language requires considerable patience.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：with patience（耐心地）；lose one's patience（失去耐心）；patience with sb（对某人有耐心）；patient（adj. 耐心的）
🧠 助记
patient（耐心的病人）+ ce → 需要耐心的状态 → 耐心
patience::/ˈpeɪʃəns/:n. 耐心:Good teachers need patience and understanding.
2. ambiguous /æmˈbɪɡjuəs/ adj. 模糊的；歧义的；不明确的
📖 英文解释
Open to more than one interpretation; not having one obvious meaning; unclear or inexact.
✏️ 例句
His response to the question was deliberately ambiguous.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：ambiguous message/statement（模糊的信息/声明）；morally ambiguous（道德上模糊）；ambiguity（n. 歧义；模糊）；unambiguous（明确的）
🧠 助记
ambi（两边）+ guous（走，来自 aggression）→ 两边都能走 → 模糊的；歧义的
ambiguous::/æmˈbɪɡjuəs/:adj. 模糊的:The contract contained several ambiguous clauses that led to disputes.
3. suspicious /səˈspɪʃəs/ adj. 可疑的；怀疑的；不信任的
📖 英文解释
Having or showing a caution about the truth or genuineness of something; causing one to have the idea that something is possibly dangerous, wrong, or illegal.
✏️ 例句
Police were called when neighbors noticed suspicious activity in the area.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：be suspicious of/about（对…持怀疑）；look suspicious（看起来可疑）；suspicious behavior（可疑行为）；suspicion（n. 怀疑；嫌疑）
🧠 助记
suspect（怀疑）→ suspicious，联想：sus（嗖嗖的眼神）→ 充满怀疑的
suspicious::/səˈspɪʃəs/:adj. 可疑的:She was suspicious of his motives for helping her.
4. polite /pəˈlaɪt/ adj. 有礼貌的；客气的；有教养的
📖 英文解释
Having or showing behavior that is respectful and considerate of other people; refined or elegant.
✏️ 例句
It is polite to thank someone after receiving a gift.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：be polite to sb（对某人有礼貌）；politely（有礼貌地）；polite society（上流社会）；politeness（礼貌）
🧠 助记
pol（polish 抛光）+ ite，经过打磨的，像抛光的 → 有礼貌的；文雅的
polite::/pəˈlaɪt/:adj. 有礼貌的:She gave a polite but firm refusal to the invitation.
5. courage /ˈkɜːrɪdʒ/ n. 勇气；胆量；勇敢
📖 英文解释
The ability to do something that frightens one; bravery; strength in the face of pain or grief.
✏️ 例句
It takes great courage to speak out against injustice.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：have/show courage（有/展示勇气）；courage to do sth（做某事的勇气）；pluck up/gather courage（鼓起勇气）；courageous（adj. 勇敢的）
🧠 助记
cour（心，来自拉丁 cor）+ age → 心中有力量 → 勇气
courage::/ˈkɜːrɪdʒ/:n. 勇气:She had the courage to admit her mistakes publicly.
6. noble /ˈnoʊbəl/ adj. 高尚的；贵族的；壮丽的 n. 贵族
📖 英文解释
Having or showing fine personal qualities or high moral principles; belonging to the hereditary class with high social or political status; a person of noble rank.
✏️ 例句
His noble intentions were undermined by poor execution.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：noble cause/intention（高尚的目标/意图）；noble gases（稀有气体）；nobleman（贵族男性）；nobility（贵族阶级；高尚）
🧠 助记
noble 联想 no（否）+ ble（blame，责备），不会被责备的人 → 高尚的；贵族
noble::/ˈnoʊbəl/:adj. 高尚的:She devoted her life to the noble goal of educating underprivileged children.
7. suspend /səˈspend/ v. 暂停；悬挂；中止；推迟
📖 英文解释
To hang from above; to temporarily prevent from continuing; to defer or postpone; to bar someone temporarily from duties.
✏️ 例句
Operations were suspended due to safety concerns.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：suspend operations/service（暂停运营/服务）；suspend sb from sth（暂时停职）；suspend judgment（暂缓判断）；suspension（n. 暂停；悬挂）
🧠 助记
sus（下面）+ pend（挂），从上面挂下去 → 悬挂；暂停（悬而未决）
suspend::/səˈspend/:v. 暂停:The athlete was suspended from competition pending investigation.
8. undermine /ˌʌndərˈmaɪn/ v. 破坏；削弱；从根基蚕食
📖 英文解释
To erode the base or foundation of something; to lessen the effectiveness, power, or ability of someone or something, especially gradually or insidiously.
✏️ 例句
Corruption undermines trust in government institutions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：undermine confidence/authority/democracy（破坏信心/权威/民主）；undermine sb's position（动摇某人的地位）；undermine efforts（削弱努力）
🧠 助记
under（在下面）+ mine（挖矿）→ 在地下挖空 → 从根基破坏
undermine::/ˌʌndərˈmaɪn/:v. 破坏:Political scandals can undermine public trust in institutions.
9. deliberate /dɪˈlɪbərət/ adj. 故意的；深思熟虑的 v. 深思；仔细考虑
📖 英文解释
Done consciously and intentionally; carefully weighed in the mind; to think carefully before making a decision.
✏️ 例句
It was a deliberate attempt to mislead the public.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：deliberate act/decision（故意的行为/决定）；deliberate over sth（仔细考虑某事）；deliberately（adv. 故意地）；deliberation（n. 慎重考虑）
🧠 助记
de + liber（自由地）+ ate，自由仔细地思考 → 深思熟虑的；故意的
deliberate::/dɪˈlɪbərət/:adj. 故意的:After deliberate consideration, she decided to reject the offer.
10. operator /ˈɒpəreɪtər/ n. 操作员；经营者；运营商；话务员
📖 英文解释
A person who operates equipment or a machine; a person or company engaged in a business or industry; a person who handles telephone calls.
✏️ 例句
The network operator launched a new data plan for customers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：tour operator（旅游运营商）；telephone operator（电话接线员）；computer operator（计算机操作员）；operate（v. 操作；经营）
🧠 助记
operate（操作）+ or（人）→ 操作的人 → 操作员；经营者
operator::/ˈɒpəreɪtər/:n. 操作员:The mobile network operator expanded its coverage to rural areas.
11. substitute /ˈsʌbstɪtjuːt/ n. 替代品；替补 v. 代替；替换
📖 英文解释
A person or thing acting or used in place of another; to use or add someone or something in place of another.
✏️ 例句
There is no substitute for hard work and dedication.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：substitute for（…的替代品）；substitute A for B（用A替换B）；serve as a substitute（充当替代品）；substitution（n. 替代）
🧠 助记
sub（在下面）+ stit（站，来自 stand）+ ute → 站在下面等替补 → 替代
substitute::/ˈsʌbstɪtjuːt/:n. 替代品:Honey can be used as a substitute for sugar in this recipe.
12. fare /fer/ n. 票价；费用；（食物）饮食 v. 进展；过得如何
📖 英文解释
The money a passenger on public transportation pays to travel; the range of food served; to perform or function in a specified way.
✏️ 例句
Train fares have increased significantly over the past decade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：bus/taxi/train fare（公共汽车/出租车/火车票价）；how did you fare?（你过得怎么样？）；fare well/badly（顺利/不顺利）
🧠 助记
fare 来自古英语'旅行'，旅行要付的钱 → 票价；饮食（旅途中的食物）
fare::/fer/:n. 票价:How did the company fare during the economic downturn?
13. pollute /pəˈluːt/ v. 污染；弄脏；败坏（道德等）
📖 英文解释
Contaminate with harmful or poisonous substances; to corrupt or defile morally.
✏️ 例句
Industrial waste has heavily polluted the river.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：pollute the environment/air/water（污染环境/空气/水）；pollution（n. 污染）；pollutant（污染物）；polluted（被污染的）
🧠 助记
pol（来自拉丁 polluo 弄脏）+ lute（琵琶？）→ 连美妙的琵琶声也被弄脏了 → 污染
pollute::/pəˈluːt/:v. 污染:Factories that pollute local rivers should be heavily fined.
14. prominent /ˈprɒmɪnənt/ adj. 重要的；杰出的；突出的；显著的

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
promin（向前伸出）+ ent → 突出在前面 → 突出的；杰出的
prominent::/ˈprɒmɪnənt/:adj. 重要的:She became a prominent voice in the environmental movement.
15. thick /θɪk/ adj. 厚的；浓密的；浓的 adv. 厚厚地 n. 最浓密处

With a large distance between opposite sides; measuring a specified number of units in this dimension; having a large number of things close together; (of a liquid) relatively firm in consistency.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：thick fog/smoke（浓雾/浓烟）；thick book（厚书）；in the thick of（在…最激烈之处）；thick and fast（接连不断）
🧠 助记
联想：thick 谐音'踢克'，踢到厚厚的墙 → 厚的；浓密的
thick::/θɪk/:adj. 厚的:In the thick of the battle, communication was almost impossible.

Improve or enhance the quality or value of; make wealthy or wealthier; to increase the nutritional value of food.

🧠 助记
en（使）+ rich（富有）→ 使富有/丰富
enrich::/ɪnˈrɪtʃ/:v. 使富有:Reading widely enriches one's vocabulary and imagination.

18. verbal /ˈvɜːrbəl/ adj. 口头的；语言的；动词的 n. 口头协议
📖 英文解释
Relating to or in the form of words; spoken rather than written; relating to verbs.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：verbal agreement/communication（口头协议/交流）；verbal abuse（言语暴力）；verbal skills（语言能力）；non-verbal（非语言的）
🧠 助记
verb（动词/语言）+ al → 关于语言的 → 口头的；语言的
verbal::/ˈvɜːrbəl/:adj. 口头的:Good verbal communication skills are essential in customer service.
19. behalf /bɪˈhɑːf/ n. 代表；代理；利益
📖 英文解释
As a representative of or in the interest of a person or group.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

be + half（一半），代替对方的那一半 → 代表；代理
behalf::/bɪˈhɑːf/:n. 代表:I would like to thank you on behalf of the entire team.
20. tolerate /ˈtɒləreɪt/ v. 容忍；忍受；容许；耐受
📖 英文解释
Allow the existence, occurrence, or practice of something that one does not necessarily like or agree with; endure pain or difficulty without complaint.
✏️ 例句
The school does not tolerate bullying of any kind.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：tolerate sth/sb（容忍某事/某人）；barely/hardly tolerate（勉强忍受）；tolerance（n. 容忍；耐受力）；tolerant（adj. 宽容的）
🧠 助记
toler（忍受，来自拉丁 tolerare）+ ate → 忍受；容忍
tolerate::/ˈtɒləreɪt/:v. 容忍:Democratic societies must tolerate a wide range of opinions.

Strange or odd; unusual; belonging exclusively to a particular person, place, or circumstance.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：peculiar to（特有于；是…独有的）；peculiar behavior（奇特的行为）；peculiarity（n. 特性；怪癖）；peculiarly（奇特地）
🧠 助记
pe + culiar（culiar 像 curious 奇怪），非常奇怪的 → 奇特的；特有的
peculiar::/pɪˈkjuːliər/:adj. 奇特的:This tradition is peculiar to the culture of the indigenous people.
