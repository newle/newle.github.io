1. elevate /ˈelɪveɪt/ v. 提升；举起；使情绪高涨
📖 英文解释
To raise to a higher position or level; to improve the quality of something.
✏️ 例句
The new policy aims to elevate the living standards of rural communities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
elevate to 提升到；elevated position/level 高位/高水平；elevation n.；elevator n. 电梯
🧠 助记
el（=ex 向外）+ lev（举起，如lever杠杆）+ ate，向上举起→提升
elevate::/ˈelɪveɪt/:v. 提升:Exercise can elevate your mood and reduce stress levels.
2. plunge /plʌndʒ/ v./n. 跳入；骤降；突然陷入
📖 英文解释
To jump or fall suddenly; to decrease or drop rapidly; a sudden fall or dive.
✏️ 例句
Stock prices plunged after the announcement of the company's losses.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
plunge into 投入/陷入；take the plunge 决然行动；plunge in price/value 价格/价值骤降
🧠 助记
plunge 含 lunge（冲刺），向水里冲刺→跳入；急剧下降像跳入深水
plunge::/plʌndʒ/:v./n. 跳入:The economy plunged into recession after the financial crisis.
3. reception /rɪˈsepʃn/ n. 接待；欢迎；招待会；信号接收
📖 英文解释
The action of receiving something; a formal party to welcome guests; the quality of radio or TV signals.
✏️ 例句
The wedding reception was held at a five-star hotel.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
warm/cool reception 热烈/冷淡的欢迎；reception desk 前台；receive v.→reception n.
🧠 助记
receive（接收）→ reception，接收的行为和场所→接待、招待会
reception::/rɪˈsepʃn/:n. 接待:The author's new novel received a mixed reception from critics.
4. boast /bəʊst/ v. 自夸；以拥有…而自豪 n. 自吹自擂
📖 英文解释
To talk with excessive pride about something; to possess something to be proud of.
✏️ 例句
The city boasts some of the finest museums in the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
boast about/of 夸耀；boast that 自称；empty boast 空话；boastful adj. 爱自夸的
🧠 助记
boast 谐音"报事"，逢人就报喜自我宣传→自夸
boast::/bəʊst/:v. 自夸:He couldn't stop boasting about his promotion to his colleagues.
5. sue /suː/ v. 起诉；控告；请求
📖 英文解释
To take legal action against a person or organization; to make a formal request.
✏️ 例句
The patient decided to sue the hospital for medical negligence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sue sb for sth 就某事起诉某人；sue for damages/compensation 索赔；lawsuit n. 诉讼
🧠 助记
sue 谐音"诉"，上诉、起诉→起诉
sue::/suː/:v. 起诉:She threatened to sue the company for wrongful dismissal.
6. tape /teɪp/ n. 胶带；磁带；录音带 v. 用胶带贴；录音
📖 英文解释
A narrow strip of material coated with adhesive; magnetic material for recording; to record on tape.
✏️ 例句
The police used tape to seal off the crime scene.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
red tape 官僚主义/繁文缛节；tape recorder 录音机；magnetic tape 磁带；duct tape 强力胶带
🧠 助记
tape 形状细长如带子，粘贴东西或录录音→胶带、磁带
tape::/teɪp/:n. 胶带:He used tape to secure the poster to the wall.
7. opera /ˈɒpərə/ n. 歌剧；歌剧院
📖 英文解释
A dramatic work combining music and singing; the genre of such works.
✏️ 例句
The performance of Verdi's opera drew a large audience to the theater.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
opera house/singer/glasses 歌剧院/歌剧演员/歌剧望远镜；soap opera 肥皂剧；operatic adj.
🧠 助记
opera 和 work（opus）相关，综合表演的作品→歌剧
opera::/ˈɒpərə/:n. 歌剧:She has been a devoted fan of Italian opera for thirty years.
8. cathedral /kəˈθiːdrəl/ n. 大教堂；主教座堂
📖 英文解释
The principal church of a diocese, containing the bishop's throne.
✏️ 例句
Notre-Dame Cathedral in Paris is one of the most iconic buildings in the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cathedral city 主教所在城市；Gothic cathedral 哥特式大教堂；cathedral school 大教堂附属学校
🧠 助记
cathed（=chair 椅子）+ ral，放主教椅子的地方→大教堂（主教座堂）
cathedral::/kəˈθiːdrəl/:n. 大教堂:Tourists flock to the medieval cathedral every year.
9. greet /ɡriːt/ v. 问候；迎接；对…作出反应
📖 英文解释
To address someone with words or actions when meeting them; to react to in a specified way.
✏️ 例句
The host greeted each guest personally at the entrance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
greet sb warmly/with applause 热情迎接/掌声迎接；greeting n. 问候；seasonal greetings 节日问候
🧠 助记
greet 谐音"给礼"，给对方礼貌的问候→问候、迎接
greet::/ɡriːt/:v. 问候:She greeted the news of her promotion with great joy.
10. knock /nɒk/ v./n. 敲；碰撞；敲门声
📖 英文解释
To strike a surface noisily; to collide with something; the sound made by hitting.
✏️ 例句
She knocked on the door three times before entering the office.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
knock on/at the door 敲门；knock down 撞倒/拆除；knock out 击倒/淘汰；knock off 完成/停止工作
🧠 助记
knock 发音模拟敲门声 'kn-ock'，'k'不发音但留着提醒你→敲
knock::/nɒk/:v./n. 敲:He knocked on his neighbor's door to borrow some sugar.
11. mortgage /ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒ/ n. 抵押贷款；按揭 v. 抵押
📖 英文解释
A legal agreement to lend money at interest in exchange for property; to pledge property as security.
✏️ 例句
They took out a 30-year mortgage to buy their first home.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
mortgage loan/payment/rate 抵押贷款/按揭付款/利率；take out a mortgage 申请抵押贷款
🧠 助记
mort（死亡，如mortal）+ gage（保证），死亡保证/到死才还完的贷款→抵押贷款
mortgage::/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒ/:n. 抵押贷款:Rising interest rates made it harder for families to pay their mortgage.
12. rubbish /ˈrʌbɪʃ/ n. 垃圾；废话 v. 批评；贬低 adj. 差劲的
📖 英文解释
Waste material; nonsense or worthless talk; to criticize something harshly.
✏️ 例句
He called the proposal absolute rubbish and refused to support it.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rubbish bin/dump 垃圾桶/垃圾场；talk rubbish 胡说；rubbish at sth 在某方面很差
🧠 助记
rubbish 含 rub（磨擦），被磨烂的东西→垃圾、废话
rubbish::/ˈrʌbɪʃ/:n. 垃圾:The streets were littered with rubbish after the festival.
13. stereotype /ˈsteriətaɪp/ n. 刻板印象；模式化观念 v. 对…形成刻板印象
📖 英文解释
A widely held but fixed and oversimplified image or idea of a type of person or thing.
✏️ 例句
We should avoid reinforcing harmful stereotypes about different cultures.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
racial/gender stereotype 种族/性别刻板印象；stereotype sb as 把某人刻板化为；stereotypical adj.
🧠 助记
stereo（固定的/立体的）+ type（类型），固定的类型模板→刻板印象
stereotype::/ˈsteriətaɪp/:n. 刻板印象:The film challenges the stereotype that women are less capable leaders.
14. decent /ˈdiːsnt/ adj. 体面的；像样的；正派的；相当好的
📖 英文解释
Conforming to generally accepted standards of morality; of an acceptable standard; quite good.
✏️ 例句
Every citizen deserves access to decent housing and healthcare.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
decent salary/standard/behavior 体面的薪水/标准/行为；decent of sb 某人做得体面/好；decency n.
🧠 助记
decent 和 decency（体面）同根，decent = de + cent，一分钱的体面→像样的、正派的
decent::/ˈdiːsnt/:adj. 体面的:He struggled to find a decent job after graduating.
15. seminar /ˈsemɪnɑːr/ n. 研讨会；研讨课；专题讨论
📖 英文解释
A small group meeting for study or discussion, especially at a university.
✏️ 例句
Students are required to attend weekly seminars in addition to lectures.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
attend/hold/conduct a seminar 参加/举办/主持研讨会；seminar room/paper 研讨室/研讨论文
🧠 助记
semi（一半）+ nar（知识），把知识分发给一半人→小组研讨
seminar::/ˈsemɪnɑːr/:n. 研讨会:The professor led an engaging seminar on environmental policy.
16. adventure /ədˈventʃər/ n. 冒险；奇遇；冒险精神 v. 冒险
📖 英文解释
An unusual and exciting experience; willingness to take risks.
✏️ 例句
Backpacking through Southeast Asia was the greatest adventure of her life.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
adventure story/sport/tourism 冒险故事/极限运动/探险旅游；adventurous adj. 爱冒险的
🧠 助记
advent（到来，如adventure = ad + venture），到来的机遇→冒险
adventure::/ədˈventʃər/:n. 冒险:The children went on a great adventure in the forest.
17. affection /əˈfekʃn/ n. 喜爱；情感；爱慕之情
📖 英文解释
A gentle feeling of fondness or liking; love or care.
✏️ 例句
The teacher showed great affection for all of her students.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
show/display/feel affection 表现/流露/感受到爱；affection for/toward 对…的爱；affectionate adj.
🧠 助记
affect（影响/情感）+ ion，情感作用的结果→喜爱、爱慕
affection::/əˈfekʃn/:n. 喜爱:Parents naturally feel deep affection for their children.
18. constitution /ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃn/ n. 宪法；章程；体质；构成
📖 英文解释
The fundamental laws or principles of a nation; a person's physical health and strength; the composition of something.
✏️ 例句
The constitution guarantees freedom of speech and religion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
write/amend the constitution 起草/修改宪法；constitutional adj. 宪法的/体质的；constitution of a body 体质
🧠 助记
constitute（构成）→ constitution，构成国家的根本→宪法；也构成人的体质
constitution::/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃn/:n. 宪法:Any law that violates the constitution can be struck down by the court.
19. definite /ˈdefɪnɪt/ adj. 明确的；确定的；肯定的
📖 英文解释
Clearly stated or decided; not vague or doubtful.
✏️ 例句
We need a definite answer by the end of the week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
definite article 定冠词（the）；definite answer/plan/result 明确的答案/计划/结果；definitely adv.
🧠 助记
define（定义/界定）→ definite，已被清晰界定的→明确的、确定的
definite::/ˈdefɪnɪt/:adj. 明确的:There is no definite evidence linking him to the crime.
20. drift /drɪft/ v./n. 漂移；漂流；逐渐变化
📖 英文解释
To be carried slowly by water or wind; to move gradually; a slow movement or change.
✏️ 例句
The boat drifted aimlessly on the calm sea.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
drift apart 逐渐疏远；drift off 迷迷糊糊睡去；get the drift 明白大意；snowdrift 雪堆
🧠 助记
drift 和 drive 相关但无方向，随波逐流→漂流、漂移
drift::/drɪft/:v./n. 漂移:After graduation, the two friends slowly drifted apart.
21. endeavor /ɪnˈdevər/ n./v. 努力；尽力；奋斗
📖 英文解释
A serious attempt or effort to do something; to try hard to achieve something.
✏️ 例句
Despite their best endeavors, the team failed to complete the project on time.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
endeavor to do 尽力做；human endeavor 人类努力；in all one's endeavors 在所有努力中
🧠 助记
en（使）+ devoir（=duty 义务，法语），使自己尽义务→努力、奋斗
endeavor::/ɪnˈdevər/:n./v. 努力:She endeavored to make her parents proud through hard work.
22. enlarge /ɪnˈlɑːrdʒ/ v. 扩大；放大；详述
📖 英文解释
To make something larger; to provide more information.
✏️ 例句
The company plans to enlarge its manufacturing capacity next year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
enlarge a photo/building/scope 放大照片/扩建/扩大范围；enlarge on/upon 详述；enlargement n.
🧠 助记
en（使）+ large（大），使变大→扩大、放大
enlarge::/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒ/:v. 扩大:Could you enlarge on that point you made about funding?
23. mineral /ˈmɪnərəl/ n. 矿物质；矿物；矿泉水 adj. 矿物的
📖 英文解释
A solid substance found in nature with a definite chemical composition; a substance obtained by mining.
✏️ 例句
The body needs minerals like iron and calcium to function properly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
mineral water/resources/wealth 矿泉水/矿产资源/矿产财富；mineral supplement 矿物质补充剂
🧠 助记
mine（矿山）→ mineral，从矿山得到的物质→矿物质
mineral::/ˈmɪnərəl/:n. 矿物质:The region is rich in mineral resources including gold and copper.
24. annoy /əˈnɔɪ/ v. 使烦恼；打扰；惹怒
📖 英文解释
To make someone feel slightly angry or impatient; to bother.
✏️ 例句
The constant noise from the construction site annoyed the residents.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
annoy sb with/by 用…烦某人；annoyed adj. 恼怒的；annoying adj. 令人恼火的；annoyance n.
🧠 助记
an（=on）+ noy（=noise 噪音），一直有噪音→使烦恼
annoy::/əˈnɔɪ/:v. 使烦恼:It really annoys me when people are late without calling.
25. bush /bʊʃ/ n. 灌木；丛林；未开化地区
📖 英文解释
A shrub or clump of shrubs; wild and uncultivated land in Australia or Africa.
✏️ 例句
A rabbit darted out from behind the bush when we approached.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
beat around the bush 拐弯抹角；bush fire 灌木丛火灾；the bush（澳大利亚）丛林地区
🧠 助记
bush 和 brush（刷子/灌木）相近，灌木丛像一把大刷子
bush::/bʊʃ/:n. 灌木:Stop beating around the bush and tell me what happened.
