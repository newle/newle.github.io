1. poem /ˈpəʊɪm/ n. 诗；诗歌；诗篇
📖 英文解释
A piece of writing arranged in short lines, often using rhyme and rhythm to express ideas or emotions.
✏️ 例句
She wrote a beautiful poem about the changing seasons.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
write/compose/recite a poem 写/作/背诵诗；poem about/on sth 关于…的诗；poetry n. 诗歌总称
🧠 助记
poem = po（倒过来是op，作品）+ em，情感（em/emotion）凝练成文字→诗
poem::/ˈpəʊɪm/:n. 诗:His poem about loss was selected for the national anthology.
2. scatter /ˈskætər/ v. 散开；撒；使分散 n. 散落
📖 英文解释
To throw things in various directions; to separate and go in different directions; to spread over a wide area.
✏️ 例句
The seeds were scattered by the wind across the field.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
scatter seeds/leaves/people 撒种子/落叶散开/人群四散；scattered adj. 分散的；scatter brain 心不在焉
🧠 助记
sc（四处）+ atter（scatter），四处飞溅→散开；类比shatter（打碎）
scatter::/ˈskætər/:v. 散开:The protesters scattered when the police arrived.
3. wipe /waɪp/ v. 擦；抹去；消灭 n. 擦拭
📖 英文解释
To clean or dry something by rubbing it with a cloth; to remove completely.
✏️ 例句
She wiped the table clean before setting out the dishes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
wipe out 消灭/彻底毁灭；wipe off 擦掉；wipe away tears 擦去眼泪；wipeable adj. 可擦的
🧠 助记
wipe 和 pipe（管子）押韵，用抹布像擦管子一样擦→擦拭
wipe::/waɪp/:v. 擦:The flood wiped out several villages in the region.
4. incredible /ɪnˈkredɪbl/ adj. 难以置信的；惊人的；极好的
📖 英文解释
Impossible or very hard to believe; extremely good, large, or impressive.
✏️ 例句
The athlete's recovery from injury was absolutely incredible.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
incredible achievement/story/speed 惊人的成就/故事/速度；incredibly adv. 令人难以置信地
🧠 助记
in（不）+ credible（可信的，如credit信任），不可置信的→难以置信的
incredible::/ɪnˈkredɪbl/:adj. 难以置信的:It is incredible that he survived such a serious accident.
5. mechanic /məˈkænɪk/ n. 机械师；技工；力学原理
📖 英文解释
A person who repairs and maintains machinery, especially vehicles; the way in which something works.
✏️ 例句
The mechanic diagnosed the problem with the engine within minutes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
car/auto mechanic 汽车技工；mechanics of sth 某事的运作机制；mechanical adj. 机械的
🧠 助记
mechan（机器）+ ic（人），操作机器的人→机械师；mechanics→力学
mechanic::/məˈkænɪk/:n. 机械师:The mechanic replaced the brake pads and adjusted the suspension.
6. enthusiastic /ɪnˌθjuːziˈæstɪk/ adj. 热情的；热心的；积极的
📖 英文解释
Having or showing intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval.
✏️ 例句
The students were enthusiastic about the new science project.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
enthusiastic about/over 对…热情；enthusiastic response/supporter 热情回应/热心支持者；enthusiasm n.
🧠 助记
en（使）+ thus（=theo神）+ iastic，被神灵附体般的热情→热情的
enthusiastic::/ɪnˌθjuːziˈæstɪk/:adj. 热情的:She received an enthusiastic welcome from the local community.
7. flourish /ˈflʌrɪʃ/ v. 繁荣；茁壮成长；挥动 n. 炫耀动作
📖 英文解释
To grow or develop in a healthy or vigorous way; to wave something about; a bold or extravagant gesture.
✏️ 例句
Small businesses flourished after the government reduced regulations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
flourish in/under 在…中繁荣；flourish a sword/pen 挥舞剑/笔；with a flourish 炫耀地
🧠 助记
flour（面粉）+ ish，面粉像花粉，植物散花→繁茂生长；也如挥动手势一样洒脱
flourish::/ˈflʌrɪʃ/:v. 繁荣:Art and culture flourished during the Renaissance period.
8. inch /ɪntʃ/ n. 英寸；少量 v. 缓慢移动
📖 英文解释
A unit of length equal to 1/12 of a foot (about 2.54 cm); to move slowly and carefully.
✏️ 例句
The rescue workers inched their way through the rubble to find survivors.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
every inch 每英寸/完全；inch by inch 一点一点地；not give/budge an inch 一步不让；within an inch of 差点
🧠 助记
inch（英寸）很小，引申为一点点移动→缓慢移动
inch::/ɪntʃ/:n. 英寸:The negotiators refused to give an inch on the key terms.
9. lung /lʌŋ/ n. 肺；肺脏
📖 英文解释
Either of the two organs in the chest that allow breathing.
✏️ 例句
Heavy smoking significantly damages the lungs over time.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lung cancer/disease/capacity 肺癌/肺病/肺活量；at the top of one's lungs 扯着嗓子；iron lung 铁肺
🧠 助记
lung 形状像充气的气球（long and round），肺里充满空气→肺
lung::/lʌŋ/:n. 肺:Regular exercise can strengthen your heart and lungs.
10. frame /freɪm/ n. 框架；骨架；帧 v. 构造；陷害
📖 英文解释
A rigid structure that surrounds or supports something; to put a picture in a frame; to set up falsely.
✏️ 例句
She framed the argument in terms of social justice and human rights.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
frame of mind 心境；frame sb 陷害某人；time frame 时间框架；reference frame 参照系
🧠 助记
frame 和 form 相关，给事物赋予形式和边界→框架、构造
frame::/freɪm/:n. 框架:The journalist framed the story as a conflict between tradition and progress.
11. profile /ˈprəʊfaɪl/ n. 侧面；轮廓；简介；知名度 v. 描述
📖 英文解释
The outline or shape of something seen from the side; a short description of a person or organization.
✏️ 例句
The company raised its public profile by sponsoring major sports events.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
high/low profile 高/低调；profile of sb 某人简介；criminal profile 犯罪档案；user profile 用户资料
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ file（文件），向前展示的文件→简介；侧面如文件侧边
profile::/ˈprəʊfaɪl/:n. 侧面:She kept a low profile during the controversy.
12. youngster /ˈjʌŋstər/ n. 年轻人；少年；小孩
📖 英文解释
A child or young person.
✏️ 例句
Local youngsters volunteered to help clean up the neighborhood.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
youngster of today/nowadays 当今年轻人；gifted youngster 有天分的孩子；youngster program 青年计划
🧠 助记
young（年轻）+ ster（人，如gangster），年轻的人→年轻人
youngster::/ˈjʌŋstər/:n. 年轻人:The program provides opportunities for youngsters from disadvantaged backgrounds.
13. brick /brɪk/ n. 砖块；方形物 v. 用砖砌
📖 英文解释
A small rectangular block of baked clay used in building; a reliable or helpful person.
✏️ 例句
The old school building was made of red bricks.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
brick wall 砖墙；hit a brick wall 碰壁；brick by brick 一点一点地；brickwork 砖砌结构
🧠 助记
brick 形状方正如砖，br-ick 发音短促如砖块碰撞声→砖块
brick::/brɪk/:n. 砖块:They built the garden wall brick by brick over the weekend.
14. dictate /dɪkˈteɪt/ v. 口述；命令；支配 n. 命令；规定
📖 英文解释
To say words aloud for someone to write down; to control or influence the way something happens; an authoritative order.
✏️ 例句
The market dictates what products companies decide to produce.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dictate to sb 向某人发号施令；dictate a letter/speech 口述信件/演讲；dictator n. 独裁者
🧠 助记
dict（说，如diction 发音）+ ate，说出来让人听从→口述；强迫别人听→命令
dictate::/dɪkˈteɪt/:v. 口述:You cannot dictate how other people should live their lives.
15. organ /ˈɔːrɡən/ n. 器官；管风琴；机构
📖 英文解释
A part of the body with a specific vital function; a large musical keyboard instrument; an organization.
✏️ 例句
The heart is the most vital organ in the human body.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
vital organ 重要器官；organ donation/transplant 器官捐献/移植；pipe organ 管风琴；state organ 国家机构
🧠 助记
organ 词根 org（组织/秩序），有组织构造的部位→器官；有序排列音管→管风琴
organ::/ˈɔːrɡən/:n. 器官:Organ donation can save the lives of multiple patients.
16. piano /piˈænəʊ/ n. 钢琴 adj./adv. 轻柔地（音乐）
📖 英文解释
A large keyboard musical instrument with felt-covered hammers that strike strings; softly in music.
✏️ 例句
She practiced piano for two hours every day before school.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
play the piano 弹钢琴；grand/upright piano 三角/立式钢琴；piano lesson/recital 钢琴课/独奏会
🧠 助记
piano 来自意大利语，原名 pianoforte（轻重），因能控制音量轻重得名→钢琴
piano::/piˈænəʊ/:n. 钢琴 adj./adv. 轻柔地（:Learning to play the piano requires years of dedicated practice.
17. twist /twɪst/ v./n. 扭转；弯曲；曲解；转折
📖 英文解释
To form into a bent or wound shape; to change or distort; an unexpected development in a story.
✏️ 例句
The road twisted through the mountains for several kilometers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
twist of fate 命运的转折；twist sb's arm 施压；plot twist 情节转折；twist the truth 曲解事实
🧠 助记
twist 和 turn 都含扭转动作，tw-twist 发音如拧紧→扭转、转折
twist::/twɪst/:v./n. 扭转:The film ends with a surprising twist that changes everything.
18. wherever /weərˈevər/ conj./adv. 无论在哪里；到处
📖 英文解释
In or to whatever place; regardless of where.
✏️ 例句
She carries a notebook wherever she goes to jot down ideas.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
wherever possible 在任何可能的情况下；wherever you go 无论你去哪里；wherever that is 不管那是哪里
🧠 助记
where（哪里）+ ever（无论），无论在哪里→wherever
wherever::/weərˈevər/:conj./adv. 无论在哪里:Wherever there is injustice, there will be resistance.
19. gaze /ɡeɪz/ v./n. 凝视；注视
📖 英文解释
To look steadily and intently at something; a steady intent look.
✏️ 例句
She gazed out of the window at the falling snow.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
gaze at/into/upon 凝视；gaze in wonder/admiration 惊奇/钦佩地注视；under the public gaze 在公众注视下
🧠 助记
gaze 谐音"给着"，一直给着（盯着）某物看→凝视
gaze::/ɡeɪz/:v./n. 凝视:He held her gaze for a moment before looking away.
20. junior /ˈdʒuːniər/ adj. 初级的；年幼的 n. 下级；晚辈
📖 英文解释
Lower in rank or status; younger in age; a person of lower rank or a younger person.
✏️ 例句
She started as a junior employee and worked her way up to management.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
junior/senior 下级/上级；junior high school 初中；junior partner 初级合伙人；junior to sb 比某人级别低
🧠 助记
junior 和 senior 对应，jun（年轻）+ ior（比较级），更年轻/更低级→初级的
junior::/ˈdʒuːniər/:adj. 初级的:He mentored several junior colleagues throughout his career.
21. subway /ˈsʌbweɪ/ n. 地铁；地下通道
📖 英文解释
An underground railway system in a city; an underground passage for pedestrians.
✏️ 例句
Taking the subway is the fastest way to get around New York City.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
subway station/line/map 地铁站/线路/地图；ride/take the subway 坐地铁；subway entrance 地铁入口
🧠 助记
sub（下面）+ way（路），地下的路→地铁；英式 underground，美式 subway
subway::/ˈsʌbweɪ/:n. 地铁:The city plans to expand its subway network by 2030.
22. pyramid /ˈpɪrəmɪd/ n. 金字塔；角锥体；等级制度
📖 英文解释
A monumental structure with a square base and sloping triangular sides; a hierarchical structure.
✏️ 例句
The food pyramid helps people understand a balanced and healthy diet.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the Egyptian pyramids 埃及金字塔；pyramid scheme 传销；corporate/social pyramid 企业/社会等级结构
🧠 助记
pyra（=fire 火，尖顶像火焰）+ mid（中间），中间有火焰一样的尖顶→金字塔
pyramid::/ˈpɪrəmɪd/:n. 金字塔:Wealth is often concentrated at the top of the economic pyramid.
23. remedy /ˈremədi/ n. 补救措施；疗法；药物 v. 补救；纠正
📖 英文解释
A solution to a problem; a medicine or treatment for a disease; to put something wrong right.
✏️ 例句
The government proposed several remedies to address the housing crisis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
remedy for/to 解决…的方法；beyond remedy 无法补救；home remedy 家庭疗法；remedial adj. 补救的
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ med（医疗）+ y，再次进行医疗→补救、疗法
remedy::/ˈremədi/:n. 补救措施:Early intervention is the best remedy for learning difficulties.
24. mature /məˈtʃʊər/ adj. 成熟的；成年的 v. 变成熟；到期
📖 英文解释
Fully developed physically or mentally; sensible and responsible; to become fully developed.
✏️ 例句
The mature student brought valuable life experience to the classroom.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
mature student/market/economy 成熟学生/市场/经济；mature into 成长为；maturity n. 成熟
🧠 助记
ma（妈）+ ture，妈妈把你养到成熟→成熟的
mature::/məˈtʃʊər/:adj. 成熟的:As he matured, he became more patient and understanding.
25. pump /pʌmp/ n. 泵；抽水机 v. 抽；泵送；注入
📖 英文解释
A device for moving liquids or gas; to move something using a pump; to supply abundantly.
✏️ 例句
The government pumped billions into the failing economy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pump water/blood/gas 抽水/泵血/充气；pump up 打气/增强；pump iron 举重；pump sb for info 套话
🧠 助记
pump 发音模拟抽压动作 pu-mp，一压一抽→泵、抽水机
pump::/pʌmp/:n. 泵:They pumped large amounts of money into the new infrastructure project.
