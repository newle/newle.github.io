1. tropical /ˈtrɒpikl/ adj. 热带的；热带地区的
📖 英文解释
Relating to the tropics; very hot and humid.
✏️ 例句
Tropical forests are home to more than half of the world species.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：tropical rainforest（热带雨林）；tropical disease（热带病）；tropical climate
🧠 助记
tropic（热带）+al → 热带地区的
tropical::/ˈtrɒpikl/:adj. 热带的:Tropical deforestation threatens global biodiversity.
2. chef /ʃef/ n. 厨师；主厨
📖 英文解释
A professional cook, typically the chief cook in a restaurant or hotel.
✏️ 例句
The chef prepared an exquisite meal for the guests.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研背景词；head chef（主厨）；masterchef（大厨）
🧠 助记
chef → 法语借词 → 专业厨师
chef::/ʃef/:n. 厨师:The hotel chef won a national cooking competition last year.
3. junk /dʒʌŋk/ n. 垃圾；废旧物；v. 丢弃
📖 英文解释
Old or discarded articles considered useless or of little value; to discard.
✏️ 例句
She spent the weekend clearing out junk from the garage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：junk food（垃圾食品）；junk mail（垃圾邮件）；junk bond（垃圾债券）
🧠 助记
junk → 发音像"扔克" → 扔掉没用的 → 垃圾
junk::/dʒʌŋk/:n. 垃圾:Children who eat too much junk food are at risk of obesity.
4. pack /pæk/ v. 打包；塞满；n. 包；一群
📖 英文解释
To put items into a bag or container; a bag or bundle; a group of animals.
✏️ 例句
She packed her belongings and left the apartment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：pack of lies（一堆谎言）；pack into（塞入）；packed（adj. 拥挤的）
🧠 助记
pack → 背包（backpack）→ 打包装入
pack::/pæk/:v. 打包:The stadium was packed with enthusiastic fans during the final match.
5. interior /inˈtiəriə/ n. 内部；内地；adj. 内部的
📖 英文解释
The inner part of something; situated within or inside.
✏️ 例句
The interior of the old church was beautifully decorated.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研辨析：interior（内部）vs. internal（国内的）vs. inner（内心的）；interior design（室内设计）
🧠 助记
inter（在内）+ior（更）→ 更在里面 → 内部
interior::/inˈtiəriə/:n. 内部:The country interior regions remain underdeveloped compared to coastal cities.
6. liability /ˌlaiəˈbiliti/ n. 责任；义务；不利因素；债务
📖 英文解释
The state of being responsible for something; a person or thing causing difficulty.
✏️ 例句
The company accepted liability for the damages caused by the accident.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：legal liability（法律责任）；liability insurance（责任险）；asset vs. liability（资产对负债）
🧠 助记
liable（有责任的）+ity → 有责任的状态 → 责任/负债
liability::/ˌlaiəˈbiliti/:n. 责任:Companies must acknowledge their liability when products cause harm.
7. bid /bid/ v./n. 出价；投标；努力争取
📖 英文解释
To offer a price for something; an attempt to obtain or achieve something.
✏️ 例句
Several companies submitted bids for the construction contract.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：bid for sth（竞标）；takeover bid（收购要约）；bid farewell（告别）
🧠 助记
bid → b（buy）+id → 买东西时出的价 → 出价/竞标
bid::/bid/:v./n. 出价:The city submitted a bid to host the 2030 World Cup.
8. rude /ruːd/ adj. 粗鲁的；无礼的；粗糙的
📖 英文解释
Offensively impolite or bad-mannered; roughly made or done.
✏️ 例句
It is rude to interrupt someone while they are speaking.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研辨析：rude（粗鲁）vs. impolite（不礼貌）vs. crude（粗俗）；rude awakening（突然的不快发现）
🧠 助记
rude → 读音像"鲁德" → 联想粗鲁的人
rude::/ruːd/:adj. 粗鲁的:His rude behavior during the meeting offended several colleagues.
9. criterion /kraiˈtiəriən/ n. 标准；准则（复数 criteria）
📖 英文解释
A principle or standard by which something may be judged or decided.
✏️ 例句
Academic performance is one of the main criteria for admission.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：注意复数 criteria；meet/satisfy criteria（满足标准）；evaluation criteria（评估标准）
🧠 助记
crit（评判）+erion → 用来评判的东西 → 标准
criterion::/kraiˈtiəriən/:n. 标准:The selection criteria for the scholarship include both grades and community service.
10. gradual /ˈɡrædʒuəl/ adj. 逐渐的；渐进的
📖 英文解释
Taking place or progressing slowly or by degrees.
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记
grade（级别）+ual → 一级一级来 → 逐渐的
gradual::/ˈɡrædʒuəl/:adj. 逐渐的:The gradual rise in sea levels poses a threat to coastal communities.
11. objection /əbˈdʒekʃn/ n. 反对；异议；障碍
📖 英文解释
An expression of opposition or disapproval; a reason for opposing something.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：raise/voice an objection；have no objection to；object v.（反对）
🧠 助记
ob（对着）+ject（扔）+ion → 朝对面扔东西 → 反对
objection::/əbˈdʒekʃn/:n. 反对:The defense lawyer raised a strong objection to the evidence presented.
12. surname /ˈsɜːneim/ n. 姓；姓氏
📖 英文解释
A hereditary name common to all members of a family, as distinct from a given name.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
sur（上面/超过）+name → 超越个人名字之上的名字 → 姓
surname::/ˈsɜːneim/:n. 姓:Many immigrants changed their surnames to assimilate into the new culture.

15. outlet /ˈautlet/ n. 出口；发泄途径；销售点；插座
