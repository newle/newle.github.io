1. festival /ˈfestəvl/ n. 节日；庆典；艺术节；adj. 节日的
📖 英文解释
A day or period of celebration; an organized series of performances or exhibitions.
✏️ 例句
The film festival attracts directors and actors from all over the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：film/music/food festival；national festival（国家节日）；festive（adj.节庆的）
🧠 助记
fest（庆祝）+ival → 庆祝活动 → 节日
festival::/ˈfestəvl/:n. 节日:The annual cultural festival promotes understanding between different ethnic groups.
2. quest /kwest/ n. 探索；寻求；追求；v. 寻找
📖 英文解释
A long search for something; an attempt to find or obtain something.
✏️ 例句
The quest for sustainable energy sources has driven major investment in renewables.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：quest for knowledge/truth/peace（寻求知识/真理/和平）；in quest of（寻求）
🧠 助记
quest → question（问题）→ 追问探索 → 探索/寻求
quest::/kwest/:n. 探索:The quest for artificial intelligence has transformed the tech industry.
3. conceive /kənˈsiːv/ v. 设想；构想；怀孕；理解
📖 英文解释
To form a plan or idea; to become pregnant; to think or believe something.
✏️ 例句
It is hard to conceive of a world without the internet.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研双义：conceive an idea（构想）；conceive a child（怀孕）；inconceivable（adj.不可思议的）
🧠 助记
con（完全）+ceive（抓住）→ 完全抓住想法 → 构想
conceive::/kənˈsiːv/:v. 设想:The founders conceived the platform as a space for open academic exchange.
4. underestimate /ˌʌndərˈestimeit/ v. 低估；看轻；n. 低估
📖 英文解释
To estimate something to be smaller or less important than it actually is.
✏️ 例句
Never underestimate the power of public opinion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：underestimate the risk/impact/difficulty；辨析 overestimate（高估）
🧠 助记
under（不足）+estimate（估计）→ 估计不足 → 低估
underestimate::/ˌʌndərˈestimeit/:v. 低估:Companies often underestimate the long-term costs of poor data security.
5. bet /bet/ v. 打赌；确信；n. 赌注；打赌
📖 英文解释
To risk money on the result of an event; to feel confident about something.
✏️ 例句
I bet you don't know the answer to this question.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：bet on（押注）；safe bet（稳妥的选择）；hedge one's bets（两面下注/对冲）
🧠 助记
bet → 押注 → 打赌
bet::/bet/:v. 打赌:Investing in education is the safest bet for long-term economic growth.
6. density /ˈdensiti/ n. 密度；人口密度；稠密
📖 英文解释
The degree of compactness of a substance; the number of people or things in a given area.
✏️ 例句
Urban population density has increased dramatically in recent decades.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：population density（人口密度）；density of information（信息密度）；dense（adj.密集的）
🧠 助记
dense（密集）+ity → 密集的程度 → 密度
density::/ˈdensiti/:n. 密度:High population density in cities creates both opportunities and challenges.
7. prescription /priˈskripʃn/ n. 处方；药方；规定；指令
📖 英文解释
An instruction written by a doctor for a patient's medicine; a recommendation.
✏️ 例句
The doctor wrote a prescription for a two-week course of antibiotics.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：prescription drug（处方药）；by prescription（凭处方）；prescriptive（adj.规定性的）
🧠 助记
pre（预先）+script（写）+ion → 预先写好的（医嘱）→ 处方
prescription::/priˈskripʃn/:n. 处方:The high cost of prescription drugs remains a major public health concern.
8. resistance /riˈzistəns/ n. 抵抗；阻力；电阻；抵制
📖 英文解释
The refusal to accept or comply with something; the ability to withstand a force.
✏️ 例句
The bacteria have developed resistance to several common antibiotics.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：resistance to change/reform；antibiotic resistance（抗生素耐药性）；armed resistance（武装抵抗）
🧠 助记
resist（抵抗）+ance → 抵抗的能力/行为 → 抵抗
resistance::/riˈzistəns/:n. 抵抗:Growing resistance to antibiotics threatens the future of modern medicine.
9. sir /sɜː/ n. 先生；爵士（称谓）
📖 英文解释
A polite or respectful form of address for a man; a title used before the name of a knight.
✏️ 例句
Sir, would you mind moving to another seat?
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研背景词；Sir + 名字（爵士称谓）；yes sir（遵命）；Dear Sir/Madam（正式书信开头）
🧠 助记
sir → s+ir → 礼貌称呼男性
sir::/sɜː/:n. 先生:The formal letter began with Dear Sir or Madam.
10. leap /liːp/ v./n. 跳跃；大幅度增加；飞跃
📖 英文解释
To jump high or far; a sudden large increase or change.
✏️ 例句
The country has made a great leap forward in renewable energy production.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：leap forward（飞跃）；leap of faith（信念的跳跃）；by leaps and bounds（突飞猛进）
🧠 助记
leap → l+eap → 一下子跳远 → 跳跃/飞跃
leap::/liːp/:v./n. 跳跃:The company made a technological leap that put it ahead of its competitors.
11. reproduce /ˌriːprəˈdjuːs/ v. 复制；繁殖；再生产；重现
📖 英文解释
To produce a copy of; for an organism to produce offspring; to recreate.
✏️ 例句
The study findings were reproduced in three independent laboratories.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：reproduce the results（复现结果）；sexually/asexually reproduce（有性/无性繁殖）；reproduction（n.）
🧠 助记
re（再次）+produce（产生）→ 再次产生 → 复制/繁殖
reproduce::/ˌriːprəˈdjuːs/:v. 复制:Scientists struggled to reproduce the experiment results under controlled conditions.
12. revolutionary /ˌrevəˈluːʃəneri/ adj. 革命性的；根本变革的；n. 革命者
📖 英文解释
Involving or causing complete or dramatic change; relating to political revolution.
✏️ 例句
The internet was a revolutionary technology that transformed global communication.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：revolutionary change/idea/technology；revolution（n.革命）；evolutionary vs. revolutionary（渐进vs.革命性）
🧠 助记
revolution（革命）+ary → 革命性的
revolutionary::/ˌrevəˈluːʃəneri/:adj. 革命性的:The discovery of penicillin was a revolutionary breakthrough in medicine.
13. shy /ʃai/ adj. 害羞的；腼腆的；v. 回避；退缩
📖 英文解释
Nervous or timid in the company of other people; to avoid doing something.
✏️ 例句
She was too shy to speak up in class, even when she knew the answer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：shy away from（回避）；camera-shy（怕镜头的）；辨析 timid（胆小）, reserved（内敛）
🧠 助记
shy → 发音像"害" → 害羞/害怕
shy::/ʃai/:adj. 害羞的:Companies often shy away from transparency when facing public scrutiny.
14. brush /brʌʃ/ n. 刷子；画笔；短暂接触；v. 刷；掠过
📖 英文解释
An implement with bristles for cleaning or painting; to touch lightly in passing.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：brush aside（置之不理）；brush up on（温习）；brush with death（与死亡擦肩而过）
🧠 助记
brush → br（quickly）+ush → 快速扫过 → 刷/掠过
brush::/brʌʃ/:n. 刷子:The government brushed aside criticism of its economic plan.

The cinema plays a significant role in reflecting and shaping social values.

考研常用：cinema industry（电影工业）；go to the cinema（去看电影）；辨析 movie theater（美式）

cinema → cine（电影）+ma → 看电影的地方 → 电影院
cinema::/ˈsinəmə/:n. 电影院:The rise of streaming services has transformed the global cinema industry.
16. essence /ˈesns/ n. 本质；精华；要素；香精
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：in essence（本质上）；the essence of sth（本质）；essential（adj.基本的）
🧠 助记
esse（存在）+nce → 存在的核心 → 本质
essence::/ˈesns/:n. 本质:In essence, the proposal calls for a complete overhaul of the tax system.
17. gun /ɡʌn/ n. 枪；炮；喷枪；v. 射击

gun → 象声词，枪声 → 枪
gun::/ɡʌn/:n. 枪:The debate over gun control remains deeply divisive in American society.

inhabitant::/inˈhæbitənt/:n. 居民:The original inhabitants of the land were displaced by colonial expansion.
19. momentum /məˈmentəm/ n. 势头；动力；冲劲；动量

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

momentum::/məˈmentəm/:n. 势头:The peace talks lost momentum after several key leaders withdrew their support.

stare::/steə/:v. 盯着看:The solution was staring them in the face all along.

wander::/ˈwɒndə/:v. 游荡:His thoughts began to wander as the presentation dragged on.

23. polish /ˈpɒliʃ/ v. 擦亮；润色；使完善；n. 光泽；鞋油；优雅

slight::/slait/:adj. 轻微的:The report showed only a slight decline in unemployment, suggesting slow progress.
