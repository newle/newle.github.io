1. tune /tjuːn/ n. 曲调；旋律 v. 调音；调整
📖 英文解释
A melody; to adjust a musical instrument to the correct pitch; to adjust a device.
✏️ 例句
She hummed a familiar tune as she cooked dinner.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in tune with 与…协调；out of tune 不协调；fine-tune 微调；tuned in 关注（考研写作）
🧠 助记
tune → 谐音 调 → 调音/调子
tune::/tjuːn/:n. 曲调:The company must stay in tune with changing consumer expectations.
2. apology /əˈpɒlədʒi/ n. 道歉；辩护
📖 英文解释
A regretful acknowledgment of an offence or failure; a formal justification.
✏️ 例句
The company issued a formal apology to all affected customers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make/offer an apology 道歉；letter of apology 道歉信；owe sb an apology 欠某人道歉
🧠 助记
apo（离开）+ logy（说话）→ 说话把事情说清楚 → 道歉/辩解
apology::/əˈpɒlədʒi/:n. 道歉:The CEO made a public apology for the data breach.
3. loud /laʊd/ adj. 响亮的；大声的；俗气的
📖 英文解释
Producing a lot of noise; conspicuously bright or gaudy.
✏️ 例句
The crowd let out a loud cheer when the team scored.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
out loud 大声地；loud and clear 清楚明了；a loud colour 俗气的颜色；loud protest 强烈抗议
🧠 助记
loud → 声音大得到处都能听到
loud::/laʊd/:adj. 响亮的:She made her objections loud and clear at the committee meeting.
4. portray /pɔːˈtreɪ/ v. 描绘；描述；扮演
📖 英文解释
To depict someone or something in a work of art or literature; to describe in a particular way.
✏️ 例句
The film portrays the war through the eyes of ordinary soldiers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
portray sb as 把某人描绘成；portray in a positive light 以正面形象描绘；portrait n. 肖像
🧠 助记
por（向前）+ tray（托盘）→ 托出来展示 → 描绘
portray::/pɔːˈtreɪ/:v. 描绘:The media tend to portray scientists as eccentric geniuses.
5. burst /bɜːst/ v./n. 爆裂；突然发生；一阵
📖 英文解释
To break open suddenly and violently; a sudden occurrence of something.
✏️ 例句
A burst of applause greeted the conductor as he took to the stage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
burst into tears 突然哭泣；burst out laughing 突然大笑；a burst of energy 一阵能量
🧠 助记
burst → 像气球砰地爆开 → 爆裂
burst::/bɜːst/:v./n. 爆裂:The dam burst after days of heavy rainfall.
6. excitement /ɪkˈsaɪtmənt/ n. 兴奋；激动；刺激
📖 英文解释
A feeling of great enthusiasm and eagerness; something that arouses such a feeling.
✏️ 例句
There was great excitement among the children on the morning of the trip.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in a state of excitement 处于兴奋状态；excitement over/about 对…的兴奋；stir up excitement 激起兴奋
🧠 助记
excite（激动）+ ment → 激动的状态 → 兴奋
excitement::/ɪkˈsaɪtmənt/:n. 兴奋:The announcement generated considerable excitement among investors.
7. geography /dʒiˈɒɡrəfi/ n. 地理学；地形
📖 英文解释
The study of the physical features of the earth and its atmosphere; the arrangement of physical features.
✏️ 例句
The geography of the region makes it highly vulnerable to flooding.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
human geography 人文地理；economic geography 经济地理；political geography 政治地理
🧠 助记
geo（地球）+ graphy（书写）→ 描写地球的学问 → 地理
geography::/dʒiˈɒɡrəfi/:n. 地理学:The country's geography has shaped its economic and political development.
8. prevalent /ˈprevələnt/ adj. 普遍的；流行的
📖 英文解释
Widespread in a particular area or at a particular time.
✏️ 例句
Respiratory diseases are particularly prevalent in heavily polluted cities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prevalent among 在…中普遍；prevalent attitude 普遍态度；increasingly prevalent 日益普遍
🧠 助记
preval（占优势）+ ent → 占优势的 → 普遍的
prevalent::/ˈprevələnt/:adj. 普遍的:Obesity is becoming increasingly prevalent in both developed and developing nations.
9. silence /ˈsaɪləns/ n. 沉默；寂静 v. 使沉默
📖 英文解释
Complete absence of sound; the fact of abstaining from speech.
✏️ 例句
There was a moment of silence before the ceremony began.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in silence 默默地；break the silence 打破沉默；silence is golden 沉默是金；silence sb 使某人沉默
🧠 助记
silent（安静）→ silence → 沉默/寂静
silence::/ˈsaɪləns/:n. 沉默:The critics were silenced by the overwhelming success of the film.
10. theoretical /ˌθɪəˈretɪkl/ adj. 理论的；假设的
📖 英文解释
Concerned with or involving theory rather than practical application.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
theory（理论）+ ical → 理论的
theoretical::/ˌθɪəˈretɪkl/:adj. 理论的:The model is theoretically sound but has yet to be tested in practice.
11. murder /ˈmɜːdə/ n. 谋杀 v. 谋杀；糟蹋
📖 英文解释
The unlawful premeditated killing of one human being by another; to kill unlawfully.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
murder case 谋杀案；first-degree murder 一级谋杀；murder mystery 推理小说；get away with murder 逃脱惩罚
🧠 助记
murder → 谐音 谋杀 → 谋杀
murder::/ˈmɜːdə/:n. 谋杀 v. 谋杀:The court found him guilty of murder and sentenced him to life imprisonment.
12. rigorous /ˈrɪɡərəs/ adj. 严格的；严密的；缜密的
📖 英文解释
Extremely thorough and careful; adhering strictly to rules and procedures.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

rigorous::/ˈrɪɡərəs/:adj. 严格的:The selection process for astronauts is rigorous and highly competitive.
13. waiter /ˈweɪtə/ n. 服务员；侍者

corn::/kɔːn/:n. 谷物:Iowa is at the heart of the corn belt in the United States.

oneself::/wʌnˈself/:pron. 自己:It is important to express oneself clearly in academic writing.
16. shadow /ˈʃædəʊ/ n. 影子；阴影 v. 遮蔽；跟踪

shadow::/ˈʃædəʊ/:n. 影子:The scandal cast a long shadow over the minister's career.

tongue::/tʌŋ/:n. 舌头:English is not her mother tongue, but she speaks it fluently.

pretend::/prɪˈtend/:v. 假装:It would be foolish to pretend that the problems do not exist.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

speculate::/ˈspekjʊleɪt/:v. 推测:It would be premature to speculate on the outcome of the investigation.
