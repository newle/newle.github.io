1. fortunate /ˈfɔːtʃənət/ adj. 幸运的；幸福的
📖 英文解释
Having or bringing good luck; favored by luck or fortune.
✏️ 例句
We were fortunate to find a parking spot so quickly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be fortunate to do sth 幸运地做某事；fortunate enough 足够幸运；fortunately adv. 幸运地
🧠 助记
fortune（财富/命运）+ ate → 得到财富眷顾的 → 幸运的
fortunate::/ˈfɔːtʃənət/:adj. 幸运的:She was fortunate to escape the accident unharmed.
2. mild /maɪld/ adj. 温和的；轻微的；淡的
📖 英文解释
Not severe, harsh, or extreme; gentle; not strong in flavor.
✏️ 例句
The doctor prescribed a mild painkiller for the headache.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
mild weather 温和的天气；mild-mannered 温文尔雅的；mild symptoms 轻微症状
🧠 助记
mild发音似「迈入」柔和之境 → 温和的
mild::/maɪld/:adj. 温和的:The patient experienced only mild side effects from the medication.
3. temptation /tempˈteɪʃn/ n. 诱惑；诱惑物
📖 英文解释
The desire to do something, especially something unwise or wrong; something that tempts.
✏️ 例句
He resisted the temptation to check his phone during the exam.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
resist temptation 抵制诱惑；give in to temptation 屈服于诱惑；yield to temptation 经不住诱惑
🧠 助记
tempt（诱惑）+ ation → 诱惑（行为/状态）
temptation::/tempˈteɪʃn/:n. 诱惑:The smell of fresh cookies was an irresistible temptation.
4. wine /waɪn/ n. 酒；葡萄酒 v. 以酒款待
📖 英文解释
An alcoholic drink made from fermented grapes or other fruits; to entertain someone with wine.
✏️ 例句
The restaurant has an extensive wine list.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
red/white wine 红/白葡萄酒；wine and dine 盛情款待；wine cellar 酒窖
🧠 助记
vine（葡萄藤）→ wine（葡萄酒），字母v→w变化
wine::/waɪn/:n. 酒:They wined and dined their important business clients.
5. cater /ˈkeɪtə(r)/ v. 迎合；承办饮食；满足需求
📖 英文解释
To provide food and drink for a social event; to provide what is needed or required by someone.
✏️ 例句
The company catered to the needs of elderly customers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cater for 为…提供服务；cater to 迎合；catering industry 餐饮业；catering service 承办饮食服务
🧠 助记
cater = 照料食物（cat + er → 像猫一样关心吃的）
cater::/ˈkeɪtə(r)/:v. 迎合:The new app was designed to cater to younger users.
6. donate /dəʊˈneɪt/ v. 捐赠；捐献
📖 英文解释
To give money, goods, or services to a charity or other worthy cause.
✏️ 例句
He donated his entire salary to the flood relief fund.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
donate to 向…捐赠；donate blood 献血；donation n. 捐款；donor n. 捐赠者
🧠 助记
don（给予，拉丁语）+ ate → 给予 → 捐赠
donate::/dəʊˈneɪt/:v. 捐赠:The company donated millions to educational charities.
7. grave /ɡreɪv/ n. 坟墓 adj. 严重的；严肃的
📖 英文解释
A place of burial for a dead body; (of a situation) giving cause for alarm; (of a person) serious.
✏️ 例句
The doctor said the patient's condition was grave.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a grave mistake 严重错误；grave concern 严重关切；beyond the grave 来自死后；graveyard n. 墓地
🧠 助记
grave发音似「格外」严重 → 严重的；字面意思是坟墓
grave::/ɡreɪv/:n. 坟墓 adj. 严重的:The prime minister expressed grave concern over the crisis.
8. historic /hɪˈstɒrɪk/ adj. 有历史意义的；历史性的
📖 英文解释
Famous or important in history; having great and lasting importance.
✏️ 例句
The signing of the peace treaty was a historic moment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
辨析：historic（有历史意义的）vs historical（历史的、历史上存在的）；historic achievement 历史性成就
🧠 助记
history + ic → 与历史相关的、有历史意义的
historic::/hɪˈstɒrɪk/:adj. 有历史意义的:The two leaders signed a historic agreement on climate change.
9. incorporate /ɪnˈkɔːpəreɪt/ v. 合并；纳入；包含；成立公司
📖 英文解释
To include something as part of a whole; to combine materials into one substance; to form a legal corporation.
✏️ 例句
The new design incorporates the latest safety features.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
incorporate into 将…纳入；be incorporated into 被并入；incorporated company 股份公司
🧠 助记
in-（进入）+ corpor（身体）+ ate → 将…纳入主体 → 合并、包含
incorporate::/ɪnˈkɔːpəreɪt/:v. 合并:The architect incorporated eco-friendly elements into the design.
10. physicist /ˈfɪzɪsɪst/ n. 物理学家
📖 英文解释
A scientist who specializes in the study of physics.
✏️ 例句
Einstein was one of the greatest physicists of the 20th century.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
theoretical physicist 理论物理学家；experimental physicist 实验物理学家；nuclear physicist 核物理学家
🧠 助记
physic（物理）+ ist（…家）→ 物理学家
physicist::/ˈfɪzɪsɪst/:n. 物理学家:The physicist won the Nobel Prize for her research on quantum mechanics.
11. prey /preɪ/ n. 猎物 v. 捕食；侵害
📖 英文解释
An animal hunted and caught by another for food; a person easily harmed or deceived; to hunt and kill for food.
✏️ 例句
Lions prey on zebras and wildebeest.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prey on/upon 捕食；以弱者为猎取对象；bird of prey 猛禽；fall prey to 成为…的受害者
🧠 助记
pray（祈祷）的谐音 → 被抓的猎物在祈祷（prey）→ 猎物
prey::/preɪ/:n. 猎物 v. 捕食:Scammers often prey on elderly people who live alone.
12. stupid /ˈstjuːpɪd/ adj. 愚蠢的；迟钝的
📖 英文解释
Lacking intelligence or common sense; done in a way that shows a lack of good judgment.
✏️ 例句
It was stupid of me to leave my wallet at home.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stupid mistake 愚蠢错误；stupid idea 荒唐主意；stupidity n. 愚蠢；stupefy v. 使发呆
🧠 助记
stu-（呆住）+ pid → 发呆（stupe）→ 愚蠢的
stupid::/ˈstjuːpɪd/:adj. 愚蠢的:Making such a stupid decision cost him his job.
13. toxic /ˈtɒksɪk/ adj. 有毒的；中毒的；有害的
📖 英文解释
Poisonous; relating to or caused by a toxin or poison; very bad or harmful.
✏️ 例句
The factory was fined for releasing toxic waste into the river.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
toxic substance 有毒物质；toxic relationship 有害关系；toxin n. 毒素；detox v. 解毒
🧠 助记
tox（毒，拉丁语toxicum）+ ic → 有毒的
toxic::/ˈtɒksɪk/:adj. 有毒的:Workers must wear protective gear when handling toxic chemicals.
14. collar /ˈkɒlə(r)/ n. 衣领；项圈 v. 抓住
📖 英文解释
The part of a shirt, coat, or other garment that goes around the neck; a band around an animal's neck; to seize someone.
✏️ 例句
He grabbed the suspect by the collar.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
white-collar 白领；blue-collar 蓝领；dog collar 狗圈；collar bone 锁骨
🧠 助记
collar发音像「抓领子」→ 衣领；抓领子 → 抓住
collar::/ˈkɒlə(r)/:n. 衣领:She straightened his collar before the interview.
15. plot /plɒt/ n. 情节；阴谋；小块土地 v. 策划
📖 英文解释
The main events of a book, film, or play; a secret plan to do something bad; a small area of land; to plan secretly.
✏️ 例句
The plot of the novel was too complicated to follow.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
plot against 密谋反对；lose the plot 失去控制；plot twist 情节转折；plot of land 一块土地
🧠 助记
plot = 在地图（plot）上画密谋路线 → 策划、阴谋
plot::/plɒt/:n. 情节:The thriller had an intricate plot full of surprising twists.
16. rainbow /ˈreɪnbəʊ/ n. 彩虹
📖 英文解释
An arch of colors visible in the sky caused by the refraction of sunlight through water droplets.
✏️ 例句
A beautiful rainbow appeared after the storm.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rainbow coalition 多元联合；over the rainbow 在彩虹彼端（乐观象征）；rainbow-colored 彩虹色的
🧠 助记
rain（雨）+ bow（弓）→ 雨后弓形彩弧 → 彩虹
rainbow::/ˈreɪnbəʊ/:n. 彩虹:The children pointed excitedly at the rainbow in the sky.
17. sheer /ʃɪə(r)/ adj. 纯然的；陡峭的；极薄的 adv. 垂直地
📖 英文解释
Nothing other than; complete; (of a surface) perpendicular to or nearly so; (of fabric) very thin and light.
✏️ 例句
The mountaineers faced a sheer cliff of two hundred meters.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sheer coincidence 纯属巧合；sheer cliff 垂直峭壁；sheer luck 纯粹运气；sheer fabric 薄透的面料
🧠 助记
sheer = 纯的 → sheer madness 纯粹是疯狂
sheer::/ʃɪə(r)/:adj. 纯然的:They succeeded through sheer determination and hard work.

She loves to sing in the school choir.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sing along 跟唱；sing out 高声唱；sing praises 称赞；singer n. 歌手；song n. 歌曲
🧠 助记
sing发音像「唱」→ 唱歌
sing::/sɪŋ/:v. 唱歌:The birds began to sing at the break of dawn.
19. soar /sɔː(r)/ v. 翱翔；高飞；急升
📖 英文解释
To fly or rise high in the air; to increase rapidly above the usual level.
✏️ 例句
House prices soared by 20% last year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
soar above 翱翔于…之上；soar to new heights 攀升至新高；soaring temperatures 急升的气温
🧠 助记
soar发音似「苏鹰」→ 苏鹰飞翔 → 翱翔、急升
soar::/sɔː(r)/:v. 翱翔:Profits soared after the company launched its new product.
20. studio /ˈstjuːdiəʊ/ n. 工作室；摄影棚；单间公寓

A room where an artist works; a place where films, recordings, or broadcasts are made; a small apartment.

recording studio 录音室；film studio 电影制片厂；studio apartment 单间公寓

study（学习/研究）→ studio（专门研究创作的地方）→ 工作室
studio::/ˈstjuːdiəʊ/:n. 工作室:The musician spent three months in the studio recording her album.
21. halt /hɔːlt/ v./n. 停止；暂停

To stop moving or proceeding; to bring to a stop; a temporary stoppage of movement or activity.

The project was brought to a halt due to lack of funding.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
come to a halt 停下来；grind to a halt 逐渐停止；call a halt to 下令停止；halt production 停产
🧠 助记
halt发音似「喊停」→ 停止
halt::/hɔːlt/:v./n. 停止:The construction was brought to a halt by bad weather.
22. nursery /ˈnɜːsəri/ n. 托儿所；苗圃；儿童房
📖 英文解释
A place where young children are cared for; a room in a house for a young child; a place where plants are grown.
✏️ 例句
She sends her daughter to the nursery three days a week.

nursery school 幼儿园；plant nursery 苗圃；nursery rhyme 儿歌；day nursery 日托所

nursery::/ˈnɜːsəri/:n. 托儿所:The nursery rhyme was taught to every child in the class.
23. expedition /ˌekspəˈdɪʃn/ n. 探险队；远征；迅速
📖 英文解释
A journey undertaken for a specific purpose; a group of people making such a journey; speed and efficiency.
✏️ 例句
The expedition to Antarctica lasted six months.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
go on an expedition 进行探险；scientific expedition 科学考察；with expedition 迅速地
🧠 助记
ex-（出）+ ped（脚）+ ition → 迈脚走出去 → 探险远征
expedition::/ˌekspəˈdɪʃn/:n. 探险队:The scientific expedition discovered new species in the rainforest.
24. outdoor /ˈaʊtdɔː(r)/ adj. 户外的；室外的
📖 英文解释
Done or used outside a building; relating to activities done in the open air.
✏️ 例句
He enjoys outdoor activities like hiking and camping.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
outdoor life 户外生活；outdoor sports 户外运动；outdoors adv. 在户外（注意：outdoors作副词）
🧠 助记
out（外）+ door（门）→ 门外的 → 户外的
outdoor::/ˈaʊtdɔː(r)/:adj. 户外的:The school organized an outdoor adventure trip for students.
25. circuit /ˈsɜːkɪt/ n. 电路；线路；巡回；环形跑道
📖 英文解释
A complete path that an electric current flows around; a regular journey around an area; a sports track.
✏️ 例句
The technician repaired a faulty circuit in the control panel.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
short circuit 短路；electric circuit 电路；circuit court 巡回法庭；circuit training 循环训练
🧠 助记
circ-（圆）+ uit → 圆形路径 → 电路（电流的圆形路径）
circuit::/ˈsɜːkɪt/:n. 电路:The racing car completed twenty circuits of the track.
