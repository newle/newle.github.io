1. simplify /ˈsɪmplɪfaɪ/ v. 简化；使简单
📖 英文解释
To make something simpler or easier to do or understand.
✏️ 例句
The new software simplifies the process of data entry.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
simplify procedures 简化程序；simplify the problem 简化问题；simplified version 简化版本
🧠 助记
simple（简单）+ fy（使）→ 使变简单 → 简化
simplify::/ˈsɪmplɪfaɪ/:v. 简化:The teacher tried to simplify the complex concept for young students.
2. cooperate /kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/ v. 合作；配合；协作
📖 英文解释
To work together toward a common end; to comply with a request.
✏️ 例句
The two countries agreed to cooperate on environmental issues.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cooperate with 与…合作；cooperate on 在…上合作；cooperation n. 合作；cooperative adj. 合作的
🧠 助记
co-（共同）+ operate（操作）→ 共同操作 → 合作
cooperate::/kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/:v. 合作:All departments must cooperate to achieve the company's goals.
3. volcano /vɒlˈkeɪnəʊ/ n. 火山
📖 英文解释
A mountain or hill having a crater from which lava, rock fragments, hot vapor, and gas are ejected.
✏️ 例句
The island was formed by volcanic activity thousands of years ago.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
active/dormant/extinct volcano 活/休眠/死火山；volcanic eruption 火山喷发；volcanic adj. 火山的
🧠 助记
Vulcan（罗马火神）→ volcano（火山）
volcano::/vɒlˈkeɪnəʊ/:n. 火山:The eruption of the volcano forced thousands of people to evacuate.
4. deem /diːm/ v. 认为；视为；相信
📖 英文解释
To regard or consider in a specified way; to have a specified opinion.
✏️ 例句
The court deemed the evidence insufficient for conviction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
deem it necessary 认为有必要；deem sb/sth + adj. 认为某人/某物…；if deemed appropriate 若认为合适
🧠 助记
deem = de（判断）+ em → 作出判断 → 认为
deem::/diːm/:v. 认为:Management deemed it necessary to restructure the entire department.
5. nowhere /ˈnəʊweə(r)/ adv./n. 无处；任何地方都不
📖 英文解释
Not in, at, or to any place; not anywhere.
✏️ 例句
The solution was nowhere to be found.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nowhere near 远不及；from nowhere 突然出现；in the middle of nowhere 在偏僻的地方；get nowhere 毫无进展
🧠 助记
no（没有）+ where（地方）→ 没有任何地方 → 无处
nowhere::/ˈnəʊweə(r)/:adv./n. 无处:After years of effort, the project seemed to be going nowhere.
6. contend /kənˈtend/ v. 竞争；争辩；断言；应对
📖 英文解释
To compete for something; to assert something as a position in an argument; to have to deal with a problem.
✏️ 例句
Three teams are contending for the championship.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
contend with 与…竞争；应付…（困难）；contend that 坚持认为；contention n. 争论
🧠 助记
con-（共同）+ tend（伸展）→ 共同伸手争夺 → 竞争、争辩
contend::/kənˈtend/:v. 竞争:The report contends that climate change is the biggest threat of our time.
7. delicate /ˈdelɪkət/ adj. 易碎的；精致的；微妙的；需小心处理的
📖 英文解释
Very fine in texture or structure; easily broken or damaged; requiring great care and sensitivity.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
delicate situation 微妙的局面；delicate balance 精妙的平衡；delicate instrument 精密仪器
🧠 助记
deli（精美食品）+ cate → 精致的、易碎的
delicate::/ˈdelɪkət/:adj. 易碎的:The surgeon performed the delicate operation with great precision.
8. mathematical /ˌmæθəˈmætɪkl/ adj. 数学的；精确的
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
mathematics（数学）→ mathematical（数学的）
mathematical::/ˌmæθəˈmætɪkl/:adj. 数学的:Her mathematical skills helped her solve complex engineering problems.
9. outer /ˈaʊtə(r)/ adj. 外面的；外侧的；远离中心的
📖 英文解释
Outside or external; further from the center or the inside.
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记

outer::/ˈaʊtə(r)/:adj. 外面的:The outer shell of the spacecraft protected it from extreme temperatures.
10. allowance /əˈlaʊəns/ n. 津贴；零花钱；限额；容忍
📖 英文解释
A sum of money paid regularly for a specific purpose; an amount permitted; the action of allowing something.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
allow（允许）+ ance → 被允许的额度 → 津贴、限额
allowance::/əˈlaʊəns/:n. 津贴:The company provides a generous travel allowance for business trips.
11. skip /skɪp/ v. 跳过；略过；跳绳 n. 跳跃
📖 英文解释
To move along lightly by stepping and jumping alternately; to omit or miss a part; to miss an event or activity.
✏️ 例句

skip::/skɪp/:v. 跳过:Many students skipped the lecture and missed important information.
12. awkward /ˈɔːkwəd/ adj. 笨拙的；令人尴尬的；不方便的
📖 英文解释
Causing difficulty; not graceful or skillful; causing embarrassment or inconvenience.
✏️ 例句
There was an awkward silence after his comment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

awkward::/ˈɔːkwəd/:adj. 笨拙的:He felt awkward being the only person who didn't know anyone at the party.
13. vague /veɪɡ/ adj. 模糊的；含糊的；不明确的
📖 英文解释

vague::/veɪɡ/:adj. 模糊的:She gave a vague response that left everyone confused.
14. cable /ˈkeɪbl/ n. 缆线；电缆；有线电视 v. 发电报

cable::/ˈkeɪbl/:n. 缆线:The engineer checked every cable in the control room.
15. emit /ɪˈmɪt/ v. 散发；排放；发出（声音/光）

e-（出）+ mit（送）→ 送出去 → 散发、排放
emit::/ɪˈmɪt/:v. 散发:The factory was fined for emitting harmful chemicals into the atmosphere.
16. manual /ˈmænjuəl/ n. 手册 adj. 手工的；手动的

manual::/ˈmænjuəl/:n. 手册 adj. 手工的:The manual explained every step of the installation process clearly.

prescribe::/prɪˈskraɪb/:v. 开处方:The physician prescribed a strict diet and regular exercise.

shell::/ʃel/:n. 壳:She had to shell out a large sum for the unexpected repairs.
19. tale /teɪl/ n. 故事；传说；谎言

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

20. bell /bel/ n. 钟；铃声；铃
📖 英文解释
A hollow metal object, typically cup-shaped, that makes a ringing sound when struck; any device that makes a ringing sound.
✏️ 例句

bell发音像铃声「叮铃」→ 铃、钟
bell::/bel/:n. 钟:The name rings a bell, but I cannot remember where I heard it.

silly::/ˈsɪli/:adj. 愚蠢的:Don't waste time on such silly arguments.
