1. tailor /ˈteɪlə(r)/ n. 裁缝 v. 定制；使适合
📖 英文解释
A person whose occupation is making fitted clothes; to make or adapt for a particular purpose.
✏️ 例句
The program was tailored to meet the needs of individual students.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tailor-made 量身定制的；tailor sth to/for 使适合；tailor a suit 定制西服
🧠 助记
tail（尾，裁剪布料的末端）+ or → 剪裁布料的人 → 裁缝
tailor::/ˈteɪlə(r)/:n. 裁缝 v. 定制:The curriculum was carefully tailored to the students' learning levels.
2. indoor /ˈɪndɔː(r)/ adj. 室内的；屋内的
📖 英文解释
Situated, conducted, or used inside a building.
✏️ 例句
They moved the sports event indoors because of the rain.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
indoor sports 室内运动；indoor plant 室内植物；indoor swimming pool 室内游泳池；indoors adv. 在室内
🧠 助记
in（里面）+ door（门）→ 门内的 → 室内的
indoor::/ˈɪndɔː(r)/:adj. 室内的:The children played indoor games when it rained.
3. offend /əˈfend/ v. 冒犯；触怒；违法
📖 英文解释
To cause someone to feel hurt, upset, or insulted; to break a rule or law.
✏️ 例句
His remark deeply offended several members of the audience.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
offend sb 冒犯某人；offensive adj. 冒犯的；offender n. 违法者；first offender 初犯
🧠 助记
of（对）+ fend（防御/打）→ 对别人打击 → 冒犯
offend::/əˈfend/:v. 冒犯:The joke was meant to be funny but it offended many people.
4. publicity /pʌbˈlɪsɪti/ n. 宣传；公众关注；公开
📖 英文解释
The notice or attention given by the media to someone or something; the promotion of a product or activity.
✏️ 例句
The scandal generated a lot of negative publicity for the company.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
good/bad publicity 好/坏宣传；publicity campaign 宣传活动；seek publicity 寻求曝光；publicize v. 宣传
🧠 助记
public（公众）+ ity → 面向公众的 → 宣传、公众关注
publicity::/pʌbˈlɪsɪti/:n. 宣传:The charity used social media to gain publicity for its fundraising campaign.
5. refrain /rɪˈfreɪn/ v. 避免；克制；忍住 n. 叠句；副歌
📖 英文解释
To stop oneself from doing something; a repeated verse in a poem or song.
✏️ 例句
Please refrain from smoking in the building.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
refrain from doing 避免做；refrain from comment 不作评论；the refrain of a song 歌曲副歌
🧠 助记
re-（重复）+ frain（约束）→ 反复约束自己 → 克制、避免
refrain::/rɪˈfreɪn/:v. 避免:He struggled to refrain from checking his phone during the meeting.
6. cherish /ˈtʃerɪʃ/ v. 珍爱；爱护；怀有（希望）
📖 英文解释
To hold something dear; to protect and care for lovingly; to keep a hope or memory in the mind.
✏️ 例句
She cherishes the memories of her childhood.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cherish a dream 怀有梦想；cherish freedom 珍视自由；cherished belief 珍视的信念
🧠 助记
cher-（亲爱，如cherie）+ ish → 对待如亲爱的 → 珍爱
cherish::/ˈtʃerɪʃ/:v. 珍爱:He cherished every moment spent with his family.
7. descend /dɪˈsend/ v. 下降；降落；传下来
📖 英文解释
To move or fall downward; to be passed down by inheritance; to make a sudden attack.
✏️ 例句
The plane began to descend for landing.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
descend from 起源于；descend on 突然到来；descent n. 下降；descending order 降序
🧠 助记
de-（向下）+ scend（爬）→ 向下爬 → 下降
descend::/dɪˈsend/:v. 下降:Temperatures descended sharply after sunset in the desert.
8. entity /ˈentɪti/ n. 实体；主体；存在物
📖 英文解释
A thing with distinct and independent existence; an organization or institution considered as a whole.
✏️ 例句
The company was established as a legal entity in 2005.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
legal entity 法人实体；business entity 商业主体；separate entity 独立实体；entity framework 实体框架
🧠 助记
ent（存在，拉丁语esse）+ ity → 存在的事物 → 实体
entity::/ˈentɪti/:n. 实体:The new subsidiary operates as a separate legal entity.
9. occasional /əˈkeɪʒənl/ adj. 偶然的；偶尔的；临时的
📖 英文解释
Occurring, appearing, or done infrequently and irregularly; produced for a particular occasion.
✏️ 例句
He pays occasional visits to his hometown.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
occasional visit 偶尔拜访；occasional rain 偶尔下雨；occasionally adv. 偶尔；on occasions 有时
🧠 助记
occasion（场合/偶然）+ al → 偶尔出现的 → 偶然的
occasional::/əˈkeɪʒənl/:adj. 偶然的:She enjoys the occasional cup of tea in the afternoon.
10. fourteen /ˌfɔːˈtiːn/ num. 十四
📖 英文解释
The cardinal number equal to four plus ten (14).
✏️ 例句
She learned to drive at the age of fourteen.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fourteen days 十四天；fourteen years old 十四岁；the fourteen points 十四点原则（威尔逊）
🧠 助记
four（四）+ teen（十几）→ 十四
fourteen::/ˌfɔːˈtiːn/:num. 十四:Fourteen countries signed the new trade agreement.
11. fry /fraɪ/ v. 油炸；油煎 n. 炸物；小鱼苗
📖 英文解释
To cook in hot fat or oil; food cooked by frying; small fish or young fish.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
fry发音似「飞入」热油 → 油炸
fry::/fraɪ/:v. 油炸:He decided to fry the fish rather than bake it.
12. syndrome /ˈsɪndrəʊm/ n. 综合症；综合征；典型表现
📖 英文解释
A group of symptoms which consistently occur together; a characteristic combination of opinions, emotions, or behavior.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
syn-（共同）+ drome（跑）→ 一起跑出来的症状 → 综合症
syndrome::/ˈsɪndrəʊm/:n. 综合症:Many remote workers experience the impostor syndrome at some point.
13. whale /weɪl/ n. 鲸；庞然大物

whale = 很大的（W-H-A-L-E = Wide Huge And Long Entity）
whale::/weɪl/:n. 鲸:They had a whale of a time at the theme park.
14. pencil /ˈpensl/ n. 铅笔 v. 用铅笔写/画

pencil::/ˈpensl/:n. 铅笔 v. 用铅笔写/画:She penciled in a meeting for Monday afternoon but didn't confirm it.

16. cease /siːs/ v. 停止；终止；结束

fold::/fəʊld/:v. 折叠:The startup folded after just one year due to lack of investment.
