1. drill /drɪl/ n./v. 练习；钻孔；操练
📖 英文解释
repeated exercises to learn something; a tool for making holes; to train through repeated practice
✏️ 例句
Students need plenty of grammar drills to internalize the rules.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：fire drill（消防演习）；drill into（反复灌输）；drill for oil（钻探石油）
🧠 助记
drill = dr + ill，反复练习直到'生病'（ill）= 操练；也像钻头钻进去
drill::/drɪl/:n./v. 练习:The army conducted regular drills to keep soldiers in peak condition.
2. sculpture /ˈskʌlptʃər/ n. 雕塑；雕刻作品；v. 雕刻
📖 英文解释
the art of making figures or objects out of hard materials; a work of art made in this way
✏️ 例句
Rodin's sculpture 'The Thinker' is one of the most famous works in the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：marble sculpture（大理石雕塑）；sculpt v.（雕刻）；sculptor n.（雕塑家）
🧠 助记
sculpt（雕刻）+ ure = 雕刻出来的东西 = 雕塑
sculpture::/ˈskʌlptʃər/:n. 雕塑:The park is dotted with modern sculptures created by local artists.
3. warmth /wɔːrmθ/ n. 温暖；热情；亲切
📖 英文解释
the quality of being warm; friendliness and affection
✏️ 例句
The warmth of the community welcome made the newcomers feel at home.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：warmth and friendliness（温暖与友好）；body warmth（体温）；radiate warmth（散发温暖）
🧠 助记
warm（温暖）+ th（名词后缀）= 温暖的状态 = 温暖
warmth::/wɔːrmθ/:n. 温暖:Despite the cold weather, the gathering was filled with warmth and laughter.
4. dump /dʌmp/ n. 垃圾场；v. 倾倒；抛弃；甩掉
📖 英文解释
a place where garbage is left; to deposit or drop carelessly; to end a relationship abruptly
✏️ 例句
He dumped all the old furniture at the recycling centre.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：dump on（倾诉苦水；过分批评）；rubbish dump（垃圾场）；down in the dumps（情绪低落）
🧠 助记
dump = d + ump，'嗯普'一声倒下去 = 倾倒；dump一个人 = 甩掉
dump::/dʌmp/:n. 垃圾场:Companies that dump toxic waste illegally face heavy penalties.
5. resign /rɪˈzaɪn/ v. 辞职；放弃；使顺从
📖 英文解释
to voluntarily leave a job or position; to accept something unpleasant as inevitable
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：resign from（从……辞职）；resign oneself to（听天由命；顺从）；resignation n.（辞职；顺从）
🧠 助记
re-（再次）+ sign（签名）= 再次签名 = 签辞职书 = 辞职
resign::/rɪˈzaɪn/:v. 辞职:He resigned himself to the fact that the project would be cancelled.

vigor（活力）+ ous = 充满活力的 = 精力充沛的
vigorous::/ˈvɪɡərəs/:adj. 精力充沛的:The government faced vigorous opposition to its proposed budget cuts.
7. leaf /liːf/ n. 叶子；叶片；书页；v. 翻页

leaf::/liːf/:n. 叶子:After his conviction, he vowed to turn over a new leaf.

manifest::/ˈmænɪfest/:v. 显示:The economic crisis manifested itself in rising unemployment and poverty.
