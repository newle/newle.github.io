1. seize /siːz/ v. 抓住；夺取；没收；理解
📖 英文解释
to take hold of suddenly; to capture; to confiscate; to understand quickly
✏️ 例句
The police seized illegal weapons during the raid.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：seize the opportunity（抓住机会）；seize power（夺取权力）；seize upon（利用；抓住）；seizure n.
🧠 助记
seize = sei（拿）+ ze，快速抓取 = 抓住；夺取
seize::/siːz/:v. 抓住:Entrepreneurs must seize opportunities when they arise.
2. authentic /ɔːˈθentɪk/ adj. 真实的；正宗的；可信的
📖 英文解释
of undisputed origin; genuine; based on facts
✏️ 例句
Tourists seek authentic local cuisine rather than tourist traps.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：authentic experience（真实体验）；authentic document（真实文件）；authenticity n.（真实性）
🧠 助记
author（作者）+ entic，作者亲自写的才是真实的 = 真实的；正宗的
authentic::/ɔːˈθentɪk/:adj. 真实的:The museum authenticated the painting as an authentic Renaissance work.
3. coordinate /koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/ v. 协调；配合；n. 坐标；adj. 同等的
📖 英文解释
to organize the different parts of an activity; a pair of numbers locating a point; of equal rank
✏️ 例句
The relief agencies coordinated their efforts to avoid duplication.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：coordinate with（与……协调）；coordinate efforts（协调努力）；coordination n.（协调；配合）
🧠 助记
co-（共同）+ ordinate（排列）= 共同排列好 = 协调、坐标
coordinate::/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/:v. 协调:International cooperation requires countries to carefully coordinate their policies.
4. hip /hɪp/ n. 髋；臀部；adj. 时髦的；了解时事的
📖 英文解释
the part of the body between the waist and the top of the leg; trendy or fashionable
✏️ 例句
She fractured her hip in a fall and required surgery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：hip fracture（髋骨骨折）；hip replacement（髋关节置换）；hip-hop（嘻哈文化）
🧠 助记
hip = h + ip，髋部是身体的'ip'（关键部位）= 髋；也有时髦义
hip::/hɪp/:n. 髋:Hip fractures in the elderly are a major public health concern.
5. feasible /ˈfiːzɪbl/ adj. 可行的；可能的；合理的
📖 英文解释
possible and practical to achieve; likely or probable
✏️ 例句
The committee examined whether the proposal was financially feasible.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：feasible solution/plan（可行方案）；technically feasible（技术上可行）；feasibility n.（可行性）；feasibility study（可行性研究）
🧠 助记
feasible = feasible来自Latin facere（做），可以做到的 = 可行的
feasible::/ˈfiːzɪbl/:adj. 可行的:A feasibility study must be conducted before approving any large-scale project.
6. gloomy /ˈɡluːmi/ adj. 阴暗的；忧郁的；悲观的
📖 英文解释
dark or poorly lit; causing or feeling sadness; pessimistic
✏️ 例句
The economic forecast remains gloomy despite recent improvements.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：gloomy outlook/prediction（悲观的前景/预测）；gloomy weather（阴暗天气）；gloom n.（阴暗；忧郁）
🧠 助记
gloom（黑暗、忧郁）+ y = 充满黑暗忧郁的 = 阴暗的；悲观的
gloomy::/ˈɡluːmi/:adj. 阴暗的:Analysts painted a gloomy picture of the housing market for next year.
7. hint /hɪnt/ n./v. 暗示；提示；少量
📖 英文解释
a slight indication; to suggest indirectly; a small amount of something
✏️ 例句
She gave him a hint that she knew about the surprise party.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：hint at（暗示）；drop a hint（暗示）；take the hint（接受暗示）；a hint of（一点点……）
🧠 助记
hint = hin（轻）+ t，轻轻一点 = 暗示；提示
hint::/hɪnt/:n./v. 暗示:The prime minister hinted at major policy changes in her speech.
8. illiterate /ɪˈlɪtərɪt/ adj./n. 文盲的；无知的；文盲
📖 英文解释
unable to read or write; showing a lack of knowledge in a particular area
✏️ 例句
A large proportion of the adult population remains functionally illiterate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：functionally illiterate（功能性文盲）；illiteracy n.（文盲状态）；computer illiterate（不懂电脑的）
🧠 助记
il-（不）+ literate（有文化的）= 没有文化的 = 文盲
illiterate::/ɪˈlɪtərɪt/:adj./n. 文盲的:Despite economic growth, millions of people remain illiterate in the region.
9. iron /ˈaɪərn/ n. 铁；熨斗；v. 熨烫；adj. 铁的；坚定的
📖 英文解释
a strong hard metal; a device for smoothing clothes; to press clothes with an iron; inflexibly firm
✏️ 例句
She ironed out all the differences before the meeting.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：iron out（消除；解决）；iron will（铁的意志）；Iron Curtain（铁幕）；strike while the iron is hot（趁热打铁）
🧠 助记
iron = i + ron，铁（iron）制造的熨斗把衣服熨平 = 铁；熨烫
iron::/ˈaɪərn/:n. 铁:The two parties worked hard to iron out their remaining differences.
10. lip /lɪp/ n. 嘴唇；边缘；v. 轻触
📖 英文解释
either of the two fleshy parts of the mouth; the edge of a container or opening
✏️ 例句
She bit her lip to stop herself from laughing.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：lip service（口惠而实不至）；keep a stiff upper lip（坚忍不拔）；slip of the lip（口误）
🧠 助记
lip = l + ip，嘴的'一排'（l）皮 = 嘴唇；容器边缘
lip::/lɪp/:n. 嘴唇:The government paid lip service to environmental protection without taking real action.
11. painter /ˈpeɪntər/ n. 画家；油漆工
📖 英文解释
an artist who paints pictures; a person whose job is painting buildings
✏️ 例句
Picasso remains one of the most celebrated painters of the 20th century.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研延伸：oil painter（油画家）；portrait painter（肖像画家）；house painter（刷房工）；painting n.（绘画；油画）
🧠 助记
paint（绘画）+ er = 会绘画的人 = 画家；也可以是油漆工
painter::/ˈpeɪntər/:n. 画家:The renowned painter donated several works to the national gallery.
12. stir /stɜːr/ v. 搅拌；唤起；引起；n. 骚动；激动
📖 英文解释
to move a liquid in circular motions; to arouse or inspire; a commotion or excitement
✏️ 例句
The controversial speech stirred up strong public reactions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：stir up（激起；搅动）；stir emotions（激动情绪）；cause a stir（引起轰动）；stir from（从……唤醒）
🧠 助记
stir = s + tir（旋转），旋转搅拌 = 搅拌；激起某种情绪
stir::/stɜːr/:v. 搅拌:The documentary caused quite a stir among environmental activists.

bitter = bit（一口）+ ter，一口咬下去苦的要命 = 苦的；痛苦的
bitter::/ˈbɪtər/:adj. 苦的:The two nations remain locked in a bitter territorial dispute.
