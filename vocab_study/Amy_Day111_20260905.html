1. workshop /ˈwɜːkʃɒp/ n. 研讨会；工作室；车间
📖 英文解释
a room or building for making things; a meeting for discussion and practical work
✏️ 例句
She attended a writing workshop during the summer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hold/run a workshop; creative/training workshop; workshop on sth.
🧠 助记
work(工作) + shop(场所) → 工作的场所
workshop::/ˈwɜːkʃɒp/:n. 研讨会:The team held a workshop to brainstorm new ideas.
2. cap /kæp/ n. 帽子；上限 v. 限制
📖 英文解释
a soft flat hat; an upper limit; to put a limit on something
✏️ 例句
The government put a cap on the interest rates.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cap on spending; salary cap; cap a record(打破纪录)
🧠 助记
联想棒球帽，帽顶是上限
cap::/kæp/:n. 帽子:The law caps the number of working hours per week.
3. likewise /ˈlaɪkwaɪz/ adv. 同样地；也
📖 英文解释
in the same way; also
✏️ 例句
He smiled, and she likewise smiled back.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
do likewise(同样如此); likewise, ...(同样地，...)
🧠 助记
like(像) + wise(方式) → 用同样的方式
likewise::/ˈlaɪkwaɪz/:adv. 同样地:The manager praised the team; likewise, the director commended them.
4. contempt /kənˈtempt/ n. 轻蔑；蔑视；藐视
📖 英文解释
the feeling that someone or something is worthless or beneath consideration
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
contempt for; hold sb. in contempt; contempt of court(藐视法庭)
🧠 助记
con-(完全) + tempt(看) → 完全看不起 → 轻蔑
contempt::/kənˈtempt/:n. 轻蔑:His contempt for the rules was obvious.
