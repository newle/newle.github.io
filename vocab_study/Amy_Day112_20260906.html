1. imitation /ˌɪmɪˈteɪʃn/ n. 模仿；仿制品；赝品
📖 英文解释
the action of imitating someone or something; a copy or simulation
✏️ 例句
The painting is just an imitation of the original masterpiece.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in imitation of(模仿); imitation leather/fur(仿皮/仿毛皮); imitate(v. 模仿)
🧠 助记
imitat-(模仿) + ion → 模仿的行为或结果
imitation::/ˌɪmɪˈteɪʃn/:n. 模仿:His imitation of the professor amused the whole class.
2. incidence /ˈɪnsɪdəns/ n. 发生率；发生次数
📖 英文解释
the rate, frequency, or scope of something occurring
✏️ 例句
The incidence of diabetes has risen sharply over the past decade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
high/low incidence; incidence of crime/disease; incident(n. 事件)
🧠 助记
incid-(落在) + ence → 落下来的频率 → 发生率
incidence::/ˈɪnsɪdəns/:n. 发生率:The incidence of lung cancer is higher among smokers.
3. invitation /ˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃn/ n. 邀请；请柬；吸引
📖 英文解释
a written or verbal request inviting someone to go somewhere or do something
✏️ 例句
She received an invitation to the president's reception.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
send/accept/decline an invitation; invitation to sth./to do sth.; open invitation
🧠 助记
invit-(邀请) + ation → 邀请行为
invitation::/ˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃn/:n. 邀请:The invitation to the wedding arrived by post.
4. mill /mɪl/ n. 磨坊；工厂 v. 磨碎；乱转
📖 英文解释
a building with machinery for grinding grain; a factory
✏️ 例句
The old windmill has been converted into a museum.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
steel/paper/textile mill; mill around(乱转); run-of-the-mill(普通的)
🧠 助记
mill 联想 grinding(磨) → 磨坊
mill::/mɪl/:n. 磨坊:The run-of-the-mill solution won't work for this complex problem.
5. super /ˈsuːpə/ adj. 极好的；超级的 adv. 非常
📖 英文解释
above average; excellent; used as an intensifier
✏️ 例句
That was a super performance by the team.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
super(prefix,超过,如 supermarket); super cool/smart; super power(超级大国)
🧠 助记
super-(超级) → 超越普通的
super::/ˈsuːpə/:adj. 极好的:The athlete gave a super performance at the championship.
6. cake /keɪk/ n. 蛋糕；块 v. 结块
📖 英文解释
a sweet baked food; to form a hardened layer
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
直接记忆，cake 即蛋糕
cake::/keɪk/:n. 蛋糕:That deal takes the cake for being the worst negotiation I've seen.
7. stroke /strəʊk/ n. 中风；击打；抚摸；一笔 v. 抚摸
📖 英文解释
a movement of the arm in swimming or hitting; a medical emergency in the brain; to caress
✏️ 例句
He suffered a stroke and lost the ability to speak.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stroke of luck/genius(一次幸运/天才之举); brush stroke(笔触); stroke of midnight
🧠 助记
strok-(划) → 划水的动作或血管里的一次冲击
stroke::/strəʊk/:n. 中风:By a stroke of luck, they found the lost document.
8. surge /sɜːdʒ/ n./v. 浪涌；猛增；涌动
📖 英文解释
a sudden powerful forward or upward movement; a sudden large increase
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
surge in prices/demand; power surge(电涌); surge forward(向前涌)
🧠 助记
联想 urge(冲动) + s → 一股冲动的力量涌上来
surge::/sɜːdʒ/:n./v. 浪涌:A surge of anger swept through her as she heard the news.
9. testify /ˈtestɪfaɪ/ v. 作证；证明；表明
📖 英文解释
to give evidence as a witness; to serve as evidence of something
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
testify against/for sb.; testify to sth.(证明); testimony(n. 证词)
🧠 助记
test-(证明) + ify(使) → 使成为证明 → 作证
testify::/ˈtestɪfaɪ/:v. 作证:His success testifies to the power of hard work.
10. cow /kaʊ/ n. 奶牛；母牛 v. 恐吓
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
联想牛的叫声 moo，cow 即母牛
cow::/kaʊ/:n. 奶牛:The workers were cowed by the threat of dismissal.
11. linger /ˈlɪŋɡə/ v. 逗留；徘徊；持续存在
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
ling-(停留) + er → 一直留在原处不走
linger::/ˈlɪŋɡə/:v. 逗留:The feeling of unease lingered long after the conversation.
12. alleviate /əˈliːvieɪt/ v. 减轻；缓和；缓解
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

alleviate::/əˈliːvieɪt/:v. 减轻:These measures should help alleviate the housing shortage.
13. downtown /ˌdaʊnˈtaʊn/ adj./adv./n. 市区的；在市区；市区

downtown::/ˌdaʊnˈtaʊn/:adj./adv./n. 市区的:The new subway line connects the suburbs to downtown.

hurricane::/ˈhʌrɪkən/:n. 飓风:The hurricane left thousands of homes without power.
