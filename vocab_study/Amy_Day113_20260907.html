1. pledge /pledʒ/ v./n. 承诺；誓言；保证
📖 英文解释
a solemn promise; to formally commit to do something
✏️ 例句
The candidates made pledges to improve public healthcare.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pledge support/allegiance; keep/fulfil a pledge; campaign pledge
🧠 助记
pl-(保证) + edge(边缘) → 站在边缘做出的郑重承诺
pledge::/pledʒ/:v./n. 承诺:The government pledged to cut carbon emissions by 2030.
2. snap /snæp/ v./n. 啪一声折断；拍照；忽然发怒
📖 英文解释
to break suddenly with a sharp sound; to take a quick photograph; to speak sharply
✏️ 例句
He snapped at her when she asked too many questions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
snap decision(仓促决定); snap out of it(振作起来); snap up(抢购)
🧠 助记
snap 发音像折断声，直接联想
snap::/snæp/:v./n. 啪一声折断:The company snapped up the chance to expand overseas.
3. undoubtedly /ʌnˈdaʊtɪdli/ adv. 毫无疑问地；确实地
📖 英文解释
without doubt; certainly
✏️ 例句
This is undoubtedly the best decision we have made.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
undoubtedly true/correct; undoubted(adj. 毫无疑问的)
🧠 助记
un-(否定) + doubt(怀疑) + edly → 没有怀疑地 → 毫无疑问地
undoubtedly::/ʌnˈdaʊtɪdli/:adv. 毫无疑问地:Climate change is undoubtedly one of the greatest challenges of our time.
4. visa /ˈviːzə/ n. 签证
📖 英文解释
an official mark in a passport allowing entry to a country
✏️ 例句
She applied for a student visa to study in the UK.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
apply for a visa; work/student/tourist visa; visa application/requirements
🧠 助记
vis-(看见/查看) + a → 官员查看后盖章的许可
visa::/ˈviːzə/:n. 签证:He was denied a visa to enter the country.
5. wing /wɪŋ/ n. 翅膀；机翼；侧翼 v. 飞；翱翔
📖 英文解释
a limb used for flying; a section of an aircraft; a part of a building
✏️ 例句
The bird spread its wings and flew away.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
wing it(临时应对); left-wing/right-wing(左翼/右翼); in the wings(在幕后)
🧠 助记
直接记忆，wing 即翅膀/机翼
wing::/wɪŋ/:n. 翅膀:She had to wing it at the job interview without any preparation.
6. commute /kəˈmjuːt/ v. 通勤；减刑 n. 通勤
📖 英文解释
to travel regularly between home and work; to reduce a legal penalty
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
com-(共同) + mute(改变) → 每天重复来回改变位置
commute::/kəˈmjuːt/:v. 通勤:His death sentence was commuted to life imprisonment.
7. dare /deə/ v. 敢于；胆敢 n. 挑战；激将

dare::/deə/:v. 敢于:She dared to challenge the professor's theory in class.
8. energetic /ˌenəˈdʒetɪk/ adj. 精力充沛的；积极的；有力的
