1. spectator /spekˈteɪtər/ n. 观众；旁观者
📖 英文解释
A person who watches at a show, game, or other event; someone who observes without participating.
✏️ 例句
Thousands of spectators gathered in the stadium to watch the championship final.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ spectator sport 观赏性运动（如足球，非参与性的） ★ a mere spectator 单纯的旁观者 ☆ spectate v. 观看；spectacle n. 壮观景象 辨析：spectator / audience / viewer：spectator 现场观众；audience 泛指受众；viewer 电视观众
🧠 助记
spect（看 spectacle）+ ator（人）——看节目的人
spectator::/spekˈteɪtər/:n. 观众:Spectators cheered loudly as the athletes crossed the finish line.
2. trivial /ˈtrɪviəl/ adj. 琐碎的；不重要的；平庸的
📖 英文解释
Of little value or importance; concerned with minor or unimportant things.
✏️ 例句
Don't waste energy on trivial disputes when there are far more important issues to address.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a trivial matter/issue/problem 琐碎的事/问题 ★ trivial pursuit 追求琐事（也是一种游戏名） ☆ triviality n. 琐事；trivialize v. 使显得微不足道 辨析：trivial / minor / petty：trivial 偏无意义的；minor 偏次要的；petty 偏心胸狭窄的
🧠 助记
tri（三）+ via（路）+ l——三岔路口的小事，无足轻重
trivial::/ˈtrɪviəl/:adj. 琐碎的:She refused to get upset over such trivial matters.
3. awake /əˈweɪk/ adj. 醒着的；v. 醒来；唤醒
📖 英文解释
Not asleep; to stop sleeping; to make someone conscious of something.
✏️ 例句
Lying awake at night, she reflected on the choices that had led to her current situation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ stay/lie awake 保持清醒/躺着睡不着 ★ awake to sth 意识到某事（正式） ☆ awaken v. 唤醒（更正式）；wakeful adj. 辨析：awake / awaken / wake up：wake up 最口语；awaken 最正式；awake 兼作形容词
🧠 助记
a + wake（清醒）——进入清醒状态，就是醒来
awake::/əˈweɪk/:adj. 醒着的:He was suddenly awake to the dangers around him.
4. faulty /ˈfɔːlti/ adj. 有缺陷的；有错误的；不完善的
📖 英文解释
Working poorly or containing defects; not functioning correctly.
✏️ 例句
The accident was attributed to faulty brakes that had not been inspected for over two years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ faulty equipment/logic/design 有缺陷的设备/逻辑/设计 ★ a faulty argument 有漏洞的论点 ☆ fault n. 缺陷，错误；at fault 有过失 辨析：faulty / defective / flawed：faulty 偏功能性缺陷；defective 偏产品瑕疵；flawed 偏本质缺陷
🧠 助记
fault（缺陷）+ y（形容词后缀）——充满缺陷的
faulty::/ˈfɔːlti/:adj. 有缺陷的:The faulty wiring caused a short circuit in the building.
5. housewife /ˈhaʊzwaɪf/ n. 家庭主妇
📖 英文解释
A married woman whose main occupation is caring for her family and managing the household.
✏️ 例句
Many housewives have become entrepreneurs by launching small businesses from home.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ full-time housewife 全职主妇 ★ 考研阅读中常与 gender roles 性别角色话题相关 ☆ homemaker 更中性的现代替换词 注意：现代英语中 homemaker 更为中性，housewife 可能有性别刻板印象
🧠 助记
house（家）+ wife（妻子）——在家里操持家务的妻子
housewife::/ˈhaʊzwaɪf/:n. 家庭主妇:The survey showed many housewives desire more career opportunities.
6. intervene /ˌɪntəˈviːn/ v. 干预；介入；调停
📖 英文解释
To come between parties in a dispute; to take action to alter the course of events.
✏️ 例句
The United Nations decided to intervene in the conflict to prevent further civilian casualties.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ intervene in sth 干预某事（高频考研搭配） ★ intervene on behalf of sb 代表某人介入 ☆ intervention n. 干预；interventionist adj. 辨析：intervene / interfere：intervene 含正面调停义；interfere 偏贬义，多余干涉
🧠 助记
inter（在…之间）+ vene（来，come）——来到中间，就是干预
intervene::/ˌɪntəˈviːn/:v. 干预:The teacher intervened to stop the argument between students.
7. luggage /ˈlʌɡɪdʒ/ n. 行李；旅行箱
📖 英文解释
Suitcases and bags containing a traveller's belongings; baggage.
✏️ 例句
Passengers are reminded that oversized luggage must be checked in at the counter, not carried on board.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ carry-on/checked luggage 随身/托运行李 ★ a piece of luggage 一件行李 ☆ luggage rack/carousel 行李架/传送带 注意：luggage 是不可数名词，不说 a luggage；说 a piece of luggage
🧠 助记
lug（费力拖拉）+ gage（测量）——费力拖着量好的箱子，就是行李
luggage::/ˈlʌɡɪdʒ/:n. 行李:Please ensure your luggage does not exceed 23 kilograms.
8. seal /siːl/ v. 密封；盖章；n. 印章；海豹
📖 英文解释
To close securely to prevent leakage or access; an official mark; a marine mammal.
✏️ 例句
The envelope was sealed with wax bearing the ambassador's official coat of arms.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ seal a deal/agreement 敲定协议（商务常用） ★ sealed envelope 密封信封 ☆ seal of approval 批准印章；官方认可 辨析：seal / stamp：seal 封口或盖章（较正式）；stamp 邮戳或盖普通章
🧠 助记
seal 像封口条粘上去的声音『嘶——』——密封
seal::/siːl/:v. 密封:The officials sealed the agreement with a handshake.
9. shout /ʃaʊt/ v./n. 喊叫；大声说
📖 英文解释
To speak or call out very loudly; a loud cry or call.
✏️ 例句
The coach shouted instructions to the players from the sideline during the tense final minutes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ shout at sb 冲某人大喊（表愤怒） ★ shout for help 大喊求救 ☆ shout sb down 喊声压过某人 辨析：shout / yell / scream：shout 较中性的大声喊；yell 带紧迫/愤怒；scream 尖叫（恐惧/激动）
🧠 助记
shout 发音像『扫特』——扫堂腿配大喊声，武打片常见
shout::/ʃaʊt/:v./n. 喊叫:She shouted for help when she saw the fire.
10. dwell /dwel/ v. 居住；沉思；详述
📖 英文解释
To live in or at a place; to think or talk at length about a subject.
✏️ 例句
It is unhealthy to dwell on past failures; instead, focus on lessons learned and future goals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ dwell on/upon sth 反复思考某事（考研写作高频） ★ dwell in 居住在（较正式） ☆ dweller n. 居民（city dweller 城市居民） 辨析：dwell / reside / inhabit：dwell 文学色彩；reside 正式；inhabit 指物种栖居
🧠 助记
dwell 谐音『的窝』——住在自己的窝里
dwell::/dwel/:v. 居住:Don't dwell on your mistakes; learn from them and move on.
11. inspiration /ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃən/ n. 灵感；激励；鼓舞
📖 英文解释
The process of being mentally stimulated to do or feel something creative; a sudden useful idea.
✏️ 例句
Many artists cite nature as their primary source of inspiration for their most celebrated works.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ draw inspiration from 从…获取灵感 ★ be an inspiration to sb 成为某人的激励 ☆ inspire v. 激励；inspiring adj. 鼓舞人心的 辨析：inspiration / motivation：inspiration 侧重创意触发；motivation 侧重持续动力
🧠 助记
in（内）+ spir（呼吸，spirit）+ ation——向内呼吸获得精神力量，就是灵感
inspiration::/ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃən/:n. 灵感:Her personal story has been an inspiration to millions.
12. merge /mɜːdʒ/ v. 合并；融合；（道路）汇合
📖 英文解释
To combine or cause to combine to form a single entity; to blend gradually into something.
✏️ 例句
The two rival companies decided to merge to create a single market leader in the industry.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ merge with / into 与…合并/并入 ★ merge A and B 合并A和B ☆ merger n. 合并（尤指企业并购） 辨析：merge / combine / integrate：merge 侧重融为一体；combine 保留各部分；integrate 系统性整合
🧠 助记
mer（海，mer-maid）+ ge——大海里的河流汇合，就是合并
merge::/mɜːdʒ/:v. 合并:The two banks merged to form one of the largest in the country.
13. shine /ʃaɪn/ v. 闪耀；照耀；出色；n. 光泽
📖 英文解释
To emit or reflect light; to be very good at something; brightness or glossiness.
✏️ 例句
Despite facing numerous obstacles, her talent continued to shine through in every performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ shine at/in sth 在某方面出色 ★ shine a light on sth 揭示某事（比喻） ☆ shiny adj. 发光的；shinny 爬（口语，注意区分） 辨析：shine / glow / gleam：shine 最通用；glow 柔和发光；gleam 微弱闪光
🧠 助记
shine 谐音『闪』——闪闪发光
shine::/ʃaɪn/:v. 闪耀:Students who work hard will eventually shine in their careers.
14. yell /jel/ v./n. 喊叫；叫喊
📖 英文解释
To shout something loudly, often in pain, anger, or excitement.
✏️ 例句
The crowd began to yell with excitement as the home team scored the winning goal.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ yell at sb 冲某人大叫（表愤怒） ★ yell for help 大声呼救 ☆ yell out 大声叫出 辨析：yell / shout / scream：yell 带有情绪（激动/愤怒）；shout 较中性；scream 尖叫
🧠 助记
yell 发音像『野了』——野性爆发，大叫出来
yell::/jel/:v./n. 喊叫:He yelled at the top of his voice to warn others of the danger.
15. fraud /frɔːd/ n. 欺骗；欺诈；骗子
📖 英文解释
Wrongful or criminal deception intended to result in financial or personal gain; a person who deceives others.
✏️ 例句
The financial fraud scheme defrauded thousands of investors of their retirement savings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ commit/detect fraud 实施/发现欺诈 ★ tax/election fraud 税务/选举舞弊 ☆ fraudulent adj. 欺诈性的；defraud v. 诈骗 辨析：fraud / deception / deceit：fraud 尤指经济犯罪；deception 泛指欺骗行为；deceit 强调惯常欺骗
🧠 助记
fraud 发音像『弗劳得』——佛劳苦得来的钱被骗走了
fraud::/frɔːd/:n. 欺骗:Online fraud has increased dramatically with the rise of e-commerce.
16. tedious /ˈtiːdiəs/ adj. 枯燥的；冗长乏味的
📖 英文解释
Too long, slow, or dull; tiresome or monotonous.
✏️ 例句
Many students find memorizing vocabulary lists tedious, yet it remains a fundamental part of language learning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a tedious task/process/lecture 乏味的任务/过程/讲座 ★ grow/become tedious 变得乏味 ☆ tedium n. 乏味；boredom 同义词 辨析：tedious / boring / dull：tedious 强调冗长；boring 强调无聊；dull 强调单调
🧠 助记
ted（泰迪熊）+ ious——泰迪熊只会坐着，太无聊了
tedious::/ˈtiːdiəs/:adj. 枯燥的:The tedious paperwork took hours to complete.
17. crazy /ˈkreɪzi/ adj. 疯狂的；狂热的；愚蠢的
📖 英文解释
Mentally deranged; extremely enthusiastic; very foolish.
✏️ 例句
It seems crazy to invest all your savings in a single asset without diversifying your portfolio.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be crazy about sth 对某事狂热（口语） ★ drive sb crazy 把某人逼疯 ☆ crazily adv.; craziness n. 注意：考研写作中建议用 irrational / absurd 替代 crazy 以显正式
🧠 助记
crazy → cr + azy（哈气）——哈气哈到发疯
crazy::/ˈkreɪzi/:adj. 疯狂的:She is crazy about classical music and attends every concert.
18. invade /ɪnˈveɪd/ v. 入侵；侵略；侵扰
📖 英文解释
To enter a place forcibly with the intent to occupy or overrun; to intrude on.
✏️ 例句
The army invaded the neighboring country under the pretext of protecting its own citizens abroad.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ invade a country/territory 入侵国家/领土 ★ invade sb's privacy 侵犯某人隐私（考研常见） ☆ invasion n. 入侵；invasive adj. 侵入性的 辨析：invade / intrude / encroach：invade 大规模强行入侵；intrude 不请自来；encroach 逐渐侵占
🧠 助记
in（进入）+ vade（走，来自 evade 逃走）——强行走入，就是入侵
invade::/ɪnˈveɪd/:v. 入侵:Social media platforms may invade users' personal privacy.
19. yard /jɑːrd/ n. 院子；码（长度单位）；工场
📖 英文解释
An enclosed area adjacent to a building; a unit of linear measure equal to 3 feet (about 0.9 m).
✏️ 例句
The children played in the yard while their parents prepared dinner inside.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ front/back yard 前院/后院 ★ a yard of fabric 一码布料 ☆ Scotland Yard 苏格兰场（英国警察总部） 注意：yard 作长度单位在考研科普类文章中偶有出现
🧠 助记
yard 发音像『呀的』——呀，院子好大啊！
yard::/jɑːrd/:n. 院子:They built a garden in the yard behind their house.
20. alphabet /ˈælfəbet/ n. 字母表；基础知识
📖 英文解释
A set of letters used in writing a language, arranged in a fixed order; the rudiments of a subject.
✏️ 例句
Learning the alphabet is the first step for children beginning to read and write in any language.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the alphabet of sth …的基础/入门 ★ alphabetical order 字母顺序 ☆ alphabetize v. 按字母顺序排列 注意：alpha（α）+ beta（β）是希腊字母前两个，合成 alphabet
🧠 助记
alpha（第一）+ bet（打赌）——打赌你认识第一个字母，就是字母表
alphabet::/ˈælfəbet/:n. 字母表:Children learn the alphabet before they learn to read sentences.

heave（举起）+ n——被举起到天上，就是天堂
heaven::/ˈhevən/:n. 天堂:After months of hiking, the hot bath felt like heaven.

hospital（医院）+ ity——医院的服务精神，就是热情款待
hospitality::/ˌhɒspɪˈtæləti/:n. 好客:Chinese hospitality is renowned worldwide for its warmth.

24. backward /ˈbækwərd/ adj. 向后的；落后的；adv. 向后

Directed or moving toward the back; slow to develop; in reverse direction.

back（后）+ ward（方向）——方向朝后，就是落后/向后
backward::/ˈbækwərd/:adj. 向后的:The country's economic policies are considered backward by modern standards.

legit（合法，俚语）+ imate——合法的（legit 的正式版）
