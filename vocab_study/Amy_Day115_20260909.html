1. lure /lʊər/ v./n. 吸引；诱惑；诱饵
📖 英文解释
To tempt a person or animal to do something by offering a reward; the quality of being powerfully attractive.
✏️ 例句
Technology companies lure talented graduates with high salaries, flexible work arrangements, and exciting projects.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ lure sb into/away from sth 诱使某人进入/离开某处 ★ the lure of money/fame 金钱/名声的诱惑 ☆ allure n. 魅力（更正式）；entice v. 诱惑 辨析：lure / attract / tempt：lure 含负面诱骗义；attract 中性；tempt 侧重诱惑去做不该做的事
🧠 助记
lure 谐音『路儿』——用一条路儿吸引你走过去
lure::/lʊər/:v./n. 吸引:The prospect of easy money lured many people into the scam.
2. rigid /ˈrɪdʒɪd/ adj. 严格的；僵硬的；不灵活的
📖 英文解释
Unable to bend or be forced out of shape; strict and unable to change or be changed.
✏️ 例句
The rigid examination system has been criticized for rewarding memorization over critical thinking.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ rigid rules/structure/framework 严格的规则/结构/框架 ★ remain rigid 保持僵硬/不妥协 ☆ rigidity n. 刚性；僵化；rigidly adv. 辨析：rigid / strict / inflexible：rigid 最强，暗示毫无弹性；strict 严格要求；inflexible 不灵活
🧠 助记
rigid → r + igid（谐音『硬的』）——硬邦邦，僵硬
rigid::/ˈrɪdʒɪd/:adj. 严格的:A rigid education system cannot adapt to students' individual needs.
3. offspring /ˈɒfsprɪŋ/ n. 子孙；后代；产物
📖 英文解释
A person's children or an animal's young; the product or result of something.
✏️ 例句
Research suggests that the offspring of highly stressed parents may develop anxiety more easily.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the offspring of sth …的产物（比喻） ★ single/multiple offspring 单/多个后代 ☆ descendants n. 后裔（更正式）；progeny n. 后代（文学） 注意：offspring 单复数同形
🧠 助记
off（离开）+ spring（生长）——从亲本生长离开的，就是后代
offspring::/ˈɒfsprɪŋ/:n. 子孙:Each generation passes its values on to its offspring.
4. violate /ˈvaɪəleɪt/ v. 违背；侵犯；亵渎
📖 英文解释
To break or fail to comply with a rule, agreement, or standard; to disturb or encroach upon.
✏️ 例句
The government was accused of violating its citizens' right to freedom of expression.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ violate a law/rule/right/agreement 违反法律/规则/侵犯权利/协议 ★ violate sb's privacy 侵犯某人隐私 ☆ violation n. 违规；violator n. 违反者 辨析：violate / infringe / breach：violate 最强；infringe 侵犯权利；breach 违反合同/承诺
🧠 助记
vi（force，强力）+ olate——强行突破，就是违反/侵犯
violate::/ˈvaɪəleɪt/:v. 违背:Downloading pirated content violates copyright law.
5. poisonous /ˈpɔɪzənəs/ adj. 有毒的；有害的；恶意的
📖 英文解释
Containing a substance that causes death or harm if absorbed; extremely harmful.
✏️ 例句
The factory illegally dumped poisonous waste into the river, causing widespread environmental damage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ poisonous gas/substance/plant 有毒气体/物质/植物 ★ a poisonous atmosphere/influence 有毒的氛围/有害的影响 ☆ poison n. 毒药；v. 毒害；toxic adj. 有毒的 辨析：poisonous / toxic / venomous：poisonous 接触/食入有毒；toxic 化学毒性；venomous 特指动物注射毒液
🧠 助记
poison（毒药）+ ous（形容词后缀）——充满毒药的
poisonous::/ˈpɔɪzənəs/:adj. 有毒的:The spill released poisonous chemicals into the water supply.
6. silent /ˈsaɪlənt/ adj. 沉默的；寂静的；无声的
📖 英文解释
Making no sound; not speaking; not expressed in speech.
✏️ 例句
The crowd fell silent as the ceremony began, out of respect for those who had sacrificed their lives.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ remain/fall silent 保持/陷入沉默 ★ silent majority 沉默的大多数（政治词汇） ★ silent letter 不发音的字母 ☆ silence n. 沉默；v. 使沉默；silently adv. 辨析：silent / quiet / still：silent 无声；quiet 安静（可有轻微声音）；still 静止不动
🧠 助记
sil（丝，silk）+ ent——如丝绸般无声
silent::/ˈsaɪlənt/:adj. 沉默的:She gave him a silent nod to indicate her agreement.
7. thereby /ˌðeəˈbaɪ/ adv. 从而；因此；由此
📖 英文解释
By that means; as a result of that.
✏️ 例句
The company reduced its energy consumption by 40%, thereby cutting costs and lowering its carbon footprint.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ thereby doing sth 从而做到某事（考研写作高频连接副词） ★ thereby solving / achieving / improving... ☆ thus / hence / consequently 近义副词 注意：thereby 后常接分词短语，用于正式写作
🧠 助记
there（那里）+ by（通过）——通过那种方式，从而实现
thereby::/ˌðeəˈbaɪ/:adv. 从而:She worked overtime, thereby completing the project ahead of schedule.
8. cling /klɪŋ/ v. 紧贴；坚持；依附
📖 英文解释
To hold tightly to something; to refuse to let go; to remain close to.
✏️ 例句
Many people cling to traditional values even as society undergoes rapid and fundamental change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ cling to sth/sb 紧抓/坚持某事/某人 ★ cling to hope/power/belief 坚守希望/权力/信念 ☆ cling film 保鲜膜（英式）；clinging adj. 过分依赖的 辨析：cling / stick / adhere：cling 动作+情感依附；stick 物理粘附；adhere 正式黏附/坚持
🧠 助记
cling 谐音『紧』——紧紧抓住
cling::/klɪŋ/:v. 紧贴:She clung to the hope that her missing son would return safely.
9. divert /daɪˈvɜːt/ v. 转移；使分心；绕道
📖 英文解释
To cause someone or something to change course or direction; to distract from.
✏️ 例句
Governments sometimes divert public attention from domestic problems by stoking external tensions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ divert attention/resources/funds 转移注意力/资源/资金 ★ divert traffic 使交通改道 ☆ diversion n. 绕道；转移；娱乐消遣 辨析：divert / deflect：divert 方向改变；deflect 偏转，尤指避开
🧠 助记
di（分开）+ vert（转，convert）——转向分开，就是转移
divert::/daɪˈvɜːt/:v. 转移:The construction work forced drivers to divert via the southern route.
10. fraction /ˈfrækʃən/ n. 小部分；分数；碎片
📖 英文解释
A small part of something; a number expressing a part of a whole; a tiny amount.
✏️ 例句
Online retailers can offer products at a fraction of the price charged by traditional brick-and-mortar stores.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a fraction of the cost/time 一小部分的成本/时间 ★ in fractions 以分数形式 ☆ fractional adj. 极小的；fracture n. 骨折 辨析：fraction / portion / segment：fraction 最小份；portion 一部分（可较大）；segment 分段
🧠 助记
fract（破碎，fracture 骨折）+ ion——破碎成小部分，就是分数/小部分
fraction::/ˈfrækʃən/:n. 小部分:Online shopping has made luxury goods available at a fraction of their original price.
11. applicable /əˈplɪkəbəl/ adj. 适用的；可应用的
📖 英文解释
Relevant or appropriate to a particular situation; able to be applied.
✏️ 例句
The new data protection regulations are applicable to all companies that process personal data.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ applicable to sb/sth 对某人/某事适用 ★ where applicable 在适用之处（表格/法规常用） ☆ apply v. 应用；application n. 申请；applied adj. 应用的 辨析：applicable / relevant：applicable 强调可直接应用；relevant 强调有关联
🧠 助记
apply（应用）+ able——能够被应用的，就是适用的
applicable::/əˈplɪkəbəl/:adj. 适用的:These regulations are applicable to all businesses operating in the country.
12. bachelor /ˈbætʃələr/ n. 学士；单身汉

★ bachelor's degree 学士学位 ★ Bachelor of Arts/Science 文/理学士 ★ confirmed bachelor 坚定的单身汉 ☆ bachelorette n. 单身女性（现代用法）
🧠 助记
bach（巴赫，音乐家）+ elor——音乐学士，单身汉
bachelor::/ˈbætʃələr/:n. 学士:She earned her bachelor's degree in economics from Peking University.
13. carrier /ˈkæriər/ n. 运输者；载体；航空公司；带菌者
📖 英文解释
A person or thing that carries something; a company that transports goods or passengers; a person who transmits a disease without showing symptoms.
✏️ 例句
The disease can be spread by carriers who show no symptoms, making containment particularly challenging.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ aircraft carrier 航空母舰 ★ disease carrier 病原体携带者 ★ mail carrier 邮递员 ☆ carry v. 携带；cargo n. 货物 辨析：carrier / courier：carrier 泛指运输者；courier 专指速递员
🧠 助记
carry（携带）+ er（人）——携带东西的人或工具
carrier::/ˈkæriər/:n. 运输者:The airline carrier suspended flights due to the typhoon warning.
14. hole /həʊl/ n. 洞；漏洞；困境；v. 打洞
📖 英文解释
A hollow place in a solid body or surface; a gap or opening; a difficult situation.
✏️ 例句
Critics identified a major hole in the government's economic plan that could lead to serious budget deficits.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a hole in the argument/plan 论点/计划中的漏洞 ★ dig oneself into a hole 越陷越深 ★ in a hole 陷入困境 ☆ loophole n. 法律漏洞（考研常见）
🧠 助记
hole 谐音『豁了』——豁了个口子，出现了洞
hole::/həʊl/:n. 洞:The lawyer found a significant hole in the prosecution's argument.
15. initiate /ɪˈnɪʃieɪt/ v. 开始；发起；使入门；n. 新成员
📖 英文解释
To cause a process or action to begin; to introduce someone to a new activity or skill.
✏️ 例句
The government initiated a comprehensive review of its immigration policy following growing public pressure.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ initiate a process/discussion/reform 启动流程/讨论/改革 ★ initiate sb into sth 引领某人入门 ☆ initiative n. 主动性；倡议（考研高频词） 辨析：initiate / begin / start：initiate 最正式，强调正式启动；begin/start 通用
🧠 助记
init（初始，IT术语）+ iate——初始化，就是开始
initiate::/ɪˈnɪʃieɪt/:v. 开始:The committee initiated a new program to support rural education.
16. mate /meɪt/ n. 伴侣；朋友；配偶；v. 交配
📖 英文解释
A companion or friend; a partner in a romantic relationship; to breed.
✏️ 例句
In the animal kingdom, many species travel thousands of miles to find an appropriate mate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ mate for life 终身伴侣 ★ classmate / teammate / roommate 同学/队友/室友 ★ running mate 竞选伙伴（政治词汇） ☆ mating season 交配季节
🧠 助记
mate 谐音『没它』——没了伴侣，就是孤独
mate::/meɪt/:n. 伴侣:Birds choose their mate based on elaborate courtship rituals.
17. meaning /ˈmiːnɪŋ/ n. 意义；含义；重要性
📖 英文解释
What is meant by a word or action; significance or purpose.
✏️ 例句
Philosophers have long debated the meaning of life and what constitutes genuine human happiness.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the meaning of life/existence 生命/存在的意义 ★ a meaningful relationship/experience 有意义的关系/体验 ☆ meaningful adj. 有意义的；meaningless adj. 无意义的 辨析：meaning / significance / implication：meaning 字面含义；significance 重要性；implication 隐含意
🧠 助记
mean（意思）+ ing——正在表达的意思，就是含义
meaning::/ˈmiːnɪŋ/:n. 意义:The meaning of a word can change depending on context.
18. presumably /prɪˈzjuːməbli/ adv. 大概；可能；据推测
📖 英文解释
Used to convey that what is asserted is very likely though not known for certain.
✏️ 例句
The package, which was delivered yesterday, presumably contains the documents requested last week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ presumably, ... 大概…（句首插入语） ★ presumably based on 据推测基于… ☆ presume v. 推测；presumption n. 推断；假定 辨析：presumably / probably / apparently：presumably 推断；probably 可能性；apparently 从表面看来
🧠 助记
pre（预先）+ sum（总结）+ ably——预先总结大概，就是推测
presumably::/prɪˈzjuːməbli/:adv. 大概:He was late, presumably due to the traffic congestion.
19. reap /riːp/ v. 收获；获得；收割
📖 英文解释
To harvest a crop; to receive a return, especially a profit or reward, for effort.
✏️ 例句
Students who invest time in mastering fundamental concepts will reap the rewards in future examinations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ reap the rewards/benefits/fruits of 收获…的成果 ★ reap what you sow 种瓜得瓜（谚语） ☆ reaper n. 收割机；Grim Reaper 死神 辨析：reap / harvest / gain：reap 比喻收获（努力的回报）；harvest 字面丰收；gain 泛指获得
🧠 助记
reap → leap（跳）换首字母——努力后跳起来收获成果
reap::/riːp/:v. 收获:Companies that invest in innovation will reap long-term benefits.
20. brake /breɪk/ n./v. 刹车；制动；放慢
📖 英文解释
A device for slowing or stopping a vehicle; to slow or stop a vehicle using a brake.
✏️ 例句
The driver applied the brakes sharply to avoid colliding with the pedestrian who stepped into the road.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ apply/put on the brake 踩刹车 ★ put the brakes on sth 遏制某事（比喻） ☆ brake ≠ break（注意拼写！） 辨析：brake / slow down：brake 专指刹车动作；slow down 泛指减速
🧠 助记
brake 谐音『不来客』——刹车，来客别进来
brake::/breɪk/:n./v. 刹车:Rising interest rates act as a brake on economic growth.

cal（call，呼叫）+ endar——每天呼叫你提醒日程，就是日历
calendar::/ˈkælɪndər/:n. 日历:The exam dates have been confirmed and added to the academic calendar.

de（下降）+ cay（谐音『蔫』）——下降变蔫，就是腐烂/衰退
