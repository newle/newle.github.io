1. ignorance /ˈɪɡnərəns/ n. 无知；愚昧；不了解
📖 英文解释
lack of knowledge or information about something
✏️ 例句
Ignorance of the law is no excuse for breaking it.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ignorance of/about sth 对某事的无知；in ignorance 在不知情的情况下
🧠 助记
ignore（忽视）+ ance = 忽视知识的状态 = 无知
ignorance::/ˈɪɡnərəns/:n. 无知:His ignorance about basic science was surprising.
2. landlord /ˈlændlɔːd/ n. 房东；地主；店主
📖 英文解释
a person who owns a house or land that they rent to others
✏️ 例句
The landlord raised the rent by 10% at the start of the new year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
landlord and tenant 房东与租客；absentee landlord 不在场的房东
🧠 助记
land（土地）+ lord（主人）= 土地的主人 = 地主、房东
landlord::/ˈlændlɔːd/:n. 房东:She called her landlord to report the broken heating.
3. precaution /prɪˈkɔːʃn/ n. 预防措施；预防；谨慎
📖 英文解释
an action taken to prevent something dangerous or unpleasant from happening
✏️ 例句
Take all necessary precautions when handling dangerous chemicals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
take precautions against 采取预防措施；as a precaution 作为预防措施
🧠 助记
pre（提前）+ caution（谨慎）= 提前谨慎 = 预防措施
precaution::/prɪˈkɔːʃn/:n. 预防措施:She took the precaution of locking all the doors.
4. prosperous /ˈprɒspərəs/ adj. 繁荣的；成功的；兴旺的
📖 英文解释
rich and successful
✏️ 例句
The country became prosperous after years of economic reform.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prosperous country/business 繁荣的国家/生意；prosperity n. 繁荣
🧠 助记
prosper（繁荣，如prosper in business）+ ous = prosperous，生意兴旺
prosperous::/ˈprɒspərəs/:adj. 繁荣的:They built a prosperous trading company over two decades.
5. tense /tens/ adj. 紧张的；绷紧的 n. 时态 v. 使紧张
📖 英文解释
feeling nervous and anxious; stretched tight; (grammar) the form of a verb showing time
✏️ 例句
The atmosphere was tense as both teams waited for the final whistle.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
past/present/future tense 过去/现在/将来时态；tense muscles 肌肉紧绷
🧠 助记
tense联想tension（紧张），绳子拉紧就是tense状态
tense::/tens/:adj. 紧张的:Everyone was tense and anxious before the exam.
6. versus /ˈvɜːsəs/ prep. 对；与……相对；对比
📖 英文解释
used to compare two things or people; used to show that two teams or sides are competing
✏️ 例句
The debate focused on individual rights versus collective responsibility.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
A versus B 甲对乙（比赛）；quality versus quantity 质量与数量之比
🧠 助记
versus来自拉丁语'against'（对抗），缩写vs.在比赛中常用
versus::/ˈvɜːsəs/:prep. 对:The match was Spain versus Germany in the final.
7. allocate /ˈæləkeɪt/ v. 分配；拨给；指定
📖 英文解释
to give something officially to someone for a particular purpose
✏️ 例句
The government allocated extra funds for public education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
allocate sth to sb/for sth 将某物分配给某人/用于某事；allocation n. 分配
🧠 助记
al（去）+ locate（放置）= 放置到某处 = 分配，对比locate（定位）
allocate::/ˈæləkeɪt/:v. 分配:Each student was allocated a specific research topic.
8. beg /beɡ/ v. 乞求；恳求；行乞
📖 英文解释
to ask someone earnestly for something; to ask for money or food
✏️ 例句
She begged her parents to let her stay out late.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
beg sb to do sth 恳求某人做某事；beg for mercy/forgiveness 求饶/请求原谅
🧠 助记
联想beggar（乞丐），beg是行为，beggar是人，'beg'跪下来求
beg::/beɡ/:v. 乞求:He begged the judge for leniency.
9. glad /ɡlæd/ adj. 高兴的；乐意的；愉快的
📖 英文解释
pleased and happy; willing to do something
✏️ 例句
I'm glad you came to the party — it wouldn't be the same without you.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
glad to do sth 乐意做某事；glad of sth 对某事感到高兴；glad tidings 好消息
🧠 助记
glad联想'gladly'（乐意地），glad是高兴，gladly是高兴地去做
glad::/ɡlæd/:adj. 高兴的:She was glad to hear that her friend had recovered.
10. infect /ɪnˈfekt/ v. 传染；感染；影响
📖 英文解释
to pass a disease to someone; to make someone feel a particular emotion
✏️ 例句
One person's enthusiasm can infect the whole team.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
infect sb with sth 用某病感染某人；infection n. 感染；infectious adj. 传染性的
🧠 助记
in（进入）+ fect（做，如affect）= 进入并影响 = 感染，联想bacteria infect body
infect::/ɪnˈfekt/:v. 传染:The virus can infect both humans and animals.
11. mess /mes/ n. 混乱；脏乱 v. 弄乱；搞砸
📖 英文解释
a dirty or untidy state; a situation that is confused or difficult
✏️ 例句
His bedroom was always in a mess after gaming all night.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make a mess 弄得一团糟；mess up 搞砸；mess around 胡闹
🧠 助记
mess联想messy（乱的），mess是名词/动词，一片mess就是乱成一锅粥
mess::/mes/:n. 混乱:Don't mess up the kitchen after I've just cleaned it.
12. troublesome /ˈtrʌblsəm/ adj. 令人烦恼的；麻烦的；棘手的
📖 英文解释
causing trouble, problems, or difficulties
✏️ 例句
The troublesome student disrupted the class repeatedly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
troublesome problem/situation 棘手的问题/情况；troublesome child 难缠的孩子
🧠 助记
trouble（麻烦）+ some（有……性质的）= 有麻烦性质的 = 令人烦恼的
troublesome::/ˈtrʌblsəm/:adj. 令人烦恼的:This has been a particularly troublesome case to solve.
13. delegate /ˈdelɪɡət/ (n.) /ˈdelɪɡeɪt/ (v.)/ n. 代表；委员 v. 委派；授权
📖 英文解释
a person chosen to represent others; to give part of your work or power to someone else
✏️ 例句
She learned to delegate tasks to her team members effectively.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
delegate to sb 委托给某人；conference delegates 会议代表；delegation n. 代表团
🧠 助记
de（向下）+ legate（大使，如legal）= 向下派出的人 = 代表，授权给下属
delegate::/ˈdelɪɡət/ (n.) /ˈdelɪɡeɪt/ (v.)/:n. 代表:Managers must learn to delegate responsibilities to their staff.
14. dive /daɪv/ v./n. 俯冲；跳水；深入探究
📖 英文解释
to jump into water with your head first; to move suddenly downward
✏️ 例句
She dived into the pool with perfect form.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dive into 跳入；深入研究；dive-bomb 俯冲轰炸；take a dive 下跌；故意输
🧠 助记
dive头朝下入水，联想'dive bar'（低档酒吧）也是'潜入'某个地方
dive::/daɪv/:v./n. 俯冲:He dived deep into the research to find the answer.
15. hop /hɒp/ v./n. 单脚跳；快速移动；短途旅行
📖 英文解释
to jump on one leg; to move quickly and lightly; a short journey
✏️ 例句
The children hopped along the path, playing games.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hop on/off 跳上/跳下（交通工具）；island-hop 在岛屿间游览；hip-hop 嘻哈
🧠 助记
hop联想rabbit hopping（兔子蹦蹦跳跳），单脚跳或短促地跳
hop::/hɒp/:v./n. 单脚跳:She hopped on the next train to the city.
16. kindergarten /ˈkɪndəɡɑːtn/ n. 幼儿园；学前班
📖 英文解释
a school or class for children between the ages of about 4 and 6
✏️ 例句
Children learn to share and cooperate in kindergarten.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
kindergarten teacher 幼儿园老师；start/attend kindergarten 上幼儿园
🧠 助记
来自德语：kinder（孩子们）+ garten（花园）= 孩子们的花园 = 幼儿园
kindergarten::/ˈkɪndəɡɑːtn/:n. 幼儿园:My daughter started kindergarten this September.
17. litter /ˈlɪtə(r)/ n./v. 垃圾；乱丢；一窝（幼崽）
📖 英文解释
pieces of rubbish left in public places; to leave rubbish in a public place; a group of young animals born at the same time
✏️ 例句
Littering in public places is subject to a heavy fine.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
litter the streets 乱丢垃圾在街上；litter bin 垃圾桶；a litter of kittens 一窝小猫
🧠 助记
litter听起来像'little'，一小堆小垃圾散落各处 = 垃圾、乱丢
litter::/ˈlɪtə(r)/:n./v. 垃圾:The park was littered with empty cans after the festival.
18. passport /ˈpɑːspɔːt/ n. 护照；通行证；手段
📖 英文解释
an official document that proves who you are; something that allows you to achieve something
✏️ 例句
A good education can be a passport to a better future.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
valid passport 有效护照；passport to success 成功的通行证；passport control 护照检查
🧠 助记
pass（通过）+ port（港口）= 通过港口的证件 = 护照
passport::/ˈpɑːspɔːt/:n. 护照:Make sure your passport is valid for at least six months.
19. pat /pæt/ v./n. 轻拍；拍打；轻触
📖 英文解释
to touch someone or something gently with your flat hand; a gentle touch with the flat hand
✏️ 例句
She patted the dog on the head to show affection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pat sb on the back 拍某人后背（表扬）；pat down 搜身；give sb a pat 拍拍某人
🧠 助记
pat是轻拍，联想pat-a-cake（拍手游戏），pat比hit轻柔温和
pat::/pæt/:v./n. 轻拍:He gave his son a reassuring pat on the shoulder.
20. prospective /prəˈspektɪv/ adj. 预期的；即将发生的；未来的
📖 英文解释
expected to do or be something in the future
✏️ 例句
The company held an open day for prospective employees.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prospective buyer/student/employer 未来的买家/学生/雇主
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ spect（看）+ ive = 向前看的 = 展望未来的，对比retrospective（回顾的）
prospective::/prəˈspektɪv/:adj. 预期的:Several prospective investors visited the new development.
21. uncle /ˈʌŋkl/ n. 叔叔；伯伯；舅舅；姑父
📖 英文解释
the brother of your mother or father, or the husband of your aunt
✏️ 例句
His uncle gave him excellent advice about starting a business.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
say uncle（美）认输；Uncle Sam 山姆大叔（美国的象征）
🧠 助记
uncle发音像'昂可'，叔叔是值得'仰望'的长辈
uncle::/ˈʌŋkl/:n. 叔叔:My uncle lives in Shanghai and works as a doctor.
22. blueprint /ˈbluːprɪnt/ n. 蓝图；详细计划；设计图
📖 英文解释
a plan that shows how something will be built or achieved
✏️ 例句
The president presented a blueprint for economic recovery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a blueprint for success/reform 成功/改革的蓝图；architectural blueprint 建筑蓝图
🧠 助记
blue（蓝）+ print（印刷）= 蓝色印刷图纸 = 建筑蓝图，引申为计划
blueprint::/ˈbluːprɪnt/:n. 蓝图:The architects studied the blueprints carefully before construction.
23. federation /ˌfedəˈreɪʃn/ n. 联邦；联盟；联合会
📖 英文解释
a country or group that is made up of a number of states or organizations
✏️ 例句
The Russian Federation is the largest country in the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
trade federation 贸易联盟；sports federation 体育联合会；federal adj. 联邦的
🧠 助记
federal（联邦的）→ federation，联邦制国家由多个state联合组成
federation::/ˌfedəˈreɪʃn/:n. 联邦:She became president of the national athletics federation.
24. telescope /ˈtelɪskəʊp/ n. 望远镜 v. 缩短；压缩
📖 英文解释
a piece of equipment in the shape of a tube that you look through to see things that are far away
✏️ 例句
He used a telescope to observe the stars from his backyard.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
radio telescope 射电望远镜；telescope into 压缩成；Hubble Telescope 哈勃望远镜
🧠 助记
tele（远）+ scope（观察）= 观察远处 = 望远镜，tele如telephone（远程电话）
telescope::/ˈtelɪskəʊp/:n. 望远镜 v. 缩短:The new telescope can detect galaxies millions of light-years away.
25. cheque /tʃek/ n. 支票（英式）
📖 英文解释
a printed form that you write on and sign as a way of paying for things
✏️ 例句
She wrote a cheque for the full amount of the rent.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
write a cheque 开支票；cash a cheque 兑现支票；cheque book 支票簿
🧠 助记
cheque是check的英式拼法，支票是银行'核查'（check）账户的工具
cheque::/tʃek/:n. 支票（英式）:Payment by cheque is no longer accepted at this store.
