1. republican /rɪˈpʌblɪkən/ adj./n. 共和制的；共和党人
📖 英文解释
relating to a republic; a member of the Republican Party
✏️ 例句
The republican candidate won the presidential election by a narrow margin.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the Republican Party 共和党；republican government 共和政府；Republican voter 共和党选民
🧠 助记
republic（共和国）+ an（人）= 共和国的人 = 共和党人，Republic → Republican
republican::/rɪˈpʌblɪkən/:adj./n. 共和制的:She switched her vote to the republican candidate this election.
2. ritual /ˈrɪtʃuəl/ n./adj. 仪式；惯例；礼仪的
📖 英文解释
a religious or social ceremony; a series of actions regularly followed
✏️ 例句
Morning coffee has become a daily ritual that she cannot do without.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
religious/social ritual 宗教/社会仪式；ritual ceremony 仪式；ritual abuse 仪式性虐待
🧠 助记
rite（宗教仪式）+ ual = ritual，宗教仪式是ritual，联想'rite of passage'成年礼
ritual::/ˈrɪtʃuəl/:n./adj. 仪式:The festival involves many ancient rituals passed down through generations.
3. vanish /ˈvænɪʃ/ v. 消失；突然不见；绝迹
📖 英文解释
to disappear suddenly and completely
✏️ 例句
The magician made the rabbit vanish into thin air.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
vanish into thin air 凭空消失；vanish without a trace 消失得无影无踪
🧠 助记
van（车）+ ish → 车子一开就不见了 = 消失，联想van消失在远处
vanish::/ˈvænɪʃ/:v. 消失:The suspect managed to vanish before police arrived.
4. petrol /ˈpetrəl/ n. 汽油（英式）；石油
📖 英文解释
liquid used as fuel for vehicles and machines (British English)
✏️ 例句
The car ran out of petrol on the motorway, causing a long delay.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
petrol station 加油站；petrol price 油价；unleaded petrol 无铅汽油
🧠 助记
petrol来自petroleum（石油）的缩短，petro（石头/油）= 从岩石中提取的油
petrol::/ˈpetrəl/:n. 汽油（英式）:Petrol prices have risen sharply due to global supply issues.
5. subsequent /ˈsʌbsɪkwənt/ adj. 随后的；后来的；其后的
📖 英文解释
happening or coming after something else
✏️ 例句
Subsequent investigations revealed that the initial report was inaccurate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
subsequent to 在……之后；subsequent events/years 随后的事件/年份；subsequently adv. 其后
🧠 助记
sub（在后）+ sequent（跟随，如sequence序列）= 跟在后面的 = 随后的
subsequent::/ˈsʌbsɪkwənt/:adj. 随后的:The initial experiment and subsequent tests all showed the same results.
6. bonus /ˈbəʊnəs/ n. 奖金；额外收益；红利
📖 英文解释
an extra amount of money added to wages; something extra and useful that you get additionally
✏️ 例句
Employees received a year-end bonus as a reward for their hard work.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
performance bonus 绩效奖金；bonus point 加分；as a bonus 额外地
🧠 助记
bonus来自拉丁语'good'（好的），额外的好处 = 奖金/红利，bona fide（真诚的）同根
bonus::/ˈbəʊnəs/:n. 奖金:The warm weather was an unexpected bonus for the outdoor event.
7. consent /kənˈsent/ n./v. 同意；许可；赞同
📖 英文解释
permission for something to happen; to give permission or agree to something
✏️ 例句
Parental consent is required for children under 16 to participate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
give/obtain consent 给予/获得同意；by mutual consent 经双方同意；age of consent 同意年龄
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ sent（感觉，如sentiment）= 共同感受 = 同意，对比dissent（反对）
consent::/kənˈsent/:n./v. 同意:She gave her consent for the operation to proceed.
8. bubble /ˈbʌbl/ n. 泡泡；气泡 v. 冒泡；起泡
📖 英文解释
a small ball of air in a liquid or solid; to produce bubbles; an economic bubble
✏️ 例句
The economic bubble burst, causing widespread financial crisis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
bubble up 冒泡上升；soap bubble 肥皂泡；housing bubble 房地产泡沫；on the bubble 边缘
🧠 助记
bubble象声词，bub-ble就像泡泡冒出的声音，联想'trouble bubble'麻烦冒泡
bubble::/ˈbʌbl/:n. 泡泡:Children love blowing bubbles in the garden.
9. grip /ɡrɪp/ v./n. 握紧；控制；吸引力
📖 英文解释
to hold something firmly; a firm hold on something; the ability to attract and hold attention
✏️ 例句
Fear gripped the audience as the suspense built up in the film.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
get a grip 控制自己；in the grip of 受……控制；grip on sth 对某物的控制
🧠 助记
grip联想'grip tape'（防滑带），手握紧不打滑，抓住、控制的感觉
grip::/ɡrɪp/:v./n. 握紧:She gripped the railing tightly as she climbed the steep stairs.
10. humble /ˈhʌmbl/ adj. 谦逊的；低微的 v. 使谦恭
📖 英文解释
not proud; of low rank or social position; to make someone feel less important
✏️ 例句
Despite his great success, he remained humble and approachable.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
humble background 卑微的出身；eat humble pie 认错道歉；humble sb 使某人感到羞辱
🧠 助记
humble来自humus（泥土），贴近大地 = 谦逊，联想'humble origins'（出身卑微）
humble::/ˈhʌmbl/:adj. 谦逊的:He was too humble to accept credit for the team's success.
11. navy /ˈneɪvi/ n. 海军；藏青色 adj. 藏青色的
📖 英文解释
the part of a country's military forces that fights at sea; a dark blue colour
✏️ 例句
He joined the navy right after finishing his studies at university.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the Royal/US Navy 皇家/美国海军；navy blue 藏青色；naval adj. 海军的
🧠 助记
navy来自拉丁语navis（船），navigat（航行）同根，海军 = 开船打仗的军队
navy::/ˈneɪvi/:n. 海军:She wore a navy blue dress to the formal dinner.
12. sociable /ˈsəʊʃəbl/ adj. 合群的；善于交际的；好社交的
📖 英文解释
friendly and enjoying the company of other people
✏️ 例句
She is very sociable and never has trouble making new friends.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sociable person/nature 善于交际的人/性格；sociable occasion 社交场合
🧠 助记
social（社交）+ able（能够）= 能够社交的 = 善于交际的
sociable::/ˈsəʊʃəbl/:adj. 合群的:He became more sociable after joining the school drama club.
13. ant /ænt/ n. 蚂蚁
📖 英文解释
a small insect that lives in large groups and is known for hard work
✏️ 例句
Ants work together in colonies with remarkable efficiency and coordination.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ant colony 蚂蚁群落；have ants in one's pants 坐立不安；army ant 行军蚁
🧠 助记
ant = 'a' tiny insect，蚂蚁是最微小的昆虫之一，勤劳的象征
ant::/ænt/:n. 蚂蚁:A line of ants marched across the kitchen floor.
14. betray /bɪˈtreɪ/ v. 出卖；背叛；泄露
📖 英文解释
to be disloyal to someone who trusts you; to reveal something accidentally
✏️ 例句
His expression betrayed his true feelings, despite his calm words.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
betray sb/a secret/one's country 出卖某人/秘密/祖国；betrayal n. 背叛
🧠 助记
be（使）+ tray（托盘，expose）= 将某人放在托盘上暴露 = 背叛、泄露
betray::/bɪˈtreɪ/:v. 出卖:She felt betrayed when she discovered her friend had lied to her.
15. handful /ˈhændfʊl/ n. 一把；少数；难以管控的人
📖 英文解释
the amount that you can hold in one hand; a small number
✏️ 例句
Only a handful of students scored above 90% on the difficult exam.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a handful of 少数的；be a handful 难以管控；by the handful 一把一把地
🧠 助记
hand（手）+ ful（满满的）= 手满满的 = 一把，少数，联想grasp a handful
handful::/ˈhændfʊl/:n. 一把:A handful of protesters gathered outside the government building.
16. imperative /ɪmˈperətɪv/ adj. 必要的；紧迫的 n. 必要的事；祈使语气
📖 英文解释
extremely important and urgent; a thing that is very important or necessary
✏️ 例句
It is imperative that all staff complete the safety training before Monday.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
moral/economic imperative 道德/经济上的必要性；imperative mood 祈使语气
🧠 助记
imper（命令，如emperor皇帝命令）+ ative = 命令性质的 = 必须的、祈使的
imperative::/ɪmˈperətɪv/:adj. 必要的:Speed is imperative when dealing with medical emergencies.
17. reckon /ˈrekən/ v. 认为；估计；计算；料想
📖 英文解释
to think or believe; to calculate something; to expect something to happen
✏️ 例句
I reckon it will take at least two hours to finish the project.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reckon with 应对；估计；reckon on 指望；be reckoned as 被认为是
🧠 助记
reckon联想'wreck'（毁坏），计算错了就会wreck（搞砸），谨慎reckon
reckon::/ˈrekən/:v. 认为:She reckoned that the journey would take about three hours.
18. acute /əˈkjuːt/ adj. 急性的；严重的；灵敏的；敏锐的
📖 英文解释
very serious; (of a disease) severe and sudden; (of senses) very sharp and sensitive
✏️ 例句
The hospital is struggling with an acute shortage of medical staff.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
acute pain/shortage 剧烈疼痛/严重短缺；acute angle 锐角；acute observation 敏锐的观察
🧠 助记
acute联想accurate（准确），两者都强调精准、敏锐，锐角acute angle是尖锐的
acute::/əˈkjuːt/:adj. 急性的:He has an acute sense of smell that helps him as a chef.
19. castle /ˈkɑːsl/ n. 城堡；堡垒；车（国际象棋）
📖 英文解释
a large strong building with thick walls, built in the past to defend against attack
✏️ 例句
The ancient castle on the hilltop overlooked the entire valley below.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sand castle 沙堡；build castles in the air 建空中楼阁；castle in chess 象棋中的车
🧠 助记
castle联想cast（投掷，防御）+ le，城堡是防御工事，cast stones at enemies
castle::/ˈkɑːsl/:n. 城堡:They visited a medieval castle during their trip to Scotland.
20. considerate /kənˈsɪdərət/ adj. 体贴的；考虑周到的；关心他人的
📖 英文解释
thinking carefully about the needs and feelings of other people
✏️ 例句
It was very considerate of him to prepare dinner for his tired colleagues.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be considerate of others 体谅他人；considerate behavior 体贴的行为
🧠 助记
consider（考虑）+ ate = considerate，考虑到别人感受的 = 体贴的
considerate::/kənˈsɪdərət/:adj. 体贴的:She was always considerate of her roommate's sleep schedule.
21. dignity /ˈdɪɡnɪti/ n. 尊严；高贵；自尊
📖 英文解释
the sense of your own importance and value; calm, serious behaviour that deserves respect
✏️ 例句
Even in poverty, she maintained her dignity with grace and composure.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
human dignity 人的尊严；beneath one's dignity 有失体面；stand on one's dignity 自尊自重
🧠 助记
dignity来自dignified（有尊严的），dign（值得）= 值得尊重的状态 = 尊严
dignity::/ˈdɪɡnɪti/:n. 尊严:She faced the criticism with great dignity and calmness.
22. heighten /ˈhaɪtn/ v. 加强；增高；提高；加剧
📖 英文解释
to increase or become more intense
✏️ 例句
The dramatic music heightened the tension in the final scene of the movie.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
heighten awareness/tension/interest 提高意识/加剧紧张/增加兴趣；heightened adj. 增强的
🧠 助记
height（高度）+ en（使……）= 使高度增加 = 加强，类比deepen（加深）
heighten::/ˈhaɪtn/:v. 加强:Security measures were heightened following the threat.
23. magnificent /mæɡˈnɪfɪsnt/ adj. 宏伟的；壮丽的；华丽的
📖 英文解释
very impressive and attractive; very good
✏️ 例句
The view from the mountain top was absolutely magnificent at sunrise.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
magnificent scenery/building 壮丽的风景/宏伟的建筑；magnificence n. 壮丽
🧠 助记
magnify（放大）+ ficent（制作）= 放大制作得非常美 = 宏伟壮丽的
magnificent::/mæɡˈnɪfɪsnt/:adj. 宏伟的:The palace was a magnificent example of baroque architecture.
24. metre /ˈmiːtə(r)/ n. 米；节拍；韵律
📖 英文解释
the basic unit of length in the metric system; the regular arrangement of stressed syllables in poetry
✏️ 例句
She ran the 100-metre sprint in just under eleven seconds.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
square/cubic metre 平方/立方米；metric system 公制；metre of a poem 诗歌韵律
🧠 助记
metre来自希腊语metron（测量），联想meter（测量仪），都是测量相关
metre::/ˈmiːtə(r)/:n. 米:The room measured six metres by four metres.

She danced with perfect rhythm, moving smoothly to the music.

rhythm::/ˈrɪðəm/:n. 节奏:He tapped his foot to the rhythm of the music.
