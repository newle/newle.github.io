1. throat /θrəʊt/ n. 咽喉；喉咙；嗓子
📖 英文解释
the passage at the back of the mouth and nose; the front of the neck
✏️ 例句
She had a sore throat and could barely speak above a whisper.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
clear one's throat 清嗓子；cut-throat competition 激烈竞争；jump down sb's throat 猛烈批评
🧠 助记
throat联想'throw'（投），声音从throat投出去，喉咙是声音的出口
throat::/θrəʊt/:n. 咽喉:The singer rested her throat before the big performance.
2. tidy /ˈtaɪdi/ adj. 整洁的；有条理的 v. 整理；收拾
📖 英文解释
arranged neatly; to make a place neat and organized
✏️ 例句
Please tidy your room before guests arrive for the dinner party.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tidy up 整理；tidy room/desk 整洁的房间/桌子；tidy sum 相当多的一笔钱
🧠 助记
tidy联想'tidy mind'（有条理的头脑），整洁的人内心也是有序的
tidy::/ˈtaɪdi/:adj. 整洁的:She liked to keep her desk tidy so she could work more efficiently.
3. wire /waɪə(r)/ n. 电线；铁丝；电报 v. 布线；发电报
📖 英文解释
a thin piece of metal used to carry electricity; to connect with wire
✏️ 例句
Electricians spent three days wiring the new office building.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
barbed wire 铁丝网；live wire 带电导线；wire transfer 电汇；get one's wires crossed 误解
🧠 助记
wire联想'wireless'（无线），有wire是有线，无wire是无线，wire是最基本的导线
wire::/waɪə(r)/:n. 电线:The suspect was wearing a wire during the undercover operation.
4. cliff /klɪf/ n. 悬崖；峭壁；绝壁
📖 英文解释
a high area of rock with a very steep side, usually next to the sea
✏️ 例句
They stood at the edge of the cliff, amazed by the view of the ocean.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cliff edge 悬崖边；cliff face 峭壁；cliff-hanger 扣人心弦的结局；off the cliff 悬崖边缘
🧠 助记
cliff联想'Cliff Richard'（悬崖理查德，英国歌手名），站在悬崖边缘的感觉
cliff::/klɪf/:n. 悬崖:The castle was built on a cliff overlooking the sea.
5. curve /kɜːv/ n./v. 曲线；弯曲；使弯曲
📖 英文解释
a smooth, rounded bend in something; to form or move in a curved line
✏️ 例句
The road curved sharply to the left before reaching the tunnel entrance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
learning curve 学习曲线；ahead of the curve 超前；throw sb a curve 出其不意
🧠 助记
curve联想curl（卷曲），字母c本身就是弯曲的形状，curve是弯曲线
curve::/kɜːv/:n./v. 曲线:The river curves gently through the valley before reaching the sea.
6. destiny /ˈdestɪni/ n. 命运；天命；定数
📖 英文解释
the things that will happen in the future, especially those that cannot be changed
✏️ 例句
She believed that meeting him was her destiny and not just a coincidence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
shape one's destiny 掌握命运；destiny awaits 命运等待；manifest destiny 天定命运
🧠 助记
destiny联想destination（目的地），命运就像你的最终目的地，destined to be
destiny::/ˈdestɪni/:n. 命运:He felt it was his destiny to become a great leader.
7. dim /dɪm/ adj. 昏暗的；模糊的；愚钝的 v. 变暗
📖 英文解释
not bright or easy to see; not clear in memory; slightly stupid
✏️ 例句
The dim lighting in the restaurant created a romantic and cozy atmosphere.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dim light/memory 昏暗的灯光/模糊的记忆；dim-witted 愚笨的；take a dim view of 不赞同
🧠 助记
dim对比bright（明亮），dim是光线少、暗淡，联想'dim sum'（粤式点心，在昏暗环境吃）
dim::/dɪm/:adj. 昏暗的:She could only see a dim outline of the figure in the fog.
8. paradox /ˈpærədɒks/ n. 悖论；矛盾；似乎自相矛盾的陈述
📖 英文解释
a statement that seems to contradict itself but may be true
✏️ 例句
It's a paradox that the more we fear death, the less we truly live.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a paradox that 悖论是……；paradoxical adj. 矛盾的；resolve a paradox 解决悖论
🧠 助记
para（超出）+ dox（观点，如orthodox正统）= 超出常规观点 = 悖论，矛盾观点
paradox::/ˈpærədɒks/:n. 悖论:The paradox of freedom is that absolute freedom leads to chaos.
9. repeatedly /rɪˈpiːtɪdli/ adv. 反复地；一再地；多次地
📖 英文解释
many times; happening again and again
✏️ 例句
She repeatedly asked her son to clean up his room with no success.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
repeatedly warned/told/asked 多次警告/告诉/要求；repeat v. 重复；repetition n. 重复
🧠 助记
repeat（重复）+ ed（已经）+ ly = repeatedly，已经重复了很多次地
repeatedly::/rɪˈpiːtɪdli/:adv. 反复地:He has repeatedly ignored warnings about his dangerous behavior.
10. resolution /ˌrezəˈluːʃn/ n. 决心；决议；分辨率；解决
📖 英文解释
a firm decision to do or not to do something; a formal decision voted on; the ability of a screen to show detail
✏️ 例句
Her New Year's resolution was to exercise regularly and eat more healthily.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pass/adopt a resolution 通过决议；make a resolution 下决心；conflict resolution 冲突解决
🧠 助记
resolve（解决/决心）→ resolution，resolve的名词，re（再次）+ solve（解决）
resolution::/ˌrezəˈluːʃn/:n. 决心:The UN Security Council passed a resolution condemning the attack.
11. scream /skriːm/ v./n. 尖叫；呼喊；大声呼叫
📖 英文解释
to make a loud high cry because you are hurt, frightened or excited
✏️ 例句
She screamed in fright when she saw the spider on her pillow.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
scream at sb 冲某人大叫；scream for help 大声呼救；a scream of laughter 开怀大笑
🧠 助记
scream象声词，like a screeching sound，高声尖叫，联想cream（奶油）前加s变成scream
scream::/skriːm/:v./n. 尖叫:The children screamed with delight on the roller coaster.
12. shade /ʃeɪd/ n. 阴凉处；遮光物；色调 v. 遮蔽
📖 英文解释
an area where the sun does not reach directly; a cover that reduces light; a slight degree of colour
✏️ 例句
They sat in the shade of a large tree to escape the afternoon heat.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in the shade 在阴凉处；shade of meaning 细微的意义差别；window shade 窗帘；shady adj. 有阴影的
🧠 助记
shade联想shadow（影子），shade是阴凉，shadow是影子，两者都由遮挡光产生
shade::/ʃeɪd/:n. 阴凉处:She chose a darker shade of blue for the bedroom walls.
13. collaborate /kəˈlæbəreɪt/ v. 合作；协作；共同完成
📖 英文解释
to work together with someone else in order to achieve something
✏️ 例句
Scientists from different countries collaborated on the groundbreaking research.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
collaborate with sb on sth 与某人在某事上合作；collaboration n. 合作；collaborative adj. 合作的
🧠 助记
col（共同）+ labor（劳动）+ ate = 共同劳动 = 合作，labor是劳动，collaborate是合作
collaborate::/kəˈlæbəreɪt/:v. 合作:The two companies agreed to collaborate on developing new products.
14. county /ˈkaʊnti/ n. 县；郡；郡治
📖 英文解释
an area of land in Britain, Ireland or the US that has its own local government
✏️ 例句
The county council voted to increase funding for local schools.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
county council/court 县议会/地方法院；county seat 县城；cross-county 越野的
🧠 助记
county联想country（国家），county比country小，是国家的行政单位
county::/ˈkaʊnti/:n. 县:She grew up in a rural county in the south of England.
15. deteriorate /dɪˈtɪəriəreɪt/ v. 恶化；变坏；退化
📖 英文解释
to become worse
✏️ 例句
His health began to deteriorate rapidly after the accident.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
deteriorate rapidly/gradually 迅速/逐渐恶化；deteriorating conditions 恶化的状况
🧠 助记
deterior（更坏，比较级）+ ate = deteriorate，使变得更坏 = 恶化，联想'deteriorate = get worse'
deteriorate::/dɪˈtɪəriəreɪt/:v. 恶化:Relations between the two countries have deteriorated significantly.
16. equation /ɪˈkweɪʒn/ n. 方程式；等式；均衡
📖 英文解释
a mathematical statement that two things are equal; a situation with several factors
✏️ 例句
Solving a quadratic equation requires understanding several algebraic principles.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
chemical/mathematical equation 化学/数学方程式；enter the equation 进入考虑；equation of motion 运动方程
🧠 助记
equal（相等）→ equation，方程式是两边相等的式子，equate → equation
equation::/ɪˈkweɪʒn/:n. 方程式:The scientist wrote the equation on the board to demonstrate the concept.
17. orange /ˈɒrɪndʒ/ n. 橙子；橙色 adj. 橙色的
📖 英文解释
a round fruit with a thick orange skin and sweet juice; the colour between red and yellow
✏️ 例句
She drank a glass of freshly squeezed orange juice every morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
orange juice 橙汁；orange peel 橘皮；blood orange 血橙；Agent Orange 橙剂（除草剂）
🧠 助记
orange是英语中少有的没有押韵词的单词，颜色和水果同名，很独特
orange::/ˈɒrɪndʒ/:n. 橙子:The setting sun painted the sky a brilliant shade of orange.
18. postpone /pəˈspəʊn/ v. 推迟；延期；搁置
📖 英文解释
to arrange for an event to take place at a later time
✏️ 例句
The outdoor concert was postponed due to heavy rain in the forecast.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
postpone sth until/to 推迟到……；postponement n. 推迟；postpone indefinitely 无限期推迟
🧠 助记
post（之后）+ pone（放置，如component组成部分）= 放到之后 = 推迟，类比postpone departure
postpone::/pəˈspəʊn/:v. 推迟:They decided to postpone the meeting until next week.
19. pronounce /prəˈnaʊns/ v. 发音；宣布；宣称
📖 英文解释
to make the sound of a letter or word; to officially state something
✏️ 例句
The judge pronounced the defendant guilty after a week-long trial.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pronounce sb dead/guilty 宣判某人死亡/有罪；hard to pronounce 难以发音；pronunciation n. 发音
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ nounce（宣布，如announce）= 向前宣告 = 宣布，发音就是'宣告'词汇
pronounce::/prəˈnaʊns/:v. 发音:Many students find it hard to pronounce English words correctly.
20. thorough /ˈθʌrə/ adj. 彻底的；详尽的；周密的
📖 英文解释
done completely with great attention to detail; careful about doing things completely
✏️ 例句
The police conducted a thorough investigation of the crime scene.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
thorough investigation/examination 彻底的调查/检查；thoroughly adv. 彻底地；thoroughness n.
🧠 助记
thorough联想'through'（穿透），彻底完成就是从头到尾'through'，thorough=thorough
thorough::/ˈθʌrə/:adj. 彻底的:She did a thorough check of all the documents before submitting them.
21. contaminate /kənˈtæmɪneɪt/ v. 污染；毒化；使受到影响
📖 英文解释
to make something impure by adding a harmful or undesirable element
✏️ 例句
Industrial waste contaminated the river, making the water unsafe to drink.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
contaminate food/water/soil 污染食物/水/土壤；contamination n. 污染；radioactive contamination 放射性污染
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ tamin（接触）+ ate = 共同接触污染物 = 污染，联想contact（接触）
contaminate::/kənˈtæmɪneɪt/:v. 污染:The chemical spill contaminated the groundwater supply.
22. creep /kriːp/ v./n. 爬行；悄悄移动；蔓延
📖 英文解释
to move slowly and quietly so as not to be noticed; to gradually spread or increase
✏️ 例句
She crept quietly into the room so as not to wake the sleeping baby.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
creep up on 悄悄靠近；make sb's skin creep 让人起鸡皮疙瘩；creep factor 令人不安的因素
🧠 助记
creep联想'crawl'（爬行），creep是轻手轻脚地爬，让人creepy（毛骨悚然）
creep::/kriːp/:v./n. 爬行:Doubts began to creep into her mind about the plan.
23. diabetes /ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz/ n. 糖尿病；尿崩症
📖 英文解释
a medical condition in which the body cannot control the level of sugar in the blood
✏️ 例句
She was diagnosed with type 2 diabetes and had to change her diet significantly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
type 1/2 diabetes 1型/2型糖尿病；diabetic adj./n. 糖尿病的/糖尿病患者
🧠 助记
diabetes来自希腊语'流过'，联想'die'（死）+ 'betes'（贝茨），记为可控但需重视的疾病
diabetes::/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz/:n. 糖尿病:Regular exercise can help prevent and manage type 2 diabetes.
24. drunk /drʌŋk/ adj. 醉酒的；沉醉的 n. 醉汉
📖 英文解释
having drunk too much alcohol; strongly affected by emotion
✏️ 例句
He was too drunk to drive home safely after the party.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
drunk driving 醉驾；dead drunk 烂醉如泥；drunk with power/joy 陶醉于权力/喜悦中
🧠 助记
drink（喝）的过去分词drunk，过度喝了就变成drunk（醉酒的）
drunk::/drʌŋk/:adj. 醉酒的:She was drunk with happiness after receiving the good news.
25. dual /ˈdjuːəl/ adj. 双重的；两部分的；二元的
📖 英文解释
having two parts or aspects
✏️ 例句
She holds dual citizenship in both the UK and Canada.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dual nationality/citizenship 双重国籍；dual role/purpose 双重角色/目的；dual carriageway 双向车道
🧠 助记
dual来自拉丁语duo（二），联想duet（二重奏）、duplex（双层），都是'双'
dual::/ˈdjuːəl/:adj. 双重的:The device serves a dual purpose as both a phone and a camera.
