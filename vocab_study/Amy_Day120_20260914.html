1. empathy /ˈempəθi/ n. 共鸣；同理心；移情
📖 英文解释
the ability to understand and share the feelings of another person
✏️ 例句
Good leaders show empathy towards their team members' struggles.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
empathy vs sympathy（同理心 vs 同情心）；show/have empathy for/with sb；empathize（v.）；考研心理、社会类阅读高频词
🧠 助记
em（进入）+ pathy（感情，参考 sympathy）→ 进入别人的感情世界 = 同理心
empathy::/ˈempəθi/:n. 共鸣:She listened with great empathy to her friend's problems.
2. overtime /ˈəʊvətaɪm/ n./adv. 加班（时间）；超时
📖 英文解释
time worked in addition to normal working hours; beyond the allotted time
✏️ 例句
Employees who work overtime are entitled to extra pay.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
work overtime（加班）；overtime pay（加班费）；go into overtime（进入加时赛）；考研劳动权益类阅读
🧠 助记
over（超过）+ time（时间）→ 超过正常时间 = 加班
overtime::/ˈəʊvətaɪm/:n./adv. 加班（时间）:She worked overtime every day to meet the project deadline.
3. priest /priːst/ n. 牧师；神父；祭司
📖 英文解释
a person ordained to perform religious duties and ceremonies, especially in the Christian Church
✏️ 例句
The priest delivered a moving sermon on compassion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
high priest（大祭司）；priest vs pastor vs minister（不同教派称谓）；priesthood（神职）
🧠 助记
priest 与 pray（祈祷）有联系，是代人祈祷的人
priest::/priːst/:n. 牧师:The priest blessed the couple during the wedding ceremony.
4. tire /taɪə/ n. 轮胎 v. 使疲倦；厌倦
📖 英文解释
a rubber covering fitted around a wheel; to become weary or bored
✏️ 例句
She never tired of exploring new places.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
flat tire（爆胎）；tire of sth（厌倦某事）；tireless（adj. 不知疲倦的）；tired vs tired of（疲倦 vs 厌倦）
🧠 助记
tire 的形状是圆形，轮胎也是圆形；使人疲劳也会让人'转圈'
tire::/taɪə/:n. 轮胎 v. 使疲倦:The car had a flat tire on the highway.
5. disastrous /dɪˈzɑːstrəs/ adj. 灾难性的；极糟的
📖 英文解释
causing great damage or suffering; extremely harmful or unfortunate
✏️ 例句
The disastrous flood destroyed thousands of homes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
disastrous consequences/results/effects；disaster（n.）→ disastrous（adj.）；考研环境、政策类阅读高频
🧠 助记
dis（坏）+ aster（星星）→ 星象不好 = 灾难性的（disaster 源自星相不吉利）
disastrous::/dɪˈzɑːstrəs/:adj. 灾难性的:The economic policy had disastrous consequences for the poor.
6. leather /ˈleðə/ n. 皮革；皮制品
📖 英文解释
a material made from the skin of an animal by tanning; made of leather
✏️ 例句
The leather jacket had been worn for over twenty years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
leather goods（皮革制品）；genuine leather（真皮）；考研消费、环保类议题
🧠 助记
leather = leath（来自古英语：皮）+ er，联想皮革的质感
leather::/ˈleðə/:n. 皮革:The sofa was made of soft Italian leather.
7. multiply /ˈmʌltɪplaɪ/ v. 乘；增加；繁殖
📖 英文解释
to perform the mathematical operation of multiplication; to increase greatly in number
✏️ 例句
Bacteria multiply rapidly in warm, moist conditions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
multiply by（乘以）；multiply 6 by 3 = 18；problems multiply（问题增多）；multiple（adj. 多重的）
🧠 助记
multi（多）+ ply（折叠）→ 折叠多次 = 乘法
multiply::/ˈmʌltɪplaɪ/:v. 乘:If you multiply 9 by 7, you get 63.
8. pigeon /ˈpɪdʒɪn/ n. 鸽子
📖 英文解释
a stout bird with a small head, typically grey and white, often found in cities
✏️ 例句
Pigeons are often used as symbols of peace.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
carrier pigeon（信鸽）；pigeon-hole（v. 将...归类/搁置）；clay pigeon（飞碟靶）
🧠 助记
pigeon 发音类似'皮鸽恩'，联想羽毛像皮的鸽子
pigeon::/ˈpɪdʒɪn/:n. 鸽子:The children fed pigeons in the park every Sunday.
9. poison /ˈpɔɪzn/ n. 毒药 v. 毒害；污染
📖 英文解释
a substance that causes death or harm to a living organism; to harm or kill with poison
✏️ 例句
Industrial waste can poison rivers and harm wildlife.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
poison sb/sth；poisonous（adj. 有毒的）；slow poison（慢性毒药）；考研环保类常见
🧠 助记
poi（破坏）+ son → 破坏生命的东西 = 毒药
poison::/ˈpɔɪzn/:n. 毒药 v. 毒害:The factory's waste poisoned the local water supply.
10. ski /skiː/ n. 滑雪板 v. 滑雪
📖 英文解释
a long narrow piece of equipment attached to a boot for gliding over snow; to travel on skis
✏️ 例句
She learned to ski when she was just five years old.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
go skiing（去滑雪）；ski resort（滑雪度假地）；skiing vs snowboarding（高山滑雪 vs 单板滑雪）
🧠 助记
ski 来自挪威语，联想雪地上划出的痕迹
ski::/skiː/:n. 滑雪板 v. 滑雪:They went to the Alps to ski during the winter holiday.
11. swim /swɪm/ v. 游泳；眩晕 n. 游泳
📖 英文解释
to propel oneself through water using the limbs; to feel dizzy
✏️ 例句
Her head was swimming after reading for hours.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
swim in/across/through（游泳穿越）；swimming pool（游泳池）；sink or swim（要么成功要么失败）
🧠 助记
swim 中间的 wi 像水波纹，联想在水中游动
swim::/swɪm/:v. 游泳:She swims 2 km every morning as part of her fitness routine.
12. utter /ˈʌtə/ v. 说出；发出（声音） adj. 完全的；彻底的
📖 英文解释
to make a sound or say something; complete or absolute
✏️ 例句
She didn't utter a single word during the entire meeting.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
utter a sound/word/cry；utter failure/nonsense（彻底的失败/胡说）；utterance（n. 发言）
🧠 助记
utter 联想 outer（外面）→ 把内心的话说出来（出口）
utter::/ˈʌtə/:v. 说出:He stared at her in utter disbelief.
13. worm /wɜːm/ n. 虫；蠕虫 v. 蠕动前进
📖 英文解释
a slender-bodied limbless invertebrate; to move by crawling
✏️ 例句
Early birds get the worm — the saying encourages waking up early.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
earthworm（蚯蚓）；worm one's way（蠕动/钻进）；computer worm（计算机蠕虫病毒）
🧠 助记
worm 形状弯曲，像它爬行的样子
worm::/wɜːm/:n. 虫:The early bird catches the worm.
14. rotate /rəʊˈteɪt/ v. 旋转；轮流；轮换
📖 英文解释
to turn in a circle around a central point; to do or cause to do things in a regular sequence
✏️ 例句
Crops should be rotated to maintain soil fertility.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rotate crops（轮作）；on a rotating basis（轮流）；rotation（n.）；rotation of staff（员工轮岗）
🧠 助记
rot（轮子根词）+ ate → 像轮子一样旋转
rotate::/rəʊˈteɪt/:v. 旋转:The Earth rotates on its axis once every 24 hours.
15. aggravate /ˈæɡrəveɪt/ v. 加重；恶化；激怒
📖 英文解释
to make a problem, injury, or offense worse; to annoy or exasperate
✏️ 例句
Stress can aggravate many physical health conditions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
aggravate a situation/condition/problem；aggravation（n.）；注意：aggravate 强调使已有问题更严重
🧠 助记
aggra（更重，来自 grave 重）+ vate → 使变得更严重 = 加重
aggravate::/ˈæɡrəveɪt/:v. 加重:Lack of sleep can aggravate the symptoms of anxiety.
16. sentiment /ˈsentɪmənt/ n. 情绪；情感；观点
📖 英文解释
a view or opinion that is held or expressed; tender or romantic feeling
✏️ 例句
The public sentiment against the new tax was overwhelming.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
public sentiment（民众情绪）；anti/pro-sentiment（反对/支持情绪）；sentimental（adj. 感情用事的）
🧠 助记
sent（感受）+ i + ment → 感受到的心情 = 情感
sentiment::/ˈsentɪmənt/:n. 情绪:The survey captured public sentiment about climate change.
17. tube /tjuːb/ n. 管子；地铁（英式）；电子管
📖 英文解释
a hollow cylinder used to hold or convey something; the London Underground railway
✏️ 例句
She took the tube to work every morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
test tube（试管）；tube station（地铁站，英式）；YouTube 中的 tube 指管子（旧指电视管）
🧠 助记
tube 形状就是管子，直接联想
tube::/tjuːb/:n. 管子:The nurse used a tube to draw blood from the patient.
18. loyal /ˈlɔɪəl/ adj. 忠诚的；忠实的
📖 英文解释
giving or showing firm and constant support to a person, institution, or product
✏️ 例句
The company rewarded its most loyal customers with special discounts.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
loyal to sb/sth；loyalty（n.）；brand loyalty（品牌忠诚度）；loyal vs faithful（都表忠诚，faithful 更强调承诺）
🧠 助记
loyal 联想 royal（皇室的）→ 对皇室（主人）忠诚
loyal::/ˈlɔɪəl/:adj. 忠诚的:He remained loyal to his friends even in difficult times.
19. pipe /paɪp/ n. 管子；烟斗；笛子 v. 用管输送
📖 英文解释
a tube used to convey water, gas, or oil; a device for smoking tobacco; a musical wind instrument
✏️ 例句
Natural gas is piped from the fields to factories.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pipe dream（白日梦）；pipeline（管道）；pipe down（安静）；pipe in（通过管道输送）
🧠 助记
pipe 联想烟斗的形状 = 空心管子
pipe::/paɪp/:n. 管子:Water is piped directly from the reservoir to homes.
20. vaccine /ˈvæksiːn/ n. 疫苗
📖 英文解释
a substance injected into the body to stimulate immunity and protect against a disease
✏️ 例句
The COVID-19 vaccine was developed in record time.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
vaccine against（针对...的疫苗）；vaccination（n. 接种疫苗）；vaccinate（v.）；考研医学、公共卫生类必考词
🧠 助记
vaccin 来自拉丁语 vacca（牛）→ 爱德华·詹纳从牛痘发现疫苗
vaccine::/ˈvæksiːn/:n. 疫苗:Scientists are working on a vaccine for the new virus.
21. canal /kəˈnæl/ n. 运河；管道
📖 英文解释
an artificial waterway constructed for navigation or irrigation; a tubular duct in the body
✏️ 例句
The Panama Canal connects the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
canal vs channel（运河 vs 海峡/频道）；ear canal（耳道）；irrigation canal（灌溉渠）
🧠 助记
can（罐子）+ al → 像长形罐子的水道 = 运河
canal::/kəˈnæl/:n. 运河:The city's old canals now serve as tourist attractions.
22. conception /kənˈsepʃn/ n. 概念；构想；受孕
📖 英文解释
the way in which something is perceived or regarded; the formation of a plan or idea; the fertilization of an egg
✏️ 例句
His conception of justice differed greatly from the judge's.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
conceive（v.）→ conception（n.）；from conception to completion（从构思到完成）；有时与 concept 互换
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ cept（抓住）+ ion → 大家共同抓住的想法 = 概念
conception::/kənˈsepʃn/:n. 概念:The project was innovative from its very conception.
23. distort /dɪˈstɔːt/ v. 扭曲；歪曲；失真
📖 英文解释
to pull or twist out of shape; to misrepresent the truth
✏️ 例句
The media was accused of distorting the facts.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
distort the truth/facts/image；distortion（n.）；考研媒体、政治类阅读常见
🧠 助记
dis（偏离）+ tort（扭，参考 torture）→ 偏离原形 = 扭曲
distort::/dɪˈstɔːt/:v. 扭曲:Politicians often distort statistics to support their arguments.
24. gravity /ˈɡrævɪti/ n. 重力；严重性；严肃
📖 英文解释
the force that attracts objects towards the earth; extreme importance; seriousness
✏️ 例句
The judge spoke about the gravity of the crime.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
law of gravity（万有引力定律）；gravity of the situation（形势的严重性）；grave（adj. 严重的）同源
🧠 助记
grave（严肃的/墓地）→ gravity 是严重的力量，也是将人拉向地面（墓地）的力
gravity::/ˈɡrævɪti/:n. 重力:The report highlighted the gravity of the environmental crisis.
25. imaginative /ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪv/ adj. 富于想象力的；创新的
📖 英文解释
having or showing creativity or inventiveness
✏️ 例句
The architect came up with an imaginative design for the new library.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
imaginative solution/approach/thinking；imagination（n.）；imaginative vs imaginary（富于想象力 vs 虚构的）
🧠 助记
imagine（想象）+ ative → 善于想象的 = 富于想象力的
imaginative::/ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪv/:adj. 富于想象力的:She was an imaginative teacher who made lessons fun.
