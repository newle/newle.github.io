1. municipal /mjuːˈnɪsɪpl/ adj. 市的；市政的；地方自治的
📖 英文解释
relating to a town or district or its governing body
✏️ 例句
Municipal elections will be held next spring.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
municipal government（市政府）；municipal services（市政服务）；municipality（n. 市政当局）
🧠 助记
muni（拉丁：义务/公共）+ cipal（主要的）→ 承担公共义务的主要机构 = 市政的
municipal::/mjuːˈnɪsɪpl/:adj. 市的:The municipal library offers free access to all residents.
2. plausible /ˈplɔːzɪbl/ adj. 似乎合理的；貌似可信的
📖 英文解释
seeming reasonable or probable; appearing valid or acceptable on the surface
✏️ 例句
He offered a plausible explanation for his absence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
plausible excuse/argument/theory；plausibly（adv.）；注意：plausible 不一定真实，只是听起来合理
🧠 助记
plaus（掌声，来自 applause）+ ible → 值得鼓掌的 = 听起来可信的
plausible::/ˈplɔːzɪbl/:adj. 似乎合理的:The scientist presented a plausible hypothesis about climate change.
3. sociology /ˌsəʊsiˈɒlədʒi/ n. 社会学
📖 英文解释
the study of the development, structure, and functioning of human society
✏️ 例句
She majored in sociology and focused on urban poverty.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sociologist（n. 社会学家）；sociological（adj.）；sociology of education/crime（教育/犯罪社会学）
🧠 助记
socio（社会）+ logy（学问）→ 研究社会的学问 = 社会学
sociology::/ˌsəʊsiˈɒlədʒi/:n. 社会学:Sociology helps us understand patterns of inequality in society.
4. crawl /krɔːl/ v. 爬行；缓慢移动 n. 爬泳
📖 英文解释
to move on hands and knees; to move slowly; a swimming stroke
✏️ 例句
Traffic was crawling along the highway during rush hour.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
crawl with（爬满）；at a crawl（缓慢地）；web crawler（网络爬虫）；the front crawl（自由泳）
🧠 助记
crawl 的 cr 发音像爬行摩擦地面的声音
crawl::/krɔːl/:v. 爬行:The baby began to crawl at the age of eight months.
5. exit /ˈeksɪt/ n. 出口；退场 v. 退出；离开
📖 英文解释
a way out of a building or place; the action of going out; to go out of
✏️ 例句
Please locate the nearest emergency exit upon entering the building.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
exit strategy（退出策略）；exit poll（出口民调）；make one's exit（退场）；Brexit = Britain + exit
🧠 助记
ex（出）+ it（它）→ 走出去 = 出口/退出
exit::/ˈeksɪt/:n. 出口:The company's exit from the market surprised many investors.
6. masculine /ˈmæskjulɪn/ adj. 阳刚的；男性的；阳性的（语法）
📖 英文解释
having qualities traditionally associated with men; relating to the male sex; of masculine gender
✏️ 例句
Society often pressures men to conform to masculine stereotypes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
masculine vs feminine（阳性 vs 阴性）；masculine gender（阳性，语法）；masculinity（n.）
🧠 助记
mascul（来自 masculus 男性）+ ine → 男性特质的
masculine::/ˈmæskjulɪn/:adj. 阳刚的:The study examined how masculine ideals affect men's mental health.
7. restrain /rɪˈstreɪn/ v. 抑制；约束；阻止
📖 英文解释
to prevent someone or something from doing something; to keep under control
✏️ 例句
She restrained herself from making a sarcastic comment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
restrain oneself；restrained（adj. 克制的）；restraint（n. 克制；约束）；考研心理、法律类阅读
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ strain（拉紧）→ 再次拉紧 = 约束、抑制
restrain::/rɪˈstreɪn/:v. 抑制:The police had to physically restrain the angry crowd.
8. weird /wɪəd/ adj. 怪异的；奇特的；不可思议的
📖 英文解释
suggesting something supernatural; very strange; inducing a sense of disbelief
✏️ 例句
She had a weird dream about flying over mountains.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
weird experience/feeling/coincidence；weirdo（n. 怪人）；考研阅读中偶见于描述异常现象
🧠 助记
weird 发音像'为的'→ 为什么会这样的？太怪了！
weird::/wɪəd/:adj. 怪异的:It was a weird coincidence that they were born on the same day.
9. advisable /ədˈvaɪzəbl/ adj. 明智的；合适的；可取的
📖 英文解释
to be recommended; sensible or wise under the circumstances
✏️ 例句
It is advisable to arrive early for the interview.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
it is advisable to do sth；advisable to/for；advise（v.）→ advisable（adj.）；与 desirable/reasonable 同类
🧠 助记
advise（建议）+ able → 值得建议的 = 明智可取的
advisable::/ədˈvaɪzəbl/:adj. 明智的:It is advisable to save your work frequently to avoid losing data.
10. avenue /ˈævənjuː/ n. 大街；林荫大道；途径
📖 英文解释
a wide road; a way of approaching something

🧠 助记
a + venue（场所）→ 通往场所的道路 = 大街/途径
avenue::/ˈævənjuː/:n. 大街:The team explored every avenue to solve the financial problem.
11. barrel /ˈbærəl/ n. 桶；枪管；大量
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
barrel 联想 barrel（啤酒桶）→ 大圆桶
barrel::/ˈbærəl/:n. 桶:The winery stores its wine in oak barrels for aging.
12. cabin /ˈkæbɪn/ n. 小木屋；机舱；船舱
📖 英文解释
a small shelter or house made of wood; the passenger compartment of an aircraft
✏️ 例句
The crew asked passengers to return to their cabins.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
cab（出租车）+ in → 像出租车一样的小空间 = 机舱/小屋
cabin::/ˈkæbɪn/:n. 小木屋:They stayed in a cozy wooden cabin in the mountains.
13. conceal /kənˈsiːl/ v. 隐藏；隐瞒；掩盖
📖 英文解释
to keep from sight; to not let people know about something
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
conceal sth from sb；concealment（n.）；concealed weapon（隐藏武器）；近义词：hide, disguise
🧠 助记
con（完全）+ ceal（遮盖，参考 ceiling 天花板）→ 完全遮住 = 隐藏
conceal::/kənˈsiːl/:v. 隐藏:He concealed the truth from his parents for months.
14. eleven /ɪˈlevn/ num. 十一；（足球等）队

eleven::/ɪˈlevn/:num. 十一:The meeting starts at eleven o'clock sharp.

formula::/ˈfɔːmjulə/:n. 公式:Scientists have not yet found a formula to cure the disease.

eligible::/ˈelɪdʒɪbl/:adj. 合格的:She was eligible for a scholarship based on her grades.
18. immense /ɪˈmens/ adj. 巨大的；极大的
