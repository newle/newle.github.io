1. charity /ˈtʃærɪti/ n. 慈善；善意；慈善机构
📖 英文解释
the voluntary giving of help to those in need; an organization set up to provide help; kindness and tolerance
✏️ 例句
She donated a large portion of her salary to charity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
charity work（慈善工作）；do charity（做善事）；charitable（adj.）；charity begins at home（慈善从家庭开始）
🧠 助记
char（亲爱的，来自 cher）+ ity → 对亲爱之人的爱 = 慈善/善意
charity::/ˈtʃærɪti/:n. 慈善:The organization raises millions each year for local charities.
2. clarify /ˈklærɪfaɪ/ v. 澄清；阐明；使清晰
📖 英文解释
to make a statement or situation less confused and more comprehensible
✏️ 例句
Could you clarify what you mean by 'sustainable growth'?
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
clarify a situation/point；clarification（n.）；seek clarification（寻求澄清）；考研写作、阅读常见
🧠 助记
clar（清晰，参考 clear）+ ify → 使清晰 = 澄清
clarify::/ˈklærɪfaɪ/:v. 澄清:The professor clarified the concept with several examples.
3. defy /dɪˈfaɪ/ v. 反抗；藐视；挑战
📖 英文解释
to openly resist or refuse to obey; to be impossible to explain or describe
✏️ 例句
The new technology defies conventional scientific thinking.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
defy authority/logic/description；defiance（n. 蔑视）；defiant（adj. 反抗的）
🧠 助记
de（去除）+ fy（信念 faith）→ 去除对权威的信念 = 反抗
defy::/dɪˈfaɪ/:v. 反抗:The young scientist defied conventional wisdom with her research.
4. gratitude /ˈɡrætɪtjuːd/ n. 感激；感谢
📖 英文解释
the quality of being thankful; a feeling of warm appreciation
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
express gratitude（表达感激）；in gratitude for（感谢...而）；grateful（adj.）；with gratitude（怀着感激）
🧠 助记
grat（感激，参考 grateful）+ itude → 感激的状态
gratitude::/ˈɡrætɪtjuːd/:n. 感激:He wrote a letter of gratitude to his former teacher.
5. morality /məˈræləti/ n. 道德；道德观；道义
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
morality vs ethics（道德观 vs 伦理学）；moral（adj.）→ morality（n.）；morality play（道德剧）
🧠 助记
moral（道德的）+ ity → 道德的品质 = 道德性
morality::/məˈræləti/:n. 道德:Society's sense of morality has shifted significantly in recent decades.

paradise::/ˈpærədaɪs/:n. 天堂:The resort is a paradise for those seeking peace and relaxation.

8. revenge /rɪˈvendʒ/ n./v. 报复；复仇

🧠 助记

revenge::/rɪˈvendʒ/:n./v. 报复:Seeking revenge rarely brings the satisfaction people expect.
