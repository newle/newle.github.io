1. chest /tʃest/ n. 胸腔；箱子；v. 用胸部阻挡
📖 英文解释
the front part of the body between the neck and abdomen; a large strong box
✏️ 例句
The doctor examined the patient's chest for signs of respiratory infection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
chest of drawers(五斗柜); chest pain(胸痛); treasure chest(宝箱); get sth off one's chest(倾诉心事)
🧠 助记
chest->胸口;大箱子->胸腔;箱子
chest::/tʃest/:n. 胸腔:He finally got it off his chest by telling his manager about the ethical concerns.

📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

foolish::/ˈfuːlɪʃ/:adj. 愚蠢的:It would be foolish to underestimate the challenges that lie ahead.
3. frustrate /frʌˈstreɪt/ v. 使挫败；使失望；阻挠
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

frustrate::/frʌˈstreɪt/:v. 使挫败:Bureaucratic obstacles continued to frustrate the company's expansion plans.
