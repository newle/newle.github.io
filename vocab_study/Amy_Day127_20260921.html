1. crew /kruː/ n. 全体船员；机组人员；团队 v. 担任船员
📖 英文解释
a group of people who work on a ship, aircraft, or train; a team working together
✏️ 例句
The crew of the aircraft welcomed passengers aboard.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
crew member（船员/机组人员）；film crew（摄制组）；crew cut（平头发型）；skeleton crew（最少人员）
🧠 助记
crew 联想 'group who row'（一组划船的人）→ 全体船员
crew::/kruː/:n. 全体船员:The rescue crew worked through the night to reach survivors.
2. envy /ˈenvi/ n./v. 嫉妒；羡慕
📖 英文解释
the feeling of wanting to have what someone else has; to feel envious of someone
✏️ 例句
She couldn't hide her envy when she saw his new car.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
green with envy（非常嫉妒）；envy sb sth（羡慕某人某事）；the envy of（令…嫉妒的）；envious adj.
🧠 助记
en-（使）+ vy（via 通过别人获得）→ 想通过别人获得的感情 → 嫉妒
envy::/ˈenvi/:n./v. 嫉妒:His success was the envy of all his colleagues.
3. illuminate /ɪˈluːmɪneɪt/ v. 照明；阐明；启发；装饰
📖 英文解释
to light something up; to make something clearer and easier to understand
✏️ 例句
Spotlights illuminated the stage during the performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
illuminate a problem（阐明问题）；illuminating adj.（有启发性的）；illumination n.；illuminated manuscript（彩饰手稿）
🧠 助记
il-（向）+ lumin（光，如 luminous）+ ate → 使有光 → 照明；阐明
illuminate::/ɪˈluːmɪneɪt/:v. 照明:The example helped to illuminate the complex concept.
4. metric /ˈmetrɪk/ n. 度量；指标 adj. 公制的；度量的
📖 英文解释
a system or standard of measurement; relating to the metric system; a measure used to assess performance
✏️ 例句
Customer satisfaction is a key metric for evaluating service quality.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
metric system（公制）；performance metric（绩效指标）；go metric（改用公制）；metrics n. 指标体系
🧠 助记
meter（米/测量）→ metric → 测量的标准 → 度量；指标
metric::/ˈmetrɪk/:n. 度量:The team uses several metrics to track project progress.
5. molecule /ˈmɒlɪkjuːl/ n. 分子；微小粒子
📖 英文解释
the smallest unit of a substance that has all the properties of that substance, consisting of atoms bonded together
✏️ 例句
A water molecule consists of two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
water/oxygen molecule；molecular adj.（分子的）；molecule structure（分子结构）
🧠 助记
mole-（小堆/摩尔）+ cule（小）→ 小小的基本粒子 → 分子
molecule::/ˈmɒlɪkjuːl/:n. 分子:Scientists studied the molecule's structure to develop the drug.
6. noisy /ˈnɔɪzi/ adj. 嘈杂的；吵闹的；喧闹的
📖 英文解释
making or characterized by a lot of noise; full of noise
✏️ 例句
The construction site next door is extremely noisy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
noisy crowd/street/neighbor；noisily adv.；noise n.；noisiness n.
🧠 助记
noise（噪音）+ y → 充满噪音的 → 嘈杂的
noisy::/ˈnɔɪzi/:adj. 嘈杂的:It was too noisy in the café to have a proper conversation.
7. particle /ˈpɑːrtɪkəl/ n. 粒子；微粒；少量；小品词
📖 英文解释
a very small piece of matter; in grammar, a short word used with a verb; a tiny amount
✏️ 例句
Dust particles in the air can trigger allergies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
subatomic particle（亚原子粒子）；particle physics（粒子物理）；not a particle of（没有一点）
🧠 助记
part-（部分）+ icle（小）→ 非常小的部分 → 粒子；微粒
particle::/ˈpɑːrtɪkəl/:n. 粒子:The laboratory studies the behavior of elementary particles.
8. residence /ˈrezɪdəns/ n. 住宅；居住；居留期
📖 英文解释
a person's home; the fact of living in a place; the period of living somewhere
✏️ 例句
The president's official residence is heavily guarded.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in residence（居住中/驻场的）；take up residence（定居）；residence permit（居留许可）；resident n.
🧠 助记
reside（居住）+ nce → 居住的地方/状态 → 住宅；居住
residence::/ˈrezɪdəns/:n. 住宅:She applied for a residence permit after marrying a local.
9. stain /steɪn/ n./v. 污点；污渍；着色；玷污
📖 英文解释
a mark left by a substance on something; to leave such a mark; to colour wood or other material; a moral blemish
✏️ 例句
She could not remove the coffee stain from her white shirt.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stain on sb's reputation（名誉污点）；stain remover（去污剂）；stained glass（彩色玻璃）；wood stain（木材着色剂）
🧠 助记
stain 联想 stay + in（留在里面的痕迹）→ 留在物体里的污点
stain::/steɪn/:n./v. 污点:The scandal left a permanent stain on his reputation.
10. tangible /ˈtændʒɪbəl/ adj. 有形的；可触摸的；真实的；切实的
📖 英文解释
perceptible by touch; clear and definite; real
✏️ 例句
We need tangible evidence to support the claim.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tangible results/benefits/assets；tangible proof；intangible（无形的）；tangibly adv.
🧠 助记
tang-（触摸，如 contact）+ ible → 可以触摸到的 → 有形的；切实的
tangible::/ˈtændʒɪbəl/:adj. 有形的:The policy produced few tangible improvements for ordinary people.
11. chaos /ˈkeɪɒs/ n. 混乱；无秩序；混沌
📖 英文解释
complete disorder and confusion; a state without any order or organization
✏️ 例句
The sudden power cut threw the office into chaos.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in chaos（处于混乱中）；cause/create chaos；utter/complete chaos；chaotic adj.；chaos theory（混沌理论）
🧠 助记
chaos 发音似'开炸'→ 像爆炸后一片混乱 → 混乱
chaos::/ˈkeɪɒs/:n. 混乱:The city descended into chaos after the election results were announced.
12. hardware /ˈhɑːrdwer/ n. 五金器具；硬件；武器

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
hard（硬）+ ware（器具）→ 硬的器具 → 五金；硬件
hardware::/ˈhɑːrdwer/:n. 五金器具:The company invested heavily in upgrading its hardware infrastructure.
13. harness /ˈhɑːrnɪs/ v. 利用；驾驭 n. 马具；保护带；线束
📖 英文解释
to control and use the force or strength of something; the straps for controlling a horse or securing a person
✏️ 例句
Solar panels harness energy from sunlight.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
harness energy/power/potential（利用能源/力量/潜力）；in harness（一起工作）；harness technology
🧠 助记
har-（马）+ ness → 驾驭马的工具 → 马具；引申为利用/控制
harness::/ˈhɑːrnɪs/:v. 利用:We must harness the power of technology to solve social problems.
14. legend /ˈledʒənd/ n. 传说；传奇人物；图例
📖 英文解释
a traditional story from long ago that may not be entirely true; a very famous person; an explanation of symbols on a map
✏️ 例句
According to legend, King Arthur pulled the sword from the stone.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
local legend（当地传说）；become a legend（成为传奇）；legend has it that（传说）；legendary adj.
🧠 助记
legen-（来自 legere，读/讲）+ d → 被人口耳相传讲述的 → 传说
legend::/ˈledʒənd/:n. 传说:She became a legend in the world of tennis.
15. premise /ˈpremɪs/ n. 前提；假设 (premises: 房屋/场所)
📖 英文解释
a statement or idea that forms the basis for an argument; (premises) a building and the land it stands on
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
on the premise that（以…为前提）；false/underlying premise；premises n. 场所（on the premises）
🧠 助记
pre-（在前）+ mise（放置）→ 放在前面的基础 → 前提
premise::/ˈpremɪs/:n. 前提:The plan rests on the premise that funding will be available.
16. refusal /rɪˈfjuːzəl/ n. 拒绝；拒绝权

flat refusal（断然拒绝）；refusal to cooperate；first refusal（优先购买权）；refuse v.
🧠 助记

refusal::/rɪˈfjuːzəl/:n. 拒绝:Her refusal to compromise ended the negotiations.
17. technician /tekˈnɪʃən/ n. 技术员；技师；专业人员
📖 英文解释
a person employed to look after technical equipment or do practical work in a laboratory; a person skilled in a particular technique
✏️ 例句
The lab technician prepared the samples for analysis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lab/dental/medical technician；IT technician；technical adj.；technique n.
🧠 助记
techni-（技术）+ cian（从业者，如 musician）→ 技术从业者 → 技术员
technician::/tekˈnɪʃən/:n. 技术员:A qualified technician was called to repair the equipment.
18. toss /tɒs/ v./n. 投掷；辗转；抛（硬币）；拌（沙拉）
📖 英文解释
to throw something lightly; to move from side to side; to flip a coin to decide something
✏️ 例句
They tossed a coin to decide who would go first.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
toss and turn（辗转反侧）；toss a coin（抛硬币）；toss salad（拌沙拉）；argue the toss（争论已决定的事）
🧠 助记
toss 发音像'扔'的动作 → 轻抛；辗转
toss::/tɒs/:v./n. 投掷:She tossed and turned all night, unable to sleep.
19. turnover /ˈtɜːrnoʊvər/ n. 营业额；人员流动率；翻转
📖 英文解释
the amount of money a business receives in a particular period; the rate at which employees leave and are replaced
✏️ 例句
The company reported a turnover of £5 million last year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
annual turnover（年营业额）；staff turnover（员工流动率）；high/low turnover；turnover rate
🧠 助记
turn（翻转）+ over（结束）→ 一轮结束带来的总量 → 营业额；人员更替
turnover::/ˈtɜːrnoʊvər/:n. 营业额:High staff turnover is costly for any organization.

ally::/ˈælaɪ/ (n.) /əˈlaɪ/ (v.):n. 伙伴:She found an unexpected ally in her new colleague.

cruise::/kruːz/:v. 巡航:They cruised down the river, enjoying the scenery.

deputy::/ˈdepjuti/:n. 副手:The deputy was left in charge while the CEO traveled abroad.

diary::/ˈdaɪəri/:n. 日记:His diary reveals a complex inner emotional life.

faint 联想 paint → 颜色很淡 → 微弱的；也联想晕倒时虚弱感
faint::/feɪnt/:adj. 微弱的:She felt faint after standing in the sun for too long.
