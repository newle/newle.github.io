1. idle /ˈaɪdəl/ adj. 无目的的；懒散的；闲置的 v. 空转；虚度
📖 英文解释
not working or being used; avoiding work; having no purpose or effect
✏️ 例句
The machines lay idle for months during the strike.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
idle away time（虚度时光）；idle gossip（无聊八卦）；idle threat（空洞威胁）；idleness n.
🧠 助记
idle 发音像'闲着呢'→ 什么都不做 → 懒散的；闲置的
idle::/ˈaɪdəl/:adj. 无目的的:He spent the afternoon in idle speculation rather than productive work.
2. lorry /ˈlɒri/ n. 货车；卡车（英式）
📖 英文解释
a large motor vehicle for transporting goods (British English for truck)
✏️ 例句
A lorry carrying cement overturned on the motorway.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lorry driver（货车司机）；articulated lorry（铰接式卡车）；flatbed lorry（平板货车）；lorry load（一卡车的量）
🧠 助记
lorry 英式英语，对应美式的 truck → 货车
lorry::/ˈlɒri/:n. 货车:The lorry was too wide to pass through the narrow village road.
3. nut /nʌt/ n. 螺母；坚果；坚硬的人/问题 adj. 疯狂的
📖 英文解释
a small flat piece of metal with a hole for a bolt; a hard-shelled fruit; (informal) a crazy person
✏️ 例句
She tightened the nut on the bicycle wheel.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nut and bolt（螺母和螺栓）；go nuts（发疯）；nut allergy（坚果过敏）；a hard nut to crack（难以解决的问题）
🧠 助记
nut 外硬内有内核 → 螺母（硬）；坚果（硬壳有核）
nut::/nʌt/:n. 螺母:The problem turned out to be a tough nut to crack.
4. oven /ˈʌvən/ n. 烤箱；炉子；烤炉
📖 英文解释
an enclosed compartment for cooking food by dry heat
✏️ 例句
She preheated the oven to 180 degrees before baking the cake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
oven temperature；microwave oven（微波炉）；Dutch oven（铸铁锅）；like an oven（非常热）；oven gloves
🧠 助记
oven 联想 'over heat'（加热）→ 烤箱
oven::/ˈʌvən/:n. 烤箱:The bread needs to bake in the oven for thirty minutes.
5. overnight /ˌoʊvərˈnaɪt/ adv./adj. 过夜；一夜之间；突然
📖 英文解释
during or for the night; suddenly or very quickly
✏️ 例句
She became an overnight sensation after her debut album was released.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stay overnight（过夜）；overnight bag（过夜行李）；overnight success（一夜成功）；overnight shift（夜班）
🧠 助记
over（越过）+ night（夜晚）→ 越过一夜 → 过夜；一夜之间
overnight::/ˌoʊvərˈnaɪt/:adv./adj. 过夜:The storm caused overnight flooding in several low-lying areas.
6. skyscraper /ˈskaɪskreɪpər/ n. 摩天大楼；超高层建筑
📖 英文解释
a very tall building with many floors
✏️ 例句
The skyscraper dominated the city skyline.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
build a skyscraper；skyscraper city；tower/skyscraper district
🧠 助记
sky（天空）+ scraper（刮）→ 高到能刮到天空的 → 摩天大楼
skyscraper::/ˈskaɪskreɪpər/:n. 摩天大楼:The new skyscraper will be the tallest building in the region.
7. summit /ˈsʌmɪt/ n. 顶点；山顶；峰会 adj. 最高级别的
📖 英文解释
the highest point of a mountain; the highest level of achievement; a formal meeting between heads of government
✏️ 例句
World leaders gathered for the G20 summit in Rome.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reach the summit（到达顶峰）；summit meeting（峰会）；G7/NATO summit；at the summit of（在…顶峰）
🧠 助记
sum-（最高）+ mit（点）→ 最高点 → 顶点；峰会
summit::/ˈsʌmɪt/:n. 顶点:She reached the summit of her career when she became CEO.
8. lens /lenz/ n. 镜头；镜片；晶状体
📖 英文解释
a piece of glass or other transparent material that focuses or disperses light rays; the part of an eye that focuses light
✏️ 例句
The camera lens was cracked during the fall.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
contact lens（隐形眼镜）；camera lens；zoom lens（变焦镜头）；through the lens of（通过…视角）
🧠 助记
lens 来自拉丁语 lens（扁豆），因镜片形状像扁豆 → 镜片；镜头
lens::/lenz/:n. 镜头:He examined the data through the lens of social inequality.
9. porter /ˈpɔːrtər/ n. 搬运工；行李员；守门人
📖 英文解释
a person employed to carry luggage or goods; a person in charge of a building entrance
✏️ 例句
The porter helped carry our bags to the hotel room.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hotel porter（门房/行李员）；station porter（站台行李员）；porter service；doorman = porter
🧠 助记
port-（搬运，如 transport 运输）+ er → 专门搬运的人 → 搬运工
porter::/ˈpɔːrtər/:n. 搬运工:The airport porter loaded the heavy suitcases onto the trolley.
10. practise /ˈpræktɪs/ v. 练习；实践；从事（专业）
📖 英文解释
to do something repeatedly to improve skill; to carry out an activity; to work in a profession
✏️ 例句
She practises the piano for two hours every day.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
practise medicine/law（行医/执业律师）；practise what one preaches（言行一致）；practice n.（英式：practice 名词，practise 动词）
🧠 助记
pract-（实践，如 practical 实践的）+ ice → 付诸实践 → 练习
practise::/ˈpræktɪs/:v. 练习:You need to practise regularly to become fluent in a language.
11. swell /swel/ v. 膨胀；肿胀；涌现 n. 涌浪；隆起 adj. 极好的（口语）
📖 英文解释
to become larger in size; to increase in amount or intensity; a slow wave in the sea
✏️ 例句
His ankle swelled up after he twisted it during the match.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
swell with pride/anger（充满自豪/愤怒）；swollen adj.（肿胀的）；swell the ranks（壮大队伍）；ocean swell
🧠 助记
swell 联想 'swelling'（肿胀）→ 膨胀；肿；涌
swell::/swel/:v. 膨胀:The river swelled dangerously after days of heavy rain.
12. sword /sɔːrd/ n. 剑；武力；军权
📖 英文解释
a weapon with a long metal blade and a handle; military power
✏️ 例句
Knights were skilled in fighting with a sword and shield.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
put to the sword（用剑杀死）；the sword of justice（正义之剑）；swordsman n.（剑客）；double-edged sword（双刃剑）
🧠 助记
sword 注意 w 不发音，联想 'swear'（发誓）→ 骑士拿剑发誓 → 剑
sword::/sɔːrd/:n. 剑:The pen is mightier than the sword.
13. vacant /ˈveɪkənt/ adj. 空的；空缺的；茫然的
📖 英文解释
not occupied; available; (of a look) showing no thought or intelligence
✏️ 例句
There are several vacant positions in the accounting department.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
vacant seat/post/room；vacant stare（茫然的眼神）；vacancy n.（空缺）；vacate v.（腾出）
🧠 助记
vac-（空，如 vacuum 真空）+ ant → 空的；空缺的
vacant::/ˈveɪkənt/:adj. 空的:The apartment has been vacant for six months.
14. violin /ˌvaɪəˈlɪn/ n. 小提琴
📖 英文解释
a stringed musical instrument played with a bow, held under the chin
✏️ 例句
She has been playing violin since she was five years old.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
play the violin；violin concerto（小提琴协奏曲）；violinist n.（小提琴手）；first/second violin（第一/第二小提琴手）
🧠 助记
viol-（中世纪弦乐器 viol）+ in → 小型弦乐器 → 小提琴
violin::/ˌvaɪəˈlɪn/:n. 小提琴:The violinist performed a breathtaking solo in the concert.
15. weep /wiːp/ v./n. 哭泣；流泪；渗出
📖 英文解释
to shed tears; to exude liquid; to express grief
✏️ 例句
She wept with joy when she heard the good news.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
weep for/over（为…哭泣）；weep with joy（喜极而泣）；weeping willow（垂柳）；weep bitterly（痛哭）
🧠 助记
weep 发音像'呜噗'→ 哭泣的声音 → 哭泣
weep::/wiːp/:v./n. 哭泣:He wept openly at his father's funeral.
16. courtesy /ˈkɜːrtəsi/ n. 礼貌；礼节；好意
📖 英文解释
polite behavior that shows respect for other people; a polite gesture or remark
✏️ 例句
He had the courtesy to call and apologize.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
out of courtesy（出于礼貌）；courtesy of（承蒙…提供）；courteous adj.（有礼貌的）；by courtesy of（由…提供）
🧠 助记
court-（宫廷）+ esy → 宫廷里的礼仪 → 礼貌；礼节
courtesy::/ˈkɜːrtəsi/:n. 礼貌:Please have the courtesy to arrive on time.
17. doom /duːm/ n. 厄运；死亡；灭亡 v. 注定；使注定失败
📖 英文解释
death, destruction, or some terrible fate; to make certain the failure or destruction of
✏️ 例句
The project was doomed from the start due to lack of funding.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
doom and gloom（悲观绝望）；sense of doom（末日感）；be doomed to fail（注定失败）；doomsday n.（末日）
🧠 助记
doom 发音像'嗡嗡'末日警报声 → 厄运；注定失败
doom::/duːm/:n. 厄运:A lack of investment doomed the project to failure.
18. inhabit /ɪnˈhæbɪt/ v. 栖息；居住于；占据
📖 英文解释
to live in or occupy a particular place
✏️ 例句
Thousands of species inhabit the Amazon rainforest.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
inhabit a region/area；inhabited island（有人居住的岛屿）；uninhabited（无人居住的）；inhabitant n.
🧠 助记
in-（在里面）+ habit（习惯）→ 习惯于在某处居住 → 栖息；居住
inhabit::/ɪnˈhæbɪt/:v. 栖息:The remote island is inhabited by only a few hundred people.
19. preferable /ˈprefrəbəl/ adj. 更好的；更合适的；更值得选择的
📖 英文解释
more desirable or suitable than another option
✏️ 例句
A peaceful solution is preferable to armed conflict.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
preferable to（优于）；far/much preferable；preferably adv.（最好是）；prefer v.
🧠 助记
prefer（更喜欢）+ able → 值得被更喜欢的 → 更好的；更合适的
preferable::/ˈprefrəbəl/:adj. 更好的:Working from home is preferable to commuting for many employees.
20. sketch /sketʃ/ n./v. 素描；草图；梗概；小品
📖 英文解释
a rough drawing made quickly; a brief written or spoken account; a short comic piece; to make a quick drawing
✏️ 例句
She sketched the mountain landscape in a few swift strokes.

sketch a portrait/plan；rough sketch（粗略草图）；sketch out（概述）；character sketch（人物素描）

sketch 联想 scratch（涂写）→ 快速涂画 → 素描；草图
sketch::/sketʃ/:n./v. 素描:The architect showed a rough sketch of the proposed building.
21. bang /bæŋ/ n./v. 巨响；猛撞；刘海 adv. 正好
📖 英文解释
a sudden loud noise; to hit something with great force; hair hanging over the forehead

The door shut with a loud bang.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
bang on（正好）；big bang（大爆炸）；with a bang（大成功地）；bang one's head（撞头）；bangs n. 刘海
🧠 助记
bang 模仿撞击声 → 巨响；猛撞
bang::/bæŋ/:n./v. 巨响:The deal went off with a bang, exceeding all expectations.
22. chamber /ˈtʃeɪmbər/ n. 室；腔；议院；商会
📖 英文解释
a room used for a special purpose; a hollow enclosed space; either of the two parts of a parliament
✏️ 例句
The lower chamber of parliament voted in favour of the bill.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
chamber of commerce（商会）；gas chamber（毒气室）；chamber music（室内乐）；upper/lower chamber
🧠 助记
chamber 联想 cave（洞穴/室）→ 封闭的空间 → 室；议院
chamber::/ˈtʃeɪmbər/:n. 室:The Senate is the upper chamber of the US Congress.
23. enlighten /ɪnˈlaɪtən/ v. 启发；开导；使了解
📖 英文解释
to give greater knowledge or understanding to someone; to inform someone about something
✏️ 例句
The documentary enlightened viewers about the realities of climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
enlighten sb about/on（向某人说明）；enlightened adj.（开明的）；enlightenment n.（启蒙）；the Enlightenment（启蒙运动）
🧠 助记
en-（使）+ lighten（变亮）→ 使内心变得明亮 → 启发；启蒙
enlighten::/ɪnˈlaɪtən/:v. 启发:The teacher enlightened her students on the history of civil rights.
24. mixture /ˈmɪkstʃər/ n. 混合；混合物；混合状态
📖 英文解释
a combination of different elements; a substance made by mixing other substances together
✏️ 例句
The cake batter is a mixture of flour, eggs, butter, and sugar.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
mixture of（…的混合）；mix v.；a mixture of emotions（复杂的情绪）；mixture n. 混合物 vs. compound（化合物）
🧠 助记
mix（混合）+ ture → 混合在一起的东西 → 混合物
mixture::/ˈmɪkstʃər/:n. 混合:She felt a mixture of excitement and nervousness before the performance.
25. pan /pæn/ n. 平底锅；盆 v. 摇摄；批评；淘（金）
📖 英文解释
a metal container used for cooking; to move a camera horizontally; to criticize strongly; to wash gravel for gold

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
frying pan（煎锅）；pan for gold（淘金）；pan a film（猛烈批评）；pan out（结果如何）；pan shot（摇镜头）
🧠 助记
pan 联想 frying pan（煎锅）→ 平底锅；摇镜头像锅左右摇
pan::/pæn/:n. 平底锅:The critics panned the film for its weak storyline.
