1. pit /pɪt/ n. 坑；陷阱；矿坑 v. 使对抗
📖 英文解释
a large hole in the ground; a mine; to mark with small scars; to set in opposition
✏️ 例句
The pit of the stomach refers to the center of the abdomen.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pit against（使与…对抗）；gravel pit（砾石坑）；pit stop（赛车加油站）
🧠 助记
pit看起来像一个往下掉的深坑
pit::/pɪt/:n. 坑:pit against each other 使两者相互对抗
2. questionnaire /ˌkwestʃəˈneər/ n. 问卷；调查表
📖 英文解释
a set of printed questions with a choice of answers used in surveying or research
✏️ 例句
Participants were asked to fill out a questionnaire about their eating habits.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fill in/complete a questionnaire（填写问卷）；design a questionnaire（设计问卷）
🧠 助记
question（问题）+ naire → 充满问题的调查表
questionnaire::/ˌkwestʃəˈneər/:n. 问卷:fill in a questionnaire 填写问卷
3. stake /steɪk/ n. 赌注；利益；木桩 v. 以…打赌；用桩固定
📖 英文解释
a sum of money risked on an outcome; a personal interest in an undertaking; a pointed wooden post
✏️ 例句
The company has a large financial stake in the project.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
at stake（处于危险中；有风险）；high stakes（高风险）；stake a claim（提出主张）
🧠 助记
stake谐音死了→ 有利益攸关，死活都在这里
stake::/steɪk/:n. 赌注:at stake 处于危险中；攸关利益
4. stomach /ˈstʌmək/ n. 胃；腹部；欲望 v. 忍受；容忍
📖 英文解释
the internal organ where digestion begins; the ability to tolerate something unpleasant
✏️ 例句
She couldn't stomach the thought of eating meat after becoming a vegetarian.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stomach ache（胃痛）；can't stomach（无法忍受）；stomach for（对…的胃口/兴趣）
🧠 助记
stomach谐音斯通马→ 像石头一样沉在胃里
stomach::/ˈstʌmək/:n. 胃:can't stomach it 无法忍受
5. treaty /ˈtriːti/ n. 条约；协定
📖 英文解释
a formally concluded and ratified agreement between states
✏️ 例句
The two countries signed a peace treaty to end the conflict.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sign/ratify a treaty（签署/批准条约）；peace treaty（和平条约）
🧠 助记
treat（对待）+ y → 通过谈判对待彼此，形成条约
treaty::/ˈtriːti/:n. 条约:sign a treaty 签署条约
6. virgin /ˈvɜːrdʒɪn/ adj. 处女的；原始的；未开发的 n. 处女
📖 英文解释
a person who has not had sexual intercourse; (of land) not yet cultivated or used
✏️ 例句
The Amazon contains vast areas of virgin rainforest.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
virgin forest/territory/land（原始森林/领域/土地）；Virgin Islands（维京群岛）
🧠 助记
virgin谐音吾进→ 我进入，但此处是原始未进入的
virgin::/ˈvɜːrdʒɪn/:adj. 处女的:virgin forest 原始森林
7. acquaint /əˈkweɪnt/ v. 使了解；使熟悉
📖 英文解释
to make someone aware of or familiar with something
✏️ 例句
The orientation helped students acquaint themselves with the campus.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
acquaint sb with（使某人了解…）；acquaintance n.（相识；点头之交）
🧠 助记
ac（加强）+ quaint（熟悉）→ 加强熟悉 = 使了解
acquaint::/əˈkweɪnt/:v. 使了解:acquaint oneself with the rules 熟悉规则
8. ascribe /əˈskraɪb/ v. 把…归因于；把…归属于
📖 英文解释
to attribute something to a cause or source; to credit or assign to a particular origin
✏️ 例句
She ascribed her success to hard work and determination.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ascribe sth to（把…归因于）；同义词 attribute, credit
🧠 助记
a + scribe（书写）→ 书写记录下归属于某人
ascribe::/əˈskraɪb/:v. 把…归因于:ascribe success to hard work 将成功归因于努力
9. awful /ˈɔːfəl/ adj. 糟糕的；可怕的；很大的
📖 英文解释
very bad or unpleasant; used to emphasize something large in size or extent
✏️ 例句
The weather was absolutely awful during the holiday.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
awful weather/mistake/mess（糟糕的天气/错误/混乱）；awfully adv.（非常）
🧠 助记
awe（敬畏）+ ful → 充满敬畏原来是因为太可怕了
awful::/ˈɔːfəl/:adj. 糟糕的:awful weather 糟糕的天气
10. button /ˈbʌtən/ n. 按钮；纽扣 v. 扣纽扣

🧠 助记
butt（末端）+ on → 末端扣上的东西 = 纽扣
button::/ˈbʌtən/:n. 按钮:push a button 按下按钮
11. cave /keɪv/ n. 洞穴；山洞 v. 坍塌；屈服
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

cave::/keɪv/:n. 洞穴:cave in to pressure 迫于压力而屈服
12. complement /ˈkɒmplɪment/ n. 补充；互补物；补语 v. 补充；与…相配
📖 英文解释
a thing that completes or brings to perfection; to make complete or enhance
✏️ 例句
The sauce is a perfect complement to the grilled fish.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

complement::/ˈkɒmplɪment/:n. 补充:complement each other 互相补充
13. confidential /ˌkɒnfɪˈdenʃəl/ adj. 机密的；保密的；可信赖的

confidential::/ˌkɒnfɪˈdenʃəl/:adj. 机密的:strictly confidential 严格保密
14. gear /gɪər/ n. 齿轮；装备；挡位 v. 调整；使适应

gear::/gɪər/:n. 齿轮:gear up for the challenge 为挑战做好准备

swallow::/ˈswɒləʊ/:v. 吞咽:hard to swallow 难以接受
