1. garage /ˈɡærɑːʒ/ n. 车库；汽车修理厂
📖 英文解释
A building for housing a motor vehicle; a place where motor vehicles are repaired.
✏️ 例句
He parked his car in the garage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
garage sale 车库甩卖；parking garage 停车场；garage band 车库乐队
🧠 助记
gar（car汽车）+ age → 汽车的房子 = 车库
garage::/ˈɡærɑːʒ/:n. 车库:The car is in the garage.
2. hut /hʌt/ n. 小屋；棚屋
📖 英文解释
A small simple house or shelter, usually made of wood or metal.
✏️ 例句
The hikers stayed in a mountain hut.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
beach hut 海滩小屋；mountain hut 山中小屋；mud hut 泥屋
🧠 助记
hut → 谐音「哈特」→ 哈特山里的小屋，联想hut=小屋
hut::/hʌt/:n. 小屋:They lived in a simple wooden hut.
3. imperial /ɪmˈpɪəriəl/ adj. 帝国的；皇帝的；威严的
📖 英文解释
Relating to an empire or emperor; majestic and grand.
✏️ 例句
The imperial palace was open to tourists.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
Imperial China 中华帝国；imperial power 帝国权力；imperial system 英制（度量衡）
🧠 助记
imper（emperor皇帝）+ ial → 皇帝的 = 帝国的/皇帝的
imperial::/ɪmˈpɪəriəl/:adj. 帝国的:The imperial army was vast.
4. ministry /ˈmɪnɪstri/ n. 部（政府机构）；内阁；神职
📖 英文解释
A government department headed by a minister; the work of a minister of religion.
✏️ 例句
She works in the Ministry of Finance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
Ministry of Education 教育部；enter the ministry 成为牧师；ministry official 部门官员
🧠 助记
mini（小）+ stry → 小事务部门 = 内阁部门（政府各部）
ministry::/ˈmɪnɪstri/:n. 部（政府机构）:The Ministry announced new policies.
5. neck /nek/ n. 脖子；颈部 v. 接吻
📖 英文解释
The part of the body connecting the head to the rest of the body; a narrow connecting part.
✏️ 例句
She wore a necklace around her neck.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
neck and neck 并驾齐驱；bottleneck 瓶颈；necklace 项链；risk one's neck 冒险
🧠 助记
neck → 形如字母 N，颈部弯曲如 N = 脖子
neck::/nek/:n. 脖子:He craned his neck to see the stage.
6. pant /pænt/ v. 喘气 n. 裤子（pants复数）
📖 英文解释
To breathe with short quick breaths; trousers (usually plural: pants).
✏️ 例句
The dog was panting in the heat.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pant for breath 喘气；pants 裤子；short pants 短裤；pant with excitement 激动得喘粗气
🧠 助记
pant → 谐音「胖特」→ 胖特跑步后喘气 = 喘气
pant::/pænt/:v. 喘气 n. 裤子（pants复数）:He was panting after the long run.
7. pest /pest/ n. 害虫；有害生物；讨厌的人
📖 英文解释
A destructive insect or animal that attacks crops or livestock; an annoying person.
✏️ 例句
Farmers use chemicals to control pests.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pest control 害虫防治；pest management 病虫害管理；a little pest 小烦人精
🧠 助记
pest → 谐音「拍死它」→ 看到害虫就想拍死它，联想pest=害虫
pest::/pest/:n. 害虫:Pests destroyed much of the harvest.
8. poster /ˈpəʊstə/ n. 海报；招贴画
📖 英文解释
A large picture or notice stuck on a wall or noticeboard as advertisement or decoration.
✏️ 例句
She put a poster of her favourite band on the wall.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
movie poster 电影海报；poster campaign 海报宣传活动；post a poster 张贴海报
🧠 助记
post（张贴）+ er → 张贴的东西 = 海报
poster::/ˈpəʊstə/:n. 海报:The poster advertised the upcoming concert.
9. pronoun /ˈprəʊnaʊn/ n. 代词
📖 英文解释
A word that can function as a noun phrase, referring to participants in discourse or to someone mentioned.
✏️ 例句
"He" and "she" are personal pronouns.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
personal pronoun 人称代词；relative pronoun 关系代词；use the correct pronoun 使用正确的代词
🧠 助记
pro（代替）+ noun（名词）→ 代替名词的词 = 代词
pronoun::/ˈprəʊnaʊn/:n. 代词:"They" is a plural pronoun.
10. reconcile /ˈrekənsaɪl/ v. 调和；使和解；使一致
📖 英文解释
To restore friendly relations between people; to make two ideas or facts compatible.
✏️ 例句
She tried to reconcile her career ambitions with her family life.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reconcile with sb 与某人和解；reconcile differences 消除分歧；be reconciled to 接受/甘心于
🧠 助记
re（再）+ concile（调解）→ 再次调解 = 和解/调和
reconcile::/ˈrekənsaɪl/:v. 调和:The two sides reconciled after talks.
11. tender /ˈtendə/ adj. 柔软的；温柔的 n. 投标 v. 提出
📖 英文解释
Gentle and caring; soft and easy to cut; an offer to do work at a stated price.
✏️ 例句
The steak was cooked until it was tender.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tender love 温柔的爱；put out a tender 公开招标；legal tender 法定货币；tender resignation 提出辞职
🧠 助记
tend（照料）+ er → 照料别人的 = 温柔的；投标也是tender
tender::/ˈtendə/:adj. 柔软的:She spoke to the child in a tender voice.
12. premier /ˈpremiə/ n. 总理；首相 adj. 首要的；第一的
📖 英文解释
A prime minister; first in importance, order, or position.
✏️ 例句
The premier announced new economic reforms.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
Premier League 英超联赛；premier event 首要赛事；prime minister/premier 总理
🧠 助记
pre（前/第一）+ mier → 第一的人 = 总理；首要的
premier::/ˈpremiə/:n. 总理:The premier addressed the nation.
13. hug /hʌɡ/ v./n. 拥抱
📖 英文解释
To hold someone tightly in one's arms, as an expression of affection.
✏️ 例句
She hugged her daughter tightly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
give sb a hug 拥抱某人；bear hug 熊抱；hug the coastline 紧靠海岸线行驶
🧠 助记
hug → 谐音「哈格」→ 哈格拥抱，联想hug=拥抱
hug::/hʌɡ/:v./n. 拥抱:He gave his mother a warm hug.
14. nasty /ˈnɑːsti/ adj. 丑陋的；令人厌恶的；凶恶的
📖 英文解释
Highly unpleasant or offensive; behaving in an unpleasant or spiteful way.
✏️ 例句
He said some really nasty things about her.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nasty smell 难闻的气味；nasty weather 恶劣的天气；a nasty person 一个令人讨厌的人
🧠 助记
nasty → 谐音「腻死他」→ 令人腻烦至极 = 令人厌恶的
nasty::/ˈnɑːsti/:adj. 丑陋的:The medicine had a nasty taste.
15. ideology /ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒi/ n. 意识形态；思想体系
📖 英文解释
A system of ideas and ideals, especially one which forms the basis of economic or political theory.
✏️ 例句
Political parties often differ in their ideology.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
political ideology 政治意识形态；dominant ideology 主流意识形态；ideological 意识形态的
🧠 助记
ideo（idea想法）+ logy（学）→ 关于想法的学问 = 意识形态
ideology::/ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒi/:n. 意识形态:The party promoted a socialist ideology.
16. laser /ˈleɪzə/ n. 激光
📖 英文解释
A device that generates an intense beam of coherent monochromatic light.
✏️ 例句
The surgeon used a laser to correct her eyesight.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
laser beam 激光束；laser surgery 激光手术；laser printer 激光打印机
🧠 助记
laser是缩写：Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation = 激光
laser::/ˈleɪzə/:n. 激光:The laser cut through the metal precisely.
17. legacy /ˈleɡəsi/ n. 遗产；遗赠物；遗留问题
📖 英文解释
Money or property left to someone in a will; something handed down from the past.
✏️ 例句
The war left a legacy of bitterness.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
leave a legacy 留下遗产/遗产；cultural legacy 文化遗产；legacy system 遗留系统
🧠 助记
leg（腿/离开）+ acy → 走后留下来的东西 = 遗产
legacy::/ˈleɡəsi/:n. 遗产:He left a substantial legacy to charity.
18. entail /ɪnˈteɪl/ v. 意味着；需要；涉及
📖 英文解释
To involve or make necessary as a consequence or result.
✏️ 例句
The job entails a great deal of travel.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
entail risk 涉及风险；entail hard work 需要努力；what does it entail? 这需要做什么？
🧠 助记
en（使）+ tail（尾巴）→ 使有尾巴跟着 = 意味着/涉及（必然带来的结果）
entail::/ɪnˈteɪl/:v. 意味着:The project entails significant investment.
19. nail /neɪl/ n. 钉子；指甲 v. 钉；精准完成
📖 英文解释
A metal spike used for fastening; the hard covering at the tip of a finger or toe; to fasten with nails.
✏️ 例句
He hammered a nail into the wall.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nail down 确定/敲定；hit the nail on the head 一针见血；nail polish 指甲油；fingernail 手指甲
🧠 助记
nail → 形如钉子，N像钉帽，ail像钉身 = 钉子
nail::/neɪl/:n. 钉子:She painted her nails red.
20. broker /ˈbrəʊkə/ n. 经纪人；中间人 v. 斡旋

A real estate broker helped them find a house.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stock broker 股票经纪人；insurance broker 保险经纪人；broker a deal 促成交易
🧠 助记
brok（broke打破）+ er → 打破买卖障碍的人 = 经纪人
broker::/ˈbrəʊkə/:n. 经纪人:The broker negotiated the best price.
21. crown /kraʊn/ n. 王冠；桂冠 v. 加冕

The queen wore a golden crown.

crown jewels 王冠珠宝；crown prince 王储；crown sb king 为某人加冕为王
🧠 助记
crown → c形王冠，联想crown=王冠
crown::/kraʊn/:n. 王冠:The king was crowned in a grand ceremony.
22. lever /ˈliːvə/ n. 杠杆；控制杆 v. 撬动
📖 英文解释
A rigid bar that pivots on a fulcrum, used to move heavy objects; to move with a lever.
✏️ 例句
She used a crowbar as a lever to open the crate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lever arm 杠杆臂；lever up 用杠杆撬起；leverage 杠杆作用/影响力
🧠 助记
lever → 谐音「力哦」→ 用力哦（杠杆用力）= 杠杆
lever::/ˈliːvə/:n. 杠杆:He levered the rock out of the ground.
23. likelihood /ˈlaɪklihʊd/ n. 可能性；概率
📖 英文解释
The probability of something happening.
✏️ 例句
There is every likelihood of success.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in all likelihood 很可能；likelihood of 某事的可能性；assess the likelihood 评估可能性
🧠 助记
likely（可能的）+ hood（状态）→ 可能的状态 = 可能性
likelihood::/ˈlaɪklihʊd/:n. 可能性:The likelihood of rain is high today.
24. limb /lɪm/ n. 肢体；手足；大树枝
📖 英文解释
An arm or leg of a person; a large branch of a tree.
✏️ 例句
He stretched his tired limbs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
out on a limb 处于危险/孤立境地；tear limb from limb 五马分尸；limb by limb 一肢一肢地
🧠 助记
limb → 谐音「腿膀」→ 手腿手膀肢体 = 肢体
limb::/lɪm/:n. 肢体:The climber broke a limb falling from the tree.
25. proclaim /prəˈkleɪm/ v. 宣告；声明；表明
📖 英文解释
To announce officially or publicly; to declare or make known.
✏️ 例句
The president proclaimed a national holiday.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
proclaim independence 宣布独立；proclaim war 宣战；proclaim sb king 宣布某人为国王
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ claim（声称）→ 向前大声声称 = 宣告
proclaim::/prəˈkleɪm/:v. 宣告:The general proclaimed victory.
