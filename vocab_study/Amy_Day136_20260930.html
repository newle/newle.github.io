1. swing /swɪŋ/ v./n. 摇摆；秋千；大幅波动
📖 英文解释
To move back and forth or in a curve; a seat hung on ropes for swinging; a shift in opinion.
✏️ 例句
The children played on the swings in the park.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
swing into action 立即行动；mood swing 情绪波动；in full swing 正在全力进行
🧠 助记
swing → 谐音「晃行」→ 晃来晃去 = 摇摆/秋千
swing::/swɪŋ/:v./n. 摇摆:Shares swung wildly on the stock market.
2. await /əˈweɪt/ v. 等待；期待；将降临于
📖 英文解释
To wait for; to be in store for someone.
✏️ 例句
A warm welcome awaited the returning soldiers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
await further instructions 等候进一步指示；long-awaited 期待已久的；await trial 等候审判
🧠 助记
a（强调）+ wait（等）→ 等待 = await（书面语）
await::/əˈweɪt/:v. 等待:She awaited the results nervously.
3. comedy /ˈkɒmədi/ n. 喜剧；喜剧片
📖 英文解释
A play or film intended to make an audience laugh; the genre of humorous entertainment.
✏️ 例句
He starred in a romantic comedy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
romantic comedy 浪漫喜剧；stand-up comedy 单口喜剧；comedy club 喜剧俱乐部
🧠 助记
come（来）+ dy → 来笑一笑 = 喜剧
comedy::/ˈkɒmədi/:n. 喜剧:The comedy made everyone laugh.
4. condemn /kənˈdem/ v. 谴责；判刑；宣判有罪
📖 英文解释
To express complete disapproval of; to sentence someone to a punishment.
✏️ 例句
World leaders condemned the terrorist attack.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
condemn sb to death 判处死刑；strongly condemn 强烈谴责；condemn a building 宣布建筑不宜居住
🧠 助记
con（强烈）+ demn（damn咒骂）→ 强烈咒骂 = 谴责
condemn::/kənˈdem/:v. 谴责:The court condemned him to prison.
5. dust /dʌst/ n. 灰尘；尘土 v. 掸去灰尘
📖 英文解释
Fine dry particles covering surfaces or carried in the air; to remove dust from something.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
dust → 谐音「大扫」→ 大扫除去掉灰尘 = 灰尘
dust::/dʌst/:n. 灰尘:She dusted the shelves carefully.
6. filter /ˈfɪltə/ n./v. 过滤器；过滤；筛选
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
fil（fill填）+ ter → 填充过滤材料 = 过滤器
filter::/ˈfɪltə/:n./v. 过滤器:Light filtered through the curtains.
7. fluid /ˈfluːɪd/ n. 流体；液体 adj. 流动的；不固定的

🧠 助记

fluid::/ˈfluːɪd/:n. 流体:The dancer moved with fluid grace.
8. incidentally /ˌɪnsɪˈdentəli/ adv. 顺便说一句；附带地
