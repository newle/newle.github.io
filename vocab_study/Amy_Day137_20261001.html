1. worship /ˈwɜːʃɪp/ v./n. 崇拜；敬拜；礼拜
📖 英文解释
To show reverence and adoration for a deity; the feeling or expression of reverence.
✏️ 例句
They gathered to worship at the church.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hero worship 英雄崇拜；place of worship 礼拜场所；worship the ground sb walks on 极度崇拜某人
🧠 助记
wor（worth价值）+ ship → 认为有价值而尊崇 = 崇拜/敬拜
worship::/ˈwɜːʃɪp/:v./n. 崇拜:She worshipped her elder sister.
2. acid /ˈæsɪd/ n. 酸；酸性物质 adj. 酸的；讽刺的
📖 英文解释
A chemical substance with a pH less than 7; having a sharp taste; sarcastic.
✏️ 例句
Lemon juice contains citric acid.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
acid rain 酸雨；acid test 严峻考验；stomach acid 胃酸；acid remark 尖刻评论
🧠 助记
acid → 谐音「爱斯的」→ 酸酸的爱 = 酸/酸性物质
acid::/ˈæsɪd/:n. 酸:The acid burned through the metal.
3. boot /buːt/ n. 靴子；（汽车）行李箱 v. 启动（电脑）；踢
📖 英文解释
A sturdy item of footwear; the trunk of a car; to start up a computer.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
boot up 启动电脑；car boot 汽车后备箱；put the boot in 痛打；tough as old boots 非常强壮
🧠 助记
boot → 谐音「步特」→ 走路需要靴子 = 靴子
boot::/buːt/:n. 靴子:She booted up her laptop.
4. extinct /ɪkˈstɪŋkt/ adj. 灭绝的；绝种的；熄灭的
📖 英文解释
No longer in existence; (of a fire) no longer burning.
✏️ 例句
The dodo became extinct in the 17th century.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
extinct species 灭绝物种；go extinct 走向灭绝；extinct volcano 死火山
🧠 助记
ex（出去）+ tinct（tinge熄灭）→ 生命熄灭了 = 灭绝的
extinct::/ɪkˈstɪŋkt/:adj. 灭绝的:Many animals are in danger of becoming extinct.
5. hamburger /ˈhæmbɜːɡə/ n. 汉堡包

hamburger::/ˈhæmbɜːɡə/:n. 汉堡包:She ate a hamburger for lunch.
6. hawk /hɔːk/ n. 鹰；强硬派 v. 兜售

hawk::/hɔːk/:n. 鹰:The hawk swooped down to catch a mouse.
