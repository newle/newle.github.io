1. riot /ˈraɪət/ n. 骚乱；暴乱；放纵 v. 暴动
📖 英文解释
A violent disturbance of the peace by a crowd; uncontrolled revelry; to take part in a violent public disturbance.
✏️ 例句
Police were called to control the riot outside the stadium.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
riot police 防暴警察；run riot 肆意横行；a riot of colors 色彩缤纷
🧠 助记
ri-（发怒）+ ot → 发怒的人群 → 骚乱；联想 violent（暴力）
riot::/ˈraɪət/:n. 骚乱:The government deployed additional police to suppress the riot in the capital.
2. situate /ˈsɪtʃueɪt/ v. 使坐落在；使处于（某种情况）
📖 英文解释
To place in a particular position or context; often used in passive form 'situated'.
✏️ 例句
The hotel is situated on the banks of the river.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be situated in/on/near 坐落于；situated 常见被动式；situation n. 情况、形势
🧠 助记
situ-（= sit 坐）+ -ate → 使坐下来 → 使坐落于某处；联想 site（地点）
situate::/ˈsɪtʃueɪt/:v. 使坐落在:The research centre is strategically situated between two major universities.
3. skate /skeɪt/ v./n. 滑冰；溜冰；回避
📖 英文解释
To glide over ice or a surface on skates; to avoid dealing with something; the boot with blades for ice skating.
✏️ 例句
She learned to skate at the age of five.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ice skate 溜冰；skate over/around 回避（问题）；skateboard 滑板
🧠 助记
联想 slate（石板）→ 在石板上滑 → 后来变成冰面 → 滑冰
skate::/skeɪt/:v./n. 滑冰:Politicians often try to skate around difficult questions during interviews.
4. specification /ˌspesɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ n. 规格；说明书；规范
📖 英文解释
A detailed description of the design and materials to be used in construction; a precise requirement or standard.
✏️ 例句
The engineer reviewed the technical specifications before starting construction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
technical/product specification 技术/产品规格；meet specifications 符合规格；spec（口语缩写）
🧠 助记
specific（具体的）+ -ation → 具体的说明 → 规格、规范
specification::/ˌspesɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/:n. 规格:All products must meet the specifications set by the quality control department.
5. sweat /swet/ n./v. 汗水；流汗；努力工作
📖 英文解释
Moisture exuded through the skin; to exude this moisture; to work hard; to worry.
✏️ 例句
He was covered in sweat after the long run.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
break into a sweat 开始出汗；sweat it out 艰难坚持；cold sweat 冷汗；sweatshop 血汗工厂
🧠 助记
联想 sweet（甜）→ 流汗才能得到甜果实；sweat 和 sweet 形近
sweat::/swet/:n./v. 汗水:The negotiations were stressful, and both sides broke into a cold sweat.
6. transit /ˈtrænzɪt/ n. 运输；运送；过境 v. 通过
📖 英文解释
The act of passing through or across a place; transportation of goods; passage through a country.
✏️ 例句
The goods were damaged in transit.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in transit 运输途中；transit visa 过境签证；public transit 公共交通
🧠 助记
trans-（穿越）+ it → 穿越过去 → 运输、过境；联想 transport
transit::/ˈtrænzɪt/:n. 运输:The rare artifacts were carefully packaged to prevent damage in transit.
7. tumble /ˈtʌmbl/ v. 跌倒；暴跌；翻滚 n. 跌落
📖 英文解释
To fall suddenly and helplessly; to decrease sharply in value; to perform acrobatic movements.
✏️ 例句
Stock prices tumbled after the announcement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tumble down 倒塌；tumble to 突然明白；a tumble in prices 价格暴跌
🧠 助记
联想 humble（谦卑）→ 跌倒了才能谦卑 → tumble 跌落；t + tumble 滚翻
tumble::/ˈtʌmbl/:v. 跌倒:Share prices tumbled sharply following the disappointing earnings report.
8. rap /ræp/ n. 说唱音乐；轻敲；责备 v. 轻敲；批评
📖 英文解释
A style of music featuring rhythmic speech; a quick sharp knock; to strike sharply; to criticize.
✏️ 例句
He performed a rap song at the school talent show.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rap music/artist 说唱音乐/歌手；rap on the door 轻敲门；take the rap 受责罚
🧠 助记
联想 wrap（包裹）→ 用节奏包裹文字 → rap 说唱；rap 也表示敲击声
rap::/ræp/:n. 说唱音乐:He took the rap for a mistake that was not entirely his fault.
9. geology /dʒiˈɒlədʒi/ n. 地质学；地质情况
📖 英文解释
The study of the Earth's physical structure, materials, and processes; the geological features of a region.
✏️ 例句
She majored in geology at university.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
study geology 学习地质学；geology of a region 某地区的地质情况；geologist n. 地质学家
🧠 助记
geo-（地球）+ -logy（学科）→ 研究地球的学问 → 地质学
geology::/dʒiˈɒlədʒi/:n. 地质学:Understanding the local geology was essential before beginning the construction project.
10. inference /ˈɪnfərəns/ n. 推理；推断；结论
📖 英文解释
A conclusion reached on the basis of evidence and reasoning; the process of drawing conclusions.
✏️ 例句
From the evidence, the detective made a brilliant inference.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
draw/make an inference 做出推断；logical inference 逻辑推理；infer v. 推断
🧠 助记
in-（进入）+ fer（带来）+ -ence → 把结论带进来 → 推断；联想 infer
inference::/ˈɪnfərəns/:n. 推理:Scientists rely on careful inference when direct observation is not possible.
11. cocaine /kəʊˈkeɪn/ n. 可卡因（毒品）
📖 英文解释
A powerful stimulant drug derived from the coca plant, classified as an illegal substance.
✏️ 例句
Cocaine abuse can lead to severe health and legal consequences.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cocaine addiction 可卡因成瘾；trafficking cocaine 贩运可卡因；考研词汇（了解即可）
🧠 助记
coca（可可叶）+ -ine（化学物质后缀）→ 从可可叶提炼的物质 → 可卡因
cocaine::/kəʊˈkeɪn/:n. 可卡因（毒品）:The police seized a large quantity of cocaine during the raid.
12. graph /ɡrɑːf/ n. 图表；图形 v. 用图表表示
📖 英文解释
A diagram showing the relationship between variable quantities; to plot or represent data in a graph.
✏️ 例句
The graph shows a steady increase in sales over five years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
bar/line/pie graph 条形/折线/饼图；graph paper 坐标纸；diagram（近义词）
🧠 助记
graph = graphic（图形的）→ 图表形式 → graph；联想 paragraph（段落也用 graph）
graph::/ɡrɑːf/:n. 图表:The research team used a series of graphs to illustrate the data trends.
13. monarch /ˈmɒnək/ n. 君主；帝王；独裁者
📖 英文解释
A sovereign ruler, such as a king, queen, or emperor; the supreme authority in a monarchy.
✏️ 例句
The monarch addressed the nation on television.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
absolute/constitutional monarch 绝对/立宪君主；monarchy n. 君主制；monarch butterfly 帝王蝴蝶
🧠 助记
mono-（唯一）+ arch（统治者）→ 唯一的统治者 → 君主
monarch::/ˈmɒnək/:n. 君主:The constitutional monarch plays a ceremonial rather than a governing role.
14. multitude /ˈmʌltɪtjuːd/ n. 大量；众多；大众
📖 英文解释
A large number of people or things; the mass of ordinary people.
✏️ 例句
A multitude of factors contributed to the economic crisis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a multitude of 大量的；the multitude 大众；cover a multitude of sins 掩盖很多问题
🧠 助记
multi-（多）+ tude（状态）→ 多的状态 → 大量；联想 multiple（多个的）
multitude::/ˈmʌltɪtjuːd/:n. 大量:A multitude of challenges faced the newly formed government.
15. succession /səkˈseʃn/ n. 连续；继承；一系列
📖 英文解释
A number of people or things following in order; the process of inheriting a title or role.
✏️ 例句
A succession of bad decisions led to the company's downfall.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in succession 连续地；succession of 一系列；succeed v. 继承；succession planning 继承规划
🧠 助记
succes-（跟随）+ -ion → 一个接一个跟着 → 连续；联想 succeed（接班）
succession::/səkˈseʃn/:n. 连续:The company went through a succession of leadership changes within one year.
16. analogy /əˈnælədʒi/ n. 类比；类推；相似
📖 英文解释
A comparison between two things showing their similarity; reasoning based on parallel cases.
✏️ 例句
The teacher used an analogy to explain the concept of electricity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
draw an analogy between 在……之间进行类比；by analogy 以此类推；analogous adj. 类似的
🧠 助记
ana-（并列）+ logy（逻辑）→ 并列的逻辑关系 → 类比；联想 analogous
analogy::/əˈnælədʒi/:n. 类比:He drew an analogy between managing a team and conducting an orchestra.
17. despair /dɪˈspeər/ n./v. 绝望；失望
📖 英文解释
The complete loss or absence of hope; to feel or cause to feel such a state.
✏️ 例句
She fell into despair after losing her job.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in despair 绝望地；despair of sth 对某事感到绝望；desperate adj. 绝望的
🧠 助记
de-（否定）+ spair（= spare 希望）→ 没有希望 → 绝望；联想 desperate
despair::/dɪˈspeər/:n./v. 绝望:After months of failed attempts, he was beginning to despair of ever finding a solution.
18. forge /fɔːdʒ/ v. 伪造；锻造；建立（关系） n. 铸造厂
📖 英文解释
To make an illegal copy of something; to shape metal by heating and hammering; to create or develop something new and strong.
✏️ 例句
He was arrested for forging banknotes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
forge a signature/document 伪造签名/文件；forge ahead 奋勇前进；forge relationships 建立关系
🧠 助记
联想 force（力量）→ 用力打造 → 锻造；也联想 fake（伪造）→ forge
forge::/fɔːdʒ/:v. 伪造:The two nations worked together to forge a lasting peace agreement.
19. hitherto /ˌhɪðəˈtuː/ adv. 迄今；到目前为止
📖 英文解释
Until now or until the point in time under discussion.
✏️ 例句
The hitherto unknown artist gained fame overnight.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hitherto unknown/unresolved 迄今未知/未解决的；常用于书面语；= up to this point
🧠 助记
hither（此处）+ to（到）→ 到这里为止 → 迄今；类比 henceforth（从此以后）
hitherto::/ˌhɪðəˈtuː/:adv. 迄今:Hitherto unexplored regions of the deep ocean may hold new scientific discoveries.
20. predecessor /ˈpriːdɪsesər/ n. 前任；前辈；前身
📖 英文解释
A person who held a position before the current holder; something that has been followed or replaced by something else.
✏️ 例句
She improved many of the policies introduced by her predecessor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
immediate predecessor 直接前任；predecessor company/model 前身公司/型号；successor 后继者
🧠 助记
pre-（之前）+ decessor（= one who goes away 离开者）→ 在我之前离去的人 → 前任
predecessor::/ˈpriːdɪsesər/:n. 前任:The new CEO built on the achievements of her predecessor.
21. whereby /weəˈbaɪ/ adv./conj. 凭借；通过……；借此
📖 英文解释
By means of which; through or because of which.
✏️ 例句
They set up a system whereby employees could report grievances anonymously.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a system/process/method whereby 一种……的方式/系统；常见于正式书面语
🧠 助记
where（哪里）+ by（通过）→ 通过哪个方法 → 凭借……；学术写作常用
whereby::/weəˈbaɪ/:adv./conj. 凭借:The company established a mechanism whereby customer feedback was directly reviewed by managers.
22. aboard /əˈbɔːrd/ adv./prep. 在船/飞机/车上；登上
📖 英文解释
On or into a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle; a welcome expression when someone joins a group.
✏️ 例句
All passengers should go aboard before departure.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
go/come aboard 上船/飞机；all aboard! 请上车；welcome aboard 欢迎加入
🧠 助记
a-（在）+ board（船板）→ 在船板上 → 在船上；联想 on board（已在船上）
aboard::/əˈbɔːrd/:adv./prep. 在船/飞机/车上:The new team member was warmly welcomed aboard at the orientation meeting.
23. beard /bɪərd/ n. 胡须；蓄须 v. 勇敢面对
📖 英文解释
Hair that grows on a man's chin and lower cheeks; to confront boldly.
✏️ 例句
He grew a thick beard during the winter months.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
grow/shave a beard 留/刮胡须；beard the lion in his den 敢于正面对抗；bearded adj. 有胡子的
🧠 助记

beard::/bɪərd/:n. 胡须:He stroked his beard thoughtfully before responding to the journalist's question.
24. bypass /ˈbaɪpɑːs/ v. 绕开；回避 n. 旁道；迂回路
📖 英文解释
To go around or avoid an obstacle; a road or passage that goes around a congested area; a surgical procedure for blocked arteries.
✏️ 例句
The new highway bypasses the town center.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
bypass a problem/procedure 绕过问题/程序；heart bypass 心脏搭桥手术；bypass road 绕行路
🧠 助记
by（旁）+ pass（通过）→ 从旁边通过 → 绕开、旁道
bypass::/ˈbaɪpɑːs/:v. 绕开:The developers tried to bypass the standard approval process to save time.
25. chemist /ˈkemɪst/ n. 化学家；药剂师（英式）；药店

A scientist specializing in chemistry; in British English, a pharmacist or a drugstore.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
at the chemist's 在药店；pharmaceutical chemist 制药化学家；analytical chemist 分析化学家
🧠 助记

chemist::/ˈkemɪst/:n. 化学家:The chemist developed a new compound that showed promising results in early trials.
