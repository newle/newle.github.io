1. chunk /tʃʌŋk/ n. 厚块；大片；大量 v. 分成大块
📖 英文解释
A thick solid piece cut or broken from something; a significant part or amount.
✏️ 例句
She cut the cheese into large chunks.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a chunk of time/money 大量时间/金钱；in chunks 一大块一大块地；chunky adj. 厚实的
🧠 助记
联想 hunk（大块肌肉）→ chunk 是任何物品的大块 → 厚块
chunk::/tʃʌŋk/:n. 厚块:A large chunk of the research budget was allocated to laboratory equipment.
2. drawback /ˈdrɔːbæk/ n. 缺点；不利条件；障碍
📖 英文解释
A disadvantage or problem that makes something less acceptable.
✏️ 例句
The main drawback of the plan was its high cost.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the drawback of/to 的缺点；major/minor drawback 主要/次要缺点；overcome drawbacks
🧠 助记
draw（拉）+ back（后）→ 把你往后拉的东西 → 缺点、障碍
drawback::/ˈdrɔːbæk/:n. 缺点:A major drawback of the remote work policy was the lack of direct team collaboration.
3. economical /ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪkl/ adj. 经济的；节约的；合算的
📖 英文解释
Giving good value or service in relation to the amount of money or effort used; not wasteful.
✏️ 例句
It is more economical to buy in bulk.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
economical use of resources 节约资源；economical with the truth 说话不完全诚实；economic vs economical
🧠 助记
economy（经济）+ -ical → 与经济相关的 → 节约的；注意与 economic（经济上的）区分
economical::/ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪkl/:adj. 经济的:The new fuel-efficient engine made the vehicle far more economical to operate.
4. ink /ɪŋk/ n. 墨水；油墨 v. 用墨水写；签署
📖 英文解释
A colored liquid used for writing or printing; to mark or sign with ink.
✏️ 例句
He signed the contract in ink.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in ink 用墨水；ink a deal 签署协议；invisible ink 隐形墨水；printer ink 打印机墨水
🧠 助记
联想 think（思考）→ think in ink 用墨水思考 → ink 墨水
ink::/ɪŋk/:n. 墨水:The two companies finally inked a deal after months of negotiations.
5. medieval /ˌmediˈiːvl/ adj. 中世纪的；守旧的
📖 英文解释
Relating to the Middle Ages (roughly 5th–15th century); very old-fashioned.
✏️ 例句
The castle had a medieval architecture that attracted tourists.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
medieval history/art/society 中世纪历史/艺术/社会；medieval attitude 守旧的态度
🧠 助记
medi-（中间）+ eval（= era 时代）→ 中间时代 → 中世纪；联想 medium（中间）
medieval::/ˌmediˈiːvl/:adj. 中世纪的:The documentary explored the political structures of medieval European kingdoms.
6. nod /nɒd/ v./n. 点头；打盹；表示赞同
📖 英文解释
To move the head up and down as a sign of agreement or greeting; a quick downward movement of the head.
✏️ 例句
She nodded her head in agreement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nod off 打盹；get the nod 获得批准；nod to sth 致敬……；on the nod 迅速通过
🧠 助记
联想 node（节点）→ 头像节点一样上下运动 → 点头；nod off → 头点着点着就睡了
nod::/nɒd/:v./n. 点头:The committee gave the nod of approval to the proposed budget revisions.
7. simplicity /sɪmˈplɪsɪti/ n. 简单；朴素；简洁
📖 英文解释
The quality of being easy to understand or do; the state of being plain and uncomplicated.
✏️ 例句
The beauty of the design lay in its simplicity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
simplicity of design/style 设计/风格的简洁；for simplicity's sake 为了简便起见
🧠 助记
simple（简单）+ -icity → 简单的特质 → 简单性、简洁；联想 complexity（复杂性）的反义
simplicity::/sɪmˈplɪsɪti/:n. 简单:The report praised the system for its simplicity and ease of use.
8. superstition /ˌsuːpəˈstɪʃn/ n. 迷信；迷信行为
📖 英文解释
A widely held but irrational belief in supernatural influences; excessively credulous belief in the supernatural.
✏️ 例句
It is a superstition that breaking a mirror brings seven years of bad luck.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
popular superstition 普遍迷信；based on superstition 基于迷信；superstitious adj. 迷信的
🧠 助记
super（超越）+ stition（= stand 站立）→ 超越理性站立的信念 → 迷信
superstition::/ˌsuːpəˈstɪʃn/:n. 迷信:Despite widespread education, many superstitions persist in modern societies.
9. triple /ˈtrɪpl/ adj. 三倍的；三重的 v. 使变为三倍 n. 三倍
📖 英文解释
Consisting of three parts; multiplied by three; to make or become three times as much.
✏️ 例句
Sales tripled in the second quarter of the year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
triple the amount/speed 使数量/速度增加三倍；triple jump 三级跳；triple threat 三重威胁
🧠 助记
tri-（三）+ ple → 三倍的；联想 triple play（棒球三杀），triple jump
triple::/ˈtrɪpl/:adj. 三倍的:The company's profits nearly tripled after entering the Asian market.
10. unfold /ʌnˈfəʊld/ v. 展开；逐渐发展；揭示
📖 英文解释
To open up from a folded state; to reveal or be revealed; to develop or become known gradually.
✏️ 例句
As the story unfolded, more details came to light.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the story/events unfold 故事/事件逐渐展开；unfold a plan 展示计划；unfold a map 打开地图
🧠 助记
un-（解除）+ fold（折叠）→ 解除折叠状态 → 展开；引申为逐渐揭示
unfold::/ʌnˈfəʊld/:v. 展开:As the investigation unfolded, it revealed a complex network of corruption.
11. accountant /əˈkaʊntənt/ n. 会计师；会计
📖 英文解释
A person whose profession is keeping financial accounts and preparing financial reports.
✏️ 例句
She hired an accountant to manage the company's finances.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
certified public accountant 注册会计师；chartered accountant 特许会计师；forensic accountant 法证会计师
🧠 助记
account（账目）+ -ant（人）→ 管账目的人 → 会计师
accountant::/əˈkaʊntənt/:n. 会计师:The accountant discovered several discrepancies during the annual financial audit.
12. bunch /bʌntʃ/ n. 束；串；一群 v. 捆在一起
📖 英文解释
A number of things growing or fastened together; a group of people; to form into a cluster.
✏️ 例句
She brought a bunch of flowers to the hospital.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a bunch of keys/grapes/people 一串钥匙/葡萄/一群人；bunch up 挤在一起
🧠 助记
联想 hunch（驼背）→ 挤在一起的东西 → bunch 一束；b + unch → 一拳打出一团
bunch::/bʌntʃ/:n. 束:A bunch of volunteers showed up to help with the community restoration project.
13. elder /ˈeldər/ adj. 年长的 n. 长者；长辈；接骨木
📖 英文解释
Of greater age; a person of greater age or seniority; a leader in some churches or tribes.
✏️ 例句
We should respect our elders.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
elder sibling 兄/姐；village/church elder 村长/教会长老；elder statesman 政界元老
🧠 助记
old（老）+ -er（比较级）→ 更老的 → 年长的；联想 elderly（老年的）
elder::/ˈeldər/:adj. 年长的 n. 长者:The community relied on the wisdom of its elders to resolve long-standing disputes.
14. erase /ɪˈreɪz/ v. 抹去；消除；擦掉
📖 英文解释
To rub out or remove marks or writing; to destroy or remove completely.
✏️ 例句
She tried to erase the pencil marks from the page.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
erase memories/data/doubt 抹去记忆/数据/疑虑；erasure n. 擦除；indelible（不可消除的）
🧠 助记
e-（出）+ rase（=scratch 刮）→ 刮出去 → 抹去；联想 race（快速划过）
erase::/ɪˈreɪz/:v. 抹去:Digital tools make it easy to erase and undo changes in documents.
15. fearful /ˈfɪərfl/ adj. 可怕的；害怕的
📖 英文解释
Causing or feeling fear; extremely unpleasant or great.
✏️ 例句
She gave a fearful cry when she heard the explosion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fearful of 害怕……；fearful consequences 可怕的后果；fearfully adv.
🧠 助记
fear（恐惧）+ -ful（充满）→ 充满恐惧的 → 害怕的；也指让人恐惧的 → 可怕的
fearful::/ˈfɪərfl/:adj. 可怕的:Many small business owners are fearful of the long-term economic consequences of the new policy.
16. glow /ɡləʊ/ n./v. 光辉；发光；红光
📖 英文解释
A steady light without flame; to emit a steady light; to feel warmth or enthusiasm.
✏️ 例句
The embers of the fire still glowed in the dark.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
glow with pride/health 因自豪/健康而发光；a warm glow 温暖的光；glowing adj. 赞扬的
🧠 助记
联想 flow（流动）→ 光流动的感觉 → glow 发光；又像 glow-worm（萤火虫）
glow::/ɡləʊ/:n./v. 光辉:Her face glowed with pride as she accepted the award on stage.
17. holy /ˈhəʊli/ adj. 神圣的；虔诚的
📖 英文解释
Dedicated to God or a religious purpose; morally and spiritually excellent; sacred.
✏️ 例句
Jerusalem is considered a holy city by three major religions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
holy war/land/water 圣战/圣地/圣水；Holy Bible 圣经；holy day → holiday 节日来源
🧠 助记
联想 whole（完整）→ 身心完整的、神圣的 → holy；whole 和 holy 有词源联系
holy::/ˈhəʊli/:adj. 神圣的:Pilgrims travel great distances to visit holy sites in the Middle East.
18. inclusive /ɪnˈkluːsɪv/ adj. 包容的；在内的；一切包含在内的
📖 英文解释
Including everything; not excluding any part of a group; covering all costs.
✏️ 例句
The price is inclusive of all taxes and fees.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
inclusive education 全纳教育；inclusive of 包含……在内；all-inclusive 全包的
🧠 助记
in-（包含）+ clus（= close 关闭）+ -ive → 把所有的都关在里面 → 包含一切的
inclusive::/ɪnˈkluːsɪv/:adj. 包容的:The school adopted an inclusive approach to education, welcoming students of all abilities.
19. jar /dʒɑːr/ n. 罐子；瓶子 v. 使不和谐；使震惊；发出刺耳声
📖 英文解释
A cylindrical container with a wide mouth; to disturb or cause discomfort; to make a harsh sound.
✏️ 例句
She stored the jam in glass jars.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a jar of jam/honey 一罐果酱/蜂蜜；jar on the nerves 刺激神经；jarring adj. 刺耳的
🧠 助记
联想 car（车）→ 车轮卡住发出刺耳声 → jar（发出刺耳声/罐子）
jar::/dʒɑːr/:n. 罐子:The constant noise of construction jarred on the residents' nerves.
20. outward /ˈaʊtwərd/ adj./adv. 向外的；外表的；表面上的
📖 英文解释
Moving or facing away from the center; relating to the external appearance; toward the outside.
✏️ 例句
Despite her outward calm, she was very nervous inside.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
outward appearance/sign 外表/表面迹象；outward journey 去程；outwardly adv.
🧠 助记
out（向外）+ ward（方向）→ 朝向外部 → 外表的、向外的
outward::/ˈaʊtwərd/:adj./adv. 向外的:He maintained an outward appearance of confidence despite feeling deeply uncertain.
21. plague /pleɪɡ/ n. 瘟疫；祸害 v. 困扰；折磨
📖 英文解释
A contagious bacterial disease causing widespread death; a large number of harmful things; to cause continual trouble.
✏️ 例句
The Black Plague killed millions of people in medieval Europe.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the plague 瘟疫；plague sb with questions 以问题困扰某人；avoid like the plague 避之唯恐不及
🧠 助记
联想 place（地方）→ 整个地方都是瘟疫 → plague；p + lague（= lake 湖）→ 瘟疫蔓延如湖
plague::/pleɪɡ/:n. 瘟疫:The organization has long been plagued by internal conflicts and poor management.
22. refute /rɪˈfjuːt/ v. 反驳；驳斥；证明……错误
📖 英文解释
To prove a statement or theory to be wrong or false; to deny a charge or accusation.
✏️ 例句
She used concrete data to refute the opposing argument.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
refute a claim/theory/allegation 反驳声明/理论/指控；irrefutable adj. 无法反驳的
🧠 助记
re-（反）+ fute（= argue 争论）→ 反向争论 → 反驳；联想 confute（驳倒）
refute::/rɪˈfjuːt/:v. 反驳:The scientist presented compelling evidence to refute the widely accepted theory.
23. spontaneous /spɒnˈteɪniəs/ adj. 自然产生的；自发的；无准备的
📖 英文解释
Performed or occurring as a result of a sudden impulse or inclination; happening without apparent cause.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
spontaneous reaction/combustion 自发反应/自燃；act spontaneously 自发地行动
🧠 助记
spont-（= of one's own will）+ -aneous → 出于自身意志的 → 自发的；联想 fountain（喷泉，自然涌出）
spontaneous::/spɒnˈteɪniəs/:adj. 自然产生的:A spontaneous act of kindness from a stranger changed her perspective on human nature.
24. adhere /ədˈhɪər/ v. 坚持；遵守；粘附
📖 英文解释
To stick firmly to a surface; to remain faithful to a belief or agreement; to follow closely.
✏️ 例句
All employees must adhere to the company's code of conduct.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
adhere to a rule/principle/schedule 遵守规则/原则/时间表；adherence n.；adhesive adj./n.
🧠 助记
ad-（朝向）+ here（=stick 粘）→ 粘在一起 → 粘附；引申为坚守原则
adhere::/ədˈhɪər/:v. 坚持:Member states are expected to adhere strictly to the terms of the international agreement.
