1. peasant /ˈpeznt/ n. 农民；庄稼汉；乡下人
📖 英文解释
A poor farmer of low social status who owns or rents a small piece of land; an ignorant or unsophisticated person.
✏️ 例句
For centuries, peasants made up the majority of the population.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研历史、社会类词汇。peasant uprising（农民起义）；peasant class（农民阶级）。
🧠 助记
peas（豌豆）+ ant → 种豌豆的蚂蚁般渺小的人 → 农民
peasant::/ˈpeznt/:n. 农民:The industrial revolution drove peasants from farmland to cities.
2. scold /skəʊld/ v. 责骂；训斥 n. 爱骂人的人
📖 英文解释
To remonstrate with or rebuke (someone) angrily; a person, typically a woman, who nags or criticizes constantly.
✏️ 例句
The teacher scolded the students for arriving late to class.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常见词。scold sb. for doing sth.（因…责骂某人）。区分：scold vs criticize（批评）。
🧠 助记
scold → 声音'嘘哦诶'骂人 → 责骂；训斥
scold::/skəʊld/:v. 责骂:Parents should guide rather than scold their children.
3. slump /slʌmp/ v./n. 暴跌；萧条；低迷；倒下
📖 英文解释
A sudden severe or prolonged fall in prices, value, or activity; to sit, lean, or fall heavily; to decline suddenly.
✏️ 例句
The housing market slumped following the interest rate hike.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研经济类高频词。economic slump（经济萧条）；slump in sales（销售暴跌）。
🧠 助记
slump → 重重地落下，'扑通' → 暴跌；倒下
slump::/slʌmp/:v./n. 暴跌:The company's profits slumped due to rising competition.
4. tail /teɪl/ n. 尾巴；尾部 v. 跟踪
📖 英文解释
The hindmost part of an animal; the rear part of an object; to follow and observe someone secretly.
✏️ 例句
The detective was hired to tail the suspicious employee.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研双义词。tail off（逐渐减少）；heads or tails（正面还是反面）。
🧠 助记
tail → tale（故事）谐音 → 跟在后面听故事 → 尾巴；跟踪
tail::/teɪl/:n. 尾巴:The police officer tailed the suspect for three days.
5. typewriter /ˈtaɪpraɪtə(r)/ n. 打字机
📖 英文解释
An electric, electronic, or manual machine with keys for producing print-like characters on paper.
✏️ 例句
Before computers, every office had a typewriter on each desk.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化、历史类词汇。typewriter代表前数字时代的技术。
🧠 助记
type（打字）+ writer（写者）→ 打字写作的机器 → 打字机
typewriter::/ˈtaɪpraɪtə(r)/:n. 打字机:The journalist composed the article on an old typewriter.
6. accent /ˈæksənt/ n. 口音；重音；强调 v. 重读；强调
📖 英文解释
A distinctive way of pronouncing a language, especially one associated with a particular region; emphasis given to a syllable or word in speech.
✏️ 例句
She spoke English with a strong French accent.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研语言类常见词。accent on（强调）；regional accent（地区口音）。
🧠 助记
ac（加强）+ cent（声音）→ 加强某个声音 → 重音；口音
accent::/ˈæksənt/:n. 口音:His regional accent made it difficult for others to understand him.
7. accordance /əˈkɔːdns/ n. 一致；和谐；符合
📖 英文解释
Agreement or harmony; conformity with rules, standards, or wishes.
✏️ 例句
The work was carried out in accordance with safety regulations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研固定搭配：in accordance with（按照；依据）是高频短语。
🧠 助记
accord（和谐/一致）+ ance → 处于一致的状态 → 一致；符合
accordance::/əˈkɔːdns/:n. 一致:The decision was made in accordance with company policy.
8. anniversary /ˌænɪˈvɜːsəri/ n. 周年纪念日
📖 英文解释
The date on which an event took place in a previous year; an annual celebration of an event.
✏️ 例句
They celebrated their 25th wedding anniversary with a trip to Paris.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常见词。wedding anniversary（结婚周年）；the anniversary of（…周年纪念）。
🧠 助记
anni（年）+ vers（转）+ ary → 时间轮转一年 → 周年纪念
anniversary::/ˌænɪˈvɜːsəri/:n. 周年纪念日:The company held a gala to mark its 50th anniversary.
9. banana /bəˈnɑːnə/ n. 香蕉
📖 英文解释
A long curved fruit with a yellow skin and soft sweet flesh; the tropical plant that bears this fruit.
✏️ 例句
Bananas are rich in potassium and are a popular snack worldwide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研词汇。banana republic（香蕉共和国，指政治不稳定的小国）是重要引申词组。
🧠 助记
ba-na-na → 三个音节像剥开香蕉 → 香蕉
banana::/bəˈnɑːnə/:n. 香蕉:Eating a banana before exercise provides quick energy.
10. bloom /bluːm/ v./n. 开花；繁荣；青春
📖 英文解释
To produce flowers; to be in or come into full beauty or health; a flower or mass of flowers; a healthy glow.
✏️ 例句
Cherry trees bloom in spring, attracting thousands of visitors.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考引申：in full bloom（盛开；全盛）；bloom with health（健康焕发）。
🧠 助记
bloom → 花朵绽放的样子 → 开花；繁荣
bloom::/bluːm/:v./n. 开花:The economy began to bloom after years of stagnation.
11. catastrophe /kəˈtæstrəfi/ n. 大灾难；大祸；惨败
📖 英文解释
An event causing great and often sudden damage or suffering; a disaster; a terrible failure.
✏️ 例句
The earthquake was the worst natural catastrophe in decades.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频词。environmental catastrophe（环境灾难）；avoid a catastrophe（避免灾难）。
🧠 助记
cata（向下）+ strophe（转）→ 向下急转 → 大灾难
catastrophe::/kəˈtæstrəfi/:n. 大灾难:Climate change could lead to an ecological catastrophe.
12. certify /ˈsɜːtɪfaɪ/ v. 证明；认证；颁发证书
📖 英文解释
To attest or confirm in a formal statement; to officially recognize as possessing certain qualifications or meeting certain standards.
✏️ 例句
The inspector certified that the building met all safety standards.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研正式词汇。certified（持证的）；certificate（证书）是同根词。certify sb. as（认证某人为…）。
🧠 助记
certi（确定）+ fy → 使确定 → 证明；认证
certify::/ˈsɜːtɪfaɪ/:v. 证明:The lab was certified to handle hazardous materials.
13. collide /kəˈlaɪd/ v. 碰撞；冲突
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
Two vehicles collided at the intersection due to icy road conditions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常见词。collide with（与…碰撞）；interests collide（利益冲突）。
🧠 助记

collide::/kəˈlaɪd/:v. 碰撞:The two theories collide on the question of human nature.
14. cord /kɔːd/ n. 绳索；电线；纽带
📖 英文解释
A length of strong flexible material made from twisted strands; an electrical flex; a part of the body resembling a rope.

The technician connected the device using a long extension cord.

考研常见词。spinal cord（脊髓）；vocal cord（声带）；umbilical cord（脐带）。
🧠 助记
cord → chord（和弦，也像绳子拨弦）→ 绳索；电线
cord::/kɔːd/:n. 绳索:The surgeon carefully avoided the spinal cord during the operation.

denial::/dɪˈnaɪəl/:n. 否认:His flat denial of involvement failed to convince the investigators.

17. lemon /ˈlemən/ n. 柠檬；劣质品；骗局

She squeezed a lemon into the water for extra flavor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研词汇，注意引申：lemon（有缺陷的产品）在消费者法律中是重要概念。

lemon::/ˈlemən/:n. 柠檬:Consumer protection laws help buyers who accidentally purchase a lemon.
18. ounce /aʊns/ n. 盎司（重量单位）；少量

A unit of weight equal to one sixteenth of a pound (28.35 grams); a very small amount of something.
✏️ 例句
Every ounce of effort counts when preparing for a major exam.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研词汇。not an ounce of（一点也没有）；every ounce（每一分）。
🧠 助记
ounce → once（一次）谐音 → 很小的一次量 → 盎司；少量
ounce::/aʊns/:n. 盎司（重量单位）:She fought with every ounce of strength she had.

pedestrian::/pəˈdestriən/:n. 行人 adj. 步行的:The city created more pedestrian zones to reduce traffic congestion.

25. slope /sləʊp/ n. 坡度；斜面 v. 倾斜
📖 英文解释
A surface of which one end or side is at a higher level than another; an upward or downward inclination; to have such an inclination.
✏️ 例句
The slippery slope argument suggests one action leads to worse consequences.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常见词。slippery slope（滑坡谬误/危险趋势）；slope of the curve（曲线斜率）。
🧠 助记
slope → s（蛇形）+ lope（跑）→ 在斜坡上跑 → 坡度；倾斜
slope::/sləʊp/:n. 坡度:The argument represents a classic slippery slope fallacy.
