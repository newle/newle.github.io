1. sob /sɒb/ v. 哭泣；抽泣 n. 哭泣声
📖 英文解释
To weep with convulsive catching of the breath; to make a sound like a sob; a convulsive drawing in of breath when crying.
✏️ 例句
She sobbed quietly in the corner after hearing the bad news.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研情感描写词。sob story（悲惨故事；博同情的故事）；sob one's heart out（放声大哭）。
🧠 助记
sob → 'so bad'（太糟糕了）→ 糟到哭出来 → 哭泣；抽泣
sob::/sɒb/:v. 哭泣:The child sobbed after losing her favorite toy.
2. warfare /ˈwɔːfeə(r)/ n. 战争；战斗；冲突
📖 英文解释
Engagement in or the activities involved in war or conflict; a sustained effort to stop or defeat an enemy or problem.
✏️ 例句
Cyber warfare has become a major concern for national security.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研军事、国际关系词汇。guerrilla warfare（游击战）；economic warfare（经济战）。
🧠 助记
war（战争）+ fare（进行）→ 战争的进行 → 战争；战斗
warfare::/ˈwɔːfeə(r)/:n. 战争:Modern warfare increasingly relies on technology and cyber capabilities.
3. juvenile /ˈdʒuːvənaɪl/ adj. 青少年的；幼稚的 n. 青少年
📖 英文解释
Relating to young people; childish and immature; a young person below the age of full legal responsibility.
✏️ 例句
The juvenile offender was sent to a rehabilitation center, not prison.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研法律、社会词汇。juvenile delinquency（青少年犯罪）；juvenile court（少年法庭）。
🧠 助记
juven（年轻）+ ile → 年轻的 → 青少年的；幼稚的
juvenile::/ˈdʒuːvənaɪl/:adj. 青少年的:The new policy aims to reduce juvenile crime through education.
4. pilgrim /ˈpɪlɡrɪm/ n. 朝圣者；香客；旅行者
📖 英文解释
A person who journeys to a sacred place for religious reasons; any person on a long journey.
✏️ 例句
Every year, millions of pilgrims travel to Mecca for the Hajj.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化类词汇。the Pilgrim Fathers（五月花号的清教徒先辈）；pilgrimage（朝圣之旅）。
🧠 助记
pilgrim → 'pil'（走路）+ grim（严肃）→ 严肃地走很远的路 → 朝圣者
pilgrim::/ˈpɪlɡrɪm/:n. 朝圣者:The pilgrims undertook a difficult journey to reach the holy site.
5. stadium /ˈsteɪdiəm/ n. 体育场；运动场
📖 英文解释
An athletic or sports arena with tiered seating for spectators.
✏️ 例句
The new stadium can accommodate up to 80,000 spectators.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研体育、城市建设词汇。stadium effect（球场效应）；purpose-built stadium（专用体育场）。
🧠 助记
stad（站立/立柱）+ ium → 有很多立柱的大场所 → 体育场
stadium::/ˈsteɪdiəm/:n. 体育场:The Olympic stadium was filled to capacity during the opening ceremony.
6. evade /ɪˈveɪd/ v. 逃避；规避；回避
📖 英文解释
To escape or avoid, especially by cleverness or trickery; to fail to respond honestly to.
✏️ 例句
The politician evaded the reporter's direct question about the scandal.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频词。evade tax（逃税）；evade responsibility（逃避责任）；evade the issue（回避问题）。
🧠 助记
e（出）+ vade（走）→ 走出去 → 逃避；回避
evade::/ɪˈveɪd/:v. 逃避:The company was fined for evading environmental regulations.
7. saturate /ˈsætʃəreɪt/ v. 使饱和；浸透；充斥
📖 英文解释
To soak thoroughly with liquid; to cause (a substance) to combine with the maximum possible amount of another substance; to fill completely.
✏️ 例句
The market was saturated with cheap imports, hurting local producers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研经济、化学类词汇。saturate the market（充斥市场）；saturated fat（饱和脂肪）。
🧠 助记
satur（满）+ ate → 使充满饱和 → 浸透；充斥
saturate::/ˈsætʃəreɪt/:v. 使饱和:The advertising campaign saturated every available media channel.
8. token /ˈtəʊkən/ n. 象征；代币；标志 adj. 象征性的
📖 英文解释
A thing serving as a visible or tangible representation of a fact, quality, or feeling; something used as a substitute for money; a symbolic gesture.
✏️ 例句
The company made only a token gesture toward environmental responsibility.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：token gesture（象征性姿态）；by the same token（同样道理）；token effort（敷衍性努力）。
🧠 助记
token → 只是个'标记'而已 → 象征；象征性的
token::/ˈtəʊkən/:n. 象征:The apology was seen as a mere token gesture with no real change.
9. conscience /ˈkɒnʃəns/ n. 良心；良知；道德感
📖 英文解释
An inner feeling or voice viewed as acting as a guide to the rightness or wrongness of one's behavior.
✏️ 例句
His conscience would not allow him to stay silent about the injustice.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频词。have a clear conscience（问心无愧）；guilty conscience（内疚）；prick of conscience（良心不安）。
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ science（知识）→ 内心共同的道德知识 → 良心
conscience::/ˈkɒnʃəns/:n. 良心:She could not in good conscience ignore the suffering around her.
10. continual /kənˈtɪnjuəl/ adj. 不断的；频繁的；持续的
📖 英文解释
Forming a sequence in which the same action or event is repeated frequently.
✏️ 例句
The teacher was frustrated by the students' continual interruptions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研易混词：continual（反复发生的）vs continuous（连续不断的，无间断）。
🧠 助记
continu（继续）+ al → 继续不停地 → 不断的（有间隔的重复）
continual::/kənˈtɪnjuəl/:adj. 不断的:Continual changes in policy create uncertainty for businesses.
11. diligent /ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/ adj. 勤奋的；用功的；刻苦的
📖 英文解释
Having or showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties; hardworking.
✏️ 例句
Her diligent study habits helped her achieve top marks in every subject.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研写作高频词。diligent student/worker（勤奋的学生/工作者）。名词diligence（勤奋）更常考。
🧠 助记
dili（努力）+ gent（人）→ 努力工作的人 → 勤奋的
diligent::/ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/:adj. 勤奋的:Diligent practice is the key to mastering any skill.
12. exemplify /ɪɡˈzemplɪfaɪ/ v. 举例说明；作为典范；例证
📖 英文解释
To be a typical example of; to illustrate by giving an example.
✏️ 例句
His behavior exemplifies the qualities expected of a leader.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频词。exemplify a principle/trend（举例说明原则/趋势）。常用于学术写作。
🧠 助记
example（例子）+ ify → 用例子说明 → 举例说明；作为典范
exemplify::/ɪɡˈzemplɪfaɪ/:v. 举例说明:The project exemplifies how technology can improve public services.
13. hail /heɪl/ v. 欢呼；招呼；冰雹打 n. 冰雹；欢呼
📖 英文解释
To greet with enthusiasm; to signal or call to; frozen rain falling as small pellets; a large number of things hurled forcefully.
✏️ 例句
Critics hailed the film as a masterpiece of modern cinema.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研双义词。hail sb./sth. as（称赞…为）；a hail of bullets（一阵弹雨）。
🧠 助记
hail → 'hi!'（打招呼）→ 热情欢迎；冰雹也'打'下来 → 欢呼；冰雹
hail::/heɪl/:v. 欢呼:The new policy was hailed as a breakthrough in environmental protection.

numerical::/njuːˈmerɪkl/:adj. 数字的:The report included numerical evidence to support its conclusions.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

orderly::/ˈɔːdəli/:adj. 有秩序的:An orderly transition of power is essential for political stability.
16. warrant /ˈwɒrənt/ v. 正当化；保证 n. 授权令；搜查令
📖 英文解释
To justify or necessitate a course of action; to officially authorize; a document authorizing police to search premises or arrest someone.
✏️ 例句
The police obtained a search warrant before entering the premises.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研法律类高频词。arrest/search warrant（逮捕令/搜查令）；warrant attention（值得关注）。
🧠 助记
warrant → guard（保护）→ 有保证/授权 → 正当化；搜查令
warrant::/ˈwɒrənt/:v. 正当化:The level of public interest warrants further investigation.
17. comprise /kəmˈpraɪz/ v. 由…构成；包括；组成
📖 英文解释
To consist of; to make up or constitute.
✏️ 例句
The committee comprises twelve members from various departments.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频词，注意用法：A comprises B（A包含B，主动态），不用'is comprised of'（避免）。
🧠 助记
com（全部）+ prise（抓取）→ 把所有部分抓在一起 → 由…构成
comprise::/kəmˈpraɪz/:v. 由…构成:The research team comprises experts from five different countries.

dental::/ˈdentl/:adj. 牙科的:Good dental hygiene includes brushing teeth twice daily.

The documentary captured the authentic flavor of life in rural China.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

flavor::/ˈfleɪvə(r)/:n. 味道:The essay captured the unique flavor of life in a small town.
20. kilogram /ˈkɪləɡræm/ n. 千克；公斤
📖 英文解释
A unit of mass equal to one thousand grams; the base unit of mass in the International System of Units.
✏️ 例句
The package weighed five kilograms and required extra postage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研词汇。kilogram是国际通用计量单位。per kilogram（每公斤）。
🧠 助记
kilo（千）+ gram（克）→ 一千克 → 千克；公斤
kilogram::/ˈkɪləɡræm/:n. 千克:The athlete's diet is carefully planned down to every kilogram of body weight.
21. preach /priːtʃ/ v. 布道；说教；传道
📖 英文解释
To deliver a sermon or religious address; to earnestly advocate something; to give moral advice in an annoying way.
✏️ 例句
The politician was accused of preaching to voters rather than listening.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研词汇。preach to the choir（向已信服的人说教，白费力气）；practice what you preach（身体力行）。
🧠 助记
preach → 'pre'（先）+ ach（说）→ 提前说好道理 → 布道；说教
preach::/priːtʃ/:v. 布道:It is easy to preach patience, but hard to practice it.
22. presently /ˈprezntli/ adv. 目前；现在；不久；一会儿
📖 英文解释
At the present time; now; in the near future; soon.
✏️ 例句
The director is presently reviewing the proposed changes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研词汇。注意英美差异：美式用'presently'表'现在'；英式常表'不久'。
🧠 助记
present（现在/礼物）+ ly → 在现在这个时候 → 目前；现在
presently::/ˈprezntli/:adv. 目前:The project is presently under review by senior management.
23. prose /prəʊz/ n. 散文；平淡的文字
📖 英文解释
Written or spoken language in its ordinary form, without metrical structure; plain or dull writing.
✏️ 例句
His prose style is admired for its clarity and precision.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文学类词汇。prose vs poetry（散文 vs 诗歌）；purple prose（华丽冗长的散文）。
🧠 助记
prose → 'pro'（专业）+ se → 专业规范的文字（非诗歌）→ 散文
prose::/prəʊz/:n. 散文:The novel is praised for its lyrical prose and vivid imagery.
24. shark /ʃɑːk/ n. 鲨鱼；骗子；高手
📖 英文解释
A large predatory fish with a cartilaginous skeleton; a person who unscrupulously exploits others; an expert in a particular field.
✏️ 例句
Loan sharks charge extremely high interest rates to vulnerable borrowers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多义词。loan shark（高利贷者）；card shark（牌技高手/骗局）；corporate sharks（商业掠夺者）。
🧠 助记
shark → 鲨鱼凶猛掠夺 → 骗子；高手（也掠夺式地赢）
shark::/ʃɑːk/:n. 鲨鱼:The documentary exposed how loan sharks prey on desperate families.
25. thirteen /ˌθɜːˈtiːn/ num. 十三
📖 英文解释
The number equivalent to the sum of six and seven; one more than twelve.
✏️ 例句
Thirteen original colonies united to form the United States.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研词汇。西方文化中13被认为不吉利（thirteen is unlucky）。baker's dozen（13个）是重要文化背景。
🧠 助记
thir（three，三）+ teen（十几）→ 十三
thirteen::/ˌθɜːˈtiːn/:num. 十三:Thirteen countries signed the historic environmental agreement.
