1. vertical /ˈvɜːrtɪkl/ adj. 垂直的；竖直的
📖 英文解释
At right angles to a horizontal plane; upright; going straight up.
✏️ 例句
The cliff face was almost vertical, making the climb extremely dangerous.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
vertical line 垂线；vertical integration 垂直整合（经济）；vertical axis 纵轴
🧠 助记
vert（转/竖）+ ical，竖着的方向就是垂直
vertical::/ˈvɜːrtɪkl/:adj. 垂直的:The building's vertical walls cast long shadows at sunset.
2. administer /ədˈmɪnɪstər/ v. 管理；执行；给予（药物）
📖 英文解释
To manage and be responsible for the running of an organization; to give someone medicine or a drug.
✏️ 例句
The nurse administered the vaccine to each patient carefully.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
administer a country 治理国家；administer an oath 主持宣誓；administration n.
🧠 助记
ad + minister（部长）= 做部长 = 管理
administer::/ədˈmɪnɪstər/:v. 管理:The doctor administered a dose of antibiotics to the patient.
3. aspire /əˈspaɪər/ v. 立志；渴望；有志于
📖 英文解释
To direct one's hopes or ambitions towards achieving something.
✏️ 例句
She aspires to become a doctor and help people in need.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
aspire to do 立志做……；aspire after 追求；aspiration n. 志向
🧠 助记
a + spire（尖顶）= 向着尖顶爬 = 渴望达到顶端
aspire::/əˈspaɪər/:v. 立志:Many young athletes aspire to compete in the Olympics.
4. attorney /əˈtɜːrni/ n. 律师；代理人
📖 英文解释
A lawyer, especially one representing a client in a court of law; a person appointed to act on behalf of another.
✏️ 例句
The defendant hired an experienced attorney to defend his case.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
district attorney 地区检察官；attorney general 司法部长；power of attorney 委托书
🧠 助记
attorney = at + torn + ey，被撕扯（torn）的案件需要律师
attorney::/əˈtɜːrni/:n. 律师:She consulted her attorney before signing the contract.
5. classification /ˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ n. 分类；类别；等级划分
📖 英文解释
The action or process of classifying something according to shared qualities or characteristics.
✏️ 例句
The library uses a classification system to organize all its books.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
classification of species 物种分类；job classification 职位分类；classify v.
🧠 助记
class（类）+ ification（化的过程）= 分类化 = 分类
classification::/ˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/:n. 分类:The scientist proposed a new classification for the newly discovered plant.
6. declaration /ˌdekləˈreɪʃn/ n. 宣告；声明；申报
📖 英文解释
A formal or explicit statement or announcement; a document formally announcing something.
✏️ 例句
The Declaration of Independence was signed in 1776.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
declaration of war 宣战；customs declaration 海关申报；declare v.
🧠 助记
declare（宣布）+ ation = 宣布的行为 = 声明
declaration::/ˌdekləˈreɪʃn/:n. 宣告:The government issued a declaration of emergency after the disaster.
7. editorial /ˌedɪˈtɔːriəl/ n./adj. 社论；编辑的
📖 英文解释
A newspaper article written by or on behalf of an editor that gives an opinion on a topical issue; relating to editing.
✏️ 例句
The newspaper's editorial criticized the government's new policy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
editorial board 编委会；editorial policy 编辑方针；editorial office 编辑部
🧠 助记
edit（编辑）+ orial = 编辑相关的 = 社论/编辑的
editorial::/ˌedɪˈtɔːriəl/:n./adj. 社论:The editorial called for urgent reforms in the education system.
8. explicit /ɪkˈsplɪsɪt/ adj. 明确的；直接的；清晰的
📖 英文解释
Stated clearly and in detail, leaving no room for confusion or doubt.
✏️ 例句
The instructions were explicit about how to assemble the furniture.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be explicit about 明确表达；explicit content 露骨内容；explicitly adv.
🧠 助记
ex（出来）+ plicit（折叠）= 把折叠的内容展开 = 明确清晰
explicit::/ɪkˈsplɪsɪt/:adj. 明确的:The contract contained explicit terms regarding payment deadlines.
9. fruitful /ˈfruːtfl/ adj. 富有成效的；卓有成效的
📖 英文解释
Producing good or helpful results; productive.
✏️ 例句
The negotiations proved fruitful, resulting in a new trade agreement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fruitful discussion 富有成效的讨论；fruitful cooperation 卓有成效的合作
🧠 助记
fruit（果实）+ ful（满）= 结满果实 = 富有成效
fruitful::/ˈfruːtfl/:adj. 富有成效的:Their research collaboration turned out to be very fruitful.
10. meantime /ˈmiːntaɪm/ n./adv. 同时；与此同时
📖 英文解释
The period of time between two events; at the same time.
✏️ 例句
Dinner will be ready in an hour; in the meantime, let's watch a movie.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in the meantime 与此同时；for the meantime 暂时地；meanwhile 同义词
🧠 助记
mean（中间）+ time（时间）= 中间的时间 = 同时/期间
meantime::/ˈmiːntaɪm/:n./adv. 同时:The report will be finished by Friday; in the meantime, keep working.
11. nightmare /ˈnaɪtmeər/ n. 噩梦；可怕的经历
📖 英文解释
A frightening or unpleasant dream; an experience that is extremely unpleasant.
✏️ 例句
The accident was a nightmare that she couldn't forget.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
have a nightmare 做噩梦；nightmare scenario 最坏情况；living nightmare 可怕的现实
🧠 助记
night（夜晚）+ mare（女妖/母马）= 夜晚的恶魔 = 噩梦
nightmare::/ˈnaɪtmeər/:n. 噩梦:Dealing with the bureaucracy became a complete nightmare.
12. ornament /ˈɔːrnəmənt/ n./v. 装饰；装饰品；点缀
📖 英文解释
A thing used to make something look more attractive; to make more attractive by adding decorative items.
✏️ 例句
The Christmas ornaments sparkled on the tree.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ornament a building 装饰建筑；garden ornament 园林装饰物；ornamental adj.
🧠 助记
ornam + ent，想象'ornament'像一件精美的艺术品装饰房间
ornament::/ˈɔːrnəmənt/:n./v. 装饰:She placed a delicate porcelain ornament on the mantelpiece.
13. perpetual /pərˈpetʃuəl/ adj. 永久的；永恒的；持续不断的
📖 英文解释
Never ending or changing; occurring repeatedly; so frequent as to seem endless.
✏️ 例句
The prisoners suffered perpetual hunger and cold.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
perpetual motion 永动机；perpetual peace 永久和平；perpetually adv.
🧠 助记
per（完全）+ pet + ual，完全抚摸（pet）不停 = 永久不断
perpetual::/pərˈpetʃuəl/:adj. 永久的:She lived in perpetual fear of losing her job.
14. pregnant /ˈpreɡnənt/ adj. 怀孕的；富有意义的
📖 英文解释
Having a child or young developing inside the womb; full of meaning or significance.
✏️ 例句
The doctor confirmed that she was three months pregnant.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pregnant pause 意味深长的停顿；pregnant with meaning 含义深远；pregnancy n.
🧠 助记
preg + nant，preg 像 press（压），肚子被压着 = 怀孕
pregnant::/ˈpreɡnənt/:adj. 怀孕的:Her words were pregnant with unspoken emotion.
15. rally /ˈræli/ v./n. 集会；集结；恢复（精力）
📖 英文解释
To bring or come together in support of a cause; a mass meeting of people making a political protest or showing support.
✏️ 例句
Thousands of supporters rallied in the city centre to demand change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rally support 争取支持；political rally 政治集会；rally around 团结在……周围
🧠 助记
rally 和 really 发音近，真的（really）聚集在一起 = 集会
rally::/ˈræli/:v./n. 集会:The team managed to rally in the second half and win the game.
16. rebel /ˈrebl/ (n.) /rɪˈbel/ (v.) n./v. 反叛者；反抗；造反
📖 英文解释
A person who resists authority or control; to resist or fight against authority.
✏️ 例句
The rebels captured the capital city after months of fighting.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rebel against 反抗；rebel forces 叛军；rebellion n. 叛乱
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ bel（战争词根）= 再次开战 = 反叛
rebel::/ˈrebl/ (n.) /rɪˈbel/ (v.):n./v. 反叛者:The teenagers often rebel against their parents' strict rules.
17. successive /səkˈsesɪv/ adj. 连续的；相继的
📖 英文解释
Following in order without interruption; coming one after another.
✏️ 例句
The team won five successive championships.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
successive years 连续几年；successive governments 历届政府；in succession 连续地
🧠 助记
success（成功）+ ive，连续成功 = 相继的
successive::/səkˈsesɪv/:adj. 连续的:The region experienced three successive droughts.
18. tin /tɪn/ n. 锡；罐头；马口铁
📖 英文解释
A silvery-white metal; a sealed container made of tin plate for preserving food.
✏️ 例句
She opened a tin of sardines for a quick lunch.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tin can 罐头；tin foil 锡箔；tin of paint 一罐油漆
🧠 助记
tin 发音像'听'，锡罐发出'叮'的声音
tin::/tɪn/:n. 锡:They stored the biscuits in a tin to keep them fresh.
19. warehouse /ˈweərhaʊs/ n. 仓库；货仓
📖 英文解释
A large building used for storing goods before they are distributed or sold.
✏️ 例句
The company rents a large warehouse to store its inventory.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
warehouse management 仓库管理；bonded warehouse 保税仓库；warehouse worker 仓库工人
🧠 助记
ware（商品）+ house（房子）= 放商品的房子 = 仓库
warehouse::/ˈweərhaʊs/:n. 仓库:The goods were shipped directly from the factory to the warehouse.
20. wit /wɪt/ n. 机智；才智；风趣
📖 英文解释
The capacity for inventive thought and quick understanding; keen intelligence; the ability to say clever and funny things.
✏️ 例句
His wit and humour made him popular at parties.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
have the wit to 有足够的智慧去；witty adj. 机智的；at one's wit's end 智穷计尽
🧠 助记
wit 和 bit（一点点）押韵，有一点点聪明 = 机智
wit::/wɪt/:n. 机智:Her sharp wit helped her navigate the difficult negotiation.
21. arithmetic /əˈrɪθmətɪk/ n. 算术；计算
📖 英文解释
The branch of mathematics dealing with the properties and manipulation of numbers.
✏️ 例句
Basic arithmetic skills are essential for everyday life.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
mental arithmetic 心算；arithmetic mean 算术平均数；arithmetic progression 等差数列
🧠 助记
arith + metic，想象'算术'就是量度（metric）的一种
arithmetic::/əˈrɪθmətɪk/:n. 算术:The student struggled with arithmetic but excelled in geometry.
22. axis /ˈæksɪs/ n. 轴；轴线；坐标轴
📖 英文解释
An imaginary line about which a body rotates; a fixed reference line for measurement.
✏️ 例句
The Earth rotates on its axis once every 24 hours.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
x-axis / y-axis 横轴/纵轴；axis of symmetry 对称轴；axes pl.
🧠 助记
axis = 斧（ax）+ is，斧头的柄就是一条轴线
axis::/ˈæksɪs/:n. 轴:The graph shows the data plotted along both the x-axis and y-axis.
23. brisk /brɪsk/ adj. 轻快的；活跃的；凛冽的
📖 英文解释
Active, fast, and energetic; (of weather) cold and fresh; (of business) lively.
✏️ 例句
She took a brisk walk in the park every morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
brisk walk 轻快散步；brisk trade 旺盛的交易；brisk wind 清冽的风
🧠 助记
brisk 里有 risk，轻快冒险才有活力
brisk::/brɪsk/:adj. 轻快的:Business was brisk at the market on Saturday morning.
24. cage /keɪdʒ/ n./v. 笼子；囚禁

A structure of bars or wires in which birds or other animals are confined; to confine in a cage.
✏️ 例句
The lion paced back and forth in its cage at the zoo.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
bird cage 鸟笼；cage in 囚禁；feel caged 感到被束缚
🧠 助记
cage 发音像'困（了）'，笼子把动物困住
cage::/keɪdʒ/:n./v. 笼子:The activists protested against keeping animals in cages.
25. casualty /ˈkæʒuəlti/ n. 伤亡者；事故受害者；伤亡
📖 英文解释
A person killed or injured in a war or accident; an accident or disaster.
✏️ 例句
The earthquake caused thousands of casualties.

casualty list 伤亡名单；civilian casualties 平民伤亡；casualty department 急诊室
🧠 助记
casual（随意的）+ ty，随意发生的事故 = 伤亡事故
casualty::/ˈkæʒuəlti/:n. 伤亡者:The battle resulted in heavy casualties on both sides.
