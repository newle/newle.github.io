1. arrogant /ˈærəɡənt/ adj. 傲慢的；自大的
📖 英文解释
Having or revealing an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities.
✏️ 例句
His arrogant attitude made it hard for his colleagues to work with him.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
arrogant behaviour 傲慢的行为；arrogantly adv.；arrogance n. 傲慢
🧠 助记
arrog（要求）+ ant = 不断要求高人一等 = 傲慢的
arrogant::/ˈærəɡənt/:adj. 傲慢的:The arrogant manager refused to listen to his team's suggestions.
2. candle /ˈkændl/ n. 蜡烛
📖 英文解释
A cylinder or block of wax with a central wick which is lit to produce light as it burns.
✏️ 例句
She lit a candle to create a romantic atmosphere for dinner.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
candlelight 烛光；candle holder 蜡烛台；not hold a candle to 远不及
🧠 助记
candle = can（能）+ dle，能够在黑暗中点亮 = 蜡烛
candle::/ˈkændl/:n. 蜡烛:The power went out and they lit candles to illuminate the room.
3. closet /ˈklɒzɪt/ n. 壁橱；小房间；密室
📖 英文解释
A small room or recess for storing clothes or household items; a small private room.
✏️ 例句
She kept all her winter clothes in the closet.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
closet space 橱柜空间；come out of the closet 公开秘密；closet romanticist 暗中浪漫主义者
🧠 助记
close（关闭）+ t = 关闭的小空间 = 壁橱
closet::/ˈklɒzɪt/:n. 壁橱:The bedroom had a large walk-in closet with plenty of storage space.
4. duplicate /ˈdjuːplɪkeɪt/ v./n./adj. 复制；重复；副本；复制的
📖 英文解释
To make or be an exact copy of; corresponding exactly to another; a copy or double.
✏️ 例句
Please make a duplicate of the document and keep it for your records.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
duplicate copy 副本；in duplicate 一式两份；duplicate key 备用钥匙
🧠 助记
dupli（双）+ cate = 做成双份 = 复制
duplicate::/ˈdjuːplɪkeɪt/:v./n./adj. 复制:She was asked to fill in the form in duplicate.
5. evaporate /ɪˈvæpəreɪt/ v. 蒸发；消散；逐渐消失
📖 英文解释
To turn from liquid into vapour; to cease to exist.
✏️ 例句
Water evaporates more quickly on hot sunny days.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
evaporate quickly 迅速蒸发；hopes evaporate 希望消散；evaporation n.
🧠 助记
e + vapor（蒸气）+ ate = 变成蒸气 = 蒸发
evaporate::/ɪˈvæpəreɪt/:v. 蒸发:His enthusiasm for the project evaporated when he heard the budget was cut.
6. ferry /ˈferi/ n./v. 渡船；渡口；摆渡
📖 英文解释
A boat that carries people and vehicles across water; to transport by boat.
✏️ 例句
They took the ferry to cross the river to the other side.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ferry boat 渡船；car ferry 汽车渡轮；ferry across 摆渡过河
🧠 助记
ferry 发音像'发利'，渡船来回发利润
ferry::/ˈferi/:n./v. 渡船:The ferry service was suspended due to rough weather conditions.
7. gossip /ˈɡɒsɪp/ n./v. 闲话；流言蜚语；说闲话
📖 英文解释
Casual or unconstrained conversation about other people involving details that are not confirmed as true; to engage in gossip.
✏️ 例句
Office gossip can be harmful to the working environment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
spread gossip 散布流言；gossip column 八卦专栏；gossip about sb 说某人闲话
🧠 助记
gossip = go + ssip（嘶嘶声），走来走去发出嘶嘶声 = 流言蜚语
gossip::/ˈɡɒsɪp/:n./v. 闲话:She refused to engage in gossip about her colleagues.
8. hopeful /ˈhəʊpfl/ adj. 有希望的；乐观的；有前途的
📖 英文解释
Feeling or inspiring optimism about a future event.
✏️ 例句
Despite the difficulties, she remained hopeful that things would improve.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be hopeful of 对……抱有希望；hopeful sign 有希望的迹象；hopeful candidate 有望成功的候选人
🧠 助记
hope（希望）+ ful = 充满希望的 = 有希望的
hopeful::/ˈhəʊpfl/:adj. 有希望的:The team was hopeful of making it to the finals.
9. immerse /ɪˈmɜːrs/ v. 浸；沉浸于；全身心投入
📖 英文解释
To dip or submerge in a liquid; to involve oneself deeply in a particular activity.
✏️ 例句
She immersed herself in work to keep her mind off her problems.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
immerse in water 浸入水中；immerse oneself in 沉浸于；immersion n.
🧠 助记
im + merse（浸），浸入其中 = 沉浸
immerse::/ɪˈmɜːrs/:v. 浸:He immersed himself in the study of Chinese history for several years.
10. impart /ɪmˈpɑːrt/ v. 传授；给予；赋予
📖 英文解释
To make information known; to pass on knowledge; to give a particular quality to.
✏️ 例句
The professor imparted his knowledge of physics to generations of students.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
impart knowledge 传授知识；impart information 传递信息；impart flavour 赋予风味
🧠 助记
im + part（部分）= 把自己的一部分给出去 = 传授
impart::/ɪmˈpɑːrt/:v. 传授:A good teacher is able to impart complex ideas in a simple way.
11. intimidate /ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪt/ v. 恐吓；威胁；使胆怯
📖 英文解释
To frighten someone in order to persuade them to do what one wants.
✏️ 例句
The suspect was accused of trying to intimidate witnesses.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
intimidate sb into 恐吓某人做……；feel intimidated 感到害怕；intimidation n.
🧠 助记
in + timid（胆怯）+ ate = 让人变得胆怯 = 恐吓
intimidate::/ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪt/:v. 恐吓:She refused to be intimidated by his aggressive behaviour.
12. neat /niːt/ adj. 整洁的；整齐的；巧妙的
📖 英文解释
Tidy and in order; done with skill and precision; (informal) clever.
✏️ 例句
His desk was always neat and well-organised.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
neat handwriting 整齐的笔迹；neat solution 巧妙的解决方案；neatly adv.
🧠 助记
neat = 'N'整齐，字母N代表整洁排列
neat::/niːt/:adj. 整洁的:She kept a neat record of all her expenses in a small notebook.
13. omit /əʊˈmɪt/ v. 省略；遗漏；忽略
📖 英文解释
To leave out or exclude; to fail to do something.
✏️ 例句
He omitted several important details from his report.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
omit from 从……中省略；omit to do 忘记做；omission n. 遗漏
🧠 助记
o + mit（发送），没有发出 = 省略/遗漏
omit::/əʊˈmɪt/:v. 省略:She accidentally omitted a key paragraph from the final draft.
14. prosper /ˈprɒspər/ v. 繁荣；兴旺；成功
📖 英文解释
To flourish or succeed, especially financially; to thrive.
✏️ 例句
The business began to prosper after they expanded into new markets.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prosper in 在……中兴旺；prosperity n. 繁荣；prosperous adj. 繁荣的
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ sper（希望）= 朝着希望前进 = 繁荣
prosper::/ˈprɒspər/:v. 繁荣:The region began to prosper after the discovery of oil.
15. scarce /skeərs/ adj. 稀少的；缺乏的；罕见的
📖 英文解释
Insufficient for the demand; not easily found.
✏️ 例句
Clean water is scarce in many parts of the developing world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
scarce resources 稀缺资源；make oneself scarce 悄悄离开；scarcity n.
🧠 助记
scarce = scar（疤）+ ce，资源稀缺像留下疤痕一样
scarce::/skeərs/:adj. 稀少的:Jobs became increasingly scarce during the economic recession.
16. thunder /ˈθʌndər/ n./v. 雷声；打雷；怒吼
📖 英文解释
A loud rumbling or crashing noise heard after a lightning flash due to the expansion of air; to sound with thunder.
✏️ 例句
The thunder was so loud it woke everyone in the house.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
thunder and lightning 雷电；thunder of applause 雷鸣般的掌声；steal sb's thunder 抢某人的风头
🧠 助记
thunder 发音像'探的'，探空发出轰隆声 = 雷声
thunder::/ˈθʌndər/:n./v. 雷声:Dark clouds gathered and thunder rolled in the distance.
17. timber /ˈtɪmbər/ n. 木材；木料；栋梁之材
📖 英文解释
Wood prepared for use in building and carpentry; trees suitable for such use.
✏️ 例句
The roof was constructed using heavy timber beams.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
timber industry 木材工业；standing timber 立木；timber merchant 木材商
🧠 助记
tim + ber，想象'lumber（木材）'的近义词
timber::/ˈtɪmbər/:n. 木材:Deforestation is causing a serious shortage of timber resources.
18. accessory /əkˈsesəri/ n./adj. 配件；附件；从犯；附属的
📖 英文解释
A thing that can be added to something to make it more useful or attractive; a person who helps in a crime.
✏️ 例句
She bought several accessories to complement her new outfit.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fashion accessory 时尚配饰；accessory to a crime 共犯；computer accessory 电脑配件
🧠 助记
access（接触）+ ory = 接触到主物的附属品 = 配件
accessory::/əkˈsesəri/:n./adj. 配件:The handbag came with a set of matching accessories.
19. awe /ɔː/ n./v. 敬畏；惊叹；使敬畏
📖 英文解释
A feeling of reverential respect mixed with fear or wonder; to inspire awe in someone.
✏️ 例句
The majestic mountains filled the travellers with awe.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in awe of 对……敬畏；awe-inspiring 令人敬畏的；awesome adj. 令人敬畏的
🧠 助记
awe 发音像'哦！'惊叹 = 敬畏
awe::/ɔː/:n./v. 敬畏:The children stood in awe as the magician performed his tricks.
20. bath /bɑːθ/ n./v. 洗澡；浴缸；给……洗澡

She soaked in a warm bath to relax after a long day.

bath = 把（ba）水（th）装进浴缸
bath::/bɑːθ/:n./v. 洗澡:She gave the baby a bath before putting him to bed.
21. bully /ˈbʊli/ v./n. 欺负；欺凌；恃强凌弱者

bully::/ˈbʊli/:v./n. 欺负:She was bullied at school because of her appearance.
22. carriage /ˈkærɪdʒ/ n. 运输；四轮马车；(火车)车厢
📖 英文解释
A four-wheeled passenger vehicle pulled by horses; the transporting of goods from one place to another; a railway carriage.
✏️ 例句
They arrived in a golden carriage at the royal ceremony.

carriage::/ˈkærɪdʒ/:n. 运输:The train's first-class carriage was comfortable and spacious.
23. cube /kjuːb/ n./v. 立方体；立方；切成丁
📖 英文解释
A symmetrical three-dimensional shape with six equal square faces; the product of a number multiplied by its square.

🧠 助记
cube = 6个方块面的立方体，像积木
cube::/kjuːb/:n./v. 立方体:The mathematician calculated the cube of 12 as 1728.
24. elevator /ˈelɪveɪtər/ n. 电梯；升降机（美式）
📖 英文解释
A platform or compartment housed in a shaft for raising and lowering people or things to different floors (North American English).
✏️ 例句
Take the elevator to the 10th floor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
elevator pitch 简短推介；elevator music 背景音乐；glass elevator 观光电梯
🧠 助记
elevate（提升）+ or = 提升人的装置 = 电梯
elevator::/ˈelɪveɪtər/:n. 电梯:The building's elevator was out of service for two days.

📖 英文解释
A piece of equipment or furniture which is fixed in position; a person or thing established in a particular place.

The light fixtures in the hall needed to be replaced.

fixture::/ˈfɪkstʃər/:n. 固定装置:The match is one of the most important fixtures of the football season.
