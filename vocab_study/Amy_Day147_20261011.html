1. honey /ˈhʌni/ n. 蜂蜜；亲爱的 adj. 甜蜜的
📖 英文解释
A sweet, sticky yellow substance made by bees; a term of endearment for someone you love.
✏️ 例句
She stirred a spoonful of honey into her tea.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：honey bee（蜜蜂）；honeymoon（蜜月）；honey trap（美人计）；as sweet as honey（甜如蜂蜜）。
🧠 助记
honey 谐音'哈尼'—— 甜蜜的'哈尼'（honey），就像蜂蜜一样甜。
honey::/ˈhʌni/:n. 蜂蜜:The couple spent their honeymoon in the Swiss Alps.
2. infectious /ɪnˈfekʃəs/ adj. 传染性的；感染性的；有感染力的
📖 英文解释
Capable of being spread from person to person through direct or indirect contact; having an effect that spreads to others (e.g., infectious laughter).
✏️ 例句
Measles is highly infectious and can spread rapidly in unvaccinated populations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：infectious disease（传染病）；infectious laughter/enthusiasm（有感染力的笑声/热情）；highly infectious（高度传染性）。
🧠 助记
infect（感染）+ ious → 容易感染他人的 → 传染性的。联想：infection（感染）→ infectious（传染性的）。
infectious::/ɪnˈfekʃəs/:adj. 传染性的:Her infectious smile immediately put everyone in the room at ease.
3. lamb /læm/ n. 羔羊；小羊；温顺的人
📖 英文解释
A young sheep; the meat of a young sheep; a gentle or innocent person.
✏️ 例句
The farmer bottle-fed the orphaned lamb until it was strong enough.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：lamb chop（羊排）；like a lamb（温顺地）；lamb to the slaughter（毫无防备地送入险境）；the Lamb of God（上帝的羔羊）。
🧠 助记
lamb 谐音'来喔'—— 小羔羊（lamb）喊'来喔来喔'，温顺地走过来。注意 b 不发音。
lamb::/læm/:n. 羔羊:She accepted the challenge like a lamb, without question or resistance.
4. merchant /ˈmɜːtʃənt/ n. 商人；贸易商 adj. 商业的
📖 英文解释
A person who buys and sells goods, especially someone who trades in large quantities; a business person.
✏️ 例句
The Venetian merchants grew wealthy through trade with the East.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：merchant bank（商业银行）；merchant ship/navy（商船/商船队）；merchant class（商人阶层）。考研历史/经济类必备词。
🧠 助记
merch（商品，merchandise 的简写）+ ant → 做商品买卖的人 → 商人。
merchant::/ˈmɜːtʃənt/:n. 商人:The merchant traded silk and spices along the ancient Silk Road.
5. pitch /pɪtʃ/ n./v. 音调；沥青；球场；投；推销
📖 英文解释
The highness or lowness of a sound; a dark sticky substance used for waterproofing; a sports field; to throw; to try to persuade someone to buy or accept something.
✏️ 例句
The salesman made his pitch to potential investors at the conference.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：sales pitch（销售推介）；pitch perfect（音调完美）；football pitch（足球场）；pitch black（漆黑）；pitch in（合力）。
🧠 助记
pitch 联想音乐中的'音调'—— 投球（pitch）时也要控制力度和角度，像调整音调（pitch）一样。
pitch::/pɪtʃ/:n./v. 音调:The startup made a compelling pitch to the venture capital firm.
6. polar /ˈpəʊlə(r)/ adj. 极地的；两极的；截然相反的
📖 英文解释
Relating to the North or South Pole or the regions near them; directly opposite in character or tendency.
✏️ 例句
Polar bears are increasingly threatened by climate change and melting ice.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：polar bear（北极熊）；polar ice cap（极地冰盖）；polar opposites（截然相反）；polar region（极地地区）。
🧠 助记
polar = pole（极点）+ ar → 与极点相关的 → 极地的。polar bear 北极熊居住在极地（polar）。
polar::/ˈpəʊlə(r)/:adj. 极地的:The two candidates held polar opposite views on economic policy.
7. reassure /ˌriːəˈʃʊə(r)/ v. 使放心；安慰；消除疑虑
📖 英文解释
To say or do something to make someone feel less afraid or worried; to restore someone's confidence.
✏️ 例句
The doctor reassured the patient that the operation was routine and safe.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：reassure sb that…（使某人相信…）；reassuring smile/words（令人安心的微笑/话语）；reassurance（安慰，名词）。
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ assure（保证）→ 再次保证 → 使放心。assure → reassure：加强了安心感。
reassure::/ˌriːəˈʃʊə(r)/:v. 使放心:The manager reassured staff that no jobs would be lost in the restructuring.
8. sand /sænd/ n. 沙；沙地 v. 用砂纸打磨
📖 英文解释
A substance consisting of tiny grains of rock or mineral, found on beaches and in deserts; to smooth a surface with sandpaper.
✏️ 例句
The children built sandcastles on the beach all afternoon.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：quicksand（流沙）；sandy beach（沙滩）；sand dune（沙丘）；sand down（打磨）；built on sand（建立在不稳固的基础上）。
🧠 助记
sand 发音似'散'—— 沙子（sand）散散的，不成形，流动性强。
sand::/sænd/:n. 沙:The politician's promises were built on sand and soon collapsed.
9. scrape /skreɪp/ v./n. 刮；擦；勉强得到；困境
📖 英文解释
To rub a surface with something sharp or rough to remove a layer; to barely manage to achieve something; a difficult situation.
✏️ 例句
She scraped the frost off the car windshield before driving.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：scrape through（勉强通过）；scrape together（艰难凑齐）；scrape by（勉强维持生计）；get into a scrape（陷入困境）。
🧠 助记
scrape 发音像'斯克雷普'—— 用指甲'刮'东西时发出'刮—'的声音，就是 scrape。
scrape::/skreɪp/:v./n. 刮:He barely scraped through the final exam with a passing grade.
10. sorrow /ˈsɒrəʊ/ n. 悲伤；悲痛；遗憾
📖 英文解释
A feeling of deep sadness caused by loss, disappointment, or misfortune; something that causes unhappiness.
✏️ 例句
She felt deep sorrow when she heard about the death of her friend.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：deep/great sorrow（深深的悲伤）；drown one's sorrows（借酒消愁）；to one's sorrow（令某人悲痛的是）；joy and sorrow（喜怒哀乐）。
🧠 助记
sorrow 谐音'说了'—— 说了一件令人悲伤（sorrow）的事，令人悲痛。
sorrow::/ˈsɒrəʊ/:n. 悲伤:He expressed his deep sorrow over the tragic accident.
11. ugly /ˈʌɡli/ adj. 丑陋的；令人不快的；危险的
📖 英文解释
Unpleasant to look at; morally unpleasant; suggesting violence or danger.
✏️ 例句
The argument turned ugly when both sides started shouting insults.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：ugly duckling（丑小鸭）；ugly scene/situation（难看的局面）；rear its ugly head（再次出现令人不快的事物）。
🧠 助记
ugly 谐音'啊格利'—— 看到丑（ugly）东西会发出'啊'的感叹。联想丑小鸭变天鹅。
ugly::/ˈʌɡli/:adj. 丑陋的:The situation turned ugly as the protests grew more confrontational.
12. whoever /huːˈevə(r)/ pron. 无论谁；任何人
📖 英文解释
Any person who; no matter who; the person who (used to refer to any person).
✏️ 例句
Whoever finishes the task first will receive a bonus.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：whoever wins（无论谁赢）；whoever is responsible（无论是谁负责）。考研语法题和阅读中均常见。注意与 whomever（宾格）区别。
🧠 助记
who（谁）+ ever（任何时候）→ 任何时候是谁 → 无论谁。
whoever::/huːˈevə(r)/:pron. 无论谁:Whoever is found guilty will face serious legal consequences.
13. captive /ˈkæptɪv/ n. 俘虏；被囚者 adj. 被俘虏的；被圈养的
📖 英文解释
A person or animal that is kept in a place and is not free to leave; kept as a prisoner or in a restricted environment.
✏️ 例句
The hostages were held captive for three weeks before being released.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：hold captive（扣押）；captive audience（被迫收听的听众）；captive animal（圈养动物）；captivity（囚禁；圈养状态）。
🧠 助记
capt（抓住，capture）+ ive → 被抓住的 → 俘虏。capt-ure → capt-ive，同根词。
captive::/ˈkæptɪv/:n. 俘虏:The documentary showed captive animals struggling in unsuitable environments.
14. cosmic /ˈkɒzmɪk/ adj. 宇宙的；无限的；宏大的
📖 英文解释
Relating to the universe or cosmos; immeasurably large or vast; of great importance or significance.
✏️ 例句
Scientists study cosmic rays to learn more about the origin of the universe.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：cosmic ray（宇宙射线）；cosmic scale（宇宙尺度）；cosmic irony（宿命般的讽刺）；cosmos（宇宙，名词）。
🧠 助记
cosm（宇宙，cosmos）+ ic → 宇宙的。cosmos（宇宙）+ ic = cosmic（宇宙的）。
cosmic::/ˈkɒzmɪk/:adj. 宇宙的:The discovery was of cosmic significance to our understanding of physics.
15. cucumber /ˈkjuːkʌmbə(r)/ n. 黄瓜
📖 英文解释
A long, green vegetable with a watery flesh, often eaten raw in salads.
✏️ 例句
She sliced the cucumber thinly and added it to the Greek salad.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：cool as a cucumber（冷静沉着）；cucumber sandwich（黄瓜三明治，英式下午茶）；pickled cucumber（腌黄瓜）。
🧠 助记
cu-cum-ber：联想'酷（cu）可（cum）爸（ber）'—— 酷的老爸（cucumber）嚼黄瓜，cool as a cucumber（冷静如黄瓜）。
cucumber::/ˈkjuːkʌmbə(r)/:n. 黄瓜:Despite the chaos around him, he remained cool as a cucumber.
16. grape /ɡreɪp/ n. 葡萄
📖 英文解释
A small round fruit that grows in clusters on a vine, used to make wine and eaten as food.
✏️ 例句
The vineyard produced exceptional grapes for red wine this year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：grape vine（葡萄藤；小道消息）；sour grapes（酸葡萄心理）；grape juice（葡萄汁）；grapefruit（葡萄柚）。
🧠 助记
grape 发音似'格雷普'—— 格外甜的雷普（grape），一串串紫色葡萄。
grape::/ɡreɪp/:n. 葡萄:His criticism of the award was dismissed as sour grapes by commentators.
17. eyebrow /ˈaɪbraʊ/ n. 眉毛
📖 英文解释
The strip of hair that grows above each eye.
✏️ 例句
She raised an eyebrow in surprise when she heard the news.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：raise an eyebrow（扬眉，表示惊讶/怀疑）；raise eyebrows（引起惊讶/质疑）；bushy eyebrows（浓密的眉毛）。
🧠 助记
eye（眼睛）+ brow（额头）→ 眼睛上方的额头区域 → 眉毛。直接合成词记忆。
eyebrow::/ˈaɪbraʊ/:n. 眉毛:The unusual proposal raised eyebrows among committee members.
18. heroin /ˈherəʊɪn/ n. 海洛因（毒品）
📖 英文解释
An illegal and highly addictive drug made from morphine, derived from the opium poppy plant.
✏️ 例句
Heroin addiction devastates individuals, families, and entire communities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：heroin addiction（海洛因成瘾）；heroin dealer（毒贩）；heroin overdose（海洛因过量）。注意与 heroine（女英雄）区分拼写。
🧠 助记
heroin vs heroine：heroin（毒品）少一个 e，heroine（女英雄）多一个 e（e = extra effort, she's a hero）。
heroin::/ˈherəʊɪn/:n. 海洛因（毒品）:Government campaigns aim to reduce heroin use among young people.
19. apparatus /ˌæpəˈreɪtəs/ n. 仪器；装置；器官；机构
📖 英文解释
A set of equipment or tools used for a particular purpose; the system or organization of a country or institution.
✏️ 例句
The laboratory was equipped with sophisticated apparatus for chemical analysis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：scientific apparatus（科学仪器）；state apparatus（国家机器）；administrative apparatus（行政机构）；gym apparatus（体操器械）。
🧠 助记
appar（appear 出现）+ atus → 使某事出现的工具 → 仪器装置。apparatus = 让实验结果'出现'的工具。
apparatus::/ˌæpəˈreɪtəs/:n. 仪器:The state apparatus was used to suppress political dissent.
20. coherent /kəʊˈhɪərənt/ adj. 连贯的；有条理的；清晰一致的
📖 英文解释
Logical and well-organized; easy to understand because ideas are clearly connected; sticking together.
✏️ 例句
The professor presented a coherent argument that convinced the entire audience.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：coherent argument/plan/policy（连贯的论点/计划/政策）；coherent thinking（条理清晰的思维）；incoherent（语无伦次的，反义）。
🧠 助记
co（共同）+ here（在这里）+ nt → 所有部分都在一起 → 连贯的。cohere（黏合）→ coherent（连贯的）。
coherent::/kəʊˈhɪərənt/:adj. 连贯的:The report lacked a coherent structure and was difficult to follow.
21. shot /ʃɒt/ n. 射击；注射；镜头；尝试 adj. 破旧的
📖 英文解释
An act of firing a gun; an injection; a photograph or film scene; an attempt; completely worn out.
✏️ 例句
The doctor gave him a flu shot before the winter season began.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：give it a shot（试一试）；long shot（成功可能性小的尝试）；big shot（大人物）；shot in the dark（瞎猜）；gunshot（枪声）。
🧠 助记
shot 联想'射'—— 射击（shot）用枪，医生打针（shot）也是射入，照片（shot）是光射入镜头。
shot::/ʃɒt/:n. 射击:It's a long shot, but we should give it a shot anyway.
22. mock /mɒk/ v. 嘲笑；模仿 adj. 模拟的；假的 n. 模拟考试
📖 英文解释
To laugh at someone or make fun of them in an unkind way; to imitate someone for ridicule; not real, but used for practice.
✏️ 例句
Students took a mock exam to prepare for the real test next month.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：mock exam（模拟考试）；mock trial（模拟审判）；mock at（嘲弄）；mockery（嘲笑；闹剧）；make a mockery of（使…成为笑柄）。
🧠 助记
mock 谐音'模克'—— 模（仿）克（服）真实考试 → 模拟考试（mock exam）。
mock::/mɒk/:v. 嘲笑:The decision made a mockery of all previous efforts to reform the system.
23. opening /ˈəʊpənɪŋ/ n. 开口；开幕；职位空缺；机会 adj. 开始的
📖 英文解释
A space or hole in something; the beginning of something; a job vacancy; an opportunity to do something.
✏️ 例句
There is a job opening in the marketing department for a senior analyst.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：opening speech/ceremony（开幕词/典礼）；job opening（职位空缺）；opening gambit（开局策略）；an opening for（…的机会）。
🧠 助记
open（打开）+ ing → 打开的状态 → 开口/开幕/机会。开一扇门（opening）就是一个机会（opening）。
opening::/ˈəʊpənɪŋ/:n. 开口:The company has several openings for experienced software engineers.
24. tractor /ˈtræktə(r)/ n. 拖拉机；牵引车
📖 英文解释
A large vehicle with large rear wheels used on farms for pulling equipment; a truck used to pull large loads.
✏️ 例句
The farmer used a tractor to plow the fields before planting season.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：tractor pull（拖拉机拉力赛）；farm tractor（农用拖拉机）。词根 tract = draw/pull，联想：attract, distract, extract。
🧠 助记
tract（拉，拖）+ or（机器）→ 拖拉的机器 → 拖拉机。词根 tract 就是'拉'的意思。
tractor::/ˈtræktə(r)/:n. 拖拉机:Modern agriculture relies heavily on tractors for efficient land preparation.
25. deploy /dɪˈplɔɪ/ v. 部署；调度；运用
📖 英文解释
To send troops or equipment to a place; to move into position for action; to use something effectively.
✏️ 例句
The government decided to deploy additional troops to the border region.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：deploy troops/forces（部署军队）；deploy resources/skills（运用资源/技能）；deployment（部署，名词）。考研军事/科技类常见。
🧠 助记
de（展开）+ ploy（策略）→ 展开策略行动 → 部署。de + ploy = deploy（展开部署）。
deploy::/dɪˈplɔɪ/:v. 部署:The company deployed its best engineers to solve the critical system failure.
