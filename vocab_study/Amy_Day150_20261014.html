1. portrait /ˈpɔːrtrɪt/ n. 肖像；描绘
📖 英文解释
a painting, drawing, or photograph of a person; a detailed description or representation of someone or something
✏️ 例句
The museum displayed a stunning portrait of the queen.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：portrait of society（社会写照）；self-portrait（自画像）；常出现在艺术/文学类文章
🧠 助记
port(携带)+rait → 携带人的形象 → 肖像
portrait::/ˈpɔːrtrɪt/:n. 肖像:The novel paints a vivid portrait of life in the 19th century.
2. procession /prəˈsɛʃən/ n. 队伍；行进；一连串
📖 英文解释
a line of people or vehicles moving forward in an orderly manner; a continuous series
✏️ 例句
A funeral procession moved slowly through the quiet streets.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：in procession（成队行进）；a procession of events（一连串事件）；常出现在社会/文化类文章
🧠 助记
pro(向前)+cession(前进) → 向前进的队伍 → 行进队伍
procession::/prəˈsɛʃən/:n. 队伍:The procession of ideas in the essay was logical and clear.
3. recognise /ˈrɛkəɡnaɪz/ v. 认出；承认；表彰（英式拼写）
📖 英文解释
to identify someone or something from previous experience; to acknowledge officially
✏️ 例句
I didn't recognise her at first because she had changed so much.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研辨析：recognise = recognize（美式）；认可/承认官方地位；常见于法律政治类文章
🧠 助记
re(再)+cognise(认知) → 再次认知 → 认出
recognise::/ˈrɛkəɡnaɪz/:v. 认出:The UN recognised the new government's authority.
4. southeast /ˌsaʊθˈiːst/ n./adj./adv. 东南（方）
📖 英文解释
the direction or region between south and east
✏️ 例句
Heavy rainfall is expected in the southeast of the country tomorrow.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研地理/新闻类词汇；Southeast Asia（东南亚）；southeastern（东南部的）
🧠 助记
south(南)+east(东) → 南加东 → 东南
southeast::/ˌsaʊθˈiːst/:n./adj./adv. 东南（方）:Southeast Asia has experienced rapid economic growth.
5. standpoint /ˈstændpɔɪnt/ n. 立场；观点
📖 英文解释
a particular position from which someone considers a situation
✏️ 例句
From an economic standpoint, the policy seems counterproductive.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：from the standpoint of（从…立场来看）；与viewpoint/perspective辨析；常见于议论文写作
🧠 助记
stand(站立)+point(点) → 站立的位置点 → 立场
standpoint::/ˈstændpɔɪnt/:n. 立场:Consider the issue from the standpoint of the consumer.
6. string /strɪŋ/ n. 细绳；弦；一串 v. 串起
📖 英文解释
a thin cord or wire; the strings of a musical instrument; a series of similar things; to thread together
✏️ 例句
She tied the package with a piece of string.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多义词：a string of（一连串）；no strings attached（无附加条件）；pull strings（走后门）
🧠 助记
string → 细绳拉紧成直线 → 弦/细绳/一串
string::/strɪŋ/:n. 细绳:There were no strings attached to the generous offer.
7. stroll /stroʊl/ v./n. 漫步；闲逛
📖 英文解释
to walk in a slow, relaxed manner
✏️ 例句
They strolled along the riverbank enjoying the evening breeze.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研写作常用词：take a stroll（散步）；stroll through（漫步穿过）；辨析stroll/saunter/wander
🧠 助记
stroll → 慢慢roll(滚动)向前 → 漫步
stroll::/stroʊl/:v./n. 漫步:We took a leisurely stroll through the botanical garden.
8. stumble /ˈstʌmbəl/ v. 绊倒；结巴；偶然发现
📖 英文解释
to trip or lose balance while walking; to speak haltingly; to find or encounter unexpectedly
✏️ 例句
He stumbled on a loose stone and nearly fell.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：stumble across/upon（偶然发现）；stumble through（结结巴巴地讲）；常考完形填空
🧠 助记
stumb(绊)+le → 绊了一下 → 绊倒
stumble::/ˈstʌmbəl/:v. 绊倒:She stumbled upon an important clue while reviewing the documents.
9. temperament /ˈtɛmpərəmənt/ n. 性情；气质
📖 英文解释
a person's or animal's natural disposition and character
✏️ 例句
His calm temperament made him an ideal leader during the crisis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：artistic temperament（艺术气质）；temperament vs. character vs. personality区别；心理/社会类文章常见
🧠 助记
temper(脾气)+ament → 天生的脾气状态 → 性情/气质
temperament::/ˈtɛmpərəmənt/:n. 性情:Dogs are chosen by their temperament for guide dog training.
10. transistor /trænˈzɪstər/ n. 晶体管；半导体收音机
📖 英文解释
a semiconductor device used to amplify or switch electronic signals
✏️ 例句
The invention of the transistor revolutionized the electronics industry.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研科技类词汇；transistor radio（半导体收音机）；常出现在科技史和技术类文章
🧠 助记
trans(转移)+ist+or → 转移电流的器件 → 晶体管
transistor::/trænˈzɪstər/:n. 晶体管:Modern computers contain billions of transistors on a single chip.
11. verse /vɜːrs/ n. 诗；诗节；韵文
📖 英文解释
writing arranged with a rhythmic pattern; a single line or stanza in a poem or song
✏️ 例句
She memorized the opening verse of the poem for her performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文学类：free verse（自由诗）；in verse（用韵文）；verse vs. poetry/prose辨析；常考翻译
🧠 助记
verse → vers(转)e → 有韵律地转换 → 诗/诗节
verse::/vɜːrs/:n. 诗:The hymn has four verses and a chorus.
12. void /vɔɪd/ n. 空虚；真空 adj. 无效的 v. 使无效
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：null and void（无效的）；fill a void（填补空缺）；void of（缺乏）；常见于法律类文章
🧠 助记
void → 空洞洞的 → 空虚/无效
void::/vɔɪd/:n. 空虚:The agreement was rendered void after one party withdrew.
13. adverse /ˈædvɜːrs/ adj. 不利的；有害的

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

adverse::/ˈædvɜːrs/:adj. 不利的:Adverse publicity caused the company's shares to fall.
