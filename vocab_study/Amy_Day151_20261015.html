1. embed /ɪmˈbɛd/ v. 嵌入；使深置；（记者）随军采访
📖 英文解释
to fix firmly in a surrounding mass; to place a journalist within a military unit; to integrate deeply
✏️ 例句
The journalist was embedded with the troops during the conflict.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：embed in（嵌入）；deeply embedded values（根深蒂固的价值观）；科技/媒体类文章常见
🧠 助记
em(进入)+bed(床/基础) → 埋入基础中 → 嵌入
embed::/ɪmˈbɛd/:v. 嵌入:Cultural values are deeply embedded in every society.
2. fuss /fʌs/ n./v. 大惊小怪；忙乱；过分关注
📖 英文解释
unnecessary excitement or activity about something unimportant; to worry or complain excessively
✏️ 例句
He made such a fuss about a minor mistake that everyone was annoyed.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：make a fuss（大惊小怪）；fuss over（过分关注）；no fuss（简单利落）；常出现在行为描写类文章
🧠 助记
fuss → 像嗡嗡乱飞的声音 → 大惊小怪地忙乱
fuss::/fʌs/:n./v. 大惊小怪:Don't make such a fuss over a small setback.
3. namely /ˈneɪmli/ adv. 即；也就是
📖 英文解释
that is to say; specifically (used to introduce more specific information)
✏️ 例句
Only one student passed the exam, namely Sarah.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研写作/阅读常用连接词：namely用于举例说明；辨析namely/that is/i.e.；常考逻辑关系词
🧠 助记
name(名字)+ly → 点名说明 → 即/也就是
namely::/ˈneɪmli/:adv. 即:The study focused on one key factor, namely temperature.
4. permeate /ˈpɜːrmieɪt/ v. 渗透；弥漫；遍及
📖 英文解释
to spread throughout something; to pass through pores or small openings
✏️ 例句
The smell of fresh bread permeated the entire bakery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：permeate society/culture（渗透社会/文化）；常出现在社会/科学类文章；辨析permeate vs. penetrate
🧠 助记
per(穿透)+meate(穿越) → 穿透弥散 → 渗透/弥漫
permeate::/ˈpɜːrmieɪt/:v. 渗透:New technology has permeated every aspect of modern life.
5. resemblance /rɪˈzɛmbləns/ n. 相似；相像
📖 英文解释
the state of being similar in appearance or quality to something else
✏️ 例句
The child bears a strong resemblance to her father.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：bear/show resemblance to（与…相像）；striking resemblance（惊人的相似）；常出现在比较类文章
🧠 助记
resemble(相似)+ance → 相似的状态 → 相似
resemblance::/rɪˈzɛmbləns/:n. 相似:There is a remarkable resemblance between the two theories.
6. sometime /ˈsʌmtaɪm/ adv. 某时；在某个时候 adj. 曾经的
📖 英文解释
at some unspecified time; at a time not yet known; formerly
✏️ 例句
Let's meet for coffee sometime next week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研辨析：sometime（某时）vs. sometimes（有时）vs. some time（一段时间）；考研完形填空易混淆词
🧠 助记
some(某)+time(时间) → 某个时间 → 某时
sometime::/ˈsʌmtaɪm/:adv. 某时:He was a sometime actor before becoming a director.
7. staple /ˈsteɪpəl/ n./adj. 主要产品；主食；订书钉 v. 用订书钉钉
📖 英文解释
a main or important element; a basic food; a metal fastener; to fasten with a staple
✏️ 例句
Rice is a staple food for more than half of the world's population.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：staple food/diet（主食）；staple industry（主导产业）；常出现在经济/文化类文章
🧠 助记
staple → 稳定(stable)的基础产品 → 主要产品/主食
staple::/ˈsteɪpəl/:n./adj. 主要产品:Coffee has become a staple of modern office culture.
8. suck /sʌk/ v. 吸；吮；吸收
📖 英文解释
to draw in liquid or air by creating suction; to absorb
✏️ 例句
The baby sucked milk from the bottle contentedly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：suck up to（拍马屁）；be sucked into（被卷入）；suck the life out of（抽空精力）；常出现在社会类文章
🧠 助记
suck → 发音像吸吮声 → 吸
suck::/sʌk/:v. 吸:She was sucked into the controversy against her will.
9. terror /ˈtɛrər/ n. 恐惧；恐怖主义；令人恐惧者
📖 英文解释
extreme fear; an act of violence or intimidation; a person or thing causing fear
✏️ 例句
The city lived in terror following a series of unexplained attacks.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：war on terror（反恐战争）；reign of terror（恐怖统治）；terrorism（恐怖主义）；时事政治类高频词
🧠 助记
terr(土地/恐惧)+or → 使大地颤抖的恐惧 → 恐惧/恐怖
terror::/ˈtɛrər/:n. 恐惧:International cooperation is essential in the fight against terror.
10. tonight /təˈnaɪt/ adv./n. 今晚；今夜
📖 英文解释
on the present or coming evening or night
✏️ 例句
The president is scheduled to address the nation tonight.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研阅读常见时间状语；tonight作名词时可与冠词连用；常出现在新闻/叙事类文章
🧠 助记
to(到)+night(夜晚) → 到了晚上 → 今晚
tonight::/təˈnaɪt/:adv./n. 今晚:Tonight's debate will focus on economic policy.
11. wardrobe /ˈwɔːrdroʊb/ n. 衣柜；全部服装；戏装室
📖 英文解释
a large cupboard for storing clothes; a person's entire collection of clothes; the department managing costumes in theater/film
✏️ 例句
She went through her entire wardrobe looking for something to wear.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：extend one's wardrobe（丰富衣橱）；wardrobe malfunction（服装事故）；常出现在文化/娱乐类文章
🧠 助记
ward(守卫)+robe(长袍) → 存放衣服的地方 → 衣柜
wardrobe::/ˈwɔːrdroʊb/:n. 衣柜:The actress's wardrobe for the film included 50 different outfits.
12. wreck /rɛk/ n. 残骸；失事 v. 摧毁；破坏
📖 英文解释
the remains of a vehicle or building that has been destroyed; to destroy or damage severely
✏️ 例句
Divers explored the wreck of the sunken ship.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：train wreck（火车事故；灾难）；wreck one's health/career（毁掉健康/事业）；常出现在新闻/叙事类文章
🧠 助记
wreck → reck(毁灭) → 残骸/毁坏
wreck::/rɛk/:n. 残骸:The scandal completely wrecked his political career.
13. blouse /blaʊz/ n. 女式衬衫；宽松上衣
📖 英文解释
a light, loose upper garment, typically worn by women
✏️ 例句
She wore a white blouse with a dark pencil skirt to the interview.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研服装词汇；注意blouse特指女式上衣；常出现在日常描写和文化类文章
🧠 助记
blouse → blo(宽松)+use → 宽松好穿的衣服 → 女式衬衫
blouse::/blaʊz/:n. 女式衬衫:She tucked her floral blouse into her high-waisted trousers.
14. bow /baʊ/ or /boʊ/ v./n. 鞠躬；弓；蝴蝶结；船头
📖 英文解释
to bend the head or upper body as a sign of respect; the front of a ship; a weapon for shooting arrows; a knot with loops
✏️ 例句
The actor bowed to the audience after the performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多义词：bow(/baʊ/)鞠躬；bow(/boʊ/)弓/蝴蝶结；bow out（退出）；常考发音和多义
🧠 助记
bow → 弯曲 → 鞠躬/弓
bow::/baʊ/ or /boʊ/:v./n. 鞠躬:She made a graceful bow before the royal audience.
15. cafeteria /ˌkæfɪˈtɪəriə/ n. 自助餐厅；食堂
📖 英文解释
a restaurant where customers serve themselves or are served at a counter
✏️ 例句
Students eat lunch in the school cafeteria every day.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常见场景词；cafeteria plan（自助式福利计划）；常出现在校园/工作场所类文章
🧠 助记
cafe(咖啡馆)+teria → 大型咖啡式自助餐厅 → 食堂
cafeteria::/ˌkæfɪˈtɪəriə/:n. 自助餐厅:The company cafeteria offers subsidized meals to all employees.
16. cheek /tʃiːk/ n. 脸颊；无礼；厚脸皮
📖 英文解释
the soft part of the face below the eye; impudent boldness
✏️ 例句
She blushed and her cheeks turned bright red.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多义词：cheek(脸颊)；cheek(无礼/厚脸皮)；turn the other cheek（以德报怨）；tongue in cheek（开玩笑地）
🧠 助记
cheek → ch(吃)+eek → 吃惊时脸颊鼓起来 → 脸颊
cheek::/tʃiːk/:n. 脸颊:He had the cheek to ask for a raise after such poor performance.
17. compatible /kəmˈpætɪbəl/ adj. 兼容的；相容的；和谐共存的
📖 英文解释
able to exist or work together without conflict; (of equipment) able to be used together
✏️ 例句
The new software is not compatible with older operating systems.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：compatible with（与…兼容）；compatibility（兼容性）；常出现在科技/心理/社会类文章
🧠 助记
com(共同)+pat(和平)+ible → 能和平共存 → 兼容的
compatible::/kəmˈpætɪbəl/:adj. 兼容的:Their personalities proved highly compatible over time.
18. courtyard /ˈkɔːrtjɑːrd/ n. 庭院；院子
📖 英文解释
an unroofed area enclosed by walls or buildings
✏️ 例句
Students gathered in the courtyard between classes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研场景词汇；常出现在建筑/文化/历史类文章；描写中国四合院等传统建筑
🧠 助记
court(法院/宫廷)+yard(院子) → 宫廷的院子 → 庭院
courtyard::/ˈkɔːrtjɑːrd/:n. 庭院:The traditional Chinese house was built around a central courtyard.
19. descent /dɪˈsɛnt/ n. 下降；血统；衰落
📖 英文解释
the action of moving downward; family background or ancestry; a decline
✏️ 例句
The plane began its descent toward the runway.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：of Chinese descent（中国血统）；descent into chaos（陷入混乱）；辨析descent/descend/descendant
🧠 助记
de(向下)+scent(攀登) → 向下走 → 下降；血统
descent::/dɪˈsɛnt/:n. 下降:She is of mixed European and Asian descent.
20. dessert /dɪˈzɜːrt/ n. 甜点；餐后点心
📖 英文解释
a sweet food eaten at the end of a meal
✏️ 例句
She ordered chocolate cake for dessert.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研辨析：dessert（甜点）vs. desert（沙漠；遗弃）；注意拼写和读音区别；常见于日常生活类文章
🧠 助记
dessert有两个s → 甜点好吃想再来一份(double s)
dessert::/dɪˈzɜːrt/:n. 甜点:The restaurant is famous for its elaborate dessert menu.
21. excursion /ɪkˈskɜːrʒən/ n. 短途旅行；游览
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：go on an excursion（出游）；day excursion（一日游）；辨析excursion/trip/journey/tour/travel
🧠 助记
ex(出去)+curs(跑)+ion → 出去跑一圈 → 短途游览
excursion::/ɪkˈskɜːrʒən/:n. 短途旅行:The tour included an excursion to a traditional fishing village.
22. expel /ɪkˈspɛl/ v. 开除；驱逐；排出
📖 英文解释
to officially force someone to leave a school, organization, or country; to force out
✏️ 例句
The student was expelled from school for repeated misconduct.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：expel from school（开除学籍）；expel a diplomat（驱逐外交官）；常出现在教育/政治类文章
🧠 助记
ex(出去)+pel(推) → 推出去 → 驱逐/开除
expel::/ɪkˈspɛl/:v. 开除:The government expelled the ambassador over espionage allegations.
23. fax /fæks/ n./v. 传真（机）
📖 英文解释
a machine that transmits and receives text and images via telephone lines; to send a document by fax
✏️ 例句
Please fax the signed contract to our office by noon.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研商务英语词汇；fax machine（传真机）；常出现在商业/通信类文章；了解其现代替代（email）
🧠 助记
fax → facsimile(传真)的缩写 → 传真
fax::/fæks/:n./v. 传真（机）:The document was sent by fax before email became widespread.

fish(鱼)+er(人)+man → 捕鱼的人 → 渔民
fisherman::/ˈfɪʃərmən/:n. 渔民:Local fishermen warned of decreasing fish stocks in the sea.
25. fleet /fliːt/ n. 舰队；船队；车队 adj. 快速的
📖 英文解释
a group of ships, aircraft, or vehicles; moving swiftly
✏️ 例句

fleet::/fliːt/:n. 舰队:The airline expanded its fleet to meet growing demand.
