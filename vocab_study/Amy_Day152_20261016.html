1. graphic /ˈɡræfɪk/ adj. 生动的；图形的；露骨的 n. 图形
📖 英文解释
giving clear and vivid descriptions; relating to visual art or computer graphics; explicit in describing unpleasant things
✏️ 例句
The documentary included graphic footage of the war's aftermath.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：graphic design（平面设计）；graphic novel（图像小说）；graphic description（生动描述）；多义词
🧠 助记
graph(图)+ic → 图形相关的/生动形象的
graphic::/ˈɡræfɪk/:adj. 生动的:The report contained graphic details of the environmental destruction.
2. haul /hɔːl/ v. 拖拉；运输 n. 一网之获；运输量
📖 英文解释
to pull or drag with force; to transport over a distance; a quantity obtained at one time
✏️ 例句
The fishermen hauled in their nets after a long day at sea.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：long haul（长途；艰巨任务）；haul over the coals（严厉批评）；常出现在交通/体育类文章
🧠 助记
haul → 发音像'号'力气 → 用力拖拉 → 拖运
haul::/hɔːl/:v. 拖拉:Recovery from the crisis will be a long haul for the country.
3. hell /hɛl/ n. 地狱；极糟糕的处境
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
hell → h(向下)+ell → 往下沉的地方 → 地狱
hell::/hɛl/:n. 地狱:Come hell or high water, she was determined to finish the project.
4. ingenious /ɪnˈdʒiːniəs/ adj. 独创的；精巧的；有创意的
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🧠 助记
ingen(创造)+ious → 有创造力的 → 独创的
ingenious::/ɪnˈdʒiːniəs/:adj. 独创的:The app offered an ingenious way to track daily habits.
