1. periodical /ˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkl/ n. 期刊；杂志 adj. 定期的；周期的
📖 英文解释
a magazine or newspaper published at regular intervals; occurring at regular intervals
✏️ 例句
The library subscribes to dozens of academic periodicals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
academic/scientific periodical（学术/科学期刊）；on a periodical basis（定期地）；考研出版/学术类
🧠 助记
period（时期）+ ical → 按时期出版的 → 期刊
periodical::/ˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkl/:n. 期刊:The findings were published in a leading medical periodical.
2. perish /ˈperɪʃ/ v. 消灭；死亡；毁灭；变质
📖 英文解释
to die, especially in a sudden or untimely way; (of food) to decay; to suffer complete ruin or destruction
✏️ 例句
Hundreds perished in the earthquake and subsequent floods.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
perish in（在…中遇难）；perishable goods（易腐品）；perish the thought（不得不这样想）；考研灾难/食品类
🧠 助记
per（彻底）+ ish（像…一样）→ 彻底死亡 → 消灭
perish::/ˈperɪʃ/:v. 消灭:Perishable foods must be refrigerated to prevent spoilage.
3. purse /pɜːs/ n. 钱包；财力 v. 收紧（嘴唇）
📖 英文解释
a small bag for carrying money; financial resources; to pucker one's lips
✏️ 例句
She pursed her lips in disapproval when she heard the news.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
public purse（公共财政）；purse strings（钱袋口；财权）；hold the purse strings（掌管财权）；考研财政类
🧠 助记
purse 谐音[普斯] → 装普通人零钱的小包 → 钱包
purse::/pɜːs/:n. 钱包:The government tightened the public purse during the austerity period.
4. shallow /ˈʃæləʊ/ adj. 浅的；肤浅的 n. 浅水处
📖 英文解释
of little depth; not showing much depth of thought or feeling; superficial
✏️ 例句
Her analysis was criticized for being too shallow and superficial.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
shallow water（浅水）；shallow understanding（肤浅理解）；shallow breathing（浅呼吸）；考研批评类
🧠 助记
sha（沙）+ llow → 沙底很浅 → 浅的/肤浅的
shallow::/ˈʃæləʊ/:adj. 浅的:The article offered only a shallow treatment of a complex issue.
5. signature /ˈsɪɡnətʃə(r)/ n. 签名；署名；特征
📖 英文解释
a person's name written in a distinctive way as a form of identification; a distinctive feature
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
signature dish/style（招牌菜/风格）；collect signatures（收集签名）；digital signature（数字签名）；考研商业/艺术类
🧠 助记
sign（签名）+ ature → 签名的行为 → 签名
signature::/ˈsɪɡnətʃə(r)/:n. 签名:Jazz was the signature sound of the Harlem Renaissance.
6. solidarity /ˌsɒlɪˈdærɪti/ n. 团结；一致；休戚与共
📖 英文解释
unity or agreement of feeling or action, especially among individuals with a common interest
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
show/express solidarity（表示团结）；solidarity with（与…团结一致）；考研社会/政治类高频词
🧠 助记

solidarity::/ˌsɒlɪˈdærɪti/:n. 团结:International solidarity is essential in combating climate change.

8. allege /əˈledʒ/ v. 宣称；断言；申辩

allege::/əˈledʒ/:v. 宣称:The company is alleged to have violated environmental regulations.
9. bud /bʌd/ n. 芽；蓓蕾 v. 发芽；开始发育

bud::/bʌd/:n. 芽:The problem should be nipped in the bud before it escalates.
