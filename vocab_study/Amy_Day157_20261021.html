1. notable /ˈnəʊtəbl/ adj. 显赫的；值得注意的；n. 名人
📖 英文解释
worthy of attention or notice; remarkable; a famous or important person
✏️ 例句
The conference attracted several notable scientists.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
notably（adv.）；notable exception/achievement；考研：学术语境高频
🧠 助记
note（注意）+ able → 值得被注意的 → 显赫的
notable::/ˈnəʊtəbl/:adj. 显赫的:She made notable progress in her research.
2. obedience /əˈbiːdiəns/ n. 服从；顺从
📖 英文解释
compliance with an order, request, or law; submissiveness to authority
✏️ 例句
Strict obedience to regulations is required in military service.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
obedience to authority/rules；in obedience to；obedient（adj.）；考研：社会/伦理语境
🧠 助记
obey（服从）+ ience → 服从的状态 → 服从
obedience::/əˈbiːdiəns/:n. 服从:He acted in obedience to his commander.
3. ruler /ˈruːlə/ n. 统治者；尺子
📖 英文解释
a person who rules or governs; a straight strip used for measuring
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
military ruler；rule with a firm hand；考研：注意双义词
🧠 助记
rule（统治）+ r → 统治的人 → 统治者
ruler::/ˈruːlə/:n. 统治者:The ruler imposed strict laws on his subjects.
4. spear /spɪə/ n. 矛；长矛；v. 用矛刺
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
spear → sp（尖）+ ear（耳）→ 尖头的武器 → 矛
spear::/spɪə/:n. 矛:He spearheaded the marketing campaign.
5. sunshine /ˈsʌnʃaɪn/ n. 阳光；快乐
📖 英文解释
direct sunlight unbroken by cloud; used to refer to cheerfulness or happiness

sunshine policy；full of sunshine；考研：比喻义「乐观/快乐」

sunshine::/ˈsʌnʃaɪn/:n. 阳光:The child was the sunshine of her life.
6. surrender /səˈrendə/ v./n. 投降；放弃
📖 英文解释
to stop resisting an opponent and submit to their authority; to give up possession of something

surrender::/səˈrendə/:v./n. 投降:They refused to surrender their weapons.
7. taboo /təˈbuː/ n./adj. 禁忌；禁止的

taboo::/təˈbuː/:n./adj. 禁忌:Mental illness remains a taboo topic.

📖 英文解释

thirst::/θɜːst/:n. 口渴:She had an insatiable thirst for knowledge.

vegetation::/ˌvedʒɪˈteɪʃn/:n. 植物:The hillside was covered in lush vegetation.

astronomy::/əˈstrɒnəmi/:n. 天文学:She studied astronomy at university.

indignation::/ˌɪndɪɡˈneɪʃn/:n. 愤慨:She expressed her indignation at the injustice.

prudent::/ˈpruːdnt/:adj. 谨慎的:A prudent investor diversifies their portfolio.
