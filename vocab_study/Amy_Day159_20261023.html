1. candy /ˈkændɪ/ n. 糖果；v. 用糖衣包裹
📖 英文解释
A sweet food made with sugar or chocolate; to coat with sugar.
✏️ 例句
On Halloween, children go from door to door collecting candy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ candy bar 糖果棒（美式） ★ cotton candy 棉花糖（美式） ☆ candid adj. 坦率的（同形近似，注意区分）
🧠 助记
candy 谐音 肯弟 -> 肯定给弟弟糖果 -> 糖果
candy::/ˈkændɪ/:n. 糖果:She offered the child a piece of candy.
2. carbohydrate /ˌkɑːbəʊˈhaɪdreɪt/ n. 碳水化合物
📖 英文解释
Any of a large group of organic compounds including sugars, starch, and cellulose, which are important energy sources.
✏️ 例句
Athletes need to consume sufficient carbohydrates before a major competition.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ complex/simple carbohydrates 复合/简单碳水化合物 ★ carbohydrate intake 碳水摄入量 ☆ carb n. 碳水（口语缩写）；low-carb diet 低碳水饮食
🧠 助记
carbo(碳，carbon) + hydrate(水合物) -> 碳的水合物 -> 碳水化合物
carbohydrate::/ˌkɑːbəʊˈhaɪdreɪt/:n. 碳水化合物:Rice and pasta are rich sources of carbohydrates.
3. cluster /ˈklʌstə/ n. 群，簇，一组；v. 聚集
📖 英文解释
A group of similar things positioned or occurring closely together.
✏️ 例句
A cluster of students gathered around the noticeboard to read the results.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a cluster of stars/galaxies 星团/星系群 ★ cluster around/together 聚集在…周围 ★ cluster bomb 集束炸弹
🧠 助记
clust(密集，close+trust=紧靠) + er -> 紧密聚在一起 -> 群/簇
cluster::/ˈklʌstə/:n. 群，簇，一组:The disease cases were clustered in the northern region.
4. coalition /ˌkəʊəˈlɪʃən/ n. 联合，联盟，联合政府
📖 英文解释
A temporary alliance or union of political parties, nations, or other groups, formed to take joint action.
✏️ 例句
The two rival parties formed a coalition government to deal with the economic crisis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ form/build a coalition 组建联盟 ★ coalition government 联合政府 ★ a coalition of sth 由…组成的联盟 ☆ coalesce v. 合并，联合（动词形式）
🧠 助记
co(共同) + al(all) + ition -> 共同合一 -> 联合，联盟
coalition::/ˌkəʊəˈlɪʃən/:n. 联合，联盟，联合政府:A broad coalition of NGOs campaigned for reform.
5. cop /kɒp/ n. 警察（口语）；v. 承受，获得
📖 英文解释
Informal word for a police officer; to receive or be caught with something.
✏️ 例句
The cop directed traffic at the busy intersection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ cop out 逃避责任（口语） ★ cop a fine/punishment 受罚（澳式口语） ☆ copper n. 铜；也是警察的俣语
🧠 助记
cop 谐音 扣 -> 把犯人扣压 -> 警察
cop::/kɒp/:n. 警察（口语）:A cop stopped the speeding car on the motorway.
6. copper /ˈkɒpə/ n. 铜；铜币；警察（英式俣语）；adj. 铜制的
📖 英文解释
A red-brown metallic chemical element that is a good conductor of heat and electricity.
✏️ 例句
Copper is widely used in electrical wiring due to its excellent conductivity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ copper wire/pipe 铜线/铜管 ★ copper-coloured 铜色的 ★ copper alloy 铜合金 ☆ 元素符号：Cu；copper age 铜器时代
🧠 助记
cop(警察俣语) + per -> 铜色皮肤的警察 -> 铜/警察
copper::/ˈkɒpə/:n. 铜:The old coins were made of copper and silver.
7. couch /kaʊtʃ/ n. 长沙发，睡椅；v. 措词，表达
📖 英文解释
A long upholstered piece of furniture for several people to sit on; to express something in a particular way.
✏️ 例句
The lawyer carefully couched his argument in cautious language.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ couch potato 整天躟沙发的懒人 ★ be couched in sth 用某种措词表达（正式） ☆ sofa / couch 均指沙发，couch 更偏躟卧功能
🧠 助记
couch 谐音 靠趣 -> 靠着很有趣 -> 长沙发；措词: 把话靠着包装说出 -> 措词
couch::/kaʊtʃ/:n. 长沙发，睡椅:He couched his criticism in very polite terms.
8. dialect /ˈdaɪəlekt/ n. 方言，地方话
📖 英文解释
A particular form of a language which is peculiar to a specific region or social group.
✏️ 例句
The novel is written partly in the local dialect, making it challenging for outsiders.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ regional dialect 地区方言 ★ speak in dialect 说方言 ☆ dialectal adj. 方言的；dialectology n. 方言学
🧠 助记
dia(穿越，dialogue) + lect(说，lecture) -> 穿越地区的说话方式 -> 方言
dialect::/ˈdaɪəlekt/:n. 方言，地方话:Some dialects of English are hard for foreigners to understand.
9. disturbance /dɪˈstɜːbəns/ n. 扰乱，骚动，干扰
📖 英文解释
The interruption of a settled condition; a breakdown of peaceful behaviour, such as a riot.
✏️ 例句
Several people were arrested following disturbances at the football match.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ cause/create a disturbance 制造骚乱 ★ mental/emotional disturbance 心理/情绪障碍 ★ sleep disturbance 睡眠障碍 ☆ disturb v. 打扰；disturbing adj. 令人不安的
🧠 助记
dis(否定) + turb(扰动，turbine涡轮) + ance -> 搞乱安定状态 -> 骚乱
disturbance::/dɪˈstɜːbəns/:n. 扰乱，骚动，干扰:Noise disturbance from the construction site was a problem.
10. empirical /ɪmˈpɪrɪkəl/ adj. 经验主义的，以实证为依据的
📖 英文解释
Based on observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic.
✏️ 例句
The researcher insisted on empirical evidence before drawing any conclusions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ empirical evidence/data 经验性证据/数据（高频！） ★ empirical research/study 实证研究 ☆ empiricism n. 经验主义；empiricist n. 经验主义者
🧠 助记
empiric(经验，来自希腌语 empeiria 经验) + al -> 基于经验的 -> 经验主义的
empirical::/ɪmˈpɪrɪkəl/:adj. 经验主义的，以实证为依据的:There is strong empirical support for this theory.
11. fasten /ˈfɑːsən/ v. 系上，扣紧，固定
📖 英文解释
To close or join securely; to fix firmly in place.
✏️ 例句
Please fasten your seatbelts before the aircraft takes off.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ fasten a seatbelt/button/zip 系安全带/扣纽扣/拉拉链 ★ fasten sth to sth 把某物固定到某处 ★ fasten on/upon sth 专注于某事 ☆ fastener n. 扣件，紧固件
🧠 助记
fast(快/固定) + en -> 使固定 -> 系紧，扣上
fasten::/ˈfɑːsən/:v. 系上，扣紧，固定:Fasten all windows before the storm arrives.
12. glue /ɡluː/ n. 胶水；v. 粘合，使紧盯
📖 英文解释
An adhesive substance used for sticking objects together; to fasten with glue; to fix one's eyes on something.
✏️ 例句
He was glued to the television screen, unable to look away.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ glue sth together 把某物粘在一起 ★ be glued to sth 紧盯着某物（高频比喻） ★ super glue 强力胶 ☆ gluey adj. 粘性的
🧠 助记
glue 发音像 咊噜 -> 吸进胶水，默默粘在一起 -> 胶水
glue::/ɡluː/:n. 胶水:The children glued their art projects together.

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ groan in/with pain 因疼痛而呼吩 ★ groan about sth 抑怨某事 ★ the floor groaned under the weight 地板在重压下嘎吵作响
🧠 助记
groan 发音像 哦恩 -> 疼痛时发出 哦恩 的声音 -> 呼吩
groan::/ɡrəʊn/:v./n. 呼吩，发牢骚:He groaned when he saw the exam paper.
14. novelty /ˈnɒvəltɪ/ n. 新奇的事物，新奇性；小玩意儿

🧠 助记
novel(新颖的) + ty(名词后缀) -> 新颖性 -> 新奇
novelty::/ˈnɒvəltɪ/:n. 新奇的事物，新奇性:The novelty of living abroad quickly gave way to routine.

proficiency::/prəˈfɪʃənsɪ/:n. 熟练，精通:Her proficiency in coding impressed the interview panel.
17. rumor /ˈruːmə/ n. 传闻，谣言；v. 谣传

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ spread/start a rumour 散布/传播谣言 ★ be rumoured to do/be 据谣传… ★ rumour has it that... 有传言称…（高频句型） ☆ rumour(英式) / rumor(美式)拼写差异
🧠 助记
rumor 谐音 如某 -> 如某人所说（但不确定） -> 传闻
rumor::/ˈruːmə/:n. 传闻，谣言:Rumour has it that they are getting divorced.

scare 谐音 死吓 -> 把人吓死 -> 恐吓
scare::/skeə/:v. 恐吓，使害怕:Don't scare the children with ghost stories at bedtime.

★ sober up 酒醒；变得清醒 ★ a sober assessment/reminder 冷静的评估/提醒 ★ sober-minded 思维清醒的 ☆ sobriety n. 清醒，禁酒

sober::/ˈsəʊbə/:adj. 清醒的，未醉的:The news had a sobering effect on everyone present.
20. stall /stɔːl/ n. 货摊，小隔间；v. 拖延，失速，息火

A booth or compartment for selling goods; to stop making progress; (of a vehicle) to stop suddenly.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ stall for time 拖延时间（高频！） ★ market stall 集市摊位 ★ stall an engine 使引擎息火 ★ talks/negotiations stall 谈判陷入僵局

stall 谐音 死拖了 -> 死拖着不前进 -> 拖延；摊位（停在那里的小店）
stall::/stɔːl/:n. 货摊，小隔间:The project stalled due to a lack of funding.
21. tomb /tuːm/ n. 坥墓，陵墓

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier 无名战士墓 ★ tombstone n. 墓碑；entomb v. 下葬，封闭 辨析：tomb / grave：tomb 大型陵墓/地下室；grave 普通坥墓

antique::/ænˈtiːk/:n. 古董，古物:The antique vase sold for thousands at auction.
