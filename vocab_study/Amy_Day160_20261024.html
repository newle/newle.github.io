1. bake /beɪk/ v. 烤，烘焙；n. 烘烤
📖 英文解释
To cook food by dry heat in an oven; to harden by heating.
✏️ 例句
She bakes a fresh loaf of bread every Sunday morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ bake a cake/bread/cookies 烤蜀糕/面包/饼干 ★ bake in the sun 在阳光下更扤 ☆ bakery n. 面包店；baker n. 面包师
🧠 助记
bake 谐音 贝克 -> 贝克老师烤面包 -> 烤，烘焙
bake::/beɪk/:v. 烤，烘焙:He baked a birthday cake for his daughter.
2. blanket /ˈblæŋkɪt/ n. 毯子，毛毯；adj. 全面的，一概的；v. 覆盖
📖 英文解释
A large piece of warm covering material; covering all cases or instances; to cover completely.
✏️ 例句
The government issued a blanket ban on plastic bag use in all public places.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a blanket ban/refusal/statement 全面禁令/拒绝/声明 ★ blanket of snow/fog 一片雪/雾（覆盖物比喻） ★ security blanket 安全毯；依赖物
🧠 助记
blank(空白) + et -> 盖住所有空白 -> 毯子；全面覆盖的
blanket::/ˈblæŋkɪt/:n. 毯子，毛毯:The policy was criticised as a blanket approach.
3. blast /blɑːst/ n. 爆炸，强风；v. 爆破，猛烈批评
📖 英文解释
A destructive wave of compressed air from an explosion; a strong gust of wind; to blow up with explosives; to criticise strongly.
✏️ 例句
The terrorists detonated a blast that shook the entire neighbourhood.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a blast of air/music 一阵风/音乐声 ★ blast sb/sth for sth 猛烈批评 ★ at full blast 全速，全力 ☆ blast off 火算点火升空
🧠 助记
blast 谐音 爆了死 -> 爆炸了就死人 -> 爆炸
blast::/blɑːst/:n. 爆炸，强风:The bomb blast destroyed several buildings in the area.
4. bless /bles/ v. 保佑，赐福；表示感谢
📖 英文解释
To pronounce words that are believed to confer God's favour on someone; to feel grateful for something.
✏️ 例句
The priest blessed the congregation at the end of the service.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ God bless you 上帝保佑你（打喷嚏后说） ★ be blessed with sth 被赋予，幸运拥有某物 ☆ blessing n. 祝福；上天的恩赐
🧠 助记
bless 谐音 蓝死 -> 蓝天神赐福保佑 -> 保佑
bless::/bles/:v. 保佑，赐福:We are blessed with good health and a loving family.
5. carrot /ˈkærət/ n. 胡萝卜；奖励，诱因
📖 英文解释
A tapering orange-coloured root eaten as a vegetable; an incentive or reward offered to encourage.
✏️ 例句
The management used a carrot and stick approach to motivate employees.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ carrot and stick 胡萝卜加大棒（奖惩并用） ★ dangle a carrot 提供诱惑/奖励 ☆ 注意拼写: carrot（两个r）
🧠 助记
carrot 谐音 卡肉特 -> 卡住肉跪的特质 -> 胡萝卜（橙色的肉质根）
carrot::/ˈkærət/:n. 胡萝卜:The bonus acted as a carrot to encourage better performance.
6. characterise /ˈkærəktəraɪz/ v. 以…为特征；描述…的特征
📖 英文解释
To be a typical or important feature of something; to describe the qualities or nature of someone or something.
✏️ 例句
The decade was characterised by rapid technological change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be characterised by sth 以某事为特征（高频！） ★ characterise sb/sth as sth 将某人/物描述为… ☆ characteristic n./adj. 特征（的）；character n. 性格，特点
🧠 助记
character(性格/特征) + ise(动词化) -> 使有特征 -> 以…为特征
characterise::/ˈkærəktəraɪz/:v. 以…为特征:His writing is characterised by vivid imagery.
7. clumsy /ˈklʌmzɪ/ adj. 笨拙的，不灵巧的
📖 英文解释
Moving or doing things in a stiff, uneasy manner; lacking grace or elegance.
✏️ 例句
He made a clumsy attempt to apologise, which only made things worse.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a clumsy attempt/remark 笨拙的尝试/评语 ★ clumsy fingers/hands 不灵活的手指/手 ☆ clumsily adv. 笨拙地；clumsiness n. 笨拙
🧠 助记
clum(紧团，clump=一堆) + sy -> 手脚都成堆，动作不协调 -> 笨拙的
clumsy::/ˈklʌmzɪ/:adj. 笨拙的，不灵巧的:The clumsy waiter knocked over a glass of wine.
8. concede /kənˈsiːd/ v. 承认（某事属实）；让步；让渡
📖 英文解释
To admit that something is true, especially when reluctant to do so; to admit defeat; to yield something.
✏️ 例句
He conceded that his initial assessment had been wrong.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ concede that... 承认…（高频！） ★ concede defeat/a goal 承认失败/失球 ★ concede a point 让步 ☆ concession n. 让步；concessionary adj. 优惠的
🧠 助记
con(完全) + cede(走，如 proceed) -> 完全走开（退步）-> 让步，承认
concede::/kənˈsiːd/:v. 承认（某事属实）:She conceded that the problem was more serious than expected.
9. enclose /ɪnˈkləʊz/ v. 围住，封闭；随函附上
📖 英文解释
To surround or close off on all sides; to include something in a letter or parcel.
✏️ 例句
Please find enclosed a copy of the contract for your review.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ enclose sth in/with a letter 随函附上某物 ★ enclose an area 将某片区域围起来 ★ please find enclosed... 请见附件（书信套语） ☆ enclosure n. 围场；附件
🧠 助记
en(使…) + close(关闭) -> 使关闭围起来 -> 围住；随函附上
enclose::/ɪnˈkləʊz/:v. 围住，封闭:I have enclosed a cheque for the full amount.
10. envisage /ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒ/ v. 设想，想象；预计
📖 英文解释
To contemplate or conceive of as a possibility in the future; to visualise.
✏️ 例句
The planners envisage a new transport hub at the centre of the development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ envisage doing sth 设想做某事 ★ envisage that... 设想…（正式） ☆ envision 美式替代词；foresee 也有预见之意
🧠 助记
en + visage(脸，vision的变体) -> 在脑海中构想出一张脸 -> 设想，想象
envisage::/ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒ/:v. 设想，想象:We envisage completing the project by the end of next year.
11. isolate /ˈaɪsəleɪt/ v. 隔离，使孤立；分离（物质）
📖 英文解释
To cause someone or something to be alone or apart from others; to identify and separate a substance.
✏️ 例句
Patients with infectious diseases must be isolated to prevent the spread of infection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ isolate sb from the group 使某人与群体隔离 ★ isolate a virus/substance 分离病毒/物质 ☆ isolation n. 隔离；isolated adj. 孤立的，偿远的
🧠 助记
isol(岛屿，island同源) + ate -> 像孤岛一样 -> 隔离，孤立
isolate::/ˈaɪsəleɪt/:v. 隔离，使孤立:The village was completely isolated after the floods.
12. oriental /ˌɔːrɪˈentəl/ adj. 东方的；n. 东方人（旧称，现多用 Asian）
📖 英文解释
Relating to the countries of eastern Asia; a person of eastern Asian origin (dated term).
✏️ 例句
The museum has an impressive collection of oriental art and artefacts.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ Oriental culture/philosophy 东方文化/哲学 ★ oriental rug/carpet 东方风格地毯 ☆ orient v. 使适应；orientation n. 取向；the Orient 东方（文学用语）
🧠 助记
orient(东方，太阳升起之处) + al -> 东方的
oriental::/ˌɔːrɪˈentəl/:adj. 东方的:The oriental garden was designed in traditional Japanese style.
13. pasture /ˈpɑːstʃə/ n. 牧场，草地；v. 在草地上放牧
📖 英文解释
Land covered with grass, used for grazing animals; to graze livestock on pasture.
✏️ 例句
The cattle grazed contentedly in the lush green pasture.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ put out to pasture 退休，下台（比喻） ★ pasture land 牧场土地 ★ new pastures 新领域，新环境（常用比喻） ☆ pastoral adj. 田园的，牧师的
🧠 助记
past(过去，经历) + ure -> 经过多年的草场 -> 牧场
pasture::/ˈpɑːstʃə/:n. 牧场，草地:The sheep roamed freely across the open pasture.
14. raid /reɪd/ n. 突击，突袭；v. 突击，袭击

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ carry out/launch a raid 发动突袭 ★ police raid 警方突击搜查 ★ raid the fridge 去冰箱找吃的（口语幽默用法） ☆ raider n. 突袭者，搜查者
🧠 助记
raid 谐音 锐的 -> 像锐利的刀一样突然刺入 -> 突袭
raid::/reɪd/:n. 突击，突袭:The army conducted a raid on the enemy's supply base.
15. refuge /ˈrefjuodʒ/ n. 避难，庇护；避难所
📖 英文解释
The condition of being safe or sheltered from pursuit, danger, or trouble; a place providing shelter.
✏️ 例句
Thousands of civilians sought refuge in churches and schools during the conflict.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ seek/take refuge in sth 在某处寻求避难 ★ a refuge from sth 逃离某事的避风港 ★ wildlife refuge 野生动物保护区 ☆ refugee n. 难民
🧠 助记
re(重新) + fuge(逃离，fugitive逃亡者) -> 重新逃离到安全地 -> 避难所
refuge::/ˈrefjuodʒ/:n. 避难，庇护:She found refuge from the storm in a small mountain hut.
16. resolute /ˈrezəluːt/ adj. 坚决的，果断的
📖 英文解释
Admirably purposeful, determined, and unwavering.
✏️ 例句
She remained resolute in her decision to pursue a medical career despite the difficulties.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ remain/be resolute in sth 对某事坚定不移 ★ a resolute refusal/stand 坚决的拒绝/立场 ☆ resolution n. 决心，决议；resolve v./n. 解决；决心
🧠 助记
re(再次) + solut(解决，solution) + e -> 反复下定决心解决 -> 坚决的
resolute::/ˈrezəluːt/:adj. 坚决的，果断的:The team was resolute in its commitment to the project.
17. rug /rʌɡ/ n. 地毯（小型）；毯子

A small carpet woven or felted from thick yarn; a thick blanket used as a wrap.
✏️ 例句
She bought a colourful rug to brighten up the living room floor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pull the rug from under sb 突然撤除某人的支持（惯用语） ★ sweep sth under the rug 掩盖问题 ☆ rug / carpet: rug 小型地毯；carpet 大型铺地毯
🧠 助记
rug 谐音 如高 -> 如高级地板上铺着 -> 小地毯
rug::/rʌɡ/:n. 地毯（小型）:The children sat on the rug in front of the fireplace.
18. sarcastic /sɑːˈkæstɪk/ adj. 讽刺的，讵讽的
📖 英文解释
Marked by or given to using irony to mock or convey contempt.
✏️ 例句
His sarcastic remark about her cooking was unkind and unnecessary.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a sarcastic comment/tone/remark 讽刺的评论/语气/话 ★ be sarcastic about sth 对某事说讽刺的话 ☆ sarcasm n. 讽刺，挜苦；sarcastically adv.
🧠 助记
sarc(肉，sarcophagus石棺) + astic -> 撒肉般的嘲讽 -> 讽刺的
sarcastic::/sɑːˈkæstɪk/:adj. 讽刺的，讵讽的:She gave a sarcastic smile and walked away without a word.
19. scrap /skræp/ n. 废料，碎片；v. 报废，废弃；打架
📖 英文解释
A small piece of something; waste material; to discard; to abandon a plan; to fight.
✏️ 例句
The government decided to scrap the controversial new policy after widespread protests.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ scrap a plan/idea 放弃计划/想法 ★ scrap metal/paper 废金属/废纸 ★ from scraps 用边角料 ☆ scrappy adj. 杂乱的；爱打架的（美式）
🧠 助记
scrap 谐音 死 卡破 -> 用破旧卡片换的废料 -> 废品；scrap掉计划 -> 废弃
scrap::/skræp/:n. 废料，碎片:The old car was sold for scrap after the accident.
20. shatter /ˈʃætə/ v. 打碎，粉碎；使浩溃；使深受打击
📖 英文解释
To break suddenly and violently into small pieces; to destroy utterly; to deeply upset or disturb.
✏️ 例句
The glass shattered when it hit the stone floor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ shatter into pieces 打碎成碎片 ★ shatter sb's hopes/dreams/illusions 打碎某人的希望/梦想/幻想 ★ be shattered (英式) 精疲力竭 ☆ shattering adj. 使人浩溃的；令人震惊的
🧠 助记
shatter 谐音 夏打 -> 夏天用力打落果子，摘得粉碎 -> 打碎，粉碎
shatter::/ˈʃætə/:v. 打碎，粉碎:The accident shattered his confidence in driving.
21. trumpet /ˈtrʌmpɪt/ n. 喊叭，小号；v. 大声宣扬，鼓吹
📖 英文解释
A brass musical instrument with a flared bell and a bright, piercing tone; to proclaim widely.
✏️ 例句
The politician was criticised for trumpeting his own achievements.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ blow one's own trumpet 自我吹嘘（习语） ★ trumpet sth to the world 向全世界宣扬某事 ☆ trumpeter n. 小号手；trumpeted adj. 被大肂宣扬的
🧠 助记
trump(王牌) + et -> 吹出王牌乐器的声音 -> 喊叭；大声宣扬
trumpet::/ˈtrʌmpɪt/:n. 喊叭，小号:He trumpeted his success to everyone he met.

a + loud(大声) -> 以大声的方式 -> 大声地
aloud::/əˈlaʊd/:adv. 大声地，出声地:He laughed aloud when he heard the joke.

★ ape sb's behaviour/accent 模仿某人的行为/口音 ★ go ape 发狂，失控（口语） ☆ ape / monkey: ape 无尾猿（更大）；monkey 猴子（通常有尾）

ape 谐音 哎咀 -> 猿类被模仿了气得哎咀叫 -> 猿；模仿
ape::/eɪp/:n. 猿，无尾猿:Children often ape the mannerisms of their parents.
