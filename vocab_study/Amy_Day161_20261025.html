1. blend /blend/ v./n. 混合，融合
📖 英文解释
To mix a substance with another substance so that they combine together; a mixture or combination.
✏️ 例句
The architect designed a building that blends modern style with traditional elements.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ blend in (with sth) 融入（某物）；不显眼 ★ blend together 融合在一起 ★ a blend of sth and sth 某物与某物的混合 ☆ blender n. 搅拌机；blend / mix: blend 更强调融合，分不清彼此；mix 可以看出各部分
🧠 助记
blend 谐音 冰冷的 -> 把冷热混在一起 -> 混合，融合
blend::/blend/:v./n. 混合，融合:The sauce is a blend of herbs, garlic, and olive oil.
2. bound /baʊnd/ v. 跳跃；adj. 一定会的，必然的；受约束的；n. 界限
📖 英文解释
To move with leaping strides; certain to happen; heading towards a destination; a boundary or limit.
✏️ 例句
The puppy bounded across the garden, barking with excitement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be bound to do sth 一定会做某事（高频！） ★ be bound by rules/duty 受规则/职责约束 ★ northbound/outward bound 北行/向外的 ★ out of bounds 越界，禁止进入 ☆ boundary n. 边界（同根）
🧠 助记
bound 谐音 绑的 -> 绑在一起跳跃 -> 跳跃；界限（被绑住的范围）
bound::/baʊnd/:v. 跳跃:She is bound to succeed with such dedication.
3. breeze /briːz/ n. 微风，清风；v. 轻松完成
📖 英文解释
A gentle wind; to come and go in a casual manner; something that is very easy to do.
✏️ 例句
A warm breeze drifted through the open window on a summer afternoon.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a gentle breeze 微风 ★ breeze through sth 轻松完成某事 ★ it's a breeze 轻而易举（口语） ☆ breezy adj. 有微风的；轻松愉快的
🧠 助记
breeze 谐音 不理死 -> 风来了不理睬，悠悠地吹过 -> 微风；轻松
breeze::/briːz/:n. 微风，清风:She breezed through the exam without any difficulty.
4. cushion /ˈkʊʃən/ n. 垫子，缓冲物；v. 减轻（冲击）
📖 英文解释
A bag of cloth stuffed with soft material used as a comfortable support; to reduce the effect of an impact.
✏️ 例句
The savings account provided a financial cushion against unexpected expenses.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ sofa cushion 沙发靠垫 ★ cushion the blow/impact/effect 缓冲打击/冲击/影响（高频！） ★ a cushion against sth 对抗某事的缓冲 ☆ cushioned adj. 有垫子的；有缓冲的
🧠 助记
cush(柔软，同 cozy) + ion -> 软的东西 -> 垫子；引申缓冲
cushion::/ˈkʊʃən/:n. 垫子，缓冲物:The thick carpet cushioned his fall.
5. dazzle /ˈdæzəl/ v. 使眼花，使目眩；使印象深刻；n. 耀眼
📖 英文解释
To make someone temporarily unable to see due to excessive brightness; to impress greatly.
✏️ 例句
The audience was dazzled by her stunning vocal performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be dazzled by sth 被某物耀到；被某物迷倒 ★ dazzle sb with sth 用某物使某人眼花/折服 ☆ dazzling adj. 耀眼的；令人赞叹的；dazzle camouflage 涂装迷彩（军事）
🧠 助记
dazzle 谐音 搭则 -> 强光搭上眼睛，则睁不开 -> 使眼花，炫目
dazzle::/ˈdæzəl/:v. 使眼花，使目眩:The young singer dazzled the crowd with her performance.
6. deplete /dɪˈpliːt/ v. 大量减少，耗尽（资源等）
📖 英文解释
To reduce something in size, number, or total; to use up or exhaust a supply.
✏️ 例句
Overfishing has severely depleted the fish stocks in the North Sea.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ deplete resources/reserves/stocks 耗尽资源/储量/库存 ★ be severely/rapidly depleted 被严重/迅速消耗 ☆ depletion n. 耗尽；the depletion of the ozone layer 臭氧层的消耗（高频话题）
🧠 助记
de(向下) + plete(充满，complete=完整) -> 把充满变成空的 -> 耗尽
deplete::/dɪˈpliːt/:v. 大量减少，耗尽（资源等）:The ozone layer has been depleted by industrial chemicals.
7. dial /ˈdaɪəl/ n. 拨号盘，刻度盘；v. 拨（电话）
📖 英文解释
A disc with a scale or markings used for measurement; to operate a device by rotating or pressing numbers.
✏️ 例句
She dialled the emergency number immediately after the accident.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ dial a number 拨号 ★ dial up/in 拨入（互联网连接，比喻加大力度） ★ tune the dial 调节刻度盘 ☆ dialling tone 拨号音；sundial 日晷（日规）
🧠 助记
dia(转，同 diameter圆直径) + l -> 转动的盘子 -> 刻度盘；拨号
dial::/ˈdaɪəl/:n. 拨号盘，刻度盘:He dialled his friend's number but there was no answer.
8. discrepancy /dɪˈskrepənsɪ/ n. 差异，不一致，矛盾
📖 英文解释
A lack of compatibility or similarity between two or more facts; a difference.
✏️ 例句
There was a discrepancy between the two witnesses' accounts of the incident.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a discrepancy between A and B A 与 B 之间的差异（高频！） ★ a discrepancy in figures/data 数据上的差异 ★ significant/slight discrepancy 显著/轻微的差异 ☆ discrepant adj. 有差异的；inconsistency 也表不一致
🧠 助记
dis(不同) + crep(噪音，叫声，discrepant) + ancy -> 两者之间声音不同 -> 差异，不一致
discrepancy::/dɪˈskrepənsɪ/:n. 差异，不一致，矛盾:Accountants investigated a discrepancy in the company's financial records.
9. disgust /dɪsˈɡʌst/ n./v. 厌恶，反感
📖 英文解释
A feeling of revulsion or profound disapproval; to cause someone to feel revulsion or strong disapproval.
✏️ 例句
She walked out of the meeting in disgust after hearing the proposal.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in disgust 带着厌恶离开 ★ express disgust at sth 对某事表示厌恶 ★ disgust sb 使某人厌恶 ☆ disgusting adj. 令人厌恶的；disgustingly adv. 辨析: disgust / repulsion / revulsion: disgust 最常用；repulsion/revulsion 程度更深，更书面
🧠 助记
dis(否定) + gust(口感，同 gusto热情) -> 完全失去口感/热情 -> 厌恶
disgust::/dɪsˈɡʌst/:n./v. 厌恶，反感:The corruption scandal filled citizens with disgust.

🧠 助记
donkey 谐音 蠢驴可 -> 驴被人骂蠢 -> 驴；蠢人
donkey::/ˈdɒŋkɪ/:n. 驴:He's been doing donkey work in the office for years.

drawer::/drɔː/:n. 抽屉:He rummaged through the drawer looking for a pen.
12. drone /drəʊn/ n. 无人驾驶飞机；嗡嗡声；雄蜂；v. 嗡嗡作响；絮絮叨叨

drone::/drəʊn/:n. 无人驾驶飞机:The drone captured aerial footage of the disaster zone.
13. eject /ɪˈdʒekt/ v. 喷射，抛出；驱逐，驱赶

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ eject sb from a place 将某人驱逐出某地 ★ the pilot ejected 飞行员弹射逃生 ★ eject a disc/CD 弹出光盘 ☆ ejection n. 弹出，驱逐；ejector seat 弹射座椅

eject::/ɪˈdʒekt/:v. 喷射，抛出:The footballer was ejected from the game after a violent tackle.
14. endow /ɪnˈdaʊ/ v. 赋予；捐赠（基金等）

endow::/ɪnˈdaʊ/:v. 赋予:She was endowed with both intelligence and creativity.
15. erosion /ɪˈrəʊʒən/ n. 侵蚀，腐蚀；（权力、价值等）逐渐削弱
📖 英文解释
The gradual destruction of something through sustained action; the process by which the surface of the earth is worn away by wind, water, etc.
✏️ 例句
Coastal erosion has caused many homes to be at risk of falling into the sea.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ soil/coastal/rock erosion 土地/海岸/岩石侵蚀 ★ the erosion of sth 某事物的逐渐削弱（比喻高频！） ★ erosion of trust/rights/values 信任/权利/价值观的侵蚀 ☆ erode v. 侵蚀（动词形式）
🧠 助记
e + ros(咬，同 erode) + ion -> 被一点点咬掉的过程 -> 侵蚀
erosion::/ɪˈrəʊʒən/:n. 侵蚀，腐蚀:The erosion of civil liberties is a serious concern.
16. flush /flʌʃ/ v. 冲洗；脸红；n. 冲洗；脸红；adj. 齐平的；充裕的

🧠 助记
flush 谐音 扑来 -> 水扑来一下冲掉 -> 冲洗；脸一下变红 -> 脸红
flush::/flʌʃ/:v. 冲洗:Her face flushed red with anger.

goodbye::/ˌɡʊdˈbaɪ/:int./n. 再见，告别:You can say goodbye to your weekend if the project is not done.
