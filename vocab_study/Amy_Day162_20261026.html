1. limp /lɪmp/ v./adj. 跛行；柔软的
📖 英文解释
to walk with difficulty due to injury; lacking firmness or strength
✏️ 例句
After twisting her ankle, she limped to the nearest bench.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：limp along（一瘸一拐地走）；作形容词：a limp handshake（软弱的握手）
🧠 助记
limp 联想 'lame'（跛的）→ 走路一瘸一拐，软绵绵地 → 跛行/柔软
limp::/lɪmp/:v./adj. 跛行:The injured soldier limped back to camp.
2. lovely /ˈlʌvli/ adj. 可爱的；美好的
📖 英文解释
exquisitely beautiful or charming; very pleasant
✏️ 例句
What a lovely day for a picnic in the park!
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：lovely weather（宜人的天气）、a lovely smile（迷人的笑容）；口语中可作感叹词
🧠 助记
love（爱）+ ly（形容词后缀）→ 让人产生爱意的 → 可爱的
lovely::/ˈlʌvli/:adj. 可爱的:She wore a lovely dress to the graduation ceremony.
3. nationality /ˌnæʃəˈnælɪti/ n. 民族；国籍
📖 英文解释
the status of belonging to a particular nation; an ethnic group
✏️ 例句
Please state your nationality on the application form.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：of Chinese nationality（中国国籍）；区分 nationality（国籍/民族）与 citizenship（公民身份）
🧠 助记
nation（国家/民族）+ al + ity → 与国家/民族相关的身份 → 国籍/民族
nationality::/ˌnæʃəˈnælɪti/:n. 民族:The form required applicants to state their nationality.
4. nominal /ˈnɒmɪnəl/ adj. 名义上的；象征性的
📖 英文解释
existing in name only; very small and below the real value
✏️ 例句
He was the nominal head of the company but had no real power.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：nominal fee（象征性费用）、nominal value（名义价值）；经济学高频词
🧠 助记
nomin-（名字）+ al → 只有名字/名义上存在的 → 名义上的
nominal::/ˈnɒmɪnəl/:adj. 名义上的:The chairman held a nominal position with little authority.
5. notify /ˈnəʊtɪfaɪ/ v. 通知；告知
📖 英文解释
to formally inform someone of something
✏️ 例句
Please notify us in advance if you cannot attend the meeting.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：notify sb of sth（通知某人某事）、advance notification（提前通知）；正式书面语
🧠 助记
note（注意）+ ify（使动）→ 让人注意某事 → 通知
notify::/ˈnəʊtɪfaɪ/:v. 通知:The school will notify parents of any changes to the schedule.
6. oak /əʊk/ n. 橡木；橡树
📖 英文解释
a large, long-lived tree producing acorns; the hard durable wood of this tree
✏️ 例句
The old oak tree in the courtyard was over 200 years old.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：oak tree（橡树）、oak wood（橡木）、oak barrel（橡木桶）；文学语境常用象征力量
🧠 助记
oak 外形联想橡树的树干 O（圆形年轮），粗壮有力
oak::/əʊk/:n. 橡木:The floor was made of polished oak.
7. pale /peɪl/ adj. 苍白的；暗淡的；v. 变苍白
📖 英文解释
light in color or having a whitish complexion; to become less impressive
✏️ 例句
She looked pale and exhausted after working through the night.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：pale face（苍白的脸）、pale into insignificance（变得微不足道）；既作形容词也作动词
🧠 助记
pale 联想 'pail'（桶）→ 空桶是苍白无色的 → 苍白的
pale::/peɪl/:adj. 苍白的:His achievements pale in comparison to those of his predecessor.
8. paperback /ˈpeɪpəbæk/ n. 平装书
📖 英文解释
a book bound in a flexible paper cover, typically cheaper than hardback
✏️ 例句
She prefers to buy paperback editions because they are lighter to carry.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：paperback edition（平装版）；对应词：hardback/hardcover（精装书）
🧠 助记
paper（纸）+ back（背/封面）→ 纸质封面的书 → 平装书
paperback::/ˈpeɪpəbæk/:n. 平装书:The novel was released in paperback six months after the hardback.
9. pavement /ˈpeɪvmənt/ n. 路面；人行道
📖 英文解释
a paved surface; a raised path for pedestrians at the side of a road
✏️ 例句
Children were playing on the pavement outside the school.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：pave the pavement（铺路面）；英式：pavement = 人行道，美式：sidewalk = 人行道
🧠 助记
pave（铺）+ ment（名词后缀）→ 铺好的地面 → 路面/人行道
pavement::/ˈpeɪvmənt/:n. 路面:The pavement was slippery after the rain.
10. pint /paɪnt/ n. 品脱（容量单位）
📖 英文解释
a unit of liquid measure equal to half a quart (about 568ml in the UK)
✏️ 例句
He ordered a pint of beer at the pub.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：a pint of milk/beer（一品脱牛奶/啤酒）；英制单位，考研阅读中出现
🧠 助记
pint 联想 'paint'（油漆）→ 买油漆也要量单位，一品脱的容量
pint::/paɪnt/:n. 品脱（容量单位）:She drank a pint of water after the workout.
11. pope /pəʊp/ n. 罗马教皇
📖 英文解释
the head of the Roman Catholic Church
✏️ 例句
The Pope delivered a speech calling for world peace.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：the Pope's visit（教皇访问）、papal（形容词，教皇的）；首字母大写时特指罗马教皇
🧠 助记
pope 联想 'pop'（流行/父亲）→ 天主教信徒称教皇为精神之父
pope::/pəʊp/:n. 罗马教皇:Thousands gathered to hear the Pope speak in Rome.
12. rehearse /rɪˈhɜːs/ v. 排练；预演
📖 英文解释
to practice a play, piece of music, or other work for later performance
✏️ 例句
The actors rehearsed their lines for three weeks before opening night.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：rehearse for（为…排练）、dress rehearsal（正式彩排）
🧠 助记
re（再）+ hearse（灵车）→ 联想：演员不断重复排练，像绕着灵车走一样郑重其事
rehearse::/rɪˈhɜːs/:v. 排练:The band rehearsed every evening before the concert.
13. repay /rɪˈpeɪ/ v. 偿还；回报
📖 英文解释
to pay back money that has been borrowed; to reward someone for their help
✏️ 例句
It took her five years to repay her student loans.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：repay a loan/debt（偿还贷款/债务）、repay sb's kindness（回报某人的善意）
🧠 助记
re（再/回）+ pay（支付）→ 把钱付回去 → 偿还
repay::/rɪˈpeɪ/:v. 偿还:He promised to repay every penny he had borrowed.
14. sack /sæk/ n. 麻袋；v. 解雇
📖 英文解释
a large bag made of a rough material; to dismiss someone from employment
✏️ 例句
The manager was sacked for repeatedly missing deadlines.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：get the sack（被解雇）、give sb the sack（开除某人）；口语化用法
🧠 助记
sack 联想：装东西的大袋子，被解雇就像被装进袋子扔走了
sack::/sæk/:n. 麻袋:The employee was sacked for misconduct.
15. skeleton /ˈskelɪtən/ n. 骨架；骨骼
📖 英文解释
the internal framework of bones of a vertebrate; the bare framework of something
✏️ 例句
The museum displayed the skeleton of a prehistoric dinosaur.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：skeleton staff（最低限度的人员）、skeleton in the cupboard（不可告人的秘密）
🧠 助记
skele- 联想骷髅 skull → 整个骨架系统 → 骨骼
skeleton::/ˈskelɪtən/:n. 骨架:The skeleton of the building was visible before the walls were added.
16. spectacular /spekˈtækjələ/ adj. 壮观的；令人叹为观止的
📖 英文解释
beautiful in a dramatic and eye-catching way; impressive
✏️ 例句
The fireworks display was absolutely spectacular.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：spectacular view/scenery（壮观的景色）、spectacular performance（精彩的表演）
🧠 助记
spectac-（奇观）+ ular → 充满奇观的 → 壮观的；联想 spectacle（奇观）
spectacular::/spekˈtækjələ/:adj. 壮观的:The Grand Canyon offers a spectacular view.
17. statue /ˈstætʃuː/ n. 雕塑；塑像
📖 英文解释
a carved or cast figure of a person or animal, especially one that is life-size or larger
✏️ 例句
The Statue of Liberty is one of the most famous monuments in the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：bronze statue（铜像）、erect a statue（建立塑像）；区分 statue（雕塑）和 stature（名望/身高）
🧠 助记
stat-（站立）+ ue → 站立不动的雕像 → 雕塑
statue::/ˈstætʃuː/:n. 雕塑:A statue of the founding president stands in the city square.
18. stereo /ˈsteriəʊ/ n./adj. 立体声（的）
📖 英文解释
sound that is directed through two or more speakers; relating to stereophonic sound
✏️ 例句
She listened to music on her stereo every morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：stereo system（立体声系统）、in stereo（立体声地）；源自希腊语 stereos（立体的）
🧠 助记
stereo 联想 'stereoscope'（立体镜）→ 立体效果 → 立体声
stereo::/ˈsteriəʊ/:n./adj. 立体声（的）:The concert was broadcast in stereo on national radio.
19. strand /strænd/ n. 缕；股；v. 使搁浅
📖 英文解释
a single thin length of fiber; to leave someone in a difficult situation without means of escape
✏️ 例句
A strand of hair was caught in the zipper.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：a strand of hair/thread（一缕头发/线）、stranded passengers（被困乘客）
🧠 助记
strand 联想 'grand'（宏大）→ 宏大的绳索是由无数细缕组成的
strand::/strænd/:n. 缕:The storm left hundreds of passengers stranded at the airport.
20. tangle /ˈtæŋɡəl/ n./v. 纠纷；纠缠
📖 英文解释
a confused mass of things twisted together; to become or cause to become intertwined
✏️ 例句
Her hair got tangled in the wind.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：tangle with（与…纠缠）、a tangle of wires（一团乱线）；比喻法律纠纷
🧠 助记
tangle 联想 'mangle'（毁坏）→ 纠缠在一起就会破坏条理 → 纠缠
tangle::/ˈtæŋɡəl/:n./v. 纠纷:The fishing lines got into a terrible tangle.
21. temple /ˈtempəl/ n. 庙宇；太阳穴
📖 英文解释
a building devoted to religious worship; the flat part on either side of the forehead
✏️ 例句
Tourists came from around the world to visit the ancient temple.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：Buddhist temple（佛寺）、temple of learning（知识的殿堂）；一词两义：庙宇/太阳穴
🧠 助记
temple 联想 'temporal'（太阳穴的/时间的）→ 太阳穴/时间之庙
temple::/ˈtempəl/:n. 庙宇:The ancient temple was built over a thousand years ago.
22. transcend /trænˈsend/ v. 超越；超出
📖 英文解释
to go beyond the limits of something; to surpass
✏️ 例句
Great art transcends cultural boundaries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：transcend boundaries/limits（超越边界/限制）；考研写作高分词汇，替代 exceed/surpass
🧠 助记
trans（跨越）+ scend（爬升）→ 跨越并向上攀升 → 超越
transcend::/trænˈsend/:v. 超越:His kindness transcended all social barriers.
23. utilise /ˈjuːtəlaɪz/ v. 利用；使用
📖 英文解释
to make practical or effective use of something
✏️ 例句
We need to utilise our resources more efficiently.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：utilise resources/technology（利用资源/技术）；英式拼写，美式为 utilize；考研写作替代 use
🧠 助记
util-（有用）+ ise → 发挥有用之处 → 利用
utilise::/ˈjuːtəlaɪz/:v. 利用:The company aims to fully utilise its workforce.
24. vegetarian /ˌvedʒɪˈteəriən/ n./adj. 素食者；素食的
📖 英文解释
a person who does not eat meat; relating to vegetarianism
✏️ 例句
As a vegetarian, she avoids all meat and fish products.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：vegetarian diet（素食饮食）、become a vegetarian（成为素食者）
🧠 助记
vegetable（蔬菜）+ arian（…主义者）→ 吃蔬菜的人 → 素食者
vegetarian::/ˌvedʒɪˈteəriən/:n./adj. 素食者:The restaurant offers a wide selection of vegetarian dishes.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
week（周）+ day（天）→ 工作周里的天 → 工作日
weekday::/ˈwiːkdeɪ/:n. 工作日:Traffic is much heavier on weekdays than on weekends.
