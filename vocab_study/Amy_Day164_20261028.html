1. hasty /ˈheɪsti/ adj. 匆忙的；轻率的
📖 英文解释
done or made too quickly; acting without enough consideration
✏️ 例句
She made a hasty decision and later regretted it.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：a hasty conclusion（草率的结论）、hasty retreat（仓皇撤退）；近义词 rash, impetuous
🧠 助记
haste（匆忙）+ y → 充满匆忙感的 → 匆忙的
hasty::/ˈheɪsti/:adj. 匆忙的:Don't make hasty judgements based on first impressions.
2. impetus /ˈɪmpɪtəs/ n. 推动力；促进；动力
📖 英文解释
a force or energy that makes something happen or happen more quickly
✏️ 例句
The new funding provided fresh impetus for the research project.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：give impetus to（给予…动力）、fresh impetus（新动力）；考研写作高级替换词
🧠 助记
im-（进入）+ pet-（冲/撞）+ us → 冲入某事的推动力 → 动力
impetus::/ˈɪmpɪtəs/:n. 推动力:The speech gave fresh impetus to the campaign.
3. lucrative /ˈluːkrətɪv/ adj. 获利的；赚钱的
📖 英文解释
producing a great deal of profit
✏️ 例句
He left his stable job for a more lucrative position in finance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：lucrative business/deal（有利可图的生意/交易）、lucrative career（高薪职业）
🧠 助记
lucr-（利润，拉丁 lucrum）+ ative → 带来利润的 → 获利的
lucrative::/ˈluːkrətɪv/:adj. 获利的:Tourism is a highly lucrative industry for the local economy.
4. obedient /əˈbiːdiənt/ adj. 顺从的；服从的
📖 英文解释
complying or willing to comply with orders or requests; submissive to authority
✏️ 例句
The obedient child always listened to his parents.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：obedient to（服从于）；近义词 compliant, submissive；反义词 disobedient
🧠 助记
obedi-（听从）+ ent → 听从命令的 → 顺从的
obedient::/əˈbiːdiənt/:adj. 顺从的:The soldier was obedient to his commanding officer.
5. ozone /ˈəʊzəʊn/ n. 臭氧
📖 英文解释
a form of oxygen with a sharp smell, found especially in the upper atmosphere
✏️ 例句
Scientists are concerned about the depletion of the ozone layer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：ozone layer（臭氧层）、ozone depletion（臭氧耗尽）、ozone hole（臭氧空洞）
🧠 助记
ozone 来自希腊语 ozein（闻），臭氧有特殊气味 → 臭氧
ozone::/ˈəʊzəʊn/:n. 臭氧:CFCs were banned because they damage the ozone layer.
6. poke /pəʊk/ v./n. 戳；刺；捅
📖 英文解释
to push or jab with a finger, elbow, or pointed object
✏️ 例句
She poked him in the ribs to wake him up.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：poke fun at（嘲弄）、poke one's nose into（多管闲事）、poke around（到处探查）
🧠 助记
poke 联想 'spoke'（辐条）→ 辐条一样的尖细物体 → 戳/刺
poke::/pəʊk/:v./n. 戳:Stop poking your nose into other people's business.
7. radiate /ˈreɪdieɪt/ v. 辐射；放射；散发
📖 英文解释
to emit energy in the form of rays or waves; to show a quality strongly
✏️ 例句
The stove radiated warmth throughout the room.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：radiate heat/light（辐射热/光）、radiate confidence（散发自信）
🧠 助记
radi-（射线/辐射）+ ate → 发出射线 → 辐射/散发
radiate::/ˈreɪdieɪt/:v. 辐射:She radiated happiness on her wedding day.
8. rein /reɪn/ n. 缰绳；v. 控制；约束
📖 英文解释
a long narrow strap attached to a bridle; to restrain or control
✏️ 例句
The government must rein in public spending.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：rein in（控制/约束）、give free rein to（给…充分自由）、hold the reins（掌握控制权）
🧠 助记
rein 联想 'rain'（雨）→ 用缰绳控制就像雨水约束洪流 → 控制/缰绳
rein::/reɪn/:n. 缰绳:The teacher needs to rein in the rowdy students.
9. rot /rɒt/ v./n. 腐烂；腐朽
📖 英文解释
to decay through natural breakdown; decomposed material
✏️ 例句
The wooden fence began to rot after years of rain.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：rot away（腐烂殆尽）、stop the rot（阻止恶化）、rot in prison（在监狱中腐朽）
🧠 助记
rot 联想 'robot'（机器人）→ 机器人不会腐烂，但有机物会 rot → 腐烂
rot::/rɒt/:v./n. 腐烂:The fruit left on the tree began to rot after the harvest.
10. sigh /saɪ/ v./n. 叹息；叹气
📖 英文解释
to emit a long deep breath expressing sadness, relief, or tiredness
✏️ 例句
She sighed deeply when she heard the disappointing news.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：heave a sigh（长叹一声）、a sigh of relief（如释重负地叹气）
🧠 助记
sigh 象声词，像叹气的声音 'siiiigh' → 叹息
sigh::/saɪ/:v./n. 叹息:He breathed a sigh of relief when the exam was over.
11. singular /ˈsɪŋɡjələ/ adj. 非凡的；单数的；异常的
📖 英文解释
remarkably great or conspicuous; single; in grammar, denoting one person or thing
✏️ 例句
She showed a singular talent for languages from an early age.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：singular achievement（非凡成就）、singular form（单数形式）；语法和文学语境均常用
🧠 助记
single（单一）+ ar → 单一的/非凡突出的 → 单数/非凡
singular::/ˈsɪŋɡjələ/:adj. 非凡的:His singular dedication to the project earned him great respect.
12. smog /smɒɡ/ n. 烟雾；雾霾
📖 英文解释
a mixture of smoke and fog forming dense haze, often from pollution
✏️ 例句
The city was covered in thick smog due to industrial emissions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：smog alert（雾霾警报）、heavy smog（浓重雾霾）；合成词 smoke + fog
🧠 助记
smog = smoke（烟）+ fog（雾）→ 烟雾混合 → 雾霾
smog::/smɒɡ/:n. 烟雾:Residents were advised to stay indoors due to heavy smog.
13. snatch /snætʃ/ v. 抢夺；猛地抓住；n. 片段
📖 英文解释
to seize something quickly and roughly; a short period of activity
✏️ 例句
A thief snatched her bag and ran into the crowd.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：snatch away（夺走）、snatch a victory（险胜）、snatch of sleep（短暂睡眠）
🧠 助记
snatch 联想 'catch'（抓住）→ 突然快速地抓住 → 抢夺
snatch::/snætʃ/:v. 抢夺:He snatched the opportunity to speak with the director.
14. startle /ˈstɑːtəl/ v. 吃惊；使惊跳
📖 英文解释
to cause to feel sudden shock or surprise
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：be startled by（被…吓到）、startling news（令人震惊的消息）
🧠 助记
start（开始/突然动）+ le（反复动词）→ 突然动了一下 → 使惊跳
startle::/ˈstɑːtəl/:v. 吃惊:The sudden noise startled the birds and they flew away.
15. upstairs /ˌʌpˈsteəz/ adv./adj./n. 楼上

✏️ 例句
The children were playing upstairs while the adults talked.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
up（上）+ stairs（楼梯）→ 走上楼梯的方向 → 楼上
upstairs::/ˌʌpˈsteəz/:adv./adj./n. 楼上:She heard footsteps upstairs in the middle of the night.
16. aftermath /ˈɑːftəmæθ/ n. 后果；余波
📖 英文解释
the consequences or after-effects of a significant unpleasant event
✏️ 例句
The city struggled to recover in the aftermath of the earthquake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
after（之后）+ math（数学/结果）→ 事件之后的计算结果 → 后果
aftermath::/ˈɑːftəmæθ/:n. 后果:The government struggled to manage the aftermath of the financial crisis.
17. applaud /əˈplɔːd/ v. 鼓掌；称赞
📖 英文解释

The audience applauded loudly at the end of the performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
ap-（朝向）+ plaud（鼓掌，拉丁 plaudere）→ 向表演者拍手 → 鼓掌
applaud::/əˈplɔːd/:v. 鼓掌:We should applaud the government's efforts to reduce pollution.
18. appraisal /əˈpreɪzəl/ n. 评估；评价
📖 英文解释
a formal assessment of the value or quality of something
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：performance appraisal（绩效评估）、property appraisal（房产评估）
🧠 助记
ap-（朝向）+ praise（赞扬）+ al → 对某事做出正式评价 → 评估
appraisal::/əˈpreɪzəl/:n. 评估:She received a positive appraisal from her manager.
19. assurance /əˈʃʊərəns/ n. 保证；保险；自信
📖 英文解释
a positive declaration intended to give confidence; certainty about something
✏️ 例句
The manager gave her staff an assurance that their jobs were safe.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：give sb an assurance（给予某人保证）、life assurance（人寿保险）；英式中 assurance 指寿险
🧠 助记
assure（确保）+ ance → 确保某事的行为/结果 → 保证/自信
assurance::/əˈʃʊərəns/:n. 保证:She spoke with assurance, clearly confident in her decision.

barber::/ˈbɑːbə/:n. 理发师:The barber gave him a short back and sides.

bullet::/ˈbʊlɪt/:n. 子弹:He had to bite the bullet and tell his boss the bad news.
24. captain /ˈkæptɪn/ n./v. 船长；机长；队长；率领
📖 英文解释
the person in command of a ship or aircraft; the leader of a sports team

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

captain::/ˈkæptɪn/:n./v. 船长:She was elected captain of the national football team.
25. despise /dɪˈspaɪz/ v. 轻视；鄙视
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
She despised dishonesty in any form.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
de-（向下）+ spise（看）→ 往下看某人 → 轻视/鄙视
despise::/dɪˈspaɪz/:v. 轻视:He despised those who used their power to bully others.
