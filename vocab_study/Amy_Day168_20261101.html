1. frown /fraʊn/ v./n. 皱眉；不赞成
📖 英文解释
To furrow one's brows in displeasure or concentration; an expression of disapproval.
✏️ 例句
She frowned at the unexpected news.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研情感描写；frown on/upon（不赞成）；近义词：scowl。
🧠 助记
f + rown（皱）→ 皱眉头
frown::/fraʊn/:v./n. 皱眉:She frowned upon his reckless behavior.
2. lateral /ˈlætərəl/ adj. 侧面的；横向的 n. 侧面
📖 英文解释
Relating to the side or sides; directed to the side.
✏️ 例句
Lateral thinking helps solve creative problems.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研学术词；lateral thinking（横向思维）；lateral movement（侧向移动）。
🧠 助记
later（后来）+ al → 侧向延伸
lateral::/ˈlætərəl/:adj. 侧面的:Lateral thinking leads to innovative solutions.
3. preface /ˈprefəs/ n. 前言；序言 v. 为…作序
📖 英文解释
An introduction to a book or speech, typically stating its purpose and scope.
✏️ 例句
The author wrote a brief preface to explain her goals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研阅读理解；preface to（前言）；近义词：foreword, introduction, preamble。
🧠 助记
pre（前）+ face（面孔）→ 书的前脸 → 序言
preface::/ˈprefəs/:n. 前言:The preface outlined the book's main themes.
4. query /ˈkwɪəri/ n./v. 疑问；质疑；查询
📖 英文解释
A question or doubt; to ask a question or express doubt about.
✏️ 例句
She raised a query about the report's accuracy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研学术/商务；raise a query（提出疑问）；database query（数据库查询）。
🧠 助记
que（问，法语词根）+ ry → 问题、疑问
query::/ˈkwɪəri/:n./v. 疑问:He queried whether the data was accurate.
5. splash /splæʃ/ v./n. 溅；飞溅；引人注目的报道
📖 英文解释
To cause liquid to move and scatter; a sound or mark made by liquid splashing.
✏️ 例句
The car splashed water on pedestrians.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研媒体类：make a splash（引起轰动）；splash out（挥霍）；front-page splash。
🧠 助记
spl（水声）+ ash → 水溅起的声音
splash::/splæʃ/:v./n. 溅:The news made a big splash in the media.
6. stride /straɪd/ v./n. 大步走；步伐；进步
📖 英文解释
To walk with long, decisive steps; a step or rate of progress.
✏️ 例句
She strode confidently into the room.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：make great strides（取得重大进步）；get into one's stride（进入状态）。
🧠 助记
str（强力）+ ide（步伐）→ 有力的大步
stride::/straɪd/:v./n. 大步走:Science has made great strides in recent decades.
7. torch /tɔːtʃ/ n. 火炬；手电筒（英式）
📖 英文解释
A portable battery-powered electric light; a burning stick used as a light source.
✏️ 例句
He carried a torch to light the dark cave.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化/体育类：Olympic torch（奥运火炬）；carry a torch for sb（暗恋某人）。
🧠 助记
torch = 火炬，形如点燃的棒
torch::/tɔːtʃ/:n. 火炬:The athlete carried the Olympic torch proudly.
8. butcher /ˈbʊtʃə(r)/ n. 屠夫；肉店 v. 屠杀
📖 英文解释
A person who slaughters and sells meat; to slaughter or kill brutally.
✏️ 例句
The butcher sold fresh beef every morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研阅读偶见；比喻义：butcher（残忍屠杀）；the butcher's（肉铺）。
🧠 助记
but（切）+ cher → 切肉的人 → 屠夫
butcher::/ˈbʊtʃə(r)/:n. 屠夫:The butcher trimmed the fat from the meat.
9. compress /kəmˈpres/ v. 压缩；压紧 n. 敷布
📖 英文解释
To squeeze or press together; to reduce in size, quantity, or time.
✏️ 例句
The file was compressed to save disk space.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研学术/IT类；compressed air（压缩空气）；compression（n.压缩）；time compression（时间压缩）。
🧠 助记
com（共同）+ press（压）→ 共同压紧 → 压缩
compress::/kəmˈpres/:v. 压缩:Data must be compressed before transmission.
10. diffuse /dɪˈfjuːz/ v. 弥漫；扩散 adj. 散漫的
📖 英文解释
To spread over a wide area; not concentrated or lacking clarity.
✏️ 例句
Light diffuses through the frosted glass.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研物理/学术类；diffuse light（漫射光）；diffusion（n.扩散）；注意与 defuse（拆除）区分。
🧠 助记
dif（分散）+ fuse（融合）→ 分散融合 → 弥漫
diffuse::/dɪˈfjuːz/:v. 弥漫:The scent diffused throughout the room.
11. discern /dɪˈsɜːn/ v. 辨别；识别；看出
📖 英文解释
To perceive or recognize something; to distinguish with difficulty by sight or other senses.
✏️ 例句
It was hard to discern the figure in the fog.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频学术词；discerning（有鉴别力的）；discernment（n.辨别力）；常见于阅读理解。
🧠 助记
dis（分开）+ cern（看，如 concern）→ 分开看清楚 → 辨别
discern::/dɪˈsɜːn/:v. 辨别:She could discern a pattern in the data.
12. goodness /ˈɡʊdnəs/ n. 善良；美德；营养价值
📖 英文解释
The quality of being morally good or virtuous; the beneficial quality.
✏️ 例句
He was moved by her goodness and generosity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研伦理/人文类；goodness of heart（善良）；thank goodness（谢天谢地，感叹语）。
🧠 助记
good（好）+ ness → 好的品质 → 善良
goodness::/ˈɡʊdnəs/:n. 善良:She was admired for her natural goodness.
13. grieve /ɡriːv/ v. 悲伤；哀悼；使悲痛
📖 英文解释
To feel intense sorrow; to cause great distress to someone.
✏️ 例句
She grieved the loss of her husband for years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研情感/心理类；grieve for/over（为…悲痛）；grief（n.悲痛）；grievance（n.不满）。
🧠 助记
grieve → grief（悲伤）的动词形式
grieve::/ɡriːv/:v. 悲伤:He grieved deeply over his father's death.
14. mute /mjuːt/ adj. 沉默的；哑的 v. 使消音 n. 哑巴
📖 英文解释
Silent; unable to speak; to deaden or muffle the sound of.
✏️ 例句
She remained mute throughout the interrogation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频；mute button（静音键）；stand mute（保持沉默）；deaf-mute（聋哑人）。
🧠 助记
mute → mu（不出声）+ te → 沉默
mute::/mjuːt/:adj. 沉默的:He pressed mute on the remote control.
15. roast /rəʊst/ v./n./adj. 烤；烧烤；烤制的
📖 英文解释
To cook food by dry heat in an oven or over fire; a piece of roasted meat.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
roast → 烤（读音像肉死特）
roast::/rəʊst/:v./n./adj. 烤:Sunday roast is a British tradition.
16. slum /slʌm/ n. 贫民窟 v. 参观贫民区
📖 英文解释
A heavily populated urban area characterized by poor living conditions.
✏️ 例句
Many families live in crowded slums without clean water.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研社会类；slum area（贫民区）；slum clearance（贫民窟清除）；slum tourism（贫民窟旅游）。
🧠 助记
slum → s（肮脏）+ lum（昏暗）→ 昏暗肮脏 → 贫民窟
slum::/slʌm/:n. 贫民窟 v. 参观贫民区:Urban slums pose major public health challenges.
17. steward /ˈstjuːəd/ n. 管家；乘务员；管理人
📖 英文解释
A person who looks after passengers on a ship, aircraft, or train; one responsible for the management of an estate.
✏️ 例句
The flight steward served drinks to passengers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研；stewardship（n.管理职责）；environmental stewardship（环境管理责任）。
🧠 助记

steward::/ˈstjuːəd/:n. 管家:He served as steward of the large estate.
18. straw /strɔː/ n. 稻草；吸管；微不足道之物
📖 英文解释
Dried stalks of grain; a thin tube for sucking liquid; something of little value.
✏️ 例句
The last straw broke the camel's back.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研习语：the last straw（压垮骆驼的最后一根稻草）；straw poll（民意抽样调查）；clutch at straws（抱侥幸心理）。
🧠 助记
straw（草）→ 稻草吸管都是空心细长物
straw::/strɔː/:n. 稻草:That mistake was the last straw for the boss.
19. viable /ˈvaɪəbl/ adj. 可行的；有活力的
📖 英文解释
Capable of working successfully; feasible; able to survive and develop.
✏️ 例句
The committee considered several viable solutions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研超高频；viable option/solution/alternative（可行的选择/方案/替代）；viability（n.可行性）。
🧠 助记
vi（生命力）+ able（能够）→ 有生命力能够成功 → 可行的
viable::/ˈvaɪəbl/:adj. 可行的:Solar energy is a viable alternative to fossil fuels.
20. afterward /ˈɑːftəwəd/ adv. 后来；以后（afterwards 的美式拼法）
📖 英文解释
At a later or future time; subsequently.
✏️ 例句
We had dinner first and went for a walk afterward.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研注意 afterward（美式）与 afterwards（英式）均可；用于叙事语境。
🧠 助记
after（之后）+ ward（方向）→ 朝后来的方向
afterward::/ˈɑːftəwəd/:adv. 后来:He left immediately and regretted it afterward.

angel::/ˈeɪndʒəl/:n. 天使:She was an angel to everyone in need.
22. blunder /ˈblʌndə(r)/ n./v. 大错；失误；犯大错
📖 英文解释
A stupid or careless mistake; to make a stupid or careless mistake.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频；commit/make a blunder（犯大错）；近义词：mistake, error, gaffe。
🧠 助记

blunder::/ˈblʌndə(r)/:n./v. 大错:His blunder in the final round cost them the game.

booth::/buːð/:n. 电话亭:Voters entered the booth to cast their ballots.

cigar::/sɪˈɡɑː(r)/:n. 雪茄烟:He lit a cigar and sat back in his chair.
