1. commonwealth /ˈkɒmənwelθ/ n. 联邦；共和国；英联邦
📖 英文解释
An independent state or community; a group of sovereign nations associated for common purposes.
✏️ 例句
Australia is a member of the Commonwealth.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研政治/历史类；the Commonwealth（英联邦）；commonwealth of nations（国家联合体）。
🧠 助记
common（共同）+ wealth（财富）→ 共同财富 → 联邦
commonwealth::/ˈkɒmənwelθ/:n. 联邦:The Commonwealth promotes shared values among nations.
2. congratulate /kənˈɡrætʃuleɪt/ v. 祝贺；向…道喜
📖 英文解释
To express one's pleasure at the success or good fortune of someone.
✏️ 例句
I congratulate you on your promotion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研；congratulate sb on sth（就某事祝贺某人）；congratulations（n.祝贺，常复数）。
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ gratulate（高兴）→ 共同高兴 → 祝贺
congratulate::/kənˈɡrætʃuleɪt/:v. 祝贺:We congratulate her on winning the award.
3. cricket /ˈkrɪkɪt/ n. 板球；蟋蟀
📖 英文解释
A game played with a bat and ball on a large grass field; a jumping insect related to grasshoppers.
✏️ 例句
Cricket is extremely popular in England and India.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化类；cricket match（板球比赛）；not cricket（不公平，俚语）；cricket（蟋蟀）在诗歌中常见。
🧠 助记
crick（咯吱声）+ et → 蟋蟀发出咯吱声
cricket::/ˈkrɪkɪt/:n. 板球:Cricket is a beloved sport in the UK.
4. diversion /daɪˈvɜːʃn/ n. 转移；绕行；娱乐消遣
📖 英文解释
The action of turning something aside from its course; a recreation or entertainment.
✏️ 例句
The road was closed due to a diversion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研；diversion of funds（资金转移）；divert traffic（分流交通）；diversionary tactic（转移注意力的手段）。
🧠 助记
di（分离）+ version（方向）→ 改变方向 → 转移
diversion::/daɪˈvɜːʃn/:n. 转移:Reading is a pleasant diversion after work.
5. ecology /iˈkɒlədʒi/ n. 生态学；生态环境
📖 英文解释
The branch of biology dealing with the relations of organisms to one another and to their physical environment.
✏️ 例句
The oil spill seriously damaged the local ecology.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研环境/科学类超高频；ecological（adj.生态的）；ecosystem（生态系统）；ecological balance（生态平衡）。
🧠 助记
eco（家园/环境）+ logy（学科）→ 研究环境的学科
ecology::/iˈkɒlədʒi/:n. 生态学:Industrial pollution threatens local ecology.
6. elbow /ˈelbəʊ/ n. 手肘 v. 用肘推挤
📖 英文解释
The joint connecting the forearm and upper arm; to push with elbows.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研；elbow room（活动空间）；elbow grease（体力劳动）；more power to your elbow（加油）。
🧠 助记
el（弯曲）+ bow（弓）→ 弯曲如弓 → 手肘
elbow::/ˈelbəʊ/:n. 手肘 v. 用肘推挤:She elbowed her way to the front of the queue.
7. equity /ˈekwɪti/ n. 公平；权益；股本
📖 英文解释
The quality of being fair and impartial; the value of shares in a company.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研超高频；equity vs equality（公正 vs 平等）；private equity（私募股权）；equity market（股市）。
🧠 助记
equi（相等）+ ty → 相等对待 → 公平
equity::/ˈekwɪti/:n. 公平:Equity in healthcare is a key policy goal.
8. guild /ɡɪld/ n. 行会；协会；同业公会
📖 英文解释
A medieval association of craftsmen or merchants; an organization of people with common interests.
✏️ 例句
The medieval guild controlled trade in the city.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研历史/经济类；trade guild（行会）；guild member（会员）；注意与 build 区分拼写。
🧠 助记
guild → guil（技艺）+ d → 有技艺的人结成的团体 → 行会
guild::/ɡɪld/:n. 行会:Craftsmen formed a guild to protect their trade.
9. instrumental /ˌɪnstrəˈmentl/ adj. 有帮助的；起重要作用的；乐器演奏的

instrumental::/ˌɪnstrəˈmentl/:adj. 有帮助的:She was instrumental in securing the deal.
10. keyboard /ˈkiːbɔːd/ n. 键盘

keyboard::/ˈkiːbɔːd/:n. 键盘:She practiced scales on the piano keyboard.
11. latitude /ˈlætɪtjuːd/ n. 纬度；自由度；空间
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

latitude::/ˈlætɪtjuːd/:n. 纬度:Students were given latitude to choose their topics.
