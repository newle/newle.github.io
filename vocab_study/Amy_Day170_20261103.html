1. endorse /ɪnˈdɔːs/ v. 支持；背书；签注
📖 英文解释
To declare one's public approval of; to sign the back of a cheque; to recommend.
✏️ 例句
The celebrity endorsed the new product.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频；endorse a candidate（支持候选人）；endorsement（n.背书/支持）；brand endorsement（品牌代言）。
🧠 助记
en（进入）+ dorse（背）→ 在背面签字 → 背书/支持
endorse::/ɪnˈdɔːs/:v. 支持:The senator endorsed the new healthcare plan.
2. expend /ɪkˈspend/ v. 花费；消耗；用尽
📖 英文解释
To spend or use up (a resource such as money, time, or energy).
✏️ 例句
They expended enormous effort on the project.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频；expend resources/energy（消耗资源/精力）；expenditure（n.支出）；近义词：spend, consume。
🧠 助记
ex（出去）+ pend（花费，如 spend）→ 花出去 → 花费
expend::/ɪkˈspend/:v. 花费:Governments expend billions on defense annually.
3. flour /flaʊə(r)/ n. 面粉；粉末 v. 撒面粉
📖 英文解释
A powder obtained by grinding grain, used to make bread, cakes, etc.
✏️ 例句
She sifted the flour before mixing it.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研基础/饮食类；plain flour（普通面粉）；self-raising flour（自发面粉）；注意与 flower 区分。
🧠 助记
flour → flow（流）+ r → 像流沙一样的细粉 → 面粉
flour::/flaʊə(r)/:n. 面粉:The recipe calls for two cups of flour.
4. forthcoming /ˌfɔːθˈkʌmɪŋ/ adj. 即将到来的；乐于提供的；坦率的
📖 英文解释
About to happen or appear; ready or made available when required.
✏️ 例句
Details of the forthcoming election were announced.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研学术/新闻类；forthcoming book/event（即将出版的书/即将到来的活动）；not very forthcoming（不太愿意提供信息）。
🧠 助记
forth（向前）+ coming（来）→ 正在向前来临 → 即将到来的
forthcoming::/ˌfɔːθˈkʌmɪŋ/:adj. 即将到来的:The forthcoming report will address these issues.
5. handy /ˈhændi/ adj. 方便的；手巧的；近便的
📖 英文解释
Convenient to use or access; skillful with one's hands.
✏️ 例句
A dictionary is handy when learning a new language.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研；come in handy（派上用场）；handy tool（实用工具）；near synonym: convenient, useful。
🧠 助记
hand（手）+ y → 用手就能搞定 → 方便的
handy::/ˈhændi/:adj. 方便的:A pocket dictionary can be very handy.
6. inventory /ˈɪnvəntri/ n. 详细目录；存货清单；盘点 v. 编制清单
📖 英文解释
A complete list of items; a quantity of goods held in stock.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研商业/经济类；inventory management（库存管理）；inventory control（存货控制）；take inventory（盘点）。
🧠 助记
invent（发现）+ ory → 找出并记录的东西 → 详细目录
inventory::/ˈɪnvəntri/:n. 详细目录:The store conducts a monthly inventory check.

🧠 助记
mo（motor 汽车）+ tel（hotel 旅馆）→ 汽车旅馆
motel::/məʊˈtel/:n. 汽车旅馆:They checked into a motel near the highway.

naval::/ˈneɪvl/:adj. 海军的:The naval fleet conducted exercises in the Pacific.

notebook::/ˈnəʊtbʊk/:n. 笔记本:She recorded her observations in a notebook.
10. palm /pɑːm/ n. 手掌；棕榈树 v. 藏于掌中

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

palm → 手掌平放如棕榈叶展开
palm::/pɑːm/:n. 手掌:The magician concealed the card in his palm.
11. relay /ˈriːleɪ/ n./v. 继电器；接力；转播

relay::/ˈriːleɪ/:n./v. 继电器:The team won gold in the 4x100m relay.
12. sandwich /ˈsænwɪdʒ/ n. 三明治 v. 夹在中间

sandwich::/ˈsænwɪdʒ/:n. 三明治 v. 夹在中间:He was sandwiched between two demanding bosses.
13. scent /sent/ n. 气味；香水；嗅迹 v. 嗅到；察觉
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
scent → sent（发送）→ 气味被发送到鼻子 → 气味
scent::/sent/:n. 气味:The detective picked up the scent of the suspect.

shrewd::/ʃruːd/:adj. 精明的:A shrewd investor anticipates market trends.

veto::/ˈviːtəʊ/:n./v. 否决:China used its veto to block the resolution.

widow::/ˈwɪdəʊ/:n. 寡妇:She was left a widow with three young children.

ambassador::/æmˈbæsədə(r)/:n. 大使:The ambassador presented her credentials to the president.
