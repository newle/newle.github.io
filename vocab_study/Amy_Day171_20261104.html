1. burglar /ˈbɜːrɡlər/ n. 夜贼；入室盗窃者
📖 英文解释
a person who illegally enters a building to steal things, especially at night
✏️ 例句
The burglar broke in through the back window while the family slept.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研法律类：burglar（入室盗贼）vs. thief（小偷）vs. robber（抢劫犯）；动词 burgle；名词 burglary
🧠 助记
burglar 发音像'爆个了'，突然爆破进门的盗贼——入室盗窃者
burglar::/ˈbɜːrɡlər/:n. 夜贼:The burglar was caught on the security camera.
2. champagne /ʃæmˈpeɪn/ n. 香槟酒
📖 英文解释
a sparkling white wine produced in the Champagne region of France, often drunk to celebrate special occasions
✏️ 例句
They opened a bottle of champagne to celebrate their victory.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化类；注意发音（非'champ-agne'）；champagne lifestyle（奢华生活方式）
🧠 助记
cham + pagne，想象'闪闪'冒泡的庆典酒——香槟
champagne::/ʃæmˈpeɪn/:n. 香槟酒:They toasted with champagne at the New Year's party.
3. conquest /ˈkɒŋkwest/ n. 征服；占领；赢得的东西
📖 英文解释
the act of conquering a country or group of people; the successful achievement of something difficult
✏️ 例句
The Norman Conquest of England occurred in 1066.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研历史类高频：the conquest of nature（征服自然）；make a conquest of（征服某人/地方）
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ quest（追求），共同追求并取得——征服
conquest::/ˈkɒŋkwest/:n. 征服:Space exploration represents humanity's conquest of the unknown.
4. convene /kənˈviːn/ v. 召集；集会；开会
📖 英文解释
to bring people together for a meeting or other purpose; to gather together formally
✏️ 例句
The president convened an emergency meeting of the cabinet.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研正式语体：convene a meeting/conference（召集会议）；对比 convention（大会；惯例）
🧠 助记
con（一起）+ vene（来，同 venue），来到一起——集会；召集
convene::/kənˈviːn/:v. 召集:The committee was convened to discuss the new policy.
5. conversion /kənˈvɜːrʒən/ n. 转变；转化；改变信仰
📖 英文解释
the process of changing from one form, system, or belief to another
✏️ 例句
The conversion of solar energy into electricity is becoming more efficient.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多领域：energy conversion（能量转化）；religious conversion（宗教改信）；动词 convert
🧠 助记
con（完全）+ version（版本/转向），完全转向另一版本——转变；转化
conversion::/kənˈvɜːrʒən/:n. 转变:The conversion of the factory into apartments took two years.
6. coward /ˈkaʊərd/ n. 懦夫；胆小鬼 adj. 懦弱的
📖 英文解释
a person who is contemptibly lacking in courage; someone who avoids dangerous or difficult situations
✏️ 例句
He was called a coward for refusing to stand up for his friend.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研对比：coward vs. brave/courageous；形容词 cowardly（懦弱的）；名词 cowardice（懦弱）
🧠 助记
cow（奶牛）+ ard，像奶牛一样温顺害怕的人——懦夫
coward::/ˈkaʊərd/:n. 懦夫:It takes more courage to admit a mistake than to remain a coward.
7. dizzy /ˈdɪzi/ adj. 头晕的；眼花的；使人头晕的
📖 英文解释
having or causing a whirling sensation and loss of balance; feeling confused or overwhelmed
✏️ 例句
She felt dizzy after spinning around so many times.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研描写类：feel dizzy（感到头晕）；a dizzy height（令人眩晕的高度）；dizzy with success（成功冲昏头脑）
🧠 助记
dizzy 发音像'的这'，这一圈转得头晕了——头晕的
dizzy::/ˈdɪzi/:adj. 头晕的:The rapid changes left him dizzy and disoriented.
8. exile /ˈeksaɪl/ n./v. 流放；放逐；流亡者
📖 英文解释
the state of being barred from one's native country; to banish someone from their homeland
✏️ 例句
The dissident was sent into exile after criticizing the government.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研政治/历史：in exile（流亡中）；go into exile（流亡）；political exile（政治流亡者）
🧠 助记
ex（出去）+ ile（岛），被赶出去到孤岛——流放；流亡
exile::/ˈeksaɪl/:n./v. 流放:The former leader lived in exile for twenty years.
9. futile /ˈfjuːtaɪl/ adj. 无效的；徒劳的；无益的
📖 英文解释
producing no useful result; pointless; having no practical purpose
✏️ 例句
It was futile to argue with him once he had made up his mind.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频形容词：a futile attempt/effort（徒劳的尝试）；名词 futility（徒劳；无益）
🧠 助记
futile 发音像'否太了'，'这也太无用了'——徒劳的；无效的
futile::/ˈfjuːtaɪl/:adj. 无效的:All their attempts to rescue the project proved futile.
10. gracious /ˈɡreɪʃəs/ adj. 亲切的；仁慈的；优雅的
📖 英文解释
courteous, kind, and pleasant, especially in a way that shows good breeding; elegant and tasteful
✏️ 例句
She was gracious enough to forgive him despite everything.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：gracious living（优雅的生活）；Good gracious!（天哪！）；搭配 gracious manner/host
🧠 助记
grace（优雅/恩典）+ -ious，充满优雅和恩典的——亲切的；优雅的
gracious::/ˈɡreɪʃəs/:adj. 亲切的:The gracious host welcomed all guests warmly.
11. hatch /hætʃ/ v. 孵化；策划 n. 舱口；小门
📖 英文解释
to emerge from an egg; to devise a plan (especially a secret one); a small opening in a floor, wall, or roof
✏️ 例句
The chicks hatched three days earlier than expected.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多义：hatch a plot/plan（策划阴谋）；hatch out（孵出）；escape hatch（紧急出口）
🧠 助记
hatch 发音像'哈出'，小鸡哈地一声破壳而出——孵化；策划
hatch::/hætʃ/:v. 孵化:They hatched a plan to escape from the building.
12. heap /hiːp/ n. 堆；大量 v. 堆积
📖 英文解释
an untidy pile of things; a large amount of something; to put things into a pile
✏️ 例句
There was a heap of clothes on the floor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研口语化表达：heaps of（大量的）= lots of；heap up（积累）；heap praise/blame on（大加赞扬/责备）
🧠 助记
heap 发音像'嗨噗'，把东西嗨地一声堆成一堆——堆；大量
heap::/hiːp/:n. 堆:She received heaps of congratulations after the award.
13. heave /hiːv/ v. 用力拉；抛；起伏 n. 用力拉；起伏
📖 英文解释
to lift or move something heavy with great effort; to rise and fall repeatedly; to throw with force
✏️ 例句
They heaved the heavy boxes onto the truck.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：heave a sigh（深深叹气）；heave at（用力拉）；海浪起伏也用 heave
🧠 助记
heave 发音像'嗨v'，用力一嗨，重物飞出去——用力拉；抛
heave::/hiːv/:v. 用力拉:She heaved a sigh of relief when the results were announced.
14. hike /haɪk/ v./n. 远足；徒步；（价格）大幅上涨
📖 英文解释
a long walk in the countryside; to walk for a long distance; to increase prices or taxes by a large amount
✏️ 例句
They went for a hike in the mountains every weekend.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研双义：go hiking（徒步旅行）；a hike in prices/taxes（价格/税收大幅上涨）
🧠 助记
hike 发音像'嗨客'，嗨到户外做徒步旅行的客人——远足；上涨
hike::/haɪk/:v./n. 远足:The government announced a hike in fuel prices.
15. hollow /ˈhɒloʊ/ adj. 空洞的；中空的；虚假的 n. 洞；坑 v. 挖空
📖 英文解释
having an empty space inside; not solid; lacking real value or significance; a hole or depression in something
✏️ 例句
The tree trunk was hollow and birds nested inside.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研引申义：hollow promises（空洞的承诺）；hollow victory（惨胜）；beat sb. hollow（彻底击败）
🧠 助记
hollow 发音像'后楼'，后楼空空如也——中空的；空洞的
hollow::/ˈhɒloʊ/:adj. 空洞的:His hollow promises could not win back her trust.
16. huddle /ˈhʌdəl/ v./n. 挤成一团；蜷缩；密谈
📖 英文解释
to crowd together in a tight group; to curl one's body into a small space; a small group of people gathered closely together
✏️ 例句
The children huddled together to keep warm in the cold.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：huddle around（围成一团）；huddle together（挤在一起）；go into a huddle（密谈）
🧠 助记
huddle 发音像'哈得了'，大家哈在一起挤成一团——挤成一团
huddle::/ˈhʌdəl/:v./n. 挤成一团:The team huddled around the coach for last-minute instructions.
17. inland /ˈɪnlænd/ adj./adv. 内陆的；内地的
📖 英文解释
situated in or relating to the interior part of a country, away from the sea or borders; towards the interior of a country
✏️ 例句
They traveled inland to explore the remote villages.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研地理类：inland areas/regions（内陆地区）；对比 coastal（沿海的）；inland sea（内陆海）
🧠 助记
in（里面）+ land（陆地），陆地里面的——内陆的
inland::/ˈɪnlænd/:adj./adv. 内陆的:The goods were transported to inland distribution centers.
18. insulate /ˈɪnsjuleɪt/ v. 隔热；绝缘；使隔离
📖 英文解释
to protect something with material that prevents heat, sound, or electricity from passing through; to protect someone from unpleasant experiences
✏️ 例句
The walls were insulated to keep the house warm in winter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研科技/环境类：insulate against heat/cold/noise（隔热/隔冷/隔音）；名词 insulation；形容词 insulated
🧠 助记
insul（岛，同 island）+ -ate，像岛屿一样隔离开来——绝缘；隔离
insulate::/ˈɪnsjuleɪt/:v. 隔热:Good insulation can insulate your home from heat loss.
19. intermediate /ˌɪntərˈmiːdiət/ adj. 中间的；中级的 n. 中间物
📖 英文解释
coming between two things in time, place, or degree; having more than basic knowledge but not yet advanced
✏️ 例句
She enrolled in an intermediate English course.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研教育类：intermediate level（中级水平）；intermediate stage（中间阶段）；intermediate product（中间产品）
🧠 助记
inter（之间）+ medi（中间）+ -ate，位于两者中间的——中间的；中级的
intermediate::/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiət/:adj. 中间的:The course is designed for intermediate learners.
20. invaluable /ɪnˈvæljuəbl/ adj. 无价的；极其宝贵的
📖 英文解释
extremely useful or valuable; too precious to be measured in monetary terms
✏️ 例句
Her experience in the field proved invaluable to the research team.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
注意：invaluable ≠ 无价值，而是'价值无法估量'= priceless；考研高频形容词
🧠 助记
in（不可）+ valuable（定价），不可定价的——无价的；极其宝贵的
invaluable::/ɪnˈvæljuəbl/:adj. 无价的:The mentor's guidance was invaluable to her career development.
21. jargon /ˈdʒɑːrɡən/ n. 行话；术语；难懂的话
📖 英文解释
special words or expressions used by a particular profession or group that are difficult for others to understand
✏️ 例句
The legal documents were full of jargon that confused ordinary people.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研语言/传播类：technical jargon（技术术语）；avoid jargon（避免行话）；medical/legal jargon
🧠 助记
jargon 发音像'扎根'，行话扎根在某个圈子里——行话；术语
jargon::/ˈdʒɑːrɡən/:n. 行话:Scientists should avoid jargon when communicating with the public.
22. jungle /ˈdʒʌŋɡəl/ n. 丛林；密林；险恶之地
📖 英文解释
a dense tropical forest with tangled vegetation; a situation that is chaotic, competitive, or dangerous
✏️ 例句
Explorers hacked through the jungle for days.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研比喻义：the concrete jungle（水泥丛林，指城市）；the law of the jungle（弱肉强食）；jungle gym（攀爬架）
🧠 助记
jungle 发音像'装格'，大丛林装满了各种格格不入的生物——丛林
jungle::/ˈdʒʌŋɡəl/:n. 丛林:The business world can be a real jungle for newcomers.
23. layman /ˈleɪmən/ n. 外行；门外汉；俗人
📖 英文解释
a person who is not an expert in a particular subject; a person who is not a member of the clergy
✏️ 例句
The book explains complex theories in terms that a layman can understand.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：in layman's terms（用通俗语言）；对比 expert/specialist；lay（外行的）形容词形式
🧠 助记
lay（放下，不专业）+ man，放下专业不懂装懂的人——外行；门外汉
layman::/ˈleɪmən/:n. 外行:Let me explain this concept in layman's terms.
24. locality /loʊˈkæləti/ n. 地区；地点；位置
📖 英文解释
an area or neighborhood; the position or site of something; the quality of having a particular location
✏️ 例句
The survey covered residents from various localities across the country.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研地理/社会类：in the locality（在当地）；local + -ity；对比 location（具体位置）vs. locality（地区）
🧠 助记
local（当地）+ -ity，当地的范围和地点——地区；地点
locality::/loʊˈkæləti/:n. 地区:The factory brought jobs to the locality.
25. noon /nuːn/ n. 正午；中午
📖 英文解释
twelve o'clock in the middle of the day; midday
✏️ 例句
We agreed to meet at noon in front of the library.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研时间表达：at noon（在正午）；high noon（正午；危急关头）；from morning to noon（从早到中午）
🧠 助记
noon 中间是 oo，像时钟的两个圈圈，指向12点——正午
noon::/nuːn/:n. 正午:The deadline is noon on Friday.
