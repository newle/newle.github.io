1. odor /ˈoʊdər/ n. 气味；臭味；名声（美式）
📖 英文解释
a distinctive smell, especially an unpleasant one; (figurative) a quality or reputation (usually negative)
✏️ 例句
There was a strange odor coming from the basement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：body odor（体味）；英式拼写 odour；odor of corruption（腐败的气息）；对比 fragrance（香味）
🧠 助记
odor 发音像'哦多了'，闻到奇怪气味——气味；臭味
odor::/ˈoʊdər/:n. 气味:The odor of fresh bread filled the whole street.
2. okay /oʊˈkeɪ/ adj./adv./int. 好的；可以；没问题
📖 英文解释
satisfactory; in good health; used to express agreement or acceptance
✏️ 例句
Is everything okay with the project schedule?
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研写作慎用（过于口语化）；缩写 OK；okay with（对…表示认可）；作动词：to okay a plan（批准计划）
🧠 助记
okay = O.K.，来源众多说法，记住'这样可以吗'——好的；可以
okay::/oʊˈkeɪ/:adj./adv./int. 好的:The supervisor gave the okay to proceed with the experiment.
3. optical /ˈɒptɪkəl/ adj. 视觉的；光学的；眼睛的
📖 英文解释
relating to light, vision, or the eye; relating to optics
✏️ 例句
The scientist studied optical illusions to understand how the brain works.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研科技类：optical fiber（光纤）；optical illusion（视觉错觉）；optical lens（光学镜头）
🧠 助记
opt（眼睛，同 optometry）+ -ical，与眼睛相关的——视觉的；光学的
optical::/ˈɒptɪkəl/:adj. 视觉的:Optical fibers are used to transmit data at high speeds.
4. optimum /ˈɒptɪməm/ adj./n. 最佳的；最适宜的；最佳条件
📖 英文解释
the best or most favorable condition or degree; most favorable or effective
✏️ 例句
The experiment was conducted under optimum conditions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研科学/管理类：optimum performance（最佳表现）；optimal（同义形容词）；optimize（优化）
🧠 助记
optim（最好，同 optimal）+ -um，最好的状态——最佳的
optimum::/ˈɒptɪməm/:adj./n. 最佳的:The factory operates at optimum efficiency during the day shift.
5. pact /pækt/ n. 协定；条约；合同
📖 英文解释
a formal agreement between two or more parties, especially between countries
✏️ 例句
The two nations signed a non-aggression pact.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研政治/外交类：make/sign a pact（缔结/签署协议）；peace pact（和平协定）；suicide pact（集体自杀协议）
🧠 助记
pact 发音像'拍克特'，两方拍手达成协定——协定；条约
pact::/pækt/:n. 协定:The trade pact reduced tariffs between the two countries.
6. persevere /ˌpɜːrsɪˈvɪər/ v. 坚持不懈；锲而不舍
📖 英文解释
to continue doing something despite difficulty or delay in achieving success
✏️ 例句
If you persevere with your studies, success will come eventually.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研励志/议论文高频：persevere in/with（坚持做）；名词 perseverance（坚韧；毅力）
🧠 助记
per（始终）+ severe（严格），始终严格要求自己坚持——坚持不懈
persevere::/ˌpɜːrsɪˈvɪər/:v. 坚持不懈:She persevered through countless setbacks to achieve her dream.
7. pharmacy /ˈfɑːrməsi/ n. 药房；药学；制药学
📖 英文解释
a shop where medicines and drugs are prepared and sold; the science of preparing and dispensing drugs
✏️ 例句
He picked up his prescription from the pharmacy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研医学/生活类：pharmacist（药剂师）；pharmaceutical（制药的）；pharmacy major（药学专业）
🧠 助记
pharma（药，同 pharmaceutical）+ -cy，卖药的地方——药房
pharmacy::/ˈfɑːrməsi/:n. 药房:The pharmacist advised her on how to take the medication.
8. powder /ˈpaʊdər/ n. 粉末；火药；粉状物 v. 把…磨成粉
📖 英文解释
a dry substance made of very small particles; a cosmetic powder for the face; to apply powder to; to reduce to powder
✏️ 例句
She added a spoonful of baking powder to the cake mixture.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：keep one's powder dry（保持战备；做好准备）；gun powder（火药）；powder room（化妆间）
🧠 助记
powder 发音像'剥的',把东西剥开磨成粉——粉末
powder::/ˈpaʊdər/:n. 粉末:The milk was converted into powder for easy storage.
9. predominant /prɪˈdɒmɪnənt/ adj. 主要的；主导的；占优势的
📖 英文解释
present as the strongest or main element; having authority or influence over others
✏️ 例句
Agriculture was the predominant industry in that region for centuries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频形容词：predominant factor/feature（主要因素/特征）；predominantly（副词：主要地）
🧠 助记
pre（最前）+ dominant（主导的），最前面最主导的——主要的；主导的
predominant::/prɪˈdɒmɪnənt/:adj. 主要的:Economic growth was the predominant concern for the government.
10. presume /prɪˈzjuːm/ v. 假定；推测；擅自；冒昧
📖 英文解释
to suppose that something is true without definite proof; to take for granted; to act in an inappropriately bold manner
✏️ 例句
I presume you have read the instructions before beginning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研逻辑/阅读：presume sb. innocent（推定某人无罪）；presumed dead（推测已死亡）；名词 presumption
🧠 助记
pre（预先）+ sume（取，同 assume），预先取定——假定；推测
presume::/prɪˈzjuːm/:v. 假定:The court presumes innocence until guilt is proven.
11. protocol /ˈproʊtəkɒl/ n. 礼仪规范；议定书；协议
📖 英文解释
a system of rules governing formal occasions; an official agreement between countries; a set of rules for data transmission in computing
✏️ 例句
The diplomat followed the strict protocol during the state visit.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研外交/科技类：diplomatic protocol（外交礼仪）；Internet protocol（互联网协议）；breach protocol（违反礼节）
🧠 助记
proto（第一，首）+ col（胶水），把第一条规则粘在最前面——礼仪规范；议定书
protocol::/ˈproʊtəkɒl/:n. 礼仪规范:The research team followed a strict safety protocol.

purple::/ˈpɜːrpəl/:n./adj. 紫色（的）:In ancient times, purple dye was more valuable than gold.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

14. reclaim /rɪˈkleɪm/ v. 收回；开垦；改造
📖 英文解释
to get something back that was lost or taken away; to make land suitable for farming or building; to reform a person

The government plans to reclaim land from the sea to build a new airport.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研环境/法律类：reclaim land（开垦土地）；reclaim one's luggage（取回行李）；reclaimed water（中水回用）
🧠 助记

reclaim::/rɪˈkleɪm/:v. 收回:The city reclaimed the abandoned factory site for a public park.

repression::/rɪˈpreʃən/:n. 镇压:Years of repression had silenced the voices of dissent.
