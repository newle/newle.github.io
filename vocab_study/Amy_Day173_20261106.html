1. trademark /ˈtreɪdmɑːrk/ n. 商标；标志 v. 注册商标
📖 英文解释
a symbol, word, or phrase legally registered by a manufacturer to distinguish its products; a distinctive characteristic
✏️ 例句
The company's logo is a registered trademark.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研商业/法律类：registered trademark（注册商标）；trademark style（标志性风格）；trademark infringement（商标侵权）
🧠 助记
trade（贸易）+ mark（标记），贸易中的标记——商标
trademark::/ˈtreɪdmɑːrk/:n. 商标:His sharp wit became the trademark of his writing style.
2. trim /trɪm/ v. 修剪；削减 adj. 整洁的；苗条的 n. 修剪
📖 英文解释
to make something neat by cutting away irregular parts; to reduce the amount, size, or number of something; neat and orderly
✏️ 例句
He asked the barber to trim his hair but not cut it short.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：trim the budget（削减预算）；trim down（减重）；in trim（身材苗条）；keep trim（保持整洁）
🧠 助记
trim 发音像'剃掉'，剃掉多余的部分——修剪；削减
trim::/trɪm/:v. 修剪:The company trimmed its workforce by fifteen percent.
3. typist /ˈtaɪpɪst/ n. 打字员
📖 英文解释
a person who types, especially one employed to type documents or correspondence
✏️ 例句
The typist quickly converted the handwritten notes into a digital document.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研职业类：professional typist（职业打字员）；speed typist（速打员）；touch typist（盲打员）
🧠 助记
type（打字）+ -ist（人），以打字为业的人——打字员
typist::/ˈtaɪpɪst/:n. 打字员:The office hired a skilled typist to handle correspondence.
4. venue /ˈvenjuː/ n. 场地；地点；举办地
📖 英文解释
the place where an event or meeting happens; a location where something takes place
✏️ 例句
The concert venue can hold up to ten thousand people.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研活动/法律类：a venue for（举办…的地点）；change of venue（换审判地点）；book a venue（预定场地）
🧠 助记
venue 发音像'闻呢'，这是什么地方，闻一闻——场地；地点
venue::/ˈvenjuː/:n. 场地:The Olympic committee selected the venue for the opening ceremony.
5. versatile /ˈvɜːrsətaɪl/ adj. 多才多艺的；多用途的；通用的
📖 英文解释
able to adapt or be adapted to many different functions or activities; having many skills
✏️ 例句
She is a versatile performer who can sing, dance, and act.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频形容词：a versatile tool/material（多用途工具/材料）；versatile athlete（全能运动员）
🧠 助记
vers（转，同 version）+ -atile，能够转换到各种用途——多才多艺的；多用途的
versatile::/ˈvɜːrsətaɪl/:adj. 多才多艺的:Python is a versatile programming language used in many fields.
6. yearly /ˈjɪərli/ adj./adv. 每年的；一年一次的
📖 英文解释
happening or produced once a year or every year; occurring or calculated annually
✏️ 例句
The company releases a yearly report summarizing its financial performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：yearly income/salary（年收入）；yearly review（年度审查）；同义词 annual；twice-yearly（每年两次）
🧠 助记
year（年）+ -ly（形/副词后缀），每年的——每年的；一年一次
yearly::/ˈjɪərli/:adj./adv. 每年的:The team holds a yearly conference to review its progress.
7. necklace /ˈnekləs/ n. 项链；颈饰
📖 英文解释
a piece of jewelry consisting of a chain or string of beads, gems, or other items worn around the neck
✏️ 例句
She received a pearl necklace as a birthday gift.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化类：pearl necklace（珍珠项链）；gold necklace（金项链）；diamond necklace（钻石项链）
🧠 助记
neck（脖子）+ lace（花边/串），戴在脖子上的串——项链
necklace::/ˈnekləs/:n. 项链:She wore a silver necklace that complemented her outfit.
8. thrift /θrɪft/ n. 节俭；节约；储蓄
📖 英文解释
the quality of using money and other resources carefully and wisely; a pink-flowered plant common on cliffs
✏️ 例句
His thrift and hard work allowed him to save enough money for a house.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研经济/生活类：practice thrift（勤俭节约）；thrift store（旧货商店）；形容词 thrifty（节俭的）
🧠 助记
thrift 发音像'掐死付',掐着钱包少花钱——节俭；节约
thrift::/θrɪft/:n. 节俭:Thrift and diligence are values that have shaped his success.
9. weary /ˈwɪəri/ adj. 疲倦的；厌倦的 v. 使疲倦
📖 英文解释
feeling or showing extreme tiredness, especially from exertion; reluctant to experience any more of something
✏️ 例句
The travelers were weary after the long journey.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：grow weary of（对…感到厌倦）；war-weary（厌战的）；weary sigh（疲惫的叹息）
🧠 助记
weary 发音像'悲儿',走了好远的路累得悲从中来——疲倦的；厌倦的
weary::/ˈwɪəri/:adj. 疲倦的:She grew weary of the endless bureaucratic procedures.
10. aeroplane /ˈeərəpleɪn/ n. 飞机（英式）
📖 英文解释
a powered flying vehicle with fixed wings (British English; American English: airplane)
✏️ 例句
She boarded the aeroplane for her first international flight.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
英式拼写（美式 airplane）；考研词汇辨析；aeronautics（航空学）；aerospace（航空航天）
🧠 助记

aeroplane::/ˈeərəpleɪn/:n. 飞机（英式）:The aeroplane landed safely despite severe turbulence.

📖 英文解释
a grey powder made by burning limestone and clay that becomes hard when mixed with water; to join or strengthen firmly
✏️ 例句
The workers mixed cement and sand to create concrete.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
cement 发音像'似门特'，像门一样坚固的水泥——水泥；巩固
cement::/sɪˈment/:n. 水泥:The agreement cemented the alliance between the two nations.

inspect::/ɪnˈspekt/:v. 检查:The auditors were sent to inspect the company's financial records.

roundabout::/ˈraʊndəbaʊt/:n. 环形交叉路口:He gave a roundabout answer instead of addressing the question directly.
14. abide /əˈbaɪd/ v. 遵守；忍受；持续
