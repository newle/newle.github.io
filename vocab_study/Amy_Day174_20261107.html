1. maid /meɪd/ n. 女仆；少女
📖 英文解释
A female domestic servant; also used to refer to a young unmarried woman.
✏️ 例句
The maid cleaned the room every morning before the guests arrived.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：maid of honor（伴娘/荣誉女侍），old maid（老处女）；考研中多作女仆义出现。
🧠 助记
maid = made（过去式谐音）→ 她是被制造来服务的人，即女仆。
maid::/meɪd/:n. 女仆:The maid swept the floor.
2. memorial /məˈmɔːriəl/ n. 纪念碑；纪念物 adj. 纪念的
📖 英文解释
Something that keeps the memory of a person or event alive, such as a monument; or relating to memory or commemoration.
✏️ 例句
A memorial was built in the city center to honor the soldiers who died in the war.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：memorial service（追悼会），war memorial（战争纪念碑）；作形容词时修饰 service/day 等。
🧠 助记
memo（备忘录）+ rial → 用纪念碑备忘历史，memorial = 纪念的。
memorial::/məˈmɔːriəl/:n. 纪念碑:They held a memorial service for the victims.
3. mercury /ˈmɜːrkjuri/ n. 汞，水银；水星
📖 英文解释
A heavy silvery liquid metal (chemical element Hg) used in thermometers; also the planet closest to the Sun.
✏️ 例句
Old thermometers contained mercury, which is now known to be toxic.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研中可考科学文章：mercury poisoning（汞中毒），liquid mercury（液态汞）；大写 Mercury 指水星。
🧠 助记
Mercury 是罗马信使神，行动迅速如水银流动，故水银和水星均以其命名。
mercury::/ˈmɜːrkjuri/:n. 汞，水银:Mercury is used in some industrial processes.
4. misunderstand /ˌmɪsʌndəˈstænd/ v. 误解，误会
📖 英文解释
To fail to understand or interpret something correctly.
✏️ 例句
She misunderstood his silence as anger, but he was simply tired.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用名词形式 misunderstanding；考研阅读常见：be easily misunderstood（容易被误解）。
🧠 助记
mis-（错误）+ understand → 错误地理解 = 误解。
misunderstand::/ˌmɪsʌndəˈstænd/:v. 误解，误会:I think you have misunderstood my intention.
5. nucleus /ˈnjuːkliəs/ n. 核；细胞核；原子核
📖 英文解释
The central part of an atom containing protons and neutrons; the central part of a cell containing DNA; figuratively, the core of something.
✏️ 例句
The nucleus of the atom was bombarded with neutrons in the experiment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：atomic nucleus（原子核），cell nucleus（细胞核），the nucleus of（…的核心）；复数 nuclei。
🧠 助记
nucle- 来自拉丁语 nux（坚果核）→ 坚果的核心 = nucleus。
nucleus::/ˈnjuːkliəs/:n. 核:DNA is stored in the nucleus of the cell.
6. pinch /pɪntʃ/ v./n. 捏，掐；少量
📖 英文解释
To squeeze a small area of skin tightly between finger and thumb; a small amount of something (especially a seasoning).
✏️ 例句
She pinched a bit of salt and sprinkled it into the soup.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
固定搭配：a pinch of salt（一撮盐），pinch pennies（节省每分钱），at a pinch（必要时）。
🧠 助记
pinch 听起来像品吃 → 捏一小撮品尝，即 pinch（捏/少量）。
pinch::/pɪntʃ/:v./n. 捏，掐:Add a pinch of salt to the dish.
7. plea /pliː/ n. 请求；辩护；借口
📖 英文解释
An urgent emotional request; in law, a statement made by or on behalf of a defendant in a court of law.
✏️ 例句
The prisoner made a plea for mercy before the judge delivered the sentence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
法律词汇：plea bargain（认罪协议），enter a plea（提出辩护）；也可作 plead（v.）。
🧠 助记
plea ≈ please 的缩写形式 → 发出 please 的那种恳求 = plea（请求）。
plea::/pliː/:n. 请求:She made a plea for help.
8. puppet /ˈpʌpɪt/ n. 木偶；傀儡
📖 英文解释
A figure controlled by strings or a hand, used in entertainment; figuratively, a person controlled by another.
✏️ 例句
The small nation was seen as a puppet state, controlled by its more powerful neighbor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
政治语境：puppet government/state/regime（傀儡政府/国家/政权）；考研常出现在政治类文章。
🧠 助记
puppet ≈ pup（小狗）+ pet → 被人牵着玩的小玩意儿 = 木偶/傀儡。
puppet::/ˈpʌpɪt/:n. 木偶:He was merely a puppet of the ruling elite.
9. republic /rɪˈpʌblɪk/ n. 共和国；共和政体
📖 英文解释
A state in which supreme power is held by the people and their elected representatives, with an elected or nominated president.
✏️ 例句
France is a republic where the president is elected by popular vote.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：the People's Republic（人民共和国），republican（共和主义者/共和党的）；考研政治/历史文章常见。
🧠 助记
re-（再次）+ public（公众）→ 权力再次归于公众 = 共和国。
republic::/rɪˈpʌblɪk/:n. 共和国:The republic was founded after the revolution.
10. reservoir /ˈrezəvwɑːr/ n. 水库；蓄水池；储备
📖 英文解释
A large natural or artificial lake used as a source of water supply; figuratively, a large supply of something.
✏️ 例句
The city's reservoir was nearly empty after months of drought.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：a reservoir of knowledge/talent（知识/人才储备）；注意拼写（reservoir 含法语借词特征）。
🧠 助记
reserve（储备）+ voir（法语看）→ 储备水的地方 = 水库。
reservoir::/ˈrezəvwɑːr/:n. 水库:The reservoir supplies water to the entire city.
11. screw /skruː/ n. 螺丝钉 v. 拧，旋转
📖 英文解释
A thin, pointed metal fastener with a raised spiral thread; to fasten by turning a screw; to turn or tighten.
✏️ 例句
He used a screwdriver to screw the shelf to the wall.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：screw up（搞砸/拧紧），have a screw loose（有点疯）；工程类文章中常见。
🧠 助记
screw 的螺旋形状像字母 S → 螺旋状的 screw（螺丝钉）。
screw::/skruː/:n. 螺丝钉 v. 拧，旋转:Screw the lid tightly before shaking the bottle.
12. stern /stɜːrn/ adj. 严厉的，坚定的 n. 船尾
📖 英文解释
Serious and unrelenting, especially in the assertion of authority; the rear end of a ship.
✏️ 例句
The teacher gave the students a stern warning about plagiarism.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见：stern look/warning/face（严厉的眼神/警告/面孔）；作名词指船尾（bow 船头对比）。
🧠 助记
stern 和 stone（石头）有相似硬感 → 像石头一样硬的态度 = 严厉的。
stern::/stɜːrn/:adj. 严厉的，坚定的 n. 船尾:The judge delivered a stern rebuke.
13. stiff /stɪf/ adj. 僵硬的；严厉的；难以弯曲的
📖 英文解释
Not easily bent or changed; moving awkwardly; (of a punishment or opposition) severe.
✏️ 例句
After sitting for hours, her neck became stiff and painful.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：stiff competition（激烈竞争），stiff penalty（严厉处罚），stiff breeze（劲风）。
🧠 助记
stiff 联想 staff（棍子）→ 棍子一样硬 = 僵硬的。
stiff::/stɪf/:adj. 僵硬的:The company faces stiff competition in the market.
14. swamp /swɒmp/ n. 沼泽 v. 淹没，压倒
📖 英文解释
An area of low-lying ground, frequently flooded and typically heavily vegetated; to overwhelm with too much of something.
✏️ 例句
The office was swamped with complaints after the policy change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
比喻用法：be swamped with work/requests（被工作/请求淹没）；考研写作可用。
🧠 助记
swamp 含 damp（潮湿）的感觉 → 又湿又软的沼泽地。
swamp::/swɒmp/:n. 沼泽 v. 淹没，压倒:She was swamped with emails after the announcement.
15. tease /tiːz/ v. 取笑，戏弄；梳理（毛发）
📖 英文解释
To make fun of someone in a playful or unkind way; to provoke or annoy repeatedly.
✏️ 例句
The older children teased him about his new haircut.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：tease sb. about sth.（就某事取笑某人）；区分 tease（取笑）vs. mock（嘲弄）。
🧠 助记
tease 发音像中文逗 → 逗人就是 tease。
tease::/tiːz/:v. 取笑，戏弄:Stop teasing your little sister.
16. tray /treɪ/ n. 托盘；浅盘
📖 英文解释
A flat, shallow container with a raised edge, used for carrying or displaying objects.
✏️ 例句
The waiter carried the drinks on a large silver tray.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研中多在生活/饮食描述语境出现；baking tray（烤盘），serving tray（餐盘）。
🧠 助记
tray 像 trey（三）→ 三层的托架，每层都是一个 tray（托盘）。
tray::/treɪ/:n. 托盘:She placed the cups on the tray carefully.
17. tug /tʌɡ/ v./n. 用力拉，拖；拖船
📖 英文解释
To pull something hard, often repeatedly; a hard, sudden pull; a small boat used to tow larger vessels.
✏️ 例句
The child tugged at his mother's sleeve to get her attention.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：tug of war（拔河；权力/利益争夺），give a tug（用力一拉）。
🧠 助记
tug 和 drag（拖拽）发音相近，都是用力拉的动作。
tug::/tʌɡ/:v./n. 用力拉，拖:He tugged at the rope with all his strength.
18. ventilate /ˈventɪleɪt/ v. 通风；公开讨论
📖 英文解释
To cause fresh air to enter and move through a room or building; to discuss or express an opinion freely.
✏️ 例句
It is important to ventilate the room regularly to prevent the spread of disease.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：ventilate a room（给房间通风），well-ventilated（通风良好的）；名词 ventilation（通风）。
🧠 助记
vent（通风口）+ ilate → 通过 vent 让空气流通 = 通风。
ventilate::/ˈventɪleɪt/:v. 通风:Open the windows to ventilate the office.
19. vocation /voʊˈkeɪʃən/ n. 职业；使命感；天职
📖 英文解释
A strong feeling of suitability for a particular career or occupation; a person's employment or main occupation.
✏️ 例句
She considered teaching not just a job but a true vocation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
辨析：vocation（有使命感的职业/天职）vs. occupation（一般职业）vs. profession（专业职业）。
🧠 助记
vocal（声音）→ 被召唤的声音 → 天职/使命感（voc- 来自拉丁 vocare 召唤）。
vocation::/voʊˈkeɪʃən/:n. 职业:Medicine was his true vocation.
20. zeal /ziːl/ n. 热情，热忱
📖 英文解释
Great energy or enthusiasm in pursuit of a cause or an objective.
✏️ 例句
The young volunteers worked with great zeal to complete the community project.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：zeal for（对…的热情），with zeal（满怀热情地）；形容词 zealous，名词 zealot（狂热者）。
🧠 助记
zeal 和 zealous → 以 Z 开头像燃烧的火焰形状，象征熊熊热情。
zeal::/ziːl/:n. 热情，热忱:She pursued her goals with remarkable zeal.
21. analytic /ˌænəˈlɪtɪk/ adj. 分析的，解析的
📖 英文解释
Relating to or using analysis or logical reasoning; skilled in analysis.
✏️ 例句
The job requires strong analytic skills to interpret complex data sets.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用：analytic thinking/skill/method（分析性思维/技能/方法）；也拼作 analytical（更常见）。
🧠 助记
analyze（分析）的形容词形式 → analytic = 分析的。
analytic::/ˌænəˈlɪtɪk/:adj. 分析的，解析的:She has a sharp analytic mind.
22. apt /æpt/ adj. 恰当的；有…倾向的；聪明的
📖 英文解释
Appropriate or suitable in the circumstances; having a tendency to do something; quick to learn.
✏️ 例句
Her remark was particularly apt given the circumstances of the meeting.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：apt to do（倾向于做），an apt description/remark（恰当的描述/评论）；考研作文好用词。
🧠 助记
apt ≈ adapt（适应）的词根 apt → 适应情境的 = 恰当的。
apt::/æpt/:adj. 恰当的:That is a very apt description of the situation.
23. benign /bɪˈnaɪn/ adj. 良性的；温和的；无害的
📖 英文解释
Gentle and kindly; (of a disease) not harmful in effect; (of a tumor) not malignant.
✏️ 例句
The doctor confirmed that the tumor was benign and did not require surgery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
医学搭配：benign tumor（良性肿瘤）vs. malignant（恶性的）；也可形容气候、政策等温和。
🧠 助记
bene-（好）+ -ign → 好的本性 = 温和的/良性的（benevolent 同根）。
benign::/bɪˈnaɪn/:adj. 良性的:The biopsy showed the growth was benign.
24. canoe /kəˈnuː/ n. 独木舟 v. 乘独木舟
📖 英文解释
A narrow lightweight boat pointed at both ends, propelled with a paddle; to travel in a canoe.
✏️ 例句
They spent the afternoon canoeing down the river through the national park.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见于旅游/体育类文章；paddle a canoe（划独木舟），canoe trip（独木舟之旅）。
🧠 助记
canoe 发音像卡努 → 卡在水里要努力划的小船 = 独木舟。
canoe::/kəˈnuː/:n. 独木舟 v. 乘独木舟:We paddled the canoe across the calm lake.
25. cassette /kəˈset/ n. 盒式磁带；暗盒
📖 英文解释
A sealed plastic unit containing a length of magnetic tape or film; formerly widely used for audio and video recording.
✏️ 例句
Before streaming services, people listened to music on cassette tapes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研中可出现在科技变迁类文章；cassette player/tape（卡式播放器/磁带）。
🧠 助记
cassette 像 case（盒子）的小型版 → 小盒子装磁带 = 盒式磁带。
cassette::/kəˈset/:n. 盒式磁带:She found an old cassette tape in the drawer.
