1. symphony /ˈsɪmfəni/ n. 交响乐；和谐
📖 英文解释
An elaborate musical composition for full orchestra, typically in four movements; a harmonious combination of elements.
✏️ 例句
Beethoven's Fifth Symphony is one of the most recognized pieces in classical music.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：symphony orchestra（交响乐团），perform a symphony（演奏交响乐）；比喻 a symphony of colors（色彩的和谐）。
🧠 助记
sym-（共同）+ phony（声音 phone 同根）→ 很多人共同发出的声音 = 交响乐。
symphony::/ˈsɪmfəni/:n. 交响乐:The symphony was performed to a full house.
2. tuck /tʌk/ v. 塞，折叠；掖 n. 褶；零食
📖 英文解释
To push, fold, or turn the ends or edges of something so as to hide them or hold them in place; to put someone in bed and cover them.
✏️ 例句
She tucked the blanket around the sleeping child.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：tuck in（掖好/大吃一顿），tuck away（收起来），tuck sb. into bed（给某人盖好被子）。
🧠 助记
tuck ≈ duck（鸭子把头tucking进水里）→ 把某物塞进去 = 折叠/掖进。
tuck::/tʌk/:v. 塞，折叠:He tucked his shirt into his trousers.
3. whatsoever /ˌwɒtsəʊˈevər/ pron./adj. 任何；无论什么（强调否定）
📖 英文解释
Used for emphasis instead of whatever in questions and negative statements; of any kind at all.
✏️ 例句
There is no evidence whatsoever to support that claim.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
强调句型：no…whatsoever（一点也没有），nothing whatsoever（什么都没有）；增强否定语气。
🧠 助记
what + so + ever → whatever 的加强版，强调无论什么都不行。
whatsoever::/ˌwɒtsəʊˈevər/:pron./adj. 任何:She showed no remorse whatsoever for her actions.
4. whip /wɪp/ n. 鞭子 v. 鞭打；迅速移动
📖 英文解释
A strip of leather or a flexible rod used to urge animals or as punishment; to beat or move with speed and force.
✏️ 例句
The chef whipped the cream into stiff peaks for the dessert.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：whip up（迅速制作/煽动情绪），whipped cream（打发奶油），whip out（迅速掏出）。
🧠 助记
whip 的发音像鞭打声 → 象声词来源，鞭打声 = whip。
whip::/wɪp/:n. 鞭子 v. 鞭打:She whipped up a quick meal before the guests arrived.
5. wrist /rɪst/ n. 手腕
📖 英文解释
The joint connecting the hand with the forearm.
✏️ 例句
He sprained his wrist playing tennis and had to rest for a week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：wrist watch（手表），wrist injury（手腕受伤），slap on the wrist（轻微惩罚）。
🧠 助记
wrist 含 rist → 联想 twist（扭转）→ 扭转的地方就是手腕。
wrist::/rɪst/:n. 手腕:The bracelet fits perfectly around her wrist.
6. applause /əˈplɔːz/ n. 掌声，喝彩
📖 英文解释
Approval or praise expressed by clapping; the act of clapping hands to show appreciation.
✏️ 例句
The audience burst into applause when the singer finished her performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：a round of applause（一阵掌声），thunderous applause（雷鸣般的掌声），win applause（赢得掌声）。
🧠 助记
ap-（加强）+ plause（拍手）→ 用力拍手 = 掌声（plaudit 同根）。
applause::/əˈplɔːz/:n. 掌声，喝彩:The speech was met with enthusiastic applause.
7. ash /æʃ/ n. 灰烬；白蜡树
📖 英文解释
The powdery residue left after burning; a type of tree with gray bark and compound leaves.
✏️ 例句
All that remained of the building after the fire was a pile of ash.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：volcanic ash（火山灰），cigarette ash（烟灰），ash tree（白蜡树）；复数 ashes 指遗骸/骨灰。
🧠 助记
ash → 想象火烧后剩下的灰白色粉末，颜色如灰 = 灰烬。
ash::/æʃ/:n. 灰烬:The campfire reduced the logs to ash by morning.
8. bail /beɪl/ n. 保释金 v. 保释；舀水
📖 英文解释
Temporary release of an accused person awaiting trial, sometimes with a security deposit; to release on bail; to scoop water from a boat.
✏️ 例句
The suspect was released on bail pending his trial next month.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
法律词汇：grant/post bail（准许/缴纳保释金），bail out（保释/援助/跳伞），jump bail（弃保潜逃）。
🧠 助记
bail 联想 jail（监狱）→ 从 jail 里出来需要 bail（保释金）。
bail::/beɪl/:n. 保释金 v. 保释:He was granted bail and released from custody.
9. bench /bentʃ/ n. 长椅；法官席；工作台
📖 英文解释
A long seat of wood or stone; in law, the seat occupied by a judge; a long table for working on.
✏️ 例句
The judge took her seat on the bench and called the court to order.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
多义词：park bench（公园长椅），the bench（法官席/全体法官），workbench（工作台），bench player（替补）。
🧠 助记
bench ≈ 板凳 → 长木板做成的凳子/长椅 = bench。
bench::/bentʃ/:n. 长椅:The ruling was made from the bench by the senior judge.
10. bolster /ˈboʊlstər/ v. 支撑，增强 n. 长枕
📖 英文解释
To support or strengthen; a long, firm cylindrical pillow.
✏️ 例句
The government introduced new policies to bolster the struggling economy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：bolster confidence/support/economy（增强信心/支持/经济）；考研写作高频词。
🧠 助记
bolster 含 boost（提升）的意味 → 给某事物一个 boost = 支撑/增强。
bolster::/ˈboʊlstər/:v. 支撑，增强 n. 长枕:The new investment will bolster the company's growth.
11. bounce /baʊns/ v. 弹跳；反弹 n. 弹力；活力
📖 英文解释
To spring back from a surface after hitting it; to move up and down repeatedly; resilience and energy.
✏️ 例句
The ball bounced off the wall and landed in the corner.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：bounce back（恢复/反弹），bounce rate（跳出率），full of bounce（充满活力）。
🧠 助记
bounce 发音像碰 → 东西碰到地面后弹起 = 弹跳。
bounce::/baʊns/:v. 弹跳:The economy is expected to bounce back next year.
12. complicate /ˈkɒmplɪkeɪt/ v. 使复杂化
📖 英文解释
To make something more difficult or complex; to introduce difficulties or confusion into a situation.
✏️ 例句
The unexpected snowstorm further complicated the rescue operation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：complicate matters（使情况复杂化），complicated relationship/issue（复杂的关系/问题）。
🧠 助记
com-（共同）+ plic（折叠，implicate 同根）→ 折叠在一起变复杂 = 使复杂化。
complicate::/ˈkɒmplɪkeɪt/:v. 使复杂化:His refusal to cooperate only complicated things further.
13. conscientious /ˌkɒnʃiˈenʃəs/ adj. 认真负责的，尽职的
📖 英文解释
Wishing to do one's work or duty well and thoroughly; diligent and careful.
✏️ 例句
She is a conscientious student who always submits assignments on time.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：conscientious worker/student（勤勉的工人/学生），conscientious objector（出于良知的拒服兵役者）。
🧠 助记
conscience（良心）+ ious → 有良心地工作 = 认真负责的。
conscientious::/ˌkɒnʃiˈenʃəs/:adj. 认真负责的，尽职的:He is known as a conscientious and reliable employee.
14. coronavirus /kəˈroʊnəvaɪrəs/ n. 冠状病毒
📖 英文解释
Any of a group of RNA viruses that cause a variety of diseases in humans and animals, including COVID-19.
✏️ 例句
The coronavirus pandemic led to unprecedented global health and economic challenges.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研时事词汇：coronavirus outbreak（冠状病毒爆发），COVID-19（新冠病毒）；corona 意为冠。
🧠 助记
corona（皇冠）+ virus → 在显微镜下形似皇冠突起的病毒 = 冠状病毒。
coronavirus::/kəˈroʊnəvaɪrəs/:n. 冠状病毒:Scientists developed vaccines against the coronavirus rapidly.
15. crane /kreɪn/ n. 起重机；鹤 v. 伸长（脖子）
📖 英文解释
A large machine for moving heavy objects; a large wading bird with long legs and neck; to stretch one's neck to see something.
✏️ 例句
Workers used a crane to lift the steel beams to the top of the building.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
多义词：construction crane（建筑起重机），crane one's neck（伸脖子张望），paper crane（纸鹤）。
🧠 助记
crane（鹤）→ 鹤脖子很长，起重机也像鹤颈伸长 → 二者共用 crane。
crane::/kreɪn/:n. 起重机:She craned her neck to see over the crowd.
16. crystal /ˈkrɪstəl/ n. 水晶；晶体 adj. 清澈的
📖 英文解释
A piece of a homogeneous solid substance having a natural geometrically regular form; highly transparent glass; very clear.
✏️ 例句
The mountain lake was so clear it looked like crystal.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：crystal clear（清晰透明），crystal ball（水晶球），crystal structure（晶体结构）。
🧠 助记
crystal → 想象阳光照射水晶后折射出的清澈透明感。
crystal::/ˈkrɪstəl/:n. 水晶:The instructions should be crystal clear to all staff.
17. culminate /ˈkʌlmɪneɪt/ v. 达到顶点；以…告终
📖 英文解释
To reach a climax or point of highest development; to end in or result in something as the final outcome.
✏️ 例句
Years of research culminated in a breakthrough discovery about cancer treatment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：culminate in/with（以…结束/告终），culminating event（最高潮事件）；正式书面语。
🧠 助记
culmin-（顶部，column 柱顶同根）+ ate → 到达顶部 = 达到顶点/告终。
culminate::/ˈkʌlmɪneɪt/:v. 达到顶点:The long negotiations culminated in a peace agreement.
18. cupboard /ˈkʌbərd/ n. 橱柜，食品柜
📖 英文解释
A cabinet with shelves or drawers, used for storing food, crockery, or clothes.
✏️ 例句
She kept all her spices neatly arranged in the kitchen cupboard.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：airing cupboard（烘干柜），cupboard love（有目的的爱），bare cupboard（空柜）。
🧠 助记
cup（杯子）+ board（板子）→ 放杯子的板架 = 橱柜（历史上指放杯盘的架子）。
cupboard::/ˈkʌbərd/:n. 橱柜，食品柜:The food is stored in the cupboard under the stairs.
19. dismay /dɪsˈmeɪ/ n./v. 沮丧；惊愕
📖 英文解释
Concern and distress caused by something unexpected; to cause someone to feel distressed and surprised.
✏️ 例句
To her dismay, she discovered she had missed the deadline by one day.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：to sb.'s dismay（令某人沮丧的是），filled with dismay（充满沮丧），dismay at（对…感到沮丧）。
🧠 助记
dis-（否定）+ may（可以）→ 事情不可以朝好的方向走 → 沮丧。
dismay::/dɪsˈmeɪ/:n./v. 沮丧:The students reacted with dismay to the exam results.
20. evacuate /ɪˈvækjueɪt/ v. 疏散；撤离；排空
📖 英文解释
To remove people from a place of danger to a safer place; to empty or vacate a place.
✏️ 例句
Residents were ordered to evacuate the coastal towns before the hurricane struck.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：evacuate an area/building（疏散地区/建筑），evacuation plan（疏散计划）；考研灾难类文章常见。
🧠 助记
e-（出）+ vacu（空，vacuum 同根）+ ate → 把人抽出来腾空 = 疏散。
evacuate::/ɪˈvækjueɪt/:v. 疏散:The fire alarm prompted staff to evacuate the building immediately.
21. feather /ˈfeðər/ n. 羽毛 v. 装羽毛；轻轻移动
📖 英文解释
Any of the flat appendages growing from a bird's skin; to fit or cover with feathers.
✏️ 例句
The peacock spread its feathers in a dazzling display to attract a mate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：light as a feather（轻如鸿毛），feather in one's cap（值得骄傲的成就），birds of a feather（物以类聚）。
🧠 助记
feather → 想象羽毛落地时的轻柔声音和触感。
feather::/ˈfeðər/:n. 羽毛 v. 装羽毛:Winning the award was a feather in her cap.
22. fertiliser /ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzər/ n. 肥料，化肥
📖 英文解释
A chemical or natural substance added to soil to increase its productivity.
✏️ 例句
The farmer applied organic fertiliser to his fields to improve crop yield.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：chemical/organic fertiliser（化肥/有机肥），apply fertiliser（施肥）；英式拼法，美式 fertilizer。
🧠 助记
fertile（肥沃）+ iser → 使土地肥沃的东西 = 化肥。
fertiliser::/ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzər/:n. 肥料，化肥:Overuse of fertiliser can cause water pollution.
23. feudal /ˈfjuːdl/ adj. 封建的，封建制度的
📖 英文解释
Relating to the feudal system, characterized by the holding of land in exchange for military service and loyalty.
✏️ 例句
Under the feudal system, peasants worked the land owned by nobles.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
历史词汇：feudal system/society（封建制度/社会），feudalism（封建主义），feudal lord（封建领主）。
🧠 助记
feud（争斗/仇恨）+ al → 封建时代充满领主之间的 feud（争斗）。
feudal::/ˈfjuːdl/:adj. 封建的，封建制度的:The feudal system dominated medieval European society.
24. fireplace /ˈfaɪərpleɪs/ n. 壁炉
📖 英文解释
A place in a room where a fire is made, typically a partially enclosed space in a wall with a chimney above it.
✏️ 例句
The family gathered around the fireplace on cold winter evenings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：light/sit by the fireplace（点燃/坐在壁炉旁），mantelpiece（壁炉架）；多见于文学描写。
🧠 助记
fire（火）+ place（地方）→ 生火的地方 = 壁炉。直接合成词。
fireplace::/ˈfaɪərpleɪs/:n. 壁炉:Stockings were hung by the fireplace on Christmas Eve.
25. fountain /ˈfaʊntən/ n. 喷泉；源泉
📖 英文解释
An ornamental structure in a pool or lake from which a jet of water is pumped; a natural spring of water; a source of something.
✏️ 例句
The tourists gathered around the magnificent fountain in the city square.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：drinking fountain（饮水器），fountain of knowledge/youth（知识/青春的源泉），fountain pen（钢笔）。
🧠 助记
fount（泉，font 同根）+ ain → 水的来源 = 喷泉/源泉。
fountain::/ˈfaʊntən/:n. 喷泉:The library is a fountain of knowledge for students.
