1. frog /frɒɡ/ n. 青蛙
📖 英文解释
A tailless amphibian with a short body, moist smooth skin, and very long hind legs for leaping.
✏️ 例句
The frog leaped into the pond with a loud splash.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
习语：frog in one's throat（嗓子嘶哑），leap-frog（蛙跳式发展），bull frog（牛蛙）。
🧠 助记
frog 的跳跃 → 联想 leap forward（蛙跳式前进），考研语境可出现在生态类文章。
frog::/frɒɡ/:n. 青蛙:Frogs are important indicators of environmental health.
2. germ /dʒɜːrm/ n. 细菌；胚芽；萌芽
📖 英文解释
A microorganism, especially one that causes disease; the initial stage of development of something.
✏️ 例句
Washing your hands regularly kills germs and prevents the spread of illness.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：germ theory（细菌论），germ cell（生殖细胞），the germ of an idea（一个想法的萌芽）。
🧠 助记
germ → germinate（发芽）→ 既是细菌（在繁殖），也是种子/胚芽（在生长）。
germ::/dʒɜːrm/:n. 细菌:The germ of a great idea began to form in her mind.
3. glide /ɡlaɪd/ v./n. 滑翔；滑动；流畅移动
📖 英文解释
To move smoothly and effortlessly; to fly without engine power by exploiting air currents.
✏️ 例句
The eagle glided effortlessly on the warm air currents high above the valley.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：glide through（顺畅通过），glider（滑翔机），glide path（降落滑行道）。
🧠 助记
glide ≈ slide（滑）→ 去掉 s 留 glide → 更优雅轻盈的滑行 = 滑翔。
glide::/ɡlaɪd/:v./n. 滑翔:The skater glided gracefully across the ice.
4. heir /eər/ n. 继承人
📖 英文解释
A person legally entitled to the property or rank of another on that person's death.
✏️ 例句
The king announced that his eldest son would be the heir to the throne.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：heir to the throne（王位继承人），sole heir（唯一继承人），heiress（女继承人）。
🧠 助记
heir 发音同 air（空气）→ 继承人就像空气一样无处不在地准备接班。
heir::/eər/:n. 继承人:She is the sole heir to her grandfather's vast estate.
5. imminent /ˈɪmɪnənt/ adj. 即将发生的，迫在眉睫的
📖 英文解释
About to happen very soon; impending.
✏️ 例句
With a storm imminent, the coastal residents were urged to seek shelter inland.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：imminent danger/threat/collapse（迫在眉睫的危险/威胁/崩溃）；区分 imminent vs. eminent（杰出的）。
🧠 助记
immin- ≈ in（进入）+ minute（分钟）→ 几分钟后就进入 = 即将发生。
imminent::/ˈɪmɪnənt/:adj. 即将发生的，迫在眉睫的:Doctors warned of imminent health risks from air pollution.
6. inhibit /ɪnˈhɪbɪt/ v. 抑制；妨碍；约束
📖 英文解释
To hinder, restrain, or prevent an action or process; to make someone self-conscious and unable to act freely.
✏️ 例句
Fear of failure can inhibit creativity and risk-taking.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：inhibit growth/development（抑制增长/发展），inhibitor（抑制剂）；名词 inhibition（抑制/拘束感）。
🧠 助记
in-（进入）+ hibit（持有，prohibit 同根）→ 持住不让进 = 抑制/妨碍。
inhibit::/ɪnˈhɪbɪt/:v. 抑制:Strict rules may inhibit innovation in the workplace.
7. inward /ˈɪnwərd/ adj./adv. 向内的；内心的
📖 英文解释
Directed or proceeding toward the interior; relating to or existing in the mind or thoughts.
✏️ 例句
She smiled outwardly, but felt an inward sense of sadness.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：inward investment（内部投资），inward-looking（内向的），turn inward（向内转）。
🧠 助记
in（内）+ ward（方向）→ 朝向内部的方向 = 向内的/内心的。
inward::/ˈɪnwərd/:adj./adv. 向内的:His inward thoughts were very different from what he expressed.
8. jazz /dʒæz/ n. 爵士乐；活力 v. 使活跃
📖 英文解释
A type of music of Black American origin characterized by improvisation and syncopated rhythm; liveliness and excitement.
✏️ 例句
New Orleans is considered the birthplace of jazz music.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：jazz up（使更有趣/活跃），jazz band（爵士乐队），all that jazz（诸如此类的东西）。
🧠 助记
jazz 字母 z 的振动感 → 充满活力振动的音乐 = 爵士乐。
jazz::/dʒæz/:n. 爵士乐:She tried to jazz up the presentation with colorful slides.
9. junction /ˈdʒʌŋkʃən/ n. 交叉口；连接处；汇合点
📖 英文解释
A point where two or more things are joined; a place where roads or railway lines meet.
✏️ 例句
Turn left at the junction and follow the road for two miles.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：road junction（路口），railway junction（铁路枢纽），at the junction of（在…的交汇处）。
🧠 助记
junc-（连接，join 同根）+ tion → 多条路连接在一起的地方 = 交叉口。
junction::/ˈdʒʌŋkʃən/:n. 交叉口:The accident occurred at a busy road junction.
10. kidnap /ˈkɪdnæp/ v. 绑架，劫持
📖 英文解释
To take someone away illegally by force, typically to obtain a ransom.
✏️ 例句
The businessman was kidnapped and held for ransom for two weeks.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：kidnap for ransom（绑架勒索），kidnapper（绑架者），kidnapping（绑架行为）。
🧠 助记
kid（小孩）+ nap（拿走）→ 把孩子 nap 走 = 绑架（历史上指偷孩子去做劳工）。
kidnap::/ˈkɪdnæp/:v. 绑架，劫持:The child was kidnapped but safely returned to her parents.
11. loop /luːp/ n. 环；圈 v. 绕成环；循环
📖 英文解释
A shape produced by a curve that bends round and crosses itself; a computer program that repeats a sequence of instructions; to form or move in a loop.
✏️ 例句
The roller coaster track made a complete loop before coming back to the start.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：in a loop（在循环中），loophole（法律漏洞），keep sb. in the loop（让某人了解情况）。
🧠 助记
loop 形状就像字母 O → 圆圈/环路。
loop::/luːp/:n. 环:Please keep me in the loop regarding any new developments.
12. missionary /ˈmɪʃəneri/ n. 传教士 adj. 传教的
📖 英文解释
A person sent on a religious mission, especially to promote Christianity in a foreign country; relating to missionary work.
✏️ 例句
The missionary spent twenty years helping communities in rural Africa.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：missionary work（传教工作），missionary zeal（传教般的热情，即极大热情）。
🧠 助记
mission（使命）+ ary → 带着使命去宣传的人 = 传教士。
missionary::/ˈmɪʃəneri/:n. 传教士 adj. 传教的:Missionaries played a significant role in colonial-era education.
13. moist /mɔɪst/ adj. 潮湿的；湿润的
📖 英文解释
Slightly wet; damp.
✏️ 例句
The soil should be kept moist but not waterlogged for the seeds to germinate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：moist climate/soil/eyes（潮湿的气候/土壤/眼睛），moisten（使湿润），moisture（湿度）。
🧠 助记
moist ≈ most + i → 最湿润的感觉，介于干和湿之间的状态。
moist::/mɔɪst/:adj. 潮湿的:She kept a moist cloth on his forehead to reduce the fever.
14. naked /ˈneɪkɪd/ adj. 裸露的；赤裸的；无遮掩的
📖 英文解释
Without clothes; exposed to view; (of the eye) unassisted by a telescope or microscope.
✏️ 例句
The stars are too faint to be seen with the naked eye.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
固定搭配：naked eye（肉眼），naked truth（赤裸裸的真相），naked aggression（赤裸裸的侵略）。
🧠 助记
naked → 联想 naked truth → 没有任何遮掩的真相 = 赤裸的。
naked::/ˈneɪkɪd/:adj. 裸露的:Bacteria are too small to be visible to the naked eye.
15. overcoat /ˈoʊvərkoʊt/ n. 大衣，外套
📖 英文解释
A long warm coat worn over other clothing.
✏️ 例句
He buttoned up his overcoat before stepping out into the freezing night.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
区分：overcoat（正式长大衣）vs. coat（外衣/涂层）vs. jacket（夹克）；over- 表示在…之上。
🧠 助记
over（在…之上）+ coat（外套）→ 穿在所有衣服之上的大外套 = 大衣。
overcoat::/ˈoʊvərkoʊt/:n. 大衣，外套:She wore a heavy wool overcoat to keep warm in winter.
16. overflow /ˌoʊvərˈfloʊ/ v./n. 溢出；泛滥；过多
📖 英文解释
To flow over the edges of something; to be excessively full; an excess of something.
✏️ 例句
Heavy rainfall caused the river to overflow its banks and flood the nearby farmland.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：overflow with（充满/溢出），overflow capacity（超负荷），the overflow（溢出部分）。
🧠 助记
over（超过）+ flow（流动）→ 水超过容量向外流 = 溢出/泛滥。
overflow::/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊ/:v./n. 溢出:The stadium was filled to overflow on the night of the concert.
17. pardon /ˈpɑːrdən/ n./v. 原谅；赦免；请再说一遍
📖 英文解释
The action of forgiving or being forgiven for an error or offense; an official cancellation of the legal consequences of a crime; to forgive someone.
✏️ 例句
The president granted a pardon to the prisoner after reviewing new evidence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
法律词汇：presidential pardon（总统特赦），beg sb.'s pardon（请求原谅）；口语 Pardon? = 请再说一遍。
🧠 助记
par-（完全）+ don（给予）→ 完全给予宽恕 = 原谅/赦免。
pardon::/ˈpɑːrdən/:n./v. 原谅:The governor issued a pardon for the wrongfully convicted man.
18. pity /ˈpɪti/ n. 怜悯；遗憾 v. 同情
📖 英文解释
A feeling of sorrow and compassion for someone who is suffering; something that causes regret or disappointment; to feel pity for.
✏️ 例句
It's a pity that you can't attend the graduation ceremony.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：take pity on（同情），What a pity!（真遗憾！），out of pity（出于同情），pitiful（可怜的）。
🧠 助记
pity 发音像皮提 → 中文皮提像怜悯叹气声 = 可怜/遗憾。
pity::/ˈpɪti/:n. 怜悯:She looked at the stray dog with pity and brought it inside.
19. plagiarism /ˈpleɪdʒərɪzəm/ n. 剽窃，抄袭
📖 英文解释
The practice of taking someone else's work or ideas and passing them off as one's own.
✏️ 例句
The professor failed the student for committing plagiarism in the research paper.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
学术词汇：commit plagiarism（抄袭），academic plagiarism（学术剽窃），plagiarize（v. 剽窃）。
🧠 助记
plagia-（拐走，拉丁语 plagium 拐卖）→ 把别人的作品拐走据为己有 = 剽窃。
plagiarism::/ˈpleɪdʒərɪzəm/:n. 剽窃，抄袭:Universities use software to detect plagiarism in student submissions.
20. plight /plaɪt/ n. 困境；苦难 v. 誓约
📖 英文解释
A dangerous, difficult, or otherwise unfortunate situation; to make a solemn pledge (archaic).
✏️ 例句
The documentary highlighted the plight of refugees fleeing war zones.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：the plight of（…的困境），dire plight（悲惨处境）；考研社会议题高频词。
🧠 助记
plight ≈ blight（枯萎/祸害）→ 都是不好的处境，发音相近联想。
plight::/plaɪt/:n. 困境:World leaders discussed the plight of climate refugees.
21. portfolio /pɔːrtˈfoʊlioʊ/ n. 文件夹；作品集；投资组合
📖 英文解释
A large, thin, flat case for carrying loose papers, drawings, or maps; a set of investments held by a person or organization; a range of products or services.
✏️ 例句
She presented her design portfolio to the creative agency during the interview.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
金融词汇：investment portfolio（投资组合），portfolio management（投资组合管理）；也指艺术/设计作品集。
🧠 助记
port（携带，portable 同根）+ folio（叶/页）→ 携带很多页的文件夹 = 文件夹/作品集。
portfolio::/pɔːrtˈfoʊlioʊ/:n. 文件夹:The fund manager diversified her investment portfolio.
22. postman /ˈpoʊstmən/ n. 邮递员，邮差
📖 英文解释
A person who delivers mail as a job.
✏️ 例句
The postman delivered a large parcel to the door early in the morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
英式用词，美式常用 mailman；postwoman（女邮递员），postal worker（邮政工作人员）。
🧠 助记
post（邮件）+ man（人）→ 送邮件的人 = 邮递员。直接合成词。
postman::/ˈpoʊstmən/:n. 邮递员，邮差:The postman knocked twice before leaving the package.
23. prototype /ˈproʊtətaɪp/ n. 原型；样机；典型
📖 英文解释
A first or preliminary model of something from which other forms are developed; a typical example of something.
✏️ 例句
The engineers built a prototype of the new electric vehicle before mass production.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：build/design a prototype（制造原型），prototype testing（原型测试）；科技文章高频词。
🧠 助记
proto-（最初）+ type（类型）→ 最初的类型/模型 = 原型。
prototype::/ˈproʊtətaɪp/:n. 原型:The startup unveiled its prototype at the technology conference.
24. punctual /ˈpʌŋktʃuəl/ adj. 准时的，守时的
📖 英文解释
Happening or doing something at the agreed or proper time; on time.
✏️ 例句
She is always punctual for meetings, never keeping colleagues waiting.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：be punctual for（准时参加），punctuality（守时），punctually（准时地）。
🧠 助记
punct-（点，puncture 刺穿同根）→ 准确刺中那个时间点 = 准时的。
punctual::/ˈpʌŋktʃuəl/:adj. 准时的，守时的:Being punctual is considered a sign of professionalism.
25. redundant /rɪˈdʌndənt/ adj. 多余的；被裁员的；冗余的
📖 英文解释
Not or no longer needed or useful; superfluous; (of a worker) no longer needed and therefore dismissed.
✏️ 例句
Many workers were made redundant when the factory introduced automated machinery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
英式英语：be made redundant（被裁员），redundancy（冗余/裁员），redundant information（冗余信息）。
🧠 助记
re-（再次）+ dund（波浪，undulate 同根）→ 波浪一波又一波，重复多余 = 冗余的。
redundant::/rɪˈdʌndənt/:adj. 多余的:Several positions were made redundant after the company merger.
