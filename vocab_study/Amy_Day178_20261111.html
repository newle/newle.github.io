1. rotten /ˈrɒtn/ adj. 腐烂的；糟糕的；堕落的
📖 英文解释
Decayed and having an unpleasant smell; very bad or unpleasant in quality.
✏️ 例句
The rotten wood of the old bridge made it dangerous to cross.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ rotten luck 真倒霉 ★ rotten to the core 彻底腐败 ★ smell rotten 闻起来腐臭 辨析：rotten / decayed：rotten 更口语，常含贬义；decayed 更正式，用于科学描述
🧠 助记
rot（腐烂）+ ten → 腐烂了十天 = 腐烂的
rotten::/ˈrɒtn/:adj. 腐烂的:The rotten apple spoiled the whole barrel.
2. scarf /skɑːrf/ n. 围巾，领巾；v. 围上围巾
📖 英文解释
A length of cloth worn around the neck, head, or shoulders for warmth or decoration.
✏️ 例句
She wrapped a warm scarf around her neck before stepping out into the cold.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ wear a scarf 围围巾 ★ silk scarf 丝巾 ★ woolen scarf 毛线围巾 辨析：scarf / shawl：scarf 通常较窄长绕颈；shawl 较宽大搭肩
🧠 助记
scarf 谐音「是卡夫」—— 卡在脖子上的布 = 围巾
scarf::/skɑːrf/:n. 围巾，领巾:He wore a red scarf to stay warm in winter.
3. shabby /ˈʃæbi/ adj. 破旧的；寒碜的；卑鄙的
📖 英文解释
In poor condition through long use; dressed in old, worn clothes; unfair or unworthy.
✏️ 例句
The old man sat on a shabby bench in his worn-out coat.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ shabby clothes/furniture 破旧的衣服/家具 ★ shabby treatment 卑鄙的对待 ★ feel shabby 感到寒酸 辨析：shabby / worn：worn 只表示破损；shabby 含贬义，暗示贫穷或道德低下
🧠 助记
shab（谐音「沙布」）+ by → 用沙布缝的衣服 = 破旧的
shabby::/ˈʃæbi/:adj. 破旧的:The shabby apartment needed urgent renovation.
4. shave /ʃeɪv/ v. 刮；剃；n. 刮胡子
📖 英文解释
To remove hair from the face or body by cutting it close with a razor or similar device.
✏️ 例句
He shaved his beard off before the important job interview.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ shave off 剃掉 ★ a close shave 侥幸脱险 ★ shave one's head 剃头 辨析：shave / trim：shave 指完全刮光；trim 指修剪整齐
🧠 助记
shave 谐音「舍了」—— 舍得剃 = 刮、剃
shave::/ʃeɪv/:v. 刮:He shaves every morning before going to work.
5. shipment /ˈʃɪpmənt/ n. 装运；运输的货物；一批货
📖 英文解释
A load of goods sent together at the same time; the act of transporting goods.
✏️ 例句
The shipment of medical supplies arrived just in time for the relief effort.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a shipment of goods 一批货物 ★ make a shipment 发货 ★ delay in shipment 运输延误 辨析：shipment / cargo：shipment 指一次运输批次；cargo 指船/飞机上的整体货物
🧠 助记
ship（船）+ ment（名词后缀）→ 船运的东西 = 装运、货物
shipment::/ˈʃɪpmənt/:n. 装运:The shipment of electronics was delayed at customs.
6. sore /sɔːr/ adj. 疼痛的；恼火的；n. 痛处，溃疡
📖 英文解释
Painful and aching; a raw or inflamed place on the body; feeling upset or angry.
✏️ 例句
My throat is sore after speaking for hours at the conference.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ sore throat 喉咙痛 ★ sore muscles 肌肉酸痛 ★ a sore point/subject 敏感话题 辨析：sore / painful：sore 多指持续性隐痛；painful 更泛指各类疼痛
🧠 助记
sore 谐音「搜啊」—— 哪里痛就搜哪里 = 痛处
sore::/sɔːr/:adj. 疼痛的:Her feet were sore after the long hike.
7. spit /spɪt/ v. 吐出；吐口水；n. 唾液；烤肉叉
📖 英文解释
To force liquid, food, or other matter out of the mouth; saliva ejected from the mouth.
✏️ 例句
He spit out the bitter medicine as soon as he tasted it.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ spit out 吐出 ★ spit at sb 朝某人吐口水 ★ spit and image 一模一样的人 辨析：spit / spew：spit 指从口中吐出；spew 指大量涌出（液体/熔岩等）
🧠 助记
spit 谐音「斯匹特」—— 匹特·布拉德吐口水 = 吐
spit::/spɪt/:v. 吐出:He spit out the seed after eating the watermelon.
8. steak /steɪk/ n. 牛排；肉排
📖 英文解释
A thick slice of meat, especially beef, or fish, cut for grilling or frying.
✏️ 例句
She ordered a medium-rare steak with mashed potatoes at the restaurant.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ beef steak 牛排 ★ rare/medium/well-done steak 三分/五分/全熟牛排 ★ grill a steak 烤牛排 辨析：steak / chop：steak 通常为大片切割；chop 带骨肋排
🧠 助记
steak 谐音「死踢客」—— 踢成薄片 = 牛排（注意与 stake 桩区分）
steak::/steɪk/:n. 牛排:He grilled a juicy steak for dinner.
9. storey /ˈstɔːri/ n. 楼层（英式拼写）
📖 英文解释
A floor or level of a building (British spelling; American: story).
✏️ 例句
The office building has twenty storeys, with the executive suite on the top floor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ ground storey 底层 ★ a five-storey building 五层楼 ★ multi-storey car park 多层停车场 辨析：storey / floor：storey 指楼层级别；floor 既可指楼层也可指地板
🧠 助记
storey 与 story（故事）同音 —— 每层楼都有自己的故事 = 楼层
storey::/ˈstɔːri/:n. 楼层（英式拼写）:They live on the third storey of the apartment block.
10. sturdy /ˈstɜːrdi/ adj. 坚固的；强健的；坚定的
📖 英文解释
Strongly and solidly built; robust and healthy; showing determination.
✏️ 例句
The hikers needed sturdy boots to navigate the rocky mountain trail.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ sturdy construction 坚固的建筑 ★ sturdy support 坚定的支持 ★ sturdy build 强健的体格 辨析：sturdy / solid：sturdy 强调强健有力；solid 强调整体坚实不中空
🧠 助记
sturdy 谐音「是铁的」—— 铁做的 = 坚固的
sturdy::/ˈstɜːrdi/:adj. 坚固的:The sturdy table survived decades of heavy use.
11. toe /toʊ/ n. 脚趾；v. 用脚趾触碰
📖 英文解释
Any of the five digits at the end of the human foot; to touch or push with the toes.
✏️ 例句
She stubbed her toe on the corner of the bed in the dark.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ stub one's toe 踢伤脚趾 ★ toe the line 遵守规则 ★ on one's toes 保持警觉 辨析：toe / finger：toe 指脚趾；finger 指手指
🧠 助记
toe 和中文「脚」形似 T 形 —— T 型就是脚趾 = 脚趾
toe::/toʊ/:n. 脚趾:He wiggled his toes in the warm sand.
12. tumor /ˈtuːmər/ n. 肿块；肿瘤
📖 英文解释
An abnormal mass of tissue that results from uncontrolled cell division; may be benign or malignant.
✏️ 例句
The doctor discovered a small tumor in her lung during a routine check-up.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ malignant/benign tumor 恶性/良性肿瘤 ★ brain tumor 脑瘤 ★ tumor removal 肿瘤切除 辨析：tumor / cancer：tumor 指肿块（可良性）；cancer 专指恶性肿瘤
🧠 助记
tumor 谐音「突摸」—— 突然摸到一个鼓包 = 肿块
tumor::/ˈtuːmər/:n. 肿块:Surgery successfully removed the tumor from his liver.
13. turmoil /ˈtɜːrmɔɪl/ n. 混乱；骚动；动荡
📖 英文解释
A state of great disturbance, confusion, or uncertainty.
✏️ 例句
The country fell into political turmoil after the sudden resignation of the president.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in turmoil 处于混乱中 ★ political/social turmoil 政治/社会动荡 ★ emotional turmoil 情感混乱 辨析：turmoil / chaos：turmoil 强调动荡不安；chaos 强调完全无秩序
🧠 助记
tur（turn 转）+ moil（辛苦）→ 转来转去很辛苦 = 混乱
turmoil::/ˈtɜːrmɔɪl/:n. 混乱:The financial turmoil caused widespread anxiety.

★ be wary of 对…保持警惕 ★ wary of strangers 对陌生人谨慎 ★ a wary look 警惕的眼神 辨析：wary / cautious：wary 强调因担心危险而警觉；cautious 泛指做事谨慎小心
🧠 助记
wary 谐音「喂呀」—— 喂，小心啊！= 机警的
wary::/ˈweri/:adj. 机警的:Be wary of online scams that seem too good to be true.
15. whistle /ˈwɪsl/ n. 口哨；汽笛；v. 吹口哨

A clear, high-pitched sound made by forcing breath through a small hole; to make such a sound.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ blow a whistle 吹口哨 ★ whistle-blower 举报人 ★ as clean as a whistle 干净利落 辨析：whistle / horn：whistle 用气流产生高音；horn 用振动产生低沉声
🧠 助记
whis（谐音「嘶嘶」）+ tle → 嘶嘶的声音 = 口哨
whistle::/ˈwɪsl/:n. 口哨:She whistled a cheerful tune while walking home.
16. wolf /wʊlf/ n. 狼；v. 狼吞虎咽
📖 英文解释
A wild carnivorous mammal resembling a large dog; to eat greedily.
✏️ 例句
A lone wolf howled at the moon on the cold winter night.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ wolf down 狼吞虎咽 ★ cry wolf 发假警报 ★ wolf in sheep's clothing 披着羊皮的狼 辨析：wolf / fox：wolf 象征凶残贪婪；fox 象征狡猾
🧠 助记
wolf 和「狼」形象相同 —— w 像狼嘴 = 狼
wolf::/wʊlf/:n. 狼:He wolfed down his dinner before running to catch the bus.
17. geometry /dʒiˈɒmɪtri/ n. 几何（学）；几何形状
📖 英文解释
The branch of mathematics dealing with shapes, sizes, positions, and properties of figures.
✏️ 例句
Understanding geometry is essential for students pursuing architecture or engineering.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ plane/solid geometry 平面/立体几何 ★ geometry problem 几何题 ★ geometric shape 几何形状 辨析：geometry / algebra：geometry 研究图形空间；algebra 研究符号与方程
🧠 助记
geo（地球）+ metry（测量）→ 测量地球形状 = 几何
geometry::/dʒiˈɒmɪtri/:n. 几何（学）:She excelled at geometry and won the math competition.
18. menace /ˈmenəs/ n. 威胁；危险人物；v. 威胁
📖 英文解释
A person or thing that is likely to cause harm; to threaten harm or danger.
✏️ 例句
Drunk drivers are a menace to public safety on the roads.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a menace to society 社会威胁 ★ pose a menace 构成威胁 ★ menacing look 威胁性的眼神 辨析：menace / threat：menace 强调带来危险的人或物；threat 强调具体的威胁行为或言语
🧠 助记
menace 谐音「没那死」—— 威胁你「没那么容易死」= 威胁
menace::/ˈmenəs/:n. 威胁:Cybercrime poses a growing menace to national security.
19. radius /ˈreɪdiəs/ n. 半径；范围；辐射状区域
📖 英文解释
A straight line from the center to the circumference of a circle or sphere; the distance within which something acts or operates.
✏️ 例句
The explosion was heard within a radius of five kilometers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ within a radius of 在…半径范围内 ★ turning radius 转弯半径 ★ radius of action 行动范围 辨析：radius / diameter：radius 是半径；diameter 是直径（= 2 × radius）
🧠 助记
radi（放射）+ us → 从圆心放射出去的长度 = 半径
radius::/ˈreɪdiəs/:n. 半径:The bomb blast damaged buildings within a radius of 200 metres.
20. semiconductor /ˌsemikənˈdʌktər/ n. 半导体
📖 英文解释
A solid substance that has electrical conductivity between that of an insulator and a conductor.
✏️ 例句
Silicon is the most widely used semiconductor material in modern electronics.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ semiconductor industry 半导体产业 ★ semiconductor chip 半导体芯片 ★ semiconductor material 半导体材料 辨析：semiconductor / conductor：conductor 导电性强；semiconductor 导电性居中，可控
🧠 助记
semi（一半）+ conductor（导体）→ 半个导体 = 半导体
semiconductor::/ˌsemikənˈdʌktər/:n. 半导体:The semiconductor shortage disrupted global supply chains.
21. statute /ˈstætʃuːt/ n. 法规；法令；章程
📖 英文解释
A written law passed by a legislative body; an established rule or regulation.
✏️ 例句
The new statute requires all companies to report their carbon emissions annually.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ by statute 依法律规定 ★ statute law 成文法 ★ statute book 法规汇编 辨析：statute / law：statute 特指议会通过的成文法；law 是更广泛的法律概念
🧠 助记
statute 谐音「是塔吐」—— 法律条文像塔一样稳固 = 法规
statute::/ˈstætʃuːt/:n. 法规:The statute prohibits discrimination in the workplace.

23. jurisdiction /ˌdʒʊərɪsˈdɪkʃn/ n. 管辖权；司法权；管辖范围
📖 英文解释
The official power to make legal decisions and judgements; the territory over which this power extends.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ under the jurisdiction of 在…管辖下 ★ jurisdiction over 对…的管辖权 ★ exceed one's jurisdiction 越权 辨析：jurisdiction / authority：jurisdiction 特指法律/司法权力；authority 泛指权威或权力

jurisdiction::/ˌdʒʊərɪsˈdɪkʃn/:n. 管辖权:This dispute falls outside the court's jurisdiction.
24. nitrogen /ˈnaɪtrədʒən/ n. 氮（化学元素N）
📖 英文解释
A colorless, odorless, gaseous element that makes up about 78% of the atmosphere, essential for life.
✏️ 例句
Liquid nitrogen is used in laboratories to preserve biological samples.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ liquid nitrogen 液氮 ★ nitrogen cycle 氮循环 ★ nitrogen oxide 氮氧化物 辨析：nitrogen / oxygen：nitrogen 占大气约78%，性质稳定；oxygen 占21%，支持燃烧和呼吸
🧠 助记
nitro（硝基）+ gen（产生）→ 产生硝基的元素 = 氮
nitrogen::/ˈnaɪtrədʒən/:n. 氮（化学元素N）:Plants absorb nitrogen from the soil through their roots.
