1. shove /ʃʌv/ v. 猛推；用力推；n. 猛推
📖 英文解释
To push someone or something roughly or carelessly; a rough push.
✏️ 例句
He shoved his way through the crowd to get to the front of the stage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ shove sb aside 把某人推到一旁 ★ shove off 滚开（口语） ★ give sb a shove 推某人一把 辨析：shove / push：shove 更粗鲁，含强行推挤之意；push 较中性
🧠 助记
shove 谐音「嘘夫」—— 嘘着把人推走 = 猛推
shove::/ʃʌv/:v. 猛推:She shoved the heavy door open with her shoulder.
2. tram /træm/ n. 有轨电车；（矿用）矿车
📖 英文解释
An electric vehicle that runs on rails laid in the road; a streetcar.
✏️ 例句
We took the tram across the city to avoid the heavy traffic.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ tram line 电车线 ★ take/catch a tram 乘电车 ★ tram stop 电车站 辨析：tram / subway：tram 在地面轨道运行；subway（metro）在地下运行
🧠 助记
tram 谐音「踏路」—— 踏着轨道走 = 有轨电车
tram::/træm/:n. 有轨电车:The tram rattled along the cobblestone street.
3. abound /əˈbaʊnd/ v. 充满；大量存在；富于
📖 英文解释
To exist in large numbers or amounts; to be full of or teeming with.
✏️ 例句
The river abounds in fish during the summer spawning season.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ abound in/with 充满；富含 ★ opportunities abound 机会大量存在 ★ rumors abound 谣言四处流传 辨析：abound / overflow：abound 强调数量丰富充足；overflow 强调超出容量溢出
🧠 助记
a（加强）+ bound（边界）→ 超出边界了 = 大量充满
abound::/əˈbaʊnd/:v. 充满:Wildlife abounds in the national park.
4. cape /keɪp/ n. 披肩；斗篷；海角，岬
📖 英文解释
A sleeveless garment hanging from the shoulders; a headland or promontory jutting into the sea.
✏️ 例句
The superhero's black cape billowed behind him as he leapt from the rooftop.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ Cape of Good Hope 好望角 ★ a velvet cape 天鹅绒披肩 ★ cape cod 科德角（地名） 辨析：cape / cloak：cape 无袖，垂挂肩背；cloak 有时有袖，更宽大
🧠 助记
cape 谐音「盖肩」—— 盖在肩上 = 披肩；也像海角突出 = 海角
cape::/keɪp/:n. 披肩:She wore a dramatic red cape to the costume party.
5. cherry /ˈtʃeri/ n. 樱桃；樱桃树；樱桃色
📖 英文解释
A small, round, red or black fruit with a stone; the tree that bears this fruit.
✏️ 例句
The children picked fresh cherries from the tree in the garden.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ cherry blossom 樱花 ★ cherry-pick 挑选（最好的） ★ the cherry on top 锦上添花 辨析：cherry / strawberry：cherry 有硬核；strawberry 为草莓，果肉软
🧠 助记
cherry 谐音「吃日」—— 红红的像太阳（日）= 樱桃
cherry::/ˈtʃeri/:n. 樱桃:The cherry trees bloom beautifully every spring.
6. dock /dɒk/ n. 码头；船坞；v. 停靠；扣减
📖 英文解释
An enclosed area of water in a port for loading, unloading, and repairing ships; to bring a ship into dock; to deduct from wages.
✏️ 例句
The cargo ship arrived at the dock just before midnight.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ dock at a port 停靠港口 ★ dock pay/wages 扣薪 ★ dry dock 干船坞 辨析：dock / port：dock 是船坞/停泊处；port 是整个港口设施
🧠 助记
dock 谐音「停靠」的缩写 —— 船停靠的地方 = 码头
dock::/dɒk/:n. 码头:The cruise ship docked at Southampton early in the morning.
7. eminent /ˈemɪnənt/ adj. 著名的；杰出的；卓越的
📖 英文解释
Famous and respected, especially in a particular profession; standing out above others.
✏️ 例句
An eminent scientist was invited to deliver the keynote speech at the symposium.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ eminent scholar/doctor 著名学者/医生 ★ eminent domain 征用权（法律） ★ an eminent position 显要地位 辨析：eminent / prominent：eminent 强调受人尊敬的声誉；prominent 强调显著突出
🧠 助记
emin（谐音「一鸣」）+ ent → 一鸣惊人 = 著名的
eminent::/ˈemɪnənt/:adj. 著名的:She is an eminent authority on medieval history.
8. ending /ˈendɪŋ/ n. 结局；结尾；词尾
📖 英文解释
The final part of something; a conclusion or resolution; the last part of a word.
✏️ 例句
The movie had a surprising ending that left the audience speechless.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ happy ending 美好结局 ★ word ending 词尾 ★ a sad/tragic ending 悲惨结局 辨析：ending / conclusion：ending 更生动，指故事/电影结局；conclusion 更正式，指论述结论
🧠 助记
end（结束）+ ing → 正在结束 = 结局
ending::/ˈendɪŋ/:n. 结局:The novel's ending was both unexpected and deeply moving.
9. epoch /ˈiːpɒk/ n. 时代；纪元；重要时期
📖 英文解释
A period of time in history marked by distinctive character or events; a particular era.
✏️ 例句
The invention of the internet marked the beginning of a new epoch in human communication.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ epoch-making 划时代的 ★ a new epoch 新纪元 ★ geological epoch 地质时代 辨析：epoch / era：epoch 通常指由重大事件开启的时代；era 更泛指一段时期
🧠 助记
epoch 谐音「一破壳」—— 破壳而出进入新时代 = 纪元
epoch::/ˈiːpɒk/:n. 时代:The Renaissance was an epoch of extraordinary artistic achievement.
10. hen /hen/ n. 母鸡；雌鸟
📖 英文解释
A female chicken kept for its eggs or meat; a female bird of any kind.
✏️ 例句
The farmer collected eggs from the hen every morning before breakfast.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ hen house 鸡舍 ★ hen party 女性聚会（婚前派对） ★ as rare as hen's teeth 极为罕见 辨析：hen / rooster：hen 是母鸡；rooster（cock）是公鸡
🧠 助记
hen 谐音「很」—— 很能下蛋的鸡 = 母鸡
hen::/hen/:n. 母鸡:The hen sat quietly on her nest of eggs.
11. intermittent /ˌɪntərˈmɪtənt/ adj. 断断续续的；间歇的
📖 英文解释
Stopping and starting at irregular intervals; not continuous or steady.
✏️ 例句
The intermittent rain made it difficult to plan any outdoor activities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ intermittent power supply 间歇性供电 ★ intermittent fasting 间歇性禁食 ★ intermittent signal 断续信号 辨析：intermittent / occasional：intermittent 强调有规律性的断续；occasional 指偶尔发生
🧠 助记
inter（之间）+ mit（送）+ tent → 中间停一下再送 = 断断续续
intermittent::/ˌɪntərˈmɪtənt/:adj. 断断续续的:There were intermittent showers throughout the afternoon.
12. jaw /dʒɔː/ n. 下巴；颌；v. 唠叨
📖 英文解释
Either of the two bones that form the framework of the mouth; to talk at length.
✏️ 例句
He broke his jaw in a cycling accident and had to eat liquid food for weeks.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ upper/lower jaw 上/下颌 ★ jaw-dropping 令人惊讶的 ★ the jaws of death 死亡的阴影 辨析：jaw / chin：jaw 指整个颌骨；chin 特指下巴末端外部
🧠 助记
jaw 谐音「抓」—— 颌骨像抓住食物的爪子 = 下巴/颌
jaw::/dʒɔː/:n. 下巴:Her jaw dropped when she heard the shocking news.
13. pepper /ˈpepər/ n. 辣椒；胡椒；v. 撒上；密集打击
📖 英文解释
A hot-tasting spice or vegetable; to scatter over or sprinkle; to hit repeatedly.
✏️ 例句
She added freshly ground pepper to the soup to enhance its flavor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ black/white/red pepper 黑/白/红胡椒 ★ pepper spray 胡椒喷雾 ★ pepper with questions 连续提问 辨析：pepper / chili：pepper 泛指胡椒或甜椒；chili 特指辣椒（辣度较高）
🧠 助记
pepper 谐音「辟啪」—— 辟啪辣口 = 辣椒
pepper::/ˈpepər/:n. 辣椒:Season the chicken with salt and pepper before roasting.
14. purify /ˈpjʊərɪfaɪ/ v. 净化；提纯；洗涤（罪孽）
📖 英文解释
To remove contaminants or impurities from something; to make spiritually or morally clean.
✏️ 例句
The plant uses a filtration system to purify the water before it reaches homes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ purify water/air 净化水/空气 ★ purify one's soul 净化心灵 ★ purification process 净化过程 辨析：purify / filter：purify 含义更广，包括物理/化学/道德净化；filter 指通过过滤去除杂质
🧠 助记
pur（pure 纯）+ ify（使…）→ 使变纯 = 净化
purify::/ˈpjʊərɪfaɪ/:v. 净化:The temple ritual was meant to purify the spirit.
15. rhetoric /ˈretərɪk/ n. 修辞（学）；花言巧语；雄辩术
📖 英文解释
The art of effective or persuasive speaking or writing; language designed to have a persuasive effect, sometimes empty or exaggerated.
✏️ 例句
The politician's speech was full of rhetoric but lacked any concrete policy proposals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ political rhetoric 政治辞令 ★ empty rhetoric 空洞的言辞 ★ rhetorical question 反问句 辨析：rhetoric / oratory：rhetoric 指修辞技巧；oratory 强调演讲能力
🧠 助记
rhetor（演说家）+ ic → 演说家的技巧 = 修辞
rhetoric::/ˈretərɪk/:n. 修辞（学）:Her speech was praised for its powerful rhetoric.
16. rope /roʊp/ n. 绳索；v. 用绳捆绑；说服

A length of thick strong cord made by twisting together strands of fiber; to tie with a rope; to persuade someone to join.
✏️ 例句
The climbers used a safety rope to secure themselves on the steep mountain face.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ jump rope 跳绳 ★ rope in sb 拉某人入伙 ★ know the ropes 熟悉情况 辨析：rope / cord：rope 更粗更强；cord 较细，多用于日常捆绑
🧠 助记
rope 谐音「绕啊」—— 绕来绕去 = 绳索
rope::/roʊp/:n. 绳索:He tied the boat to the dock with a heavy rope.
17. sideways /ˈsaɪdweɪz/ adv. 侧身地；斜向一边地；adj. 向侧面的
📖 英文解释
To or toward one side; with one side forward; not directly forward.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ move sideways 横向移动 ★ a sideways glance 斜视 ★ sideways career move 横向职业转换 辨析：sideways / sideward：sideways 更常用，表示动作或位置；sideward 较书面
🧠 助记
side（侧面）+ ways（方向）→ 侧面方向 = 侧身地
sideways::/ˈsaɪdweɪz/:adv. 侧身地:She looked at him sideways with a suspicious expression.
18. swarm /swɔːrm/ n. 群（尤指昆虫）；v. 成群移动；蜂拥
📖 英文解释

A swarm of bees emerged from the hive and circled the garden.

★ a swarm of bees/ants 一群蜜蜂/蚂蚁 ★ swarm with 挤满了… ★ tourists swarm 游客蜂拥 辨析：swarm / flock：swarm 多指昆虫或人群蜂拥；flock 指鸟群或羊群
🧠 助记
swarm 谐音「死旺」—— 旺得死，数量极多 = 群
swarm::/swɔːrm/:n. 群（尤指昆虫）:Tourists swarm the old town every summer.
19. symposium /sɪmˈpoʊziəm/ n. 讨论会；专题研讨会；座谈会
📖 英文解释
A formal meeting or conference at which experts discuss a particular topic; a collection of essays by different authors.
✏️ 例句
The professor presented her research findings at an international symposium on climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ hold/attend a symposium 举办/参加研讨会 ★ symposium on 关于…的研讨会 ★ annual symposium 年度研讨会 辨析：symposium / seminar：symposium 规模较大，多为学术会议；seminar 规模较小，常为教学研讨
🧠 助记
sym（一起）+ posium（举杯饮酒）→ 古希腊一起饮酒讨论 = 研讨会
symposium::/sɪmˈpoʊziəm/:n. 讨论会:She organized a symposium on artificial intelligence ethics.
20. thermometer /θərˈmɒmɪtər/ n. 温度计；体温表
📖 英文解释
An instrument for measuring temperature, especially of the human body.
✏️ 例句
The nurse placed a thermometer under the patient's tongue to check for fever.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ mercury thermometer 水银温度计 ★ digital thermometer 电子温度计 ★ oral/rectal thermometer 口腔/肛门温度计 辨析：thermometer / thermostat：thermometer 测量温度；thermostat 自动控制温度
🧠 助记
thermo（热）+ meter（计量）→ 计量热度 = 温度计
thermometer::/θərˈmɒmɪtər/:n. 温度计:The thermometer showed a temperature of 38.5 degrees Celsius.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ withhold information/payment 扣留信息/款项 ★ withhold consent 拒绝同意 ★ withhold tax 预扣税 辨析：withhold / conceal：withhold 是主动不提供；conceal 是主动隐藏

with（反对）+ hold（持有）→ 反对给出，持在手里 = 扣留
withhold::/wɪðˈhoʊld/:v. 保留:The company was accused of withholding evidence from investigators.
