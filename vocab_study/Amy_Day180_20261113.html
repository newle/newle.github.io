1. buzz /bʌz/ n. 嗡嗡声；电话；轰动；v. 嗡嗡叫
📖 英文解释
A low, continuous humming sound; a phone call (informal); to make such a sound; excited activity.
✏️ 例句
There was a buzz of excitement in the hall before the announcement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ give sb a buzz 给某人打电话 ★ buzz around 忙碌穿行 ★ buzz cut 平头发型 辨析：buzz / hum：buzz 更尖锐，如蜂鸣；hum 更低沉平稳，如机器声
🧠 助记
buzz 象声词 —— 蜜蜂嗡嗡叫的声音 = 嗡嗡声
buzz::/bʌz/:n. 嗡嗡声:The bees buzzed around the flowers in the garden.
2. cab /kæb/ n. 出租车；驾驶室（司机座舱）
📖 英文解释
A taxi; the driver's compartment in a truck, bus, or train.
✏️ 例句
She hailed a cab outside the hotel and asked to be taken to the airport.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ hail/take a cab 拦/乘出租车 ★ cab driver 出租车司机 ★ cab rank 出租车候车站 辨析：cab / taxi：两词通用；cab 更口语，taxi 更正式
🧠 助记
cab 谐音「开吧」—— 快开车吧 = 出租车
cab::/kæb/:n. 出租车:He jumped into a cab to avoid being late for his meeting.
3. calcium /ˈkælsiəm/ n. 钙（化学元素Ca）
📖 英文解释
A soft grey metallic element essential for living organisms, especially for bones and teeth.
✏️ 例句
Drinking milk regularly is an effective way to increase calcium intake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ calcium supplement 钙补充剂 ★ calcium deficiency 缺钙 ★ calcium carbonate 碳酸钙 辨析：calcium / iron：calcium 元素符号Ca，构成骨骼；iron 元素符号Fe，参与血液循环
🧠 助记
cal（谐音「抗」）+ cium → 抗骨折的元素 = 钙
calcium::/ˈkælsiəm/:n. 钙（化学元素Ca）:Children need adequate calcium for healthy bone development.
4. compass /ˈkʌmpəs/ n. 指南针；圆规；范围；v. 理解
📖 英文解释
An instrument for finding direction using a magnetic needle; a device for drawing circles; extent or range.
✏️ 例句
The hiker relied on a compass to navigate through the dense fog.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ magnetic compass 磁罗盘 ★ compass needle 罗盘指针 ★ beyond one's compass 超出能力范围 辨析：compass / protractor：compass 画圆或指方向；protractor 量角度
🧠 助记
com（共同）+ pass（通过）→ 大家都能通过它辨方向 = 指南针
compass::/ˈkʌmpəs/:n. 指南针:Without a compass, they would have been lost in the wilderness.
5. downstairs /ˌdaʊnˈstɛrz/ adv. 在楼下；往楼下；adj. 楼下的
📖 英文解释
On or to a lower floor of a building.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ go downstairs 下楼 ★ the downstairs bathroom 楼下的浴室 ★ run downstairs 跑下楼 辨析：downstairs / basement：downstairs 泛指下层楼；basement 特指地下室
🧠 助记
down（下）+ stairs（楼梯）→ 走下楼梯 = 楼下
downstairs::/ˌdaʊnˈstɛrz/:adv. 在楼下:He rushed downstairs to answer the door.

dye 谐音「代」—— 用颜色代替原色 = 染色

★ blow a fuse 保险丝断；大发雷霆 ★ fuse box 保险丝盒 ★ fuse together 融为一体 辨析：fuse / wire：fuse 是安全保护装置；wire 泛指导线
🧠 助记
fuse 谐音「副死」—— 副业就是保护不被电死 = 保险丝
fuse::/fjuːz/:n. 保险丝:The two companies fused to form a single corporation.

host（主人）+ age → 被主人扣押的人 = 人质
hostage::/ˈhɒstɪdʒ/:n. 人质:Police negotiators worked to secure the release of the hostages.
