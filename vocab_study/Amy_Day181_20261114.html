1. barren /ˈbærən/ adj. 贫瘠的；不育的；无效的
📖 英文解释
Too poor in quality to produce plants or crops; unable to have children; lacking interest or value.
✏️ 例句
The settlers struggled to grow crops on the barren, rocky soil.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ barren land/soil 贫瘠的土地 ★ barren woman 不孕女性（旧用） ★ barren of ideas 毫无新意 辨析：barren / infertile：barren 更强调完全不毛；infertile 更科学，指土壤或人的不育
🧠 助记
barren 谐音「把扔」—— 把种子扔了也没用 = 贫瘠的
barren::/ˈbærən/:adj. 贫瘠的:The vast barren desert stretched endlessly in all directions.
2. basin /ˈbeɪsn/ n. 盆地；流域；脸盆；水盆
📖 英文解释
A large bowl-shaped area of land; the area drained by a river; a bowl for water.
✏️ 例句
The Amazon Basin is one of the most biodiverse regions on Earth.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ river basin 河流流域 ★ wash basin 洗手盆 ★ the Pacific Basin 太平洋地区 辨析：basin / bowl：basin 更大，可指地理区域；bowl 通常指餐具或较小容器
🧠 助记
basin 谐音「盆身」—— 盆的形状 = 盆地/水盆
basin::/ˈbeɪsn/:n. 盆地:Millions of people live in the Yangtze River Basin.
3. bat /bæt/ n. 蝙蝠；球棒；v. 用棒击打
📖 英文解释
A flying mammal with webbed wings that is active at night; a wooden club used in sports.
✏️ 例句
A bat flew out of the dark cave as the hikers approached.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ baseball bat 棒球棒 ★ bat an eye 眨眼 ★ blind as a bat 近乎失明 辨析：bat / racket：bat 是实心木棒；racket 是有网框的球拍
🧠 助记
bat 谐音「爸踢」—— 爸爸踢开了蝙蝠 = 蝙蝠/球棒
bat::/bæt/:n. 蝙蝠:The bat swooped silently through the night air catching insects.
4. beast /biːst/ n. 野兽；残暴的人；令人讨厌的事
📖 英文解释
A large, dangerous, or wild animal; a cruel or disgusting person or thing.
✏️ 例句
The beast lurked in the shadows of the forest, waiting for prey.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ wild beast 野兽 ★ beast of burden 役畜 ★ the beast in sb 某人内心的兽性 辨析：beast / animal：beast 含贬义，强调野性危险；animal 中性
🧠 助记
beast 谐音「必死」—— 见到野兽必死无疑 = 野兽
beast::/biːst/:n. 野兽:The mythological beast was said to devour villages.
5. brass /brɑːs/ n. 黄铜；铜管乐器；厚颜无耻；adj. 黄铜制的
📖 英文解释
An alloy of copper and zinc with a yellow color; the brass section of an orchestra; boldness.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ brass instrument 铜管乐器 ★ brass tacks 实质问题（get down to brass tacks） ★ bold as brass 厚颜无耻 辨析：brass / bronze：brass 铜锌合金（黄铜）；bronze 铜锡合金（青铜）
🧠 助记
brass 谐音「不拉死」—— 金属结实不会拉断 = 黄铜
brass::/brɑːs/:n. 黄铜:The brass band played a lively march at the parade.
6. breach /briːtʃ/ n. 违反；缺口；破裂；v. 违反；突破
📖 英文解释
An act of breaking a rule, law, or agreement; a gap in a wall or barrier; to make a gap in.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ breach of contract/trust 违反合同/背信 ★ breach of security 安全漏洞 ★ step into the breach 挺身而出 辨析：breach / violation：breach 更常用于合同、法律层面；violation 用于规则、权利
🧠 助记
breach 谐音「不理齐」—— 不遵守规矩 = 违反；缺口不整齐 = 缺口
breach::/briːtʃ/:n. 违反:A data breach exposed millions of users' personal information.
7. broom /bruːm/ n. 扫帚；金雀花

A long-handled brush used for sweeping floors; a shrub with yellow flowers.

★ broom closet 扫帚柜 ★ sweep with a broom 用扫帚扫 ★ new broom 新官（new broom sweeps clean） 辨析：broom / mop：broom 扫干燥地面；mop 拖湿地板
🧠 助记
broom 谐音「扫扫」的形象 —— 伸出手（br）扫（oom）= 扫帚
broom::/bruːm/:n. 扫帚:He used a broom to sweep the kitchen floor after dinner.

canvas 谐音「看画死」—— 在布上画画看 = 画布/帆布
canvas::/ˈkænvəs/:n. 帆布:The painter stretched the canvas over the wooden frame.

19. diameter /daɪˈæmɪtər/ n. 直径

A straight line passing from side to side through the centre of a circle or sphere.
✏️ 例句

★ in diameter 直径为… ★ inner/outer diameter 内/外径 ★ diameter of a circle 圆的直径 辨析：diameter / radius：diameter 是直径；radius 是半径（直径的一半）

diameter::/daɪˈæmɪtər/:n. 直径:The circle has a diameter of ten centimetres.
