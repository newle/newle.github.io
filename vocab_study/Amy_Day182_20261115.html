1. fluent /ˈfluːənt/ adj. 流利的；流畅的；口若悬河的
📖 英文解释
Able to express oneself easily and articulately in a language; smooth and flowing.
✏️ 例句
After three years of study, she became fluent in Mandarin and could hold complex conversations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ fluent in a language 语言流利 ★ speak fluent English 说流利英语 ★ fluent writing style 流畅的写作风格 辨析：fluent / proficient：fluent 强调表达流畅自然；proficient 强调技能熟练精通
🧠 助记
flu（流）+ ent → 语言像流水一样 = 流利的
fluent::/ˈfluːənt/:adj. 流利的:She is fluent in three languages, including French and Spanish.
2. friction /ˈfrɪkʃn/ n. 摩擦；摩擦力；冲突；不和
📖 英文解释
The resistance encountered when one object moves over another; conflict or disagreement.
✏️ 例句
The friction between the two tires and the road brought the car to a stop.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ reduce friction 减少摩擦 ★ cause friction 引起摩擦/冲突 ★ friction between colleagues 同事间的矛盾 辨析：friction / conflict：friction 可指物理摩擦或轻微人际矛盾；conflict 指更激烈的冲突
🧠 助记
fric（摩）+ tion → 摩擦动作 = 摩擦力
friction::/ˈfrɪkʃn/:n. 摩擦:Friction between the two departments slowed decision-making.
3. frost /frɒst/ n. 霜；严寒；v. 结霜；使结霜
📖 英文解释
Ice crystals that form on surfaces when temperatures fall below freezing; very cold weather.
✏️ 例句
A thick layer of frost covered the windshield on the cold winter morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ heavy/light frost 大/小霜冻 ★ frost damage 霜冻损害 ★ Jack Frost 霜寒拟人化 辨析：frost / ice：frost 是水汽凝华为冰晶；ice 是液态水冻结
🧠 助记
frost 谐音「弗洛斯特」—— 冰霜冰天雪地 = 霜
frost::/frɒst/:n. 霜:The frost killed most of the flowers in the garden overnight.
4. glacier /ˈɡleɪsiər/ n. 冰川；冰河
📖 英文解释
A slowly moving mass of ice formed from compressed snow, found in mountain valleys or polar regions.
✏️ 例句
Climate change is causing glaciers around the world to melt at an alarming rate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ glacier retreat 冰川退缩 ★ glacier melt 冰川融化 ★ polar glacier 极地冰川 辨析：glacier / iceberg：glacier 是陆地上的冰川；iceberg 是漂浮于海中的冰山
🧠 助记
glac（冰，拉丁语）+ ier → 冰的体 = 冰川
glacier::/ˈɡleɪsiər/:n. 冰川:The glacier has retreated by several kilometres over the past century.
5. glorious /ˈɡlɔːriəs/ adj. 辉煌的；壮丽的；荣耀的
📖 英文解释
Having, worthy of, or bringing fame or admiration; strikingly beautiful or impressive.
✏️ 例句
The tourists watched in awe as the sun set in a glorious display of orange and pink.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a glorious victory 辉煌胜利 ★ glorious weather 绝佳天气 ★ glorious past 光辉历史 辨析：glorious / magnificent：glorious 侧重荣耀光辉；magnificent 侧重宏大壮观
🧠 助记
glory（荣耀）+ ous → 充满荣耀的 = 辉煌的
glorious::/ˈɡlɔːriəs/:adj. 辉煌的:The team celebrated their glorious championship victory.
6. grim /ɡrɪm/ adj. 可怕的；冷酷的；严峻的；阴沉的
📖 英文解释
Stern and forbidding in appearance; dreary and depressing; very serious.
✏️ 例句
The doctor's grim expression told her that the news was not going to be good.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ grim reality 严酷现实 ★ grim-faced 面无表情的 ★ a grim situation 严峻形势 辨析：grim / stern：grim 带有可怕或令人沮丧的色彩；stern 更强调严厉、不苟言笑
🧠 助记
grim 谐音「死人」—— 像死人一样阴沉 = 可怕的/冷酷的
grim::/ɡrɪm/:adj. 可怕的:The economic outlook remains grim for many families.
7. grocer /ˈɡroʊsər/ n. 食品杂货商；杂货店
📖 英文解释
A person who sells food and small household goods; a grocery store.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ local grocer 当地杂货店 ★ grocer's shop 食品杂货店 ★ grocery shopping 买杂货 辨析：grocer / supermarket：grocer 通常指小型独立食品店；supermarket 是大型连锁超市
🧠 助记
gross（大批）+ er → 批量卖食品的人 = 杂货商
grocer::/ˈɡroʊsər/:n. 食品杂货商:The grocer recommended the freshest seasonal produce.
8. grope /ɡroʊp/ v. 摸索；探索；猥亵触摸

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ grope for sth 摸索找东西 ★ grope in the dark 在黑暗中摸索；茫无头绪 ★ grope toward a solution 努力寻找解决方案 辨析：grope / feel：grope 强调在黑暗或不确定中摸索；feel 更泛指触摸感觉
🧠 助记
grope 谐音「摸破」—— 摸到破了才找到 = 摸索
grope::/ɡroʊp/:v. 摸索:He groped for his phone in the dark to check the time.
9. hinge /hɪndʒ/ n. 铰链；关键点；v. 依…而定

hinge 谐音「恨结」—— 连接两扇门的结 = 铰链
hinge::/hɪndʒ/:n. 铰链:The success of the plan hinges on getting enough funding.
