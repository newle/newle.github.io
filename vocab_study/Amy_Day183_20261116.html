1. petty /ˈpeti/ adj. 小的；琐碎的；心胸狭窄的
📖 英文解释
of little importance; trivial, or giving too much attention to small details.
✏️ 例句
She was annoyed by his petty complaints about the office temperature.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ petty crime 轻罪、小罪 ★ petty cash 零用金 petty-minded 心胸狭窄的 常考：表示"琐碎的、不重要的"，与 trivial 近义
🧠 助记
pet（宠物）+ ty → 宠物的事都是小事 → 琐碎的、小的
petty::/ˈpeti/:adj. 小的:He made a petty excuse for being late.
2. pickup /ˈpɪkʌp/ n. 皮卡车；好转；拾取 v. 捡起；接人
📖 英文解释
a small truck with an open back, or the act of collecting someone or something.
✏️ 例句
The farmer loaded his tools onto the old pickup truck.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pickup truck 皮卡车 pick up 捡起；好转；接送 常考短语：things are picking up 情况好转
🧠 助记
pick（捡）+ up（起来）→ 捡起来 → 皮卡车（用来装载捡起的货物）
pickup::/ˈpɪkʌp/:n. 皮卡车:She drove a pickup truck to the farm.
3. pillar /ˈpɪlə(r)/ n. 柱子；栋梁；支柱
📖 英文解释
a tall vertical structure used as a support in a building, or a person who is important to a community.
✏️ 例句
The ancient temple was supported by massive stone pillars.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a pillar of society 社会栋梁 ★ pillar of strength 精神支柱 常考：既指建筑柱子，也比喻"支柱人物"
🧠 助记
pill（药片）+ ar → 药片竖起来像柱子 → 柱子
pillar::/ˈpɪlə(r)/:n. 柱子:He is a pillar of the local community.
4. plural /ˈplʊərəl/ adj. 复数的；多元的 n. 复数
📖 英文解释
relating to a word form that refers to more than one person or thing.
✏️ 例句
The plural of 'mouse' is 'mice' in English.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ plural form 复数形式 plural society 多元社会 常考：语法术语，singular（单数）的反义词
🧠 助记
plur（源自 plus 多）+ al → 多个的 → 复数的
plural::/ˈplʊərəl/:adj. 复数的:The plural of 'child' is 'children'.
5. porch /pɔːtʃ/ n. 门廊；走廊
📖 英文解释
a covered shelter at the entrance to a building.
✏️ 例句
They sat on the porch watching the sunset over the mountains.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ front porch 前廊 back porch 后廊 常考：美式英语中常指住宅门廊，考阅读时注意场景
🧠 助记
porch 谐音"铺车"→ 门口铺着的地方 → 门廊
porch::/pɔːtʃ/:n. 门廊:We relaxed on the porch after dinner.
6. preclude /prɪˈkluːd/ v. 妨碍；排除；阻止
📖 英文解释
to prevent something from happening or someone from doing something.
✏️ 例句
His injury precluded him from participating in the competition.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ preclude sb from doing sth 阻止某人做某事 ★ 常用于正式语境，近义词：prevent, prohibit 考研高频：表示"事先阻止"
🧠 助记
pre（预先）+ clude（关闭，同 close）→ 预先关闭 → 妨碍、阻止
preclude::/prɪˈkluːd/:v. 妨碍:Bad weather precluded any outdoor activities.
7. pretext /ˈpriːtekst/ n. 借口；托辞
📖 英文解释
a false reason given to justify an action, used to hide the real reason.
✏️ 例句
He used the meeting as a pretext to avoid doing his homework.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ under the pretext of 以...为借口 ★ on the pretext that 以...为借口 常考：与 excuse 区别，pretext 更强调"虚假的"借口
🧠 助记
pre（在前）+ text（文字）→ 写在前面掩盖真相的文字 → 借口
pretext::/ˈpriːtekst/:n. 借口:She left early under the pretext of feeling ill.
8. rabbit /ˈræbɪt/ n. 兔子；家兔
📖 英文解释
a small furry mammal with long ears that lives in burrows.
✏️ 例句
The rabbit nibbled quietly on the fresh grass in the garden.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ rabbit hole 兔子洞；比喻陷入复杂局面 bred like rabbits 大量繁殖 常考：考阅读中可能出现比喻用法
🧠 助记
r + abbit → 想象一只兔子（rabbit）跑（run）快如闪电
rabbit::/ˈræbɪt/:n. 兔子:The rabbit hopped across the meadow.
9. rack /ræk/ n. 架子；搁架 v. 使痛苦；折磨
📖 英文解释
a frame or shelf for holding things, or to cause great pain or worry.
✏️ 例句
She was racked with guilt after lying to her friend.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ rack one's brain 绞尽脑汁 ★ coat rack 衣架 ★ rack and ruin 破坏、毁灭 常考：动词"折磨"用法高频
🧠 助记
rack 谐音"撕裂克"→ 撕裂般的折磨 → 使痛苦
rack::/ræk/:n. 架子:He racked his brain trying to remember the answer.
10. rash /ræʃ/ adj. 轻率的；鲁莽的 n. 皮疹
📖 英文解释
acting too quickly without thinking carefully; also a skin condition causing red spots.
✏️ 例句
It was rash of him to quit his job without finding another one first.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a rash decision 草率的决定 a rash of incidents 一连串事件 常考：形容词"鲁莽的"是考研重点
🧠 助记
rash 谐音"惹事"→ 鲁莽惹事 → 轻率的
rash::/ræʃ/:adj. 轻率的:Making a rash promise can lead to trouble.
11. remnant /ˈremnənt/ n. 残余；遗留物；残余部分
📖 英文解释
a small part that is left after the rest has been used, removed, or destroyed.
✏️ 例句
Only a few remnants of the ancient civilization survive today.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ remnants of 遗留物、残余 常考：与 remains, relic 近义，注意用法区别 常用于历史、考古语境
🧠 助记
remain（剩余）的变形 → remnant → 残余物
remnant::/ˈremnənt/:n. 残余:The remnants of the old wall were still visible.
12. reproach /rɪˈprəʊtʃ/ v./n. 责备；指责；批评
📖 英文解释
to express disapproval or disappointment towards someone for their behavior.
✏️ 例句
She reproached herself for not calling her parents more often.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ reproach sb for sth 因某事责备某人 ★ beyond reproach 无可指责的 常考：比 blame 更正式，常见于书面语
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ proach（接近）→ 再次靠近质问 → 责备
reproach::/rɪˈprəʊtʃ/:v./n. 责备:His conduct was beyond reproach.
13. restless /ˈrestləs/ adj. 坐立不安的；烦躁的；不安分的
📖 英文解释
unable to relax or stay still, often due to anxiety or boredom.
✏️ 例句
The children grew restless waiting for the movie to begin.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ grow/become restless 变得烦躁 restless night 辗转难眠的夜晚 常考：rest（休息）+ less（无）→ 无法休息的
🧠 助记
rest（休息）+ less（没有）→ 没法休息 → 坐立不安
restless::/ˈrestləs/:adj. 坐立不安的:She felt restless and unable to sleep.
14. revolt /rɪˈvəʊlt/ v./n. 反抗；起义；使厌恶
📖 英文解释
to take violent action against a government or authority, or to feel strong disgust.
✏️ 例句
The workers revolted against the unfair working conditions in the factory.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ revolt against 反抗、起义 be revolting 令人恶心的 常考：与 rebel, uprising 近义；也可表示"使厌恶"
🧠 助记
re（反）+ volt（伏特，电力）→ 反向通电 → 反抗、起义
revolt::/rɪˈvəʊlt/:v./n. 反抗:The people revolted against the corrupt government.
15. ripe /raɪp/ adj. 成熟的；时机成熟的
📖 英文解释
fully developed and ready to be eaten; also means the right time for something.
✏️ 例句
The time is ripe for economic reform in the country.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ ripe for 时机成熟，适合于... ★ ripe old age 高龄 常考：比喻用法"ripe for change"在考研阅读高频
🧠 助记
ripe 谐音"赖普"→ 苹果赖在树上不掉，熟透了 → 成熟的
ripe::/raɪp/:adj. 成熟的:The apples were ripe and ready to pick.
16. robe /rəʊb/ n. 长袍；礼服；浴袍

a long loose garment worn as a formal or ceremonial dress.

The judge walked into court wearing his traditional black robe.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
robe 谐音"罗布"→ 罗布林卡（西藏宫殿）里穿长袍 → 长袍
robe::/rəʊb/:n. 长袍:The priest wore a white robe during the ceremony.
17. sailor /ˈseɪlə(r)/ n. 水手；海员；船员
📖 英文解释
a person who works on a ship or who sails as a sport.
✏️ 例句
The old sailor had spent decades at sea before retiring.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ sailor suit 水手服 常考：与 seaman 近义，注意区分 sailor（普通船员）和 captain（船长）
🧠 助记
sail（航行）+ or（人）→ 航行的人 → 水手
sailor::/ˈseɪlə(r)/:n. 水手:The sailor navigated the ship through the storm.
18. saint /seɪnt/ n. 圣人；圣徒；品德高尚的人
📖 英文解释
a person officially recognized by the church as holy, or someone of great virtue.
✏️ 例句
She was known as a saint for her selfless work helping the poor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ patron saint 守护圣人 ★ be no saint 并不是完人 常考：Saint 常缩写为 St.（地名前缀）
🧠 助记
saint 谐音"圣特"→ 圣人有特别的品德 → 圣人
saint::/seɪnt/:n. 圣人:She was practically a saint for her charitable work.
19. sceptical /ˈskeptɪkl/ adj. 怀疑的；持怀疑态度的
📖 英文解释
not easily convinced; having doubts about something.
✏️ 例句
Many scientists are sceptical about the effectiveness of the new drug.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be sceptical about/of 对...持怀疑态度 ★ scepticism n. 怀疑主义 常考：英式拼写（skeptical 为美式），考研倾向英式
🧠 助记
scept（源自希腊语 skeptomai 观察）→ 仔细观察而产生怀疑 → 怀疑的
sceptical::/ˈskeptɪkl/:adj. 怀疑的:He was sceptical about the politician's promises.
20. seventeen /ˌsevnˈtiːn/ num. 十七
📖 英文解释
the number 17.
✏️ 例句
She started learning the piano at the age of seventeen.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at seventeen 在十七岁时 常考：数词用法，注意 seventeen 和 seventy 的区别
🧠 助记
seven（7）+ teen（十几）→ 7+10 = 17 → 十七
seventeen::/ˌsevnˈtiːn/:num. 十七:There are seventeen students in the class.
21. shiver /ˈʃɪvə(r)/ v./n. 颤抖；战栗；发抖
📖 英文解释
to shake slightly because of cold or fear.
✏️ 例句
She shivered in the cold wind as she waited for the bus.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ shiver with cold/fear 因寒冷/恐惧而颤抖 shivers down one's spine 令人不寒而栗 常考：与 tremble, quiver 近义
🧠 助记
shiver 谐音"是哦我"→ 寒冷时说"是哦我"（发抖）→ 颤抖
shiver::/ˈʃɪvə(r)/:v./n. 颤抖:The thought of the exam made him shiver.
22. smuggle /ˈsmʌɡl/ v. 走私；偷运
📖 英文解释
to move goods or people secretly and illegally across a border.
✏️ 例句
Customs officers discovered drugs being smuggled into the country.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ smuggle goods 走私货物 smuggler n. 走私者 常考：常与 drugs, weapons 搭配，常见于法律/社会话题
🧠 助记
smug（自鸣得意）+ gle → 走私者自鸣得意地偷运货物 → 走私
smuggle::/ˈsmʌɡl/:v. 走私:He was arrested for trying to smuggle diamonds.
23. sneeze /sniːz/ v./n. 打喷嚏
📖 英文解释
to suddenly expel air from the nose and mouth due to irritation.
✏️ 例句
He sneezed so loudly that everyone in the library looked up.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ not to be sneezed at 不可小看 常考：习语 not to be sneezed at 表示"不可忽视"是考研考点
🧠 助记
sneeze 谐音"死你子"→ 打喷嚏的声音很响 → 打喷嚏
sneeze::/sniːz/:v./n. 打喷嚏:She sneezed repeatedly due to her allergy.
24. sour /saʊə(r)/ adj. 酸的；变酸的；不愉快的 v. 使变酸；使恶化
📖 英文解释
having an acid taste like lemon; or becoming unpleasant or disagreeable.
✏️ 例句
Their friendship turned sour after the argument over money.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ turn/go sour 变坏、恶化 ★ sour grapes 酸葡萄心理（得不到就说不好） 常考：比喻用法"turn sour"在阅读中高频
🧠 助记
sour 谐音"酸哦"→ 发现变酸了叫"酸哦" → 酸的
sour::/saʊə(r)/:adj. 酸的:The milk had gone sour in the heat.
25. specimen /ˈspesɪmən/ n. 样本；标本；样品
📖 英文解释
a sample of something used for scientific testing or as an example of its type.
✏️ 例句
The doctor sent a blood specimen to the laboratory for analysis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ blood/urine specimen 血液/尿液样本 ★ a fine specimen of 优秀的典型例子 常考：科学/医学语境高频词
🧠 助记
speci（种类）+ men（人）→ 代表这种类型的人/物 → 标本、样本
specimen::/ˈspesɪmən/:n. 样本:The museum displayed rare insect specimens.
