1. spoon /spuːn/ n. 勺子；汤匙 v. 用勺舀
📖 英文解释
a utensil with a round bowl and a long handle used for eating or serving food.
✏️ 例句
She stirred the soup slowly with a wooden spoon.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ born with a silver spoon 含着金汤匙出生（出身富贵） spoon-feed 填鸭式教育 常考：习语 silver spoon 在阅读中有所考查
🧠 助记
spoon 形状像碗（bowl）加把手 → 想象舀汤的勺子
spoon::/spuːn/:n. 勺子:He used a spoon to eat the yogurt.
2. stagnant /ˈstæɡnənt/ adj. 停滞的；不流动的；死气沉沉的
📖 英文解释
not flowing or changing; showing no activity or development.
✏️ 例句
The economy remained stagnant for several years after the crisis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ stagnant economy 停滞的经济 ★ stagnant water 死水 stagnate v. 停滞不前 常考：经济类文章高频词
🧠 助记
stag（雄鹿停在原地）+ nant → 停滞不动 → 停滞的
stagnant::/ˈstæɡnənt/:adj. 停滞的:Stagnant wages have led to social unrest.
3. stitch /stɪtʃ/ n. 针脚；缝线；一针 v. 缝；缝合
📖 英文解释
a movement of a needle and thread through fabric, or a loop of yarn in knitting.
✏️ 例句
The doctor used ten stitches to close the wound on his arm.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a stitch in time saves nine 及时一针省九针（防患于未然） stitch up 缝合；（口语）欺骗 常考：谚语考点
🧠 助记
stitch 谐音"死揪"→ 死死揪住布料缝 → 缝合
stitch::/stɪtʃ/:n. 针脚:She stitched the torn fabric back together.
4. stoop /stuːp/ v. 弯腰；屈尊；堕落 n. 弯腰姿势
📖 英文解释
to bend the body forwards and downwards; to lower one's moral standards.
✏️ 例句
I never thought he would stoop to cheating on the exam.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ stoop to doing sth 屈尊去做（贬义） ★ stoop down 弯腰俯身 常考：比喻用法"stoop to"表示道德堕落高频考查
🧠 助记
stoop 谐音"低头"→ 低头弯腰 → 弯腰；屈尊
stoop::/stuːp/:v. 弯腰:He stooped to pick up the fallen book.
5. submarine /ˌsʌbməˈriːn/ n. 潜水艇 adj. 海底的
📖 英文解释
a vessel designed to operate underwater, used in naval warfare or research.
✏️ 例句
The nuclear submarine can remain underwater for months at a time.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ submarine cable 海底电缆 submarine warfare 潜艇战 常考：sub（在下面）+ marine（海洋）→ 海底的
🧠 助记
sub（在下面）+ marine（海洋）→ 在海洋下面 → 潜水艇
submarine::/ˌsʌbməˈriːn/:n. 潜水艇 adj. 海底的:The submarine dived to avoid detection.
6. subtract /səbˈtrækt/ v. 减去；扣除
📖 英文解释
to take a number or amount away from another number or total.
✏️ 例句
If you subtract 15 from 40, you get 25.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ subtract A from B 从B中减去A subtraction n. 减法 常考：与 deduct 近义，注意区分 subtract（数学减法）
🧠 助记
sub（在下面减少）+ tract（拉）→ 往下拉减少 → 减去
subtract::/səbˈtrækt/:v. 减去:Subtract the discount from the total price.
7. suffice /səˈfaɪs/ v. 足够；满足需要
📖 英文解释
to be enough or adequate for a particular purpose.
✏️ 例句
A brief explanation will suffice; there is no need for lengthy detail.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ suffice to do sth 足以做某事 ★ suffice it to say 只需说；总而言之 常考：正式用语，近义词 be sufficient
🧠 助记
suf（足够）+ fice（做）→ 做得足够 → 足够
suffice::/səˈfaɪs/:v. 足够:One example will suffice to illustrate the point.
8. thrust /θrʌst/ v. 猛推；刺入 n. 推力；要点
📖 英文解释
to push suddenly and forcefully; also the main point of an argument.
✏️ 例句
The main thrust of his argument was that education needs more funding.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ thrust of an argument 论点的核心 ★ thrust into 强行推入 thrust sb into the spotlight 使某人成为焦点 常考："thrust of"考查论点中心思想
🧠 助记
thrust 谐音"投入死"→ 猛力投入进去 → 猛推；刺入
thrust::/θrʌst/:v. 猛推:She thrust the letter into his hand.
9. torment /ˈtɔːment/ n./v. 折磨；痛苦；苦恼
📖 英文解释
severe physical or mental suffering; to cause great suffering to someone.
✏️ 例句
The loss of his son was a torment he could not escape.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in torment 在痛苦之中 be tormented by 被...折磨 常考：与 torture, agony 近义
🧠 助记
tor（扭曲）+ ment（状态）→ 被扭曲折磨的状态 → 折磨
torment::/ˈtɔːment/:n./v. 折磨:She was tormented by feelings of guilt.
10. torrent /ˈtɒrənt/ n. 急流；洪流；大量
📖 英文解释
a strong, fast-moving flow of water or a large quantity of something.
✏️ 例句
A torrent of criticism followed the politician's controversial speech.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a torrent of 大量的（水、批评等） torrential rain 倾盆大雨 常考：比喻用法"a torrent of criticism/abuse"
🧠 助记
torrent 谐音"涛浪特"→ 涛浪特别急 → 急流、洪流
torrent::/ˈtɒrənt/:n. 急流:A torrent of water flooded the streets.
11. trunk /trʌŋk/ n. 树干；躯干；象鼻；行李箱；车尾箱
📖 英文解释
the main stem of a tree, or the main body of a person or animal, or a large box for luggage.
✏️ 例句
She put the suitcases in the trunk of the car before the trip.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ trunk of a tree 树干 ★ car trunk 汽车后备箱 bathing trunks 游泳短裤 常考：一词多义，需结合语境判断
🧠 助记
trunk 想象大象的鼻子（trunk）像树干（trunk），也像行李箱的形状
trunk::/trʌŋk/:n. 树干:The car trunk was full of camping equipment.
12. vicinity /vɪˈsɪnəti/ n. 附近；邻近地区
📖 英文解释
the area near or surrounding a particular place.
✏️ 例句
There are several good restaurants in the vicinity of the hotel.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in the vicinity of 在...附近；大约（数字前） 常考：in the vicinity of + 数字表示"大约"是考研常考点
🧠 助记
vicin（邻近，同 vicinity）+ ity → 邻近的地方 → 附近
vicinity::/vɪˈsɪnəti/:n. 附近:Police searched the vicinity for clues.
13. violet /ˈvaɪələt/ n. 紫罗兰；紫色 adj. 紫色的

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ violet light 紫色光 ultraviolet 紫外线（ultra + violet） 常考：物理/光学语境，紫外线 ultraviolet
🧠 助记
violet → 想象紫罗兰（violet）花朵是紫色的（violet color）
violet::/ˈvaɪələt/:n. 紫罗兰:She wore a violet dress to the party.
14. wagon /ˈwæɡən/ n. 四轮马车；货车；手推车
📖 英文解释
a vehicle with four wheels pulled by horses or an engine, used for carrying goods.
✏️ 例句
The pioneers traveled west in covered wagons across the plains.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on the wagon 戒酒中 station wagon 旅行车（美式） 常考：习语 on the wagon（戒酒）
🧠 助记
wagon 谐音"外跟"→ 跟在外面走的有轮子的车 → 马车、货车
wagon::/ˈwæɡən/:n. 四轮马车:The farmer used a wagon to carry hay.
15. yawn /jɔːn/ v./n. 打哈欠；令人厌倦
📖 英文解释
to open the mouth wide and breathe in deeply due to tiredness or boredom.
✏️ 例句
She tried to suppress a yawn during the long, boring lecture.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ yawn with boredom 无聊地打哈欠 a yawn 令人厌倦的事 常考：比喻用法表示无聊的事情
🧠 助记
yawn 谐音"哟嗯"→ 困了发出"哟嗯"的声音 → 打哈欠
yawn::/jɔːn/:v./n. 打哈欠:He yawned and stretched after waking up.
16. fume /fjuːm/ n. 烟雾；废气 v. 冒烟；气愤
📖 英文解释
strong-smelling smoke, gas, or vapor; also to feel very angry.
✏️ 例句
The workers were exposed to toxic fumes from the chemical plant.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ exhaust fumes 废气 ★ fume with anger 怒气冲冲 常考：环境污染语境高频，注意动词"愤怒"义
🧠 助记
fume 谐音"烦么"→ 冒烟烦死了 → 烟雾；发怒
fume::/fjuːm/:n. 烟雾:Factory fumes polluted the surrounding air.
17. parachute /ˈpærəʃuːt/ n. 降落伞 v. 跳伞；空降
📖 英文解释
a device that allows a person to fall slowly from an aircraft.
✏️ 例句
The soldiers parachuted into the enemy territory at midnight.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ parachute into 空降进入 golden parachute 黄金降落伞（高管离职补偿） 常考：商业语境中"golden parachute"
🧠 助记
para（防止）+ chute（滑落）→ 防止急速下落 → 降落伞
parachute::/ˈpærəʃuːt/:n. 降落伞 v. 跳伞:The pilot ejected and used a parachute to land safely.
18. superfluous /suːˈpɜːfluəs/ adj. 多余的；过剩的；不必要的
📖 英文解释
more than is needed or wanted; unnecessary.
✏️ 例句
The editor removed all superfluous words to make the article more concise.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ superfluous details 多余的细节 常考：super（超过）+ flu（流）+ ous → 超过所需而流出 → 多余的 近义词：redundant, excessive
🧠 助记
super（超过）+ flu（流动）+ ous → 超过需要量往外溢出 → 多余的
superfluous::/suːˈpɜːfluəs/:adj. 多余的:Her explanation was full of superfluous information.
19. turbine /ˈtɜːbaɪn/ n. 涡轮机；汽轮机
📖 英文解释
a machine with a rotor that is turned by a flow of water, steam, or gas.
✏️ 例句
Wind turbines convert wind energy into electricity on a large scale.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ wind turbine 风力涡轮机 steam turbine 蒸汽轮机 常考：能源/环境类话题高频词
🧠 助记
turb（旋转，同 turbulent）+ ine → 旋转的机器 → 涡轮机
turbine::/ˈtɜːbaɪn/:n. 涡轮机:The power plant uses large turbines to generate electricity.
20. aerial /ˈeəriəl/ adj. 空中的；航空的 n. 天线
📖 英文解释
relating to the air or aircraft; also a rod or wire for receiving radio or TV signals.
✏️ 例句
The aerial photograph revealed the extent of the flood damage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ aerial photography 航拍 ★ aerial view 鸟瞰图 TV aerial 电视天线 常考：aerial photography 在地理/科技类阅读高频
🧠 助记
aer（空气）+ ial → 空气中的 → 空中的；天线（从空中接收信号）
aerial::/ˈeəriəl/:adj. 空中的:The aerial view of the city was breathtaking.
21. brow /braʊ/ n. 眉毛；前额；眉头
📖 英文解释
the forehead or the ridge above the eye.
✏️ 例句
He furrowed his brow in concentration while reading the difficult text.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ furrow one's brow 皱眉 ★ highbrow/lowbrow 高雅的/通俗的 常考：highbrow 文化品味高雅，是考研热词
🧠 助记
brow 谐音"包"→ 眉毛像包住眼睛上方的带子 → 眉毛、前额
brow::/braʊ/:n. 眉毛:She wiped the sweat from her brow.
22. defer /dɪˈfɜː(r)/ v. 推迟；顺从；听从
📖 英文解释
to delay something to a later time; or to yield to someone else's opinion.
✏️ 例句
The board decided to defer the decision until more information was available.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ defer to sb 听从某人意见 ★ defer a decision/payment 推迟决定/付款 常考：两种含义都重要，注意语境区分
🧠 助记
de（向下）+ fer（带来）→ 把事情向后推 → 推迟；顺从
defer::/dɪˈfɜː(r)/:v. 推迟:She deferred to her mentor's judgment on the matter.
23. flare /fleə(r)/ v. 突然燃烧；突然爆发 n. 闪光；耀眼的光；信号弹
📖 英文解释
to burn suddenly and brightly; a sudden burst of flame or light used as a signal.
✏️ 例句
Tempers flared during the heated debate on immigration policy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ flare up 突然发火；（病情）复发 ★ a flare for drama 对戏剧有天赋（注：此处应是 flair） 常考：flare up 表示"爆发、恶化"高频
🧠 助记
flare → 像火焰（flame）突然闪耀（glare）→ 突然燃烧
flare::/fleə(r)/:v. 突然燃烧:The dispute flared up again without warning.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ humiliate sb in public 公开羞辱某人 humiliating adj. 令人羞耻的 humiliation n. 羞辱 常考：社会/心理类话题词
🧠 助记
humili（低下，同 humble）+ ate → 让人低下头 → 羞辱
humiliate::/hjuːˈmɪlieɪt/:v. 羞辱:She felt humiliated by his cruel remarks.
