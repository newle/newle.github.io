1. massacre /ˈmæsəkə(r)/ n./v. 大屠杀；屠杀
📖 英文解释
the killing of a large number of people, especially in a cruel and unjust way.
✏️ 例句
The massacre of civilians during the war was condemned by the international community.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ commit a massacre 实施大屠杀 the Nanjing Massacre 南京大屠杀 常考：历史/人权类话题词
🧠 助记
mass（大量）+ acre（英亩，大面积）→ 大范围的杀戮 → 大屠杀
massacre::/ˈmæsəkə(r)/:n./v. 大屠杀:The massacre shocked the entire nation.
2. monotonous /məˈnɒtənəs/ adj. 单调的；乏味的；无变化的
📖 英文解释
lacking variety and interest; tediously repetitive.
✏️ 例句
The monotonous hum of the machines made it hard to concentrate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ monotonous work 单调的工作 ★ a monotonous voice 单调的声音 monotony n. 单调 常考：mono（单一）+ tone（音调）→ 单一音调 → 单调的
🧠 助记
mono（单一）+ tone（音调）+ ous → 只有一种音调 → 单调乏味的
monotonous::/məˈnɒtənəs/:adj. 单调的:She found the repetitive task monotonous and draining.
3. rejoice /rɪˈdʒɔɪs/ v. 欢喜；庆贺；感到高兴
📖 英文解释
to feel or show great happiness about something.
✏️ 例句
The whole nation rejoiced when the team won the championship.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ rejoice in/at sth 为某事感到高兴 ★ rejoice over 庆贺 常考：正式用语，近义词 celebrate, delight
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ joice（joy 快乐）→ 再次感到快乐 → 欢喜
rejoice::/rɪˈdʒɔɪs/:v. 欢喜:They rejoiced at the birth of their first child.
4. sly /slaɪ/ adj. 狡猾的；诡计多端的；偷偷的
📖 英文解释
acting in a clever, secretive way to get what you want; cunning.
✏️ 例句
With a sly smile, he slipped the note under the door.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on the sly 秘密地，偷偷地 ★ sly as a fox 狡猾如狐 常考：近义词 cunning, crafty；注意"偷偷"义
🧠 助记
sly 谐音"斯莱"→ 《哈利波特》斯莱特林学院（机智狡猾）→ 狡猾的
sly::/slaɪ/:adj. 狡猾的:The sly fox outwitted the hunter.
5. speciality /ˌspeʃiˈæləti/ n. 专长；特产；特色菜
📖 英文解释
a particular area of expertise or a food item for which a place is well known.
✏️ 例句
Peking duck is a culinary speciality of Beijing.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ speciality of the house 招牌菜 ★ one's speciality 某人的专长 常考：英式拼写（specialty 为美式）
🧠 助记
special（特别）+ ity → 特别擅长的事物 → 专长、特产
speciality::/ˌspeʃiˈæləti/:n. 专长:Sushi is a regional speciality of Japan.
6. strawberry /ˈstrɔːbəri/ n. 草莓
📖 英文解释
a small, red, sweet fruit with tiny seeds on its surface.
✏️ 例句
She picked fresh strawberries from the garden every morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ strawberry fields 草莓田 strawberry blonde 草莓金发色 常考：水果词汇，注意发音 /ˈstrɔːbəri/（不读 straw）
🧠 助记
straw（稻草）+ berry（浆果）→ 长在稻草地里的浆果 → 草莓
strawberry::/ˈstrɔːbəri/:n. 草莓:Strawberry jam is popular with children.
7. synthesis /ˈsɪnθəsɪs/ n. 综合；合成；综合体
📖 英文解释
the combination of different elements to form a new whole; the production of a substance by chemical reaction.
✏️ 例句
The paper offers a synthesis of research from the past twenty years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ synthesis of information 信息综合 ★ chemical synthesis 化学合成 synthesize v. 综合；合成 常考：学术写作高频词
🧠 助记
syn（一起）+ thesis（论点）→ 把各种论点放在一起 → 综合
synthesis::/ˈsɪnθəsɪs/:n. 综合:The study provides a synthesis of existing theories.
8. throne /θrəʊn/ n. 王位；宝座；王权
📖 英文解释
the ceremonial chair of a king or queen; also the position of being a monarch.
✏️ 例句
The young prince ascended to the throne after his father's death.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ ascend/come to the throne 登基 ★ abdicate the throne 退位 常考：历史/政治类话题词
🧠 助记
throne 谐音"托隆"→ 被人托举登上的龙椅 → 王位
throne::/θrəʊn/:n. 王位:The queen sat on her golden throne.
9. vanity /ˈvænəti/ n. 虚荣；自负；虚无
📖 英文解释
excessive pride in one's appearance or achievements; something with no real value.
✏️ 例句
His vanity prevented him from admitting that he had made a mistake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ out of vanity 出于虚荣 vanity fair 名利场 常考：七宗罪之一，文学作品常见主题
🧠 助记
van（空的）+ ity → 内心空虚却自以为是 → 虚荣
vanity::/ˈvænəti/:n. 虚荣:Her vanity made her spend hours in front of the mirror.
10. velocity /vəˈlɒsəti/ n. 速度；速率（含方向）
📖 英文解释
the speed of something in a given direction; the rate at which something moves.
✏️ 例句
The velocity of the spacecraft increased as it left Earth's atmosphere.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ high velocity 高速 ★ velocity of light 光速 常考：物理学术语，注意与 speed（标量）的区别：velocity 是矢量
🧠 助记
veloc（快速，同 velociraptor 迅猛龙）+ ity → 速度
velocity::/vəˈlɒsəti/:n. 速度:The bullet was fired at high velocity.
11. wedge /wedʒ/ n. 楔子；楔形物 v. 楔入；强行挤入
📖 英文解释
a piece of wood or metal with a thin edge used for splitting or fixing things; to force into a narrow space.
✏️ 例句
She wedged a doorstop under the door to keep it open.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ drive a wedge between 在...之间制造裂痕 wedge issue 楔形议题（分裂选民的政治议题） 常考：比喻用法"drive a wedge"高频
🧠 助记
wedge 形状像三角形的楔子 → 强行挤入的工具 → 楔子
wedge::/wedʒ/:n. 楔子:He drove a wedge between the two friends.
12. allegiance /əˈliːdʒəns/ n. 忠诚；效忠；忠心
📖 英文解释
loyalty and support for a leader, country, or belief.
✏️ 例句
Soldiers pledge their allegiance to their country when they join the military.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pledge allegiance to 宣誓效忠 ★ transfer one's allegiance 改换效忠对象 常考：政治/军事语境
🧠 助记
alle（全部）+ giance（忠诚）→ 全部的忠诚 → 忠诚；效忠
allegiance::/əˈliːdʒəns/:n. 忠诚:Citizens were asked to pledge allegiance to the new government.
13. bronze /brɒnz/ n. 青铜；铜牌 adj. 青铜色的
📖 英文解释
a brown-colored alloy of copper and tin; a medal given for third place.
✏️ 例句
She was disappointed to win bronze when she had been aiming for gold.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ Bronze Age 青铜时代 ★ bronze medal 铜牌 常考：体育赛事和历史考古语境常见
🧠 助记
bronze 谐音"不隆资"→ 不够隆重（铜牌不如金牌）→ 铜牌；青铜
bronze::/brɒnz/:n. 青铜:He won a bronze medal at the Olympic Games.
14. chalk /tʃɔːk/ n. 粉笔；白垩 v. 用粉笔写
📖 英文解释
a soft white rock used for writing or drawing on blackboards.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ chalk and cheese 截然不同的两人/物 ★ chalk up 记录；取得（成绩） 常考：习语 chalk and cheese 表示"截然不同"
🧠 助记
chalk 谐音"超克"→ 用粉笔在黑板上超克（超速书写）→ 粉笔
chalk::/tʃɔːk/:n. 粉笔:She chalked up another victory for her team.
15. commemorate /kəˈmeməreɪt/ v. 纪念；为纪念...而举行仪式
📖 英文解释
to do something special to remember and honor an important person or event.
✏️ 例句
The monument was built to commemorate the soldiers who died in the war.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ commemorate an event 纪念某一事件 commemoration n. 纪念活动 常考：与 memorize（记住）区分，commemorate 指"仪式性纪念"
🧠 助记
com（共同）+ memor（记忆）+ ate → 共同记忆 → 纪念
commemorate::/kəˈmeməreɪt/:v. 纪念:A ceremony was held to commemorate the anniversary.
16. dumb /dʌm/ adj. 哑的；沉默的；愚笨的
📖 英文解释
unable to speak; temporarily silent; or (informal) stupid.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ struck dumb 震惊得说不出话来 ★ dumbfounded 目瞪口呆的 常考：注意区分 deaf-mute（聋哑）和 dumb（哑）
🧠 助记
dumb 谐音"当姆"→ 哑巴像当哑铃一样不说话 → 哑的
dumb::/dʌm/:adj. 哑的:She was struck dumb by the unexpected announcement.
17. elapse /ɪˈlæps/ v. （时间）流逝；过去
📖 英文解释
of time: to pass by.
✏️ 例句
Several years elapsed before he returned to his hometown.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ time elapses 时间流逝 常考：通常主语是 time/years/months 等时间名词 近义：pass, go by
🧠 助记
e（出去）+ lapse（滑落）→ 时间悄悄滑落流过 → 流逝
elapse::/ɪˈlæps/:v. （时间）流逝:Five years elapsed before they met again.

generator::/ˈdʒenəreɪtə(r)/:n. 发电机:The factory installed a new generator to ensure power supply.

idiom::/ˈɪdiəm/:n. 成语:Learning idioms helps you understand native speakers better.

moisture::/ˈmɔɪstʃə(r)/:n. 水分:The cream helps lock in moisture for dry skin.

mortal::/ˈmɔːtl/:adj. 致命的:All mortals must face death eventually.
