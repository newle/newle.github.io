1. plateau /ˈplætəʊ/ n. 高原；平稳阶段 v. 达到平稳期
📖 英文解释
a large flat area of land at a high altitude; a period of little or no change after development.
✏️ 例句
After years of rapid growth, the economy has reached a plateau.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ reach a plateau 达到平稳期 Tibetan Plateau 青藏高原 常考：比喻用法"reach a plateau"在经济/学习类阅读中高频
🧠 助记
plat（平面）+ eau（法语水）→ 高处平坦的地方 → 高原；平稳阶段
plateau::/ˈplætəʊ/:n. 高原:Her performance reached a plateau after initial rapid improvement.
2. pork /pɔːk/ n. 猪肉；政治肥缺
📖 英文解释
the meat from a pig; also political spending used to gain favor with voters.
✏️ 例句
Pork barrel spending has long been criticized as wasteful by reformers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pork barrel 政治分肥（用纳税人的钱换取政治支持） roast pork 烤猪肉 常考：政治语境 pork barrel 是考研常见词
🧠 助记
pork 谐音"破壳"→ 破壳而出的猪 → 猪肉
pork::/pɔːk/:n. 猪肉:Politicians were accused of pork barrel spending.
3. propagate /ˈprɒpəɡeɪt/ v. 传播；繁殖；宣传
📖 英文解释
to spread ideas, beliefs, or knowledge widely; to breed or grow plants.
✏️ 例句
Social media has made it easier to propagate misinformation rapidly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ propagate ideas/beliefs 传播思想/信仰 propagate plants 繁殖植物 常考：媒体/信息类话题高频词
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ pagate（传播）→ 向前传播 → 传播；繁殖
propagate::/ˈprɒpəɡeɪt/:v. 传播:The internet helps propagate information worldwide.
4. puff /pʌf/ v./n. 喷出；抽（烟）；吹气；膨胀
📖 英文解释
a short burst of air, smoke, or wind; to breathe in short bursts; to swell up.
✏️ 例句
He puffed on his pipe while reading the evening newspaper.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ puff up 膨胀；使傲慢 puff pastry 泡芙；酥皮糕点 ★ out of puff 气喘吁吁 常考：注意比喻义"puff up"表示自傲
🧠 助记
puff 模拟吹气的声音 → 吹气；抽烟
puff::/pʌf/:v./n. 喷出:The wind puffed the sails of the boat.
5. reciprocal /rɪˈsɪprəkl/ adj. 相互的；互惠的 n. 倒数
📖 英文解释
involving an equal exchange between two parties; mutual.
✏️ 例句
The two countries signed a reciprocal trade agreement to boost their economies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ reciprocal agreement 互惠协议 ★ reciprocal relationship 互惠关系 常考：外交/贸易语境高频词
🧠 助记
recipro（来回，同 reciprocate 回报）+ cal → 来来去去互相往来 → 相互的
reciprocal::/rɪˈsɪprəkl/:adj. 相互的:They have a reciprocal respect for each other.
6. retrospect /ˈretrəspekt/ n. 回顾；追溯 v. 回顾过去
📖 英文解释
a survey or review of past events or achievements.
✏️ 例句
In retrospect, she realized that the decision had been a mistake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in retrospect 回顾起来；事后看来 retrospective adj. 追溯的；回顾的 常考："in retrospect"是考研必考短语
🧠 助记
retro（向后）+ spect（看）→ 向后看 → 回顾
retrospect::/ˈretrəspekt/:n. 回顾:In retrospect, they should have planned more carefully.
7. rib /rɪb/ n. 肋骨；肋排 v. 取笑；开玩笑
📖 英文解释
one of the curved bones forming the chest; also to tease someone in a friendly way.
✏️ 例句
The doctor confirmed that he had cracked two ribs in the fall.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ rib cage 胸腔 spare ribs 排骨 常考：解剖学词汇；动词"取笑"义需注意
🧠 助记
rib 谐音"肋"→ 肋骨（rib）
rib::/rɪb/:n. 肋骨:She bruised her ribs when she fell off the bike.
8. tan /tæn/ v./n. 晒黑；棕褐色 adj. 棕褐色的
📖 英文解释
to make or become brown by exposure to the sun; a yellowish-brown color.
✏️ 例句
She came back from holiday with a deep tan.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ get a tan 晒黑；晒成古铜色 tan leather 鞣制皮革 常考：颜色词汇；也指日晒变黑
🧠 助记
tan 谐音"滩"→ 在沙滩上晒成古铜色 → 晒黑；棕褐色
tan::/tæn/:v./n. 晒黑:After a week at the beach, she had a golden tan.
9. aggregate /ˈæɡrɪɡət/ n. 总计；集合体 adj. 总计的 v. 聚集；合计
📖 英文解释
a whole formed by combining several separate elements; to combine into a total.
✏️ 例句
The aggregate score of the two matches determined the winner.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in the aggregate 总计；总体上 ★ aggregate demand 总需求 aggregate supply 总供给 常考：经济学术语高频
🧠 助记
aggre（聚合）+ gate（门）→ 聚集在大门口 → 总计；聚集
aggregate::/ˈæɡrɪɡət/:n. 总计:The aggregate profit for the year exceeded expectations.
10. anyhow /ˈenihaʊ/ adv. 无论如何；不管怎样；随便地
📖 英文解释
in spite of what has been said; used to change or end a subject.
✏️ 例句
I don't think it will work, but we can try anyhow.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ anyhow 无论如何（= anyway） 常考：口语/非正式语体；考研阅读中用于转折
🧠 助记
any（任何）+ how（方式）→ 用任何方式 → 无论如何
anyhow::/ˈenihaʊ/:adv. 无论如何:It's risky, but let's proceed anyhow.
11. arch /ɑːtʃ/ n. 拱形；拱门 v. 呈拱形 adj. 主要的；淘气的
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ arch enemy 头号敌人 arch over 弓起；拱起 arch-rival 主要对手 常考：arch- 前缀表"主要的、最重要的"
🧠 助记
arch → 想象拱门（arch）弯曲的弧形 → 拱形；主要的
arch::/ɑːtʃ/:n. 拱形:The arch of the bridge was an engineering marvel.
12. avail /əˈveɪl/ n. 效益；用处 v. 有益于；利用
📖 英文解释
use or benefit; to be of use to someone.
✏️ 例句
All his efforts to persuade her were of no avail.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ of no/little avail 无济于事 ★ to no avail 徒劳；无效 avail oneself of 利用（机会等） 常考：固定短语 to no avail 高频考查
🧠 助记
avail → a（加强）+ vail（价值）→ 有价值；有用
avail::/əˈveɪl/:n. 效益:Her attempts to fix the machine were to no avail.

17. beware /bɪˈweə(r)/ v. 当心；小心；提防

🧠 助记
be（是）+ ware（注意，同 aware）→ 要注意 → 当心
beware::/bɪˈweə(r)/:v. 当心:Beware of falling rocks in the mountain area.
