1. herald /ˈherəld/ n. 先驱；预兆 v. 预示
📖 英文解释
n. a person or thing that signals the approach of something; a harbinger v. to signal the approach of; to be a sign of what is to come
✏️ 例句
The robins herald the arrival of spring every year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
herald + n.（预示某事）；herald of change（变革的先驱）；常见搭配：herald a new era（预示新时代）
🧠 助记
her（她）+ ald（老）→ 老奶奶走在前面领路 → 先驱
herald::/ˈherəld/:n. 先驱:Falling leaves herald the coming of autumn.
2. herb /hɜːb/ n. 药草；香草
📖 英文解释
n. a plant whose leaves, seeds, or flowers are used for flavoring food, medicine, or perfume
✏️ 例句
She added fresh herbs to the soup to enhance the flavor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
herb garden（草药园）；medicinal herb（药草）；常考搭配：herbal medicine（草药）
🧠 助记
herb谐音赫伯 → 赫伯伯的花园种满了药草
herb::/hɜːb/:n. 药草:Basil is a popular herb used in Italian cooking.
3. hoist /hɔɪst/ v. 升起；举起 n. 起重机
📖 英文解释
v. to raise or lift something heavy, especially using a rope or machinery n. a device used for lifting heavy loads
✏️ 例句
The workers hoisted the flag at dawn.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hoist a flag（升旗）；hoist onto（举上）；考研常考义：用机械吊起重物
🧠 助记
hoist谐音好使 → 起重机好使，能把重物升起来
hoist::/hɔɪst/:v. 升起:They hoisted the cargo onto the ship with a crane.
4. hose /həʊz/ n. 水管；软管 v. 用水管冲洗
📖 英文解释
n. a flexible tube for carrying water or other fluids v. to water or wash with a hose
✏️ 例句
The firefighter used the hose to put out the blaze.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
garden hose（花园水管）；hose down（用水管冲洗）；fire hose（消防水管）
🧠 助记
hose谐音喉咙 → 水管像喉咙，水从里面流过
hose::/həʊz/:n. 水管:Please hose down the car before we leave.
5. humidity /hjuːˈmɪdɪti/ n. 湿度；潮湿
📖 英文解释
n. the amount of water vapor present in the air; the degree of moisture in the atmosphere
✏️ 例句
The high humidity in summer makes the heat feel unbearable.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
relative humidity（相对湿度）；humidity level（湿度水平）；常考：tropical humidity（热带湿度）
🧠 助记
humid（潮湿的）+ ity（名词后缀）→ 潮湿的程度 → 湿度
humidity::/hjuːˈmɪdɪti/:n. 湿度:The humidity in the rainforest can reach nearly 100%.
6. hurl /hɜːl/ v. 用力投掷；愤怒地说
📖 英文解释
v. to throw something with great force; to utter forcefully or angrily
✏️ 例句
He hurled the ball across the field with all his strength.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hurl abuse（怒骂）；hurl at（朝…投掷）；常考搭配：hurl accusations（猛烈指责）
🧠 助记
hurl谐音呼儿 → 呼着力气把东西甩出去 → 用力投掷
hurl::/hɜːl/:v. 用力投掷:The protesters hurled stones at the police vehicles.
7. icon /ˈaɪkɒn/ n. 偶像；图标；圣像
📖 英文解释
n. a person or thing regarded as a symbol or representative of something; a small image on a computer screen
✏️ 例句

cultural icon（文化偶像）；icon of（…的象征）；考研常考：click on the icon（点击图标）
🧠 助记
icon发音像爱看 → 大家都爱看的人 → 偶像
icon::/ˈaɪkɒn/:n. 偶像:The singer has become a global pop icon.
8. intelligible /ɪnˈtelɪdʒɪbl/ adj. 可理解的；清晰的
📖 英文解释
adj. able to be understood; clearly expressed or communicated
✏️ 例句
His explanation was barely intelligible due to his thick accent.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
intelligible to（对…来说可理解）；barely intelligible（几乎无法理解）；反义词：unintelligible
🧠 助记
intel（智）+ ligible（读）→ 能读懂的 → 可理解的
intelligible::/ɪnˈtelɪdʒɪbl/:adj. 可理解的:The speech was clear and intelligible to everyone in the room.
9. lad /læd/ n. 小伙子；男孩
📖 英文解释
n. a boy or young man; used informally to address a male person

🧠 助记
lad谐音来的 → 来的那个小伙子 → 小伙子
lad::/læd/:n. 小伙子:The lad worked hard every day to support his family.
10. liner /ˈlaɪnər/ n. 班轮；大型客轮；衬里

line（线路）+ r → 走固定线路的大船 → 班轮
liner::/ˈlaɪnər/:n. 班轮:The luxury liner sailed from Southampton to New York.
11. locker /ˈlɒkər/ n. 储物柜；锁柜

locker::/ˈlɒkər/:n. 储物柜:He forgot his gym shoes in his locker.
12. lubricate /ˈluːbrɪkeɪt/ v. 润滑；给…加润滑油
📖 英文解释
v. to apply oil or grease to something to reduce friction; to make something operate smoothly
✏️ 例句
Remember to lubricate the door hinges to stop the squeaking.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lubricate with（用…润滑）；well-lubricated（润滑良好的）；考研延伸：figuratively ease a process
🧠 助记
lubric（油滑）+ ate（使）→ 使之油滑 → 润滑
lubricate::/ˈluːbrɪkeɪt/:v. 润滑:The mechanic lubricated all the moving parts of the engine.
13. marble /ˈmɑːbl/ n. 大理石；弹珠 adj. 大理石制的

marble::/ˈmɑːbl/:n. 大理石:The ancient temple was built entirely of white marble.

18. notwithstanding /ˌnɒtwɪθˈstændɪŋ/ prep./conj. 尽管；虽然
📖 英文解释
prep./conj. in spite of; despite; nevertheless
✏️ 例句
Notwithstanding the bad weather, the event proceeded as planned.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
notwithstanding + n.（尽管…）；可置于句首或句尾；正式书面用语
🧠 助记
not + with + standing → 不与困难站在一起 → 尽管（有困难）
notwithstanding::/ˌnɒtwɪθˈstændɪŋ/:prep./conj. 尽管:Notwithstanding his lack of experience, he performed brilliantly.

📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
paradigm shift（范式转变）；dominant paradigm（主流范式）；考研高频词：学术写作

para（旁）+ digm → 旁边立着的典型 → 范例
paradigm::/ˈpærədaɪm/:n. 范例:This experiment established a new paradigm for future research.

paste::/peɪst/:v. 粘贴:He copied the text and pasted it into the new document.
