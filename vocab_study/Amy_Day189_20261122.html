1. peach /piːtʃ/ n. 桃子；桃树 adj. 桃色的
📖 英文解释
n. a soft, juicy fruit with a fuzzy skin and a single stone, or the tree bearing it
✏️ 例句
She bit into a ripe peach and juice ran down her chin.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
peach blossom（桃花）；peach tree（桃树）；peachy（极好的，口语）
🧠 助记
peach谐音批次 → 一批次桃子熟了 → 桃子
peach::/piːtʃ/:n. 桃子:The orchard was full of ripe peaches ready to be picked.
2. pedal /ˈpedl/ n. 踏板；脚蹬 v. 踩踏板
📖 英文解释
n. a lever operated by the foot on a bicycle, piano, or other machine v. to ride a bicycle by pushing the pedals
✏️ 例句
She pedaled hard to climb the steep hill.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pedal a bicycle（骑自行车）；gas pedal（油门踏板）；brake pedal（刹车踏板）
🧠 助记
ped（脚）+ al → 用脚踩的板 → 踏板
pedal::/ˈpedl/:n. 踏板:He pedaled his bicycle all the way to school.
3. pendulum /ˈpendjʊləm/ n. 钟摆；摆锤
📖 英文解释
n. a weight hung on a rod or cord that swings back and forth under gravity, used in clocks or as a metaphor for changing situations
✏️ 例句
The pendulum of the old clock swung steadily.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pendulum swing（钟摆摆动）；比喻：the pendulum of opinion（舆论的摆动）
🧠 助记
pen（笔）+ dulum → 像钢笔一样挂着来回摇 → 钟摆
pendulum::/ˈpendjʊləm/:n. 钟摆:The pendulum of public opinion has swung toward reform.
4. peninsula /pəˈnɪnsjʊlə/ n. 半岛
📖 英文解释
n. a piece of land that is almost surrounded by water but connected to the mainland on one side
✏️ 例句
Korea is a peninsula located in East Asia.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the Iberian Peninsula（伊比利亚半岛）；peninsula region（半岛地区）；常考地理描述
🧠 助记
pen（几乎）+ insula（岛）→ 几乎是岛的陆地 → 半岛
peninsula::/pəˈnɪnsjʊlə/:n. 半岛:The Italian peninsula extends into the Mediterranean Sea.
5. petition /pəˈtɪʃn/ n. 请愿书；申请书 v. 请愿；申请
📖 英文解释
n. a formal written request signed by many people, asking an authority to take action v. to make or present a formal request
✏️ 例句
Thousands of residents signed a petition to stop the construction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sign a petition（签署请愿书）；petition for（为…请愿）；petition against（反对…请愿）
🧠 助记
petit（小）+ ion → 小民向上请愿 → 请愿
petition::/pəˈtɪʃn/:n. 请愿书:They submitted a petition with 10,000 signatures to the mayor.
6. pirate /ˈpaɪrət/ n. 海盗；盗版者 v. 盗版
📖 英文解释
n. a person who robs at sea; a person who copies and sells another person's work illegally v. to reproduce or use something without authorization
✏️ 例句
The film was pirated and sold on the black market.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
software pirate（软件盗版者）；pirate copy（盗版复制品）；考研常考：intellectual property
🧠 助记
pi（派）+ rate（比率）→ 按比率抢劫的海盗 → 海盗
pirate::/ˈpaɪrət/:n. 海盗:Pirate websites distribute movies without paying the studios.
7. plantation /plænˈteɪʃn/ n. 种植园；大农场
📖 英文解释
n. a large farm, especially in a tropical country, where crops such as sugar, cotton, or tobacco are grown
✏️ 例句
The cotton plantation required hundreds of workers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tea plantation（茶园）；rubber plantation（橡胶园）；plantation economy（种植园经济）
🧠 助记
plant（种植）+ ation（地方）→ 大规模种植的地方 → 种植园
plantation::/plænˈteɪʃn/:n. 种植园:Coffee plantations cover vast areas of the Brazilian highlands.
8. pond /pɒnd/ n. 池塘
📖 英文解释
n. a small, still body of water, either natural or man-made
✏️ 例句
Ducks were swimming in the pond in the park.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fish pond（鱼塘）；lotus pond（荷花池）；pond water（池塘水）
🧠 助记
pond谐音砰当 → 石头扔进池塘砰当一声 → 池塘
pond::/pɒnd/:n. 池塘:The children caught frogs by the pond on summer afternoons.
9. postage /ˈpəʊstɪdʒ/ n. 邮费；邮资
📖 英文解释
n. the charge for sending a letter or parcel by post; a postage stamp
✏️ 例句
The postage for sending a parcel abroad can be quite expensive.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
postage stamp（邮票）；postage fee（邮费）；prepaid postage（预付邮资）
🧠 助记
post（邮递）+ age（费用）→ 邮递的费用 → 邮费
postage::/ˈpəʊstɪdʒ/:n. 邮费:Please affix sufficient postage before mailing the package.
10. recede /rɪˈsiːd/ v. 后退；渐渐消失；减弱
📖 英文解释
v. to move back or away from a previous position; to gradually diminish
✏️ 例句
As the tide receded, shells were revealed on the beach.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
re（向后）+ cede（走）→ 向后走 → 后退
recede::/rɪˈsiːd/:v. 后退:The floodwaters began to recede after three days of sunshine.
11. reed /riːd/ n. 芦苇；芦管乐器簧片

reed::/riːd/:n. 芦苇:The swan glided silently through the reed beds.
12. saddle /ˈsædl/ n. 马鞍；车座 v. 使负担

saddle::/ˈsædl/:n. 马鞍:She was saddled with heavy student loans after graduation.
13. salvation /sælˈveɪʃn/ n. 拯救；救赎；救星
