1. stun /stʌn/ v. 使震惊；击晕 n. 震惊
📖 英文解释
v. to shock someone greatly; to knock unconscious or daze temporarily
✏️ 例句
The news of his sudden death stunned the entire community.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stunned silence（震惊的沉默）；stun gun（电击枪）；stunning（极好的；令人震惊的）
🧠 助记
stun谐音死吨 → 被吨位砸中，立刻昏过去 → 击晕
stun::/stʌn/:v. 使震惊:The audience was stunned into silence by the announcement.
2. tariff /ˈtærɪf/ n. 关税；税率表
📖 英文解释
n. a tax imposed on imported or exported goods; a list of fixed charges
✏️ 例句
The government raised tariffs on imported steel to protect domestic industry.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
import tariff（进口关税）；tariff barrier（关税壁垒）；tariff war（关税战）；常考：国际贸易
🧠 助记
tar（焦油）+ iff → 进口商品被卡住（焦油粘住）→ 关税
tariff::/ˈtærɪf/:n. 关税:High tariffs on foreign cars made them too expensive for most consumers.
3. thermal /ˈθɜːml/ adj. 热的；保温的 n. 上升热气流
📖 英文解释
adj. relating to heat; designed to retain body heat n. a rising current of warm air used by birds and gliders
✏️ 例句
She wore thermal underwear to stay warm in the cold weather.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
thermal energy（热能）；thermal insulation（隔热）；thermal spring（温泉）
🧠 助记
therm（热）+ al → 关于热的 → 热的
thermal::/ˈθɜːml/:adj. 热的:Birds soar on thermal currents to save energy during flight.
4. timid /ˈtɪmɪd/ adj. 胆怯的；羞怯的
📖 英文解释
adj. showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
timid about（对…感到胆怯）；timid nature（胆小的性格）；反义词：bold, courageous
🧠 助记
tim（蒂姆）+ id → 蒂姆总是胆小怕事 → 胆怯的
timid::/ˈtɪmɪd/:adj. 胆怯的:She was too timid to speak in front of the large audience.
5. twinkle /ˈtwɪŋkl/ v./n. 闪烁；闪耀

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
twinkle in one's eye（眼里的光）；Twinkle Twinkle Little Star（小星星）；twinkle with（因…而闪烁）
🧠 助记
twin（双）+ kle → 两颗星星轮流闪烁 → 闪烁
twinkle::/ˈtwɪŋkl/:v./n. 闪烁:There was a playful twinkle in her eye as she told the story.
