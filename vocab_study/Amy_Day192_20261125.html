1. artery /ˈɑːtəri/ n. 动脉；干道
📖 英文解释
n. a blood vessel that carries blood from the heart to the rest of the body; a major transport route
✏️ 例句
The surgeon successfully repaired the damaged artery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
coronary artery（冠状动脉）；main artery（主干道）；artery disease（动脉疾病）
🧠 助记
art（艺术）+ ery → 动脉是生命的艺术线 → 动脉
artery::/ˈɑːtəri/:n. 动脉:The highway is the main artery connecting the two cities.
2. auditorium /ˌɔːdɪˈtɔːriəm/ n. 礼堂；观众席
📖 英文解释
n. a large room or building used for public performances, meetings, or lectures, with seating for an audience
✏️ 例句
The students gathered in the auditorium for the graduation ceremony.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
school auditorium（学校礼堂）；fill the auditorium（坐满礼堂）；auditorium seating（观众座席）
🧠 助记
audit（听）+ orium（地方）→ 听讲的地方 → 礼堂
auditorium::/ˌɔːdɪˈtɔːriəm/:n. 礼堂:The performance attracted a full auditorium of enthusiastic fans.
3. autumn /ˈɔːtəm/ n. 秋天；秋季
📖 英文解释
n. the season of the year between summer and winter; fall; a period of maturity and preparation for decline
✏️ 例句
The leaves turn golden and red in autumn.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
autumn leaves（秋叶）；autumn harvest（秋收）；in autumn（在秋天）
🧠 助记
autumn谐音奥腾 → 奥腾树叶变黄的秋天 → 秋天
autumn::/ˈɔːtəm/:n. 秋天:They went hiking through the forest to enjoy the autumn colors.
4. bandage /ˈbændɪdʒ/ n. 绷带 v. 包扎；用绷带绑
📖 英文解释
n. a strip of material used to bind up a wound or injured part of the body v. to bind with a bandage
✏️ 例句
The nurse carefully bandaged the wound on his arm.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
apply a bandage（敷绷带）；bandage up（包扎）；elastic bandage（弹性绷带）
🧠 助记
band（绑）+ age → 绑在伤口上的布 → 绷带
bandage::/ˈbændɪdʒ/:n. 绷带 v. 包扎:She wrapped a bandage tightly around the sprained ankle.
5. bark /bɑːk/ n. 树皮；狗叫声 v. 吠叫；厉声说
📖 英文解释
n. the outer layer of a tree; the sound made by a dog v. to make the sound of a dog; to speak sharply or abruptly
✏️ 例句
The dog barked loudly at the stranger at the door.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tree bark（树皮）；bark at（对…吠叫）；bark orders（厉声发号施令）
🧠 助记
bark谐音把客 → 狗把客人叫住，汪汪叫 → 吠叫
bark::/bɑːk/:n. 树皮:The dog barked nonstop throughout the night.
6. bibliography /ˌbɪbliˈɒɡrəfi/ n. 参考书目；书目
📖 英文解释
n. a list of sources used in a research paper or book; the academic study of books and their history
✏️ 例句
Students must include a bibliography at the end of their essays.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
annotated bibliography（注释书目）；bibliography list（参考书目）；考研学术写作常考
🧠 助记
biblio（书）+ graphy（记录）→ 书的记录 → 参考书目
bibliography::/ˌbɪbliˈɒɡrəfi/:n. 参考书目:The professor asked students to submit a bibliography with their papers.
7. blossom /ˈblɒsəm/ n. 花；开花期 v. 开花；繁荣
📖 英文解释
n. a flower or mass of flowers, especially on a tree or shrub v. to produce flowers; to develop and flourish
✏️ 例句

in blossom（正在开花）；blossom into（发展成为）；cherry blossom（樱花）
🧠 助记
bloss（爆）+ om → 花苞爆开 → 开花
blossom::/ˈblɒsəm/:n. 花:She blossomed into a confident young woman over the years.
8. camel /ˈkæml/ n. 骆驼

camel::/ˈkæml/:n. 骆驼:The camel is often called the ship of the desert.

cannon::/ˈkænən/:n. 大炮:Soldiers loaded the cannon before the battle began.

19. denounce /dɪˈnaʊns/ v. 谴责；公开批评；举报

deplore::/dɪˈplɔː/:v. 谴责:The teacher deplored the students' lack of respect for books.

depress::/dɪˈpres/:v. 使沮丧:Rising unemployment tends to depress consumer spending.
