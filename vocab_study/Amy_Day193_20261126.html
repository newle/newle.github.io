1. emphasise /ˈemfəsaɪz/ v. 强调，着重指出
📖 英文解释
To give special importance or attention to something; to stress a point.
✏️ 例句
The teacher emphasised the importance of reading widely to improve vocabulary.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ emphasise that… 强调…… ★ emphasise the need for / importance of 强调对……的需要／重要性 ★ 近义：stress, highlight, underline 辨析：emphasise（英式）= emphasize（美式），考研两种拼法均可
🧠 助记
em-pha-sis → 「恩发死」，一再强调某事，直到「死」为止 = 强调
emphasise::/ˈemfəsaɪz/:v. 强调，着重指出:She emphasised the need for careful planning.
2. exterior /ɪkˈstɪəriə(r)/ n. 外部，外表；adj. 外部的，外面的
📖 英文解释
The outside surface or outward appearance of something; relating to the outside.
✏️ 例句
Despite his tough exterior, he was a kind and generous person at heart.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the exterior of 建筑物的外部 ★ exterior design 外观设计 辨析：exterior（名词/形容词，外部）vs. internal（内部）；exterior wall 外墙
🧠 助记
ex-（向外）+ terior（比较级后缀）→ 更向外 = 外部的
exterior::/ɪkˈstɪəriə(r)/:n. 外部，外表:The exterior of the building was painted white.
3. fabricate /ˈfæbrɪkeɪt/ v. 捏造，伪造；制造，建造
📖 英文解释
To invent or produce false information in order to deceive; to construct or manufacture something.
✏️ 例句
The suspect was accused of fabricating evidence to mislead the investigation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ fabricate a story / evidence / an alibi 捏造故事／证据／不在场证明 ★ fabricate sth from 由……制造某物 辨析：fabricate（捏造，含贬义）vs. manufacture（制造，中性）
🧠 助记
fabric（布料/工厂）+ ate → 工厂里做出来的 → 引申为「捏造」出来的
fabricate::/ˈfæbrɪkeɪt/:v. 捏造，伪造:He fabricated the entire story to avoid punishment.
4. fellowship /ˈfeləʊʃɪp/ n. 友情，友谊；团体，协会；奖学金（研究员职位）
📖 英文解释
A feeling of friendship and mutual support among people sharing an interest; a group of people; a research grant or position.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a sense of fellowship 一种团体归属感 ★ research fellowship 研究奖学金 ★ fellowship program 学员计划 考研常考学术语境：fellowship = 奖学金或研究职位
🧠 助记
fellow（同伴）+ ship（状态）→ 与同伴在一起的状态 = 友谊；团体
fellowship::/ˈfeləʊʃɪp/:n. 友情，友谊:He applied for a fellowship to fund his doctoral research.

foam 谐音「否么」→ 你说「否么」，嘴边就起泡沫了 = 泡沫
foam::/fəʊm/:n. 泡沫:The cappuccino was topped with thick milk foam.

🧠 助记
free（自由）+ lance（长矛，中世纪雇佣兵）→ 拿着长矛自由揽活的雇佣兵 = 自由职业者
freelance::/ˈfriːlɑːns/:adj./adv. 自由职业的:Many journalists prefer to work freelance for flexibility.

goat::/ɡəʊt/:n. 山羊:The farmer kept several goats for milk and cheese.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

inverse::/ˌɪnˈvɜːs/:adj. 相反的，倒置的:Speed and accuracy are often in inverse proportion.
