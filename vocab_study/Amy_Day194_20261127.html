1. nylon /ˈnaɪlɒn/ n. 尼龙；nylons 尼龙袜
📖 英文解释
A strong, lightweight synthetic polymer used to make fabrics, ropes, and other products.
✏️ 例句
Nylon stockings became popular in the 1940s as a replacement for silk.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ nylon rope / fabric / stockings 尼龙绳／布料／丝袜 ★ 考研多作为材料类名词出现，注意其合成材料属性
🧠 助记
nylon 是商标名，NY（纽约）+ lon（London 伦敦）→ 两大城市联名的合成材料 = 尼龙
nylon::/ˈnaɪlɒn/:n. 尼龙:The parachute was made of strong nylon fabric.
2. obstruction /əbˈstrʌkʃn/ n. 阻碍，阻塞；障碍物
📖 英文解释
The act of blocking or the state of being blocked; something that blocks a path or prevents progress.
✏️ 例句
The fallen tree caused an obstruction on the main road for several hours.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ obstruction of justice 妨碍司法 ★ cause / create an obstruction 造成阻碍 ★ remove an obstruction 清除障碍 辨析：obstruction（具体或抽象的阻塞）vs. obstacle（障碍物，更偏实体）vs. hindrance（妨碍，过程）
🧠 助记
ob-（对着）+ struct（建造/结构）+ ion → 在路上建了东西堵住了 = 阻塞；障碍
obstruction::/əbˈstrʌkʃn/:n. 阻碍，阻塞:The police removed the obstruction from the emergency exit.
3. opaque /əʊˈpeɪk/ adj. 不透明的；晦涩难懂的
📖 英文解释
Not able to be seen through; not transparent; hard to understand or make sense of.
✏️ 例句
The report was written in opaque jargon that few readers could understand.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ opaque glass 不透明玻璃 ★ opaque language / reasoning 晦涩的语言／论证 辨析：opaque（不透明，反义 transparent）vs. translucent（半透明）vs. obscure（模糊，不清晰）
🧠 助记
opaque 谐音「哦派克」→ 派克钢笔的墨水是「不透明」的黑色 = 不透明的
opaque::/əʊˈpeɪk/:adj. 不透明的:The policy document was so opaque that journalists struggled to interpret it.
4. outing /ˈaʊtɪŋ/ n. 远足，郊游；公开（某人的隐私，尤指性取向）
📖 英文解释
A short pleasure trip; the practice of revealing someone's private information, especially sexual orientation, without consent.
✏️ 例句
The family went on a Sunday outing to the botanical garden.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a family / school outing 家庭郊游／学校远足 ★ outing（揭露他人性取向）此义在考研中较少，但阅读可能出现 辨析：outing（短途郊游）vs. excursion（有组织的旅行）vs. trip（泛指旅行）
🧠 助记
out-（出门）+ ing → 出门活动 = 郊游
outing::/ˈaʊtɪŋ/:n. 远足，郊游:The kindergarten organised an outing to the local zoo.
5. pathetic /pəˈθetɪk/ adj. 可怜的，令人怜悯的；无能的，差劲的（口语）
📖 英文解释
Arousing pity or sadness; miserably inadequate.
✏️ 例句
It was pathetic to see such a talented athlete give up after one setback.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pathetic fallacy 感情移入谬误（文学术语，将情感投射到自然） ★ a pathetic excuse 可怜巴巴的借口 辨析：pathetic（令人怜悯或鄙视）vs. pitiful（令人同情）vs. pitiable（值得同情）
🧠 助记
path-（感情，同 sympathy）+ etic → 激起感情的 → 令人可怜的
pathetic::/pəˈθetɪk/:adj. 可怜的，令人怜悯的:His attempt to explain his absence sounded pathetic and unconvincing.
6. patriotic /ˌpætriˈɒtɪk/ adj. 爱国的，有爱国心的
📖 英文解释
Having or expressing devotion to and vigorous support for one's country.
✏️ 例句
The soldiers fought with patriotic fervour to defend their homeland.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ patriotic spirit / sentiment 爱国精神／情感 ★ patriot（名词）爱国者 ★ patriotism（名词）爱国主义 辨析：patriotic（爱国的）vs. nationalistic（民族主义的，含过激倾向）
🧠 助记
patri-（父亲/祖国，如 patriarch）+ otic → 爱祖国如爱父亲 = 爱国的
patriotic::/ˌpætriˈɒtɪk/:adj. 爱国的，有爱国心的:Singing the national anthem is a patriotic tradition at sporting events.
7. pebble /ˈpebl/ n. 鹅卵石，小圆石
📖 英文解释
A small, smooth, rounded stone, especially one found on a beach or in a river.
✏️ 例句
Children skipped pebbles across the surface of the calm lake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pebble beach 鹅卵石海滩（Pebble Beach 也是著名高尔夫球场） ★ like a pebble dropped in water 投石入水（引起连锁反应） 辨析：pebble（光滑小圆石）vs. stone（泛指石头）vs. rock（大石块）vs. gravel（碎石）
🧠 助记
pebble → pe（小）+ bble（气泡形状）→ 像小气泡形状的小圆石 = 鹅卵石
pebble::/ˈpebl/:n. 鹅卵石，小圆石:She picked up a smooth pebble from the riverbed.
8. peel /piːl/ v. 剥皮，削皮；脱落；n. 果皮，外皮
📖 英文解释
To remove the outer skin of a fruit or vegetable; to come off in strips; the outer covering of a fruit.
✏️ 例句
She peeled the oranges and arranged the segments on a plate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ peel an orange / potato / apple 剥橙子／土豆／苹果皮 ★ peel off 剥掉；脱离（队伍） ★ orange peel / lemon peel 橙皮／柠檬皮 辨析：peel（剥皮，皮是薄的）vs. skin（剥皮，更通用）vs. shell（剥壳，坚硬的外壳）
🧠 助记
peel 谐音「皮儿」→ 剥掉的「皮儿」= 果皮；剥皮
peel::/piːl/:v. 剥皮，削皮:Peel the potatoes before boiling them.
9. perfume /ˈpɜːfjuːm/ n. 香水；香气；v. 使充满香气
📖 英文解释
A fragrant liquid worn on the skin; a pleasant smell; to add a pleasant scent to something.
✏️ 例句
The perfume of roses filled the garden on warm summer evenings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ wear / apply perfume 涂香水 ★ a bottle of perfume 一瓶香水 ★ the perfume of flowers 花香 辨析：perfume（香水，名词/动词）vs. fragrance（香味，更文学）vs. scent（气味，可好可坏）
🧠 助记
per-（通过）+ fume（烟雾/气味）→ 通过烟雾散发香气 = 香水；香气
perfume::/ˈpɜːfjuːm/:n. 香水:The perfume of jasmine drifted through the open window.
10. pneumonia /njuːˈməʊniə/ n. 肺炎
📖 英文解释
A serious infectious disease causing inflammation of one or both lungs, with the air sacs filling with fluid.
✏️ 例句
The elderly patient was hospitalised with severe pneumonia and put on antibiotics.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ catch / develop pneumonia 患肺炎 ★ bacterial / viral pneumonia 细菌性／病毒性肺炎 ★ pneumonia 中 p 不发音（pn → n） 注意发音：/njuːˈməʊniə/，p 不发音
🧠 助记
pneumo-（肺，来自希腊语 pneumon）+ ia（疾病后缀）→ 肺的疾病 = 肺炎
pneumonia::/njuːˈməʊniə/:n. 肺炎:He was bedridden for two weeks after contracting pneumonia.
11. recollect /ˌrekəˈlekt/ v. 回忆，记起
📖 英文解释
To remember something from the past, often with effort.
✏️ 例句
She could not recollect where she had put her keys that morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ recollect doing sth / that… 记起做过某事 ★ as far as I can recollect 就我所记得的 辨析：recollect（努力回忆）vs. remember（记得，自然）vs. recall（正式，主动回想）
🧠 助记
re-（再次）+ collect（收集）→ 再次收集脑中的记忆 = 回忆
recollect::/ˌrekəˈlekt/:v. 回忆，记起:He struggled to recollect the details of the accident.
12. retort /rɪˈtɔːt/ v./n. 反驳，回嘴；（化学）蒸馏瓶
📖 英文解释
To say something sharp or witty in reply; a sharp, angry, or witty reply; a glass vessel for distillation.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ make a sharp retort 进行尖锐的反驳 ★ retort that… 反驳说…… 辨析：retort（机智或生气地反驳）vs. reply（普通回答）vs. rebut（有逻辑地反驳，正式）
🧠 助记
re-（反回）+ tort（扭转，同 torture）→ 把话扭回去反击 = 反驳
retort::/rɪˈtɔːt/:v./n. 反驳，回嘴:"Mind your own business," he retorted sharply.
