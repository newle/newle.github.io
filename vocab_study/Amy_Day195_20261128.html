1. stipulate /ˈstɪpjuleɪt/ v. 规定，明确要求（作为协议条件）
📖 英文解释
To demand or specify something as a condition or requirement in an agreement.
✏️ 例句
The contract stipulates that the project must be completed within six months.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ stipulate that… 规定…… ★ stipulate sth as a condition 将……规定为条件 ★ stipulation（名词）规定，条款 辨析：stipulate（合同/协议中明确规定）vs. specify（详细说明）vs. prescribe（规定，法规）
🧠 助记
stipul-（拉丁语，合同上的文字）+ ate → 在合同上写明 = 规定
stipulate::/ˈstɪpjuleɪt/:v. 规定，明确要求（作为协议条件）:The agreement stipulates that both parties must provide 30 days' notice.
2. sunset /ˈsʌnset/ n. 日落，夕阳；（比喻）晚年，衰落期
📖 英文解释
The time in the evening when the sun disappears below the horizon; the period of decline.
✏️ 例句
They sat on the cliff and watched the magnificent sunset over the ocean.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at sunset 在日落时分 ★ sunset clause / provision 落日条款（到期自动失效的法规） ★ sunset industry 夕阳产业 辨析：sunset（日落）vs. sunrise（日出）vs. dusk（黄昏，更文学）vs. twilight（暮色）
🧠 助记
sun（太阳）+ set（落下）→ 太阳落下 = 日落
sunset::/ˈsʌnset/:n. 日落，夕阳:The sunset industry of coal mining is gradually being phased out.
3. thereafter /ˌðeərˈɑːftə(r)/ adv. 此后，在那之后（正式）
📖 英文解释
After that time or event; subsequently.
✏️ 例句
She graduated in 2010 and worked as a journalist for several years thereafter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ shortly / soon thereafter 不久之后 ★ 用于正式书面语，替代 after that 辨析：thereafter（此后，正式）vs. afterwards（以后，通用）vs. subsequently（随后，学术）
🧠 助记
there（那时）+ after（之后）→ 在那之后 = 此后
thereafter::/ˌðeərˈɑːftə(r)/:adv. 此后，在那之后（正式）:The treaty was signed in 1945, and peace prevailed thereafter.
4. trench /trentʃ/ n. 沟渠，壕沟；战壕；v. 挖沟
📖 英文解释
A long, narrow ditch dug in the ground, especially for military defence; to dig a trench.
✏️ 例句
Soldiers in World War I lived in muddy trenches for months at a time.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ trench warfare 堑壕战 ★ trench coat 风衣（源于一战军装） ★ in the trenches 在第一线，在艰苦的工作中 ★ dig a trench 挖沟
🧠 助记
trench 谐音「战壕」→ trench = 战壕，挖出来的壕沟 = 壕沟；战壕
trench::/trentʃ/:n. 沟渠，壕沟:Workers dug a trench along the road to lay new pipes.
5. ultraviolet /ˌʌltrəˈvaɪələt/ adj. 紫外线的；n. 紫外线（辐射）
📖 英文解释
Relating to electromagnetic radiation with a wavelength just shorter than that of violet light; UV radiation.
✏️ 例句
Prolonged exposure to ultraviolet radiation can cause skin cancer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ ultraviolet rays / radiation / light 紫外线 ★ UV（缩写） ★ ultraviolet index（UVI）紫外线指数 辨析：ultraviolet（紫外线）vs. infrared（红外线）
🧠 助记
ultra-（超越）+ violet（紫色）→ 超越紫色光谱范围的光 = 紫外线
ultraviolet::/ˌʌltrəˈvaɪələt/:adj. 紫外线的:She always applies sunscreen to protect against ultraviolet radiation.

🧠 助记
uni-（一）+ anim-（精神/意志，同 animal）+ ous → 所有人意志合一 = 全体一致的

7. voltage /ˈvəʊltɪdʒ/ n. 电压（伏特数）

🧠 助记
volt（伏特）+ age（量/程度）→ 伏特的量 = 电压
voltage::/ˈvəʊltɪdʒ/:n. 电压（伏特数）:The device requires a stable voltage of 220 volts to operate safely.
