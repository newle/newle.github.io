1. preposition /ˌprepəˈzɪʃn/ n. 介词，前置词
📖 英文解释
A word governing a noun or pronoun and expressing a relation to another word, such as 'in', 'on', 'at', 'by'.
✏️ 例句
Students often struggle with choosing the correct preposition in English sentences.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ prepositional phrase 介词短语 ★ common prepositions: in, on, at, by, with, for, of, to ★ preposition of time / place / direction 时间／地点／方向介词
🧠 助记
pre-（在前）+ position（位置）→ 放在（名词）前面的词 = 介词
preposition::/ˌprepəˈzɪʃn/:n. 介词，前置词:The sentence is missing a preposition before the noun phrase.
2. radioactive /ˌreɪdiəʊˈæktɪv/ adj. 放射性的，有辐射的
📖 英文解释
Emitting or relating to the emission of ionizing radiation or particles.
✏️ 例句
Radioactive waste from nuclear power plants must be stored safely for thousands of years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ radioactive material / waste / decay 放射性物质／废料／衰变 ★ radioactivity（名词）放射性 ★ radioactive isotope 放射性同位素
🧠 助记
radio-（辐射）+ active（活跃的）→ 辐射活跃的 = 放射性的
radioactive::/ˌreɪdiəʊˈæktɪv/:adj. 放射性的，有辐射的:Scientists use radioactive tracers to study how substances move through the body.

★ ruthless efficiency / ambition 冷酷的效率／野心 ★ be ruthless in 在……方面毫不留情 ★ ruthlessness（名词）冷酷无情 辨析：ruthless（无情，缺乏同情心）vs. merciless（残忍，不饶恕）vs. heartless（铁石心肠）
🧠 助记
ruth（怜悯，古英语）+ less（没有）→ 没有怜悯心的 = 无情的
ruthless::/ˈruːθləs/:adj. 无情的，残忍的，冷酷的:The company adopted a ruthless cost-cutting strategy that led to mass layoffs.
4. watt /wɒt/ n. 瓦特（功率单位）
📖 英文解释
A unit of power in the International System of Units equal to one joule per second.
✏️ 例句
This energy-efficient bulb uses only 9 watts to produce the same light as a 60-watt incandescent.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ kilowatt (kW) 千瓦 ★ megawatt (MW) 兆瓦 ★ watt-hour (Wh) 瓦时（电量单位） ★ 以发明家 James Watt（蒸汽机改良者）命名
🧠 助记
Watt = James Watt（瓦特发明家）→ 功率单位 = 瓦特
watt::/wɒt/:n. 瓦特（功率单位）:The solar panels generate up to 300 watts of electricity under full sunlight.
5. adore /əˈdɔː(r)/ v. 崇拜，热爱；（口）非常喜欢

🧠 助记
ad-（到）+ ore（黄金，源于 aurum）→ 向往黄金般珍贵的人 = 崇拜；热爱
adore::/əˈdɔː(r)/:v. 崇拜，热爱:The fans adore their favourite musician and attend every concert.

8. appal /əˈpɔːl/ v. 使震惊，使骇然（= appall）
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

appal::/əˈpɔːl/:v. 使震惊，使骇然（= appall）:Visitors were appalled by the conditions in the refugee camp.

assassinate::/əˈsæsɪneɪt/:v. 暗杀，刺杀（政治人物）:The president survived the assassination attempt and recovered fully.
