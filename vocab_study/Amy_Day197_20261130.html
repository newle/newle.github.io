1. dew /djuː/ n. 露水，露珠
📖 英文解释
Tiny drops of water that form on cool surfaces outdoors during the night.
✏️ 例句
The morning dew glistened on the grass as the sun rose over the hills.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ morning dew 晨露 ★ dew point 露点（气象） ★ heavy dew 浓重的露水 ★ covered in dew 沾满露水
🧠 助记
dew 谐音「滴屋」→ 夜里水气「滴」落在屋外草上 = 露水
dew::/djuː/:n. 露水，露珠:Leaves were still wet with dew when we set out on our morning hike.
2. discrete /dɪˈskriːt/ adj. 离散的，各自独立的，不连续的
📖 英文解释
Individually separate and distinct; not continuous.
✏️ 例句
The course is divided into discrete modules, each covering a different topic.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ discrete units / steps / elements 各自独立的单元／步骤／元素 ★ discrete mathematics 离散数学 辨析：discrete（各自独立的，不连续）vs. discreet（谨慎的，慎言的）— 同音不同义！
🧠 助记
dis-（分开）+ crete（完整）→ 分开各自完整的 = 离散的；各自独立的
discrete::/dɪˈskriːt/:adj. 离散的，各自独立的，不连续的:The algorithm processes data in discrete time intervals.
3. drip /drɪp/ v./n. 滴落，一滴一滴地落；点滴（医）；（口）无聊的人
📖 英文解释
To fall in drops; a small drop of liquid; a device for administering fluid slowly into a vein.
✏️ 例句
Water dripped steadily from the leaking tap, creating a small puddle.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ drip with 充满，渗透（dripping with sweat 汗流浃背） ★ drip-dry 免熨烫（衣物） ★ on a drip（医）挂点滴 ★ drip irrigation 滴灌
🧠 助记
drip 拟声词，模仿水滴落下的「滴」声 = 滴落
drip::/drɪp/:v./n. 滴落，一滴一滴地落:The patient was put on a drip to receive fluids and medication.
4. eclipse /ɪˈklɪps/ n. 日食；月食；（声望的）黯然失色；v. 使黯然失色，超越
📖 英文解释
An occasion when the sun or moon is obscured by another body; to cause something to lose its importance or fame.
✏️ 例句
Thousands of tourists gathered to witness the total solar eclipse.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ solar eclipse 日食 ★ lunar eclipse 月食 ★ total / partial eclipse 全食／偏食 ★ eclipse sb's achievement 使某人的成就黯然失色（比喻义）
🧠 助记
e-（out 出）+ clipse（clipse = clip，遮住）→ 被遮住而消失 = 日食；黯然失色
eclipse::/ɪˈklɪps/:n. 日食:Her breakthrough novel eclipsed everything she had written before.
5. electrician /ɪˌlekˈtrɪʃn/ n. 电工，电气技师
📖 英文解释
A person who installs and maintains electrical equipment.
✏️ 例句
They called an electrician to fix the faulty wiring in the kitchen.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ licensed electrician 持牌电工 ★ call / hire an electrician 请电工 ★ 后缀 -ician 表职业（musician, physician, technician）
🧠 助记
electric（电）+ ian（职业者）→ 和电打交道的人 = 电工
electrician::/ɪˌlekˈtrɪʃn/:n. 电工，电气技师:The electrician replaced the faulty circuit breaker in less than an hour.
6. electron /ɪˈlektrɒn/ n. 电子（基本粒子）
📖 英文解释
A stable subatomic particle with a charge of negative electricity, found in all atoms.
✏️ 例句
Electrons orbit the nucleus of an atom in shells at different energy levels.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ electron microscope 电子显微镜 ★ free electron 自由电子 ★ electron cloud 电子云 ★ proton / neutron / electron 质子／中子／电子
🧠 助记
electr-（电）+ on（粒子后缀，如 proton, neutron）→ 带电的粒子 = 电子
electron::/ɪˈlektrɒn/:n. 电子（基本粒子）:The electron microscope revealed the structure of the virus in detail.
7. encyclopedia /ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiə/ n. 百科全书
📖 英文解释
A comprehensive reference work containing articles on a wide range of subjects.
✏️ 例句
Wikipedia has become the world's largest online encyclopedia.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ encyclopaedia（英式拼法）= encyclopedia（美式） ★ encyclopedic（形容词）百科全书式的，渊博的 ★ encyclopedic knowledge 渊博的知识
🧠 助记
encyclo-（圆形/全面，同 cycle）+ pedia（教育/知识）→ 全面的知识 = 百科全书
encyclopedia::/ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiə/:n. 百科全书:He has an encyclopedic knowledge of classical music history.
8. enquire /ɪnˈkwaɪə(r)/ v. 询问，打听；调查（= inquire）
📖 英文解释
To ask for information; to investigate formally.
✏️ 例句
She enquired at the reception desk about the availability of rooms.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ enquire about 询问…… ★ enquire into 调查，深入了解 ★ enquiry（名词）询问；调查（英式） 辨析：enquire（英式）= inquire（美式）；enquire（询问）vs. investigate（正式调查）
🧠 助记
en-（进入）+ quire（寻求，同 query/quest）→ 进入寻求信息的过程 = 询问
enquire::/ɪnˈkwaɪə(r)/:v. 询问，打听:He enquired about the application deadline for the scholarship.
9. equator /ɪˈkweɪtə(r)/ n. 赤道
📖 英文解释
An imaginary line drawn around the middle of the Earth, equidistant from both poles.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at / near the equator 在赤道附近 ★ the equatorial region 赤道地区 ★ magnetic equator 磁赤道 ★ equatorial climate 赤道气候
🧠 助记
equat-（相等，same as equal）+ or → 把地球平分为南北两等份的线 = 赤道
equator::/ɪˈkweɪtə(r)/:n. 赤道:Singapore is located just one degree north of the equator.

forehead::/ˈfɔːhed/ or /ˈfɒrɪd/:n. 前额，额头:He frowned, deep lines creasing his forehead as he thought.
11. fright /fraɪt/ n. 惊吓，恐惧；（口）吓人的东西

fright 谐音「非赖特」→ 「赖特兄弟飞机」突然出现，把人「吓」一跳 = 惊吓
fright::/fraɪt/:n. 惊吓，恐惧:The unexpected knock at the door gave her quite a fright.

15. generalise /ˈdʒenrəlaɪz/ v. 泛论，一概而论；推广，普及（= generalize）
📖 英文解释
To make a general statement based on specific examples; to apply a principle more widely.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ generalise from 从……中归纳出 ★ broadly / widely generalise 广泛泛化 ★ generalisation（名词）一般化；笼统概括 辨析：generalise（笼统推论，可带贬义）vs. conclude（得出结论）vs. infer（推断）
🧠 助记
general（一般的）+ ise → 把特殊情况变成「一般」= 一概而论；推广
generalise::/ˈdʒenrəlaɪz/:v. 泛论，一概而论:You cannot generalise from a single case study to the entire population.

graceful::/ˈɡreɪsfl/:adj. 优雅的，优美的:She accepted the criticism in a graceful and dignified manner.

18. henceforth /ˌhensˈfɔːθ/ adv. 从此以后，今后（正式用语）
📖 英文解释
Starting from this time; from now on.
✏️ 例句
The new regulations will, henceforth, apply to all businesses operating in the region.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ 正式书面语，多见于法律文件、学术写作 ★ 同义词：hereafter, from now on, thereafter, subsequently 辨析：henceforth（从此时起，正式）vs. from now on（口语/书面，通用）
🧠 助记
hence-（从此）+ forth（向前）→ 从这里向前 = 从此以后
henceforth::/ˌhensˈfɔːθ/:adv. 从此以后，今后（正式用语）:The committee resolved that all meetings would henceforth be recorded.
