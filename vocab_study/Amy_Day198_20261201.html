1. lantern /ˈlæntən/ n. 灯笼
📖 英文解释
A portable lamp with a transparent or translucent case protecting the flame or electric bulb.
✏️ 例句
The children carried colourful paper lanterns during the festival parade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：lantern festival（灯节）；paper lantern（纸灯笼）；考研语境多出现于文化节日描写。
🧠 助记
lan（兰）+ tern（燕鸥）→ 一只燕鸥嘴里叼着灯笼飞舞。
lantern::/ˈlæntən/:n. 灯笼:The lantern glowed softly in the dark.
2. lid /lɪd/ n. 盖子；眼睑
📖 英文解释
A removable or hinged cover for the top of a container; also the eyelid.
✏️ 例句
She lifted the lid of the pot to check if the soup was boiling.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：put a lid on sth（压制某事）；flip one's lid（大发雷霆）；eyelid（眼睑）。
🧠 助记
lid → 把 'lid' 倒过来看像 'pil'，想象枕头（pillow）上盖着一个盖子。
lid::/lɪd/:n. 盖子:He removed the lid carefully.
3. longitude /ˈlɒŋɡɪtjuːd/ n. 经度
📖 英文解释
The angular distance of a place east or west of the meridian at Greenwich, England.
✏️ 例句
The ship's navigator calculated the longitude to determine their exact position.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：longitude and latitude（经纬度）；考研地理、航海类语境常见。
🧠 助记
long（长）+ itude → 地球上纵向的'长'线，就是经度。
longitude::/ˈlɒŋɡɪtjuːd/:n. 经度:The city lies at 120 degrees of longitude.
4. lyric /ˈlɪrɪk/ n. 抒情诗；歌词 adj. 抒情的
📖 英文解释
A short poem expressing personal emotions or thoughts, or the words of a song.
✏️ 例句
The lyrics of the song moved the audience to tears.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：lyric poetry（抒情诗）；song lyrics（歌词）；lyrical（adj. 抒情的，富有诗意的）。
🧠 助记
lyric → lyre（竖琴）+ ic → 弹竖琴唱出的抒情诗。
lyric::/ˈlɪrɪk/:n. 抒情诗:She memorised all the lyrics to the song.
5. manoeuvre /məˈnuːvə/ n./v. 操纵；策略；机动
📖 英文解释
A movement or series of moves requiring skill and care; to manipulate cleverly.
✏️ 例句
The driver performed a difficult manoeuvre to park in the tight space.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：military manoeuvre（军事演习）；political manoeuvre（政治手段）；room to manoeuvre（回旋余地）。
🧠 助记
man + oeuvre（法语：工作）→ 人精心操作的工作，即策略/机动。
manoeuvre::/məˈnuːvə/:n./v. 操纵:The general planned a clever manoeuvre.
6. melon /ˈmelən/ n. 甜瓜；瓜
📖 英文解释
A large round fruit with sweet juicy flesh and many seeds.
✏️ 例句
We ate slices of cool melon on the hot summer afternoon.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：watermelon（西瓜）；cantaloupe melon（哈密瓜）；melon seed（瓜子）。
🧠 助记
melon → me + lon → '我'吃瓜（LOL），联想一个人吃甜瓜。
melon::/ˈmelən/:n. 甜瓜:The melon was sweet and refreshing.
7. microphone /ˈmaɪkrəfəʊn/ n. 麦克风；话筒
📖 英文解释
An instrument for converting sound waves into electrical energy for amplification or transmission.
✏️ 例句
The singer stepped up to the microphone and began to perform.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：speak into the microphone（对着麦克风说话）；wireless microphone（无线麦克风）；缩写 mic。
🧠 助记
micro（微小）+ phone（声音）→ 把微小的声音放大的装置。
microphone::/ˈmaɪkrəfəʊn/:n. 麦克风:Please speak clearly into the microphone.
8. militant /ˈmɪlɪtənt/ n. 激进分子 adj. 激进的；好战的
📖 英文解释
Favouring confrontational or extreme methods in support of a cause; an activist using such methods.
✏️ 例句
Militant protesters blocked the road outside the parliament building.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：militant group（激进团体）；militant stance（激进立场）；考研政治社会类文章常见。
🧠 助记
milit（军队）+ ant（蚂蚁）→ 像蚂蚁一样成群的军事激进分子。
militant::/ˈmɪlɪtənt/:n. 激进分子 adj. 激进的:The militant group demanded immediate change.
9. mist /mɪst/ n. 薄雾 v. 变得朦胧
📖 英文解释
A thin layer of water droplets suspended in the atmosphere, reducing visibility.
✏️ 例句
A light mist covered the valley in the early morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：morning mist（晨雾）；lost in the mist（迷失在雾中，比喻模糊不清）；misty（adj. 朦胧的）。
🧠 助记
mist → miss + t → 在薄雾中迷失（miss）方向。
mist::/mɪst/:n. 薄雾 v. 变得朦胧:The mountains were hidden in the mist.
10. mosaic /məʊˈzeɪɪk/ n. 马赛克；镶嵌图案
📖 英文解释
A picture or pattern produced by arranging together small pieces of stone, tile, or glass.
✏️ 例句
The floor of the ancient Roman villa was decorated with a beautiful mosaic.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：mosaic art（镶嵌艺术）；cultural mosaic（文化多元）；考研文化艺术类语境。
🧠 助记
mosaic → Moses（摩西）+ aic → 摩西带领族人铺砌马赛克地板。
mosaic::/məʊˈzeɪɪk/:n. 马赛克:The mosaic depicted scenes from history.
11. mosquito /məˈskiːtəʊ/ n. 蚊子
📖 英文解释
A slender long-legged fly whose female bites through the skin to suck blood.
✏️ 例句
She applied repellent spray to protect herself from mosquito bites.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：mosquito net（蚊帐）；mosquito bite（蚊子叮咬）；mosquito-borne disease（蚊媒疾病）。
🧠 助记
mosquito → mosque（清真寺）→ 清真寺附近水多，蚊子多。
mosquito::/məˈskiːtəʊ/:n. 蚊子:Mosquitoes spread malaria in tropical regions.
12. mushroom /ˈmʌʃruːm/ n. 蘑菇 v. 迅速增长
📖 英文解释
A fungal growth with a stem and a domed cap; to increase or develop rapidly.
✏️ 例句
New coffee shops have been mushrooming all over the city.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：mushroom cloud（蘑菇云）；mushroom growth（迅速增长）；edible mushroom（食用菌）。
🧠 助记
mush（糊状）+ room（房间）→ 蘑菇长在阴暗潮湿的房间里。
mushroom::/ˈmʌʃruːm/:n. 蘑菇 v. 迅速增长:The business mushroomed within just two years.
13. mutter /ˈmʌtə/ v./n. 咕哝；抱怨
📖 英文解释
To speak in a low voice, often to oneself, in a way that is difficult to hear.
✏️ 例句
He muttered something under his breath and walked away angrily.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：mutter complaints（低声抱怨）；mutter to oneself（自言自语）；mutter about（嘀咕关于……）。
🧠 助记
mutt（杂种狗）+ er → 杂种狗不停地低声咆哮、咕哝。
mutter::/ˈmʌtə/:v./n. 咕哝:She muttered an apology and looked away.
14. napkin /ˈnæpkɪn/ n. 餐巾；纸巾
📖 英文解释
A square piece of cloth or paper used at meals to wipe the fingers or lips.
✏️ 例句
Please fold your napkin neatly before placing it on your lap.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：paper napkin（纸餐巾）；napkin ring（餐巾环）；table napkin（桌上餐巾）。
🧠 助记
nap（小睡）+ kin（亲戚）→ 吃饭时塞餐巾，让亲戚小睡也不怕弄脏。
napkin::/ˈnæpkɪn/:n. 餐巾:He wiped his mouth with a napkin.
15. nephew /ˈnefjuː/ n. 侄子；外甥
📖 英文解释
A son of one's brother or sister.
✏️ 例句
My nephew is five years old and very energetic.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
对应词：niece（侄女/外甥女）；搭配：nephew and niece（侄辈）。
🧠 助记
neph（nef→ next）+ ew → 下一代（next）中的男孩就是侄子。
nephew::/ˈnefjuː/:n. 侄子:Her nephew visited every summer holiday.
16. noodle /ˈnuːdl/ n. 面条
📖 英文解释
A strip, ring, or tube of pasta used in soups or other dishes.
✏️ 例句
She cooked a bowl of noodles with vegetables for dinner.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：instant noodles（方便面）；noodle soup（面条汤）；noodle bar（面馆）。
🧠 助记
noodle → nude（裸体）+ le → 面条像裸露的细长条。
noodle::/ˈnuːdl/:n. 面条:He ordered a large bowl of beef noodles.
17. nostalgic /nɒˈstældʒɪk/ adj. 怀旧的；乡愁的
📖 英文解释
Feeling or evoking a sentimental longing or wistful affection for the past.
✏️ 例句
Looking at old photographs always makes me feel nostalgic.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：feel nostalgic（感到怀旧）；nostalgic memories（怀旧记忆）；名词：nostalgia（怀旧情绪）。
🧠 助记
nostalgia → nostos（希腊语：返乡）+ algos（痛苦）→ 思乡之痛，怀旧情绪。
nostalgic::/nɒˈstældʒɪk/:adj. 怀旧的:The old song made her nostalgic for her childhood.
18. oath /əʊθ/ n. 誓言；誓词
📖 英文解释
A solemn promise, often invoking a divine witness, regarding one's future action.
✏️ 例句
The president took the oath of office in front of thousands of people.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：take an oath（宣誓）；oath of allegiance（效忠誓言）；under oath（在宣誓下）。
🧠 助记
oath → o（圆圈）+ ath（path路）→ 绕一圈路，郑重地走上誓言之路。
oath::/əʊθ/:n. 誓言:He swore an oath to protect the country.
19. obsolete /ˈɒbsəliːt/ adj. 过时的；废弃的
📖 英文解释
No longer produced or used; out of date.
✏️ 例句
Many old computer programs have become obsolete with the rise of smartphones.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：become obsolete（变得过时）；obsolete technology（过时技术）；考研科技社会类常见。
🧠 助记
ob（反对）+ solete（sole 鞋底）→ 连鞋底都磨平被反对穿了，过时了。
obsolete::/ˈɒbsəliːt/:adj. 过时的:This machinery is now completely obsolete.
20. overhear /ˌəʊvəˈhɪə/ v. 偷听；无意中听到
📖 英文解释
To hear a conversation by accident without the speakers knowing.
✏️ 例句
I accidentally overheard my neighbours arguing through the thin wall.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：overhear a conversation（无意中听到对话）；overhear sb doing sth（听到某人做某事）。
🧠 助记
over（从上方）+ hear（听）→ 从上方偷偷听，即偷听。
overhear::/ˌəʊvəˈhɪə/:v. 偷听:She overheard the secret plan by accident.
21. paralyse /ˈpærəlaɪz/ v. 使瘫痪；使丧失功能
📖 英文解释
To cause someone to become unable to move or function normally.
✏️ 例句
The heavy snowstorm paralysed transport across the entire city.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：be paralysed with fear（被恐惧吓得动弹不得）；paralyse the economy（使经济瘫痪）。
🧠 助记
para（旁边）+ lyse（溶解）→ 旁边的东西都被溶解，人动不了——瘫痪。
paralyse::/ˈpærəlaɪz/:v. 使瘫痪:The accident left him partially paralysed.
22. passerby /ˌpɑːsəˈbaɪ/ n. 过路人；行人
📖 英文解释
A person who happens to be going past something on foot.
✏️ 例句
A passerby helped the old woman who had fallen on the icy pavement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
复数：passersby；搭配：random passerby（随机路人）；ask a passerby（问路人）。
🧠 助记
passer（经过的人）+ by（在旁边）→ 从旁边经过的人，即过路人。
passerby::/ˌpɑːsəˈbaɪ/:n. 过路人:A passerby witnessed the accident.
23. paw /pɔː/ n. 爪子 v. 用爪抓
📖 英文解释
An animal's foot having claws and pads; to scrape or scratch with a paw.
✏️ 例句
The dog raised its paw as if asking for a handshake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：cat's paw（猫爪；比喻被人利用的人）；paw at（用爪子抓）；bear paw（熊掌）。
🧠 助记
paw 发音像 '爬'→ 动物用爪子爬，就是爪子。
paw::/pɔː/:n. 爪子 v. 用爪抓:The puppy placed its tiny paw on her hand.
24. peanut /ˈpiːnʌt/ n. 花生
📖 英文解释
A leguminous plant widely cultivated for its edible seeds which develop underground.
✏️ 例句
She spread peanut butter thickly on her morning toast.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：peanut butter（花生酱）；peanut oil（花生油）；peanuts（口语：极少的钱）。
🧠 助记
pea（豌豆）+ nut（坚果）→ 像豌豆又像坚果的东西，就是花生。
peanut::/ˈpiːnʌt/:n. 花生:Peanut allergy is common among children.
25. pillow /ˈpɪləʊ/ n. 枕头 v. 枕于
📖 英文解释
A rectangular cloth bag stuffed with soft material, used to support the head in bed.
✏️ 例句
She fluffed up her pillow before settling down to sleep.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：pillow case/cover（枕套）；pillow fight（枕头大战）；pillow talk（枕边细语）。

pill（药丸）+ ow（哦）→ 睡觉要吃安眠药，枕在枕头上说'哦'。
pillow::/ˈpɪləʊ/:n. 枕头 v. 枕于:He buried his face in the soft pillow.
