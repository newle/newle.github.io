1. porcelain /ˈpɔːsəlɪn/ n. 瓷；瓷器 adj. 瓷制的
📖 英文解释
A hard, translucent ceramic material made by firing a pure clay and then glazing it.
✏️ 例句
The museum displayed a rare collection of ancient Chinese porcelain.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：porcelain vase（瓷瓶）；porcelain doll（瓷娃娃）；fine porcelain（精瓷）；考研文化艺术类常见。
🧠 助记
porcelain → pour（倒）+ ce + lain（躺）→ 把泥土倒入模具躺着烧制，成为瓷器。
porcelain::/ˈpɔːsəlɪn/:n. 瓷:The porcelain cup was delicate and expensive.
2. radiant /ˈreɪdiənt/ adj. 辐射的；容光焕发的；灿烂的
📖 英文解释
Sending out light or heat; looking very happy and full of energy.
✏️ 例句
She looked radiant on her wedding day, full of joy and happiness.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：radiant energy（辐射能）；radiant smile（灿烂的微笑）；名词：radiance（光辉，喜悦）。
🧠 助记
radi（radiation辐射）+ ant（形容词后缀）→ 向四周辐射光芒，光彩照人。
radiant::/ˈreɪdiənt/:adj. 辐射的:The bride's face was radiant with joy.
3. redeem /rɪˈdiːm/ v. 赎回；补偿；兑换
📖 英文解释
To compensate for the faults of; to gain or regain possession of in exchange for payment.
✏️ 例句
He worked hard to redeem himself after the earlier failure.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：redeem a voucher（兑换券）；redeem oneself（洗刷自己）；redeemable（可兑换的）；redemption（救赎）。
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ deem（认为）→ 再次被认可——从失败中赎回自己。
redeem::/rɪˈdiːm/:v. 赎回:She redeemed her loyalty points for a free flight.
4. reel /riːl/ n. 卷筒；线轴 v. 摇晃；卷绕
📖 英文解释
A cylinder on which film, wire, or thread is wound; to feel very shocked or confused.
✏️ 例句
She reeled in the fishing line slowly after feeling a tug.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：reel of film（一卷胶片）；reel off（轻松说出/列出）；reel back（向后踉跄）。
🧠 助记
reel → real（真实）+ l → 卷轴放出真实的影像，或人像轴一样打转。
reel::/riːl/:n. 卷筒:He was still reeling from the shocking news.
5. relish /ˈrelɪʃ/ v. 喜欢；享受 n. 乐趣；调味品
📖 英文解释
To greatly enjoy something; a keen or pleasurable enjoyment of something.
✏️ 例句
She relished the opportunity to travel abroad for the first time.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：relish the challenge（享受挑战）；eat with relish（津津有味地吃）；relish doing sth（乐于做某事）。
🧠 助记
rel（rely依赖）+ ish（像）→ 像依赖一样享受某事，乐在其中。
relish::/ˈrelɪʃ/:v. 喜欢:He relished every bite of the home-cooked meal.
6. remainder /rɪˈmeɪndə/ n. 剩余物；余数；廉价出售的书
📖 英文解释
A part, number, or quantity that is left over; something that remains.
✏️ 例句
For the remainder of the journey, they travelled in silence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：the remainder of（……的剩余部分）；mathematical remainder（余数）；remainder bin（折扣品）。
🧠 助记
re + main（主要）+ der → 主要部分用完后，留下来的就是剩余物。
remainder::/rɪˈmeɪndə/:n. 剩余物:She spent the remainder of her savings on books.
7. riddle /ˈrɪdl/ n. 谜；谜语 v. 充满（漏洞）
📖 英文解释
A question or statement phrased to require ingenuity in finding its answer; to make many holes in.
✏️ 例句
Can you solve this riddle: what has hands but cannot clap?
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：solve a riddle（解谜）；riddle with bullets（弹孔密布）；riddled with（充满……）。
🧠 助记
rid（摆脱）+ dle → 想猜谜就得先摆脱（rid）固有思维。
riddle::/ˈrɪdl/:n. 谜:The ancient text was a complete riddle to scholars.
8. salient /ˈseɪliənt/ adj. 突出的；显著的；要点的
📖 英文解释
Most noticeable or important; prominent.
✏️ 例句
The report highlighted the salient points of the research findings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：salient features（显著特征）；salient point（要点）；考研学术写作高频词。
🧠 助记
sali（盐，sal）+ ent → 盐是菜中最突出的调味品，如同显著的要点。
salient::/ˈseɪliənt/:adj. 突出的:He identified the salient issues in his speech.
9. sane /seɪn/ adj. 心智健全的；理智的
📖 英文解释
Of sound mind; not mentally ill; sensible and rational.
✏️ 例句
After weeks of isolation, she struggled to stay sane.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：sane and rational（理智清醒）；keep sb sane（保持某人理智）；反义词：insane（疯狂的）。
🧠 助记
sane → same（相同）→ 思维与常人相同，就是心智健全的。
sane::/seɪn/:adj. 心智健全的:Anyone in their sane mind would refuse such an offer.
10. seam /siːm/ n. 缝；接缝；煤层 v. 缝合
📖 英文解释
A line of stitching joining two pieces of fabric; a thin layer of coal or other mineral.
✏️ 例句
The dress burst at the seams when she tried to zip it up.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：burst at the seams（快撑破了；超出容量）；coal seam（煤层）；seamless（无缝的）。
🧠 助记
seam → sea（海）+ m → 海浪把布料的缝合处冲开——seam 缝。
seam::/siːm/:n. 缝:The tailor sewed the seam very carefully.
11. shutter /ˈʃʌtə/ n. 快门；百叶窗 v. 关闭
📖 英文解释
A device that opens and closes to allow light into a camera; a hinged panel for a window.
✏️ 例句
The photographer clicked the shutter to capture the perfect moment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：camera shutter（相机快门）；shutter speed（快门速度）；put up the shutters（关门停业）。
🧠 助记
shut（关闭）+ ter → 快门就是能开关（shut）的装置。
shutter::/ˈʃʌtə/:n. 快门:She opened the shutters to let in the morning light.
12. siege /siːdʒ/ n. 围攻；围困
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
The castle withstood a siege of three months before finally surrendering.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
siege → see（看）+ age（时代）→ 历史上那个被看守包围的时代——围攻。
siege::/siːdʒ/:n. 围攻:The town was under siege for several weeks.
13. slipper /ˈslɪpə/ n. 拖鞋；软底鞋

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：bedroom slippers（卧室拖鞋）；glass slipper（玻璃鞋，灰姑娘）；pair of slippers（一双拖鞋）。
🧠 助记
slip（滑）+ per → 穿上容易滑的鞋——拖鞋（在地板上滑行）。
slipper::/ˈslɪpə/:n. 拖鞋:She bought new slippers for cold winter mornings.

snob（势利鬼）+ bish（ish后缀，带有……性质）→ 带有势利鬼性质，即势利的。
snobbish::/ˈsnɒbɪʃ/:adj. 势利的:His snobbish remarks offended everyone at the party.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

sprout → spread out（散开）→ 种子散开发芽，萌出嫩芽。
sprout::/spraʊt/:n. 芽:New leaves began to sprout from the bare branches.

🧠 助记
stag（雄鹿）+ ger → 受伤的雄鹿踉跄而行，步履蹒跚。
stagger::/ˈstæɡə/:v. 蹒跚:He staggered home after the long journey.
17. static /ˈstætɪk/ adj. 静态的；静止的 n. 静电；干扰
📖 英文解释
Lacking in movement, action, or change; electrical interference causing noise.
✏️ 例句
The television produced a lot of static during the thunderstorm.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：static electricity（静电）；static analysis（静态分析）；remain static（保持静止）。
🧠 助记
sta（站立/静止）+ tic（滴答）→ 时钟滴答却一直站在原地——静态的。
static::/ˈstætɪk/:adj. 静态的:House prices have remained static for two years.

She sat on a tall stool at the kitchen counter.

搭配：bar stool（酒吧凳）；piano stool（琴凳）；stool pigeon（线人，告密者）。

stool → stood（站立的过去式）→ 站的时候用的坐具——凳子。
stool::/stuːl/:n. 凳子:He pulled up a stool and sat at the bar.
19. streamline /ˈstriːmlaɪn/ v. 简化；使流线型 adj. 流线型的
📖 英文解释
To make more efficient by simplifying or adopting the latest techniques; to give a smooth outline.
✏️ 例句
The company decided to streamline its operations to cut costs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：streamline the process（简化流程）；streamlined design（流线型设计）；考研商务管理类常见。
🧠 助记
stream（流）+ line（线）→ 沿流线形状设计，使之更流畅简化。
streamline::/ˈstriːmlaɪn/:v. 简化:They streamlined the application process significantly.
20. summarise /ˈsʌməraɪz/ v. 概括；总结
📖 英文解释
To give a brief statement of the main points of something.
✏️ 例句
Could you briefly summarise the main points of your report?
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：summarise the findings（总结发现）；summarise in one sentence（用一句话概括）；名词：summary。
🧠 助记
sum（总和）+ marise → 把所有内容求和，即概括总结。
summarise::/ˈsʌməraɪz/:v. 概括:Please summarise your argument in three bullet points.
21. supersonic /ˌsuːpəˈsɒnɪk/ adj. 超声波的；超音速的
📖 英文解释
Involving or denoting a speed greater than that of sound; relating to ultrasound.
✏️ 例句
The supersonic aircraft broke the sound barrier over the Pacific Ocean.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：supersonic speed（超音速）；supersonic jet（超音速喷气机）；区分：ultrasonic（超声波，用于医疗）。
🧠 助记
super（超级）+ sonic（声音的）→ 超越声音速度，超音速。
supersonic::/ˌsuːpəˈsɒnɪk/:adj. 超声波的:The new plane can fly at supersonic speeds.
22. swan /swɒn/ n. 天鹅 v. 漫游；闲逛
📖 英文解释
A large waterbird with a long neck, typically pure white; to move or travel without purpose.
✏️ 例句
A pair of swans glided gracefully across the still lake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：swan lake（天鹅湖）；swan song（天鹅之歌，临终绝唱）；swan dive（燕式跳水）。
🧠 助记
swan → swam（游泳的过去式）→ 优雅地游过水面的鸟，就是天鹅。
swan::/swɒn/:n. 天鹅 v. 漫游:The swan's graceful neck curved over the water.
23. symmetry /ˈsɪmɪtri/ n. 匀称；对称；均衡
📖 英文解释
The quality of being made up of exactly similar parts facing each other or around an axis.
✏️ 例句
The butterfly's wings display perfect symmetry on both sides.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：bilateral symmetry（两侧对称）；perfect symmetry（完美对称）；symmetrical（adj. 对称的）。
🧠 助记
sym（同）+ metry（测量，如geometry）→ 两边测量相同，就是对称。
symmetry::/ˈsɪmɪtri/:n. 匀称:The architect designed the building with strict symmetry.
24. tempo /ˈtempəʊ/ n. 节奏；拍子；速度
📖 英文解释
The speed at which a passage of music is played or intended to be played; the pace of an activity.
✏️ 例句
The conductor increased the tempo as the orchestra reached the climax.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：tempo of life（生活节奏）；keep the tempo（保持节拍）；up tempo（快节奏的）。
🧠 助记
tempo → temp（时间）+ o → 时间的节奏，就是速度/节拍。
tempo::/ˈtempəʊ/:n. 节奏:The tempo of the music suddenly changed to a fast beat.
25. temporal /ˈtempərəl/ adj. 暂时的；世俗的；时间的
📖 英文解释
Relating to time; of or relating to practical matters in worldly affairs rather than spiritual ones.
✏️ 例句
The temporal power of the church declined during the Renaissance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：temporal power（世俗权力）；temporal lobe（颞叶，大脑部位）；temporal matters（世俗事务）。
🧠 助记
tempor（拉丁语：时间，temporary）+ al → 与时间有关的，暂时的，世俗的。
temporal::/ˈtempərəl/:adj. 暂时的:She focused on temporal concerns rather than spiritual ones.
