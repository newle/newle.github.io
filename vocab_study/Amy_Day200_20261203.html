1. tiresome /ˈtaɪəsəm/ adj. 讨厌的；令人厌倦的
📖 英文解释
Causing one to feel bored, irritated, or impatient.
✏️ 例句
It became tiresome to repeat the same instructions every single day.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：tiresome task（令人厌倦的任务）；tiresome person（让人烦躁的人）；近义词：tedious, boring。
🧠 助记
tire（轮胎/疲倦）+ some（带有……的）→ 让人感到疲倦的——讨厌的。
tiresome::/ˈtaɪəsəm/:adj. 讨厌的:The long meeting was incredibly tiresome.
2. trifle /ˈtraɪfl/ n. 琐事；少量 v. 浪费；轻视
📖 英文解释
A thing of little value or importance; a small amount; to treat without seriousness.
✏️ 例句
Don't trifle with people's emotions — it can cause serious harm.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：a trifle（稍微，有点）；trifle with（轻视，玩弄）；trifling（微不足道的）。
🧠 助记
tri（三）+ fle → 三样小事加在一起，都是琐事/微不足道的东西。
trifle::/ˈtraɪfl/:n. 琐事:He dismissed the problem as a mere trifle.
3. tub /tʌb/ n. 浴盆；桶；浴缸
📖 英文解释
A wide, open container used for washing, storing liquids, or growing plants.
✏️ 例句
She filled the tub with hot water and bath salts for a relaxing soak.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：bathtub（浴缸）；tub of butter（一桶黄油）；hot tub（热水浴缸）。
🧠 助记
tub → tube（管道）→ 装水的管形大容器，就是浴盆/桶。
tub::/tʌb/:n. 浴盆:The baby splashed happily in the tub.
4. understanding /ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ/ n. 理解；理解力；协议 adj. 善解人意的
📖 英文解释
The ability to understand something; sympathetic awareness; an informal agreement.
✏️ 例句
Mutual understanding between the two countries is essential for lasting peace.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：come to an understanding（达成共识）；on the understanding that（基于……的理解）；beyond understanding（超出理解）。
🧠 助记
under（在下面）+ stand（站）+ ing → 站在别人下方，设身处地——理解。
understanding::/ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ/:n. 理解:She showed great understanding of the difficult situation.
5. valve /vælv/ n. 阀门；瓣膜
📖 英文解释
A device for controlling the passage of fluid or gas through a pipe; a flap in the heart or a vein.
✏️ 例句
The engineer turned off the safety valve to stop the gas leak.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：heart valve（心脏瓣膜）；safety valve（安全阀；发泄途径）；valve control（阀门控制）。
🧠 助记
valve → valv（旋转）+ e → 通过旋转控制液体流量，就是阀门。
valve::/vælv/:n. 阀门:The plumber replaced the broken valve in the pipe.
6. variance /ˈveəriəns/ n. 变化；差异；方差
📖 英文解释
The fact or quality of being different; a quantity equal to the square of the standard deviation.
✏️ 例句
There is a significant variance between the two sets of test results.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：at variance with（与……不一致）；statistical variance（统计方差）；variance analysis（方差分析）。
🧠 助记
vari（变化，varied）+ ance → 变化的程度，即差异/方差。
variance::/ˈveəriəns/:n. 变化:His behaviour was at variance with his stated values.
7. vase /vɑːz/ n. 花瓶
📖 英文解释
A decorative container, typically made of glass or china, used as an ornament or for holding flowers.
✏️ 例句
She arranged fresh roses in the elegant crystal vase on the table.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：flower vase（花瓶）；porcelain vase（瓷花瓶）；vase painting（瓶画）。
🧠 助记
vase → vast（广阔）→ 宽口的容器用来插花，就是花瓶。
vase::/vɑːz/:n. 花瓶:The antique vase was worth a fortune at auction.
8. vest /vest/ n. 背心；马甲 v. 授予；赋予
📖 英文解释
A sleeveless garment; to confer or bestow (power, authority, or property) on someone.
✏️ 例句
He wore a bullet-proof vest under his uniform during the dangerous mission.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：life vest（救生衣）；vest in（赋予某人权力）；be vested in（属于……）。
🧠 助记
vest → invest（投资）→ 投资是把资金赋予项目，vest 是把权力授予某人。
vest::/vest/:n. 背心:Authority is vested in the board of directors.
9. vibrate /vaɪˈbreɪt/ v. 颤动；振动；回响
📖 英文解释
To move with small, rapid, back-and-forth movements; to quiver with emotion.
✏️ 例句
The phone vibrated silently on the table to signal an incoming call.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：vibrate with excitement（因激动而颤抖）；vibrating string（振弦）；vibration（n. 振动）。
🧠 助记
vibr（振动，vibration）+ ate → 产生振动——颤动。
vibrate::/vaɪˈbreɪt/:v. 颤动:The building began to vibrate during the earthquake.
10. volatile /ˈvɒlətaɪl/ adj. 不稳定的；易挥发的 n. 挥发物
📖 英文解释
Liable to change rapidly and unpredictably; (of a substance) easily evaporated at normal temperatures.
✏️ 例句
The volatile stock market made investors extremely nervous.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：volatile market（动荡市场）；volatile substance（易挥发物质）；volatile situation（局势不稳）。
🧠 助记
volat（飞，拉丁语 volare）+ ile → 像飞一样容易散掉，不稳定/易挥发。
volatile::/ˈvɒlətaɪl/:adj. 不稳定的:The political situation in the region remains highly volatile.
11. volt /vəʊlt/ n. 伏特（电压单位）
📖 英文解释
The SI unit of electromotive force, equal to the difference of potential that would carry one ampere of current.
✏️ 例句
The standard household electricity supply in the UK is 230 volts.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：voltage（电压）；volt meter（伏特计）；high-voltage（高压）；以科学家Alessandro Volta命名。
🧠 助记
volt → Volta（伏打，电池发明人）→ 纪念发明电池的科学家伏打，单位为伏特。
volt::/vəʊlt/:n. 伏特（电压单位）:The circuit operates at 12 volts.
12. waterfall /ˈwɔːtəfɔːl/ n. 瀑布
📖 英文解释
A cascade of water falling from a height, formed when a river flows over a precipice.
✏️ 例句
The spectacular waterfall attracted thousands of tourists every year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：waterfall model（瀑布模型，软件开发）；Niagara Falls（尼亚加拉大瀑布）；waterfall effect（瀑布效应）。
🧠 助记
water（水）+ fall（落下）→ 水往下落，就是瀑布。
waterfall::/ˈwɔːtəfɔːl/:n. 瀑布:They hiked three hours to reach the waterfall.
13. wax /wæks/ n. 蜡 v. 打蜡；（月亮）渐圆；变得
📖 英文解释
A soft yellowish substance secreted by bees; to apply wax to; (of the moon) to increase.
✏️ 例句
The candle dripped hot wax onto the tablecloth as it burned.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：wax and wane（盛衰；月圆月缺）；wax lyrical（热情洋溢地谈论）；ear wax（耳垢）。
🧠 助记
wax → wane → 月亮 wax（渐圆）和 wane（渐缺），像蜡烛一样消耗。
wax::/wæks/:n. 蜡 v. 打蜡:She waxed enthusiastic about her new project.
14. whirl /wɜːl/ v./n. 旋转；回旋；一阵忙碌
📖 英文解释
To move or cause to move rapidly round and round; a rapid rotation or circular movement.
✏️ 例句
Autumn leaves whirled in the air as the strong wind swept through the park.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：whirlpool（漩涡）；whirlwind（旋风）；in a whirl（头脑发昏，不知所措）。
🧠 助记
whirl → whisk + curl → 快速旋转卷曲，就像奶油被打发的样子。
whirl::/wɜːl/:v./n. 旋转:Her mind was in a whirl after hearing the news.
15. witch /wɪtʃ/ n. 女巫；巫婆
📖 英文解释
A woman thought to have magic powers, especially evil ones; a person who practises witchcraft.
✏️ 例句
In the fairy tale, the witch cast a spell that turned the prince into a frog.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：witch hunt（政治迫害；猎巫行动）；witch doctor（巫医）；bewitch（施魔法迷住）。
🧠 助记
witch → which（哪个）→ 你猜哪个老太太是女巫——witch！
witch::/wɪtʃ/:n. 女巫:The witch in the story had a black cat and a broomstick.
16. wool /wʊl/ n. 羊毛；毛线
📖 英文解释
The fine soft curly or wavy hair forming the coat of a sheep or goat, used in making yarn or fabric.
✏️ 例句
She bought several balls of wool to knit a warm scarf for winter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：wool sweater（羊毛衫）；pull the wool over sb's eyes（蒙骗某人）；woollen（adj. 毛料的）。
🧠 助记
wool → well（好）→ 羊毛真好，保暖又舒适。
wool::/wʊl/:n. 羊毛:The blanket was made of pure wool.
17. wrench /rentʃ/ n. 扳手；猛扭；痛苦 v. 猛拧；扭伤
📖 英文解释
A tool for gripping and turning nuts and bolts; a sudden violent twist; a painful feeling.
✏️ 例句
He used a wrench to tighten the bolt on the pipe.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：monkey wrench（活动扳手）；wrench apart（强行分开）；feel a wrench（感到揪心）。
🧠 助记
wrench → wring（拧）+ench → 用力拧的工具，就是扳手。
wrench::/rentʃ/:n. 扳手:Leaving home was a real wrench for the young student.
18. wrinkle /ˈrɪŋkl/ n. 皱纹；褶皱 v. 起皱
📖 英文解释
A small line or fold in something such as cloth or skin; to make or become wrinkled.
✏️ 例句
She smoothed out the wrinkles in her shirt before the important interview.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：wrinkle one's nose（皱鼻子）；iron out the wrinkles（消除障碍）；wrinkle-free（防皱的）。
🧠 助记
wrinkle → ring（圆环）+ kle → 皮肤上一圈圈的纹路，就是皱纹。
wrinkle::/ˈrɪŋkl/:n. 皱纹:Her face was lined with deep wrinkles from years of sun exposure.
19. according to /əˈkɔːdɪŋ tuː/ prep. 据……所说；根据；按照
📖 英文解释
As stated by or in; in a way that agrees with.
✏️ 例句
According to the weather forecast, it will rain heavily tomorrow.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：according to the report（根据报告）；according to plan（按计划）；注意：不能用于第一人称或第二人称。
🧠 助记
accord（一致）+ ing to → 与某信息来源一致——根据……。
according to::/əˈkɔːdɪŋ tuː/:prep. 据……所说:According to the survey, most people prefer coffee.
20. accustom /əˈkʌstəm/ v. 使习惯于
📖 英文解释
To make familiar with something through constant practice or experience.
✏️ 例句
It took months to accustom herself to the cold climate of the northern city.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：accustom oneself to（使自己习惯于）；be accustomed to（习惯于，常用被动）。
🧠 助记
ac（加强）+ custom（习俗/习惯）→ 加强习俗，使之形成习惯。
accustom::/əˈkʌstəm/:v. 使习惯于:He soon accustomed himself to getting up early.
21. adverb /ˈædvɜːb/ n. 副词
📖 英文解释
A word or phrase that modifies the meaning of an adjective, verb, or other adverb.
✏️ 例句
In the sentence 'she sings beautifully', the word 'beautifully' is an adverb.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：adverb of time（时间副词）；adverb of manner（方式副词）；adverbial（adj./n. 副词性的/状语）。
🧠 助记
ad（添加）+ verb（动词）→ 在动词旁边添加修饰，就是副词。
adverb::/ˈædvɜːb/:n. 副词:Quickly is an adverb that modifies the verb run.
22. air conditioning /ˈeə kənˌdɪʃənɪŋ/ n. 空调；空气调节
📖 英文解释
A system that controls and regulates the temperature, humidity, and quality of air in a building.
✏️ 例句
The office was so hot that everyone was grateful for the air conditioning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：air-conditioned（有空调的）；air conditioner（空调机，AC）；central air conditioning（中央空调）。
🧠 助记
air（空气）+ condition（调节）+ ing → 对空气进行调节，就是空调。
air conditioning::/ˈeə kənˌdɪʃənɪŋ/:n. 空调:The hotel room had powerful air conditioning.
23. albeit /ɔːlˈbiːɪt/ conj. 虽然；尽管
📖 英文解释
Even though; although.
✏️ 例句
She agreed to help, albeit reluctantly, when she saw how desperate he was.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
用于让步分句，常与形容词或副词配合：albeit briefly（虽然短暂）；albeit slowly（尽管缓慢）；书面语较常见。
🧠 助记
al（全部）+ be（是）+ it（它）→ 全面承认它，但……即'虽然'。
albeit::/ɔːlˈbiːɪt/:conj. 虽然:The film was good, albeit a little too long.
24. analog /ˈænəlɒɡ/ adj. 模拟的 n. 类似物
📖 英文解释
Relating to or using signals or information represented by a continuously variable physical quantity; an analogue.
✏️ 例句
Many music lovers prefer the warm sound of analog recordings over digital formats.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：analog signal（模拟信号）；analog clock（指针表）；analog vs digital（模拟与数字对比）。
🧠 助记
ana（类似）+ log（记录）→ 类似连续记录的方式，即模拟信号。
analog::/ˈænəlɒɡ/:adj. 模拟的 n. 类似物:The old radio used an analog tuner.
25. apologise /əˈpɒlədʒaɪz/ v. 道歉；认错
📖 英文解释
To express regret for something that one has done wrong.
✏️ 例句
He apologised sincerely to his colleague for interrupting her presentation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：apologise for（为……道歉）；apologise to sb（向某人道歉）；apology（n. 道歉）；owe sb an apology（欠某人道歉）。
🧠 助记
apo（远离）+ log（说话）+ ise → 通过说话来化解罪过，即道歉。
apologise::/əˈpɒlədʒaɪz/:v. 道歉:She apologised for arriving late to the meeting.
