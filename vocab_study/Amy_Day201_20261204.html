1. April /ˈeɪprəl/ n. 四月
📖 英文解释
The fourth month of the year, in the northern hemisphere usually considered the second month of spring.
✏️ 例句
The cherry blossoms are at their most beautiful in April.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：April Fool's Day（愚人节，4月1日）；April showers（四月阵雨）；in April（在四月）。
🧠 助记
April → a + pril（联想 spring，春天）→ 春天的月份就是四月。
April::/ˈeɪprəl/:n. 四月:The conference is scheduled for late April.
2. archeology /ˌɑːkiˈɒlədʒi/ n. 考古学
📖 英文解释
The study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and artefacts.
✏️ 例句
She studied archeology to better understand ancient civilisations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：archeology department（考古学系）；field archeology（田野考古）；archeologist（考古学家）。
🧠 助记
archeo（古老，如archive档案）+ logy（学科）→ 研究古老事物的学科，考古学。
archeology::/ˌɑːkiˈɒlədʒi/:n. 考古学:Archeology reveals much about ancient cultures.
3. artefact /ˈɑːtɪfækt/ n. 人工制品；文物；手工艺品
📖 英文解释
An object made by a human being, typically one of cultural or historical interest.
✏️ 例句
The museum acquired several ancient artefacts from the excavation site.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：cultural artefact（文化文物）；historical artefact（历史文物）；美式拼写：artifact。
🧠 助记
arte（艺术，art）+ fact（制作）→ 经过艺术制作的东西，就是人工制品/文物。
artefact::/ˈɑːtɪfækt/:n. 人工制品:The artefact was carbon-dated to 3000 BC.
4. August /ˈɔːɡəst/ n. 八月 adj. 令人敬畏的；威严的
📖 英文解释
The eighth month of the year; respected and impressive.
✏️ 例句
The hottest days of summer usually fall in August.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：in August（在八月）；august institution（令人敬畏的机构）；Augustus Caesar（奥古斯都皇帝，命名来源）。
🧠 助记
August → Augustus（奥古斯都）→ 以罗马皇帝命名，带有威严感。
August::/ˈɔːɡəst/:n. 八月 adj. 令人敬畏的:The results will be announced in August.
5. aural /ˈɔːrəl/ adj. 听觉的；耳朵的
📖 英文解释
Relating to the ear or the sense of hearing.
✏️ 例句
The language test included an aural component to assess listening skills.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：aural skills（听觉技能）；aural comprehension（听力理解）；区分：oral（口头的）。
🧠 助记
aur（耳朵，拉丁语 auris）+ al → 与耳朵有关的，听觉的。
aural::/ˈɔːrəl/:adj. 听觉的:The composer had exceptional aural memory.
6. Bible /ˈbaɪbl/ n. 圣经；权威著作
📖 英文解释
The Christian scriptures, consisting of the Old and New Testaments; an authoritative book on a subject.
✏️ 例句
Many literary works contain references to stories from the Bible.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：the Holy Bible（圣经）；Bible study（读经班）；a bible for sth（某领域的圣经/权威著作）。
🧠 助记
Bible → bi（二）+ ble（书）→ 分为两部分的书（旧约+新约），即圣经。
Bible::/ˈbaɪbl/:n. 圣经:This textbook has become the bible of modern economics.
7. botany /ˈbɒtəni/ n. 植物学
📖 英文解释
The scientific study of the physiology, structure, genetics, ecology, distribution, and classification of plants.
✏️ 例句
She developed a passion for botany after exploring the rainforest.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：botany class（植物学课）；botanical garden（植物园）；botanist（植物学家）。
🧠 助记
botan（grass/plant，希腊语 botanē）+ y → 研究植物的学科，植物学。
botany::/ˈbɒtəni/:n. 植物学:Botany and ecology are closely related sciences.
8. bowel /ˈbaʊəl/ n. 肠；内脏；（the bowels）深处
📖 英文解释
The intestines; the deep or innermost parts of something.
✏️ 例句
A healthy diet can help maintain regular bowel movements.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：bowel cancer（肠癌）；bowel movement（排便）；the bowels of the earth（地球深处）。
🧠 助记
bowel → bowl（碗）+ el → 像碗一样弯曲的管道，就是肠子。
bowel::/ˈbaʊəl/:n. 肠:The surgeon operated on the patient's bowel.
9. bowling /ˈbəʊlɪŋ/ n. 保龄球（运动）
📖 英文解释
A game in which players roll a heavy ball down a lane to knock down a group of pins.
✏️ 例句
They went bowling every Friday night as a way to unwind after work.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：go bowling（打保龄球）；bowling alley（保龄球馆）；bowling ball（保龄球）。
🧠 助记
bowl（碗，bowling ball圆形像碗）+ ing → 用圆球击倒瓶子的运动，保龄球。
bowling::/ˈbəʊlɪŋ/:n. 保龄球（运动）:The bowling alley was crowded on the weekend.
10. brace /breɪs/ n. 支撑；支架；括号 v. 支撑；使做好准备
📖 英文解释
A device that holds things firmly together; to support or prepare oneself for something difficult.
✏️ 例句
She braced herself for the bad news before opening the envelope.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：brace for（为……做准备）；dental brace（牙套）；brace oneself（稳住自己，做好心理准备）。
🧠 助记
brace → embrace（拥抱，加强）→ 用力抱紧就是支撑，做好准备。
brace::/breɪs/:n. 支撑:The city is bracing for a severe storm.
11. brandy /ˈbrændi/ n. 白兰地酒
📖 英文解释
A strong alcoholic spirit distilled from wine or fermented fruit juice.
✏️ 例句
After dinner, he poured himself a small glass of brandy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：glass of brandy（一杯白兰地）；brandy snap（白兰地薄饼）；fruit brandy（果味白兰地）。
🧠 助记
brandy → brand（品牌）+ y → 名牌蒸馏酒，就是白兰地。
brandy::/ˈbrændi/:n. 白兰地酒:She sipped brandy by the fireplace on Christmas Eve.
12. buffer /ˈbʌfə/ n. 缓冲；缓冲器 v. 缓冲
📖 英文解释
Something that reduces a shock or forms a barrier between two things; a temporary storage area.
✏️ 例句
Savings can act as a financial buffer against unexpected job loss.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：buffer zone（缓冲区）；buffer state（缓冲国）；computer buffer（计算机缓冲区）。
🧠 助记
buff（抛光，缓和）+ er → 用来缓和冲击的装置，就是缓冲器。
buffer::/ˈbʌfə/:n. 缓冲:The wetlands act as a buffer against flooding.
13. cabbage /ˈkæbɪdʒ/ n. 卷心菜；白菜
📖 英文解释
A leafy green vegetable with a round head of tightly packed leaves.
✏️ 例句
She made a fresh salad with shredded cabbage and carrots.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：cabbage soup（白菜汤）；red cabbage（红卷心菜）；Chinese cabbage（中国大白菜）。
🧠 助记
cab（出租车）+ bage（包包）→ 像包包一样圆的蔬菜，就是卷心菜。
cabbage::/ˈkæbɪdʒ/:n. 卷心菜:Cabbage is rich in vitamins and very affordable.
14. Catholic /ˈkæθlɪk/ n. 天主教徒 adj. 天主教的；普遍的
📖 英文解释
Relating to the Roman Catholic Church; (of a person) including a wide variety of things.
✏️ 例句
The Catholic Church has played an important role in European history.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：Roman Catholic（罗马天主教）；Catholic school（天主教学校）；catholic taste（广泛的品味，小写c）。
🧠 助记
cathol（希腊语 katholikos，普遍的）+ ic → 普世教会，即天主教。
Catholic::/ˈkæθlɪk/:n. 天主教徒 adj. 天主教的:She was raised in a devout Catholic family.
15. Christ /kraɪst/ n. 基督；耶稣基督
📖 英文解释
The title given to Jesus, from a Greek word meaning 'anointed'; used as a name or title.
✏️ 例句
Christians believe that Christ died to save humanity from sin.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：Jesus Christ（耶稣基督）；Christ-like（有基督精神的）；Christmas（基督降生节）。
🧠 助记
Christ → chrism（圣油，希腊语）→ 受膏者，指被圣油涂抹的基督。
Christ::/kraɪst/:n. 基督:The church was named after Jesus Christ.
16. Christian /ˈkrɪstʃən/ n. 基督徒 adj. 基督教的
📖 英文解释
A person who has received Christian baptism or professes belief in the Christian faith.
✏️ 例句
Many Christian communities gather for prayer every Sunday morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：Christian faith（基督教信仰）；Christian values（基督教价值观）；Christianity（n. 基督教）。
🧠 助记
Christ（基督）+ ian（……的人）→ 信仰基督的人，就是基督徒。
Christian::/ˈkrɪstʃən/:n. 基督徒 adj. 基督教的:She identified herself as a Christian and attended church regularly.
17. Christmas /ˈkrɪsməs/ n. 圣诞节
📖 英文解释
The annual festival of the Christian church commemorating the birth of Jesus, celebrated on December 25.
✏️ 例句
The whole family gathered around the Christmas tree to open presents.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：Merry Christmas（圣诞快乐）；Christmas Eve（平安夜）；Christmas tree（圣诞树）；White Christmas（白色圣诞）。
🧠 助记
Christ（基督）+ mas（弥撒）→ 纪念基督诞生的弥撒节，即圣诞节。
Christmas::/ˈkrɪsməs/:n. 圣诞节:Christmas is the most popular holiday in many countries.
18. cigaret /ˌsɪɡəˈret/ n. 香烟（cigarette 的变体）
📖 英文解释
A thin cylinder of finely cut tobacco rolled in paper for smoking.
✏️ 例句
He stepped outside to smoke a cigaret before the meeting began.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
标准拼写为 cigarette；搭配：light a cigarette（点烟）；cigarette butt（烟蒂）；cigarette break（抽烟休息）。
🧠 助记
cigar（雪茄）+ et（小，法语后缀）→ 小一号的雪茄，就是香烟。
cigaret::/ˌsɪɡəˈret/:n. 香烟（cigarette 的变体）:Smoking cigarettes is harmful to your health.
19. civilise /ˈsɪvəlaɪz/ v. 使文明；教化
📖 英文解释
To bring to a stage of social, cultural, and moral development considered to be more advanced.
✏️ 例句
Education has long been seen as a way to civilise and uplift society.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：civilise a society（教化社会）；civilised behaviour（文明行为）；civilisation（n. 文明）。
🧠 助记
civil（公民的/文明的）+ ise（使……）→ 使人变得文明，即教化。
civilise::/ˈsɪvəlaɪz/:v. 使文明:They sought to civilise the population through education.
20. clockwise /ˈklɒkwaɪz/ adv./adj. 顺时针方向（地）
📖 英文解释
In the direction in which the hands of a clock move.
✏️ 例句
Turn the key clockwise to lock the door.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
反义词：anticlockwise（英）/ counterclockwise（美）；搭配：rotate clockwise（顺时针旋转）。
🧠 助记
clock（时钟）+ wise（方向）→ 按照时钟指针走的方向，即顺时针。
clockwise::/ˈklɒkwaɪz/:adv./adj. 顺时针方向（地）:Tighten the bolt by turning it clockwise.
21. clothes /kləʊðz/ n. 衣服；服装（常复数）
📖 英文解释
Items worn to cover the body.
✏️ 例句
She packed all her summer clothes into the suitcase before the trip.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：clothes shop（服装店）；change clothes（换衣服）；second-hand clothes（二手衣物）；注意：没有单数形式 cloth=布料。
🧠 助记
cloth（布料）+ es → 由布料制成的东西，就是衣服。
clothes::/kləʊðz/:n. 衣服:He donated old clothes to the charity shop.
22. cock /kɒk/ n. 公鸡；旋塞 v. 使翘起
📖 英文解释
A male bird, especially a domestic fowl; a tap or valve; to tilt or turn something.
✏️ 例句
The cock crowed loudly at dawn, waking the entire farm.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：cock-a-doodle-doo（公鸡叫声）；cock one's head（把头歪向一侧）；weathercock（风向标）。
🧠 助记
cock → clock（时钟）→ 公鸡像钟一样准时在早晨鸣叫。
cock::/kɒk/:n. 公鸡:The cock announced the arrival of morning.
23. colonel /ˈkɜːnl/ n. 上校；陆军上校
📖 英文解释
A rank of officer in the army and in the US air force, above a lieutenant colonel and below a brigadier.
✏️ 例句
The colonel led his regiment into battle with great courage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：colonel in command（指挥官上校）；Lieutenant Colonel（中校）；Colonel-in-Chief（名誉上校）。
🧠 助记
colonel 发音是 /ˈkɜːnl/，拼写很奇特——记住：'colonel' 读'kernel'，军队里的核心人物。
colonel::/ˈkɜːnl/:n. 上校:Colonel Smith received a medal for his bravery.
24. communism /ˈkɒmjʊnɪzəm/ n. 共产主义
📖 英文解释
A theory or system of social organisation in which all property is owned by the community.
✏️ 例句
Communism advocated the abolition of private property and social classes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：communism vs capitalism（共产主义 vs 资本主义）；communist（n./adj. 共产主义者/共产主义的）。
🧠 助记
commun（共同，community）+ ism → 一切共同拥有的主义，就是共产主义。
communism::/ˈkɒmjʊnɪzəm/:n. 共产主义:The Cold War was largely a conflict between communism and capitalism.
25. concurrent /kənˈkɜːrənt/ adj. 同时发生的；并发的；并行的
📖 英文解释
Existing, happening, or done at the same time; (computing) involving simultaneous processes.
✏️ 例句
The two concurrent trials took place in different courtrooms.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：concurrent processing（并发处理）；concurrent sentences（并行刑期）；concurrently（adv. 同时地）。
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ current（流动）→ 多股水流同时流动，即同时发生。
concurrent::/kənˈkɜːrənt/:adj. 同时发生的:The two projects were running concurrently.
