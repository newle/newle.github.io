1. corrode /kəˈrəʊd/ v. 腐蚀；侵蚀；逐渐破坏
📖 英文解释
To destroy or damage slowly, especially by chemical action; to be destroyed in this way.
✏️ 例句
Salt water corrodes metal pipes over time, causing serious damage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：corrode metal（腐蚀金属）；corrode trust（侵蚀信任）；corrosion（n. 腐蚀）；corrosive（adj. 腐蚀性的）。
🧠 助记
cor（彻底）+ rode（骑/侵）→ 被彻底侵蚀，就是腐蚀。
corrode::/kəˈrəʊd/:v. 腐蚀:The acid began to corrode the metal surface.
2. cradle /ˈkreɪdl/ n. 摇篮；发源地 v. 轻柔地抱持
📖 英文解释
A baby's bed on rockers; the place where something originated; to hold gently.
✏️ 例句
Mesopotamia is often called the cradle of civilisation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：cradle of civilisation（文明摇篮）；from cradle to grave（从生到死）；cradle a child（轻抱孩子）。
🧠 助记
cra（cry哭）+ dle → 宝宝哭了就放在摇篮里摇，cradle 摇篮。
cradle::/ˈkreɪdl/:n. 摇篮:Greece is regarded as the cradle of democracy.
3. cunning /ˈkʌnɪŋ/ adj. 狡猾的；精明的 n. 狡猾
📖 英文解释
Having or showing skill in achieving one's ends by deceit; cleverness or ingenuity.
✏️ 例句
The cunning fox managed to outwit all the other animals in the fable.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：cunning plan（狡猾的计划）；cunning scheme（狡诈的阴谋）；by cunning（靠狡猾）。
🧠 助记
cun（拼音：村）+ ning → 村里最精明（聪明反被聪明误）的人，就是狡猾的。
cunning::/ˈkʌnɪŋ/:adj. 狡猾的:She used cunning tactics to win the negotiation.
4. cylinder /ˈsɪlɪndə/ n. 汽缸；圆筒；柱体
📖 英文解释
A three-dimensional shape with straight parallel sides and a circular cross-section; an engine component.
✏️ 例句
The car engine misfired because one of the cylinders was damaged.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：engine cylinder（发动机气缸）；cylinder head（气缸盖）；gas cylinder（煤气罐）；cylindrical（adj. 圆柱形的）。
🧠 助记
cylin（圆）+ der → 圆形的容器/空间，就是圆筒/气缸。
cylinder::/ˈsɪlɪndə/:n. 汽缸:The gas was stored in a large metal cylinder.
5. data /ˈdeɪtə/ n. 数据；资料
📖 英文解释
Facts and statistics collected together for reference or analysis.
✏️ 例句
Researchers analysed the data carefully before publishing their findings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：data analysis（数据分析）；data collection（数据收集）；big data（大数据）；raw data（原始数据）。
🧠 助记
data → date（日期）→ 每一个日期记录就是一条数据，数据就是被记录的事实。
data::/ˈdeɪtə/:n. 数据:The software processes large amounts of data quickly.
6. daunting /ˈdɔːntɪŋ/ adj. 令人生畏的；艰巨的
📖 英文解释
Seeming difficult to deal with in a way that causes a loss of confidence.
✏️ 例句
The sheer scale of the project was daunting at first.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：daunting task（艰巨的任务）；daunting challenge（令人生畏的挑战）；daunt v.（使胆怯）。
🧠 助记
daunt（使胆怯）+ ing → 让人感到胆怯的，令人生畏的。
daunting::/ˈdɔːntɪŋ/:adj. 令人生畏的:Moving to a new country can feel daunting at first.
7. deceit /dɪˈsiːt/ n. 欺骗；虚伪；诡计
📖 英文解释
The action or practice of deceiving someone by concealing or misrepresenting the truth.
✏️ 例句
The scandal revealed years of deceit at the highest levels of the company.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：act of deceit（欺骗行为）；practise deceit（行骗）；deceitful（adj. 欺骗性的）；deceive（v. 欺骗）。
🧠 助记
de（彻底）+ ceit（cieve→取，deceive）→ 彻底误导对方，就是欺骗。
deceit::/dɪˈsiːt/:n. 欺骗:She was shocked to discover the extent of his deceit.
8. December /dɪˈsembə/ n. 十二月
📖 英文解释
The twelfth month of the year, in the northern hemisphere usually considered the first month of winter.
✏️ 例句
The snow began to fall heavily in December this year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：in December（在十二月）；December holidays（十二月假期）；来源：decem（拉丁语：十）→ 原为罗马历第十个月。
🧠 助记
decem（拉丁语：十）+ ber → 原是第十月，历法改变后成为第十二月。
December::/dɪˈsembə/:n. 十二月:Christmas falls on the 25th of December.
9. decimal /ˈdesɪməl/ n./adj. 小数（的）；十进制（的）
📖 英文解释
Relating to or denoting a system of numbers based on the number ten; a fraction expressed in tenths.
✏️ 例句
She calculated the answer to two decimal places for greater accuracy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：decimal point（小数点）；decimal system（十进制）；decimal fraction（小数）。
🧠 助记
deci（十分之一，decimeter分米）+ mal → 以十分之一为单位，就是小数/十进制。
decimal::/ˈdesɪməl/:n./adj. 小数（的）:The price is listed to two decimal places.
10. desolate /ˈdesələt/ adj. 荒凉的；孤寂的 v. 使荒凉；使悲痛
📖 英文解释
Deserted of people and in a state of bleak and dismal emptiness; feeling very lonely and unhappy.
✏️ 例句
After the war, the once-bustling city was reduced to a desolate wasteland.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：desolate landscape（荒凉的景象）；feel desolate（感到孤寂）；desolation（n. 荒凉；孤独）。
🧠 助记
des（彻底）+ solate（sole一个人）→ 彻底只剩一人，孤寂而荒凉。
desolate::/ˈdesələt/:adj. 荒凉的:The abandoned village looked desolate and forgotten.
11. despatch /dɪˈspætʃ/ v./n. 派遣；发送；急件
📖 英文解释
To send off to a destination or for a purpose; (also: dispatch) the sending of a message or goods.
✏️ 例句
The commander despatched a unit of soldiers to secure the bridge.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
与 dispatch 同义；搭配：despatch a letter（发信）；despatch rider（传令兵）；with despatch（迅速地）。
🧠 助记
des（向外）+ patch（补丁/派遣）→ 向外派遣人员，就是派遣。
despatch::/dɪˈspætʃ/:v./n. 派遣:An ambulance was despatched to the scene immediately.
12. dialog /ˈdaɪəlɒɡ/ n. 对话；对话框（dialogue 的变体）
📖 英文解释
A conversation between two or more people; an exchange of ideas or opinions; (computing) a dialog box.
✏️ 例句
The novel is full of witty dialog between the main characters.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：open a dialog（开展对话）；dialog box（对话框）；inter-cultural dialog（跨文化对话）。
🧠 助记
dia（两个，dia-logue）+ log（说话）→ 两人之间的说话，就是对话。
dialog::/ˈdaɪəlɒɡ/:n. 对话:The peace talks opened a constructive dialog between the parties.
13. dove /dʌv/ n. 鸽子；温和派 v. dive 的过去式（美式）
📖 英文解释
A stocky bird with a small head, short legs, and a cooing voice; a symbol of peace.
✏️ 例句
A white dove was released into the sky as a symbol of peace.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：dove of peace（和平鸽）；dove vs hawk（鸽派 vs 鹰派）；turtledove（斑鸠）。
🧠 助记
dove → love（爱）→ 爱与和平的象征，就是鸽子。
dove::/dʌv/:n. 鸽子:The dove has long been a symbol of hope.
14. Easter /ˈiːstə/ n. 复活节
📖 英文解释
The most important Christian festival, celebrating the resurrection of Jesus Christ.
✏️ 例句
Children hunt for colourful Easter eggs hidden around the garden.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：Easter egg（复活节彩蛋）；Easter Sunday（复活节主日）；Easter holiday（复活节假期）。
🧠 助记
Easter → east（东方）→ 太阳从东方升起，象征基督复活，故名复活节。
Easter::/ˈiːstə/:n. 复活节:Easter is celebrated on a Sunday between late March and April.
15. ebb /eb/ n. 退潮；衰退 v. 退潮；减弱
📖 英文解释
The movement of the tide out to sea; a gradual decline; to recede or diminish.
✏️ 例句
The tide began to ebb after reaching its highest point at noon.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：ebb and flow（潮起潮落；盛衰）；at a low ebb（处于低潮）；ebb away（逐渐消退）。
🧠 助记
ebb → eb（音似'退'的英语谐音）→ 潮水退去，衰退。
ebb::/eb/:n. 退潮:His enthusiasm for the project began to ebb.
16. economics /ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks/ n. 经济学；经济因素

The branch of knowledge concerned with the production, consumption, and transfer of wealth.
✏️ 例句
She studied economics at university before working in finance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：economics degree（经济学学位）；the economics of（……的经济因素）；economist（经济学家）。
🧠 助记
eco（家园，eco-system）+ nomics（管理）→ 研究家园/社会资源管理的学科，经济学。
economics::/ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks/:n. 经济学:The economics of running a small business can be challenging.

em（进入）+ bass（bass/base 基地）+ y → 进入外国建立的基地，就是大使馆。
embassy::/ˈembəsi/:n. 大使馆:The protesters gathered outside the embassy.
18. fable /ˈfeɪbl/ n. 寓言；神话故事
📖 英文解释
A short story, typically with animals as characters, conveying a moral.
✏️ 例句
Aesop's fables, such as 'The Tortoise and the Hare', are known worldwide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：Aesop's fables（伊索寓言）；fable of（……的寓言）；fabulous（神话般的，出色的）。
🧠 助记
fable → fab（fabulous神奇）+ le → 神奇的故事，就是寓言/神话。
fable::/ˈfeɪbl/:n. 寓言:The teacher told a fable to illustrate the moral lesson.
19. farther /ˈfɑːðə/ adj./adv. 更远的（地）（物理距离）
📖 英文解释
At or to a greater distance in space (used for physical distance); comparative of 'far'.
✏️ 例句
The second village is farther from the city than the first one.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
区分：farther（物理距离更远）vs further（程度更深，如 further study）；搭配：go farther（走更远）。
🧠 助记
far（远）+ ther（比较级后缀）→ 比 far 更远，即更远的。
farther::/ˈfɑːðə/:adj./adv. 更远的（地）（物理距:They had to walk farther than expected to find the campsite.

following::/ˈfɒləʊɪŋ/:adj. 下列的:Please read the following instructions carefully.

25. further /ˈfɜːðə/ adj./adv. 更远的（地）；进一步的（地） v. 促进

fur（fur 毛皮，延伸）+ ther → 延伸得更远，即进一步的，更深入的。
further::/ˈfɜːðə/:adj./adv. 更远的（地）:We need to discuss this matter further.
