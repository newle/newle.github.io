1. gaol /dʒeɪl/ n./v. 监狱；监禁（jail 的英式拼写）
📖 英文解释
A place for the confinement of people accused or convicted of a crime (British spelling of jail).
✏️ 例句
He was sentenced to three years in gaol for fraud.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
英式拼写，与 jail 同义；搭配：in gaol（在监狱里）；gaolbird（惯犯）；gaolbreak（越狱）。
🧠 助记
gaol 发音同 jail → 拼法不同，意思完全相同，英国人写 gaol，美国人写 jail。
gaol::/dʒeɪl/:n./v. 监狱:The thief was sent to gaol for two years.
2. gay /ɡeɪ/ adj. 同性恋的；快乐的（旧义） n. 同性恋者
📖 英文解释
Relating to or used by homosexual people; (older usage) light-hearted and carefree.
✏️ 例句
Many cities hold annual gay pride parades in support of LGBTQ+ rights.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：gay rights（同性恋权益）；gay marriage（同性婚姻）；gay community（同性恋群体）。
🧠 助记
gay → 原意是'快乐的'，现代主要指同性恋的——联想 pride（骄傲游行）。
gay::/ɡeɪ/:adj. 同性恋的:The gay community celebrated the new equality legislation.
3. glamor /ˈɡlæmə/ n. 魅力；迷人之处（glamour 的变体）
📖 英文解释
The attractive or exciting quality that makes certain people or things seem appealing.
✏️ 例句
She was attracted to the glamor of the fashion industry.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：glamour of Hollywood（好莱坞魅力）；add glamour（增加魅力）；glamorous（adj. 迷人的）。
🧠 助记
glam（闪光，glamorous）+ or → 散发闪光魅力的东西，就是 glamor。
glamor::/ˈɡlæmə/:n. 魅力:The glamour of city life soon wore off for her.
4. goods /ɡʊdz/ n. 货物；商品；物品
📖 英文解释
Merchandise or possessions; things that are made to be sold.
✏️ 例句
The shop imported a wide variety of goods from Europe and Asia.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：consumer goods（消费品）；goods and services（货物与服务）；deliver the goods（兑现承诺）。
🧠 助记
good（好的）+ s → 好东西，就是货物/商品。
goods::/ɡʊdz/:n. 货物:All goods must be inspected before delivery.
5. grease /ɡriːs/ n. 油脂；润滑脂 v. 涂油脂；润滑
📖 英文解释
A thick, oily substance used as a lubricant; animal fat used in cooking.
✏️ 例句
He greased the hinges of the door to stop them from squeaking.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：grease the wheels（疏通关系；行贿）；elbow grease（力气；苦干）；grease lightning（快如闪电）。
🧠 助记
grease → Greece（希腊）→ 地中海橄榄油（油脂）的故乡——grease 油脂。
grease::/ɡriːs/:n. 油脂:The mechanic applied grease to the engine parts.
6. grown-up /ˈɡrəʊn ʌp/ n. 成年人 adj. 成年的；成熟的

grown（长大）+ up（向上）→ 向上长大了，就是成年人。
grown-up::/ˈɡrəʊn ʌp/:n. 成年人 adj. 成年的:A grown-up should take responsibility for their actions.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

headquarters::/ˈhedkwɔːtəz/:n. 总部:The police set up a temporary headquarters near the crime scene.
8. horsepower /ˈhɔːspaʊə/ n. 马力（功率单位，缩写 hp）
📖 英文解释
A unit of power equal to 550 foot-pounds per second, used to measure engine power.
✏️ 例句
The sports car produces over 400 horsepower, giving it incredible acceleration.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：engine horsepower（发动机马力）；brake horsepower（制动马力）；symbol: hp or BHP。
🧠 助记
horse（马）+ power（力量）→ 一匹马的力量，就是马力这个单位。
horsepower::/ˈhɔːspaʊə/:n. 马力（功率单位，缩写 hp）:This motorbike has 150 horsepower.

The hounds followed the scent trail across the fields for miles.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
hound → hunt（狩猎）→ 用于狩猎（hunt）的猎狗。
hound::/haʊnd/:n. 猎狗:Journalists hounded the celebrity wherever she went.

To make a long, loud, wailing cry, as a dog or wolf does; to weep and cry noisily.
✏️ 例句
The wolves howled in the distance as night fell over the forest.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
howl → owl（猫头鹰）→ 猫头鹰在夜里发出嚎叫声——howl 嚎叫。
howl::/haʊl/:v./n. 嚎叫:The child howled with laughter at the clown.
11. hysterical /hɪˈsterɪkl/ adj. 歇斯底里的；极度兴奋的；极度滑稽的
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

hysterical::/hɪˈsterɪkl/:adj. 歇斯底里的:She became hysterical when she heard the terrible news.

13. inflict /ɪnˈflɪkt/ v. 使遭受；施加（苦难/惩罚）
📖 英文解释
To cause something unpleasant or painful to be suffered by someone.
✏️ 例句
The new regulations inflicted heavy costs on small businesses.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：inflict pain（造成痛苦）；inflict damage（造成损害）；inflict punishment（施加惩罚）；inflict on/upon sb（使某人遭受）。
🧠 助记
in（向内）+ flict（击打，conflict）→ 向内击打，使人遭受打击/痛苦。
inflict::/ɪnˈflɪkt/:v. 使遭受:War inflicts enormous suffering on civilians.
14. influenza /ˌɪnflʊˈenzə/ n. 流行性感冒；流感
📖 英文解释
A highly contagious viral infection of the respiratory passages causing fever and severe aching.
✏️ 例句
Hundreds of students were absent from school due to influenza.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
缩写：flu；搭配：influenza virus（流感病毒）；catch influenza（得流感）；influenza vaccine（流感疫苗）。
🧠 助记
influ（影响，influence）+ enza → 像星星的影响力一样蔓延的疾病，就是流感。
influenza::/ˌɪnflʊˈenzə/:n. 流行性感冒:The hospital was full of patients with influenza last winter.
15. infrared /ˌɪnfrəˈred/ adj. 红外线的 n. 红外线
📖 英文解释
Electromagnetic radiation having a wavelength just greater than that of red light.
✏️ 例句
The security camera uses infrared technology to detect movement in the dark.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：infrared rays（红外线）；infrared sensor（红外传感器）；near-infrared（近红外）。
🧠 助记
infra（低于，低频）+ red（红色）→ 频率低于红光的光线，就是红外线。
infrared::/ˌɪnfrəˈred/:adj. 红外线的 n. 红外线:Infrared cameras can detect heat sources at night.

instalment::/ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/:n. 分期付款:The mortgage is repaid in monthly instalments.

invoke::/ɪnˈvəʊk/:v. 援引:She invoked her right to remain silent.
19. January /ˈdʒænjʊəri/ n. 一月

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

January::/ˈdʒænjʊəri/:n. 一月:Classes resume in January after the winter holiday.
