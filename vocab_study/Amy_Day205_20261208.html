1. oar /ɔːr/ n. 桨；橹 v. 划（桨）
📖 英文解释
A long pole with a flat blade used to row a boat through water.
✏️ 例句
The rowers pulled their oars in perfect synchrony as the racing shell glided across the lake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pull/use an oar 划桨 put/stick one's oar in 多管闲事（习语） oarsman n. 划桨手 ship the oars 收桨
🧠 助记
oar发音像"哦"，想象划桨时发出"哦哦"的声音
oar::/ɔːr/:n. 桨:He pulled on the oars to steer the boat to shore.
2. o'clock /əˈklɒk/ adv. 点钟（表示时间）
📖 英文解释
Used after a number to specify the hour of the day.
✏️ 例句
The train departs at six o'clock sharp, so please arrive at the station fifteen minutes early.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at ... o'clock 在…点钟 on the dot 正点 注意：只用于整点，不用于含分钟的时间 five o'clock shadow 下午五点的胡茬（文化词汇）
🧠 助记
源自of the clock（时钟的），缩略为o'clock，表示"几点正"
o'clock::/əˈklɒk/:adv. 点钟（表示时间）:The meeting starts at nine o'clock.
3. October /ɒkˈtoʊbər/ n. 十月
📖 英文解释
The tenth month of the year, between September and November.
✏️ 例句
The autumn foliage reaches its peak beauty in October, drawing tourists from around the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in October 在十月 October Revolution 十月革命（历史词汇） Oktoberfest 十月啤酒节（德国文化） mid-October 十月中旬
🧠 助记
octo（拉丁语八）→ 原为罗马历第八月，后变为第十月
October::/ɒkˈtoʊbər/:n. 十月:The harvest festival takes place every October.
4. odds /ɒdz/ n. 可能性；机会；赔率；差异（常用复数）
📖 英文解释
The probability or likelihood of something happening; in betting, the ratio determining winnings.
✏️ 例句
Despite the odds being against them, the underdog team managed to win the championship.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ against the odds 克服重重困难（高频短语） ★ odds are (that) 很可能… at odds (with) 与…不一致/冲突 odds-on 很可能的
🧠 助记
odd（奇怪的；奇数的）的复数，引申为"不均等的概率"
odds::/ɒdz/:n. 可能性:The odds of winning the lottery are extremely low.
5. ought to /ˈɔːt tə/ modal v. 应该；理应（表义务或推测）
📖 英文解释
Used to indicate duty, obligation, or a strong likelihood.
✏️ 例句
You ought to apologize for your behavior; it was completely inappropriate.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ ought to do sth 应该做某事（义务） ★ ought to have done 本应该做（但没做） 与should区别：ought to语气更强，更正式 ought not to 不应该
🧠 助记
ought源自owe（欠）的过去式，欠下的"义务" = 应该
ought to::/ˈɔːt tə/:modal v. 应该:You ought to exercise more regularly for your health.
6. outskirts /ˈaʊtskɜːrts/ n. 郊区；市郊（通常用复数）
📖 英文解释
The outer parts of a town or city, away from the center.
✏️ 例句
They moved to a quiet neighborhood on the outskirts of the city to escape the urban noise.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on the outskirts of 在…郊区（高频考点） live on the outskirts 住在郊区 近义词：suburb, periphery outskirts of town 城郊
🧠 助记
out（外面）+ skirts（裙摆/边缘）= 城市的边缘 = 郊区
outskirts::/ˈaʊtskɜːrts/:n. 郊区:The factory was built on the outskirts of the industrial city.
7. overt /oʊˈvɜːrt/ adj. 公开的；明显的；不隐蔽的
📖 英文解释
Done or shown openly; not secret or hidden.
✏️ 例句
The government's overt discrimination against minorities drew international condemnation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ overt discrimination/hostility 公开的歧视/敌意 ★ overt action 公开行动（与covert相对） overtly adv. 公开地 covert vs overt 隐蔽的vs公开的（对比记忆）
🧠 助记
over（过）+ t → 过分明显，掩盖不住 = 公开的
overt::/oʊˈvɜːrt/:adj. 公开的:His overt criticism of management surprised everyone.
8. owing to /ˈoʊɪŋ tuː/ prep. 由于；因为（表原因）
📖 英文解释
Because of; as a result of.
✏️ 例句
The match was postponed owing to heavy rain and lightning in the area.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ owing to + 名词/动名词（作原因状语） due to, because of, owing to 三者用法相近 ★ owing to the fact that + 从句 注意：不能直接跟句子
🧠 助记
owe（欠）→ owing to 因为欠了某种原因导致…= 由于
owing to::/ˈoʊɪŋ tuː/:prep. 由于:The delay was owing to unexpected technical difficulties.
9. pants /pænts/ v. 气喘吁吁；喘气 n. 裤子（美式）；内裤（英式）
📖 英文解释
To breathe quickly and loudly, as after exertion; also clothing worn on the lower body.
✏️ 例句
The exhausted runner was panting heavily as he crossed the finish line in first place.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pant for breath 喘气 pant with excitement 激动得喘气 ★ pants作名词：（美）裤子，（英）内裤 be caught with one's pants down 措手不及
🧠 助记
想象跑步时喘气的声音"pants pants"（喘气声）
pants::/pænts/:v. 气喘吁吁:The dog was panting after running in the hot sun.
10. parasite /ˈpærəsaɪt/ n. 寄生虫；寄生植物；依赖他人者
📖 英文解释
An organism that lives on or in a host organism and gets its food at the host's expense; also used metaphorically for someone who exploits others.
✏️ 例句
Social scientists sometimes describe certain corporations as economic parasites that extract wealth without contributing.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ internal/external parasite 体内/体外寄生虫 ★ parasitic adj. 寄生的（派生词） parasitism n. 寄生关系 parasite on society 社会的寄生虫（比喻义）
🧠 助记
para（旁边）+ site（地方）→ 住在别人旁边吃白食 = 寄生虫
parasite::/ˈpærəsaɪt/:n. 寄生虫:The parasite weakened the host organism over time.
11. peep /piːp/ v. 偷看；窥视；悄悄地看 n. 一瞥；窥视；啾啾声
📖 英文解释
To look quickly and secretly at something; also the high-pitched sound made by young birds.
✏️ 例句
The curious child peeped through the keyhole to see what was happening in the room.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ peep at/through/into 偷窥… ★ peep out 悄悄探出头来 peeping Tom 偷窥狂（文化词汇） not a peep 一点声音都没有
🧠 助记
peep发音像小鸟"哔哔"的叫声，引申为悄悄地看（如小鸟探头）
peep::/piːp/:v. 偷看:She peeped out from behind the curtain to see who arrived.
12. persecute /ˈpɜːrsɪkjuːt/ v. 迫害；骚扰；使苦恼
📖 英文解释
To treat someone cruelly and unfairly, especially because of their religion, race, or political beliefs.
✏️ 例句
Religious minorities were persecuted for centuries under various authoritarian regimes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ persecute sb for sth 因…迫害某人 ★ persecution n. 迫害（高频派生词） persecutor n. 迫害者 religious/political persecution 宗教/政治迫害
🧠 助记
per（彻底）+ secute（追随，来自拉丁语sequi）→ 穷追不舍地追踪 = 迫害
persecute::/ˈpɜːrsɪkjuːt/:v. 迫害:The regime persecuted anyone who spoke against it.
13. physics /ˈfɪzɪks/ n. 物理学
📖 英文解释
The branch of science concerned with the nature and properties of matter and energy.
✏️ 例句
Advances in quantum physics have revolutionized our understanding of the subatomic world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ nuclear/quantum physics 核物理/量子物理 physics teacher 物理老师 ★ physical adj. 物理的；身体的（区分考点） the laws of physics 物理定律
🧠 助记
physic（身体/自然）的复数，古希腊语physis意为"自然" = 物理学
physics::/ˈfɪzɪks/:n. 物理学:Physics explains the fundamental laws of the universe.
14. pistol /ˈpɪstəl/ n. 手枪
📖 英文解释
A small firearm designed to be held and fired with one hand.
✏️ 例句
The detective found a loaded pistol hidden under the floorboards of the suspect's apartment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ pistol shot 手枪射击 hold a pistol to one's head 用手枪顶着某人的头；逼迫（比喻） ★ pistol-whip 用手枪枪托打 starting pistol 起跑信号枪
🧠 助记
pistol来自意大利语pistola，想象一只手握着小手枪（pis-tol）
pistol::/ˈpɪstəl/:n. 手枪:The officer drew his pistol when he heard the alarm.
15. plaintiff /ˈpleɪntɪf/ n. 原告（法律术语）
📖 英文解释
A person who brings a legal action against another in a court of law.
✏️ 例句
The plaintiff filed a lawsuit against the company for negligence that resulted in serious injuries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ plaintiff vs defendant 原告vs被告（必考法律对比） ★ the plaintiff claims that... 原告声称… plaintiff's attorney 原告律师 注意：plaintiff说话是complaint（投诉）
🧠 助记
plain（平民/抱怨）+ tiff（争吵）→ 平民争吵的人 = 原告（去法院投诉的一方）
plaintiff::/ˈpleɪntɪf/:n. 原告（法律术语）:The plaintiff sought compensation for medical expenses.
16. politics /ˈpɒlɪtɪks/ n. 政治；政治学；政治活动
📖 英文解释
The activities and affairs involved in managing a country or area; also the academic study of government.
✏️ 例句
She has always been passionate about politics and decided to run for local council at age 25.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ engage in politics 从政 ★ office politics 办公室政治（常考） political adj. 政治的；political science 政治学 play politics 耍政治手腕
🧠 助记
polis（古希腊城邦）+ tics（活动）→ 城邦的管理活动 = 政治
politics::/ˈpɒlɪtɪks/:n. 政治:Students of politics study how governments make decisions.
17. preceding /prɪˈsiːdɪŋ/ adj. 在前的；先前的；前面的
📖 英文解释
Coming before in time, order, or position.
✏️ 例句
The results of the current experiment were consistent with findings from the preceding studies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the preceding chapter/year 前一章/年 ★ in the preceding days/weeks 在前几天/周 precede v. 在…之前（动词形式） 对比：following（随后的）vs preceding（在前的）
🧠 助记
pre（在前）+ cede（走）+ ing = 走在前面的 = 在前的
preceding::/prɪˈsiːdɪŋ/:adj. 在前的:The preceding paragraph introduces the main argument.
18. proceedings /prəˈsiːdɪŋz/ n. 诉讼；会议记录；进程（常用复数）
📖 英文解释
Legal actions; also the record of activities at a meeting or conference.
✏️ 例句
The judge suspended the legal proceedings until all the relevant documents were submitted.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ legal proceedings 法律诉讼（法律高频词） court proceedings 庭审程序 ★ conference proceedings 会议记录 take proceedings against sb 对某人提起诉讼
🧠 助记
proceed（进行）+ ings = 正在进行的（法律/会议）活动 = 诉讼；记录
proceedings::/prəˈsiːdɪŋz/:n. 诉讼:The judge opened the proceedings with a brief statement.
19. projector /prəˈdʒektər/ n. 放映机；投影仪；投影器
📖 英文解释
A device that projects an image or film onto a screen or other surface.
✏️ 例句
The teacher connected her laptop to the projector to display the presentation slides.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ slide/film projector 幻灯片/电影放映机 overhead projector 投影仪（高考词汇） ★ digital projector 数字投影仪 projector screen 投影屏幕
🧠 助记
project（投射）+ or（做…的人/物）= 进行投射的设备 = 放映机
projector::/prəˈdʒektər/:n. 放映机:The conference room has a high-definition projector installed.
20. promising /ˈprɒmɪsɪŋ/ adj. 有前途的；有希望的；大有可为的
📖 英文解释
Showing signs of future success; likely to develop in a good way.
✏️ 例句
The young scientist showed a promising start to her career with several groundbreaking discoveries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a promising student/career 有前途的学生/职业 ★ show/look promising 显示出前途 promising future 光明的前途 注意：promising 本意是"有承诺的"→ 有希望的
🧠 助记
promise（承诺）+ ing → 充满承诺的 = 有前途的
promising::/ˈprɒmɪsɪŋ/:adj. 有前途的:She had a promising start as a young researcher.
21. prophet /ˈprɒfɪt/ n. 先知；预言者；（宗教）先知
📖 英文解释
A person regarded as an inspired teacher or proclaimer of the will of God; someone who predicts future events.
✏️ 例句
The ancient prophet foretold that great upheaval would come before a period of peace.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a prophet of doom 悲观主义者；末日预言者 ★ no man is a prophet in his own country 英雄难逃乡里人（谚语） prophesy v. 预言；prophecy n. 预言 the Prophet 先知穆罕默德（伊斯兰教）
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ phet（说话，来自希腊语phemi）→ 向前说话的人 = 先知
prophet::/ˈprɒfɪt/:n. 先知:The prophet warned the people of coming hardships.
22. provided /prəˈvaɪdɪd/ conj. 假如；以…为条件 adj. 已提供的
📖 英文解释
On the condition that; as long as (used as a conjunction).
✏️ 例句
You may borrow the equipment provided that you return it in perfect condition by Friday.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ provided (that) + 从句 = 只要…（条件状语从句） ★ providing (that) 同义表达 provided for 有人供养的；已规定的 对比：provided vs if：provided语气更强，表唯一条件
🧠 助记
provide（提供）→ provided that 提供一个条件 = 只要
provided::/prəˈvaɪdɪd/:conj. 假如:You can attend the event provided you register in advance.
23. proximate /ˈprɒksɪmɪt/ adj. 最接近的；最近的；近因的（法律/学术）
📖 英文解释
Nearest in space, time, or order; immediately preceding or following.
✏️ 例句
The proximate cause of the accident was driver fatigue, though other factors also contributed.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ proximate cause 近因；直接原因（法律术语） proximate analysis 近似分析 ★ proximate vs ultimate cause 近因vs根本原因 proximity n. 接近；邻近（派生词）
🧠 助记
proxim（接近）+ ate = 接近的，来自拉丁语proximus（最近的）
proximate::/ˈprɒksɪmɪt/:adj. 最接近的:Investigators identified the proximate cause of the fire.
24. qualitative /ˈkwɒlɪtətɪv/ adj. 质的；定性的；质量上的
📖 英文解释
Relating to the quality or character of something rather than its quantity.
✏️ 例句
The research team conducted both qualitative interviews and quantitative surveys to get a complete picture.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ qualitative research/analysis 定性研究/分析（学术词汇） ★ qualitative vs quantitative 定性vs定量（常考对比） qualitative change 质变 qualitatively adv. 在质量上
🧠 助记
quality（质量）+ ative = 与质量有关的 = 质的；定性的
qualitative::/ˈkwɒlɪtətɪv/:adj. 质的:The study used qualitative methods to explore user experiences.
25. quarantine /ˈkwɒrəntiːn/ n./v. 检疫；隔离（对疑似感染者或动物）

A period of isolation imposed to prevent the spread of disease; to isolate in this way.
✏️ 例句
Passengers arriving from the affected region were required to undergo a 14-day quarantine.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in quarantine 在隔离中 ★ put in quarantine 进行隔离 quarantine period 隔离期（新冠疫情高频词） quarantine regulations 检疫规定
🧠 助记
quarante（法语四十）→ 古时船只需在港口隔离40天才能靠岸 = 检疫/隔离
quarantine::/ˈkwɒrəntiːn/:n./v. 检疫:The animals were placed in quarantine upon arrival.
