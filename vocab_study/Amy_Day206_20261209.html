1. quart /kwɔːrt/ n. 夸脱（容量单位，约1.14升）
📖 英文解释
A unit of liquid capacity equal to a quarter of a gallon, approximately 0.95 liters (US) or 1.14 liters (UK).
✏️ 例句
The recipe called for a quart of milk, which she bought from the local dairy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a quart of milk/water 一夸脱牛奶/水 ★ 1 quart = 2 pints = 1/4 gallon you can't put a quart into a pint pot 强人所难（习语） quart container 夸脱容器
🧠 助记
quart来自quarter（四分之一），= gallon的四分之一 = 夸脱
quart::/kwɔːrt/:n. 夸脱（容量单位，约1.14升）:He bought a quart of orange juice for breakfast.
2. queer /kwɪər/ adj. 奇怪的；古怪的；同性恋的（现代用法）n. 同性恋者（当事人自称）
📖 英文解释
Strange or odd; in modern usage, also used by LGBTQ+ people as an identity term.
✏️ 例句
There was something queer about the old mansion that made visitors feel uneasy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a queer feeling/sensation 奇怪的感觉 ★ queer theory 酷儿理论（学术词汇） queer sb's pitch 破坏某人的计划（英式俚语） in queer street 陷入困境
🧠 助记
queer发音像"奇"（qi）→ 奇怪的
queer::/kwɪər/:adj. 奇怪的:She had a queer feeling that something was wrong.
3. quilt /kwɪlt/ n. 被子；棉被；绗缝被 v. 绗缝
📖 英文解释
A warm bed covering made of padding enclosed between layers of fabric and kept in place by lines of stitching.
✏️ 例句
Her grandmother had spent years making the patchwork quilt that now covered the guest bed.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ patchwork quilt 拼布被 quilted jacket 绗缝夹克（日常词汇） ★ quilt cover 被套 quilting n. 绗缝工艺
🧠 助记
quilt联想"guilt"（内疚）→ 被子盖住的感觉让人安心，正好消除内疚感
quilt::/kwɪlt/:n. 被子:She snuggled under the warm quilt on the cold night.
4. quiver /ˈkwɪvər/ v./n. 颤抖；颤动；箭筒
📖 英文解释
To tremble or shake with slight, rapid movements; also the case in which arrows are carried.
✏️ 例句
Her voice quivered with emotion as she delivered the farewell speech at the ceremony.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ quiver with fear/cold/excitement 因恐惧/寒冷/兴奋而颤抖 ★ quiver of arrows 一筒箭 quivering lips/hands 颤抖的嘴唇/双手 quiver with anticipation 兴奋地期待
🧠 助记
quiver联想"shiver"（发抖），qu→快速颤动 = 颤抖
quiver::/ˈkwɪvər/:v./n. 颤抖:The leaf quivered in the gentle breeze.
5. razor /ˈreɪzər/ n. 剃刀；刮脸刀
📖 英文解释
A sharp-edged instrument used to shave hair from the skin.
✏️ 例句
He carefully ran the razor along his jaw, leaving his face perfectly smooth.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ razor blade 刀片 ★ razor-sharp 极其锋利的；聪明敏锐的（比喻义） Occam's razor 奥卡姆剃刀（哲学原则） on a razor's edge 在危险边缘；在关键时刻
🧠 助记
raze（夷平）+ or → 可以刮平的东西 = 剃刀
razor::/ˈreɪzər/:n. 剃刀:He shaved with a sharp razor every morning.
6. rectangle /ˈrektæŋɡəl/ n. 矩形；长方形
📖 英文解释
A plane figure with four straight sides and four right angles, especially one with unequal adjacent sides.
✏️ 例句
The architect designed a building with a simple rectangle as its floor plan.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a rectangle of 长方形的… rectangular adj. 矩形的（派生词） ★ rectangle vs square 长方形vs正方形（区别） perimeter/area of a rectangle 矩形的周长/面积
🧠 助记
rect（直的，如rectify）+ angle（角）= 直角形 = 矩形
rectangle::/ˈrektæŋɡəl/:n. 矩形:The swimming pool was shaped like a large rectangle.
7. regarding /rɪˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/ prep. 关于；至于（正式用语）
📖 英文解释
In reference to; with respect to; about.
✏️ 例句
I am writing regarding the job application I submitted last week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ regarding + 名词/动名词（作介词） ★ with regard to = regarding 关于（常考近义词） as regards 关于（正式） regarding your question 就您的问题而言
🧠 助记
regard（看待；关于）+ ing = 正在"看待"某事 = 关于
regarding::/rɪˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/:prep. 关于:He wrote a memo regarding the new company policy.
8. remains /rɪˈmeɪnz/ n. 遗体；遗迹；剩余物（常用复数）
📖 英文解释
A dead body; the parts of something that are left after the rest has been destroyed or used.
✏️ 例句
Archaeologists discovered the remains of an ancient Roman settlement beneath the modern city.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ human/mortal remains 人的遗体（正式表达） ★ the remains of an ancient city 古城遗迹 remain v. 剩下；留下（区分词性） cremated remains 火化后的骨灰
🧠 助记
remain（剩余）+ s = 剩余的东西；遗留下的（身体/建筑）
remains::/rɪˈmeɪnz/:n. 遗体:The remains of the old castle attracted many tourists.

A cold-blooded vertebrate animal of a class including snakes, lizards, crocodiles, and turtles; metaphorically, a despicable person.
✏️ 例句
Snakes, turtles, and crocodiles are all examples of reptiles that have existed since the age of dinosaurs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ reptile house 爬虫馆（动物园） ★ reptilian adj. 爬行动物的；卑鄙的 reptile brain 爬虫脑（神经科学比喻） cold-blooded reptile 冷血爬行动物
🧠 助记
rept（爬行，如crawl）+ ile = 会爬行的动物 = 爬行动物
reptile::/ˈreptaɪl/:n. 爬行动物:The museum had an impressive collection of reptile fossils.
10. resultant /rɪˈzʌltənt/ adj. 结果的；因而产生的 n. 合力；结果
📖 英文解释
Occurring as a result; in physics, the single force that is the equivalent of two or more forces.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the resultant effect/situation 由此产生的效果/状况 ★ resultant force 合力（物理术语） resultant from = resulting from 由…产生 as a result = consequently = hence
🧠 助记
result（结果）+ ant = 结果的；由结果产生的
resultant::/rɪˈzʌltənt/:adj. 结果的:The resultant damage from the storm was extensive.

18. so-called /ˌsoʊˈkɔːld/ adj. 所谓的；号称的（常带讽刺意味）
📖 英文解释
Used to indicate that something or someone is commonly designated by the name or term specified, often with an implied doubt about the accuracy of that designation.
✏️ 例句
The so-called experts failed to predict the economic crisis that was obvious to many observers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the so-called experts/solution 所谓的专家/解决方案（含讽刺） ★ so-called improvement 所谓的改进 注意：so-called表示对该称谓的质疑 a so-called friend 所谓的朋友（实则不是）
🧠 助记
so（如此）+ called（被称为）= 被这样称为的 = 所谓的
so-called::/ˌsoʊˈkɔːld/:adj. 所谓的:The so-called breakthrough turned out to be a minor improvement.
