1. statistics /stəˈtɪstɪks/ n. 统计；统计数据；统计学
📖 英文解释
The practice or science of collecting and analyzing numerical data in large quantities; a collection of numerical data.
✏️ 例句
According to the latest statistics, unemployment has fallen to its lowest level in twenty years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ according to statistics 根据统计数据 ★ vital statistics 人口统计数字（生命统计） statistical adj. 统计的（派生词） statistics show/indicate that 数据显示…
🧠 助记
state（国家）+ istics → 国家收集数字数据 = 统计；统计学
statistics::/stəˈtɪstɪks/:n. 统计:Statistics indicate that the crime rate has decreased significantly.
2. sting /stɪŋ/ v./n. 刺；蜇；刺痛；诈骗（俚语）
📖 英文解释
A sharp pain caused by a prick or wound from an insect's stinger; also means to cheat someone.
✏️ 例句
The bee sting on her arm caused immediate swelling that required medical attention.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ bee/wasp sting 蜜蜂/黄蜂蜇伤 ★ a stinging criticism 尖锐的批评 sting sb for money 诈取某人的钱 sting operation 警方的诱捕行动
🧠 助记
sting发音像刺入时的声音，"st"给人刺穿的感觉
sting::/stɪŋ/:v./n. 刺:The wasp sting made her arm swell up immediately.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ silk stockings 丝袜 Christmas stocking 圣诞袜 ★ in one's stocking(ed) feet 只穿袜子（不穿鞋） stocking mask 长筒袜面具（抢劫者用）
🧠 助记
stock（储存/存放）+ ing → 存放东西的袋子（圣诞袜装礼物）= 长筒袜
stocking::/ˈstɒkɪŋ/:n. 长筒袜:She wore sheer stockings to the formal dinner.
4. strenuous /ˈstrenjuəs/ adj. 繁重的；费力的；精力充沛的
📖 英文解释
Requiring or using great effort or energy; vigorous and determined.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ strenuous exercise/activity 剧烈运动/繁重活动 ★ strenuous efforts/opposition 艰苦努力/强烈反对 strenuously adv. 努力地 strenuous work 艰苦工作
🧠 助记
stren（强壮，如strength）+ uous = 需要强壮身体的 = 费力的；繁重的
strenuous::/ˈstrenjuəs/:adj. 繁重的:Mountain climbing is a strenuous physical activity.
5. strife /straɪf/ n. 冲突；斗争；纷争

★ civil/political strife 内乱/政治纷争（高频词） ★ stir up strife 挑起争端 strife-torn 被冲突撕裂的 industrial strife 劳资纠纷
🧠 助记
strife联想strive（奋斗）→ 激烈奋斗时引发的冲突 = 冲突；纷争
strife::/straɪf/:n. 冲突:The region has been torn by ethnic strife for decades.
6. striking /ˈstraɪkɪŋ/ adj. 显著的；引人注目的；惊人的
📖 英文解释
Attracting attention by reason of being unusual, extreme, or prominent; visually impressive.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a striking similarity/contrast 惊人的相似/对比 ★ striking beauty/appearance 引人注目的美貌/外表 strike v. 罢工；打（动词原形） strikingly adv. 显著地；惊人地
🧠 助记
strike（打；突出）+ ing = 像被打中一样令人印象深刻 = 引人注目的
striking::/ˈstraɪkɪŋ/:adj. 显著的:The architect designed a striking modern building for the city center.
7. sulphur /ˈsʌlfər/ n. 硫；硫磺（英式拼写，美式为sulfur）
📖 英文解释
A yellow nonmetallic element that burns with a blue flame and a suffocating smell, used in making gunpowder and matches.
✏️ 例句
The volcanic area smelled strongly of sulphur due to the natural geological activity nearby.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ sulphur dioxide 二氧化硫（酸雨成因） ★ sulphuric acid 硫酸（化学词汇） smell of sulphur 硫磺味（火山/火柴） sulphurous adj. 含硫的
🧠 助记
sul（太阳黄色，如sol）+ phur → 太阳般黄色的元素 = 硫磺
sulphur::/ˈsʌlfər/:n. 硫:The volcano emitted clouds of sulphur dioxide.
