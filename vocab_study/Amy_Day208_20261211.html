1. vinegar /ˈvɪnɪɡər/ n. 醋；尖刻的态度
📖 英文解释
A sour liquid made by fermenting dilute alcoholic liquids; also used metaphorically for a sharp or bitter manner.
✏️ 例句
The salad dressing was made with olive oil, vinegar, and a pinch of herbs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ rice/white/balsamic vinegar 米醋/白醋/香醋 ★ full of vinegar 精力充沛；充满活力（习语） vinegar smell 醋味 oil and vinegar 格格不入的两种东西
🧠 助记
vin（葡萄酒，如wine）+ egar（酸）→ 变酸的葡萄酒 = 醋
vinegar::/ˈvɪnɪɡər/:n. 醋:She added a splash of vinegar to the cucumber salad.
2. waken /ˈweɪkən/ v. 醒来；唤醒；使警觉
📖 英文解释
To stop sleeping; to cause someone to stop sleeping; to make alert.
✏️ 例句
She was wakened by the sound of thunder during the night and couldn't fall back asleep.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ waken (sb) up 叫醒某人 ★ waken to reality/danger 意识到现实/危险 wake/waken/awake/awaken 四词区别（考点） waken sb's interest 唤起某人的兴趣
🧠 助记
wake（醒）+ n = waken，比wake更正式的变体，意思相同
waken::/ˈweɪkən/:v. 醒来:The nurse wakened the patient gently for the morning checkup.
3. Wednesday /ˈwenzdeɪ/ n. 星期三
📖 英文解释
The day of the week between Tuesday and Thursday.
✏️ 例句
The company holds its board of directors meeting on the first Wednesday of every month.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on Wednesday 在星期三 last/next Wednesday 上/下个星期三 Wednesday afternoon 周三下午 Woden's day（北欧主神奥丁之日）= Wednesday
🧠 助记
Woden's day（北欧主神Woden/Odin之日）= Wednesday，注意拼写中含d
Wednesday::/ˈwenzdeɪ/:n. 星期三:The training session is scheduled for Wednesday morning.
4. well-known /ˌwelˈnoʊn/ adj. 众所周知的；著名的；驰名的
📖 英文解释
Widely and favorably known; famous.
✏️ 例句
She is a well-known scientist whose research on climate change has influenced global policy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a well-known fact 众所周知的事实 ★ well-known for sth 因…而知名 well-known figure/person 知名人物 对比：well-known（知名度广）vs famous（名声大）
🧠 助记
well（好）+ known（知道）= 被很多人知晓 = 众所周知的
well-known::/ˌwelˈnoʊn/:adj. 众所周知的:Shakespeare is a well-known playwright studied worldwide.
5. wicked /ˈwɪkɪd/ adj. 邪恶的；恶毒的；顽皮的；极好的（俚语）
📖 英文解释
Evil or morally wrong; also used informally to mean excellent.
✏️ 例句
The villain in the story was a wicked queen who poisoned anyone who threatened her power.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ wicked deed/behavior 邪恶的行为 ★ wickedness n. 邪恶（派生词） a wicked sense of humor 刻薄的幽默感 wicked（俚语）= very good 非常棒的
🧠 助记
wicked联想witch（女巫）→ 女巫的行为 = 邪恶的
wicked::/ˈwɪkɪd/:adj. 邪恶的:The fairy tale featured a wicked stepmother who mistreated the children.
6. wink /wɪŋk/ v./n. 眨眼；使眼色；闪烁
📖 英文解释
To close and open one eye quickly as a signal or greeting; such an act.
✏️ 例句
He winked at his friend across the table to signal that the plan was working perfectly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ wink at sb 向某人眨眼示意 ★ forty winks 小睡；打盹 not sleep a wink 一点都没睡 in the wink of an eye 转瞬间；一眨眼
🧠 助记
wink发音模拟眨眼的快速动作
wink::/wɪŋk/:v./n. 眨眼:She gave a friendly wink as she passed by in the corridor.
7. X-ray /ˈeksreɪ/ n. X射线；X光片 v. 用X光检查
📖 英文解释
Electromagnetic radiation of high energy and very short wavelength; a photographic image produced using such radiation; to examine with X-rays.
✏️ 例句
The doctor ordered an X-ray to check if the patient had any broken bones after the accident.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ take an X-ray 拍X光片 ★ X-ray examination 透视检查 X-ray machine 透视机 chest X-ray 胸透
🧠 助记
X（未知的）+ ray（射线）= 神秘的未知射线，发现初期被命名为X射线
X-ray::/ˈeksreɪ/:n. X射线:The dentist took an X-ray of her teeth before the procedure.
8. yours /jɔːrz/ pron. 你的；你们的（所有格代词）
📖 英文解释
Used to refer to something belonging to or associated with the person being addressed.
✏️ 例句
The decision is entirely yours to make; I will support whatever you choose to do.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ Yours sincerely/faithfully 此致敬礼（信件结束语） ★ yours truly 本人（自称） It's yours 是你的 yours 信件结尾常用语
🧠 助记
you（你）的所有格代词，your + s = yours（你的东西）
yours::/jɔːrz/:pron. 你的:This package was delivered to the wrong address—it should be yours.
9. yourselves /jɔːrˈselvz/ pron. 你们自己（yourself的复数）
📖 英文解释
Used as the object of a verb or preposition when the subject is 'you' (plural) and refers to the same people; used for emphasis.
✏️ 例句
Please help yourselves to the refreshments at the back of the room.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ help yourselves 请随便吃/用 ★ by yourselves 你们自己；独立地 enjoy yourselves 玩得开心 do it yourselves 你们自己做（DIY的相关表达）
🧠 助记
your（你们的）+ selves（自己们）= 你们自己 = yourselves
yourselves::/jɔːrˈselvz/:pron. 你们自己（yourself的:You should be proud of yourselves for all your hard work.
10. zigzag /ˈzɪɡzæɡ/ n./adj./v. 曲折（线）；之字形（的）；蜿蜒而行
📖 英文解释
A line or course having alternate right and left turns; to proceed in such a course.
✏️ 例句
The mountain path zigzagged up the steep hillside, making the climb more gradual but longer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a zigzag path/pattern 曲折小路/之字形图案 ★ zigzag between 在…之间穿行 cut in a zigzag 锯齿形裁剪 run in a zigzag 之字形跑动（战术）
🧠 助记
zig（向一边）+ zag（向另一边）= 左右摆动 = 之字形
zigzag::/ˈzɪɡzæɡ/:n./adj./v. 曲折（线）:The river follows a zigzag course through the valley.
11. zoom /zuːm/ v. 飞驰；急剧上涨；变焦 n. 变焦；快速移动
📖 英文解释
To move or travel very quickly; (of prices or values) to rise rapidly; to use a zoom lens on a camera.
✏️ 例句
The sports car zoomed past us at what must have been twice the speed limit.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ zoom in/out 放大/缩小（相机词汇） ★ prices zoom up 价格急剧上涨 zoom lens 变焦镜头 Zoom（视频会议软件，现在常用动词）
🧠 助记
zoom模拟快速飞驰的声音"嗖"，联想摩托车飞驰的声音
zoom::/zuːm/:v. 飞驰:The photographer zoomed in to capture the bird's details.
