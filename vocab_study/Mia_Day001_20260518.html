1. conversation /ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃn/ n. 对话，交谈
📖 英文解释
A talk between two or more people in which thoughts, feelings, or ideas are expressed.
✏️ 例句
They had a long conversation about the future of AI and its impact on education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：have/hold a conversation with sb；strike up a conversation（主动攀谈）；out of conversation（不再使用，废弃）。考研语境多出现于人际沟通、语言学习类阅读。
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ vers（转，turn）+ ation → 大家一起'转来转去'交流 → 对话。联想：converse（v. 交谈）是同根词。
conversation::/ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃn/:n. 对话，交谈:She started a conversation with the professor after class.
2. research /rɪˈsɜːtʃ/ n./v. 研究，调查
📖 英文解释
The systematic investigation into materials and sources to establish facts and reach new conclusions.
✏️ 例句
Scientists conduct extensive research to develop new vaccines for emerging diseases.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：do/conduct research on/into；research paper；groundbreaking research（突破性研究）。注意：research 作名词通常不可数，不说 a research，而说 a piece of research。
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ search（搜寻）→ 一次次深入搜寻 → 研究。谐音'瑞搜奇'→ 反复搜索奇特现象。
research::/rɪˈsɜːtʃ/:n./v. 研究，调查:The team's research into climate change produced alarming results.
3. single /ˈsɪŋɡl/ adj. 单一的，单个的；单身的 v. 挑出，选出
📖 英文解释
Only one; not one of several; unmarried or not in a relationship.
✏️ 例句
A single mistake in the experiment could invalidate months of research.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频多义词！① every single day（每一天，强调）② single out：选出、挑出（重要搭配）③ single-parent family（单亲家庭）④ 'not a single'强调'一个也没有'。
🧠 助记
联想乒乓球'单打'(singles)，一个人打 → 单个。故事：Single先生是单身汉，每天只做一件single（单一）的事。
single::/ˈsɪŋɡl/:adj. 单一的，单个的；单身的 v. 挑出，选出:She was singled out for praise by the committee.
4. family /ˈfæmɪli/ n. 家庭，家族；（生物）科；类，族
📖 英文解释
A group consisting of parents and their children; all descendants of a common ancestor.
✏️ 例句
Researchers found that family background significantly influences a child's academic achievement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
① nuclear family（核心家庭）② extended family（大家庭）③ family values（家庭价值观）④ 生物分类：the cat family（猫科）。考研社会学文章高频词。
🧠 助记
经典首字母缩写：Father And Mother, I Love You → FAMILY → 家庭。
family::/ˈfæmɪli/:n. 家庭，家族；（生物）科；类，族:The policy aims to support low-income families with children.
5. parent /ˈpeərənt/ n. 父（母）；双亲；根源 adj. 母（公司）的
📖 英文解释
A person's father or mother; an organization that owns or controls a subsidiary.
✏️ 例句
The parent company announced it would acquire three subsidiaries in the coming year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多义词：① 父母：parental leave（育儿假）② 母公司：parent company ③ 亲本（生物学）：parent cell。考研社会话题热词。
🧠 助记
parent → pare（削皮，如 pare an apple）→ 父母为孩子去粗取精、悉心培育。联想：par（同等）→ 现代教育中父母与孩子平等相待。
parent::/ˈpeərənt/:n. 父（母）；双亲；根源 adj. 母（公司）的:Parental involvement is crucial to a child's early development.
6. increase /ɪnˈkriːs/ (v.) /ˈɪŋkriːs/ (n.) v./n. 增加，增长，增强
📖 英文解释
To become or make greater in size, amount, intensity, or degree.
✏️ 例句
The number of international students has increased dramatically over the past decade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
注意动词与名词重音不同！搭配：on the increase（在增加中）；increase by 20%（增加了20%）；increase to 200（增加到200）。反义词：decrease / decline / fall / drop。
🧠 助记
in（进入）+ crease（褶皱）→ 不断折叠，折痕越来越多 → 增加。
increase::/ɪnˈkriːs/ (v.) /ˈɪŋkriːs/ (n.):v./n. 增加，增长，增强:The increase in carbon emissions is threatening global climate stability.
7. paragraph /ˈpærəɡrɑːf/ n. 段落；短文，小节
📖 英文解释
A distinct section of a piece of writing, usually dealing with a single theme.
✏️ 例句
The topic sentence of each paragraph should clearly state the main idea of that section.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研阅读题必备词：'In paragraph 3, the author suggests...'是真题常见题型。词根：para（旁边）+ graph（写）→ 独立成行的一段文字。
🧠 助记
para（旁边）+ graph（写/图，如 photograph）→ 写在一旁独成一节 → 段落。联想 graph 词根：biography（传记）、autograph（亲笔签名）。
paragraph::/ˈpærəɡrɑːf/:n. 段落；短文，小节:Read the first paragraph carefully to identify the author's main argument.
8. provide /prəˈvaɪd/ v. 提供，供给；（法律条款）规定
📖 英文解释
To make available for use; to stipulate in a legal document.
✏️ 例句
The government provides financial support for students from low-income families.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
超高频动词！① provide sb with sth = provide sth for sb ② provide that...（法律文本中）规定... 搭配：provide evidence / a solution / care / support。近义辨析：supply（补充供应）；offer（主动提出）；furnish（正式书面）。
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ vide（看，如 video）→ 向前预见需要，提前准备 → 提供。词根 vide = see：evidence（证据）、video、provide。
provide::/prəˈvaɪd/:v. 提供，供给；（法律条款）规定:The scholarship provides students with an opportunity to study abroad.
9. home /həʊm/ n. 家；故乡 adv. 回家；深入地 adj. 本国的，家庭的
📖 英文解释
The place where one lives; one's native country; (adv.) brought fully to mind.
✏️ 例句
The report brought home the reality of poverty to readers who had never experienced it.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多义副词用法：① drive/bring sth home：使…充分理解 ② home in on：瞄准，集中于（考研真题词） ③ at home with：对…熟悉 ④ nursing home（养老院）；home office（居家办公）。
🧠 助记
home 谐音'好美'→ 家总是最美的地方。延伸：homeless（无家可归）；homesick（思乡的）。
home::/həʊm/:n. 家；故乡 adv. 回家；深入地 adj. 本国的，家庭的:The documentary brought home the devastating effects of plastic pollution.
10. correspond /ˌkɒrɪˈspɒnd/ v. 相符，相当（to/with）；通信（with）
📖 英文解释
To be in agreement or harmony with; to communicate by exchanging letters.
✏️ 例句
The field data did not correspond with the predictions made by the theoretical model.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
两大含义：① correspond to/with：与…相符（= match）② correspond with sb：与某人通信。派生词：corresponding（对应的）；correspondingly；correspondence（信件往来；一致性）；correspondent（记者）。
🧠 助记
cor（共同）+ respond（回应）→ 双方互相回应 → 通信；彼此吻合 → 相符。
correspond::/ˌkɒrɪˈspɒnd/:v. 相符，相当（to/with）；通信（with）:His actions did not correspond to his stated beliefs.
11. education /ˌedʒuˈkeɪʃn/ n. 教育，培育；教育学；学识，修养
📖 英文解释
The process of receiving or giving systematic instruction.
✏️ 例句
Higher education plays a crucial role in fostering critical thinking and innovation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：higher education；compulsory education（义务教育）；vocational education（职业教育）；lifelong education（终身教育）。写作万能句：Education is the cornerstone of social progress.
🧠 助记
e（出）+ duc（引导，如 conduct）+ ation → 把内在潜能引导出来 → 教育。词根 duc = lead：produce、introduce、reduce。
education::/ˌedʒuˈkeɪʃn/:n. 教育，培育；教育学；学识，修养:Access to quality education remains unequal across different regions.
12. language /ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ/ n. 语言；措辞，语气；（计算机）语言

The method of human communication using words in a structured way; a system of symbols used in programming.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
① body language（肢体语言）② programming language（编程语言）③ language barrier（语言障碍）④ native language / mother tongue ⑤ figurative language（比喻语言）。语言学类文章必背词。
🧠 助记
词源：拉丁语 lingua（舌头）→ 用舌头发出的声音系统 → 语言。联想：bilingual（双语的）、lingual（语言的）。
language::/ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ/:n. 语言；措辞，语气；（计算机）语言:Learning a foreign language opens doors to new cultures and opportunities.

government::/ˈɡʌvənmənt/:n. 政府，内阁；管辖，统治；政体:Government investment in infrastructure can stimulate economic growth.
14. college /ˈkɒlɪdʒ/ n. 学院，大学；（英）大学学院；（美）四年制本科大学
📖 英文解释
An educational institution providing higher education or specialized training.
✏️ 例句
Many students choose a liberal arts college for its emphasis on critical thinking across multiple disciplines.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
英美差异：① 美式：college ≈ university（四年制本科）② 英式：university 下设的学院（如牛津各 college）③ community college（社区学院，两年制）④ college of law/medicine（法/医学院）。
🧠 助记
col（共同）+ lege（选择，来自拉丁 legere）→ 被共同选出的知识精英聚集之地 → 学院。联想：colleague（同事）= col + league → 同一联盟的人。
college::/ˈkɒlɪdʒ/:n. 学院，大学；（英）大学学院；（美）四年制本科大学:She graduated from a prestigious college with a degree in economics.
15. system /ˈsɪstəm/ n. 系统，体制；制度；方法，秩序

✏️ 例句
The educational system in Finland is renowned for its emphasis on equality and teacher autonomy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：solar system（太阳系）；immune system（免疫系统）；system of government（政府体制）；beat the system（钻漏洞）。派生：systematic（adj.）；systematically（adv.）。
🧠 助记
sys（共同，syn-）+ tem（放置）→ 把各部分有序放在一起 → 系统。
system::/ˈsɪstəm/:n. 系统，体制；制度；方法，秩序:A reliable healthcare system is essential for social stability.
16. result /rɪˈzʌlt/ n. 结果，成效；（pl.）成绩 v. 导致（in）；由…产生（from）
📖 英文解释
A consequence or outcome; to happen as a consequence.
✏️ 例句
The experiment resulted in a groundbreaking discovery that changed our understanding of genetics.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研必考词，注意介词：① result in sth：导致…（= lead to）② result from sth：由…导致（= arise from）③ as a result (of)：因此（写作常用）④ without result：徒劳。
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ sult（跳，如 somersault 翻筋斗）→ 事情之后'跳出来'的东西 → 结果。
result::/rɪˈzʌlt/:n. 结果，成效；（pl.）成绩 v. 导致（in）；由…产生（from）:Poor sleep habits can result in decreased concentration and academic performance.
17. money /ˈmʌni/ n. 钱，金钱；财富；货币

money::/ˈmʌni/:n. 钱，金钱；财富；货币:Investing public money wisely is one of the government's core responsibilities.
18. believe /bɪˈliːv/ v. 相信，认为；信仰；料想
📖 英文解释
To accept as true; to have religious faith; to think something is likely.
✏️ 例句
Many researchers believe that early childhood education has a lasting impact on cognitive development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
写作常用：① believe in sth：信仰，信任 ② It is believed that...（普遍认为，被动句式）③ lead sb to believe：使某人相信 ④ beyond belief：难以置信。辨析：believe（相信真实性）vs trust（信任人）。
🧠 助记
be + lieve（life 的词根）→ 用生命去接受某事为真 → 相信。趣记：believe 里藏着 lie（谎言）→ 相信前要辨别谎言。
believe::/bɪˈliːv/:v. 相信，认为；信仰；料想:It is widely believed that technology is transforming the nature of work.

computer::/kəmˈpjuːtə/:n. 计算机，电脑；（旧义）计算者:Computer literacy has become an essential skill in the modern workplace.

24. during /ˈdjʊərɪŋ/ prep. 在…期间；在…（某一时刻）
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
词根 dur = last/endure（持续）：duration（持续时间）；durable（耐久的）；endure（忍耐）→ 持续着的那段时间 → 在…期间。
during::/ˈdjʊərɪŋ/:prep. 在…期间；在…（某一时刻）:The economy contracted sharply during the financial crisis of 2008.

The new regulations do not allow companies to collect user data without explicit consent.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
结构：① allow sb to do sth（最常见）② allow for sth：考虑到，预留余地（= take into account）③ allow of：容许（正式）。辨析：permit（正式）；allow（中性）；let（口语）。
🧠 助记
al（加强）+ low（低，谦逊）→ 低下头谦逊地同意 → 允许。谐音：'阿喽'→ 低声说'喽，允许了'。
allow::/əˈlaʊ/:v. 允许，准许；给予，提供；承认，考虑到:The flexible schedule allows students to balance academic work with extracurricular activities.
27. require /rɪˈkwaɪə/ v. 需要，要求；规定，命令
📖 英文解释
To need for a particular purpose; to officially insist on; to order.
✏️ 例句
The scholarship program requires applicants to submit a research proposal with their application.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
结构辨析：① require sth ② require sb to do sth ③ require doing sth（= need doing，需要被做）④ as required（按要求）。派生：requirement（n.要求，条件）；required course（必修课）。
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ quire（寻求，拉丁 quaerere）→ 一再寻求追问 → 需要、要求。词根 quir/quest = ask：inquiry（询问）；question；quest（探索）。
require::/rɪˈkwaɪə/:v. 需要，要求；规定，命令:Most graduate programs require students to complete a thesis or dissertation.
28. car /kɑː/ n. 轿车，汽车；车厢；（缆）车
📖 英文解释
A road vehicle with four wheels powered by an engine or electric motor; any vehicle running on rails.
✏️ 例句
The shift toward electric cars is accelerating as governments implement stricter emissions standards.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研环保话题常见：① electric car / self-driving car（无人驾驶）② car emissions（汽车排放）③ car pool（拼车）④ cable car（缆车）；dining car（餐车，火车上）⑤ car-sharing scheme（共享汽车）。
🧠 助记
谐音'卡'→ 开车被红灯'卡'住了 → 车。词源：凯尔特语 carros → 拉丁 carrus → 英语 car。
car::/kɑː/:n. 轿车，汽车；车厢；（缆）车:Car dependency has contributed to urban sprawl and increased carbon emissions.
29. statement /ˈsteɪtmənt/ n. 声明，陈述；报告；（账单）结算单
📖 英文解释
A definite or clear expression of something; a formal account of facts or views.
✏️ 例句
The minister issued a formal statement denying any involvement in the financial scandal.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研阅读题高频词：'the author's statement in paragraph 2 suggests that...'。搭配：make a statement；mission statement（使命宣言）；bank statement（银行对账单）；official/public/written statement。
🧠 助记
state（陈述，状态）+ ment（名词后缀）→ 正式陈述某种状态 → 声明。联想：国家（state）级的正式陈述 → statement。
statement::/ˈsteɪtmənt/:n. 声明，陈述；报告；（账单）结算单:The company's official statement failed to address the core concerns of its employees.
30. society /səˈsaɪəti/ n. 社会；社团，协会；上流社会
📖 英文解释
The aggregate of people living together in an ordered community; an organization formed for a particular purpose.
✏️ 例句
A democratic society depends on an informed citizenry that actively participates in governance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
① civil society（公民社会）② consumer society（消费社会）③ society at large / as a whole（整个社会）④ high society（上流社会）⑤ student society（学生社团）。派生：social；socialize；societal（书面正式）；sociology（社会学）。
🧠 助记
词根 soc/soci = companion（同伴）：social；society；associate（同事）；sociology。soci → 同伴聚集在一起 → 社会。
society::/səˈsaɪəti/:n. 社会；社团，协会；上流社会:Modern society faces unprecedented challenges from technological disruption.
