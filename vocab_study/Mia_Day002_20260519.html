1. lead /liːd/ v. 带领，引导；导致；n. 领先，线索
📖 英文解释
To guide or direct someone or something; to be in front or in charge.
✏️ 例句
Effective teachers lead students to think critically rather than simply memorize facts.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ lead to sth 导致某事（因果关系高频句型） ★ lead to + doing / n. 后接名词或动名词 ☆ take the lead 带头，率先 辨析：lead / guide / direct：lead 侧重走在前面带路；guide 侧重提供指导；direct 侧重发出指令
🧠 助记
lead = 领(l)+前(ead谐音"前"的粤语)——站在最前面领路
lead::/liːd/:v. 带领，引导:Hard work leads to success.

📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
Researchers are working to build a more sustainable model of economic development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ build up 积累，增强（build up confidence / strength） ★ build on sth 在…基础上发展（build on existing research） ☆ build bridges 消除隔阂，搭建沟通桥梁（常见比喻用法）
🧠 助记
build 谐音「毕了」——毕业之后建造自己的事业大厦
build::/bɪld/:v. 建造，建立:They built a bridge over the river.
3. off /ɒf/ adv./prep. 离开，关闭；不工作；从…脱落
📖 英文解释
Away from a place or position; not operating or functioning; indicating removal or distance.
✏️ 例句
The scientist switched off the equipment after the experiment was complete.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ call off 取消（call off a meeting） ★ put off 推迟（put off doing sth 高频搭配） ★ set off 出发；引发（set off an alarm） 辨析：off / away：off 侧重状态（灯关了、人离开了）；away 侧重距离上的远离
🧠 助记
off 像开关断开的形状——电源断了就是 off
off::/ɒf/:adv./prep. 离开，关闭:Please turn off the lights before leaving.
4. spend /spend/ v. 花费（时间/金钱）；度过
📖 英文解释
To use money to pay for something; to use time doing a particular activity.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ spend time/money on sth 在某事上花时间/金钱 ★ spend time doing sth 花时间做某事（注意用 doing 不用 to do） 辨析：spend / cost / pay / take：spend 主语是人；cost 主语是物；pay 强调付款行为；take 主语常为事情（It takes sb time to do）
🧠 助记
spend → s+pend（钱袋，如 pendulum）——钱从口袋里摆出去就花掉了
spend::/spend/:v. 花费（时间/金钱）:She spends two hours reading every day.

6. cost /kɒst/ n. 费用，代价；v. 花费，使付出代价

10. effect /ɪˈfekt/ n. 效果，影响，结果；v. 实现，引起
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ have an effect on 对…有影响（核心搭配） ★ in effect 实际上，事实上 ★ take effect 生效，起效 辨析：effect / affect：effect 通常作名词（结果）；affect 通常作动词（影响）
🧠 助记
effect = e（出来）+fect（做）——做出来的东西就是效果
effect::/ɪˈfekt/:n. 效果，影响，结果:Exercise has a positive effect on health.
11. comprehension /ˌkɒmprɪˈhenʃn/ n. 理解，理解力；阅读理解（题型）
📖 英文解释
The ability to understand something; an exercise in which students read a text and answer questions about it.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ reading comprehension 阅读理解（考研题型） ★ beyond comprehension 难以理解，超出理解范围 ★ 词根：com（全部）+prehend（抓住）+ion → 全部抓住 → 理解 辨析：comprehension / understanding：comprehension 更正式，常用于学术；understanding 更口语化
🧠 助记
com（全）+pre（提前）+hension（抓住）→ 提前把所有意思都抓住 = 理解
comprehension::/ˌkɒmprɪˈhenʃn/:n. 理解，理解力:This passage tests reading comprehension.
12. something /ˈsʌmθɪŋ/ pron. 某事，某物；有些，有点

★ something of a 有点，算是（He is something of an expert.） ★ something like 大约，有几分像 ★ make something of oneself 有所成就 辨析：something / anything：something 用于肯定句；anything 用于否定/疑问句
🧠 助记
some（某些）+ thing（事物）→ 某些事物 = something，直接组合记忆
something::/ˈsʌmθɪŋ/:pron. 某事，某物:She said something important in the meeting.
