1. care /kɛr/ v./n. 关心；在意；照料
📖 英文解释
To feel concern or interest about something or someone; to look after or provide for someone.
✏️ 例句
Researchers argue that governments should care more about the long-term environmental costs of economic development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ care about sth 关心某事（最常考搭配） ★ care for sb 照料某人；喜欢某人 take care of 负责；照顾
🧠 助记
care = c（看）+ are（是）→ 你「是」要「看」顾的人，所以是「关心」。
care::/kɛr/:v./n. 关心:She cares deeply about education reform.
2. benefit /ˈbenɪfɪt/ n. 益处；好处；福利 v. 受益；对…有益
📖 英文解释
An advantage, profit, or gain; something that promotes well-being or improves a situation.
✏️ 例句
The study found that moderate exercise provides significant health benefits for people of all ages.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ benefit from sth 从某事中受益（高频考点） ★ for the benefit of 为了…的利益 mutual benefit 互利
🧠 助记
bene-（好）+ fit（适合）→ 适合好事 → 好处、益处。bene 同 bonus（奖金）词根。
benefit::/ˈbenɪfɪt/:n. 益处:Regular reading benefits vocabulary development.
3. program /ˈproʊɡræm/ n. 项目；计划；节目；程序 v. 编程
📖 英文解释
A plan or schedule of activities, procedures, or events; a set of coded instructions for a computer.
✏️ 例句
The government launched a new program to provide affordable housing for low-income families.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ government program 政府项目（阅读高频词） ★ training program 培训计划 program director 项目负责人
🧠 助记
pro（前）+ gram（写）→ 提前写好的计划 → 项目、方案。
program::/ˈproʊɡræm/:n. 项目:The scholarship program benefits thousands of students.
4. worker /ˈwɜːrkər/ n. 工人；劳动者；工作者
📖 英文解释
A person who is employed for wages or salary, especially at a manual or industrial job.
✏️ 例句
Factory workers in developing countries often face long hours and low wages despite producing goods for global markets.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ skilled worker 技术工人 ★ white-collar/blue-collar worker 白领/蓝领 migrant worker 外来务工人员（社会话题高频）
🧠 助记
work（工作）+ -er（人）→ 工作的人 → 工人。超简单构词。
worker::/ˈwɜːrkər/:n. 工人:Workers demanded better safety conditions.
5. market /ˈmɑːrkɪt/ n. 市场；交易市场 v. 销售；推销
📖 英文解释
A place or system in which commercial dealings take place; the buying and selling of goods and services.
✏️ 例句
The rapid expansion of the digital market has fundamentally changed how businesses reach their customers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ market economy 市场经济（政治经济类必考） ★ job market 就业市场 ★ market share 市场份额
🧠 助记
market = mart（商店，如 Walmart）+ et → 很多商店聚集的地方 → 市场。
market::/ˈmɑːrkɪt/:n. 市场:The global market for electric vehicles is growing rapidly.
6. teach /tiːtʃ/ v. 教；讲授；教导
📖 英文解释
To impart knowledge or instruct someone in a subject; to show or explain how to do something.
✏️ 例句
Studies suggest that the most effective way to teach critical thinking is through open-ended discussion rather than rote memorization.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ teach sb sth / teach sth to sb 教某人某事 ★ teach sb to do sth 教某人做某事 teach a lesson 给…一个教训
🧠 助记
t + each（每个）→ 对每个人都讲 → 教学。联想：老师对每个学生都讲解。
teach::/tiːtʃ/:v. 教:She teaches chemistry at a local high school.
7. support /səˈpɔːrt/ v./n. 支持；资助；赡养；支撑
📖 英文解释
To bear weight; to give assistance, encouragement, or financial help to someone or something.
✏️ 例句
The report argues that public libraries play a critical role in supporting lifelong learning in communities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ support for sth 对某事的支持（介词搭配常考） ★ in support of 支持、赞成 ★ financial support 经济支持
🧠 助记
sup-（下面）+ port（扛、带）→ 从下面扛着 → 支撑、支持。port 同 portable（可携带的）。
support::/səˈpɔːrt/:v./n. 支持:The policy received strong public support.
8. friend /frend/ n. 朋友；友人
📖 英文解释
A person whom one knows, likes, and trusts; a person who is on good terms with another.
✏️ 例句
Social media has changed how people define friendship, making it possible to have hundreds of online friends while remaining socially isolated.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ close/best friend 亲密朋友 ★ make friends with 与…交朋友 friend of a friend 朋友的朋友（社会学语境常见）
🧠 助记
fr + end → friend 到最「end」（终）都在的人 → 真正的朋友。
friend::/frend/:n. 朋友:A true friend supports you in difficult times.
9. skill /skɪl/ n. 技能；技术；技巧
📖 英文解释
The ability to do something well, usually gained through training or experience.
✏️ 例句
Employers increasingly value soft skills such as communication and teamwork alongside technical expertise.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ develop/acquire skills 培养/获得技能 ★ skill set 技能组合（职场/教育话题高频） life skills 生活技能
🧠 助记
skill → 联想：「死磕 ill（苦）」→ 死磕出来的苦功夫 → 技能。
skill::/skɪl/:n. 技能:Critical thinking is a vital skill for graduate students.
10. move /muːv/ v. 移动；搬家；感动；采取行动 n. 行动；步骤
📖 英文解释
To change position or place; to cause someone to feel strong emotion; to take action.
✏️ 例句
The documentary was so powerful that it moved the audience to donate generously to the charity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ move to do sth 采取行动做某事 ★ be moved by 被…感动 ★ make a move 采取行动；走棋
🧠 助记
move → 联想电影（movie）→ 电影让人「感动」，而且画面在「移动」。
move::/muːv/:v. 移动:The speech moved many listeners to tears.
11. rather /ˈræðər/ adv. 相当；宁愿；更确切地说
📖 英文解释
To a significant degree; used to indicate preference or to introduce a more accurate description.
✏️ 例句
The results were rather surprising, suggesting that diet plays a more important role than genetics in determining health outcomes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ rather than 而不是（考研最高频搭配，替换关系） ★ would rather...than... 宁愿…而不愿 ★ or rather 更确切地说
🧠 助记
rather = r + ather → rather than 想象「宁愿跑（r-）也不愿」→ 宁愿…而不是。
rather::/ˈræðər/:adv. 相当:He chose to study rather than watch TV.
12. create /kriˈeɪt/ v. 创造；创建；引起
📖 英文解释
To bring something into existence; to produce or cause something to happen.
✏️ 例句
Technological innovation has the potential to create new industries while simultaneously displacing traditional jobs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ create opportunities 创造机会 ★ create awareness 提高意识 create a problem 制造问题（负面语境）
🧠 助记
cre-（生长，同 grow）+ ate → 使其生长出来 → 创造。联想：Creator（造物主）。
create::/kriˈeɪt/:v. 创造:Scientists aim to create cleaner energy sources.
13. century /ˈsentʃəri/ n. 世纪；百年
📖 英文解释
A period of one hundred years, especially as used in numbering years from a particular epoch.
✏️ 例句
The 21st century has witnessed unprecedented advances in artificial intelligence and biotechnology.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the 21st century 21世纪（时代背景常用） ★ for centuries 几个世纪以来 turn of the century 世纪之交
🧠 助记
cent-（百，同 percent）+ ury → 百年 → 世纪。cent 还出现在 centimeter（厘米）、century plant（百年仙人掌）。
century::/ˈsentʃəri/:n. 世纪:Climate change is the defining challenge of this century.
14. eat /iːt/ v. 吃；进食；侵蚀
📖 英文解释
To put food into the mouth and swallow it; to gradually destroy or wear away.
✏️ 例句
Research indicates that people who eat a diet rich in plant-based foods tend to have lower rates of chronic disease.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ eat into 侵蚀；耗尽（时间/资金语境） ★ eat out 在外就餐 eat healthy 健康饮食（阅读话题）
🧠 助记
eat → 联想：E-A-T → Every Afternoon Tea → 每天下午喝茶（吃东西）。
eat::/iːt/:v. 吃:Eating processed foods regularly can harm your health.
15. consider /kənˈsɪdər/ v. 考虑；认为；顾及
📖 英文解释
To think carefully about something, especially before making a decision; to regard something in a specified way.
✏️ 例句
The committee will consider all proposals before making a final recommendation on the new policy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ consider doing sth 考虑做某事（接动名词，不接不定式） ★ consider sb/sth (to be) 认为…是 ★ take into consideration 考虑进去
🧠 助记
con-（一起）+ sider（星星，同 sidereal）→ 古人「一起观星」占卜做决定 → 仔细考虑。
consider::/kənˈsɪdər/:v. 考虑:Consider all the evidence before reaching a conclusion.
16. industry /ˈɪndəstri/ n. 工业；行业；勤勉
📖 英文解释
Economic activity concerned with the processing of raw materials and the manufacture of goods; a particular branch of economic activity.
✏️ 例句
The rise of the gig economy has disrupted traditional industry models and raised questions about workers' rights.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the ... industry ...行业（阅读高频结构：the fashion/tech industry） ★ heavy/light industry 重工业/轻工业 ★ industrial revolution 工业革命
🧠 助记
indus-（勤）+ try → 勤奋努力地做 → 工业（需要大量劳动）；也有「勤勉」之意。
industry::/ˈɪndəstri/:n. 工业:The tourism industry suffered greatly during the pandemic.
17. themselves /ðəmˈselvz/ pron. 他们自己；他们亲自（反身代词）
📖 英文解释
The reflexive form of 'they'; used when the subject and object of the action are the same people.
✏️ 例句
Students who teach themselves a foreign language often develop stronger problem-solving abilities than those who rely solely on classroom instruction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ by themselves 独自；靠自己（= alone / on their own） ★ in themselves 就其本身而言 teach/support themselves 自学/自力更生
🧠 助记
them + selves → 他们（them）的自我（selves）→ 他们自己。构词逻辑同 myself、yourself。
themselves::/ðəmˈselvz/:pron. 他们自己:They completed the project entirely by themselves.
18. process /ˈprɑːses/ n. 过程；进程；程序 v. 处理；加工
📖 英文解释
A series of actions or steps taken to achieve a particular end; to perform a series of operations on data or materials.
✏️ 例句
The process of globalization has accelerated cultural exchange while also intensifying economic competition between nations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in the process of 在…过程中 ★ decision-making process 决策过程 ★ process information 处理信息（认知科学语境）
🧠 助记
pro-（向前）+ cess（走，同 access）→ 向前走的步骤 → 过程。
process::/ˈprɑːses/:n. 过程:Learning a language is a long process requiring persistence.
19. rate /reɪt/ n. 比率；速率；费率 v. 评价；估价
📖 英文解释
A measure, quantity, or frequency expressed as a proportion of another; to assign a value or rank to something.
✏️ 例句
The unemployment rate has fallen to its lowest level in a decade, reflecting a significant recovery in the labor market.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at a rate of 以…的速度/比率 ★ growth/crime/birth rate 增长/犯罪/出生率 ★ rate sth as 把某事评为
🧠 助记
rate → r + ate（吃）→ 「吃」掉的速度 → 速率；引申为评分。
rate::/reɪt/:n. 比率:The literacy rate in the country rose sharply after the reform.
20. house /haʊs/ n. 房子；家庭；议院 v. 给…提供住所
📖 英文解释
A building for human habitation; to provide accommodation or storage space.
✏️ 例句
Rising house prices in major cities have made homeownership increasingly unaffordable for young professionals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ house prices 房价（社会话题热词） ★ housing market 房产市场 ★ the House of Representatives 众议院（政治文章）
🧠 助记
house → h + ouse → 联想汉字「屋」→ 房子。
house::/haʊs/:n. 房子:Many young adults cannot afford to buy a house.
21. culture /ˈkʌltʃər/ n. 文化；文明；培养；培植
📖 英文解释
The ideas, customs, and social behaviour of a particular people or society; the cultivation of bacteria or cells.
✏️ 例句
Understanding the culture of a society is essential for designing effective public health interventions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ cultural diversity 文化多样性 ★ pop culture 流行文化 ★ corporate culture 企业文化（管理学语境）
🧠 助记
cult-（耕种、培育）+ ure → 人类精心培育的精神成果 → 文化。agriculture（农业）同根。
culture::/ˈkʌltʃər/:n. 文化:Culture shapes how people perceive the world around them.
22. power /ˈpaʊər/ n. 力量；权力；功率；电力 v. 驱动；供电
📖 英文解释
The ability to do something or act in a particular way; political authority; energy produced by mechanical, electrical, or other means.
✏️ 例句
The concentration of economic power in the hands of a few corporations raises serious concerns about market competition.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ power to do sth 做某事的能力 ★ in power 执政；当权 ★ nuclear/solar power 核能/太阳能（环境话题）
🧠 助记
power → pow（打击声）+ er → 打出去的力 → 力量、权力。
power::/ˈpaʊər/:n. 力量:Renewable power sources are crucial for a sustainable future.
23. share /ʃɛr/ v. 分享；共享；分担 n. 份额；股份
📖 英文解释
To have, use, or give a portion of something; one of the equal parts into which a company's capital is divided.
✏️ 例句
Sharing economy platforms such as ride-hailing apps have transformed urban transportation by enabling individuals to share resources.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ share sth with sb 与某人分享某事 ★ market share 市场份额（商业阅读必考） ★ share the view/opinion 持相同观点
🧠 助记
share → sh + are → 「are」（是）的部分 → 各自的一份 → 分享、份额。
share::/ʃɛr/:v. 分享:The company's market share grew by 15% last year.
24. million /ˈmɪljən/ num./n. 百万；大量
📖 英文解释
The number 1,000,000; an extremely large number or amount.
✏️ 例句
Over three million people are expected to attend the annual cultural festival this year, making it the largest in the country's history.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ millions of 数以百万计的（强调大量） ★ one in a million 百里挑一 billion（十亿）、trillion（万亿）数量级词同组记忆
🧠 助记
mill（磨）+ ion → 磨了一百万转 → 百万。联想 millionaire（百万富翁）。
million::/ˈmɪljən/:num./n. 百万:Millions of people worldwide lack access to clean water.
25. lot /lɒt/ n./adv. 很多；大量；抽签；地块
📖 英文解释
A large number or amount; to divide into lots; a plot of land.
✏️ 例句
A lot of research has been conducted on the relationship between sleep deprivation and cognitive performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a lot of / lots of 大量（口语书面都常用） ★ quite a lot 相当多 ★ a lot 作副词修饰动词：improve a lot

26. sense /sens/ n. 感觉；意义；判断力 v. 感知；意识到
📖 英文解释
A faculty by which the body perceives an external stimulus; meaning; good judgment or wisdom.
✏️ 例句
In a broad sense, education encompasses not only formal schooling but also the learning that takes place throughout life.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

sens-（感，同 sensitive）+ e → 感知 → 感觉；引申为「感觉到的意义」。
sense::/sens/:n. 感觉:It makes sense to invest in education early.
27. lose /luːz/ v. 失去；迷失；输掉；亏损
📖 英文解释
To be deprived of or cease to have something; to fail to win; to become unable to find.
✏️ 例句
Communities that lose their traditional industries often struggle to attract new investment and rebuild their economies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ lose sight of 忽视；忘记目标 ★ lose one's way 迷失方向（比喻义） ★ lose vs. loose 注意拼写区别
🧠 助记
lose → l + ose → 联想「唉~哦（o-se）」→ 输了叹气的声音 → 失去、输掉。
lose::/luːz/:v. 失去:We cannot afford to lose focus on the key issues.
28. against /əˈɡenst/ prep. 反对；违反；靠着；对比
📖 英文解释
In opposition to; in contact with; in contrast or competition with.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be against sth 反对某事（最常见） ★ argue/vote against 争辩/投票反对 ★ against a background of 在…背景下（书面高频）

against::/əˈɡenst/:prep. 反对:Many citizens voted against the proposed tax increase.

The rise of social media has fundamentally altered the way political campaigns are conducted and how voters receive information.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ give rise to 引起；导致（书面高频搭配） ★ rise to power 掌权 ★ on the rise 在上升中（形容趋势）

rise → r + ise → 联想「日升」（日/sun rises）→ 太阳升起 → 上升。
rise::/raɪz/:v. 上升:Temperatures have been on the rise for the past decade.

The strength and vitality required for sustained physical or mental activity; power derived from physical or chemical resources.
✏️ 例句
Transitioning to renewable energy sources is widely regarded as the most effective strategy for combating climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

en-（进入）+ ergy（工作，同 work/erg）→ 进入工作状态的驱动力 → 能量、精力。
energy::/ˈenərdʒi/:n. 精力:Solar energy is becoming increasingly cost-effective.
