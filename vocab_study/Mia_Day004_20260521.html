1. control /kənˈtroʊl/ v./n. 控制；管理；抑制
📖 英文解释
To exercise authoritative or dominating influence over something; the power to direct or restrain something.
✏️ 例句
The government introduced stricter regulations to control carbon emissions from industrial facilities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ under control 处于控制之中 ★ out of control 失控 ★ control over/of 对…的控制
🧠 助记
con-（一起）+ trol（转，同 roll）→ 把所有方向都转过来 → 全面掌控。
control::/kənˈtroʊl/:v./n. 控制:Effective leaders know how to control their emotions.
2. space /speɪs/ n. 空间；太空；间距 v. 隔开
📖 英文解释
A continuous area or expanse which is free, available, or unoccupied; the universe beyond the earth's atmosphere.
✏️ 例句
Urban planners argue that green spaces in cities are essential for residents' mental health and well-being.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ outer space 外太空 ★ living/working space 生活/工作空间 ★ space for 留有…空间（比喻义：space for creativity）
🧠 助记
space → s + pace（步）→ 走几步就能到达的「空间」 → 空间、太空。
space::/speɪs/:n. 空间:Cities need more open space for community activities.
3. ability /əˈbɪlɪti/ n. 能力；才能；才干
📖 英文解释
The capacity to do something; the possession of the means or skill to do something.
✏️ 例句
Critical reading is an ability that students must develop in order to succeed in graduate-level academic work.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the ability to do sth 做某事的能力（最常见结构） ★ ability in/at 在某方面的能力 ★ natural/innate ability 天生能力
🧠 助记
able（能够）+ -ity（名词后缀）→ 能够做的程度 → 能力。
ability::/əˈbɪlɪti/:n. 能力:Children have an amazing ability to learn new languages.
4. animal /ˈænɪməl/ n. 动物；兽 adj. 动物的
📖 英文解释
A living organism that feeds on organic matter, typically having specialized sense organs and nervous system.
✏️ 例句
The rapid destruction of natural habitats threatens thousands of animal species with extinction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ animal rights 动物权益（伦理话题） ★ animal behavior/instinct 动物行为/本能 endangered animal 濒危动物
🧠 助记
anim-（生命、灵魂，同 animate）+ al → 有生命的 → 动物。
animal::/ˈænɪməl/:n. 动物:Animal experiments are strictly regulated in many countries.
5. enough /ɪˈnʌf/ adj./adv. 足够的；充分地
📖 英文解释
As much or as many as required; to the required degree or extent.
✏️ 例句
Many critics argue that governments are not doing enough to address the root causes of poverty and inequality.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ enough to do sth 足以做某事 ★ not ... enough 不够…（adj./adv. + enough） ★ sure enough 果然（书面常用副词短语）
🧠 助记
e + nough → 联想「已经够了（e-nough）」→ 够了，足够。nough 谐音「够」。
enough::/ɪˈnʌf/:adj./adv. 足够的:There is not enough funding for public education.
6. value /ˈvæljuː/ n. 价值；重要性；价值观 v. 重视；估价
📖 英文解释
The regard that something is held to deserve; the importance or usefulness of something; principles and standards.
✏️ 例句
Economic analyses rarely capture the full value of ecosystem services such as clean water and biodiversity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ place/attach value on 重视（某事） ★ core values 核心价值观 ★ face value 表面价值；票面价值
🧠 助记
val-（强壮、价值，同 valid）+ ue → 有价值的东西 → 价值；引申为重视。
value::/ˈvæljuː/:n. 价值:Society should place greater value on environmental protection.
7. week /wiːk/ n. 周；星期
📖 英文解释
A period of seven days; the period from Monday to Friday as contrasted with the weekend.
✏️ 例句
The government announced that the new policy would come into effect within two weeks of parliamentary approval.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ this/last/next week 本/上/下周 ★ week after week 一周又一周（表持续） ★ on a weekly basis 每周（副词短语）
🧠 助记
week → w + eek → 联想「一周七天就一声 eek（叹气）」→ 过了一个星期。
week::/wiːk/:n. 周:Students should review vocabulary at least once a week.
8. individual /ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl/ adj. 个人的；独特的 n. 个人；个体
📖 英文解释
Relating to a single person or thing rather than to a group; a single human being as distinct from a group.
✏️ 例句
While collective action is necessary, individual choices about consumption and lifestyle also play a significant role in reducing environmental impact.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ individual rights 个人权利 ★ on an individual basis 逐个地 ★ individual vs. society 个人与社会（哲学/社会常考对比）
🧠 助记
in-（不）+ divid-（分）+ ual → 不可再分割的单位 → 个人、个体。divide（分割）同根。
individual::/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl/:adj. 个人的:Each individual is responsible for their own learning.
9. term /tɜːrm/ n. 条款；期限；学期；术语 v. 称为
📖 英文解释
A word or phrase used to describe a thing or express a concept; a fixed period of time; conditions under which action is taken.
✏️ 例句
The contract included several terms that restricted the company's ability to hire from competitors.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in terms of 就…而言（超高频，写作必备） ★ long-term / short-term 长期/短期 ★ term of office 任期
🧠 助记
term → t + erm → 联想「这个…嗯（erm）…术语」→ 术语；也联想到「期限」像期末（term）。
term::/tɜːrm/:n. 条款:In terms of quality, this product is superior.
10. away /əˈweɪ/ adv. 离开；在远处；消失
📖 英文解释
To or at a distance from a particular place, person, or thing; so as to be removed or gone.
✏️ 例句
As rural populations continue to move away from agriculture, governments must find ways to support displaced workers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ give away 放弃；赠送；泄露 ★ take/throw away 拿走/扔掉 ★ far away 遥远的（常修饰地点）
🧠 助记
a + way → 向那条「路（way）」走去 → 离开、远去。
away::/əˈweɪ/:adv. 离开:Young people are moving away from rural areas to cities.
11. fall /fɔːl/ v. 下降；倒下；降落 n. 秋天；下降；瀑布
📖 英文解释
To move downward under the force of gravity; to decrease in amount or level; the autumn season.
✏️ 例句
Global stock markets fell sharply following the announcement of new trade tariffs between major economies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ fall behind 落后（学业/工作） ★ fall into 陷入（困境） ★ fall short of 未达到（期望/标准）
🧠 助记
fall → 联想：「瀑布（waterfall）」往下「落」 → 下降、秋天（万物凋落）。
fall::/fɔːl/:v. 下降:Temperatures fall sharply in the northern regions during winter.
12. art /ɑːrt/ n. 艺术；美术；技巧；人文学科
📖 英文解释
The expression of human creative skill, typically in visual form; the various branches of creative activity; skill in doing something.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ work of art 艺术作品 ★ arts and sciences 文科与理科 ★ the art of ... 做某事的艺术/技巧（如 the art of communication）
🧠 助记
art → ar（联想「啊」感叹）+ t → 让人发出「啊！」感叹的东西 → 艺术。
art::/ɑːrt/:n. 艺术:Great art often reflects the social issues of its time.
13. decision /dɪˈsɪʒən/ n. 决定；决议；决策
📖 英文解释
A conclusion or resolution reached after consideration; the action of deciding something.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ make a decision 做出决定（最常见搭配） ★ reach/come to a decision 达成决定 ★ decision-making process 决策过程
🧠 助记

decision::/dɪˈsɪʒən/:n. 决定:Making an informed decision requires careful analysis.
14. interest /ˈɪntrəst/ n. 兴趣；利息；利益 v. 使感兴趣

interest::/ˈɪntrəst/:n. 兴趣:A conflict of interest should always be declared upfront.

drive::/draɪv/:v. 驾驶:Curiosity drives scientific discovery.

produce::/prəˈdjuːs/ (v.) /ˈprɒdjuːs/ (n.)/:v. 生产:The factory produces over 10,000 units per day.
18. complete /kəmˈpliːt/ adj. 完全的；完整的 v. 完成；填写

brain::/breɪn/:n. 大脑:Regular reading stimulates brain activity and memory.
