1. knowledge /ˈnɒlɪdʒ/ n. 知识；了解
📖 英文解释
facts, information, and skills acquired through experience or education
✏️ 例句
A thorough knowledge of economics is essential for understanding the current job market crisis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ knowledge of sth 对某事的了解 ★ general/common knowledge 常识（考研作文高频） prior knowledge 先验知识
🧠 助记
know（知道）+ ledge（岩架）→ 站在知识的岩架上俯瞰世界
knowledge::/ˈnɒlɪdʒ/:n. 知识:Her knowledge of biology helped her pass the exam.
2. stress /stres/ n./v. 压力；强调
📖 英文解释
pressure or tension exerted on a material object; to emphasize a word or idea
✏️ 例句
Researchers found that chronic stress can significantly impair cognitive performance in graduate students.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ under stress 在压力下（写作常用） ★ stress on sth 强调某事 stress management 压力管理
🧠 助记
s + tress（辫子）→ 被辫子捆住，压力很大
stress::/stres/:n./v. 压力:Too much stress affects memory and focus.
3. matter /ˈmætər/ n./v. 事情；问题；重要
📖 英文解释
physical substance; a subject or situation; to be of importance
✏️ 例句
No matter how difficult the circumstances, perseverance will ultimately determine your success.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ no matter how/what/when 无论……（让步状语从句） ★ as a matter of fact 事实上 it doesn't matter 没关系
🧠 助记
mat（垫子）+ ter → 垫子上的事情，都很重要
matter::/ˈmætər/:n./v. 事情:It doesn't matter if you fail once; try again.
4. involve /ɪnˈvɒlv/ v. 涉及；包含；使卷入
📖 英文解释
to include or affect as a necessary part; to cause someone to take part in something
✏️ 例句
The research project involved collecting data from over five hundred college graduates.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ involve doing sth 包含做某事 ★ be involved in 参与；卷入 involve sb in sth 使某人参与某事
🧠 助记
in（进入）+ volve（旋转）→ 旋转进入→卷入其中
involve::/ɪnˈvɒlv/:v. 涉及:The task involves reading hundreds of papers.
5. raise /reɪz/ v. 举起；提出；筹集；抚养
📖 英文解释
to lift something to a higher position; to bring up a topic; to collect money
✏️ 例句
The professor raised an important question about the methodology of the experiment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ raise a question/issue 提出问题（写作常用） ★ raise awareness 提高意识 raise children 抚养孩子（注意：rise 不及物，raise 及物）
🧠 助记
raise → 举手大喊（raise your voice）→ 提出、举起
raise::/reɪz/:v. 举起:He raised his hand to answer the teacher's question.
6. identify /aɪˈdentɪfaɪ/ v. 识别；鉴定；认同
📖 英文解释
to recognize and name someone or something; to associate oneself closely with
✏️ 例句
Scientists were able to identify the gene responsible for the rare neurological disorder.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ identify sth/sb as 确认……是…… ★ identify with sb 认同某人 identification n. 身份识别；鉴定
🧠 助记
ident（同一）+ ify → 确定这就是同一个→ 识别
identify::/aɪˈdentɪfaɪ/:v. 识别:Can you identify the main problem in this essay?
7. population /ˌpɒpjuˈleɪʃn/ n. 人口；种群
📖 英文解释
all the inhabitants of a particular place; a group of animals or plants of the same species
✏️ 例句
The aging population poses a significant challenge to the sustainability of public pension systems.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ aging/ageing population 老龄化人口（常考话题） ★ population explosion 人口爆炸 population growth 人口增长
🧠 助记
popul（人）+ ation → 一群人的总数 → 人口
population::/ˌpɒpjuˈleɪʃn/:n. 人口:China has a large population of over 1.4 billion.
8. risk /rɪsk/ n./v. 风险；冒险
📖 英文解释
a situation involving exposure to danger; to expose oneself to the chance of harm
✏️ 例句
Economists warn that trade protectionism poses a serious risk to global economic stability.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at risk 处于危险中 ★ risk doing sth 冒险做某事 ★ take/run a risk 冒险
🧠 助记
risk → 想象老鼠在薄冰上滑行 → 危险
risk::/rɪsk/:n./v. 风险:Smoking puts you at serious health risk.
9. medium /ˈmiːdiəm/ adj./n. 中等的；媒介；媒体
📖 英文解释
intermediate in size or degree; a means of communication or expression
✏️ 例句
The internet has become the most influential medium for spreading information in modern society.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ the media/medium 媒体（复数 media） ★ medium of communication 通讯媒介 medium-sized 中等大小的
🧠 助记
medi（中间）+ um → 中间的大小 → 中等
medium::/ˈmiːdiəm/:adj./n. 中等的:Television is still a powerful medium of communication.
10. lack /læk/ v./n. 缺乏；不足
📖 英文解释
to be without something; an absence of something needed
✏️ 例句
The government's inability to act was attributed to a lack of political will rather than resources.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ lack of sth 缺乏某物（注意不接冠词） ★ lack sth 缺少（动词用法） ★ for lack of 由于缺乏
🧠 助记
lack 谐音莱克 → 莱克没有水 → 缺乏
lack::/læk/:v./n. 缺乏:A lack of sleep can affect your grades.
11. strong /strɒŋ/ adj. 强壮的；强烈的；坚固的
📖 英文解释
having physical power; great in intensity or degree; firmly held
✏️ 例句
A strong correlation between education level and income has been consistently observed in sociological studies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ strong evidence/argument 有力的证据/论点（写作高频） ★ strong emphasis on 非常强调…… strengthen v. 加强（词形变化常考）
🧠 助记
strong = str + ong → 钢铁发出翁翁声 → 强壮
strong::/strɒŋ/:adj. 强壮的:She has a strong desire to improve her English.
12. fill /fɪl/ v. 填满；充满
📖 英文解释
to make full; to occupy a space completely; to fulfill a position
✏️ 例句
The essay should fill the reader with a clear understanding of the research question and its significance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ fill in/out 填写（表格） ★ fill sth with 用……填满 fill a gap/role 填补空缺/担任角色
🧠 助记
fill → 非要填完才了结
fill::/fɪl/:v. 填满:Please fill in the blank with the correct word.
13. patient /ˈpeɪʃnt/ adj./n. 有耐心的；病人
📖 英文解释
able to wait calmly; a person receiving medical treatment
✏️ 例句
A good researcher must be patient enough to repeat experiments many times to ensure accuracy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be patient with sb/sth 对……有耐心 ★ patient care 病人护理 patience n. 耐心（不要拼错）
🧠 助记
pati（忍受）+ ent → 忍受痛苦的人 → 病人；有耐心
patient::/ˈpeɪʃnt/:adj./n. 有耐心的:Be patient with your learning process.
14. rule /ruːl/ n./v. 规则；统治；判定
📖 英文解释
a statement of what must or should be done; to exercise authority; to make an official decision
✏️ 例句
The Supreme Court ruled that the new policy violated constitutional principles of equal protection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ as a rule 通常；一般来说 ★ rule out 排除（选项/可能性） ★ rule over 统治
🧠 助记
rule = r-u-l-e → 你（u）要遵守 → 规则
rule::/ruːl/:n./v. 规则:As a rule, he studies for two hours every evening.
15. receive /rɪˈsiːv/ v. 收到；接受；接待
📖 英文解释
to be given something; to experience or be subjected to; to welcome visitors
✏️ 例句
Students who received scholarships were more likely to complete their postgraduate education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ receive education/training 接受教育/训练 ★ receive attention 受到关注 reception n. 接待；接收
🧠 助记
re（再）+ ceive（拿）→ 再次拿到 → 收到
receive::/rɪˈsiːv/:v. 收到:I received a letter from the university today.
16. record /ˈrekəd/ n. /rɪˈkɔːd/ v. n./v. 记录；档案；唱片
📖 英文解释
a written account kept for reference; to capture sound or video; a best achievement
✏️ 例句
The research team recorded all experimental data in a secure digital database.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ keep a record of 保存……的记录 ★ on record 有记录在案 ★ break a record 打破纪录
🧠 助记
re（再）+ cord（绳子）→ 再次用绳子绑住信息 → 记录
record::/ˈrekəd/ n. /rɪˈkɔːd/ v.:n./v. 记录:Keep a record of your daily vocabulary learning.
17. employee /ɪmˈplɔɪiː/ n. 雇员；员工
📖 英文解释
a person who works for another person or a company in exchange for payment
✏️ 例句
Studies indicate that employee satisfaction is directly linked to organizational productivity and innovation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ employee welfare/benefits 员工福利 ★ employer vs employee 注意区分（考研常考） employment n. 就业
🧠 助记
employ（雇用）+ ee（被……的人）→ 被雇用的人 → 雇员
employee::/ɪmˈplɔɪiː/:n. 雇员:Happy employees are more productive at work.
18. sell /sel/ v. 卖；出售；销售
📖 英文解释
to exchange goods or services for money; to promote an idea persuasively
✏️ 例句
The company decided to sell its overseas assets to cope with the financial downturn.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ sell out 售完；背叛 ★ sell off 低价抛售 sale n. 销售（on sale 打折/出售）
🧠 助记
sell 与 cell（细胞）谐音 → 卖掉细胞 → 出售
sell::/sel/:v. 卖:The bookstore sold all its copies within a week.
19. note /nəʊt/ n./v. 笔记；注意；纸币

note::/nəʊt/:n./v. 笔记:Note that the deadline is next Friday.

month::/mʌnθ/:n. 月:She practiced vocabulary for one hour every month.

community::/kəˈmjuːnɪti/:n. 社区:She volunteered for community service every weekend.
22. demand /dɪˈmɑːnd/ n./v. 要求；需求

demand::/dɪˈmɑːnd/:n./v. 要求:There is a high demand for bilingual graduates.

personal::/ˈpɜːsənl/:adj. 个人的:Keep your personal notes organized and clear.

instead::/ɪnˈsted/:adv. 代替:He chose to walk instead of taking the bus.
