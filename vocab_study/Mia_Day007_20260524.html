1. usually /ˈjuːʒuəli/ adv. 通常；平常
📖 英文解释
under normal or typical circumstances; more often than not
✏️ 例句
Students who usually review vocabulary daily tend to perform significantly better on standardized tests.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ usually = generally / typically（写作替换） ★ more than usually 超乎寻常地 usual adj. 通常的（as usual 照常）
🧠 助记
use（使用）+ ual + ly → 平时经常使用的方式 → 通常
usually::/ˈjuːʒuəli/:adv. 通常:She usually wakes up early to study.
2. average /ˈævərɪdʒ/ adj./n./v. 平均的；平均值；平均
📖 英文解释
the result obtained by adding several quantities and dividing by their number; typical or ordinary
✏️ 例句
The average income of urban residents has grown considerably faster than that of rural populations over the past decade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on average 平均来说（写作常用） ★ above/below average 高于/低于平均水平 ★ average out 平均分配
🧠 助记
aver（均匀）+ age → 均匀地分配到每个年龄 → 平均
average::/ˈævərɪdʒ/:adj./n./v. 平均的:On average, students spend four hours studying each day.
3. although /ɔːlˈðəʊ/ conj. 尽管；虽然
📖 英文解释
in spite of the fact that; even though; used to introduce a concessive clause
✏️ 例句
Although the sample was limited, the study's findings were consistent with broader patterns observed in the literature.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ although / though 引导让步状语从句 ★ although 不可与 but 同用 ★ although vs. despite（although 接句，despite 接名词/动名词）
🧠 助记
al（全部）+ though（尽管）→ 全都如此尽管 → 虽然
although::/ɔːlˈðəʊ/:conj. 尽管:Although it was difficult, she never gave up.
4. eye /aɪ/ n./v. 眼睛；注视
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ keep an eye on 注意；监视 ★ in the eyes of 在……看来 ★ eye-catching 引人注目的（形容词）
🧠 助记
eye 形状像眼睛 → 两个圆圈夹一个 i → 眼睛
eye::/aɪ/:n./v. 眼睛:Keep an eye on the time during the exam.
5. break /breɪk/ v./n. 打破；中断；休息
📖 英文解释
to separate into pieces by force; an interval of rest; to fail to comply with a rule
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ take a break 休息一下 ★ break through 突破 ★ break down 分解；崩溃（多义词）
🧠 助记
break → br（断裂音）+ eak → 啪嗒一声断了 → 打破
break::/breɪk/:v./n. 打破:Take a ten-minute break after every hour of study.
6. common /ˈkɒmən/ adj. 普通的；共同的；常见的
📖 英文解释
occurring or found frequently; shared by two or more things; not special or unusual
✏️ 例句
It is common knowledge that regular exercise improves both physical health and academic concentration.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ common knowledge 常识（写作引用） ★ have sth in common 有共同之处 ★ commonly adv. 通常；普遍地
🧠 助记
com（共同）+ mon（人）→ 大家共同的 → 普通；共同
common::/ˈkɒmən/:adj. 普通的:It is common to feel nervous before a big exam.
7. condition /kənˈdɪʃn/ n. 条件；情况；状况
📖 英文解释
the state of something; a factor necessary before something else is possible; an illness
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on condition that 条件是……（写作结构） ★ living/working conditions 生活/工作条件 ★ conditional adj. 有条件的
🧠 助记
con（加强）+ dition（说法）→ 强调的说法 → 条件
condition::/kənˈdɪʃn/:n. 条件:The experiment was conducted under controlled conditions.
8. behavior /bɪˈheɪvjər/ n. 行为；举止；表现
📖 英文解释
the way a person or animal acts in response to a situation

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
be（是）+ have（有）+ ior → 拥有某种方式存在 → 行为
behavior::/bɪˈheɪvjər/:n. 行为:His study behavior improved dramatically this semester.

global::/ˈɡləʊbl/:adj. 全球的:Global cooperation is essential to tackle environmental problems.

11. light /laɪt/ adj./n./v. 轻的；光；点亮

light::/laɪt/:adj./n./v. 轻的:In light of the new data, we revised our conclusion.
