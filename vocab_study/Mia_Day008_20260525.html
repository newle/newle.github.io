1. translation /trænsˈleɪʃn/ n. 翻译；译文；转换
📖 英文解释
the process of converting text from one language to another; a version in another language
✏️ 例句
Accurate translation of academic texts requires not only linguistic competence but also deep subject knowledge.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ translation of sth 某物的翻译 ★ machine translation 机器翻译（科技话题） ★ translate v. / translator n.（词族）
🧠 助记
trans（跨越）+ lation（语言）→ 跨越语言的壁障 → 翻译
translation::/trænsˈleɪʃn/:n. 翻译:The translation of this novel took three years.

🧠 助记
en（进入）+ joy（快乐）→ 进入快乐状态 → 享受
enjoy::/ɪnˈdʒɔɪ/:v. 享受:She enjoys reading academic journals on weekends.
