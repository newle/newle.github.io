1. story /ˈstɔːri/ n. 故事；报道；楼层
📖 英文解释
A narrative account of events, real or fictional; also a floor of a building.
✏️ 例句
The documentary told the story of how climate change is reshaping coastal cities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ tell a story 讲述一个故事 ★ news story 新闻报道 short story 短篇小说
🧠 助记
stor- 来自 history（历史），历史就是一个个故事
story::/ˈstɔːri/:n. 故事:The story of his success inspired many.
2. rich /rɪtʃ/ adj. 富有的；丰富的；肥沃的
📖 英文解释
Having a great deal of money or assets; abundant in supply or content.
✏️ 例句
The region is rich in natural resources, attracting investors from around the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be rich in 富含…… ★ rich experience 丰富的经验 rich soil 肥沃的土壤
🧠 助记
rich 和『日积』谐音——日积月累才能变富
rich::/rɪtʃ/:adj. 富有的:This area is rich in cultural heritage.
3. air /eər/ n. 空气；神情 v. 播出；晾晒
📖 英文解释
The invisible gaseous substance surrounding the earth; to broadcast or ventilate.
✏️ 例句
The government launched a campaign to improve air quality in major industrial cities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ air pollution 空气污染（高频） ★ on air 播出中 fresh air 新鲜空气
🧠 助记
air 就像汉字爱的发音——空气无处不在，像爱一样
air::/eər/:n. 空气:Air pollution remains a serious concern.
4. night /naɪt/ n. 夜晚；夜间
📖 英文解释
The period of darkness between sunset and sunrise.
✏️ 例句
Many students study late into the night before important exams.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ at night 在夜间 ★ night shift 夜班 overnight 通宵
🧠 助记
night 里有 ight，联想眼皮沉——天黑了该睡觉
night::/naɪt/:n. 夜晚:He worked through the night to finish the report.
5. contain /kənˈteɪn/ v. 包含；容纳；抑制
📖 英文解释
To have or hold something within; to keep under control.
✏️ 例句
The report contains detailed statistics on unemployment rates across the country.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ contain information/data 包含信息/数据 ★ contain costs 控制成本 contain emotions 抑制情绪
🧠 助记
con（共同）+tain（持有）→共同持有→包含
contain::/kənˈteɪn/:v. 包含:The solution contains several key elements.
6. main /meɪn/ adj. 主要的 n. 主干；总管道
📖 英文解释
Chief or most important; principal.
✏️ 例句
The main argument of the essay focuses on the relationship between education and economic growth.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ main point/argument 主要论点（作文高频） ★ main reason 主要原因 mainstream 主流
🧠 助记
main 谐音美因——最美的原因就是最主要的
main::/meɪn/:adj. 主要的 n. 主干:The main challenge is funding.
7. sound /saʊnd/ n. 声音 adj. 健全的；合理的 v. 听起来
📖 英文解释
Vibrations that travel through the air as noise; healthy or well-founded.
✏️ 例句
The economist's proposal sounds reasonable but lacks empirical evidence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ sound judgment/advice 合理的判断/建议 ★ sound sleep 酣睡 sound policy 健全的政策
🧠 助记
sound 既是声音又是稳健的——稳健的政策听起来很有声望
sound::/saʊnd/:n. 声音 adj. 健全的:That sounds like a sound strategy.
8. account /əˈkaʊnt/ n. 账户；描述；说明 v. 解释；占（比例）
📖 英文解释
A record of financial transactions; a report or description; to give an explanation.
✏️ 例句
Technology accounts for a significant proportion of China's GDP growth in recent years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ account for 占（比例）；解释（考研超高频） ★ take into account 考虑到 on account of 由于
🧠 助记
ac+count（计数）→账户里要不断计数
account::/əˈkaʊnt/:n. 账户:Social media accounts for much of their time.
9. return /rɪˈtɜːn/ v./n. 返回；归还；回报
📖 英文解释
To come or go back; to give something back; profit from an investment.
✏️ 例句
Many overseas students return to their home country after graduation to contribute to development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in return (for) 作为回报 ★ return on investment 投资回报 return to normal 恢复正常
🧠 助记
re（再）+turn（转）→再转回来→返回
return::/rɪˈtɜːn/:v./n. 返回:He returned to his hometown after years abroad.
10. affect /əˈfekt/ v. 影响；感动；侵袭
📖 英文解释
To have an effect on; to touch emotionally; (of disease) to attack.
✏️ 例句
Chronic stress can significantly affect both physical and mental health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ affect sb's health/life 影响某人的健康/生活 ★ be affected by 受……影响 adversely affect 不利地影响
🧠 助记
af+fect（做）→对某事做出动作→影响；注意与 effect(n.)区分
affect::/əˈfekt/:v. 影响:The policy will affect millions of people.
11. translate /trænsˈleɪt/ v. 翻译；转化；解读
📖 英文解释
To express the sense of words in another language; to convert into another form.
✏️ 例句
It is challenging to translate abstract policies into concrete actions at the local level.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ translate ... into ... 把……翻译成；把……转化为 ★ translate theory into practice 将理论转化为实践 translation n. 翻译
🧠 助记
trans（跨越）+late（携带）→跨越语言携带意思→翻译
translate::/trænsˈleɪt/:v. 翻译:We need to translate vision into action.
12. describe /dɪˈskraɪb/ v. 描述；描绘；形容
📖 英文解释
To give a detailed account of something in words.
✏️ 例句
The author describes the social inequalities in post-industrial cities with striking clarity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ describe ... as ... 将……描述为…… ★ describe in detail 详细描述 description n. 描述
🧠 助记
de+scribe（写）→写下来→描述
describe::/dɪˈskraɪb/:v. 描述:She described the scene vividly.
13. purpose /ˈpɜːpəs/ n. 目的；意图；决心
📖 英文解释
The reason for which something is done or created; determination.
✏️ 例句
The purpose of higher education has shifted from knowledge acquisition to skill development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on purpose 故意地 ★ for the purpose of 为了……的目的 serve a purpose 达到目的
🧠 助记
pur+pose（姿势）→保持某种姿势→有目的地做
purpose::/ˈpɜːpəs/:n. 目的:The purpose of this study is to explore trends.
14. appear /əˈpɪər/ v. 出现；显得；似乎
📖 英文解释
To come into sight; to seem; to be published.
✏️ 例句
New evidence appears to suggest that diet plays a crucial role in preventing chronic disease.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ appear to do 似乎做…… ★ it appears that 看来…… appearance n. 外貌；出现
🧠 助记
ap+pear（梨）→梨突然出现→出现
appear::/əˈpɪər/:v. 出现:It appears that the situation is improving.
15. impact /ˈɪmpækt/ n./v. 影响；冲击；撞击
📖 英文解释
A marked effect or influence; to have a strong effect on something.
✏️ 例句
The impact of globalization on local cultures has been both positive and negative.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ have an impact on 对……产生影响 ★ positive/negative impact 积极/消极影响 impact assessment 影响评估
🧠 助记
im+pact（压缩）→用力压缩→冲击；impact 比 affect 更强调力度
impact::/ˈɪmpækt/:n./v. 影响:Technology has had a huge impact on education.
16. period /ˈpɪəriəd/ n. 时期；阶段；句号
📖 英文解释
A length or portion of time; a punctuation mark ending a sentence.
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记
peri（周围）+od→绕一圈的时间→一段时期
period::/ˈpɪəriəd/:n. 时期:This was a critical period for the company.
