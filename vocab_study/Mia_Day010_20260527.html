1. memory /ˈmeməri/ n. 记忆；记忆力；纪念
📖 英文解释
The faculty by which the mind stores and remembers information; something remembered.
✏️ 例句
Collective memory plays a vital role in shaping national identity and cultural continuity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in memory of 纪念…… ★ short-term/long-term memory 短期/长期记忆 from memory 凭记忆
🧠 助记
memo（备忘录）+ry→备忘录就是记忆的辅助
memory::/ˈmeməri/:n. 记忆:She recited the poem from memory.
2. whole /həʊl/ adj. 全部的；完整的 n. 整体
📖 英文解释
All of something; complete; not divided or broken; the entire thing.
✏️ 例句
The whole educational system needs reform, not just individual schools or policies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ on the whole 总体上（过渡词） ★ as a whole 作为整体 whole-hearted 全心全意的
🧠 助记
whole 和 hole（洞）同音——整体里没有洞才完整
whole::/həʊl/:adj. 全部的:The whole team contributed to the success.
3. writer /ˈraɪtər/ n. 作者；作家；撰稿人
📖 英文解释
A person who has written a particular text or who writes for a profession.
✏️ 例句
The writer argues that consumerism has eroded traditional values in modern society.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ ghost writer 代笔作者 ★ feature writer 专题撰稿人 writer's block 写作瓶颈
🧠 助记
write（写）+r→写作的人→作者
writer::/ˈraɪtər/:n. 作者:The writer conveys her ideas persuasively.
4. death /deθ/ n. 死亡；消亡；终结
📖 英文解释
The end of the life of a person or organism; the permanent cessation of vital functions.
✏️ 例句
The death toll from the epidemic highlighted the urgent need for improved public health infrastructure.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ death rate 死亡率 ★ put to death 处死 to death 极度地（如 bored to death）
🧠 助记
death 里有 eat——不吃东西会死，联想辅助
death::/deθ/:n. 死亡:The death of the policy brought relief.
5. academic /ˌækəˈdemɪk/ adj. 学术的；学业的 n. 学者
📖 英文解释
Relating to education and scholarship; a teacher or scholar in a university.
✏️ 例句
Academic integrity is the cornerstone of a trustworthy and productive research environment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ academic performance/achievement 学业表现/成就 ★ academic research/paper 学术研究/论文 academic integrity 学术诚信
🧠 助记
academ（学院）+ic→学院里的→学术的；来自雅典柏拉图的学园
academic::/ˌækəˈdemɪk/:adj. 学术的:Academic success requires consistent effort.
6. bear /beər/ v. 承受；忍受；生育；携带 n. 熊
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ bear the cost/responsibility 承担费用/责任 ★ bear in mind 记住；牢记 bear fruit 结果；取得成效
🧠 助记
bear（熊）→熊很能扛——承受重量，联想负重
bear::/beər/:v. 承受:Employees should not bear all the risk.
7. importance /ɪmˈpɔːtns/ n. 重要性；重要
📖 英文解释
The state or fact of being of great significance or value.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ attach importance to 重视…… ★ of great importance 非常重要 underestimate the importance 低估重要性
🧠 助记
import（重要）+ance→进口的东西才重要→重要性
importance::/ɪmˈpɔːtns/:n. 重要性:We must recognize the importance of data.
8. decline /dɪˈklaɪn/ v. 下降；拒绝；衰退 n. 衰退；下降
📖 英文解释
To become smaller, fewer, or less; to politely refuse; a gradual decrease.
✏️ 例句
The sharp decline in birth rates poses a long-term challenge to the sustainability of pension systems.

★ decline in 在……方面的下降 ★ on the decline 在下降中 decline to do 拒绝做……
🧠 助记
de（向下）+cline（倾斜）→向下倾斜→下降、拒绝
decline::/dɪˈklaɪn/:v. 下降:Birth rates have declined steadily for decades.
9. claim /kleɪm/ v./n. 声称；要求；索赔；主张
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
claim 联想抢名——大声宣称这是我的→声称，要求
claim::/kleɪm/:v./n. 声称:He claimed the experiment was flawless.
10. model /ˈmɒdl/ n. 模型；模范；模特 v. 模仿；塑造

✏️ 例句

🧠 助记

model::/ˈmɒdl/:n. 模型:She served as a role model for students.
11. wrong /rɒŋ/ adj. 错误的；不道德的 adv. 错误地 n. 错误
📖 英文解释
