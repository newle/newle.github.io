1. prove /pruːv/ v. 证明；证实；结果是
📖 英文解释
To demonstrate the truth of something; to be found to be something after experience.
✏️ 例句
The long-term study proved that early childhood education significantly improves adult outcomes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ prove to be 结果证明是…… ★ prove a point 证明观点 proven adj. 经证实的
🧠 助记
prove 联想破坏疑云——通过证据破坏疑云→证明
prove::/pruːv/:v. 证明:Research has proved this theory correct.
2. compare /kəmˈpeər/ v. 比较；对比；比喻
📖 英文解释
To examine the similarities and differences between things; to liken.
✏️ 例句
When compared to previous generations, today's youth have greater access to information but face more uncertainty.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ compare ... with/to ... 与……比较 ★ beyond compare 无与伦比 compared to/with 与……相比（作文过渡）
🧠 助记
com（共同）+pare（相等）→放在一起看是否相等→比较
compare::/kəmˈpeər/:v. 比较:Compared to last year, sales have improved.
3. competition /ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃn/ n. 竞争；比赛；竞争对手

🧠 助记
compet（共同争）+ition→大家共同争一个东西→竞争
competition::/ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃn/:n. 竞争:Global competition drives technological innovation.
