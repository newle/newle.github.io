1. farm /fɑːrm/ n. 农场；v. 耕种
📖 英文解释
an area of land used for growing crops or keeping animals
✏️ 例句
The government encouraged farmers to farm the land more efficiently to increase grain output.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ farm out（外包）；farm subsidies（农业补贴）；考研阅读常见于农业政策、乡村社会话题。
🧠 助记
farm → f + arm（手臂），农民用手臂耕作的地方就是农场。
farm::/fɑːrm/:n. 农场:The government encouraged farmers to farm the land more efficiently.
2. approach /əˈprəʊtʃ/ v./n. 接近；方法；途径
📖 英文解释
to come near to something or someone; a way of dealing with a situation or problem
✏️ 例句
Researchers need a new approach to studying the cognitive development of children.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ approach to doing sth（处理……的方法）；a fresh/novel approach（新方法）；考研写作高频词，可替换method/way。
🧠 助记
ap-proach → 向前（ap）伸手（reach变体），靠近某人时伸手接触，引申为'接近/方法'。
approach::/əˈprəʊtʃ/:v./n. 接近:Researchers need a new approach to studying the cognitive development of children.
3. correct /kəˈrekt/ adj. 正确的；v. 更正，纠正
📖 英文解释
free from error; to make something right by changing what is wrong
✏️ 例句
The teacher corrected the student's grammar mistakes before submitting the essay.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ correct a mistake（纠正错误）；politically correct（政治正确）；考研阅读中常作形容词修饰观点或答案。
🧠 助记
cor-rect → cor（心）+ rect（直），内心正直→正确、纠正。
correct::/kəˈrekt/:adj. 正确的:The teacher corrected the student's grammar mistakes before submitting the essay.
4. special /ˈspeʃl/ adj. 特别的，特殊的；n. 特别节目
📖 英文解释
different from what is ordinary or usual; particularly important or loved
✏️ 例句
The university has a special program designed to support students from disadvantaged backgrounds.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ special attention to（特别关注）；specialist（专家）；specialize in（专攻）；考研完形填空常考近义词辨析。
🧠 助记
spec（看）+ ial → 值得特别看的 → 特别的。
special::/ˈspeʃl/:adj. 特别的，特殊的:The university has a special program designed to support students from disadvantaged backgrounds.
5. someone /ˈsʌmwʌn/ pron. 某人
📖 英文解释
a person who is not known or not specified
✏️ 例句
Someone needs to take responsibility for the policy failure in the report.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ someone else（别人）；考研中常作主语或宾语出现在阅读长句中，注意与anyone/everyone区分。
🧠 助记
some（某）+ one（一个人）= 某一个人。
someone::/ˈsʌmwʌn/:pron. 某人:Someone needs to take responsibility for the policy failure in the report.
6. speaker /ˈspiːkər/ n. 说话者；演讲者；扬声器
📖 英文解释
a person who speaks, especially at a formal event; a device that converts electrical signals to sound
✏️ 例句
The keynote speaker at the conference argued that AI would fundamentally reshape the labor market.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ native speaker（母语者）；public speaker（公众演讲者）；考研听力和阅读中高频出现。
🧠 助记
speak（说话）+ -er（人/物） = 说话的人 或 发出声音的设备。
speaker::/ˈspiːkər/:n. 说话者:The keynote speaker argued that AI would fundamentally reshape the labor market.
7. intelligence /ɪnˈtelɪdʒəns/ n. 智力；情报
📖 英文解释
the ability to learn and understand things; secret information gathered about an enemy
✏️ 例句
Artificial intelligence has transformed how businesses analyze and interpret large data sets.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ artificial intelligence（人工智能）；emotional intelligence（情商）；intelligence agency（情报机构）；考研热点词汇。
🧠 助记
intel（理解）+ lig + ence → 理解事物的能力 = 智力；intel也是情报的缩写。
intelligence::/ɪnˈtelɪdʒəns/:n. 智力:Artificial intelligence has transformed how businesses analyze large data sets.
8. picture /ˈpɪktʃər/ n. 图片，画面；v. 想象
📖 英文解释
a drawing, painting, or photograph; to form a mental image of something
✏️ 例句
The documentary painted a vivid picture of urban poverty that resonated with audiences worldwide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ paint a picture of（描绘……的景象）；get the picture（明白了）；考研阅读中常作比喻义'描绘情景'。
🧠 助记
pict（画）+ ure → 画出来的东西 = 图片；picture也有'在脑中描绘'之意。
picture::/ˈpɪktʃər/:n. 图片，画面:The documentary painted a vivid picture of urban poverty that resonated with audiences worldwide.
9. survey /ˈsɜːrveɪ/ n./v. 调查；测量；综览
📖 英文解释
a study in which information is collected from a large number of people; to examine and record the features of an area
✏️ 例句
A nationwide survey revealed that more than half of respondents supported stricter environmental regulations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ conduct a survey（开展调查）；survey data（调查数据）；考研阅读中常作论据引入词。
🧠 助记
sur（超过/全面）+ vey（看，同view）= 全面地看 → 调查/综览。
survey::/ˈsɜːrveɪ/:n./v. 调查:A nationwide survey revealed that more than half of respondents supported stricter environmental regulations.
10. everyone /ˈevriwʌn/ pron. 每人，人人
📖 英文解释
every person; all people
✏️ 例句
Not everyone agrees that economic growth should take priority over environmental protection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ everyone + 单数谓语；考研中常出现在否定句和让步从句中，注意not everyone表示'并非所有人'的部分否定用法。
🧠 助记
every（每个）+ one（一个）= 每一个人。
everyone::/ˈevriwʌn/:pron. 每人，人人:Not everyone agrees that economic growth should take priority over environmental protection.
11. event /ɪˈvent/ n. 事件；赛事；结果
📖 英文解释
a thing that happens or takes place, especially one of importance; a competition in a sports program
✏️ 例句
In the event of a financial crisis, governments must act swiftly to stabilize the economy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in the event of（万一，如果……发生）；current events（时事）；考研阅读/完形高频短语。
🧠 助记
e-vent → vent（来临），事情来临就是'事件'。
event::/ɪˈvent/:n. 事件:In the event of a financial crisis, governments must act swiftly to stabilize the economy.
12. either /ˈiːðər/ pron./adj. 两者之一；adv. 也（用于否定句）
📖 英文解释
used to refer to each of two things or people; used in negative sentences meaning 'also'
✏️ 例句
Either the government reduces carbon emissions, or it faces mounting pressure from international organizations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ either...or...（要么……要么……）；not...either（也不）；考研中注意either作副词出现在否定句末的用法。
🧠 助记
ei-ther → 联想'一（ei听ei）this or that'，两者选其一。
either::/ˈiːðər/:pron./adj. 两者之一:Either the government reduces carbon emissions, or it faces mounting pressure from international organizations.
13. habit /ˈhæbɪt/ n. 习惯；惯例
📖 英文解释
a settled or regular tendency or practice, especially one that is hard to give up
✏️ 例句
Developing healthy reading habits in childhood is crucial for long-term academic success.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ form/develop a habit（养成习惯）；break a habit（改掉习惯）；out of habit（出于习惯）；考研常见搭配。
🧠 助记
habit → 把它（habit的谐音'哈比特'）变成一种固定的做法 = 习惯。
habit::/ˈhæbɪt/:n. 习惯:Developing healthy reading habits in childhood is crucial for long-term academic success.
14. responsibility /rɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪlɪti/ n. 责任；职责
📖 英文解释
the state or fact of being responsible for something; a duty or obligation
✏️ 例句
Corporations have a social responsibility to minimize their environmental impact.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ take/accept responsibility for（承担责任）；a sense of responsibility（责任感）；考研写作高频名词。
🧠 助记
response（回应）+ ibility → 对事情作出回应的能力 = 责任。
responsibility::/rɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪlɪti/:n. 责任:Corporations have a social responsibility to minimize their environmental impact.
15. similar /ˈsɪmɪlər/ adj. 相似的，类似的
📖 英文解释
resembling someone or something but not exactly the same
✏️ 例句
The two studies reached similar conclusions about the effects of social media on adolescent mental health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ similar to（与……相似）；similarly（副词，同样地）；考研写作中可用于同义替换same/alike。
🧠 助记
simil（like，像）+ ar → 像某物的 = 相似的。simile（明喻）同根。
similar::/ˈsɪmɪlər/:adj. 相似的，类似的:The two studies reached similar conclusions about the effects of social media on mental health.

📖 英文解释
required to be done, achieved, or present; needed for a particular purpose

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ it is necessary to do（有必要做……）；if necessary（如有必要）；unnecessary（不必要的）；考研写作常用句型。
🧠 助记
nece（need）+ ss + ary → 需要（need）的 → 必要的。
necessary::/ˈnesəseri/:adj. 必要的，必需的:It is necessary for policymakers to consider both short-term and long-term consequences.

chance::/tʃɑːns/:n. 机会:Students who study abroad have a better chance of developing cross-cultural communication skills.

foreign::/ˈfɒrɪn/:adj. 外国的:Foreign investment plays a vital role in boosting economic development in emerging markets.
19. apply /əˈplaɪ/ v. 应用；申请；适用
📖 英文解释
to make a formal request for something; to put something to practical use
✏️ 例句
The principles of behavioral economics can be applied to improve public health policy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ apply for（申请）；apply to（适用于）；apply sth to（将……应用于）；考研中三种用法均需掌握。
🧠 助记
ap（去）+ ply（折叠/层叠）→ 将知识叠加到实践上 = 应用；去提交多层材料 = 申请。
apply::/əˈplaɪ/:v. 应用:The principles of behavioral economics can be applied to improve public health policy.

father::/ˈfɑːðər/:n. 父亲:Charles Darwin is often regarded as the father of modern evolutionary biology.

anything::/ˈeniθɪŋ/:pron. 任何事，任何东西:Advances in gene therapy suggest that almost anything is possible in modern medicine.
