1. manager /ˈmænɪdʒər/ n. 经理；管理者
📖 英文解释
a person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group
✏️ 例句
Effective managers must balance short-term performance targets with long-term organizational development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ senior manager（高级经理）；project manager（项目经理）；management（管理，名词）；考研商务类阅读常见。
🧠 助记
manage（管理）+ -r = 进行管理的人 = 经理。
manager::/ˈmænɪdʒər/:n. 经理:Effective managers must balance short-term performance targets with long-term organizational development.
2. land /lænd/ n. 陆地；土地；v. 着陆；获得
📖 英文解释
the part of the earth's surface that is not covered by water; to arrive by plane or boat
✏️ 例句
Competition for arable land intensifies as the global population continues to grow.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ land on（降落在）；land a job（获得工作）；land use（土地利用）；考研多义词，注意动词'获得'的语境。
🧠 助记
land → 大地（land）是踩脚下的，飞机也降落到大地上 = 陆地/着陆。
land::/lænd/:n. 陆地:Competition for arable land intensifies as the global population continues to grow.
3. solve /sɒlv/ v. 解决；解答
📖 英文解释
to find a solution to a problem or question
✏️ 例句
Technology alone cannot solve the complex social and political problems facing modern societies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ solve a problem（解决问题）；solution（名词，解决方法）；考研写作'提出解决方案'段落必备词。
🧠 助记
sol（太阳，solve词根同soluble溶解）→ 像太阳光照射让冰化开 = 解决/溶解问题。
solve::/sɒlv/:v. 解决:Technology alone cannot solve the complex social and political problems facing modern societies.
4. function /ˈfʌŋkʃn/ n. 功能；职责；v. 运作，起作用
📖 英文解释
the purpose or role of something; to work or operate in a particular way
✏️ 例句
The primary function of the immune system is to protect the body from harmful pathogens.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ function as（起……的作用）；in function（在功能上）；functional（形容词）；考研科技/学术类阅读高频词。
🧠 助记
func（做）+ tion → 做事情的目的 = 功能；做事的方式 = 运作。
function::/ˈfʌŋkʃn/:n. 功能:The primary function of the immune system is to protect the body from harmful pathogens.
5. management /ˈmænɪdʒmənt/ n. 管理；经营；管理层
📖 英文解释
the process of dealing with or controlling things or people; the people at the top of an organization
✏️ 例句
Poor waste management practices are a leading cause of environmental degradation in urban areas.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ management of（对……的管理）；risk management（风险管理）；crisis management（危机管理）；考研热词。
🧠 助记
manage（管理）+ ment（名词后缀）= 管理这件事/管理层。
management::/ˈmænɪdʒmənt/:n. 管理:Poor waste management practices are a leading cause of environmental degradation in urban areas.
6. phrase /freɪz/ n. 短语；措辞；v. 措辞表达
📖 英文解释
a small group of words that form a unit; to express something in a particular way
✏️ 例句
The phrase 'sustainable development' has become central to global environmental policy discourse.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ set phrase（固定短语）；prepositional phrase（介词短语）；to phrase（措辞）；考研语言学话题常见。
🧠 助记
phrase → 联想'弗（ph=f）雷（rase）滋'，说话时发出的语音串 = 短语。
phrase::/freɪz/:n. 短语:The phrase 'sustainable development' has become central to global environmental policy discourse.
7. experiment /ɪkˈsperɪmənt/ n. 实验；v. 做实验，尝试
📖 英文解释
a scientific procedure to test a hypothesis; to try something new
✏️ 例句
Researchers conducted a carefully controlled experiment to measure the effects of sleep deprivation on memory.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ conduct an experiment（进行实验）；experiment with（尝试/试验）；experimental（形容词）；考研科学类必备词。
🧠 助记
ex（外）+ per（经历，perience同根）+ ment → 通过外部经历来验证 = 实验。
experiment::/ɪkˈsperɪmənt/:n. 实验:Researchers conducted a carefully controlled experiment to measure the effects of sleep deprivation on memory.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ social/political structure（社会/政治结构）；sentence structure（句子结构）；structural（形容词）；考研阅读高频词。
🧠 助记
struct（建造，construct同根）+ ure → 建造出来的样子 = 结构/建筑物。
structure::/ˈstrʌktʃər/:n. 结构:The social structure of many developed nations is being reshaped by demographic change and immigration.

🧠 助记

everything::/ˈevriθɪŋ/:pron. 每件事，一切:In competitive markets, timing is everything—being first to market can determine long-term success.

sit::/sɪt/:v. 坐:The committee sits twice a month to review applications from research grant applicants.

seek::/siːk/:v. 寻求:Many young professionals seek employment opportunities abroad in search of higher wages and better prospects.

message::/ˈmesɪdʒ/:n. 消息:The central message of the article is that economic growth must be balanced with environmental responsibility.
