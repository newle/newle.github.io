1. reader /ˈriːdər/ n. 读者；读物；朗读者
📖 英文解释
a person who reads; a book containing texts for learning a language
✏️ 例句
The author challenges the reader to question long-held assumptions about economic inequality.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ reader-friendly（易读的）；readership（读者群）；考研阅读中reader常指目标受众，注意作者与读者关系。
🧠 助记
read（阅读）+ -er = 阅读的人 = 读者。
reader::/ˈriːdər/:n. 读者:The author challenges the reader to question long-held assumptions about economic inequality.
2. serious /ˈsɪəriəs/ adj. 严肃的；认真的；严重的
📖 英文解释
demanding careful consideration; not joking; having important or dangerous consequences
✏️ 例句
Air pollution poses a serious threat to public health, particularly in densely populated urban areas.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ serious about（对……认真）；serious consequences（严重后果）；seriously（副词）；考研写作表严重性的核心词。
🧠 助记
seri（一连串，series同源）+ ous → 引发一连串后果的 = 严重的；对一连串事情都认真 = 严肃的。
serious::/ˈsɪəriəs/:adj. 严肃的:Air pollution poses a serious threat to public health, particularly in densely populated urban areas.

serve::/sɜːrv/:v. 服务:Public institutions must serve the interests of all citizens, not just those of the wealthy elite.
