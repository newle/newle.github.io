1. nearly /ˈnɪərli/ adv. 几乎，差不多
📖 英文解释
Almost but not completely; very close to being a particular amount, number, or situation.
✏️ 例句
The project took nearly three years to complete.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nearly + 数词/形容词；not nearly = 远不及；nearly always 几乎总是（考研完形、阅读高频）
🧠 助记
near（近）+ ly → 接近地 → 几乎
nearly::/ˈnɪərli/:adv. 几乎，差不多:The experiment took nearly six months to finish.
2. girl /ɡɜːrl/ n. 女孩，姑娘
📖 英文解释
A female child or young woman.
✏️ 例句
The little girl smiled and waved at the camera.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
girl 在考研中多出现于社会议题语境，如 girl education、girl empowerment；注意与 woman 的语用区别
🧠 助记
发音像够儿 → 女孩（音近助记）
girl::/ɡɜːrl/:n. 女孩，姑娘:The girl scored highest in the national exam.
3. status /ˈsteɪtəs/ n. 状态，地位，身份
📖 英文解释
The official or legal position of a person, organization, or country; the level of importance or prestige someone has.
✏️ 例句
Her social status changed significantly after she was promoted.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
social/legal/marital/economic status；status quo（现状）= 考研必备短语；status symbol 地位象征
🧠 助记
stat（统计/状态）+ us → 我们的状态 → 地位/状态
status::/ˈsteɪtəs/:n. 状态，地位，身份:His professional status improved after the promotion.
4. exercise /ˈeksərsaɪz/ v./n. 锻炼，练习，行使（权利）
📖 英文解释
Physical or mental activity done to stay healthy or improve a skill; to use a right or authority.
✏️ 例句
Regular exercise is essential for maintaining good health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
take/do exercise；exercise caution/restraint/judgment 行使谨慎/克制/判断力（考研写作搭配）；exercise one's rights
🧠 助记
ex（出去）+ er + cise（切割）→ 出去切开身体 → 锻炼（联想）
exercise::/ˈeksərsaɪz/:v./n. 锻炼，练习，行使（权利）:Daily exercise helps students maintain focus and energy.
5. various /ˈveəriəs/ adj. 各种各样的，多种多样的
📖 英文解释
Many different kinds or types of something.
✏️ 例句
The museum displays various artifacts from ancient civilizations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
various + 名词复数；for various reasons；注意与 varied（变化多样的）、diverse（多元的）的区别
🧠 助记
vari（变化）+ ous → 变化多多的 → 各种各样的
various::/ˈveəriəs/:adj. 各种各样的，多种多样的:Students can approach the topic from various angles.
6. basic /ˈbeɪsɪk/ adj. 基本的，根本的；n. 基本原则
📖 英文解释
Forming the most important part of something; simple and not complicated.
✏️ 例句
Learning the basic rules of grammar is essential for language mastery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
basic needs/skills/principle；back to basics 回归基础；on a basic level 在基本层面
🧠 助记
base（基础）+ ic → 基础性的 → 基本的
basic::/ˈbeɪsɪk/:adj. 基本的，根本的:The basic principles of economics are taught in the first year.
7. fit /fɪt/ adj. 健康的，合适的；v. 适合；n. 合身
📖 英文解释
Healthy and strong; suitable or appropriate for a particular purpose; to be the right size or shape.
✏️ 例句
The new employee proved to be a perfect fit for the company culture.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fit for/to do；keep fit 保持健康；fit in (with) 融入；survival of the fittest 适者生存
🧠 助记
fit → 身体好 → 健康，fit in → 融入
fit::/fɪt/:adj. 健康的，合适的:The candidate was considered fit for the senior management role.
8. above /əˈbʌv/ prep./adv. 在……上面，超过，高于
📖 英文解释
At a higher level or position; more than a specified amount or level.
✏️ 例句
Temperatures above 40 degrees can be dangerous for elderly people.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
above all（最重要的是）= 考研写作必备；above average 高于平均水平；above mentioned 上文提到的
🧠 助记
a + bove（上方）→ 在上方 → 在上面
above::/əˈbʌv/:prep./adv. 在……上面，超过，:Above all, honesty is the most important virtue in academia.
9. occur /əˈkɜːr/ v. 发生，出现；（想法）浮现
📖 英文解释
To happen, especially without being planned; (of a thought or idea) to come into someone's mind.
✏️ 例句
It occurred to her that she had left her keys at home.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
occur to sb（想法出现在某人脑中）；occur in/at/during；常与 rarely/frequently/suddenly 搭配
🧠 助记
oc（=ob，在……）+ cur（跑）→ 跑出来 → 发生
occur::/əˈkɜːr/:v. 发生，出现:Climate change is occurring at an alarming rate globally.
10. refer /rɪˈfɜːr/ v. 提及，涉及；参考；转介
📖 英文解释
To mention or speak about something; to look at something for information; to direct someone to a specialist.
✏️ 例句
The professor referred to several key studies in her lecture.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
refer to（提及/参考）；refer to sth as（把…称为）；reference n. 参考文献；with reference to 关于
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ fer（带来/带去）→ 再次带回来看 → 参考，提及
refer::/rɪˈfɜːr/:v. 提及，涉及:The report refers to three major environmental studies.
11. die /daɪ/ v. 死亡；消失；n. 模具
📖 英文解释
To stop living; to stop existing or functioning; to have a strong wish for something.
✏️ 例句
He died for his beliefs during the revolution.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
die of/from（因病/伤死亡）；die out 灭绝；die down 平息；die hard 顽固不化；never-say-die 不屈不挠
🧠 助记
die → 与汉字歹形近，歹徒相关 → 死
die::/daɪ/:v. 死亡:The old customs gradually died out over the centuries.
12. town /taʊn/ n. 城镇，市区
📖 英文解释
A place with many houses, shops, and other buildings, larger than a village but smaller than a city.
✏️ 例句
The small coastal town attracted thousands of tourists each summer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
go to town（认真做事/大肆做）；town hall 市政厅；out of town 出差；ghost town 废弃小镇
🧠 助记
town 发音像逃温 → 逃去温暖的小城镇
town::/taʊn/:n. 城镇，市区:The town's economy depends heavily on tourism.
13. maintain /meɪnˈteɪn/ v. 维持，保持；坚持（观点）；保养
📖 英文解释
To keep something in good condition or at the same level; to strongly assert that something is true.
✏️ 例句
It is difficult to maintain a healthy work-life balance in modern society.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
maintain + n.（balance/order/standards）；maintain that 坚持认为（考研写作常用）；maintenance n.
🧠 助记
main（主要）+ tain（持有）→ 持有主要的东西 → 维持
maintain::/meɪnˈteɪn/:v. 维持，保持:Governments must maintain stable economic policies during crises.
14. failure /ˈfeɪljər/ n. 失败，失败者；故障；未能
📖 英文解释
Lack of success; a situation where something stops working; someone who is not successful.
✏️ 例句
Failure is often a stepping stone to eventual success.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
failure to do（未能做某事）；end in failure 以失败告终；organ failure 器官衰竭；heart failure 心脏衰竭
🧠 助记
fail（失败）+ ure（名词后缀）→ 失败
failure::/ˈfeɪljər/:n. 失败，失败者:The failure to address climate change will have severe consequences.
15. composition /ˌkɒmpəˈzɪʃn/ n. 构成，成分；作品；作文；合成
📖 英文解释
The way in which something is made up; a piece of music, art, or writing; the act of composing.
✏️ 例句
The chemical composition of water is H₂O.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the composition of（……的构成）；musical composition；essay composition；in composition（在构成上）
🧠 助记
compose（组成）→ composition（组成后的名词）→ 构成/作品
composition::/ˌkɒmpəˈzɪʃn/:n. 构成，成分:The composition of the committee reflects regional diversity.
16. attend /əˈtend/ v. 出席，参加；照料，照顾；伴随
📖 英文解释
To be present at an event; to take care of someone; to give attention to something.
✏️ 例句
All students are required to attend the graduation ceremony.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
attend a meeting/class/event；attend to（处理/照料）；attendance n. 出席率（考研统计类常见）
🧠 助记
at（在）+ tend（倾向/趋向）→ 趋向于在场 → 出席
attend::/əˈtend/:v. 出席，参加:Students are expected to attend all scheduled lectures.
17. critical /ˈkrɪtɪkl/ adj. 批评的；关键的；危急的
📖 英文解释
Expressing disapproval or finding faults; very important for the success of something; in a dangerous situation.
✏️ 例句
Critical thinking is a fundamental skill for academic success.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
critical thinking 批判性思维；critical of sb 批评某人；at a critical moment 在关键时刻；critically ill 病危
🧠 助记
critic（批评家）+ al → 批评家的 → 批评的；critical moment → 关键
critical::/ˈkrɪtɪkl/:adj. 批评的:Critical analysis skills are essential for graduate-level research.
18. alone /əˈloʊn/ adj./adv. 单独的，孤独的；仅仅
📖 英文解释
Without other people; used to emphasize that only one person or thing is involved.
✏️ 例句
She preferred to work alone rather than in a team.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
leave sb alone 别打扰某人；let alone（更不用说）= 考研阅读关键短语；go it alone 单干；stand alone 独树一帜
🧠 助记
all + one → 全部只有一个人 → 孤独/单独
alone::/əˈloʊn/:adj./adv. 单独的，孤独的:Let alone the cost, the project poses serious ethical risks.
19. meeting /ˈmiːtɪŋ/ n. 会议，集会；相遇
📖 英文解释
A gathering of people for a particular purpose, especially to discuss or decide something.
✏️ 例句
The board meeting was held to discuss the company's annual budget.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hold/attend/chair a meeting；meeting point 集合点；come to a meeting of minds 达成共识
🧠 助记
meet（见面）+ ing → 见面的进行时 → 会议
meeting::/ˈmiːtɪŋ/:n. 会议，集会:The annual policy meeting attracted delegates from fifty nations.
20. tax /tæks/ n. 税，税款；v. 对……征税；使……负重担
📖 英文解释
Money that must be paid to the government; to impose a tax; to put excessive demands on someone.
✏️ 例句
The government raised income tax to fund public services.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
income/sales/property tax；tax reform 税制改革；tax cut 减税；tax sb on sth；tax evasion 逃税
🧠 助记
tax → 发音像踏客 → 政府踏过来收钱 → 税
tax::/tæks/:n. 税，税款:Progressive tax systems help reduce income inequality in society.
21. feature /ˈfiːtʃər/ n. 特征，特色；容貌；v. 以……为特色
📖 英文解释
An important, interesting, or typical quality of something; a part of someone's face; to include something as a main part.
✏️ 例句
The most striking feature of the proposal was its cost efficiency.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a key/distinctive feature；feature in（出现在）；feature film 故事片；geological features 地质特征
🧠 助记
feat（特技）+ ure → 有特技的东西 → 特征/特色
feature::/ˈfiːtʃər/:n. 特征，特色:A key feature of effective communication is clarity.
22. express /ɪkˈspres/ v. 表达，表示；adj. 快速的，明确的；n. 快车
📖 英文解释
To show or communicate feelings, thoughts, or opinions; done or sent quickly; a fast train or service.
✏️ 例句
She expressed her concerns about the new policy during the meeting.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
express oneself 自我表达；express train 快车；express delivery 快递；express + 情绪词
🧠 助记
ex（出）+ press（压）→ 压出来 → 表达
express::/ɪkˈspres/:v. 表达，表示:It is important to express disagreement in a respectful manner.
23. shift /ʃɪft/ v./n. 转移，转换；轮班；变化
📖 英文解释
To move or change from one position or state to another; a period of work; a change in attitude or policy.
✏️ 例句
There has been a significant shift in public opinion on this issue.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a shift in/from/to；shift one's focus/attention；day/night shift；paradigm shift 范式转变（考研高频）
🧠 助记
shift → 洗脚换鞋 → 转换
shift::/ʃɪft/:v./n. 转移，转换:The paradigm shift in education emphasizes critical thinking over memorization.
24. simple /ˈsɪmpl/ adj. 简单的，朴素的；单纯的
📖 英文解释
Easy to understand or do; not elaborate; having no unnecessary parts.
✏️ 例句
The teacher explained the complex concept in simple terms.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
simple solution；put it simply 简而言之（写作过渡语）；simplify v.；simply adv.；simplicity n. 简单性
🧠 助记
sim（same，相同）+ ple → 都一样，没变化 → 简单的
simple::/ˈsɪmpl/:adj. 简单的，朴素的:A simple change in policy can have far-reaching consequences.
25. successful /səkˈsesfʊl/ adj. 成功的
📖 英文解释
Having achieved an aim or purpose; having achieved wealth, fame, or social status.
✏️ 例句
A successful entrepreneur knows how to adapt to changing markets.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
successful in doing/at sth；a highly/enormously successful；success n.；succeed v.；successfully adv.
🧠 助记
success（成功）+ ful（充满）→ 充满成功的 → 成功的
successful::/səkˈsesfʊl/:adj. 成功的:A successful policy must be both effective and equitable.
26. theory /ˈθɪəri/ n. 理论，学说；看法，推测
📖 英文解释
A formal set of ideas used to explain something; an idea used to account for a situation.
✏️ 例句
Darwin's theory of evolution changed the way we understand life on Earth.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in theory（理论上）；theory of everything；theoretical adj.；theoretical framework 理论框架；conspiracy theory 阴谋论
🧠 助记
theo（神）+ ry → 神的道理 → 理论（联想古代以神解释世界）
theory::/ˈθɪəri/:n. 理论，学说:The theory of cognitive dissonance has wide applications in marketing.
27. key /kiː/ n. 钥匙，关键；adj. 关键的；v. 键入

A metal instrument for locking/unlocking; the most important element; to type something on a keyboard.

key factor/issue/role；the key to（……的关键）；key in 键入；keynote speech 主题演讲

key = 钥匙，开门的关键物 → 关键
key::/kiː/:n. 钥匙，关键:The key to academic success lies in consistent effort and reflection.
28. image /ˈɪmɪdʒ/ n. 形象，肖像；图像；印象

public/corporate image；image of；imagery（意象）文学分析用词；self-image 自我形象

image::/ˈɪmɪdʒ/:n. 形象，肖像:A politician's public image greatly affects their electoral success.
29. couple /ˈkʌpl/ n. 情侣，夫妻；几个；v. 结合，连接

✏️ 例句

a couple of（几个，数量不确定）；married couple；couple with（与……结合）；in a couple of days

30. whose /huːz/ pron. 谁的；（引导定语从句）其……的
📖 英文解释
Used to ask about or state who something belongs to; used as a relative pronoun to introduce a clause.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
who（谁）+ se → 谁的 → whose
whose::/huːz/:pron. 谁的:The author whose work influenced generations was awarded a lifetime achievement award.
