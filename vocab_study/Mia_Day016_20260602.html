1. catch /kætʃ/ v./n. 抓住；捕捉；感染；门扣
📖 英文解释
To take hold of something moving; to become infected with an illness; a hidden difficulty in something.
✏️ 例句
He managed to catch the ball just before it hit the ground.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
catch a cold/disease；catch up with（赶上）；catch on（流行/理解）；catch sb's attention 吸引注意
🧠 助记
cat + ch → 猫抓人 → 抓住
catch::/kætʃ/:v./n. 抓住:The new policy failed to catch the public's attention effectively.
2. select /sɪˈlekt/ v./adj. 选择；精选的
📖 英文解释
To carefully choose from a group; of superior quality and chosen carefully.
✏️ 例句
Candidates are selected based on academic performance and leadership skills.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
select from/for；carefully selected；selection n.；selective adj.（selective school 精英学校）
🧠 助记
se（分开）+ lect（读/选）→ 分开来挑选 → 选择
select::/sɪˈlekt/:v./adj. 选择:Universities should select students based on both academic merit and potential.
3. card /kɑːrd/ n. 卡片，纸牌；名片；v. 要求出示证件
📖 英文解释
A piece of stiff paper used for writing or games; a small piece of plastic used for payment; to check someone's ID.
✏️ 例句
She handed him her business card after the networking event.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
credit/debit card；card game 纸牌游戏；wild card 外卡/意外因素；on the cards 有可能发生
🧠 助记
card → 卡 → 卡片
card::/kɑːrd/:n. 卡片，纸牌:The wild card in the election was the surge of young voters.
4. cover /ˈkʌvər/ v. 覆盖，遮盖；涉及；报道；n. 封面；掩护
📖 英文解释
To put something over or in front of something; to include or deal with a subject; the outside of a book.
✏️ 例句
The report covers all aspects of environmental policy reform.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cover a topic/distance；under cover of（在……掩护下）；cover story 封面故事/掩护故事；cover up 掩盖
🧠 助记
co + ver（真实）→ 盖住真实 → 覆盖/掩护
cover::/ˈkʌvər/:v. 覆盖，遮盖:The new legislation covers a broad range of environmental protection issues.
5. earn /ɜːrn/ v. 挣得，赚取；赢得，博得
📖 英文解释
To receive money in return for work; to gain something you deserve through effort or behavior.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
earn a living/salary；earn respect/praise；well-earned 理所应得的；earnings n. 收入
🧠 助记
earn → 谐音爱恩 → 用爱和恩情挣来的 → 赚得/赢得
earn::/ɜːrn/:v. 挣得，赚取:Nations must earn their credibility through consistent and transparent governance.
6. farmer /ˈfɑːrmər/ n. 农民，农场主
📖 英文解释
A person who owns or manages a farm.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
small/large-scale farmer；farmer's market 农贸市场；subsistence farmer 自给自足的农民
🧠 助记
farm（农场）+ er → 在农场的人 → 农民
farmer::/ˈfɑːrmər/:n. 农民，农场主:Small farmers are often the first victims of climate-related crop failures.
7. trend /trend/ n. 趋势，倾向；v. 趋向
📖 英文解释
A general direction in which something is developing or changing; to develop in a particular direction.
✏️ 例句
There is a growing trend toward remote work in the technology sector.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
current/global/emerging trend；trend toward/away from；trend-setter 引领潮流者；trendy adj. 时髦的
🧠 助记
trend → t + rend（撕裂）→ 朝某方向撕裂前进 → 趋势
trend::/trend/:n. 趋势，倾向:The trend toward urbanization poses challenges for rural communities worldwide.
8. attract /əˈtrækt/ v. 吸引，引起（兴趣）
📖 英文解释
To cause someone to have interest in or admiration for something; to draw something toward itself.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
attract attention/investment/talent；attractive adj.；attraction n.；attract sb to/toward
🧠 助记
at（朝向）+ tract（拉）→ 朝向拉过来 → 吸引
attract::/əˈtrækt/:v. 吸引，引起（兴趣）:The policy aims to attract foreign investment into emerging technology sectors.
9. clean /kliːn/ adj. 干净的；v. 清洁；adv. 完全地

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
clean energy/water/air；keep clean；clean up 清理；clean-cut 整洁利落；come clean 坦白
🧠 助记
clean → 谐音可灵 → 可以灵活清洁的 → 干净的
clean::/kliːn/:adj. 干净的:Transitioning to clean energy is critical for achieving carbon neutrality.
10. method /ˈmeθəd/ n. 方法，方式；条理
📖 英文解释
A particular way of doing something, especially one that is systematic; orderly arrangement.
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记

method::/ˈmeθəd/:n. 方法，方式:The research employed a mixed-methods approach to gather diverse data.
11. promise /ˈprɒmɪs/ n./v. 承诺，许诺；前途
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

promise::/ˈprɒmɪs/:n./v. 承诺，许诺:The new initiative shows great promise in reducing urban unemployment rates.

desire::/dɪˈzaɪər/:n./v. 渴望，欲望:The desire for social justice motivates grassroots activism globally.
13. lie /laɪ/ v. 说谎；躺；n. 谎言；位置
