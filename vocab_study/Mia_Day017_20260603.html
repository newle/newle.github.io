1. device /dɪˈvaɪs/ n. 装置，设备；方法，手段
📖 英文解释
A piece of equipment made for a specific purpose; a method or trick used to achieve something.
✏️ 例句
Mobile devices have fundamentally changed the way people access information.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
electronic/smart device；a literary device 文学手法；device for doing；handheld device
🧠 助记
de（下）+ vice（副/工具）→ 副工具 → 装置/设备
device::/dɪˈvaɪs/:n. 装置，设备:Smart devices have become indispensable tools in modern educational environments.

✏️ 例句

🧠 助记
court → 英国法院 → 法庭；court 球场（网球、篮球）
court::/kɔːrt/:n. 法庭，法院:The supreme court's ruling set a landmark precedent for environmental law.

6. solution /səˈluːʃn/ n. 解决方案；溶液

7. essential /ɪˈsenʃl/ adj. 必不可少的，基本的；n. pl. 基本要素

essential::/ɪˈsenʃl/:adj. 必不可少的，基本的:Access to clean water is essential for public health and economic development.
8. particular /pəˈtɪkjʊlər/ adj. 特定的，特别的；挑剔的；n. 细节
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

particular::/pəˈtɪkjʊlər/:adj. 特定的，特别的:In particular, the report highlights the need for investment in renewable infrastructure.
