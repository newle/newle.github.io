1. negative /ˈneɡətɪv/ adj. 消极的；否定的；负面的
📖 英文解释
expressing denial, refusal, or disagreement; having a harmful or unfavorable effect; (of a number) less than zero
✏️ 例句
The report had a negative impact on investor confidence, causing the stock price to fall sharply.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
negative attitude/effect/result; have a negative impact on; negative feedback; test negative for (检测结果为阴性)
🧠 助记
negate（否定）+ ive → 带有否定色彩 → 消极的、负面的
negative::/ˈneɡətɪv/:adj. 消极的:The experiment yielded a negative result.
2. restaurant /ˈrestrɒnt/ n. 饭馆；餐厅
📖 英文解释
a place where people pay to sit and eat meals that are cooked and served on the premises
✏️ 例句
They celebrated their anniversary at a French restaurant in the city center.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
go to a restaurant; open/run a restaurant; a fast-food restaurant; restaurant industry
🧠 助记
rest（休息）+ au + rant → 休息进餐的地方 → 餐厅
restaurant::/ˈrestrɒnt/:n. 饭馆:The restaurant offers a wide variety of dishes.
3. federal /ˈfedərəl/ adj. 联邦的；联邦制的
📖 英文解释
relating to a system of government in which several states form a unity but remain independent in internal affairs
✏️ 例句
The federal government announced new regulations to combat air pollution across all states.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
federal government/law/court/agency; federal budget; the Federal Reserve (美联储)
🧠 助记
feder（联合、拉丁语）+ al → 联合起来的 → 联邦的
federal::/ˈfedərəl/:adj. 联邦的:Federal taxes are collected by the central government.
4. labor /ˈleɪbər/ n. 劳动力；劳工；分娩 v. 努力工作
📖 英文解释
work, especially physical work; the workforce of a country; the process of childbirth
✏️ 例句
The factory relies heavily on cheap labor to keep production costs low.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
labor market/force/cost; labor union (工会); divide labor; manual labor; skilled labor
🧠 助记
lab（实验室，努力工作的地方）+ or → 劳动、劳工
labor::/ˈleɪbər/:n. 劳动力:The construction project required a large amount of skilled labor.
5. progress /ˈprəʊɡres/ (n.) /prəˈɡres/ (v.) n./v. 进步；进展；前进
📖 英文解释
forward or onward movement toward a destination or goal; development toward a better or more complete state
✏️ 例句
The student made remarkable progress in mathematics after receiving extra tutoring.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make progress; in progress (进行中); progress report; economic/social progress; progress toward a goal
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ gress（走，同grade）→ 向前走 → 进步、进展
progress::/ˈprəʊɡres/ (n.) /prəˈɡres/ (v.):n./v. 进步:Scientists are making steady progress in cancer research.
6. robot /ˈrəʊbɒt/ n. 机器人；自动装置
📖 英文解释
a machine capable of carrying out a complex series of actions automatically, especially one programmable by a computer
✏️ 例句
Industrial robots have replaced many manual workers in automobile assembly lines.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
industrial robot; robot technology; robot arm; robotic (adj. 机器人的、机械的)
🧠 助记
来自捷克语 robota（强制劳动）→ 代人劳动的机器 → 机器人
robot::/ˈrəʊbɒt/:n. 机器人:The factory uses robots to perform repetitive tasks.
7. movement /ˈmuːvmənt/ n. 运动；活动；动作；（社会）运动
📖 英文解释
an act of moving; a group of people working together to advance their shared political, social, or artistic ideas
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
social/political movement; freedom of movement; peace movement; movement of goods/people; body movement
🧠 助记
move（移动）+ ment（名词后缀）→ 移动、运动
movement::/ˈmuːvmənt/:n. 运动:The environmental movement has gained momentum worldwide.
8. weather /ˈweðər/ n. 天气；气候 v. 经受（困难）；风化
📖 英文解释
the state of the atmosphere at a particular place and time; to come safely through a difficult situation
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
weather forecast/conditions; in all weathers; weather a crisis/storm (度过危机); under the weather (不舒服)
🧠 助记
wea（风吹）+ ther → 风吹雨打 → 天气；经受风雨
weather::/ˈweðər/:n. 天气:The expedition had to weather extreme conditions in the mountains.
9. mistake /mɪˈsteɪk/ n. 错误；误解 v. 误解；错误地认为
📖 英文解释
an action or judgment that is misguided or wrong; to wrongly identify someone or something
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make a mistake; by mistake (错误地); mistake A for B (把A误认为B); common/serious mistake; admit a mistake
🧠 助记
mis（错误地）+ take（拿）→ 拿错了 → 错误
mistake::/mɪˈsteɪk/:n. 错误:She made the mistake of ignoring the early warning signs.
10. infer /ɪnˈfɜːr/ v. 推断；推论；暗示
📖 英文解释
to deduce or conclude (information) from evidence and reasoning rather than from explicit statements
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
infer from; infer that...; inference (n. 推断); inferential (adj.); infer a conclusion
🧠 助记
in（进入）+ fer（带来，同transfer）→ 从信息中带出结论 → 推断
infer::/ɪnˈfɜːr/:v. 推断:We can infer from his behavior that he is under stress.
11. enter /ˈentər/ v. 进入；参加；登记
📖 英文解释

enter::/ˈentər/:v. 进入:The company plans to enter the Asian market next year.
12. charge /tʃɑːrdʒ/ v./n. 收费；索价；充电；指控；负责
📖 英文解释

charge::/tʃɑːrdʒ/:v./n. 收费:She is in charge of the marketing department.
