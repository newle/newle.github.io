1. article /ˈɑːrtɪkl/ n. 文章；条款；冠词；物品
📖 英文解释
a piece of writing in a newspaper or magazine; a clause or item in a legal document; a grammatical determiner
✏️ 例句
The academic article challenged conventional theories about the causes of the financial crisis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
write/publish an article; news article; article of clothing; article of faith; definite/indefinite article
🧠 助记
art（技艺）+ icle → 有技艺地写出来的东西 → 文章、条款
article::/ˈɑːrtɪkl/:n. 文章:She published an article on climate policy in a leading journal.
2. weight /weɪt/ n. 体重；重量；重要性 v. 加重；赋予权重
📖 英文解释
a body's relative mass; the degree of importance given to something; to make heavy or heavier
✏️ 例句
The survey gave greater weight to responses from experienced professionals in the final analysis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lose/gain weight; carry weight (有影响力); weight of evidence; throw one's weight behind; dead weight
🧠 助记
weigh（称重）+ t → 称出来的量 → 重量
weight::/weɪt/:n. 体重:The committee gave considerable weight to economic factors.
3. trip /trɪp/ n. 旅行；短途旅游 v. 绊倒；失误
📖 英文解释
a journey or excursion; to catch one's foot and stumble; to make a mistake
✏️ 例句
The research trip to the Amazon provided valuable data on biodiversity and deforestation patterns.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
business/field trip; round trip (往返); take a trip; ego trip; trip over/up; trip wire
🧠 助记
trip → 旅途中一不小心 trip（绊倒）→ 旅行、绊倒
trip::/trɪp/:n. 旅行:The scientists went on a field trip to collect samples.
4. investment /ɪnˈvestmənt/ n. 投资；投入；值得花费的东西
📖 英文解释
the action of investing money for profit; a thing worth buying because it may be profitable
✏️ 例句
Foreign investment in renewable energy has surged significantly over the past decade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
investment in/on; return on investment (ROI); foreign investment; investment bank; invest (v.); investor (n.)
🧠 助记
invest（投资）+ ment → 投资这个行为/结果
investment::/ɪnˈvestmənt/:n. 投资:Education is the best investment a society can make.
5. due /djuː/ adj. 预期的；到期的；应付的；应有的
📖 英文解释
expected at or planned for at a certain time; owed or owing; proper or adequate
✏️ 例句
The loan is due for repayment in six months, and failure to pay will incur a penalty.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
due to (由于); be due on/in; due date; overdue; in due course (适时); give due credit
🧠 助记
due → 想象 due date（截止日期）→ 到期的、预期的
due::/djuː/:adj. 预期的:The report is due next Friday.
6. aspect /ˈæspekt/ n. 方面；外观；朝向；（动词）体
📖 英文解释
a particular part or feature of something; a position facing a particular direction
✏️ 例句
The environmental aspect of the development project requires thorough assessment before approval.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
aspect of; in all aspects; from all aspects; consider every aspect; positive/negative aspect; aspect ratio
🧠 助记
a（加强）+ spect（看，如inspect）→ 从各角度看 → 方面
aspect::/ˈæspekt/:n. 方面:Every aspect of the policy has been carefully examined.
7. wear /weər/ v. 穿着；磨损；忍受 n. 穿着；磨损
📖 英文解释
to have on one's body as clothing; to damage by friction; to become less effective over time
✏️ 例句
The constant pressure of work began to wear him down, affecting both his health and productivity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
wear out (磨损/疲惫); wear away; wear off (消退); daily wear; wear and tear; footwear; outerwear
🧠 助记
wear → 想象每天穿（wear）衣服 → 穿着；磨损（穿久了）
wear::/weər/:v. 穿着:Years of exposure to the elements had worn the statue smooth.
8. define /dɪˈfaɪn/ v. 定义；规定；界定；使轮廓清晰
📖 英文解释
to state or describe exactly the nature or scope of; to mark out the boundary or limits of
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
define a term/concept; define as; well-defined; definition (n.); definite (adj.); undefined
🧠 助记
de（完全）+ fine（终点、界限）→ 划出界限 → 定义
define::/dɪˈfaɪn/:v. 定义:The report defines success in terms of measurable outcomes.
9. balance /ˈbæləns/ n./v. 平衡；余额；权衡；结余
📖 英文解释
an even distribution of weight; a situation in which different elements are equal or in the correct proportions
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
balance of power/trade; on balance (总的来说); balanced diet; balance sheet; throw off balance
🧠 助记
bal（两边，来自bi）+ ance → 两边相等 → 平衡
balance::/ˈbæləns/:n./v. 平衡:Maintaining work-life balance is essential for long-term productivity.
10. enable /ɪˈneɪbl/ v. 使能够；使成为可能；激活
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
en（使）+ able（能够）→ 使某人能够 → 使能够
enable::/ɪˈneɪbl/:v. 使能够:This software enables users to manage their finances more effectively.
