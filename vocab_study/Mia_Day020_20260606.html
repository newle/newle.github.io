1. predict /prɪˈdɪkt/ v. 预测；预言；预报
📖 英文解释
to say or estimate that a specified thing will happen in the future, based on present indications
✏️ 例句
Economists find it increasingly difficult to predict market behavior in an era of rapid technological change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
predict that; predict the outcome; hard to predict; prediction (n.); predictable (adj.); unpredictable
🧠 助记
pre（提前）+ dict（说，如dictate）→ 提前说出来 → 预测
predict::/prɪˈdɪkt/:v. 预测:Scientists predict that global temperatures will rise by 2°C by 2100.
2. despite /dɪˈspaɪt/ prep. 尽管；不管
📖 英文解释
without being affected by; in spite of
✏️ 例句
Despite significant obstacles, the team succeeded in completing the mission ahead of schedule.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
despite + noun/gerund; despite the fact that; in spite of (同义); despite efforts/resistance
🧠 助记
de（向下）+ spite（蔑视）→ 向下踩着困难 → 尽管（有困难）
despite::/dɪˈspaɪt/:prep. 尽管:Despite the challenges, the project was completed on time.

morning::/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ/:n. 早晨:The committee held its first session early in the morning.
