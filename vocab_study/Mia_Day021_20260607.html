1. conduct /ˈkɒndʌkt/ v. 实施、开展；n. 行为、品行
📖 英文解释
To organize and carry out an activity or process; to behave in a particular way.
✏️ 例句
The researchers conducted a survey to understand students reading habits.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ conduct a survey/experiment/research 开展调查/实验/研究 ★ conduct oneself 表现自己 辨析：conduct / carry out / perform：conduct 多用于正式活动；carry out 强调执行；perform 强调完成某项技艺
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ duct（引导）= 共同引导 → 实施、开展
conduct::/ˈkɒndʌkt/:v. 实施、开展:They conducted a thorough investigation into the accident.
2. direct /dɪˈrekt/ adj. 径直的、直接的；v. 指示、导演
📖 英文解释
Going straight from one place to another without stopping; to aim or manage something.
✏️ 例句
She gave him a direct answer without any hesitation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ direct result/effect 直接结果/影响 ★ direct sb to do sth 指示某人做某事 辨析：direct / straight：direct 侧重无中间步骤；straight 侧重物理路径笔直
🧠 助记
di（两点）+ rect（直线）= 两点之间直线最短 → 直接的
direct::/dɪˈrekt/:adj. 径直的、直接的:The manager directed the team to finish the project by Friday.

📖 英文解释
To make certain that something will happen or be the case.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ ensure + that 从句（高频写作句型） ★ ensure sth / ensure that sb does sth 辨析：ensure / assure / insure：ensure 确保某事发生；assure 向人保证；insure 投保
🧠 助记
en（使）+ sure（确定）= 使确定 → 确保
ensure::/ɪnˈʃʊə/:v. 确保、保证:We must ensure that all students have equal access to education.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ food/public/road safety 食品/公共/道路安全 ★ safety measures/regulations 安全措施/法规 辨析：safety / security：safety 侧重避免意外伤害；security 侧重防止威胁
🧠 助记
safe（安全）+ ty（名词后缀）= 安全状态
safety::/ˈseɪfti/:n. 安全:The factory implemented new safety measures to protect workers.
