1. film /fɪlm/ n. 电影；v. 拍摄
📖 英文解释
A story recorded as a series of moving images; to record moving images.
✏️ 例句
The documentary film raised public awareness about climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ make/shoot a film 拍电影 ★ film industry/festival 电影工业/节 辨析：film / movie：film 偏英式、艺术性；movie 偏美式、娱乐性
🧠 助记
film（胶片）= 老式电影用胶片拍摄，film 即电影
film::/fɪlm/:n. 电影:The director's latest film won several international awards.
2. review /rɪˈvjuː/ v./n. 复习、回顾、评论
📖 英文解释
To examine or assess something again; a critical appraisal of a book, film, etc.
✏️ 例句
Students should review their notes regularly to improve retention.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ review for an exam 备考复习 ★ book/film review 书评/影评 辨析：review / revise：review 广义复习；revise 英式英语中特指备考复习
🧠 助记
re（再）+ view（看）= 再次看 → 复习、回顾
review::/rɪˈvjuː/:v./n. 复习、回顾、评论:The committee will review the policy before making a final decision.

To make an effort to do something; an act of trying to achieve something.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ attempt to do sth 试图做某事 ★ make an attempt at/to do sth 尝试做某事 辨析：attempt / try / endeavor：attempt 侧重尝试困难之事；try 最通用；endeavor 最正式
🧠 助记
at（向）+ tempt（诱惑/试探）= 向着目标试探 → 尝试
attempt::/əˈtempt/:v./n. 试图、企图:Scientists made several attempts to develop a vaccine before succeeding.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ accumulate/distribute wealth 积累/分配财富 ★ a wealth of information/experience 大量信息/经验 辨析：wealth / fortune / riches：wealth 最常用；fortune 指大笔财富或运气；riches 文学性
🧠 助记
wealthy（富有的）→ wealth（名词）= 财富
wealth::/welθ/:n. 财富、大量:Education can help redistribute wealth more equitably across society.

📖 英文解释
To connect in the mind; to spend time with someone; a partner or colleague.
✏️ 例句
Success is often associated with hard work and persistence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ associate A with B 将A与B联系起来 ★ be associated with 与……有关 辨析：associate / connect / link：三者都表联系，associate 多含心理联想；link 强调因果；connect 最宽泛
🧠 助记
as（向）+ sociate（社会）= 向社会靠近 → 结交、联系
associate::/əˈsəʊʃɪeɪt/:v. 联系、结交:Pollution is closely associated with industrial development.

prefer::/prɪˈfɜː/:v. 偏好、更喜欢:Researchers prefer qualitative methods for studying human behavior.

10. ill /ɪl/ adj. 有病的、坏的；adv. 不利地
📖 英文解释
Not in good health; morally wrong; in a way that is bad or unfavorable.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ fall ill 生病 ★ ill effects/will 坏影响/恶意 辨析：ill / sick：ill 更正式，多用表语；sick 可作定语（sick leave），美式更常用 sick
🧠 助记
ill → i（我）+ ll（两条腿不稳）= 我两腿无力 → 生病
ill::/ɪl/:adj. 有病的、坏的:Poor nutrition can lead to a range of ill effects on public health.
