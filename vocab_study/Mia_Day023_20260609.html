1. danger /ˈdeɪndʒə/ n. 危险
📖 英文解释
The possibility of suffering harm or injury; a person or thing likely to cause harm.
✏️ 例句
The scientists warned of the danger of rising sea levels to coastal communities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be in danger of 有……的危险 ★ pose a danger to 对……构成威胁 辨析：danger / hazard / risk：danger 最强烈，指迫在眉睫；hazard 指潜在威胁；risk 侧重概率性风险
🧠 助记
danger（危险）= d（死亡）+ anger（愤怒）→ 愤怒带来死亡危险
danger::/ˈdeɪndʒə/:n. 危险:Climate change poses a serious danger to global food security.

🧠 助记
piece（一件/碎片）= p（分开）+ iece → 分开后的一块 → 碎片
piece::/piːs/:n.（一）件、碎片、作品:Each piece of evidence must be carefully evaluated before drawing conclusions.
