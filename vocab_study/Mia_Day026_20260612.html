1. conclusion /kənˈkluːʒən/ n. 结论；结尾；缔结
📖 英文解释
A judgment or decision reached after consideration; the end of something.
✏️ 例句
The report reaches the conclusion that climate change demands immediate global action.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
draw/reach/come to a conclusion 得出结论 in conclusion 综上所述（写作收尾高频） jump to conclusions 仓促下结论 conclude v. 得出结论；conclusive adj. 确定的
🧠 助记
con(完全)+clus(关)+ion→全部关上→得出结论；结尾
conclusion::/kənˈkluːʒən/:n. 结论:In conclusion, education is the key to solving many social problems.
