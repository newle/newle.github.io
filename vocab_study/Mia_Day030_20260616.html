1. crop /krɒp/ n. 庄稼，农作物；v. 收割；剪短
📖 英文解释
Plants grown on a large scale for food or other uses; to cut or trim something short.
✏️ 例句
Farmers in this region grow crops such as wheat and corn to feed the local population.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ food crop / cash crop 粮食作物 / 经济作物（考研高频搭配） ★ crop up 突然出现（注意引申义） ☆ a bumper crop 丰收 辨析：crop / harvest：crop 指庄稼本身；harvest 指收获的行为或收获量
🧠 助记
crop = c + rop（绳子）——用绳子把庄稼捆起来收割
crop::/krɒp/:n. 庄稼，农作物:The crop harvest this year exceeded all expectations.
