1. previous /ˈpriːviəs/ adj. 以前的，先前的；过早的
📖 英文解释
Existing or occurring before in time or order; coming before in sequence.
✏️ 例句
Previous studies on the topic did not account for the long-term effects on mental health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ previous experience / research / year 以往经验 / 研究 / 年份 ★ the previous day / chapter 前一天 / 章节 ☆ previously adv. 之前地（学术写作高频） 辨析：previous / former / prior：previous 泛指之前；former 指两者中的前者；prior 较正式，prior to = before
🧠 助记
pre(之前)+vious(路)——走在这条路之前的=以前的
previous::/ˈpriːviəs/:adj. 以前的，先前的:Her previous experience in marketing made her the ideal candidate.
