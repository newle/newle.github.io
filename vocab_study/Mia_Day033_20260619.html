1. inform /ɪnˈfɔːrm/ v. 通知；告诉；告发
📖 英文解释
to give someone facts or information; to tell someone about something
✏️ 例句
Please inform me of any changes to the schedule.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
inform sb of/about sth 通知某人某事；keep sb informed 让某人了解最新情况
🧠 助记
in（进入）+ form（形式）→ 把信息送进对方脑中 → 通知
inform::/ɪnˈfɔːrm/:v. 通知:The doctor informed the patient of the diagnosis.
2. twice /twaɪs/ adv. 两次；两倍
📖 英文解释
two times; on two occasions; to double the degree or amount
✏️ 例句
I have been to Paris twice this year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
twice a week 每周两次；twice as much/many 两倍之多
🧠 助记
two（两）的变体 + -ce → 两次
twice::/twaɪs/:adv. 两次:She reads twice as fast as her classmates.
3. thank /θæŋk/ v. 感谢；道谢
📖 英文解释
to express gratitude to someone; to show appreciation for a kindness
✏️ 例句
I want to thank everyone who supported me.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
thank sb for sth/doing 因某事感谢某人；thank you for… 常用礼貌表达
🧠 助记
联想 think（思考）→ 用心思去感谢 → 感谢
thank::/θæŋk/:v. 感谢:She thanked her teacher for the patient guidance.
4. illness /ˈɪlnəs/ n. 疾病；生病
📖 英文解释
a disease or period of sickness affecting the body or mind
✏️ 例句
He missed school due to illness.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
mental illness 精神疾病；serious/chronic illness 严重/慢性疾病
🧠 助记
ill（生病的）+ -ness（名词后缀）→ 生病的状态 → 疾病
illness::/ˈɪlnəs/:n. 疾病:Her illness kept her in hospital for two weeks.
5. altogether /ˌɔːltəˈɡeðər/ adv. 总共；完全地；总体上
📖 英文解释
taking everything into account; completely; in total
✏️ 例句
There were fifty guests altogether at the party.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
altogether different 完全不同；not altogether 并非完全；in the altogether 赤裸（习语）
🧠 助记
all（所有）+ together（在一起）→ 所有合在一起 → 总共
altogether::/ˌɔːltəˈɡeðər/:adv. 总共:The project cost $5 million altogether.
6. welfare /ˈwelfer/ n. 福利；幸福；救济金
📖 英文解释
the health, happiness, and safety of a person or group; financial support for people in need
✏️ 例句
The government is responsible for the welfare of its citizens.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
social welfare 社会福利；welfare state 福利国家；animal welfare 动物福利
🧠 助记
well（好）+ fare（情况）→ 情况好 → 福利
welfare::/ˈwelfer/:n. 福利:Child welfare is a top priority for the organization.
7. blood /blʌd/ n. 血；血液；血统
📖 英文解释
the red liquid that circulates in the arteries and veins of animals; family relationship
✏️ 例句
The blood test results came back normal.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
blood pressure 血压；blood type 血型；in cold blood 蓄意地
🧠 助记
形象记忆：红色液体，联想 flood（洪水）→ 血液如洪流
blood::/blʌd/:n. 血:Blood samples were collected for analysis.
8. calorie /ˈkæləri/ n. 卡路里；热量单位
📖 英文解释
a unit of energy in food; the amount of heat needed to raise the temperature of one gram of water by one degree Celsius
✏️ 例句
A typical adult needs about 2,000 calories per day.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
calorie intake 热量摄入；burn calories 消耗卡路里；low-calorie diet 低热量饮食
🧠 助记
联想 calor（拉丁语：热）→ 热量单位 → 卡路里
calorie::/ˈkæləri/:n. 卡路里:Exercise helps you burn more calories each day.
9. herself /hərˈself/ pron. 她自己；她亲自
📖 英文解释
the reflexive form of 'she'; used when the subject and object are the same female person; used for emphasis
✏️ 例句
She cooked the entire meal herself.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
by herself 独自地；for herself 为了她自己；她亲自做某事强调用法
🧠 助记
her（她）+ self（自己）→ 她自己
herself::/hərˈself/:pron. 她自己:She made the decision herself without any help.
10. intelligent /ɪnˈtelɪdʒənt/ adj. 聪明的；有才智的
📖 英文解释
having or showing intelligence, especially of a high level; able to learn and understand quickly
✏️ 例句
She is one of the most intelligent students in the class.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
intelligent design 智能设计；highly intelligent 非常聪明；intelligent system 智能系统
🧠 助记
intel（信息情报）+ -igent → 能处理大量信息的 → 聪明的
intelligent::/ɪnˈtelɪdʒənt/:adj. 聪明的:An intelligent approach to the problem saved time.
11. cite /saɪt/ v. 引用；援引；提到
📖 英文解释
to quote or refer to a book, paper, or author as evidence or example; to officially summon to appear in court
✏️ 例句
The researcher cited several studies to support her argument.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cite sb/sth as 引用……作为；cite evidence 援引证据；be cited for 因……被传唤
🧠 助记
c + ite → 联想 site（地点）→ 在文中标注来源地点 → 引用
cite::/saɪt/:v. 引用:The essay cites three important academic sources.
12. guide /ɡaɪd/ v./n. 指引；向导；指南
📖 英文解释
to show the way to someone; to direct or influence someone's behavior; a person who shows the way or a book of instructions
✏️ 例句
A local guide led us through the ancient city.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
guide sb through 引导某人经历；tourist guide 旅游向导；guide book 旅行指南
🧠 助记
联想 guy（家伙）→ 带路的家伙 → 向导；指引
guide::/ɡaɪd/:v./n. 指引:The teacher guided students through the complex problem.
13. household /ˈhaʊshoʊld/ n. 家庭；一家人；adj. 家庭的
📖 英文解释
all the people who live together in a house; relating to the home and family
✏️ 例句
Many households in the country lack access to clean water.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
house（房子）+ hold（持有/保有）→ 一起居住在房子里的人 → 家庭
household::/ˈhaʊshoʊld/:n. 家庭:The survey covered over 1,000 households across the city.
14. pupil /ˈpjuːpəl/ n. 学生；瞳孔
📖 英文解释
a child or student who is being taught; the dark circular opening in the center of the eye
✏️ 例句
The pupils sat quietly while the teacher explained the lesson.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

pupil::/ˈpjuːpəl/:n. 学生:Each pupil received a personalized feedback report.
15. insurance /ɪnˈʃʊərəns/ n. 保险；保险费；保障
📖 英文解释
an arrangement in which an organization undertakes to provide a guarantee of compensation for specified loss or damage in exchange for payment
✏️ 例句
It is important to have health insurance in case of emergencies.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

insurance::/ɪnˈʃʊərəns/:n. 保险:The company offers comprehensive insurance coverage for employees.
16. criticism /ˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/ n. 批评；评论；指责
📖 英文解释
the expression of disapproval of someone or something; the analysis and judgment of the merits and faults of something

criticism::/ˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/:n. 批评:The film received widespread criticism for its weak plot.
17. exchange /ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ/ v./n. 交换；兑换；交流
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
exchange for 用……交换；exchange rate 汇率；stock exchange 股票交易所；in exchange for 作为……的交换
🧠 助记
ex（出）+ change（改变）→ 改变出入的东西 → 交换
exchange::/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ/:v./n. 交换:The two countries agreed to exchange trade representatives.
18. ship /ʃɪp/ n. 船舶；v. 装运；运送

🧠 助记

ship::/ʃɪp/:n. 船舶:The company ships products to more than 50 countries.
19. label /ˈleɪbəl/ n. 标签；v. 贴标签；把……称为

label::/ˈleɪbəl/:n. 标签:The researcher labeled each sample with a unique code.
20. differ /ˈdɪfər/ v. 不同；有差异；持异议
📖 英文解释

differ::/ˈdɪfər/:v. 不同:The two studies differ significantly in their methodology.
21. commit /kəˈmɪt/ v. 把…交托给；承诺；犯（罪）
📖 英文解释
to carry out or perpetrate (a crime or mistake); to pledge or bind oneself to a course of action
✏️ 例句

commit::/kəˈmɪt/:v. 把…交托给:She committed to finishing the report by Friday.
22. admit /ədˈmɪt/ v. 承认；准许进入；接纳

admit::/ədˈmɪt/:v. 承认:The university admitted 500 new students this year.

objective::/əbˈdʒektɪv/:adj. 客观的:The primary objective of the study is to measure learning outcomes.

contribution::/ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃən/:n. 贡献:His contribution to the research was widely recognized.

context::/ˈkɒntekst/:n. 上下文:The word 'bank' has different meanings depending on context.
