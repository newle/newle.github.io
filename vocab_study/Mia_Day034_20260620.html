1. rent /rent/ v./n. 租；租金
📖 英文解释
to pay money in exchange for the use of property or equipment; the money paid for such use
✏️ 例句
They rent a small apartment in the city centre.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rent out 出租；rent-free 免租金；for rent 供出租；monthly rent 月租
🧠 助记
r + ent（进入）→ 付钱才能进入使用 → 租
rent::/rent/:v./n. 租:The young couple decided to rent rather than buy a house.
2. video /ˈvɪdiəʊ/ n. 录像；视频；adj. 视频的
📖 英文解释
a recording of moving visual images; the system of recording, reproducing, or broadcasting moving visual images
✏️ 例句
She posted a short video of her trip on social media.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
video conference 视频会议；video clip 视频片段；go viral（视频）病毒式传播
🧠 助记
vid（看，拉丁词根）+ eo → 用来看的东西 → 视频
video::/ˈvɪdiəʊ/:n. 录像:The company used a promotional video to attract new customers.
3. brother /ˈbrʌðər/ n. 兄弟；同伴；同行
📖 英文解释
a man or boy in relation to other sons and daughters of his parents; a male fellow member of a religious community
✏️ 例句
His older brother works as a doctor in London.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
brother-in-law 内兄；big brother 老大哥（比喻权威监控）
🧠 助记
联想：bro（兄弟的口语）+ ther → 兄弟
brother::/ˈbrʌðər/:n. 兄弟:His brother encouraged him to pursue his passion for music.
4. capital /ˈkæpɪtəl/ n. 首都；大写字母；资本；adj. 重要的
📖 英文解释
the city that functions as the seat of government; wealth in the form of money or assets; an uppercase letter
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
capital punishment 死刑；venture capital 风险资本；capital letter 大写字母
🧠 助记
cap（头部）+ ital → 头部/最重要的地方 → 首都；头部资金 → 资本
capital::/ˈkæpɪtəl/:n. 首都:Foreign capital played a key role in the country's economic development.
5. circumstance /ˈsɜːrkəmstæns/ n. 情况；处境；环境（常用复数）
📖 英文解释
a fact or condition connected with or relevant to an event or action; the conditions that affect someone
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
under the circumstances 在这种情况下；in/under no circumstances 决不；extraordinary circumstances 特殊情况
🧠 助记
circum（周围）+ stance（站立）→ 站在周围的情况 → 环境；情况
circumstance::/ˈsɜːrkəmstæns/:n. 情况:The committee considered all the circumstances before reaching a decision.
6. illustrate /ˈɪləstreɪt/ v. 说明；加插图；阐明
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
il（进入）+ lustrate（光亮）→ 用光照亮 → 阐明
illustrate::/ˈɪləstreɪt/:v. 说明:The graph illustrates the steady rise in global temperatures.
7. target /ˈtɑːrɡɪt/ n. 目标；靶子；v. 把……作为目标

target::/ˈtɑːrɡɪt/:n. 目标:The company set an ambitious sales target for the new fiscal year.
