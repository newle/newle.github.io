1. welcome /ˈwelkəm/ v./adj./n. 欢迎；受欢迎的
📖 英文解释
to greet someone arriving in a glad and friendly way; a greeting or reception given to someone on arrival
✏️ 例句
We welcome all new students to the university.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
welcome sb to 欢迎某人来到；you're welcome 不客气；welcome change 受欢迎的变化
🧠 助记
well（好）+ come（来）→ 好好地来 → 欢迎
welcome::/ˈwelkəm/:v./adj./n. 欢迎:The committee welcomed the government's decision to increase funding.
2. switch /swɪtʃ/ v. 切换；改变；n. 开关；转变
📖 英文解释
to change or exchange; a device for turning electricity on and off; a change from one thing to another
✏️ 例句
She decided to switch careers after ten years in finance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
switch to 转换到；switch off/on 关掉/打开；light switch 电灯开关
🧠 助记
联想 stitch（缝合）→ 把线接到另一处 → 切换
switch::/swɪtʃ/:v. 切换:Many consumers are switching to renewable energy sources.
3. complaint /kəmˈpleɪnt/ n. 抱怨；投诉；疾病
📖 英文解释
a statement that something is unsatisfactory; a reason for dissatisfaction; a mild illness
✏️ 例句
The company received dozens of complaints about the faulty product.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make a complaint 提出投诉；complaint against 对……的投诉；lodge a complaint 提交投诉
🧠 助记
complain（抱怨）+ -t → 抱怨的具体内容/行为 → 投诉
complaint::/kəmˈpleɪnt/:n. 抱怨:The customer service department handles hundreds of complaints each week.
4. interpret /ɪnˈtɜːrprɪt/ v. 口译；解释；理解
📖 英文解释
to explain the meaning of; to understand something in a particular way; to translate orally
✏️ 例句
The data can be interpreted in many different ways.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
interpret as 把……解释为；interpret for 为……做口译；misinterpret 误解
🧠 助记
inter（在……之间）+ pret（解释）→ 在两种语言/观点之间解释 → 口译；解释
interpret::/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪt/:v. 口译:It is important not to misinterpret the results of the study.
5. lawyer /ˈlɔɪər/ n. 律师；法律顾问
📖 英文解释
a person who practises or studies law; an attorney or a solicitor
✏️ 例句
She hired a lawyer to help with the contract dispute.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
defense/prosecution lawyer 辩护/控方律师；consult a lawyer 咨询律师；corporate lawyer 企业律师
🧠 助记
law（法律）+ yer（人）→ 从事法律的人 → 律师
lawyer::/ˈlɔɪər/:n. 律师:The company's lawyer advised against signing the contract without revision.
6. nowadays /ˈnaʊədeɪz/ adv. 现今；如今；当今
📖 英文解释
at the present time, in contrast with the past
✏️ 例句
Nowadays, most people have access to the internet.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nowadays 用于句首或句尾；in the past vs. nowadays 过去与现在的对比
🧠 助记
now（现在）+ a + days（日子）→ 现在的日子里 → 如今
nowadays::/ˈnaʊədeɪz/:adv. 现今:Nowadays, remote work has become standard practice in many industries.
7. priority /praɪˈɒrəti/ n. 优先；优先权；重要事项
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
prior（在前的）+ ity → 排在前面的 → 优先
priority::/praɪˈɒrəti/:n. 优先:The company has made customer satisfaction its highest priority.
8. military /ˈmɪlɪteri/ adj. 军事的；n. 军队

military::/ˈmɪlɪteri/:adj. 军事的:The military operation aimed to restore peace in the conflict zone.
9. cope /kəʊp/ v. 处理；应对；对付
📖 英文解释

cope::/kəʊp/:v. 处理:The healthcare system struggled to cope with the sudden influx of patients.
10. propose /prəˈpəʊz/ v. 提议；建议；求婚

🧠 助记

propose::/prəˈpəʊz/:v. 提议:The research team proposed a new model to explain the phenomenon.
11. relative /ˈrelətɪv/ n. 亲戚；adj. 比较的；相对的
