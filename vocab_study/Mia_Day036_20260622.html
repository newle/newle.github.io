1. humanity /hjuːmænɪti/ n. 人类；人性；人文学科
📖 英文解释
The quality of being human; human beings collectively; the study of subjects such as literature, philosophy, and history.
✏️ 例句
It was an act that threatened the very foundation of humanity and civilized society.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
humanity常与crimes against humanity搭配（反人类罪）；也指人文学科the humanities；考研常见于社会与科技对比类文章。
🧠 助记
human（人）+ ity（名词后缀）→ 人类的本质 → 人性、人类
humanity::/hjuːmænɪti/:n. 人类:crimes against humanity 危害人类罪
2. preserve /prɪzɜːrv/ v. 保护；保存；维持 n. 专属领域；保护区
📖 英文解释
To keep something in its original state or in good condition; to protect from harm or decay.
✏️ 例句
The government passed new laws to preserve the natural environment and protect endangered species.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
preserve常与preserve+名词搭配：preserve peace/tradition/culture；考研阅读中常见于环保、文化传承主题。
🧠 助记
pre-（提前）+ serve（服务/保管）→ 提前保管 → 保存、保护
preserve::/prɪzɜːrv/:v. 保护:preserve the environment 保护环境
3. explanation /ˌekspləneɪʃn/ n. 解释；说明；理由
📖 英文解释
A statement or account that makes something clear; a reason given for an action or belief.
✏️ 例句
The teacher gave a clear explanation of the difficult concept, and all students understood it immediately.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
explanation for/of sth（对……的解释）；give/provide/offer an explanation；考研写作常用：one explanation is that...
🧠 助记
explain（解释）+ ation（名词后缀）→ 解释的行为 → 说明、解释
explanation::/ˌekspləneɪʃn/:n. 解释:offer an explanation for 为……提供解释
4. flow /fləʊ/ v./n. 流动；流量；涌来
📖 英文解释
To move steadily and continuously in one direction; a smooth steady movement of liquid, air, or traffic.
✏️ 例句
Information flows freely across the internet, connecting people from all corners of the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
flow of information/traffic/capital（信息/交通/资本流动）；cash flow（现金流）；考研常出现于经济和社会类文章。
🧠 助记
联想：水flow（流）过石头，流动、流量
flow::/fləʊ/:v./n. 流动:the flow of information 信息流
5. technique /tekniːk/ n. 技术；技巧；方法
📖 英文解释
A specific method or skill used to accomplish something, especially in art, science, or sport.
✏️ 例句
Scientists have developed a new technique for detecting cancer at a very early stage.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
technique for doing sth（做某事的技巧）；区分technique（具体技巧）与technology（技术体系）；考研常见于科技类文章。
🧠 助记
tech（技术）+ nique → 具体操作的技术 → 技巧、方法
technique::/tekniːk/:n. 技术:develop a new technique 开发新技术
6. wake /weɪk/ v. 醒来；唤醒 n. 守夜；尾迹
📖 英文解释
To stop sleeping or cause someone to stop sleeping; the track left behind a moving ship.
✏️ 例句
The noise from the construction site woke the entire neighborhood at six in the morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
wake up（醒来）；in the wake of（在……之后，因……而引起）—考研高频短语！in the wake of the financial crisis（金融危机之后）。
🧠 助记
联想：闹钟wake you up（叫醒你）；in the wake of = 在尾迹中 → 紧随其后
wake::/weɪk/:v. 醒来:in the wake of the crisis 在危机之后
7. hurt /hɜːrt/ v. 伤害；疼痛 adj. 受伤的 n. 伤害
📖 英文解释
To cause pain or injury to someone or something; to feel pain.
✏️ 例句
Prolonged exposure to noise can hurt your hearing and overall mental health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hurt sb s feelings（伤害某人感情）；区分hurt（伤害/疼痛）、harm（危害）、injure（身体受伤）；考研常用于情感或社会影响类分析。
🧠 助记
联想：heart（心）去掉e → hurt，伤心就是hurt
hurt::/hɜːrt/:v. 伤害:hurt sb feelings 伤害某人的感情
8. consist /kənˈsɪst/ v. 由……组成；在于
📖 英文解释
To be made up of; to have something as the main or essential feature.
✏️ 例句
The committee consists of representatives from fifteen different countries and organizations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
consist of（由……构成，不用被动）；consist in（在于）—两个搭配考研均考！注意不说be consisted of。
🧠 助记
con-（共同）+ sist（站立）→ 共同站在一起 → 由……组成
consist::/kənˈsɪst/:v. 由……组成:consist of several parts 由几部分组成
9. master /mɑːstər/ n. 主人；硕士；大师 v. 精通；掌握
📖 英文解释
A person who has control over something or great skill in it; to gain complete knowledge or skill in something.
✏️ 例句
It takes years of practice to master a foreign language and speak it fluently.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
master+技能（master English/skills）；master s degree（硕士学位）；考研写作可用：one must master the fundamentals before advancing。
🧠 助记
master = 主人 → 主人掌控一切 → 精通、掌握
master::/mɑːstər/:n. 主人:master a new skill 掌握新技能
10. smile /smaɪl/ v./n. 微笑
📖 英文解释
To form one's features into a pleased, kind, or amused expression, typically with the corners of the mouth turned up.
✏️ 例句
A genuine smile can instantly make someone feel welcomed and at ease in an unfamiliar environment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
smile at sb（对……微笑）；smile with/in（带着……微笑）；考研中常出现于心理学或人际关系类文章，讨论非语言交流。
🧠 助记
s + mile → 笑一英里 → 灿烂的微笑
smile::/smaɪl/:v./n. 微笑:a genuine smile 真诚的微笑
11. convince /kənˈvɪns/ v. 使信服；说服
📖 英文解释
To cause someone to believe firmly in the truth of something; to persuade someone to do something.
✏️ 例句
The lawyer failed to convince the jury of his client's innocence despite presenting numerous pieces of evidence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
convince sb of sth / convince sb that…（使某人相信）；convince sb to do（说服某人做）；区分convince（使信服）vs. persuade（说服行动）。
🧠 助记
con-（完全）+ vince（征服）→ 彻底征服对方的想法 → 说服、使信服
convince::/kənˈvɪns/:v. 使信服:convince sb of the truth 使某人相信真相
12. delay /dɪˈleɪ/ v./n. 推迟；延迟；耽误
📖 英文解释
To make something happen later than planned or expected; a period of time by which something is late.
✏️ 例句
The flight was delayed by three hours due to severe weather conditions at the destination.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
delay+doing（推迟做某事，注意接动名词）；without delay（立即）；consider without delay（考研写作句型）。
🧠 助记
de-（向下/不）+ lay（放）→ 不放行 → 推迟、延迟
delay::/dɪˈleɪ/:v./n. 推迟:delay making a decision 推迟做决定
13. broad /brɔːd/ adj. 宽的；广泛的；大致的
📖 英文解释
Wide in extent; covering many subjects or areas; not detailed but giving the main points.
✏️ 例句
The university offers a broad range of courses in science, arts, and technology.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
broad+名词：broad knowledge/range/perspective（广泛的知识/范围/视角）；in broad daylight（大白天）—习语；broad-minded（思想开明的）。
🧠 助记
联想：broad和abroad（在国外）→ 国外很宽广 → 宽阔的
broad::/brɔːd/:adj. 宽的:a broad range of subjects 广泛的学科
14. sufficient /səˈfɪʃnt/ adj. 足够的；充分的
📖 英文解释
Enough for a particular purpose; as much as is needed.
✏️ 例句
The researcher argued that there was not sufficient evidence to support the controversial theory.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sufficient+名词；sufficient for（足以……）；sufficient to do（足够做某事）；考研逻辑题中常见：sufficient condition（充分条件）vs. necessary condition（必要条件）。
🧠 助记
suffice（足够）+ ient → 足够的、充分的
sufficient::/səˈfɪʃnt/:adj. 足够的:sufficient evidence 充分的证据
15. mostly /məʊstli/ adv. 主要地；大部分；通常
📖 英文解释
On most occasions or in most cases; mainly or chiefly.
✏️ 例句
The participants were mostly young people between the ages of eighteen and twenty-five.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
mostly用于描述大多数情况；区分mostly（大部分时间/情况）、most（最）、almost（几乎）；考研阅读中常用于数据分析。
🧠 助记
most（最多/大多数）+ ly（副词后缀）→ 大多数地 → 主要地
mostly::/məʊstli/:adv. 主要地:mostly young students 大部分是年轻学生
16. unlike /ʌnˈlaɪk/ prep. 与……不同；adj. 不同的
📖 英文解释
Different from; not typical of; in contrast to.
✏️ 例句
Unlike her brother who loved sports, she spent most of her time reading books in the library.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
unlike+名词（与……不同，引出对比）—考研写作对比论证常用；unlike vs. dislike（不喜欢）注意区分。
🧠 助记
un-（否定）+ like（像）→ 不像 → 与……不同
unlike::/ʌnˈlaɪk/:prep. 与……不同:unlike traditional methods 与传统方法不同
17. destroy /dɪˈstrɔɪ/ v. 摧毁；破坏；消灭
📖 英文解释
To damage something so badly that it no longer exists or cannot be repaired.
✏️ 例句
The earthquake destroyed thousands of homes and left hundreds of thousands of people homeless.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
destroy+名词（destroy buildings/hopes/environment）；destruction（n. 破坏）；destructive（adj. 破坏性的）—词族考研均有考察。
🧠 助记
de-（完全）+ stroy（建造的反义）→ 彻底拆毁 → 摧毁
destroy::/dɪˈstrɔɪ/:v. 摧毁:destroy the environment 破坏环境

联想：meet（遇见）和meat（肉）谐音 → 遇见一块肉
meat::/miːt/:n. 肉:red meat consumption 红肉消费

The minimum wage has been raised several times this decade to help low-income workers keep up with inflation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
wage+名词：wage war/campaign（发动战争/运动）；minimum wage（最低工资）；wage gap（工资差距）—考研经济类高频词。
🧠 助记
联想：wage（工资）要争取 → war（战争）wage（发动）→ 两用词
wage::/weɪdʒ/:n. 工资:minimum wage policy 最低工资政策
20. vision /vɪʒn/ n. 视力；想象力；愿景；幻象

The CEO presented a bold vision for the company's growth over the next ten years.

vision for the future（对未来的愿景）；long-term vision（长远眼光）；tunnel vision（目光短浅）；考研写作常用：have a clear vision of...
🧠 助记
vis-（看）+ ion → 与视觉相关 → 视力、愿景
vision::/vɪʒn/:n. 视力:a long-term vision 长远愿景

wealth（财富）+ y → 有财富的 → 富裕的
wealthy::/welθi/:adj. 富裕的:a wealthy nation 一个富裕的国家

To show or display publicly; to demonstrate a particular quality or behavior.
✏️ 例句
The museum will exhibit rare artifacts from ancient civilizations next month.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
exhibit+名词（展示物品/特征）；on exhibit（在展览中）；exhibition（n. 展览会）；exhibit behavior/symptoms（表现出行为/症状）—考研常见搭配。

ex-（向外）+ hibit（持有/拿）→ 拿出来给人看 → 展示
exhibit::/ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/:v. 展示:exhibit signs of stress 表现出压力迹象

A solid piece of material; a section of a town; to prevent movement or progress.
✏️ 例句
Construction work blocked the main road for several hours, causing heavy traffic throughout the city.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
block+名词（block traffic/access/progress）；building block（基础构件）；a block away（一个街区远）；road block（路障）。
🧠 助记
block像一块砖头 → 砖头可以堵路 → 阻止；也像街区的方块

Of a colour between green and violet in the spectrum; feeling sad or depressed.
✏️ 例句
She felt blue after hearing the disappointing news about her job application.

feel blue（感到忧郁）—情感表达；out of the blue（突然地，毫无预兆地）—考研高频习语！blue-collar（蓝领工人）vs. white-collar（白领）。

blame::/bleɪm/:v. 责备:blame the failure on external factors 将失败归咎于外因
