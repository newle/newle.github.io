1. grammar /ɡræmər/ n. 语法；语法书；语法规则
📖 英文解释
The whole system and structure of a language, including rules about how words are combined and used.
✏️ 例句
Mastering English grammar is essential for writing clear and persuasive academic essays.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
English grammar（英语语法）；grammar rule（语法规则）；grammar school（文法学校，英国）；考研写作中grammar的正确使用是高分要素。
🧠 助记
gram（写/字）+ mar → 关于写字规则的学问 → 语法
grammar::/ɡræmər/:n. 语法:master the rules of grammar 掌握语法规则
2. peace /piːs/ n. 和平；平静；安宁
📖 英文解释
Freedom from disturbance; a state of tranquility; freedom from war or violence.
✏️ 例句
The international community worked tirelessly to maintain peace and stability in the conflict-ridden region.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
world peace（世界和平）；peace of mind（内心平静）；at peace（处于和平状态）；make peace with（与……和解）；peacefully（adv.）。
🧠 助记
peace谐音皮士 → 皮士爱和平 → 和平、平静
peace::/piːs/:n. 和平:maintain world peace 维护世界和平
3. otherwise /ʌðərwaɪz/ adv. 否则；在其他方面 adj. 不同的
📖 英文解释
In different circumstances; in other respects; used to introduce an alternative.
✏️ 例句
You should submit your application before the deadline; otherwise, your request will not be considered.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
otherwise用于引出结果条件（否则）—考研写作常用连接词；or otherwise（或者其他情况）；think otherwise（有不同想法）。
🧠 助记
other（其他）+ wise（方式）→ 以其他方式 → 否则（换另一种方式）
otherwise::/ʌðərwaɪz/:adv. 否则:submit on time otherwise be rejected 按时提交，否则被拒
4. emphasis /emfəsɪs/ n. 强调；重点；重视
📖 英文解释
Special importance, value, or prominence given to something.
✏️ 例句
The new curriculum places greater emphasis on critical thinking skills and collaborative learning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
place/put emphasis on（强调，重视）—考研高频搭配！with emphasis（强调地）；shift the emphasis（转移重点）；emphatic（adj.）。
🧠 助记
em-（加强）+ phasis（说）→ 加强语气说 → 强调
emphasis::/emfəsɪs/:n. 强调:place emphasis on practical skills 重视实践技能
5. efficient /ɪˈfɪʃnt/ adj. 有效的；高效的；能胜任的
📖 英文解释
Achieving maximum productivity with minimum wasted effort or expense; working well and quickly.
✏️ 例句
An efficient public transportation system can significantly reduce traffic congestion and air pollution.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
efficient+名词（efficient system/use/method）；fuel-efficient（省油的）；efficiency（n.）；effective（有效果的）vs. efficient（有效率的）—考研常考区别。
🧠 助记
effect（效果）+ ient → 有效果的 → 高效的
efficient::/ɪˈfɪʃnt/:adj. 有效的:an efficient use of resources 资源的高效利用
6. survival /sərˈvaɪvl/ n. 幸存；存活；延续
📖 英文解释
The state or fact of continuing to live or exist, especially in difficult conditions.
✏️ 例句
The survival of many species depends on the protection of their natural habitats.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
survival of the fittest（适者生存）—考研社会达尔文主义文章必现；survival rate（存活率）；survival skills（生存技能）；survive（v.）。
🧠 助记
survive（幸存）+ al → 幸存的状态 → 幸存
survival::/sərˈvaɪvl/:n. 幸存:survival of the fittest 适者生存
7. escape /ɪˈskeɪp/ v./n. 逃跑；逃避；泄漏；忽略
📖 英文解释
To get free from a place or situation; to avoid something unpleasant; to leak out.
✏️ 例句
It is almost impossible to escape the influence of social media in today's highly connected world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
escape from（从……逃跑）；escape+名词（escape punishment/notice）—it escaped my notice（我没注意到）；escape route（逃跑路线）。
🧠 助记
es-（出）+ cape（斗篷/地岬）→ 从斗篷下溜出 → 逃跑
escape::/ɪˈskeɪp/:v./n. 逃跑:escape from poverty 摆脱贫困
8. import /ɪmpɔːrt/ v. 进口；输入 n. 进口商品；重要性
📖 英文解释
To bring goods into a country from abroad; goods brought into a country; great significance.
✏️ 例句
China imports large quantities of raw materials to support its massive manufacturing industry.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
import from（从……进口）；of great import（非常重要）—import作名词有重要性含义，考研偶考；import duty（进口税）；export vs. import。
🧠 助记
im-（进入）+ port（港口）→ 从港口进来 → 进口
import::/ɪmpɔːrt/:v. 进口:import raw materials from abroad 从国外进口原材料
9. club /klʌb/ n. 俱乐部；社团；球棒；夜总会 v. 用棍打
📖 英文解释
An association of people with a shared interest; a heavy stick used as a weapon; to hit with a heavy stick.
✏️ 例句
Joining a book club can help readers discover new perspectives and discuss literature with like-minded people.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
join a club（加入俱乐部）；night club（夜总会）；golf club（高尔夫球棒/俱乐部）；考研有时出现club作动词（棍打）于阅读中。
🧠 助记
联想：club = 聚集的人群 → 俱乐部；也像棍棒形状 → 球棒
club::/klʌb/:n. 俱乐部:join a student club 加入学生社团
10. red /red/ adj. 红色的 n. 红色；赤字；共产主义者
📖 英文解释
Of a colour at the end of the spectrum; a communist; in debt (in the red).
✏️ 例句
The company has been in the red for three consecutive years due to poor financial management.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in the red（亏损）vs. in the black（盈利）—考研财经高频习语！red tape（官僚主义繁文缛节）；red-handed（当场）。
🧠 助记
red = 红色 → 红色警报 → 赤字/亏损
red::/red/:adj. 红色的 n. 红色:the company is in the red 公司处于亏损
11. invention /ɪnˈvenʃn/ n. 发明；发明物；捏造
📖 英文解释
The action of inventing something; a thing that has been invented; a false statement.
✏️ 例句
The invention of the internet has fundamentally transformed the way humans communicate and access information.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
invention of（……的发明）；by invention（出于编造）；invent（v.）；inventor（n.）；inventive（adj. 有创意的）—考研科技史文章核心词族。
🧠 助记
in-（进入）+ vent（来）+ ion → 新的东西被带来 → 发明
invention::/ɪnˈvenʃn/:n. 发明:the invention of the printing press 印刷机的发明
12. discovery /dɪˈskʌvəri/ n. 发现；被发现的事物
📖 英文解释
The action or process of finding or learning something for the first time.
✏️ 例句
The discovery of penicillin by Alexander Fleming revolutionized modern medicine and saved millions of lives.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
discovery of（……的发现）；make a discovery（有所发现）；scientific discovery（科学发现）；区分discovery（发现已存在的）vs. invention（发明未存在的）。
🧠 助记
dis-（去掉）+ cover（覆盖）→ 去掉覆盖物 → 发现
discovery::/dɪˈskʌvəri/:n. 发现:scientific discovery and progress 科学发现与进步
13. agent /eɪdʒənt/ n. 代理；经纪人；特工；动因
📖 英文解释
A person who acts on behalf of another; a spy; a substance that produces a particular effect.
✏️ 例句
The literary agent helped the author negotiate a publishing contract with several major publishing houses.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
travel agent（旅行社）；real estate agent（房产中介）；agent of change（变革推动者）；考研中agent常见于商业和社会变革语境。
🧠 助记
ag-（行动）+ ent（人）→ 代为行动的人 → 代理人
agent::/eɪdʒənt/:n. 代理:an agent of social change 社会变革的推动者
14. dominate /dɒmɪneɪt/ v. 支配；主导；占优势；俯视
📖 英文解释
To have a commanding influence on; to be the most important or influential person or thing.
✏️ 例句
A small number of tech companies dominate the global digital economy and control vast amounts of data.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dominate+名词（dominate the market/discussion）；dominant（adj. 占主导地位的）；domination（n.）—考研市场、政治类文章高频词。
🧠 助记
dom-（统治/家）+ inate → 统治领域 → 支配、主导
dominate::/dɒmɪneɪt/:v. 支配:dominate the global market 主导全球市场
15. pace /peɪs/ n. 速度；步伐；步调 v. 踱步；定速
📖 英文解释
The speed at which someone or something moves or a situation develops; to walk back and forth.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
at a ... pace（以……速度）；keep pace with（跟上……的步伐）—考研高频！set the pace（领跑）；pace oneself（合理分配精力）。
🧠 助记
pace像步 → 每步的速度 → 速度、步调
pace::/peɪs/:n. 速度:keep pace with technological change 跟上技术变革
16. element /elɪmənt/ n. 元素；要素；成分；少量
📖 英文解释
A part or aspect of something; a chemical substance that cannot be split into simpler substances; a small amount.
✏️ 例句
Trust is a fundamental element in building any successful long-term business relationship.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
element of（……的要素）；key/essential element（关键要素）；the elements（恶劣天气）；out of one's element（不在行）—考研阅读多义考察。
🧠 助记
ele-（初始）+ ment → 最基本的东西 → 元素、要素
element::/elɪmənt/:n. 元素:a key element of success 成功的关键要素
17. impose /ɪmˈpəʊz/ v. 强加；课以（税/义务）；强制推行
📖 英文解释
To force something unwelcome to be accepted or put in place; to levy a tax or fine.
✏️ 例句
The government imposed strict restrictions on public gatherings during the health emergency.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
impose+名词+on（把……强加于）—考研高频！impose a tax/ban/fine on；impose one's will on（把意志强加于人）。
🧠 助记
im-（进入）+ pose（放置）→ 强行放进去 → 强加
impose::/ɪmˈpəʊz/:v. 强加:impose restrictions on imports 对进口施加限制

Citizens in a democracy have both the right and the responsibility to vote in every election.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
vote for/against（投票支持/反对）；vote on（就……进行表决）；popular vote（普选票）；veto（否决权）注意区分。

vote谐音沃特 → 沃特先生去投票 → 投票
vote::/vəʊt/:v./n. 投票:the right to vote 投票权

When asked if they supported the new policy, the majority of participants said yes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
yes vote（赞成票）；yes-man（唯唯诺诺的人，贬义）—考研写作偶见；say yes to（答应……）；考研中yes出现频率不高，以词汇辨析角度了解即可。

yes::/jes/:adv. 是:say yes to new opportunities 答应新机会

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
narrow the gap（缩小差距）—考研高频！narrow+名词（narrow mind/margin/escape）；narrowly（adv. 勉强地）；narrow-minded（心胸狭窄的）。
🧠 助记
联想：narrow像箭（arrow）但更窄 → 狭窄的
narrow::/nærəʊ/:adj. 狭窄的:narrow the gap between rich and poor 缩小贫富差距
21. transfer /trænˈsfɜːr/ v. 转移；传输；转学 n. 转移；调动；换乘
📖 英文解释
To move from one place, person, or situation to another; the action of moving something.
✏️ 例句
The technology transfer agreement allowed developing countries to access advanced clean energy solutions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
transfer+名词+to（将……转移到）；technology transfer（技术转让）；bank transfer（银行转账）；考研科技、经济类常见。
🧠 助记
trans-（横跨）+ fer（带来）→ 从一端带到另一端 → 转移
transfer::/trænˈsfɜːr/:v. 转移:technology transfer to developing countries 向发展中国家技术转让
22. copy /kɒpi/ v. 复制；抄写；模仿 n. 副本；一份；文案
📖 英文解释
A thing made to be similar or identical to another; to make a replica of; to imitate.
✏️ 例句
Copyright law protects authors from having their work copied and distributed without permission.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
copy+名词（copy a document/behavior）；hard copy（纸质版）；soft copy（电子版）；copyright（版权）—考研知识产权文章常见。
🧠 助记
copy = 拷贝，直接英文借词 → 复制、副本
copy::/kɒpi/:v. 复制:protect against illegal copying 防止非法复制
23. vary /veəri/ v. 变化；不同；使多样化
📖 英文解释
To be different or to change in different situations; to modify or diversify.
✏️ 例句
Attitudes toward work-life balance vary significantly across different cultures and generations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
vary from ... to ...（从……到……不等）；vary in（在某方面不同）；vary with（随……而变化）—考研阅读描述差异高频词。
🧠 助记
vary和variety（多样性）同根 → 变化造就多样性 → 变化、不同
vary::/veəri/:v. 变化:vary from culture to culture 因文化而异
24. participate /pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪt/ v. 参与；参加；分享
📖 英文解释
To take part in an activity or event; to have a share of something.
✏️ 例句
Students who actively participate in class discussions tend to develop stronger analytical and communication skills.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
participate in（参与……）—注意介词in；participation（n.）；participant（n. 参与者）；考研社会、教育类文章高频词。
🧠 助记
parti-（部分）+ cipate（拿取）→ 拿取其中一部分 → 参与、分享
participate::/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪt/:v. 参与:participate in community activities 参与社区活动
25. advise /ədˈvaɪz/ v. 建议；劝告；通知
📖 英文解释
To recommend a course of action; to give advice; to inform or notify officially.
✏️ 例句
Doctors advise patients to avoid processed foods and maintain a balanced diet for better health outcomes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
advise sb to do / advise doing（建议做某事）；advise sb on（就……给建议）；advice（n. 建议，不可数）—advise vs. advice区分是考研常考易错点。
🧠 助记
ad-（向）+ vise（看）→ 向某人展示正确方向 → 建议、劝告
advise::/ədˈvaɪz/:v. 建议:advise students to read widely 建议学生广泛阅读

recommend::/ˌrekəˈmend/:v. 推荐:recommend adopting a new approach 建议采用新方法
