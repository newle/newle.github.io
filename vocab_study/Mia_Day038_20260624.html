1. valuable /væljuəbl/ adj. 有价值的；贵重的 n. 贵重物品（复数）
📖 英文解释
Worth a great deal of money; extremely useful or important.
✏️ 例句
Time is the most valuable resource we have, and once lost, it can never be recovered.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
valuable+名词（valuable experience/asset/lesson）；valuables（n. 贵重物品）；invaluable（adj. 无价的）—注意invaluable是加强而非否定！
🧠 助记
value（价值）+ able → 有价值的 → 宝贵的
valuable::/væljuəbl/:adj. 有价值的:a valuable lesson from failure 失败的宝贵教训
2. addition /əˈdɪʃn/ n. 加；加法；增加；附加物
📖 英文解释
The action or process of adding; a person or thing added.
✏️ 例句
In addition to academic qualifications, employers increasingly value soft skills like communication and teamwork.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in addition (to)（此外，除……之外）—考研写作递进高频连接词！addition of（增加……）；additional（adj. 额外的）。
🧠 助记
add（加）+ ition → 加的动作 → 加法、增加
addition::/əˈdɪʃn/:n. 加:in addition to regular coursework 除常规课程之外
3. contract /kɒntrækt/ n. 合同；合约 v. 缩小；感染；签合同
📖 英文解释
A written agreement; to become smaller or shorter; to catch a disease.
✏️ 例句
The company signed a long-term contract with the supplier to ensure stable delivery of raw materials.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sign/breach a contract（签订/违反合同）；contract a disease（感染疾病）；contract muscles（收缩肌肉）—考研多义词重点。
🧠 助记
con-（共同）+ tract（拉）→ 双方拉在一起 → 合同；反向拉 → 收缩
contract::/kɒntrækt/:n. 合同:sign a contract with a supplier 与供应商签合同
4. description /dɪˈskrɪpʃn/ n. 描述；描写；类型
📖 英文解释
A spoken or written account of something; a type or category of thing.
✏️ 例句
The witness gave a detailed description of the suspect that helped police identify and apprehend him.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
description of（对……的描述）；beyond description（难以描述）；of every description（各种类型的）—考研阅读细节题常见。
🧠 助记
describe（描述）+ tion → 描述的行为 → 描述
description::/dɪˈskrɪpʃn/:n. 描述:a detailed description of the event 对事件的详细描述
5. alcohol /ælkəhɒl/ n. 酒精；含酒精饮料；醇类
📖 英文解释
A colourless volatile flammable liquid; drinks containing this substance; a class of organic compounds.
✏️ 例句
Excessive alcohol consumption is a leading cause of liver disease and traffic accidents worldwide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
alcohol abuse（酗酒）；alcohol content（酒精含量）；alcoholic（n. 酗酒者 adj. 含酒精的）；考研健康、社会问题类文章常见。
🧠 助记
al + cohol → alcohol，酒精的化学名称
alcohol::/ælkəhɒl/:n. 酒精:excessive alcohol consumption 过量饮酒
6. district /dɪstrɪkt/ n. 地区；区域；行政区
📖 英文解释
An area of a country or city with defined boundaries for administrative purposes.
✏️ 例句
The school district allocated additional funds to improve the quality of education in underfunded areas.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
district+名词（district court/school/government）；business district（商业区）；electoral district（选区）；考研常见于城市规划、教育类文章。
🧠 助记
dis-（分开）+ trict（拉）→ 被划分出来的地区 → 行政区
district::/dɪstrɪkt/:n. 地区:the school district budget 学区预算
7. dry /draɪ/ adj. 干燥的；枯燥的；禁酒的 v. 使干燥；变干
📖 英文解释
Free from moisture; dull and uninteresting; not allowing alcohol; to remove moisture.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dry season（旱季）；dry up（干涸）；dry humor（冷幽默）；go dry（禁酒）；dry run（预演）—考研多义词重点。
🧠 助记
dry = 干燥 → 无水 → 无聊（无生气）→ 禁酒（无酒）
dry::/draɪ/:adj. 干燥的:a prolonged dry season 持续的旱季
8. evening /iːvnɪŋ/ n. 傍晚；晚上；晚会
📖 英文解释
The period of time at the end of the day, usually from about 6 p.m. to bedtime.
✏️ 例句
Many people find that taking an evening walk helps them unwind after a stressful day at work.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
evening+名词（evening class/news/shift）；in the evening（在傍晚）；evening event（晚间活动）；考研语境中evening常作时间状语。
🧠 助记
even（平静）+ ing → 一天平静下来的时候 → 傍晚
evening::/iːvnɪŋ/:n. 傍晚:attend an evening class 参加夜校课程
9. breakfast /brekfəst/ n. 早饭；早餐 v. 吃早饭
📖 英文解释
The first meal of the day, eaten in the morning.
✏️ 例句
Research suggests that students who eat a nutritious breakfast perform significantly better on academic tests.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
have breakfast（吃早饭）；skip breakfast（不吃早饭）；breakfast on（以……为早餐）；bed and breakfast（民宿）—考研健康、教育类文章出现。
🧠 助记
break（打破）+ fast（禁食）→ 打破一夜的禁食 → 早饭
breakfast::/brekfəst/:n. 早饭:skip breakfast regularly 经常不吃早饭

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
gene+名词（gene therapy/editing/expression）；genetic（adj. 遗传的）；genome（基因组）；考研生物、医学类文章核心词。
🧠 助记
gene谐音基因 → 直接联想 → 基因
gene::/dʒiːn/:n. 基因:gene therapy and medicine 基因治疗与医学
11. wave /weɪv/ v. 挥手；波动 n. 波浪；浪潮；波动
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
wave of（……浪潮）；heat wave（热浪）；wave goodbye（挥手告别）；make waves（引起轰动）—考研社会变迁类文章常用。
🧠 助记
wave = 波浪 → 手挥动像波浪 → 挥手；浪潮来临 → 浪潮、波动
wave::/weɪv/:v. 挥手:a new wave of technology 新一波技术浪潮
12. moreover /ˌmɔːrˈəʊvər/ adv. 而且；此外；再者
📖 英文解释
As a further matter; besides; in addition to what has been said.
✏️ 例句
The new policy reduces costs; moreover, it improves efficiency and benefits all stakeholders involved.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
moreover用于递进关系（而且、此外）—考研写作高分连接词！位置：句首或句中；区分furthermore、moreover、in addition三者语气强度。
🧠 助记
more（更多）+ over（超过）→ 在之上还有更多 → 而且、此外
moreover::/ˌmɔːrˈəʊvər/:adv. 而且:Moreover the evidence suggests... 此外，证据表明……

capable::/keɪpəbl/:adj. 有能力的:capable of solving complex problems 能够解决复杂问题
