1. apart /əˈpɑːrt/ adv. 相隔；分开地；除……之外
📖 英文解释
separated by a distance or gap; away from each other; into pieces
✏️ 例句
The two cities are 200 miles apart. They live apart since the divorce.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
apart from(除……之外,也表示擇开……不谈); fall apart(崩溃); come apart(散开); tear apart(撕开); set apart(使与众不同)
🧠 助记
a(一个) + part(部分) → 分成一个个部分 → 分开、相隔
apart::/əˈpɑːrt/:adv. 相隔:They stood far apart, neither willing to speak first.

🧠 助记
pleas(使高兴) + ure(名词后缀) → 让人高兴的事 → 愉快、乐趣
pleasure::/ˈpleʒər/:n. 愉快:Reading is one of her greatest pleasures in life.
