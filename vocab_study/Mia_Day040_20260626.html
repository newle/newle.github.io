1. partly /ˈpɑːrtli/ adv. 部分地；不完全地
📖 英文解释
to some extent but not completely; in part
✏️ 例句
The delay was partly due to bad weather. She is partly responsible for the mistake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
partly because(部分原因是); partly due to(部分由于); partly to blame(部分有责任); partly cloudy(多云); partly right(部分正确)
🧠 助记
part(部分) + ly(副词后缀) → 以部分的方式 → 部分地
partly::/ˈpɑːrtli/:adv. 部分地:The project failed partly because of poor planning and partly due to lack of funding.

🧠 助记
exist(存在) + ence(名词后缀) → 存在这个状态 → 存在
existence::/ɪɡˈzɪstəns/:n. 存在:The discovery challenged the very existence of long-held scientific theories.

🧠 助记
