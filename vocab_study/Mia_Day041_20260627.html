1. throw /θroʊ/ v. 扔；抛；使陷入 n. 投掷（threw/thrown）
📖 英文解释
to propel something through the air; to move one's body suddenly; to confuse or disconcert someone
✏️ 例句
She threw the ball to her teammate. The news threw him into confusion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
throw away(扔掉;浪费); throw out(赶出;扔掉); throw up(呕吐;建造); throw light on(阐明); throw a party(举办派对)
🧠 助记
thr(三个动作:提、挥、放) + ow → 提起来、挥动、放出去 → 扔、投
throw::/θroʊ/:v. 扔:He threw himself into his work after the difficult breakup, staying late every night.

ambition::/æmˈbɪʃn/:n. 雄心:Her ambition to reform the education system drove her into a career in politics.
