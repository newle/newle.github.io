1. recall /rɪˈkɔːl/ v./n. 回忆；召回
📖 英文解释
to bring back into the mind; to order the return of
✏️ 例句
She could barely recall his name after so many years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
recall doing sth 回忆起做某事；recall sb/sth 召回；常考搭配：total recall 完全记忆
🧠 助记
re(再) + call(叫) → 再叫回来 → 回忆/召回
recall::/rɪˈkɔːl/:v./n. 回忆:The company recalled thousands of defective products.
2. winter /ˈwɪntər/ n. 冬天；冬季
📖 英文解释
the coldest season of the year, between autumn and spring
✏️ 例句
Winter in Beijing can be bitterly cold and dry.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in winter 在冬天；winter break 寒假；winter solstice 冬至
🧠 助记
联想：win(赢) + ter → 能赢得冬天的人很坚强
winter::/ˈwɪntər/:n. 冬天:The river freezes over every winter.
3. assistant /əˈsɪstənt/ n. 助手；助理 adj. 辅助的
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
teaching assistant 助教；assistant professor 助理教授；administrative assistant 行政助理
🧠 助记
assist(协助) + ant(人) → 协助他人的人 → 助手
assistant::/əˈsɪstənt/:n. 助手:His assistant handled all the scheduling.
4. coffee /ˈkɒfi/ n. 咖啡；咖啡色
📖 英文解释
a hot drink made from roasted and ground coffee beans

coffee break 短暂休息；coffee shop 咖啡馆；考研中常出现在描述生活方式语境
🧠 助记
谐音：卡肥 → 喝咖啡令人提神
coffee::/ˈkɒfi/:n. 咖啡:Let's discuss this over coffee.
5. overseas /ˌəʊvəˈsiːz/ adv./adj. 海外的；在国外

overseas::/ˌəʊvəˈsiːz/:adv./adj. 海外的:She has lived overseas for ten years.
6. crash /kræʃ/ v./n. 碰撞；崩溃；坠毁
📖 英文解释

crash::/kræʃ/:v./n. 碰撞:The computer crashed and lost all my data.

administration::/ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃn/:n. 管理:She studied public administration in graduate school.

excessive::/ɪkˈsesɪv/:adj. 过度的:The judge criticized the excessive punishment.
