1. institute /ˈɪnstɪtjuːt/ n. 机构；学院 v. 建立；创立
📖 英文解释
an organization for the promotion of a cause; to establish or start something
✏️ 例句
The research institute published a landmark study on climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
institute a policy 制定政策；institute proceedings 提起诉讼；research institute 研究机构
🧠 助记
in + stat(站) + ute → 让某事站立起来 → 建立/机构
institute::/ˈɪnstɪtjuːt/:n. 机构:She founded an institute dedicated to cancer research.
2. unusual /ʌnˈjuːʒuəl/ adj. 不寻常的；罕见的
📖 英文解释
not habitually or commonly occurring or done; remarkable
✏️ 例句
It is unusual for temperatures to drop so low in April.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
it is unusual for sb to do 某人做某事不寻常；unusual circumstances 特殊情况；nothing unusual 没什么异常
🧠 助记
un(不) + usual(通常的) → 不通常的 → 不寻常的
unusual::/ʌnˈjuːʒuəl/:adj. 不寻常的:The scientist made an unusual discovery in the lab.
3. rare /reər/ adj. 罕见的；稀有的；半熟的
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
rare → 联想：稀有如'空气'(air在里面)
rare::/reər/:adj. 罕见的:This is a rare chance to see the northern lights.

🧠 助记

fun::/fʌn/:n./adj. 乐趣:The children had a lot of fun at the festival.

smell::/smel/:v./n. 闻:She could smell danger in the tense atmosphere.
