1. extreme /ɪkˈstriːm/ adj. 极端的；极度的 n. 极端
📖 英文解释
reaching a high or the highest degree; far from moderate
✏️ 例句
Extreme weather events are becoming more frequent due to climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
extreme sports 极限运动；in the extreme 极其；go to extremes 走极端；extreme poverty 极度贫困
🧠 助记
extra(额外) + eme → 超出额外的极限 → 极端的
extreme::/ɪkˈstriːm/:adj. 极端的:The researcher warned against taking extreme positions.
2. introduction /ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃn/ n. 介绍；引进；导论
📖 英文解释
the action of introducing something; a preliminary section of a book
✏️ 例句
The introduction of new technology created both opportunities and challenges.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
by way of introduction 作为介绍；introduction to 某学科入门；letter of introduction 介绍信
🧠 助记
intro(进入) + duct(引导) + ion → 引导进入 → 介绍/导入
introduction::/ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃn/:n. 介绍:The professor wrote an introduction to the edited volume.
3. seldom /ˈseldəm/ adv. 很少；不常
📖 英文解释
not often; rarely
✏️ 例句
He seldom speaks in class, but when he does, everyone listens.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
seldom if ever 几乎从不；seldom used 很少使用的；注意：seldom放句首时需倒装
🧠 助记
sel + dom → 少有的领域(dom) → 很少
seldom::/ˈseldəm/:adv. 很少:She seldom makes the same mistake twice.
4. tip /tɪp/ n. 小费；建议；尖端 v. 给小费；倾斜
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tip of the iceberg 冰山一角；give a tip 给小费/提供建议；tip over 翻倒
🧠 助记
tip → 最顶端的一点，也是最重要的建议那'点'
tip::/tɪp/:n. 小费:She tipped the waiter generously for the excellent service.
5. minority /maɪˈnɒrɪti/ n. 少数；少数群体；未成年
📖 英文解释
the smaller number or part; a group differing from others in the population
✏️ 例句
Minority groups deserve equal rights and representation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
minor(较小的) + ity → 较小的群体 → 少数/少数群体
minority::/maɪˈnɒrɪti/:n. 少数:The policy aimed to protect the rights of religious minorities.
6. relevant /ˈreləvənt/ adj. 相关的；切题的；有意义的
📖 英文解释
closely connected or appropriate to what is being done
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
relev(举起/提到) + ant → 被提到的事情 → 相关的
relevant::/ˈreləvənt/:adj. 相关的:The committee only considered evidence relevant to the case.

🧠 助记

cool::/kuːl/:adj. 凉爽的:She kept a cool head during the crisis.
8. deserve /dɪˈzɜːrv/ v. 值得；应得

🧠 助记

repeat::/rɪˈpiːt/:v./n. 重复:Please do not repeat this confidential information.

politician::/ˌpɒlɪˈtɪʃn/:n. 政客:The politician promised to reform the tax system.
