1. reduction /rɪˈdʌkʃən/ n. 减少；降低；缩小
📖 英文解释
The action of making something smaller or less in amount, degree, or size.
✏️ 例句
There has been a significant reduction in the number of traffic accidents this year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：reduction in sth（在某方面的减少）；price reduction（降价）；tax reduction（减税）。考研写作常用：bring about a reduction in…
🧠 助记
re-（再次）+ duct（引导）+ -ion → 反复往下引导 → 减少。联想：导演（director）把预算一再往下压，reduction！
reduction::/rɪˈdʌkʃən/:n. 减少:a dramatic reduction in carbon emissions
2. everyday /ˈevriˌdeɪ/ adj. 每日的；日常的；普通的
📖 英文解释
Happening or used every day; ordinary and not special.
✏️ 例句
Smartphones have become an essential part of everyday life.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
注意 everyday（形容词，修饰名词）vs every day（副词短语，表频率）。考研阅读常出现：everyday language / everyday experience。
🧠 助记
every + day → 每一天都有的 → 日常的。想象：每天（every day）用的东西就是 everyday items。
everyday::/ˈevriˌdeɪ/:adj. 每日的:an everyday occurrence that most people ignore
3. percentage /pərˈsentɪdʒ/ n. 百分比；百分率；比例
📖 英文解释
A rate, number, or amount in each hundred; a proportion of a whole.
✏️ 例句
A high percentage of students passed the final examination.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：a high/low percentage of…；express sth as a percentage。考研图表作文必备词汇，常与 proportion、ratio 互换使用。
🧠 助记
per（每）+ cent（百）+ -age → 每百分之…→ 百分比。cent = 100，想想一分钱（cent）是一美元的百分之一。
percentage::/pərˈsentɪdʒ/:n. 百分比:the percentage of urban residents has risen sharply
4. version /ˈvɜːrʒən/ n. 版本；说法；改编本
📖 英文解释
A particular form of something that differs in some way from other forms of the same thing.
✏️ 例句
The film is based on a stage version of the novel.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常见：updated version（更新版本）；official version（官方说法）；different versions of events（对事件的不同说法）。
🧠 助记
vers-（转）+ -ion → 转变出来的形态 → 版本。联想：verse（诗节）也是语言转换的一种形式。
version::/ˈvɜːrʒən/:n. 版本:the revised version of the policy drew public debate
5. dramatic /drəˈmætɪk/ adj. 戏剧性的；引人注目的；显著的
📖 英文解释
Sudden and striking; having a strong, exciting effect; relating to drama.
✏️ 例句
The new policy led to a dramatic decrease in unemployment rates.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频形容词。搭配：dramatic change/increase/drop/improvement。副词形式 dramatically 在图表作文中极常用：rise dramatically。
🧠 助记
drama（戏剧）+ -tic → 像戏剧一样的 → 戏剧性的、引人注目的。想象：戏剧舞台上的夸张表演。
dramatic::/drəˈmætɪk/:adj. 戏剧性的:a dramatic shift in public attitudes toward the environment
6. stimulate /ˈstɪmjuleɪt/ v. 刺激；激励；促进
📖 英文解释
To encourage something to grow, develop, or become more active; to make someone feel excited or enthusiastic.
✏️ 例句
Reading widely can stimulate curiosity and critical thinking in students.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：stimulate economic growth（促进经济增长）；stimulate interest/creativity；stimulate sb to do sth。考研写作议论文核心动词。
🧠 助记
stimul-（刺激）+ -ate → 给予刺激使其行动。联想：stimulus（刺激物）是名词形式，stimulus → stimulate。
stimulate::/ˈstɪmjuleɪt/:v. 刺激:policies designed to stimulate innovation in small businesses
7. arrange /əˈreɪndʒ/ v. 安排；整理；改编
📖 英文解释
To organize or plan something in advance; to put things in a particular order.
✏️ 例句
The teacher arranged the desks in a semicircle for group discussion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：arrange a meeting/trip；arrange for sb to do sth；arrange sth in order。名词：arrangement（安排；协议）。
🧠 助记
ar-（加强）+ range（范围/排列）→ 把事物排入范围内 → 安排、整理。想象把乱七八糟的东西 arrange 进它该在的范围。
arrange::/əˈreɪndʒ/:v. 安排:she arranged for a tutor to help her son every weekend
8. faculty /ˈfæklti/ n. 能力；才能；（大学）系；全体教员
📖 英文解释
An inherent mental or physical power; a group of university departments; the teaching staff of a university.
✏️ 例句
He retained all his mental faculties well into old age.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研两个常见义：① mental/physical faculty（能力/官能）；② the faculty of a university（大学系/院）或 faculty member（教员）。
🧠 助记
fact（做）+ -ulty → 做事的能力 → 才能。联想：factory（工厂）也含 fact- 根，工厂是展示制造才能的地方。
faculty::/ˈfæklti/:n. 能力:the faculty of critical reasoning is central to academic success
9. praise /preɪz/ v./n. 赞美；表扬；称赞
📖 英文解释
To express warm approval or admiration of someone or something; the expression of approval.
✏️ 例句
The teacher praised the students for their outstanding performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：praise sb for sth；high praise（高度赞扬）；in praise of（赞颂…）。辨析：compliment 多指具体恭维，praise 更正式。
🧠 助记
praise 与 raise（提升）押韵 → 把人的地位提升 → 表扬。想象：老师 raise 举起学生的作业说 praise 真棒！
praise::/preɪz/:v./n. 赞美:the professor received widespread praise for her groundbreaking research
10. volunteer /ˌvɒlənˈtɪər/ n. 志愿者 v. 自愿做；义务服务
📖 英文解释
A person who freely offers to do something; to freely offer to do something without being forced.
✏️ 例句
Many college students volunteer at local community centers during weekends.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：volunteer for sth/to do sth；volunteer work/service；考研写作常见：voluntary work, volunteer spirit（志愿精神）。
🧠 助记
volunt-（意愿）+ -eer（人）→ 有意愿的人 → 志愿者。词根 vol- = will，如 voluntary（自愿的）。
volunteer::/ˌvɒlənˈtɪər/:n. 志愿者 v. 自愿做:thousands of volunteers signed up to help after the natural disaster
11. stone /stəʊn/ n. 石头；石块 v. 用石头砸
📖 英文解释
The hard solid substance found in the ground, often used as a building material; a small piece of rock.
✏️ 例句
The ancient temple was built entirely of stone.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见短语：stepping stone（垫脚石；跳板）；stone cold（冷如石头，极冷）；leave no stone unturned（不遗余力）。
🧠 助记
stone 形似 store（储存）→ 大自然储存的坚硬物质 → 石头。或想象 stones 铺成的小路，每步都踩着 stone。
stone::/stəʊn/:n. 石头:education is often seen as a stepping stone to a better career
12. chapter /ˈtʃæptər/ n. 章节；重要阶段；地方分会
📖 英文解释
A main division of a book; an important period or event in a person's life or in history.
✏️ 例句
The first chapter of the book introduces the main themes of the novel.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：a new chapter in history（历史新篇章）；chapter by chapter；a dark chapter（黑暗的一页）。考研阅读文章结构常提及。
🧠 助记
chap（小伙子）+ -ter → 每个小伙子（人物）各占一章 → 章节。或联想：chapter 里有 cap（帽子），换帽子就是换章节。
chapter::/ˈtʃæptər/:n. 章节:China has opened a new chapter in international cooperation
13. meanwhile /ˈmiːnˌwaɪl/ adv. 与此同时；在此期间
📖 英文解释
In the time between two events; at the same time as something else is happening.
✏️ 例句
The report was delayed; meanwhile, the committee continued its investigation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研写作衔接词，表示时间并行关系：meanwhile / in the meantime / at the same time。常出现在阅读文章段落转折处。
🧠 助记
mean（中间）+ while（一段时间）→ 在中间的时间里 → 与此同时。mean time = 中间的时间段。
meanwhile::/ˈmiːnˌwaɪl/:adv. 与此同时:the economy was slowing down; meanwhile, unemployment was rising
14. perceive /pərˈsiːv/ v. 感知；察觉；理解；认为
📖 英文解释
To become aware of something through the senses; to understand or regard something in a particular way.
✏️ 例句
People often perceive the world differently based on their cultural backgrounds.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：perceive sth as sth（把…看作…）；perceived threat（被感知的威胁）；名词：perception（感知；看法）。考研阅读高频词。
🧠 助记
per-（完全）+ ceive（抓取）→ 完全抓取信息 → 感知、察觉。同根词：receive（接收）、conceive（构想）。
perceive::/pərˈsiːv/:v. 感知:how individuals perceive risk influences their decision-making process
15. merely /ˈmɪərli/ adv. 仅仅；只不过
📖 英文解释
Used to emphasize that something is small or unimportant; only; just.
✏️ 例句
He is not merely a teacher but also a mentor and a friend.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研阅读常见强调副词。辨析：merely 强调程度之小或范围之限，比 only 更正式，常用于书面语。搭配：not merely…but also…
🧠 助记
mere（仅仅的）+ -ly → 仅仅地。mere 本身就有"仅仅"的意思，如：a mere child（仅仅一个孩子）。
merely::/ˈmɪərli/:adv. 仅仅:success is not merely about talent but also about persistent effort
16. absorb /əbˈzɔːrb/ v. 吸收；理解；使专注
📖 英文解释
To take in a substance, information, or influence; to engage someone's full attention.
✏️ 例句
Plants absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen through photosynthesis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：absorb information/knowledge（吸收知识）；be absorbed in sth（专注于…）；absorb immigrants（融合移民）。考研阅读高频词。
🧠 助记
ab-（加强）+ sorb（吸入）→ 完全吸入 → 吸收。想象海绵（sponge）absorb 水分，完全吸进去。
absorb::/əbˈzɔːrb/:v. 吸收:children can absorb new languages much faster than adults
17. belong /bɪˈlɒŋ/ v. 属于；归属；是…的成员
📖 英文解释
To be a member of a group; to be in the right place; to be owned by someone.
✏️ 例句
Everyone needs a sense of belonging to feel truly happy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：belong to（属于）；a sense of belonging（归属感）——考研写作高频表达！belong 通常不用进行时。
🧠 助记
be + long（渴望）→ 内心渴望归属 → 属于、归属。或想：be long in a place → 一直在那里 → 属于那里。
belong::/bɪˈlɒŋ/:v. 属于:a strong sense of belonging motivates students to engage more actively

weak::/wiːk/:adj. 虚弱的:a weak regulatory framework can undermine public trust in institutions

As an undergraduate, she published two research papers in academic journals.

辨析：undergraduate（本科生）vs graduate（研究生/毕业生）vs postgraduate（研究生）。考研必备学术词汇。

under-（在…之下）+ graduate（毕业生）→ 还在毕业门槛之下 → 本科生。尚未 graduate 的人就是 undergraduate。
undergraduate::/ˌʌndəˈɡrædʒuət/:n./adj. 大学本科生:undergraduate research experience is highly valued by graduate schools

assign::/əˈsaɪn/:v. 分配:teachers often assign group projects to foster collaborative skills

30. announce /əˈnaʊns/ v. 宣布；宣告；公布
📖 英文解释
To make a formal public statement about a fact, occurrence, or intention.
✏️ 例句
The government announced a series of new economic policies last week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：announce a decision/plan/result；announcement n.（公告）；announcer n.（播音员）。辨析：announce 正式宣布，declare 更强调声明立场。
