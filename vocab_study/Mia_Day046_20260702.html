1. exposure /ɪkˈspəʊʒər/ n. 暴露；揭发；曝光；接触
📖 英文解释
The state of being exposed to something; the revelation of something secret; contact with something.
✏️ 例句
Early exposure to a second language greatly improves a child's linguistic ability.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：exposure to sth（接触/暴露于…）；media exposure（媒体曝光）；overexposure（过度曝光）。考研写作：gain exposure to diverse cultures。
🧠 助记
ex-（出来）+ pos-（放置）+ -ure → 被放到外面去 → 暴露。expose v. → exposure n.，想象底片曝光（photographic exposure）。
exposure::/ɪkˈspəʊʒər/:n. 暴露:early exposure to reading builds a strong foundation for academic achievement
2. acknowledge /əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/ v. 承认；认可；致谢；回应
📖 英文解释
To accept or admit the existence or truth of something; to recognize the importance of something.
✏️ 例句
The author acknowledged the contributions of her colleagues in the preface.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：acknowledge that…；acknowledge sb's contribution；acknowledgement n.（致谢；承认）。考研阅读高频：it is widely acknowledged that…
🧠 助记
ac-（加强）+ knowledge（知识）→ 对某事有充分了解并承认 → 承认、认可。拥有知识（knowledge）了才能 acknowledge（承认）。
acknowledge::/əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/:v. 承认:it is widely acknowledged that education is the foundation of social progress
3. imagination /ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃən/ n. 想象力；想象；创造力
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
A good teacher can capture students' imagination and make learning enjoyable.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：use one's imagination；fire/capture sb's imagination（激发想象力）；imaginative adj.（富有想象力的）。考研写作：foster imagination and creativity。
🧠 助记
image（图像）+ -ination → 在脑中制造图像的能力 → 想象力。imagine v. → imagination n.，一步步：看 image，脑中 imagine，这就是 imagination。
imagination::/ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃən/:n. 想象力:education should cultivate imagination alongside knowledge and critical skills
4. locate /ləʊˈkeɪt/ v. 找到；确定位置；把…设在
📖 英文解释
To discover the exact place or position of something; to establish in a particular place.
✏️ 例句
Researchers used GPS technology to locate the missing hikers in the mountains.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：locate sth/sb；be located in/at（位于）；location n.（位置；地点）。考研阅读：the factory is located in the industrial district。
🧠 助记
loc-（地方）+ -ate（使动）→ 使某物在某地 → 定位、设在。locus = 地点，local（当地的）同根。
locate::/ləʊˈkeɪt/:v. 找到:researchers were able to locate the source of the water contamination quickly
5. incentive /ɪnˈsentɪv/ n. 激励；动机；奖励 adj. 激励性的
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
in-（进入）+ cent（唱歌/开始）+ -ive → 给人唱歌开场的动力 → 激励。词根 cant/cent = 唱，有唱歌的冲动 = 有激励。
incentive::/ɪnˈsentɪv/:n. 激励:tax incentives can effectively encourage businesses to invest in green technology
6. victim /ˈvɪktɪm/ n. 受害者；牺牲品；被骗者

🧠 助记
vict-（胜利/征服）+ -im → 被征服的人 → 受害者。victor（胜利者）的对立面：被 victor 打败的人是 victim。
victim::/ˈvɪktɪm/:n. 受害者:victims of online fraud often suffer both financial loss and psychological trauma

airline::/ˈeərˌlaɪn/:n. 航空公司:the airline industry faced unprecedented challenges during the global pandemic

confident::/ˈkɒnfɪdənt/:adj. 自信的:students who receive timely feedback tend to become more confident learners

preference::/ˈprefrəns/:n. 偏好:consumer preference for organic products has grown steadily over the past decade

🧠 助记
ap-（加强）+ prove（证明）→ 通过证明后认可 → 批准、赞成。prove something → approval follows（证明了就会批准）。
approve::/əˈpruːv/:v. 批准:the board approved the new strategy to expand into emerging markets
11. borrow /ˈbɒrəʊ/ v. 借；借用；借鉴
📖 英文解释
To take and use something belonging to someone else with the intention of returning it.
✏️ 例句
Students can borrow up to ten books from the university library at one time.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
辨析：borrow（借入）vs lend（借出）vs loan（贷款/借出）。搭配：borrow sth from sb；borrow an idea/concept（借鉴概念）。
🧠 助记
borrow 与 burrow（挖洞）相近 → 像动物挖洞取出食物，借用别人的东西 → 借。或：borrow = 偷偷（bo）借来（rrow）又还回去。
borrow::/ˈbɒrəʊ/:v. 借:many languages borrow vocabulary from other languages over time

assignment::/əˈsaɪnmənt/:n. 作业:completing challenging assignments helps students develop problem-solving abilities

reliable::/rɪˈlaɪəbl/:adj. 可靠的:a reliable public transport system is essential for sustainable urban development

entire::/ɪnˈtaɪər/:adj. 整个的:the entire education system needs to be reformed to meet the demands of the modern world

optimistic::/ˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪk/:adj. 乐观的:researchers remain cautiously optimistic about the potential of artificial intelligence
