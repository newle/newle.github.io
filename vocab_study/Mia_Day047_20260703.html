1. satisfaction /ˌsætɪsˈfækʃən/ n. 满意；满足感；乐事
📖 英文解释
Fulfillment of one's needs or desires; pleasure derived from achieving something.
✏️ 例句
The greatest satisfaction for a teacher is seeing students grow and succeed.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：gain/derive satisfaction from；job satisfaction（工作满意度）；to sb's satisfaction（令某人满意地）；satisfying adj.；satisfactory adj.（令人满意的）。
🧠 助记
satisfy（满足）+ -faction/-action → 满足的行为结果 → 满意、满足感。satisfy → satisfaction 规则变化，思路清晰。
satisfaction::/ˌsætɪsˈfækʃən/:n. 满意:job satisfaction is often more closely linked to recognition than to salary
2. recover /rɪˈkʌvər/ v. 恢复；康复；找回；弥补
📖 英文解释
To return to a normal state of health or prosperity; to regain something lost.
✏️ 例句
The economy is expected to recover gradually over the next two years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：recover from illness/loss；recover data；economic recovery（经济复苏）；recovery n.（恢复；康复）。考研写作：recover from setbacks。
🧠 助记
re-（再次）+ cover（覆盖）→ 再次被覆盖/保护 → 恢复。像伤口重新被皮肤（cover）覆盖 → 康复。
recover::/rɪˈkʌvər/:v. 恢复:developing nations often struggle to recover from the economic impact of natural disasters
3. apartment /əˈpɑːrtmənt/ n. 公寓；套间；房间
📖 英文解释
A set of rooms for living in, usually on one floor of a large building.
✏️ 例句
Renting an apartment in major cities has become increasingly unaffordable for young graduates.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：rent/share an apartment；apartment building（公寓楼）；studio apartment（单间公寓）。注意：美式英语用 apartment，英式多用 flat。
🧠 助记
apart（分离）+ -ment → 分开的居住空间 → 公寓（各户独立的单元）。apartment = a part + ment，每户人家都是 apart（分开）的。
apartment::/əˈpɑːrtmənt/:n. 公寓:the rising cost of renting an apartment is a major concern for young urban workers

sister::/ˈsɪstər/:n. 姐妹:the two institutions are sister universities that share academic resources and faculty

6. justify /ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪ/ v. 证明…有理；为…辩护；排版对齐
📖 英文解释
To show or prove to be right or reasonable; to provide a good reason for.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：justify a decision/action；justify doing sth；justify oneself（为自己辩护）；justification n.（正当理由）。考研写作：justify one's argument。
🧠 助记
just（公正/正当）+ -ify（使）→ 使之显得正当合理 → 证明有理。justice（正义）同根，justify = 使行为具有 justice（正当性）。
justify::/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪ/:v. 证明…有理:increased funding for education is justified by its long-term economic and social returns

🧠 助记
dead 联想 lead（引领）→ 死亡不能再 lead（引领）了。或：d-ead，ear（耳朵）死了听不到 = 停止活动 → 死。
dead::/ded/:adj. 死的:without active use and transmission, a language is effectively dead within two generations

ceremony::/ˈserəməni/:n. 典礼:the award ceremony recognized outstanding contributions to scientific research

elsewhere::/ˌelsˈweər/:adv. 在别处:students unable to find internships locally often have to seek opportunities elsewhere
10. procedure /prəˈsiːdʒər/ n. 程序；步骤；手续；手术

procedure::/prəˈsiːdʒər/:n. 程序:a clear application procedure makes it easier for students to access financial aid

unlikely::/ʌnˈlaɪkli/:adj. 不太可能的:rapid social change is unlikely to occur without corresponding shifts in public education

29. aside /əˈsaɪd/ adv./prep. 在旁边；撇开；除…之外
📖 英文解释
To one side; out of the way; used to set something apart from the main discussion.
✏️ 例句
Joking aside, the issue of educational inequality deserves serious attention.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：set/put aside（搁置；储蓄）；aside from（除了…之外）；step aside（让步；退开）；jokes aside（说正经的）。
🧠 助记
a-（在）+ side（侧面）→ 在侧面 → 旁边；撇开。想象把某件事 put aside（放在一旁），先不管它。
aside::/əˈsaɪd/:adv./prep. 在旁边:aside from academic achievement, universities also assess applicants' extracurricular contributions
30. bind /baɪnd/ v. 捆绑；约束；使有义务 n. 困境
📖 英文解释
To tie or fasten tightly; to unite people; to impose a legal or moral obligation.
✏️ 例句
A shared sense of purpose can bind a community together during difficult times.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
搭配：bind sb to sth（使某人受…约束）；be bound by rules/law；double bind（双重困境）；binding agreement（有约束力的协议）。
🧠 助记
bind 联想：find（找到）→ 找到并绑定 → 捆绑。bound（被绑住的）是过去分词，be bound to do = 必然要做（像被绑住一样）。
bind::/baɪnd/:v. 捆绑:legal contracts bind both parties to fulfill their agreed-upon obligations
