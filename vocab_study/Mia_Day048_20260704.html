1. capture /ˈkæptʃər/ v. 捕获；拍摄；引起注意 n. 战利品
📖 英文解释
to catch or take control of a person, animal, or place by force; to record or represent accurately
✏️ 例句
The photographer managed to capture the exact moment the lightning struck.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
capture sb's attention/imagination 引起某人的注意/激起想象；capture a city 攻占城市；be captured alive 被活捉
🧠 助记
cap（帽子）+ ture → 用帽子扎住 → 捕获
capture::/ˈkæptʃər/:v. 捕获:The film captures the spirit of the era perfectly.
2. satisfy /ˈsætɪsfaɪ/ v. 使满意；使确信；满足（需求）
📖 英文解释
to please someone by giving them what they want or need; to fulfill a requirement or condition
✏️ 例句
Nothing could satisfy her curiosity about the ancient ruins.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
satisfy sb's needs/demands 满足需求；satisfy a condition 满足条件；satisfy oneself that 使自己确信
🧠 助记
satis（足够）+ fy → 给够了 → 满足
satisfy::/ˈsætɪsfaɪ/:v. 使满意:The results satisfied the examiners completely.
3. recruit /rɪˈkruːt/ v. 招募；征兵 n. 新兵；新成员

🧠 助记
re（再）+ cruit（生长）→ 让队伍再生长 → 招募
recruit::/rɪˈkruːt/:v. 招募:They recruited volunteers from local universities.

皮肤像薄薄的 skin（皮）包着身体
skin::/skɪn/:n. 皮肤:The skin of the apple was red and shiny.

retail::/ˈriːteɪl/:n./v. 零售 adj. 零售的:The company expanded its retail operations overseas.
