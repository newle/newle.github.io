1. admission /ədˈmɪʃn/ n. 允许进入；承认；入场费
📖 英文解释
permission to enter a place or join an organization; a statement admitting something is true
✏️ 例句
The university's admission requirements have become increasingly competitive.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
admission to 进入/被录取；free admission 免费入场；admission fee 入场费；gain admission 获准进入；admission of guilt 认罪
🧠 助记
admit（承认/进入）+ sion → 允许进入的行为 → 入场
admission::/ədˈmɪʃn/:n. 允许进入:Her admission to the law school was a dream come true.
2. regardless /rɪˈɡɑːdləs/ adv. 不颃；不管怎样 adj. 不颃一切的
📖 英文解释
without being affected by something; despite anything that happens
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
regardless of 不管；regardless of cost/risk 不计代价/风险；carry on regardless 不管后果继续
🧠 助记
regard（关注）+ less（少）→ 少了关注 → 不颃
regardless::/rɪˈɡɑːdləs/:adv. 不颃:The policy applies to everyone regardless of background.

🧠 助记
em（进入）+ pty → 小空间 → 空的
empty::/ˈempti/:adj. 空的:The streets were empty in the early hours of the morning.
