1. witness /ˈwɪtnəs/ n. 目击者；证人 v. 目击；见证；亲历
📖 英文解释
a person who sees an event happen; to see something happen; to officially sign a document
✏️ 例句
Several witnesses reported seeing the suspect near the scene.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
eyewitness 目击者；witness to 目击；bear witness to 证明；witness a change/event 见证变化/事件；key witness 关键证人
🧠 助记
wit（智慧）+ ness → 用智慧观察的人 → 见证人
witness::/ˈwɪtnəs/:n. 目击者:She witnessed the entire accident from her office window.
2. branch /brɑːntʃ/ n. 树枝；分支；部门 v. 分叉
📖 英文解释
a part of a tree growing from the trunk; a division of a larger organization; to divide into smaller parts
✏️ 例句
The company opened a new branch in Singapore last year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
branch out 开拓；branch office 分公司；branch of study 学科；branch off 分岔；tree branch 树枝
🧠 助记
branch 含 ranch（农场）→ 农场里树木有很多枝干 → 分支
branch::/brɑːntʃ/:n. 树枝:The bank has branches in over fifty countries worldwide.
3. literary /ˈlɪtərəri/ adj. 文学的；书面的；文雅的
📖 英文解释
connected with literature; used in writing rather than in ordinary speech
✏️ 例句
She has won several prestigious literary awards for her novels.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
literary work/criticism 文学作品/批评；literary style/language 文学风格/语言；literary circle 文学圈；literary talent 文学才华
🧠 助记
litera（字母/文字）+ ry → 与文字相关的 → 文学的
literary::/ˈlɪtərəri/:adj. 文学的:The poem demonstrates a high level of literary skill and imagination.
4. reference /ˈrefrəns/ n. 提及；参考；引用；推荐信
📖 英文解释
the act of mentioning something; a source of information; a letter recommending someone for a job
✏️ 例句
The report makes several references to earlier studies on the topic.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make reference to 提及；for reference 以供参考；reference book/library 参考书/图书馆；with reference to 关于；give sb a reference 给某人写推荐信
🧠 助记
refer（参考）+ ence → 参考的行为/来源 → 参考
reference::/ˈrefrəns/:n. 提及:The author includes a comprehensive list of references at the end.
5. minister /ˈmɪnɪstər/ n. 部长；牧师 v. 照料；服务于
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
mini（小）+ ster → 为上方的大人物服务的人 → 部长
minister::/ˈmɪnɪstər/:n. 部长:The prime minister called an emergency meeting of the cabinet.
6. register /ˈredʒɪstər/ v. 登记；注册；表现 n. 登记册；语域
📖 英文解释
to officially record information; to show an emotion or feeling; an official list
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

register::/ˈredʒɪstər/:v. 登记:Students must register for their courses before the semester begins.
7. declare /dɪˈkleər/ v. 宣布；声明；申报；表白
📖 英文解释
to announce something officially; to say clearly that something is true; to state goods for customs
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
de（加强）+ clare（清楚）→ 清楚地说出 → 宣布
declare::/dɪˈkleər/:v. 宣布:The authorities declared the building unsafe and ordered it to be demolished.

everywhere::/ˈevrɪweər/:adv. 到处:Signs of economic recovery are visible everywhere in the country.
9. invent /ɪnˈvent/ v. 发明；创造；捏造

🧠 助记
in（进入）+ vent（来）→ 新东西进入世界 → 发明
invent::/ɪnˈvent/:v. 发明:She invented a completely new technique for treating the disease.
10. maybe /ˈmeɪbi/ adv. 也许；可能 n. 不确定的事

maybe::/ˈmeɪbi/:adv. 也许:Maybe the best solution is to tackle both problems simultaneously.

volume::/ˈvɒljuːm/:n. 音量:The sheer volume of data makes analysis extremely difficult.
