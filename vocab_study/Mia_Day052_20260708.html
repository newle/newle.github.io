1. flexible /ˈfleksɪbl/ adj. 灵活的；柔韧的；可变通的
📖 英文解释
able to change or be changed easily according to the situation; able to bend without breaking
✏️ 例句
The company offers flexible working hours to attract talented employees.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
flexible schedule 灵活的时间表；flexible approach 灵活方式；mentally flexible 思维灵活
🧠 助记
flex（弯曲/炫耀肌肉）+ ible（能够），能弯曲而不断裂就是'灵活的'
flexible::/ˈfleksɪbl/:adj. 灵活的:We need a flexible plan that can adapt to changing circumstances.
2. cat /kæt/ n. 猫；v. 呕吐（俚语）
📖 英文解释
a small domesticated carnivorous mammal; any member of the cat family
✏️ 例句
The cat sat on the windowsill watching the birds in the garden.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cat nap 短暂小睡；let the cat out of the bag 泄露秘密；cat and mouse 猫鼠游戏
🧠 助记
cat三个字母，c-a-t，最简单的英语单词之一，直接记忆
cat::/kæt/:n. 猫:The cat curled up and fell asleep by the fire.
3. royal /ˈrɔɪəl/ adj. 皇家的；王室的；n. 王室成员
📖 英文解释
relating to or having the status of a king or queen; belonging to the royal family
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
royal family 王室；royal palace 皇宫；royal blue 宝蓝色；royal decree 皇家法令
🧠 助记
royal联想'罗亚尔'，法语roi = king（国王），royal = 王室的/皇家的
royal::/ˈrɔɪəl/:adj. 皇家的:The palace is the official residence of the royal family.
4. highlight /ˈhaɪlaɪt/ v. 突出；强调；n. 亮点；精彩部分
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

highlight::/ˈhaɪlaɪt/:v. 突出:The speech highlighted the need for urgent action on climate change.
5. remark /rɪˈmɑːk/ v./n. 评论；备注；说
📖 英文解释
to say something as a comment; a spoken or written comment
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记
re（再次）+ mark（标记），'再次标记'就是'评论'，remark = 评论/评注
remark::/rɪˈmɑːk/:v./n. 评论:He remarked that the proposal needed further consideration.
6. comparison /kəmˈpærɪsn/ n. 比较；对比；比喻
📖 英文解释

comparison::/kəmˈpærɪsn/:n. 比较:By comparison, last year's results were much worse.
7. regulate /ˈreɡjuleɪt/ v. 管理；调节；规范
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

regulate::/ˈreɡjuleɪt/:v. 管理:Laws regulate how companies can advertise to children.
8. contrary /ˈkɒntrəri/ adj. 相反的；对立的；n. 对立面
📖 英文解释
opposite in nature or direction; a fact or thing that is opposite

contrary::/ˈkɒntrəri/:adj. 相反的:Contrary to expectations, the project was completed ahead of schedule.
9. rush /rʌʃ/ v. 冲；赶紧；催促；n. 急促；冲动

🧠 助记
rush谐音'冲'，就是冲动地快速行动，rush = 冲/赶
rush::/rʌʃ/:v. 冲:Don't rush the decision; take your time to think it through.
10. interact /ˌɪntərˈækt/ v. 互动；交流；相互作用

interact::/ˌɪntərˈækt/:v. 互动:The new software allows users to interact with the system using voice commands.
