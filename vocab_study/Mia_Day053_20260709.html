1. guideline /ˈɡaɪdlaɪn/ n. 指导方针；准则；指导原则
📖 英文解释
a general rule or piece of advice given to help achieve something
✏️ 例句
The health ministry issued new guidelines on food safety standards.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
follow guidelines 遵循准则；set guidelines 制定准则；safety guidelines 安全准则
🧠 助记
guide（指导）+ line（线），'指导的线'就是'指导方针/准则'
guideline::/ˈɡaɪdlaɪn/:n. 指导方针:The company has strict guidelines on data privacy and security.
2. route /ruːt/ n. 路线；路径；途径
📖 英文解释
a way or course taken in getting from a starting point to a destination
✏️ 例句
The bus route has been changed due to road construction in the city center.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
trade route 贸易路线；en route 在途中；escape route 逃跑路线；route map 路线图
🧠 助记
route谐音'路特'，走'路'上的'特'定线路，route = 路线
route::/ruːt/:n. 路线:Travelers can take a scenic route through the national park.
3. whenever /wenˈevər/ conj./adv. 无论何时；每当
📖 英文解释
at any time or every time that; used to emphasize that you do not know when something will happen
✏️ 例句
Whenever I feel stressed, I go for a long walk in the park.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
whenever possible 尽可能；whenever you like 随时；whenever that may be 不管什么时候
🧠 助记
when（什么时候）+ ever（总是），'总是在什么时候'就是'无论何时'，whenever = 每当
whenever::/wenˈevər/:conj./adv. 无论何时:You are welcome to call me whenever you need help.

📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

solid::/ˈsɒlɪd/:adj. 固体的:She built a solid reputation for hard work and integrity.

tap::/tæp/:v. 轻拍:The company plans to tap into the growing Asian market.
