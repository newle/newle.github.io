1. bridge /brɪdʒ/ n. 桥；桥梁；v. 架桥于；消除分歧
📖 英文解释
A structure built to span a physical obstacle such as a body of water or valley; also means to reduce or eliminate a gap or difference.
✏️ 例句
The government invested millions to bridge the gap between rural and urban education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
bridge the gap 弥合差距；bridge the divide 消除鸿沟；build a bridge, cross a bridge
🧠 助记
bridge = br(兄弟)+idge，兄弟之间搭一座桥消除隔阂
bridge::/brɪdʒ/:n. 桥:build a bridge between theory and practice
2. grass /ɡrɑːs/ n. 草；草地；草坪
📖 英文解释
A low-growing plant with narrow green leaves that covers lawns and meadows; a common ground cover plant.
✏️ 例句
The children played on the soft grass in the park.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cut the grass 割草；grass-roots 基层的（考研高频形容词）；green as grass 非常幼稚
🧠 助记
grass 发音像 ge-la-si，想象一片绿色格子状草坪
grass::/ɡrɑːs/:n. 草:grass-roots movement spreads nationwide
3. slave /sleɪv/ n. 奴隶；苦工；v. 拼命工作
📖 英文解释
A person who is legally owned by another and forced to work without pay; one who works extremely hard.
✏️ 例句
Millions of people were enslaved and forced to work on plantations in the 18th century.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
slave to sth 受...支配；slave labor 奴役劳动；考研历史类语境
🧠 助记
slave = s+lave（洗），奴隶不停地洗东西（拼命干活）
slave::/sleɪv/:n. 奴隶:be a slave to fashion and consumerism
4. privilege /ˈprɪvɪlɪdʒ/ n. 特权；特别荣幸；v. 给予特权
📖 英文解释
A special right, advantage, or immunity granted to a particular person or group; a special honor.
✏️ 例句
Access to quality education should be a right, not a privilege.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
privilege of doing 有幸做某事；privileged class 特权阶层；考研社会公平话题高频词
🧠 助记
priv（私）+i+lege（法律）= 私人法律 = 特权
privilege::/ˈprɪvɪlɪdʒ/:n. 特权:enjoy the privilege of higher education
5. restore /rɪˈstɔːr/ v. 恢复；归还；修复；重建
📖 英文解释
To bring back to a former or original condition; to return something to its original state.
✏️ 例句
The city launched a project to restore the historic buildings to their former glory.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
restore order 恢复秩序；restore confidence 重拾信心；restoration n.恢复
🧠 助记
re（重新）+ store（储存）= 重新存放 = 恢复原状
restore::/rɪˈstɔːr/:v. 恢复:restore public confidence in the government
6. upset /ʌpˈset/ v. 使心烦；打乱；adj. 不安的；n. 意外结果
📖 英文解释
To make someone feel unhappy or worried; to disturb or disrupt a plan or situation unexpectedly.
✏️ 例句
The unexpected exam results upset many students.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
upset the balance 打破平衡；be upset about 对...感到不安；upset stomach 胃不舒服
🧠 助记
up（翻）+set（放）= 把东西翻倒 = 心情翻倒 = 不安
upset::/ʌpˈset/:v. 使心烦:upset the existing social order unexpectedly
7. counterpart /ˈkaʊntəpɑːrt/ n. 对应的人或物；副本；相对应的职位
📖 英文解释
A person or thing that corresponds to or has the same function as another in a different place or organization.
✏️ 例句
The Chinese minister met with his American counterpart to discuss trade issues.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sb's counterpart 某人的对应者；考研外交、比较类语境高频
🧠 助记
counter（对面）+part（部分）= 对面那部分 = 对应的人
counterpart::/ˈkaʊntəpɑːrt/:n. 对应的人或物:the president met his foreign counterpart
8. accelerate /əkˈseləreɪt/ v. 加速；促进；提前
📖 英文解释
To begin to move more quickly; to cause something to happen sooner or at a faster rate.
✏️ 例句
Technological advances have accelerated the pace of globalization.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
accelerate the pace of 加快...的步伐；accelerate economic growth 加速经济增长
🧠 助记
ac+celer（快，如celery芹菜）+ate = 使加快
accelerate::/əkˈseləreɪt/:v. 加速:accelerate the pace of urbanization and reform
9. era /ˈɪərə/ n. 时代；纪元；历史时期
📖 英文解释
A long and distinct period of history having a particular characteristic feature; an epoch.
✏️ 例句
We are living in the era of big data and artificial intelligence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the era of 某时代；usher in a new era 开创新时代；post-war era 战后时代
🧠 助记
era 像 yi-la，每个亿年就是一个大时代
era::/ˈɪərə/:n. 时代:usher in a new era of technological innovation
10. false /fɔːls/ adj. 错误的；虚假的；不真实的
📖 英文解释
Not true or correct; based on mistaken ideas; intended to deceive.
✏️ 例句
The advertisement made false claims about the health benefits of the product.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
false impression 错误印象；false alarm 虚惊；true or false 判断正误
🧠 助记
false 发音像 fo-ls，否了斯 = 东西被否了 = 假的
false::/fɔːls/:adj. 错误的:give a false impression of the situation
11. uniform /ˈjuːnɪfɔːrm/ adj. 统一的；一致的；n. 制服；v. 使统一
📖 英文解释
Remaining the same in all cases and at all times; not varying; a distinctive set of clothes.
✏️ 例句
The policy aims to establish uniform standards across all schools.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
uniform distribution 均匀分布；in uniform 穿制服；uniform standard 统一标准
🧠 助记
uni（一）+form（形状）= 一个形状 = 统一的
uniform::/ˈjuːnɪfɔːrm/:adj. 统一的:maintain a uniform standard nationwide
12. pair /peər/ n. 一对；一双；v. 配对；成对
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
pair 发音像 pei-er，两个配在一起
pair::/peər/:n. 一对:work in pairs to solve problems efficiently
13. profitable /ˈprɒfɪtəbl/ adj. 有利可图的；有益的；盈利的

🧠 助记

profitable::/ˈprɒfɪtəbl/:adj. 有利可图的:a profitable long-term investment strategy

breath::/breθ/:n. 呼吸:take a breath of fresh air in nature

dam::/dæm/:n. 水坝:build a dam to generate hydroelectric power
