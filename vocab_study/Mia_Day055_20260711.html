1. greenhouse /ˈɡriːnhaʊs/ n. 温室；暖房
📖 英文解释
A glass or plastic building used for growing plants that need warmth and protection; also refers to the greenhouse effect.
✏️ 例句
Rising carbon emissions are intensifying the greenhouse effect and global warming.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
greenhouse gas 温室气体；greenhouse effect 温室效应；greenhouse emissions 温室排放
🧠 助记
green（绿）+house（房子）= 绿色植物的房子 = 温室
greenhouse::/ˈɡriːnhaʊs/:n. 温室:reduce greenhouse gas emissions to combat climate change
2. visual /ˈvɪʒuəl/ adj. 视觉的；可见的；n. 视觉材料
📖 英文解释
Relating to seeing or sight; done or executed by sight; something visible such as a picture or film.
✏️ 例句
Visual aids such as charts and graphs make complex data easier to understand.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
visual effect 视觉效果；visual arts 视觉艺术；visual impairment 视力障碍
🧠 助记
vis（看，如 vision）+ual = 和看有关的 = 视觉的
visual::/ˈvɪʒuəl/:adj. 视觉的:use visual aids to enhance classroom learning
3. electric /ɪˈlektrɪk/ adj. 电的；电动的；令人激动的
📖 英文解释
Of, worked by, or charged with electricity; having the quality of exciting or thrilling.
✏️ 例句
Electric vehicles are becoming increasingly popular as governments push for greener transport.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
electric car 电动汽车；electric power 电力；electric atmosphere 令人激动的氛围
🧠 助记
electr（电）+ic = 和电有关的
electric::/ɪˈlektrɪk/:adj. 电的:electric vehicles reduce carbon emissions significantly
4. luxury /ˈlʌkʃəri/ n. 奢侈；奢侈品；享受；adj. 豪华的
📖 英文解释
The state of great comfort and extravagant living; an item that is expensive and not essential.
✏️ 例句
In many developing countries, clean drinking water is a luxury rather than a right.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
luxury goods 奢侈品；luxury car 豪华汽车；afford the luxury of 有条件享受...
🧠 助记
lux（光，如 deluxe 豪华的）+ury = 明亮豪华的享受
luxury::/ˈlʌkʃəri/:n. 奢侈:luxury goods consumption reflects social inequality
5. tall /tɔːl/ adj. 高的；高大的；（任务）艰巨的
📖 英文解释
Of great height; above average height; used to describe a demanding or difficult task.
✏️ 例句
It is a tall order to eliminate poverty without addressing structural inequality.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tall order 艰难的任务；tall building 高楼；stand tall 昂首挺胸
🧠 助记
tall 发音像 tuo-l，高高的 T 字形 = 高大的
tall::/tɔːl/:adj. 高的:a tall order to achieve in a short time
6. entrance /ˈentrəns/ n. 入口；进入；入学；v. 使陶醉
📖 英文解释
An opening, such as a door or gate, that allows access to a place; the act of entering; admission to an institution.
✏️ 例句
Entrance to the prestigious university requires excellent academic performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
entrance exam 入学考试；entrance fee 入场费；make an entrance 登场
🧠 助记
en+trance（道路）= 进入的道路 = 入口
entrance::/ˈentrəns/:n. 入口:pass the entrance exam to a top university
7. snack /snæk/ n. 零食；小吃；v. 吃零食
📖 英文解释
A small amount of food eaten between meals; to eat small amounts of food between main meals.
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记

snack::/snæk/:n. 零食:replace unhealthy snacks with nutritious alternatives

🧠 助记
rat 发音像 la-te（落特），落后了就被叫老鼠
rat::/ræt/:n. 老鼠:use lab rats to test experimental medicine
9. channel /ˈtʃænl/ n. 渠道；频道；海峡；v. 引导

channel::/ˈtʃænl/:n. 渠道:channel resources into education and research

virtue::/ˈvɜːrtʃuː/:n. 美德:by virtue of hard work and perseverance
