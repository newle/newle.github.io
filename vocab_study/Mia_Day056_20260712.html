1. tour /tʊər/ n. 旅行；参观；巡回演出；v. 游览；巡回
📖 英文解释
A journey for pleasure in which several different places are visited; a trip to perform or compete in several places.
✏️ 例句
The government launched a nationwide tour to promote environmental awareness.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tour guide 导游；go on a tour 去旅行；concert tour 演唱会巡演
🧠 助记
tour 发音像 tu-er（图儿），带着地图去旅行
tour::/tʊər/:n. 旅行:go on a tour to promote cultural exchange
2. steel /stiːl/ n. 钢；钢铁；v. 使坚定；使强硬
📖 英文解释
A hard, strong metal made of iron mixed with carbon; to make oneself determined or resolute.
✏️ 例句
China is the world's largest producer of steel, accounting for over half of global output.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
steel industry 钢铁业；nerves of steel 钢铁般的神经；steel oneself 使自己坚定
🧠 助记
steel 发音像 si-ti-er（斯-提-儿），提炼出来的硬金属 = 钢
steel::/stiːl/:n. 钢:the steel industry is vital to national development
3. beneath /bɪˈniːθ/ prep. 在...下面；低于；不值得；adv. 在下面
📖 英文解释
Extending or directly below; of lower rank or level than; not worthy of.
✏️ 例句
Beneath the surface of economic growth lie deep structural inequalities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
beneath the surface 表面之下；beneath one's dignity 有损尊严；beneath notice 不值一提
🧠 助记
be+neath（like 'nether' 下方）= 在下方 = 在...下面
beneath::/bɪˈniːθ/:prep. 在...下面:beneath the surface lies a complex social structure
4. discount /ˈdɪskaʊnt/ n. 折扣；v. 打折；不重视；忽视
📖 英文解释
A deduction from the usual cost; to deduct an amount from the price; to regard as unimportant.
✏️ 例句
The report should not discount the social costs of rapid industrialization.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
at a discount 打折；discount the risk 忽视风险；offer a discount 给予折扣
🧠 助记
dis（否定）+count（计数）= 把价格减掉 = 折扣
discount::/ˈdɪskaʊnt/:n. 折扣:do not discount the environmental cost of development
5. trace /treɪs/ v. 追踪；溯源；描摹；n. 痕迹；微量；踪迹
📖 英文解释
To find or discover by investigation; to follow the course of something; a mark or sign left by something.
✏️ 例句
Scientists can trace the origins of the pandemic to a specific animal host.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
trace back to 追溯到；leave a trace 留下痕迹；trace element 微量元素
🧠 助记
trace 发音像 tui-si（推斯），一步步推查痕迹 = 追踪
trace::/treɪs/:v. 追踪:trace the root causes of social inequality
6. unfortunately /ʌnˈfɔːrtʃənətli/ adv. 不幸地；遗憾地
📖 英文解释
Used to say that something is sad or that you wish it were not true.
✏️ 例句
Unfortunately, access to quality education remains unequal across different regions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
unfortunately 常用于引出坏消息；unfortunately for sb 对某人不幸的是
🧠 助记
un（不）+fortune（运气）+ately = 运气不好地 = 不幸地
unfortunately::/ʌnˈfɔːrtʃənətli/:adv. 不幸地:unfortunately poverty continues to affect millions
7. laboratory /ləˈbɒrətri/ n. 实验室；研究室
📖 英文解释
A room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching; a place for testing and analysis.
✏️ 例句
The government is investing heavily in establishing state-of-the-art research laboratories.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
laboratory experiment 实验室实验；laboratory test 实验室检测；lab for short
🧠 助记
labor（劳动）+atory = 劳动工作的地方 = 实验室
laboratory::/ləˈbɒrətri/:n. 实验室:conduct experiments in the laboratory to test hypotheses
8. belt /belt/ n. 带；皮带；地带；v. 用皮带抽；猛击
📖 英文解释
A strip of leather worn around the waist; a region or area with particular features; to hit hard.
✏️ 例句
The manufacturing belt in northern China has been hit hard by automation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
belt of land 地带；rust belt 锈带（衰落工业区）；seat belt 安全带
🧠 助记
belt 发音像 bei-er-te，背着皮带（belt）防身
belt::/belt/:n. 带:the rust belt struggles with economic transformation
9. march /mɑːrtʃ/ v. 行进；游行；n. 游行；行军；三月
📖 英文解释
To walk with regular steps as a soldier; to walk in a purposeful way; a procession of people.
✏️ 例句
Thousands marched in the capital to demand government action on climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
march against 游行反对；march for 游行支持；on the march 行进中
🧠 助记
march = mar（发音像 ma，妈）+ch，妈妈带队游行
march::/mɑːrtʃ/:v. 行进:march to demand equal rights and social justice
10. tendency /ˈtendənsi/ n. 倾向；趋势；癖好
📖 英文解释
An inclination toward a particular characteristic or type of behavior; a trend.
✏️ 例句
There is a growing tendency for companies to outsource labor to developing countries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tendency to do 倾向于做；tendency toward 趋向于；general tendency 普遍趋势
🧠 助记
tend（倾向）+ency = 名词 = 倾向/趋势
tendency::/ˈtendənsi/:n. 倾向:the tendency toward urbanization is accelerating
11. accomplish /əˈkʌmplɪʃ/ v. 完成；实现；达到（目的）
📖 英文解释
To succeed in doing or achieving something; to complete successfully.
✏️ 例句
No meaningful reform can be accomplished without the support of the public.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
ac+complish（完成，=complete）= 真正完成 = 实现
accomplish::/əˈkʌmplɪʃ/:v. 完成:accomplish the goal of sustainable development
12. ruin /ˈruːɪn/ v. 毁坏；破坏；n. 废墟；毁灭；损毁
📖 英文解释
To damage something so badly that it loses all value; complete destruction; the remains of a destroyed building.
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记

ruin::/ˈruːɪn/:v. 毁坏:corruption can ruin the foundation of democracy
13. entertain /ˌentəˈteɪn/ v. 娱乐；款待；考虑（想法）；持有（想法）
📖 英文解释
To provide someone with amusement or enjoyment; to give hospitality to; to consider an idea.

entertain::/ˌentəˈteɪn/:v. 娱乐:entertain diverse ideas before making a decision
