1. keen /kiːn/ adj. 热忱的；敏锐的；渴望的
📖 英文解释
Having a strong interest or enthusiasm; intellectually sharp and perceptive.
✏️ 例句
She is keen on learning new languages and practices every day.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
keen on sth / keen to do sth（热衷于……）；考研常考 keen competition（激烈竞争）、keen interest（浓厚兴趣）
🧠 助记
keen → 像'刃'一样锋利，切入事物——对事情'锐利'地感兴趣
keen::/kiːn/:adj. 热忱的:She is keen on environmental protection.
2. theft /θeft/ n. 偷窃；盗窃罪
📖 英文解释
The act of stealing; the unlawful taking of someone else's property.
✏️ 例句
Identity theft has become a serious problem in the digital age.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
petty theft（小偷小摸）；identity theft（身份盗窃）；考研常出现在犯罪相关阅读中
🧠 助记
theft → the + ft（偷走了 the 东西，ft = 飞腿跑了）
theft::/θeft/:n. 偷窃:Theft of intellectual property is a growing concern.
3. ethnic /ˈeθnɪk/ adj. 民族的；种族的
📖 英文解释
Relating to a group of people with a common national or cultural tradition.
✏️ 例句
The city celebrates its ethnic diversity with annual cultural festivals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ethnic group（民族群体）；ethnic minority（少数民族）；考研社会话题高频词
🧠 助记
eth-nic → 'eth'联想ethics（伦理），不同民族有不同伦理文化
ethnic::/ˈeθnɪk/:adj. 民族的:Ethnic minorities deserve equal rights and opportunities.
4. historian /hɪˈstɔːriən/ n. 历史学家
📖 英文解释
An expert or student of history, especially one who writes about it.
✏️ 例句
The historian spent years researching the causes of World War I.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研人文类阅读常见词；注意区分 historian（学家）vs history（历史）vs historic（历史性的）vs historical（历史的）
🧠 助记
history + -an → 研究历史的人
historian::/hɪˈstɔːriən/:n. 历史学家:Historians debate the long-term impact of colonialism.
5. supreme /suːˈpriːm/ adj. 最高的；最重要的；最优的
📖 英文解释
Highest in rank, power, or authority; greatest in importance or degree.
✏️ 例句
The Supreme Court has the final say on constitutional matters.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
Supreme Court（最高法院）；supreme power（最高权力）；考研法律、政治类阅读高频
🧠 助记
su-preme → super + 顶端，冠于所有之上
supreme::/suːˈpriːm/:adj. 最高的:The Supreme Court ruled the law unconstitutional.
6. restrict /rɪˈstrɪkt/ v. 限制；约束；限定
📖 英文解释
To put a limit on; to keep under control by rules or regulations.
✏️ 例句
The government decided to restrict access to certain websites.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
restrict sth to sth（将……限制在……范围内）；restricted area（限制区域）；考研写作和阅读常用
🧠 助记
re-strict → strict（严格）再加重——严格再严格就是限制
restrict::/rɪˈstrɪkt/:v. 限制:They restricted the number of participants to fifty.
7. democratic /ˌdeməˈkrætɪk/ adj. 民主的；民主主义的
📖 英文解释
Relating to or supporting democracy or its principles.
✏️ 例句
A democratic society allows citizens to vote and express their opinions freely.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
democratic election（民主选举）；democratic process（民主程序）；考研政治类阅读高频词
🧠 助记
demo（人民）+ cratic（统治）→ 人民统治 → 民主的
democratic::/ˌdeməˈkrætɪk/:adj. 民主的:Democratic values include freedom and equality.
8. compensate /ˈkɒmpenseɪt/ v. 补偿；赔偿；弥补
📖 英文解释
To make up for something unwanted or unpleasant by having something good; to pay someone for work or loss.
✏️ 例句
The company agreed to compensate the workers for their extra hours.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
compensate for sth（补偿……）；compensate sb for sth（赔偿某人……）；考研经济、法律类常见
🧠 助记
com-pens-ate → pension（养老金）补上来 → 补偿
compensate::/ˈkɒmpenseɪt/:v. 补偿:Nothing can compensate for the loss of a loved one.
9. thin /θɪn/ adj. 薄的；细的；稀薄的 v. 使变薄
📖 英文解释
Having very little depth between opposite surfaces; not dense or substantial.
✏️ 例句
The ice on the pond was too thin to walk on safely.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
thin air（稀薄的空气）；thin majority（微弱多数）；考研中常考引申义 thin = 不充分的（理由等）
🧠 助记
thin → 想象一张薄薄的纸，几乎透明——薄的
thin::/θɪn/:adj. 薄的:The argument was thin and lacked supporting evidence.
10. anxious /ˈæŋkʃəs/ adj. 焦虑的；渴望的；担心的
📖 英文解释
Experiencing worry or unease; strongly wishing for something to happen.
✏️ 例句
She was anxious about the results of her medical test.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
anxious about sth（对……感到担忧）；anxious to do sth（渴望做……）；anxiety n. 焦虑
🧠 助记
anxi = angry + 悬——愤怒悬在心头 → 焦虑
anxious::/ˈæŋkʃəs/:adj. 焦虑的:Parents are often anxious about their children's future.
11. admire /ədˈmaɪər/ v. 钦佩；羡慕；欣赏
📖 英文解释
To regard with respect, pleasure, or approval.
✏️ 例句
I admire her courage in speaking out against injustice.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
admire sb for sth（因……而钦佩某人）；admiration n. 钦佩；admirable adj. 令人钦佩的
🧠 助记
ad-mire → 'ad'（向）+ 'mire'（镜子）→ 对着镜子欣赏 → 欣赏、钦佩
admire::/ədˈmaɪər/:v. 钦佩:We all admire her dedication to public service.
12. guard /ɡɑːrd/ v./n. 保卫；警戒；看守；警卫
📖 英文解释
To watch over and protect from harm or danger; a person who protects.
✏️ 例句
Security guards were stationed at every entrance of the building.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
guard against（防范）；on guard（警戒中）；bodyguard（保镖）；考研安全、社会类文章常见
🧠 助记
guard → 联想'瓜大'（卫士很高大）站岗保卫
guard::/ɡɑːrd/:v./n. 保卫:We must guard against overconfidence in our plans.
13. intention /ɪnˈtenʃn/ n. 意图；打算；目的
📖 英文解释
A plan or aim that you intend to carry out; what you mean to do.
✏️ 例句
His intention was to finish the project before the deadline.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
with the intention of doing sth（意图做……）；intend v. 打算；intentional adj. 故意的
🧠 助记
in-tent-ion → tent（帐篷）里 → 在帐篷里谋划 → 意图
intention::/ɪnˈtenʃn/:n. 意图:She had no intention of giving up her career.
14. lean /liːn/ v. 倾斜；倚靠 adj. 瘦的；精干的
📖 英文解释
To bend or incline towards something; having little fat; efficient with no excess.
✏️ 例句
He leaned against the wall and waited for her to arrive.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lean on sb（依靠某人）；lean towards（倾向于）；lean management（精益管理）——考研管理类文章常见
🧠 助记
lean → 身体向前倾斜 lean，精干的人才lean得动
lean::/liːn/:v. 倾斜:She leaned toward a more flexible work schedule.
15. generous /ˈdʒenərəs/ adj. 慷慨的；大方的；丰盛的
📖 英文解释
Willing to give money, help, or time freely; larger or more than expected.
✏️ 例句
It was very generous of him to donate half his salary to charity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
generous with（在……方面慷慨）；generosity n. 慷慨；考研作文美德类词汇
🧠 助记
gene-rous → 基因（gene）里有慷慨的成分 → 天生慷慨
generous::/ˈdʒenərəs/:adj. 慷慨的:A generous donation helped rebuild the community center.
16. honest /ˈɒnɪst/ adj. 诚实的；正直的；坦率的
📖 英文解释
Telling the truth and being free from deception; morally correct.
✏️ 例句
An honest politician is rare but essential for a healthy democracy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
to be honest（说实话）；honest with sb（对某人诚实）；honesty n. 诚实
🧠 助记
hon-est → honor（荣誉）的基础就是诚实
honest::/ˈɒnɪst/:adj. 诚实的:She gave an honest assessment of the situation.
17. shoulder /ˈʃəʊldər/ n. 肩膀；路肩 v. 承担
📖 英文解释
The joint connecting the arm to the body; to take on a responsibility.
✏️ 例句
She shouldered the responsibility of caring for her elderly parents.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
shoulder the burden（承担重担）；shoulder to shoulder（并肩）；考研常考动词义：承担（责任）
🧠 助记
shoulder → 'shou'（手）上面是 shoulder，扛在肩上 → 承担
shoulder::/ˈʃəʊldər/:n. 肩膀:He shouldered the blame for the team's failure.
18. inner /ˈɪnər/ adj. 内部的；内心的；精神的
📖 英文解释
Situated inside or further in; relating to a person's thoughts or feelings.
✏️ 例句
Meditation helps people find peace in their inner world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
inner city（市区内部）；inner conflict（内心冲突）；inner peace（内心平静）；考研心理、文化类文章常见
🧠 助记
inner = in（里面的）+ -ner → 更里面的 → 内部的、内心的
inner::/ˈɪnər/:adj. 内部的:She struggled with inner doubts about her decision.
19. resist /rɪˈzɪst/ v. 抵制；抵抗；忍住不做
📖 英文解释
To withstand the action or effect of; to fight against; to stop oneself from doing something.
✏️ 例句
It's hard to resist the temptation of checking your phone every few minutes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
resist temptation（抵制诱惑）；resistance n. 抵抗；resistant adj. 有抵抗力的
🧠 助记
re-sist → sit（坐）着不动 re（反复）→ 坚持不动 → 抵抗
resist::/rɪˈzɪst/:v. 抵制:Citizens resisted the government's unfair policies.
20. sky /skaɪ/ n. 天空；晴天
📖 英文解释
The region of the atmosphere and outer space above the earth.
✏️ 例句
The clear blue sky lifted everyone's spirits after a week of rain.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sky-high（极高）；skyscraper（摩天大楼）；the sky's the limit（潜力无限）；考研写作描写环境常用
🧠 助记
sky → 四个字母指向四方，天空包围一切
sky::/skaɪ/:n. 天空:Pollution has darkened the sky over many major cities.
21. artistic /ɑːˈtɪstɪk/ adj. 艺术的；有艺术才能的
📖 英文解释
Relating to art or an artist; having or revealing natural creative skill.
✏️ 例句
The artistic design of the building attracted tourists from all over the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
artistic talent（艺术才能）；artistic expression（艺术表达）；art → artist → artistic
🧠 助记
art + istic → 艺术的风格 → 艺术的
artistic::/ɑːˈtɪstɪk/:adj. 艺术的:The film was praised for its artistic cinematography.
22. marine /məˈriːn/ adj. 海的；船舶的 n. 海军陆战队员
📖 英文解释
Of or relating to the sea; connected with navigation or trade by sea.
✏️ 例句
Marine pollution is threatening countless species of ocean life.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
marine biology（海洋生物学）；marine environment（海洋环境）；考研环境、科学类文章高频词
🧠 助记
marine → 'mar'在西班牙语是海 → 海的
marine::/məˈriːn/:adj. 海的:Marine ecosystems are under threat from climate change.
23. sensible /ˈsensɪbl/ adj. 明智的；合情理的；有判断力的
📖 英文解释
Having or showing good sense or judgment; practical and reasonable.
✏️ 例句
It's sensible to save money for emergencies rather than spending everything.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sensible decision（明智决定）；注意与 sensitive（敏感的）区分；考研阅读常考
🧠 助记
sens(e)ible → 有感觉（sense）能力的人 → 明智的
sensible::/ˈsensɪbl/:adj. 明智的:The most sensible approach is to gather more data first.
24. legislation /ˌledʒɪˈsleɪʃn/ n. 立法；法规
📖 英文解释
Laws considered collectively; the process of making laws.
✏️ 例句
New legislation was passed to protect the rights of migrant workers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
introduce legislation（提出立法）；piece of legislation（一项法规）；legislate v. 立法；考研政治、法律类文章必备词
🧠 助记
legi（法律）+ slat（板块）+ ion → 一块块制定的法律 → 立法
legislation::/ˌledʒɪˈsleɪʃn/:n. 立法:Stronger environmental legislation is urgently needed.
25. owe /əʊ/ v. 欠（钱、债）；归因于；感激
📖 英文解释
To be required to pay money to someone; to feel gratitude towards someone.
✏️ 例句
She owes her success to years of hard work and perseverance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
owe sb sth（欠某人某物）；owe sth to sb（把……归因于某人）；I owe you one（欠你一个人情）
🧠 助记
owe → 发音'哦'——欠了钱叹气'哦'
owe::/əʊ/:v. 欠（钱、债）:He owes his promotion to his mentor's guidance.
26. theatre /ˈθɪətər/ n. 剧院；电影院；戏剧
📖 英文解释
A building where plays, shows, or films are performed; the writing and production of plays.
✏️ 例句
She has been involved in theatre since she was twelve years old.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
theatre = theater（美式）；operating theatre（手术室）；go to the theatre（看戏/看剧）
🧠 助记
the-atre → 想象'剧院'的高档感，theatre 有种古典剧场的味道
theatre::/ˈθɪətər/:n. 剧院:The city's theatre scene is thriving with new productions.
27. controversy /ˈkɒntrəvɜːsi/ n. 争论；争议；论战
📖 英文解释
Prolonged public disagreement or heated discussion, especially about a social or political issue.
✏️ 例句
The new policy sparked widespread controversy among educators and parents.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cause/stir controversy（引发争议）；controversial adj. 有争议的；考研写作和阅读核心词
🧠 助记
contro（反对）+ versy（转动方向）→ 各方意见转动碰撞 → 争议
controversy::/ˈkɒntrəvɜːsi/:n. 争论:The research findings remain mired in controversy.
28. venture /ˈventʃər/ v. 冒险；敢于 n. 风险项目；冒险
📖 英文解释
To dare to do or go somewhere that may be dangerous; a risky or daring business undertaking.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
venture capital（风险投资）；joint venture（合资企业）；venture out（冒险外出）；考研商业类文章高频

ven-ture → adventure（冒险）去掉 ad → 精简版的冒险
venture::/ˈventʃər/:v. 冒险:They ventured into new territory with their latest product.
29. ray /reɪ/ n. 光线；射线；少许（希望等）
📖 英文解释
A narrow beam of light or radiation; a small amount of a positive quality.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ray of hope（一线希望）；X-ray（X光）；ray of sunshine（阳光般的人/事）；考研物理、心理类文章常见
🧠 助记
ray → 光线像 ray 一样直直射出来
ray::/reɪ/:n. 光线:There was still a ray of hope for a peaceful resolution.
30. frequent /ˈfriːkwənt/ adj. 频繁的；常见的 v. 常去（某地）
📖 英文解释
Occurring often; happening again and again at short intervals.
✏️ 例句
Frequent handwashing is one of the most effective ways to prevent illness.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

fre-quent → free + 去（went）→ 频繁自由地去 → 频繁的
frequent::/ˈfriːkwənt/:adj. 频繁的:She made frequent trips to the library during exam season.
