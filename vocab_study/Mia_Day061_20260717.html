1. modest /ˈmɒdɪst/ adj. 谦虚的；适度的；朴素的
📖 英文解释
Not boasting about one's achievements; not extreme or excessive; simple and unpretentious.
✏️ 例句
Despite her outstanding achievements, she remained modest and avoided the spotlight.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
modest salary（适度的薪水）；modest improvement（小幅改善）；modesty n. 谦虚；考研写作美德类词汇
🧠 助记
mod-est → mode（模式）+ est → 走平常模式，不张扬 → 谦虚的
modest::/ˈmɒdɪst/:adj. 谦虚的:He was modest about his role in the project's success.
2. personnel /ˌpɜːrsəˈnel/ n. 员工；人事部门；全体工作人员
📖 英文解释
People employed in an organization or engaged in an organized undertaking; the department that manages employees.
✏️ 例句
The company's personnel department handles all recruitment and training.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
personnel management（人事管理）；military personnel（军事人员）；注意与 personal（个人的）区分
🧠 助记
person-nel → 很多 person（人）→ 全体员工
personnel::/ˌpɜːrsəˈnel/:n. 员工:All personnel are required to complete the safety training.
3. collapse /kəˈlæps/ v./n. 倒塌；崩溃；瓦解
📖 英文解释
To suddenly fall down or give way; to fail suddenly and completely; a sudden failure or breakdown.
✏️ 例句
The ancient bridge collapsed under the weight of the heavy truck.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
collapse of（……的崩溃）；economic collapse（经济崩溃）；考研经济、社会类文章高频词
🧠 助记
col-lapse → col（全部）+ lapse（失效）→ 全面失效 → 倒塌、崩溃
collapse::/kəˈlæps/:v./n. 倒塌:The financial system was on the brink of collapse in 2008.
4. angry /ˈæŋɡri/ adj. 生气的；愤怒的
📖 英文解释
Having a strong feeling of displeasure and antagonism; feeling or showing anger.
✏️ 例句
The customers were angry about the unexplained delays in delivery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
angry at/with sb（对某人生气）；angry about sth（对某事生气）；anger n. 愤怒；考研心理类文章常见
🧠 助记
ang-ry → anguish（痛苦）的简化，内心痛苦就会生气
angry::/ˈæŋɡri/:adj. 生气的:Citizens were angry about the lack of government transparency.
5. extensive /ɪkˈstensɪv/ adj. 广泛的；大量的；广阔的
📖 英文解释
Covering a large area; having a wide range; large in amount or degree.
✏️ 例句
The researcher conducted extensive interviews with communities across the country.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
extensive knowledge（广博知识）；extensively adv. 广泛地；extend v. 扩展；考研写作中常用来强调范围广
🧠 助记
ex-tens-ive → extend（延伸）→ 延伸很广 → 广泛的
extensive::/ɪkˈstensɪv/:adj. 广泛的:Extensive research was conducted before the policy was implemented.
6. resort /rɪˈzɔːrt/ n. 度假胜地 v. 诉诸；采取（通常是不好的方法）
📖 英文解释
A place frequented for holidays or recreation; to turn to a course of action as a final option.
✏️ 例句
The government resorted to emergency measures to control the outbreak.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
resort to（求助于；被迫采取）；as a last resort（最后手段）；beach resort（海滩度假村）
🧠 助记
re-sort → 重新排序（sort）→ 换另一种方法 → 求助于、诉诸
resort::/rɪˈzɔːrt/:n. 度假胜地 v. 诉诸:Companies should not resort to deception to attract customers.
7. assistance /əˈsɪstəns/ n. 帮助；援助；协助
📖 英文解释
The action of helping someone by sharing work; aid or support.
✏️ 例句
International assistance was crucial in rebuilding the country after the disaster.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
with the assistance of（在……的帮助下）；financial assistance（财政援助）；assist v. 协助；assistant n. 助手
🧠 助记
assist-ance → assist（帮助）的名词 → 帮助
assistance::/əˈsɪstəns/:n. 帮助:The NGO provided medical assistance to the refugee camp.
8. insect /ˈɪnsekt/ n. 昆虫
📖 英文解释
A small invertebrate animal with six legs, a segmented body, and usually two pairs of wings.
✏️ 例句
Many insect species are disappearing at alarming rates due to habitat loss.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
insect repellent（驱虫剂）；insect bite（虫咬）；insecticide n. 杀虫剂；考研生物、环境类文章常见
🧠 助记
in-sect → 'sect'（切断）→ 身体有切痕（分节）的小动物 → 昆虫
insect::/ˈɪnsekt/:n. 昆虫:Insect populations serve as key indicators of ecosystem health.
9. insight /ˈɪnsaɪt/ n. 洞察力；深刻理解；见解
📖 英文解释
The capacity to gain an accurate and deep understanding of someone or something.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
gain insight into（深入了解）；insightful adj. 富有洞察力的；考研写作中表达深刻理解
🧠 助记
in + sight → 看进去（内部）→ 洞察、深刻理解
insight::/ˈɪnsaɪt/:n. 洞察力:The report provides deep insight into consumer behavior trends.
10. metal /ˈmetl/ n. 金属 adj. 金属的
📖 英文解释
A hard shiny material such as iron, gold, or copper that conducts heat and electricity.
✏️ 例句
Rare metals are increasingly important for manufacturing electronic devices.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
heavy metal（重金属）；metal detector（金属探测器）；metallic adj. 金属的；考研科技类文章常见
🧠 助记
metal → 'me'+ tal（硬）→ 硬的材料 → 金属
metal::/ˈmetl/:n. 金属 adj. 金属的:Recycling metal reduces the need for new mining operations.
11. nearby /ˌnɪərˈbaɪ/ adj./adv. 附近的；在附近
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

near + by → 近旁边 → 附近的
nearby::/ˌnɪərˈbaɪ/:adj./adv. 附近的:A nearby university provides internship opportunities for students.
12. exciting /ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ/ adj. 令人兴奋的；激动人心的
📖 英文解释
Causing great enthusiasm and eagerness; thrilling or stimulating.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
exciting news/opportunity（令人兴奋的消息/机会）；注意区分 exciting（物令人兴奋）vs excited（人感到兴奋）
🧠 助记
excit(e) + ing → 令人 excite（兴奋）的事 → 令人兴奋的
exciting::/ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ/:adj. 令人兴奋的:New breakthroughs in AI are exciting yet raise ethical questions.
13. freeze /friːz/ v./n. 冻结；冰冻；停顿；结冰
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
freeze → fr + eeze（联想冷风嗖嗖）→ 冷到结冰
freeze::/friːz/:v./n. 冻结:The central bank froze interest rates at the current level.
14. lake /leɪk/ n. 湖；湖泊

lake::/leɪk/:n. 湖:Acid rain has severely damaged many mountain lakes.
15. flat /flæt/ adj. 平坦的；扁的 n. 公寓 adv. 平地

flat::/flæt/:adj. 平坦的:Economic growth has been flat despite government stimulus measures.
16. lend /lend/ v. 借出；提供；赋予

lend → l + end → 把东西放到（l）别人那端（end）→ 借出
lend::/lend/:v. 借出:International organizations lend expertise to developing nations.

consult::/kənˈsʌlt/:v. 咨询:Companies consult external experts to improve their strategies.
18. interrupt /ˌɪntəˈrʌpt/ v. 打断；中断；妨碍

interrupt::/ˌɪntəˈrʌpt/:v. 打断:Technical failures interrupted the live broadcast unexpectedly.

dictation::/dɪkˈteɪʃn/:n. 听写:The dictation of terms by powerful nations is increasingly resisted.

shall在法律文件中表义务（= must）；Shall I...?（我来……好吗？）；考研法律类文章常见

shall::/ʃæl/:modal v. 将要（表将来、义务或建:All parties shall adhere to the terms of the agreement.
