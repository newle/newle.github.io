1. square /skweər/ n. 正方形；广场 adj. 正方的；公平的 v. 使成直角
📖 英文解释
A plane figure with four equal sides and four right angles; a large open area in a town; fair and honest.
✏️ 例句
The protesters gathered in the main square to demand political reform.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
square meal（丰盛的一餐）；square up（结清债务）；Times Square（时代广场）；back to square one（从头再来）
🧠 助记
square → sq（四角）→ 四角等长的正方形，也联想公平（不偏不倚）
square::/skweər/:n. 正方形:The deal seemed square and fair to both parties involved.
2. defense /dɪˈfens/ n. 防卫；防御；辩护
📖 英文解释
The action of defending from attack; arguments used to support or justify something.
✏️ 例句
The country invested heavily in its national defense capabilities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in defense of（为……辩护）；self-defense（自卫）；defense budget（国防预算）；defense lawyer（辩护律师）；考研政治、法律类文章高频词
🧠 助记
de-fense → de（抵住）+ fense（围栏）→ 用围栏抵挡 → 防御
defense::/dɪˈfens/:n. 防卫:A strong legal defense is essential for a fair justice system.
3. facilitate /fəˈsɪlɪteɪt/ v. 促进；使便利；协助
📖 英文解释
To make an action or process easy or easier; to help bring about a result.
✏️ 例句
Technology facilitates communication between people across the globe.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
facilitate dialogue/cooperation（促进对话/合作）；facilitator n. 促进者；facility n. 设施；考研写作核心动词
🧠 助记
facil-itate → facile（容易的）→ 让……变容易 → 促进、便利
facilitate::/fəˈsɪlɪteɪt/:v. 促进:Bilateral agreements facilitate trade and economic cooperation.
4. soft /sɒft/ adj. 软的；柔和的；温和的；宽松的
📖 英文解释
Easy to mold or compress; not harsh; gentle in sound or light; lacking firmness.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
soft power（软实力）；soft skills（软技能）；software（软件）；soft drink（软饮料）；考研政治类文章常见
🧠 助记
soft → s + oft（经常柔软）→ 经常柔软的 → 软的
soft::/sɒft/:adj. 软的:Soft power is increasingly important in international diplomacy.
5. combination /ˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃn/ n. 结合；组合；联合

🧠 助记

combination::/ˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃn/:n. 结合:A combination of policies is needed to address housing affordability.
6. shake /ʃeɪk/ v. 摇；震动；握（手）；动摇 n. 摇动

shake::/ʃeɪk/:v. 摇:The revelation shook the foundations of the organization.
