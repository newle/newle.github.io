1. edition /ɪˈdɪʃ(ə)n/ n. 版本；版次
📖 英文解释
A particular version of a book, magazine, or newspaper that is printed at one time; a specific version of a broadcast program.
✏️ 例句
The latest edition of the dictionary includes many new words.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：new/revised/updated edition（新版／修订版）；first edition（第一版）；edition of a newspaper（报纸的某期）
🧠 助记
edit（编辑）+ ion（名词后缀）→ 经过编辑后出来的产品 → 版本
edition::/ɪˈdɪʃ(ə)n/:n. 版本:the third edition of the textbook
2. induce /ɪnˈdjuːs/ v. 引诱；引起；诱导
📖 英文解释
To succeed in persuading or influencing someone to do something; to cause or produce a particular effect or condition.
✏️ 例句
Nothing could induce me to take that risk.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：induce sb. to do sth.（诱使某人做某事）；induce sleep/labor（诱发睡眠／分娩）；drug-induced（药物引起的）
🧠 助记
in（进入）+ duce（引导，如 duke 公爵带领人）→ 引导进入 → 引诱、引起
induce::/ɪnˈdjuːs/:v. 引诱:stress can induce serious health problems
3. portion /ˈpɔːʃ(ə)n/ n. 部分；一份；命运
📖 英文解释
A part or share of a whole; a quantity of food suitable for one person.
✏️ 例句
A large portion of the budget is spent on education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：a large/small portion of（……的很大／小部分）；portion out（分配）；portion of food（一份食物）
🧠 助记
port（带走）+ ion → 从整体带走的那一部分 → 部分、一份
portion::/ˈpɔːʃ(ə)n/:n. 部分:a significant portion of the population

classmate::/ˈklɑːsmeɪt/:n. 同班同学:reconnect with old classmates at a reunion

soccer::/ˈsɒkər/:n. 足球（美式称法）:soccer tournaments attract global audiences
