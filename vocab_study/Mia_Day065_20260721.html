1. prosperity /prɒˈsperɪtɪ/ n. 繁荣；兴旺；昌盛
📖 英文解释
The state of being successful, especially financially; economic well-being.
✏️ 例句
The country enjoyed a period of great prosperity after the war.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：economic prosperity（经济繁荣）；prosperity and stability（繁荣稳定）；prosper v.；prosperous adj.
🧠 助记
prosper（兴旺）+ ity → 兴旺的状态 → 繁荣
prosperity::/prɒˈsperɪtɪ/:n. 繁荣:shared prosperity requires equitable distribution of wealth
2. badly /ˈbædlɪ/ adv. 糟糕地；严重地；非常（想要）
📖 英文解释
In an unsatisfactory or inadequate way; to a great or serious degree; very much.
✏️ 例句
The building was badly damaged by the earthquake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：badly needed（急需）；badly hurt/damaged（严重受伤/损坏）；badly off（经济拮据）；want sth. badly（非常想要）
🧠 助记
bad（糟糕）+ ly → 糟糕地；also intensifier：非常地
badly::/ˈbædlɪ/:adv. 糟糕地:rural areas are badly in need of infrastructure investment
3. diminish /dɪˈmɪnɪʃ/ v. 削弱；减少；贬低
📖 英文解释
To make or become less; to reduce the authority, dignity, or reputation of someone.
✏️ 例句
The scandal diminished his reputation significantly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：diminish in importance（降低重要性）；diminish the value（降低价值）；diminishing returns（收益递减）；diminution n.
🧠 助记
di（分）+ min（小）+ ish → 使变小 → 削弱、减少
diminish::/dɪˈmɪnɪʃ/:v. 削弱:automation may diminish the demand for low-skilled labor

考研常考搭配：imitate the style of（模仿……的风格）；imitation n.；imitative adj.；not to be imitated（不可效仿）
🧠 助记
im（进入）+ itate（如 iterate 重复）→ 进入别人的方式重复 → 模仿
imitate::/ˈɪmɪteɪt/:v. 模仿:developing countries imitate economic models of advanced nations
