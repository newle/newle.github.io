1. storage /ˈstɔːrɪdʒ/ n. 储存；仓库
📖 英文解释
the process of keeping things in a special place until they are needed; a place where things are kept
✏️ 例句
The warehouse provides cold storage for perishable goods.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
storage space/capacity/facility; in storage（储存中）; storage of data（数据存储）
🧠 助记
store（存储）+ age → 储存的行为或地方
storage::/ˈstɔːrɪdʒ/:n. 储存:Food storage must be kept at the right temperature.
2. commodity /kəˈmɒdɪti/ n. 商品；货物；有价值之物
📖 英文解释
a product or raw material that can be bought and sold, especially between countries
✏️ 例句
Oil is one of the world's most important commodities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
commodity market/price; agricultural/basic commodity; treat sth as a commodity（把…当商品）
🧠 助记
commod（适宜）+ ity → 适合交易之物 → 商品
commodity::/kəˈmɒdɪti/:n. 商品:Gold is traded as a commodity on world markets.
3. negotiate /nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt/ v. 谈判；协商；成功越过
📖 英文解释
to try to reach an agreement through discussion; to successfully deal with a difficult situation
✏️ 例句
The two sides are negotiating a peace deal.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
negotiate with sb; negotiate a contract/deal/settlement; negotiate one's way through（设法通过）
🧠 助记
neg（否定）+ oti（otium 闲暇）+ ate → 没有闲暇，努力讨价还价 → 谈判
negotiate::/nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt/:v. 谈判:They negotiated a ceasefire after weeks of talks.
4. compromise /ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/ n./v. 妥协；折中；损害
📖 英文解释
an agreement reached by both sides making concessions; to weaken or damage something
✏️ 例句
We reached a compromise after hours of discussion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reach/make a compromise; compromise on sth; compromise one's principles（损害原则）
🧠 助记
com（共同）+ promise（承诺）→ 双方共同承诺让步 → 妥协
compromise::/ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/:n./v. 妥协:Neither side was willing to compromise on the key issues.
5. endure /ɪnˈdjʊə/ v. 忍受；持续；经受住
📖 英文解释
to suffer something difficult or unpleasant patiently; to continue to exist for a long time
✏️ 例句
She endured years of hardship without complaint.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
endure pain/hardship/suffering; endure for centuries（持续几个世纪）
🧠 助记
en（使）+ dure（持久，如during）→ 使之持续 → 忍耐并持续
endure::/ɪnˈdjʊə/:v. 忍受:The ancient ruins have endured for thousands of years.
6. immune /ɪˈmjuːn/ adj. 免疫的；不受影响的
📖 英文解释
protected against a disease; not affected by something unpleasant
✏️ 例句
Once vaccinated, you become immune to the virus.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
immune to disease/criticism; immune system（免疫系统）; immune response（免疫反应）
🧠 助记
im（不）+ mune（munus 义务）→ 不受义务约束 → 免除，免疫
immune::/ɪˈmjuːn/:adj. 免疫的:No one is immune to the effects of stress.
7. pleasant /ˈpleznt/ adj. 令人愉快的；和蔼的
📖 英文解释
giving a feeling of enjoyment or happiness; having a friendly and likeable manner
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pleasant weather/surprise/manner; make oneself pleasant（讨人喜欢）
🧠 助记
please（取悦）+ ant → 能取悦人的 → 令人愉快的
pleasant::/ˈpleznt/:adj. 令人愉快的:The hotel staff were very pleasant and helpful.
8. celebrate /ˈselɪbreɪt/ v. 庆祝；颂扬；赞美
📖 英文解释
to do something special on an important occasion; to praise or honor someone publicly
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
celebrate an anniversary/victory/achievement; celebrate with friends
🧠 助记
celebr（著名，如celebrity）+ ate → 让人出名（庆祝）→ 庆祝
celebrate::/ˈselɪbreɪt/:v. 庆祝:Thousands gathered to celebrate the national holiday.
9. command /kəˈmɑːnd/ n./v. 命令；指挥；控制；俯瞰
📖 英文解释
an order given with authority; to be in charge of; to deserve and get something
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
under the command of; in command of; command respect/attention（博得尊重/关注）
🧠 助记
com（共同）+ mand（命令，如mandate）→ 共同遵守的命令
command::/kəˈmɑːnd/:n./v. 命令:She commands great respect in her field.
10. span /spæn/ n. 跨度；一段时间 v. 跨越
📖 英文解释
a length of time or space from one end to another; to extend across a period or area
✏️ 例句
The bridge spans the entire river.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a span of time; life span（寿命）; span the globe/centuries（跨越全球/数世纪）
🧠 助记
联想 expand（扩展）去掉ex → 跨越一段距离或时间
span::/spæn/:n. 跨度:Her career spanned four decades of achievement.
11. norm /nɔːm/ n. 规范；标准；常态
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
norm 联想 normal（正常的）→ 被认为正常的标准 → 规范
norm::/nɔːm/:n. 规范:Working from home has become the norm for many people.
12. hazard /ˈhæzəd/ n. 危险；危害 v. 冒险提出

hazard::/ˈhæzəd/:n. 危险:Ice on the road is a hazard for drivers.
13. joint /dʒɔɪnt/ adj. 联合的 n. 关节；接合处

🧠 助记

joint::/dʒɔɪnt/:adj. 联合的 n. 关节:The two companies launched a joint research project.
