1. passion /ˈpæʃn/ n. 激情；热情；热爱
📖 英文解释
a very strong feeling of love, enthusiasm, or other emotion; a very strong interest in something
✏️ 例句
She has a passion for classical music.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
passion for sth; with passion; passionate about（热衷于）
🧠 助记
pass（通过）+ ion → 情绪涌过全身 → 激情
passion::/ˈpæʃn/:n. 激情:His passion for teaching inspired generations of students.
2. settlement /ˈsetlmənt/ n. 定居；解决；协议；结算
📖 英文解释
an official agreement that ends a dispute; a place where people have established a community
✏️ 例句
The two parties reached a settlement out of court.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reach a settlement; out-of-court settlement（庭外和解）; early settlement（提前还款）
🧠 助记
settle（安定）+ ment → 安定下来的地方或解决方法
settlement::/ˈsetlmənt/:n. 定居:A financial settlement was agreed upon by both parties.
3. sudden /ˈsʌdn/ adj. 突然的；意外的
📖 英文解释
happening or done quickly and unexpectedly
✏️ 例句
There was a sudden change in the weather.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
all of a sudden（突然）; sudden death（突然死亡；加时决胜）; sudden increase
🧠 助记
联想 thunder（雷声）→ 突如其来的雷声 → 突然的
sudden::/ˈsʌdn/:adj. 突然的:All of a sudden, the lights went out and the room fell silent.
4. biology /baɪˈɒlədʒi/ n. 生物学；生物特性
📖 英文解释
the scientific study of the natural processes of living things
✏️ 例句
She studied biology at university.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
molecular biology（分子生物学）; biology class; human biology（人类生物学）
🧠 助记
bio（生命）+ logy（学科）→ 研究生命的学科 → 生物学
biology::/baɪˈɒlədʒi/:n. 生物学:Advances in biology have led to new treatments for diseases.
5. parliament /ˈpɑːləmənt/ n. 议会；国会
📖 英文解释
the group of people who are elected to make laws in a country
✏️ 例句
The bill was passed by parliament last week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
member of parliament（国会议员）; in parliament; dissolve parliament（解散议会）
🧠 助记
parle（说话，法语）+ ment → 讨论说话的地方 → 议会
parliament::/ˈpɑːləmənt/:n. 议会:The new law was debated and approved by parliament.
6. map /mæp/ n. 地图 v. 绘制地图；规划
📖 英文解释
a drawing of an area showing roads, rivers, and other features; to plan or chart something
✏️ 例句
She unfolded the map to find the route.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
road/map; map out（规划）; put sth on the map（使出名）
🧠 助记
直接记忆，map = 地图；联想 plan 一样，map out 规划
map::/mæp/:n. 地图 v. 绘制地图:They mapped out a detailed plan for the product launch.
7. penalty /ˈpenəlti/ n. 罚款；处罚；点球
📖 英文解释
a punishment given for breaking a law or rule; a free kick at goal in football
✏️ 例句
The company faced a heavy penalty for the violation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
death penalty（死刑）; penalty clause（罚款条款）; penalty kick（点球）
🧠 助记
pen（惩罚，如penal）+ alty → 惩罚的结果 → 罚款、处罚
penalty::/ˈpenəlti/:n. 罚款:There is a penalty for late submission of tax returns.
8. tooth /tuːθ/ n. 牙齿；齿状物
📖 英文解释
one of the hard, white structures in the mouth used for biting and chewing; a pointed part
✏️ 例句
She brushed her teeth twice a day.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tooth and nail（拼命地）; sweet tooth（爱吃甜食）; toothache（牙痛）
🧠 助记
直接记忆，tooth = 牙齿，复数 teeth
tooth::/tuːθ/:n. 牙齿:He fought tooth and nail to defend his position.
9. yesterday /ˈjestədeɪ/ n./adv. 昨天；过去
📖 英文解释
the day before today; used to refer to the recent past
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
yesterday morning/evening; the day before yesterday（前天）; yesterday's news（过时的事）
🧠 助记
yester（昨，古英语）+ day → 昨天
yesterday::/ˈjestədeɪ/:n./adv. 昨天:Yesterday's mistakes should teach us lessons for tomorrow.
10. distract /dɪˈstrækt/ v. 转移注意力；使分心
📖 英文解释
to take someone's attention away from what they are doing
✏️ 例句
Loud music distracts me when I'm studying.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
distract sb from sth; distract one's attention（转移注意力）
🧠 助记
dis（分开）+ tract（拉）→ 把注意力拉开 → 使分心
distract::/dɪˈstrækt/:v. 转移注意力:Don't let minor issues distract you from your main goal.
11. enemy /ˈenəmi/ n. 敌人；反对者；天敌
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

enemy::/ˈenəmi/:n. 敌人:Complacency is the enemy of continuous improvement.
12. abstract /ˈæbstrækt/ adj. 抽象的 n. 摘要 v. 摘录
📖 英文解释
existing as an idea without physical existence; a short summary of a text
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
abs（离开）+ tract（拉）→ 脱离具体事物 → 抽象的
abstract::/ˈæbstrækt/:adj. 抽象的 n. 摘要 v. 摘录:The paper began with a 200-word abstract summarizing the findings.
13. automobile /ˈɔːtəməbiːl/ n. 汽车
