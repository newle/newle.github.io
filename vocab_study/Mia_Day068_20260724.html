1. cloud /klaʊd/ n. 云；阴影 v. 使模糊；使忧郁
📖 英文解释
a white or grey mass of water droplets in the sky; something that causes worry or sadness
✏️ 例句
Dark clouds gathered before the storm.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cloud computing（云计算）; cloud over（变阴暗）; every cloud has a silver lining（苦难之中有希望）
🧠 助记
直接记忆：cloud = 云，联想 cloudy（多云的）
cloud::/klaʊd/:n. 云:Cloud computing has transformed how businesses store and access data.
2. interfere /ˌɪntəˈfɪə/ v. 干涉；妨碍；干扰
📖 英文解释
to become involved in a situation that is not your concern; to prevent something from happening
✏️ 例句
Please don't interfere in matters that don't concern you.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
interfere with（妨碍）; interfere in（干涉）; interference（干涉，干扰）
🧠 助记
inter（之间）+ fere（打，如warfare）→ 插手打入中间 → 干涉
interfere::/ˌɪntəˈfɪə/:v. 干涉:Emotional stress can interfere with one's ability to concentrate.
3. poet /ˈpəʊɪt/ n. 诗人
📖 英文解释
a person who writes poems
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
romantic/lyric poet; poet laureate（桂冠诗人）
🧠 助记
poem（诗）→ poet = 写诗的人
poet::/ˈpəʊɪt/:n. 诗人:The poet's words captured the beauty of nature in vivid images.
4. soul /səʊl/ n. 灵魂；人；精神实质
📖 英文解释
the spiritual part of a person; a person's moral or emotional nature; a person
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
soul music（灵魂乐）; bare one's soul（敞开心扉）; not a soul（没有人）
🧠 助记
联想 sole（唯一的）→ 每个人唯一的灵魂
soul::/səʊl/:n. 灵魂:The jazz festival had a soul that drew musicians from around the world.
5. alive /əˈlaɪv/ adj. 活着的；充满活力的；意识到的
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

alive::/əˈlaɪv/:adj. 活着的:The city comes alive with music and colour during the festival.
6. exert /ɪɡˈzɜːt/ v. 施加；运用；努力
📖 英文解释
to use power, influence, or a quality; to make a great effort

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

exert::/ɪɡˈzɜːt/:v. 施加:Gravity exerts a powerful force on all objects on Earth.

pioneer::/ˌpaɪəˈnɪə/:n. 先驱:The company pioneered the use of artificial intelligence in customer service.

alike::/əˈlaɪk/:adj./adv. 相似的:The policy applies to citizens and foreigners alike.
