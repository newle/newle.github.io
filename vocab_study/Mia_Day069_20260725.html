1. platform /ˈplætfɔːrm/ n. 平台；站台；讲台
📖 英文解释
a raised level surface on which people or things can stand; a computing or digital environment
✏️ 例句
The company launched its new digital platform for online learning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
platform for sth（某事的平台）；digital/online platform（数字平台）；launch a platform
🧠 助记
plat（平的）+ form（形式）→ 平的形式 → 平台
platform::/ˈplætfɔːrm/:n. 平台:a platform for change
2. enforce /ɪnˈfɔːrs/ v. 强迫执行；实施（法律）
📖 英文解释
to make people obey a rule or law by punishing those who do not
✏️ 例句
The police are responsible for enforcing the law fairly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
enforce a law/rule（执法）；enforce compliance（强制遵守）；strictly enforced
🧠 助记
en-（使）+ force（力量）→ 用力使之执行
enforce::/ɪnˈfɔːrs/:v. 强迫执行:enforce the regulations
3. battery /ˈbætəri/ n. 电池；一系列；炮兵连
📖 英文解释
a device that produces electric current; a number of similar things used together
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
battery life（电池寿命）；a battery of tests（一系列测试）；rechargeable battery
🧠 助记
bat（击打）+ tery → 一排炮 → 电池组
battery::/ˈbætəri/:n. 电池:battery life of 10 hours
4. convenience /kənˈviːniəns/ n. 便利；方便设施
📖 英文解释
the quality of being easy to use or suitable for one's needs
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
con + veni（来）+ ence → 随时可以来 → 方便
convenience::/kənˈviːniəns/:n. 便利:at your convenience

silicon::/ˈsɪlɪkən/:n. 硅（元素）:silicon semiconductor chip

impress::/ɪmˈpres/:v. 给…留下印象:impress the judges with her skills
