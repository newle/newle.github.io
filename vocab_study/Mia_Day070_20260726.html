1. nutrition /njuːˈtrɪʃn/ n. 营养；营养学
📖 英文解释
the process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth
✏️ 例句
Good nutrition is essential for children's physical and mental development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nutrition label（营养标签）；nutrition science（营养学）；poor nutrition（营养不良）
🧠 助记
nutri（滋养）+ tion → 滋养身体 → 营养
nutrition::/njuːˈtrɪʃn/:n. 营养:balanced nutrition for growth

suburb::/ˈsʌbɜːrb/:n. 郊区:live in a quiet suburb
