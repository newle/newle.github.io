1. ear /ɪər/ n. 耳朵；听觉；穗
📖 英文解释
The organ of hearing in humans and other vertebrates; also refers to the ability to distinguish sounds.
✏️ 例句
She has a good ear for music and can identify any melody after hearing it once.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ have an ear for sth 对某事有天赋/敏感 ★ play by ear 凭耳朵演奏；随机应变 ☆ turn a deaf ear to 对…充耳不闻 辨析：ear 指器官或听觉能力；hearing 指听力或听证会
🧠 助记
ear = 耳——形似耳朵的英文单词，e是耳廓，ar是耳道
ear::/ɪər/:n. 耳朵:She has a sharp ear for foreign languages.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in session 开会中，开庭中 ★ a session of 一届/一期… ☆ parliamentary session 议会会期 辨析：session 强调一段专门时间；meeting 泛指会议；term 指学期
🧠 助记
session = sess(坐)+ion——大家坐在一起开会的时段
session::/ˈseʃn/:n. 会议:The parliamentary session opened with a debate on tax reform.

con(一起)+fuse(融合)+ion——很多东西混在一起=混乱/困惑
