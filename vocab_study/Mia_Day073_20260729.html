1. enthusiasm /ɪnˈθjuːziæzəm/ n. 热情；热忱；积极性

enthusiasm::/ɪnˈθjuːziæzəm/:n. 热情:His enthusiasm for the project motivated the entire team.

29. decorate /ˈdekəreɪt/ v. 装饰；布置；授勋

To make something look more attractive by adding ornamental features; to give a medal to.
✏️ 例句
The community decorated the town hall with banners and flowers for the festival.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ decorate a room/cake 装饰房间/蛋糕 ★ decorate sb for bravery 因勇敢授勋 ☆ decoration n. 装饰；decorative adj. 装饰性的 辨析：decorate 强调美化；adorn 较文学化；ornament 强调添加装饰品
🧠 助记
decor(装饰)+ate——进行装饰的行为=装饰
decorate::/ˈdekəreɪt/:v. 装饰:Students decorated the classroom with colorful artwork for the open day.
30. rough /rʌf/ adj. 粗糙的；粗暴的；艰难的；粗略的；adv. 粗暴地
📖 英文解释
Having an uneven or irregular surface; difficult and unpleasant; not exact.
✏️ 例句
The report provides a rough estimate of costs, which will be refined later.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ rough estimate/idea 粗略估计/大致想法 ★ rough conditions 艰苦条件 ☆ rough draft 草稿；rough and ready 粗糙但有效的 辨析：rough 多义；coarse 强调质地粗糙；tough 强调坚硬/困难
🧠 助记
rough = r+ough(嗷)——摸起来让人说嗷的感觉=粗糙的
rough::/rʌf/:adj. 粗糙的:She made a rough sketch of the building's layout before starting the design.
