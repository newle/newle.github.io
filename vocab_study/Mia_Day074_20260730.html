1. scope /skəʊp/ n. 范围；余地；机会；v. 审视
📖 英文解释
The extent of the area or subject matter that something deals with; the opportunity to do something.
✏️ 例句
The scope of the research project was broadened to include environmental and social factors.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ within/beyond the scope of 在…范围之内/之外 ★ scope for improvement 改进的余地 ☆ out of scope 超出范围；broad scope 广泛范围 辨析：scope 强调覆盖范围；range 强调变化幅度；extent 强调程度大小
🧠 助记
scope = s+cope(应对)——能够应对的范围=范围
scope::/skəʊp/:n. 范围:The new policy expanded the scope of environmental protection measures.
