1. provision /prəˈvɪʒən/ n. 提供、供给；规定；准备
📖 英文解释
the act of providing something; a condition or requirement in a legal document; supplies of food and other necessities
✏️ 例句
The government made special provision for the homeless during winter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make provision for（为…做准备）；provision of services（服务提供）；under the provisions of（依据…规定）
🧠 助记
pro（前）+ vis（看）+ ion → 预先看好的东西 → 提前准备/供给
provision::/prəˈvɪʒən/:n. 提供、供给:The law includes a provision that protects workers' rights.
2. temper /ˈtempər/ n. 脾气；情绪 v. 缓和；调节
📖 英文解释
a person's state of mind in terms of calmness or irritability; to moderate or soften something
✏️ 例句
He lost his temper when the project was delayed again.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lose one's temper（发脾气）；keep one's temper（控制脾气）；temper justice with mercy（以仁慈调和正义）
🧠 助记
temp（温度）+ er → 像温度一样忽高忽低的情绪 → 脾气
temper::/ˈtempər/:n. 脾气:She has a calm temper and rarely gets angry.
3. exhaust /ɪɡˈzɔːst/ v. 耗尽；使筋疲力尽 n. 废气
📖 英文解释
to use up completely; to tire out completely; the waste gases from an engine
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
exhaust one's resources（耗尽资源）；exhaust pipe（排气管）；be exhausted（精疲力竭）
🧠 助记
ex（出）+ haust（抽）→ 把里面的东西全抽出来 → 耗尽
exhaust::/ɪɡˈzɔːst/:v. 耗尽:Running the marathon exhausted her completely.
4. holder /ˈhoʊldər/ n. 持有者；保持架

🧠 助记
hold（持有）+ er（人）→ 持有某物的人 → 持有者
holder::/ˈhoʊldər/:n. 持有者:She is the holder of three world records.
5. linguistic /lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk/ adj. 语言的；语言学的

🧠 助记

linguistic::/lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk/:adj. 语言的:The study revealed significant linguistic differences between the two groups.
6. mine /maɪn/ pron. 我的 n. 矿；地雷 v. 开采
📖 英文解释
belonging to me; an excavation for extracting minerals; to extract minerals from the earth
✏️ 例句

mine::/maɪn/:pron. 我的 n. 矿:This information is a gold mine for researchers.
7. servant /ˈsɜːrvənt/ n. 仆人；公务员；服务者

reflection::/rɪˈflekʃən/:n. 反射:After careful reflection, he decided to change careers.

bread::/bred/:n. 面包:Teaching is his bread and butter.
