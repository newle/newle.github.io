1. heritage /ˈherɪtɪdʒ/ n. 遗产；文化遗产；传统
📖 英文解释
Something passed down from previous generations; cultural or historical legacy of a society.
✏️ 例句
The Great Wall is an important part of China's cultural heritage that attracts millions of tourists.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ cultural heritage 文化遗产（高频搭配） ★ natural heritage 自然遗产 ★ world heritage site 世界遗产地 辨析：heritage / legacy：heritage 侧重文化/历史传承；legacy 侧重某人留下的精神财富或遗产
🧠 助记
heritage = her + it + age——她(her)随着年龄(age)传下去的东西，就是遗产
heritage::/ˈherɪtɪdʒ/:n. 遗产:We must protect our cultural heritage.

🧠 助记

30. thick /θɪk/ adj. 厚的；浓密的；浓重的；adv. 厚厚地
📖 英文解释
Having a large distance between opposite surfaces; dense or heavy; (of a person) stupid.
✏️ 例句
A thick layer of smog hung over the city, reducing visibility and causing respiratory problems.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ thick fog / smoke / forest 浓雾/浓烟/密林 ★ thick accent 浓重的口音 ★ thick and fast 接连不断地 辨析：thick / dense：thick 描述物体本身厚实；dense 强调密度高（人群、森林等）
🧠 助记
thick 谐音「是厚」——是厚的那一面，就是thick
thick::/θɪk/:adj. 厚的:The book has a thick cover and many pages.
