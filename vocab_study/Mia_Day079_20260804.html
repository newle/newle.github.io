1. enrich /ɪnˈrɪtʃ/ v. 丰富；充实；使富裕
📖 英文解释
To improve the quality or value of something; to make someone wealthier.
✏️ 例句
Studying abroad can greatly enrich your understanding of different cultures and perspectives.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ enrich one's life / experience 丰富生活/经历（写作高频） ★ enrich the soil 使土壤肥沃 ★ enrichment n. 丰富；充实 辨析：enrich / enhance：enrich 侧重增添内容使更丰富；enhance 侧重提升质量/价值
🧠 助记
en（使）+ rich（富有）——使变得富有，就是丰富/充实
enrich::/ɪnˈrɪtʃ/:v. 丰富:Travel can enrich your life and broaden your horizons.

🧠 助记
guy 谐音「该」——该来的那个人，就是那家伙
guy::/ɡaɪ/:n. 家伙:He seems like a really nice guy.

verbal::/ˈvɜːbl/:adj. 口头的:She gave her verbal agreement to the proposal.

🧠 助记

behalf::/bɪˈhɑːf/:n. 代表:I am writing on behalf of our entire team.
