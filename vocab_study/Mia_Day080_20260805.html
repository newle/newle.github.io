1. fate /feɪt/ n. 命运；天数；结局；v. 注定
📖 英文解释
The development of events beyond a person's control; the ultimate outcome for someone or something.
✏️ 例句
The fate of the entire ecosystem depends on whether governments take decisive action to reduce emissions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ leave sth to fate 听天由命 ★ seal sb's fate 决定某人的命运（写作常用） ★ a fate worse than death 极其悲惨的命运 ★ fatal adj. 致命的；fatally adv.
🧠 助记
fate 谐音「发它」——发给你的那个结局，就是命运
fate::/feɪt/:n. 命运:The fate of the project depends on funding.

📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ have a chat with sb 与某人聊天 ★ chat show 脱口秀（英式）= talk show（美式） ★ online chat 网聊 ★ chatbot 聊天机器人（科技阅读新词）
🧠 助记
chat 谐音「扯它」——扯着话头聊下去，就是聊天
chat::/tʃæt/:v./n. 聊天:They had a friendly chat over coffee.
