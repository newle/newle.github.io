1. elevate /ˈelɪveɪt/ v. 提升，举起；提高（地位、水平）
📖 英文解释
To raise something or someone to a higher position or level; to improve status or quality.
✏️ 例句
Regular exercise can elevate your mood and reduce stress significantly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ elevate sb/sth to sth 将某人/物提升至某地位 ★ elevated 作形容词：elevated status / elevated blood pressure ☆ elevate the quality of 提升…的质量（写作常用） 辨析：elevate / raise / lift：elevate 偏正式，常用于抽象意义；raise 通用；lift 强调物理动作
🧠 助记
ele(电梯elevator的前缀)+vate → 坐电梯往上升 → 提升
elevate::/ˈelɪveɪt/:v. 提升，举起:Education can elevate one's social status.
2. plunge /plʌndʒ/ v./n. 猛冲，骤降；投入；跳入
📖 英文解释
To move suddenly downward or forward; a sudden sharp drop or fall.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ plunge into sth 突然投入，陷入 ★ take the plunge 决然行动，冒险一试 ☆ prices/temperatures plunge 价格/气温骤降（常见搭配） 辨析：plunge / drop / fall：plunge 强调急剧性和突然性，语气最重
🧠 助记
plunge ≈ 扑(pl)+洶涌(unge) → 扑通跳进水里 → 骤降/猛冲
plunge::/plʌndʒ/:v./n. 猛冲，骤降:The temperature plunged to -10°C overnight.
