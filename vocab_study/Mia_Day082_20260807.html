27. bid /bɪd/ v./n. 出价，投标；努力争取；邀请
📖 英文解释
To offer a price for something; an attempt to achieve something; an offer of a price.
✏️ 例句
Several cities submitted bids to host the Olympic Games.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ bid for sth 竞标/争取某物 ★ a bid to do sth 试图做某事 ★ bid farewell 告别（正式） ☆ competitive bidding 竞争性投标 辨析：bid / offer：bid 侧重竞价或争取；offer 更通用
🧠 助记
bid → b+id → 想"buy it"→ 出价竞标
bid::/bɪd/:v./n. 出价，投标:The company made a successful bid for the government contract.
28. rude /ruːd/ adj. 粗鲁的，无礼的；突然的，激烈的
📖 英文解释
Offensively impolite or ill-mannered; sudden and unpleasant.
✏️ 例句
It is rude to interrupt others while they are speaking.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ be rude to sb 对某人无礼 ★ a rude awakening 突然的打击/领悟 ★ rudeness n. 粗鲁；rudely adv. ☆ rude shock 意外冲击（a rude shock to sb） 辨析：rude / impolite / offensive：rude 最口语常用；impolite 正式；offensive 有冒犯性
🧠 助记
rude → r+ude → 想到"crude"(粗糙) → 粗鲁的
rude::/ruːd/:adj. 粗鲁的，无礼的:It is rude to talk with your mouth full.
29. criterion /kraɪˈtɪəriən/ n. 标准，准则（单数）；criteria 为复数
📖 英文解释
A principle or standard by which something may be judged or decided.
✏️ 例句
Academic performance is just one criterion for university admission.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ criteria for sth 某事的评判标准 ★ meet/satisfy the criteria 满足标准 ★ evaluation criteria 评估标准 ☆ 注意：criterion 单数，criteria 复数，不可混用 辨析：criterion / standard：criterion 偏判断性标准；standard 偏质量水准
🧠 助记
crit(评判)+erion → 评判的依据 → 标准
criterion::/kraɪˈtɪəriən/:n. 标准，准则（单数）:Safety is the primary criterion for the project's approval.
30. gradual /ˈɡrædʒuəl/ adj. 逐渐的，渐进的
📖 英文解释
Taking place or progressing slowly or by degrees.
✏️ 例句
The company saw a gradual improvement in productivity after the reforms.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ gradual change/process/improvement 渐进的变化/过程/改善 ★ gradually adv. 逐渐地（作文高频副词） ☆ 辨析：gradual / progressive：gradual 侧重缓慢；progressive 有进步/逐步发展含义
🧠 助记
grad(步骤)+ual → 一步一步(grade by grade) → 逐渐的
gradual::/ˈɡrædʒuəl/:adj. 逐渐的，渐进的:There has been a gradual shift towards renewable energy.
