1. objection /əbˈdʒekʃn/ n. 反对，异议；理由
📖 英文解释
An expression of opposition or disapproval; a reason for disagreeing.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ raise / voice / express an objection 提出反对意见 ★ objection to sth 对某事的反对 ★ have no objection to doing 不反对做… ☆ overrule an objection 驳回异议（法律） 辨析：objection / opposition / protest：objection 较温和，多为正式表达；opposition 更广泛；protest 更强烈
🧠 助记
ob(反对)+ject(扔)+ion → 将议题扔回去 → 反对，异议
objection::/əbˈdʒekʃn/:n. 反对，异议:She raised no objection to the new regulations.
