1. salesman /ˈseɪlzmən/ n. 推销员
📖 英文解释
A man whose job is to sell goods or services.
✏️ 例句
The salesman demonstrated the product features to potential buyers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ salesman / saleswoman / salesperson(通用) ★ door-to-door salesman 上门推销员 ☆ 考研常在商业语境中出现
🧠 助记
sales(销售) + man(人) = 做销售的人
salesman::/ˈseɪlzmən/:n. 推销员:The salesman persuaded me to buy the new model.
2. summary /ˈsʌməri/ n. 摘要，总结；adj. 概括的
📖 英文解释
A brief statement of the main points of something.
✏️ 例句
Please write a summary of the article in no more than 200 words.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in summary 总之，综上所述(写作高频) ★ a summary of sth 某事的摘要 ☆ executive summary 执行摘要
🧠 助记
sum(合计) + mary = 把所有内容合在一起的报告 = 摘要
summary::/ˈsʌməri/:n. 摘要，总结:In summary, the policy has achieved its main objectives.
3. monopoly /məˈnɒpəli/ n. 垄断；专有权
📖 英文解释
Exclusive control of a commodity or service in a particular market.
✏️ 例句
The government broke up the company's monopoly over the telecommunications sector.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ have/hold a monopoly on sth 垄断某物 ★ monopoly capitalism 垄断资本主义 ☆ mono-(单一) + poly(卖) = 一家独大
🧠 助记
mono(单) + poly(卖) = 只有一家卖 = 垄断
monopoly::/məˈnɒpəli/:n. 垄断:The company held a monopoly on oil production for decades.
4. dose /dəʊs/ n. 剂量；一剂；v. 给…服药
📖 英文解释
A measured quantity of medicine taken at one time.
✏️ 例句
The doctor prescribed a dose of antibiotics twice a day.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a dose of medicine 一剂药 ★ in small/large doses 少量/大量地(比喻用法) ☆ overdose 过量——医学/社会类常见词
🧠 助记
dose 谐音多些——医生说多些药 = 一剂
dose::/dəʊs/:n. 剂量:A small dose of humor can ease tension in difficult situations.
5. gross /ɡrəʊs/ adj. 总的；粗鲁的；n. 总额
📖 英文解释
Total amount before any deductions; unpleasantly obvious or blatant.
✏️ 例句
The gross income of the company increased by 15% last year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ gross domestic product (GDP) 国内生产总值 ★ gross income/profit 总收入/毛利润 ☆ gross negligence 严重疏忽 辨析：gross 指税前总额；net 指净额
🧠 助记
gross = 过筛之前的全部 = 总的
gross::/ɡrəʊs/:adj. 总的:The gross national product grew by 3% this quarter.
6. laughter /ˈlɑːftə(r)/ n. 笑声，笑
📖 英文解释
The action or sound of laughing.
✏️ 例句
The comedian's jokes filled the room with laughter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ burst into laughter 突然大笑 ★ roar with laughter 放声大笑 ☆ laughter is the best medicine(谚语)
🧠 助记
laugh(笑) + ter = 笑的声音 = 笑声
laughter::/ˈlɑːftə(r)/:n. 笑声，笑:Her laughter was infectious and made everyone smile.
7. trail /treɪl/ n. 小径；踪迹；v. 跟踪；落后
📖 英文解释
A path through countryside or forest; a track or series of marks left behind.
✏️ 例句
Hikers followed the trail through the dense forest.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ trail behind 落在后面 ★ leave a trail of 留下一连串 ☆ paper trail 书面记录 辨析：trail 自然小径；path 泛指道路；track 强调轨迹
🧠 助记
trail 像 train 的轨道但更窄 = 小径/踪迹
trail::/treɪl/:n. 小径:The investigation left a clear trail of evidence.
8. discard /dɪsˈkɑːd/ v. 抛弃，丢弃；n. 被丢弃的东西
📖 英文解释
To get rid of something as no longer useful or desirable.
✏️ 例句
She decided to discard her old notes after the exam.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ discard sth as useless/outdated 以无用为由丢弃 ★ discard a theory/idea 放弃某理论 ☆ 写作常用于表达摒弃旧观念 辨析：discard 不再保留；abandon 完全放弃
🧠 助记
dis(不要) + card(卡片) = 把卡片扔掉 = 丢弃
discard::/dɪsˈkɑːd/:v. 抛弃，丢弃:Scientists discarded the hypothesis after the experiments failed.
9. absolute /ˈæbsəluːt/ adj. 绝对的，完全的；确定的
📖 英文解释
Not qualified or diminished in any way; total and complete.
✏️ 例句
Freedom of speech is not an absolute right; it has limits.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ absolute power/authority 绝对权力 ★ in absolute terms 从绝对值来看 ☆ absolutely 副词，口语中常表强调
🧠 助记
ab(加强) + solute(解开) = 完全解开束缚 = 绝对的
absolute::/ˈæbsəluːt/:adj. 绝对的，完全的:The judge had absolute authority in that courtroom.
10. classify /ˈklæsɪfaɪ/ v. 分类，归类
📖 英文解释
To arrange or assign things to groups based on shared qualities.
✏️ 例句
Biologists classify organisms into kingdoms, phyla, and species.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ classify sth as/into 将某物分类为 ★ classified information 机密信息 ☆ classification n. 分类
🧠 助记
class(类) + ify(使) = 使归入某类 = 分类
classify::/ˈklæsɪfaɪ/:v. 分类，归类:The library classifies books by subject and author.
11. festival /ˈfestɪvl/ n. 节日，节庆；adj. 节日的
📖 英文解释
A day or period of celebration, typically for religious or cultural reasons.
✏️ 例句
The Spring Festival is the most important celebration in China.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ music/film/art festival 音乐/电影/艺术节 ★ festival atmosphere 节日气氛 ☆ festive adj. 欢庆的
🧠 助记
fest(宴席) + ival = 举办盛大宴席的日子 = 节日
festival::/ˈfestɪvl/:n. 节日，节庆:The town holds a folk music festival every summer.
12. quest /kwest/ n. 探索，寻求；v. 寻找

🧠 助记
quest 像 question(疑问)——为了回答问题不断探索
quest::/kwest/:n. 探索，寻求:The quest for social equality remains an ongoing challenge.
13. conceive /kənˈsiːv/ v. 想象，构思；怀孕

con(共同) + ceive(拿) = 在脑中共同拿到一个想法 = 构思
conceive::/kənˈsiːv/:v. 想象，构思:She conceived a bold plan to reform the entire system.
14. underestimate /ˌʌndərˈestɪmeɪt/ v. 低估；n. 低估

underestimate::/ˌʌndərˈestɪmeɪt/:v. 低估:Many people underestimate the time needed for deep learning.
