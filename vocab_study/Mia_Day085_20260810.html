1. wander /ˈwɒndə(r)/ v. 游荡；漫步；走神
📖 英文解释
To walk without a fixed destination; to let thoughts drift.
✏️ 例句
She wandered through the old streets of the city without any particular plan.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ wander around/through 漫游于 ★ wander off 走散；偏离 ★ mind wanders 心思不集中 ☆ 辨析：wander / roam / stroll：wander 无目的漫游；roam 强调范围广；stroll 散步，较轻松
🧠 助记
wander = wonder(想知道)的变形——边走边想 = 游荡
wander::/ˈwɒndə(r)/:v. 游荡:His thoughts began to wander during the long lecture.

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
extra(额外) + ordinary(普通) = 超出普通的 = 非凡的
extraordinary::/ɪkˈstrɔːdnri/:adj. 非凡的:She showed extraordinary courage in the face of adversity.
