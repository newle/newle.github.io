1. tune /tjuːn/ n. 曲调；调子；v. 调音；调谐
📖 英文解释
A melody or musical piece; to adjust to the correct pitch or frequency.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ in tune with 与……协调一致 ★ out of tune 走调；与……不协调 ★ tune in 收听；tune out 不理会 ☆ tuneful adj. 旋律优美的
🧠 助记

tune::/tjuːn/:n. 曲调:The policy is in tune with the needs of the local community.
