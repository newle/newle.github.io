1. lest /lest/ conj. 以免，唯恐
📖 英文解释
for fear that; in order to prevent the possibility of something happening
✏️ 例句
She studied hard lest she should fail the exam.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lest + (should) + 动词原形；常用于表示担忧或预防的从句，考研常考连词辨析
🧠 助记
less + t → 少了点担心就用 lest，「以免」少了什么
lest::/lest/:conj. 以免，唯恐:He whispered lest anyone should hear.
2. supervise /ˈsuːpəvaɪz/ v. 监督，管理
📖 英文解释
to watch over and direct the work or activities of others to ensure they are done correctly
✏️ 例句
The manager supervises the daily operations of the department.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
supervise sb./sth.; supervision n.; supervisor n.；常考近义词 oversee, monitor
🧠 助记
super（超级）+ vise（看）→ 超级看管 → 监督
supervise::/ˈsuːpəvaɪz/:v. 监督，管理:She was asked to supervise the project.
3. liable /ˈlaɪəbl/ adj. 有义务的；有责任的；易于……的
📖 英文解释
legally responsible for something; likely to do something or to be affected by something
✏️ 例句
The company is liable for any injuries caused on its premises.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be liable for sth. 对……负责; be liable to do 易于做; legal liability 法律责任
🧠 助记
li + able → 有能力（承担）的义务 → 有责任的
liable::/ˈlaɪəbl/:adj. 有义务的:He is liable for the damages.
4. senator /ˈsenətə(r)/ n. 参议员
📖 英文解释
a member of a senate, the upper house of a legislature
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
senator 参议员; Senate 参议院; congressman 众议员；政治类词汇考研高频
🧠 助记
senat(e)（参议院）+ or（人）→ 参议院里的人 → 参议员
senator::/ˈsenətə(r)/:n. 参议员:The senator voted against the bill.
5. abolish /əˈbɒlɪʃ/ v. 废除，废止
📖 英文解释
to formally put an end to a law, system, practice, or institution
✏️ 例句
The government decided to abolish the death penalty.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
abolish sth. 废除某事物; abolition n. 废除; 考研常考反义词 establish
🧠 助记
a + bol（球）+ ish → 把球（制度）扔掉 → 废除
abolish::/əˈbɒlɪʃ/:v. 废除，废止:They abolished the old regulation.
6. foresee /fɔːˈsiː/ v. 预见，预料
📖 英文解释
to be aware of and prepared for something before it happens
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
foresee sth./that clause; foreseeable adj. 可预见的; in the foreseeable future 在可预见的将来
🧠 助记
fore（前）+ see（看）→ 向前看 → 预见
foresee::/fɔːˈsiː/:v. 预见，预料:She failed to foresee the risks.
7. hardship /ˈhɑːdʃɪp/ n. 艰难，困苦

hardship::/ˈhɑːdʃɪp/:n. 艰难，困苦:They suffered years of hardship.

hormone::/ˈhɔːməʊn/:n. 激素，荷尔蒙:Stress can affect hormone levels.

recipient::/rɪˈsɪpiənt/:n. 接受者，受领者:She was the recipient of a scholarship.

12. hypothesis /haɪˈpɒθəsɪs/ n. 假设，假说
📖 英文解释
