1. prey /preɪ/ n. 猎物；v. 捕食；受害者
📖 英文解释
an animal that is hunted and killed by another; to hunt and kill for food; to exploit someone
✏️ 例句
The eagle swooped down on its prey with great speed.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prey on/upon sth. 捕食……; fall prey to 成为……的受害者; predator n. 捕食者
🧠 助记
prey → pray（祈祷）→ 猎物在祈祷别被吃 → 猎物
prey::/preɪ/:n. 猎物:Mice are common prey for owls.
2. stupid /ˈstjuːpɪd/ adj. 愚蠢的，笨的
📖 英文解释
lacking intelligence or common sense; showing a lack of judgement or reason
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stupid mistake/idea 愚蠢的错误/想法; stupidity n. 愚蠢; stupidly adv.; clever/intelligent（反）
🧠 助记
stu（困住）+ pid → 困住的笨蛋 → 愚蠢的
stupid::/ˈstjuːpɪd/:adj. 愚蠢的，笨的:That was a stupid decision.

🧠 助记

toxic::/ˈtɒksɪk/:adj. 有毒的:Toxic fumes filled the building.
4. collar /ˈkɒlə(r)/ n. 衣领，项圈；v. 抓住

🧠 助记

collar::/ˈkɒlə(r)/:n. 衣领，项圈:He grabbed him by the collar.
5. plot /plɒt/ n. 情节；阴谋；v. 密谋；绘制

plot::/plɒt/:n. 情节:The plot of the film was brilliant.

rainbow::/ˈreɪnbəʊ/:n. 彩虹:A rainbow appeared after the rain.
