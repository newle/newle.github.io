1. manual /ˈmænjuəl/ n. 手册，说明书；adj. 手工的
📖 英文解释
a book giving instructions or information; done with the hands rather than automatically
✏️ 例句
Read the manual carefully before operating the machine.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
instruction manual 操作手册; manual labor/work 体力劳动; manual vs. automatic 手动与自动
🧠 助记
manu（手）+ al → 用手（操作的）→ 手工的；手册
manual::/ˈmænjuəl/:n. 手册，说明书:Consult the manual for instructions.
2. prescribe /prɪˈskraɪb/ v. 开处方；规定，指定
📖 英文解释
to write or give instructions for a medicine; to officially recommend or require
✏️ 例句
The doctor prescribed antibiotics for the infection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prescribe medicine 开药; as prescribed 按规定; prescription n. 处方; prescribed reading 规定读物
🧠 助记
pre（预先）+ scribe（写）→ 预先写下 → 开处方
prescribe::/prɪˈskraɪb/:v. 开处方:The doctor prescribed bed rest.
3. shell /ʃel/ n. 壳，外壳；炮弹
📖 英文解释
the hard outer covering of an animal, nut, or egg; a lightweight frame; an explosive projectile
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
shell out 支出大量钱; shell shock 炮弹震惊; come out of one's shell 变开朗; nutshell 简而言之
🧠 助记
shell → 看起来像个空壳 → 壳
shell::/ʃel/:n. 壳，外壳:The shell of the egg cracked.
4. tale /teɪl/ n. 故事，传说；叙述
📖 英文解释
a spoken or written account of a real or imaginary event; a story
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
tale → tell（讲）→ 讲出来的 → 故事
tale::/teɪl/:n. 故事，传说:She told a fascinating tale.
5. bell /bel/ n. 钟，铃，钟声
📖 英文解释
a hollow metal object that makes a ringing sound when struck; a device that makes a ringing sound
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ring a bell 听起来耳熟; door bell 门铃; bell tower 钟楼; bell curve 钟形曲线
🧠 助记
bell → b + ell → 发音像「叮」→ 铃
bell::/bel/:n. 钟，铃，钟声:The bell rang at exactly noon.
6. competent /ˈkɒmpɪtənt/ adj. 有能力的，胜任的
📖 英文解释
having the necessary ability, knowledge, or skill to do something successfully

competent::/ˈkɒmpɪtənt/:adj. 有能力的，胜任的:She is a highly competent teacher.

hat::/hæt/:n. 帽子:She bought a new summer hat.

prestige::/preˈstiːʒ/:n. 声望，威望:The school has great academic prestige.

silly::/ˈsɪli/:adj. 愚蠢的，可笑的:Don't be so silly about it.

split::/splɪt/:v./n. 分开，分裂:They split the work equally.
