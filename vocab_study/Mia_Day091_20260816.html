1. drill /drɪl/ n. 练习；钻头；演习 v. 钻孔；操练
📖 英文解释
Intensive instruction or training through repetition; a tool for making holes; a military exercise; to train repeatedly.
✏️ 例句
The fire drill ensured all employees knew the emergency exit routes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：fire drill (消防演习)；drill sb in sth (反复训练某人某事)；dental drill (牙钻)；drill down into (深入研究)；military drill (军事操练)。
🧠 助记
drill 联想 thrill(激动) → 操练(drill)让人有成就感的激动(thrill)。钻孔机发出的声音也令人印象深刻。
drill::/drɪl/:n. 练习:Students were drilled in grammar rules until they became second nature.
2. sculpture /ˈskʌlptʃər/ n. 雕塑；雕刻 v. 雕刻
📖 英文解释
The art of making three-dimensional forms by carving, casting, or modeling; a work created in this way.
✏️ 例句
Michelangelo's sculpture of David is considered a masterpiece of Renaissance art.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：sculpt v. 雕刻；sculptor n. 雕塑家；sculptural adj. 雕塑的；marble/bronze sculpture (大理石/青铜雕塑)；abstract sculpture (抽象雕塑)。
🧠 助记
sculpt(雕刻) + ure(名词后缀) → 雕刻的作品 → 雕塑。记住 sculpt，其余加后缀变化。
sculpture::/ˈskʌlptʃər/:n. 雕塑:The city's public squares are adorned with sculptures by local and international artists.

🧠 助记

warmth::/wɔːrmθ/:n. 温暖:The warmth with which the community welcomed the new immigrants was heartening.
