1. gloomy /ˈɡluːmi/ adj. 阴暗的；悲观的；沮丧的
📖 英文解释
Dark and difficult to see in; causing feelings of sadness or depression; pessimistic.
✏️ 例句
The economic forecast painted a gloomy picture for the coming year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：gloomy outlook/forecast/picture (悲观的前景/预测/景象)；gloomy weather/atmosphere (阴暗的天气/气氛)；gloomily adv.；gloom n. 忧郁/黑暗。
🧠 助记
gloom(黑暗/忧郁) + y(形容词) → 充满黑暗的 → 阴暗的/悲观的。gloom 联想 doom(厄运)，都是黑暗情绪。
gloomy::/ˈɡluːmi/:adj. 阴暗的:Analysts painted a gloomy picture of the economy for the next fiscal year.

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
hint 联想 mint(薄荷) → 薄荷只放一点点(a hint of mint) → 暗示/少量。
hint::/hɪnt/:n. 暗示:The minister's speech contained a hint that tax reforms might be announced soon.

illiterate::/ɪˈlɪtərət/:adj. 文盲的:Millions of adults remain functionally illiterate despite years of formal schooling.

✏️ 例句
He is merely an acquaintance, not a close friend — I've only met him twice.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

San Francisco Bay is one of the most beautiful natural harbors in the world.

29. bite /baɪt/ v. 咬；刺痛；上钩 n. 咬；一口；刺痛
