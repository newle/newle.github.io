1. dimension /dɪˈmenʃən/ n. 维度；尺寸；方面
📖 英文解释
A measurable extent of a particular kind, such as length, breadth, depth, or height; an aspect or feature of a situation.
✏️ 例句
The problem has several dimensions that need to be considered carefully.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：a new dimension to sth（为某事增添新维度）；political/social dimension（政治/社会层面）；three-dimensional（三维的）
🧠 助记
di（二）+ mension（量），二维→多维，记住是量度、维度
dimension::/dɪˈmenʃən/:n. 维度:The crisis added a new dimension to the debate.
2. precision /prɪˈsɪʒən/ n. 精确；精密；精度
📖 英文解释
The quality, condition, or fact of being exact and accurate; the degree to which repeated measurements under unchanged conditions show the same results.
✏️ 例句
The surgeon performed the operation with great precision.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：with precision（精确地）；precision instrument（精密仪器）；precision bombing（精确轰炸）；词根 precis-（precise 的名词）
🧠 助记
pre（前）+ cis（切）+ ion，提前精准切割 → 精确
precision::/prɪˈsɪʒən/:n. 精确:She described the process with great precision.
3. shore /ʃɔːr/ n. 海岸，岸边 v. 支撑，加固
📖 英文解释
The land along the edge of a sea, lake, or wide river; to support or strengthen something that is at risk of failing.
✏️ 例句
They walked along the rocky shore at sunset.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：shore up（支撑；巩固）；on shore（在岸上）；offshore（离岸的；近海的）；shoreline（海岸线）
🧠 助记
联想：sure（确定）谐音 shore，站在岸边才觉得脚踏实地
shore::/ʃɔːr/:n. 海岸，岸边 v. 支撑，加固:The government took measures to shore up the economy.
4. superficial /ˌsuːpərˈfɪʃəl/ adj. 表面的；肤浅的；浅薄的
📖 英文解释
Existing or occurring at or on the surface; not thorough or deep; concerned only with obvious aspects.
✏️ 例句
His understanding of the topic was superficial at best.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：superficial knowledge/understanding（肤浅的了解）；superficial resemblance（表面相似）；superficial wound（浅表伤口）
🧠 助记
super（上面）+ fici（面/表面）+ al，在表面之上 → 表面的、肤浅的
superficial::/ˌsuːpərˈfɪʃəl/:adj. 表面的:The report offered only a superficial analysis of the problem.
