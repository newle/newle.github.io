1. tea /tiː/ n. 茶；茶叶；下午茶
📖 英文解释
A hot drink made by infusing the dried, crushed leaves of the tea plant in boiling water; the dried leaves used to make this drink; a light afternoon meal.
✏️ 例句
She poured herself a cup of green tea and sat by the window.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：tea ceremony（茶道）；afternoon tea（下午茶）；not one's cup of tea（不是某人所喜欢的）；herbal tea（草药茶）
🧠 助记
tea 发音如'踢哦'，茶杯被踢倒啦，好好记住 → 茶
tea::/tiː/:n. 茶:Not everyone's cup of tea, but he loved opera.

🧠 助记
