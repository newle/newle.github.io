1. beside /bɪˈsaɪd/ prep. 在…旁边；与…相比
📖 英文解释
At the side of; next to; compared with; apart from that.
✏️ 例句
She sat beside him on the bench.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：beside the point（离题；无关紧要）；beside oneself（失控；极度激动）；注意 beside 与 besides 的区别（besides = in addition to）
🧠 助记
be + side（旁边）→ 在旁边 → 在…旁边
beside::/bɪˈsaɪd/:prep. 在…旁边:That question is beside the point entirely.

📖 英文解释
Settling an issue; producing a definite result; having or showing the ability to make decisions quickly and effectively.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：decisive victory/moment/action（决定性胜利/时刻/行动）；decisive factor（决定性因素）；decisively（果断地）；indecisive（优柔寡断的）
🧠 助记
decide（决定）+ ive → 具有决定作用的 → 决定性的；果断的
decisive::/dɪˈsaɪsɪv/:adj. 决定性的:The battle proved decisive in ending the war.
