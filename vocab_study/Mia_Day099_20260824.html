1. imperative /imˈperətiv/ adj. 紧急的；祈使的；n. 必要的事
📖 英文解释
Of vital importance; crucial. Expressing a command or instruction.
✏️ 例句
It is imperative that we act immediately to address the crisis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：it is imperative that + 虚拟语气；imperative need/task；辨析：urgent, critical, pressing
🧠 助记
im(进入)+per(通过)+ative → 必须穿越的 → 紧迫的、命令的
imperative::/imˈperətiv/:adj. 紧急的:It is imperative that the report be submitted before Friday.
2. reckon /ˈrekən/ v. 估算；认为；料想
📖 英文解释
To estimate or calculate; to think or believe something.
✏️ 例句
I reckon it will take about two hours to finish the project.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：reckon with（应对/考虑到）；reckon on（依赖/期待）
🧠 助记
reckon ≈ reckoning（清算）→ 算清楚 → 估算/认为
reckon::/ˈrekən/:v. 估算:We must reckon with the potential risks before proceeding.
3. acute /əˈkjuːt/ adj. 严重的；敏锐的；急性的
📖 英文解释
Very serious or severe; having a sharp understanding; of a disease coming suddenly.
✏️ 例句
The shortage of clean water is an acute problem in many developing countries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：acute shortage/crisis/pain；acute angle（锐角）；对比 chronic（慢性）
🧠 助记
a+cute（尖的）→ 角度尖锐 → 敏锐的；病情尖峰 → 急性的
acute::/əˈkjuːt/:adj. 严重的:The region faces an acute shortage of medical supplies.
4. castle /ˈkɑːsl/ n. 城堡；（象棋）车
📖 英文解释
A large medieval stone building with towers and thick walls; also a chess piece.
✏️ 例句
The ancient castle on the hill attracts thousands of tourists every year.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研背景词；build castles in the air（白日做梦）
🧠 助记
castle → cas（house）+tle → 大房子 → 城堡
castle::/ˈkɑːsl/:n. 城堡:Stop building castles in the air and start working toward your goals.
5. considerate /kənˈsidərət/ adj. 体贴的；考虑周到的
📖 英文解释
Careful not to cause inconvenience or hurt to others; thoughtful and attentive.
✏️ 例句
She is always considerate of others when making decisions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研辨析：considerate（体贴）vs. considerable（相当大的）；be considerate of/towards sb
🧠 助记
consider（考虑）+ate → 总是考虑别人的 → 体贴的
considerate::/kənˈsidərət/:adj. 体贴的:It is considerate of you to offer your seat to the elderly.
6. dignity /ˈdigniti/ n. 尊严；庄严；高贵
📖 英文解释
The state or quality of being worthy of honor or respect.
✏️ 例句
Every human being deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：maintain/preserve/uphold dignity；dignity of labor（劳动的尊严）
🧠 助记
dign（值得）+ity → 值得尊重的品质 → 尊严
dignity::/ˈdigniti/:n. 尊严:The elderly should be allowed to live with dignity and independence.
7. heighten /ˈhaitn/ v. 加强；增加；提高
📖 英文解释
To make or become more intense or pronounced; to increase in degree.
✏️ 例句
The security measures were heightened after the incident.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：heighten awareness/tension/concern；近义词：intensify, enhance, amplify
🧠 助记
height（高度）+en → 使高度增加 → 提高、加强
heighten::/ˈhaitn/:v. 加强:The news heightened public concern about food safety.
8. magnificent /mæɡˈnifisnt/ adj. 宏伟的；壮丽的；极好的
📖 英文解释
Impressively beautiful, elaborate, or extravagant; very fine or excellent.
✏️ 例句
The magnificent scenery of the mountains left everyone speechless.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研描述景观/成就/建筑；近义词：splendid, grand, spectacular；magnify同根
🧠 助记
magni（大）+fic（做）+ent → 做得很宏大的 → 壮丽的
magnificent::/mæɡˈnifisnt/:adj. 宏伟的:The magnificent cathedral stands as a symbol of the city history.
9. metre /ˈmiːtə/ n. 米；诗的格律
📖 英文解释
The basic unit of length in the metric system; the rhythm of poetry.
✏️ 例句
The swimming pool is fifty metres long.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
英式拼写（美式 meter）；考研注意：metre 也指诗歌韵律
🧠 助记
metre → 来自希腊语 metron（测量）→ 米（测量单位）
metre::/ˈmiːtə/:n. 米:She ran one hundred metres in under twelve seconds.
10. rhythm /ˈriðəm/ n. 节奏；韵律；规律性
📖 英文解释
A strong regular repeated pattern of sound, movement, or events.
✏️ 例句
The rhythm of the music made everyone want to dance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：rhythm of life（生活节奏）；natural rhythm（自然规律）；辨析 rhyme（押韵）
🧠 助记
rhythm → 拼写助记：Really Hot Young Teenagers Have More
rhythm::/ˈriðəm/:n. 节奏:Children develop a natural rhythm in language through reading aloud.
11. throat /θrəut/ n. 喉咙；咽喉
📖 英文解释
The passage which leads from the back of the mouth of a person or animal.
✏️ 例句
She cleared her throat before beginning her speech.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用短语：clear throat（清嗓子）；a lump in throat（哽咽）
🧠 助记
throat → thro（through）+at → 通过去的地方 → 喉咙
throat::/θrəut/:n. 喉咙:He had a sore throat and could barely speak.
12. tidy /ˈtaidi/ adj. 整洁的；v. 收拾；整理
📖 英文解释
Arranged neatly and in order; to make a place or things neat and orderly.
✏️ 例句
Please tidy your room before the guests arrive.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：tidy up（整理）；tidy sum（一大笔钱）；辨析 neat, orderly
🧠 助记
tidy → tide（潮水）+y → 潮水退后留下整洁的沙滩 → 整洁
tidy::/ˈtaidi/:adj. 整洁的:She always keeps her desk tidy to stay focused and efficient.
13. wire /ˈwaiə/ n. 电线；铁丝；v. 接线
📖 英文解释
Metal drawn out into a thin flexible thread or rod; to install electrical wires.
✏️ 例句
The electrician spent hours connecting the wires in the new building.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：wire transfer（电汇）；barbed wire（铁丝网）；live wire（带电线）
🧠 助记
wire → 发音像"外尔" → 外面拉一条线 → 电线
wire::/ˈwaiə/:n. 电线:The suspect was caught trying to cut the barbed wire fence.
14. cliff /klif/ n. 悬崖；峭壁
📖 英文解释
A steep high face of rock, earth, or ice.
✏️ 例句
They stood at the edge of the cliff and looked down at the sea.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：cliff-hanger（悬疑结局）；fiscal cliff（财政悬崖）；over the cliff（急剧下滑）

cliff → cl（close）+iff → 靠近就会掉下去 → 悬崖
cliff::/klif/:n. 悬崖:The economy seems to be heading toward a fiscal cliff.
15. curve /kɜːv/ n. 曲线；弯道；v. 弯曲
📖 英文解释
A line or outline that gradually deviates from being straight; to form or move in a curve.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
curve → curv（弯）+ e → 弯弯的线 → 曲线
curve::/kɜːv/:n. 曲线:The data shows a steady downward curve in unemployment rates.
16. destiny /ˈdestini/ n. 命运；天命；定数
📖 英文解释
The events that will necessarily happen to a particular person in the future; fate.
✏️ 例句
She believed that it was her destiny to become a great scientist.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研辨析：destiny（注定命运）vs. fate（命运，多含消极）；manifest destiny（天定命运论）
🧠 助记
destine（注定）+y → 已被注定的事 → 命运
destiny::/ˈdestini/:n. 命运:He took his destiny into his own hands and worked tirelessly.
17. dim /dim/ adj. 暗淡的；模糊的；v. 变暗
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

dim::/dim/:adj. 暗淡的:The prospects for a peaceful solution look increasingly dim.
18. paradox /ˈpærədɒks/ n. 悖论；矛盾现象
📖 英文解释
A statement that seems impossible because it contains two opposite facts.
✏️ 例句
It is a paradox that the more choices we have, the less satisfied we feel.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：the paradox of choice；paradoxically（adv.）；辨析 contradiction
🧠 助记
para（超越）+dox（观点）→ 超越常规观点 → 悖论
paradox::/ˈpærədɒks/:n. 悖论:The paradox of modern society is that people are connected yet lonely.
19. repeatedly /riˈpiːtidli/ adv. 重复地；再三地

🧠 助记

repeatedly::/riˈpiːtidli/:adv. 重复地:The company has repeatedly failed to comply with environmental regulations.

resolution::/ˌrezəˈluːʃn/:n. 解决方案:The two sides reached a peaceful resolution after months of negotiation.
21. scream /skriːm/ v./n. 尖叫；喊叫
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
scream → sc（sharp cry）+ ream → 尖锐的喊声 → 尖叫
scream::/skriːm/:v./n. 尖叫:The victim screams were heard throughout the neighborhood.
22. shade /ʃeid/ n. 阴影；遮光；v. 遮蔽
📖 英文解释
Comparative darkness caused by shelter from direct sunlight; a slight amount of a quality.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

shade::/ʃeid/:n. 阴影:There are many shades of meaning in the word freedom.
