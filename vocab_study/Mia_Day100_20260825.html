1. contaminate /kənˈtæmineit/ v. 污染；毒化；弄脏
📖 英文解释
To make something impure or unsuitable by contact or mixture with something unclean.
✏️ 例句
Industrial waste has contaminated the local river for decades.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：contaminate water/soil/food supply；contamination（n.）；decontaminate（去污）
🧠 助记
con（共同）+tamin（触碰）+ate → 被污染物接触 → 污染
contaminate::/kənˈtæmineit/:v. 污染:The oil spill contaminated hundreds of kilometers of coastline.
2. creep /kriːp/ v. 爬；蔓延；悄悄移动；n. 令人不安者
📖 英文解释
To move slowly and carefully; to spread gradually; to make someone feel uneasy.
✏️ 例句
Ivy crept up the walls of the old building over the years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：creep up on（悄悄靠近）；creeping inflation（蔓延性通货膨胀）
🧠 助记
creep → cr（crawl，爬）+eep → 慢慢爬行
creep::/kriːp/:v. 爬:Inflation has been creeping up steadily over the past few months.
3. diabetes /ˌdaiəˈbiːtiːz/ n. 糖尿病
📖 英文解释
A metabolic disease in which the body cannot regulate blood sugar properly.
✏️ 例句
Regular exercise can help prevent type 2 diabetes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研医学类词汇；diabetic（adj. 糖尿病的）；type 1/type 2 diabetes
🧠 助记
dia（通过）+betes（走）→ 糖分通过而不被吸收 → 糖尿病
diabetes::/ˌdaiəˈbiːtiːz/:n. 糖尿病:The prevalence of diabetes has increased sharply in developed countries.

🧠 助记
drunk → drink（喝）的过去分词 → 喝多了 → 醉的
drunk::/drʌŋk/:adj. 醉酒的:Laws against drunk driving have saved thousands of lives.

dual::/ˈdjuːəl/:adj. 双重的:The policy has a dual purpose of reducing costs and improving efficiency.
