1. municipal /mjuːˈnisəpl/ adj. 市的；市政的；地方自治的
📖 英文解释
Relating to a municipality; of or concerning the local government of a town or city.
✏️ 例句
The municipal government launched a new public transportation plan.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：municipal government/council（市政府/市议会）；municipality（n.市/市政当局）
🧠 助记
muni（城市）+cipal（首要的）→ 城市首要的 → 市政的
municipal::/mjuːˈnisəpl/:adj. 市的:Municipal authorities are responsible for maintaining local infrastructure.
2. plausible /ˈplɔːzəbl/ adj. 似乎是真的；貌似合理的
📖 英文解释
Seeming reasonable or probable; skilled at producing convincing explanations.
✏️ 例句
The suspect offered a plausible explanation for his whereabouts.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：a plausible argument/theory/excuse；implausible（adj.难以置信的）；辨析 convincing, credible
🧠 助记
plaus（掌声）+ible → 听起来值得鼓掌的 → 似乎合理的
plausible::/ˈplɔːzəbl/:adj. 似乎是真的:The company offered a plausible but ultimately unconvincing account of its actions.
3. sociology /ˌsəusiˈɒlədʒi/ n. 社会学
📖 英文解释
The study of the development, structure, and functioning of human society.
✏️ 例句
She majored in sociology and went on to work in social policy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研学科词汇：sociologist（n.社会学家）；sociological（adj.社会学的）
🧠 助记
socio（社会）+logy（学科）→ 研究社会的学科 → 社会学
sociology::/ˌsəusiˈɒlədʒi/:n. 社会学:Sociology helps explain how social institutions shape individual behavior.
4. crawl /krɔːl/ v. 爬；匍匐；缓慢移动；n. 爬行

crawl::/krɔːl/:v. 爬:The economic recovery has been crawling along at a painfully slow pace.

exit::/ˈeksit/:n. 出口:Investors are looking for an exit strategy from their long-term holdings.
