1. paradise /ˈpærədaɪs/ n. 天堂；乐园
📖 英文解释
A place or state of perfect happiness; heaven.
✏️ 例句
The tropical island felt like paradise to the exhausted travelers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
paradise for sb 对某人来说是乐园；a lost paradise 失落的乐园（考研阅读常见意象）
🧠 助记
para（旁边）+ dice（骰子）→ 骰子旁的美好地方 → 天堂乐园
paradise::/ˈpærədaɪs/:n. 天堂:The island is a paradise for marine biologists.
2. prince /prɪns/ n. 王子；诸侯
📖 英文解释
A male member of a royal family; the son of a king or queen.
✏️ 例句
The prince gave up his royal title to marry the woman he loved.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
crown prince 王储；prince charming 白马王子；常出现在文学/历史语境
🧠 助记
prin（主要）+ ce → 主要的那个 → 王子
prince::/prɪns/:n. 王子:The prince was crowned king after his father's death.
3. revenge /rɪˈvendʒ/ n./v. 复仇；报复
📖 英文解释
The action of hurting someone in return for a wrong suffered.
✏️ 例句
She sought revenge on those who had betrayed her.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
take/seek revenge on sb 向某人复仇；in revenge for 作为对…的报复；revenge多指私人报复
🧠 助记
re（再）+ venge（惩罚）→ 再次惩罚 → 复仇
revenge::/rɪˈvendʒ/:n./v. 复仇:He swore revenge on his enemies.
4. van /væn/ n. 厢式货车；先锋队
📖 英文解释
A medium-sized motor vehicle with a box-shaped body; the forefront of a movement.
✏️ 例句
The moving van arrived at dawn to collect all the furniture.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
van of progress 进步的先锋；delivery van 送货车；in the van of 在…前列（考研写作可用）
🧠 助记
van → 谐音 搬 → 搬家用的厢式车
van::/væn/:n. 厢式货车:The band loaded their equipment into the van.
5. champion /ˈtʃæmpiən/ n. 冠军；捍卫者 v. 支持；捍卫
📖 英文解释
A person who has won first place in a competition; to support or defend a cause.
✏️ 例句
She became the world chess champion at the age of fifteen.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
champion the cause of 倡导…事业；defending champion 卫冕冠军；考研常考v.用法
🧠 助记
champ（咬住）+ ion → 咬住不放的人 → 捍卫者 → 冠军
champion::/ˈtʃæmpiən/:n. 冠军:He championed the rights of the poor throughout his career.
6. chess /tʃes/ n. 国际象棋
📖 英文解释
A board game for two players, each with sixteen pieces, played on a checkered board.
✏️ 例句
He spent hours each day practicing chess to improve his ranking.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
play chess 下象棋；chess player 棋手；常见于智力/策略类比喻
🧠 助记
chess → 谐音 抓 → 棋盘上抓对方棋子 → 象棋
chess::/tʃes/:n. 国际象棋:Chess teaches strategic thinking and patience.
7. chocolate /ˈtʃɒklət/ n. 巧克力；深褐色
📖 英文解释
A food made from roasted cacao beans, typically sweet and used in confectionery.
✏️ 例句
Dark chocolate has been shown to have health benefits in moderation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
chocolate box 巧克力盒；a box of chocolates 常作比喻用语（Life is like a box of chocolates）
🧠 助记
choco（可可）+ late → 晚熟的可可果 → 巧克力
chocolate::/ˈtʃɒklət/:n. 巧克力:She gave him a box of chocolate for Valentine's Day.
8. dear /dɪə/ adj. 亲爱的；昂贵的 n. 亲爱的人
📖 英文解释
Regarded with affection; used as a term of endearment; expensive.
✏️ 例句
This old photograph is very dear to me.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dear to sb 对某人珍贵；Dear Sir/Madam 信函开头；英式英语中也表昂贵
🧠 助记
dear → 谐音 滴儿 → 爱人轻声叫你 → 亲爱的
dear::/dɪə/:adj. 亲爱的:These memories are very dear to her.
9. deceive /dɪˈsiːv/ v. 欺骗；蒙蔽
📖 英文解释
To cause someone to believe something that is not true; to mislead.
✏️ 例句
He deceived investors by falsifying the company's financial records.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
deceive sb into doing sth 欺骗某人做某事；deceive oneself 自欺欺人；近义：cheat, mislead, delude
🧠 助记
de（向下）+ ceive（拿）→ 暗中拿走 → 欺骗
deceive::/dɪˈsiːv/:v. 欺骗:She was deceived into signing the contract.
10. horror /ˈhɒrə/ n. 恐惧；令人恐惧的事物
📖 英文解释
An intense feeling of fear, shock, or disgust; a thing causing such feelings.
✏️ 例句
She watched in horror as the car skidded toward the crowd.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
to sb's horror 令某人恐惧的是；horror film 恐怖片；a horror of 对…的极度厌恶
🧠 助记
horror → 双重 hor → 叠加恐惧 → 恐惧
horror::/ˈhɒrə/:n. 恐惧:The children screamed in horror at the scene.
11. juice /dʒuːs/ n. 果汁；汁液
📖 英文解释
The liquid obtained from pressing fruit or vegetables; bodily fluid.
✏️ 例句
Fresh orange juice is rich in vitamin C and antioxidants.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fruit juice 果汁；juice up 使有活力；stew in one's own juice 自食其果（考研习语）
🧠 助记
juice → 谐音 就是 → 就是水果的汁 → 果汁
juice::/dʒuːs/:n. 果汁:She squeezed fresh juice from the oranges every morning.
12. princess /ˈprɪnses/ n. 公主；王妃
📖 英文解释
A daughter or granddaughter of a king or queen; the wife of a prince.
✏️ 例句
The princess devoted her life to charitable causes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
crown princess 王储妃；princess cut 公主形切割（珠宝）；常见于文学与比喻
🧠 助记
princess = prince（王子）+ ss → 女版王子 → 公主
princess::/ˈprɪnses/:n. 公主:The princess opened the new children's hospital.
13. shower /ˈʃaʊə/ n. 淋浴；阵雨 v. 淋浴；大量给予
📖 英文解释
A brief period of rain; a device for washing with running water; to give abundantly.
✏️ 例句
A sudden shower caught us without umbrellas.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
take a shower 淋浴；shower sb with sth 给某人大量…；meteor shower 流星雨
🧠 助记
shower = show（展示）+ er → 展示雨水 → 淋浴/阵雨
shower::/ˈʃaʊə/:n. 淋浴:He showered the children with gifts on their birthday.
14. threshold /ˈθreʃhəʊld/ n. 门槛；阈值；起点
📖 英文解释
The strip at the bottom of a doorway; the point at which something begins to happen.
✏️ 例句
The pain threshold varies greatly between different individuals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
on the threshold of 在…门槛上；pain threshold 痛觉阈值；threshold value 阈值（学术常用）
🧠 助记
thresh（打谷）+ hold → 打谷场入口处 → 门槛
threshold::/ˈθreʃhəʊld/:n. 门槛:We are on the threshold of a new era in technology.
15. vocal /ˈvəʊkl/ adj. 直言不讳的；声音的 n. 声乐

🧠 助记
voc（声音）+ al → 声音的 → 直言不讳
vocal::/ˈvəʊkl/:adj. 直言不讳的:The professor was vocal in his criticism of the government's decision.
16. autonomy /ɔːˈtɒnəmi/ n. 自治；自主权
📖 英文解释
The right or condition of self-government; freedom from external control.
✏️ 例句
The region was granted greater autonomy to manage its own affairs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
regional autonomy 区域自治；personal autonomy 个人自主权；考研政治/社会学高频词

autonomy::/ɔːˈtɒnəmi/:n. 自治:The university prizes academic autonomy above all else.
17. cloudy /ˈklaʊdi/ adj. 多云的；模糊的
📖 英文解释
Covered with clouds; not transparent; unclear or vague.

cloudy::/ˈklaʊdi/:adj. 多云的:Her memory of that night remained cloudy and uncertain.
18. grief /ɡriːf/ n. 悲伤；忧愁
📖 英文解释
Deep sorrow, especially caused by someone's death; trouble or annoyance.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
come to grief 遭到失败；grief over/at 为…悲痛；give sb grief 给某人麻烦（口语）
🧠 助记
grief → grieve（悲痛）的名词 → 联想格外难受 → 悲痛
grief::/ɡriːf/:n. 悲伤:The nation shared in the family's grief.
19. hierarchy /ˈhaɪərɑːki/ n. 等级制度；层级体系
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
The strict hierarchy of the military left little room for individual initiative.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
social hierarchy 社会等级；hierarchy of needs 需求层次（马斯洛）；corporate hierarchy 企业层级
🧠 助记
hier（神圣）+ archy（统治）→ 神权统治 → 等级制度
hierarchy::/ˈhaɪərɑːki/:n. 等级制度:The company has a flat hierarchy that encourages open communication.
20. probable /ˈprɒbəbl/ adj. 很可能的；大概的

Likely to happen or be true; appearing likely based on evidence.

probable cause 合理理由（法律）；the most probable explanation 最可能的解释；副词：probably
🧠 助记
prob（检验）+ able → 能被检验为真 → 很可能
probable::/ˈprɒbəbl/:adj. 很可能的:A rise in interest rates seems probable given the current economic data.
21. wooden /ˈwʊdn/ adj. 木制的；木讷的；不灵活的

wooden::/ˈwʊdn/:adj. 木制的:His wooden manner made it hard for him to connect with the audience.
22. comparative /kəmˈpærətɪv/ adj. 比较的；相对的 n. 比较级
📖 英文解释
Measured or judged by comparison with something else; relating to systematic comparison.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
comparative advantage 比较优势（经济学核心）；in comparative terms 相对而言；comparative literature 比较文学

compar（比较）+ ative → 比较的
comparative::/kəmˈpærətɪv/:adj. 比较的:China has a comparative advantage in manufacturing.
23. cartoon /kɑːˈtuːn/ n. 卡通；漫画；动画片
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
The political cartoon captured the absurdity of the situation perfectly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cartoon character 卡通人物；political cartoon 政治漫画；考研阅读常见media类语境
🧠 助记
car（车）+ toon → 车上的小人 → 卡通
cartoon::/kɑːˈtuːn/:n. 卡通:The newspaper's cartoon mocked the politician's broken promises.
24. medal /ˈmedl/ n. 奖章；勋章
📖 英文解释

gold/silver/bronze medal 金银铜牌；medal ceremony 颁奖典礼；medal tally 奖牌榜

medal ≈ metal（金属）→ 金属做的奖章 → 奖牌
medal::/ˈmedl/:n. 奖章:The athlete dedicated her medal to her late coach.
25. detach /dɪˈtætʃ/ v. 分开；拆卸；使超然

detach from 从…分离；detached house 独立式住宅；emotionally detached 情感超然（考研心理/社会类）

detach::/dɪˈtætʃ/:v. 分开:It is important to detach yourself from personal bias when writing an analysis.
