1. progressive /prəˈɡresɪv/ adj. 进步的；渐进的 n. 进步人士
📖 英文解释
Happening or developing gradually; favouring social reform; moving forward continuously.
✏️ 例句
The party adopted a progressive agenda focused on climate and equality.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
progressive tax 累进税；progressive reform 渐进式改革；progressive disease 进行性疾病
🧠 助记
progress（进步）+ ive → 朝前进步的 → 渐进的
progressive::/prəˈɡresɪv/:adj. 进步的:The progressive tax system charges higher earners at a greater rate.
2. ratio /ˈreɪʃiəʊ/ n. 比率；比例
📖 英文解释
The quantitative relation between two amounts, showing the number of times one contains the other.
✏️ 例句
The debt-to-income ratio is a key measure of financial health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ratio of A to B A与B的比率；debt ratio 债务比率；price-earnings ratio 市盈率（考研经济类）
🧠 助记
ratio → rate（比率）的拉丁形式 → 比率
ratio::/ˈreɪʃiəʊ/:n. 比率:The ratio of students to teachers has improved significantly.
3. subscribe /səbˈskraɪb/ v. 订阅；赞同；捐款
📖 英文解释
To pay regularly to receive a publication; to agree with or support an idea.
✏️ 例句
Many readers subscribe to the journal for its in-depth analysis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
subscribe to a view 赞同某观点；subscribe to a magazine 订阅杂志；subscriber 订阅者
🧠 助记
sub（下面）+ scribe（写）→ 在下面签名 → 订阅/赞同
subscribe::/səbˈskraɪb/:v. 订阅:She does not subscribe to the view that competition is always harmful.
4. tactic /ˈtæktɪk/ n. 策略；战术
📖 英文解释
An action or strategy carefully planned to achieve a specific end.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tactical vs. strategic 战术vs.战略；delay tactic 拖延战术；negotiation tactic 谈判策略
🧠 助记
tact（接触）+ ic → 接触性手段 → 战术/策略
tactic::/ˈtæktɪk/:n. 策略:The negotiators employed a range of tactics to reach an agreement.
5. constrain /kənˈstreɪn/ v. 限制；强迫；约束
📖 英文解释
To severely restrict the scope, extent, or activity of something.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
constrain sb to do sth 迫使某人；constrained by rules 受规则限制；constraint n. 约束
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ strain（拉紧）→ 共同拉紧 → 约束
constrain::/kənˈstreɪn/:v. 限制:Economic conditions constrained the government's ability to invest.
6. dentist /ˈdentɪst/ n. 牙科医生

7. prone /prəʊn/ adj. 倾向于…的；俯卧的
📖 英文解释
Likely or liable to suffer from, do, or experience something undesirable; lying face downward.
✏️ 例句
Children are prone to catching colds during the winter months.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prone to 易于；accident-prone 容易出事故的；error-prone 容易出错的（考研高频搭配）
🧠 助记
prone → pro（向前）→ 向前倒下 → 俯卧 → 倾向于
prone::/prəʊn/:adj. 倾向于…的:The region is prone to severe flooding every spring.
8. refresh /rɪˈfreʃ/ v. 使恢复；更新；刷新

🧠 助记
re（再次）+ fresh（新鲜）→ 再次变新鲜 → 刷新
refresh::/rɪˈfreʃ/:v. 使恢复:The training session will refresh staff on the new procedures.

cooperative::/kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/:adj. 合作的:The cooperative model allows farmers to share resources and reduce costs.
