1. disposal /dɪˈspəʊzl/ n. 处理；处置；支配
📖 英文解释
The action or process of throwing away or getting rid of something; the sale of an asset.
✏️ 例句
The company was fined for improper disposal of chemical waste.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
at sb's disposal 供某人支配；waste disposal 废物处理；disposal of assets 资产处置
🧠 助记
dis（分开）+ pose（放置）→ 分开放置处理掉 → 处理
disposal::/dɪˈspəʊzl/:n. 处理:The funds are at the committee's disposal for research purposes.
2. fever /ˈfiːvə/ n. 发烧；狂热
📖 英文解释
A body temperature higher than normal; a state of nervous excitement.
✏️ 例句
The child developed a high fever overnight and was taken to hospital.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
run a fever 发烧；gold fever 淘金热；fever pitch 极度兴奋；scarlet fever 猩红热
🧠 助记
fever → 谐音 发烧 → 发烧
fever::/ˈfiːvə/:n. 发烧:Football fever swept the country during the World Cup.
3. forgive /fəˈɡɪv/ v. 原谅；宽恕；免除（债务）
📖 英文解释
To stop feeling angry or resentful toward someone for an offence; to cancel a debt.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
for（完全）+ give（给）→ 完全给予宽容 → 原谅
forgive::/fəˈɡɪv/:v. 原谅:It took years before she could forgive him for what he had done.
