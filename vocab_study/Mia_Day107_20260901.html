1. skyscraper /ˈskaɪskreɪpə(r)/ n. 摩天大楼
📖 英文解释
A very tall building with many floors, typically found in cities.
✏️ 例句
The city's skyline is dominated by gleaming skyscrapers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
glass skyscraper 玻璃摩天楼；office skyscraper 办公摩天大楼；skyscraper city 高楼林立的城市
🧠 助记
sky（天空）+ scraper（刮的东西）→ 刮到天空的建筑 → 摩天大楼
skyscraper::/ˈskaɪskreɪpə(r)/:n. 摩天大楼:The new skyscraper will be the tallest building in the country.
2. summit /ˈsʌmɪt/ n. 顶点；山顶；峰会
📖 英文解释
The highest point of a hill or mountain; the highest level of achievement; a meeting between heads of government.
✏️ 例句
World leaders gathered at the summit to discuss climate change.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reach the summit 登顶；summit meeting 首脑会议；G7 summit 七国峰会；at the summit of 在…顶峰
🧠 助记
sum（总和）+ mit（顶）→ 集所有高度于一点 → 顶点
summit::/ˈsʌmɪt/:n. 顶点:The climbers finally reached the summit after three days.
3. lens /lenz/ n. 镜头；透镜；晶状体
📖 英文解释
A piece of glass or other transparent substance with curved sides, used in optical instruments; the transparent part of the eye behind the pupil.
✏️ 例句
She chose a wide-angle lens for landscape photography.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
contact lens 隐形眼镜；camera lens 镜头；telephoto lens 长焦镜头；through the lens of 通过…的视角
🧠 助记
lens形如豆形（lentil豆）→ 豆形的透明体 → 透镜
lens::/lenz/:n. 镜头:The microscope lens magnified the sample a thousand times.
4. porter /ˈpɔːtə(r)/ n. 搬运工；门房；行李员
📖 英文解释
A person employed to carry luggage or other loads; a doorkeeper at a hotel or apartment building.
✏️ 例句
The porter helped carry her luggage up to the hotel room.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hotel porter 酒店行李员；porter's lodge 门卫室；Porter's five forces 波特五力模型
🧠 助记
port（搬运，拉丁语portare）+ er → 搬运的人 → 搬运工
porter::/ˈpɔːtə(r)/:n. 搬运工:The porter at the train station helped carry the heavy suitcases.
5. practise /ˈpræktɪs/ v. 练习；实践；从事（英式拼写）
📖 英文解释
To perform an activity repeatedly in order to improve; to carry out a profession (British spelling: practise; American: practice).
✏️ 例句
She practises the violin for two hours every day.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
practise medicine/law 行医/执法；practise what you preach 言行一致；英式：practise (v.) / practice (n.)
🧠 助记
practical（实际）→ practise（付诸实践练习）
practise::/ˈpræktɪs/:v. 练习:You need to practise speaking English every day to improve fluency.
6. swell /swel/ v. 膨胀；涌；增大 n. 涌浪 adj. 极好的
📖 英文解释
To become larger or rounder; (of the sea) to move in large waves; an area of slowly moving waves.
✏️ 例句
Her ankle swelled up after the fall.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
swell with pride 因自豪而挺胸；swell up 肿起；ocean swell 海浪涌动；groundswell 民意浪潮
🧠 助记
swell = 肿胀（swelling）的根 → 膨胀、涌
swell::/swel/:v. 膨胀:The river began to swell as heavy rain continued to fall.
7. sword /sɔːd/ n. 剑；武力
📖 英文解释
A weapon with a long metal blade and a hilt, used especially in the past; military power.
✏️ 例句
The knight drew his sword to defend the castle.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
double-edged sword 双刃剑；sword fight 剑斗；the pen is mightier than the sword 笔比剑利
🧠 助记
sword = swear（发誓）by this word（剑）→ 剑
sword::/sɔːd/:n. 剑:Globalization is a double-edged sword with both benefits and risks.
8. vacant /ˈveɪkənt/ adj. 空的；空缺的；茫然的
📖 英文解释
Not occupied; empty; (of a post or position) not filled; showing no thought or expression.
✏️ 例句
There are still several vacant positions in the department.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
vacant seat 空位；vacant post 职位空缺；vacant expression 茫然的表情；vacancy n. 空缺
🧠 助记
vac-（空，如vacuum真空）+ ant → 空的
vacant::/ˈveɪkənt/:adj. 空的:The apartment has been vacant for six months.
9. violin /ˌvaɪəˈlɪn/ n. 小提琴
📖 英文解释
A stringed musical instrument of treble pitch, played with a horsehair bow.
✏️ 例句
She has been playing the violin since she was five years old.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
play the violin 拉小提琴；violin concerto 小提琴协奏曲；violinist n. 小提琴手
🧠 助记
viol（维奥尔琴）+ in → 小型的维奥尔琴 → 小提琴
violin::/ˌvaɪəˈlɪn/:n. 小提琴:The violinist performed a stunning solo at the concert.
10. weep /wiːp/ v. 哭泣；流泪；渗出
📖 英文解释
To shed tears; to cry; to ooze liquid slowly.
✏️ 例句
She wept when she heard the sad news.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
weep for joy 喜极而泣；weep over 为…哭泣；weep + tears 流泪；weeping willow 垂柳
🧠 助记
weep发音似「哭」→ 哭泣
weep::/wiːp/:v. 哭泣:He wept openly at his father's funeral.
11. courtesy /ˈkɜːtəsi/ n. 礼貌；谦恭；好意
📖 英文解释
Polite behavior; a polite remark or action; something provided free of charge.
✏️ 例句
He held the door open out of courtesy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
by courtesy of 承蒙…的好意；courtesy call 礼节性拜访；common courtesy 基本礼貌；courteous adj. 有礼貌的
🧠 助记
court（宫廷）+ esy → 宫廷礼仪 → 礼貌
courtesy::/ˈkɜːtəsi/:n. 礼貌:It is common courtesy to reply to emails within 24 hours.
12. doom /duːm/ n./v. 厄运；注定；末日
📖 英文解释
Death or a terrible fate; to make someone certain to suffer a terrible fate.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
doom发音似「冬末」→ 冬天末日之感 → 厄运、末日
doom::/duːm/:n./v. 厄运:Poor planning doomed the mission to failure.
13. inhabit /ɪnˈhæbɪt/ v. 栖息；居住于
📖 英文解释
To live in or occupy a place; to be present in.
✏️ 例句

🧠 助记

inhabit::/ɪnˈhæbɪt/:v. 栖息:Scientists discovered new creatures that inhabit the deep ocean.
14. preferable /ˈprefrəbl/ adj. 更好的；更可取的；更合适的

preferable::/ˈprefrəbl/:adj. 更好的:Learning online is preferable to commuting long distances every day.

chamber::/ˈtʃeɪmbə(r)/:n. 室:The trade dispute was discussed in the chamber of commerce.

enlighten::/ɪnˈlaɪtn/:v. 启发:The documentary sought to enlighten the public about climate change.

pan::/pæn/:n. 平底锅:The film was panned by critics for its weak plot.
