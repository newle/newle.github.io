1. bold /boʊld/ adj. 大胆的；粗体的
📖 英文解释
willing to take risks; not afraid of danger; (of text) printed in thick dark letters
✏️ 例句
She made a bold decision to quit her job and start her own business.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常见搭配：bold move（大胆的举动）；bold claim（大胆的声明）；be bold enough to do sth
🧠 助记
bold = b + old，'老' (old) 年人依然'勇敢'，真大胆！
bold::/boʊld/:adj. 大胆的:It takes a bold leader to challenge the status quo.
2. clarity /ˈklærəti/ n. 清晰；明确
📖 英文解释
the quality of being clear and easy to understand; the ability to think clearly
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：clarity of thought（思路清晰）；with clarity（清楚地）；seek clarity on sth（寻求对某事的明确说明）
🧠 助记
clarity = clear + ity，clear就是清楚，加名词后缀-ity = 清晰
clarity::/ˈklærəti/:n. 清晰:We need clarity on the new policy before proceeding.

dirt听起来像'脏'的拼音，脏的地方就是dirt！
dirt::/dɜːrt/:n. 污垢:Remove the dirt from your shoes before entering the house.

disclose::/dɪsˈkloʊz/:v. 揭露:The whistleblower decided to disclose the corruption scandal.

embody::/ɪmˈbɒdi/:v. 体现:Her work embodies the principles of scientific research.

con-（共同）+ fer（带来）= 大家把意见带到一起 = 商讨
confer::/kənˈfɜːr/:v. 商讨:The president conferred with his advisers before making the decision.
