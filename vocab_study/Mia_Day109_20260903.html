1. discharge /dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒ/ v./n. 释放；排出；履行（职责）；解雇
📖 英文解释
to officially allow someone to leave; to release a substance; to perform a duty; to dismiss from employment
✏️ 例句
The patient was discharged from hospital after a week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：discharge from（从……释放/排出）；discharge duties（履行职责）；discharge sewage（排放污水）
🧠 助记
dis-（解除）+ charge（负担、装载）= 解除负担 = 释放；解除装载 = 排出
discharge::/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒ/:v./n. 释放:The factory was fined for discharging toxic waste into the river.
2. drain /dreɪn/ v. 排出；耗尽；n. 下水道；消耗
📖 英文解释
to cause liquid to flow away; to use up resources gradually; a pipe that carries away water or waste
✏️ 例句
The long project drained all his energy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：brain drain（人才外流）；drain of resources（资源消耗）；down the drain（白费）；drain on（对……的消耗）
🧠 助记
drain = d + rain（雨）= 把雨水排走 = 排出；不断排出精力 = 耗尽
drain::/dreɪn/:v. 排出:The war proved to be a serious drain on the national economy.

📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
Please take out the garbage before you go to bed.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研延伸：garbage in, garbage out（输入垃圾，输出垃圾）；garbage collection（垃圾收集，也指编程中的内存回收）；garbage bin（垃圾桶）
🧠 助记
garbage = gar（嘎）+ bage，嘎嘎响地扔垃圾 = 垃圾
garbage::/ˈɡɑːrbɪdʒ/:n. 垃圾:The report was dismissed as garbage by leading experts in the field.

考研搭配：handicap sb（妨碍某人）；be handicapped by（受……妨碍）；physical handicap（身体残障）

handicap::/ˈhændɪkæp/:n./v. 障碍:The language barrier proved to be a major handicap for the foreign students.
