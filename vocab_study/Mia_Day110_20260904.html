1. sweater /ˈswetər/ n. 毛衣；运动衫
📖 英文解释
a warm knitted garment worn on the upper body
✏️ 例句
She put on a thick sweater to keep warm in the cold office.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研延伸：sweater来自sweat（出汗），原指运动出汗时穿的衣服；pullover（套头毛衣）；cardigan（开衫）
🧠 助记
sweater = sweat（汗）+ er，流汗时穿的衣服 = 运动衫；毛衣
sweater::/ˈswetər/:n. 毛衣:The children wore bright red sweaters for the school photo.

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：trash talk（诋毁性言论）；trash can（垃圾桶）；trash bin（垃圾箱）；white trash（白人贫民，贬义）
🧠 助记
trash = t + rash（皮疹），皮肤上的废物/垃圾 = 垃圾
trash::/træʃ/:n. 垃圾:The critics trashed the film, but audiences loved it.

🧠 助记
whisper = whis（嘶）+ per（per=through穿过）= 声音细细穿过 = 低语
whisper::/ˈwɪspər/:v./n. 低语:There were whispers in the corridors about the company's impending merger.
