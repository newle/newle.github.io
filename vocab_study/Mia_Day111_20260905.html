1. duration /djʊˈreɪʃn/ n. 持续时间；期间
📖 英文解释
the length of time that something lasts or continues
✏️ 例句
The duration of the concert was about two hours.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
for the duration of (在……期间); short/long duration; duration of a contract
🧠 助记
dur-(持久) + -ation(名词后缀) → 持续的时间段
duration::/djʊˈreɪʃn/:n. 持续时间:The war lasted for the duration of four years.
2. resent /rɪˈzent/ v. 愤恨；憎恶
📖 英文解释
to feel bitter or indignant about someone or a situation
✏️ 例句
She resented being treated like a child.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
resent sb./sth.; resent doing sth.; deeply/bitterly resent
🧠 助记
re-(反复) + sent(感受) → 反复感受到不满 → 愤恨
resent::/rɪˈzent/:v. 愤恨:He resented the fact that she got all the praise.
3. slack /slæk/ adj. 松弛的；懈怠的 n. 松弛
📖 英文解释
not tight or tense; not putting enough care or effort into work
✏️ 例句
The rope went slack after the knot was loosened.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
slack off(偷懒); take up the slack(补缺); slack period(淡季)
🧠 助记
联想 slick(光滑) → 绳子松松地滑下去
slack::/slæk/:adj. 松弛的:Production slowed during the slack season.
4. unify /ˈjuːnɪfaɪ/ v. 统一；使一致
📖 英文解释
to join or merge separate things so they form a single unit
✏️ 例句
The new policy aims to unify the fragmented education system.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
unify + noun; unification(统一,名词); unified standard
🧠 助记
uni-(一) + -fy(使) → 使成为一个 → 统一
unify::/ˈjuːnɪfaɪ/:v. 统一:The government sought to unify the nation under one law.
5. upright /ˈʌpraɪt/ adj. 笔直的；正直的 adv. 竖直地
📖 英文解释
vertical; morally honest and responsible
✏️ 例句
He was known as an upright judge who never accepted bribes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stand upright(站直); an upright citizen(正直公民); upright posture
🧠 助记
up(向上) + right(正确) → 站得直、行得正
upright::/ˈʌpraɪt/:adj. 笔直的:She sat upright in the chair during the interview.
6. chew /tʃuː/ v. 咀嚼；深思
📖 英文解释
to bite and grind food with teeth; to think about something carefully
✏️ 例句
Chew your food thoroughly before swallowing.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
chew on(思考); chew over(反复思量); chew the fat(闲聊)
🧠 助记
发音似嚼，直接联想咀嚼动作
chew::/tʃuː/:v. 咀嚼:He chewed over the problem before giving an answer.
7. dense /dens/ adj. 密集的；浓密的；迟钝的
📖 英文解释
closely packed together; thick; slow to understand
✏️ 例句
The forest was so dense that sunlight barely reached the ground.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dense population/fog/text; density(密度,名词)
🧠 助记
den(兽穴) + se → 兽穴里拥挤密集
dense::/dens/:adj. 密集的:The city has a dense network of public transport.
8. finite /ˈfaɪnaɪt/ adj. 有限的；有穷的
📖 英文解释
having a limit or end; not infinite
✏️ 例句
Resources are finite and must be managed wisely.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
finite resources/time; finite verb(限定动词); 对比 infinite
🧠 助记
fin-(结束，如 finish, final) → 有终点的 → 有限的
finite::/ˈfaɪnaɪt/:adj. 有限的:The planet's fossil fuels are finite.
9. impatient /ɪmˈpeɪʃnt/ adj. 不耐烦的；急切的
📖 英文解释
easily annoyed by delays; restlessly eager
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
im-(否定) + patient(耐心的) → 不耐心的
impatient::/ɪmˈpeɪʃnt/:adj. 不耐烦的:She was impatient to hear the results of her exam.
10. intuition /ˌɪntjuˈɪʃn/ n. 直觉；直觉力
📖 英文解释
the ability to understand something immediately without reasoning
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
in-(内在) + tuition(教导) → 内在的知识 → 直觉
intuition::/ˌɪntjuˈɪʃn/:n. 直觉:He acted on intuition and made the right call.
11. boil /bɔɪl/ v./n. 沸腾；煮沸；疖子
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
联想波澜——水波沸腾
boil::/bɔɪl/:v./n. 沸腾:The whole issue boils down to a lack of communication.
12. bureaucracy /bjʊˈrɒkrəsi/ n. 官僚主义；官僚机构
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
bureau(办公桌/机构) + -cracy(统治) → 机构统治 → 官僚体制
bureaucracy::/bjʊˈrɒkrəsi/:n. 官僚主义:The reform was stalled by endless bureaucracy.
13. comprehend /ˌkɒmprɪˈhend/ v. 理解；领悟

comprehend::/ˌkɒmprɪˈhend/:v. 理解:She could not comprehend why he had lied.
14. framework /ˈfreɪmwɜːk/ n. 框架；体系；结构

15. hamper /ˈhæmpə/ v. 阻碍；妨碍 n. 大篮子
