1. flock /flɒk/ n. 群；大批 v. 聚集；成群结队
📖 英文解释
a group of birds, sheep, or people; to gather or move in large numbers
✏️ 例句
Tourists flock to the city every summer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a flock of birds/sheep; flock to(蜂拥至); flock together(聚集在一起)
🧠 助记
fl-(飞) + ock → 一大群鸟飞来
flock::/flɒk/:n. 群:People flocked to see the new exhibition.
2. hybrid /ˈhaɪbrɪd/ n./adj. 混合体；杂种；混合动力的
📖 英文解释
something made from two different elements; a plant or animal of mixed origin
✏️ 例句
The company produces a range of hybrid electric vehicles.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hybrid car/model/system; hybrid approach; hybrid learning(混合式学习)
🧠 助记
hy-(两者) + brid(桥梁) → 横跨两者的混合体
hybrid::/ˈhaɪbrɪd/:n./adj. 混合体:The hybrid approach combines online and in-person learning.
3. infinite /ˈɪnfɪnət/ adj. 无限的；无穷的
📖 英文解释
limitless or endless in space, extent, or size
✏️ 例句
The universe appears to be infinite in size.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
infinite patience/possibilities; to an infinite degree; infinity(n. 无限)
🧠 助记
in-(否定) + finite(有限的) → 无限的
infinite::/ˈɪnfɪnət/:adj. 无限的:There seem to be infinite ways to solve this problem.
4. intrinsic /ɪnˈtrɪnsɪk/ adj. 内在的；本质的；固有的
📖 英文解释
belonging to the essential nature of something; inherent
✏️ 例句
Hard work has intrinsic value beyond just earning money.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
intrinsic value/motivation/worth; intrinsically(adv.); intrinsic to sth.
🧠 助记
intr-(内部) + insic → 内部存在的 → 内在的
intrinsic::/ɪnˈtrɪnsɪk/:adj. 内在的:Learning has intrinsic value regardless of career outcomes.
5. masterpiece /ˈmɑːstəpiːs/ n. 杰作；代表作；巨著
📖 英文解释
a work of outstanding artistry, skill, or workmanship
✏️ 例句
The novel is widely regarded as a literary masterpiece.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a masterpiece of art/literature/engineering; create a masterpiece
🧠 助记
master(大师) + piece(作品) → 大师级作品 → 杰作
masterpiece::/ˈmɑːstəpiːs/:n. 杰作:The painting is considered a masterpiece of the Renaissance.
6. vulgar /ˈvʌlɡə/ adj. 粗俗的；庸俗的；通俗的
📖 英文解释
lacking sophistication; making explicit reference to sex or bodily functions
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
vulgar language/display/taste; vulgarity(n. 粗俗); vulgarly(adv.)
🧠 助记
vul-(多)+ gar → 联想人太多嘈杂粗俗
vulgar::/ˈvʌlɡə/:adj. 粗俗的:The advertisement was criticized for its vulgar imagery.
7. aviation /ˌeɪviˈeɪʃn/ n. 航空；飞行；航空业
📖 英文解释
the flying or operating of aircraft; the aircraft industry

aviation::/ˌeɪviˈeɪʃn/:n. 航空:She pursued a career in aviation after graduating.
8. gentle /ˈdʒentl/ adj. 温柔的；和缓的；轻柔的

9. mud /mʌd/ n. 泥；烂泥 v. 弄脏；混淆

mud::/mʌd/:n. 泥:His reputation was dragged through the mud by the press.
10. navigation /ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃn/ n. 导航；航行；航海术
