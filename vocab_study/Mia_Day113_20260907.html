1. tender /ˈtendə/ adj. 温柔的；嫩的；疼痛的 v. 提出 n. 招标
📖 英文解释
gentle and loving; soft and easy to chew; to formally offer something
✏️ 例句
She spoke tender words of comfort to the grieving family.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tender age(幼小年龄); tender offer(收购要约); tender for a contract(投标)
🧠 助记
tend-(照料) + er → 照料他人的温柔
tender::/ˈtendə/:adj. 温柔的:The company put in a tender for the construction project.

🧠 助记
premi-(第一) + er → 第一位置的人/事
premier::/ˈpremiə/:n. 总理:She is one of the world's premier experts in climate science.
