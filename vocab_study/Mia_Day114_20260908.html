1. weave /wiːv/ v. 编织；穿行；n. 织法
📖 英文解释
To make fabric or baskets by crossing threads or strips over and under each other; to move by turning and changing direction.
✏️ 例句
She weaved her way through the crowded market, pausing to admire the handwoven cloth.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ weave through / between 穿行于（高频写作搭配） ★ weave sth into sth 将…编织入；将…融入 ☆ weave a story / plot 构筑情节 辨析：weave / knit：weave 指梭织（经纬交叉）；knit 指针织（线圈套接）
🧠 助记
weave = we + ave（大道）——我们在大道上交叉穿行，像编织一样
weave::/wiːv/:v. 编织:She weaves traditional patterns into modern designs.
2. blur /blɜːr/ v./n. 模糊；使模糊不清
📖 英文解释
To make or become unclear or less distinct; a smear or vague shape.
✏️ 例句
Years of overwork had begun to blur the distinction between his professional and personal life.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ blur the line/boundary between A and B 模糊A与B之间的界限（考研写作高频） ★ blur one's vision 使视线模糊 ☆ in a blur 一片模糊；晕晕乎乎 辨析：blur / obscure：blur 侧重轮廓变模糊；obscure 侧重遮蔽、晦涩
🧠 助记
blur → 联想 blue（蓝色）在水中晕开，变得模糊
blur::/blɜːr/:v./n. 模糊:Fatigue can blur your judgment.
3. contemplate /ˈkɒntəmpleɪt/ v. 沉思；打算；凝视
📖 英文解释
To think deeply and at length about something; to consider doing something; to look at thoughtfully.
✏️ 例句
The researcher sat quietly by the window, contemplating the implications of her latest findings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ contemplate doing sth 打算做某事（不接不定式） ★ contemplate the possibility of 考虑…的可能性 ☆ in contemplation 在考虑中 辨析：contemplate / ponder / meditate：contemplate 兼含凝视与深思；ponder 更强调反复权衡；meditate 偏冥想
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ temple（神庙）+ ate——大家在神庙里沉思冥想
contemplate::/ˈkɒntəmpleɪt/:v. 沉思:He contemplated changing careers after graduation.
4. discriminate /dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪt/ v. 区分；辨别；歧视
📖 英文解释
To recognize a distinction between things; to treat a person or group unfairly based on prejudice.
✏️ 例句
The law prohibits employers from discriminating against workers on the basis of gender or race.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ discriminate between A and B 区分A与B ★ discriminate against sb 歧视某人（高频考研搭配） ☆ discrimination n. 歧视；辨别力 辨析：discriminate / distinguish：discriminate 常含价值判断（含歧视义）；distinguish 更中性
🧠 助记
dis（否定）+ criminal（罪犯）+ ate——不该因某人像罪犯就歧视他
discriminate::/dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪt/:v. 区分:It is illegal to discriminate against minorities.

handsome::/ˈhænsəm/:adj. 英俊的:The company made a handsome profit last year.

30. bulb /bʌlb/ n. 电灯泡；球茎；球状物

A rounded glass enclosure containing a wire filament that produces light when electricity passes through; a rounded underground storage organ of a plant.

Switching from incandescent bulbs to LED lighting can dramatically reduce energy consumption.
