1. erect /ɪˈrekt/ v. 建立；竖立；adj. 直立的；笔直的
📖 英文解释
To build or put up a structure; to stand straight and tall; upright in posture.
✏️ 例句
The city erected a monument in the central square to commemorate the victims of the disaster.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ erect a building/statue/barrier 建立/竖立建筑/雕像/屏障 ★ stand erect 笔直站立 ☆ erection n. 建造；建筑物 辨析：erect / construct / build：erect 强调竖起/建立；construct 强调工程建造；build 最通用
🧠 助记
e + rect（直的，rectangle 矩形）——笔直竖起来，就是建立/直立
erect::/ɪˈrekt/:v. 建立:The government erected barriers along the flood-prone riverbank.
2. expire /ɪkˈspaɪər/ v. 期满；到期；终止；死亡
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ expire on a date 在某日到期 ★ a contract/passport/licence expires 合同/护照/执照到期 ☆ expiry n. 到期；expiry date 有效期 辨析：expire / lapse：expire 正式用语；lapse 强调因疏忽而失效
🧠 助记
ex（出）+ pire（fire，火）——火焰熄灭呼出来，就是到期/终止
expire::/ɪkˈspaɪər/:v. 期满:The warranty on the product will expire next month.

29. quantify /ˈkwɒntɪfaɪ/ v. 量化；用数字表示

It is difficult to quantify the long-term social benefits of investing in early childhood education.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ quantify the impact/risk/benefit 量化影响/风险/收益 ★ difficult to quantify 难以量化（考研阅读常见） ☆ quantitative adj. 定量的；quantity n. 数量 辨析：quantify / measure：quantify 侧重转化为数字；measure 泛指测量

quantify::/ˈkwɒntɪfaɪ/:v. 量化:Economists attempt to quantify the cost of environmental degradation.
30. respective /rɪˈspektɪv/ adj. 各自的；各个的
📖 英文解释
Belonging or relating separately to each of the people or things previously mentioned.
✏️ 例句
The students returned to their respective classrooms after the assembly in the main hall.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ their respective roles/duties 各自的角色/职责 ★ respectively adv. 分别地（常用于列举） ☆ respect v. 尊重（注意区分 respective 和 respectful） 辨析：respective / individual：respective 强调各归其主；individual 强调每个人单独
🧠 助记
respect（尊重各自）+ ive——各自的，各有所属
respective::/rɪˈspektɪv/:adj. 各自的:The two teams returned to their respective hotels after the match.
