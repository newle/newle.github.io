1. riot /ˈraɪət/ n. 骚乱；暴乱；v. 暴动；纵情享乐
📖 英文解释
A violent disturbance of the peace by a crowd; to take part in a public disturbance.
✏️ 例句
The government deployed riot police to maintain order after the protest turned violent.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ a riot breaks out 骚乱爆发 ★ riot police 防暴警察 ★ run riot 横行无忌；疯长（植物） ☆ rioter n. 暴徒；rioting n. 暴动 辨析：riot / protest / uprising：riot 强调暴力失控；protest 可和平；uprising 含政治反抗义
🧠 助记
riot 谐音『乱跑的』——人群乱跑，就是骚乱
riot::/ˈraɪət/:n. 骚乱:The riot was sparked by long-standing social inequality and economic frustration.

🧠 助记
situ（原位，in situ 原位）+ ate——放在原位，就是坐落/置于
situate::/ˈsɪtʃueɪt/:v. 使坐落于:The hotel is beautifully situated overlooking the lake.
