1. nod /nɒd/ v./n. 点头；打盹
📖 英文解释
to move your head up and down as a way of agreeing or greeting; a movement of the head
✏️ 例句
She nodded in agreement when the teacher explained the solution.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nod at/to sb 向某人点头；nod off 打盹；give a nod 示意
🧠 助记
发音像'no'但意思是同意——点头表示yes，摇头表示no，别搞混！
nod::/nɒd/:v./n. 点头:He nodded to show he understood.
2. simplicity /sɪmˈplɪsɪti/ n. 简单；朴素；简朴
📖 英文解释
the quality of being easy to understand or do; the quality of having no unnecessary features
✏️ 例句
The simplicity of his design made it both elegant and functional.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
simplicity of design/style 设计/风格的简洁；for simplicity 为简便起见
🧠 助记
simple（简单的）+ icity → simplicity，simple的名词形式
simplicity::/sɪmˈplɪsɪti/:n. 简单:The beauty of the plan lies in its simplicity.
3. superstition /ˌsuːpəˈstɪʃn/ n. 迷信；迷信行为
📖 英文解释
a belief or practice that is not based on reason or scientific knowledge
✏️ 例句
Breaking a mirror is considered bad luck in many superstitions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
superstition about sth 关于某事的迷信；superstitious adj. 迷信的
🧠 助记
super（超过）+ stition（站立，来自stand）→ 超越理性站立的信仰 = 迷信
superstition::/ˌsuːpəˈstɪʃn/:n. 迷信:He refuses to walk under ladders because of superstition.
4. triple /ˈtrɪpl/ adj./v./n. 三倍的；增至三倍；三倍
📖 英文解释
three times as much or as many; to make something three times as large
✏️ 例句
The company's profits tripled in the last three years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
triple the amount/size 三倍的数量/大小；triple jump 三级跳远
🧠 助记
tri（三）+ ple → triple，tri是三的前缀，如triangle三角形
triple::/ˈtrɪpl/:adj./v./n. 三倍的:Production has tripled since the new factory opened.
5. unfold /ʌnˈfəʊld/ v. 展开；显露；逐渐发展
📖 英文解释
to open or spread out; to gradually become known or to develop
✏️ 例句
The story unfolds slowly, revealing the truth chapter by chapter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
unfold a plan/story 展开计划/故事；events unfold 事态发展
🧠 助记
un（打开）+ fold（折叠）= 打开折叠 = 展开，引申为故事情节展开
unfold::/ʌnˈfəʊld/:v. 展开:The dramatic events unfolded over several days.
6. accountant /əˈkaʊntənt/ n. 会计师；会计员
📖 英文解释
a person whose job is to keep or inspect financial accounts
✏️ 例句
She hired an accountant to help prepare her tax returns.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
certified accountant 注册会计师；chartered accountant 特许会计师
🧠 助记
account（账目）+ ant（人）= 管账目的人 = 会计师
accountant::/əˈkaʊntənt/:n. 会计师:The accountant found several errors in the financial report.
7. bunch /bʌntʃ/ n. 束；串；一群 v. 聚集成堆
📖 英文解释
a number of things of the same type fastened or growing together; a group of people
✏️ 例句
She brought a bunch of flowers to the hospital.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a bunch of grapes/keys 一串葡萄/一串钥匙；bunch together 聚集在一起
🧠 助记
bunch听起来像'绑在一起'，一bunch就是一bundle，一堆捆在一起的东西
bunch::/bʌntʃ/:n. 束:A bunch of students gathered outside the classroom.
8. elder /ˈeldə(r)/ adj./n. 年长的；长者；长辈
📖 英文解释
older (used of family members); a person who is older and has more experience
✏️ 例句
We should respect our elders and learn from their experience.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
elder sibling 年长的兄弟姐妹；village elders 村中长老；elder statesman 政界元老
🧠 助记
old（老的）的比较级变形 → elder，专用于人，尤其是家庭成员
elder::/ˈeldə(r)/:adj./n. 年长的:Her elder brother works as an engineer.
9. erase /ɪˈreɪz/ v. 擦掉；抹去；消除
📖 英文解释
to remove writing or other marks by rubbing; to destroy completely
✏️ 例句
He tried to erase the bad memories of his childhood.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
erase sth from sth 从某处抹去某物；eraser n. 橡皮擦；erasure n. 擦除
🧠 助记
e + rase（刮）= 刮擦掉 = 擦除，联想用橡皮erase错误
erase::/ɪˈreɪz/:v. 擦掉:The data was erased from the computer's hard drive.
10. fearful /ˈfɪəfl/ adj. 害怕的；可怕的；担心的
📖 英文解释
feeling afraid; causing fear; very great or terrible
✏️ 例句
She was fearful of making mistakes in front of the audience.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fearful of/that... 担心……；fearful accident/storm 可怕的事故/风暴
🧠 助记
fear（恐惧）+ ful（充满）= 充满恐惧的 = 害怕的
fearful::/ˈfɪəfl/:adj. 害怕的:The child was fearful of the dark.
11. glow /ɡləʊ/ v./n. 发光；发红光；容光焕发
📖 英文解释
to produce a steady light; to look attractive because you feel happy or healthy
✏️ 例句
Her cheeks glowed with health after the long walk.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
glow with pride/health 因骄傲/健康而容光焕发；a warm glow 温暖的光芒
🧠 助记
glow联想glow-worm萤火虫，它发出柔和的glow光芒
glow::/ɡləʊ/:v./n. 发光:The embers still glowed in the fireplace.
12. holy /ˈhəʊli/ adj. 神圣的；圣洁的；虔诚的
📖 英文解释
considered to be sacred; devoted to the service of God
✏️ 例句
Jerusalem is a holy city for three major religions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the Holy Bible/Land 圣经/圣地；holy war 圣战；holy water 圣水
🧠 助记
联想'whole'（完整）→ 神圣的东西是完整无缺的，holy是神圣完整的
holy::/ˈhəʊli/:adj. 神圣的:They made a pilgrimage to the holy shrine.
13. inclusive /ɪnˈkluːsɪv/ adj. 包括的；包含一切的；无差别的
📖 英文解释
including everything or everyone; not excluding any particular group
✏️ 例句
The price is inclusive of all taxes and service charges.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
include（包含）→ inclusive，包含一切的，对比exclusive（排他的）
inclusive::/ɪnˈkluːsɪv/:adj. 包括的:The resort offers an all-inclusive package for families.
14. jar /dʒɑː(r)/ n. 罐子；广口瓶 v. 使不舒服；震动
📖 英文解释
a wide-mouthed glass or ceramic container; to have an unpleasant or annoying effect
✏️ 例句
The loud noise jarred on her nerves during the exam.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a jar of jam/honey 一罐果酱/蜂蜜；jar on sb's nerves 使某人紧张不安
🧠 助记
jar既是罐子（装东西），又能'jar'（刺激神经），联想瓶子发出刺耳的声音
jar::/dʒɑː(r)/:n. 罐子:She kept her pencils in an old jam jar.
15. outward /ˈaʊtwəd/ adj./adv. 向外的；外表的；往外
📖 英文解释
moving towards or away from the outside; relating to how something appears rather than what it really is
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
outward appearance/sign 外表/外在表现；outward journey 出发旅程
🧠 助记
out（外）+ ward（方向）= 向外的方向，对比inward（向内的）
outward::/ˈaʊtwəd/:adj./adv. 向外的:Despite her outward confidence, she felt very nervous.
16. plague /pleɪɡ/ n. 瘟疫；灾难 v. 折磨；烦扰
📖 英文解释
a dangerous disease that spreads very quickly; to cause worry or pain over a period of time
✏️ 例句
Economic problems have plagued the country for decades.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the plague 鼠疫；plague sb with questions 不停地向某人提问；avoid like the plague 避之唯恐不及
🧠 助记
plague发音像'玩了个'游戏——这'游戏'就是黑死病瘟疫，联想中世纪欧洲
plague::/pleɪɡ/:n. 瘟疫:The project was plagued by technical problems.
17. refute /rɪˈfjuːt/ v. 驳斥；反驳；证明……错误
📖 英文解释
to prove that a statement or idea is wrong or false
✏️ 例句
The scientist refuted the claim with solid experimental evidence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
refute an argument/claim/theory 驳斥论点/主张/理论；refutation n. 反驳
🧠 助记
re（反）+ fute（来自confute驳斥）= 反过来说 = 驳斥，联想refuse（拒绝）的亲戚
refute::/rɪˈfjuːt/:v. 驳斥:She refuted the accusations with detailed evidence.
18. spontaneous /spɒnˈteɪniəs/ adj. 自发的；自然产生的；无拘束的
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
spont（自己）+ aneous → 自己发生的 = 自发的，联想'spontaneous'就像自动弹出来的
spontaneous::/spɒnˈteɪniəs/:adj. 自发的:Her smile was warm and completely spontaneous.

adhere::/ədˈhɪə(r)/:v. 粘附:The paint adheres well to most surfaces.
20. aluminum /əˈluːmɪnəm/ n. 铝（美式）；铝制品
📖 英文解释
a light, silver-coloured metal used for making containers, cooking equipment, etc.
✏️ 例句
Aluminum cans are lightweight and easy to recycle.

🧠 助记

aluminum::/əˈluːmɪnəm/:n. 铝（美式）:The frame is made of lightweight aluminum.
21. bleed /bliːd/ v. 流血；出血；渗出
📖 英文解释
to lose blood from the body; to lose money or resources over a period of time
✏️ 例句
He was bleeding heavily from the wound on his arm.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
bleed to death 流血致死；bleeding heart 滥好人；bleed dry 榨干（资源/金钱）
🧠 助记
blood（血液）→ bleed，blood里的oo变成ee = bleed，血液流出
bleed::/bliːd/:v. 流血:The cut on his finger began to bleed.
22. catalog /ˈkætəlɒɡ/ n. 目录；产品目录 v. 将……编入目录
📖 英文解释
a complete list of items, usually in alphabetical order; to make a list of all the items in something
✏️ 例句
The library has a comprehensive online catalog of its collection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a catalog of errors 一系列错误；catalog items 将物品编目；mail-order catalog 邮购目录
🧠 助记
cata（完全）+ log（列出）= 完全列出的清单 = 目录，catalogue是英式拼法
catalog::/ˈkætəlɒɡ/:n. 目录:She spent hours cataloging the museum's collection.
23. erupt /ɪˈrʌpt/ v. 爆发；喷发；突然发生
📖 英文解释
when a volcano erupts, it sends out rocks, lava etc.; to suddenly happen or start
✏️ 例句
The crowd erupted in cheers when the final goal was scored.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a volcano erupts 火山喷发；erupt in/into laughter/anger 突然大笑/发怒
🧠 助记
e（出）+ rupt（破裂）= 破裂冒出 = 爆发，interrupt是中间断裂
erupt::/ɪˈrʌpt/:v. 爆发:Violence erupted in the streets after the election results.
24. explosive /ɪkˈspləʊsɪv/ adj. 爆炸性的；激增的 n. 炸药
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
explode（爆炸）+ ive/sive = explosive，爆炸 → 爆炸性的
explosive::/ɪkˈspləʊsɪv/:adj. 爆炸性的:Experts safely removed the explosive device.
25. fluctuate /ˈflʌktʃueɪt/ v. 波动；起伏；变动

fluctuate::/ˈflʌktʃueɪt/:v. 波动:Her mood tends to fluctuate throughout the day.
