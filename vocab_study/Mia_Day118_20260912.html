1. parcel /ˈpɑːsl/ n. 包裹；邮包 v. 打包；分配
📖 英文解释
something wrapped in paper or put in a box to be sent by post; to divide something into parts
✏️ 例句
She received a parcel from her friend abroad containing local specialties.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
parcel post 包裹邮件；part and parcel of 重要组成部分；parcel out 分配
🧠 助记
parcel联想'partial'（部分），包裹是把东西分成部分打包，part + cel
parcel::/ˈpɑːsl/:n. 包裹:A large parcel was waiting for her at the post office.
2. sheep /ʃiːp/ n. 羊；绵羊；盲目跟从者
📖 英文解释
a farm animal with thick wool; someone who follows others without thinking
✏️ 例句
The shepherd guided his sheep across the mountain pass.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
black sheep 害群之马；follow like sheep 盲目跟风；sheep and goats 好人与坏人
🧠 助记
sheep的单复数相同，联想'sheep counting'数羊助眠，shee→sheer（纯粹）
sheep::/ʃiːp/:n. 羊:The black sheep of the family caused constant trouble.
3. summon /ˈsʌmən/ v. 召唤；传唤；鼓起（勇气）
📖 英文解释
to officially order someone to come to a place; to call for or gather courage or strength
✏️ 例句
He summoned all his courage to give the speech in public.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
summon sb to sth 传唤某人到某处；summon up 鼓起（勇气/力量）；summons n. 传票
🧠 助记
summon = sum（总和）+ mon（提醒）= 把所有力量召集起来 = 召唤
summon::/ˈsʌmən/:v. 召唤:She was summoned to appear before the committee.
4. terminal /ˈtɜːmɪnl/ n. 终点；终端；航站楼 adj. 晚期的；最终的
📖 英文解释
the end of something; a building at an airport where passengers arrive and depart; a fatal illness in its final stages
✏️ 例句
He was diagnosed with a terminal illness and given six months to live.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
terminal illness/cancer 晚期病/癌症；airport terminal 机场航站楼；computer terminal 电脑终端
🧠 助记
terminal来自terminus（终点），terminate（终止），都有结束的含义
terminal::/ˈtɜːmɪnl/:n. 终点:Flight departures are from Terminal 2 at the airport.
5. tremble /ˈtrembl/ v./n. 颤抖；战栗；摇动
📖 英文解释
to shake slightly because you are cold, frightened, or excited
✏️ 例句
Her hands were trembling as she opened the exam results.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tremble with fear/cold 因恐惧/寒冷而颤抖；tremble at the thought 一想到就不寒而栗
🧠 助记
tremble联想tremor（震动，如地震tremor），都表示震颤，tremble是主动颤抖
tremble::/ˈtrembl/:v./n. 颤抖:The old man's voice trembled as he told his story.
6. tribute /ˈtrɪbjuːt/ n. 贡品；颂词；致敬；进贡
📖 英文解释
something said or done to show respect or admiration; a payment made by a weaker group to a stronger one
✏️ 例句
The concert was a tribute to the legendary musician who had passed away.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pay tribute to sb 向某人致敬；a tribute to sb's hard work 对某人努力的褒奖
🧠 助记
tribute来自contribute（贡献），tri（给）+ bute（给），向上级进贡或致敬
tribute::/ˈtrɪbjuːt/:n. 贡品:The poem was written as a tribute to the fallen soldiers.
7. devil /ˈdevl/ n. 魔鬼；恶魔；调皮鬼
📖 英文解释
an evil spirit or the most powerful evil spirit; someone mischievous
✏️ 例句
The devil is in the details — small mistakes can ruin the whole project.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the devil 魔鬼；devil-may-care 无所顾忌的；between the devil and the deep blue sea 进退两难
🧠 助记
devil发音像'得了'，联想魔鬼'得了'人的灵魂，devil = evil + d（做了evil的事）
devil::/ˈdevl/:n. 魔鬼:Speak of the devil — here comes John just as we were talking about him.
8. diplomatic /ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk/ adj. 外交的；有手腕的；圆滑的
📖 英文解释
connected with diplomacy; good at dealing with people in a sensitive way
✏️ 例句
The ambassador handled the crisis with great diplomatic skill.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
diplomatic relations/mission 外交关系/使命；diplomatic immunity 外交豁免权
🧠 助记
diplomat（外交官）+ ic = diplomatic，外交官需要圆滑处事的能力
diplomatic::/ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk/:adj. 外交的:She gave a very diplomatic answer that offended no one.
9. displace /dɪsˈpleɪs/ v. 取代；移置；迫使离开
📖 英文解释
to take the place of someone or something; to force people to leave their homes
✏️ 例句
Automation has displaced many workers in the manufacturing industry.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
displace sb/sth 取代某人/某物；displaced person 流离失所的人；displacement n.
🧠 助记
dis（移走）+ place（位置）= 从位置上移走 = 取代、迁移
displace::/dɪsˈpleɪs/:v. 取代:Thousands of people were displaced by the flooding.
10. echo /ˈekəʊ/ n./v. 回声；回响；重复
📖 英文解释
a sound that you hear again after it reflects off a surface; to repeat or copy what someone says
✏️ 例句
His words echoed around the empty hall, creating an eerie atmosphere.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
echo chamber 回音室；echo sb's words/views 重复某人的话/观点；echo effect 回声效果
🧠 助记
Echo是希腊神话中只能重复别人话语的仙女，echo = 回声/重复
echo::/ˈekəʊ/:n./v. 回声:Her concerns echoed those of many students in the class.
11. evoke /ɪˈvəʊk/ v. 唤起；引起；召唤（情感/记忆）
📖 英文解释
to bring a feeling, memory or image into your mind
✏️ 例句
The old photo evoked memories of his happy childhood.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
evoke a response/feeling/memory 引起反应/感情/记忆；evocative adj. 令人回想的
🧠 助记
e（出）+ voke（声音，如vocal）= 把声音/情感唤出来 = 唤起，invoke是调用
evoke::/ɪˈvəʊk/:v. 唤起:The music evoked a sense of nostalgia in the audience.
12. funeral /ˈfjuːnərəl/ n. 葬礼；丧礼
📖 英文解释
a ceremony for burying or burning the body of a dead person
✏️ 例句
Hundreds of people attended the former president's funeral.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
funeral service/procession 葬礼仪式/送葬队伍；funeral home 殡仪馆；That's your funeral. 那是你的事。
🧠 助记
fun + eral → 没有'fun'的场合 = 葬礼（反向记忆），funeral对应birth（诞生）
funeral::/ˈfjuːnərəl/:n. 葬礼:She wore black to attend her colleague's funeral.
13. mammal /ˈmæml/ n. 哺乳动物
📖 英文解释
any animal that gives birth to live babies and feeds them on milk from the mother's body
✏️ 例句
Whales are the largest mammals on Earth, despite living in the ocean.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
marine mammal 海洋哺乳动物；mammalian adj. 哺乳动物的；mammal species 哺乳动物物种
🧠 助记
mammal来自mamma（妈妈/乳房），哺乳动物用妈妈的奶水喂养幼崽
mammal::/ˈmæml/:n. 哺乳动物:Dolphins are intelligent marine mammals with complex social behavior.
14. midst /mɪdst/ n. 中间；中部；当中
📖 英文解释
the middle of something; the time when something is happening
✏️ 例句
In the midst of the chaos, she remained surprisingly calm.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in the midst of sth 在某事进行中；in our midst 在我们当中；amid/amidst
🧠 助记
midst = mid（中间）+ st（加强）= 正中间，联想middle、midway都是mid前缀
midst::/mɪdst/:n. 中间:He left in the midst of an important meeting.
15. offset /ˈɒfset/ v. 抵消；补偿 n. 抵消；弥补
📖 英文解释
to balance one thing against another so that the effect is not as great
✏️ 例句
The government offered tax cuts to offset the rising cost of living.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
offset losses/costs 抵消损失/费用；carbon offset 碳抵消；offset against 用……抵消
🧠 助记
off（偏移）+ set（设置）= 通过偏移来平衡 = 抵消，联想两个相反的力互相抵消
offset::/ˈɒfset/:v. 抵消:Higher wages helped to offset the increase in living costs.
16. peasant /ˈpeznt/ n. 农民；庄稼人；农夫
📖 英文解释
a farmer who owns or rents a small piece of land (especially historically or in poor countries)
✏️ 例句
The peasants worked long hours in the fields to grow enough food.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
peasant farmer 农民；peasant uprising 农民起义；peasantry n. 农民阶级
🧠 助记
peasant联想peas（豌豆）+ ant（人）= 种豌豆的人 = 农民，形象记忆
peasant::/ˈpeznt/:n. 农民:Many peasants left the countryside to find work in the cities.
17. scold /skəʊld/ v. 责骂；训斥；斥责
📖 英文解释
to speak angrily to someone because they have done something wrong
✏️ 例句
The teacher scolded the student for cheating on the test.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
scold sb for sth 因某事责骂某人；scold sb harshly 严厉训斥某人
🧠 助记
scold发音像'斯扣得'——声音严厉地斥责，联想cold（冷）的语气骂人
scold::/skəʊld/:v. 责骂:She scolded her son for staying up too late.
18. slump /slʌmp/ v./n. 暴跌；倒下；萧条
📖 英文解释
to fall or drop suddenly; to suddenly fall or lean over; a sudden drop in price or value
✏️ 例句
Stock prices slumped dramatically following the negative earnings report.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
an economic slump 经济萧条；slump in prices/sales 价格/销量暴跌；slump into a chair 瘫坐在椅子上
🧠 助记
slump联想'slug'（弹丸打击），被打了一下就slump倒下了，价格也暴跌
slump::/slʌmp/:v./n. 暴跌:The housing market slumped after interest rates rose sharply.
19. tail /teɪl/ n. 尾巴；尾部 v. 跟踪；尾随
📖 英文解释
the part at the back end of an animal's body; to follow someone secretly
✏️ 例句
The detective was hired to tail the suspect for a week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tail off/away 逐渐减少；heads or tails 正面还是反面；turn tail 落荒而逃
🧠 助记
tail和tale同音，尾巴就像故事的尾声，结尾部分，联想detail（细节=详细的尾部）
tail::/teɪl/:n. 尾巴:The dog wagged its tail excitedly when it saw its owner.
20. typewriter /ˈtaɪpraɪtə(r)/ n. 打字机
📖 英文解释
a machine with keys that are pressed to print letters and numbers on paper
✏️ 例句
She could type sixty words per minute on the old typewriter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
electric typewriter 电动打字机；typewriter ribbon 打字机色带
🧠 助记
type（打字）+ writer（写作者）= 打字的写作机器 = 打字机，直接合成词
typewriter::/ˈtaɪpraɪtə(r)/:n. 打字机:The journalist worked through the night on his old typewriter.
21. accent /ˈæksənt/ n. 口音；重音；强调 v. 重读；强调
📖 英文解释
a way of pronouncing words characteristic of a region; emphasis on something
✏️ 例句
She spoke English with a strong French accent that charmed everyone.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
regional/foreign accent 地区口音/外国口音；put the accent on 着重强调；accentuate v. 强调
🧠 助记
ac（到）+ cent（中心）= 把重音放到中心位置 = 重音，口音就是特定发音重心
accent::/ˈæksənt/:n. 口音:His strong southern accent made him stand out in the class.
22. accordance /əˈkɔːdns/ n. 一致；符合；依照
📖 英文解释
agreement with something; the state of being in agreement
✏️ 例句
The project was carried out in accordance with the original plan.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in accordance with 与……一致；符合；according to的正式表达
🧠 助记
accord（和谐，如music accord和弦）+ ance = accordance，和谐一致地
accordance::/əˈkɔːdns/:n. 一致:All work must be done in accordance with safety regulations.
23. anniversary /ˌænɪˈvɜːsəri/ n. 周年纪念（日）
📖 英文解释
the date on which an important event happened in a previous year
✏️ 例句
They celebrated their 25th wedding anniversary with a trip to Paris.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
wedding/birthday anniversary 结婚/生日周年纪念；annual anniversary 每年的纪念日
🧠 助记
anni（年，如annual年度的）+ versary（转动）= 一年转一圈 = 周年纪念
anniversary::/ˌænɪˈvɜːsəri/:n. 周年纪念（日）:The company celebrated its 50th anniversary with a grand banquet.
24. banana /bəˈnɑːnə/ n. 香蕉
📖 英文解释
a long curved tropical fruit with a yellow skin
✏️ 例句
A banana provides a quick source of energy before exercise.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
go bananas 发疯；banana republic 香蕉共和国（政治不稳定的国家）；banana split 香蕉船（甜品）
🧠 助记
banana发音节奏ba-na-na像打鼓，黄色弯曲的水果，颜色像月亮
banana::/bəˈnɑːnə/:n. 香蕉:She slipped on a banana peel, just like in cartoons.
25. bloom /bluːm/ v./n. 开花；繁盛；兴旺
📖 英文解释

in bloom/full bloom 盛开；bloom into 成长为；algal bloom 藻类繁殖

bloom联想blossom（盛开），同义词，联想'blue moon'下花朵盛开
bloom::/bluːm/:v./n. 开花:The roses are in full bloom this time of year.
26. catastrophe /kəˈtæstrəfi/ n. 大灾难；灾祸；惨败
📖 英文解释
a sudden event that causes very great trouble or destruction
✏️ 例句
The oil spill was an environmental catastrophe that affected the entire coast.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
natural/environmental catastrophe 自然/环境灾难；catastrophic adj. 灾难性的
🧠 助记
cata（向下）+ strophe（转变）= 突然向下转变 = 灾难，戏剧结构中的转折点
catastrophe::/kəˈtæstrəfi/:n. 大灾难:The earthquake was a catastrophe that killed thousands of people.
27. certify /ˈsɜːtɪfaɪ/ v. 证明；证实；颁发证书
📖 英文解释
to officially state that something is true or correct
✏️ 例句
The doctor certified that the patient was fit to return to work.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
certify sb/sth as 证明某人/某事为……；certified accountant 注册会计师；certificate n. 证书
🧠 助记
certify来自certain（确定），cert（确定）+ ify（使……）= 使确定 = 证明
certify::/ˈsɜːtɪfaɪ/:v. 证明:The product was certified as safe for human consumption.
28. collide /kəˈlaɪd/ v. 碰撞；冲突；相撞
📖 英文解释
to hit something hard when moving; to come into conflict
✏️ 例句
The two trains collided head-on at a crossing, injuring many passengers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
collide with sth 与某物相撞；collision n. 碰撞；collide over sth 在某事上发生冲突
🧠 助记
col（共同）+ lide（打击）= 共同撞到一起 = 碰撞，类比collision insurance（碰撞险）
collide::/kəˈlaɪd/:v. 碰撞:Their interests inevitably collided during the negotiation.
29. cord /kɔːd/ n. 绳索；细绳；一根线；电线

umbilical cord 脐带；vocal cords 声带；extension cord 延长线；cord off 用绳索隔开

cord联想chord（和弦），绳子像吉他弦，都是cord/chord，一根线
cord::/kɔːd/:n. 绳索:She tripped over the phone cord in the dark.
30. denial /dɪˈnaɪəl/ n. 否认；拒绝；否定
📖 英文解释
a statement that something is not true; a refusal to allow someone to have something

deny（否认）→ denial，deny + al = denial，denial就是deny的名词形式
denial::/dɪˈnaɪəl/:n. 否认:Her denial of the accusations seemed unconvincing to the jury.
