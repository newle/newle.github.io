1. dormitory /ˈdɔːmɪtri/ n. 集体宿舍；学生宿舍楼
📖 英文解释
a large building at a school or college where students live
✏️ 例句
The university dormitory was crowded during the first week of term.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
student dormitory 学生宿舍；dormitory room 宿舍房间；dorm（口语缩写）
🧠 助记
dorm（睡觉，如dormant休眠）+ itory = dormitory，睡觉的地方 = 宿舍
dormitory::/ˈdɔːmɪtri/:n. 集体宿舍:He lived in the dormitory throughout his first year at university.
2. lemon /ˈlemən/ n. 柠檬；柠檬色；劣品
📖 英文解释
a yellow citrus fruit with sour juice; something of poor quality
✏️ 例句
She squeezed fresh lemon juice into the water to make lemonade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lemon juice/tree 柠檬汁/柠檬树；lemon yellow 柠檬黄；a lemon（俚）次品、废品
🧠 助记
lemon是酸的水果，'when life gives you lemons, make lemonade'逆境中找到出路
lemon::/ˈlemən/:n. 柠檬:The car turned out to be a lemon — it broke down every month.
3. ounce /aʊns/ n. 盎司（重量单位）；少量
📖 英文解释
a unit of weight equal to 28.35 grams; a very small amount
✏️ 例句
Every ounce of effort counts when preparing for a major competition.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fluid ounce 液量盎司；an ounce of 一点点的；not an ounce of 一点……都没有
🧠 助记
ounce联想once（一次），一盎司就是一小份，联想'an ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure'
ounce::/aʊns/:n. 盎司（重量单位）:She didn't have an ounce of selfishness in her.
4. pedestrian /pəˈdestriən/ n. 行人；步行者 adj. 步行的；平凡的
📖 英文解释
a person who is walking in a street or town; not interesting or imaginative
✏️ 例句
A pedestrian crossing was installed to protect people walking across the road.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pedestrian crossing 人行横道；pedestrian street 步行街；pedestrian writing 平淡的文章
🧠 助记
ped（脚，如pedal踏板）+ estrian = 用脚走的人 = 行人，pedestrian也可形容平淡无奇
pedestrian::/pəˈdestriən/:n. 行人:Pedestrians should use the crossing when the light turns green.
5. sail /seɪl/ v. 航行；驾船 n. 帆；航行
📖 英文解释
to travel on water in a boat or ship; a piece of cloth that catches wind to move a boat
✏️ 例句
They sailed across the Pacific Ocean in a small sailboat over three months.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
set sail 起航；sail through 顺利通过；under sail 扬帆；sail close to the wind 冒险
🧠 助记
sail和sale同音——扬帆(sail)出海做生意，做买卖(sale)赚钱
sail::/seɪl/:v. 航行:The ship set sail at dawn heading for the distant island.
6. salad /ˈsæləd/ n. 沙拉；色拉；生菜
📖 英文解释
a dish made from a mixture of vegetables, usually cold, often with a dressing
✏️ 例句
She prepared a fresh Caesar salad with homemade dressing for lunch.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fruit/green salad 水果/绿色沙拉；salad dressing 沙拉酱；salad days 青春岁月
🧠 助记
salad来自拉丁语sal（盐），古代用盐腌制蔬菜，salted vegetables = salad
salad::/ˈsæləd/:n. 沙拉:A healthy salad makes a great light lunch in summer.
7. scenario /səˈnɑːriəʊ/ n. 场景；方案；剧情梗概
📖 英文解释
a description of a possible situation or series of events
✏️ 例句
The team prepared for the worst-case scenario by developing a backup plan.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
worst-case/best-case scenario 最坏/最好的情况；disaster scenario 灾难情景
🧠 助记
scenario来自scene（场景），就是一个具体的场景或情景描述
scenario::/səˈnɑːriəʊ/:n. 场景:In the best-case scenario, the project will be completed by June.
8. sightseeing /ˈsaɪtsiːɪŋ/ n. 观光；游览；参观名胜
📖 英文解释
the activity of visiting famous or interesting places as a tourist
✏️ 例句
They spent the afternoon sightseeing in the historic center of the city.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
go sightseeing 去观光；sightseeing tour/bus 观光旅游/观光巴士；sightseeing spot 景点
🧠 助记
sight（视野/景色）+ see（看）+ ing = 观看景色 = 观光，非常直观的合成词
sightseeing::/ˈsaɪtsiːɪŋ/:n. 观光:We spent three days sightseeing in Rome and Florence.
9. silk /sɪlk/ n. 丝绸；蚕丝 adj. 丝质的
📖 英文解释
a delicate, soft cloth made from thread produced by silkworms
✏️ 例句
China has been famous for its silk production for thousands of years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
silk road 丝绸之路；silk worm 蚕；silk screen 丝网印刷；as smooth as silk 光滑如丝
🧠 助记
silk发音圆滑流畅，就像丝绸触感一样smooth，联想丝绸之路Silk Road
silk::/sɪlk/:n. 丝绸:She wore a beautiful silk dress to the wedding ceremony.
10. slope /sləʊp/ n./v. 斜坡；坡度；倾斜
📖 英文解释
a surface that is higher at one end than the other; to have or form a sloping surface
✏️ 例句
The skiers zoomed down the steep slope at high speed.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ski slope 滑雪坡；slope of a hill 山坡；slippery slope 不可收拾的局面；on the slope 处于下坡
🧠 助记
slope联想'slip'（滑），在slope上容易slip，斜坡让人滑倒
slope::/sləʊp/:n./v. 斜坡:The road slopes sharply downward after the village.
11. sob /sɒb/ v./n. 哭泣；抽噎；呜咽
📖 英文解释
to cry noisily, taking in sharp breaths; the sound of crying in this way
✏️ 例句
She sobbed quietly after hearing the sad news about her grandmother.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sob one's heart out 痛哭流涕；sob story 令人同情的故事；in sobs 抽泣着
🧠 助记
sob象声词，听起来像抽泣的声音，吸鼻子的sniffle也类似，sob更强烈
sob::/sɒb/:v./n. 哭泣:The child was sobbing uncontrollably in the corner of the room.
12. warfare /ˈwɔːfeə(r)/ n. 战争；战斗；冲突
📖 英文解释
the activity of fighting in a war, especially using particular methods
✏️ 例句
Chemical warfare is banned under international humanitarian law.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
chemical/guerrilla/nuclear warfare 化学战/游击战/核战争；psychological warfare 心理战
🧠 助记
war（战争）+ fare（进行，如welfare福利）= 进行战争 = 战争，welfare是well-fare
warfare::/ˈwɔːfeə(r)/:n. 战争:Modern warfare relies heavily on technology and intelligence.
13. juvenile /ˈdʒuːvənaɪl/ adj. 青少年的；幼稚的 n. 青少年

🧠 助记
juvenile来自拉丁语juvenis（年轻的），联想junior（初级的），都是年轻相关
juvenile::/ˈdʒuːvənaɪl/:adj. 青少年的:The film was criticized for its juvenile humor and shallow plot.
14. pilgrim /ˈpɪlɡrɪm/ n. 朝圣者；香客；清教徒移民

pilgrim::/ˈpɪlɡrɪm/:n. 朝圣者:The pilgrims endured a long journey to reach the sacred shrine.

evade::/ɪˈveɪd/:v. 逃避:He managed to evade capture by hiding in the forest for days.

satur（充满，如satisfaction满足）+ ate = 使充满 = 饱和，联想'saturated solution'饱和溶液
saturate::/ˈsætʃəreɪt/:v. 使饱和:Heavy rain saturated the ground, causing widespread flooding.

20. continual /kənˈtɪnjuəl/ adj. 不断的；频繁的；连续发生的

continual::/kənˈtɪnjuəl/:adj. 不断的:The machine required continual maintenance to keep it running.

diligent联想'diligence'（勤奋），de + ligent（选择），精心选择努力工作 = 勤勉
diligent::/ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/:adj. 勤勉的:His diligent research led to a major scientific breakthrough.

exemplify::/ɪɡˈzemplɪfaɪ/:v. 举例说明:This building exemplifies the architectural style of the 1920s.
