1. preach /priːtʃ/ v. 说教；讲道；宣扬
📖 英文解释
to give a religious speech in a church; to advocate or urge (a belief or course of action) in an earnest or moralizing way
✏️ 例句
He preached that hard work was the key to success, but few students listened.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
preach to sb / preach about sth；考研常见搭配：preach the gospel（传福音）、preach a sermon（布道）；引申义：空洞说教
🧠 助记
pre（提前）+ ach（ache 痛苦）→ 提前让人感到痛苦的说教
preach::/priːtʃ/:v. 说教:The pastor preached a powerful sermon on forgiveness.
2. presently /ˈprezntli/ adv. 不久；目前；现在
📖 英文解释
soon; at the present time; currently
✏️ 例句
The manager is presently unavailable; please call back later.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考两义：① soon（不久）② now/currently（目前）；注意区分 currently（现在）与 recently（最近）
🧠 助记
present（现在）+ ly → 现在或即将到来的时间
presently::/ˈprezntli/:adv. 不久:She is presently working on a new research project.
3. prose /prəʊz/ n. 散文；平淡无奇的表达
📖 英文解释
written or spoken language in its ordinary form, without metrical structure; plain or dull writing
✏️ 例句
He admired the clarity and elegance of her prose style.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prose vs poetry（散文 vs 诗歌）；考研阅读常见：prose style、written in prose；prose fiction = 散文小说
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ se（自身）→ 向前流淌的语言 = 散文
prose::/prəʊz/:n. 散文:The novel is written in simple but beautiful prose.
4. shark /ʃɑːk/ n. 鲨鱼；骗子
📖 英文解释
a large predatory fish; informally, a person who swindles others
✏️ 例句
The loan sharks charged exorbitant interest rates.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考引申义：loan shark（高利贷者）；card shark（赌牌骗子）
🧠 助记
shark 的形状锋利如刀，代表危险的捕食者或骗子
shark::/ʃɑːk/:n. 鲨鱼:Beware of loan sharks who prey on desperate borrowers.
5. thirteen /ˌθɜːˈtiːn/ num. 十三
📖 英文解释
the number 13; one more than twelve
✏️ 例句
There are thirteen students in the advanced seminar.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
基础数词；考研中数字常出现在图表分析作文；thirteen = 13，thirty = 30，注意拼写区别
🧠 助记
three（三）+ teen（青少年后缀）→ 13
thirteen::/ˌθɜːˈtiːn/:num. 十三:The committee has thirteen members in total.
6. vertical /ˈvɜːtɪkl/ adj. 垂直的；竖直的
📖 英文解释
at right angles to a horizontal plane; going straight up or down
✏️ 例句
The cliff face was almost vertical, making the climb extremely dangerous.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
vertical vs horizontal（垂直 vs 水平）；vertical integration（垂直整合，经济学考研常考）
🧠 助记
vertex（顶点）→ vertical 从顶点向下垂直
vertical::/ˈvɜːtɪkl/:adj. 垂直的:The vertical drop of the waterfall is over 100 meters.
7. administer /ədˈmɪnɪstə/ v. 管理；执行；给予（药物）
📖 英文解释
to manage the affairs of; to give or apply (a drug, punishment, or oath) formally
✏️ 例句
The nurse administered the vaccine to the patients.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
administer a test（主持考试）、administer medicine（给药）、administer an oath（监誓）；administration（管理；政府）
🧠 助记
ad（向）+ minister（部长）→ 向部长汇报 = 管理；部长执行职务
administer::/ədˈmɪnɪstə/:v. 管理:The government administers public health programs nationwide.
8. aspire /əˈspaɪə/ v. 渴望；立志；有志于
📖 英文解释
to direct one's hopes or ambitions towards achieving something; to long or hope for
✏️ 例句
She aspired to become the first female president of the university.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
aspire to do sth / aspire to sth；aspiration（n. 抱负、理想）；考研常见：aspire to excellence
🧠 助记
a + spire（尖顶）→ 向尖顶攀登 = 渴望攀登高峰
aspire::/əˈspaɪə/:v. 渴望:Many young scientists aspire to win the Nobel Prize.
9. attorney /əˈtɜːni/ n. 律师；代理人
📖 英文解释
a person appointed to act for another in business or legal matters; a lawyer
✏️ 例句
The defendant's attorney argued that the evidence was inadmissible.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
attorney-at-law（执业律师）；power of attorney（授权委托书）；district attorney（地区检察官，缩写 DA）
🧠 助记
at（在）+ torn（撕碎）+ ey → 在法庭把对方论据撕碎的人 = 律师
attorney::/əˈtɜːni/:n. 律师:She hired an experienced attorney to handle the case.
10. classification /ˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ n. 分类；类别；等级划分
📖 英文解释
the action or process of classifying something according to shared qualities or characteristics
✏️ 例句
The classification of species is a fundamental task in biology.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
classify（v.）→ classification（n.）；考研常见：security classification（保密等级）、tax classification（税收分类）
🧠 助记
class（类别）+ ification（化）→ 分成类别的过程
classification::/ˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/:n. 分类:The library uses the Dewey Decimal classification system.
11. declaration /ˌdekləˈreɪʃn/ n. 宣告；声明；申报
📖 英文解释
a formal or explicit statement or announcement; the act of declaring
✏️ 例句
The Declaration of Independence was signed in 1776.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
declare（v.）→ declaration（n.）；customs declaration（海关申报）；declaration of war（宣战）
🧠 助记
de + clare（清楚）+ ation → 清楚地公开陈述 = 声明
declaration::/ˌdekləˈreɪʃn/:n. 宣告:The government issued an official declaration of emergency.
12. editorial /ˌedɪˈtɔːriəl/ n. 社论；编者按 adj. 编辑的
📖 英文解释
a newspaper or magazine article that expresses the editor's opinion; relating to editing
✏️ 例句
The newspaper published a strongly worded editorial criticizing the new policy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
editorial board（编辑委员会）；editorial staff（编辑人员）；考研阅读中 editorial 常出现在新闻媒体语境
🧠 助记
editor（编辑）+ ial → 编辑写的文章 = 社论
editorial::/ˌedɪˈtɔːriəl/:n. 社论:The editorial called for immediate reform of the education system.
13. explicit /ɪkˈsplɪsɪt/ adj. 明确的；清楚的；直截了当的
📖 英文解释
stated clearly and in detail, leaving no room for confusion or doubt
✏️ 例句
The contract contains explicit instructions about payment deadlines.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
explicit vs implicit（明确 vs 含蓄/隐含）；考研必考对比词；explicit content（露骨内容）
🧠 助记
ex（出来）+ plic（折叠）+ it → 把折叠的东西展开来 = 明确说出
explicit::/ɪkˈsplɪsɪt/:adj. 明确的:The teacher gave explicit instructions before the exam.
14. fruitful /ˈfruːtfl/ adj. 富有成效的；多产的；丰硕的
📖 英文解释
producing good or helpful results; productive; fertile
✏️ 例句
The negotiations proved fruitful, resulting in a comprehensive trade agreement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fruitful discussion/cooperation/research；fruit（果实）→ fruitful（结果实的 = 有成效的）
🧠 助记
fruit（果实）+ ful → 充满果实的 = 富有成效
fruitful::/ˈfruːtfl/:adj. 富有成效的:The two teams had a fruitful collaboration on the project.
15. meantime /ˈmiːntaɪm/ n./adv. 同时；其间；在此期间
📖 英文解释
the period of time between two events; at the same time; in the intervening time
✏️ 例句
The repairs will take two weeks; in the meantime, use the side entrance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in the meantime = meanwhile（同时，考研写作常用替换词）；meantime 多用于 in the meantime 结构
🧠 助记
mean（中间）+ time（时间）→ 中间的时间 = 同时、其间
meantime::/ˈmiːntaɪm/:n./adv. 同时:In the meantime, students should review their notes.
16. nightmare /ˈnaɪtmeə/ n. 恶梦；可怕的经历
📖 英文解释
a frightening or unpleasant dream; an extremely unpleasant event or situation
✏️ 例句
The traffic jam turned the morning commute into a nightmare.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
nightmare scenario（噩梦般的情景）；a nightmare for sb（对某人来说是噩梦）；考研写作可用于表达极端困境
🧠 助记
night（夜晚）+ mare（母马，传说中踩人胸口的恶灵）→ 夜间的恶灵 = 恶梦
nightmare::/ˈnaɪtmeə/:n. 恶梦:The exam was a nightmare for students who hadn't prepared.
17. ornament /ˈɔːnəmənt/ n. 装饰品；装饰 v. 装饰
📖 英文解释
a thing used to make something look more attractive; to make more attractive by adding decorative items
✏️ 例句
The Christmas tree was covered with colorful ornaments.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ornament vs decoration（装饰品，近义词）；ornamental（adj. 装饰性的）；考研偶见于艺术、建筑类阅读
🧠 助记
orn（拉丁语：装饰）+ ament → 装饰的东西
ornament::/ˈɔːnəmənt/:n. 装饰品:The garden was adorned with stone ornaments and fountains.
18. perpetual /pəˈpetʃuəl/ adj. 永久的；永恒的；不断的
📖 英文解释
never ending or changing; occurring repeatedly; so frequent as to seem endless
✏️ 例句
The northern regions experience perpetual daylight in summer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
perpetual motion（永动机）；in perpetual conflict（永久冲突）；perpetually（adv.）；区分：permanent（永久的，更正式）
🧠 助记
per（全程）+ pet（追赶）+ ual → 一直被追赶 = 不断的、永久的
perpetual::/pəˈpetʃuəl/:adj. 永久的:She lived in perpetual fear of losing her job.
19. pregnant /ˈpreɡnənt/ adj. 怀孕的；意味深长的
📖 英文解释
having a child or young developing inside the body; full of meaning or significance
✏️ 例句
Her words were pregnant with unspoken criticism.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pregnant pause（意味深长的停顿）；pregnant with meaning（充满含义）；考研常考引申义
🧠 助记
pre（之前）+ gnant（来自 genus 生育）→ 生育之前的状态 = 怀孕
pregnant::/ˈpreɡnənt/:adj. 怀孕的:There was a pregnant silence before she answered.
20. rally /ˈræli/ n. 集会；恢复 v. 集合；重新振作
📖 英文解释
a mass meeting for a common cause; to come together for united action; to recover in health
✏️ 例句
Thousands attended the political rally in the city center.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rally support（召集支持）；rally around（团结在...周围）；stock market rally（股市反弹，考研经济类常见）
🧠 助记
re + ally（盟友）→ 重新聚集盟友 = 集会、重新振作
rally::/ˈræli/:n. 集会:The team rallied in the second half to win the game.
21. rebel /ˈrebl/ (n.) /rɪˈbel/ (v.) n. 叛逆者 v. 反抗；造反
📖 英文解释
a person who resists authority; to resist or fight against authority or control
✏️ 例句
The rebels launched an attack on the government forces.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rebel against（反抗）；rebellion（n. 叛乱）；rebellious（adj. 叛逆的）；注意名词和动词重音不同
🧠 助记
re（再次）+ bel（战争，参考 bellicose）→ 再次开战 = 反抗
rebel::/ˈrebl/ (n.) /rɪˈbel/ (v.):n. 叛逆者 v. 反抗:Teenagers often rebel against their parents' authority.
22. successive /səkˈsesɪv/ adj. 连续的；接连的；相继的
📖 英文解释
following in order without interruption; consecutive
✏️ 例句
The team won five successive championships.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
successive governments（历届政府）；on successive days（连续几天）；注意区分：successful（成功的）vs successive（连续的）
🧠 助记
success（成功）+ ive → 一个接一个成功 = 连续的
successive::/səkˈsesɪv/:adj. 连续的:The country suffered three successive years of drought.
23. tin /tɪn/ n. 锡；马口铁；罐头
📖 英文解释
a silvery-white metallic element; a metal container for preserving food
✏️ 例句
The canned goods were stored in tin containers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tin can（锡罐）；tin foil（锡纸）；tinned food（罐装食品，英式英语）；元素符号 Sn
🧠 助记
tin 短小精悍，像一个小金属罐子
tin::/tɪn/:n. 锡:She opened a tin of sardines for lunch.
24. warehouse /ˈweəhaʊs/ n. 仓库；货栈
📖 英文解释
a large building for storing goods, especially before they are sold
✏️ 例句
The company rented a warehouse near the port to store imported goods.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
warehouse management（仓库管理）；bonded warehouse（保税仓库）；考研物流、商业类阅读常见
🧠 助记
ware（商品，参考 hardware）+ house（房子）→ 存放商品的房子 = 仓库
warehouse::/ˈweəhaʊs/:n. 仓库:The goods were shipped directly to the distribution warehouse.
25. wit /wɪt/ n. 机智；才智；妙趣
📖 英文解释
the capacity for inventive thought and quick understanding; clever and amusing remarks
✏️ 例句
Her wit and intelligence made her the most popular speaker at the conference.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
witty（adj. 机智的）；quick wit（机智敏捷）；at one's wit's end（智穷计尽）；wit vs wisdom（机智 vs 智慧）
🧠 助记
wit 与 wise（智慧）同源，都是快速的思维能力
wit::/wɪt/:n. 机智:His sharp wit kept the audience laughing throughout the speech.
26. arithmetic /əˈrɪθmətɪk/ n. 算术；算法
📖 英文解释
the branch of mathematics dealing with the properties and manipulation of numbers
✏️ 例句
Mental arithmetic skills are essential for everyday calculations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
arithmetic mean（算术平均数，统计学常用）；arithmetic progression（等差数列）；考研数学/逻辑题背景词
🧠 助记
a + rith（rhythm 节奏）+ metic → 有节奏地计算 = 算术
arithmetic::/əˈrɪθmətɪk/:n. 算术:Simple arithmetic shows that the plan is not cost-effective.
27. axis /ˈæksɪs/ n. 轴；轴线（复数 axes）
📖 英文解释
an imaginary line about which a body rotates; a central line that bisects a figure
✏️ 例句
The Earth rotates on its axis once every 24 hours.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
x-axis / y-axis（x轴/y轴）；axis of symmetry（对称轴）；the Axis Powers（轴心国，历史）
🧠 助记
ax（斧头）→ 斧头的把是轴，切下去形成轴线
axis::/ˈæksɪs/:n. 轴:The graph has time on the horizontal axis and temperature on the vertical axis.
28. brisk /brɪsk/ adj. 轻快的；活跃的；凉爽清新的
📖 英文解释
active and energetic; quick and efficient; pleasantly fresh and invigorating

She took a brisk walk in the park every morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
brisk walk（快走）；brisk business（生意兴隆）；brisk wind（清新的风）；考研体力、健康类阅读

brisk 听起来像 breeze（微风）+ risk → 清爽有活力
brisk::/brɪsk/:adj. 轻快的:Trade at the market was brisk on the holiday weekend.
29. cage /keɪdʒ/ n. 笼子；囚室 v. 关进笼子

in a cage（在笼子里）；caged animal（被关的动物）；cage 引申为束缚、监禁；考研环保类议题

cage 形似 cave（洞穴），都是被围住的封闭空间
cage::/keɪdʒ/:n. 笼子:She felt like a bird in a cage, longing for freedom.
30. casualty /ˈkæʒuəlti/ n. 伤亡人员；事故受害者
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

heavy/light casualties（重大/轻微伤亡）；civilian casualties（平民伤亡）；casualty ward（急诊室，英式）；注意勿混淆 casual（随意的）
🧠 助记
casual（偶然的）+ ty → 偶然发生的事故导致的伤亡
casualty::/ˈkæʒuəlti/:n. 伤亡人员:The military operation resulted in minimal casualties on both sides.
