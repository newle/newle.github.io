1. ferry /ˈferi/ n. 渡船；摆渡 v. 摆渡；运送
📖 英文解释
a boat or ship for conveying passengers across a body of water; to transport from one place to another
✏️ 例句
They took the ferry across the river to reach the island.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ferry service（轮渡服务）；car ferry（汽车渡轮）；ferry sb across（把某人摆渡过去）
🧠 助记
ferry 联想 carry（携带）→ 用船把人携带过去 = 摆渡
ferry::/ˈferi/:n. 渡船:The ferry runs every hour between the two ports.
2. gossip /ˈɡɒsɪp/ n. 闲话；八卦 v. 说闲话；议论
📖 英文解释
casual conversation about other people's private affairs; to engage in such conversation
✏️ 例句
Office gossip can damage workplace relationships.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
spread gossip（传播闲话）；gossip about sb；gossip column（八卦专栏）；gossipmonger（爱说闲话的人）
🧠 助记
goss（鹅）+ ip → 像鹅一样嘎嘎叫个不停 = 闲话连篇
gossip::/ˈɡɒsɪp/:n. 闲话:She refused to participate in office gossip.
3. hopeful /ˈhəʊpfl/ adj. 有希望的；充满希望的 n. 有望成功者
📖 英文解释
feeling or inspiring optimism about a future event; expecting something desired
✏️ 例句
Scientists are hopeful that the new drug will prove effective.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hopeful about/of sth；hopeful sign（有希望的迹象）；hopeful candidate（有希望的候选人）
🧠 助记
hope（希望）+ ful → 充满希望的
hopeful::/ˈhəʊpfl/:adj. 有希望的:She was hopeful that her application would be accepted.
4. immerse /ɪˈmɜːs/ v. 浸；使沉浸；使专注于
📖 英文解释
to dip or submerge in a liquid; to involve oneself deeply in a particular activity
✏️ 例句
She immersed herself in her work to forget the stress.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
immerse in（沉浸于）；immersive experience（沉浸式体验）；immersion（n. 沉浸；浸泡）
🧠 助记
im（进入）+ merse（浸，参考 submerge）→ 进入水中 = 浸，沉浸
immerse::/ɪˈmɜːs/:v. 浸:He immersed himself in the local culture during his travels.
5. impart /ɪmˈpɑːt/ v. 传授；赋予；透露
📖 英文解释
to make known; to bestow; to pass on information or qualities to someone
✏️ 例句
A good teacher can impart a love of learning to students.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
impart knowledge/skills/wisdom；impart information to sb；考研教育、传播类阅读常见
🧠 助记
im（进入）+ part（部分）→ 把一部分给出去 = 传授/赋予
impart::/ɪmˈpɑːt/:v. 传授:The professor tried to impart his enthusiasm for science to students.
6. intimidate /ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪt/ v. 恐吓；威胁；使胆怯
📖 英文解释
to frighten or overawe someone, especially to make them do what one wants
✏️ 例句
The defendant claimed that witnesses had been intimidated.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
intimidate sb into doing sth；intimidating（adj. 令人生畏的）；intimidation（n.）
🧠 助记
intim（亲密 intimate）+ idate → 逼近太亲密让人感到威胁 = 恐吓
intimidate::/ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪt/:v. 恐吓:The large crowd was intimidating for the young speaker.
7. neat /niːt/ adj. 整洁的；简洁的；纯（酒）
📖 英文解释
arranged in a tidy way; done with precision; pure or undiluted
✏️ 例句
She kept her desk neat and organized at all times.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
neat and tidy（干净整洁）；neat solution（简洁的解决方案）；neat whisky（纯威士忌）
🧠 助记
neat 联想 net（网）→ 网格般整齐有序
neat::/niːt/:adj. 整洁的:His handwriting was always neat and easy to read.
8. omit /əˈmɪt/ v. 省略；遗漏；忽略
📖 英文解释
to leave out or exclude; to fail to include something
✏️ 例句
Don't omit any important details in your report.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
omit sth from sth；omission（n.）；omit doing sth（未做某事）；注意：omit 强调故意或无意地省略
🧠 助记
o + mit（送出）→ 把东西送出去（删掉）= 省略
omit::/əˈmɪt/:v. 省略:She accidentally omitted a key paragraph from her essay.
9. prosper /ˈprɒspə/ v. 成功；兴旺；繁荣
📖 英文解释
to develop in a healthy or vigorous way; to succeed, especially financially
✏️ 例句
The small business prospered under her careful management.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
prosper financially/economically；prosperity（n.）；prosperous（adj. 繁荣的）；近义词：thrive, flourish
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ sper（希望，参考 hope）→ 向着希望前进 = 繁荣
prosper::/ˈprɒspə/:v. 成功:The region has prospered since the new factory opened.
10. scarce /skeəs/ adj. 稀少的；缺乏的；罕见的
📖 英文解释
insufficient for the demand; not easily found; existing in insufficient quantities
✏️ 例句
Clean water is scarce in many parts of the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
scarce resources（稀缺资源）；make oneself scarce（溜走）；scarcely（adv. 几乎不）；scarcity（n.）
🧠 助记
s + care（稀缺就让人担忧）+ ce → 让人担心的稀缺
scarce::/skeəs/:adj. 稀少的:Jobs were scarce during the economic recession.
11. thunder /ˈθʌndə/ n. 雷声；轰鸣 v. 打雷；轰鸣
📖 英文解释
the loud rumbling or crashing noise heard after a lightning flash; to make a loud rumbling sound
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
thunder and lightning（雷电）；steal sb's thunder（抢风头）；thunderstorm（雷暴）
🧠 助记
thunder 的发音本身就像轰隆的雷声
thunder::/ˈθʌndə/:n. 雷声:Dark clouds gathered and thunder rumbled in the distance.

timber::/ˈtɪmbə/:n. 木材:The old bridge was constructed entirely from heavy timber.
13. accessory /əkˈsesəri/ n. 配件；附件；从犯 adj. 附属的
📖 英文解释
a thing which can be added to something else to make it more useful or attractive; a person who assists in a crime
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

accessory::/əkˈsesəri/:n. 配件:He was convicted as an accessory to the crime.
14. awe /ɔː/ n. 敬畏；惊叹 v. 使敬畏
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

awe::/ɔː/:n. 敬畏:The children gazed in awe at the meteor shower.
15. bath /bɑːθ/ n. 浴缸；洗澡 v. 给...洗澡

bath 联想 bathroom（浴室）→ 在浴室洗澡
bath::/bɑːθ/:n. 浴缸:A hot bath is the best way to relax after a long day.
