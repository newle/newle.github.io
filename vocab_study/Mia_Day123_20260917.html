1. ugly /ˈʏgli/ adj. 丑陋的；难看的
📖 英文解释
very unpleasant to look at; not attractive in appearance
✏️ 例句
The old building was considered ugly by most residents of the city.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

ugly::/ˈʏgli/:adj. 丑陋的:The ugly truth about the scandal shocked everyone.

whoever::/huːˈevər/:pron. 任何人:Whoever is responsible for this mistake must apologize.
