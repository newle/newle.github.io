1. susceptible /səˈseptɪbl/ adj. 易受影响的；敏感的；容易的
📖 英文解释
easily influenced or affected by something; likely to be affected by a particular condition or disease
✏️ 例句
Young children are especially susceptible to respiratory infections in winter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
susceptible to(易受…影响); susceptible to disease(易患疾病); susceptible personality(易受影响的性格)
🧠 助记
sus(下面)+cept(接受,词根)+ible -> 容易接受(负面影响)的 -> 易受影响的
susceptible::/səˈseptɪbl/:adj. 易受影响的:People with weakened immune systems are more susceptible to infection.

🧠 助记

agony::/ˈaɡəni/:n. 极度痛苦:The athlete writhed in agony after sustaining a serious knee injury.

bizarre::/bɪˈzɑːr/:adj. 奇异的:The researcher discovered a bizarre correlation between coffee consumption and creativity.
4. conversely /ˈkɒnvɜːsli/ adv. 相反地；反过来说
