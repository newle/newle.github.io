1. embed /ɪmˈbed/ v. 嵌入；把…深植于
📖 英文解释
to fix something firmly into a surrounding substance, or to incorporate something as part of a larger whole
✏️ 例句
The journalist was embedded with the military unit during the conflict.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be embedded in sth（被嵌入/根植于某事物中）；embed sth in sth；常用于技术、文化、社会语境
🧠 助记
em-（进入）+ bed（床）→ 把东西放进床里 → 嵌入、深植
embed::/ɪmˈbed/:v. 嵌入:Technology is embedded in every aspect of modern life.
2. fuss /fʌs/ n./v. 大惊小怪；小题大做；忙乱
📖 英文解释
unnecessary excitement or activity about something unimportant; to be very concerned or worried about small details
✏️ 例句
There's no need to make such a fuss about a small scratch on the car.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
make a fuss（大惊小怪）；fuss about/over sth（为小事忙乱）；fuss and bother 过度焦虑
🧠 助记
fuss 发音像'烦死'→ 小事让人烦死 → 大惊小怪
fuss::/fʌs/:n./v. 大惊小怪:Stop making a fuss over nothing.
3. namely /ˈneɪmli/ adv. 即；也就是；换句话说
📖 英文解释
used to introduce more specific information about something just mentioned
✏️ 例句
Only one student passed the exam, namely Tom.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
用于引出前文所说事物的具体内容，相当于 that is / i.e.；正式书面语常见
🧠 助记
name + ly → 用名字具体说明 → 即、也就是
namely::/ˈneɪmli/:adv. 即:She has one major weakness, namely her inability to delegate.
4. permeate /ˈpɜːrmieɪt/ v. 渗透；弥漫；遍及
📖 英文解释
to spread through and be present throughout (a substance or place)
✏️ 例句
The smell of coffee permeated the entire office building.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
permeate through sth；permeate sth with sth；常考搭配：ideas/values permeate society
🧠 助记
per-（完全）+ meate（来自 meat 肉的纹路，四处渗入）→ 完全渗透
permeate::/ˈpɜːrmieɪt/:v. 渗透:A sense of hopelessness permeated the community.
5. resemblance /rɪˈzembləns/ n. 相似；类似；相像之处
📖 英文解释
the state of being similar in appearance or character to someone or something else
✏️ 例句
There is a striking resemblance between the two sisters.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
bear a resemblance to（与…相像）；strong/striking/close resemblance；in resemblance to
🧠 助记
resemble（相像）+ -ance（名词后缀）→ 相像的状态 → 相似
resemblance::/rɪˈzembləns/:n. 相似:The painting bears a close resemblance to a Rembrandt.
6. sometime /ˈsʌmtaɪm/ adv. 改天；某时；曾经 adj. 以前的
📖 英文解释
at some unspecified time in the past or future; formerly (adj.)
✏️ 例句
Let's get together sometime next week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sometime in the future/past；注意与 sometimes（有时）区别；sometime也可作形容词表示'以前的'
🧠 助记
some（某个）+ time（时间）→ 某个时间 → 改天
sometime::/ˈsʌmtaɪm/:adv. 改天:We should have lunch sometime soon.
7. staple /ˈsteɪpəl/ adj. 主要的 n. 主食；订书钉 v. 用订书钉订
📖 英文解释
a main or important element; a basic food that is eaten regularly; a U-shaped metal pin
✏️ 例句
Rice is a staple food in many Asian countries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
staple food/diet（主食）；staple industry（主要产业）；staple together（用订书钉装订）
🧠 助记
stable（稳定的）→ staple → 稳定常备的东西 → 主食；订书钉
staple::/ˈsteɪpəl/:adj. 主要的 n. 主食:Wheat and rice are staple foods for most of the world's population.
8. suck /sʌk/ v. 吸；啜；使陷入；令人失望
📖 英文解释
to draw liquid or air into the mouth by using the lips and tongue; to pull something with force; (informal) to be very bad
✏️ 例句
The vacuum cleaner sucked up all the dirt from the carpet.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
suck in/up（吸入/卷入）；be sucked into（被卷入）；suck sb dry（榨干某人）
🧠 助记
发音如'吸客'→ 把客人/东西都吸过来 → 吸
suck::/sʌk/:v. 吸:The whirlpool sucked the boat down.
9. terror /ˈterər/ n. 恐惧；恐怖；令人恐惧的事
📖 英文解释
extreme fear; something that causes extreme fear; violent action for political purposes (terrorism)
✏️ 例句
The children were struck with terror when they heard the explosion.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in terror（在恐惧中）；terror attack（恐怖袭击）；reign of terror（恐怖统治）；terrify v.
🧠 助记
terr-（大地震颤）+ or → 令大地震颤的恐惧感 → 恐惧
terror::/ˈterər/:n. 恐惧:The earthquake struck terror into the hearts of the residents.
10. tonight /təˈnaɪt/ adv./n. 今晚；今夜
📖 英文解释
the evening or night of the present day
✏️ 例句
Tonight's concert has been sold out for weeks.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tonight's news/show；used as both adv. and n.；late tonight
🧠 助记
to + night → 到了夜晚 → 今晚
tonight::/təˈnaɪt/:adv./n. 今晚:The game will be broadcast live tonight.
11. wardrobe /ˈwɔːrdroʊb/ n. 衣柜；个人全部衣物；戏服部门
📖 英文解释
a large cupboard for storing clothes; all the clothes owned by someone; the department in a theatre responsible for costumes
✏️ 例句
She opened the wardrobe and chose a dress for the evening.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a wardrobe of clothes（全部衣物）；wardrobe malfunction（服装事故）；build a wardrobe
🧠 助记
ward（守护）+ robe（长袍）→ 守护长袍的地方 → 衣柜
wardrobe::/ˈwɔːrdroʊb/:n. 衣柜:She has an extensive wardrobe for every occasion.
12. wreck /rek/ n. 残骸；失事船只；精神崩溃的人 v. 破坏；毁坏
📖 英文解释
the remains of something that has been badly damaged; to cause something to be destroyed or ruined
✏️ 例句
Rescuers searched the wreck for survivors.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
wreck a plan/car（破坏计划/撞毁汽车）；nervous wreck（精神紧张的人）；wreckage n.
🧠 助记
wreck 发音像'肋克'→ 肋骨被克（打断）→ 残骸，破坏
wreck::/rek/:n. 残骸:The storm completely wrecked the coastal village.
13. blouse /blaʊz/ n. 女衬衫；宽松上衣
📖 英文解释
a light shirt, typically worn by women
✏️ 例句
She wore a silk blouse and tailored trousers to the interview.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
wear/tuck in a blouse；silk/cotton/linen blouse；常与裙子或裤子搭配描述
🧠 助记
发音类似'布劳兹'→ 布做的、有点宽松的女衬衫
blouse::/blaʊz/:n. 女衬衫:She chose a floral blouse for the spring party.
14. bow /baʊ/ (鞠躬) /boʊ/ (弓) v./n. 鞠躬；弯腰 n. 弓；弓形；蝴蝶结
📖 英文解释
to bend the upper part of the body forward as a sign of respect; the weapon used for shooting arrows; a knot tied in two loops
✏️ 例句
The performers bowed deeply after the final curtain.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
bow to sb（向某人鞠躬）；bow and arrow（弓箭）；bow tie（领结）；bowing pressure
🧠 助记
bow（弯弓/弯腰）→ 弓的形状=弯腰=鞠躬；注意双音标
bow::/baʊ/ (鞠躬) /boʊ/ (弓):v./n. 鞠躬:The actor took a bow as the audience applauded.
15. cafeteria /ˌkæfəˈtɪriə/ n. 自助餐厅；学生食堂
📖 英文解释
a restaurant in which customers collect their food at a counter and take it to tables
✏️ 例句
Students usually have lunch in the school cafeteria.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
school/company cafeteria；cafeteria-style（自助式的）；cafeteria plan（弹性福利计划）
🧠 助记
cafe（咖啡馆）+ teria → 更大的cafe → 自助餐厅
cafeteria::/ˌkæfəˈtɪriə/:n. 自助餐厅:The hospital cafeteria serves hot meals throughout the day.
16. cheek /tʃiːk/ n. 脸颊；厚颜无耻；臀部之一侧
📖 英文解释
either side of the face below the eye; rudeness or boldness
✏️ 例句
She blushed and her cheeks turned red.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cheek by jowl（紧挨着）；turn the other cheek（以德报怨）；have the cheek to do（厚颜无耻地做）
🧠 助记
cheek 和 check 相近→ 检查脸颊 → 脸颊
cheek::/tʃiːk/:n. 脸颊:He had the cheek to ask for a pay rise after being late every day.
17. compatible /kəmˈpætɪbəl/ adj. 兼容的；相容的；和谐共存的
📖 英文解释
able to exist or work with something else without problems; (of people) able to have a harmonious relationship
✏️ 例句
The new software is compatible with all major operating systems.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
compatible with（与…兼容）；mutually compatible；incompatible（不兼容的）；compatibility n.
🧠 助记
com-（共同）+ pat（拍/和）+ ible → 能和睦相处的 → 兼容的
compatible::/kəmˈpætɪbəl/:adj. 兼容的:Their personalities are not compatible, so they often argue.
18. courtyard /ˈkɔːrtjɑːrd/ n. 院子；庭院；天井
📖 英文解释
an unroofed area that is completely or partly enclosed by the walls of a building
✏️ 例句
The hotel has a beautiful courtyard with a fountain in the centre.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
open/inner courtyard；courtyard garden；常见于建筑、历史语境描述
🧠 助记
court（宫廷/球场）+ yard（院子）→ 宫廷里的院子 → 庭院
courtyard::/ˈkɔːrtjɑːrd/:n. 院子:Children played in the courtyard after school.
19. descent /dɪˈsent/ n. 下降；下坡；血统；衰落
📖 英文解释
the action of moving downwards; family origins; a moral decline
✏️ 例句
The plane began its descent into the airport.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
of Chinese/noble descent（有中国/贵族血统）；descent into chaos（陷入混乱）；steep descent
🧠 助记
de-（向下）+ scent（攀登 ascent）→ 向下 → 下降；也引申为血统往下传
descent::/dɪˈsent/:n. 下降:She is of French descent and speaks the language fluently.
20. dessert /dɪˈzɜːrt/ n. 甜点；餐后甜食
📖 英文解释
a sweet food eaten at the end of a meal
✏️ 例句
We had chocolate cake for dessert.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
for dessert（作为甜点）；dessert menu/trolley；注意与 desert（沙漠/抛弃）区分：dessert有两个s
🧠 助记
dessert 有两个s → sweet stuff（甜食两s）→ 甜点；desert只有一个s
dessert::/dɪˈzɜːrt/:n. 甜点:The restaurant is famous for its elaborate desserts.
21. excursion /ɪkˈskɜːrʒən/ n. 短途旅行；远足；偏离
📖 英文解释
a short journey made for pleasure, especially one taken by a group of people
✏️ 例句
The class went on an excursion to the local history museum.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
go on an excursion（去远足）；day excursion（一日游）；excursion into（涉足某领域）
🧠 助记
ex-（出去）+ cursion（奔跑，来自 course）→ 出去跑一趟 → 短途旅行
excursion::/ɪkˈskɜːrʒən/:n. 短途旅行:The school organized a day excursion to the botanical gardens.
22. expel /ɪkˈspel/ v. 驱逐；开除；排出
📖 英文解释
to force someone to leave a school, organization, or country; to push air or liquid out of something
✏️ 例句
The student was expelled from school for cheating.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
expel sb from（将某人驱逐出）；expel air/gas（排出气体）；expulsion n.
🧠 助记
ex-（出去）+ pel（驱赶，如 propel 推进）→ 把人驱赶出去 → 驱逐
expel::/ɪkˈspel/:v. 驱逐:The diplomat was expelled from the country for espionage.
23. fax /fæks/ n./v. 传真；传真机
📖 英文解释
a system for transmitting documents electronically over a telephone line; to send a document in this way
✏️ 例句
Please fax the signed contract to our office by 5 pm.

fax machine（传真机）；send/receive a fax；fax number；by fax（通过传真）
🧠 助记
fax = facsimile（影印）的缩写 → 传真
fax::/fæks/:n./v. 传真:She faxed the report to headquarters before the deadline.

fisherman::/ˈfɪʃərmən/:n. 渔夫:The fisherman returned to shore with a large catch.

fleet::/fliːt/:n. 舰队:The navy deployed its entire fleet for the exercise.
