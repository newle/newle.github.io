1. meditation /ˌmedɪˈteɪʃən/ n. 冥想；沉思；默想
📖 英文解释
the practice of thinking deeply and quietly to achieve mental calmness; a written or spoken expression of deep thought
✏️ 例句
She practises meditation every morning to reduce stress.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
deep in meditation（深思中）；meditation on（对…的沉思）；meditate v.；meditative adj.
🧠 助记
medi-（中间）+ tate（状态）→ 进入内心中间的状态 → 冥想
meditation::/ˌmedɪˈteɪʃən/:n. 冥想:Meditation has been shown to reduce anxiety and improve focus.

✏️ 例句

🧠 助记
merchant（商人）+ ise → 商人的东西 → 商品
merchandise::/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪz/:n. 商品:The concert merchandise sold out within hours.

🧠 助记

oppress::/əˈpres/:v. 压迫:The colonizers oppressed the indigenous population for centuries.
