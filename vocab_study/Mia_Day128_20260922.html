1. hygiene /ˈhaɪdʒiːn/ n. 卫生；卫生习惯；保健
📖 英文解释
conditions or practices that help to maintain health and prevent disease
✏️ 例句
Good personal hygiene is essential for preventing the spread of infection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
personal/oral/food hygiene；hygiene standards；hygienic adj.（卫生的）；unhygienic（不卫生的）
🧠 助记
hygi-（健康，来自希腊神 Hygieia 健康女神）+ ene → 健康状态 → 卫生
hygiene::/ˈhaɪdʒiːn/:n. 卫生:The restaurant failed its hygiene inspection and was closed temporarily.
2. negligible /ˈneɡlɪdʒɪbəl/ adj. 微不足道的；可忽略不计的
📖 英文解释
so small or unimportant as to be not worth considering
✏️ 例句
The risk of side effects from this medication is negligible.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
negligible amount/effect/risk；almost negligible；negligibly adv.；neglect v./n.（忽视）
🧠 助记
neglect（忽视）+ ible → 可以被忽视的 → 微不足道的
negligible::/ˈneɡlɪdʒɪbəl/:adj. 微不足道的:The difference in cost between the two options is negligible.

penny::/ˈpeni/:n. 便士（英国货币单位）:She saves every penny she earns for her children's education.

statesman::/ˈsteɪtsmən/:n. 政治家:The prime minister was praised as a true statesman for his handling of the crisis.
6. stationary /ˈsteɪʃəneri/ adj. 稳定的；静止的；不动的
📖 英文解释

stationary::/ˈsteɪʃəneri/:adj. 稳定的:Prices have remained stationary for the past three months.
