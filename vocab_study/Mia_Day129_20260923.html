1. facet /ˈfæsɪt/ n. 方面；（多面体的）面
📖 英文解释
one of the many parts or aspects of something; one of the flat surfaces of a cut gemstone
✏️ 例句
We need to examine every facet of the problem before making a decision.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
facet of sth（…的方面）；常用于 every/each facet，考研阅读中频繁出现
🧠 助记
face（脸/面）+ t → 脸有很多面，引申为“方面”
facet::/ˈfæsɪt/:n. 方面:every facet of life 生活的方方面面
2. freight /freɪt/ n. 货运；货物 v. 运送货物
📖 英文解释
goods transported in bulk by truck, train, ship, or aircraft; the transport of goods in bulk
✏️ 例句
The freight train carried tons of coal across the country.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
freight train（货运列车）；air freight（空运）；freight charges（运费）
🧠 助记
fre + ight → 货物运输要付费才能自由流通
freight::/freɪt/:n. 货运:air freight 空运货物
3. hover /ˈhɒvər/ v. 盘旋；徘徊；悬停
📖 英文解释
to remain suspended in one place in the air; to wait or linger close to someone or something
✏️ 例句
A helicopter hovered over the accident scene.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hover over/above sth（在…上方盘旋）；hover around（在周围徘徊）
🧠 助记
ho + ver → 像直升机一样呆悬停在空中
hover::/ˈhɒvər/:v. 盘旋:hover above the ground 悬停在地面上方
4. hum /hʌm/ v./n. 哼唱；崞崞声；忙碌运转
📖 英文解释
to make a low, steady continuous sound; to sing with closed lips; to be in a state of activity
✏️ 例句
She hummed a tune while doing the dishes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hum with activity（忙碌运转）；the hum of traffic（车流声）；考研写作可用
🧠 助记
发音“哼”，模拟哼歌或崞崞声
hum::/hʌm/:v./n. 哼唱:hum with activity 忙碌地运转
5. impair /ɪmˈpeər/ v. 损害；削弱
📖 英文解释
to weaken or damage something, especially a faculty or function
✏️ 例句
Drinking alcohol can seriously impair your ability to drive.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
impair health/vision/judgment（损害健康/视力/判断力）；impaired adj. 受损的
🧠 助记
im（使）+ pair（一对）→ 破坏一对好东西，使之受损
impair::/ɪmˈpeər/:v. 损害:impair judgment 损害判断力
6. levy /ˈlevi/ v. 征收（税款）n. 税款；征税
📖 英文解释
to impose a tax, fee, or fine; an amount of money, such as a tax, that has to be paid
✏️ 例句
The government decided to levy a new tax on luxury goods.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
levy a tax on sth（对…征税）；考研经济类阅读高频词
🧠 助记
levy谐音“厉”→ 厉害的税，征收重税
levy::/ˈlevi/:v. 征收（税款）n. 税款:levy a tax on imports 对进口品征税
7. mainland /ˈmeɪnlænd/ n. 大陆；本土
📖 英文解释
the main area of land of a country, not including islands or other areas away from the coast
✏️ 例句
Many people move from the islands to the mainland for better opportunities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the mainland（大陆本土）；mainland China（中国大陆）
🧠 助记
main（主要的）+ land（土地）→ 主要的陆地 = 大陆
mainland::/ˈmeɪnlænd/:n. 大陆:on the mainland 在大陆
8. northeast /ˌnɔːrθˈiːst/ n./adj./adv. 东北（的/方向）
📖 英文解释
the direction or region halfway between north and east
✏️ 例句
The storm moved in a northeast direction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
northeast of（在…东北方）；the Northeast（东北地区）
🧠 助记
north（北）+ east（东）= 东北，方向合成词
northeast::/ˌnɔːrθˈiːst/:n./adj./adv. 东北（的/方向:northeast of the city 城市东北方
9. notorious /nəʊˈtɔːriəs/ adj. 臭名昭著的；声名狼藉的
📖 英文解释
famous or well known, typically for some bad quality or deed
✏️ 例句
He was a notorious criminal who evaded police for years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
notorious for（因…而出名）；notorious criminal/case；与 famous 区别：含贬义
🧠 助记
note（注意）+ orious → 被人“注意”是因为坏事出名
notorious::/nəʊˈtɔːriəs/:adj. 臭名昭著的:notorious for corruption 因腐败而臭名昭著
10. pamphlet /ˈpæmflɪt/ n. 小册子；宣传册
📖 英文解释
a small booklet or leaflet containing information or arguments about a single subject

🧠 助记
pam + phlet → 像palm（手掌）大小的小册子
pamphlet::/ˈpæmflɪt/:n. 小册子:distribute pamphlets 散发小册子
11. periodical /ˌpiəriˈɒdɪkəl/ n. 期刊；定期刊物 adj. 定期的
📖 英文解释

🧠 助记

periodical::/ˌpiəriˈɒdɪkəl/:n. 期刊:academic periodical 学术期刊
12. perish /ˈperɪʃ/ v. 死亡；消亡；腐烂
📖 英文解释
to die, especially in a sudden or untimely way; (of food) to decay or rot
✏️ 例句
Many soldiers perished in the battle.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
per（完全）+ ish（消失）→ 完全消失了 = 死亡
perish::/ˈperɪʃ/:v. 死亡:perish in the disaster 在灾难中罹难
13. purse /pɜːrs/ n. 錢包；手提包 v. 撇起（嘴唇）

purse::/pɜːrs/:n. 錢包:purse one's lips 撇起嘴唇
14. shallow /ˈʃæləʊ/ adj. 浅的；肤浅的 n. 浅滚

🧠 助记

shallow::/ˈʃæləʊ/:adj. 浅的:shallow understanding 肤浅的理解

solidarity::/ˌsɒlɪˈdærɪti/:n. 团结:in solidarity with workers 与工人团结一致

allege::/əˈledʒ/:v. 宣称:alleged misconduct 所谓的不当行为
19. bud /bʌd/ n. 芽；花辕 v. 发芽；萌芽

bud::/bʌd/:n. 芽:nip it in the bud 将其扬杀在萌芽状态

contrive::/kənˈtraɪv/:v. 设法做到:contrive to escape 设法逃脱

credential::/krɪˈdenʃəl/:n. 资质:check credentials 核查资质证书
