1. tick /tɪk/ n. 滴答声；蜱虫；一会儿；打勾 v. 滴答作响
📖 英文解释
a regular short, sharp sound; a small bloodsucking arachnid; a mark to show correctness
✏️ 例句
The clock ticked loudly in the quiet room.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tick off（打勾；使恼火）；tick over（运转；维持）；what makes sb tick（某人的动力）
🧠 助记
tick模拟钟表滴答声
tick::/tɪk/:n. 滴答声:what makes sb tick 某人的内在动力
2. tile /taɪl/ n. 瓷砖；瓦片 v. 铺瓷砖
📖 英文解释
a thin slab of fired clay, stone, or glass used for covering roofs, floors, or walls
✏️ 例句
The bathroom floor was covered with white ceramic tiles.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tile a floor/roof（铺地板/屋顶）；ceramic tile（陶瓷砖）
🧠 助记
tile谐音太了→ 太多瓦片铺满地
tile::/taɪl/:n. 瓷砖:ceramic tile 陶瓷砖
3. umbrella /ʌmˈbrelə/ n. 雨伞；保护伞；综合性的
📖 英文解释
a device consisting of a circular canopy on a folding metal frame; a protection or cover
✏️ 例句
She opened her umbrella when it started to rain.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
umbrella organization/term（总括性组织/术语）；under the umbrella of（在…的保护下）
🧠 助记
umbra（影子）+ ella → 在影子下遮雨 = 雨伞
umbrella::/ʌmˈbrelə/:n. 雨伞:umbrella organization 总括性组织
4. verge /vɜːrdʒ/ n. 边缘 v. 接近；濒临
📖 英文解释
an edge or border; to be on the border or edge of something
✏️ 例句
The company was on the verge of bankruptcy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
on the verge of（处于…边缘）；verge on（接近于；近乎）
🧠 助记
verge谐音危→ 危险的边缘
verge::/vɜːrdʒ/:n. 边缘 v. 接近:on the verge of collapse 处于崩溃边缘
5. aisle /aɪl/ n. 过道；通道（飞机/剧院/超市）
📖 英文解释
a passage between rows of seats in a building or vehicle, or between shelves in a store
✏️ 例句
The flight attendant walked down the aisle to check on passengers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
aisle seat（过道座位）；walk down the aisle（走上红毯，结婚）
🧠 助记
aisle中间有个s，像一条竖着的过道
aisle::/aɪl/:n. 过道:aisle seat 靠过道的座位
6. badge /bædʒ/ n. 徽章；证件；标志
📖 英文解释
a small piece of metal, plastic, or fabric bearing a design, worn as decoration or to identify someone
✏️ 例句
All employees must wear their identity badges at all times.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
wear/display a badge（佩戴徽章）；badge of honor（荣誉徽章）
🧠 助记

badge::/bædʒ/:n. 徽章:badge of honor 荣誉的象征

bean::/biːn/:n. 豆:spill the beans 泄露秘密

bump::/bʌmp/:v./n. 碰撞:bump into sb 偶然遇见某人
10. bundle /ˈbʌndəl/ n. 捆；一批；大量 v. 捆绑；裹
📖 英文解释
a collection of things fastened together; a large amount; to tie or wrap things together
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a bundle of（一捆/一批）；bundle of nerves（极度紧张的人）

bundle::/ˈbʌndəl/:n. 捆:bundle of nerves 极度紧张的人
