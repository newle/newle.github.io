1. notable /ˈnəʊtəbəl/ adj. 著名的；值得注意的 n. 名人
📖 英文解释
worthy of attention or notice; remarkable; a famous or important person
✏️ 例句
The conference attracted notable scientists from around the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
notable exception/achievement/example（显著的例外/成就/例子）；notably adv.（尤其）
🧠 助记
note（注意）+ able → 值得被注意的
notable::/ˈnəʊtəbəl/:adj. 著名的:notable exception 显著的例外
2. obedience /əˈbiːdiəns/ n. 服从；顺从；听话
📖 英文解释
compliance with an order, request, or law, or submission to authority
✏️ 例句
The soldiers showed complete obedience to their commander.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in obedience to（服从…）；blind obedience（盲目服从）；obedient adj.
🧠 助记
obey（服从）→ obedience，服从的名词
obedience::/əˈbiːdiəns/:n. 服从:blind obedience 盲目服从
3. ruler /ˈruːlər/ n. 统治者；尺子
📖 英文解释
a person who rules or governs; a straight strip used for measuring or drawing
✏️ 例句
The ancient ruler built magnificent temples throughout the kingdom.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
absolute ruler（绝对统治者）；ruler of a country（一国之君）
🧠 助记
rule（统治/规则）+ r → 统治的人；量规则的工具
ruler::/ˈruːlər/:n. 统治者:absolute ruler 绝对统治者
4. spear /spɪər/ n. 矛；长矛 v. 用矛刺
📖 英文解释
a weapon with a long shaft and a pointed tip; to pierce or thrust with a sharp object
✏️ 例句
Ancient warriors used spears in battle.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
spear fishing（鱼叉捕鱼）；spearhead v.（带头；主导）
🧠 助记
spear→sp（速）+ ear（耳形尖头）→ 速快尖头武器
spear::/spɪər/:n. 矛:spearhead a campaign 带头发起运动
5. sunshine /ˈsʌnʃaɪn/ n. 阳光；晴天；快乐的来源
📖 英文解释
direct sunlight unbroken by cloud; a source of happiness
✏️ 例句
The children played in the sunshine after school.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ray of sunshine（一缕阳光；带来快乐的人）；sunshine policy（阳光政策）
🧠 助记
sun（太阳）+ shine（发光）→ 太阳发出的光 = 阳光
sunshine::/ˈsʌnʃaɪn/:n. 阳光:ray of sunshine 带来快乐的人或事
6. surrender /səˈrendər/ v./n. 投降；屈服；放弃
📖 英文解释
to stop resisting an enemy and submit to their authority; to give up something under compulsion
✏️ 例句
After weeks of fighting, the troops finally surrendered.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
surrender to（向…投降；屈服于）；surrender rights（放弃权利）
🧠 助记
sur（超过）+ render（交出）→ 完全交出 = 投降
surrender::/səˈrendər/:v./n. 投降:surrender to temptation 屈服于诱惑
7. taboo /təˈbuː/ n./adj. 禁忌（的）；忌讳（的）
📖 英文解释
a social or religious custom prohibiting or forbidding discussion of a particular practice or forbidding association with a person
✏️ 例句
In many cultures, discussing death is considered taboo.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
social taboo（社会禁忌）；taboo subject/topic（禁忌话题）
🧠 助记
taboo谐音太不哦→ 太不应该说的 = 禁忌
taboo::/təˈbuː/:n./adj. 禁忌（的）:social taboo 社会禁忌
8. thirst /θɜːrst/ n. 口渴；渴望 v. 渴望
📖 英文解释
a feeling of needing or wanting to drink; a strong desire for something
✏️ 例句
She had a thirst for knowledge that drove her to read constantly.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
thirst for knowledge/power（对知识/权力的渴望）；thirsty adj.
🧠 助记
thirst → t + hirst（渴）→ 喉咙渴了
thirst::/θɜːrst/:n. 口渴:thirst for knowledge 对知识的渴望
9. vegetation /ˌvedʒɪˈteɪʃən/ n. 植被；植物（总称）
📖 英文解释
plants considered collectively, especially those found in a particular area or habitat
✏️ 例句
The tropical island was covered in lush vegetation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dense/sparse vegetation（茂密/稀疏的植被）；desert vegetation（荒漠植被）
🧠 助记
vegetable（蔬菜）→ vegetation，植物的集合名词
vegetation::/ˌvedʒɪˈteɪʃən/:n. 植被:dense vegetation 茂密的植被
10. astronomy /əˈstrɒnəmi/ n. 天文学
📖 英文解释
the branch of science that deals with celestial objects, space, and the physical universe
✏️ 例句
She studied astronomy at university and now works at an observatory.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
astronomy vs. astrology（天文学 vs 占星术）；amateur astronomy（业余天文学）
🧠 助记
astro（星星）+ nomy（学问）→ 研究星星的学问
astronomy::/əˈstrɒnəmi/:n. 天文学:study astronomy 研究天文学
11. chop /tʃɒp/ v. 砍；切碎 n. 切碎的肉；砍
📖 英文解释
to cut something into pieces with repeated sharp blows; a thick slice of meat including a rib
✏️ 例句
She chopped the vegetables into small pieces.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
chop down（砍倒）；chop up（切碎）；pork/lamb chop（猪/羊排）
🧠 助记
chop模拟砍切的声音，短促有力
chop::/tʃɒp/:v. 砍:chop down a tree 砍倒一棵树
12. doctrine /ˈdɒktrɪn/ n. 教义；主义；学说；政策原则
📖 英文解释
a belief or set of beliefs held by an organization; an official policy statement
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
doc（文件）+ trine（三位一体）→ 有文件记录的正式信条
doctrine::/ˈdɒktrɪn/:n. 教义:political doctrine 政治主义
13. indignation /ˌɪndɪgˈneɪʃən/ n. 愤慨；义愤

indignation::/ˌɪndɪgˈneɪʃən/:n. 愤慨:righteous indignation 义愤填膺
14. prudent /ˈpruːdənt/ adj. 谨慎的；明智的；精明的

prudent::/ˈpruːdənt/:adj. 谨慎的:prudent approach 谨慎明智的做法
15. disposition /ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃən/ n. 性情；倾向；排列；处置

16. perplex /pəˈpleks/ v. 使困惑；使茫然；使复杂化

17. anchor /ˈæŋkər/ n. 锚；主播；依靠 v. 锚定；固定
