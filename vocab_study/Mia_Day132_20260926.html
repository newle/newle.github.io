1. affiliate /əˈfɪliət/ v. 使附属；n. 附属机构
📖 英文解释
to officially connect or associate with a larger organization; a branch or subsidiary organization
✏️ 例句
The university is affiliated with several research institutes around the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
affiliate with/to sth; an affiliated company/organization; affiliate 常考搭配：political affiliate（政治附属），affiliate marketing（联盟营销）
🧠 助记
af(加强)+fili(儿子，如filial孝顺的)+ate→把儿子纳入麾下→使附属
affiliate::/əˈfɪliət/:v. 使附属:The local hospital is an affiliate of the national medical network.
2. align /əˈlaɪn/ v. 使成一线；使一致；使结盟
📖 英文解释
to arrange things in a straight line or to make them agree with each other; to support a particular group or cause
✏️ 例句
You need to align your goals with the company's strategy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
align with sb/sth; be aligned with; alignment n.一致；考研常考：align interests（使利益一致），align policies（协调政策）
🧠 助记
a+line(线)→把…排成一条线→对齐，一致
align::/əˈlaɪn/:v. 使成一线:The two countries aligned their foreign policies on climate change.

🧠 助记

assault::/əˈsɔːlt/:n./v. 攻击:The city came under assault from enemy forces at dawn.

6. consolidate /kənˈsɒlɪdeɪt/ v. 巩固；合并；加强
