1. fasten /ˈfɑːsn/ v. 系上；扣紧；固定；集中
📖 英文解释
to close or attach something firmly; to direct your attention or eyes on something
✏️ 例句
Please fasten your seatbelt before the plane takes off.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fasten a seatbelt/button/lock；fasten sth to sth；fasten on/upon（把注意力集中于）；fasten the blame on（归咎于）
🧠 助记
fast(固定，fast=firmly fixed)+en→使固定→系上，扣紧
fasten::/ˈfɑːsn/:v. 系上:He fastened the document to the board with a clip for all to see.
2. glue /ɡluː/ n. 胶水；v. 粘贴；使专注
📖 英文解释
a sticky substance used for joining things together; to join with glue; to be unable to look away from something
✏️ 例句
He glued the broken pieces of the vase back together.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
glue sth together/to sth；be glued to the screen（盯着屏幕看）；super glue（强力胶）；come unglued（崩溃）
🧠 助记
glue听起来像flow(流)→胶水流动后粘合→胶水
glue::/ɡluː/:n. 胶水:The children were glued to the television screen during the championship final.
3. groan /ɡrəʊn/ v./n. 呻吟；抱怨；发出嘎吱声
📖 英文解释
to make a deep sound expressing pain, displeasure, or disapproval; a deep sound of pain or unhappiness
✏️ 例句
The patient groaned in pain when the nurse touched the wound.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
groan with pain/effort；groan under the weight of（不堪重负）；groan at sth（对某事发出抱怨）
🧠 助记
gr(发喉音)+oan→喉咙发出的低沉声音→呻吟
groan::/ɡrəʊn/:v./n. 呻吟:The audience groaned when the comedian's joke fell completely flat.

📖 英文解释
the quality of being new, original, or unusual; a new or unusual experience; a small cheap toy or ornament
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
the novelty of sth；novelty wears off（新鲜感消退）；novelty item（新奇玩意儿）；考研心理/文化类
🧠 助记

novelty::/ˈnɒvlti/:n. 新奇:The novelty of working remotely has worn off for many employees after two years.

📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pierce the skin/silence/darkness；piecing cry（刺耳的叫声）；pierce through（穿越）；ear-piercing（震耳欲聋的）
🧠 助记
pier(码头，尖锐的桩)+ce→像码头的桩打入水中→穿透
pierce::/pɪəs/:v. 穿透:A piercing cry broke through the silence of the empty street at midnight.
