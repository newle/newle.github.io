1. resolute /ˈrezəluːt/ adj. 坚决的；果断的；毅然的
📖 英文解释
admirably purposeful, determined, and unwavering in pursuing goals despite opposition
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

resolute::/ˈrezəluːt/:adj. 坚决的:The government took resolute action to address the growing public health emergency.
