1. jewel /ˈdʒuːəl/ n. 珠宝；宝石
📖 英文解释
A precious stone or a piece of jewellery made with precious stones.
✏️ 例句
The crown was set with jewels of every colour.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
jewel box 珠宝盒；a jewel in the crown 皇冠上的明珠（喻最宝贵之物）
🧠 助记
jew（犹太人）+ el → 犹太商人善做珠宝生意，联想jewel=珠宝
jewel::/ˈdʒuːəl/:n. 珠宝:The ring is a priceless jewel.
2. kidney /ˈkɪdni/ n. 肾；肾脏
📖 英文解释
Either of two organs in the abdomen that filter blood and produce urine.
✏️ 例句
He was diagnosed with kidney disease.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
kidney failure 肾衰竭；kidney transplant 肾移植；kidney stone 肾结石
🧠 助记
kid（孩子）+ ney → 孩子的小肾，联想kidney=肾
kidney::/ˈkɪdni/:n. 肾:The kidney filters waste from the blood.
3. kilometre /ˈkɪləmiːtə/ n. 公里；千米
📖 英文解释
A unit of length equal to 1,000 metres.
✏️ 例句
The city is about 50 kilometres from here.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
kilometres per hour (km/h) 千米/小时；kilometre marker 里程碑
🧠 助记
kilo（千）+ metre（米）→ 一千米=公里
kilometre::/ˈkɪləmiːtə/:n. 公里:We drove 300 kilometres today.
4. lapse /læps/ n./v. 失效；流逝；（短暂的）失误
📖 英文解释
A temporary failure or decline; to pass gradually (of time); to become invalid.
✏️ 例句
There was a brief lapse in his concentration.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lapse of time 时间流逝；lapse into 陷入（某种状态）；lapse of memory 一时失忆
🧠 助记
lap（圈）+ se → 跑了一圈时间就流逝了，联想lapse=流逝/失效
lapse::/læps/:n./v. 失效:His insurance policy lapsed last month.
5. lease /liːs/ n./v. 租约；租赁
📖 英文解释
A legal agreement by which property is rented; to rent property under a lease.
✏️ 例句
They signed a two-year lease on the apartment.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
lease out 出租；on lease 租赁中；a new lease of life 新的活力/转机
🧠 助记
lease → 谐音「利市」→ 签租约带来利市，联想lease=租约
lease::/liːs/:n./v. 租约:She leased the office for three years.
6. limp /lɪmp/ v./n./adj. 跛行；柔软的；无力的
📖 英文解释
To walk with difficulty due to a leg injury; lacking firmness or stiffness.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
walk with a limp 走路一瘸一拐；limp handshake 软弱无力的握手
🧠 助记
limp → 谐音「懒」→ 懒洋洋地跛行，联想limp=跛行/柔软
limp::/lɪmp/:v./n./adj. 跛行:She walked with a limp after the accident.
7. lovely /ˈlʌvli/ adj. 可爱的；美丽的；令人愉快的
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🧠 助记
love（爱）+ ly → 充满爱意的 = 可爱的
lovely::/ˈlʌvli/:adj. 可爱的:She has a lovely smile.
8. nationality /ˌnæʃəˈnæləti/ n. 民族；国籍

nationality::/ˌnæʃəˈnæləti/:n. 民族:She has dual nationality.
9. nominal /ˈnɒmɪnəl/ adj. 名义上的；象征性的；极小的
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
nom（名字）+ inal → 只有名字的 = 名义上的
nominal::/ˈnɒmɪnəl/:adj. 名义上的:The rent was a nominal sum.
10. notify /ˈnəʊtɪfaɪ/ v. 通知；告知

To inform someone officially or formally about something.

🧠 助记
note（注意）+ ify → 让人注意到 = 通知
notify::/ˈnəʊtɪfaɪ/:v. 通知:The school notified parents of the change.

oak::/əʊk/:n. 橡树:The door is carved from oak.
