1. furnace /ˈfɜːnɪs/ n. 熔炉；火炉；锅炉
📖 英文解释
An enclosed structure in which material can be heated to very high temperatures; a heating system.
✏️ 例句
The steel was melted in the furnace.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
blast furnace 高炉；furnace room 锅炉房；like a furnace 像火炉一样热
🧠 助记
fur（毛皮）+ nace → 毛皮都要烧掉的热炉 = 熔炉
furnace::/ˈfɜːnɪs/:n. 熔炉:The factory has a large industrial furnace.
2. textile /ˈtekstaɪl/ n. 纺织品；纺织业 adj. 纺织的
📖 英文解释
A type of cloth or woven fabric; relating to the making of cloth.
✏️ 例句
The country is famous for its textile industry.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
textile factory 纺织厂；textile industry 纺织业；textile design 纺织品设计
🧠 助记
text（编织）+ ile → 编织出来的东西 = 纺织品
textile::/ˈtekstaɪl/:n. 纺织品:Cotton is a widely used textile material.
3. canteen /kænˈtiːn/ n. 食堂；餐厅；水壶
📖 英文解释
A restaurant provided by an employer for workers; a small container for carrying water.
✏️ 例句
The workers ate lunch in the factory canteen.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
school canteen 学校食堂；army canteen 军用水壶；canteen food 食堂餐食
🧠 助记
can（罐）+ teen（十几岁）→ 带罐子去吃饭的学生，联想canteen=食堂
canteen::/kænˈtiːn/:n. 食堂:The canteen serves hot meals daily.
4. condense /kənˈdens/ v. 凝结；压缩；浓缩
📖 英文解释
To change from a gas to a liquid; to make something shorter while keeping the essential meaning.
✏️ 例句
Steam condenses into water when cooled.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
condense water vapour 冷凝水蒸气；condensed milk 炼乳；condense a speech 压缩演讲
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ dense（密集）→ 使密集在一起 = 凝结/压缩
condense::/kənˈdens/:v. 凝结:He condensed his report into one page.
5. denote /dɪˈnəʊt/ v. 表示；指示；意味着
📖 英文解释
To be a sign of something; to mean something; to represent.
✏️ 例句
The symbol @ denotes 'at' in email addresses.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
denote meaning 表示含义；denote a change 表明变化；what does X denote 表示什么
🧠 助记
de（下）+ note（注释）→ 下面加了注释，表示含义 = 表示/意味着
denote::/dɪˈnəʊt/:v. 表示:Red usually denotes danger.
6. differentiate /ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪt/ v. 区别；区分；使有差异
📖 英文解释
To recognize or express the difference between things; to make or become different.
✏️ 例句
It's hard to differentiate between the two products.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
differentiate from 与…区分；differentiate between A and B 区分A和B；product differentiation 产品差异化
🧠 助记
different（不同）+ iate → 使不同 = 区分/区别
differentiate::/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪt/:v. 区别:The test differentiates strong students from weak ones.
7. grin /ɡrɪn/ v./n. 露齿而笑；咧嘴笑
📖 英文解释
To smile broadly, showing the teeth.
✏️ 例句
He grinned from ear to ear when he heard the good news.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
grin and bear it 逆来顺受；ear-to-ear grin 咧嘴大笑；grin at sb 对某人咧嘴笑
🧠 助记
grin → 谐音「咧嘴」→ 咧嘴露出牙齿 = 咧嘴笑
grin::/ɡrɪn/:v./n. 露齿而笑:She grinned when she saw the gift.
8. harbor /ˈhɑːbə/ n. 港口；避风港 v. 藏匿；心存（怀疑等）
📖 英文解释
A place on the coast where ships may shelter; to give shelter to someone; to hold a feeling.
✏️ 例句
The ship sailed into the harbor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
harbor resentment 怀恨在心；harbor criminals 窝藏罪犯；natural harbor 天然港湾
🧠 助记
har（海）+ bor（bore孔穴）→ 海边的孔穴 = 港口/避风港
harbor::/ˈhɑːbə/:n. 港口:The village has a small fishing harbor.
9. haste /heɪst/ n. 急速；匆忙
📖 英文解释
Excessive speed or urgency of movement or action.
✏️ 例句
In her haste, she forgot her keys.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in haste 匆忙地；make haste 赶快；haste makes waste 欲速则不达
🧠 助记
haste → 谐音「嗨速」→ 嗨速跑起来 = 急速/匆忙
haste::/heɪst/:n. 急速:He left in haste without saying goodbye.
10. posture /ˈpɒstʃə/ n. 姿势；姿态；立场
📖 英文解释
The position in which someone holds their body; an attitude or approach.
✏️ 例句
Good posture is important for your health.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
post（位置）+ ure → 保持的位置状态 = 姿势/姿态
posture::/ˈpɒstʃə/:n. 姿势:She sat with a perfect posture.
11. radar /ˈreɪdɑː/ n. 雷达
📖 英文解释
A system for detecting the presence, direction, distance, and speed of aircraft and ships using radio waves.
✏️ 例句
The plane disappeared from the radar screen.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
radar screen 雷达屏幕；under the radar 不引人注意；radar detector 雷达探测器
🧠 助记
radar是回文词：Radio Detection And Ranging = 雷达，正反都一样
radar::/ˈreɪdɑː/:n. 雷达:The ship was tracked by radar.
12. nickname /ˈnɪkneɪm/ n. 昵称；绰号 v. 给…起绰号
📖 英文解释
A familiar or humorous name given to a person or thing instead of the real name.
✏️ 例句
His nickname at school was 'Red' because of his hair.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
earn a nickname 得到绰号；give sb a nickname 给某人起绰号；be nicknamed 被称为
🧠 助记
nick（缺口/小名）+ name（名字）→ 有缺口的名字（不完整）= 昵称/绰号
nickname::/ˈnɪkneɪm/:n. 昵称:Her nickname is Sunny.
13. preside /prɪˈzaɪd/ v. 主持；担任主席
📖 英文解释

preside::/prɪˈzaɪd/:v. 主持:She presided over the committee meeting.
14. rip /rɪp/ v./n. 撕裂；猛拉

rip::/rɪp/:v./n. 撕裂:She ripped the letter open excitedly.
15. toast /təʊst/ n. 烤面包；祝酒词 v. 烤；举杯祝酒

vacuum::/ˈvækjuəm/:n. 真空:She vacuumed the living room carpet.

balloon::/bəˈluːn/:n. 气球:The debt ballooned over the years.

flesh::/fleʃ/:n. 肉体:She cut into the white flesh of the coconut.
