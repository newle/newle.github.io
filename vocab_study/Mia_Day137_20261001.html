1. poke /pəʊk/ v./n. 戳；刺；捅
📖 英文解释
To push a finger or a pointed object at something; a quick push with a finger or pointed object.
✏️ 例句
She poked him in the ribs to wake him up.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
poke fun at 嘲弄；poke around 到处翻找；poke one's nose in 干涉他人事务
🧠 助记
poke → 谐音「剖克」→ 用尖物剖开 = 戳/刺
poke::/pəʊk/:v./n. 戳:He poked the fire with a stick.
2. radiate /ˈreɪdieɪt/ v. 辐射；散发；流露
📖 英文解释
To emit energy as radiation; to send out rays of light or heat; to express a quality strongly.
✏️ 例句
The sun radiates heat and light.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
radiate energy 辐射能量；radiate confidence 散发自信；radiate heat 散热
🧠 助记
radi（radius半径）+ ate → 向四周（半径方向）散发 = 辐射
radiate::/ˈreɪdieɪt/:v. 辐射:She radiates warmth and happiness.
3. rein /reɪn/ n. 缰绳；控制 v. 用缰绳控制；抑制
📖 英文解释
A long narrow strap attached to a horse's bridle; to control or limit.
✏️ 例句
She pulled on the reins to stop the horse.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
give free rein to 给…自由发挥；rein in 控制/抑制；hold the reins 掌权
🧠 助记
rein → 谐音「拉紧」→ 拉紧缰绳 = 缰绳/控制
rein::/reɪn/:n. 缰绳:The government reined in public spending.
4. rot /rɒt/ v./n. 腐烂；变质；废话
📖 英文解释
To decompose through bacterial or fungal action; the process of rotting.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rot away 腐烂殆尽；tooth rot 龋齿；stop the rot 阻止恶化；what rot! 胡说！
🧠 助记
rot → 谐音「烂了」→ 腐烂了 = 腐烂
rot::/rɒt/:v./n. 腐烂:Fallen leaves rot into compost.
5. sigh /saɪ/ v./n. 叹气；叹息
📖 英文解释
To emit a long deep breath expressing sadness, relief, or tiredness.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
sigh → 谐音「唉」→ 叹气发「唉」声 = 叹气
sigh::/saɪ/:v./n. 叹气:He sighed and shook his head sadly.
6. singular /ˈsɪŋɡjələ/ adj. 单数的；非凡的；奇特的
📖 英文解释
Referring to just one person or thing (grammar); remarkably good or great; strange.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
single（单个）+ ar → 只有一个的 = 单数的；也引申为非凡的
singular::/ˈsɪŋɡjələ/:adj. 单数的:She showed a singular dedication to her work.
7. smog /smɒɡ/ n. 烟雾；雾霾

🧠 助记

smog::/smɒɡ/:n. 烟雾:The city issued a smog warning.

startle::/ˈstɑːtəl/:v. 使惊吓:The cat was startled by the doorbell.
