1. lap /læp/ n. 膝部；重叠部分 v. 重叠；轻拍；舐
📖 英文解释
The flat area formed by the thighs when sitting; one circuit of a track; to overlap; to take up liquid with the tongue.
✏️ 例句
The cat curled up on her lap.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in the lap of luxury 非常舒适；lap up 贪婪地接受；lap a competitor 套圈对手
🧠 助记
联想 flap（拍打）→ 膝盖轻拍 → lap（膝部/轻拍）；lap 赛道的一圈
lap::/læp/:n. 膝部:The runner completed the final lap in record time.
2. litre /ˈliːtər/ n. 公升（英式拼写，等于 liter）
📖 英文解释
A metric unit of capacity equal to 1,000 cubic centimeters or approximately 1.76 pints.
✏️ 例句
She drank two litres of water during the hike.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
litre of water/petrol 一升水/汽油；half a litre 半升；millilitre 毫升
🧠 助记
联想 little（小）→ 比大桶小一点的量 → 公升；liter 美式，litre 英式
litre::/ˈliːtər/:n. 公升（英式拼写，等于 liter）:The fuel tank holds about 50 litres of petrol.

🧠 助记

lofty::/ˈlɒfti/:adj. 高的:She pursued lofty academic goals throughout her career.

magnet::/ˈmæɡnɪt/:n. 磁铁:The new tech hub became a magnet for talent from around the world.
