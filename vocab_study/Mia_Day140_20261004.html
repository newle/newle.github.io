1. thirsty /ˈθɜːsti/ adj. 口渴的；渴望的
📖 英文解释
Feeling a need to drink; having a strong desire for something.
✏️ 例句
After the marathon, the runners were extremely thirsty.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
thirsty for knowledge/power 渴望知识/权力；feel thirsty 感到口渴；thirst n. 口渴
🧠 助记
thirst（渴）+ -y → 充满渴望的 → 口渴的；联想 first（第一需求就是水）
thirsty::/ˈθɜːsti/:adj. 口渴的:She was thirsty for recognition after years of working without acknowledgment.
2. censorship /ˈsensəʃɪp/ n. 审查制度；新闻检查
📖 英文解释
The practice of officially examining books, films, etc. and suppressing unacceptable parts; control of information.
✏️ 例句
Internet censorship limits citizens' access to information in some countries.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
media/internet censorship 媒体/网络审查；impose censorship 实施审查；censor v./n.
🧠 助记
censor（审查员）+ -ship（制度）→ 审查员的制度 → 审查制度
censorship::/ˈsensəʃɪp/:n. 审查制度:Critics argued that the new regulation amounted to a form of press censorship.
3. slot /slɒt/ n. 槽；位置；空档 v. 放入槽中；安排
📖 英文解释
A long narrow opening; a position or time allotted in a schedule; to fit into a slot.
✏️ 例句
Insert the coin into the slot to start the machine.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
time slot 时间段；slot machine 老虎机；slot into 嵌入；prime-time slot 黄金时段
🧠 助记
联想 slit（缝隙）→ slot 是更方形的缝隙 → 槽；插入卡片的槽
slot::/slɒt/:n. 槽:She was given a prime-time slot to present her research at the conference.
4. affirm /əˈfɜːm/ v. 肯定；断言；确认
📖 英文解释
To state or assert positively; to confirm or ratify; to declare publicly one's support.
✏️ 例句
The CEO affirmed the company's commitment to sustainability.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
affirm that 肯定……；affirm a decision 确认决定；affirmative adj. 肯定的
🧠 助记
af-（加强）+ firm（坚定）→ 坚定地说 → 肯定、断言；联想 confirm（确认）
affirm::/əˈfɜːm/:v. 肯定:The court affirmed the lower court's decision in a unanimous ruling.
5. assimilate /əˈsɪmɪleɪt/ v. 吸收；同化；消化理解
📖 英文解释
To take in and fully understand information; to absorb into a group; to make similar.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
assimilate information/ideas 吸收信息/思想；be assimilated into 被同化；assimilation n.
🧠 助记
as-（朝向）+ simil-（相似）+ -ate → 使相似 → 同化；联想 similar
assimilate::/əˈsɪmɪleɪt/:v. 吸收:New employees need time to assimilate the company culture and working practices.
6. frown /fraʊn/ v./n. 皱眉；不赞成

✏️ 例句

🧠 助记

frown::/fraʊn/:v./n. 皱眉:Society tends to frown upon those who cut in line or ignore social norms.
7. lateral /ˈlætərəl/ adj. 侧面的；横向的 n. 侧面

lateral::/ˈlætərəl/:adj. 侧面的:She made a lateral career move to gain experience in a different department.
8. preface /ˈprefɪs/ n. 前言；序言 v. 以……开头

preface::/ˈprefɪs/:n. 前言:She prefaced her remarks with a brief explanation of the project's background.
9. query /ˈkwɪəri/ n./v. 疑问；质问；查询

query::/ˈkwɪəri/:n./v. 疑问:The manager received numerous queries from staff about the new policy changes.
