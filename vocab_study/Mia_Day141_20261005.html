1. commonwealth /ˈkɒmənwelθ/ n. 联邦；共同体；共和国
📖 英文解释
A group of nations or states united by a common interest or shared history; a political association of countries, esp. former British territories.
✏️ 例句
Australia is a member of the Commonwealth of Nations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常考搭配：the Commonwealth of Nations（英联邦）；commonwealth of learning（学术共同体）。考研常考其政治意义。
🧠 助记
common（共同）+ wealth（财富/繁荣）→ 共同繁荣的联合体 → 联邦
commonwealth::/ˈkɒmənwelθ/:n. 联邦:Australia joined the commonwealth for mutual benefit.
2. congratulate /kənˈɡrætʃuleɪt/ v. 祝贺；道喜
📖 英文解释
To express joy or acknowledgment to (someone) on the occasion of success, good fortune, or achievement.
✏️ 例句
She congratulated him on winning the championship.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
固定搭配：congratulate sb. on sth.（就某事祝贺某人）。考研写作常用。
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ gratul（感激）+ ate → 共同感激庆贺 → 祝贺
congratulate::/kənˈɡrætʃuleɪt/:v. 祝贺:We congratulate you on your graduation.
3. cricket /ˈkrɪkɪt/ n. 板球（运动）；蟋蟀
📖 英文解释
1. An outdoor game played with a bat and ball between two teams of eleven players. 2. A small jumping insect that makes a chirping sound.
✏️ 例句
Cricket is extremely popular in India and England.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研阅读中常见英国文化背景词。注意两个截然不同的含义：运动 vs 昆虫。
🧠 助记
cr（爬）+ icket → 蟋蟀爬行时发声；板球起源于英国乡村草地
cricket::/ˈkrɪkɪt/:n. 板球（运动）:He played cricket every weekend in summer.
4. diversion /daɪˈvɜːʃn/ n. 转移；消遣；绕行
📖 英文解释
The act of diverting or turning aside; something that amuses or relaxes; a detour or alternative route.
✏️ 例句
The magician used a diversion to distract the audience.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：diversion of attention（转移注意力）；create a diversion（制造转移）。
🧠 助记
di（偏离）+ version（方向）→ 偏离方向 → 转移；绕行
diversion::/daɪˈvɜːʃn/:n. 转移:The road closure caused a lengthy diversion.
5. ecology /iˈkɒlədʒi/ n. 生态学；生态
📖 英文解释
The branch of biology dealing with the relationships between organisms and their environment; the study of ecosystems.
✏️ 例句
Human activities have severely disrupted the ecology of the rainforest.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频学术词。搭配：ecological balance（生态平衡）；ecosystem（生态系统）。
🧠 助记
eco（家园/生态）+ logy（学科）→ 研究生态的学科 → 生态学
ecology::/iˈkɒlədʒi/:n. 生态学:We must protect the ecology of coastal wetlands.
6. elbow /ˈelbəʊ/ n. 肘部 v. 用肘推挤
📖 英文解释
The joint between the upper and lower parts of the arm; to push one's way using one's elbows.
✏️ 例句
He elbowed his way through the crowd to reach the front.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考词组：elbow room（活动余地）；elbow one's way（挤出一条路）。
🧠 助记
el（L形）+ bow（弯曲）→ 手臂弯曲的L形部位 → 肘部
elbow::/ˈelbəʊ/:n. 肘部 v. 用肘推挤:She elbowed past the crowd to get on the train.
7. equity /ˈekwɪti/ n. 公平；股权；净资产
📖 英文解释
The quality of being fair and impartial; in finance, the value of shares or ownership interest in a company.
✏️ 例句
The company distributed equity to its early employees.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研经济、法律语境必考词。区分：equity（公平/股权）vs equality（平等）。
🧠 助记
equ（equal平等）+ ity → 公平公正的状态 → 公平；股权
equity::/ˈekwɪti/:n. 公平:Equity in education remains a major social challenge.
8. guild /ɡɪld/ n. 行会；协会；同业公会
📖 英文解释
A medieval association of craftsmen or merchants; a modern association of people with shared interests or professions.
✏️ 例句
The writers' guild negotiated better pay for its members.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研历史、文化语境词。搭配：trade guild（贸易行会）；writers' guild（作家协会）。
🧠 助记
guild = gold（黄金）的变体 → 金子般宝贵的商人联盟 → 行会
guild::/ɡɪld/:n. 行会:The guild protected the rights of local craftsmen.
9. instrumental /ˌɪnstrəˈmentl/ adj. 有帮助的；器乐的；起重要作用的
📖 英文解释
Serving as an instrument or means; of crucial importance in achieving something; relating to musical instruments.
✏️ 例句
She was instrumental in bringing about the peace agreement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频词。固定搭配：be instrumental in doing sth.（在…中起关键作用）。
🧠 助记
instrument（工具/乐器）+ al → 像工具一样起关键作用 → 有帮助的
instrumental::/ˌɪnstrəˈmentl/:adj. 有帮助的:He was instrumental in founding the new research center.
10. keyboard /ˈkiːbɔːd/ n. 键盘
📖 英文解释
A panel of keys used to operate a computer, typewriter, or musical instrument; the set of keys arranged in rows.
✏️ 例句
She typed rapidly on the keyboard without looking down.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研科技类阅读常见词。keyboard shortcut（键盘快捷键）；keyboard skills（打字技能）。
🧠 助记
key（键）+ board（板）→ 有很多键的板 → 键盘
keyboard::/ˈkiːbɔːd/:n. 键盘:The keyboard of this laptop is very comfortable to type on.
11. latitude /ˈlætɪtjuːd/ n. 纬度；自由度；回旋余地
📖 英文解释
The angular distance of a place north or south of the equator; freedom from narrow restrictions; scope for action.
✏️ 例句
Teachers should allow students some latitude in their creative projects.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研双义词。latitude在地理意义外，常考引申义：give sb. latitude（给某人自由度）。
🧠 助记
lati（宽）+ tude → 宽广的范围 → 纬度（横向延伸）；自由度
latitude::/ˈlætɪtjuːd/:n. 纬度:The new policy gives employees more latitude in decision-making.
12. liquor /ˈlɪkə(r)/ n. 烈酒；酒精饮料
📖 英文解释
Alcoholic drink, especially distilled spirits; any liquid substance used in a particular process.
✏️ 例句
The store is not allowed to sell liquor to minors.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研社会类阅读词。liquor store（酒类专卖店）；hard liquor（烈酒）。
🧠 助记
liquor → liquid（液体）的变体 → 特指含酒精的液体 → 烈酒
liquor::/ˈlɪkə(r)/:n. 烈酒:Excessive liquor consumption poses serious health risks.
13. manuscript /ˈmænjuskrɪpt/ n. 手稿；原稿；抄本
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句
The author submitted his manuscript to the publisher last week.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研学术写作高频词。manuscript vs. published work（手稿 vs 已发表作品）。
🧠 助记
manu（手）+ script（写）→ 用手写的东西 → 手稿
manuscript::/ˈmænjuskrɪpt/:n. 手稿:The professor's manuscript was accepted by a top journal.
14. plough /plaʊ/ v./n. 耕犁；费力前行
📖 英文解释

plough发音像'扒啊'→ 扒开土地 → 耕犁
plough::/plaʊ/:v./n. 耕犁:She ploughed through dozens of research papers for her thesis.
15. suite /swiːt/ n. 套房；套件；组曲

suite::/swiːt/:n. 套房:The hotel suite offered a stunning view of the ocean.

workout::/ˈwɜːkaʊt/:n. 锻炼:A regular workout improves both physical and mental health.

advent::/ˈædvent/:n. 到来:The advent of AI has revolutionized many industries.
