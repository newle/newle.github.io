1. inventory /ˈɪnvəntri/ n. 存货；清单；库存 v. 盘存
📖 英文解释
A complete list of items such as property, goods in stock, or the contents of a building; to make such a list.
✏️ 例句
The store manager conducted an inventory of all remaining products.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研商科常见词。inventory management（库存管理）；take inventory（盘点库存）。
🧠 助记
invent（发现）+ ory → 把所有东西都发现记下来 → 清单；库存
inventory::/ˈɪnvəntri/:n. 存货:The warehouse team updated the inventory every month.
2. motel /məʊˈtel/ n. 汽车旅馆
📖 英文解释
A roadside hotel designed primarily for motorists, typically having rooms adjacent to a parking area.
✏️ 例句
They stopped at a motel for the night during their road trip.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研词汇，motor（汽车）+ hotel（旅馆）合并词。常见于旅行类阅读。
🧠 助记
mot（motor汽车）+ el（hotel旅馆）→ 汽车旅馆
motel::/məʊˈtel/:n. 汽车旅馆:The travelers checked into a motel along the highway.
3. naval /ˈneɪvl/ adj. 海军的；舰队的
📖 英文解释
Relating to a navy or navies; connected with warships or naval forces.
✏️ 例句
The country strengthened its naval forces to protect trade routes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研军事、历史类阅读词。naval base（海军基地）；naval battle（海战）。
🧠 助记
nav（航行）+ al → 与航行/海军有关 → 海军的
naval::/ˈneɪvl/:adj. 海军的:The naval exercise demonstrated the country's military strength.
4. notebook /ˈnəʊtbʊk/ n. 笔记本；笔记本电脑
📖 英文解释
A small book with blank or ruled pages for writing notes; a portable laptop computer.
✏️ 例句
She filled an entire notebook with research observations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研科技类词汇。notebook computer（笔记本电脑）；lab notebook（实验记录本）。
🧠 助记
note（笔记）+ book（书）→ 记笔记的书 → 笔记本
notebook::/ˈnəʊtbʊk/:n. 笔记本:The student kept a detailed notebook of lecture notes.
5. palm /pɑːm/ n. 手掌；棕榈树 v. 藏于掌中
📖 英文解释
The inner surface of the hand; a tropical tree with a crown of long leaves; to conceal in the hand.
✏️ 例句
The magician palmed the coin so skillfully that no one noticed.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研双义词。palm off（以次充好）；grease sb.'s palm（贿赂）。
🧠 助记
palm → 手掌形状像棕榈叶 → 手掌；棕榈树
palm::/pɑːm/:n. 手掌:She held the tiny bird in the palm of her hand.
6. relay /ˈriːleɪ/ v. 传递；转播 n. 接力；中继
📖 英文解释
To receive and pass on (information or a message); a race in which teams take turns; an electrical device for controlling a circuit.
✏️ 例句
The journalist relayed the breaking news to viewers across the country.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：relay race（接力赛）；relay information（传递信息）。
🧠 助记
re（再）+ lay（放置）→ 再次放置/传递 → 传递；接力
relay::/ˈriːleɪ/:v. 传递:The message was relayed through multiple communication channels.
7. sandwich /ˈsænwɪdʒ/ n. 三明治 v. 夹入；挤入
📖 英文解释
Two slices of bread with a filling between them; to insert or squeeze between two things.
✏️ 例句
The new building was sandwiched between two older structures.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常见引申动词用法：be sandwiched between（被夹在中间）。sandwich course（三明治课程）。
🧠 助记
sandwich → sand（沙子）+ wich → 像沙粒被夹住一样 → 三明治；夹入
sandwich::/ˈsænwɪdʒ/:n. 三明治 v. 夹入:The small apartment was sandwiched between two noisy restaurants.
8. scent /sent/ n. 气味；香味；线索 v. 察觉；使充满香气
📖 英文解释
A distinctive smell, especially a pleasant one; a trail indicated by a smell; to sense by smell; a perfume.
✏️ 例句
The garden was filled with the sweet scent of roses.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考搭配：scent of danger（危险的气息）；follow a scent（追踪气味/线索）。
🧠 助记
scent → sent（送来）谐音 → 飘来的气味 → 气味；香味
scent::/sent/:n. 气味:The detective followed the scent of the suspect through the forest.
9. shrewd /ʃruːd/ adj. 精明的；眼光锐利的；狡猾的
📖 英文解释
Having or showing sharp powers of judgement; astute; clever at understanding and profiting from situations.
✏️ 例句
The shrewd investor bought shares at the lowest point in the market.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频形容词。shrewd businessman（精明的商人）；shrewd move（精明之举）。
🧠 助记
shrew（泼妇，聪明厉害的人）+ d → 精明厉害 → 精明的
shrewd::/ʃruːd/:adj. 精明的:Her shrewd negotiation saved the company millions of dollars.
10. specialise /ˈspeʃəlaɪz/ v. 专门研究；专攻；专业化
📖 英文解释
To concentrate on and become expert in a particular subject or skill; to adapt for a specific function.
✏️ 例句
He decided to specialise in environmental law after graduation.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常见词（英式拼写，美式specialize）。specialise in（专门从事）是固定搭配。
🧠 助记
special（特别的）+ ise → 做特别的、专门的事情 → 专攻
specialise::/ˈspeʃəlaɪz/:v. 专门研究:She chose to specialise in pediatric medicine after medical school.
11. stance /stæns/ n. 立场；姿势；态度
📖 英文解释
The way in which someone stands; a person's attitude or position on a particular issue.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频词。take a stance on（对…表明立场）；political stance（政治立场）。
🧠 助记
stan（站立）+ ce → 站立的方式 → 姿势；立场
stance::/stæns/:n. 立场:The candidate clarified her stance on immigration policy.
12. veto /ˈviːtəʊ/ n./v. 否决权；否决
📖 英文解释

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
veto拉丁语'我禁止'→ 我禁止这个 → 否决权
veto::/ˈviːtəʊ/:n./v. 否决权:The UN Security Council member threatened to use its veto.
13. waist /weɪst/ n. 腰部；腰身

waist::/weɪst/:n. 腰部:Regular exercise helps maintain a healthy waist measurement.
