1. ridge /rɪdʒ/ n. 脊；山脊；屋脊
📖 英文解释
A long, narrow elevated section of land, a mountain range, or the top edge of a roof.
✏️ 例句
We hiked along the ridge of the mountain and enjoyed the panoramic view.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
mountain ridge 山脊；ridge of a roof 屋脊；搭配 along the ridge
🧠 助记
ridge = rid + ge，想象一条'脊背'把两边分开，如同山脊
ridge::/rɪdʒ/:n. 脊:We hiked along the ridge of the mountain.
2. rust /rʌst/ v./n. 生锈；铁锈
📖 英文解释
A reddish-brown coating formed on iron when exposed to air and moisture; to become covered with rust.
✏️ 例句
The old bike was left outside and started to rust after the rainy season.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
rust away 锈蚀掉；rust-proof 防锈的；考研常见于描述废弃、腐蚀
🧠 助记
rust 和 dust（灰尘）押韵，金属和灰尘一样都是废弃物的象征
rust::/rʌst/:v./n. 生锈:The iron gate began to rust in the damp air.
3. sauce /sɔːs/ n. 酱汁；调味汁
📖 英文解释
A liquid or semi-liquid mixture served with food to add flavor or moisture.
✏️ 例句
She poured tomato sauce over the pasta and sprinkled some cheese on top.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tomato sauce 番茄酱；soy sauce 酱油；hot sauce 辣酱
🧠 助记
sauce 发音像'扫死'，厨师扫了一勺酱汁倒进去
sauce::/sɔːs/:n. 酱汁:Add a spoonful of sauce to enhance the flavor.
4. sibling /ˈsɪblɪŋ/ n. 兄弟姐妹（统称）
📖 英文解释
A brother or sister; a person who has the same parents as another person.
✏️ 例句
She has two siblings: an older brother and a younger sister.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sibling rivalry 兄弟姐妹间的竞争；考研阅读中常代替 brother/sister 出现
🧠 助记
sib + ling，sib 是'亲属'的古语词根，ling 是小词缀，合起来是小亲属
sibling::/ˈsɪblɪŋ/:n. 兄弟姐妹（统称）:Growing up with siblings teaches children how to share.
5. slap /slæp/ v./n. 掴；打耳光；侮辱
📖 英文解释
To hit someone or something with the palm of the hand; a smacking blow.
✏️ 例句
She slapped him across the face when he said something offensive.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
slap in the face 打耳光（也喻指羞辱）；slap on the back 拍背鼓励
🧠 助记
slap 发音有爆发感，'slap！'一巴掌声
slap::/slæp/:v./n. 掴:He gave the table a slap to emphasize his point.
6. stale /steɪl/ adj. 陈腐的；不新鲜的；无聊的
📖 英文解释
No longer fresh; having lost its original taste, quality, or interest.
✏️ 例句
The bread had gone stale after sitting out for two days.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stale bread 不新鲜的面包；stale ideas 陈旧的想法；stale air 污浊的空气
🧠 助记
stale = s + tale，陈年老故事（tale）就是 stale（陈腐的）
stale::/steɪl/:adj. 陈腐的:His jokes were so stale that nobody laughed.
7. strap /stræp/ n./v. 皮带；带子；用带捆绑
📖 英文解释
A narrow strip of flexible material used for fastening, carrying, or holding things together.
✏️ 例句
She fastened the strap of her backpack before heading out.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
shoulder strap 肩带；chin strap 下颚带；strap in 系好安全带
🧠 助记
strap = str（强壮）+ ap，用结实的带子捆住
strap::/stræp/:n./v. 皮带:Make sure to strap your luggage securely before the trip.
8. submerge /səbˈmɜːrdʒ/ v. 潜入水中；浸没；淹没
📖 英文解释
To put or go under water or another liquid; to cover or overflow with water.
✏️ 例句
The submarine submerged quickly to avoid detection.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
submerge in water 浸入水中；submerge oneself in 沉浸于；考研常用于自然灾害语境
🧠 助记
sub（下）+ merge（融合）= 往下沉入水中融合
submerge::/səbˈmɜːrdʒ/:v. 潜入水中:The flood waters submerged the entire village.
9. supper /ˈsʌpər/ n. 晚餐；晚饭
📖 英文解释
A meal eaten in the evening, typically the main meal of the day.
✏️ 例句
The family gathered around the table for supper every evening.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
have supper 吃晚饭；supper time 晚餐时间；与 dinner 相比更口语化
🧠 助记
supper 和 super（超级）同音，晚饭是超级重要的一餐
supper::/ˈsʌpər/:n. 晚餐:She cooked a warm supper for the whole family.
10. tilt /tɪlt/ v./n. 倾斜；倾侧
📖 英文解释
To move or cause to move into a sloping position; an inclination or slope.
✏️ 例句
He tilted his head to one side as he listened carefully.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
tilt at windmills 做徒劳的事；at full tilt 全速；tilt angle 倾斜角
🧠 助记
tilt 的字母形状就像斜着写的，本身就是倾斜
tilt::/tɪlt/:v./n. 倾斜:The tower began to tilt dangerously after the earthquake.
11. trademark /ˈtreɪdmɑːrk/ n. 商标；标志；特征
📖 英文解释
A symbol, word, or phrase legally registered to represent a company or product; a distinctive characteristic.
✏️ 例句
The company's trademark is recognized worldwide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
registered trademark 注册商标；trademark infringement 商标侵权；考研商业法律语境
🧠 助记
trade（贸易）+ mark（标记）= 商业标记 = 商标
trademark::/ˈtreɪdmɑːrk/:n. 商标:They filed for a trademark to protect their brand name.
12. trim /trɪm/ v./n. 修剪；装饰；整齐的
📖 英文解释
To cut away irregular or unwanted parts; to decorate something; neat and tidy.
✏️ 例句
The barber trimmed his beard to make it look neat.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
trim the budget 削减预算；in trim 状态好；trim down 减少
🧠 助记
trim = tr + im，想象用剪刀剪掉（trim）多余部分
trim::/trɪm/:v./n. 修剪:She trimmed the hedges along the garden path.
13. typist /ˈtaɪpɪst/ n. 打字员
📖 英文解释
A person who types documents, especially as a job.
✏️ 例句
The typist could type 80 words per minute without making any mistakes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研中主要作为职业词汇出现；fast typist 快速打字员
🧠 助记
type（打字）+ ist（人）= 打字的人 = 打字员
typist::/ˈtaɪpɪst/:n. 打字员:The company hired an experienced typist to handle correspondence.
14. venue /ˈvenjuː/ n. 审判地；犯罪地点；举办地点
📖 英文解释
The place where an event or meeting is held; the location where a legal case is tried.
✏️ 例句
The concert venue was packed with thousands of excited fans.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
venue for a conference 会议场地；change of venue 变更审判地点（法律）
🧠 助记
venue 和 avenue（大道）有相同词根，来到某处的地点
venue::/ˈvenjuː/:n. 审判地:They chose a beautiful outdoor venue for the wedding ceremony.
15. versatile /ˈvɜːrsətaɪl/ adj. 多才多艺的；通用的；多功能的
📖 英文解释
Able to adapt or be adapted to many different functions or activities.
✏️ 例句
She is a versatile actress who can play both comedic and dramatic roles.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
versatile tool 多功能工具；versatile player 多面手；考研高频形容词
🧠 助记
vers（转）+ atile，能转向各种方向 = 多才多艺
versatile::/ˈvɜːrsətaɪl/:adj. 多才多艺的:A versatile skill set is highly valued in today's job market.
16. yearly /ˈjɪərli/ adj./adv. 每年的；一年一次的
📖 英文解释
Happening or produced once a year or every year.
✏️ 例句
The company holds a yearly review to assess employee performance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
yearly income 年收入；yearly report 年度报告；yearly basis 每年
🧠 助记
year（年）+ ly（副词/形容词后缀）= 每年的
yearly::/ˈjɪərli/:adj./adv. 每年的:The park conducts a yearly count of migratory birds.
17. necklace /ˈnekləs/ n. 项链
📖 英文解释
A piece of jewellery worn around the neck, typically a string of beads or a chain.
✏️ 例句
She wore a gold necklace to the formal dinner.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
diamond necklace 钻石项链；bead necklace 珠串项链；考研词汇表基础词
🧠 助记
neck（脖子）+ lace（花边/细带）= 脖子上的细带 = 项链
necklace::/ˈnekləs/:n. 项链:She received a beautiful pearl necklace as a birthday gift.
18. thrift /θrɪft/ n. 节俭；节约
📖 英文解释
The quality of using money and other resources carefully and not wastefully.
✏️ 例句
Her thrift allowed her to save enough money to buy a house.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
thrift shop 二手店；practice thrift 厉行节约；thrifty adj. 节俭的
🧠 助记
thrift 和 drift（漂移）押韵，节俭的人钱不会乱漂移
thrift::/θrɪft/:n. 节俭:Thrift is considered a virtue in many traditional cultures.
19. weary /ˈwɪəri/ adj./v. 疲倦的；厌烦的；使疲倦
📖 英文解释
Feeling or showing extreme tiredness, especially as a result of excessive exertion; to make someone tired.
✏️ 例句
After the long journey, the travellers were weary and needed rest.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
weary of 对……感到厌倦；grow weary 变得疲惫；war-weary 厌战的
🧠 助记
weary 里有 wear（穿戴），穿了一整天东西很累 = 疲倦
weary::/ˈwɪəri/:adj./v. 疲倦的:She grew weary of answering the same questions over and over.
20. aeroplane /ˈeərəpleɪn/ n. 飞机（英式）
📖 英文解释
A powered flying vehicle with fixed wings and a weight greater than that of the air it displaces (British English).
✏️ 例句
The aeroplane took off smoothly despite the strong winds.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
英式英语，美式为 airplane；aeroplane ticket 机票；考研偶见
🧠 助记
aero（空气）+ plane（平面/飞机）= 在空中的平面 = 飞机
aeroplane::/ˈeərəpleɪn/:n. 飞机（英式）:The aeroplane landed safely at Heathrow Airport.
21. cement /sɪˈment/ n./v. 水泥；巩固（关系）
📖 英文解释
A grey powder that is mixed with water and used in building to join bricks or make concrete; to fix firmly.
✏️ 例句
The workers mixed cement and sand to lay the foundation of the building.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cement a relationship 巩固关系；cement block 水泥砖；考研常见比喻用法
🧠 助记
ce + ment，想象工人在 cement（水泥）里 ment（埋）了什么
cement::/sɪˈment/:n./v. 水泥:The treaty helped to cement the alliance between the two nations.
22. inspect /ɪnˈspekt/ v. 检查；视察；检验
📖 英文解释
To look at something carefully in order to assess its condition or to detect any problems.
✏️ 例句
The health inspector came to inspect the restaurant's kitchen.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
inspect the troops 检阅部队；inspect for defects 检查缺陷；inspection n.
🧠 助记
in + spect（看）= 往里看 = 仔细检查
inspect::/ɪnˈspekt/:v. 检查:Officials arrived to inspect the construction site for safety violations.
23. roundabout /ˈraʊndəbaʊt/ adj./n. 迂回的；环形交叉路口
📖 英文解释
Not using the shortest or most direct route; a road junction at which traffic moves in one direction around a central island.
✏️ 例句
He took a roundabout way home to avoid the traffic jam.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in a roundabout way 以迂回方式；traffic roundabout 环形路口
🧠 助记
round（圆）+ about（关于/周围）= 绕圈子 = 迂回的
roundabout::/ˈraʊndəbaʊt/:adj./n. 迂回的:She gave a roundabout answer instead of addressing the question directly.
24. abide /əˈbaɪd/ v. 遵守；忍受；停留
📖 英文解释
To accept or act in accordance with a rule or decision; to be unable to tolerate someone or something.
✏️ 例句
All employees must abide by the company's code of conduct.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
abide by the rules 遵守规则；can't abide 无法忍受；abide with 与……同住（古语）
🧠 助记
a + bide（等待/留住）= 留在规则里 = 遵守
abide::/əˈbaɪd/:v. 遵守:Citizens are expected to abide by the laws of the country.
25. ashore /əˈʃɔːr/ adv. 上岸；在岸上
📖 英文解释
To or on the shore or land, as opposed to at sea.
✏️ 例句
After weeks at sea, the sailors were glad to come ashore.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
go ashore 上岸；washed ashore 被冲上岸；swim ashore 游上岸
🧠 助记
a + shore（岸）= 到岸边 = 上岸
ashore::/əˈʃɔːr/:adv. 上岸:The survivors swam ashore after the boat capsized.
26. chimney /ˈtʃɪmni/ n. 烟囱
📖 英文解释
A vertical channel or pipe that allows smoke and combustion gases to escape from a fireplace or furnace.
✏️ 例句
Smoke rose from the chimney of the old farmhouse.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
chimney sweep 烟囱清洁工；smoke like a chimney 抽烟很凶；chimney stack 烟囱管
🧠 助记
chimney 里有 him，烟囱里藏着'他'（圣诞老人从烟囱进屋）
chimney::/ˈtʃɪmni/:n. 烟囱:The old brick chimney needed to be repaired before winter.
27. claw /klɔː/ n./v. 爪；爪子；用爪抓
📖 英文解释
A curved, pointed horny nail on each digit of the foot in birds, lizards, and some mammals; to scratch or tear with claws.
✏️ 例句
The cat used its claws to scratch the furniture.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
claw one's way 艰难地挣扎前进；claw back 收回、夺回；claws of a crab 蟹爪
🧠 助记
claw 发音像'抓哦'，爪子抓东西
claw::/klɔː/:n./v. 爪:The eagle seized the fish with its sharp claws.
28. correspondent /ˌkɒrɪˈspɒndənt/ n. 通讯记者；通信者
📖 英文解释
A journalist who reports for a newspaper, TV, or radio from a particular country or on a particular subject.
✏️ 例句
She worked as a foreign correspondent for a major news agency.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
foreign correspondent 驻外记者；war correspondent 战地记者；correspond v.
🧠 助记
correspond（通信/对应）+ ent（人）= 通信的人 = 通讯员
correspondent::/ˌkɒrɪˈspɒndənt/:n. 通讯记者:The correspondent filed her report from the conflict zone.
29. crab /kræb/ n. 蟹；螃蟹
📖 英文解释
A crustacean with a broad carapace, stalked eyes, and five pairs of legs, the first pair of which are modified as pincers.
✏️ 例句
We caught several crabs at the beach and cooked them for dinner.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
crab meat 蟹肉；crab apple 野苹果；walk like a crab 横着走
🧠 助记
crab 发音像'抓不'，螃蟹横着走抓不住
crab::/kræb/:n. 蟹:The fisherman brought back a bucket full of crabs.
30. ditch /dɪtʃ/ n./v. 沟渠；抛弃；丢弃
📖 英文解释
A narrow channel dug in the ground, typically used for drainage; to get rid of or abandon.
✏️ 例句
The car skidded off the road and ended up in a ditch.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ditch a plan 放弃计划；irrigation ditch 灌溉渠；last-ditch 最后一搏的
🧠 助记
ditch = d + itch，痒（itch）了就想丢掉（ditch）它
ditch::/dɪtʃ/:n./v. 沟渠:She decided to ditch her old phone and buy a new one.
