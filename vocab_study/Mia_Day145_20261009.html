1. indignant /ɪnˈdɪɡnənt/ adj. 愤慨的；义愤的
📖 英文解释
Feeling or showing anger or annoyance at what is perceived as unfair treatment.
✏️ 例句
She was indignant at being accused of cheating when she had done nothing wrong.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
feel indignant 感到愤慨；indignant at/about 对……感到义愤；indignation n.
🧠 助记
in + dign（尊严）+ ant，尊严受损 = 愤慨
indignant::/ɪnˈdɪɡnənt/:adj. 愤慨的:He was deeply indignant at the false accusations made against him.
2. innumerable /ɪˈnjuːmərəbl/ adj. 无数的；数不清的
📖 英文解释
Too many to be counted; very many.
✏️ 例句
The night sky was filled with innumerable stars.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
innumerable times 无数次；innumerable difficulties 数不清的困难；= countless
🧠 助记
in（不）+ numerable（可数的）= 不可数的 = 无数的
innumerable::/ɪˈnjuːmərəbl/:adj. 无数的:There are innumerable reasons why the project was delayed.
3. irrespective /ˌɪrɪˈspektɪv/ adj. 不考虑的；不管的
📖 英文解释
Not taking a particular thing into account; regardless.
✏️ 例句
The rule applies to everyone, irrespective of age or background.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
irrespective of 不管；不论；常与 of 连用；= regardless of
🧠 助记
ir（不）+ respective（各自的/考虑的）= 不各自考虑 = 不顾
irrespective::/ˌɪrɪˈspektɪv/:adj. 不考虑的:The policy applies equally to all employees, irrespective of seniority.
4. irrigate /ˈɪrɪɡeɪt/ v. 灌溉；冲洗（伤口）
📖 英文解释
To supply water to land or crops by means of pipes, streams, or channels; to wash out a wound with a continuous stream of liquid.
✏️ 例句
Farmers use canals to irrigate their fields during the dry season.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
irrigate farmland 灌溉农田；irrigation system 灌溉系统；irrigation n.
🧠 助记
ir + rig（操纵）+ ate，操纵水流 = 灌溉
irrigate::/ˈɪrɪɡeɪt/:v. 灌溉:Modern technology allows farmers to irrigate large areas efficiently.
5. magnitude /ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/ n. 大小；量级；重要性；震级
📖 英文解释
The great size or extent of something; the degree of brightness of a star; the size of an earthquake.
✏️ 例句
The earthquake measured 7.0 on the Richter scale — a magnitude that caused widespread destruction.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
magnitude of the problem 问题的严重程度；of great magnitude 规模巨大；order of magnitude 数量级
🧠 助记
magni（大）+ tude = 大的程度 = 量级/大小
magnitude::/ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/:n. 大小:Scientists underestimated the magnitude of the environmental damage.
6. maid /meɪd/ n. 侍女；女仆；少女
📖 英文解释
A female domestic servant; an unmarried girl or young woman (literary).
✏️ 例句
The hotel room was cleaned by the maid every morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
maid of honour 伴娘；old maid 老处女；housemaid 家庭女仆
🧠 助记
maid 发音像'没的'，没的选择只能做侍女
maid::/meɪd/:n. 侍女:The maid brought fresh towels to the guest room each day.
7. memorial /məˈmɔːriəl/ n./adj. 纪念碑；纪念馆；纪念的
📖 英文解释
A structure or event established in memory of a person or event; serving to commemorate someone or something.
✏️ 例句
They built a memorial to honour the soldiers who died in the war.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
war memorial 战争纪念碑；memorial service 追悼会；in memorial of 纪念……
🧠 助记
memorial = memory（记忆）+ al = 有关记忆的东西 = 纪念碑
memorial::/məˈmɔːriəl/:n./adj. 纪念碑:A memorial ceremony was held on the anniversary of the disaster.
8. mercury /ˈmɜːrkjəri/ n. 水银；汞；墨丘利（神）
📖 英文解释
A heavy silver-white metal that is liquid at room temperature, used in thermometers and barometers (symbol: Hg).
✏️ 例句
The thermometer contains mercury to measure temperature accurately.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
mercury thermometer 水银温度计；mercury poisoning 汞中毒；Mercury 水星/信使神
🧠 助记
Mercury 是罗马神话信使神，速度像水银一样流动不定
mercury::/ˈmɜːrkjəri/:n. 水银:Mercury levels in the river were found to be dangerously high.
9. misunderstand /ˌmɪsʌndəˈstænd/ v. 误解；误会
📖 英文解释
To fail to interpret or understand correctly.
✏️ 例句
I think you misunderstood what I said — I was not criticising you.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
misunderstand sb's intention 误解某人的意图；misunderstanding n. 误会
🧠 助记
mis（错误）+ understand（理解）= 错误地理解 = 误解
misunderstand::/ˌmɪsʌndəˈstænd/:v. 误解:There was a misunderstanding between the two parties about the delivery date.
10. nucleus /ˈnjuːkliəs/ n. 核；细胞核；原子核；核心
📖 英文解释
The central and most important part of an object or group; the positively charged dense centre of an atom.
✏️ 例句
The nucleus of the cell contains most of the genetic material.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cell nucleus 细胞核；atomic nucleus 原子核；form the nucleus of 构成……的核心
🧠 助记
nucle（核）+ us，原子核心 = 核
nucleus::/ˈnjuːkliəs/:n. 核:The nucleus of the atom is made up of protons and neutrons.
11. pinch /pɪntʃ/ v./n. 捏；一撮；窘况
📖 英文解释
To grip the skin between finger and thumb; a small amount of a substance between the finger and thumb.
✏️ 例句
Add a pinch of salt to improve the flavour of the soup.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
pinch of salt 一撮盐/将信将疑；in a pinch 必要时；pinch pennies 节省花费
🧠 助记
pinch 发音像'捏住'，用手指捏一下
pinch::/pɪntʃ/:v./n. 捏:She gave his cheek a gentle pinch as a sign of affection.
12. plea /pliː/ n. 恳求；抗辩；借口；申辩
📖 英文解释
A request made in an urgent and emotional manner; a formal statement by or on behalf of a defendant.
✏️ 例句
She made a desperate plea for help after the accident.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
plea for mercy 恳求宽恕；enter a plea 作出申辩；guilty plea 认罪答辩
🧠 助记
plea 和 please（请求）同源，发出请求 = 恳求
plea::/pliː/:n. 恳求:The defendant entered a plea of not guilty in court.
13. puppet /ˈpʌpɪt/ n. 木偶；傀儡
📖 英文解释
A figure controlled from above with strings; a person controlled by another.
✏️ 例句
The children enjoyed watching the puppet show at the festival.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
puppet = pup（小狗）+ pet（宠物），被人牵着线的玩具 = 木偶
puppet::/ˈpʌpɪt/:n. 木偶:Critics accused him of being a puppet of foreign interests.
14. republic /rɪˈpʌblɪk/ n. 共和国；共和政体
📖 英文解释
A state in which supreme power is held by the people and their elected representatives, with an elected president.
✏️ 例句
France is a democratic republic with a strong central government.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
republic of China 中华民国；republican adj. 共和制的；republic vs. monarchy
🧠 助记
re + public（公众）= 公众的事务 = 共和国
republic::/rɪˈpʌblɪk/:n. 共和国:The new constitution declared the country a federal republic.
15. reservoir /ˈrezəvwɑːr/ n. 水库；蓄水池；储备
📖 英文解释
A large natural or artificial lake used as a source of water supply; a place where something is kept in store.
✏️ 例句
The reservoir supplies fresh water to the entire city.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reservoir of talent 人才储备；underground reservoir 地下水库；oil reservoir 油藏
🧠 助记
reserve（储备）+ voir（法语：看），储备水源的地方
reservoir::/ˈrezəvwɑːr/:n. 水库:The drought caused the reservoir to drop to dangerously low levels.
16. screw /skruː/ n./v. 螺丝；螺钉；拧紧
📖 英文解释
A metal fastener with a spiral ridge, driven into wood or other material by rotating it; to turn in a twisting motion.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
screw 发音像'丝扭'，螺丝扭紧
screw::/skruː/:n./v. 螺丝:Make sure to screw the lid tightly before storing the container.
17. stern /stɜːrn/ adj./n. 严厉的；严格的；船尾
📖 英文解释
Serious and unrelenting, especially in the assertion of authority; the rearmost part of a ship or boat.
✏️ 例句
The teacher gave the students a stern warning about cheating.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
stern look 严厉的表情；stern measures 严厉措施；from stem to stern 从头到尾
🧠 助记
stern 里有 stern（船尾），船尾的方向是固定的，像严厉的人不弯曲
stern::/stɜːrn/:adj./n. 严厉的:The judge took a stern approach in sentencing the offender.
18. stiff /stɪf/ adj. 硬的；僵硬的；严厉的；强劲的
📖 英文解释
Not easily bent or changed in shape; unable to move easily; severe or strong.
✏️ 例句
After sitting at the desk all day, his neck felt stiff.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
stiff = s + tiff（小争论），硬邦邦地不松口 = 僵硬
stiff::/stɪf/:adj. 硬的:The market faces stiff competition from foreign companies.
19. swamp /swɒmp/ n./v. 沼泽；淹没；使不堪重负

🧠 助记

swamp::/swɒmp/:n./v. 沼泽:The office was swamped with applications after the job posting went viral.

tease::/tiːz/:v./n. 取笑:She gently teased her friend about always being late.
21. tray /treɪ/ n. 盘；托盘；浅盘

tray = tr + ay，托（tray）着一堆东西端出来
tray::/treɪ/:n. 盘:She brought breakfast on a tray to her bedridden mother.

tug::/tʌɡ/:v./n. 拖:The child tugged at her mother's hand, eager to leave the store.
23. ventilate /ˈventɪleɪt/ v. 通风；使……通气；公开讨论
📖 英文解释
To cause air to enter and circulate in a room or building; to discuss an issue openly.
✏️ 例句

ventilate::/ˈventɪleɪt/:v. 通风:The hospital room was well ventilated to prevent the spread of infection.

vocation::/vəʊˈkeɪʃn/:n. 职业:He chose medicine as his vocation and dedicated his life to helping others.

zeal::/ziːl/:n. 热情:The volunteers worked with great zeal to clean up the neighbourhood.
