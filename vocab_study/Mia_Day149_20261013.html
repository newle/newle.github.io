1. wolf /wʊlf/ n. 狼；贪婪的人 v. 狼吞虎咽
📖 英文解释
A wild animal related to the dog that lives and hunts in groups; a person who is greedy or dangerous; to eat food quickly and greedily.
✏️ 例句
The wolves coordinated their hunt to bring down the large elk.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：cry wolf（狼来了；虚报警报）；wolf in sheep's clothing（披着羊皮的狼）；keep the wolf from the door（勉强维持温饱）；wolf down（狼吞虎咽）。
🧠 助记
wolf 谐音'吞了'—— 狼（wolf）贪婪地把食物'吞了'（wolf down）。
wolf::/wʊlf/:n. 狼:He wolfed down his lunch in five minutes before rushing back to work.
2. geometry /dʒiˈɒmɪtri/ n. 几何；几何学
📖 英文解释
The branch of mathematics dealing with shapes, sizes, and properties of figures and spaces; the arrangement of parts of something.
✏️ 例句
Students study geometry to understand the properties of angles and shapes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：Euclidean geometry（欧式几何）；geometry problem（几何题）；geometric（几何的，形容词）；sacred geometry（神圣几何）。
🧠 助记
geo（地球/土地）+ metry（测量）→ 测量土地的学科 → 几何学。古希腊时用于测量土地边界。
geometry::/dʒiˈɒmɪtri/:n. 几何:The geometry of the cathedral's vaulted ceiling is a masterpiece of medieval engineering.
3. menace /ˈmenəs/ n./v. 威胁；危险人物；威吓
📖 英文解释
Something or someone that is likely to cause harm or danger; to threaten.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：a menace to society（社会威胁）；public menace（公众危害）；menacing look/tone（威胁性的眼神/语气）；menacing（adj. 威胁的）。
🧠 助记
men（男人们）+ ace（尖）→ 一群拿着尖刀的男人 → 威胁。更好的联想：mena = mean（恶意的）→ menace（威胁）。
menace::/ˈmenəs/:n./v. 威胁:Climate change poses an increasing menace to global food security.
4. radius /ˈreɪdiəs/ n. 半径；范围；桡骨
📖 英文解释
The distance from the center of a circle to its edge; the range within which something operates or can reach; a bone in the forearm.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：within a radius of（在…半径范围内）；turning radius（转弯半径）；blast radius（爆炸半径）。复数 radii 或 radiuses。
🧠 助记
radi（辐射/光线）+ us → 从中心向外辐射的距离 → 半径。radiation（辐射）→ radius（半径），都是从中心向外。
radius::/ˈreɪdiəs/:n. 半径:The new shopping centre will serve customers within a ten-kilometre radius.
5. semiconductor /ˌsemikənˈdʌktə(r)/ n. 半导体

🧠 助记

semiconductor::/ˌsemikənˈdʌktə(r)/:n. 半导体:The global semiconductor shortage disrupted automobile and electronics production worldwide.

29. symposium /sɪmˈpəʊziəm/ n. 讨论会；专题研讨会；座谈会
📖 英文解释
A formal meeting at which experts discuss a particular subject; a collection of essays or papers on a subject.
✏️ 例句
Leading economists gathered for a symposium on sustainable development.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：international symposium（国际研讨会）；hold/attend a symposium（举行/参加研讨会）；symposium proceedings（会议论文集）。
🧠 助记
sym（共同）+ posium（饮宴，来自希腊语 symposion）→ 共同饮宴讨论 → 学术讨论会。古希腊人边喝酒边讨论哲学。
symposium::/sɪmˈpəʊziəm/:n. 讨论会:The annual symposium brings together researchers from around the world.
30. thermometer /θəˈmɒmɪtə(r)/ n. 温度计；体温计
📖 英文解释
An instrument for measuring temperature, typically consisting of a narrow sealed glass tube marked with a graduated scale.
✏️ 例句
The nurse used a thermometer to check the patient's temperature.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常见搭配：oral/rectal thermometer（口腔/肛门体温计）；mercury thermometer（水银温度计）；digital thermometer（数字温度计）。
🧠 助记
thermo（热，如 thermal）+ meter（测量）→ 测量热度的仪器 → 温度计。thermo = heat，meter = measure。
thermometer::/θəˈmɒmɪtə(r)/:n. 温度计:The thermometer showed a temperature of 39 degrees, confirming the patient had a fever.
