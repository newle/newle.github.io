1. withhold /wɪðˈhoʊld/ v. 扣留；拒绝给予
📖 英文解释
to refuse to give or provide something; to keep back information or payment
✏️ 例句
The company decided to withhold the bonus until the project was completed.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：withhold information/payment/consent；考研常考辨析：withhold vs. retain（retain侧重保留，withhold侧重拒绝给出）
🧠 助记
with(反对) + hold(持有) → 反着拿住 → 扣留不给
withhold::/wɪðˈhoʊld/:v. 扣留:They withheld the report from the public.
2. arrow /ˈæroʊ/ n. 箭；箭头符号
📖 英文解释
a thin straight stick with a sharp point at one end, shot from a bow; a symbol shaped like an arrow indicating direction
✏️ 例句
The archer released the arrow and it flew straight to the target.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用搭配：arrow key（方向键）；as straight as an arrow（笔直）；常出现在科技类阅读中
🧠 助记
arrow = a + row → 一排排飞出去的箭
arrow::/ˈæroʊ/:n. 箭:Follow the arrow to the exit.
3. baggage /ˈbæɡɪdʒ/ n. 行李；（思想上的）包袱
📖 英文解释
bags and suitcases packed with belongings for travel; emotional or mental burden from the past
✏️ 例句
She checked her baggage at the airport counter before boarding.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研辨析：baggage（美式，不可数）= luggage（英式）；emotional baggage（心理包袱）常出现在社会文化类文章
🧠 助记
bag(包) + gage → 一堆包的总称 → 行李
baggage::/ˈbæɡɪdʒ/:n. 行李:He carries a lot of emotional baggage from his childhood.
4. blunt /blʌnt/ adj. 钝的；直言不讳的 v. 使变钝；削弱
📖 英文解释
not having a sharp edge or point; saying what you think without trying to be polite; to make less sharp or effective
✏️ 例句
She gave a blunt response that surprised everyone in the meeting.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常考：blunt instrument（钝器）；blunt criticism（直率批评）；blunt the edge of（削弱…的锋芒）
🧠 助记
b + lunt → 鲁钝 → 钝的；说话也直钝无锋
blunt::/blʌnt/:adj. 钝的:He was blunt about his dissatisfaction with the plan.
5. bruise /bruːz/ n./v. 淤伤；挫伤
📖 英文解释
a mark on the skin caused by injury where blood vessels break; to injure the skin without breaking it
✏️ 例句
She had a large bruise on her arm after the fall.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：bruise one's ego（伤自尊）；bruised feelings（受伤的感情）；常出现在医学及心理类文章
🧠 助记
bruise发音像'不漏死' → 皮没破但内部受伤 → 淤伤
bruise::/bruːz/:n./v. 淤伤:The fruit bruises easily if handled roughly.
6. buzz /bʌz/ n./v. 嗡嗡声；忙碌喧嚣

🧠 助记

buzz::/bʌz/:n./v. 嗡嗡声:The office buzzed with rumors about the merger.

📖 英文解释

🧠 助记
cal(卡路里/石灰)+ci+um → 石灰里有钙 → 钙
calcium::/ˈkælsiəm/:n. 钙（化学元素）:Children need adequate calcium for bone development.
9. compass /ˈkʌmpəs/ n. 罗盘；圆规；范围
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研辨析：compass（罗盘/范围）；within the compass of（在…范围内）；常考within/beyond one's compass
🧠 助记
com(共同)+pass(通过) → 大家一起通过 → 靠罗盘导航
compass::/ˈkʌmpəs/:n. 罗盘:The task falls within the compass of his responsibilities.

🧠 助记
down(向下)+stairs(楼梯) → 向下走楼梯 → 楼下
downstairs::/ˌdaʊnˈstɛrz/:adv./adj. 楼下（的）:Please come downstairs for dinner.
11. dye /daɪ/ n. 染料 v. 染色

dye::/daɪ/:n. 染料 v. 染色:She decided to dye her hair a vibrant red.
