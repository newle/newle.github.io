1. grim /ɡrɪm/ adj. 严酷的；令人不快的；坚定的
📖 英文解释
very serious or gloomy; causing worry or distress; stern and determined
✏️ 例句
The doctor gave a grim prognosis for the patient's recovery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常用：grim reality/situation（严峻现实/形势）；grim determination（坚定决心）；常出现在新闻/社会类文章
🧠 助记
grim → gri(灰暗)+m → 灰暗严酷的 → 严酷的
grim::/ɡrɪm/:adj. 严酷的:The report painted a grim picture of the country's economic future.
