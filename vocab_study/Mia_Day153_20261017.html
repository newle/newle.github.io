1. remnant /ˈremnənt/ n. 残余；遗留物
📖 英文解释
a small remaining quantity of something; a surviving trace or vestige
✏️ 例句
Only a remnant of the ancient forest survived the fire.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
remnant of sth（某物的残余）；remnants of a meal（剩饭剩菜）；常与 of 搭配，考研阅读中常指文化/历史遗存
🧠 助记
re-（再）+ mn + ant（蚂蚁）→ 再次找到的像蚂蚁一样小的东西 → 残余
remnant::/ˈremnənt/:n. 残余:Only a remnant of the old culture survived.
2. reproach /rɪˈprəʊtʃ/ v./n. 责备；指责
📖 英文解释
to express to someone your disapproval of or disappointment in their actions; an expression of blame or disappointment
✏️ 例句
She reproached him for his careless attitude toward work.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reproach sb for/with sth（因某事责备某人）；beyond reproach（无可指责）；考研写作中用于表达批评
🧠 助记
re-（再）+ proach（接近approach）→ 再次走近指责 → 责备
reproach::/rɪˈprəʊtʃ/:v./n. 责备:His conduct is beyond reproach.
3. restless /ˈrestləs/ adj. 不安宁的；焦虑不安的
📖 英文解释
unable to rest or relax as a result of anxiety or boredom; constantly active or moving
✏️ 例句
The children became restless after sitting for two hours.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
restless night（不眠之夜）；grow/become restless（变得烦躁）；考研中描述社会或个人的焦虑状态
🧠 助记
rest（休息）+ less（缺乏）→ 缺乏休息的 → 不安宁的
restless::/ˈrestləs/:adj. 不安宁的:She felt restless and unable to concentrate.
4. revolt /rɪˈvəʊlt/ v./n. 反抗；起义；使厌恶
📖 英文解释
to take violent action against the people in power; to make someone feel disgust
✏️ 例句
The peasants revolted against their oppressive rulers.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
revolt against（反抗）；revolt sb（使某人厌恶）；in revolt（在反抗中）；考研历史/社会类文章常见
🧠 助记
re-（反）+ volt（伏特/转）→ 反转 → 反抗
revolt::/rɪˈvəʊlt/:v./n. 反抗:Citizens revolted against the corrupt government.
5. ripe /raɪp/ adj. 熟的；成熟的；时机成熟的
📖 英文解释
fully developed and ready to be eaten; having reached a stage of full development
✏️ 例句
The time is ripe for economic reform.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
ripe for（时机成熟）；ripe old age（高龄）；考研中常用 the time is ripe for 表示时机成熟
🧠 助记
联想 ripe = ri（日）+ pe → 日晒充足，果实成熟
ripe::/raɪp/:adj. 熟的:The country is ripe for democratic change.
6. robe /rəʊb/ n. 长袍；礼服；浴袍
📖 英文解释
a long loose outer garment; a dressing gown
✏️ 例句
The judge entered the courtroom in his black robe.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
judge's robe（法官袍）；bathrobe（浴袍）；考研法律/历史类语境
🧠 助记
robe 谐音[罗布] → 罗布林卡的藏袍 → 长袍
robe::/rəʊb/:n. 长袍:She wrapped herself in a warm robe after the shower.
7. sailor /ˈseɪlə(r)/ n. 水手；海员
📖 英文解释
a person who works on a ship as a member of its crew
✏️ 例句
The sailor spent months at sea without seeing land.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
sailor = sail（航行）+ or（人）；experienced sailor（经验丰富的水手）；考研航运/历史类语境
🧠 助记
sail（航行）+ or（人）→ 航行的人 → 水手
sailor::/ˈseɪlə(r)/:n. 水手:The old sailor told stories of distant seas.
8. saint /seɪnt/ n. 圣人；极有美德的人
📖 英文解释
a person acknowledged as holy or virtuous; a very virtuous or patient person
✏️ 例句
She was regarded as a saint for her lifelong dedication to the poor.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
patron saint（守护圣人）；saint-like patience（圣人般的耐心）；考研文化/宗教类文章
🧠 助记
saint 谐音[圣特] → 特别圣洁的人 → 圣人
saint::/seɪnt/:n. 圣人:You'd need to be a saint to put up with him.
9. sceptical /ˈskeptɪkl/ adj. 怀疑的；持怀疑态度的
📖 英文解释
not easily convinced; having doubts or reservations about something
✏️ 例句
Scientists are sceptical about the new findings until they are replicated.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be sceptical of/about（对…持怀疑态度）；sceptical mind（怀疑的心态）；考研科技/学术类文章
🧠 助记
scep（skip 跳过）+ tical → 跳过轻信 → 怀疑的（美式拼法 skeptical）
sceptical::/ˈskeptɪkl/:adj. 怀疑的:I remain sceptical about his promises.
10. seventeen /ˌsevnˈtiːn/ num. 十七
📖 英文解释
the number 17; equivalent to seven plus ten
✏️ 例句
She started learning the piano at the age of seventeen.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研词汇中的基础数字词，常出现在统计数据、年龄描述场景
🧠 助记
seven（7）+ teen（十几）→ 十七
seventeen::/ˌsevnˈtiːn/:num. 十七:The program runs for seventeen consecutive weeks.
11. shiver /ˈʃɪvə(r)/ v./n. 发抖；哆嗦；颤抖
📖 英文解释
to shake slightly and uncontrollably as a result of cold or fear
✏️ 例句
She shivered with cold standing in the snow.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
shiver with cold/fear（因冷/恐惧而颤抖）；send shivers down one's spine（令人毛骨悚然）
🧠 助记
shiver 谐音[失我] → 冷得失去自我控制 → 发抖
shiver::/ˈʃɪvə(r)/:v./n. 发抖:He shivered at the thought of what might happen.
12. smuggle /ˈsmʌɡl/ v. 走私；偷运
📖 英文解释
to move goods or people secretly and illegally into or out of a country
✏️ 例句
Criminals tried to smuggle drugs across the border.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
smuggle sth into/out of（把…偷运进/出）；smuggle sb（偷运某人）；考研法律/社会类
🧠 助记
smug（自鸣得意）+ gle → 自以为聪明地偷运货物 → 走私
smuggle::/ˈsmʌɡl/:v. 走私:He was arrested for trying to smuggle antiques abroad.
13. sneeze /sniːz/ v./n. 打喷嚏
📖 英文解释
to suddenly expel air through the nose and mouth uncontrollably
✏️ 例句
He sneezed repeatedly due to the dust in the room.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
sneeze 谐音[斯尼兹] → 感冒了斯斯文文地打喷嚏 → 打喷嚏
sneeze::/sniːz/:v./n. 打喷嚏:An offer of a million dollars is not to be sneezed at.
14. sour /saʊə(r)/ adj. 酸的；不愉快的 v. 变酸；变糟

sour::/saʊə(r)/:adj. 酸的:Relations between the two countries have turned sour.
15. specimen /ˈspesɪmən/ n. 标本；样品；典型例子

specimen::/ˈspesɪmən/:n. 标本:The lab requires a blood specimen for analysis.
16. spoon /spuːn/ n. 勺子；汤匙 v. 用勺舀

spoon::/spuːn/:n. 勺子:Students should not be spoon-fed all the answers.
17. stagnant /ˈstæɡnənt/ adj. 停滞的；不流动的；不发展的

✏️ 例句

stagnant economy（经济停滞）；stagnant water（死水）；考研经济类文章高频词

stagnant::/ˈstæɡnənt/:adj. 停滞的:Wages have been stagnant for over a decade.
18. stitch /stɪtʃ/ n./v. 针脚；缝合；缝补

stitch::/stɪtʃ/:n./v. 针脚:A stitch in time saves nine — deal with problems early.

stoop::/stuːp/:v. 弯腰:I won't stoop to lying just to win the argument.

submarine::/ˌsʌbməˈriːn/:n. 潜水艇 adj. 水下的:Submarine cables carry most of the world's internet traffic.

subtract::/səbˈtrækt/:v. 减去:Subtract the costs from the revenue to get the profit.

suffice::/səˈfaɪs/:v. 足够:Suffice it to say that the experiment was a success.
23. thrust /θrʌst/ v./n. 推力；猛推；刺；要旨

thrust::/θrʌst/:v./n. 推力:The main thrust of his speech was economic reform.
