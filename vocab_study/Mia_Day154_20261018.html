1. fume /fjuːm/ n. 烟；臭气；怒气 v. 冒烟；发怒
📖 英文解释
gas, smoke, or vapour that smells strongly or is dangerous to inhale; to feel very angry
✏️ 例句
Toxic fumes from the factory caused residents to complain.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
exhaust fumes（尾气）；fume at/over（对…大发雷霆）；in a fume（大怒）；考研环境/情绪类
🧠 助记
fume 谐音[福母] → 母亲被烟熏得发怒 → 烟气/发怒
fume::/fjuːm/:n. 烟:Residents were fuming over the new policy.
2. parachute /ˈpærəʃuːt/ n. 降落伞 v. 跳伞；空降
📖 英文解释
a device used to slow the descent of a person or object falling through the air
✏️ 例句
The skydiver deployed her parachute at 3,000 metres.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
parachute into（空降到）；parachute regiment（伞兵团）；考研科技/军事类，注意拼写
🧠 助记
para（保护/防止）+ chute（滑道）→ 防止快速坠落的装置 → 降落伞
parachute::/ˈpærəʃuːt/:n. 降落伞 v. 跳伞:Supplies were parachuted into the remote disaster zone.
3. superfluous /suːˈpɜːfluəs/ adj. 多余的；不必要的；过剩的
📖 英文解释
unnecessary, especially through being more than enough; redundant
✏️ 例句
The lengthy introduction seemed superfluous to the main argument.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
super（超过）+ flu（流动）+ ous → 流出超出需要的量 → 多余的
superfluous::/suːˈpɜːfluəs/:adj. 多余的:Any further explanation would be superfluous.

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记

turbine::/ˈtɜːbaɪn/:n. 涡轮:The new turbine can generate enough power for 500 homes.
