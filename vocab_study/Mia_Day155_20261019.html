1. idiom /ˈɪdiəm/ n. 成语；惯用语；风格
📖 英文解释
a group of words with a meaning not deducible from those of the individual words; a characteristic style in art, music, or literature
✏️ 例句
The expression 'kick the bucket' is a well-known idiom meaning to die.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
idiomatic expression（惯用语）；in the idiom of（用…的风格）；考研翻译中常考习语理解
🧠 助记
idio（自己的 idiot 傻瓜）+ m → 傻瓜才望文生义，idiom 要整体理解 → 成语
idiom::/ˈɪdiəm/:n. 成语:Understanding idioms is key to mastering a foreign language.
2. moisture /ˈmɔɪstʃə(r)/ n. 水分；湿气；潮气
📖 英文解释
water or other liquid diffused in a small quantity as vapour, within a solid, or condensed on a surface
✏️ 例句
The soil retains moisture well, making it ideal for agriculture.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
moisture content（含水量）；retain/absorb moisture（保持/吸收水分）；考研农业/环境类
🧠 助记
moist（潮湿的）+ ure → 潮湿的状态 → 水分/湿气
moisture::/ˈmɔɪstʃə(r)/:n. 水分:Excessive moisture in the air accelerates corrosion.

🧠 助记
mort（死亡 mort-）+ al → 会死的 → 凡人的/致命的
mortal::/ˈmɔːtl/:adj. 凡人的:The accident inflicted a mortal blow to his career.
