1. decree /dɪˈkriː/ n./v. 法令；颁布
📖 英文解释
an official order issued by a legal authority; to officially order something
✏️ 例句
The government issued a decree banning public gatherings.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
issue/pass a decree; decree that + clause；考研常见搭配：by decree（以法令形式）
🧠 助记
de（下）+ cree（创造）→ 从上向下创造规则 → 法令
decree::/dɪˈkriː/:n./v. 法令:The king ruled by decree.
2. dilute /daɪˈluːt/ v. 稀释；adj. 稀薄的
📖 英文解释
to make a liquid weaker or thinner by adding water or another liquid; to weaken the strength or effect of something
✏️ 例句
Dilute the solution with distilled water before use.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dilute with water；dilute effect/power；考研常考：比喻义「稀释影响力」
🧠 助记
di（分开）+ lute（流）→ 分流 → 稀释
dilute::/daɪˈluːt/:v. 稀释:Dilute the acid carefully.
3. disappoint /ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪnt/ v. 使失望
📖 英文解释
to fail to satisfy the hopes, wishes, or expectations of someone
✏️ 例句
The film disappointed many fans who had high expectations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
be disappointed at/with/in sth；disappoint sb's expectations；考研：disappointed（形）vs disappointing（令人失望的）
🧠 助记
dis（否定）+ appoint（安排好的）→ 打破安排 → 使失望
disappoint::/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪnt/:v. 使失望:He disappointed his parents.
4. discreet /dɪˈskriːt/ adj. 谨慎的；考虑周到的
📖 英文解释
careful and prudent in speech or actions, especially to avoid causing offense or revealing confidential information
✏️ 例句
She was discreet about her colleague's personal problems.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
discreet silence/behavior；与 discrete（分离的）是同音异义词，考研必考辨析
🧠 助记
dis + creet（辨别）→ 善于辨别 → 谨慎
discreet::/dɪˈskriːt/:adj. 谨慎的:Be discreet about the matter.
5. dissipate /ˈdɪsɪpeɪt/ v. 消散；挥霍
📖 英文解释
to scatter or disperse; to waste or squander money, energy, or resources
✏️ 例句
The morning fog dissipated as the sun rose higher.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
dissipate energy/fear/anger；dissipate a fortune；考研：常考比喻义「消散」
🧠 助记
dis（散开）+ sip（小饮）+ ate → 一点一点消耗掉 → 消散
dissipate::/ˈdɪsɪpeɪt/:v. 消散:The tension gradually dissipated.
6. dynasty /ˈdɪnəsti/ n. 王朝；朝代
📖 英文解释
a series of rulers from the same family, or a period during which they rule
✏️ 例句
The Tang Dynasty was known for its cultural achievements.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
found/establish a dynasty；the Han Dynasty；考研：中国历史文化语境常见
🧠 助记
dynas（力量）+ ty → 凭借力量统治的家族 → 王朝
dynasty::/ˈdɪnəsti/:n. 王朝:The Ming Dynasty lasted 276 years.
7. edible /ˈedɪbl/ adj. 可食用的；n. 食品
📖 英文解释
fit or suitable to be eaten; not harmful if eaten
✏️ 例句
Not all wild mushrooms are edible; some are poisonous.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
edible plants/fungi；edibles（n.复数，食品）；与 inedible（不可食用）对比
🧠 助记
ed（eat 的词根）+ ible（可以）→ 可以吃的 → 可食用的
edible::/ˈedɪbl/:adj. 可食用的:Identify edible plants in the wild.
8. elastic /ɪˈlæstɪk/ adj. 有弹性的；灵活的
📖 英文解释
able to return to its original shape after being stretched; flexible and adaptable
✏️ 例句
Elastic demand changes significantly with price.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
elastic band/material；price elastic demand；考研经济学：弹性需求
🧠 助记
e + last（持续）+ ic → 伸展后还能持续回弹 → 有弹性的
elastic::/ɪˈlæstɪk/:adj. 有弹性的:The elastic cord stretched easily.
9. escalate /ˈeskəleɪt/ v. 升级；逐步增强
📖 英文解释
to increase rapidly in intensity, extent, or magnitude; to become or make more serious
✏️ 例句
The conflict escalated into a full-scale war.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
escalate into/to sth；escalating tensions/costs；考研：常考「局势升级」语境
🧠 助记
escal（阶梯 escalator）+ ate → 一步步向上 → 升级
escalate::/ˈeskəleɪt/:v. 升级:Violence escalated in the region.
10. escort /ˈeskɔːt/ (n.) /ɪˈskɔːt/ (v.) n./v. 护送；陪同
📖 英文解释
a person or group accompanying another for protection or as a mark of honor; to accompany and protect someone
✏️ 例句
Police provided an escort for the diplomat's motorcade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
under escort；escort sb to/from；考研：常考正式/安全语境
🧠 助记
es（外出）+ cort（court 宫廷）→ 出宫要有人陪 → 护送
escort::/ˈeskɔːt/ (n.) /ɪˈskɔːt/ (v.):n./v. 护送:She was escorted to her seat.
11. exceedingly /ɪkˈsiːdɪŋli/ adv. 极度地；非常
📖 英文解释
to a very great degree; extremely
✏️ 例句
The exam was exceedingly difficult for most students.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
exceedingly rare/complex/important；程度副词，强于 very；考研：修饰形容词
🧠 助记
exceed（超过）+ ing + ly → 超过一般程度地 → 极度地
exceedingly::/ɪkˈsiːdɪŋli/:adv. 极度地:She performed exceedingly well.
12. excite /ɪkˈsaɪt/ v. 刺激；激起（兴趣/反应）
📖 英文解释
to cause strong feelings of enthusiasm or eagerness; to stimulate a physical or chemical reaction
✏️ 例句
The discovery excited considerable interest among scientists.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
excite interest/curiosity/suspicion；考研：注意「激起反应」而非单纯「兴奋」
🧠 助记
ex（向外）+ cite（引用/激活）→ 激发出来 → 刺激、激起
excite::/ɪkˈsaɪt/:v. 刺激:The news excited the crowd.
13. exquisite /ˈekskwɪzɪt/ adj. 精致的；强烈的
📖 英文解释
extremely beautiful and delicately made; intensely felt (pain or pleasure)
✏️ 例句
The museum displayed exquisite works of ancient craftsmanship.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
exquisite detail/taste/pain；考研：常搭配 craftsmanship, beauty
🧠 助记
ex（彻底）+ quis（寻找）+ ite → 经过彻底筛选的 → 精致的
exquisite::/ˈekskwɪzɪt/:adj. 精致的:The jade carving was exquisite.
14. fairy /ˈfeəri/ n. 仙女；精灵
📖 英文解释
a small imaginary being with magical powers, typically depicted as a tiny human with wings
✏️ 例句
The children listened to the fairy tale with wide eyes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
fairy tale/story；tooth fairy；fairy godmother；考研：多在文学语境出现
🧠 助记
fair（美丽）+ y → 美丽的小存在 → 仙女
fairy::/ˈfeəri/:n. 仙女:She dressed up as a fairy.
15. feast /fiːst/ n. 宴会；v. 盛宴款待
📖 英文解释
a large and elaborate meal; to eat and drink sumptuously
✏️ 例句
The wedding feast lasted well into the night.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
feast on sth（大吃）；a feast for the eyes（视觉享受）；考研：比喻用法
🧠 助记
feast 谐音「肥是特」→ 特别丰盛的肥美食物 → 宴会
feast::/fiːst/:n. 宴会:They feasted on roast lamb.
16. fist /fɪst/ n. 拳头
📖 英文解释
the hand when the fingers are bent inwards towards the palm and held there tightly
✏️ 例句
He clenched his fist in anger but stayed silent.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
clench/raise a fist；iron fist（铁腕）；考研：常见短语 iron fist in a velvet glove（外柔内刚）
🧠 助记
fist 形似紧握的手 → 拳头
fist::/fɪst/:n. 拳头:He shook his fist at the driver.
17. fortnight /ˈfɔːtnaɪt/ n. 两星期；十四天
📖 英文解释
a period of two weeks (chiefly British English)
✏️ 例句
She stayed in London for a fortnight during the summer.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
a fortnight's holiday；in a fortnight（两周后）；考研英语阅读英式用法
🧠 助记
fort（fourteen）+ night（夜）→ 十四个夜晚 → 两星期
fortnight::/ˈfɔːtnaɪt/:n. 两星期:I'll see you in a fortnight.

fury（狂怒）+ ous → 充满狂怒的
furious::/ˈfjʊəriəs/:adj. 狂怒的:He was furious about the decision.
19. gauge /ɡeɪdʒ/ n./v. 测量；判断
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
gauge 和 judge 发音相近 → 判断、衡量
gauge::/ɡeɪdʒ/:n./v. 测量:We need to gauge their reaction.
20. gorgeous /ˈɡɔːdʒəs/ adj. 华丽的；极好的
📖 英文解释
beautiful and very attractive; used to emphasize how pleasant or enjoyable something is
✏️ 例句
The sunset over the ocean was absolutely gorgeous.

gorgeous weather/scenery/dress；考研：语气强于 beautiful，偏感情色彩
🧠 助记
gorge（峡谷，壮丽）+ ous → 如峡谷般壮丽 → 华丽的
gorgeous::/ˈɡɔːdʒəs/:adj. 华丽的:She looked gorgeous in that dress.
21. greeting /ˈɡriːtɪŋ/ n. 问候；贺词

exchange greetings；season's greetings；greeting card；考研：正式书信用语
🧠 助记
greet（问候）+ ing → 正在问候 → 问候、贺词
greeting::/ˈɡriːtɪŋ/:n. 问候:Send my greetings to your family.
22. grind /ɡraɪnd/ v. 磨；研磨；n. 苦差事
📖 英文解释
to reduce to small pieces by crushing; to press two surfaces together with a circular motion; hard tedious work
✏️ 例句
She spent hours grinding coffee beans for the morning brew.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
grind to a halt（逐渐停止）；the daily grind（日常苦差事）；考研短语 grind to a halt
🧠 助记
grind 声音像磨东西发出的 grrrr 声 → 研磨
grind::/ɡraɪnd/:v. 磨:The economy ground to a halt.

play the guitar；acoustic/electric guitar；考研：词汇识别题偶见

guitar 来自西班牙语 guitarra → 直接记忆
guitar::/ɡɪˈtɑː/:n. 吉他:She learned to play the guitar.

employee/student handbook；an instruction handbook；考研：偶见词汇理解

handbook::/ˈhændbʊk/:n. 手册:Refer to the safety handbook.

📖 英文解释
the principal or head teacher of a school, especially a private school

headmaster / headmistress（女校长）；British English 用法；考研：教育语境

headmaster::/ˌhedˈmɑːstə/:n. 校长（中小学男校长）:The headmaster gave a speech.

herald::/ˈherəld/:n. 先驱:The discovery heralded a new age.

herb::/hɜːb/ (英) /ɜːrb/ (美):n. 药草:Add fresh herbs to the salad.
