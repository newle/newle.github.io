1. hurl /hɜːl/ v. 用力投掷；大声叫嚷
📖 英文解释
to throw something with great force; to utter with great force
✏️ 例句
The protestors hurled stones at the police vehicles.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
hurl abuse/insults at sb；hurl oneself into（投身于）；考研：比喻义「猛烈抛出言语」
🧠 助记
hurl 谐音「吼了」→ 用力吼出去 → 用力投掷、叫嚷
hurl::/hɜːl/:v. 用力投掷:He hurled insults at his opponents.
2. icon /ˈaɪkɒn/ n. 偶像；图标；圣像
📖 英文解释
a person or thing regarded as a representative symbol; a small picture on a computer screen; a religious image
✏️ 例句
She became a fashion icon admired around the world.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
cultural/fashion icon；desktop icon；考研：比喻义「偶像/象征」常考
🧠 助记
icon → I（我）+ con（认识）→ 人人都认识的 → 偶像
icon::/ˈaɪkɒn/:n. 偶像:She is a pop culture icon.
3. intelligible /ɪnˈtelɪdʒɪbl/ adj. 可理解的；清晰的
📖 英文解释
able to be understood; comprehensible
✏️ 例句
The lecturer's explanation was barely intelligible due to the noise.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
intelligible speech/language；与 intelligent（聪明的）辨析；考研易混词
🧠 助记
intellig（理解）+ ible → 可以被理解的 → 可理解的
intelligible::/ɪnˈtelɪdʒɪbl/:adj. 可理解的:His speech was barely intelligible.

🧠 助记
lad → 联想 ladder（梯子）→ 爬梯子的小伙子 → 男孩
lad::/læd/:n. 小伙子:He's a bright young lad.

lubricate::/ˈluːbrɪkeɪt/:v. 润滑:Lubricate the hinges to stop squeaking.
