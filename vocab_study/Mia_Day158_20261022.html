1. reed /riːd/ n. 芦苇；簧片
📖 英文解释
a tall, slender-leaved plant growing in water or marshy ground; a thin piece of cane or metal in a wind instrument
✏️ 例句
The riverbanks were lined with tall reeds swaying in the breeze.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
reed instrument（簧乐器）；reed bed；考研：自然/音乐语境
🧠 助记
reed → read（读）谐音 → 芦苇像书签，便于阅读 → 芦苇
reed::/riːd/:n. 芦苇:Herons nest among the reeds.
2. saddle /ˈsædl/ n. 鞍；马鞍；v. 使承担
📖 英文解释
a seat fastened on a horse's back for a rider; to burden someone with a task
✏️ 例句
The rider adjusted the saddle before mounting the horse.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
in the saddle（骑马；掌权）；saddle sb with sth（使某人承担）；考研：习语义
🧠 助记
saddle → sad（悲伤）+ dle → 驮着负担让人悲伤 → 鞍；比喻让人承担
saddle::/ˈsædl/:n. 鞍:She was saddled with enormous debt.
3. salvation /sælˈveɪʃn/ n. 拯救；救赎
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
seek salvation；salvation Army（救世军）；考研：宗教/比喻义
🧠 助记
salv（拯救）+ ation → 拯救的行为 → 救赎
salvation::/sælˈveɪʃn/:n. 拯救:Technology may be humanity's salvation.

saucer::/ˈsɔːsə/:n. 碟子:A flying saucer was reported in the sky.
