1. enquiry /ɪ nˈkwaɪərɪ/ n. 咋询，询问，调查
📖 英文解释
A request for information or an official investigation into a matter.
✏️ 例句
The committee launched an enquiry into the causes of the financial crisis.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ make an enquiry about sth 就某事进行询问 ★ conduct/launch an enquiry into sth 对某事展开调查 ☆ public enquiry 公众调查 辨析：enquiry / inquiry：英式用 enquiry，美式用 inquiry
🧠 助记
en+quiry -> 进入(en)问题(query变体) -> 进入追问状态 -> 咋询
enquiry::/ɪ nˈkwaɪərɪ/:n. 咋询，询问，调查:She made an enquiry about the application process.
2. hypocrisy /hɪˈpɒkrəsɪ/ n. 伪善，虚伪
📖 英文解释
The practice of claiming to have moral standards to which one's own behaviour does not conform.
✏️ 例句
His hypocrisy was revealed when he preached honesty while cheating on his taxes.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ sheer/blatant hypocrisy 十足的虚伪 ★ expose/denounce sb's hypocrisy 揭露/谴责某人的虚伪 ☆ 形容词：hypocritical 虚伪的；名词人物：hypocrite 伪君子 辨析：hypocrisy / pretence：hypocrisy 强调言行不一；pretence 更广，指假装任何状态
🧠 助记
hypo(下面) + crisy(危机藏在下面) -> 表面光鲜内里虚假 -> 伪善
hypocrisy::/hɪˈpɒkrəsɪ/:n. 伪善，虚伪:Political hypocrisy erodes public trust.

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ user interface (UI) 用户界面 ★ interface with sth/sb 与…相互连接/合作 ☆ interface between A and B A 与 B 的交界面
🧠 助记
inter(之间) + face(面) -> 两者之间的面 -> 接口/界面
interface::/ˈɪntəfeɪs/:n. 接口，界面:The interface between science and policy is complex.
4. invert /ɪnˈvɜːt/ v. 颠倒，反转，倒置
📖 英文解释
To put something in the opposite position, order, or arrangement; to turn upside down.
✏️ 例句
If you invert the triangle, the pattern changes completely.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ invert sth 将某物颠倒/倒置 ★ inverted commas 引号（英式用法） ☆ inversion n. 倒置；inverse adj. 相反的 辨析：invert / reverse：invert 多指物理上的颠倒；reverse 更广，指顺序或方向的逆转
🧠 助记
in(相反) + vert(转) -> 向相反方向转 -> 颠倒
invert::/ɪnˈvɜːt/:v. 颠倒，反转，倒置:Invert the bowl carefully onto the plate.

7. adjective /ˈædʒɪktɪv/ n. 形容词

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ attributive adjective 定语形容词（放名词前） ★ predicative adjective 表语形容词（放 be 后） ☆ adjectival phrase 形容词短语 辨析：adjective / adverb：adjective 修饰名词；adverb 修饰动词、形容词或副词
🧠 助记
ad(加在旁边) + ject(扔/加) + ive -> 附加在名词旁的词 -> 形容词
adjective::/ˈædʒɪktɪv/:n. 形容词:Choose an adjective to describe the weather.
