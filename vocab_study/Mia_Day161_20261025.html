1. emphasise /ˈemfəsaɪz/ v. 着重，强调
📖 英文解释
To give special importance or prominence to something in speaking or writing.
✏️ 例句
The teacher emphasised the importance of regular revision before the exam.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
★ emphasise that... 强调…（高频！） ★ emphasise the need/importance of sth 强调某事的需要/重要性 ☆ emphasis n. 强调，重点；emphatic adj. 着重的 辨析: emphasise / stress / highlight: 三者均表强调；emphasise 最通用；highlight 侧重突出展示
🧠 助记
em + phase(阶段/相位) + ise -> 让某阶段更显眼 -> 着重，强调
emphasise::/ˈemfəsaɪz/:v. 着重，强调:She emphasised the need for careful planning in advance.
