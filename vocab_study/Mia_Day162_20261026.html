1. patriotic /ˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪk/ adj. 爱国的
📖 英文解释
having or expressing devotion to and vigorous support for one's country
✏️ 例句
The patriotic soldier fought bravely to defend his homeland.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：patriotic spirit（爱国精神）、patriotic sentiment（爱国情感）；考研常考词组 patriotism（爱国主义）
🧠 助记
patri-（父亲/祖国）+ -otic（形容词后缀）→ 像爱父亲一样爱祖国 → 爱国的
patriotic::/ˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪk/:adj. 爱国的:The patriotic spirit of the people inspired the entire nation.
2. pebble /ˈpebəl/ n. 鹅卵石
📖 英文解释
a small smooth stone, especially one found on a beach or riverbed
✏️ 例句
She collected pebbles along the riverbank as souvenirs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：throw pebbles（扔石子）、pebble beach（鹅卵石海滩）；考研中作具体名词使用
🧠 助记
pebb- 联想 'pebble' → 圆润的鹅卵石，像小气泡（bubble）一样圆
pebble::/ˈpebəl/:n. 鹅卵石:Children love throwing pebbles into the lake.
3. peel /piːl/ n./v. 果皮；剥皮
📖 英文解释
the outer covering of a fruit or vegetable; to remove the outer layer of something
✏️ 例句
She peeled the orange and shared it with her sister.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：peel off（剥落、脱落）、banana peel（香蕉皮）；既可作名词也可作动词
🧠 助记
peel 和 'appeal'（吸引）谐音，→ 果皮被剥去才能看到诱人的内里
peel::/piːl/:n./v. 果皮:He peeled the apple carefully before eating it.
4. perfume /ˈpɜːfjuːm/ n. 香水；香味
📖 英文解释
a fragrant liquid typically made from essential oils, used to give a pleasant smell
✏️ 例句
The perfume of roses filled the entire garden.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：wear perfume（擦香水）、the perfume of flowers（花的芬芳）；注意 per-fume：per=through，fume=烟气 → 透过烟气散发的香味
🧠 助记
per（透过）+ fume（烟雾/气味）→ 透过空气飘散的香气 → 香水
perfume::/ˈpɜːfjuːm/:n. 香水:She wore a light perfume that everyone admired.
5. pneumonia /njuːˈməʊniə/ n. 肺炎
📖 英文解释
a lung infection causing inflammation of the air sacs, which may fill with fluid
✏️ 例句
The elderly man was hospitalized with severe pneumonia.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：bacterial pneumonia（细菌性肺炎）、catch pneumonia（患肺炎）；p不发音，考研中需正确拼写
🧠 助记
pneumo-（肺）+ -nia（疾病）→ 肺部疾病 → 肺炎；联想 'new money' 发音类似
pneumonia::/njuːˈməʊniə/:n. 肺炎:The doctor diagnosed the child with pneumonia.
6. recollect /ˌrekəˈlekt/ v. 回忆；想起
📖 英文解释
to remember or recall something from the past, often with effort
✏️ 例句
She tried to recollect the details of the meeting.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：recollect doing sth（记得做过某事）；与 remember 区别：recollect 强调主动努力回想，remember 更自然
🧠 助记
re（再）+ collect（收集）→ 重新收集记忆 → 回忆
recollect::/ˌrekəˈlekt/:v. 回忆:I can barely recollect what happened that night.
7. retort /rɪˈtɔːt/ v./n. 反驳；回嘴
📖 英文解释
to say something sharp or witty in reply; a sharp or witty reply
✏️ 例句
When accused of lying, he retorted angrily that he had been misunderstood.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：retort angrily/sharply（愤怒地/尖刻地反驳）；考研常考近义词 rebut, refute
🧠 助记
re（回）+ tort（扭/回击）→ 扭转回去 → 反驳；联想 'distort'（扭曲）
retort::/rɪˈtɔːt/:v./n. 反驳:She retorted with a sharp comment that silenced everyone.
8. royalty /ˈrɔɪəlti/ n. 版税；王室成员；专利费
📖 英文解释
a sum paid to an author or inventor; people of royal rank collectively
✏️ 例句
The author received substantial royalties from book sales worldwide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：pay royalties（支付版税）、royalty fee（版权费）；一词多义，注意区分王室成员和版税两义
🧠 助记
royal（王室）+ -ty（名词后缀）→ 王室的权利/收益 → 版税/王室
royalty::/ˈrɔɪəlti/:n. 版税:The singer earns millions in royalties every year.
9. saw /sɔː/ n. 锯；v. 锯开
📖 英文解释
a tool with a toothed blade for cutting hard materials; to cut with a saw
✏️ 例句
The carpenter used a saw to cut the wooden plank.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：saw off（锯断）、jigsaw（拼图/线锯）；注意与 see 的过去式 saw 区分
🧠 助记
saw 形状像锯齿的 S 和 W → 锯子
saw::/sɔː/:n. 锯:He sawed the branch off the tree carefully.
10. scout /skaʊt/ n./v. 侦察员；侦察
📖 英文解释
a person sent ahead to gather information; to explore and observe an area
✏️ 例句
The scout reported enemy movements to the commander.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：scout for talent（物色人才）、talent scout（星探）；既可作名词也可作动词
🧠 助记
scout 联想 'shout'（喊叫）→ 偷偷观察不能喊叫的侦察员
scout::/skaʊt/:n./v. 侦察员:The company sent a scout to find new business opportunities.
11. sew /səʊ/ v. 缝；缝制
📖 英文解释
to join, fasten, or repair by making stitches with a needle and thread
✏️ 例句
My grandmother taught me how to sew buttons onto shirts.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：sew up（缝合）、sew on（缝上）；注意拼写，区分 sew/sow/so
🧠 助记
sew 发音像 'so'（所以），联想：针线缝合，所以衣服穿上了
sew::/səʊ/:v. 缝:She spent the afternoon sewing a new dress.
12. shampoo /ʃæmˈpuː/ n./v. 洗发水；洗头
📖 英文解释
a liquid soap for washing hair; to wash hair with shampoo
✏️ 例句
She bought a new shampoo that smelled like lavender.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：shampoo your hair（洗头）、dry shampoo（干洗发剂）；来自印地语 champo（按摩）
🧠 助记
sham（假）+ poo（便便）→ 假装不是很脏的头发 → 用洗发水洗头，幽默记忆
shampoo::/ʃæmˈpuː/:n./v. 洗发水:He used a gentle shampoo for his sensitive scalp.
13. shepherd /ˈʃepəd/ n. 牧羊人；v. 牧羊；引导
📖 英文解释

sheep（羊）+ herd（牧群）→ 牧羊的人 → 牧羊人
shepherd::/ˈʃepəd/:n. 牧羊人:The teacher shepherded the students safely across the road.
14. siren /ˈsaɪrən/ n. 警报器；汽笛；妖女

siren::/ˈsaɪrən/:n. 警报器:The police car's siren cleared the way through the busy street.

slaughter::/ˈslɔːtə/:n./v. 屠杀:The massacre was described as a senseless slaughter.

sophomore::/ˈsɒfəmɔː/:n./adj. 大二学生:He transferred to a new university in his sophomore year.

spicy::/ˈspaɪsi/:adj. 辛辣的:The spicy curry made her eyes water.

stab::/stæb/:v./n. 刺:She felt a stab of guilt after telling the lie.
20. stationery /ˈsteɪʃənri/ n. 文具；书写用品

stationery::/ˈsteɪʃənri/:n. 文具:The office supply cabinet was stocked with stationery.

stipulate::/ˈstɪpjuleɪt/:v. 规定:The agreement stipulates that both parties must agree to changes.
22. sunset /ˈsʌnset/ n. 日落；黄昏

They watched the beautiful sunset from the hilltop.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

sun（太阳）+ set（下沉）→ 太阳下沉 → 日落
sunset::/ˈsʌnset/:n. 日落:The sunset painted the sky in shades of orange and pink.
23. thereafter /ˌðeərˈɑːftə/ adv. 此后；在那之后
📖 英文解释
after that time; from that time onwards
✏️ 例句
He graduated in 2020 and thereafter found a job in finance.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用于书面语/正式文体；近义词：subsequently, afterward；考研写作高分词汇
🧠 助记
there（那里）+ after（之后）→ 在那之后 → 此后
thereafter::/ˌðeərˈɑːftə/:adv. 此后:She moved to London and thereafter began her career.

trench::/trentʃ/:n. 沟:Workers dug a trench to lay the water pipes.

ultraviolet::/ˌʌltrəˈvaɪələt/:adj./n. 紫外线的:The doctor advised using sunscreen to block ultraviolet rays.
