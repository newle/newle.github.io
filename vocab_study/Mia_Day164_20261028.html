1. buffet /ˈbʊfeɪ/ n. 自助餐；v. 反复打击
📖 英文解释
a meal where guests serve themselves from a variety of dishes; to strike repeatedly
✏️ 例句
The hotel offers a delicious breakfast buffet every morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：buffet dinner/lunch（自助晚餐/午餐）；作动词：buffeted by winds（被风吹打）；一词两义
🧠 助记
buffet 来自法语，联想 'buff'（打磨）→ 自助餐台被人反复光顾/打磨 → 自助餐
buffet::/ˈbʊfeɪ/:n. 自助餐:Passengers were buffeted by strong winds on the mountain pass.
2. cellar /ˈselə/ n. 地窖；地下室
📖 英文解释
a room below ground level used for storage
✏️ 例句
They stored wine in the cool cellar beneath the house.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：wine cellar（酒窖）、salt cellar（盐瓶）；区分 cellar（地窖）vs basement（地下室，有居住功能）
🧠 助记
cell（小室）+ ar → 地下的小房间 → 地窖
cellar::/ˈselə/:n. 地窖:The restaurant boasts an impressive wine cellar.
3. cemetery /ˈsemɪtri/ n. 墓地；公墓
📖 英文解释
a large burial ground, especially one not adjoining a church
✏️ 例句
Flowers were placed on the graves at the cemetery.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：visit a cemetery（扫墓）、military cemetery（军人公墓）；区分 cemetery vs graveyard（教堂旁）
🧠 助记
ceme-（睡眠）+ tery → 长眠之地 → 墓地（希腊语 koimeterion，睡觉的地方）
cemetery::/ˈsemɪtri/:n. 墓地:The old cemetery has graves dating back to the 18th century.
4. coarse /kɔːs/ adj. 粗糙的；粗鲁的
📖 英文解释
rough or harsh in texture; rude and offensive in manner
✏️ 例句
The coarse fabric irritated her sensitive skin.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：coarse texture（粗糙质地）、coarse language（粗俗语言）；注意区分 coarse（粗糙）vs course（课程）
🧠 助记
coarse 联想 'horse'（马）→ 马的皮肤是粗糙的 → 粗糙的
coarse::/kɔːs/:adj. 粗糙的:He used coarse sandpaper to smooth the wooden surface.
5. comet /ˈkɒmɪt/ n. 彗星
📖 英文解释
a celestial object with a nucleus of ice and dust, forming a tail when near the sun
✏️ 例句
Halley's Comet is visible from Earth approximately every 75 years.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：comet tail（彗星尾）、comet nucleus（彗核）；天文类阅读高频词
🧠 助记
comet 联想 'come'（来）→ 彗星周期性地来访地球 → 彗星
comet::/ˈkɒmɪt/:n. 彗星:Astronomers tracked the comet as it passed close to the sun.
6. cordial /ˈkɔːdiəl/ adj. 热忱的；诚恳的；n. 甜果汁饮料
📖 英文解释
warm and friendly; a sweet fruit-flavored drink
✏️ 例句
The two leaders held a cordial discussion on trade relations.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：cordial relations（友好关系）、a cordial welcome（热忱的欢迎）；外交语言高频
🧠 助记
cord-（心，拉丁 cor）+ ial → 发自内心的 → 热忱的/诚恳的
cordial::/ˈkɔːdiəl/:adj. 热忱的:The two countries enjoy cordial diplomatic relations.
7. cosy /ˈkəʊzi/ adj. 亲切友好的；舒适温暖的
📖 英文解释
giving a feeling of comfort, warmth and relaxation; (informal) too friendly with
✏️ 例句
They spent a cosy evening by the fireplace.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：a cosy atmosphere（温馨的氛围）、cosy relationship（亲密关系）；英式拼写，美式 cozy
🧠 助记
cosy 联想 'cozy' → 暖和和的、让人放松的感觉 → 舒适/亲密
cosy::/ˈkəʊzi/:adj. 亲切友好的:The small café had a cosy and welcoming atmosphere.
8. darling /ˈdɑːlɪŋ/ n./adj. 亲爱的；心爱的人
📖 英文解释
a person who is very much loved; used as a term of endearment
✏️ 例句
Come here, darling, and tell me what happened.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：my darling（我亲爱的）、the darling of the media（媒体宠儿）；既作名词也作形容词
🧠 助记
dar-（亲爱）+ ling（小称后缀）→ 亲爱的小宝贝 → 亲爱的
darling::/ˈdɑːlɪŋ/:n./adj. 亲爱的:She was the darling of the fashion world for a decade.
9. deduct /dɪˈdʌkt/ v. 扣除；减去
📖 英文解释
to take away an amount from a total
✏️ 例句
Tax is automatically deducted from your monthly salary.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：deduct from（从…扣除）、tax deduction（税款扣除）、deductible（可扣除的）
🧠 助记
de-（去除）+ duct（引导）→ 引导出去/拿走一部分 → 扣除
deduct::/dɪˈdʌkt/:v. 扣除:Points will be deducted for spelling mistakes.
10. degenerate /dɪˈdʒenəreɪt/ v. 退化；堕落；adj. 退化的
📖 英文解释
to decline from a higher to a lower level; having lost the qualities that are considered normal
✏️ 例句
The peaceful protest degenerated into violence.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：degenerate into（退化/堕落为）、degenerate behavior（堕落行为）
🧠 助记
de-（向下）+ generate（产生/生成）→ 向下产生变化 → 退化
degenerate::/dɪˈdʒenəreɪt/:v. 退化:Without care, the discussion quickly degenerated into an argument.
11. dew /djuː/ n. 露水
📖 英文解释
tiny drops of water that form on cool surfaces at night
✏️ 例句
The morning dew sparkled on the grass in the sunlight.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：morning dew（晨露）、dewdrop（露珠）；自然/文学语境中常见
🧠 助记
dew 发音像 'do'（做）→ 大自然在夜晚做出来的水珠 → 露水
dew::/djuː/:n. 露水:The leaves were still covered with dew at dawn.
12. discrete /dɪˈskriːt/ adj. 不连续的；分离的
📖 英文解释
individually separate and distinct; not continuous
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：discrete units（分离单元）、discrete mathematics（离散数学）；注意区分 discrete（分离的）vs discreet（谨慎的）
🧠 助记
dis-（分开）+ crete（具体的）→ 分开且各自具体存在 → 分离的/不连续的
discrete::/dɪˈskriːt/:adj. 不连续的:The course is divided into a number of discrete modules.
13. drip /drɪp/ v./n. 滴；滴水

🧠 助记

drip::/drɪp/:v./n. 滴:A candle was dripping wax onto the table.

eclipse::/ɪˈklɪps/:n./v. 日/月食:Her performance eclipsed all others at the competition.

encyclopedia::/ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiə/:n. 百科全书:Wikipedia is the world's largest online encyclopedia.

generalise::/ˈdʒenərəlaɪz/:v. 概括:Scientists warn against generalising research findings too broadly.
