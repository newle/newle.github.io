1. brandy /ˈbrændi/ n. 白兰地酒
📖 英文解释
A strong alcoholic drink distilled from wine or fermented fruit juice.
✏️ 例句
He poured himself a glass of brandy to warm up.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：brandy glass（白兰地杯）；常作名词，偶见于阅读文化背景文章。
🧠 助记
bran（谷物）+dy → 用谷物发酵成的烈酒
brandy::/ˈbrændi/:n. 白兰地酒:He sipped brandy by the fire.
2. buffer /ˈbʌfə(r)/ n./v. 缓冲；缓冲器
📖 英文解释
Something that reduces the impact of a shock or prevents conflict between opposing forces; to lessen the impact of something.
✏️ 例句
The wetlands act as a buffer against flooding.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：buffer zone（缓冲区）；buffer state；作动词表缓冲、减轻。
🧠 助记
buff（缓冲垫）+ er → 专门缓冲的东西
buffer::/ˈbʌfə(r)/:n./v. 缓冲:Savings can buffer against financial shocks.
3. cabbage /ˈkæbɪdʒ/ n. 卷心菜；甘蓝
📖 英文解释
A vegetable with thick green or purple leaves that form a round head.
✏️ 例句
She made a salad with cabbage and carrots.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研较少直接考，偶尔出现在饮食/农业阅读中；注意拼写。
🧠 助记
cab（出租车是绿色）+ bage → 绿色圆头蔬菜
cabbage::/ˈkæbɪdʒ/:n. 卷心菜:Cabbage is rich in vitamins.
4. Catholic /ˈkæθəlɪk/ n./adj. 天主教徒；天主教的
📖 英文解释
Relating to the Roman Catholic Church, or a member of it.
✏️ 例句
She was raised in a Catholic household.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化类文章常见；注意首字母大写；catholic（小写）意为广泛的、包容的。
🧠 助记
Cath（凯瑟琳是虔诚的）+ olic → 天主教信徒
Catholic::/ˈkæθəlɪk/:n./adj. 天主教徒:The Catholic Church has a long history.
5. Christ /kraɪst/ n. 基督
📖 英文解释
The title given to Jesus, regarded by Christians as the Messiah and Son of God.
✏️ 例句
Christmas celebrates the birth of Christ.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化/宗教背景文章出现；注意：Jesus Christ；B.C.（Before Christ）。
🧠 助记
发音类似 kraist → 基督
Christ::/kraɪst/:n. 基督:Christians believe in Jesus Christ.
6. Christian /ˈkrɪstʃən/ n./adj. 基督徒；基督教的
📖 英文解释
A person who believes in Christianity; relating to or professing Christianity.
✏️ 例句
Many Christian values emphasize compassion and forgiveness.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研常见于宗教、历史、文化类文章；区分 Christian（人/形容词）与 Christianity（名词，基督教）。
🧠 助记
Christ + ian → 信仰基督的人
Christian::/ˈkrɪstʃən/:n./adj. 基督徒:He has strong Christian beliefs.
7. Christmas /ˈkrɪsməs/ n. 圣诞节
📖 英文解释
The annual Christian festival celebrating Christ's birth, observed on December 25.
✏️ 例句
Children eagerly await Christmas gifts.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化类文章；Christmas Eve（平安夜）；Merry Christmas！
🧠 助记
Christ + mas（弥撒）→ 基督弥撒之节
Christmas::/ˈkrɪsməs/:n. 圣诞节:We exchanged gifts on Christmas morning.
8. cigaret /ˌsɪɡəˈret/ n. 香烟（cigarette 的变体写法）
📖 英文解释
A thin cylinder of finely cut tobacco rolled in paper for smoking.
✏️ 例句
He lit a cigaret and stared out the window.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研注意 cigarette 为标准拼写；cigaret 是非正式变体；常见搭配 smoke a cigarette。
🧠 助记
cigar（雪茄）的小型版 → 小雪茄 → 香烟
cigaret::/ˌsɪɡəˈret/:n. 香烟（cigarette 的变体写:Smoking cigarettes is harmful to health.
9. civilise /ˈsɪvəlaɪz/ v. 使文明；开化
📖 英文解释
To bring a person or society to a stage of social, cultural, and moral development considered more advanced.
✏️ 例句
Education helps civilise a society.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频动词；civilise/civilize（英/美拼法）；派生词：civilised（文明的），civilization（文明）。
🧠 助记
civil（文明的）+ ise → 使变文明
civilise::/ˈsɪvəlaɪz/:v. 使文明:Trade helped civilise ancient societies.
10. clockwise /ˈklɒkwaɪz/ adv./adj. 顺时针方向地；顺时针的
📖 英文解释
In the direction of rotation of the hands of a clock, viewed from the front.
✏️ 例句
Turn the lid clockwise to close it.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研理工科阅读/翻译偶见；反义词：anticlockwise/counterclockwise（逆时针）。
🧠 助记
clock（时钟）+ wise（方向）→ 顺时针
clockwise::/ˈklɒkwaɪz/:adv./adj. 顺时针方向地:Rotate the dial clockwise by 90 degrees.
11. clothes /kləʊðz/ n. 衣服；服装（复数）
📖 英文解释
Items worn to cover the body; garments.
✏️ 例句
She packed her clothes for the trip.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研基础词；注意 clothes 无单数形式；clothing（总称）；put on/take off clothes。
🧠 助记
cloth（布料）+ es → 由布料做的东西 → 衣服
clothes::/kləʊðz/:n. 衣服:He bought new clothes for the interview.
12. cock /kɒk/ n. 公鸡；雄鸟
📖 英文解释
A male bird, especially a domestic fowl (rooster).
✏️ 例句
The cock crowed at dawn.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研偶见于文学/描述性文章；注意语境；rooster（美式说法更常用）。
🧠 助记
cock = 公鸡；联想鸡叫声咯咯
cock::/kɒk/:n. 公鸡:The cock woke everyone at sunrise.
13. colonel /ˈkɜːnl/ n. 上校
📖 英文解释
A rank of officer in the army or air force, above a lieutenant colonel.
✏️ 例句
The colonel led his regiment into battle.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研军事/历史文章出现；注意发音与拼写不一致（拼 colonel，读 /kɜːnl/）。
🧠 助记
发音同 kernel（核心）→ 上校是军队核心
colonel::/ˈkɜːnl/:n. 上校:Colonel Smith commanded the operation.
14. communism /ˈkɒmjʊnɪzəm/ n. 共产主义
📖 英文解释
A political and economic theory advocating collective ownership and a classless society.
✏️ 例句
Communism was the dominant ideology in the Soviet Union.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研政治/历史类文章必考；communist（共产主义者/共产主义的）；communism vs capitalism。
🧠 助记
common（共同）+ ism → 共同所有制 → 共产主义
communism::/ˈkɒmjʊnɪzəm/:n. 共产主义:Communism shaped 20th-century politics.
15. concurrent /kənˈkɜːrənt/ adj. 同时发生的；并发的
📖 英文解释
Existing, happening, or done at the same time.
✏️ 例句
The two trials were held concurrently.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频学术词；concurrent sentences（并期服刑）；concurrently（副词）；区分 simultaneous（强调同一时刻）。
🧠 助记
con（共同）+ current（流）→ 同一水流中 → 同时进行
concurrent::/kənˈkɜːrənt/:adj. 同时发生的:Concurrent processing speeds up computation.
16. corrode /kəˈrəʊd/ v. 腐蚀；侵蚀
📖 英文解释
To destroy or damage metal, stone, etc. slowly by chemical action; to weaken gradually.
✏️ 例句
Acid rain corrodes metal surfaces.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研理工科/环境类文章常见；corrosion（n.腐蚀）；corrosive（adj.腐蚀性的）。
🧠 助记
cor（核心）+ rode（骑入）→ 骑入核心 → 腐蚀到核心
corrode::/kəˈrəʊd/:v. 腐蚀:Salt water corrodes iron quickly.
17. cradle /ˈkreɪdl/ n. 摇篮；发源地 v. 轻轻抱着
📖 英文解释
A small bed for a baby that rocks; the place where something originated.
✏️ 例句
Greece is often called the cradle of democracy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频比喻用法：cradle of civilization（文明摇篮）；from the cradle to the grave（从生到死）。
🧠 助记
cra（哭）+ dle → 婴儿哭就摇的床 → 摇篮
cradle::/ˈkreɪdl/:n. 摇篮:Mesopotamia was the cradle of civilization.
18. cunning /ˈkʌnɪŋ/ adj./n. 狡猾的；狡猾

✏️ 例句
The cunning fox outsmarted the hunters.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研阅读中描述人物特征；cunning plan（狡猾的计划）；近义词：crafty, sly, wily。
🧠 助记
cun（寸）→ 寸步不让地耍手段 → 狡猾
cunning::/ˈkʌnɪŋ/:adj./n. 狡猾的:His cunning strategy defeated the enemy.
19. cylinder /ˈsɪlɪndə(r)/ n. 汽缸；圆筒；圆柱体
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研理工科阅读；cylinder head（缸盖）；fire on all cylinders（全力以赴）。
🧠 助记
cylin（旋转）+ der → 旋转的圆柱形物体
cylinder::/ˈsɪlɪndə(r)/:n. 汽缸:The car's engine has six cylinders.

🧠 助记
date（日期）+ a → 记录日期等信息 → 数据
data::/ˈdeɪtə/:n. 数据:The data shows a clear upward trend.
21. daunting /ˈdɔːntɪŋ/ adj. 令人畏惧的；令人望而生畏的

The task seemed daunting at first, but she managed it.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频形容词；a daunting challenge/task/prospect；近义词：intimidating, formidable。
🧠 助记
daunt（使畏惧）+ ing → 令人畏惧的
daunting::/ˈdɔːntɪŋ/:adj. 令人畏惧的:Climbing Everest is a daunting challenge.
22. deceit /dɪˈsiːt/ n. 欺骗；诡计
📖 英文解释
The action or practice of deceiving someone by concealing or misrepresenting the truth.
✏️ 例句
Their relationship was built on deceit.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
de（去掉）+ ceit（诚信）→ 去掉诚信 → 欺骗
deceit::/dɪˈsiːt/:n. 欺骗:He was accused of deceit and fraud.
23. December /dɪˈsembə(r)/ n. 十二月

Christmas falls in December.

考研基础词；缩写：Dec.；注意大写。

decem（十，拉丁语）+ ber → 罗马历法第十月 → 现在第十二月
December::/dɪˈsembə(r)/:n. 十二月:The conference is scheduled for December.

decimal::/ˈdesɪml/:n./adj. 小数:Pi is approximately 3.14 in decimal.
25. desolate /ˈdesələt/ adj. 荒凉的；孤寂的 v. 使荒芜
📖 英文解释
Uninhabited and giving an impression of bleak emptiness; feeling or showing misery.
✏️ 例句
The village was desolate after the earthquake.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研描述环境/心情；desolate landscape（荒凉的风景）；desolation（n.荒凉）。
🧠 助记
de（去掉）+ solate（太阳）→ 没有阳光照耀 → 荒凉
desolate::/ˈdesələt/:adj. 荒凉的:A desolate wasteland stretched for miles.
26. despatch /dɪˈspætʃ/ v./n. 派遣；发送（dispatch 的英式拼法）
📖 英文解释

考研注意英式（despatch）与美式（dispatch）拼法；常考于新闻/军事语境。
🧠 助记
de（出发）+ spatch（快速）→ 快速发出/派遣
despatch::/dɪˈspætʃ/:v./n. 派遣:Reinforcements were despatched immediately.
27. dialog /ˈdaɪəlɒɡ/ n. 对话；对白（dialogue 的美式拼法）

考研注意 dialog（美式）/ dialogue（英式）；political dialogue（政治对话）；enter into dialogue（开展对话）。

dia（二）+ log（说话）→ 两人之间的说话 → 对话
dialog::/ˈdaɪəlɒɡ/:n. 对话:Open dialogue is key to resolving conflicts.

dove::/dʌv/:n. 鸽子:The politician was known as a dove on foreign policy.
