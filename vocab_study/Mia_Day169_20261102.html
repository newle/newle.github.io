1. economics /ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks/ n. 经济学；经济情况
📖 英文解释
The branch of knowledge concerned with the production, consumption, and transfer of wealth.
✏️ 例句
She studied economics at university.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研超高频；economics as a subject；economics of（…的经济学）；economic（adj.经济的）。
🧠 助记
economy（经济）+ ics（学科）→ 研究经济的学科
economics::/ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks/:n. 经济学:Economics helps explain resource allocation.
2. embassy /ˈembəsi/ n. 大使馆；大使馆全体人员
📖 英文解释
The official residence or offices of an ambassador; the ambassador and staff.
✏️ 例句
He sought help at the British embassy.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研外交/国际类文章；embassy staff（大使馆工作人员）；区分 embassy（大使馆）与 consulate（领事馆）。
🧠 助记
em（往里）+ bas（基地）+ sy → 驻外的基地 → 大使馆
embassy::/ˈembəsi/:n. 大使馆:The protest took place outside the embassy.
3. fable /ˈfeɪbl/ n. 寓言；神话；谎言
📖 英文解释
A short story, typically with animals as characters, conveying a moral; a myth or legend.
✏️ 例句
Aesop's fables teach moral lessons.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文学/文化类；fable vs myth vs legend（寓言/神话/传说）；morality fable（道德寓言）。
🧠 助记
fab（精彩）+ le → 精彩的道德故事 → 寓言
fable::/ˈfeɪbl/:n. 寓言:The fable of the hare and tortoise teaches patience.
4. farther /ˈfɑːðə(r)/ adj./adv. 更远的；更远地（空间距离）
📖 英文解释
At or to a greater distance; more distant in space.
✏️ 例句
The next town is farther than we thought.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研注意 farther（空间距离）vs further（程度/比喻）；两者在口语中常互换。
🧠 助记
far（远）的比较级 → 更远的
farther::/ˈfɑːðə(r)/:adj./adv. 更远的:The destination was farther than expected.
5. February /ˈfebruəri/ n. 二月
📖 英文解释
The second month of the year, containing 28 days (or 29 in a leap year).
✏️ 例句
Valentine's Day is in February.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研基础词；缩写：Feb.；注意发音 /ˈfebruəri/。
🧠 助记
Feb → 发布（谐音）→ 二月发布新事物
February::/ˈfebruəri/:n. 二月:The report is due in February.
6. following /ˈfɒləʊɪŋ/ adj. 下列的；接着的 prep. 在…之后 n. 追随者
📖 英文解释
Coming after in time or order; a body of supporters.
✏️ 例句
The following day, she submitted her report.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研超高频学术词；the following（下列）；following the meeting（会议之后）。
🧠 助记
follow（跟随）+ ing → 紧跟在后面的
following::/ˈfɒləʊɪŋ/:adj. 下列的:The following measures will be implemented.
7. fragrant /ˈfreɪɡrənt/ adj. 馥郁的；芳香的
📖 英文解释
Having a pleasant or sweet smell.
✏️ 例句
The garden was filled with fragrant roses.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研阅读描述自然/环境；fragrant air（芳香的空气）；fragrance（n.香气）。
🧠 助记
frag（碎片）+ rant（唱）→ 香气飘散四方
fragrant::/ˈfreɪɡrənt/:adj. 馥郁的:The fragrant flowers attracted many bees.
8. franchise /ˈfrænʧaɪz/ n. 特权；选举权；特许经营权 v. 授予特许经营权
📖 英文解释
An authorization to sell a company's goods; the right to vote; a special privilege.
✏️ 例句
The company sold franchises to local entrepreneurs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研商业/政治类高频；franchise business（特许经营业务）；universal franchise（普选权）。
🧠 助记
franc（法国/自由）+ hise → 自由经营的权利 → 特许权
franchise::/ˈfrænʧaɪz/:n. 特权:The fast-food franchise expanded globally.
9. Friday /ˈfraɪdeɪ/ n. 星期五
📖 英文解释
The day of the week before Saturday and following Thursday.
✏️ 例句
The meeting is scheduled for Friday afternoon.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研基础词；缩写 Fri.；注意大写；Good Friday（耶稣受难日）。
🧠 助记
Frig（北欧爱神弗里格）+ day → 弗里格之日 → 星期五
Friday::/ˈfraɪdeɪ/:n. 星期五:The deadline is this Friday.
10. further /ˈfɜːðə(r)/ adj./adv./v. 更远的；进一步的；促进
📖 英文解释
At or to a greater extent or degree; to help advance or promote.
✏️ 例句
We need to look further into this problem.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研超高频；further education（继续教育）；furthermore（此外）；without further delay（毫不拖延）。
🧠 助记
fur（更多）+ ther → 更进一步
further::/ˈfɜːðə(r)/:adj./adv./v. 更远的:We need no further evidence.
11. gaol /dʒeɪl/ n./v. 监狱；关押（jail 的英式拼法）
📖 英文解释
A place for the confinement of people accused or convicted of a crime (British spelling of jail).
✏️ 例句
He was sent to gaol for theft.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研注意英式（gaol）vs 美式（jail）拼法；gaoler（看守）。
🧠 助记
gaol 读 jail → 监狱
gaol::/dʒeɪl/:n./v. 监狱:The thief was sentenced to gaol for two years.
12. gay /ɡeɪ/ adj./n. 同性恋的；快乐的（旧义）
📖 英文解释
Homosexual (typically used of a man); formerly meaning lighthearted and carefree.
✏️ 例句
The city held a gay pride parade.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研注意语义演变；现代用法指同性恋；旧义为快乐的；gay rights（同性恋权利）。
🧠 助记
gay → 轻松愉快，后引申为同性恋群体的自我认同
gay::/ɡeɪ/:adj./n. 同性恋的:The city celebrated gay pride month.
13. glamor /ˈɡlæmə(r)/ n. 魅力；迷人（glamour 的美式拼法）
📖 英文解释
The attractive or exciting quality that makes certain people or things seem appealing.
✏️ 例句
Hollywood glamour attracted many aspiring actors.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

🧠 助记
glam（闪耀）+ or → 闪耀的光彩 → 魅力
glamor::/ˈɡlæmə(r)/:n. 魅力:The actress epitomized old Hollywood glamour.
14. goods /ɡʊdz/ n. 货物；商品；（道德）品质

goods::/ɡʊdz/:n. 货物:Consumer goods prices rose sharply last year.
15. grease /ɡriːs/ n. 油脂；润滑脂 v. 涂油

18. horsepower /ˈhɔːspaʊə(r)/ n. 马力（功率单位）

A unit of power equal to 550 foot-pounds per second, used to measure engine power.

horsepower::/ˈhɔːspaʊə(r)/:n. 马力（功率单位）:The new engine produces 300 horsepower.

24. influenza /ˌɪnfluˈenzə/ n. 流行性感冒；流感
📖 英文解释
An infectious viral disease causing fever, muscle pain, and respiratory illness.
✏️ 例句
The influenza epidemic spread rapidly last winter.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

influenza::/ˌɪnfluˈenzə/:n. 流行性感冒:Influenza vaccines are updated annually.

infrared::/ˌɪnfrəˈred/:adj./n. 红外线的:Infrared imaging is used in night vision.
