1. July /dʒuˈlaɪ/ n. 七月
📖 英文解释
The seventh month of the year, containing 31 days.
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研基础词；缩写：Jul.；注意大写。
🧠 助记
July → Julius Caesar（凯撒大帝）的月份
July::/dʒuˈlaɪ/:n. 七月:The summit is scheduled for July.

🧠 助记

June::/dʒuːn/:n. 六月:The exams take place in June.
