1. oar /ɔːr/ n. 桨；橹
📖 英文解释
a long pole with a flat blade used to row a boat through water
✏️ 例句
He grabbed an oar and began rowing toward the shore.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：pull an oar（划桨）；rest on one's oars（停止努力）；考研中常在航行、体育语境出现
🧠 助记
oar 发音像 'or'，桨在水中划动时会发出'哦'的声音——船桨
oar::/ɔːr/:n. 桨:He pulled the oar hard to cross the river.
2. o'clock /əˈklɒk/ adv. …点钟
📖 英文解释
used after a number to specify an exact hour when telling the time
✏️ 例句
The meeting starts at nine o'clock sharp.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
固定表达：at + 数字 + o'clock；考研阅读中偶见时间描述，注意缩写形式
🧠 助记
o'clock = of the clock，从'钟上'读时间——…点钟
o'clock::/əˈklɒk/:adv. …点钟:She woke up at six o'clock every morning.
3. October /ɒkˈtoʊbər/ n. 十月
📖 英文解释
the tenth month of the year, coming after September and before November
✏️ 例句
The autumn leaves are most beautiful in October.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
专有名词，首字母大写；考研写作中用于举例说明时间背景
🧠 助记
Oct- 来自拉丁语 octo（八），古罗马历法中是第八月，后移为第十月
October::/ɒkˈtoʊbər/:n. 十月:The conference is scheduled for October 15th.
4. odds /ɒdz/ n. 可能性；赔率；不平等
📖 英文解释
the probability that something will happen; the ratio between the amount bet and the potential winnings
✏️ 例句
The odds of winning the lottery are extremely low.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频：odds are that...（很可能…）；against all odds（克服重重困难）；at odds with（与…不一致）
🧠 助记
odds = odd + s，奇奇怪怪的数字就是赔率——可能性；赔率
odds::/ɒdz/:n. 可能性:Against all odds, she passed the exam.
5. ought to /ˈɔːt tə/ modal. 应该；理应
📖 英文解释
used to indicate duty, correctness, or strong probability; similar to 'should' but often implies moral obligation
✏️ 例句
You ought to apologize to her for what you said.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研语法：ought to do（应该做）；ought to have done（本应做却没做）；与 should 可互换但语气更强
🧠 助记
ought 听起来像'哦该'，提醒自己'哦，该做这件事了'——应该
ought to::/ˈɔːt tə/:modal. 应该:Students ought to review their notes daily.
6. outskirts /ˈaʊtskɜːrts/ n. 郊区；城市边缘地带
📖 英文解释
the areas on the edge of a city or town, away from the center
✏️ 例句
They moved to the outskirts of the city to escape the noise.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
常用搭配：on the outskirts of（在…郊区）；考研社会类话题常涉及城市化与郊区发展
🧠 助记
out（外面）+ skirts（裙边），城市的'裙边'就是郊区——郊区
outskirts::/ˈaʊtskɜːrts/:n. 郊区:The factory is located on the outskirts of town.
7. overt /oʊˈvɜːrt/ adj. 公开的；明显的
📖 英文解释
done or shown openly; not secret or hidden
✏️ 例句
There was no overt sign of conflict between the two parties.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研对比词：overt vs. covert（隐蔽的）；常用于政治、心理学语境；搭配 overt discrimination
🧠 助记
o-vert，想成 'over t-shirt'，把T恤翻到外面穿，非常公开明显——公开的
overt::/oʊˈvɜːrt/:adj. 公开的:His overt hostility made everyone uncomfortable.
8. owing to /ˈoʊɪŋ tuː/ prep. 由于；因为
📖 英文解释
because of; as a result of
✏️ 例句
The flight was delayed owing to bad weather conditions.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研写作常用连接词：owing to = due to = because of；后接名词或动名词；正式书面语
🧠 助记
owing（欠着）+ to，欠了什么原因，'由于'某原因——因为；由于
owing to::/ˈoʊɪŋ tuː/:prep. 由于:Owing to his hard work, he got promoted.
9. pants /pænts/ n. 裤子；内裤（英式）
📖 英文解释
trousers (American English); underpants or underwear (British English)
✏️ 例句
He wore a pair of black pants to the interview.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
注意英美差异：美式 pants = trousers（长裤）；英式 pants = underpants（内裤）；考研词汇辨析常考
🧠 助记
pants 和'盼兹'谐音，盼着穿上新裤子——裤子
pants::/pænts/:n. 裤子:She bought a new pair of pants for the trip.
10. parasite /ˈpærəsaɪt/ n. 寄生虫；寄生植物；靠别人生活的人
📖 英文解释
an organism that lives on or in another organism and benefits at the host's expense; a person who habitually exploits others
✏️ 例句
Tapeworms are parasites that live in the intestines of animals.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研生物、社会类话题：parasite on society（社会寄生虫）；搭配 parasitic relationship（寄生关系）
🧠 助记
para（旁边）+ site（地点），住在别人旁边蹭吃蹭喝——寄生虫
parasite::/ˈpærəsaɪt/:n. 寄生虫:He was called a parasite for never contributing to the team.
11. peep /piːp/ v./n. 偷看；窥视；微弱声音
📖 英文解释
to look quickly or secretly through a small opening; a quick or secret look; a short high sound
✏️ 例句
She peeped through the keyhole to see who was inside.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研用法：peep at/through（偷看）；not a peep（一声不响）；引申义表微弱声音或光线
🧠 助记
peep 象声词，像小鸟'pip pip'叫声，也像偷偷探头看的动作——偷看
peep::/piːp/:v./n. 偷看:He peeped through the curtain at the street below.
12. persecute /ˈpɜːrsɪkjuːt/ v. 迫害；虐待；纠缠
📖 英文解释
to treat someone cruelly and unfairly, especially because of their race, religion, or beliefs; to harass persistently
✏️ 例句
Many minorities were persecuted for their religious beliefs.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研历史/人权话题高频词；搭配 persecute sb. for sth.；名词形式 persecution
🧠 助记
per（持续）+ secute（追，同 pursue），一直追着别人——迫害
persecute::/ˈpɜːrsɪkjuːt/:v. 迫害:Dissidents were persecuted by the authoritarian regime.
13. physics /ˈfɪzɪks/ n. 物理学
📖 英文解释
the scientific study of matter, energy, force, and motion, and the way these affect each other
✏️ 例句
Einstein revolutionized physics with his theory of relativity.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研科技类话题；注意复数形式但作单数使用；搭配 laws of physics（物理定律）
🧠 助记
physics 发音像'飞着'，物理学研究飞行等运动规律——物理学
physics::/ˈfɪzɪks/:n. 物理学:Physics explains the fundamental laws of the universe.
14. pistol /ˈpɪstəl/ n. 手枪
📖 英文解释
a small gun that is held and fired with one hand
✏️ 例句
The detective drew his pistol before entering the building.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研犯罪/法律话题；区分 pistol（手枪）vs. rifle（步枪）vs. shotgun（猎枪）
🧠 助记
pistol 发音像'劈死他'，用手枪对付人——手枪
pistol::/ˈpɪstəl/:n. 手枪:The robber was armed with a pistol.
15. plaintiff /ˈpleɪntɪf/ n. 原告
📖 英文解释
a person who brings a legal action against another person in a court of law
✏️ 例句
The plaintiff sued the company for wrongful dismissal.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研法律类高频词；对比 defendant（被告）；搭配 the plaintiff's claim（原告的诉求）
🧠 助记
plain（抱怨）+ tiff（争吵），去法院抱怨争吵的人——原告
plaintiff::/ˈpleɪntɪf/:n. 原告:The plaintiff demanded compensation for damages.
16. politics /ˈpɒlɪtɪks/ n. 政治；政治活动；政治学
📖 英文解释
the activities involved in governing a country; the science of government; the complex relationships between people in authority
✏️ 例句
She has always been interested in international politics.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研社会高频词；复数形式单数用；搭配 engage in politics（参与政治）；office politics（办公室政治）
🧠 助记
politics = poli（城市，希腊语）+ tics，城市里的种种活动——政治
politics::/ˈpɒlɪtɪks/:n. 政治:Politics affects every aspect of people's daily lives.
17. preceding /prɪˈsiːdɪŋ/ adj. 在前的；先前的
📖 英文解释
coming immediately before something in time, order, or position
✏️ 例句
Please refer to the preceding chapter for more information.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研阅读理解常见：the preceding paragraph（上一段）；对比 following（其后的）；动词 precede
🧠 助记
pre（前）+ cede（走，让步），走在前面的——先前的；在前的
preceding::/prɪˈsiːdɪŋ/:adj. 在前的:The preceding discussion laid the groundwork for this argument.
18. proceedings /prəˈsiːdɪŋz/ n. 诉讼程序；会议记录；活动经过
📖 英文解释
legal action taken in a court; the official record of events at a conference or meeting; a series of actions or events
✏️ 例句
The proceedings of the conference were published in a journal.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研法律/学术语境：legal proceedings（法律诉讼）；conference proceedings（会议记录）
🧠 助记
proceed（继续进行）+ -ings，持续进行的法律或会议程序——诉讼；会议记录
proceedings::/prəˈsiːdɪŋz/:n. 诉讼程序:The court proceedings lasted three days.
19. projector /prəˈdʒektər/ n. 投影仪；放映机
📖 英文解释
a device that projects an image or film onto a screen or surface
✏️ 例句
The teacher used a projector to display the slides.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研科技/教育类；搭配 overhead projector（高射投影仪）；动词 project（投影；预测）
🧠 助记
project（投射）+ -or（做…的人/机器），投射图像的机器——投影仪
projector::/prəˈdʒektər/:n. 投影仪:The projector displayed the presentation on the white screen.
20. promising /ˈprɒmɪsɪŋ/ adj. 有前途的；有希望的
📖 英文解释
showing signs of future success or excellence; likely to develop well
✏️ 例句
She is one of the most promising young scientists in the field.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研高频形容词：a promising career（有前途的职业）；promising results（令人鼓舞的结果）
🧠 助记
promise（承诺）+ -ing，有承诺（未来）的——有前途的
promising::/ˈprɒmɪsɪŋ/:adj. 有前途的:The new treatment showed promising results in clinical trials.
21. prophet /ˈprɒfɪt/ n. 先知；预言者；倡导者
📖 英文解释
a person regarded as an inspired teacher or proclaimer of God's will; someone who predicts the future
✏️ 例句
The prophet warned the people of coming hardships.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研宗教/文化类；注意与 profit（利润）区分发音；搭配 a prophet of doom（末日预言者）
🧠 助记
pro（向前）+ phet（说），向前预言的人——先知
prophet::/ˈprɒfɪt/:n. 先知:He was seen as a prophet who foresaw the economic crisis.
22. provided /prəˈvaɪdɪd/ conj. 假如；以…为条件
📖 英文解释
on the condition that; if and only if
✏️ 例句
You may borrow my car provided you return it by evening.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研写作/语法：provided (that) = providing (that) = as long as；引导条件状语从句
🧠 助记
provide（提供）的过去式，提供条件就是'假如'——假如；以…为条件
provided::/prəˈvaɪdɪd/:conj. 假如:I'll help you provided that you follow the instructions.
23. proximate /ˈprɒksɪmət/ adj. 最近的；直接的
📖 英文解释
immediately preceding or following in a chain of events; very close or near
✏️ 例句
The proximate cause of the accident was driver fatigue.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研法律/逻辑类高频：proximate cause（直接原因）；对比 ultimate cause（根本原因）
🧠 助记
proxim（接近，同 approximate）+ -ate，最接近的——最近的；直接的
proximate::/ˈprɒksɪmət/:adj. 最近的:Stress was identified as the proximate cause of his illness.
24. qualitative /ˈkwɒlɪtətɪv/ adj. 质的；定性的
📖 英文解释
relating to or based on the quality or character of something rather than its quantity or amount
✏️ 例句
The study used qualitative methods to analyze people's experiences.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研研究方法类高频：qualitative vs. quantitative（定性 vs. 定量）；qualitative research（定性研究）
🧠 助记
quality（质量）+ -ative，关于质量的——定性的；质的
qualitative::/ˈkwɒlɪtətɪv/:adj. 质的:The report provides a qualitative analysis of consumer behavior.
25. quarantine /ˈkwɒrəntiːn/ n./v. 隔离；检疫
📖 英文解释
a period of isolation imposed on people or animals to prevent the spread of disease; to put someone or something in quarantine
✏️ 例句
All travelers had to undergo a 14-day quarantine upon arrival.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研医学/公共卫生类高频词（尤其后疫情语境）；搭配 in quarantine；quarantine measures
🧠 助记
quarantine 来自意大利语 quarantina（四十天），古时船只需隔离40天——隔离
quarantine::/ˈkwɒrəntiːn/:n./v. 隔离:The infected animals were quarantined to prevent spread.
26. quart /kwɔːrt/ n. 夸脱（液量单位）
📖 英文解释
a unit of liquid capacity equal to a quarter of a gallon; approximately 0.95 liters (US) or 1.14 liters (UK)
✏️ 例句
The recipe calls for one quart of milk.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研单位换算：1 quart ≈ 0.95升；quarter（四分之一）的衍生词；实际考试少见但需识别
🧠 助记
quart = quarter（四分之一），四分之一加仑——夸脱
quart::/kwɔːrt/:n. 夸脱（液量单位）:She bought a quart of orange juice at the store.
27. queer /kwɪər/ adj. 奇怪的；不寻常的 n. 同性恋者（口语）
📖 英文解释
strange or odd; not normal; (informal/identity) relating to a sexual or gender identity that does not conform to heterosexual norms
✏️ 例句
There was something queer about his behavior that evening.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研注意语境：传统义 strange/odd；现代常作身份认同用语；queer theory（酷儿理论）见于社会学文章
🧠 助记
queer 发音像'亏了'，遇到奇怪的事情觉得'真亏了'——奇怪的
queer::/kwɪər/:adj. 奇怪的:It seemed queer that no one had noticed the mistake.
28. quilt /kwɪlt/ n. 被子；棉被 v. 缝制棉被
📖 英文解释
a warm bed covering made of padding enclosed between layers of fabric and kept in place by lines of stitching; to make a quilt
✏️ 例句
Grandmother spent months sewing a beautiful patchwork quilt.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化类；patchwork quilt（拼布被）常作比喻：a quilt of cultures（文化拼图）
🧠 助记
quilt 发音像'哦被',棉被让人感到温暖——被子
quilt::/kwɪlt/:n. 被子:She wrapped herself in a warm quilt on the cold night.
29. quiver /ˈkwɪvər/ v./n. 颤抖；颤动；箭袋
📖 英文解释
to shake with small rapid movements; a slight trembling movement; a case for holding arrows
✏️ 例句
Her lip quivered as she tried to hold back her tears.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研描写类：quiver with fear/cold/excitement（因恐惧/寒冷/兴奋而颤抖）；双义词（颤抖+箭袋）
🧠 助记
quiver 发音像'快we啊'，快速颤动的感觉——颤抖
quiver::/ˈkwɪvər/:v./n. 颤抖:The leaves quivered in the gentle breeze.
30. razor /ˈreɪzər/ n. 剃刀；刮胡刀
📖 英文解释
a tool with a sharp blade used for shaving or cutting hair
✏️ 例句
He cut himself with the razor while shaving.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研搭配：razor-sharp（极其锐利的）；Occam's razor（奥卡姆剃刀，哲学原则）；razor-thin margin（极小的差距）
🧠 助记
razor 含 raze（夷平/剃除），用锋利刀刮除——剃刀
razor::/ˈreɪzər/:n. 剃刀:The team won by a razor-thin margin in the final match.
