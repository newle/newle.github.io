1. rectangle /ˈrektæŋɡəl/ n. 长方形；矩形
📖 英文解释
a flat shape with four straight sides and four right angles, where opposite sides are equal and parallel
✏️ 例句
Draw a rectangle that is twice as long as it is wide.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研数学/几何类：rectangular（矩形的）；rectangle vs. square（长方形 vs. 正方形）
🧠 助记
rect（直）+ angle（角），全是直角的图形——长方形
rectangle::/ˈrektæŋɡəl/:n. 长方形:The room was shaped like a perfect rectangle.
2. regarding /rɪˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/ prep. 关于；就…而言
📖 英文解释
concerning; in respect of; used to introduce the subject being discussed
✏️ 例句
I am writing to you regarding your recent application.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研写作高频：regarding = concerning = with regard to = as for；正式书面语
🧠 助记
regard（关注/考虑）+ -ing，正在关注某事——关于
regarding::/rɪˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/:prep. 关于:The manager issued a statement regarding the new policy.
3. remains /rɪˈmeɪnz/ n. 遗体；遗迹；剩余物
📖 英文解释
the parts that are left after other parts have been removed, used, or destroyed; a dead person's body; ancient ruins or artifacts
✏️ 例句
Archaeologists discovered the remains of an ancient settlement.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研考古/历史：human remains（人类遗骸）；the remains of（…的遗迹）；动词 remain（保持；剩余）
🧠 助记
remain（留下来）的复数，留下来的东西——遗体；遗迹
remains::/rɪˈmeɪnz/:n. 遗体:The remains of the ancient temple attracted many tourists.
4. reptile /ˈreptaɪl/ n. 爬行动物；卑鄙的人
📖 英文解释
a cold-blooded vertebrate animal such as a snake, lizard, or crocodile; an unpleasant or despicable person
✏️ 例句
Snakes, lizards, and turtles are all reptiles.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研生物类：reptilian（爬行动物的）；引申义指卑鄙小人；reptile brain（爬虫脑，情绪反应中枢）
🧠 助记
rept（爬，同 crawl）+ ile，爬来爬去的动物——爬行动物
reptile::/ˈreptaɪl/:n. 爬行动物:The museum has a collection of reptile fossils on display.
5. resultant /rɪˈzʌltənt/ adj. 由此产生的 n. 合力；结果
📖 英文解释
occurring as a result or consequence of something; the force or velocity that is the combined effect of two or more forces
✏️ 例句
The recession and the resultant unemployment caused widespread hardship.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研逻辑/物理类：resultant force（合力）；the resultant effect（由此产生的效果）
🧠 助记
result（结果）+ -ant，产生结果的——由此产生的；合力
resultant::/rɪˈzʌltənt/:adj. 由此产生的 n. 合力:High inflation and the resultant economic instability led to protests.
6. rim /rɪm/ n. 边缘；轮缘；框 v. 给…加边
📖 英文解释
the outer edge of something circular, such as a wheel, glass, or crater; to form or serve as an edge
✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：the rim of a glass/wheel/canyon（杯沿/轮缘/峡谷边）；rimmed glasses（有框眼镜）
🧠 助记
rim 发音像'沿'，轮子、杯子的边沿——边缘；轮缘
rim::/rɪm/:n. 边缘:The archaeologist found pottery with a decorated rim.
7. salute /səˈluːt/ v./n. 敬礼；致敬；迎接
📖 英文解释

✏️ 例句

🌍 考研用法 & 搭配

salute::/səˈluːt/:v./n. 敬礼:We salute the pioneers who built this great institution.

Saturday::/ˈsætərdeɪ/:n. 星期六:The community event is scheduled for Saturday afternoon.

scissors::/ˈsɪzərz/:n. 剪刀（复数）:She needed a pair of scissors to open the package.
