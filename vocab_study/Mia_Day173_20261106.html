1. tanker /ˈtæŋkər/ n. 油轮；罐车；加油机
📖 英文解释
a large ship, truck, or aircraft designed to carry liquid or gas in bulk, especially oil
✏️ 例句
An oil tanker ran aground off the coast, causing a massive spill.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研环境/交通类：oil tanker（油轮）；tanker accident（油轮事故）；fuel tanker（燃料罐车）
🧠 助记
tank（大缸/坦克）+ -er，装满液体的大家伙——油轮；罐车
tanker::/ˈtæŋkər/:n. 油轮:The oil tanker spilled thousands of barrels into the ocean.
2. Thanksgiving /ˌθæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ/ n. 感恩节
📖 英文解释
a national holiday celebrated in the United States on the fourth Thursday of November, originally giving thanks for the autumn harvest
✏️ 例句
Families across America gather for a turkey dinner on Thanksgiving.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化类：Thanksgiving Day（感恩节）；Thanksgiving dinner（感恩节晚餐）；感恩节后为 Black Friday
🧠 助记
thanks（感谢）+ giving（给予），给予感谢的节日——感恩节
Thanksgiving::/ˌθæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ/:n. 感恩节:Thanksgiving is a time for families to reunite and give thanks.
3. thigh /θaɪ/ n. 大腿
📖 英文解释
the part of the leg between the hip and the knee
✏️ 例句
The runner felt a sharp pain in her thigh during the race.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研医学/体育类：thigh muscle（大腿肌肉）；thigh bone = femur（股骨）；chicken thigh（鸡腿）
🧠 助记
thigh 发音像'拜'，大腿是用来弯曲跪拜的部位——大腿
thigh::/θaɪ/:n. 大腿:He strained his thigh muscle during the marathon training.
4. thorn /θɔːrn/ n. 荆棘；刺；痛苦根源
📖 英文解释
a sharp pointed projection on the stem of a plant; a source of constant irritation or trouble
✏️ 例句
She pricked her finger on the rose thorns.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研比喻义：a thorn in one's side/flesh（眼中钉；烦恼根源）；no rose without a thorn（没有无刺的玫瑰）
🧠 助记
thorn 发音像'痛'，被刺了会很痛——荆棘；刺
thorn::/θɔːrn/:n. 荆棘:The unresolved issue remained a thorn in the company's side.
5. Thursday /ˈθɜːrzdeɪ/ n. 星期四
📖 英文解释
the day of the week after Wednesday and before Friday
✏️ 例句
The weekly staff meeting takes place every Thursday morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
专有名词，首字母大写；来源于北欧雷神 Thor；Thurs./Thu. 为缩写
🧠 助记
Thor（雷神）+ 's + day，雷神之日——星期四
Thursday::/ˈθɜːrzdeɪ/:n. 星期四:The conference begins on Thursday and ends on Saturday.
6. topic /ˈtɒpɪk/ n. 题目；话题；主题
📖 英文解释
a subject that is discussed, studied, or written about
✏️ 例句
Climate change is a hot topic in international politics today.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研写作高频：topic sentence（主题句）；hot topic（热门话题）；off-topic（偏题）
🧠 助记
topic 发音像'托匹克'，放在顶上的话题——题目；话题
topic::/ˈtɒpɪk/:n. 题目:The professor asked students to choose a research topic carefully.
7. towel /ˈtaʊəl/ n. 毛巾；手巾 v. 用毛巾擦
📖 英文解释
a piece of thick absorbent cloth or paper used for drying oneself or wiping things
✏️ 例句
She grabbed a towel to dry her hair after the shower.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：throw in the towel（认输；放弃）= give up；paper towel（纸巾）；towel rack（毛巾架）
🧠 助记
towel 发音像'头我'，头的毛巾帮我擦干——毛巾
towel::/ˈtaʊəl/:n. 毛巾:He finally threw in the towel after three failed attempts.
8. trousers /ˈtraʊzərz/ n. 裤子（英式）
📖 英文解释
a garment covering the body from the waist to the ankles, with a separate part for each leg (British English; American English: pants)
✏️ 例句
He wore a white shirt and black trousers to the interview.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
英式英语，美式为 pants；考研词汇辨析；a pair of trousers（一条裤子）；wear the trousers（当家）
🧠 助记
trousers 发音像'拖着走了'，裤子拖着腿走路——裤子
trousers::/ˈtraʊzərz/:n. 裤子（英式）:She ironed her trousers carefully before the presentation.
9. Tuesday /ˈtjuːzdeɪ/ n. 星期二
📖 英文解释
the day of the week after Monday and before Wednesday
✏️ 例句
The election results will be announced on Tuesday evening.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
专有名词，首字母大写；来源于北欧战神 Tyr；Tue./Tues. 为缩写
🧠 助记
Tyr（战神）+ s + day，战神之日——星期二
Tuesday::/ˈtjuːzdeɪ/:n. 星期二:The committee meets every Tuesday afternoon at three o'clock.
10. typhoon /taɪˈfuːn/ n. 台风
📖 英文解释
a tropical cyclone occurring in the western Pacific Ocean or South China Sea, equivalent to a hurricane
✏️ 例句
The typhoon caused widespread flooding along the coastal areas.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研气象/地理类：typhoon season（台风季）；typhoon warning（台风警报）；与 hurricane（飓风）比较
🧠 助记
typhoon 发音像'台风'，中文直译——台风
typhoon::/taɪˈfuːn/:n. 台风:The typhoon forced thousands of residents to evacuate.
11. underlying /ˌʌndərˈlaɪɪŋ/ adj. 根本的；潜在的；基础的
📖 英文解释
lying under or beneath something; fundamental and important but not immediately obvious
✏️ 例句
The doctors searched for the underlying cause of her symptoms.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研议论文高频：underlying cause/problem/assumption（根本原因/问题/假设）；underlying meaning（深层含义）
🧠 助记
under（在下面）+ lying（潜伏），潜伏在下面的——根本的；潜在的
underlying::/ˌʌndərˈlaɪɪŋ/:adj. 根本的:The underlying message of the article was a call for reform.
12. up-to-date /ˌʌp tə ˈdeɪt/ adj. 最新的；现代的；当代的
📖 英文解释
incorporating the most recent information or developments; modern and not old-fashioned
✏️ 例句
Make sure your software is up-to-date to prevent security vulnerabilities.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：keep sb. up-to-date（让某人了解最新情况）；up-to-date information/technology（最新信息/技术）
🧠 助记
up to date，跟上到今天的日期——最新的；现代的
up-to-date::/ˌʌp tə ˈdeɪt/:adj. 最新的:The database must be kept up-to-date at all times.
13. used /juːzd/ or /juːst/ adj. 旧的；习惯的 modal. 过去常常
📖 英文解释
previously owned; accustomed to something; (used to) describing a past habit or state that no longer exists
✏️ 例句
I used to walk to school when I was young.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研语法：used to do（过去常做）vs. be used to doing（习惯于做）vs. be used to do（被用来做）——三种用法必考
🧠 助记
use（使用）的过去式，被使用过的——旧的；过去常常
used::/juːzd/ or /juːst/:adj. 旧的:She used to spend hours reading before bed every night.
14. vein /veɪn/ n. 静脉；叶脉；纹路；倾向
📖 英文解释
a blood vessel carrying blood toward the heart; a thin strip of a different shade in rock, ore, or marble; a mood or tendency
✏️ 例句
The doctor had difficulty finding a vein in which to insert the needle.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研多义：in the same vein（以同样的风格）；vein of humor（幽默的倾向）；对比 artery（动脉）
🧠 助记
vein 发音像'vane'（风向标），像血管一样在体内'指路'——静脉；纹路
vein::/veɪn/:n. 静脉:His argument continued in the same vein throughout the speech.
15. velvet /ˈvelvɪt/ n. 天鹅绒；丝绒 adj. 柔软光滑的
📖 英文解释
a closely woven fabric with a thick short pile on one side, giving it a soft luxurious feel; resembling velvet in smoothness
✏️ 例句
She wore a beautiful velvet dress to the concert.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研文化类：velvet revolution（天鹅绒革命，和平政治变革）；velvet glove（铁腕柔性手段）
🧠 助记

velvet::/ˈvelvɪt/:n. 天鹅绒:The velvet revolution transformed the country without violence.
16. vinegar /ˈvɪnɪɡər/ n. 醋；酸味
📖 英文解释
a sour-tasting liquid produced by fermenting dilute alcoholic liquids, used as a condiment or preservative
✏️ 例句
She added a splash of vinegar to the salad dressing.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研饮食/化学类：apple cider vinegar（苹果醋）；wine vinegar（葡萄醋）；比喻用法 vinegary（尖酸刻薄的）
🧠 助记
vini（葡萄酒）+ egar（尖刻），葡萄酒变酸了——醋
vinegar::/ˈvɪnɪɡər/:n. 醋:A small amount of vinegar can enhance the flavor of many dishes.
17. waken /ˈweɪkən/ v. 唤醒；觉醒；唤起
📖 英文解释
to stop sleeping or cause someone to stop sleeping; to become aware of or alert to something
✏️ 例句
She was wakened by the sound of thunder in the early morning.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：waken = awaken = wake up；waken to（意识到）；引申义：waken people's curiosity（唤起好奇心）
🧠 助记
wake（醒）+ -en（使），使某人醒来——唤醒；觉醒
waken::/ˈweɪkən/:v. 唤醒:The speech wakened a new sense of responsibility in the audience.

The report is due by Wednesday at the latest.

Wednesday::/ˈwenzdeɪ/:n. 星期三:The weekly review meeting is always held on Wednesday morning.
19. well-known /ˌwel ˈnoʊn/ adj. 众所周知的；著名的

known by many people; famous

Einstein is a well-known figure in the history of science.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：a well-known fact（众所周知的事实）；well-known for（因…著名）；对比 notorious（臭名昭著的）

well-known::/ˌwel ˈnoʊn/:adj. 众所周知的:It is well-known that regular exercise improves mental health.

to close and open one eye quickly as a signal or gesture; an act of winking
✏️ 例句
He winked at her across the room to signal that everything was fine.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研：not sleep a wink（一点没睡）；wink at（对…假装不见）；in the wink of an eye（瞬间）

wink::/wɪŋk/:v./n. 眨眼:He winked and said the plan would work perfectly.
22. X-ray /ˈeks reɪ/ n. X光；X射线 v. 用X射线检查
📖 英文解释
electromagnetic radiation of short wavelength, able to pass through most solid objects; a photograph made using X-rays; to examine with X-rays
✏️ 例句
The doctor ordered an X-ray to check for broken bones.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研医学/科技类：X-ray examination（X光检查）；chest X-ray（胸部X光）；引申义 X-ray vision（超强洞察力）
🧠 助记
X（未知）+ ray（射线），神秘射线能透视内部——X射线
X-ray::/ˈeks reɪ/:n. X光:The airport uses X-ray machines to screen all luggage.
23. yours /jɔːrz/ pron. 你的；你们的（所有物）
📖 英文解释
used to refer to something belonging to or associated with the person being addressed

Is this umbrella yours or mine?
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研书信体结尾：Yours sincerely（此致，正式）；Yours faithfully（谨启，英式正式书信结尾）；yours truly（我本人）
🧠 助记
your（你的）+ -s，属于你的东西——你的（所有物）
yours::/jɔːrz/:pron. 你的:The decision is ultimately yours to make.
24. yourselves /jɔːrˈselvz/ pron. 你们自己；你们亲自
📖 英文解释
used as the object of a verb or preposition when referring to the people being addressed; used to emphasize 'you'

You should be proud of yourselves for finishing the project.
🌍 考研用法 & 搭配
考研反身代词：Do it yourselves.（你们自己做。）；by yourselves（独自地）；注意与 yourself（单数）区分
🧠 助记
your（你们）+ selves（自己们），你们自己——你们自己
yourselves::/jɔːrˈselvz/:pron. 你们自己:You have only yourselves to blame for the misunderstanding.
25. zigzag /ˈzɪɡzæɡ/ n./adj./adv./v. 之字形；锯齿形；曲折前进

zigzag::/ˈzɪɡzæɡ/:n./adj./adv./v. 之字形:The road zigzagged through the mountain valley.
