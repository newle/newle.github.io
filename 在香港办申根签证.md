## Required documents (all documents should be presented in Spanish or English)

**1.** [![](https://www.exteriores.gob.es/_layouts/images/icpdf.png)**Schengen visa application ​form**](https://www.exteriores.gob.es/DocumentosSC/China/HONG%20KONG%20%28C%29/Visados/Impreso%20solicitud%20visado%20Schengen%20espa%c3%b1ol-ingl%c3%a9s.pdf "It opens in new window")**.** Each applicant must complete and sign an official application form, filling in each of its sections. If the applicant is a minor, one of their parents must sign the application. 

**2.** **Photograph.** A recent, passport-size, colour photograph, taken against a light background, facing forward, without dark or reflective glasses, or any garments concealing the oval of the face. Photographs of minors must not show any part of the adult holding them.  

**3.** **Valid, unexpired passport.** Original and a photocopy of the page or pages of the passport that contain biometric data. The passport must be valid for at least 3 months beyond the planned departure date from the Schengen territory. It must also contain at least two blank pages. Passports issued more than 10 years ago will not be accepted.  

**4.** **Travel medical insurance.** The insurance should be provided by EU or local insurance companies and it must cover the costs of repatriation for medical reasons or death, as well as for urgent healthcare and/or emergency hospital treatment for the entire stay and throughout the Schengen area. Coverage must be of at least €30,000 or its equivalent in local currency. If the application is for a multiple entry visa, the medical insurance must be valid for the first planned entry. The applicant must undertake to purchase insurance for future trips. 

**5.** **Payment of the [![](https://www.exteriores.gob.es/_layouts/images/icpdf.png)visa fee](https://www.exteriores.gob.es/Consulados/hongkong/en/ServiciosConsulares/Documents/Listado_de_Precios1_HK.pdf "It opens in new window") (in HK Dollars, by cash or FPS).** The amount of the visa fee is 90 euros for people aged over 12 and 45 euros for children of 6 to 11. Children aged 5 and under are exempt from the fee. Different fees are applied for nationals of Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Cabo Verde, Gambia, and Russia, pursuant to the respective Visa Facilitation Agreements formalized with these countries.   

  

**6.** **Documents corroborating the purpose of travel and the specifics of the stay, that the visa applicant has sufficient financial means, as well as the intention of the visa applicant to leave the Schengen area before the visa expires.**   

- Letter from your employer (must be from Hong Kong or Macau) or school confirming your leave.  
    
- Round trip Flight Bookings / Itinerary _(This Consulate General suggests you not to make any payments until your visa is approved.)​_  
    
- Accommodation proof covering whole period of stay in Schengen Area. _([![](https://www.exteriores.gob.es/_layouts/images/icgen.gif)Invitation​ Letter​](https://www.interior.gob.es/opencms/en/servicios-al-ciudadano/tramites-y-gestiones/extranjeria/regimen-general/carta-de-invitacion/ "It opens in new window") or Hotel Bookings)_ 
- 3-months bank statements issued by a local bank in Hong Kong or Macau
- Invitation letter from Spain stating the purpose and schedule of this trip _(for business trip or academic conference only)_  
    

**7.** **Hong Kong or Macau Residence Permit** -- Original and photocopy of Hong Kong or Macau ID Card. It must still be valid after returning from Europe. **If you are not a permanent HK resident, a valid HK residence visa will also be needed.**​  

  

**8.** If the applicant is a **minor**, application must include a notarized written consent signed by parent(s) who is not travelling together. The minor’s birth certificate must also be provided. The birth certificate should be legalized through Apostille or Diplomatic Legalization, except it is issued from Hong Kong, Macau, or EU Countries.​  

  

When necessary to assess the application, the Consular Office may request additional documents or data and may also ask the applicant to come in for a personal interview.
